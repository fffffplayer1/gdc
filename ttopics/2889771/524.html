....

 

- Talmid Show is a free-time project made for amusement.  When it's no longer fun, it's no longer working on Talmid Show.  If it reaches that point, and it has reached it before, I take a break.  I regather my thoughts and when I'm ready to work again I do, but it does not take over.  Talmid Show isn't a job, it's just a hobby, a highly interesting hobby. And I do like this hobby.  Everytime I make a new episode, I tend to discover new techniques to my overall animating method.  One can strive for perfection, even to only reach an unknown fraction of it in the ultimate.

 

- Also be open to improvisation.  If I'm in the middle of filming a scene, and I think, "Hey wait, this works," or "This makes things better," or "Scratch that, I'm going with this," there's nothing wrong with going ahead with that branch.  Talmid Show started from an idea.  It's good to keep using the powerful tool that is an idea.

 

- Also... Talmid Show was created under the guidelines of LEGO.com, and working within them has also forged a little tradition, but it is also still a guide.  Whatever the safety rules of LEGO.com are, Talmid Show should follow them.  Also, episodes should be made with the intention that they can and will be uploaded to LEGO.com and be viewed by LEGO.com's community.  To do this, 5 MB versions of episodes or episode parts have to be created, since that's a rule of the LEGO.com reality.  And of course they must be child-appropriate, at the very least to LEGO's standards, and at most to the best there can be.

 

 

So basically....

ClutchPowers99, I'm not sure if I can put your idea into effect exactly as written.  With my own ideas, they don't usually get translated to the computer screen in 100% consistency.  Sometimes I can get close, tho, usually details fall apart but then they get replaced with new ones.  But I want you to know, the idea starts it all, and your ideas may be of inspiration to future episodes, even in only small areas.  Getting deeper into the other Universes is something I've thought about, tho not too specifically yet.  Inspiration can be an influence to making great ideas, and I think you have inspired me, also in ways I have yet to see too.

 

So truly, I'm very thankful to you for sharing your ideas with me. Thank you!

 

 

 

Should anyone also have ideas to share, they are appreciated.

 

Also... I intend for Talmid Show to work within the same continuities established and continually being established in our stories here, in the LU Forum and elsewhere.  We can make connections where we see them, and for the most part they work.


Since there's only so much I can do when it comes to animating videos, jsyk, I also write stories sometimes. Sometimes they even get completed. But the best of things need not be reserved for Talmid Show videos... we can write them too.  "Pictures tell a thousand words" it is said, but words can make som pretty convincing pictures too.  We live with abilities that can translate our ideas in many ways.  Even if one way doesn't work out, there are other ways.

 

Speaking of writing I ought to write the next parts of Charcoal & Embers and Faces, and take part more in Trail of Flames.  Also, Soul Searching is cancelled, but it's not the last we'll see of Intrepid, Cyclone, my other characters, and their connections and relationships.  I have more ideas planned.  I don't plan to disappoint.

 

(I just read a book that was full of possibilities but it only followed through with a few - I don't want to write like that! )
