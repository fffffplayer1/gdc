Anyways, it's kind of annoying to me to always see posts like "I'm going away!" or "I'm leaving the MBs!" contaminating (interesting use of the word, hehehehe ) the "Most Liked" bar.  For one thing, I don't overly like the idea of likes, which subsequently means I don't like the like bar, but that's not the point.

 

Now, what I could be saying is that seeing those "Bye!" posts (as I shall now call them) being the most liked, almost makes it seem like we actually like it when people go away!  We should get a "Unlike!" button, so we can unlike all those "Bye!" posts instead, to show how we don't like it went people go away. Actually I'm torn on this.  I remember on the Creation Lab, when someone posted a creation with sad news, I would see its average rating at 3 out of 5 stars.  My theory on this:

 

50% of users see the sad news as horrible, and in sadness press the 1 star button because it is horrible.

50% of users see the sad news as horrible, but because it isn't polite to press the 1 star button, put politeness over honesty and press the 5 star button, just to show that they saw the creation and support the user who created the creation.

 

I'm the latter 50%!

 

But anyways, at the start of the Comic Sans MS font, I said "Now, what I could be saying" but I never said that was what I was actually saying.  Rather, I was saying what I could be interpreted as saying.  What I really am saying is that I am annoyed that these "Bye!" posts are so popular!  Is a post saying you're going away really all that much of a quality post?  Guess it's not me to decide.  Maybe I'm just meddling.  No wait, not maybe.  I really am meddling, and I'm thinking way too much into this.  No, I'm inserting myself way too much into all of this.  I'm inserting my mindset into what I assume everyone elses' mindsets to be.  I keep forgetting, EVERYONE IS NOT ME.  WE'RE ALL DIFFERENT.

 

This is a quality post, right?
