“Whoa.” Said Sam.

People were all around, talking, laughing, and trading. Nexus Force vendors stood each at their own stations, talking to one another, and everyone else. They stood there in awe for a few minutes, taking it all in.

“Come on, let’s go get our factions!” John said.

“Hello there!” he said when they arrived. “My name is Nexus Jay. You can call me Jay for short. Are you here to join the Nexus Force?”

“Indeed we are!” Said John.

“Ok, so, head on over to Brick Annex, and talk to Mardolf the Orange to get approval for Assembly, then come back to me with the Assembly Badge!” Jay said.

“Will do!” Sam said.
