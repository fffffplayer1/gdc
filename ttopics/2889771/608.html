According to the decision we made a few months back, that means we shall interfere and continue the Scattered Ashes until further notice. However, before we jump to writing, there are some things we need to plan. Aside from a general outline, which would be helpful, I think we need to deal with some important plots Thingguy has started in the story:

1. Most striking of all, the two Thingguys. Many speculations can be made on what has happened. My personal opinion is that they are either both real or both fake. An explanation I have not thought of though.

2. Thingguy mentions something about a map. What sort of map is this? Where does it lead to?

3. It was Thingguy's intention, I think, to have the events of Rhoddwr Marwolaeth occur at the time of this story. We should find the best way to connect the two stories.

 

I should also like to let you know that the 16th for me also signifies that in one month my exams (I believe I have mentioned these exams before) begin. Thus, I'm not certain how much I'll be able to commit myself in the labouring to make Scattered Ashes, but I don't suppose I'll have the time to write until the exams are finished.
