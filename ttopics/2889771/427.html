Nex5573 wrote:
I need help with making a good storyline itself.

Alas, that is perhaps one of my greatest weaknesses in writing. I'll help you as much as I can, but I'm no expert. Also, might I direct you to the topic called "Any Authors Still Around???" in this forum. A lot of people have given me some good advice concerning storylines on that topic, if you feel up to reading it.

 

Other than that, I recommend not beginning with the Venture Explorer. (Assuming that you are writing about LU) That's a somewhat over-used beginning, though there are some authors who manage to pull it off. It's lots of fun to start with mysterious scenes to hook your readers. I guess one of the most important parts of a good plot (in my opinion) is for it to be very intertwined with your character(s). It's simply no good to have a character who is in a story that he/she can't connect to and/or grow from. Both the character and the plot have to depend on one another to continue in complexity. Sorry if this isn't much help. I struggle with storylines a lot.
