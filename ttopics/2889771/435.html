My tips for writing a mystery story is to put in some red herrings. If you put in some not-exactly-related but very suspicious people/places/things/events/whateveryoudecidetodo it helps to throw the reader off and make it harder to guess what's going on. Plus, sometimes laying false trails for the readers ends up giving you ideas for new subplots, or even change the original plot in favor of a false trail that turned out better than it!

 

Hope this helps!
