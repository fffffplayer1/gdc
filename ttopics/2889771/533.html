It is only a few days after another dimension was obliterated. As always, many of the heroes (or villains) known to us have migrated to another dimension. However, soon many people come to the realisation that the Supreme Duckz Commander, most Duckz and the Duckz Empire are nowhere to be found. Usually duckz fill the place while the Empire and SDC tend to make themselves noticable. Thus, the lack of their appearance has made uneasy even people like Super Duck. Where are they? Some have started searching, but to no avail.

 

Then, suddenly a big projection of the Supreme Duckz Commander appears in some places of the dimension, as if there was a screen on the sky.

"Greetings, dimension! This is the Supreme Duckz Commander! Some of you might have noted my and most duckz' absence. I thought I should inform you that, despite destroying them, the Dux and Duxz incident has infuriated me. Thus, I have decided to leave you ungrateful fools forever! I have already left and this is a recorded message that I set to be transmitted 5 days afterwards, which is now for you who're listening to this. No matter how hard you seek me anywhere in the dimension, you won't find me! So, don't bother! However, due to my endless generosity, I'll grant you all a warning! They... are... coming! Farewell!"

The transmission ends and the Supreme Duckz Commander's projection disappears. Where had he gone, taking most duckz with him? And who was coming? Questions who may never be answered... until my next post!
