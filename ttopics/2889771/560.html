It all started half a year ago in a dimension far, far away.....

 

It was a peaceful world, Duckziton. There was peace throughout the land, and all the Duckz that lived there led happy lives and went about their day, happy as could be.

 

But not a curious Flapper McChicken, who was never really on anybody's good side, and hadn't many salts to speak of. (On this planet, they called money salts)

 

One peaceful day, Flapper looked to the sky from his house and saw an odd sight. A large, black speck was being followed by a small, bright pink speck.

 

As the thing got closer, Flapper realized that the black speck was a Cookie-Class Star Destroyer, and the pink speck was SuperDuck.

 

SuperDuck was obviously trying to catch up to the cookie-class destroyer, but alas, it hit the ground before she could stop it. (This isn't in the archives because this is a dimension nobody knows of, except Flapper McChicken.)

The heroes converged before the eyes of Flapper, and as he watched, a top hat poked out of the rubble.

 

The Supreme Duckz Commander.
