My story is the following:

I used to play games (and some times I still do) at those web pages that have many games available. Some of those games have leaderboards-thingies in which you have to put a name to see what your place is. One time I wrote something like that: "fffffffffff" (I don't remember the number of fs). When I did, I decided that I would become known on the Internet like that. So, when I made my LEGO ID I put five fs, because I didn't want to have too many fs and 5 is a good number. I put player, because I'd be a player of a game and I put 1, because it was the first such account I made (I later created a fffffplayer2 and a fffffplayer3). So, there it is: fffffplayer1!!!
