Oh, I also met some people in Crux Origins!!!

 

In response to Ro's question, I am going to set one, too. If you do not wish to answer it it's okay. What's the story behind your username?

 

My story is the following:

I used to play games (and some times I still do) at those web pages that have many games available. Some of those games have leaderboards-thingies in which you have to put a name to see what your place is. Don't feeling like thinking of some kind of name I would just press random keys the result being like this: aDsgrfjd. One time I wrote something like that: "fffffffffff" (I don't remember the number of fs). When I did, I decided that I would become known on the Internet like that. Thus, I wanted to start playing some kind of online game to have this as a username. And LEGO Universe came! So, when I made my LEGO ID I put five fs, because I didn't want to have too many fs and 5 is a good number. I put player, because I'd be a player of a game and I put 1, because it was the first such account I made (I later created a fffffplayer2 and a fffffplayer3). So, there it is: fffffplayer1!!! One day I may also continue this story by telling you why in my story the team of the hero is named FFFFF Team!

My dad named mine. I didn't care at the time, it was an account to play on LEGO Universe. Now I kinda care, but this is how I'm known, and I don't care anymore.  

I have you attention if you are reading this, and I suppose it will stay that way until this signature ends, or you could look away, but then you wouldn't get the fulfillment of reading through this, even though it is very boring.

Lukey3449, Founder of KOTOS
