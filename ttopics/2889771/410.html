Gizzy started off doing things he did every day: Take a bath, then head downstairs and keep on guard.

 

He found Sammy exactly where he expected him to be: Sleeping on the couch. However, Gizzy knew his friend, and he could snap awake  at any moment, tie on his super-cape he made, and fly off (litterally) as Supercat. "How's dat chariot 2.0 going?" Sammy asked, looking around.

 

"I've told you, it's a battle jet, but we can call it ze chariot. It is slow going, but it is getting done," Gizzy replied.

 

Then the alarm rang.

 

"Computer! Relay ze issue!" Gizzy meowed as he ran.

 

"Issue: Birds and rats combined attacking the fortress. Allies assisting: chickens. Engage with only nessisary firepower," the computer stated.

 

"We can't take dem! We are outgunned and outnumbered!" Sammy meowed.

 

"We'll just have to do what we can zen!" Gizzy hissed as he hit the attic door.

 

That's were the defenses were.

 

Sammy took an ion cannon on the left, and Gizzy manned a laser system on the right.

 

"Here dey come!" howled Sammy as the bird jets came into range.

 

"Zey're attacking from ze ground, too!" howled Gizzy as he left his station and went down to ground floor.
