Jam jams on. The same!

Jammers

                “I am investigating a case of some fugitives and I thought you might be of some help as the fugitives are known to have come to the area. Perhaps, you noticed anything suspicious? Did you see anyone?”

                Halfway through Brocktree’s explanation, Drum and the two other Nexus Force companions of the Knights, followed by Alfred, entered the room silently. The gathered minifigures looked at each other gravely. Could this man be up to something or did he just enquire for possible information the Lady could have as he had said? The woman must be asking the same question, but they hoped it would not show on her face. The little they knew of her, though, assured them she wouldn’t fail them, voluntarily or not.

                “I am afraid I cannot help you with that. I haven’t noticed anything out of the ordinary” she said.

                “Lady Charlotte, would you say that your loyalties lie with thedude? I am sorry to ask, but it is necessary”

                “I do not see the point of this question. King thedude visited me himself a few weeks ago to get an answer to very same question and I believe he left satisfied by the one I gave him. Did he not?”

                “If he didn’t, I have not been informed of such a thing. However, if new evidence occurs one must review the facts anew”

                “What new evidence?”

                “You have not answered my question?”

                “My loyalties lie with the King”

                There was a short silence as if Brocktree contemplated that or examined her and then “Which King?”

                “We only have one King, don’t we?”

                “And he is?”

                “thedude”

                “So, you wouldn’t say that you still consider yourself aligned with the outlaw, Matthias? You do not still consider him your King?”

                “Why would I? He is King of nothing”

                “Yet, are you still friendly to him or any of his accomplices?”

Jammers off

The Return of Jammers

Jammers

                However, as their heads turned towards the window, their hearts sank. A Paradox Rogue with a Wormholer in hand was looking at them menacingly through the window. It was a trap. The following events seemed to happen in slow motion. The moment their gaze fell upon the man he pulled the trigger of his weapon and a deadly blast was released from it. The window was shattered in a million little shards and everyone raised their arms over their heads protectively. They heard the sound of something heavy slumping on the floor. The same person who had urged them to get out of the window, being closer to it than all others, had been hit by the blast and fallen lifeless to the floor some metres from where he was standing previously. Two people, one being Drumr, screamed and that sound was accompanied by the sound of swords being unsheathed. The battle had begun.

                In all this fuss, no one really knew at which point the servant had got out of the door heading downstairs.

                “Drumr, Joss, get out of here!” Ruby commanded the Nexus-Forcers, while John charged at the Renegade “You may still be able to get out of here!”

                The Paradox Rogue had gotten to fire once more in the meantime, but he had missed and he didn’t get the chance to fire ever again. John’s charge had caused him to fall from the windowsill. The Knight looked below him, outside the window and saw more Rogues climbing ropes intending to get to them. He quickly got his head back in, for there were immediately many projectiles headed for him.

A new Jammer

Jammers
[Some fighting commences, but for now, the incoming intruders are not too many and the Knights can cope. However:]

                Suddenly, something small fell near the entrance. Squid and John jumped away only a moment before it exploded. Three minifigures entered the room through the smokes. Two of them were Paradox Rogues, but what was the third, the one in the middle? A Sentinel Rogue? He walked with such an air of importance and had equipment that seemed so superior that the Knights knew immediately that it must be Lord Brocktree.


[They fight Brocktree for a while, but they can see he's too strong, while more and more Rogues come. So, they escape through the window (with the very same ropes used by the Rogues to climb the wall of the Tower). However, even when they get on the ground, where enemies are less now, it's very hard to escape (survive I should say), until:]

                For, to the surprise of everyone, there was suddenly an explosion from the first floor of the Tower. A big part of the wall of the first level blew up and debris was flung all around resulting to the smashing of many of thedude’s soldiers. Dust and ashes were everywhere. This served as a diversion and created an opening for the Knights, who were not hurt, to run away from the fight.

                They started running through the debris and dust when someone jumped from the hole on the first floor. They looked to find Drum landing on the body of a fallen Shinobi.

                “Drum!” they exclaimed.

                “What about Joss?”

                “He didn’t make it”

[Through that chaos, they manage to get a headstart running towards the borders of Calibus Park and things seem good since their pursuers are slower. However, Brocktree reappears. The Knights manage to escape in some way which I shall not spoil and they reach the forest. There, they dress as Paradox Rogues with the suits of some Renegades they defeat]
