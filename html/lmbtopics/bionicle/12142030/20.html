"Brave Agori, Glatorian, Vorox and Zesk!" Teron begins.  "You are all noble beings, warriors and villagers alike - and yet we have found ourselves set against each other, enemies when we should be allies.  This is a state that will now no longer stand."

 

"I will not talk of the battle previously for long, for it was a vicious fight that should never have taken place.  Ultimately we are all beings who have a right to freedom from violence and pain.  Much better that we work together - and work together we shall!"

 

Teron hands over to Karzov.

"Once we were great allies; the peoples of Bota magna. Though we had our differences, and though they were many, we always found nessecity in each other. The Battle that took place was a sorely miscalculated loss, a deep misunderstanding that was the fruit of faults on both sides, I'm sure Teron can attest. But Together we can cure this black mark on our past. Together we can rebuild Foros ten times over in splendour and glory! Let it not be said that the Vorox, Glatorian, Agori and Zesk are primitive, like the beasts that stalk the jungles; let the name of Foros be heralded by the Great Beings of Spherus Magna, as we have reunited our peoples under single banner!"

Satisfied with Teron and karzov's speeches, Xherod teleports back to his fortress.

 

Now there are more pressing matters. 

 

Free from the sight of the villagers, Xherod falls to his knees in morbidity. The Legion were coming. This he knew for certain. Already the deeds of a small number he could feel across the girdle of the planet. It was as if they were playing a game of chess, but white's pieces were hidden around the room, and black had already begun to move. The legion was ready, and moving as it were.

 

But something was not right. The Legion had not attacked the planet as they should have done. The weapons systems aboard their ships could have smashed all living things in a year, and yet they had not even begun. Something was holding them back. 

 

Whatever it is, Xherod thought, I will make sure it gives us the time we need to find and prepare our...pieces...
