OOC: Kinda nervous, jumpy. Pretty shaken.

IC:

The villager turns and walks into his hut, slamming and locking the door.

The door is knocked on by a concerned Zesk.

"Go away! ....Actually, where is Teron?"

"How would I know?  If anywhere, he's probably in the Tri-Elemental Spire in the centre of the city; it looks important."

"Okay..."

Ferris(For that is who it is) exits via the back door and proceeds towards the SPire.

He reaches the Spire and goes looking for Teron.

The Hall Guard and Vorox Berserkers guarding the Spire don't let him in.  They do tell him, however, that Teron isn't present.

"Well, where is he then?"

One Glatorian of Earth shrugs, while a Beserker informs him of hearing a large roaring sound coming from near the production areas - maybe that has something to do with it.

OOC: This feels like an RPG game with quests.

IC:

Ferris thanks him and goes to the production areas.

OOC: Don't worry, it will end soon.

 

He arrives at a large factory constructing tanks, mechs and aircraft.  To one side of the factory he encounters Teron talking with Karzov and another, taller being that he doesn't recognise.

 

OOC: By the way, commandercody113 - could I go ahead with elaborating on the Landcrusher and Tremorcrusher (Teron's 'flagship Landcrusher') designs for a profile in the profiles topic?

OOC: Good.

IC:

Ferris is overcome with memories of the machines he used to build before the Plauge.  He waits for Teron to finish his conversation.

Teron raises his hand in farewell to the taller being as he flies off and continues talking to Karzov.  "So, what do you think about Kolokvar now?  He seems benevolent enough."
