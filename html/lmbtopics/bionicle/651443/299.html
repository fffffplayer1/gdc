"Shredder" and Furyix find themselves looking at the very big wall that used to be the top of the doorway.

 

"We can climb this. We're both strong enough. I'll just claw my way– AAAAAGH!" The shorter Dark Hunter recoils in pain from trying to dig into the wall: they are broken and twisted.

 

"Here, brother. I'll create the handholds." Furyix punches the wall, and continues to make dents as he climbs. "Shredder" slowly follows as best he can with his damaged hands.

 

Furyix reaches the top...

To discover that far, far below is a great, big ocean

"Go back down, there's not way we can do this."

"What? Why not?"

"It's all upside-down. The sky used to be a lot bigger than the ocean."

"Meaning...?

"The ocean is very far down."

"Blast."

 

Already struggling to climb up, "Shredder" is now forced to descend. He does so without much event. "Well? What now?"

"Let's go check on the others."

"Right."

 

"Shredder," aided by Furyix, goes to collect his squad.
