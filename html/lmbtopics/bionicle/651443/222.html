‎02-29-2012 09:56 PM

Toa Vezon walks in, and picks up the Mask of Light and Shadow. Another flashback occurs:
The mutated Toa Roland is in a boat, having left Artahka. He unwraps the two packages that he was given by his friend Artahka. One is a Kanohi Avokhii; one is a Kanohi Kraahkan. Each deciding his fate.
Torn between a path of light or darkness, Toa Roland attempts to put BOTH of the masks on at once...
Voices erupt from inside him as he manages to put both masks on, fusing them into the Mask of Light and Shadow; one of Makuta Teridax and another of Mata Nui. The voices together (plus the mask) too much to bear for him, he rips the mask off, letting it fall to the depths of the ocean.
Toa Roland ignored Artahka's words... and for that, he ignorantly mimics the form, and memory, of the seventh Piraka: Vezon. From that point on, he has been known as Toa Vezon, and even crafted himself weapons to fit those of his "memory."

Toa Vezon, ripped from the flashback, puts the mask on. Suddenly, his form begins changing. He is submerged in a burst of light and shadow...
"I am Toa Roland-Makuta Vorpal," he says, after the transformation is completed, "I have resolved my conflict and have a clear view of what my path is: A path of both Light and Shadow, serving Mata Nui for the best of all."

OOC: Although Toa Roland is now part Makuta, I will still be part of the Toa HQ!
