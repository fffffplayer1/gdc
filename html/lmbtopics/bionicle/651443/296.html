Alright, I'm gonna join, if that is ok.

Name: Devok

 

Speices: Skakdi

 

Mask: None, due to being a Skakdi

 

Element: Iron

 

Gender: Male

 

Weapons: Zamor Launcher with green and black zamors, giant hammer

 

Power's and abilitys: Due to tampering ago from the Makuta Spiriah, he can use Iron powers without having to be with another Skakdi of the same type. He also is very strong, even stronger than a toa, and has tons of endurance. He also has heat vision. His green Zamors are filled with antidermis, which can turn matorian into mindless slaves and even do a bit of damage to whoever it hits. His black zamors can track a target from anywhere when they hit via and energy pluse. He prefers, however, to batter and smash his opponets to submission with his giant hammer. His hammer, when hit aginst the ground, can generate shockwaves to knock down his opponets, not as big as an earthquake, but still enough to knock down a toa.

 

Bio: Once an Skakdi on the Island of Zakaz, everything changed when an myesterious being rumored to be an makuta appeared and started turn the Island's inhabitants from peaceful creatures into complete monsters, including Devok. The myesterious figure took special interset in Devok due to his rare element of Iron, and made him even stronger than the rest of his fellow Islanders. Devok was able to escape though, and went in hiding for many years. After a while, the makuta died, and Devok came out of hiding, but he was now bitter and cold and still hungry for vengance aginst the Makuta for what they did to his home. Becoming an dark hunter, Devok has a severe hate for the makuta, but in the end is neither good or evil, he works for whichever side pays more.

 

Appearance: A tall Skakdi with a jet black body with red lines all over that glow along with his red eyes when he is angry. He also has bigger feet than an normal Skakdi which he uses as an advantage in combat.

 

If you have any questions or if I made any mistakes, please tell me, this is my first time roleplaying!
