Work on projects Nova and Genesis begin.

(Snipped the jammers)

Lab experiments involving many variants of elemental technology are performed in Oculus Magna's labs.

Someone on the Hau Nui throws a rock out into space. It is then pounded with elemental stone energies, growing it rapidly.

     Half of the elemental experiments are jettisoned and activated. Some fizzle and do nothing while others cause varying degrees of elemental explosion. All are monitored and taken into account for the next round of experiments.

The other half is tested inside for non-explosive reasons. As expected, none of them accomplish what they are supposed to. New ones are designed.

     The rock gets to the size of a small mountain and is then filled with enlargement energies. It grows to immense size, and the Hau Nui starts to cover it with a thin layer of metal; half a foot thick at the most.

Hau Nui receives a warning transmission from Manas, stating that the asteroid easily capable of wiping out most of the life in a Wahi is a potential threat and must be demolished.

     Hau Nui sends a response transmission that that thought had not even crossed their minds; However, they see the potential threat and agree to remove it.

     A few pulses of Pehkui energy later, and they are retrieving some very dense protosteel from a mountainous sphere. Said sphere is destroyed via Jutlin, though the shrunken and densified metal is saved for experimental purposes.

     Another round of elemental devices; Another round of explosions, and another round of failed mixtures of energy. Rinse and repeat.

♫...When allied together a threat men display...♪
♪...Divide them with doubt it will all wash away...♪
