starwars7495 wrote:

@White Alligator and the Bionicle Roleplay Comunity

 

 

But wait. What if a character, such as mine, (Korvohk), has a Mask of Gravity? A Mask of Gravity is a relatively common, (maybe not common, but you know what I mean), mask to have, is it now illegal to have them? I also just want to voice my opinion, but... a lot of what makes this roleplay fun is a lot of the OP-ness.

 

The theme of Bionicle has so many abilities to choose from, mix and match, and they're so much fun to use, that it gives the players a huge range of freedom to do absolutely ridiculous stuff, like create a miniature sun and suck someone into it, or use gravity to level walls and structures. That may sound unfair to other players in the Roleplay, but they are just as powerful as the next, and getting sucked into a small sun aint no big deal, and as for destroying buildings, the Makuta, Toa and others can reconstruct them in around two posts easily.

 

And all of these abilities exist in the Bionicle World. Unlike in Star Wars where only a few weapons that are considered to be OP exist, (Death Star, The Force, Etc.), but in Bionicle items with abilities like that, (maybe not as powerful as a Death Star), are a dime a dozen, and basically every user on this Roleplay has an item that could be considered OP.

 

What I'm saying, is that the Bionicle Roleplay as it is, grants much more freedom in terms of fighting, defending and building almost to an OP level, but it isn't OP here because everyone technically is OP. This makes our Roleplay one of the more unique Roleplays in the forums.

Garai are legal.

 

Well… Look at canon. How often are buildings leveled by a person in a second? Besides, it would take a lot of fun out of RP if you could win instantly.

 If I may, if everybody is OP, then really, OP is no longer OP, because there is nobody to overpower except NPCs, unless those too are made as powerful.

Where in the fun in it, though?
