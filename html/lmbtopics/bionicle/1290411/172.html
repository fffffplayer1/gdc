OOC: Ah. I see.

IC: *I shoot a teleport disk at it, making it appear somewhere in Spherus Magna* *I shoot a large pulse from my Staff of Erasing at you*

Slicer_Vorzakh

*I form a wall of earth in front of me, which the staff takes its effect on instead of me*
*a flash of green appears and the golem forms again behind me*
This is powered from powder with the power to give matoran the strength of makuta (and Fikou Spiders the power of Kanohi Dragons, but thats a story for a different time), he can't just be poofed away by a simple staff.
*it roars and sends a white hot blast of fire at you*

*I shoot a freeze disk at the fire, turning it into water* *I flip onto it's back and ride it like a horse, confusing it*

OOC: Is this thing mindless? It's just stone.

ooc: kinda, its controlled by me
ic: *I leap into the air and kick you in the side, making you lose your grip and fall to the ground so far below*
*I fire a blast of water at you*

OOC: Wait, if you're saying so far below, how tall is it?

IC:*I shoot a freeze disk at it, before it hits me* *I fire crosses between electricity and Staff of Erasing pulses*

Slicer_Vorzakh

ooc: really big. During my battle with sepublic, I waited a couple turns to make it. Plus its charged with a really powerful substance from a mine where Voidarix originated
ic: *I splatter you with a thin stream of water, then manipulate that into the blasts from the electricity staff, which causes a chain reaction that causes you to be severely shocked*
*a large boulder rises out of the ground and catches all of the staff of erasing blasts*

OOC: The staff of erasing and electricity were not seperate, they were one type of pulse. Also, I'm powered by electricity. I can't really be electricly shocked......

ooc: okay, I'll re-do it, but if enough electricity got into your system, there would probably be an overload.
ic: *I raise a boulder out of the ground and make a stream of water come off it which catches the blast. the erasing part destroys it while the electricity hits the water. I move it to you where the overload makes it hard to move*

OOC: That still doesn't work, because the electricity was part of what charged me, and you'd just be returning my energy back to me.

ooc: but wouldn't there be an overload your system couldn't handle?

OOC: No, because I didn't make new electricity after I shot it out. You would be restoring my health.

ooc: fine, I'll do it again a third time
ic: *I send a blast of fire at the bursts, knocking it off course and electrifying a nearby matoran, then makes its right arm disappear*
What's the matter? I thought Vahki were supposed to look out for matoran, not blow 'em up!
*I send a tidal wave of water towards you*

OCC: Better.

IC: At this point, if I don't fight you, they won't be worth protecting.*I teleport for a second until the water washes away* *I teleport back and shoot you with many RaR disks*

Slicer_Vorzakh
