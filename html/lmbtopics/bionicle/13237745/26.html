"Behold, the dream is cast.  The rest is yours."

 

With sudden, frantic energy, the villagers go to work, beginning the bare groundwork of the city before them.

Within his newly conjured dwelling, The Uncreator floats unmoving in the center of an entire galaxy, surrounded by countless bright and shining tiny orbs of light and color, all clustered together around him into a slowly swirling spectacle of mesmerizing wonder. The blackness of space stretches endlessly in all directions, with the faint twinkle of many other galaxies just barely visible in the far distance.

 

 With a push of his will, the galaxy begins to rapidly expand, with stars and celestial bodies growing larger and larger as he zooms in on a single location within the star system: the Solis Magna System. The great ball of fire hovers in front of him, barely larger than a Zamor sphere compared to his enormous presence. Locked in a slow orbit around the sun is none other than an equally-miniature Spherus Magna, just one of many, many more celestial bodies and vibrant nebulaes.

 

 As he is observing some yet obscure attribute of the galaxy, something else grasps his attention... something unnaccounted for. The astral map dissolves into sections and fades, revealing a fairly large and rounded chamber, with The Uncreator standing in the center upon a raised, floating platform. He vanishes in a flash of blue light, and the platform sinks to the floor in his absence.

 

 In another powerful flash of bright blue light, he reappears atop the peak of the Wahi's volcano, at the edge of the basin. With a margin of interest in the unexpected, he gazes out upon the dazzling holographical structures of a city that certainly did not exist a moment ago. Within an instant of further observation, he notes that the shimmering skyline stretched out before him is but a blueprint; he identifies that the true construction beneath is being executed with vigorous enthusiasm by all the residents of a nearby shanty village.

 

"Peculiar."

 

 The Uncreator slightly enhances his field of view, and quickly spots the shining makuta standing across from himself on the opposite end of the crater.
