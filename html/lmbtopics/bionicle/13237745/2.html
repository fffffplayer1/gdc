Jalkron resurfaces at the bottom of the volcano and enters into the secret base.  Upon entering he keys a comm and says, "I'm back.  Let's talk."  He heads towards the center of the mountain.

 

The other three toa are there when he arrives.  "Did it work?" Rullz asks.  "Yeah, it did.  Remind me not to do that again, it hurt worse than karzanhi.  Have you finished installing the weapons systems?"  "Yes, we did." Dralik said.  "You have no idea how much I want to use them right now."  Ender rolled his eyes.  "Trust me, we can.  You've only been talking about it since we started."

 

"Enough bickering.  The makuta have a base in every single Wahi, meaning if they were to declare war they would already have very defendable areas from which to start invading each Wahi.  We need to take them out, one way or another.  They cannot be allowed to stand.  We need to form plans to take them out, but we have time.  I want to be the first to strike, but for now the makuta have tried to keep their relations with us intact, they will not attack just yet.  We each pick wahis, scout out the makuta bases, and bring back every peice of info you can.  Don't be noticed, and report back after each base.  Let's go."
