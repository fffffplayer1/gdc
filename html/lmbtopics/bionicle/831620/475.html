NAME: Kiram [KEE-RAHM]

 

TOA OF: Shadow

 

WEAPONS: Blade of Shadow, Rotating Shadow Blades and Modified Zamor Sphere Launcher (mounted on shoulder)

 

GENDER: Male

 

AGE: Around 15 or 17

 

KANOHI: Mask of Conjuring

 

POWERS: Shadow

 

PERSONALITY: Smart, wise, and quick in planning. Can be angered if his friends are hurt, or his friends are in danger. He shows that not all Shadow is evil. He is kind, and knows that fighting is not the key to everything.

 

HOME WORLD: Unknown, he has amnesia.

 

COLORS: Black and White.

 

HISTORY: Kiram does not know much about his past as he is an amnesiac, but he does know he is a Toa of Shadow. He knows he is a Toa and must therefore do his duty as one: Protect those who cannot protect themselves. He has no evil intentions, and is trying to master his powers and harness them for good. He does not know what it is to have a friend and to be one, because he never had one. Therefore, he does not fit into society well. They think he is evil, because he wields Shadow, and he does not have any experience in socializing. He is completely against the Makuta for the evil and harm they cause.
