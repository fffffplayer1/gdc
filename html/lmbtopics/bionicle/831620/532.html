Powers:

 

  • Twirkle is simply liked by other people, even hostile ones! There's just something about her that everyone loves and no one can place.
  • With at least half-strength remaining, monstrous, powerful creatures (everything from kraata to Manas crabs) seem to melt into slavish, fawning Rahi when she gives them her attention.
  • Twirkle can see the best in people, as well as their potential, and can also sense their powers. The capability of seeing clearly in the most inky darkness. The power of seeing through all but the most potent illusions.
  • By touching someone, Twirkle can make someone feel the way she wants them to: calm, exhilerated, afraid, heroic, etc.
  • Given time, she can force even strong-willed beings to preform acts of pacificy, such as throwing aside their weapons, taking off their masks, dismissing guards, etc. This is not mind control; it only lasts for a moment. It's very related to #4.

Weaknesses:

 

  • Twirkle is often afraid to use her own powers, and just dislikes them in general.
  • She normally tries to calm people down rather than fight, and would rather flee, even then.
  • She is deeply affected by speeches.
  • Twirkle refuses to touch weapons, except, perhaps, to destroy them.
  • She was bred to fight Makuta and their minions. While fighting them, she feels calm; battling anything else, say, a Toa, makes her feel like a fish out of water.
  • Twirkle hates loud noises more than anything.

 

Note: Twirkle's mask can do the same things as her element, it just makes her more powerful.
