*Karunax tears through one last door leading into a small chamber. Inside are two beings: A shocked Toa of Psionics, and a oblivious Toa Karun. Before the Toa of Psionics can react, Karunax uses elasticity to stretch his arm the length of the room, striking her across the mask with a resounding crack. Her Kanohi shatters to pieces, and she falls to the ground without so much as a grunt. Confident that she wouldn't be getting back up anytime soon, Karunax strides across the room and grasps Karun by the throat, lifts him into the air, and slams him into the nearest wall*

 

"I knew I'd find you here, you miserable little stone rat."

 

*Karun simply stares past him, eyes unfocused, with a blank expression on his mask. Karunax smirks*

 

"Looks like our separation took a heavier toll on you than I thought. I suppose you've even forgotten how to scream."

 

*Karun's eyes slowly settle on Karunax, but eventually drift away. Karunax's smile vanished, and he tightens his grip. The Toa of Iron makes a choking sound, but does nothing to try and free himself. His eyes, however, do drift back to stare into Karunax's. The Makuta saw nothing. Eyes widening, Karunax dropped the Toa and stepped back*

 

"Without me... what are you? What have you become?"

 

*Karun simply slumped to the floor without a response*

 

-Makuta Karunax, First Lieutenant-

*Having followed Karunax in, Arxtann is just in time to see his discourse with Karun*

If you don't think he's in your class Makuta, then why not try that on someone your own size.

 

*Arxtann does the next best thing and unleashes all the heat he absorbed at Karunax. 10,000 degrees Kelvin wash over him*

Hello, fellow Toa. I have been thinking about joining this HQ for a bit now, and wondered if you would accept my enlistment.

 

 

Name: Toa Korvohk

 

Description: A Toa of Ice. Seven and a half tall, somewhat thin… and wiry. Extremely agile, and possessing strength one would think would belong to a more muscular being. A smooth-talker, who never misses the opportunity to shoot his mouth off. Loves to joke, especially when fighting. Semi insane… but not a danger to other Toa. A rather dark Toa, and prefers to work alone. Inventive.

 

Past: No one knows...

 

Abilities: Can accomplish almost any acrobatic feat possible. Due to a glitch up in his creation, his voice seems to carry a sense of hypnosis in it, and he can sway lesser being to do his will and disturb ones with stronger minds. Is able to fire energy bolts from his fingertips, (and by clapping hard creates a small sonic shockwave), and can turn his hands and arms into razor-like acid swords. If needed, he can create a shield that protects him by using the energy particals in his fingers and hands. Extreme durability and stamina.

 

Mask: Kanohi Garai, the mask of gravity.

 

Weapons: Prefers not to use weapons. Of course he carries one weapon though… one he created himself. An energy staff that can extend in length, decrease in length, shoot powerful blasts of energy, channel his power, or just temporarily electrocute anything it touches.

 

 

If you did find me worthy enough to join, I would like to know these few things.

 

1. Who is in charge?

2. Who are we fighting currently?

3. Are there any missions I could take?

4. Do I need to join a team?

5. Do you have a specific place I need to live in? I'm a bit of a wanderer...
