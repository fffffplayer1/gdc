IC: Anranok has simply been building up energy behind the stasis field, and when ready, he simultaneously activates his teleport power and invulnerability power to appear right in front of you and be, for at least a few seconds, completely immune to all damage. When he appears in front of you, he unleashes a highly focused mini-nova blast of shadow from his hand into Tredixs, who is all of six inches away from Anranok's hand.
To put it simply, Tredixs is being hit with a blast that, if it wasn't focused into a person sized area, would be able to level nearly a third of Metru-Nui.

OOC: It is possible to make the magnetic field into a reflective like force field and redirect the attack. Or it would at least just absorbed it and maybe sent a little bit towards Anranok. Tredixs like others who can control electricity can control the bioelectric currents in brains of other people of creatures. This is assuming these Rahi have one, but he has done this to a Dark Hunter to make him use his own psionic attack against himself. I always disliked that Makuta are a gas, nothing to redirect psychic attacks.

IC:   *Tredixs is sent flying and is eventually hurled into the ground, leaving a crater. The previously controled surge of electricity is now out of control with Tredixs not currently able to concintrate on it being most likely knocked out.
