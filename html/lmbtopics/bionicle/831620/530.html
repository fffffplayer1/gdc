OOC: Storylines and plot twists are a major part of RPing.

IC: *Inharax lowers his blade.*

Teranis. . . Back when I was Toa Argai, in another dimension, there was a Toa of Lightning I worked with near the Island of Treachery. Some Matoran (OOC: one of them being the namesake of my ooooooold HF RP character that is still alive after all these years. ) wanted to explore the island, and we went there. I was trampled by a herd of Kikanalo, and I had to be taken to be fixed. The Makuta promised they would fix me, so they took all my memories and turned my organic parts to Antidermis- a new experiment of theirs.

I met Teranis again and we started anew, she knowing who I once was but never telling me. Soon later, the Makuta turned against Mata Nui and I refused to join them. I tried to take Teranis with me through a teleport device to the Red Star, but she was caught between the Toa Mangai, thinking her a traitor, and Antroz, Gorast, and Kojol, thinking her to be another Toa. I lost my greatest part of me that day.

I then - I shouldn't be telling you this, you may not understand - used the Red Star to send me to another dimension, frying its engines. I have now risen through the ranks of the Makuta to Head Scientist. I was turned back into a Toa once. . . But I am Makuta again. I lost all purpose in life after I was destroyed the first time, and ever since then I have been wanting everyone else to feel the same. What about you?
