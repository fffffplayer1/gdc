No?

*Zaylax draws a crackling sword that turns into a Rhotuka and launches itself into some random toa*

~Ultimate Tritan~

Alright. But first, let's take on the fifty Toa that just tackled me.

And yes, SDR, I still fight strongly in my struggle for ultimate peace, and Come closer all the time, recently it has seemed as if the time shall be soon when all HQs are finally united.

~Ultimate Tritan~

*breaks free of ice* Tritan, you have betrayed us all. I know you are determined to, under any circumstance, gain power over everyone. I allowed you to join the Toa to give you a second chance, but, I must admit, to keep an eye on you. I also let you join the Toa Zarrex, hoping you could learn from the kindness of those such as Toa Zulu. Instead, you shrunk away from it all, formed your own, secret, groups, and accepted an unimaginable burden. Thinking your plan was as it is now, I forced your failure at Nemorax's Task. I was a fool when that happened, but now I know I must intervene in your plan to a greater extent. *I ready my midak skyblaster*
Ultimate Avkazot

It is war. War is too great for a one on one battle. War is peace. Peace is war. All out war is impossible. All out peace is impossible. We force our own destinies. I fight for truth. I fight for nothing. I speak for truth. I speak for nothing. You blindly fight each other. Without meaning. You can also make peace... without meaning. Perhaps a lesson is to be learned... That betrayal does not exist. No one is ever permanently loyal and pure to a single group. Everything has a purpose, but the purpose may be nothing. But one is not the only being of the blind. If one is blind, we are all blind. If one is a fool, we are all fools. It is not a team, but a relation. If one feels a certain way, we all feel that way. Balance is Corruption. Corruption is Balance. We are the same... just from different experiences. We all fail to realize this by not hesitating. Hesitation makes us stronger and weaker than everyone else.

*My transforming sword separates itself into two daggers.*

*The two daggers grow into copies of both of you*

You will blindly fight, you will blindly make peace... But everything ends. The Beings of Balance both died, when they were meant to live forever. The Cursed One disappeared, when he was meant to haunt everyone. If one fails... we all fail. If one dies... we all die.

*one of the copies kills the other, and falls down and dies immediately after the other one dies*

If one betrays another... we all betray each other.

Iun, Are you saying that Tritan and I are somehow connected?
Ultimate Avkazot
