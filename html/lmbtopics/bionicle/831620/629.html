"I did not just fight my way through hordes of Dethalx and go through several very deadly tests just to finally come home to have someone tell me I'm the bad guy."

"In war the lines between Good and Evil, the Light and the Dark, Construction and Destruction, are often blured. The longer you go into constant fighting and the longer you stay in the Dark Pit, the more it corrupts you. You begin to think that the only way to defeat your enemy is to take up their ways of doing things and to 'fight fire with fire'. I tell you, who ever 'fights fire with fire' is a fool. For if you fight like your enemy and use the same things they do, they have already won the battle. It matters not if they lose the war if you become like them and approve of their ways. Becoming what you were suppose to defeat is the very thing they want. I say fight fire with water or covering it with dirt or even choking the flames by taking its air away. If you stay in the Light and follow the Will of Mata Nui and never stray you will win. Nothing destroys Darkness' Grip like standing up in what is right and in your ways without changing despite what pain they may deal.

 

Though you two are corrupt, as most of the other Toa as it would seem, there is hope. I was created for this very purpose, in the case the Toa would trample the Code underneath their feet and become like the Makuta. I am here to restore the Code and the Virtues you have let go of. I am Veihrl, which is the Matoran word for Virtuous Healer; I am a Warrior of Artahka, a member of his Hand, and Servant of Mata Nui. Help me, for I cannot put the Toa back into place alone."
