OOC:In retrospect, I realize it's awkward to have not a single drop of back story. So, if you allow, I'll give you 4, short IC   stories to explain my back-story.

part 1

IC:Vinkius stares at the grey stone Toa Kerzuq had given him. After a little hesitation, he placed it at the Suva. A great light shone out, and he could see the Po-matoran and the Ba-matoran next to him bathed in light as they grow. He noticed the same thing was happening to him. A blinding light showered his sight. when the light subsided, he saw 5 strong toa standing close by him. He looked at himself and in shock, it dawned on him: He was a Toa.

part 2

IC:Vinkius sat at his lab within a vast cave, underwater, beneath the island of Voya nui. There was a rumble, followed by an earthquake. He just shrugged it of and returned to his work he was sure this anti-matter cannon will be the deadliest weapon ever. The quality of weapons are unrivaled, even by xian-made weapons. He smiled for a minute, and look out of the mouth of his cave, which was sealed by glass. He saw a great big mass of land falling from the surface. The last thing he saw was a white warlord escaping a pit before the glass shattered under pressure and he began to drown...

part 3

IC: The dead Toa of iron's body, which was found in the beach, began to vibrate as the ebergy of life itself flowed into his body. His corrosion are healed, his organics restored and his very form alters slightly. The dead warrior opened his eyes for the first time in over 2 millenia. Toa Vinkius is reborn.

IC: Toa Vinkius held in his hand the Kanohi Vahi, which he had obtained with much difficulty. With it, along with the Olmak he accidentally brought, perhaps he could go home. He made his decision.
