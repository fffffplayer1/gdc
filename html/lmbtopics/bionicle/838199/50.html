NAME: Toa Loki
MASK: Kanohi Hau
ELEMENT: Fire
POWERS: Fire Blast, Armor Destruction, Sword Slash, Soul Demolition
ATTACK POWER: Fire Blast ( FB ) Blasts a wave of flames at an enemy. Armor Destruction ( AD ) Destroys an enemy's armor. ( depends on how strong ) Sword Slash ( SS ) A simple sword attack. Soul Demoliton ( SD ) Stuns the enemy for a short time.



BIO: As a Ta - Matoran, Loki was one of the guards at the Ta - Koro gate. Admiring the Chronicler, Loki set out on his own adventures with his friend, Rohu. Loki and Rohu set out on another adventure, not knowing it may be their last. Loki was looking for a hidden cave near Le - Wahi. Loki and Rohu got seperated when a group of Nuhvok attacked them. Rohu has never been located since. Loki, feeling depressed at his friends dissapearence found the hidden cave. Loki finds an ancient tablet that predicts one Toa's destiny. Loki picks up the tablet, thinking that he must take it to Turaga Vakama. The caves entrance is blocked off by falling rocks. Several days later, Levahk attack the side of the cave, and Loki sneaks out of the new opening undetected. Loki ran and ran until he reached the outskirts of Le - Koro. Loki sits down and starts to weep, but suddenly, the ground opens up and Loki falls through the sinkhole. Unconscious, Loki learns in a dream that the prophecy on the tablet is about him. Loki realizes that he must become one with the three virtues. "Unity......Duty.......Destiny......" he thinks. Loki wakes up to a rumbling sound. A mask falls from the opening in the sinkhole. " You know what you must do......" a deep voice grumbles from somewhere. Loki puts on the mask that has fallen to him and transforms into Toa Loki. He rises up from the sinkhole. His gold armor gleaming in the sunlight, one long fire sword burns in his hand. One thing echoes in Loki's mind...... " I must help fight Makuta. Mata Nui needs all the help he can get." Toa Loki walks off into the Le - Koro jungle, knowing what he must accomplish, knowing his destiny..............
