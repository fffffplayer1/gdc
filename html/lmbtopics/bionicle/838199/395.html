Name: The Doctor (TD)

Affiliation: All and None

Species: Unknown

Powers: The ability to instantly know the workings, inner and outer, of any being, regardless of species, mutations, or otherwise, merely by glancing at it. He can also determine genetic makeup of any organic meterial, as well as the former state of any object.

Masks: Mask of Telekinesis, Mask of Reanimation, Mask of Regeneration, Mask of Truth, Mask of Wisdom.

Backstory: The Doctor, whose real name is unknown, was created along with Mata Nui. He was made as the ultimate healer, able to fix, heal, or repair anything. His purpose was to bring life and order into the Matoran Universe, instead of destruction. He was, is, and will never be affiliated with any race, tribe, group, cult, or any other bias. He lives only to serve the universe.

Equipment: The Doctor carries with him a pocket universe, from which he can get the materials neccessary for any repair beyond his mask abilities. For example, he has a nearly endless supply of Masks of Elemental Energy. He also has a great pocket dimension full of Antidermis, which he uses to repair Makuta.

Other Information: The Doctor can be called to help by any user, regardless of who or what they are, or who they are affiliated with, by merely stating the words "Doctor, Doctor, please help me!". The Doctor will then come, and help heal you. He will not, however, help you fight. If you trick him into helping you fight, he will be very, very angry.
