Idrisk is now complete, rising into the sky like a giant stalagmite. It could almost be described as beautiful if its walls were not so emotionless and threatening. Boasting hidden weapon systems, legions of visorak, and complete monitoring equipment. This tower was built to reflect the cold, calculating, personality of Charix in addition to showcasing the latest in technological marvels that have spun out of the head scientist's head. 

 

A visorak manning some of the monitoring equipment begins running an in depth scan of the area to see just how many Order members are running around.
