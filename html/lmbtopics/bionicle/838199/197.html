Meaning?
*I form an axe in my hand and use it to propell me to my feet, though I sway back and forth*

Meaning that there's a system of tunnels nearby, I just opened it, and made the sinkhole a lot less stable. One landslide might become a whole cave-in.

Soooo... your willing to bring this thing down on both of us, eh? Heh, looks like you ARE darker than most Toa. Looks like this is out of my control. Well, on the bright side
*two chunks of rock break off the walls of the tunnel, turning into golems*
I still get the satisfaction of taking you down.
*The golems pick you up and throw you against the wall. One pins you down while the other one starts gathering energy in its mouth*

"You, sir, are even darker (but I should expect that from a full-Makuta). You insist on destroying me when a route of escape is available for both of us!"

*I kick the one golem off of me, then headbutt it*

Toa Roland-Makuta Vorpal

Oooh... thanks Toa!
*I bolt for the escape, and when I pass through, I shoot an energy blast at the ceiling, causing a bunch of boulders to fall down.*
*The other golem shoots the energy from its mouth in a powerful beam, knocking you into a wall. Both golems morph into a coat of mud and rocks around you, trapping you... again. The dust from the landslide makes it stronger, again*
buh-bye, Toa!

I quickly make calculations in my mind; as the landslide tumbles down, a quick decision comes to my mind...

If you were to be able to look back, here's what you'd see (): *I emit a sphere of light, then release it, smashing into the walls of the sink hole at high force, collapsing them*

While you're walking in the tunnels , this is what you see/feel (): *a huge rumbling noise shakes the tunnels; after the noise ends, you hear something else in the distance*

Toa Roland-Makuta Vorpal

*I hurry my pace. I can see a light, the outside, up ahead*

OOC: You're about ??? feet down; even if I calculated and filled in that distance, you'd be nowhere near the surface (for comparison, the earth's deepest sinkhole is about 2,172 ft deep). Nice try, though. I mean, this is a horizontal tunnel, not a vertical one...

IC: When you reach the light, you find a lit chamber, with several empty, circular holes in the wall (possibly for canisters?). As you turn around, you see the remains of the tunnel you came through, devastated by the collapse.

Well... not my ideal vacation spot, but put up a few posters and some furniture and I'm set.
*I form a chair and sit on it, then fall asleep*

In one of the holes, a mask, left by a former hero, begins to shake and glow.

*I wake up*
*I cautiously back away from the mask (I've had enough experiance with magical objects underground to know when to run), morph my axe into a drill, and begin tunneling out and towards the surface*

About ???/4 (that's supposed to be a fraction) feet up, you are caught, surrounded by a wall of hard rock that refuses to give way to your drill.

*I reform my axe. The runes begins shining in an intense light and the mask below stops shining*


ooc: remember the source of my axe's power in our first battle?

OOC: Kind of.

IC: You hear noises far above, as if someone, or something, is trying to drill its way down.

*I unleash a torrent of energy from my axe, destroying the rock in the direction of the noise revealing ???*

A few second later, about a dozen Pahrak and Nuhvok burst through the stone and earth, showering you with a few pounds of dirt and debris.

OOC: The mask's power was to create and/or control Krana; this applied to nearby semi-broken, buried Bohrok shells. Since the mask's former owner is nearby (coughRoland-Vorpalcough), it activated.

What are these overgrown bowling balls doing down here!?
*I send a large burst of energy through the tunnel they came through, incinerating all of them*
Ah, well. It doesn't matter. What are a few bohrok against GB tech?
*I begin to follow the tunnel made by the bohrok*
Wow, things went to karz in a single day. Goodbye, Lego Message Boards, you'll always have a special place in my heart.
