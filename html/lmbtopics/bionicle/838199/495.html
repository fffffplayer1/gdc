Charix suddenly reappears in a flurry of shadows which immediately release shockwaves loaded with confusion powers which surge through the area. Charix then emits a brilliant blast of light along with a powercream  which rip through the area blinding everyone around. When it subsides, Karunax and Charix are gone, both transported away and into the shadow zone.

 

Inside the shadow zone. Charix stoops over Karunax, his eyes two blue, blazing balls of fire. The sight of his leader in such a unfortunate predicamate fills Charix with fury. Everything that Charix had held dear over the years was, in this situation attacked ruthlessly by these toa. Karunax, the very symbol of the Brother-Hood had been laid low by the toa. Everything was wrong. Everything was infuriating. It made Charix want to vent his frustrations. But there would be time later.

 

For now, Charix simply speaks to Karunax, in a voice that does not quite hide the fury he is feeling. "My leader. What Can I do? I have come, as always to serve."

 

Charix begins preparing a mixture of regeneration, mental stability, and other healing mixtures insdie himself.
