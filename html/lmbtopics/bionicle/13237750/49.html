He does so.

He encounters a trio of beings. A Toa of Air, A Toa of Lightning and a third. "Who trespasses the domain of The Sky Father, Atem?!" The third roars.

 

OOC: Cause I'm too lazy to go to the mountains of Ko-De-Ce-Kra Wahi whatever.

"Uh... I do. Procella wants to talk to you."

Thunderaan gasps. "That was fast...uh, Atem was looking for her? Hold on." They disappear before coming back with a fourth figure, Atem.

 

"Take me to Procella."

Leres obliges, nearly falling out of the sky at one point.

They follow, reaching a lake. Procella looks at Atem with joy. "Atem! I'm stuck again...can you help out?" She pouts, and Atem chuckles.

 

"Yes, Procella." He murmurs before grabbing her. Stuff later and then Procella now can slither around.

Leres watches really awkwardly.

She slithers over, grinning. "Meet Leres, Ambassador of Procella and the Ocean." She says with a grin.

 

"He has wings." Atem notes.

 

"Clearly, so he can move faster. Right Leres?" She says.

"How should I know why I have wings? I just do. They're useful for flying, but seeing as how I'm terrible at flying, they're not much help."

She facepalms. "I meant, you are my ambassador, right?"

"Yeah."

"Lovely." Atem snorts at Procella's choice of words.

"Uhh, so, is there anything we're supposed to do now?"

"Well, Leres, we need to find allies. Strong allies. Powerful allies. And collect our armies."

"...Armies?"

"Yes, our followers." Atem says.

We have been betrayed. Our trust, misguided. Our faith, put wrongly. Have they no shame? But we will live on, my brothers. We will strive forward, we will endure. We will ascend. Free of limitations, free of fear.
