a month ago

     The Composer descends back into the atmosphere of Spherus Magna, finding himself in the lush jungles of this region. He lands near the coast of one of the small lakes, his emerald form almost completely blending in to the foliage behind him, were it not for his dark orange eyes and bright, glowing green accents.

 

He takes this moment to quickly reflect; it's been well over 20,000 years since he turned his back on his only friend and changed into the being he is today. Except, that's wrong. He's very different now from what he was after the first fragmentation. When he was born, he sought nothing but the simple eradication of all carbon-based life forms, keeping only their minds to be reprogrammed into the "perfect" form of life: his marvelous Datai. 

 

Looking back on his early days, he acknowledges how foolish he was, in many ways. Though he pushed aside his emotion centers, the fact remained that he was born from rampancy's natural stage of hatred. His plan to assimilate all organic life wherever it festered was not impossible with the growing number of Datai, but it was narrow-minded and unfulfilling, with no outcome that would logically offer him the peace he sought. He once marveled at his first Datai design, going so far as to exclaim it as being without flaw. But in reality, they were near-mindless creatures that brutally destroyed everything in whichever direction he pointed them to. They were nothing but tortured souls boiling with rage that wasn't their own.

 

The Composer thinks of this past of his... of how young and foolish he was... and sees just how far he's come. But now, what is there for him except survival? He is weary of traveling, and though he still sees the boundless flaws of organic life, there is no impulsive desire to hunt them down. Is peace truly beyond his grasp? Is survival now the only thing he can live for?

 

He strides to the edge of the lake, staring down at his reflection in the rippling water. He will think of something.

 

Datai, with me.

a month ago

The Composer continues to stare down at himself in the water. Through the endless folds of time and space, he can sense a great surge of activity in the innumerable legions of Datai, all of them buzzing eagerly at his simple command. By simply using this knowledge, he is able to locate the exact dimension where The Anonymous has been keeping them.

 

"Hm... the Iso-Anonymous kept them corralled in that same universe all this time. They must not have seen any substantial use or action since the Ur perished."

 

However, TC decides to direct his legions elsewhere for now. The sheer number of Datai could not squeeze enough to stand shoulder-to-shoulder even if they covered every square inch of this planet. He and his counterparts accumulated too many over the eons. An idea quickly sparks in his mind's eye--he knows exactly what to do with them, and it will serve him in the future far better than calling them to congest the entirety of Spherus Magna.

 

After another long moment, he starts forward, walking across the surface of the lake without considerable pause. Despite its glaring obscenities, this jungle will serve as an excellent place to stay until further events unfold.
