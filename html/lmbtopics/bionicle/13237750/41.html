"You're scared of me. Interesting. The only logical explanation for you to be scared of me is because you realized that I simply used you as a tool, a mere object to act as a punishment to the Skadiki government for wasting my time? Well, I put a tracker on you that can be picked up by my mental abilities, but I need you to help me with a fight." Zakaku explains.

 

"And why should I help you?" The Mask Thief questions.

 

Unbeknownst to the Mask Thief, Zakaku looks through his memories, and then retracts once he finds what he needs.

 

"Well, you've had some trouble with Xian security in the past, correct?" Zakaku inquires.

 

"How did you-"

"...How would that benefit me?"

"...Deal."
