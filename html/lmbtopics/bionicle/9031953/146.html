  • Onu-Wahi: Mountains and ravines of huge variation in height.
  • Po-Wahi: An area entirely without soil or grass, instead simply displaying bare Stone.  Has some hills and caves.
  • Le-Wahi: The main body of the land is much lower down than the others, and so hotter; this generates giant thermals which keep masses of incredibly light rock afloat far above them.
  • Vo-Wahi: Plains with giant thunderstorms constantly raging overhead.
  • Fe-Wahi: Essentially looks like the surface of Mars due to soil with very high Metal content. There are rich ore deposits underground.
  • Ba-Wahi: Plains with incredibly dense underground rock, so much so that it increases the region's Gravity by a significant amount.
  • De-Wahi: Rocky area criss-crossed with canyons, in which even whispers echo on for miles.
  • Fa-Wahi: Plains with Magnetic ground, making it exceedingly hard for beings without Magnetism powers to even walk.
  • Bo-Wahi: Formerly Bota Magna.  Nothing much has changed.
  • Su-Wahi: Strange phenomena focus heat into the upper atmosphere over these plains, superheating it into Plasma.
  • Av-Wahi: You've seen this already in Tesara.
  • Kra-Wahi: Heavy cloud cover and black soil that absorbs all remaining light.
  • Unnamed Sand Region: A desert.  No Matoran prefix for this one, so we'll need to think of something.
