This is a work in progress, but I propose we make a rule for travel. Note that there will be one no matter what, but I want general consensus as to what actually goes into this rule.

 

  • You must explicitly state you are leaving your current location before going to another.
  • You cannot access any location that is hidden (OoMN/Destral [which is not hidden but merely missing]) or locked (such as an enemy fortress). You will have to either have to use a Rode, Arthtron, etc. or break your way into the fortress. Note that the rules still apply regarding walls and buildings – they can't be destroyed in one post or by a single weapon.

Please state your opinions or any additional thoughts. Thanks!

 

Additional note: You must have already been to a location to teleport there. This also applies to alternate dimensions.

And I quote from a discussion about this roleplay that I had with some friends,  "Everyone and their brother has the portal mask."

Seriously.

 

It is getting out of hand. People are popping through walls and into HQs using Olmaks or other portals, and we might as well pack all the HQs into one city-sized area with no walls and walk wherever we want.

 

From this point onward, if there is a continued use of the Mask of Dimensional Gates or other quick-travel powers, I intend to null or severely limit the Olmak, as well as take appropriate action against other powers.
