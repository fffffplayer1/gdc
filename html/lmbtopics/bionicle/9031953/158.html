Okay, good. Now then, Keps, I need you to approve a character...

 

JAMS ON KEPS ONLY.

Jammers

So, basicially, my idea is that, since the Shadow Being's pretty much run out of support (Shadow Hound's at Destral, his former Toa Hagah are now turned back to normal, the General's been crushed underneath a protosteel pillar, and since the last Experiments he sent out to do a job failed miserably, he doesn't want to use them again), he decides to abduct a Toa using his powers and then proceed to do extensive surgery involving Shadow Leeches, Visorak Venom, Energized Protodermis, and other good stuff. This results in the creation of the General Nui, a hybrid of the Makuta's main forces, at least to the Shadow Being (Bohrok, Visorak, Exo-Toa, Rahkshi, Vahki, and Shadow/rogue Toa).

 

The General Nui's mindset is basicially "wait for orders, then proceed to do said orders even better then originially intended". And to help with that mindset, the guy's built like a TANK. The reason why is because the Shadow Being used Rahi Nui DNA in order to make sure he wasn't hindered by Gravity, Magnetism, Iron, and other such annoyances. Along with that, he's got the original eight Kanoka powers, all the Krana powers, the ability to shoot Rhoutaka (with the powers of the six Visorak types) off his back, and to top it all off, he has the ability to wield any Bohrok shield, Rahkshi staff, and Vahki staff, and he's got a copy of the General's blaster.

 

Of course, this guy has weaknesses. Because this guy kinda needs them. A lot. (seriously, I feel like I wrote a Gary Stu...) Specifically, the guy's hyper-sensitive to pain, the Shadow Being put in a safeguard that causes the General Nui to freeze in place and vent out elemental energy once it reaches a specific limit, and while elemental energy can't hurt him...pretty much everything else will.
