AGB-GB War Timeline

 

let's see, I'm only giving out the major-ish events. And if things are on the same line, they happened in the same year

 

The War's Start

3 years

Battle for the Desert

1 year

Raid on AGB city of Vitox

2 years

Excidia's birth, Seige of Vitox, Itrox betrays the GBs in favor of the AGBs, the AGBs reclaim Vitox and wipe out GB forces in the city

2 years

Excidia's younger siblings are born, the King falls in combat to a Great Being, the elderly AGB Kaonitus discovers a new type of science, which he calls Kaon, Nixot absorbs Kaonitus and becomes the new chief Scientist

5 years

Excidia is declared the new queen while the elder queen takes her place as Empress

6 months

Excidia allies with other forces such as Apollyon, Abaddon and others, Excidia realizes a deception and finds the entire royal family is now smashed

3 months

Excidia pleads for the GBs to cease the war, but efforts from the chaotic Abaddon prove this to be impossible, Abaddon tricks Excidia into progressing the War

3 months

Abaddon is destroyed by a conjoint effort from Kiton and Verith, Kiton suffers grave injuries and is smashed from such

1 Year

The GBs drive the AGBs into the capital, where the AGBs fake defeat

2 years

The AGBs storm the GBs during their celebrations and what-not. General Akilos, Oikan and General Verith among many others are smashed. There is only one survivor, Excidia. Excidia manages to retrieve all of the AGB remains and place them in Angam Surehps, the capital, before, with the help from Iniuria, they eject Angam-Surehps into space, it slowly forming into it's own planet

Construction of the GSR

Excidia tells Iniuria and Cruciatus to hide safely and take care of the very young Lethiane, before the AGB hides in a Matoran Body, before fleeing to a Makuta Body

 

 

many years were not included, for the war lasted thousands of years. These are the most important events however.
