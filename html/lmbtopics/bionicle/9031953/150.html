Le should not go with Ko or De. At all. Like...put them on opposite sides of the planet..

 

Ga-Bo-Ce? Nooo.

 

Why not, this.

 

Ko is on a snowy mountain, at the bottom of the mountain range is a lake, where the Ga Matoran live in a bunch of interconnecting lilypads. De-Matoran also inhabit the mountains, in a special area by a lava pool.

 

Ta live near Lava Stream from the Volcano, while the Su-Matoran live inside some indents WITHIN the Volcano. Av go in the less warm places.

 

Bo-Le-Kra. Le live in villages built on the treetops, while the Bo live on the ground or in trees, made into homes that the Le matoran sometimes share with them. Kra hides in the shadows, in dark areas, with lots of overgrowth.

 

Po live in a canyon above the ground of the Onu-Matoran and near the Ov. Beneath said canyon and the desert is the Onu, a series of caves and tunnels. Ov lives in Desert..simple. Ba live in the more...gravitationally diverse areas, where Onu matoran prefer to avoid due to the gravity shift.

 

Fe-Vo-Fa. Um..I'll let someone else describe this...

 

Ce. Why not just have them in plains? Easy.

 

I'm just gonna say this though. Put Ko-De together.

We have been betrayed. Our trust, misguided. Our faith, put wrongly. Have they no shame? But we will live on, my brothers. We will strive forward, we will endure. We will ascend. Free of limitations, free of fear.
