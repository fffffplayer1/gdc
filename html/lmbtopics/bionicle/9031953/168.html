It has come to my attention that some people have created bases or spread forces throughout entire regions. There is one problem with this... it's madness. Considering that a single region is very likely at least as large as ALL OF EARTH'S LANDMASS COMBINED, there should be no way anyone could cover the entire thing with the numbers involved in this RP. Scanning is one thing, building bases all over and creating big roads or something, I getcha, but expanding bases to cover entire regions, this is completely out of hand. It would take way more than the hidden millions of troops that I assume have been created by the various factions to create underground bases which encompass the entire Wahi's. Tunnels are one thing, although it would still take forever to network a whole region, but entire bases? This is unacceptable and must be addressed.
