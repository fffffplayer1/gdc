tuvok9597 wrote:

What are the rule about secret conversations?

Like, is it posible to eavesdrop with an invisible person?

     For anything not jammed, so long as your character has a legitimate reason to be present, they can eavesdrop. Or throw a toaster at them and stand on their head, for that matter.

     

     For jammed things, don't break the rules of jammers. For an example, read the following two sets of jammers:

JAMMERS! GEORGE ONLY!

Jammers
"Hey George, help me destroy the Makuta!!!"

^If you see jammers like this, it's usually best not to eavesdrop on them.

 

JAMMERS! GEORGE AND RODE/ELDA-WEARERS ONLY!

Jammers
Toa Billharax sneaks past the gate with his Huna and signals to his ally George by throwing a rock.

^This kind of jammers is completely fine to let your character act on, presuming you have a Rode or Elda to detect them with.

 

     Am I making sense or just confusing you utterly?
