The issue with that idea is that it's somewhat confusing.  A quick demonstration for you:

That last bit wouldn't make sense to me.  A topic called 'Tajun Village' that isn't called Tajun nor is a village?  Right...

Having 'Ov' as the Matoran prefix for Sand, however, I like and second.  Is that based on anything in particular?
