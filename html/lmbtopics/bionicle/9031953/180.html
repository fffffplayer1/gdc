YJF has spoken and I feel the need to defend myself.  Let us begin.

 

Firstly, YJF has commented on how quickly I managed to forge an army.  I count that as a product of my own ingenuity more than anything else, and I think that now's a good time to explain how I did it for anybody who doesn't understand.

 

Essentially, Fesur, Vonai and Sukan retrieved the Matoran from all the way back when the Hauden Nuva evacuated the universe from the pocket dimension where they put them to keep them safe.  They then moved to another, empty pocket dimension where time moves much, much more quickly than that of the Matoran Universe (as confirmed by Takanuva's adventures, this is a thing).  They used this time advantage to transform the entire pocket dimension into one vast factory where the troops and vehicles were assembled in what was seconds in the Matoran Universe; they then transferred these troops and vehicles back across to the Matoran Universe.

 

I would also like to point out that this force is much smaller than the Makuta's; Keplers, for example, has used one million interceptors in a single attack alone.  A pocket dimension can only produce and store so many soldiers.

 

As for the Mangai, YJF is right in that, if they wanted to, they could go Nova and literally consume the entire universe with their Elements.  Might I ask why on Spherus Magna they would do such a thing?  Anyway, other than letting their powers go wild like this, I agree the Mangai are powerful - but they are also glass cannons and fragile.  Aside from having their being made up of energy that anyone of their Element can consume, they have no auxiliary Kanohi powers that normal Toa do, which opens up Toa like Sukan and Demaron with fewer defensive capabilities up to attack.

 

As well as that, the Mangai cannot be everywhere.  On a planet with a surface area several times the size of earth, this matters.  My characters are comprised of: the Mangai, Kapronan, Teron.  That's it.  Most of you can reach further than I can.
