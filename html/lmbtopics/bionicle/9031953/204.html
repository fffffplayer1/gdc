IMPORTANT ANNOUNCEMENT 

 

Hello everyone,

 

First, some clarifications ...

 

1. There will be no reset of this roleplay.

2. Only a Mod or the roleplay rep can hold an official vote. Any other vote is totally unofficial and will not impact the roleplay.

3. The only way for a new roleplay rep to be chosen is by the Mods. Unless the Mods have declared a new rep then there is no new rep.

 

Please remember that this subforum was created to allow you all the chance to continue a roleplay that is based on a theme that was retired years ago. This roleplay's very existance is an exception to the normal rules. We allow it based on the promise that you would all have a peaceful, constructive roleplay. That is not the case here and has not been for a very long time.

 

The Mods are inclined to retire the roleplay as it is argumentative, negative, and there is very little roleplay happening here. We are giving you all one last chance to turn things around. We will be checking in regularly but without notice. If this roleplay does not get on and stay on track during our period of observation it will be retired.
