I have been talking with Greg Farshtey over on the LEGO General forum.  I asked him about the potential for a Sonics user to generate heat with his powers.  He took a short while to think about it, then responded by saying that, while certainly possible, such a manipulation would require immense amounts of sound energy.  This means that a Sonics user would probably not want to do this, for a two reasons:

 

1. Such energy would deafen everyone in the immediate area (especially the Sonics user, who, let's not forget, has heightened hearing).

2. It would be incredibly draining for such an energy output to be maintained.  Only someone like Demaron after his Nuva transformation would be able to have both the skill and Elemental capacity to do so for a protracted length of time.

 

There you have it.  Make of it what you will.
