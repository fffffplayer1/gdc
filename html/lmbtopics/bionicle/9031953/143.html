Blarkrok: An idiot, a complete failure, hated by all.

Kratark: He had the makings of a great Makuta, but he threw that away. His life was claimed in the dumbest argument ever.

The Shelei: A nice Toa team that had interesting character development, but ultimately, they were underused.

The Makuta Hunters: A team that I was looking forward to playing, but I never got around to using them enough to have fun with them.

 

The Tajun Village topic is scarred. A nasty argument thread will forever taint its memory. To boot, many more topics are scarred similarly. Who is to blame? Me. I have failed miserably to follow the one simple rule of the internet, the sacred law that nobody should break: Don't. Feed. The troll.

 

It is obvious to me now two things.

 

1. This isn't fun anymore. I don't enjoy this roleplay anymore. I cannot escape my reputation as a metagamer and a light user. And 60% of the time spent here was squandered on arguing, be it to barely keep a character alive, or even to make simple moves. The more I try to push for my characters and plotlines, the more I realise that I've been met with opposition every single time. I've contributed precious little, dare I say, nothing, to this roleplay. I am not needed, and I doubt, wanted.

 

2. The rival. I will not name this user, but we all know who he is. The minute I came here, I found myself at odds with this person, and ever since, I've been the subject of his scrutiny and wrath. Throughout my stay here he has mired me in nulls, insults, and has even specifically marked some of my most favorite characters for- ahem, "doom". I didn't want to be driven to this point, but I don't believe it's worth it anymore.

 

But yes, I am leaving. I don't suspect to be gone forever, but for now, I will not participate in the roleplay anymore. For the reasons stated above.
