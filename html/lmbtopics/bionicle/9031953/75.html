This discussion about Mata Nui has gone on for long enough.  I have though about the issue, and now I think that I have a compromise that will suit everyone.

 

The Hauden and Kapronan go to Karda Nui and, using the original method described by the Keystones, reawaken Mata Nui... as Inharax.  Inharax, true to form, crushes Mata Nui's spirit, then reveals himself.  However, Kapronan still has the Ignika, and uses it to revive Mata Nui.  The Ignika, being sentient, guides Kapronan's energy to create a body for Mata Nui where he needs it most - Bara Magna, where the Prototype Robot is located.

 

Cold Steel recognises the threat to the Matoran Universe, and, using his mechanical mastery, locks in mental battle with Inharax.  They end up in a stalemate, Cold Steel not able to shift the Makuta but Inharax unable to hurt Cold Steel.  Inharax finally comes to the decision to launch Cold Steel out of the MU.  Guess where he ends up.

 

Meanwhile, Hauden lead the counterattack on Inharax from the inside, recruiting MU beings to their cause and striking at key points around the universe with the end goal of destroying him.  Through doing this, they manage to keep Inharax on his toes, but simply aren't enough of a threat to finish him off.  Inharax manages at last to fully gain control of his new body.

 

By this point, however, Mata Nui has had his misadventures, travelled to Bota Magna, returned to Bara Magna with an army of Earth Glatorian (who, without a major threat to keep them in check, probably have more numbers than the rest of the tribes) and taken control of the Prototype Robot.  Cold Steel has also managed to find the third robot, the one that Mata Nui was supposed to pilot aiding Teridax in the canon universe or Inharax in this universe, and fire it up.  The two detect each other, converse and decide to team up to fight Inharax, who has sensed the two of them.

 

Inharax lands on Bara Magna, the three robots fight, Demaron and Co. escape and fight in Battle of Bara Magna.

 

Thoughts?
