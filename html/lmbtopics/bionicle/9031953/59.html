The Hauden Nuva and Kapronan gain Adaptive Armour.

The Hauden Nuva and Kapronan are sent by Artakha to Karda Nui.

The Hauden Nuva and Kapronan rescue the Av-Matoran and free the Shadow Matoran to protect them from the storm unleashed upon Karda Nui once Mata Nui is reawakened.

Kapronan sacrifices himself to reawaken Mata Nui (due to the fact that there is no need for bringing Mata Nui back from the dead; after all, here he has been asleep for a matter of weeks, not a thousand years).

Inharax takes control; Mata Nui's spirit is cast out into space in the Ignika.

Mata Nui and the Ignika crash-land on Bara Magna.

Mata Nui meets the inhabitants of Bara Magna and, with their help, defeats the Skrall incursion.

Mata Nui ventures into the Valley of the Maze with his new companions.

 

And this is where I stop with my prospective storyline.  Why?  Because what I thought would be brilliant would be to continue the potential story that had been set out for Bionicle in 2011/12.

 

Most of you will know of a story concept drawn up by the team that worked on TLR.  It featured Mata Nui and his team of Glatorian (and Berix, naturally) travelling through the Valley of the Maze and facing the Element Lords along the way.  After that, they were supposed to be transported to Bota Magna and face a cohort of biomechanical dinosaurs.  Only in 2013 would they return to Bara Magna, rebuild the Prototype Robot and finally confront Teridax.  Due to Bionicle's cancellation this never was discussed, and the storyline was shortened.  However, we have no such limitations here...

 

Thoughts?

Any thoughts?  At all?

It's definitely an interesting idea; my only problem is that I don't want to adhere to the story all that much. I just want us to get to Spherus Magna.

Oce we're at SM, some of us can go to the Valley of the Maze & such.
