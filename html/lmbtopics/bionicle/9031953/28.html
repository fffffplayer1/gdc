I have a question.

 

What exactly is the deal with everyone wanting to be Nocturn? It seems like everyone is trying to sink themselves an island nowadays. This would all be fine and dandy, but last I checked there were only two known reports of an island being sunk.

 

The one everyone uses as an excuse is that of Nocturn. However, Nocturn's island had an extremely weak point and he hit it with enough force to shatter the island. Notice, though, I said shatter. The quote people are referring to doesn't say the island sunk, it said he hit it in just the right spot and it shattered.

 

The second one would be that of Voya-Nui. People also seem to be referring to that when they attack the pillar supporting whatever island is being attacked. Except that Voya-Nui was out of place and didn't belong there. It can't be used as a basis for what other islands are connected to. Also, it didn't just sink when the cord holding it up was destroyed, it plummeted. Meaning instant annihilation for whatever was on the island. So, I guess if you want to follow Voya-Nui as a guideline, that'll mean every island in this RP is going to be oblitered in no time flat.

 

 

Anyways, here's my point: stop sinking islands. It's not something you can actually just go out and do.
