Okay, most people here will know about how the Hauden actually discovered the Plan of the Makuta via mrcqm and are now obliterating the Brotherhood's base (and doing quite well if I do say so myself).  Now, this discovery has set the roleplay off track compared to how the story should be wondering.  However, I have a Plan for the Makuta.  And this is it:

 

Let the Hauden win.

 

No, really.  Let them win.  Put up only a token resistance to the Hauden's attack and let them think that the Makuta are destroyed.  Then emerge from the ashes of the base and continue the Plan as normal while the Hauden carry out the tasks necessary to awaken Mata Nui.  Hauden go to Karda Nui, awaken Great Spirit, boom - Makuta take over universe.

 

What do we think about this?
