Name: Kortall

Species: Toa

Affiliation: Rogue

Element: Sand

Mask: Mask of Aging.

Weapons: An energy siphon blade and an arm-mounted plasma cannon.

Appearance: Light tan armor with brown highlights and yellow eyes.

Backstory: Originally, Kortall was a sand glatorian. While the GSR was being built, Kortall stumbled across the device one of the GB's intended to use to be one of the MU inhabitants. Out of curiosity he activated it, being turned into a toa via the machine. Fearing the wrath of whoever managed to build this thing, Kortall fled into the GSR. Eventually he realized he could control sand (due to the tech in the toa body) and wandered for years, never choosing a side in the never-ending wars raging across the robot.

Behavior: Kortall never learned the toa code, and as such will smash his opponents if necessary. Being originally a glatorian, he doesn't socialize much with anyone.

♪...Divide them with doubt it will all wash away...♪
