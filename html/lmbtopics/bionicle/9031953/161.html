@Keps and Brickobot.

 

Generally speaking, bringing Charix back the way he was would immediately have a couple of effects. One, it would most likely help the Brother-Hood if they excepted him back. He would bring his tactical mind, his sense of honor, and his loyalty back to the organization. However, he would also go right back to work trying to reform the Brother-Hood into allignment with his own, personal vision for what it should be. That would, I assume, automatically put him at odds with several characters,  including Inharax who he would necessarily still remember as the great traitor and usurpor of the past.

 

Charix's great pride would be an issue again, and his overall views on Makuta politics.

 

I'm sharing this simply to point out a few reasons some characters might want (or not want) Charix to make a return.

 

In the short term, what I have available is an evil, war hungry Dark Charix, an innocent Light Charix whose loyalty to the Brother-Hood is highly questionable, and Tyglax, who is currently working with light Charix, but who is not completely sure whether he supports his new, almost Toa like lord.
