Hi everyone

 

Please blame me for some of the issues in here recently. It was my idea to appoint Keplers as the roleplay rep in here. I just want you guys to understand why so that you can be fair on him and so that you can understand that we're just trying to help you keep this roleplay alive!

 

So, us mods had been discussing how best to deal with the arguments in here without closing the forum down. We could see that the main two arguing users had both liked Keplers and Keplers had liked them. We could also see that Keplers liked the majority of users in this RP and received likes back from them. In addition, Keplers is very knowledgeable on Bionicle and roleplaying and is well respected by both the majority of users and us mods. There are some other people who fit the same bill in here and they could well also have been chosen. Keplers just happened to tick a lot of the necessary boxes and we had to choose one person. So, with that said, please lighten up on Keplers. He likes you guys and just wants to help keep the forum open!

 

Keplers is not here to hold court over everything that happens here. He's here to be your representative to us, so that we can help resolve disputes and, again, keep the RP open. As we hope is becoming clear, we (and Keplers) are putting a lot of energy and effort into attempting to keep your RP going. So, if you have issues in the RP, please go to Keplers and he'll come to us. Don't attack Keplers because a) he's done nothing to warrant the attack and b) you'll just perpetuate the problems that we're attempting to resolve.

 

So, just to reiterate, we want to help you guys keep this RP open. Presuming you guys want the same thing, we can oficially say that we're all on the same side. With that said, what do you want to do about next steps? We suggest you use this topic to discuss together how best to go forward. Use Keplers as the excellent sounding board that he is and Keplers can come to us with your combined recommendations.

 

While you do that, we have one request of you. Please attack the argument and not the user! Too many of you are making this personal and it's not at all! The RP is here for you guys to have fun and so the sooner we can get back to that the better. We'll never return to it if people are attacking each other and throwing insults. So, please go ahead and discuss the way forward without resorting to that stuff. We don't want to but we will have to take stronger action on those who do continue with that stuff.
