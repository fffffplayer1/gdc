Jammers for keplers only:

Jammers
Greetings.

I don't think that it's hard to notice the absolutely vast amount of backlash that I've been having about the Hauden Nuva.  This means that the plan that I've always had for the Hauden has been somewhat put in jeopardy.

I have always thought that the Hauden would have not one but two transformations, the first to Nuva and then into 'ultimate Elemental forms', in which each Toa would become similar to a Makuta but with Elemental energy in gaseous form instead of antidermis.  This second transformation will allow them almost complete control over their individual Elements but would come with a lot of limitations, such as an inability to use mask powers, the danger of armour-destruction and the fact that any user of their Element in contact with their actual form would literally be able to absorb them in a one-hit K.O..

My problem is that I fear that a change like this would turn the entirety of the community here against me.  I have deliberately put in these weaknesses in an attempt to make these new Toa balanced, but suspect that people will either not think that I have gone far enough or miss the point of them completely.  Any ideas?
