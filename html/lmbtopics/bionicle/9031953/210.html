I quote:

The Great Beings were originally rulers of Spherus Magna, administrating and overseeing the various elemental tribes that were present on the planet. However, they later retired in order to devote more time to their creations, passing on their duties to the Element Lords.

In the early years of their presence on the planet of Spherus Magna, they encountered the native entity Annona who tried to use its powers to feed on their dreams.

The Great Beings were leaders of the planet, and collectively ruled the Agori.

Soon after Mata Nui had departed their homeland, the Great Beings' world was severely damaged by the spread of the Energized Protodermis, and shattered into pieces.
