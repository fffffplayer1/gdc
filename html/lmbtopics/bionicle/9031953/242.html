Facundo141 wrote:

What in Karzahni is the GSR? ... What happened to it? Why can't we roleplay anymore? Can someone update me on the general state of the Roleplay in the last 6 months/whole year?

To answer your questions:

 

  • 'GSR' stands for Great Spirit Robot, first Mata Nui's and then Teridax's form - in the canon universe.  Here it was first Mata Nui's and then Inharax's body.
  • We decided a while ago that we would follow a plotline similar to the canon story, with Inharax usurping Mata Nui and then being defeated in a climactic battle.  As such, the roleplay is now on Spherus Magna (which has been terraformed into five Elemental regions by Mata Nui) and has access to Agori, Glatorian, Vorox, Zesk, and the various non-intelligent species of Bara Magna and Bota Magna.
  • I am happy to report that we can and we are!
  • Here you can essentially refer to the second bullet point.  After those events, four main factions remained: the combined Matoran and Agori, under the protection of the Toa and Elementally-charged Glatorian; the Xians; the Brotherhood of Makuta and their various armies; and the alliance of Bota Magnan Glatorian and Vorox living on the former Bota Magna.  Inharax, wishing to take revenge for his defeat in the Battle of Bara Magna, unleashed an attack on Atero Nui, the city of the Agori and Glatorian, during which the Kanohi Ignika was shattered.  The forces of Light called upon Xia for aid; this call was answered, forging an alliance that has lasted to this day.  Helpfully, Inharax's fellow Makuta decided that, since his forces were elsewhere, it would be a good time to take power for themselves.  They smashed him, kicking off a civil war that essentially ended the assault.  A being known as 'The Voidwalker' then showed up and started destroying everything, though fortunately that tide has turned.

      Meanwhile, the Bota Magnans didn't do much.

 

Anyone available for a less good-centric take on things?
