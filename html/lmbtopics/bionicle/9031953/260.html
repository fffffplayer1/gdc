Jammers
Okay, here's what I was thinking.  Since the Ignika has split into six fragments, I would suggest that those who received the Life energy of one also gained a certain passive ability based on a certain aspect of the Ignika's powers.  The Mask of Life has both mental and physical aspects to its powers; these can generally be divided into restoration, transformation and destruction.  As such, here would be the various abilities for each fragment; feel free to preemptively claim them for your own characters where you feel that they would create an interesting dynamic.  Alternatively if you think that you have a better idea for one, please do point it out!

1. Physical Restoration: Returns to life upon smashing.  Kapronan has this ability.
2. Physical Transformation: Form adapts to the general situation (Mask of Adaptation power).
3. Physical Destruction: Able to destroy living or previously-alive matter with a touch.
4. Mental Restoration: Mind is unable to be affected, Psionically or otherwise.
5. Mental Transformation: Rapidly analyses and mentally adapts to any given situation.
6. Mental Destruction: Able to erode the mind of a Psionic assailant.

With the variety of abilities both here and across the Life energy-bearing characters as a whole combined with the various different perspectives on life that they have, I feel that a 'ragtag bunch of misfits' feeling could be achieved, the common cause between all of them being the requirement to restore the Ignika.
