Alright, so, yeah, in case you saw any of the recent posts in the Grazer's Junkyard, the Grazers have had to evacuate their home, and are now in migration to find a new place to live. As a result, I figured I might as well post their info. First, I'm going to do the backstory for the Grazer's existence.

 

Backstory: Originally, there weren't so many Grazers. There were only 10, created by the Great Beings to act as Mata Nui's digestive system. They gave the Grazers matoran level intellegence, and the knowledge about how to create other Grazers. So, they left them in a junkyard. And the Grazers fed. However, there was one thing the Great Beings left out: the knowledge of coding. As a result, the most the Grazers could do was make their new kin around as intelligent as Rahi.

Then, one day 99,000 years ago, one of the original 10 Grazers turned agaisnt the others. Swallowing a Shadow Leech and allowing it to go into it's furnace unharmed, the Grazer became a creature of the shadow, savagely attacking the others, his CPU twisted. Of the original 10 Grazers, only 2 remain: one is the Shadow Grazer, and the other was the one who defeated him. The battle left the Grazers in a wounded state. With only one sane leader, what could they do?

They did find something to do: spread. The hero Grazer guided the Grazers, giving Grazers with black highlights instructions on how to make other Grazers and structures using molten junk. Long and hard, the hero worked to get the Grazers into a better condition.

However, what they didn't know is that most people mistook them for pests. They did not know that their savageness displayed outside Gra-Koro, as they began to call their home, and increasing population meant that people made them out as a threat.

And then Rusty came along. He was the first owner of the junkyard to actually do something about the Grazers. Now, because of him, they're back where they started.
