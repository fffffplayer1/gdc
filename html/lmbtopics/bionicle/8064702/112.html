"Experiment # 2154: The Defender of Destral."

 

"They're watching me now. I can't deny it. The Brotherhood's looking into the dissapearances; they actually hired a team of Toa to try to find the one responsible. Normally, I would be happy, if I was not the one they're looking for. My work needs to be kept hidden. I am breaking the Three Virtues, but it's all for a greater cause. It's all for peace. But that's the thing. I find myself enjoying this more and more, the trapping of my own kind, sickingly telling them how it's going to end, and then reaching into their armor to get that lucious, powerful antidermis...

 

"No, no, I have to get back to the audio log, I can't think about that, I can't give in to the corruption...now, I myself am particularly worried...I've started thinking about what might happen if my plan fails...if they manage to catch me...I need to stay hidden. I'm nearly powerful enough to overthrow the Brotherhood and return the Makuta to their old ways, I just need more time...but, I've created something. A back-up plan, in case I fail; in case Destral needs a protector from the Toa.

 

"I took a Zyglak and brought it to my lab. There, I got some materials and mixed them together. Kardas genes, Tahtorak DNA, Visorak Venom, energized Protodermis, antidermis, and a dash of testosterone. I made this mixture in large amounts, took me a while to get the ingredients, but when I was done, I pumped all of it into the Zyglak. The result is what I call The Defender of Destral.

 

"The Defender is a massive beast, around 200 feet in height and 300 feet in length. The mixture has drastically changed the Zyglak's appearance, the main body structure and coloring the only thing remaining left of the Zyglak. The Defender is incredibly strong, and surprisingly agile for it's size. At least, in the water that is."

(I've had to retype this profile three times now because it keeps getting deleted. I think the MBs are trying to send a message.)

 

Name: Falas

 

Species: Fa-Toa

 

Mask: Kanohi Ruru

 

Weapons/Tools/Abilities: Double Scythe (Think Matoro's claw-blades as the ends.)

 

Appearance: Toa Falas is about the average size of a Toa, wearing very, very battle and weather worn gunmetal grey armor. Rust and foliage coats about 20% of his armor, and little bits of sand can be seen falling out of small cracks in it.

 

Backstory: Toa Falas is a Toa of Magnetism hailing from an alternate universe – a universe where Makuta Teridax had been defeated on the sands of Sphereus Magna, and peace had been restored to the universe. Of course, that peace didn't last very long, as over the next 100,000ish years, the Toa were slowly hunted to extinction by Marender, leaving the Matoran unprotected. The Glatorian were quickly dispatched, leaving the Matoran and Agori at the mercy of the Skakdi, who had utilized the time to establish their own empire.

 

Only two Toa remained – a Toa of Magnetism named Falas, and a Toa of Lightning whose name remains unknown. The two had been fighting a losing battle to at least save the Matoran from the empire, only to learn that the Matoran – as well as the rest of the enslaved races – had been completely brainwashed by the Skakdi, who now seemed, to them, to be their benevolent rulers. Falling into despair, the two continued to flee from Marender as well as the wrath of the empire.

 

Eventually, the two Toa broke, deciding to lead a final charge against the capital of the Skakdi empire. Amassing an army of Rahi who had not been enslaved by the Skakdi just yet, they made their last stand inside the capital. However, little did they know that Marender had followed them, and in its single-minded pursuit of them laid waste to the capital. Both sides had many, many casualities, until the pair of Toa were suddenly attacked by a strange being.

NOTE: I've been playing Kingdom Hearts, so these would be the BIONICLE equivelent of some random Nobodies from Organization XIII

 

Name: Xeorna

Alias: The Queen of the Hypnotic Dance

Species: Unknown

Element: Cloud and Sound

Abilties: Enhanced Agility and Speed, immunity to Heat and Shadow, Enhanced Eyesight

Powers: Hypnosis

Personality: Cold. Xeorna is detatched and emotionless. She is a quiet a nice girl.

Gender: Female

Weapons/Tools: Protosteel Chains. Lightly coated in a material that makes them extremely light, Xeorna swings them at foes. She has a back up of Knifes in her armor.

Appearence: Xeorna has two light angelic wings, like the rest of her, is light themed. She also has several Knifes inside her Armor which act as barbs that cut enemies trying to sneak up on her. She also has a Hood.

Backstory: Xeorna was once Orena, a kind sweet girl. However she eventually fell into The Source's grasp, causing her to become Xeorna. She eventually made a series of moves. Her theme is Dance based, she is notable for the Hypnotix Dance.

 

Famous moves:

Hypnotic Dance: Xeorna kicks one leg up, she puts it down, she shakes her hip, then she does the splits and back up.

Dance of Doom: Xeorna basically does Ballet and Fighting combined

Well, before we can continue discussions of MLP and weird dancing characters that have no place here, I'll post an update of what I am biased to say is a real profile.

 

Name: Fendrak.

 

Alias: The Lion's Maw. The Iron Tide.

 

Species: Matoran (formerly), toa

 

Element: Iron (Cannot manipulate someone's armor without their consent).

 

Weapons: Furnace spear: A spear with molten metal inside it. Its strikes cause the molten iron inside to slosh around, and might cause burning. Sword: A regular sword, serves as Fendrak's side arm of sorts. Dual broadswords: Fendrak's preferred weapons are capable of large cleaving blows. Iron shield: A shield that is awkward for him to use, so he prefers to use it as a mode of in-combat transportation, he can simulate water physics in metal and thus uses this shield to surf on the pathes of ron he creates. Wrist blaster: Doubles as wrist armor while still being able to fire off spikes of iron, effective for situations when melee isn't much of an option.

 

Colors: SIlver, grey, gold.

 

Appearance: The gallery version ofhim that is available right now is outdated. I will update it at some point.
