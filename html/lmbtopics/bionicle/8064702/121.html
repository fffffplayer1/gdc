Name: Corden

Affiliation: Dark hunters.

Weapons: None.

Powers: Reanimation, passive corruption on the area around him, absorbs life energy of living things touching the energy currents inside of his armor, and can form solid objects of shadow energy if they are connected to the hole in his back by said energy.

Appearance:Cracked and heavily damaged white armor with a large hole in the back and a similarly damaged mask of undeath for a face. In place of organic protodermis is what appear to be mostly transparent currents of blue energy.

Behavior: If he can speak, he never uses this ability. His armor remains creepily still when he doesn’t have a reason to move.

Backstory: Corden is the outcome of a destined “fixed” ko-matoran wearing a mask of undeath meeting energized protodermis. He lost most of his memory as another result, although he still remembered basic things such as how to speak. Matoran shunned him as some creature of evil -- not surprising considering his appearance. The dark hunters recruited him after one of their agents observed him having an intense battle with two skakdi, Akarus and Karau. They dubbed him “Corden” after the matoran words for “desolation” and “spirit”.
