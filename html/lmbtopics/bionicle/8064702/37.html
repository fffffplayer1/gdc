Name: Jawbreaker.

Affliction: Vahki.

Type: Rorzakh.

Weapons: Incredibly large GS-9000 Hydralic Hammer/Mallet.

Personality: Serious most of the time, although he lightens up around Insanity and other close friends, and is overall, a fun-loving guy. Although, he can be a bit irritable around those who he KNOWS will likely hurt Insanity or other friends if given the chance. (In other words, try not to get him within close proximity of Frost, Karunax, Manas, or any RPC like them, or he will probably swing that hammer faster then you can say 'ouch'.)

Backstory: Jawbreaker was made by the same creator of Insanity, and, like all Vahki that the Creator made, was put into an academy to train. Now, somewhere along the line, Jawbreaker was put into a class. That class in particular had Insanity. Now, as this was before Insanity went completely insane, Jawbreaker noticed he was a little rough around the edges, but didn't think much of him. Until one day, Insanity thought of an insane strategy, and presented it to the class. Everyone thought it was illogical, including Jawbreaker. The teacher in that class told Insanity that, and he stormed out. Worried, Jawbreaker followed...Only to find Insanity surpassing pretty much all the security measures to stop Vahki from escaping, and heading out. Even more worried, Jawbreaker quickly grabbed his new hammer, which he recieved earlier that day, and followed Insanity. What he saw was Insanity flawlessly executing his strategies on a travelling group of Dark Hunters...And succeding. However, Jawbreaker noticed that, during the end, One of the Dark Hunters was right behind Insanity, ready to crush him, and the poor Vahki in front didn't even notice. Insanity would've been smashed if it wasn't for Jawbreaker running in and swinging that hammer directly at the DH, knocking him out. At that moment, a beautiful friendship was born.

Instead, he found secret parts of the temple, including a training room and a well of energy. There were so many scrolls, that he nearly gave up. However, when he discovered the scrolls contained the secret to using the gauntlets, he began with fresh enthusiasm. Over the years, he developed his current martail arts prowess, until he came to the last scroll. It detailed a way to use the gauntlets to open the door. He opened the door and left, destroyign the scrolls as the last one had instucted.
