Name: Torkin
Affiliation: The good in this world
Element: Iron
Mask: Kanohi Pakari
Appearence: He's on the LEGO Gallery
Personality: An honorable warrior, committed to the Toa-code and the Three Virtues. He is willing to sacrifice anything for what he knows to be right. Torkin rarely makes a desicion based on emotion, preferring logic to be relevant and critical thinking in any situation. He is a fair fighter and will not smash unless absolutely nessacary.
Tool(s): A dual-edged iron spear and the strange Fe-Matoran Irok. Irok has the ability to transform from Matoran, to jet-pack, to iron gun. His jet-pack mode can reach sub-sonic speeds and his iron gun mode hurls balls of metal that Torkin manipulates into various shapes during flight.
Power(s): Torkin's powers over his element are uncanny, having developed them through years of practice and experience. He is rather slow and not very agile, but his natural strength is to be feared. He is a competent weapons master, able to wield nearly anything. Torkin tends to use his abilities in very unexpected ways, so expect the unexpectable when fighting him.
Backstory: He will appear in War for the Southern islands.

New RPCs/RPC!

Name: Pyro
Affiliation: The good in this world.
Element: Fire
Species: Toa
Mask: Kanohi Kakama(Has a Seletu in his Suva.)
Personality: Risk-taking, but very patient, owing to his former occupation as a Kanoka-maker.
Tools: A nearly indestructible shield, that can be thrown like a discus and can deflect energy beams.
Powers: Precise Fire powers, being skilled enough to burn small symbols and slice through rocks so that they have nearly no trace of being cut. As he and Cyro have a strong sense of Unity, and both of them have a large amount of willpower, they can maintain their merged form for longer than most Toa.

Name: Cyro
Affiliation: Good
Element: Ice
Kanohi: Pakari
Personality: He has the usual traits of a Ko-Matoran Scholar, but with a mischievous streak.
Tools: Twin katanas that he is very apt at using.
Powers: Precise Ice powers, and previously mentioned merging.

Name: Fotia-Pagos
Element: Fire/Ice
Species: Merged Toa
Kanohi: A merged mask with the powers of both the Kakama and Pakari
Personality: The traits of both Cyro and Pyro.
Tools: Pyro's shield, and Cyro's blades. Cyro's blades are merged into one blade akin to Kopaka Nuva's.
