Name: Unknown

Title: "The Mask-Collector"

Species: Steltian

Kanohi: Every Canon, non-legendary mask, plus the masks of any opponent he defeats, minus Rode and Olmakk.

Element: None

Powers: None

Weapons: None

Backstory: One an ordinary Steltian with little skill, "The Mask-Collector" begain to grow an interest for Kanohi. This interest later turned into a single obsession: Get masks. TMC was a law-abiding citizen until an ambassador from a large village on the Southern Continent came to Stelt wearing a Great Mask of Intangibility, a rare and valuable mask. TMC coveted that Kanohi to the point that he stole it... from the smashed ambassador. It looked natural enough that nobody suspected TMC. After he continued serial-smashing anyone with a valuable Mask, a Dark Hunter recruiting agent took him into the orginization, suspecting there was a pattern with the wearers of rare masks coincidentally being smashed.  After a fake Mask-Wearer baited TMC, he was taken to the DHHQ, where he was trained in combat (this was not normal, but the leader at the time was desperate for the abilty to make assassinations look natural). TMC, with his wide array of Kanohi, basic combat skills, and the talent of making things look natural, was generally paired with other Dark Hunters (generally the "all brawn, no brains" type) so they could make the most out of both their abilities. However, TMC was able to take on solo assassination missions, searching for those with rare, valuable Kanohi. However, his collection was almost complete, and TMC deserted without warning in order to finish his collection, taking his masks with him. After finishing his collection of every canon, non-unique mask other than the rare Rode and Olmakk, he placed them in a Suva in an unknown location, and continued wandering in search of any rare, significant, or unique masks.

Talents: Basic combat abilities, and extreme coverup skills. Every Kanohi, but not at the same time.
