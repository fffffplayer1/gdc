I'm bringing in someone from a long time ago........Tazzuk. *que the Gasps*

 

Anyway, yeah.

 

 

 

Name: Tazzuk, but currenty believes he is an entirely different being named Zarion

Species: Makuta

Orgainzation: Himself, but he technically is loyal to the Brotherhood

Personality: While Tazzuk loves to use psychological Torrment on his foes, Zarion does not talk. At all. He may use Telepathy at times.

Tools/Weapons: Zarion has a Cannon for an Arm, which shoots Ionized Shadow or Poison, or Molten Hot Plasma. In his other arm, he has a Razor Sharp Protosteel Claw tipped in Poison. He, unlike Tazzuk, is not Antidermis. His Armor is extremely hard, near Protosteel but not quite that level. He has hidden Ionic Discharge Daggers attatched to his Ankles. He also has Wings. He has a Soul Gemstone imbeddeded inide his Armor which enhanced his Soul Powers.

Element: Shadow and Soul

Powers: He is by nature, strong. However Zarion's specialty is Agility Based. He has all the Makuta Powers, except Accuracy(is incredibly skilled so does not need it so he purged it), Fear and Laser Vision.

Special Power: Hyper-Intelligence

Abilities: Enhanced Speed, Creativity

Backstory: While Tazzuk was imprisoned inside Infernum, Tazzuk experienced intense Psycological Torture. Due to his Calibur of sheer power, the Guards naturally used his talent against him: Psionics. A unique Fiend named Xijan entered his mind and tore it up and shredded it. Levira, seeing some usage of Tazzuk, secretly healed his mind to an extent. The result unfortunetely, caused his mind to slowly deteriorate on it's own. His Cellmate, a mysterious Creature unknown, secretely probed him in his brain, or the equivelent. He started to show excruciatingly disturbing levels of Insanity. He then developed two other personalities. One, named Zarion who is karzahni-bent on taking complete control over the body, and Zariona, who simply sits back and watches. Zariona only appears when both Zarion and Tazzuk are gone or uncapable of being dominant.

We have been betrayed. Our trust, misguided. Our faith, put wrongly. Have they no shame? But we will live on, my brothers. We will strive forward, we will endure. We will ascend. Free of limitations, free of fear.
