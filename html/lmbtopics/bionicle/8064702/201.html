Name: Skyquake
Description: Imagine a Lockheed F-117 Nighthawk Stealth Jet
Weapons/Systems: Experimental Sonic Bolt Launchers attached to the wings capable of firing devastating Sonic Blasts, and add several regenerative kanoka built into it. Oh, also there's a X-Ray Tracker and Heat Sensor for aiming. Oh, and there's some Kraata Missles(missles filled with Kraata in them)
Speed: 9/10
Other: They are also sort of quiet when not launching the Sonic Bolt Launchers

Name: Juggerknaut
Description: I can't really describe this except for. Take Transformers Animated Lugnut's vehicle mode, turn it black and light purple, remove the atomic thing stripes, and you've got it in a nutshell. Oh, and make the wind-shield a translucent purple.
Weapons/Systems: Kraata Bombs. Seriously, that's it. Take the engine pod things that turn into Lugnut's hands, fill them with various Kraata, and allow them to drop. You've got it. Did I mention that the bombs reload? Other than that, it's got nothing else except the ability to reload the bombs.
Speed: 5/10
Other: The Juggerknaut is somewhat rare

Name: Fire Bird
Description: Grumman 4-14 Tomcat in a nut shell. Then, for the Phoenix, give it a large cannon on top of it.
Weapons/Systems: A thin, highly flammable slime covering the outer shell of the Fire Bird, capable of making the entire thing light on fire upon activation. Then two flame-throwers underneath the cockpit.

Name: Phoenix
Description: Grumman 4-14 Tomcat with a large, triple barrel laser cannon
Weapons/Systems: Like I said, a triple barrel laser cannon. One barrel fires a basic laser, the other a heat beam, and the third an antidermis filled zamor sphere like beam.

also, the Skyquake has 2 empty Toa Stones, the Juggerknaut has 5, the Firebird 3 and the Phoenix 6(one on each barrel and the the others in assorted places)
We have been betrayed. Our trust, misguided. Our faith, put wrongly. Have they no shame? But we will live on, my brothers. We will strive forward, we will endure. We will ascend. Free of limitations, free of fear.

Name: Rinowill.

Species: New prototype toa.

Kanohi: Great mask of fusion.

Element: None (yet).

Weapons: Sword. Nynrah ghost blaster (both versions, in forearm).

Armor: None. He is made of protosteel fused with regeneration kanoka.

Other: A toa mind in a robotic body, his willpower was artificially and naturally enhacned through training. Has the ability to switch between toa elements, but only in his elemental shift room in Destral, process is extremly painful. Teleportation.

Bio: Designed to be a prototype for an army of element changing toa, but too time consuming and a waste of resources. Now a valuble (but no doubt expendable... sigh) member of the brotherhood. He chose the mask himself, and his ironwill easyly dominated control over the kanohi.

Status: RPC

 

 

Name: Kolak ()

Species: Shadow toa.

Kanohi: Great Akaku, (formerly, replaced with) Great Felnas.

Element: Shadow, Ice.

Weapons: Twin blades.

Armor: Fused with adaption kanoka.

Bio: (yet to happen) Thought to have been (smashed) for so long by the makuta, until Nuhkal found him in a stasis pod floating in space. Kolak was teleported down, and awakened by Krakarda, but not before being shadow leeched (with a different mental barrier, klakk is the wrong frequency to free him) and given a Felnas to replace his old kanohi.

Status: RPC.
