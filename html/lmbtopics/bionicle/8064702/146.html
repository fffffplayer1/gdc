I came up with an interesting idea....

Name: Leres

Species: Fa-Matoran

Weapons:

The Blades of Huakina:

Two shortswords with teh abilities of Kazi's Echo Forks and Balta Repolsurs.

The Battler-Chargers:

Four jointed peices of armor that Leres attaches to his arms and legs. They can increase any two attributes of his greatly.

Kanohi: Powerless Komau

Other: Leres travels with an item he calls the 'Box'

 

Name: The Box

Species: Unknown

Desctription:

The Box is a 1 ft cube, shiny silver and covered in ornate runes, in the language of the Great Beings. It levitates, following Leres at head level nearly anywhere he goes. As of yet, nothing has been found that can destroy it.

The Box is highly enigmatic, sometimes protecting Leres at all costs, sometimes seeming to step back and let him fight his own battles. It has been seen to contain various types of tools, in many makes and models, from something as simple as a crowbar to something as complex as a handheld laser cutter. On occasion, it has displayed hacking abilities on par with Cold Steel, though this is rare. Another thing it has done is project a life-support energy bubble around Leres, allowing him to traverse dangerous environments.

Name: Mokopia

Creator: The Shadow Being's Master

Location: Whawhai Nui

Role: Near Bottom of Food Chain / Hit and Runners

Description: ...The Mokopia, put simply, is bizzare. Incredibly bizzare. To the FULLEST extent. For one, it has the oddest color scheme of any Rahi. With a lot of bright colors with some dark colors thrown in, it's an eyesore to look at due to the colors. Although, to be fair, this WAS The Shadow Being's Master's first attempt at making Rahi, so there's no way to blame him for testing a few things out. Appearance-wise, it has a large hump, with some spikes here and there, a short tail, short legs, and an enlongated neck. It also appears to have a parasite on its head...but this is just a fake head to make predators less likely to attack it, so as to not get the "parasite" themselves. The REAL head is actually inside the hump, hidden by the shadows casted by the hollow hump and two skin-flaps at the side.

 

It only gets more odd when it comes to defence mechanisms. Unlike another prey Rahi that resides on Whawhai Nui; the Seismic Turtle, the Mokopia is extremely vulnerable in terms of armor, which ended up leading to the development of...being able to have its body parts flash light. Not to mention, it's actually quite fast for a creature its size. So, what does it do when it encounters a dangerous predator like the Whawhai Goldenclaw? Simple: spin rapidly while flashing it's body parts. I don't think I need to explain what this does to the enemy. And it's BECAUSE of this odd defence mechanism that led to them being used by the rebel forces, who now ride them into battle and use this Rahi's bizzare defence to temporarily cripple the army of Hiku-ahua's and Rahkshi for a few moments before turning tail.
