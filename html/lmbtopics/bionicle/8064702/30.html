‎03-30-2014 08:05 PM

The Toa Excalibur is a Toa Team founded for the sole purpose of driving off the Makuta from Toa Territories. I made "Toa Excalibur" a preliminary name, but I'll roll with it. The list goes as follows:

  • Leader: Xalance, Toa of the Cycle. Uses a Kanohi to allow him to change element in a predetermined pattern. Plans on defeating the Makuta to uphold the Toa Code.
  • Sub-General: Bakud the Attourney, Toa of Gravity. He lived the same life as a Matoran, over and over again, because of time travel, and then escaped this loop, losing his time-travel abilities in the process.
  • Dynamo: Regulum, Derranged Toa of Plasma. He had a mask called the "Mask of Absorbation" which let him absorb elemental energy. With that and his natural heat-draining powers, he attempted to absorb an entire volcanic eruption while guarding Artidax from Makuta. He succeeded... then spontaneously went Nova, smashing all his team members, and traumatising him to the point in which... well, just don't say the word "Makuta" around him.
  • Offensive Support: Synthyth, Psycho Toa of Psionics. Toa of Psionics. were female because the GB's believed psionics required a "Gentler Touch." Yeah, you're not exacly getting that. Totally, utterly insane. Regulum was given order-level mental sheilding so she wouldn't blow him up for fun. Uses Psionics to mentally assault foes in hopes of shattering their minds.
  • Manager: Mentium, Sane Toa of Air. Specialised in Engineering and psychology as a Matoran. Wears a Mask of Power Detection with a telescopic attachment, which allows him to tell when nearby beings are going to use their power. His primary function is to keep Regulum and Synthyth from destroying everything.

Anyway, like The Avengers, these Toa will be introduced individually: Xalance and Bakud have already been introduced, and Regulum is just about to make an appearance. Synthyth will also probably appear, and Mentium... Mentium exists only so the team doesn't blow itself up.

Name: Deathchill.

Alias: The ice princess.

Gender: Female.

Personality: Cold, calculating. Loyal, feared. Honest, fair.

Traits: Deathchill is a master strategist, able to fight and beat kopaka with here strategies.

Age: In human years around 11(Jellico is 12 in human years) in bionicle years she is 11000 while Jellico is 12000

Species: Ice mountain, Rahkshi fusion.

Element: Shadow and Ice.

Mask: Rahkshi head shaped calix.

Colors: White black purple and gunmetal grey.

Weapons: Twin frozen shadow blades.

Backstory:

Deathchill is the younger sister of Bloodshed, and cousin of Jellico. She was given the opportunity to rule over Ko-Metru by Makuta Teridax and Tazzuk themself. She accepted. She then was given the opportunity to join the makuta Doomix which she accepted as well. The Makuta Doomix are makuta that have dual elements one of them being one of the six primary elements and are the best of that element, Deathchill was the best for ice. Deathchill ruled in Ko-metru in her ice palace. She was rumored to be able to fight kopaka and win due to her straiegies. She has the ability in a super cold envirorment to freeze beings in a block of ice that look similar to crystals. She is rumored to be the source of all ice power.

Name: Regulum

Title: The Derranged

Species: Toa

Kanohi: Mask of Aborbation: Allows the user to absorb elemental energies, can be stacked with natural Toa powers. Mask of Silence: Why would he use this mask? To deafen himself. Why would he do that? You'll see.

Element: Plasma.

Powers: Plasma, energy increases to levels dangerous to allies due to various triggers, pain being one of them. 

Weapons: Two double-ended, curved blades occasionally used in Melee or in chanelling powers.

Backstory: Regulum once had a wonderful, nice Toa Team of his own. With this wonderful nice Toa Team, he was tasked with guarding the island of Artidax from an oncoming group of Makuta attempting to find and destroy several Toa Stones on the island. One of the Makuta used his destructive powers to cause a Volcanic Eruption, and Regulum decided to do the heroic thing and stop it. With his Mask of Abosrbation and his own Heat-Absorbing powers, he managed to absorb the entire thing. Someone probably should have taught him about how absorbing that much power leaves one supercharged somewhere along the line, but nobody did. He could not control his power, and released it all in a giant explosion that disfigured the island, smashed his entire Toa Team, and melted the Protosteel and Antidermis of the Makuta, along with sending him flying, and causing him to land on his head, and become traumatised. They say his powers broke somehow that day, and he reacts violently to anything that would remind him of it. "Makuta" is one of these triggers (No, you can't give your character the knowledge to somehow break the fourth wall, read this, and omit that word from conversation) along with the members of his team.

Talents: He's a gangling madman who explodes when you say "Makuta." He has no talent other than that.

Appearence: Abnormaly tall, skinny, and ungraceful. The term "gangling" describes him well.
