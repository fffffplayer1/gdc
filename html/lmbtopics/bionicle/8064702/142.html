Name: Skettis 

 

Species: Mutated Toa/Plant Monster

 

Mask: N/A

 

Element: Jungle

 

Weapons/Tools/Defenses/Abilities: The horrific plant monster's body itself could be described as a massive weapon, though its main and most apparent weapon was its dagger-like claws that were attached to the most humanoid part of its body – its arms.

 

It's assumed that the creature moves via telekinesis due to the fact that it never even touches the ground except when 'feeding' (which it does by inserting its five tendrils into whatever it's feeding from, similarly to an octopus, except it draws directly from the tendrils. For example, to feed from an island it would plant itself within the ground, completely depriving it of life within minutes.)

 

Appearance: The unimaginable monster looked as if it had been pulled straight from a nightmare, barely resembling the Toa of Jungle it had once been at all. Its head was like a cross between a Xenomorph(Alien) and a Venus Flytrap, but despite this it was still capable of limited speech. Further down was a spindly, hunched torso, covered in a thick forest of flowers, leaves, and other plantlife. Six spider-like limbs extended from the side of the torso, giving it a spider-like appearance. The torso widened out a bit further down to its legs, which were five tendril-like vines that seemed to be its 'legs' despite the fact that it never used them for movement.

 

The creature moved by simply hovering a few feet above the ground, darting around at a speed rivaling a Kakama. It could easily scale large objects with its spider-like scythes/daggers, as well as shapeshift. However, it could only shapeshift into plants (trees, bushes, etc.). The only problem with this was that wherever it was, it had likely already drained all of the plantlife in the area, making it stand out dramatically as a healthy tree/bush/whatever.

 

Backstory: A Toa of Jungle, Sket, bathed in some Energized Protodermis. Then this happened.

"Oh, his food is fine. Just...make sure to get the 'boneless' meat."

Dinner on the rails! Or...off the rails...whichever works.
