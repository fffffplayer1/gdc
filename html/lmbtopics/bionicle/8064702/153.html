Hello, I am new here. Can you tell me if this profile is acceptable?

Name: Nyx
Aliases: Ethereal of Night, Mistress of Darkness, The Princess of The Ethereal
Species: Ethereal
Powers: Nyx has a very large plethora of Dark Abilities. She has very powerful Umbrakinetic abilities, surpassing Makuta and even a Toa Nuva of Shadow! However due to her amnesia she has forgotten a lot of it. She is also stronger at night or dark places and she has Shadow Mimicry abilities, able to turn into living shadows. She is also a very good liar. She also, as an Ethereal, is immune to Power Absorption or Theft, or even disabling of it, but it does however slightly dampen her usage of it, but can't remove it.
Special Ability: Nyx can inflict such terror and fear in her foes, that they will do anything out of fear. This however, is only accessable when Nyx is in her True Shadow Form, which severely drains her afterwards, and she forgot how to access that for. Technically though, this power is present naturally, but is severely weakened.
Personality: Nyx was a very violent and hateful Ethereal, in fact, so violent and feared, she was actually BANISHED from Asiol, which broke the sacred law of all Ethereal, much like Hades was banished from Olympus. She harbored an intense grudge, and still does. When Nyx gained amnesia, she became much more peaceful and benevolent, but with a subconscious hateful and violent mentality. Nyx is currently trying to regain her memories.
Backstory: Nyx is an Ethereal, thus, she is very powerful and ancient. Nyx is one of the 7 original Ethereal's. Nyx is twin of Hemera, Ethereal of Day. Nyx is the mother Nesoi and Arioa; Ethereal of Islands an Music
Fan of Greek Mythology, Percy Jackson and BIONICLE! What more could someone want?
