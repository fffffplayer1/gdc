Name: Botarax

Species: Toa of Plantlife

Element: Plantlife

Gender: Male

Weapons: Poisonous Tentacle Whips, Boneblades, Claws

Details on Weapons: His Poisonous Tentacles are black as night, and coated with a dark green liquid. His boneblades are just that, blades made out of bone on his shoulders, he can use them as extra tentacles. His claws on his feet are made out of a special heat resistant variant of Protosteel.

Personality: Sweet. He, unlike his twin sister(who was born before him), is a idiot, yet sweet and kind and nice. Some could mirror it to Cragger and Crooler's relationship, yet Crooler would be nicer and less manipulative and devious. And Cragger would be much more idiotic, sweet, kind and loyal.

Bio: Botarax is the other member of the team from Bota Magna. He used to be friends with the Great Being who touched the Ignika, untill he was forced to leave his friend(he secretly stayed in contact). His mother was like a Ga-Matoran, always insisting upon Unity. He was always popular. He and his sister were guarding one day, when Velika(unknown then) blew up the building they were in. Botarax saw his mother and father dying. He was screaming, shouting "MOMMY, DADDY", he pleaded for help. His friend, the Great Being attempted to heal them, but ended up turning them into Toa, a Toa of Air and Toa of Lightning. He was crying, that's when he saw Velika. He nearly finished off Velika, but "Stone King" held him back. He then, along with his sister, was recruited into the Servants of The Shard. He personally recommended his parents to join. Levonirax and Votarina. They managed to get in. They all held memories, and kept it a secret. Although there is no biological relationship between them(except for Botanara and Botarax), they act as if it is still there. They will not stop till they find Velika..........and end him. However they have been convinced a Matoran(Stonarix) is Velika, and are thus hunting him, nothing will stop them from them getting there revenge.....nothing

Name: Fenoraknh

Species: Toa of Iron

Element: Iron

Weapons: He has but 1 weapon. His legendary Chainsaw, Vetkrex. Made out of a form of Protosteel combined with Crystalline Protodermis inside a BLACK HOLE. Thus resulting in it being incredibly hard, and sharp.

Details: His weapon is one of a kind. It is the second of the Black Hole line of weaponry. Though it as a hole is unique, the material is not. He often goes to Batoraxa and Kratella for extra Black Hole Protosteel. Any Toa of Shadow, combined with Toa of Gravity can create it with some difficulty, but add a Toa of Iron, it is a piece of cake.

Personality: Honest. He is a honest, reliable, fun dude.

Bio: He was once a proud Fe-Matoran blacksmith. He discovered a rare material, black hole protodermis. He learned how to create it, and how to create black hole Protosteel as well. He was the mentor of Deytanira, as well as her best friend. He would find a Toa Stone and train her. Later when she left, he was confronted. He was confronted by the deceased Dark Hunters "Wateraxel", Wateraxe and "Magmablaze". He would be forced to destroy them to save everyone. He felt like he betrayed everyone. But he got over it. He would then guard the island. He eventually left, leaving his apprentice(currently unknown), to guard the island. He would travel to Xia, becoming a high ranked member of there society. In fact, he was considered the PRINCE of Xia. He would create and develop the idea of the Black Hole weaponry, but he kept it to himself. He joined The Shard with Deytanira. Happy to see her once more.

Name: Cetalia

Species: Toa of Psionics

Element: Psionics

Weapons: Her Black Hole Gauntlets. They are simple in appearence. By verbally shouting "Reinforce", her power can be tripled, however it takes a toll. If she uses it to much, she could lapse into a Coma. Thus she rarely does it. She has more commands, such as "Assault", "Override" and more, they have various effects. She also weilds claws.

Kanohi: Suletu, Komau, Mask of Rahi Control, Mask of Psychometry, Calix and a Mask of Sensory Aptitude.

Personality: Mysterious. Cetaia is rarely seen most of the time, only recently has she decided to show herself. Thus it is not known by most what her personality is. However, she has a keen mind, akin to a Solipsil Persona, but at a much lesser scale.

Bio: Cetalia was always a Toa. She was always in mountain ranges, or caves or mysterious places. She rarely showed herself to most. She practiced hard, thus her Psionic abilities are at there peak. She later was recruited. She usually hangs out with Kratella, Konalia, Onurakna and Batoraxa. She is known for her bravery, courage and cunning. She was sent on many missions. She honed her Black Hole Gauntlets, and they are embedded with energized psionic gems. She helped but Fenoraknh's energized iron gems in his chainsaw. Her Gauntlets were the first Black Hole weapons, and thus the most experimental. Her Psionic Gems store bits of energy, which charge her gauntlets. She later went with a fellow Toa of Psionics, who was described as "sassy and rude as heck!" in her own words. She successfully destroyed the fellow Toa, and no one suspected a thing. She soon became more like Tuyet, but she is not 100% insane, and she has morals and values and honor. She eventually grew close to Kratella. She grew interested in Kratella's Toa of Light brother, his mentality being curious to her. She however sensed a aura around it, not being abled to fully understand it. She and Kratella exterminated a mutant combination of Frostelus and Protocarin race

Toa Team: Hauden

Name Derives From: A combination of Iden, 'spirit', and Hau, 'shielding'.  The name translates to 'protectors of the spirit'.

Goal: Prevent the Great Spirit Mata Nui from falling into any misfortune.

Team Members: Demaron (Toa of Sonics, Leader - missing), Fesur (Toa of Iron, Deputy), Vonai (Toa of Lightning), Fanos (Toa of Magnetism), Sukan (Toa of Plasma), Batudon (Toa of Gravity), Bohak (Toa of Plantlife) and Kenisa (Toa of Psionics).

History: The Hand of Artakha was one group dedicated to serving Mata Nui - the Toa Hauden were another.  Some of the first ever Toa to be created and named for their elemental prefixes, these Toa of the secondary elements were to protect Mata Nui from coming to any harm.

 

When they learned of the threat from the Brotherhood of Makuta, they headed to their fortress on Destral, but were soundly beaten by the Brotherhood's forces.  Only Demaron escaped.  The remainder of the group were kept in statis for several hundred years until the destruction of the Kraata of Statis Field holding them allowed them to escape.  Since then, they have searched the universe for their lost leader, but have been unable to find him.

 

I will begin to post profiles for each Toa below.

Name: Vonai

Type: Toa of Lightning

Team: Toa Hauden

Position: Member

Personality: Rather irritable, but very enthusiastic about the team's chances.  In fact, so enthusiastic that she will regularly jump into a mission without thinking about the consequences, forcing her teammates to go in after her.

Weapons: Bolt Javelin (javelin that electrically stuns the target and returns to Vonai's hand after throwing), Electrified Shuriken Launcher

Kanohi: Sanok, Kakama, Volitak, Pehkui, Mask of Psychometry, Zatth

Change to Bolt Javelin: Instead of electrically stunning the target, it creates a spark between the javelin and the target if it misses by two metres or fewer.
