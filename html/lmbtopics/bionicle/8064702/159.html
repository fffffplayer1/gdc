Name: Rasu(Rat-a-sue)

Codename: The Hunter

Species: A.I. Unit fused with a Vortixx

Appearence: Rasu looks like a mix between Sidorak, Heltroz, a Niazesk and a Toa

Powers: As standard, Rasu has incredible healing abilities, except his is very strange. His healing manifests as something, usually a sword or cordak bullet, striking his inflicted area's, healing them. Rasu is notable for being the most calm of the A.I. Units. He, however, when enraged, is very chaotic, and a force to be wreckoned with. Rasu's unique ability is rather simple, honestly. He can analyze an opponent, allowing him to predict outcomes and moves, making him the best tactician. Rasu also is extremely experienced in hand to hand combat, specializing in Swords. He also has Toa level Iron powers.  He is the Weapon's Specialist of the team.

Abilities: Rasu when enraged, is much more powerful. He also has enhanced Agility and Strength, but with Strength, it only is active when he has a Sword or Weapon in hand. He also has telepathy.

Personality: Rasu is....difficult. He is very calm, in appearence anyway. In actuality, he is very chaotic, vengeful, and prone to wrath. He is also an experienced liar, or at least, he's very good at acting. He is hated by Surosuna because he can so easily mask his emotions. In reality, he only desires praise, admiration and to be remembered. Due to that, he can easily be manipulated. He also enjoys hunting, referring to battles as, hunts, he is also a very experienced and powerful hunter. He only speaks through telepathy, unless required.

Backstory: Once during the Xian/Makuta Wars and the Black Order Ordeals, he was a simple Vortixx named Hunter. He encountered a strange substance(which is considered Liquid Energized Metasteel), and, realizing it's properties, he merged himself with an A.I. Unit. Most times, the A.I. is in control, but sometimes the Vortixx will take control. He joined the A.I. Team when Puraido recruited him, making him grateful and loyal to Puraido.

Rasuta has the special power to manipulate the environment at will, as well as having the ability to merge with most things. He also has Iron powers, on pair with a Toa. Rasu also is extremely experienced in hand to hand combat, as well as Martial Arts. He is typically Puraido's enforcer, due to his ability to manipulate the environment. He also shares his position of Puraido's enforcer with Rasuto.

Abilities: Rasuta has extremely powerful telekinesis. Rasuta also gave every other A.I. Unit on the team Telekinesis, although at a lower level. Rasuta is also very crafty. Rasuta also has extremely powerful Sonic Screaming.

Personality: Rasuta is very childish. Rasuta desires for praise, admiration and to be worshipped, yes. He looks to Puraido as a fatherly figure. Rasuta also looks to Surosuna as a mother figure. Rasuta is enraged when something bad happens to them. Rasuta is also very diligent and a hard worker.

Backstory: Originally, Rasuta was the residiual energy of a battle between Surosuna, Puraido and a very upset Tazzuk. This energy formed into an extremely powerful being, but was killed by Tazzuk. When an A.I. Unit was built, the remains of Rasuta merged with it. It then joined the team, and Rasuta fears Tazzuk. Rasuta formed a bond with Surosuna and Puraido, a Mother-Father-Son relationship. Although Surosuna and Puraido dislike each other(for reasons of their own), they are willing to fake it for Rasuta's sake. Eventually they did form a real relationship. Rasuta was overjoyed by this.

He also has normal Shadow powers. Puraido also is extremely experienced in manipulation. Puraido's powers are essentially Shadow on pair with Charix. But his shadows are unable to be manipulated by others. He can create Meta-Shadows, which are better in every way than normal shadows. Abilities: Puraido is able to teleport through Shadows as well as normal teleport. Puraido is extremely fast and agile, but on his own, he isn't strong. But his Shadows are strong and can absorb other powers

Personality: Puraido is extremely prideful, arrogant and cruel. During battle he taunts and is quite cruel. He also makes small chit chat with his allies, before reverting to his usual demenor.
