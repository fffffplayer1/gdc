Name: Kortall

Species: Toa

Affiliation: Rogue

Element: Sand

Mask: Mask of Aging.

Weapons: An energy siphon blade and an arm-mounted plasma cannon.

Appearance: Light tan armor with brown highlights and yellow eyes.

Backstory: Originally, Kortall was a sand glatorian. While the GSR was being built, Kortall stumbled across the device one of the GB's intended to use to be one of the MU inhabitants. Out of curiosity he activated it, being turned into a toa via the machine. Fearing the wrath of whoever managed to build this thing, Kortall fled into the GSR. Eventually he realized he could control sand (due to the tech in the toa body) and wandered for years, never choosing a side in the never-ending wars raging across the robot.

Behavior: Kortall never learned the toa code, and as such will smash his opponents if necessary. Being originally a glatorian, he doesn't socialize much with anyone.

Name: Malgorexa

Alias(es): The Third Sign of the Apocalypse, WinterMoon Blaze and, most commoly: The Life Master

Species: One-Of-A-Kind

Loyalties: None

Element: Lightning, Gravity and Water

Powers: Malgorexa has the powers of basic elements. Lightning, Gravity and Water. It also utilizes an enhanced version of Poison, Anger, Fear, Illusion, Shattering and Weather Control.

Personality: Malgorexa is the neutral one of the group, also known as Morally Grey. Where Jindrethiel is Morally White and Mandrexiel is Morally Black, Malgorexa is Morally Grey. Malgorexa is also known for having many mood swings that it cannot control.

Tool(s): Malgorexa has an extremely diverse assortment of Tools. Malgorexa typically uses a Bow and Arrow, but is also known to use a Winterborne Axe, Wintermoon Summit Shield and some other things. Malgorexa has honed it's abilities so much so, that it's Accuracy surpasses that of the Makuta power of Accuracy

Backstory: Malgorexa was once a poor child on Avarx III, Malgorexa never had any friends. When Malgorexa became afficalated with the Black Phoenix Gang, Malgorexa learned and became extremely deadly. Malgorexa bonded deeply with two other members, Silver Hornet and Golden Fury were their names. Malgorexa, Silver Hornet and Golden Fury were inseperable, on a mission, Malgorexa saw both of their demises up close. She snapped, and tried to merge with them in order to bring them back, what resulted? Well, she died as a result. However, she was given an opportunity to live once again, and she gladly accepted when she was tricked into thinking her family and friends would join her. Eventually, Malgorexa merged with all of them, resulting in a metamorphisis, turning it, into something different and strange. Malgorexa became The Third Sign of the Apocalypse!

We have been betrayed. Our trust, misguided. Our faith, put wrongly. Have they no shame? But we will live on, my brothers. We will strive forward, we will endure. We will ascend. Free of limitations, free of fear.
