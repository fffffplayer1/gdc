Just some rahi i intend to use alot more.

 

 

Name: Speed Striker

Species: Rahi

Weapons: Speed Strikers have no Weapon Weapons. They have Incredibly Sharp Teeth containing a special Venom that can paralyze small Rahi with one bite, however on anything bigger than a Matoran it takes an entire Pack to fend off something like a Toa. They have little Hooks on the ends of they're tails, which contains a stronger doesage of the Venom, but it isn't as sharp so it cannot penetrate armor as easily. They also have Claws, which can only scratch Protodermis minorly and have nothing special in them.

Abilities: Speed Strikers are incredibly fast, but they are weak in Strength and Endurability.

Other: Speed Strikers can speak, and when they do, it is in a Metallic voice, and they put emphasis on the letter "a".

Speed Strikers are semi-sentient, they can speak and make decicisions, but they cannot function properly without a Pack Leader, which is usually known as they are larger and have a Row of Spines down they're pack.

 

Name: Sky Striker or Wing Striker

Species: Rahi

Weapons: Sky Strikers have little Rhoutuka which enhance things such as Agility/Intelligence/Strength or Endurability if they wish, or, they can create an uncontrollable pain like Avohkii's(credit to AD). They also have Six, insectoid wings that, unlike actual insects, are quite durable and razor sharp, they also have long tails, which end in what appears to be a Warhammer. But the Queen's have Eight Wings, Two Heads and one tail.

Abilities: Sky Strikers are known for creating a cloud of smoke when in large groups, but they are all hive-minded. Meaning they can only be controlled by a Queen unlike they're land bound bretheren(Speed Strikers), which are Pack Dominated meaning the control of the group can change. When a Queen should give an order, they will not ever stop until the Queen makes a new order or tells them to stop. The Queen is completely Sentient with two minds, a defense oriented oen and attack oriented one.

(This chracter is very, very roughly based off of the Starcraft character 'Zeratul', so go ahead and look him up if you want a vague reference to his appearance – especially his weapon.

Also, he'll be serving as the Toa Orde of this universe )

 

Name: Velaz

 

Species: Toa

 

Mask: Huna

 

Element: Psionics

 

Weapons/Tools/Defenses/Abilities: Velaz wields twin forearm-mounted golden 'hilts', from which he can channel his immense psionic powers into to create extremely powerful blades of pure mental energy which can easily slice through anything short of layered Protosteel like warm butter. He also, obviously, has great amounts of psionic power at his disposal and is extremely mentally disciplined, despite being outright insane. Also, due to his breathing apparatus, he is unable to speak and instead uses his mental abilities to project controlled, emotionless thoughts onto single or multiple targets of his choosing.

 

Appearance: Velaz, despite having been mutated as a result of being a Pit prisoner, surprisingly did not deviate too greatly from his land form. He still wears the deep golden-blue armor that had been granted to him upon his creation, but it was heavily damaged from countless battles. His mask had also been modified into a water-breathing apparatus. Though he has been made slightly taller from his Pit transformation, his now-hunched stature brings him back down to the height of other Toa.

 

Backstory: Velaz was the first Toa of Psionics, as well as one of the first Toa to ever be created. During a mission to calm the Zyglak, but he drove them into a state of such insanity that the Great Beings had him exiled to The Pit as its first prisoner. Guarded by Malixos Robots who, being completely mechanical, were immune to his powers. Thousands of years later, a great cataclysm shook the world, causing his cell – and that of many others to be demolished.

Unfortunately, with this prison break, many of the prisoners of the Pit – in fact, almost all of them began to break out into a massive battle, picking up whatever makeshift weapons they could get their hands on and hurling at groups of combatants. Velaz silently stalked through the battle, completely frying the minds of anyone who had gotten within ten meters of him or giving them horrific hallucinations of their very being literally slowly taken apart before their own eyes.

 

After the war died down with barely a tenth of the prisoners that had been held captive there walking away in one piece, Velaz dwelled on the outskirts of Barraki territory, keeping his Huna active for prolonged periods of time to avoid detection. However, this came with the effect of him often phasing in and out of visibility and his mask activating randomly without his permission (however, this was later solved as his psionic powers continued to develop). He eventually decided to raid the Barraki armory when they were at each other's throats for the Mask of Life, acquiring twin golden hilts that once housed intricate wristblades. He later shaped these weapons into tools that he could use to channel his psionic abilities into weapons of pure mental power.

 

Soon, he began to hear the thoughts of strange sky-bound Makuta...below the sea's bottom? Intrigued, he began to carve his way through the rock with his psi-blades, carving deeper and deeper until he began to hear the thoughts of Matoran as well. Eagerly, he continued to dig until the rock felt even lighter. Not soon after, with the last swipe of his blade he found an immense cave, immesurable by the naked eye. He also realized that he was falling down a newly created waterfall...straight into his demise.
