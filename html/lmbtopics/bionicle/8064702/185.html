I would like to announce an expanded backstory for Murky cus, well... Murky

 

Name: "Murky"

Kanohi: Noble miru, felnas

Bio: As a Matoran, Murky had a very proud and successful life. He was manager of the chute transport system of Metru Nui, and he enjoyed his work. However, he was also secretly a professional recruiter for the Illidakii. Every time he had finished with his usual job, he would don a felnas and sneak out to the southern chains, in search of Toa with unusual hatred for the Brotherhood. These kinds of Toa would be taken through a random route every day, under cover of darkness, they were taken to be offered to join the rank and file of the Makuta Hunters. One fateful day though, that felnas of his got him blasted with elemental energy so bad, that he had to be taken to Karzahni for emergency repairs. Where he was robbed of all his memories and any prior life he may of had. He is presumed dead by those he worked with.

The original Illidak backstory kind of contradicts the backstory of other characters. I'd like to make a few small changes.

 

Name: Illidak.

Species: Toa that has been fused with a dead Makuta. (EP is wonderful )

Elements: slightly limited plasma (physically (not lawfully) unable to perform extremely taxing attacks such as nuva blasts). Extremely limited shadow (barely able to get out a reasonable shadow bolt).

Powers: Heat vision (from antidermis eyes), anger, fear, poison, fragmentation, and plasma (able to create and maintain just enough plasma to coat his blades in the stuff without any cost to elemental energy using this power).

Kanohi: Jutlin.

Weapons: Plasma-proof warglaives (he manually coats them in the stuff after all)

Appearance: On teh gallery.

Physiology: He has no natural eyes and instead uses unliving antidermis that has been permanently fused to his face to see. He is able to see just as well as he would have if he had regular eyes, but has the added benefit of being able to sense nearby Makuta. This is a very minute amount of antidermis which is identityless (trust me when I say that this is possible). He has above average agility and strength thanks to both the fusion and his prior training. He has wing-like appendages that allow him to perform the previously-thought-to-be-impossible double jump technique which up until now has been limited to video game physics. His additional kraata powers besides heat vision are granted by the EP fusion, which rendered the Makuta he was fused with practically dead.

Personality: No matter what he is always serious and accomplishes tasks with efficiency and and priority. He hates having to take a break from a hunt to protect innocents, and would rather be chasing after Makuta than fighting their minions if at all possible. He grossly hates Makuta to an extreme point, but has the discipline to unleash this hatred constructively. He's not social, but is capable of teamwork and even leadership.
