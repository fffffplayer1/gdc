Name: Hgiz

Species: He looks like a Toa. However, he is actually a Makuta experiment

Element: N/A

Powers: Creation of VIruses, virus control

Weapons: A sword and Zamor launcher. The launcher is connected via a teleportation system to a vault of Zamor spheres.

Backstory: He was created by the Makuta to try to create a more effecient method for making viruses. While this was a success, he ran away, and watched Matoran on the Southern Continent for many years. He longed to be a brave Toa and protect Matoran. ONe day, he found a certain extradimensional Makuta with a Mask of Mutation. The Makuta gave him the form of a Toa, and found him a Kakama to wear. From then one, he has wandered, seeking villains to put in their place.

Other: He does not follows the Toa COde, having never been taught it. INstead, he follows a code of honor that all repectable beings in the alternate dimension Stellax is from follow. This does not prohibit killing in honest and fair combat, but prevents things such as killing in a sneak attack, and general cliche samurai stuff.

Weaknesses: (So as not to incur the wrath of the Rolrplay Rep ) Virus creation takes a long time, though he has 'programmed' a few attacks so to be able to create those viruses quickly.  These consist of:

A simple knockout virus.

A virus that can heal his organic parts

and a virus that eats away at metal.

These all are short-lived: The knockout lives for about two seconds, which is enough to KO most beings. The healer last for about fifteen minutes. The metal-eating one acts fast, but only lasts ten minutes. By expending more energy, he can create longer-lived ones, but these are hard to make and he is very vulnerable while trying to make them.

Plural: Spheroid's

Prey: Anything really.

Predator: Tahtorak.

Habitat: Scalpasc. A muddy and rainy world. It is good for planting due to the soil. It is filled with poisons and toxins.

Name: Megawhatt

Plural: Megawhatt's

Powers and abilities: Megawhatt's can control electricity and can inhabit electrical devices, controlling them from the inside. Megawhatt's grow stronger by absorbing electricity and can multiply if split in half, or if they have enough power. It should be noted that the young ones might not know they can. They are around the strength of a toa in physical stength. Another notable ability is that the noises Megawhatt's normally use to communicate with, at a certain pitch and volume, can be used as a sonic scream which can shatter glass. They can control lightning and electricity at the level of a high powered Toa.

Weakness: They are weak against water, and powerless against insulators. They are also vulnerable to those who can absorb them. After there energy is drained they fall asleep.

Prey: Electricity/Lightning, and those who store it.

Predator: Corzelians

Appearence: The females appear as buzzshock from omniverse, while the males appear as buzzshock from omniverse but with the color scheme of the original series Megawhatt's.

Behavior:

Megawhatt's exist to have "fun," which in their case is to cause trouble wherever they go. While they cause a lot of damage, Megawhatt's do not seem to be evil, and in fact are not openly hostile to any species except prey, merely lacking any self-control or sense of the danger they may be causing.

Megawhatt's will openly attack if they are provoked.

The Megawhatt's language consists of a series of high-pitched squeaking and chirping noises. That is how WILD Megawhatt's behave. While normal ones are very energetic, but are not troublesome, they have some self control, but not much. They are hostile to threats. The queen of each colony is the oppisite, she loves to play, but she has MAJOR self control, to the point of having 100% self control.
