Because I'm really good at thinking up more characters than I need:

Name: Gatava

Species: Ga-Toa.

Krana: She carries a mixture of six Krana, excluding Vu and Yo.

Weaponry: Gahlok shields and enhanced mechanical parts relative to most toa. She commands a swarm of Gahlok.

Personality: In a nutshell? Clean it all it must be cleaned.

Backstory: Long ago, Gatava and her companion, Kolei, went to fend off a swarm of Bohrok. Unfortunately for them, some Gahlok adapted to their tactics and assimilated the two into the swarm. Eventually the swarm went back to dormancy and only recently have the two awoken once more.

Appearance: Orange eyes and krana, blue armor. Average Ga-toa appearance otherwise.

 

Name: Kolei-Kal [Koe-lay-kahl]

Species: Ko-toa-Kal

Element: Sonics, due to Kal mutagen.

Krana: He carries around a mixture of six Krana-Kal, excluding Xa-Kal and Ca-Kal.

Weaponry: Kohrok and Kohrok-kal shields. He plans to rally the bohrok, rather than let them be a forgotten and obsolete species...

Personality: Cold and cunning.

Backstory: Long ago, Kolei was fighting a swarm of bohrok. Some Gahlok adapted to his tactics and assimilated him into the swarm. He was taken to the bohrok nest, where he and his krana were exposed to Kal mutagen. Eventually he went into dormancy with the rest of the swarm until very recently.

Appearance: Silver armor, silver krana, light blue eyes. Average toa appearance otherwise.

Species: Bo/Kra-toa. His moral light is at a weird spot, being half-drained and a mental barrier in place, yet still able to replenish itself to exactly half. Thus he can wield elemental shadow from one hand, and The Green from the other with temporary effects on his moral status.

Kanohi: Due to his odd moral status, he sees nothing wrong with masks usually considered immoral. Thus he makes use of the Crast, the Mask of intangibility, Zatth, Mask of rahi control, Tryna, and Mask of scavenging.

Weaponry: An energy extraction rifle and a battleaxe, both capable of channeling his elements.

Personality: Despite having very ambiguous morals and thus not being moved by immoral things, he does sometimes worry about the fact that he doesn’t worry about such things. He will, in dire situations, extravagantly use his plantlife powers to bolster his moral shadow, allowing him to fight more efficiently.

Appearance: He has a black and dark blue-green color scheme. Similar to Mutran, if his green was a darker version of Bo-toa green. Otherwise, a little taller than your average toa, with a pair of purely aesthetic wings due to his subconscious image of toa on the event of his becoming one.

Name: Unknown. Called the Paladin.

Powers:

  • The Paladin's ironclad will can resist most forms of mental assault.
  • He has incredible endurance and strength, on par with Pakari users.
  • He can teleport himself and allies at will.
  • His protosteel/exsidian nanoweave armor is extremely strong, albeit quite heavy.
  • "Situational Awareness" the Paladin passively absorbs telepathic info from nearby nonsheilded beings, giving him an awareness of the situation.
  • Teleportation: the Paladin can teleport. His operates on a different frequency, allowing him to get through most shields, but also takes much longer to activate.
  • Power disabling: A single hit of his protoexsidian warhammer disables the target's powers for a minute(post). COnsecutive hits increase the time esponentially, up to a max of (six posts).
  • He has access to all the data and records of his predecessors(See spoilers)

Backstory: Though he is at the point where he is in the storybooks of little Agori, the original Paladin was a guardian employed by the Great Beings before the failed Baterra project.  However, there is more to this then meets the eye...

SPOILERS

Jammers
In fact, the title, armor and weapon are passed down from father to child. The powers are passed down as well, through some genius nanotech. The current Paladin is a younger Fire Glatorian, who is rather naive in his perception of the world.

Name: Landcrusher

Type: Tank

Crew: One Glatorian driver; one Agori gunner - reloading is performed automatically

Dimensions: Hull is 8m long, 4m wide and 3m tall; gun turret is a further 1.5m tall

Weaponry: Turret-mounted gun, 4 rapid-fire front-mounted Plasma launchers, vibrational boring assembly

Ammunition: 100 'Earthshock' kinetic bolts, 10 'Farstriker' guided missiles

Abilities: Vibrational boring assembly causes the entire hull to vibrate rapidly when activated to dislodge earth and as such burrow through the ground or to hurt enemies, diverting power from the tracks.

 

Name: Tremorcrusher

Type: Flagship Tank

Crew: Teron - reloading and activation of the Plasma emitters is performed automatically

Dimensions: Hull is 24m long, 16m wide and 6m tall; gun turret is a further 1.5m tall

Weaponry: Ultra-heavy turret-mounted gun with 8 secondary standard guns wrapped around it in gatling gun fashion, front-mounted Plasma array consisting of 30 rapid-fire Plasma emitters, 5 rapid-fire side-mounted Plasma emitters per side, vibrational boring assembly

Heavy Gun Ammunition: 25 'Colossus' heavy kinetic bolts, 5 'Obliterator' explosive and anti-metal virus-releasing bolts

Secondary Gun Ammunition: 1000 'Earthshock' kinetic bolts, 250 'Farstriker' guided missiles

Abilities: Vibrational boring assembly causes the entire hull to vibrate rapidly when activated to dislodge earth and as such burrow through the ground or to hurt enemies, diverting power from the tracks.

Trivia: It is a mystery to the Agori who constructed this monster how Kolokvar has managed to decrease the weight of the thing enough to prevent it from collapsing in on itself or simply sinking into the ground.  They apparently don't have a wide knowledge of weight distribution; the Tremorcrusher features four sets of very, very wide tracks.  These tracks are mounted in a similar way to a train's bogies to slightly improve the tank's abysmal manoueverability.
