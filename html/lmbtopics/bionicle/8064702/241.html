Backstory: Like every other Makuta, in the beginning, Zakaku was assigned to an island to watch over. His island was known as Whawhai Nui, where Toa trained themselves to use their tools, powers, and masks in creative ways to better protect the Matoran. Zakaku didn't like his job. He thought of it as a nuisance that was in the way of him making scientific progress, so he eventually started work on some creations to do the job of overseeing the island. Then, when he was making his officers, he had a thought: how would the Toa and Matoran react to an oppressive government with much more power than they had? Zakaku had heard some rumors about a group of war lords planning to take control of the MU, and while they would obviously fail, he could create a small scale version of what their vision most likely was with the right tools. A social experiment. He could never publish his experiment to the public, of course, but it sounded like an intriguing idea to him. So, he made his former officers war hungry psychopaths, gave them a large amount of supplies, tips to conquer the island, and the threat that if they ever tried to extend past Whawhai Nui, they would never see the light of day again. One of the generals learned that he meant what he said the hard way, and ever since, the remaining generals never dared to go outside Whawhai Nui...until the GSR crashed. Then they had to move. Zakaku found them later, and now they serve as Zakaku's assistants, guards, and other positions in his lab.
