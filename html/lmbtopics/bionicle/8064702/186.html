So, the characters I mentioned in the discussion topic...

Name: Komariak.

Name Meaning: ‘Extremely strong controller of ice’

Species: Toa of ice.

Kanohi: Akaku, Sanok, Adaptation, Sensory Aptitude, Power Amplification(Credit to Gold), Akrarod, fanon mask of perceptory filtration; Allows the user to apply a ‘perceptory filter’ to a target. Basically the target will be unnoticed, like someone out of focus in a picture of a crowd, until they draw attention to themselves somehow. Pierced by Elda and the active level of a Rode. Doesn’t affect mentally shielded peeps.

Weaponry: Komariak wields a Twilight Blade, a shield which looks somewhat like an enlarged Kohrahk shield but with the saw replaced by a rhotuka launcher, and an Impact Crystal Launcher. His rhotuka can form zamor crystal, knowledge crystal, and the form of crystal used by the griffin gauntlet which can pierce protosteel.

Personality: Typical Ko-toa; quiet, apparently emotionless, ruthlessly logical and cold. True to his name he commands his element and his body with equal prowess.

Appearance: White armor, light blue eyes. His armor is currently in bad condition.

Backstory: Komariak is a toa who defended Metru Nui with ruthless skill. Eventually Dark Hunters were hired to lure him out of the city and keep him out. Said Hunters succeeded after a vicious struggle, and brought him back to Odina to be kept in stasis as a trophy akin to Varian. Per universe breaking he has been freed.

Name: Cekara.

Name Meaning: ‘Wise and powerful psychic’

Species: Toa of psionics.

Kanohi: Iden, Kualsi, Psychometry, Healing, Mask of heat(Does what it says on the tin), Cevaku, Fanon mask of foresight; allows the wielder to percieve their senses from 2-3 seconds in the future. This future, unlike that seen by a mask of clairvoyance, can be changed, allowing the user to parry and dodge with great skill. It is, however, mentally taxing and distracting to use for any period of time, and requires a good bit of skill to use effectively. Cekara has not yet mastered this mask.

Weaponry: A rhotuka launching shield as well as a group of 20 thin near-identical blades she wields via telekinesis. These are designed to fit together to form 2 thicker blades she stores on her back. They can be moved to overlap and resemble wings. The middle two of each larger blade(Making a total of four) are attached to her back via thin metal cables, allowing them to be used for maneuvering. Her rhotuka power accelerates a target’s mental processes for a time. This can apply negative acceleration, and thus can make a target see everything in slow-mo or fast-forward.

Personality: Mysterious. Is mute and therefore quiet, but will on occasion speak via telepathy.

Appearance: Mostly gold armor with blue highlights(Or shading?). Amber eyes, her armor is pretty bulky. I guess that’s it.

Backstory: Cekara was going to Xia to get her blades upgraded when the universe flipped over. Oh, and Xia was missing but that’s less important right now.

♪...Divide them with doubt it will all wash away...♪

Name: Fekari.

Name Meaning: ‘Iron strength’.

Species: Toa of iron.

Kanohi: Pakari, Kiril, Growth, Emulation, Biomechanics, Arthrari, fanon mask of reflection. This mask reflects the force of attacks into the attacker, akin to Balta’s repellers. However, the user must directly focus on the attack to reflect it; If the attack is too stealthy or fast to focus on it cannot be blocked.

Weaponry: A rhotuka launcher mounted on his shoulder. He also wields a blade which can emit a sonic hum akin to that of a Boggarak. His rhotuka power gives an effect similar to a Jutlin.

Personality: Iron will, blunt as a hammer, bitter about the Nynrah Ghosts.

Appearance: Fekari’s armor is mostly silver, with rusty-orange highlights. The boots have been worn down a bit to a duller gray color, and his left shoulder armor is bulkier to support the rhotuka launcher. Pretty average armor otherwise. His eyes are grayish.

Backstory: Once upon a time, Fekari dreamt of becoming one of the legendary Nynrah Ghosts. Unfortunately for him, his skill was insufficient, and he could not achieve this goal. Bitter at the Nynrah Ghost’s superior skill, he spent his time brooding and storming through any place with few others around. On one of these angered walks, he was found and recruited by an OoMN agent who thought he could be trained to be an effective fighter. Eventually he became a toa, and scourge to the Makuta… Atleast, until the Dark Hunters hunted him down and brought him in. He was put in stasis as a trophy and a testament to the Dark Hunter’s might.

Not so sure about this one, as it might bend the rules a bit with the back story .___. This also explains why there are no toa of light in the RP, other than the ban.

 

Name: Razen

 

Species: Kra-toa, was originally destined to be av-toa.

 

Mask: mahiki

 

Weapons: Shadow katana

 

Appearance: Razen is slightly tall and just a bit muscular, as most toa are, with crimson-colored eyes. His armor is almost ornate in design, with colors of black and gold. He carries his katana on his back, as well as he always seems to appear slightly blurry, an aura of shadow surrounding himself at nearly all times.

 

Personality: Razen is usually a serious individual, having no time for antics of any sorts. Though all of his moral light has been drained, he still upholds the teachings of unity, duty, and destiny. He loyally carries out the orders of the makuta of Destral without question, unafraid of any task he is given.

 

Back story: Razen was one of the few av-matoran, and was supposed to become a toa of light. When word came to the brotherhood that a toa of light would emerge, they took every action to stop him. Just before he became toa, a thought-to-be defeated makuta threw a shadow leech on Razen, draining him of all his moral light before he became a toa. Razen emerged as a toa of shadow, and was taken to Destral where he would act as a guardian for many thousands of years. Now, the barrier keeping him from gaining his moral light back is too strong to be broken, and he will never become what he was destined to be millenias ago.
