Name: N/A

 

Species: Baterra (Heavily modified)

 

Mask: None

 

Element: Shapeshifting...?

 

Weapons/Tools/Defenses/Abilities: This Baterra, along with all of the Baterra's standard abilities, also is much, MUCH stronger than the average Baterra, which is saying something. Instead of the standard claws, he was equipped with more hook-shaped weapons that were attached to a chain which could be extended to pull foes towards him.

 

Appearance: The Baterra retains most of the recognizable features visible on a normal Baterra, however his armor is much, much stronger, while also allowing even more agility. He also appeared much more humanoid than other beings of his kind, making him seem even more menacing somehow.

 

Backstory: This certain Baterra was made to act as a commander, and thus was given much, much better AI than the simple directive of the rest. Eventually, one of the Great Beings began to notice the Brotherhood of Makuta's suspicious behavior, and decided to pluck one of the Baterra from Sphereus Magna, giving it modifications to recognize who was an enemy and who wasn't (as opposed to "Smash anyone with a weapon"). It then was given vastly superior weapons and armor to the classic Baterra model to act as a one man army of sorts, and was sent to the  Great Spirit Robot to fufill its mission of enforcing the will of the Great Beings.

"Oh, his food is fine. Just...make sure to get the 'boneless' meat."

Name: Balaki (formerly Balak)

Affiliation: Toa

Tribe: Earth

Appearance: Imagine a Toa Metru with Lewa's 2015 mask on him, with golden armor.

Personality: Serious, Confident, and occasionally cruel. 

Weapon(s): Razor Blades which can destroy anything composed of dirt, sand, and thick grass.

Power(s): Has been known to crystalize enemies into sand or dirt, and can also shatter the ground if he's with either a Toa of Iron, a Toa of Stone, or is strong enough.

Backstory: Balak was once a humble Po-Matoran (is that correct?) in Metru Nui who enjoyed digging and spent his spare time doing it. It was when he was heading from the village to a meadow that he encountered a Visorak. He somehow managed to befriend it, and even when he became a Toa and even beyond that, the Visorak was a loyal ally to him. Balak, however, noticed that the days turned sour, and that all wasn't well. The Turaga knew this and advised them to leave to another place. It was during this that he learned he would become the next Toa of Earth. But before this would happen, he must meet with five other Matoran, band together, and locate a place where the Makuta (the reason for this) wouldn't get them. Balak and the five others managed, the the Earth Village found a new village on another part of Po-Metru (right?). The six Matoran soon became Toa, and fought foes who threatened Metru Nui for a long time...until they retired and became Turaga. But before Balaki became a Turaga, he hoped that when he retired, a new Matoran would rise and take his name as Toa of Earth. And boy, would this come true!

Armor: He has golden armor in the shape of Visorak, to symbolize his interest in them.

His adventures began in Shadow Hills #1, a serial I invented.

Winner Of The 2015 Ninjago Story Awards: " Semi-active as of 11/28/15 "Wait, I forgot to drop the mic." -Batman, "The LEGO Batman Movie" teaser
