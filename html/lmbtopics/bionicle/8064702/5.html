Well, since I feel like having a little fun (And introducing a new sub-plot.), I'm going to introduce a few new characters to the RP. 7 new characters to be precise. So, prepare to meet...THE SEPTET OF DOOM!

 

Backstory for all of them: A long time ago, the Great Beings created 7 eneties in the form of spheres of energy that could manipulate the enviorment. These eneties were SUPPOSED to be governors of sorts, but a mistake during their programming turned them into raging machines of destruction, each one using the energy of a weakness of the beings of the Matoran Universe, a crime agaisnt themselves. The Great Beings managed to defeat them, and seperated their leader, the most powerful of them all, into 4 pieces, scattering them across the land, and into the relams of the unknown. In order to safely keep them in, they made a program so that, whenever one of the safes was opened, which they made sure never happened, they would all be activated. They also made it so that every 100 years, the position of the safe that would activate them all would switch, causing a cycle that takes thousands of years. Unfortunately, my RPC Fereain happened to open the safe containing the Enity of Sloth when his safe was the one that would activate them all, causing he, and soon, the other eneties to rise. Be warned, these machines of destructions are tough to beat, and they get harder and harder as they escape. I will post them in the order of least deadly being first, to most deadly being last.

Name: Snoroseum.

Alias: Enity of Sloth.

Affliction: No one, as he is a tool of destruction, nothing else.

Powers: Extreme Telekenisis, likely the strongest in the universe, Unable to feel pain.

Appearance: A giant, stone, Three-Toed Sloth. (Yes, I know a 3-Toed Sloth is a real life animal. But, hey, he's the enity of Sloth, so, yeah.)

Personality: Lazy, lazy, and LAZY. He's probably one of the laziest beings in the universe. He also disapproves of hard workers, and often chooses them out to be tortured.

Weaknesses: He has one weak point that all these beings share: Their orb. If reached and hit with a powerful enough attack, they will be knocked out for a few hours. Also, he often falls asleep in mid-battle, which is a good time frame to get to him. However, during his sleep, he will often grab several objects with telekinesis, which is unfortunate, due to the fact that his telekinesis is, in fact, stronger when he's asleep, and because he's not in control, it often causes pendimonium.

 

Keep in mind, this guy is the WEAKEST of them all!

Name: Ovekiru.

Alias: Enity of...Well, Love as LM sometimes portrays it in her revenge comedies...

Affliction: None, as she is a tool of destruction, like the rest of these guys.

Appearance: A female toa made of vines, although with noticable berries on her chest.

Personality: Combine Rouge the Bat with every single popular/pretty/flirty girl stereotype you can think of, as well as Poision Ivy from Batman, and you get Ovekiru in a nutshell.

Powers: Control over plants and Rahi, and if she has enough power, can break the laws of a Bio's mind and cast a spell on them, which usually results in: 

A. The victim falling madly in love-in-the-wrong-way with someone, or...

B. Causing them to fall in love-in-the-wrong-way with HER, causing them to guard her at all costs.

Weaknesses: Of course, her orb is her weak point. However, since she is technically stronger then Snoroseum, her orb is stronger, and needs a more powerful hit to take her down. And, of course, being the Enity of Love-in-the-wrong-way, she is often attracted to Male warriors, and often focuses in on them. Unfortunately, these are also the ones she will most likely cast her spell on, and, not to mention, she is more aggresive towards females, as she sees them as a threat.
