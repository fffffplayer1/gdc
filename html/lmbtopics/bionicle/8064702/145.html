Location: Whawhai Nui

Role: Undisturbed Residents / Living Tanks

Description: The Hiku-ahua is an odd creature. Normally, it is a creature that appears to be completely helpless. It's black and grey cocoon-like body is limbless, only having a head with one, singular red eye. However, these creatures are intellegent...sort of. They're close to a Matoran's intellegence, but still pretty brutish. And these creatures also have powerful psionics abilities, which lead to one of their most baffling defence mechanisms: they use said powers to create a humanoid body for themselves out of the environment. The concentration of holding this body together means they don't really use their psionics for much except using it with objects they grab in order to give their bodies superhuman strength. Should a part of their body break off, they'll just grab it with telekinesis and reassemble it. However, since they're passive by nature, they are harmless unless you provoke them.

 

However, Whawhai Nui's conflict has negatively affected these creatures, as it has lead to an increasing amount of mutated specimens that are used in the war by the officer's forces. Mutated specimens grow in size, as well as gaining four spikes where limbs would be. This increases their psionic powers, allowing them to create bigger forms...however, due to the Visorak Venom giving the Hiku-ahua a new beastial nature and severely dampening their intelligence, these larger forms are often abstract ambombinations meant only to destroy. Be warned, these specimens are extremely aggressive.
