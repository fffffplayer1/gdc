Name: Devok

 

Speices: Skakdi

 

Mask: None, due to being a Skakdi

 

Element: Iron

 

Gender: Male

 

Weapons: Zamor Launcher with green and black zamors, giant hammer

 

Power's and abilitys: Due to tampering ago from the Makuta Spiriah, he can use Iron powers without having to be with another Skakdi of the same type. He also is very strong, even stronger than a toa, and has tons of endurance. He also has heat vision. His green Zamors are filled with antidermis, which can turn matorian into mindless slaves and even do a bit of damage to whoever it hits. His black zamors can track a target from anywhere when they hit via and energy pluse. He prefers, however, to batter and smash his opponets to submission with his giant hammer. His hammer, when hit aginst the ground, can generate shockwaves to knock down his opponets, not as big as an earthquake, but still enough to knock down a toa.

 

Bio: Once an Skakdi on the Island of Zakaz, everything changed when an myesterious being rumored to be an makuta appeared and started turn the Island's inhabitants from peaceful creatures into complete monsters, including Devok. The myesterious figure took special interset in Devok due to his rare element of Iron, and made him even stronger than the rest of his fellow Islanders. Devok was able to escape though, and went in hiding for many years. After a while, the makuta died, and Devok came out of hiding, but he was now bitter and cold and still hungry for vengance aginst the Makuta for what they did to his home. Becoming an dark hunter, Devok has a severe hate for the makuta, but in the end is neither good or evil, he works for whichever side pays more.

 

Appearance: A tall Skakdi with a jet black body with red lines all over that glow along with his red eyes when he is angry. He also has bigger feet than an normal Skakdi which he uses as an advantage in combat.

 

If you have any questions or if I made any mistakes, please tell me, this is my first time roleplaying!

Name: Virexia

Species: Toa

Element: Psionics and Plantlife

Kanohi: Kaukau, Mask of Sonics, Komau, Mask of Charisma, Felnas and an Avsa

Weapons: Virexia wields Protosteel Swords, nothing fancy about them, but she can, of course, channel elemental energy through them. Virexia has bladed wings, allowing her to fly.

Abilities: Virexia is most known for being strong and endurable. Virexia has the ability to turn her armor into a crystalline structure that increases her durability a lot. Virexia also can project herself through her plants, able to talk to them, although an unfortunate backlash is that when they are hurt, she is hurt. Virexia is somewhat of a Poison Ivy character when it comes to her plants.

Personality: Virexia is a manipulative, cunning, sly, witch of a Toa. Abandoning all morals, Virexia is the (supposed) ultimate personification of evil among Toa. Virexia tends to manipulate her enemies against one another, usually twisting words into another sentance, sometimes playing on her foes inner desire. Virexia is also very vain and prideful, considering her above all other species, even above the Great Beings themselves. Virexia also considers Toa to be pathetic, protecting the Matoran. Virexia is also a strong and firm believer of Survival of the Fittest.

Backstory: Virexia came from Metru Nui long ago, living as Six different Matoran, one from each Metru, how, this is unknown. These six Matoran found a large vat of Energized Protodermis, where they shed their disguise and were revealed to be one being, something stuck inbetween Matoran and Toa, not a full Toa or a full Matoran, but not even close to Turaga, she dived headfirst into the vat, turning into a true Toa, but it warped her mind. She thought the six matoran she mascaraded as were real, and that they followed them, giving those near her to think she's insane. She also developed a Poison Ivy Complex, being obsessed with Plantlife and treating them as if they were sentient.

Name: Xherod

 

Species: Great being

 

Element: Creation, specializes in Lightning and Shadow

 

Mask: Mask of Umbramortis

 

Weapons: The Voidblade, Lightning Energy Castor, Energy Gauntlets

 

Armor: Xherod wears thick Protosteel armor made from the same armor as the GSR.

 

Bio: Originally, Xherod was the Great being Shadow specialist, but during a Civil skirmish, the GB Lightning specializer was smashed by him. He then claimed his specialty. At one point, he fused a Skrall with an Iron Tribe warrior to create the being Ebenym.

 

 

When the GSR was in production, the GB leader asked for ideas about a failsafe. Xherod suggested the Mask of Umbramortis, which when plugged into the deep deep foundations of the Core Processor, or the deep, deep bowels of the Codrex, would usurp the mind and motor functions of the Great Spirit, and blanket the universe in shadow, eliminating nonessential lifeforms, and infusing the others in a Void mind where work for the Great Spirit is all they can do. This Idea was shot down because it was too heartless, and replaced with the Ignika idea.

 

It was his idea that Mata Nui would create beings of Shadow from Antidermis to protect and populate the universe. Because of this, he feels some slight form of attachment to the Makuta

Xherod also supplied the Bota Magnan Vorox with Lightning Weapons.
