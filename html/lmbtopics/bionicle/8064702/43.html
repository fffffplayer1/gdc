Can fire their elements and powers via eyebeams.

All have adaptive abilities to a limited extent.

Their minds and other important parts are protected by semi-antigation technology, which prevents wielders of elements such as magnetism and iron from crushing them from the inside.

All wield dual bohrok shield/claws that are used for close combat, these channel and slightly amplify the users powers

Ok, I'm introducing a new RPC. Why? I have no idea.

 

Name: ??? (It's unknown.)

Alias: Saser the ever-changing

Appearance: Depends

Affliction: (Possibly) Toa

Species: Plantlife Toa

Kanohi: Mask of Adaption, abilities enchanced by visorak venom

Personality: A wild Rahi, having lost all sanity and now purely relies on instinct.

Backstory: Saser the ever-changing once lived a fine life. He was a Toa, with his team, and they always managed to defend their home. No makuta to worry about, no Dark Hunters, no villains of any kind, really. It was a peaceful little jungle oasis, and Saser loved it. But, one day, his little paradise was about to come to an end. One day, his team faced an enemy they've never faced before: Visorak. They didn't know how they got there, but they wanted to stop them. Unfortunately, a lot of the teammates were taken down by the Visorak, until there was only Saser and the lightning Toa of the team. Saser tried to slice at them with his sword, but all he got was a Visorak bite in return. Saser, being an expert on wildlife, knew well enough that a Visorak's venom would mutate the user into a monster that was like a wild Muaka, tearing up anything in it's path. Or, at least, that's what he believed, living in sucah a paradise. He dropped his sword, and dove into the water, hoping to drown himself before he could harm anyone. However, he forgot that he was wearing a Mask of Adaptation, and he was immediately mutated. But, that mutation was fed by Visorak venom. Unsure what to say, he swam until he found land, and it was then he wandered, his only goal to stay as far away from his peaceful home...At least for a little while. The stress and the side-effect of Visorak venom drove him mad, and he became like the Rahi he once loved, a scavenger. Now he continues to wander, but what happens if he wanders into the wrong place?

Tools: A powerful laser cannon that has the same power as a large rocket launcher.
Powers: Garmord is a tank. He walks stiffly and shoots things and makes things go boom in big ways. His strength and durability is such that he can withstand over 90,000 psi and temperatures exceeding 5,000 degrees Celsius. His mind is practically a computer, predicting over 100,000 different battle scenarios in under a hundredth of a second. He has another power that enables him to transform into a tank. In this mode his speed increases as does his fire-power. Unfortunately it limits his mobility.
