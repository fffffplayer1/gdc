Name: Makuta Angaarok

Mask: Kanohi Matatu, mask of telekinesis.  Looks like Toa Vakama’s and Tahu 2015’s masks combined.

Appearance: Very noble looking, tall, sleek black armor which almost seems to be one piece, although it isn’t, black cape.

Personality: Loyal to the brotherhood, considers all non makuta ‘unworthy’, cruel.  He will also look down on makuta who do not meet his standards (which are very high) and views them as lesser makuta.

Weapons: Crystal Longsword; Completely black, sharp enough to cut through protosteel with difficulty, has a shadow aura and can channel shadow powers easily:   Rhotuka Shield; Rhotuka cause black crystals to form around area of impact, very difficult to break and ends are sharp, but area of impact is small when rhotuka is uncharged, and not much bigger when it is charged.

Abilities: Standard makuta powers, cape can transform into wings.

 

The sword is crystal simply because it fits his personality better, and I think it's cooler.  There's no physical difference between it and protosteel.

Name: Zeta.

Species: Robot.

Powers: Can absorb nearly any elemental energy thrown at him and use it for his own purposes. This effectively makes him toa-proof. This system is designed to an almost paranoid extreme, to handle the sheer output of the toa Mangai and leave Zeta unharmed(Note this system cannot drain energy from the environment unless said environment is being manipulated with elemental energy. It's only useful against toa or other element users.) Being mechanical he has immense strength as well.

 

Weaponry: Aside from the elemental energy discharging systems, a sword with a whole lot of mini-buzzsaws built into the blade, allowing it to not only go throw protosteel like butter but make sparks which can be a problem for the main users of protosteel armor: Makuta, and the toa Mangai. He also has a rifle which fires spikes designed to penetrate armor and suck out whatever's inside, for example, gaseous essence.

Backstory: Crimax built this guy for the sole purpose of leading a force which will eliminate the toa Mangai, or any other element-using threat, should they become hostile to Crimax & Co.

Here are a few profiles for characters that I am going to start using again. They are all familar to some of you, but I wanted to post profiles for newer users.

 

Name: Makuta Charix

Species: Makuta

Powers: Makuta powers. Specializes in the usage of destructive or constructive energies. Possesses an overdeveloped ability to control shadow, which allows him to use shadows as extensions of his mind and physical body. 

Description: Previously a high ranking Makuta, Charix preferred taking on the form of either a tall, white armored being in the presence of allies or an equally tall, intangible shadow form around his enemies. 

 

Currently, due to a developing plot, and a previous occurence, Charix is mentally and physically spilt in half, one half being cowardly and corrupt whith the other part being honorable and courageous.

 

Dark Charix: The darker nature of Charix

Appearance: A mostly intangble being of shadow who is either incapable, or simply unwilling to take on a physically solid form for any length of time. 

 

Powers: All shadow related powers. Limited and weaker degrees of other Makuta powers.

 

White Charix: A younger, more innocent Charix, untinged by corruption.

Appearance: A tall and winged armoured warrior with a large shiny broadsword. 

 

Powers: Emphasis on lightning powers. Limited degrees of other Makuta powers.

Name: Thought Tweeter

 

Appearance: A small insect, similar to Protodites. Though a bit larger and with only one head.

 

Abilities and Traits: Thought Tweeters create what Equinox refers as “thought echoes.” These thought echoes are like broadcasters, stretching and sending out the thoughts of creatures and beings on a unique psionic frequency. Effectively, they create a mental recording of other peoples’ thoughts and then sends them to Wisnowlo. While their mind reading abilities are light enough that a majority of being don’t even notice it, the psionic signals they send out are somewhat detectable, as it makes an annoying buzzing sound to beings with telepathy. Thought Tweeters tend to travel in small swarms and are often preyed upon by many other Rahi. Like their Protodite cousins, Thought Tweeters can split in half to clone themselves.

 

 

Notes: Can't be used to read jammers and need to be at least thirty feet from the target. Equinox lost many of these creatures with the MU's destruction and won't be creating enough of the to spy on the entire planet as it is too large and risky. Currently, a majority of the ones left are spread around the mountain range where Equinox is building a base. Which is part of how he was able to detect Dralik's presense.

Name: Wisnowlo

 

Appearance: An owl Rahi about the size of Matoran. Primary color is purple, with various secondary colors depending on what area they live in.

 

Abilities and Traits: Wisnowlo have Psionic powers, though it is primarily used to receive messages from Thought Tweeters. These Rahi are intelligent enough to be able to sort out all the thoughts sent to them and create summarized reports. If these reports contain what they consider important information, they then telepathically send it to Equinox. They are also capable of sending images/whatever they see, making them useful flying spy drones. Though to Matoran and other beings, these owl often just stand in one place, not moving for long periods of time. Trying to capture or attack one is never a good idea, as they are often two steps ahead of their attacker. Also preys on small insect Rahi.

Name: Power Wasp

 

Appearance: White, silver, and yellow wasp-like insects. Size various a bit, but average around an inch in length.

Name: Devour Stone

 

Appearance: Various, but they start as small grey stones.

 

Abilities and Traits: Devour Stones were designed after Scadion and possess similar abilities. They can “eat” just about anything and extremely fast, adding all the absorbed matter to their own mass. They also can shapeshift and texture themselves to look like anything, but lack any voice to speak. However, unlike the intelligent madman Scadion, Devour Stones are mindless creatures. Meaning that they don’t use their powers very effectively. Whereas Scadion could practically be invincible by continuously absorbing matter, the stones don’t have the mental capacity to do this and will just charge blindly towards their next meal. While controllable and obedient, they are open to mind attacks and will ignore commands when extremely hungry.

Name: Vahtakran

 

Appearance: Strikingly similar to the Fire Drones (including its small stature), but a bit closer to Matoran.

 

Abilities and Traits: The Vahtakran have the powers of the Kakama, Pakari, Pehkui, and Mask of Growth. They are able to use three of the four powers at once, making them speedy workers that can reach, fit through, and carry many things. Though they can only use these powers for so long before requiring rest. They are considered only semi-sentient as they don’t have individual personalities, but they are easily as intelligent as Matoran. Vahtakran require little direction and can build or complete relatively complex things with little trouble. There are a couple of variant Vahtakran, such as ones with a single extra power like Rahi Control or Shattering. Elite Vahtakran are ones that are created with Protosteel, some with the regular set of powers and some with extra. The Vahtakran Hagah are a team of six unique, Protosteel version of the Vahtakran that are fully sentient and have their own personalities. All Vahtakran know and follow Equinox’s will.

 

Notes: They are Equinox's answer to both Rahkshi, Matoran, and Toa. Though for the most part, Vahtakran aren't fighters or vicious destroyers like Rahkshi or Toa and aren't really meant for combat. The Vahtakran Hagah are the exception to this however.
