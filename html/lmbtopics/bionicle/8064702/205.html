Species: Makuta

He believes that peace must be established at any price, and will ally with anyone he believes can help him achieve that goal, within reason of course. He carries great pride in the Makuta race but sees the Brotherhood as a force of evil that must be stopped. Embedded within his sub-conciousness is a fierce hatred of the Makuta called Lohraz, as he was partially created solely for the purpose of that being's destruction. He is efficient and logical, but lacks some self control, emphasis on 'some'.

 

Appearance: The original version of Kratark that was on the galleries, but with more silver.
