This is someone I thought up, so I'd like feedback. I will probably be using him in the RP very soon.

 

Name: The Scribe(Or TS for short.)

Side: The pursuit of knowlage

Species: One I made up.

Powers: Teleportation, flight, mental shielding, and perfect memory.

Weapons: None, persay, but he does have two tools. The first one is his Tablet, that can hold any amount of writing he chooses. It is very hard,making it impossible for anything but Plasma, intense Heat or Laser vision, or his Stylus, to write on. The second one is his Stylus, that projects a powerful energy beam from its tip that can, with adjustment, slice through very tough substances, and carve writing into nearly any known material. It has an extremely limited range, requiring nearly direct contact to work.

Personality: He tries to record everything and protect all writings, hence his name. He will get very angry if anyone attempts to attack writings in any way.
