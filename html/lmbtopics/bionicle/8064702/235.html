Weaponry: Two non-power-based blasters like the explosion-sphere-launchers used in the Core War are attached to his wrists.

Backstory:

As you know if you've read his backstory, Zul is a notorious prankster. When he ran into a Zyglak who had neglected to show up when the angrification happened, he was interested. When said Zyglak turned out to enjoy sneaking into Matoran villages, hiding behind Toa beds, yelling "BOO!" and running away real fast, Zul was downright giddy. After he and Derv witnessed the Zyglak literally get tossed out of a Nynrah Ghose village, though, Zul resolved to help. Some stolen supplies later, Zul promptly incorporated himself into several urban mythologies as something of a trickster god.

Personality: See above.

It just occured to me that, while having used him for months now, I have not actually made a profile for Deviox. Oops.

Name: Deviox

Species: Glatorian, but mechanically enhanced

Appearance: Underneath his ragged, black trench-coat, he wears highly-technological, red armor

Weapons: Twin blasters, which he can fire with flawless, pinpoint accuracy and several, small devices hidden in his armor. This includes EMPs, smoke bombs, grappling hooks, etc. His coat is also quite strong, and can deflect some attacks.

Abilities: Uncannily agile and accurate with his blasters, all of his strengths come from pure experience and skill rather than powers.

Backstory: As a veteran of the Core War, Deviox blamed the Great Beings for ruining the planet. Eventually, he partnered with Zurakia, an ex-Great Being who shared the same hatred of his past brothers. Together, they developed the Conquest, a plan to destroy the Great Beings who wasted their potential and rebuild their empire. With Zurakia's passing, Deviox decided to continue the Conquest himself, utilizing Zurakia's abandoned facility and whatever resources he can gather to make his dream a reality.
