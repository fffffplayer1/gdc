 Name: Amra

 

Gender: Male

 

Element: Stone

 

Colors: Mostly brown, but with grey limbs.

 

Kanohi: a brown, powerless Mask of Biomechanics

 

Weapons: A stone hammer and a disk launcher.

 

Powers and abilities: Amra has ring like objects permanently on all his fingers. These bumps/rings glow upon use and each contain an individual power of each Vahki staff(Command, Loyalty, Erasing, Suggestion, Presence, Confusion, and Convincing) and powers of the Kralhi(prison and energy draining sphere), Kranua(turning into living sand), and Kraahu(hands able to come off and fight in different directions. Also able to release a stun gas and give electric shocks to those who touch him). Though Amra can only use one power at a time without a significant loss of energy. Using two or more powers together will wear Amra out quickly. Amra can fire all Vahki staff powers like a lightning bolt or a sphere, but is mainly suppose to be channeled through his hands by physical contact. The only staff power Amra doesn't have to fire or punch someone with is the Staff of Convincing, which affects a small area. The Staff of Convincing is similar to a Mask of Charisma, but more powerful, being able to make any Matoran believe or do whatever the user wants by "convincing" them. Though this power was intended to only convince other Matoran that Amra is a normal Matoran and that he has always been with them, not some strange new comer. As seen, Amra is stronger than actual Po-Matoran, but not by much. He is also a very skill carver and has good aim.

When in doubt, read the instructions.
All I've done is RP and debate, but have I been a good light or just a broken mirror that failed to reflect the light into the darkness?...
