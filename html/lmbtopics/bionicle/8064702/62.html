I have decided to revise Tazzuk's profile so there is no confusion

Name: Tazzuk

Alias: King of Death

Species: Makuta

Weapons: Protosteel razor nails(able to shred through protosteel with ease), Flipable wrist chainsaws, Shoulder blades which have virtually no effect except for awesomeness but they can detatch and be used as explosives. Back elemental absorber/deflecter(it can take elemental power and redirect it, or they could be stored for later use, but they are not limitless), Double sided chainsaw staff(which folds up into a little cordak blaster ammo), and a shadow blade(which looks like a black version of Rotor's rotor but only one of them) and razor sharp bat wings.

Abilities: Enhanced strength, slightly enhanced speed(but not much), regular durability, all the makuta powers, enhanced intelligence.

Mask: Shadax Nuva, allows user to manipulate pain to extremes.

Personality: Manipulative, a excellent liar. He is actually extremely loyal to those who are more powerful than himself or equal in strength(Karunax being an example), however he is also power hungry. He will do almost anything to achive his goals. He can be humiliated 300000000 years ago, yet paitently bidding his time. He is incredibly patient and calm, however he always snaps when someone mentions Inharax. He is known to claim that fear is his ally, because he fears nothing.....except the wrath of the core and Denthraxx, and Queen Chrysalis(but it is mutual), but luckily they are not in this universe.

Aligence: Karunax, but most of the time himself. If Karunax were to disappear his only master would be himself.

Trivia: He thought he could just ursurp Teridax and give back leadership to Miserix. The only reason he sided with Teridax was because he was the last 3 to join a side.

 

 

 

 

Ta-ta

Name: Elax

Species: Toa Nuva of Iron

Kanohi: The Kanohi Huud, Mask of Aim. Similar to the Calix and Sanok, it can passively enhance the user's aim greatly. For example, if they just fired at a tank, the mask would enhance their aim to hit a vital spot. Elax uses it for Spiderman-style cableswinging

Powers: Nuva-level Iron

Weapons: Besides being able to create new weapons easily, he wears two grappling hook launchers on his forearms. The hooks are made with Ko-Metru gravity disks, enhancing the aim even further and ensuring the hook is not dislodged.

Other: Can speak in languages of several Rahi, like Kaulus. He can speak most bird languages, Venom Flyer, Visorak, and is particularly fluent in Gukko.

Backstory: Elax was a normal Fe-Matoran on an isolated island. One day, he was flying a Toa Stone he had found to the Turaga of the island on Elax's Gukko, Lightning, when Lightning lost control and the two fell into a pool of Energized Protodermis. When they emerged, Elax was a Toa Nuva and Lightning was quite a bit bigger, with several new traits.

 

Name: Lightning

Species: Transformed Gukko

Powers: Without Elax on his back, he can reach speeds of up to Mach Three. When doing this, he is surrounded by a force field that protects him from falling objects and enhances his maneuverablility.  With Elax on his back, he can reach a max of Mach 1.2 without Elax falling off. He is easily big enough to carry two Toa, and his wings crackle with energy at high speeds.

 

He can charge and launch bolts of energy from his beak tip
