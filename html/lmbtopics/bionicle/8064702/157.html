Name: Kraahkan.

Element: Shadow, Inika-shadow.

Species: One-of-a-kind. Formerly a member of Johmaks species.

Kanohi: None.

Weapons: None, though he can forge solid shadow weapons when necessary.

Power/Abilities:

  • Kualsi LVL teleport (though not instant for long distances), can also be used to completely "vanish" for half a second.
  • Eye-contact allows Kraahkan to disable targets connection from their mind to their body temporarily (like the protocairn, but for a shorter time) yet only possible if within 1.5 bio.
  • Telekinesis. Can be used to "hover" slightly above the ground.
  • Telepathy.
  • As a member of Johmaks species, Kraahkan still has the ability to shatter into a crystal cloud.
  • As an O.O.M.N. member, he is mentally shielded from minds he is not currently probing.

Weakness: He loses shadow and teleportation abilities when in open light... to prevent this, he wears a cloak.

Story: The Order experemented on him, using Shadow Inika disks (the disks of toa inika of shadow) along with various other disks including Matatu, Suletu, and Kualsi, to give him imence power.

Personality: Dark, Darker, Darkest. He respects the brotherhoods control and manipulation of shadow, but despises them not using it for good...

ElementForce One part 2

 

Name: Lightning

Weapons and Devices:

Lightning is extremely atheletic. Her boots convert into rollerblades to allow her to go faster, and her gloves can electrocute on contact. She carries dual Tesla pistols, which fire powerful blasts of lightning. She is immune to electricity thanks to a special channeling disk-based technology.

Bio: Lightning, despite being experienced, acts like a newbie often. She is more impulsive then Fire, if that's possible, but make sup for this with her incredible fighting skills.

 

Name: Sonics

Weapons and Devices:

Sonics has handheld devices that emit high-amplitude Sonics at varying frequencies. Her visor is custom-made to block out harmful sounds. Her armor has been silenced. She has a fragmenter gun that uses Sonic Shockwaves to break stuff.

Bio: Sonics is creepy by any standard,talking little and lurking about in the shadows, and then grinning madly as enemies writhe in pain from her sonics, before smashing them with her blaster. Most everyone is slightly afraid of her.

 

Name: Water

Weapons and Devices:

Water has a synthesizer on her back, like Fire's, but hers makes water. She has  asimilar system as Fire, but hers fire high-pressure water. They can also produce steam, mist, or fog for a quick escape. Her visor is waterproof and allows her to breathe water.

Bio: Water is surprisingly normal among the others. She is often in teh background, not very noticable, but always there to back one up or hold off enemeies with high-pressure water. 

 

Name: Ice

Weapons and Devices:

Retractable blasters on his forearms can cover his hands, enebling him to fire freeze-beams and ice projectiles, as well as create structures out of ice.

Bio: Ice speaks little. Like Water, he is often in the background, but unlike Water, it is intentional. He prefers to hang back, letting the others do the work and not wasting his energy. He is the team's strategist, finding weakpoints and using them to his advantage.

Name: Enbi

Codename: The Serpent

Species: A.I. Unit

Appearence: Enbi is tall with a slight muscular frame. Incredibly sharp blades project themselves out of the head. Vials filled with Energized Protodermis are equipped into the sash Enbi wears, along with other projectiles and such(usually Poison or Venom filled Vials) in case. Enbi also has two large swords.

Powers: As standard, Enbi has incredible healing abilities. Enbi can shapeshift on a whim, no extra energy is required(such as absorbtion) making Enbi very efficient. Enbi also the unique ability to 'eat' attacks, such as a fire blast, and redirect them, very similar to "Vanisher"'s power.

Abilities: Enbi is very strong, agile and fast. Although not very endurable, Enbi can heal. Enbi is notable for being able to 'leech' onto others, when in a very weak state.

Personality: Enbi is...very sickening. Enbi loves to torture others emotionally, and physically, as well as psycologically. Enbi is very emotionally distressed himself, desiring to find friendships with others, but instead acts hateful and violent. Enbi is easily the cruelist of the A.I. Units.

Backstory: Enbi has the second most complicated backstory. Enbi started off as a Vahki, Matoran and Rahkshi. As a Vahki, Enbi was a Kivahk, which had the powers of ALL Vahki, not just one, with the power of shapeshifting. Enbi was usually monitoring a special Matoran, named Kivon. When a Rahkshi attacked, the three fell into a vat of Energized Protodermis and merged together to form a special body, but no soul was in it. It was later shipped to an island, where an A.I. Unit went into it, forming the A.I. Unit known as Enbi. Tazzuk later convinced Enbi and his team(who Enbi bonded with) to join him.

We have been betrayed. Our trust, misguided. Our faith, put wrongly. Have they no shame? But we will live on, my brothers. We will strive forward, we will endure. We will ascend. Free of limitations, free of fear.

Codename: The Hoarder

Powers: As standard, Gurido has incredible healing abilities(although less powerful than the others). Gurido is notable for having the ability to turn his body into a near indestructable material(it shatters easily when either he is mentally assaulted, unable to concentrate or if you do a chain reaction involving lightning) Gurido also has an identic memory.

Abilities: Gurido is very strong and powerful, and can go through burts of very quick speed and agility.

Personality: Gurido is very greedy and manipulative. He desires everything in the world, hoarding them, true to his code name. He also is very notable for giving long lectures

Backstory: Gurido is nothing special. He's an A.I. which, alongside his team, was bought by Tazzuk.

 

Name: Glyrin(Goo-Lie-RINH)

Codename: The Prince of Thieves

Appearence: Glyrin is generally considered smaller than a Toa, but much larger than a Turaga or Matoran. Glyrin has a spine of Fins which heavily resembles a Lerak spine, except it starts in silver and ends in a shiny purple with glitter in it.
