Name: Bakud

Title: The Defenestrator

Species: Matoran/Quantum Anomaly

Element: Gravity (inaccesable)

Powers: Stable Time Travel (limited), Destroying Time-Altering Objects, Inability to be killed or maimed (however, he can be injured or defeated), Limited Future sight.

Weapons: The BetaSword  is a sword that can, literally, slice through anything. From toilet paper, to plastic, to steel, to protosteel, to Karunax's armor, to IO, to the fourth wall, to reality, to hardlight, to the acrimony in someone's voice. However, there are two main hinderences to this weapon. Firstly, it totally exhausts the user (It can only work on Bakud due to it's nature, which is nested in obscure quantum physics), based off of what material is being sliced. It takes on effort to slice toilet paper, but you are going to be breaking a sweat after slicing up a bit of protosteel. It's second weakness is liquids and gasses. It can slice through both liquids and gasses, however, if they are fluid enough to reform after this slicing, the attack will mainly be ineffective. He can slice a Makuta, but he can't slice it's antidermis.

Backstory: Long story, to shorten it: He lived in a different universe, then he messed something up, and then was suddenly turned into a quantum anomaly. His life is basically a mobius strip, due to time travel, and the red star. 

Talents: Skilled in swordplay. If unarmed, he is able to fight savagley. Intellegence rivals the most brilliant Makuta scientist (now, keplers and BrickMe, start fighting over that and get this topic locked again! That was sarcasm. Don't really do it.) Insane. Also, he's Bakud. That too.

He has done so by creating a mental program allowing him to maintain that stability unconsciously.
