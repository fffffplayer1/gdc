Name: Atem (Ah-Tem)

 

Title: The Wind Father, The Sky King

 

Appearance: Tall, Atem is broad-shouldered and tall. Lanky, his arms are also rather long. He has large armor plates on his wrists. He's standard in build, with two clawed feet. He also has chains wrapped around his torso

 

Element: Air and Lightning

 

Weapon: Atem has a pair of Chains attatched to his wrists, which can have either a blade, a cluster of thorns, a mace, a hammer, an axe, a hook or even a saw at the end. He also has seven more attatched to his back and wrapping around his torso.

 

Personality: Courageous and noble, Atem is loyal and dependable. He is just and fair, despising those who lie and deceive. Atem however also puts too much loyalty, willing to do whatever to save his closest allies. He's usually calm, and it is hard to break his cool.

 

Kanohi: Atem uses the Miru, Zaath and an Akaku

 

Abilities: Atem is swift and agile(not as much as Procella) but is very intelligent. Atem has great strength however, able to match Acharne when he does not use his Pakari.

 

Backstory: The first of the Elemental Lords, Atem is ancient. He does not speak fondly of his past, most of the time. What is known is that he wandered often, doing whatever he could to help. On his journeys, he met Procella, one like him. They stuck together, including Acharne(to Atem's displeasure), and later on Zemlya. Atem usually stayed away from the others as much as he could, but he eventually took Procella as his 'queen' and she became his voice to the others. Reclusive, Atem studied for more knowledge. Atem had been furious when Acharne and Zemlya banished Procella, and almost destroyed them both. When the coup d'etat began, Zemlya had poisoned Atem, then Acharne had sneaked up on him and attacked him from behind. Later on, as Acharne fled, Atem took back control, and tried to exert his will over Zemlya, who would not bow. Fearful, due to being upon her terrain, he fled to Ko-De-Ce-Kra Wahi, the only one where he knew Zemlya had no power.

We have been betrayed. Our trust, misguided. Our faith, put wrongly. Have they no shame? But we will live on, my brothers. We will strive forward, we will endure. We will ascend. Free of limitations, free of fear.

Name: Zemlya (Z-Emm-Lie-Ah)

 

Title: The Earth Mother, The Woman In the Cave

 

Appearance: Tall. Like, really, really tall. She's like 17 feet tall and has the muscle and strength to go with it. She's got heels and magma crags decorating it. Her face is also long and narrow, with spindly things on the side, she's also got long hair.

 

Element: Earth, Stone and Plantlife

 

Weapon: She uses either a mace or a wrist mounted blade/axe

 

Personality: Zemlya is...difficult. On one side she's sweet, patient and kind, if not a bit over-protective, the other side she's rude, violent and easily offended. Zemlya is fiercely protective of her favorites, it really just depends on if you earn her favor or not. If you have, she's going to be kind and courteous. She'll be patient and helpful. But if you disrespect her and earn her ire, she'll be a force of nature. She will be violent, cruel and sadistic. No matter if you have her favor or ire, she is calm, she won't be rash when angered. She'll be calm about it, unlike her 'king' Acharne.

 

Abilities: Somewhat fast, Zemlya uses her long legs to stride across distances, making her seem like she's fast. Zemlya is brutishly strong, having hundreds of Pakari disks melded inside her arms and body in general. Zemlya is also very intelligent and calculating, but this is clouded by her emotions.

 

Backstory: The fourth Elemental Lord to join, Zemlya was quickly favored by Acharne who 'groomed' her to be loyal to him. Zemlya was the one who proposed banishing Procella. She poisoned Atem. When Atem took back control, she stubbornly refused to bow to him and fled to Po-Onu-Ba-Ov Wahi.

 


The eldest is Atem, followed by Zemlya, Acharne and then Procella. However the order in which the joined is Atem(founder), Procella, Acharne and then Zemlya.
