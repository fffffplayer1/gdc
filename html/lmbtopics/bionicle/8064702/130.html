Name: Valix

Species: Toa

Mask: Corrupted Metalika 

Element: Fire

Weapons/tools: Zamor Launchers

Appearance: Valix looks like a Toa Inika but with black and red armor.

Backstory: Valix was once part of a team of 8 Toa (All the main elements plus Lightning and Iron). Their "Destiny" was to travel to a distant an uncharted island in the southernmost reaches of the Matoran Universe to save a village of Ta, Bo, Fe, and Ce-Matoran from an exiled Makuta. Their ship was attacked by a large squid-like Rahi which are the Toa of Air moments before they fended it off. They sailed their badly damaged ship the rest of the way to the island where they shipwrecked half a mile offshore. They wandered the island for days and were mysteriously picked off one by one, causing them to doubt their "destiny". After Volix, The Toa of Water, Toa of Stone, and Toa of Iron were left they decided to head back to shore as they had decided they had either arrived too late or their destiny was misinterpreted. When they got to shore, the Toa of Water had revealed she had been working for the exiled Makita here this whole time before washing away the Toa of Stone with a massive wave, disappearing after him. The exiled Makuta then appeared and forced the Toa to make a mask that gave the wearer metalworking skills. He corrupted the mask and forced it on Valix, ordering him to make a robot army so he could force his way back into the Brotherhood. The Toa of Iron flawed this mask after the Makuta had put it on, making the Makuta loose control of Valix. The now insane and mutated Water Toa pulled the Makuta under, never to be seen again. Valix then became half corrupted and destroyed the Iron Toa by targeting the weak points of his armor, but not before he forced the Iron Toa to summon a large amount of Metal to the surface. Valix then proceeded to make the robot army and an airship so he could stop Mata Nui from awakening.

(How many of you will believe me if I said this is my last Toa character?)

 

Name: Lupra

 

Species: Toa

 

Mask: Mask of Healing

 

Element: Earth

 

Weapons/Tools/Defenses/Abilities: Lupra, having once been a Toa Hagah, bears a few pieces of Protosteel armor – notably his thigh pieces, chestpiece, and pauldrons. He also wields a Seismic Halberd (essentially a cross between an axe if a spear if you don't know) and a Rhotuka Shield of paralysis which flies completely silently.

 

Appearance: Lupra bears the metallic Protosteel armor characteristic of a Toa Hagah, though most of his armor consists of standard black and grey Toa armor. When it comes to Toa of Earth, Lupra is rather slender for his element, though this has not decreased his immense strength.

 

 

Backstory: Lupra was one of the few first Toa of Earth in existence, wandering without a purpose until he was selected to be the leader of one of the Toa Hagah teams. He was then commisioned to act as the leader of the group guarding (Karunax, Inharax, Charix, or anyone really with a Makuta that'd be willing to have them as Lupra's primary target). After many years of serving the Brotherhood of Makuta, he soon discovered their true colors and led his team to storm the fortress.

 

However, in stealing the Mask of Light, Lupra found that they had been led into a trap and thus was the only one who was able to escape after making a valiant stand against the forces of the Brotherhood. Assuming his team was smashed, he solemnly returned to Metru Nui and gave the mask to the Turaga there for them to figure out what to do with it. He then began to wander aimlessly, guilt-ridden and lost. In his wanderings, however, he allowed a burning hatred to build up in his heartlight, eventually causing him to go onto a rampaging hunt to destroy any Makuta or Rahkshi he came across.
