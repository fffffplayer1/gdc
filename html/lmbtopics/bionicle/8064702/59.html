Name: To'kustar

Plural: To'kustara

Powers and abilities: To'kustars have thick armor resistant to most attacks, whether they be physical or energy, as well as their size giving them extraordinary strength, even in proportion to their size. Their strength allows them to defeat a normal-sized army and lift objects and creatures around their own size with little effort, and even throw them into space. To'kustars can blast strong cosmic rays by positioning their arms in an L shape. These rays are powerful enough to disintegrate a small fleet of spaceships and could destroy a planet in one shot.

To'kustars can create and control cosmic storms, which can also be used for inhabitation and transportation. And can surrvive in space. However creating a cosmic storm drains them heavily.

Weakness: If a To'kustar's head fin is damaged, it causes paralyzing pain throughout their body. And if damaged enough, can even cause destruction.

Despite their immense durability, To'kustars are still vulnerable to foes with sufficient strength.

Personality: They are peaceful, and nonviolent. They would rather talk problems out than fight. Even if attacked or insulted, they would rather talk it out. Which makes many think of them as weak.

Appearence: Same as Way big from Omniverse.

Habitat: Comic storma. A stormy area, it is shrouded in fog, and it is roughly the size of ten Metru nui's along with a part of the northern continent. It is covered in cosmic storms.

Predator: A virus.

Prey: Meat and fruit along with veggies. But they refuse to eat living meat.

Size: Titanically collossal!

 

 

Note: While they may SEEM OP. They are not, as they are very peaceful, not to mention there weakness.
