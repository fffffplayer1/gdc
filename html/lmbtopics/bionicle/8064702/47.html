Overall Appearance:

They vary between the height of a toa to slightly taller than such.

They all have rahkshi like heads and hordika torso armor.

They all from the torso up have a very similar design.

 

Overall Abilities/Traits:

Can fire their elements and powers via eyebeams.

They have all phantom light powers.

All have some unknown energy that solidifies into matter they can use this to create and repair their weapons.

 

All have adaptive abilities, meaning that they can change their appearance (beefing-up, beefing-down, that kind of stuff) they can also adapt to the point of having other technology (e.g. they could adapt their hand into a cordak, but they wouldn't have any ammo unless they made it out of their powers).

 

Their minds and other important parts are protected by semi-antigation technology, which prevents wielders of elements such as magnetism and iron from crushing them from the inside.

 

Can access and create portals to the matrix

 

Overall Equipment:

All wield dual bohrok shield/claws that are used for close combat, these channel and slightly amplify the users powers

 

Modified... for now.

Name: Hadixan

Title: Supreme Commander of New Xia

Gender: Male

Affiliation: Vortixx HQ and New Xia

Race: Vortixx

Appearance: An average height Vortixx, clad in shiny black armor and wearing a helmet with a pair of "eyes" on the back of it (these "eyes" are artificial eyes that allow him to see what's at his back, but he can't see his front while using them). He has a pair of sharpened claws, which have an extremely strong grip. His right arm is completely mechanical, and various retractible weapons are installed into it.

Personality: Hadixan is somewhat of a control freak, and can easily manipulate people into doing his work. He is to never be trusted, for he is a dirty backstabber, and will betray anyone for his own profit. Thought he has enough combat skills to fend off enemies himself, more than one enemy can overwhelm him, so his combat style is mentally attacking and weakening his enemies before finishing them off or using stealth to attack them while they're distracted, and commanding his armies to do the dirty work for him.

 Weapons: Hadixan has a whole arsenal of Vortixx weaponry at his disposal, but his favorite weapon is the Judgement Cannon, which destroys his enemies' will to fight. Aside from that, Hadixan's mechanical right arm is equipped with a retractible Confusion Rifle, and a Rhotuka Launcher, which makes his enemies temporarily declare allegiance to Hadixan and attack its allies (similar to Sidorak's Rhotuka).

Background: Hadixan used to be a smuggler before getting his current position in Xia. He was eventually required to climb The Mountain with a partner, but thanks to his manipulative personality, he managed to get his partner to do the dirty work for him unti they got to the top. He then disposed of his partner, which got him a higher position in Xia upon returning, but lost his arm in the process, which was replaced with a mechanical arm. Using his manipulation and control skills, he eventually got to being Supreme Commander of Xia.

Name: "Dusk"

Species: Mutated Antidermis Composite

Kanohi: Mutated Felnas

Powers: "Dusk" is not only able to levitate and pass through walls, but is extremley bulky, tanky, hard to take down, pick your adjective. Can also hit hard enough to deal serious damage, but not an extreme amount. "Dusk" can also absorb many direct elemental attacks (such as "I crush you with gravity" but not including "I use gravity to send a burning building on top of you") and use them to heal itself. Despite being composed of Antidermis, "Dusk" does not have Makuta powers due to it's inability to focus, but still has the ability to use telepathy to overwhelm some beings.

Backstory: "Dusk" was formed from a Makuta that had absorbed eighty-four other beings into itself. Bloated from their abilities, this Makuta was destroyed by another Makuta experimenting with energized protodermis. As it's antidermis floated away, the Makuta bottled it up and added energized protodermis, and several other Makuta viruses. the result was a monstrosity, capable of extreme destruction, which destroyed the laboratory. 

Weaknesses: "Dusk" is the kind of character that you need to list the weaknesses of, or else the character would be OP. "Dusk" is weak to chemical attacks, water, and fire, though granted he can still survive these kinds of attacks for a good amount of time. It is damaged by Psionic attacks, but is not neccisarily weak to them. Everything else does, in a nutshell, nothing. To my knowledge, there is literally no character incapable of creating fire, water, chemical, or mental attacks, directly or indirectly, so this shouldn't be a problem.

Affiliation: "Dusk" is more of a Rahi than a sentient being, just like some Rahi are more sentient than beast. It can be succeptable to whatever is suggested of it, within reason, and does not stray from this command until it is fulfilled. Because of this, "Dusk" easily follows what is commanded of it, such as bounties, mercenary deals, etc. with blind devotion.
