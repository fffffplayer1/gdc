"Experiment #568: The General."

 

"Recently, I've been thinking about the Brotherhood forces. Visorak, Bohrok, Exo-Toa, Rahkshi...they all have generals. But they're usually of the same species as the race. Wouldn't it be better if there was just ONE general that inhibited traits from all the main forces we have? I mean, sure, we could have a Makuta do it, but all Makuta are prone to corruption; turning away from the Three Virtues, including myself. I then decided to make my own hypothetical general come to life. It was a complete success.

 

"At first glance, The General may appear to be made of protosteel. HOWEVER, I did not make it out of protosteel, due to the glaring weakness of magnetism, iron, air, and water Toa. Instead, using my own knowledge of chemistry, I made an entirely different compound that I used to build The General, a plastic that has the same durability as Protosteel, but without the weaknesses. And, as an added bonus, it has the same melting point AND is a poor conductor of electricity. This eliminates any weakness to plasma Toa, fire Toa, and lightning Toa. I am a genius!

 

"In terms of combat skills, I'd say The General is a very powerful opponent, he could even take down some of the weaker Makuta in the Brotherhood! From the Rahkshi, I gave The General equal amounts of agility and speed. He can use any Rahkshi staff proffesionally and without a problem. From the Visorak, he has great jumping ability, blades within the forearms that can inject Visorak Venom, and on his main weapon there is a Rhoutaka launcher that can launch any of the 6 main Visorak rhoutaka. Also, intrestingly enough, The General can use the Visorak Venom as a way to mind control the victim, similar to the Bohrok's method of flinging a Krana at their opponent. However, most of the time he only uses it as a paralyzing agent." 

 

To be continued...
