Yeah...does anyone remember that profile that I posted a while ago? The one on page 2 called, "Be Prepared..."? Well, in case you don't know, the last post I made with him had Mis-file leading him into an ally in New Xia, and I have never mentioned him since. However, he still lives on...sort of. You'll see what I mean.

 

Name: Redemption. 

Soon-to-be-Affliction: OoMN.

Species: None.

Weapons: None.

Personality: Redemption is merciful to most people, and believes that all should be safe from the thing known as betrayal. (Not the being.) However, Redemption cannot stand Mis-file.

Appearance: A large being, 1.3 times larger then a Krannu, painted white, and has a Vahki head. When possible, wears a cloak.

Powers: Has the strength of five normal vahki, and the speed of two. Can turn into liquid metal.

Backstory: In the ally where "The Creature" and Mis-file talked, The Creature was at first happy to see their leader. However, Mis-file cut him off, and revealed that, while he'd been away, he'd studied their programming and told them that the head unit, which they had lost, was the only thing keeping their strength and speed at it's normal level, and that it had been slowly draining away. The Creature tested this out, and sure enough, Mis-file was right. Mis-file then stated that he did not need then anymore for this reason, and promptly used his Genetic Overload attack on them, a giant energy blast that was the equilvelent of a Nova Blast. Being metal, The Creature melted under the intense heat, and Mis-file left. However, unknown to Mis-file, the energy was a bit too much, and there was enough left of it to act as an alternative source of power, allowing the Creature to stay online. However, he discovered that the energy blast had fried most of his circuitry, making it so that he could not use his Vahki Staffs. Angry, he vowed to save people from the same fate that he suffered, and shot a teleportation Kanoka at himself.
