Alright! Now, I'm finally going to reveal it...The Orgin of Insanity. However, I'm going to split his profile into 2, one for Insanity, and one for Mis-file, condsidering he has something to do with Insanity's backstory as well.

 

Name: Insanity.

Affliction: Vahki.

Powers: Increased speed and agility, possibly defying the laws of physics, Berserker Charge.

Appearance: Just your average Vorzakh.

Personality: Seemingly insane, although with a heart of gold. He often does random things just because he feels like it, and is unpredictable. 

Backstory: Unlike other Vahki, Insanity wasn't made by Trona. He was made by a matoran who was also inspired by Nuparu's work, and had decided to build his own Vahki. However, these ones were different. The Creator attempted to make it so that his Vahki were stronger, faster, and overall more powerful. He did this by reinstalling the program for Vahki Kaita into the Vahki's normal programming, and added a little something extra: The Berserker Charge. This program would use a Vahki's last hour and turn it into a nightmare for those who needed to be captured, by making the Vahki turn into an insane berserker that had even more speed and strength then before.

However, these Vahki quickly went rogue and turned on their master, causing him to permenately shut the Vahki down. He then got to work making normal Vahki, although a bit adjusted. However, one Vahki somehow got the previous programs in. Somehow, he got the program even though he was supposed to be normal. That Vahki, was Insanity. The Creator made his Vahki so that, instead of knowing everything, they didn't know all the techniques, so that way he could train them himself. However, Insanity was...Different from the others. While he did have a soilder's mind, he thought of crazy strategies that would never work, and when he was rejected, he rebelled, and went out on his own. And every single time they found him, he had defeated his opponents with his strategies.

During tests, Insanity's speed was unmatched, and he was able to predict where and what the traps were. There were rumors going around about Insanity seemingly defying the laws of physics. The Creator wasn't exactly sure if that last one actually mattered or not, but he assumed Insanity was actually sane. However, when asked how he did it, Insanity replied with this: "Because Gold wanted me to!" The Creator then quickly realized that Insanity was even more insane then he thought if he thought a piece of gold was sentient. When told gold wasn't sentient, Insanity just replied with: "Not that gold! The-Wait, I can't tell you, otherwise I would break the 4th wall!" The Creator, frustrated, asked Insanity how mentioning what he actually meant would break the 4th wall of the room, only got: "Er, how about I just go no-" It was that comment that got The Creator to finally give Insanity the boot. Insanity then found the Vahki HQ, and signed himself up as quickly as possible.

 

And now, Mis-file!

Name: Mis-file.

Affliction: Vahki.

Personality: Calm, sadistic, isn't hesitant to pull the trigger if he needs to.

In order to keep him truely locked up, The Creator put his AI into his computer, never to be seen again. However, Mis-file didn't intend to lose. He put himself and some of the original programming into a Vahki that was supposed to be normal in order to make that one rebel. However, that Vahki was Insanity. As Insanity progressed, Mis-file messed with his programming, making him less and less sane.

Name: Arxtann

Affiliation: Forces of Good

Race: Toa

Element: Fire

Mask: Kanohi Calix, the Mask of Fate

Quote: Yes, of course I'm back! You won't get rid of me that easily.

Tool(s): Plasmace; very sturdy and hard to destroy, conducts Arxtann's fire abilities and fires rotuhka spinners, it also possesses a rudimentary sentience and can respond to Arxtann's mental commands, returning to him after being thrown, etc. Arxtann also possesses a gadget he calls a Strange Device. He picked it up after a journey into an alternate universe and has found many uses for it. It pretty much is the Swiss Army Knife of Bionicle.

Personality: The years have tempered his initial hot-headedness (is that a word?) and he has become a patient, tactical, and calculating individual. Yet he still retains the same dedication he had when he first became a Toa, and is always eager to help those in need and battle evil with the same zeal he had. Arxtann's intelligence has increased, becoming a master strategist, laying clever traps for his opponenents by studying their strength and weakness and using it against them.

Power(s): Arxtann possesses great strength, agility, and durability (all augmented by his plasmace). His armor is thick, yet allows freedom of movement. It has survived much, including a solid blow from Karunax and then several head-slams from the juggernaut. He wields the standard fire powers given to a Toa of Fire, but he has a special ability due to his transformation. When in great need, Arxtann can call upon a form of pure fire energy that enables flight, increased strength, and, well, he turns into fire. This form is not susceptible to physical injury.

Weakness: He is susceptible to cold temperatures and the fact that most of his opponents are much more powerful than he is detriments his odds of survival.

Backstory: Read Chronicles of the End Time: Seeds of Doom and find out!

Power(s): Gurthang is incredibly strong, agile, swift on foot and in flight, and his armor is able to survive an impact with a planet. His body is built for space travel and as such he can thrive in a vacuum and enter into a planet's atmosphere without bodily harm. Gurthang also has complete control over his molecular stability, but cannot effect that of others.

Personality: Gurthang is a very wise, intelligent, and courageous warrior, dedicated to the cause of Mata Nui. He speaks in a much older dialect of Matoran (which I represent as olde English) which can get confusing. His is the direct approach when it comes to battle, prefering to get in close and rip his enemies apart with his claws and talons. He has a strong sense of honor and of what is right and wrong.

Weakness: Gurthang's stamina does not last forever and can be worn down if his opponent is smart enough to stay out of reach.
