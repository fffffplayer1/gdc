Name: Surosu

Codename: The Slacker

Species: A.I. Unit

Appearence: Surosu is extremely muscular. Surosu has claw like blades on his feet. Surosu also has chains on his wrists, which he can use to hurt enemies. The chains are coated in a strong thermokinetic energy chemical, which allows them to become extremely hot, or extremely cold, on choice.

Powers: As standard, Surosu has incredible healing abilities(although slower than others). Surosu is notable for being extremely fast when he wants to, and extremely strong. His endurance also is very powerful, but Surosu isn't very intelligent. Surosu also has the powers of a Hau built into him, as well as a Pakari, Kakama and Akaku. But the Pakari and Kakama increase his strength and Speed respectively, they aren't the cause of his already enhightened abilities.

Abilities: Surosu is extremely fast and strong as well as endurable. But Surosu isn't very smart.

Personality: Surosu is.....blegh. Surosu is extremely lazy, true to his codename. He also is trying to get out of his job constantly. Surosu also has a different side. When push comes to shove, Surosu is a force not to be messed with. He will, if he loses too much, will go on a rampage, and will use his powers to his full extent. Surosu also has a hobby, which is his favored thing to do: collect things. Surosu often tries to sleep, as he finds sleep is the easiest way to escape labor. As such, he will even ask enemies if he can stop fighting and sleep. Thus, he is not very popular on the team.

Backstory: Surosu is literally just an A.I. who got lucky and was hired by Tazzuk to join a team of other A.I. Units.

We have been betrayed. Our trust, misguided. Our faith, put wrongly. Have they no shame? But we will live on, my brothers. We will strive forward, we will endure. We will ascend. Free of limitations, free of fear.

Keps, tell me if these two are alright so I can or can't send my Skakdi to energized protodermis.

Name: Jehko.

Species: Energized Protodermis-ized Skakdi.

Powers/Abilities: Elasticity, is strong, x-ray vision, and a quite unique state; Jehko's torso behaves weirdy. Should anyone, including himself, reach into it, their hand would go through and find a surprisingly spacious storage space. With a mental toggle, this will act like a portal to a location of Jehko's choice instead, presuming Jehko actually has seen that location. Note that, oddly enough, an attack will not trigger this portal-like effect, meaning he is not invincible. His rhotuka power will freeze the target's sensory input for a few seconds depending on the charge.

 

Equipment: A rhotuka launcher on his back and whatever he steals.

Backstory: Akarus fell in energized protodermis. Yay.

Behaviour: Jehko is insanely greedy and will steal anything of value he sees.

Appearance: Brown armor, white eyes and clawed hands and feet.

 

Name: Elkronus.

Species: Same as above.

Powers/abilities: Shapeshifting and power copying in a fashion similar to a mask of emulation + Matau's mask, and hunger vision; He can shoot eyebeams that drain energy from the target. His rhotuka will animate the target.

Equipment: Elkronus shapeshifts himself equipment as he needs it.

Backstory: Karau fell in too. Sploosh.

Behaviour: Elkronus is currently convinced that he is perfect. So yeah.

Appearance: Although Elkronus is a shapeshifter and can look however he wants, he is oddly fond of a form with pale blue armor, electric blue eyes, and a face reminescent of an Elda.

And he went about as a mercenary, Smashing for money.

Name: Surosuna

Codename: The Apathetic

Species: Energized Protodermis-ized A.I. Unit that was mutated

Appearence: Surosuna is fairly simple. She looks like Nirekx, except with a Dark Blue and Shiny Black, with Silver highlights.

Powers: As standard, Surosuna has incredible healing abilities, except hers is a strange one. Her healing manifests as Water striking her, healing her, through various ways of her choice, the default is of a Cage trapping her, and then closing in on her. Surosuna is notable for being easily one of the most intelligent, if not the most intelligent. Surosuna also can turn her self into Water(which cannot evaporate or freeze). Which makes her the most resilient. Surosuna also has water powers on Toa level, but she is extremely inexperienced, and is thus, learning. But Surosuna does not know how to absorb water, or create it, only manipulate it.

Abilities: Surosuna is very intelligent. Surosuna is also very knowledgable about many subjects, in fact, one of her main goals is her thirst for knowledge, and is prone to spouting information randomly, or ask for information randomly. Surosuna is also extremely strong, and with slight increase of speed, her endurability is horrid, but she can quickly fix it.

Personality: Surosuna is cold, and true to her codename, is apathetic. Although capable of feeling emotions, Surosuna tries as hard as possible to contain them, and when she can't, she usually will quickly get it out. Though she does appear to show entertainment in Matoran, once saying "It's rather, amusing, how they think they are so important. I can't wait to see the despair when I tell them otherwise." By that quote, it indicates she enjoys other's suffering, and she doesn't try to contain that. She does, care for her teammates, however.

Backstory: Surosuna was once an A.I. Unit. She got mutated, and then fell into Energized Protodermis. She then was reccomended by Toxica, to join the A.I. Unit team. And she was accepted by Tazzuk, thanks to Toxica.
