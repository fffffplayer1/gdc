The Dark Toa Team

Backstory: A toa team assigned to guard Koval in the MU, turned to the Dark Hunters about 10K years before the battle of Metu Nui. Have been in Xia since the evacuation of the MU.

 

Krahral

Species: Toa

Element: Shadow

Description: The leader of his team, he has high tolerance for mistakes. He carries a Cordak blaster and twin "Shadow swords." Is the tallest of the team and has a deep commanding voice, he is also very chatty.

 

Ferag

Species: Toa

Element: Iron

Description: Krahals lieutenant, and unlike his superior officer, he has a short fuse. Is also armed with a Codak blaster and twin Iron Claws. Talks even more than Krahal.

 

Cepal

Species: Toa

Element: Pisionics

Description: One of the smartest members of the team and only speaks up when she has something important to say. Almost never loses her temper. Is armed with a Codak blaster and an axe.

 

Superst

Species: Toa

Element: Plasma

Description: Is a loner and the rest of the team (Except Taracks) avoids him because of the extreme heat that surrounds from him. Is very logical and almost never laughs. Equipped with a Cordak blaster and a plasma shield.

 

Vorest

Species: Toa

Element: Lighting

Description: The darkest of all of the team, she always seems kinda insane. Has very random mood swings. Won't seek out other toa, but does not mind socializing with the other members, if they don't mind themselves. Has a Cordak blaster and two long blades. Is a chemist also and makes many new tech, like new Kanoka disks.

 

Taracks

Species: Toa

Element: Fire

Description: Is best friends with Superst. Does not talk much, but has a temper as hot as his fire. Is prone to going off from team meetings in a huff. Armed with a Codack blaster and a lava sword.

Powers: Every standard Makuta ability.

Weapons: Homemade bombs he created which are based around the more corruptive Rahkshi powers. He can also change the way the bombs discharge their contents, should it be a large explosion or a cloud of smoke.

Personality: Lomois is a mad-scientist in every sense of the word. He thinks only of his work and how it can be used in the most destructive way. Traits such as kindness or humility you won't find in him. He is a wretched Makuta whose only aim is to be a plague unto his enemies.

Name: Zharka

Species: Augmented Vortixx

Abilities: Really good mechanical intuition developed over thousands of years.

  • Her arm has been replaced by an Earth Claw-like appendage. It is endlessly reconfigurable, allowing Zharka to use it to mimic nearly any technology she's seen and understood.
