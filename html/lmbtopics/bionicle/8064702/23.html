Well, as no one seems to mind having multiple characters as long as they aren't all used at once, I suppose I'll post the profiles for two RPC's I'd like to use.

 

Name: Lukanov.

Species: Hokanuka. They are a Makuta hunting race who are born in a small, rahi-like form, but can go through a metamorphasis once in their lifetime to unlock their real potential.

Allegiance: Order of Mata Nui.

Kanohi: None, cannot wear Kanohi.

Weapons: Protosteel greatsword. Think buster sword. Natural claws, capable of damaging protosteel, but not as well as a protosteel weapon. A number of throwing knives and smoke 'grenades.'

Powers: Like all Hokanuka, he has the power to absorb incoming powers/energies, automatically reverse what they are, and shoot them back out when he wants to. The energy absorbed is stored, and whatever harmful capabilities it has are disabled. Lukanov absorbs it through his weapons, claws, and hands. Absorbing must be done willfully, and is not automatic.

Like all Hokanuka, Lukanov's mind cannot be read, detected, attacked, or afflicted by any power-based influence.

Abilities: Heightened reflexes, great stealth, excellent swordsmanship. Proficient in a less well-known form of Runask martial arts.

Lukanov is often the "cleanup crew" for a botched Hokanuka operation. He sneaks in, makes a mess where it needs to be made, and leaves.

Fortunately, Jor does not have the powerful mimic ability our blabber-mouth DH has and so will always loses in a fight with him. The longer a fight lasts, the stronger Jor gets.
Backstory: Jor was once a Steltian merchant who was down on his luck until he agreed to volunteer for a genetic engineering experiment that the Vortixx were conducting to see if they could weaponize the natural healing and adaptive factor found in most of the races from the Southern Islands. The experiment wound up failing horribly and left Jor in dependency of his Exo-suit for survival. He's now a nasty tempered merc out for his own welfare.

Jor will appear in War for the Southern Islands.
