Name: Visher

Species: Modelocked Krahka. He's stuck as a Kanohi Dragon.

Powers: All the regular powers of a big Kanohi Dragon. His powers are unusually strong, though, due to him managing to bypass the mode lock somewhat. His fire-breath can go hot enough to soften Makuta armor, and he can also light himself on fire. His tail blades go hot and sharp enough to chop through Makuta armor.

Backstory: Visher was an ordinary Krahka, hiding out in a Zakaz cave. One day, he ran into an exceptionally problematic Makuta named Umbrax. Umbrax was in a less moral stage at the time, and modelocked him as a Kanohi Dragon when he fell asleep.  Visher was, at first, disheartened but began to get used to it. One day, he ran into a Skakdi who had gotten thrown out for his shapeshifting abilities, named Cerls. He trained Cerls, helped him past his Skakdish anger, and the two decided to dump this stupid rock. They did so.

 

Name: Cerls

Species: Skakdi, but varies.

Powers:

Elemental Iron(Skakdi-level.)

LAZAH VISION!!!

Shapeshifting: When he touches a being, he can copy them, including any powers(Losing his others in the process.) He also gets bits and pieces of their personality, making him (Often embarrassingly) copy their mannerisms and ways of thinking. He can consciously suppress this ability, but it requires effort.

Weapons: 

Iron Gun: Can form and fire metal projectiles, with shapes limited only by Cerls's imagination. Normally, it draws on Cerls's elemental Iron powers to form projectiles, but it also has six shots if that is not available.

Name: Akurionite

Species: Rahi

Appearance: Akurionite is a scorpion-like Rahi. The Akurionites have blue-purple crystals growing on their claws, back, and tail tip. They have a huge geode mineral on their tail tip. The Akurionite rests and hides underground, protruding their large mineral tail tip on the surface. If they is aware of nearby enemies, the tail breaks out of the sand and swings around violently, hurting anything nearby. It is possible to temporarily break the tail crystal, but the geode will quickly grow back. Male Akurionites tend to be larger and have bigger crystals and more aggressive, while the females have lime-green crystals which are smaller.

Powers: The huge geode mineral on the tail tip can shoots out a high-pressure liquid which can slow a foe down by covering them in crystals, like the ones on their body. It is possible to temporarily break the tail crystal, but the geode will quickly grow back. They have protosteel teeth and acidic saliva, much like Guratoni, allowing them to chew crystals. They can also create Shadow Balls, or Light Balls depending on gender(female is light, male is dark), and rapid-fire them. They can also secrete the liquid to grow the crystals which they often eat. They can also speak normally, and telepathically(not just others with the same base genetic code). If all else fails, Akurionites can teleport up to fifty feet away.

Behavior Patterns: Akurionite's are very social, unlike others they travel in swarms and are rather intelligent. Akurionite's also care for one another and are very resourceful. They tend to be prey for creatures like Arachne-Scylla due to this.

Name: Noface, "Face-Lack" in treespeak

Species: Rahi

Appearance: Noface are Rahi with unusual appearance. They are tall creatures with long streams of appendages covering their body and elongated legs on some sort of stalks. They have long mandibles that are their only way of seeing environment by touching it. Color varies from one to another.

Traits: They are sensitive to Sonics and Seismic activities. Commonly live in swamps, docile and Territorial creatures. They wont attack unless they have to or under possession. Their legs are considered to be delicate by some other Rahi, however they are toxic. Noface got their names from the fact their face is just large, blank, black and rounded flat with two, large yet blind eyes of purple color. They seem to have no gender separations. The Pit Mutagen and other mutagens for that matter have little to no effect on them. According to some Matoran biologists they sleep standing, however due to their rarity this claim can be considered a rumor. They are both nocturnal and diurnal creatures, depending on the environment they live in.

Name: Moiraia Atropos

Species: Rahi

Appearance: Moiraia Atropos is a massive black dragon, with a long, serpentine neck and tail, along with a pair of large wings. Her body is covered in rigid black scales and lined with white spines. Her head features four backwards-facing horns and a row of enlarged bottom teeth. Moiraia Atropos has four scarlet eyes. Did we mention that Moiraia Atropos has four smaller, yet still rather large, wings?

Powers: Moiraia Atropos has elemental Air capabilities, along with elemental fire. Moiraia Atropos can also spread an extremely flammable dust which when lit will both burn and explode. Did we mention Moiraia Atropos is smarter than even some Matoran?

Behavior Patterns: Unpredictable. Moiraia Atropos is largely apathetic to the world around it, preferring to snack on whatever she can get, and protect her less intelligent siblings.

Size: Around two Kanohi Dragon's size.


Name: Miralia

Species: Rahi

Appearance: Miralia is a very large, semi-aquatic reptile. Its body structure is reminiscent of Moiraia Atropos, with relatively short limbs and a long tail and neck. Its body is filled with a magma-like substance which pools at the base of the tail, the chest, and at each shoulder. Each of these pools directs magma flow to certain parts of the body. On its back are a pair of wing-like appendages. Seemingly useless in terms of flight, these "wings" feature cannon-like shoots which can extrude globules of magma, both as a means of defense and as a way to jettison excess magma. Miralia's head features a frill-like structure and an upturned snout. Miralia also has a massive mangled horn jutting out of it's forehead

Powers: Miralia is capable of firing globs of Lava-Magma. Other than that, Miralia has extraordinary strength and endurance. It is also immune to heat, preferring to sleep in lava.

Size: Around the size of Moiraria Atropos but with a much longer tail. And stronger tail. With spiky scales.

It's come to my attention that all the Rahi I was using in the siege on the OoMN were land based. Which is not good so here, I provide some useful beasts to have:

 

Species: Lagiacross

Description: Based on the "Lagiacrus" (or was that with a z?) from the Monster Hunter series of games, the Lagiacross is a cross between a sea serpent, an anaconda, and a crocodile. It has a deadly lightning breath which it can use to surge the surrounding water with lightning, shocking most anything nearby. It can also hurl bolts of raw electrical energy from its mouth, though this is more effective when it is on land. Oh yeah, it's slightly semi-aquatic. The lagiacross does breathe air, but they almost always prefer aquatic prey. A variant that is black has gills and has even stronger electrical attacks.

 

Species: Plesionn

Description: Take your basic wyvern, and make it a fish that swims underwater. That's a plesionn. A green variant is tougher for some reason. It too can venture onto land, where it launch highly pressurized streams of concentrated water from its mouth, though this attack is hard to control. They mostly stick to water, where they're more at home.

Another sea dwelling Rahi:

 

Specios: Ceadios

Description: The ceadios is a gigantic, ultra long, bearded whale. Its top is defined by a tough, yet streamlined carapace. And its underside is very soft by comparison. The ceadios does breathe fresh air, but it only comes to the surface on rare occasion. This is because it is able to store large amounts of oxygen, as it has several lungs that are specially designed to interchangeably store, and release oxygen into its system. The glowing spots on its underbelly are an indicator as to where these are (it is bioluminescent). The ceadios also has two large ram-like horns on its head. When angered, it will ram its body into any and all its aggressors. Due to its sheer size, it can withstand a relatively large amount of punishment. The ceadios is an endangered species because of its very low birthrate. In spite of this, they have no natural predators, and even if they were to cross paths with any particularly deadly sea-dwelling rahi, It has the surprising ability to out-swim almost any pursuer. Its most potent defense is to suck in a large amount of water, and then blast it all back out, creating a large vortex in front of it. The water in this vortex it creates is extremely pressuized, and can rip enemies to shreds.

Name: Sir Reggie

Nicknames: The Regginator. Reggie, sir, Reggie

Species: Sirregios

Description: The Sirregios is a very dangerous rahi that was created to be an apex predator, as all the rahi that Kratark released into the ecosystem are quite deadly and will need to be culled.
