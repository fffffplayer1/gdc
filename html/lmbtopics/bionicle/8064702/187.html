"So...I've finally come to it. I suppose I should introduce myself, only, I have no name. See, this is my first experiment log - because my master is gone. It's been six years since my master has been..."put down"...and three years since I classed these five as experiments, and I believe it's time for me to actually record an experiment log - or rather, the start of a second generation of experiment logs. Not that I see myself recording any more after this, but still. I can't see into the future. Now, I suppose I should go back to where it started.

"I remember it as clear as day - only minutes after I had been given a physical form, I had dashed to my master's assistance, only to pass directly through him. I hadn't exactly gained control over my tangibility yet, and even now I occasionally kick myself for not gaining control fast enough. As my master slipped away, he managed to give me one of his creations, Experiment #6987; an incredibly mutated and hideous creature my master had created during one of his...outbursts(OOC: Referring back to 6682's profile). He told me to use it on 'her', and almost immediately I knew what he was talking about. 6987 was basically a Shadow Leech on Energized Protodermis; it turned the poor victim into a Makuta, and also gave them a nasty temper. Master had exterminated all but one, planning to save it for someone he deemed 'worthy'. And, of course, the Ga-Toa leader of the team was one of the most strict Toa in terms of following the Toa Code. So, I decided to throw it at her, using my newly gained powers. Seeing that I had my master's Accuracy, it was pretty much guaranteed to hit.

So, long story short, it didn't work as intended; the Ga-Toa was turned into a hybrid due to her team saving her, I convinced the Ga-Toa that she {smash}ed her whole team, and I ended up breaking the others minds. Now, I know that sounds rather cruel of me, but she's not what we're focusing on. We're focusing on her team, and what I did with them..."
