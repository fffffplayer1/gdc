Traits: They will not go out of there way to harm others, but they ARE aggressive, however they just like to play, they play aggressively(not on purpose) but if you attack them when they play, they will growl and attack.

It took a team of eleven veteran toa and three rookie toa to corner him, it took twelve more consisting of rookie and veteran to stop him, he was contained.
