I suppose I should post this before I do it...

Name: Xoriniak.[Zorr-in-yak]

Species: Fanon/fanon/toa fusion.

Powers: Element of earth, Znanak's powers(Knowledge and learning-related stuff), and manipulation of emotions.

Mask: Arthron.

Weapons: Transvector staff and transvector sword. The sword is a one-handed double-edged weapon made of a dark green-blue metal, and grants the wielder teleportation in a similar manner to that of a mask of quick travel. The wielder must be aware of the location they are teleporting to either by sight or some other method(E.G. Arthron.) The staff, on the other hand, is a12-foot long staff made of the same metal, and is divided by almost imperceptibly small notches about every 6 inches, into 24 segments. For reference, three inches is about the width of one's palm. Each segment can teleport independently, but when doing so remains invisibly tethered to the others, allowing for interesting strategies. Also, each segment must remain within a 2 foot radius of another when using this power. Lastly, the two's handles can be stuck together to form the transvector spear, which retains the sword's ability but swaps the staff's ability for the power to slash open portals to other places that the user knows exist. This drains their energy a good bit, meaning one can't use the portals excessively.

Appearance: Xoriniak's armor is the same dark blue-green color his weapons are, and he towers at 12 feet tall. He has a pair of black wings proportioned to the rest of his body. His eyes glow lime green.

Behavior: Xoriniak rarely speaks, though when he does it's usually either a compliment, order, or insult. He's aggressive in battle and will not stop until either he or his foe is unable to keep fighting.
