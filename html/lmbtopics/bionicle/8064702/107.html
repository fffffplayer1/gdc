Okay, so I had this idea of having a rag-tag team of The Shadow Being's Master's experiments that have now been assembled by the SB for (currently) one reason: to snap the Hauden back to their senses and realize that Blarkrok is a moron. ...Well, not that last part, but STILL. So, I figured I should create their profiles first, as I may use these guys later on for other reasons.

 

"Experiment #97: Hive Soldier."

 

"Recently, I was looking for an experiment when I found a Krata on the floor. Just sitting there. A Bohrok must've seen an intruder and tried to catch it. But no matter.
