And here are the profiles for the weaponized Grazers! Enjoy!

 

Name: Grazer Drone Ta

Formed From: Red Grazers

Weapons: Two blasters. They can fire Disentigration, Molecular Disruption, and Fragmentation blasts.

Description: Grazer Drone Ta are the standard footsoldiers of the Grazer Drones. The most noticable adjustments are that their claws have been replaced with blasters, they have a blast mask that can be flipped down, they have several Toa Stones on their armor in order to prevent elemental manipulation, and that the leftover scrap from whatever they shred is teleported rather then shot out of an exhaust. The latter also applies to any Grazer Drone with the shredding function.

 

Name: Grazer Drone Le

Formed From: Green Grazers

Weapons: Same as Ta, along with some heavy duty cordak.

Description: Grazer Drone Le are now significantly larger then before, and their mouths have been replaced with cockpits that Grazer Drone Ta can enter by curling up into a ball and connecting with the interface. Its wings have been replaced with helicopter blades, and Toa Stones integrated into its armor as well. It also has larger models of Ta's blasters and some cordak. It also has a shredding function activated by opening a bottom compartment. To summarize, it's basicially a Grazer Drone Ta, except bigger, more heavily armed, can carry Grazer Drone Ta into the battlefield, and can fly. 

 

Name: Grazer Drone Ga

Formed From: Blue Grazers

Weapons: Giant drill claws.

Description: Originally, Blue Grazers only worked in the water and went offline anywhere else. Zakaku thought that was a moronic function, and proceeded to upgrade them. Now they can not only walk on land, they can also tunnel through anything as long as it isn't harder then Protosteel, and also have Toa Stones in their armor. However, their element is the water; not only are they much faster, but their shredding jaws combined with drill claws equal {smash} to any ship in the way.

Name: Grazer Drone Ko

Formed From: White Grazers

Weapons: Itself

Description: Grazer Drone Ko are small and fast kamikazes. Their only purpose is to run into a highly populated area, and then proceed to use up almost all of their energy to denonate a stasis field charge that traps anything within a three meter radius. The rest of the energy is then used to teleport itself to where the scrap metal from Ta, Le, and Ga go to. Zakaku didn't bother putting Toa Stones on this one; if elemental 

 

Name: Grazer Drone Onu

Formed From: Black Grazers

Weapons: N/A

Description: This is where all the scrap metal (and the occasional Grazer Drone Ko) are teleported to: directly into the hot furnace that's inside a Grazer Drone Onu mouth. Not only are they capable of melting metal (with a cooling system to make sure it itself doesn't melt, along with Toa Stones in their armor), they also have the molds nessecary to build (almost) any Grazer Drone. They also work incredibly fast and have a constant supply of vital components (such as a power source, weapons, and eyes) being mass produced and teleported to them by Zakaku.

 

Name: Grazer Drone Po

Formed From: Brown Grazers

Weapons: Giant shields attached to their arms

Description: The only purpose of the Grazer Drone Po is to defend the Grazer Drone Onu at all costs. After all, they're the ones making more Grazers, and therefore need to be protected. Their giant bulky armor equipped with Toa Stones helps with this duty, along with one particularly special ability: they have a large amount of spines on them, and the reason for this is that they can detach their limbs in order to curl up into a ball and roll around. Obviously, getting hit by a large, spiny ball the size of your torso can be very effective.

 

Name: Grazer Drone Blank

Formed From: Scrap Metal

Description: These things can be almost any model of Grazer Drone, and will act just like them...but these guys don't have Toa Stones in their armor. The ones that survive will get Toa Stones.
