As most of you who had been at Makuta HQ know, I made a new character called Kratark.

 

Name: Kratark

 

Element: Shadow, like, REALLY REALLY REALLY evil shadow.

 

Weapons: He can corrupt any weapon that isn't already morally aligned (for instance he couldn't corrupt anything that is touched by light, such as Blarkrok's double bladed sword) and curse it with evil enchantments, making it wieldable to none but the wickedest souls.

 

Personality: Cruel, brutal, efficient, intelligent, calculating, unusually sane

 

powers: Teleportation, poison, fear, anger, hunger, control over peculiar shadow rahi resembling hounds, the only makuta to be able to sometimes rarely create stage 8 kraata, and anything shadow related.

 

Bio:

 

 Some things are better off not being known about, Kratark was and is an evil makuta from the past, he fondly recalls duels with Karun before him turning, he was present for his transformation into Karunax. He had a bit of a StarScream complex, he would always be gunning for a higher position no matter if he deserved it or not. He was not driven by brotherhood teachings but sought his own agenda for world domination. As he delved deeper into his mastery of the dark arts he reached a pinnacle, he found his way into a depth not even onu-toa dare explore. Not even a makuta would explore. A dark, twisted lair, there he found vicious shadow rahi, and befriended the entire pack. They are phantasmal in nature, and Kratark only needs to give the order to have them overrun an opponent. They know all the tricks, and are even more effective than rahkshi. He might of made his way onto Karunax's throne if it weren't for one fateful order: "go destroy a village". Kratark figured he could assert his dominance after the village had been decimated, how wrong he was. Once he attacked the first matoran, the light touched him. It drove him mad, and he retreated to the woods. There his identity was removed and replaced with that of Blarkok's. For the time being that is... MUAHAHA!!

Apparently, even if I translate it, I can't use a foreign language in my signature.

Backstory: Xarthen was a member of a Fe-Matoran village. When the Makuta sent out forces to attack the village, he hid, and was not found by the squads of Visorahk and Rahkshi. He locked himself up in a dark bunker with a recharging station to maintain his minimal energy. This bunker included a peep-hole, which he looked out during the Makuta attack, and saw all his friends fall before his very eyes. After a few thousand years, his charger broke, and he was forced out of hiding. He was not found for a good while until he ran across a Makuta. Xarthen was spared, as this Makuta wanted something simple: Power. Karunax's armor was nigh-impenertrable, and this clearly stemmed from his powers as a Toa of Iron. He was given a Toa Stone and turned into a Toa, and afterwards was painfully experimented on. Xarthen has no real memory of what the experiments entailed, and the next thing he remembered was waking up in a field with protosteel weaponry. He believes that his Makuta captor decided to have mercy on him after recieving his power. However, there are no Makuta with Karunax-level armor other than Karunax himself to this day...

Abilities: Xarthen is an able fighter. His sense of justice is severe, and no Makuta could sway him. Interestingly enough, he is highly resilient to mental attacks, sleep in particular.

Personality: Xarthen is far from morally gray. He refuses to bring death to an enemy, no matter who that enemy is. He follows the Toa Code to the strictest degree. He wouldn't act surreptitiously for the life of himself. However, he has no remorse in inflicting damage or pain upon anything evil, even if it was a mind-controlled Kra-Matoran.
