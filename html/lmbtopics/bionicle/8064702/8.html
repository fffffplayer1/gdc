The Septet of Doom! #7: There's a reason why the GB's scattered this guy to the winds...

Options

  • Bookmark
  • Subscribe to RSS Feed
  • Highlight
  • Print

‎12-10-2013 04:21 PM

Name: Taradces.

Alias: Enity of Anger, or, as his team perfers to call him, Enity of the Acopylpse.

Affliction: Tool of Destruction...Again...

Appearance: Take every single Acopoylpse you can think of, and personify them all. Merge all those beings into one, then cover the new being with armor, give him an epic sword, and light him on fire. That's what this guy, in a nutshell, looks like.

Kanohi: Unlike the others, Taradces has a Kanohi. His Kanohi is the Mask of the Seven Sins, which gives the user the power of the Acopoylpse. Fortunately, it can only be worn by Taradces, even if someone else shapeshifts their face to match Taradces' face perfectly, it won't work.

Weapon: Taradces also has a weapon, the Hatred Blade. It can slice through MOST things, unlike Bakud's BetaSword, which can slice through everything. However, what makes it dangerous is that Taradces is DEADLY accurate, and is so precise he could slice an atom.

Powers: He actually has all 49 makuta powers (Well, except for any shadow ones. They're replaced with fire versions of themselves.), as well as all the powers of the other 6, and to top it all off, he has a limited ability to warp reality.

Personality: This guy is, fortunately, also sentient, making him one of the only three sentient members. However, his personality...It makes him even MORE dangerous. He is deadly serious all the time, and has no morals to ANYONE, since his is the Enity of Anger. This means that if anyone stands in his way, he will not hesitate to smash them.

Weaknesses: His orb. However, since he is the most powerful, his orb is the most durable. 

 

And that's the last one! And don't worry, he's not coming out for a while. It will require his armor, his Kanohi, and his blade for the other 6 to unlock him. And, as I said, they're scattered to the winds. And by scattered to the winds, I mean in random, abandoned HQ's.
