    This one has gray armor adorned with several gemstones and holds a metal club with red crystalline spikes. 

Cerls levels his metal-blaster at the Skakdi.

     Jade points his club in Cerls' direction, unleashing an explosive blast of fire in his direction. One of the gems on his armor glows, and Cerls finds his loose arm has become magnetically attached to his leg.

Cerls responds by dodging to the side, unleashing a nasty hail of assorted sharp objects, and Lazah Visioning the gem.

     Jade's gem flashes brightly for a moment, and the flurry of metal goes flying back via magnetism. The gem turns completely black until the laser vision strikes, and is apparently absorbed by the gem. The gem now gives off a red-white glow instead of a gray one.

     "Ow."

     A blast of impact vision tears it off, and Jade hurls a wide wave of flame at Cerls from his club.

"This is getting really annoying," Cerls mutters as he leaps over the wave and fires several magnesium pellets and more sharp stuff. He also tries to lazah the club.

     Jade ducks and uses his psionics gem to jab at Cerls' mind. As mental attacks go, it's relatively weak, but likely enough to give him a painful headache.

Cerls mutters something under his breath in pain. and fires off a ton of super sharp metal dust.
