A so-called "molecular bond" is only the magnetic force created when one atom in a molecule has more negative force than another, which has more posotive force. When nuclear fission occurs, a proton, neutron, or electron actually disappears. Just like that. Then, the energy that was formerly in the proton, neutron, or electron is released, creating an atom-scale explosion. Then due to a chain reaction (This does not have to do with the explosion, but rather parts of the old atom, not split) a whole bunch of protons, neutrons, or electrons burst into energy, creating a huge explosion.
