‎06-23-2015 10:05 PM

As events transpire, Karunax sits upon his broken throne within his shattered throne room. His eyes are focused on nothing in particular, yet he is listening and watching acutely, his empowered mind stretching over Destral. His prized First Lieutenant was desecrated and destroyed. He hears the tireless bickering of his brethren, sees them trade pointless blows with one another, and sees Inharax's forces attempt to suppress them. Their ongoing telepathic conversation was obnoxious, but told Karunax everything he needed to know about the Brotherhood's current state: Inharax, self-appointed leader. Kratark, disgusting purist. Viperax, loyal but unassertive. Tazzuk, irritating narcissist. And so forth, and so forth. Liars. Deserters. Backstabbers. Extortioners. Fools. Traitors.

    

Karunax's mind was racing and swirling with thoughts, diligently assessing the situation. The Anonymous's work left him more aware than ever, but was this a curse? Beforehand, his decision would've been simple: humor Inharax for the benefit of The Plan, purge blunders like Kratark, and obliterate everything that wasn't loyal to the Brotherhood. But now, as Karunax observed the chaos, it all seemed so... petty. Meaningless. Even The Plan seemed too nearsighted when he considered its intended endgame. What was the point of it all? Oh, right... peace.

To Conquer All (2)

‎06-23-2015 10:40 PM

Karunax sneered and clenched the ends of his armrests, forcing them to crumble apart through his fingers. He could feel the boiling pit of rage within him longing to erupt, reverting him to a beast that would level everything in sight. He knew the Brotherhood was lost under his leadership, but he would not accept failure. In its time, the Brotherhood of Makuta fought for peace under their rule. Karunax had believed he fought for a just cause... for peace... but he now realized it wasn't why he fought at all. Not ever. He fought for glory. Power. Conquest. He lived to crush the weak under his mighty heel because it pleased him, not because he thought it would bring about a better tomorrow. Were The Plan to succeed, that would all end, leaving him without purpose.

 

Or would it? The Anonymous was generous. Karunax now knew there were countless other worlds littered throughout the black space outside the Robot. His rage and frustration was matched by a surge of anticipation. In that moment, he knew who's side he would take, regardless of his own personal feelings. Conquest was everything to him, and he would ensure that it didn't end here. His hand was cast. As he stood up, another nagging thought crossed his mind, freezing him halfway out of his chair: this change of mind was so... unprecedented... for him. Did The Anonymous have an ulterior motive for sharing so much information? Karunax shook it off. No, that was irrelevant to him.

 

He stood all the way up and started forward, grinning maliciously and stepping with newfound purpose. As he approached the torn-down gate to the throne room, he stopped and looked back at the remains of his throne. His smile contorted into a disgusted scowl, and twin beams of heat vision blazed from his eyes to melt the entirety of the chair to slag. He turned back and proceeded out of the chamber, muttering to himself,

 

"All hail Inharax."
