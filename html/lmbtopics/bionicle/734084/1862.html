IC:

Zeytran responds by shouting out. "Fatronis!" He roars, to which his hands are no longer magnetized. A gunmetal gray figure steps out, covered in a tattered cloak.

 

"This the one ye want, boss?" Fatronis asks. "Not particualarly, but Umbrax. You need not join me, you just musn't be against me. Can you do that?"

 

Fatronis prepares to crush Umbrax, as a Silver and Purple and Black female Toa steps out, tilting her head. "You are doing it wrong, Fatronis. Your chi won't flow properly in such a stance."

The genre-savvy Makuta Umbrax recognizes that Fatronis is obviously a Toa of Magnetism, and responds to Zeytran

"Sure. I'm just against that guy!"

Even as he says "sure" his warhammer is swinging towards Fatronis's face, guided by Gravity and Vacuum. It's also Silenced.

Zeytran shrugs, carelessly. "My Toa Hagah know their duty, well."

 

Assuming Umbrax can throw it fast, it slams into Fatronis's face, sending him flying back into a pillar. His kanohi is nearly fractured.

 

Batoraxa chuckles and points her finger at Fatronis. "I told you! Your chi wasn't flowing properly, like so." She dashes to Umbrax, using her Gravitational powers to speed her up. A ring of purple energy surrounds her face, swirling.

We have been betrayed. Our trust, misguided. Our faith, put wrongly. Have they no shame? But we will live on, my brothers. We will strive forward, we will endure. We will ascend. Free of limitations, free of fear.
