@Cold Steel.


 

Cold Steel ponders the situation at lightspeed. He came here to flatten the Makuta for they quite plainly deserved it. Anyone with the stupidity of Lohraz clearly deserved to die. However, his smashing had already taken place, and the Makuta were already pulling back together to fight back, he felt no purpose in being there. His objectives in destroying the Brotherhood's power had utterly failed. However, he no longer felt the same way as before.

 

"When I came here, I came to destroy all semblance of power in the Brotherhood. But now, I'm simply curious to see what will happen under the new leader. Thus, I shall take my leave of this place. Perhaps we'll be enemies, perhaps not. It simply depends on how things shall work out. But regardless of how events work out, it is a pleasure to finally meet you, in person, at last."

 

With that, Cold Steel vanishes, teleporting far outside the fortress and somehow overriding the teleportation jammer currently up.

 

As he now stands in the open plain he reflects on his next course of action. As much as the current events were exhilerating, another thought was now constatnly creeping into his mind. Revenge. He had still yet to deal vengance on his mortal foe, and he was longing more than ever to begin the hunt. However, his revenge's hope as of yet hung on a thread. Where his foe was, hundreds of thousands of years could have passed; he could be long gone for all he knew. Cold Steel was not overly worried about this, however. At the worst, he could simply return here and continue with life. However, a suddenly spike in his mind told him that something powerful was at hand, something surpassing in power anything he had ever seen except...

 

Pain...

 

He vanished once again. Whatever was at Atero Nui, it certainly couldn't be good.
