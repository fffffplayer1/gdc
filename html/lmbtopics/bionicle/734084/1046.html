But the more aggression we give to you the more you will retaliate. Therefore a propose that both sides, put away their weapons. And discuss things, civily. I will take two sane makuta, not you blue vapor thing, how about... Maybe Inharax and Charix. Step forward..."

 

He partially relents the grip on their legs until they do so.

*Karunax snorts,*

 

"I thought you wanted a sane Makuta, not Inharax... "

 

*Unable to use his own gravitational powers to counter, he uses his great strength to drag his feet forward a short distance. He looks over at Charix.*

 

"Tell this self-appointed 'peacemaker' your opinion, Charix."

 

-Makuta Karunax, Leader of the Brotherhood-
