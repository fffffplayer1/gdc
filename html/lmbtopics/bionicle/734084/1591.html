Where am I? Right...the lab drone. But just moments ago I–

 

NO! Cold Steel! He did this... I'll have my armies teleport into him and destroy EVERYTHI– where are they?

 

Almost all my interceptors, tanks, gone. My Rogue Makuta Strikeforce, reduced to only the two hundred on Destral. And we? Somewhere amongst the blackened mountains to the north of that horrible landmass. At least Destral is well-defended, but what of the greater prize? Metru Nui? I was hit in the head. It must have been destroyed. So my army there is gone, too. And only a few Makuta left...whose intents are dubious. Is there anyone of unwavering loyalty left? No matter. I shall bring them all before me and they can testify their allegiances.

 

"Exo-suit! Fetch my armor. And bring the Rogue Makuta Strikeforce to the convocation hall. The Brotherhood is having a new meeting."
