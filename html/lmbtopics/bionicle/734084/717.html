‎12-26-2013 11:35 PM

The most ambitious building project ever attempted by the islands is complete. Resources have been mined and gathered, matoran employed, and the visorak have worked hard. The result? A large fleet of ironclads, the largest ever assembled by the forces of Charix, sits in the harbor beside Tyndax. About a third of this fleet (a decent sized amount) sets sail towards the island upon which Fort Metru rests, the other part of the fleet stays put.

 

A revoloutionary new breed of visorak has been created, possesing new natural powers and inspired by the Kahgarak and the Sukkurak.  Although few in number currently, plans are already being made for their use.

 

The Island of Kazzot: The tower Idrisk and the costal fort Malunax II.

 

An order is recieved from Chagrin and the visorak moniter workers respond. All power is routed to the longranged sensors and a scan of the surrounding islands and seas is initiated. The visorak begin looking for any signals or power emmisions that would suggest a base belonging to their enemies.  The scan gradually extends in its range, going farther and farther away from the islands.

 

 

The Telanterent Islands: The costal fortress Tyndax and the fort Chagrin.

 

Likewise, all power is routed to the longranged scanners and, after inputting the locations of known enemy bases, Zakkaz, along with his team of visorak attempt to produce the most complete and real time map of the northern reaches of the world. This map, if completed, would be streamed to the computer database and would be accesable at any Brother-Hood Stronghold.
