Nightmare looks around the room and moves towards the head of it, positioning himself in front of the throne. He takes in the setting around him; it is familiar, although slightly different after years of rule by other leaders and wars by other armies. It has retained the same appearance that it always maintained during the powerful Makuta's previous reign.

 

He gazes upon the members of the new Brotherhood of the generation. They all look angered, confused, and generally unpleasant. They all were looking for a way to rekindle the flame, to regain their lands and to be feared once more.

 

Now, Nightmare has assumed the role of the new ruler.

 

"I'll make this brief," Nightmare begins. "These are desperate times for the Brotherhood. You have been crushed. Defeated. And worst of all, humiliated and embarassed." He shakes his head. "This will not do. Not for us."

 

"So from now on, there is a new regime in charge. New management. From now on, I ask for loyalty to me. Dedication to the new empire that we will build across this land."

 

He looks at each individual member. "Your skeptical, naturally. I've been gone for some time. You don't know me, my success. I have accomplished more just on my own than you and your armies have. I have experience. I know exactly how to dismantle these fools."

 

"Now if you follow me... you will be rewarded. And you will see the triumphs you are waiting to witness. This is no longer a time to sit and remain stagnant in loss. This is a new era. A new reign. MY reign," Nightmare finishes.

 

"The time has come for my return to glory."
