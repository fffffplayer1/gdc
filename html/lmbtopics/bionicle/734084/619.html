Horez stands in the exact center of the Makuta fortress and broods. By all rights, he should be quite insane. The chaotic dimension he had been trapped in for so long was clearly the work of a mad mind. But, Horez was a Makuta of action. He enjoyed a fight, and there was no shortage of fighting to do in that dimension, and that had managed to keep him occupied. Here, however, things were beginning to feel dull. He didn't feel as if he belonged here, and he craved to once again have the war, the fighting, and the destruction he indulged in previously.

 

He stands to his feet and yells with all his might, "Karunax! I grow weary of this peacefullness! Name our foes and send me to ruin them all!"

 

OOC: In other words, Horez is bored.

And I hadn't posted here lately, so I thought it would be wise to "clock in" and say I'm still here.
