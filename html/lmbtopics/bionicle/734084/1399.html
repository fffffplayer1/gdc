Vestak wrote:
Nicholas749 wrote:

OOC: Hm... the blue energies here are concentrations of Nova Vapor, which is a gaseous substance engineered by Pain himself to house his consciousness and serve as his offensive and defensive capabilities. Of all the things he can do with it, what you probably need to know the most here is that it acts similar to Antidermis, where solid objects just pass right through. However, any engulfed matter is steadily corroded on the atomic level, with the speed at which this happens being dependent upon how much power is available to Pain, as well as how dense the matter is.

 

Energy attacks that require striking a solid object to work (like Rhotuka) will not affect the NV unless said attack has an area-of-effect and manages to strike a solid object that is in proximity to the NV. Energy attacks that affect anything in their path (like a disruption beam) have the potential to affect the NV as they pass through it. Energies such as light, regular fire, and electricity will only strengthen the NV.

 

Additionally, Pain can change the Nova Vapor's potency and composition to become lightning-like, allowing him to travel much quicker and attack with greater ferocity and concentrated power.

 

Complicated, I know, but I designed Pain to be a sort of puzzle. So, do you think Kralkron's power could affect that? Because in all sincerity, I have no idea.

OOC: I think it would.  You say it is gaseous and similar to antidermis.  Antidermis burns.

OOC: Antidermis burns with heat, but I also said the NV can absorb fire to gain power. What kind of a burn are we talking, here? If it's like the Amaterasu (black fire that can't be extinguished and burns literally everything), then yes, it'd hurt him. Don't let him touch you though, or else this power of yours could be his.
