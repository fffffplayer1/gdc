Inharax, brother, is there anyone else on the adjenda or can I return to my experiments?" Toxica glares at Crystalia, but remains silent, her Emerald eyes narrowing. Jams on Kolokvar ONLY, no Inharax, no nothing.

Jammers
"Karunax could prove beneficial. What do you think?" She pauses. "Toxica could very well end up ruining this. She's a fool, a brilliant fool, but a fool nonetheless." She grimaces. "Karunax is unlikely to join us. You have favoring with him...if we could manipulate him to our own ends..." she smiles before cackling. "This could prove very good, my king. Soon we will rule the cosmos." She frowns for some strange reason....peculiar.
