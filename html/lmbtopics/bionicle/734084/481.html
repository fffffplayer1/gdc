Invulnerability: I'm sure some wonder why Makuta even bother to have this power when they already have Adaption, Fire Resistance, Ice Resistance, and Density Control. Well the others do make their armor tougher and hard to break, none of them makes the Makuta invincible like Invulnerability. My guess is that this power is harder to use and requires more energy for it to work, so the other powers are just easier to use.

 

Laser Vision: I don't understand why Makuta didn't lose this power when they become completely beings of darkness, but believe it or not this power is the Makuta's very limited control over light. Laser Vision is done by taking the light that their eyes have received and then releasing it at an object or person as a beam of concentrated light. This concentrated form of light can burn through objects without melting them. This makes cleaner cuts than Heat Vision and can't be controlled by Toa of Fire and they'll be hurt more by it as well. However this makes Toa of Light able to control and redirect this power.

 

Magnetism: The Makuta's limited control over magnetism is similar to its limited control over gravity. While they do have quite a powerful control over it, Toa of Magnetism can do more things than a Makuta can with magnetism such as creating magnetic fields. This power allows Makuta to alter the magnetic field around objects to throw them or to rip metals a part and limited ability to reshape metal. Because they only had a very basic control over magnetism it had become necesary to eliminate those who, like the Toa, control it better and have more power over it.

 

Mind Reading: Another of the psychic powers of the Makuta, this power allows them to read the minds of creatures and people. Able to know what attacks are coming before they do and then dodge or counter then before they happen this power is very vaulable in battle. Even more so when trying to retreive information from people.
