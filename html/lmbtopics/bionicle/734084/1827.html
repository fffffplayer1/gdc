OOC: And now, to plot shenanigans!

 

IC: Zakaku appears in his lab, along with Rahori, the latter being promptly magnetised to an protosteel table much bigger than he is by Rahkshi. Zakaku just pours the liquid he had created earlier into a syringe. Rahori stares wide eyed in fear.

 

"You know, I wasn't lying when I said I was working on something revolutionary." Zakaku comments. "And it wouldn't be just for the Makuta, either, if it wasn't for the imbeciles who keep this war alive. That is why I must become the leader of the Brotherhood. This conflict is in the way of revolutions, and the revolutions that do happen are for the war. Once I become leader, I'll return the Makuta to Rahi making while I do what I love. Not to mention the perk that Toa won't be on guard as much to stop my...more immoral experiments. Well, what they consider immoral, anyway. The problem with most scientists who claim to be on the 'good side' is that they don't have the courage to do what I've done and will do. May have to {smash} off a few of the more zealous Toa once I'm in charge, now that I think about it. But enough talk. Allow me to show you what this does." And with that, Zakaku injects Rahori with the liquid.

 

Rahori cries in agony, then starts going into spasms as he begins to glow a sickly yellow. His arms and legs begin to lengthen, and his body starts to gain muscle mass. He feels something inside him grow while his emotions spiral out of control. New, primal instincts begin to settle into his mind. By this point, he's reached the size of a Toa, but he keeps going. His strength increases dramatically, his hands start to turn into claws, and his red and yellow colors start to flicker. By the end, a ten foot tall Toa is in the place of the frightened Matoran. Or, rather, what was supposed to be a Toa Nuva, but was an obvious failure in that regard. That isn't what Zakaku's worried about.

 

"This could be a problem..." Zakaku muses, looking at the Av-Toa(?)'s armor.
