In the cluttered mess of a laboratory, a stasis pod sits. This stasis pod had quietly sat and done its job as long as had been necessary. That time, unfortunately, had just expired. The energy halting the flow of time within sputtered and died away, leaving a trapped and irritated being inside. This being wasted no time in smashing his way out.

Looking back at the pod, the being reflected upon how often he was placed in these things. Ah, well. With a shrug, he set himself to repurposing the tides of power within him. The moisture in the air slowly gathered, forming a cloud of fog around him. A buzzing grew inside, and two lights flared to life, shining out of the mist. With a squelch, the being set off to complete the task given to him, leaving a trail of slime across footprints burned into the floor behind him.

(Just to prove that I didn't leap into this without setting it up, here's the action beforehand, right up there.)

The being stopped just before the door to the Convocation Hall. He could hear all sorts of howling and shrieking and blasting rattling around inside. That'd be the time for an entrance, then.

 

From the perspective of the folks inside, a portion of the door simply crumbled and fell apart. Fog wafted through the hole, and a buzzing noise began to build. The fog continued to sift through the door and collect. In a matter of seconds, the hole in the door was completely obscured. Two small lights flickered on, gleaming out of the mist.

 

The pinpricks shifted back and forth, as if scanning the room. As several weaponed grunts coordinated their fire, the lights within flickered out. An awful rumbling started to build in the mist, escalating to an awful shriek. A portion of the fog billowed outwards towards where the grunts were concentrating their fire, carried by a column of sonic energy rushing to what was hopefully the invisible target.
