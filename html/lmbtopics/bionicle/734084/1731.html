@Somewhere in space.

 

The forces that were fighting the Toa, now float in space in a daze. Charix glances about, angered by the complete failure of his forces to even put up a fight in the face of that staff... Charix's thoughts churn with constant play back, and rewind of the past fight. What could have been done differently? What could be invented, produced, placed, or otherwise put into play that would have changed the outcome? Never having faced a problem like this, Charix is forced to push it back in his mind, instead, focusing on the escape he was forced to follow through with.

 

The flying Wing that so brazenly flew into the room floats nearby; it's form seeming slightly disrupted, but regenerating rapidly. It suddenly begins to expand, growing in size, and revealing itself as a "Dread Wing."

It's form wavers a bit, and the craft, for a moment, appears somewhat less black. Every RMS agent, visorak, and Rahkshi floating in the area are suddenly teleported away, vanishing from view.

 

The Dread Wing resumes it's initial, featureless form, and adapts to space, before scanning its surroundings to locate Spehrus Manga. Various energies are prepared, and the craft, upon finding its target, teleports away.
