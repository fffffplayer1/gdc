~Ultimate Tritan~ "Yes, we are. What does it mean to you, Tritan?"

We can, and, with your consent, will."











~Ultimate Tritan~



IC: "Tritan, you're pushing your luck. Again. There was no extension in either alliance contract. And by myself, I WILL NOT agree to any alliance. Besides, if the Makuta did agree, you'd probably try for an alliance between us and the Order of Mata Nui, which will not happen!"





~Makuta Ikirro~





No alliance is permanent. Isolationism is always possible. Noninvolvement, as a simpleton calls it. Try for the upper hand. Keep a little temporary alliance with others, and when you see it useless, there are no rules against breaking it. Only the hatred of your enemies grows, but, like a force of war, if you use force, you ought to use overwhelming force. If you are to be hated, you ought to be overwhelmingly hated. A treaty is a simple document. Nothing more. Two friends may love each other one day, and be at each others' throats the next. You may think that another is trying to take an advantage of your alliance, which may end up the same way as what I speak of, but you are receiving the upper hand in calling for an alliance. Look at it this way: If they reject, it means war and then peace and then it repeats. If they accept, you can control the cycle of war and peace. The ones that are to be allied for your benefit are the one of the largest Empires in all the land. Why not all the everyone and betray them all? You will decimate them, but keep the peace for a while. Power resides in those who accept it.
An intersting concept Ackar, though it owuld seem that it would be better said to them in jammers, unless, of course, you purposefully allowed thoe outside of the Makuta to read it.... As it is, if the Makuta really believe that their two or three person HQ will be able to overthrow, as you correctly put it, one of the largest empires in the land, then perhaps old argh is losing his mental edge over time. But, it would be wise for them to join with NO intent of betrayal. And as for what I said about an alliance by extension, all I meant was that, because we and the Makuta are allies of the DH and Matoran, they cannot attack us without having some coflict between themselves and the Matoran and DH, as those HQs are allied with us both.





~Ultimate Tritan~
Ha ha ha ha ha! What a joke! You think that a few Makuta can't take you on? That's where you're dead wrong. Ever since I joined the Makuta, I've been holding back from using a Makuta's full potential just to be fair, though certainly not accurate to the Bionicle Storyline. I can't imagine the Toa having more then five or six members, which is ten or twenty fewer then you'd need to stop three Makuta. There was only one occasion where I used my full potential, and with it I flattened an entire army.

You tell him Ikirro.
