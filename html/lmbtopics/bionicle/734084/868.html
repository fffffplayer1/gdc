Typhoid42 wrote:
Brickme wrote:

Charix continues to stare at Chonix and she gets the feeling that he can see into her mind.

OOC: Charix is just analyzing her mind and looking at anything that seems interesting such as backstory, powers and other useful stuff like that.

Charix finds what he was looking for.

Chonix once was one of the only Makuta to be a member of the Order of Mata Nui and recieved excelent training. By the time of the Great Matoran War, she deserted them because she became bored with their rules. For months she ran from the Order, fighting them off when she was found. It didn't take long for her to go insane and find a unique interest in the pain of others. She found a village of matoran in Karda Nui and stole some supplies for her own wicked purpose. Using her newly aquired goods, she hand-crafted several instruments of pain. Her most precious of these was her double-sidded Jagged Energy Spear. The only being to ever live after an encounter with her was a wandering Ce-Matoran named Tasina. Chonix modified her body to be able to relese Shadow Leaches when they were created. Her mask came to entertain her when she discovered it's power, and used her enemies' shells to practice and hone her technique. Her special abilities include the manipulation of poison, shadows, and air. Her air abilities are weak, but she is practicing a new torture system with it.

Favorite saying: "I don't have a superiority complex, I just know I'm better than you."

Current objectives: Wreck Toa HQ, Find and Collect a Mask of Emulation.

Expert at: Crafting and Smithing.

Personality: Insane and creul. She happily lets those in control be in control, she has no interest in betrayal or rebelions. She expects good treatment from others, but shows little mercy to those in her power.

OOC: Karunax wears a Mask of Emulation. That's not going to be an object of temptation, is it?
