In some shadowy chamber, Kolokvar appears, and begins walking down the familiar corridors to  Inharax's old chamber. 

 

When he arrives he opens the door, and looks in. Not much time has passed since Inharax's passing, and yet the chamber seems desolate as ever. Kolokvar smiles. To think the greatest leader of the Makuta in all history could be smiten and forgotten by his people so quickly. Now these pretenders claim leadership; Charix and Lohraz, the Himler and Goring. How pitiful. But not for long shall they taint the name makuta.

 

Kolokvar enters the chamber and paces across the ebon floor to the grandiose throne set in the center. No makuta had ever so much as touched the throne, let alone sat upon it without Inharax's eternal wrath, and few were insane enough to dare try. But a reaction was just what kolokvar was aiming for. He touched the arms of the seat, and descended down into it. A long sigh came from his lips, and a smile began to form.

 

"Now...to begin."

 

A mental message flashes across Spherus Magna and enters the minds of every Makuta on the planet. The message bears few words, but it's meaning is known instantaneously;

 

Destral. Is. Mine.

Zenila, still within Destral tending to her Garden, pauses. She continues. Zenila has not participated in the politics of the civil war. She never really has, after all it would be detrimental to her garden's growth should she participate in such silly efforts. She looks at her favored plant: The Sapphire Lotus, contrary to it's name it's not blue at all. No, instead it's a deep crimson red. Surrounded by lesser plants, bright green vines tangle around the Sapphire Lotus as the cherry blossom tree's hover over it, the pink leaf clusters each sporting a pink crystal.

 

Zenila however, pauses. Destral is his? Just who exactly does Kolokvar think he is? She sents out a message to Crystalia and Astrona, before snarling. Jammers on Kolokvar.

Jammers
Crystalia contacts Kolokvar. "Kolokvar, Zenila just contacted me. She's a good asset. Ferocious at times. She believes you will destroy her Greenhouse, or thinks you will. If I may, suggest that on the contrary, you are going to protect it from Charix and Lohraz. She never liked Lohraz, and is not fond of Charix either, if you can convince her that they have, dear Mata Nui help us all, been rude to any plants and have damaged any, she might join you. She's a neutral one. Her motives are based exclusively off of how Plantlife will be treated. At least, that within her Garden." Crystalia pauses, frowning. "And Astrona just screamed at me. Same issue, instead of plants, it's animals. Not a garden, she's got some animal cave on Destral somewhere." She shrugs mentally and exits the conversation. OOC: I want Astrona and Zenila more active
We have been betrayed. Our trust, misguided. Our faith, put wrongly. Have they no shame? But we will live on, my brothers. We will strive forward, we will endure. We will ascend. Free of limitations, free of fear.
