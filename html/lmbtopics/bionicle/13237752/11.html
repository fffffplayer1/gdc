"You... you... "

 

"I am sorry."

 

"You... your... "

 

*The Anonymous watches the orb's glow fluctuate violently. He could only imagine the emotion that is choking Solipsil this very moment, and... unsurprisingly... he found it difficult. A long time ago, he chose to erase the memories of those feelings. All the pain and sadness, all the regret and hatred... the fear... the joy... even in recollection, such emotions were just a distraction to him. He could never again feel their sting, nor could he remember how they felt, which is exactly how he wanted it.

 

As such, seeing Solipsil fall apart for good right in front of him... The Anonymous feels nothing. His apology is only a fake sympathetic word, the use of which would only stimulate Solipsil into further grief. His counterpart... his predecessor... has realized that he is truly alone. All of his hope is gone. He will cease to wish to continue on, and The Anonymous would graciously put him out of his misery. And then one more sin would be cleansed off the face of the past.*

 

"I... I can't... you... "

 

"Solipsil, I can give yo-... "

 

" ... Your words are... are just empty... "

 

What's this?

 

"... Your... Your words are just as empty as you are!!!!"

 

*The Anonymous takes a step backwards. The orb is sizzling with energy, distorting the air around it with immense potency.*

 

"You threw him away!!! You had him!!! You had secured him for forever!!! And you threw him away??!!!"

 

"I... "

 

"You... You're a waste!!! A shell!!!! Just a cold shell of nothing!!!! There really isn't anything inside you, is there??!! Just some wretched hollow... trying to find some twisted solace at the expense of others... others and yourself!!!! How could you??!! HOW COULD YOU???!!!!"

 

The hatred has overpowered his grief. He is still Pain at his core.

 

"Your feelings are consuming you. When this is over, you will finally be at peace."

 

"When this is over??!! When this is over... I' m  g o i n g  t o  t e a r  y o u  a p a r t  ."
