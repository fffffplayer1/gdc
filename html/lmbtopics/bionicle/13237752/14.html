As a side affect of the sonic dampening Volitak-based field currently in place, the sonic hum is suppressed.

Annoyed by the failure, the Boggarak launches its Rhotuka at the cylinder. The Kellerak follows suit with its own, firing at a different cylinder.

Each attacked cylinder has the sides open, and three Nektann come out. They disable the Rhotuka with RaR blasts and fire Shelek energies at the Visorak.

The Keelerak rushes forward and attacks two Nektann with its feet (there are six, correct?)

The base, which was monitoring the situation, deploys Adaptation Rahkshi with shoulder-mounted Cordak blasters.

It's blasted with intense Rahi COntrol and Confusion.

If this succeeds in controlling it, it is sent to attack the Adaptation Rahkshi.

The two othe pods teleport-fly at the Adaptation Rahkshi, breaking open for the Nektann to blast RaR at the Adaptation Rahkshi.

OOC: Where did you get the Kraata powers?

 

IC: The Rahkshi instantly adapts immunity to the energy and the Visorak, and manages to distract both long enough for an Accuracy Rahkshi to snipe both. The Adaptation Rahkshi fires at the pod.

OOC: Xian energy tech is compatible with Kraata powers. Also, Rahi Control is a mask power.

 

IC: The Nektann fall back, repairing the exploded ones with Kiril beams. The pods tele-fly back to the Xian dome, their payload delivered.

A Slowness Rahkshi bring the Nektann to a standstill, allowing some Guurahk to finally finish them off.

The Nektann fire Kakama beams at one another, countering the Slow field enough to get out of it and fire Felnas at the Rahkshi.

Even the beams fail – Slowness can bring a Kakama Nuva to a halt.

The Nektan switch tactics and use Teleport beams to zap each other out of the field. Then they continue their previous attacks.

An anti-teleport field is over th area of the base, rendering this impossible.
