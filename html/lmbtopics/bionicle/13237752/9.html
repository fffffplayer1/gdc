*At first Karunax is taken aback by this exclamation, but he quickly becomes suspicious.*

 

"No, Pain was torn asunder when the Great Spirit Robot's core was ignited; when The Plan reached fruition. We both were certain!"

 

"And that we were. But try to understand, that was only half. My counterparts and I ultimately decided to have two hosts to reduce future risk of interference. We tore him apart several times, but the two largest remnants were preserved and each placed in separate hosts. You, with your previous alterations, were only half the equation. We also selected another strong mind from a different universe to account for the opposite half."

 

"You've got to be joking... but then I don't think that's possible."

 

"After destroying your Pain, I left in search of the other. I quickly located and accquired the host, but there was... surprisingly... no trace of his Pain. Not even in memory. He even lacked the Hardlight prototype equipment my counterpart left behind to make the mental insertion possible. Admittedly, I am still puzzled by the conundrum."

 

"And where is this other host now?"

 

"There is a reason he was selected for such an important role in my history. He is brilliant for an organic... so he managed to escape."

 

"Yeah? How? And why don't you just destroy this Pain like the other one?"

 

"He escaped by taking advantage of and nearly reversing what I had done to him. And I will not destroy this Pain yet, because I want to know how he escaped. But then there is another conundrum: he is somehow far more developed than your Pain. He has managed to supress the rampant negative emotion imprisoned within him, something you know very well your Pain never did."

 

"Yeah, he was a crosswired sadist."

 

"But this Pain here... his mind's outer layers are constructed well and incredibly difficult to bypass. I cannot yet intrude enough to get what I desire from him."

 

*Karunax grins.*

 

"I bet I could squeeze it out of him."

 

"Perhaps... but not the way you are thinking."

*The Anonymous steps closer to the pedestal, and a terminal materializes between it and him. He places the tips of his fingers upon its screen in a specific pattern, triggering the orange prison to shrink and the pedestal to sink into the floor as if its melting. When it is gone, he turns back around and walks to the room's exit. The terminal fades away at his departure.*

 

"You wish to know more about your universe? Follow."

 

*He leaves the chamber. Karunax begins to go after him, but briefly stops to look back at where the pedestal had been. If this "Pain" was so important to The Anonymous, and the other host was hunted down for it... then what could that mean for himself? Something isn't adding up.

 

He forces himself to set those suspicions aside for now, and leaves with The Anonymous. But he will be back.*
