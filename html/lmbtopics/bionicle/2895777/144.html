Welp, here's my RPC I've been talking about.

Name: Genetic Organism with Hybridized Scardon DNA (or GOHSD for short pronounced Johsed)
Race: Steltian/Cyborg/Scardon gene hybrid
Affiliation: Money()
Appearence: Black and Orange armor, Inika build, green HF 2.0 head with backwards Evo piece.
Personality: Goshd is a greedy, trigger-happy individual who is willing to do anything to make a profit. He has a quiet loathing for authority which he vents in mild insubordination. The only thing keeping him tied to Xia is the fact that only the Vortixx know how to keep him alive.
Tools: Nynrah ghost blaster (standard), sword that can capture energy and redirect it.
Powers: Gohsd has been genetically engineered to exhibit the Scardon's (a fanon species of mine) adaptive healing factor. This allows him to recover from normally crippling injuries in a matter of minutes, and not be felled by it again. His body is constantly upgrading itself to deal with attacks, and gaining experience in the process. However this comes at a price. If he exhausts himself or fights beyond the limits that his adaptive factor can endure, than he will pass out. This can be prevented by a serum developed by the Vortixx. Of course, this serum is the only thing keeping Gohsd in Xia He is an accomplished acrobat and marksman, as well as a skilled fighter in hand-to-hand combat.
Backstory: Gohsd used to be a Steltian merchant who was a little down on his luck. So when the Vortixx offered him money to allow them to experiment on him, he readily accepted. Looking back, Gohsd really wishes he had declined. The experiments advanced his age significantly, and caused slow deterioration of his body. The only thing keeping him alive is his suit, and the Vortixx serum.

How's that?
Soldier of Christ! -Kolbitar-> Da Renaissance man!
