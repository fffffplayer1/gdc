IC: Cold Steel...

 

Cold Steel quickly arrives to the Mask of Life/Statue's Location, a telltale flicker betraying him switching out with a time dupe. As he rushes along the streets, tearing his way around or through anything in his path, he begins to notice something he hadn't before. When he lost his armor, the pinnacle of his creations, something began to change about him. When he took off the Legionaire's armor, not entirely trusting them in the first place, he quickly realized what was different. His former armor made him almost unstoppable, but had it's weaknesses and dampened his power. Now, he could truly unveil his power, which included...

 

His sword turned partly blue as a light blue energy swirled around his arms before hurtling through the air, ripping through it and straight into a tank. Immediately the energy sweeps over it, freezing the machine in a several inch thick layer of ice in addition to cracking the outside armor. Amidst all the carnage, he smiled. After a hundred thousand years, it felt good to be truly free.
