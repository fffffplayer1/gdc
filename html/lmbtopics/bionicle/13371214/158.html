Phase 1. TV invades and begins spawning Domain rifts, but is weakened while in the fabric of existence.

Solution: Overwhelming physical attacks will damage him.

 

Phase 2. TV retreats into a Domain rift, quickly repairs himself, and continues to work from there. He is no longer weakened.

Solution: Enter Domain with linked minds to disperse negative effects and continue to fight him physically. He will not collapse the Domain upon himself. Overwhelming physical attacks to force him outside the Domain rift. (This phase took so long that I made TV just leave the rift on his own to move on to phase 3)

 

Phase 3. TV is back outside the spherical rift, but still remains inside the Domain. Nothing can touch him. From there, he begins collapsing the various Domain rifts across the city, severely weakening the fabric of existence in the area to prepare for final erasure.

Solution: He still uses his senses to perceive the world around him, and is much closer to existence in his personalized Domain than when in the Domain rift. Intense light and sound will severely shock him and drive his mind into an involuntary frenzy.

 

Phase 4. TV's consciousness is going bananas trying to fight off the blight of existence and its principles, which directly contradict his corrupt nature. Trapped in a state of shock, he drags all his opponents into the Domain with him to try and eliminate them physically.

Solution: You are all in the Domain, which means he is physically vulnerable. Blow him to smithereens.

Solution 2: His mind is severely handicapped. Invading his consciousness is relatively safe. Trying to forcibly damage it is out of the question, but imposing the ideals of that which he rejected is not.

 

Phase 5 (only occurs if solution 2 is found in previous phase). He becomes unresponsive, and tries to retreat into the Void and collapse the Domain on Atero Nui.

Solution: Blow him to smithereens. He won't fight back.

Solution 2: Mercy. His weakened mind is open to suggestion. Convince him to stop.
