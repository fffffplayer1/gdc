BrickmeC wrote:

If anyone possessed the ability to moniter energy scans, that person would suddenly notice that the whole area was all at once being scanned, thouroughly, and quickly.

 

Fifteen blurs suddenly slash across the sky, each moving to fast for identification. With slightly above super sonic speeds they split into three groups and ever so slightly dip towards the city, each group release an attack aimed at three of the most heavily equiped fortifications in or around the City. If their are no heavily equiped fortifications, then they will aim at the most concentrated groupings of Makuta forces. If their are no concentrated groupings of Makuta forces, then they will aim at all fighting groups of beings. 

 

Crash! The damage done by the attack is almost imemdiate. The projectiles fired off were to fast to be observed, but the damage is done. Whatever was targeted is smacked with a multitude of forceful and voilent explosions which possess the power to level fortified walls and release shockwaves that would throw unsecured beings and objects several yards away. Any beings or objects that are exposed to the attack find themselves unnaturally weaker as well.

 

The blurs which did the damage are gone, but they leave one other thing to remember them by. A shattering Boom is suddenly heard which pounds into eardrums, and causes every object to shiver with shockwaves.

 

 

A few Nektann are blown up, and the entire cloud is scattered. Repairing their own with Kiril and using various disks to restore their strength, they return to their rotating position.

 

This data is sent to Xia.

Five Xiajets break the sound barrier over the city, rapidly accelerating to Mach 4 as they fly a search pattern, looking for the source of the explosions.

@Atero Defense

 

High up in the sky, a visable shiver in space occurs as an emmense, long and pointed dead black flying wing emerges from nowhere. The thing looks like some nightmarish creature of the night. 

 

 

Immediately, the craft stikes up an intense barrage on the collected groups of Toa and forces of light. Mini missiles by the hundreds slash downwards at the enemies. The barrage is not centered on any one area, instead targeting any and all sizable groups of enemies. Any targeted group is set upon by dozens of these rockets, slashing down in swarms and releasing the powers of weaken, chain lightning, explosion, fragmentation, shattering, and sonics. All together, this causes widespread high voltage electrical storms, shockwaves that weaken substances on contact, many explosions, flying fragmentation and shattering missiles, and release of damaging sonic waves which ripple through the air and ground.

 

Buildings surrounding the targeted regions would neccessarily be struck, and falling debri would be present.

 

The whole attack would be over in fifteen seconds, at which point the last of the missiles would have fallen.
