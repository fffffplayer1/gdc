+The previously hacked (by Nuhkal) telescreens and speakers activate again...+

 

     ...The sounds of war are on the other end. Explosions, the crashing of towers, and the agony of fallen soldiers. Xiajets zoom overhead, shooting down Intercepters which spiral out of control before colliding with the island of Destral in a ball of fire. Other Intercepters fight back, but to what avail? Tron units and Order forces march across the Great Stronghold, smashing any Brotherhood agents who stand in their way. Then the Throne room comes into view, four toa -one the famed Jalkron- have managed to deactivate the powers of the makuta in the room. Now, instead of taking their foes captive, they continue the fight, torturing the defenceless Brotherhood leaders at will. One of the makuta was lucky, for his armor now lies empty on the floor. He is no longer able to feel the pain of the toa, but is free from the bonds of reality, free from the curses of life.

 

     A makuta with a kanohi Calix is pinned against the wall, his mouth fused shut as a result of one of the toa's little "jokes." Then a voice is heard across the speakers, filled with sorrow and grief, but a will behind his words suggests a hidden strength.

 

     "I don't know if you can hear me, this is the first time quantum communication has been tested... it may also be the last. What you see before you is the battle of Destral. The Brotherhood of Makuta requested assistance from the Toa, Order of Mata-Nui, and Xians to help solve their disunity. But instead, the allies saw a weakness, and turned on the Brotherhood, breaking their own values in doing so. We now attempt to hold of the Light-affiliated, but the numbers are against us. If we had nothing to stand for, this would be the end. But we do. As long as the toa cast illusions over you, we will fight. Even if there is only one of you left under their spell, our lives will be the barganing chips. We will succede, because we have t-"

 

     +The communication is cut.+
