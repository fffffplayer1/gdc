@Atero Defense

 

High up in the sky, a visable shiver in space occurs as an emmense, long and pointed dead black flying wing emerges from nowhere. The thing looks like some nightmarish creature of the night. 

 

 

Immediately, the craft stikes up an intense barrage on the collected groups of Toa and forces of light. Mini missiles by the hundreds slash downwards at the enemies. The barrage is not centered on any one area, instead targeting any and all sizable groups of enemies. Any targeted group is set upon by dozens of these rockets, slashing down in swarms and releasing the powers of weaken, chain lightning, explosion, fragmentation, shattering, and sonics. All together, this causes widespread high voltage electrical storms, shockwaves that weaken substances on contact, many explosions, flying fragmentation and shattering missiles, and release of damaging sonic waves which ripple through the air and ground.

 

Buildings surrounding the targeted regions would neccessarily be struck, and falling debri would be present.

 

The whole attack would be over in fifteen seconds, at which point the last of the missiles would have fallen.

 

     A few seconds into the attack, project Home Turf retaliates, elementally blowing the projectiles into eachother with powerful winds, destroying them with Fire, or simply intercepting them with quickly created shards of Stone. I leave it to you to tell how effective this defense is.

     Oculus Magna scans the Space Wing with Akaku-Rode tech.

IC: Nartyez flings a blast of light to which Zeytran replies with a Shadow Sphere, which encases the Light. A white liquid surrounds it before exploding, and a black tinted light blast appears. It explodes, hitting both Zeytran and Nartyez. A gleaming aura of Light surrounds Nartyez, while a hazy aura of Shadow surrounds Zeytran. They dart at each other, Twilight Sparkle vs Lord Tirek style The Streaks of Yellow and Black follow lazily. The explosion from the collision could be seen miles away. If one looked inside the sphere, they would see no more Zeytran or Nartyez. The sphere explodes, shattering into millions of pieces.

Where Nartyez once was, an angel made out of pure light stands, in contrast, where Zeytran was, a demon of Pure Shadows stands. They are opposites. They will fight. They will win. Kraka, the Spirit of Shadows, hisses and creates several Shadow Lances, which all fling at Avka, the Spirit of Light, who in turn creates a shield out of Light. Light envelops the Lances, turning them into Light Spears, which in turn fly at Kraka, who disintegrates them.

A guttural roar escapes Kraka as he charges Avka with his twisted sword in one hand and saw shield in the other. Avka responds with his spear and rounded shield, they two Spirits. The angel, the demon. Titans Clash, as the ground rumbles from the impact.
