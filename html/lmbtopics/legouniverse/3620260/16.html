The strange, bony creature dove after the two, quiet as night. Its stealthy paws leaped up through the air, tackling Awesome to the ground. The samurai drew his sword, desperately trying to slash upwards at the beast.

Damian locked an arrow in his fingers and held the bow, drawing back the string, aiming for the eye. He fingered the tip, and then let the arrow whiz through the air, striking its target. The wolf howled with pain before leaping backwards from Awesome, who scrambled to his feet. The wolf, not to be outdone, cast away its agony and charged after Damian.

Damian froze, the smile still on his face after freeing Awesome. It took him a couple of seconds to process that the beast was after him now. His grin slowly sank into a frown as he flung the bow over his shoulder and started to take a mad attempt at dashing away.

“Let’s go!” Damian shouted to Awesome who was fumbling with his sword. The samurai looked up, probably surprised that the beast wanted to continue to attack even with a strike to the eye.

Awesome nodded and leapt forward, dashing past tents with the wolf howling behind them.

Sadly, they were the only ones there. This begged the question. Where did the rest of the Nexus Force troops go?
