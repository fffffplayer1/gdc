IC: 

As Sparrowhawk cruised towards the center of town, he listened to the radio. nothing interested him, so he turned it off and looked out the window. There were multiple police vehicles flying towards him. Wanting to avoid injuring his hopefuly soon to be employers law enforcement, Sparrowhawk took a sharp right. The police followed him into a back alley, where he sprung out of the seat about ten feet, held aloft by red smoke emanating from his pocket, and climbs on the roof. 

 

"Sorry boys, i needed a ride. Just drive it back to the dealer in reverse, it'll be good as new!"

 

With a small bow and a cocky grin, Sparrowhawk begins running across the rooftops, making his way towards the center of town. Suddenly, a red light flashed in his peripheral vision, making him think the police followed him. He turned around, begrudgingly ready to beat the tar out of them, and found nothing. The red light slowly apeared again, this time accompanying a splitting headache and a whispering yet extremely loud voice, talking in a tongue that was foreign to Sparrowhawk, yet he was somehow able to understand.

 

Free me. Free me and become my Avatar. Worry not about riches or contracts, instead feel what true power is. 

 

Fighting Archangel's thoughts, Sparrowhawk clenched his teeth and shut his eyes, gripping the sides of his head in pain. suddenly, it became to much, and Sparrowhawk passed out. but as his brain shut down, Archangel lost his connection, and was trapped once more in the knife. Soon, the law enforcement converged around the knocked out Sparrowhawk, putting him in handcuffs and loading him in the back of their patrol car. He stayed passed out the entire ride to the station.  

~Sparrowhawk
