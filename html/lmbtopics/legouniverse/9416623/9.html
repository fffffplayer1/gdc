The silver hood of the car breaks through the top of a cloud, and Bucky brings it to a stop on top of said cloud. With his free hand, he reaches into the glove compartment, and extracts The Orb of the Baron. He taps it, and it glows red. It levitates into the air, held up by a coating of DI. Bucky floats out of the top of the car, orb at his side. Another Vortex springs into being in his other hand, and they both grow in size, growing to look like a pair of large shields. He flies up into space, leaving his car parked on the cloud.

 

It’s unmistakable now, the intake of DI is having a positive effect on Bucky’s size; he now stands a full eight studs tall, proportions unchanged. He carries on for a bit, Orb bobbing along behind him, until Giovanni is well behind him. (Sufficiently far away enough that any battle that might take place wouldn’t affect the surface.) Once satisfied with his position, he stops. Out of one Vortex springs a table and chairs, out of the other, a teaset, complete with biscuits. They set themselves up in front of Bucky, and grow to match his size. He sits, and watches as the teaspoon begins to stir up his tea.

 

-BuckyKat, The Kingmaker

‎07-13-2014 11:36 AM

At a beach house on Giovanni, near the city of Piltover, a man is relaxing on his back patio enjoying his dinner. Just as he's taken a big bite out of his juicy, succulent hamburger, his phone rings. Grumbling, he puts down his plate and answers the call with a "Hello?" distorted through the hamburger. As the person on the other end of the line talks, his face grows serious. He hastily chews and swallows the bite, and listens to the other person with rapt attention, before finally ending with "I'll be there."

 

Casting a forlorn glance back at his hamburger, he enters his house, grabs an overnight bag out of the closet, throws a change of clothes in, then heads for a suspiciously fortified door. He enters, and emerges a few minutes later with  the bag stuffed to bulging. With another wistful gaze at the hamburger, he walks out the front door to the driveway, where a sleek, sporty convertible hovercar awaits him. He tosses his bag in the trunk, then hops in and presses several buttons. Two overly large thrusters pop out of the sides of the car as the convertible top raises to form a solid canopy. The man then stomps on the gas, sending the car hurtling into the evening sky of Giovanni. After attaining cruising altitude, he sets the car on autopilot, sends a message to the number that ruined his wonderful dinner, and leans back the seat. It'll be a bit of a flight.

The Iron Hand of the Swords of Light

A well stocked refrigerator provided Miranda with plenty of choices for her dinner, but a simple pasta ends up being the ordre du jour. She carefully carries a tray of food back to the library where she sits down at one of several large desks. A large book of Giovannian Case Law had been her companion, but she pushed it aside to eat. She wasn't prone to thinking of her past, prefering live in the present, but she indulged herself this time.


"Will the Prosecution give their opening statement, please?"

 

"Yes, your honor. Ladies and gentlemen of the court, today this court stands..."


A messanger comes into the court, nothing new and nothing to disturb Miranda from her opening statement. 

 

"...to give seven men of maximum punishment the state is allowed..."

 

She argued well, soundly breaking her way through the Defense's evidence. Three hours later, the judge called a recess for the rest of the evening, but Mirdana knew she had already won. Just one more witness, the one who would clear away all doubt as to the guilt of those....

 

"Mrs. Harris." some one called out, "Hold on a moment."

 

Hartford City vs. Nexus Intercity Mob. No prosecutor wanted their name attached to the downfall, or attempted dismantlement, of this extremely powerful crime network. But Miranda took the job Fully knowing the danger associated with this case. Unlike other presecutors, Miranda had no children, no family to be threatened. Her husband agreed to take a vacation as far away as possible. 

 

It wasn't far enough. "Mrs. Harris, the police in Gnarled Forest found your husband. The Mob found him first."

 

The trial ended. All seven men paid for their various crimes against the city and its people. Justice was served. But her husband was gone, and with that loss Miranda devoted herself to her work. To her, every defendant was guilty. 

 

The Firm recruited her two years later.

The entire house came to life, suddenly, as klaxons and alarms rang throughout the building. Down from the windows rolled huge steel coverings, rendering the inside of the house pitch black. Every door locked from the inside, and steel walls descened from the ceiling to block off every possible entrance. Quickly, yellow safety lights winked on near the floor, giving a rather dim light to guide the way through the house.

 

 Mr. Kat's voice began speaking quickly:

 

"Come in. This is a Code Blue. Repeat, Code Blue. Please remain calm and follow the Code Blue Protocol. Code Blue."

 

Miranda blinked as her eyes adjusted the the darkness, her flashlight already taken out of her breifcase and switched on. 

 

"Resident, please make your way to the cellar. Resident, please make your way to the cellar." A new voice, like one of those late night disc jockies on the radio, repeated this command. Miranda decided that following the safety lights towards out of the library and into the main hall was the best course of action. So she picked up her mess, ran the dishes to the kitchen, and then followed the safety lights down into the cellar.

 

"Okay, Mr. Kat. What is going on?"

Anyone who was still on the surface would have seen a very intriguing sight. The glowing forms of two old ladies with gold beetle-shaped broochs pinned to their dresses were hobbling down the street, looking at the destruction caused by the goo-wielder.

 

"I say, Sue, look at the monster! He's so BIG and STRONG. He reminds me of my son, y'member him? The big, rugged, tough one who HAD NO RESPECT FOR THE SIMPLE RIGHTS OF HUMAN DECENCY?!"
