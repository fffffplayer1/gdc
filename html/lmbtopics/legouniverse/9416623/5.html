Upon Sparrowhawk entering his workshop, Abathor looks up. He looks for a moment, and then removes his magnification spectacles, and looks again. When he speaks, it’s this sort of old, gravely tone.

 

“Oh. Mmmm. Hmm.”

 

He gets up from his counter, and picks up a measuring tape.

 

“I think you misunderstand. You don’t arm yourself. This is not an.. eh… arsenal. You don’t pick one off the wall. This is my workshop, where I create weapons just for you. We will start with measurements.”

 

Abathor ushers Sparrowhawk over to a little platform of the sort you might see in a tailor’s shop. And indeed, that’s exactly what it is. Abathor takes a bunch of measurements, asking Sparrowhawk to raise this arm, lower that one, spin in a circle, and all that.

 

“Now, an exoskeleton, eh? Hmm. I’ll need to order some parts for that... Let me get you the form.”

 

He putters back behind his counter, and rummages in the drawers for a moment before extracting a slightly worn clipboard with a stack of forms on it, and a pen. He hands both of these to Sparrowhawk.

 

“Fill this out. Figure out what kind of augmentations you want on it. Integrated weapons, all that.  And don’t click the pen. The next page has some… well, a lot of weapon suggestions. Pick some of them, give me lots of detail.”

 

Sparrowhawk sits back on the measurement platform and spends some time filling out the forms. They’re quite extensive, and have a lot of possible choices and options. Meanwhile, Abathor tinkers with what appears to be a metallic fish.

 

 Sparrowhawk finishes filling out the forms, and returns them to Abathor.

 

“Mmmm… Hmm. Good choices. Come back here in a week, and we’ll do the fitting.”

 

OOC: I wasn’t kidding about ‘Master Weaponsmith’. Tell me, in jammers if you like, what you need, and he’ll get it together.

A delicate looking glass ship appears just above the surface of Giovanni.  A dapper looking gentleman transmits from the bridge.

 

"Greetings old friend.  My name is Alpha.  You may remember my creator, one Barcha McBarcha Von Barchastein.  I caught word of your future operations and have come to offer you some services in good faith."

 

On cue, a swath of stars breaks away from the sky and descends.  The entity resembles a massive butterfly with wings of plasma.  They flicker in every color imaginable, burning with a light bright enough to outshine the nearby sun.  Its body proper is a mass of crystalline slabs in the rough shape of a man, white hot plasma, flowning between the irregular shapes,  its wings tapering to delicately hook to its back.  As the creature approaches, its sheer size becomes apparent.  It could hold the entire planet in one extended wing.

 

"This is Mu.  I think his purpose is quite clear.  He will follow your commands exactly.  Have fun."

 

With that, Alpha's ship vanishes just as abruptly as it came.

 

There are several seconds of awkward silence, then a booming telepathic voice cuts through the silence.
