Nothing is heard for a few moments, but before long, Bucky floats back up, now carrying a small cardboard box.

“There we are.”

 

Bucky sets the box on the floor between the front seats, and the car resumes its flight, now heading decidedly upwards.

 

“In regard to the Maelstrom offensive, and the current battle at NT, I don’t really think we can do much right now. Keep in mind, my military currently consists of The Meridian, a handful of Lance wings, and a few thousand Hands. I can’t very well swoop in there and save the Nexus Force from the Maelstrom single-handedly. And also, ‘allies’ is a very strong word to describe my relations with them. Sure, we worked together at Vanguard, but it isn’t as if I’m tripping over myself to go help them, not to mention all of the grief they’ve caused me in the past. For now, I’m satisfied with the Maelstrom being smart enough to not pick a fight with me, and allowing me to build my forces. Which makes for an incredibly smooth transition into our next stop.”

 

The car breaks through a cloud, into the very blackness of space. Luna, the single moon of Giovanni, is directly ahead. Some time passes as they approach Luna, as it grows to fill their view. The car makes a smooth landing on the surface, next to a shiny new spaceship.

 

“Sparrowhawk, this is your as of yet unnamed ship. While you were out, I got all of your armaments in order, including but not limited to this ship, your exo-suit, the weapons you requested from Abathor, and that list of gadgets you wanted. They’re all inside.”

 

The ship looks absolutely marvelous. It brings to mind thoughts of the Pimston, with similar size and curvature, in addition to the very obvious silver armoring. It has more visible gun mounts, however, and is visually more of a ship of war than a home among the stars.

 

"Your thoughts?"
