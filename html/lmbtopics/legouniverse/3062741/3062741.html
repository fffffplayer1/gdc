Hey everyone! Sorry it has taken me so long to finish this book, but it's back! For everyone who hasn't heard of the Sentinel Books then please read Sentinels of Creativity!

Prolouge

Destiny is cruel, thought Swift, the Rank 3 Sentinel Knight. He trudged through Auru Mar and the heat burnt his purple skin.

But he didn't care. He was with the maelstrom now. No...he WAS the maelstrom, and the maelstrom was him. His power was unlimited, for years one thought had possessed his mind: Kill the Baron.

Now was his chance...but he still needed help. As he walked farther and farther through the rocky surface, he looked back to make sure no one was following. Then Swift stopped. He lifted up his hand and hit a button on his wrist.

Swift then evaporated and reappeared on a moving, rocky platform. He heard the flapping of maelstrom wings against the hard body of a maelstrom dragon.

"Whoa! What the brick is that!" said a high pitched voice. "Smash it!" screamed a deeper voice. Swift looked up and saw a Rank 2 Daredevil aiming his guns and a Rank 3 Assembly Engineer.

The Daredevil shot his guns and Swift leaped into motion. He jumped over the flares and landed right next to the Engineer. The Engineer lifted his weapons trying to defend himself. But he was to slow.

Swift grabbed his duel swords and with three quick cuts, the Engineer was in peices. "Noooo!" screamed the Daredevil. Swift looked and saw the Daredevil charging at him.

The Daredevil got to the edge of a rock platform before he was ingulfed in flames. Swift looked up and a crooked smile appeared on his face.

"Butterscorch."

Chapter 1: The Return

Super, the Rank 3 Samurai, cursed as he saw the Stromling Admiral start blasting at some newbies. The newbies were flung back from the impact and the Admiral started to walk toward them.

"Hey purple-face!" shouted Super. It turned and looked at Super. THIS was why Super hated the Gnarled Forest. Full of the ugly...and purple. Super hated the color purple.

"Behind you!" screamed one of the newbies. Super turned and saw a Stromling Ape pounding the ground next to him. Super rolled out of the way and looked back. He hadn't just gotten the attention of the one Admiral, now there were about 5 walking toward him along with the massive ape.

Super leapt into action and knocked an arrow into his bow and a loud TWANG! came from it. The arrow slammed into one of the Admirals and knocked him down. The Ape was then bored of doing nothing and charged at Super. Super tried to lift his scimitar in defense, but he was to slow.

Super then met 400 pounds of huge, muscular, purple brick.

He was run over and stunned on the ground. When he opened his eyes, little Stromling Apes ran around him and he heard birds chirping! He shook his head and stood back up. The Ape was on the ground holding it's head. It ran right into the wall.

Super didn't have time to bask in his victory though, he was shot forward by an Admiral who fired his cannon at him. When Super recovered, he instinctively rolled to the left of his former position. He saw two Maelstrom Cannons rip through the ground.

Super lunged forward and slashed down one of the Admirals with a swift, clean, blow to the stomach. It crumpled and fell to the ground. He then jumped and dodged the second Admirals Anchor. He landed right behind it and stabbed it. It too fell and lay unmoving on the ground.

Super then knocked an arrow in his bow as fast as lightning! He shot the arrow and it smashed the third Admiral. Then he fired arrow after arrow at the fourth Admiral. There was a THUD! THUD! THUD! (or as the Admiral heard it: OW! OW! OW!) and the Admiral was smashed to bits.

The last Admiral was hesitant to attack, seeing his comrades smashed to bits. But in it's hesitance, he was slashed in half as Super took the initiative. Super then turned around and saw the Ape STILL shaking it's head. "Wow...I guess they really do have pea-sized heads."

He knocked an arrow into his bow and pulled back as far as he could. Super felt a familiar buzz in his arm and a warmth in his body. Then there was a bright flash of light as Super let go of the arrow. It thudded into the back of the Ape and it shuddered then fell.

Super then ran to cover. He was exhausted! He ran up on a rock and then sheathed his scimitar and put his bow away. He smiled to himself, "Man..." he said to himself, "I'm outta shape."

"Yes you are." said a cold and evil voice from behind Super. Super whirled around and gasped.

Standing before him, was Redgie, the Rank 3 Space Ranger. He was glowing with a purple light.
