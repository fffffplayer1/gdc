Oh, and here's a little look at the next book... sort of a plot line and bit of story.

*JAMMERS ON!!!! AUTHORS ONLY!(X, Briana, Buck)Unless, you want a spoiler on the next book.....*


You see, Roseann and the rest of the good guys (X, Briana, Misnon, Buck, Ben, ect.) get a letter from Amanda Exeter, Duke Exeter's sister. It turns out that the faction leaders are captured and are being impersonated at that very instant. Roseann realizes the person is luring them in, though she knows that Amanda is telling the truth. They decide they'll do what the enemy wants, though she has a little surprise in store for the enemy... the enemy is The Darkitect. He learned that X, Roseann, Briana, Misnon, Buck and Ben had defeated Layab, mostly X, though, one of the biggest enemies of the Mystic River Secret Agency. With the surprise Roseann springs on the Darkitect, the Darkitect sends ten thousand soldiers to Nexus City to capture it. Roseann, X, Briana, Buck, Misnon and Ben are the only ones who can stop the head of the army... Tali Reeko! (He's way harder in the story than in the game) (Oh, and another thing, they disguise themselves as ordinary people to sneak into the Nexus Tower... nobody but the faction leaders and The Darkitect know about them. That's why they're secret agents!)


Here's part of what is gonna happen... the beginning post, that is. Gives you some time to plan what you're gonna post.

Roseann set down the cup of coffee, and looked up. "X, it was amazing how you defeated Layab... amazing! I never could have done that," she said. She looked at Briana as she and Misnon walked down the steps of the beach house. "Anyway," she said, standing up, "We'd better check the agency," she said, walking to the stove. She pressed a button, and it flipped around, revealing a door. Roseann pulled the door open, and walked down. "Come on, everyone," she said. She stepped down the dark stairs, into a room with computers. They were each flashing green. "New message from a leader or their sibling," she said, walking to one. She pressed a button, and a message from Amanda Exeter showed up. "Amanda," whispered Roseann. She read. "Roseann, help! The Duke, Vanda, Albert and Hael are being impersonated! You must hurry!" read the short letter. Roseann shook her head. "X, Buck, Briana, Misnon, get down here!" she yelled. As the others thundered down the steps, Roseann was exhaling. "Look!" she said, pointing at the screen. When the others read it, their mouths dropped open. Roseann composed herself, and glared into the shadowy corner on the other side of the hidden room. "Whoever did this, I'll, I'll...." she muttered, her hands trembling, "I'll either get them, or give them a huge piece of my mind right now!" she said, stomping into the other corner. She looked up. "This makes no sense... only the Darkitect would do that, and why would he do that instead of-" Roseann stopped short, and laughed. "I know what you're doing," she muttered. "You won't succeed."

By the way, The Darkitect couldn't hear what she, "Said," to him.
