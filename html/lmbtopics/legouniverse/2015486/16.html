Roseann opened her eyes. Seeing Hovawart and X near, she was struck with fear. "Hovawart, I tried to help you," she muttered, a touch of sadness in her voice. "Maybe you are like my brother." With that, Roseann completely disappeared. So did her horse. Nobody knew what happened to her.


What happened....

Roseann awoke, lying on a bed. It was in a dark room, but the bed was warm. Then a door opened, letting in a stream of light. Then, to her surprise, in walked Mr. Dark. "Brother, w-w-why?" she mumbled. He forced a smile at her. "Because, despite us being on different sides, you're my sister." Roseann smiled at him. She knew he had some good still in him! If only she could get it to overpower the evil... "What happened?" she asked. "I thought that you were completely bad!" Mr. Dark grinned. "I am. Well, I think. But even evilness can't stop me from making sure you're safe." She hugged her brother. She had loved him so! They had spent so many times playing together, and swimming in their pool and going to the Noble Star fair! Riding their horse's in the fields, doing all those fun things. Her thoughts stopped when Mr. Dark pulled a sharp needle out of her arm. "Owww," she said. "Still the Docter, aren't you?" Mr. Dark smiled. "Yes."

About three hours later....

Roseann had fallen asleep. When she woke up, Buck was standing over her in a bright field. "What happened?" she asked him.
