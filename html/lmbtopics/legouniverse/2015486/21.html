What would happen when Hovawart found her, if he knew that she had done it, she wasn't going to think about now. Wondering where Wolf was, she opened the link that she had created with him and tried to send a mental picture of where she was to him. She could feel him perk up, and begin to run with purpose. Satisfied, she shut down that link, then, curious, switched back to Hovawart. Strangely, once again she felt nothing from him. She shrugged. Having never transferred emotions before, she had no idea what was going to happen. Opening her eyes, she saw Roseann at the cave entrance, her hands glowing with a strange light.
