Roseann walked into a different room. It was the armory. Nobody else knew about it. Roseann leaned against a pole. She looked at the scar on her hand.

(This is where you find out how she got it)

[Flashback] >>>>>>> Roseann back flipped from the ridge. Her black eyes piercing the night. The Maelstrom was at large, and she had to help stop it. Her black cloak swished around, and her long brown hair swished. She tore off her cape and started fighting. After fighting for many hours, Roseann got tired. She started to run back towards the launchpad on the beach, but suddenly, something hit her on the head. She was knocked out. Later, when she woke up, she was lying on the beach. She lifted her hand to see a deep scar. It glowed a dark purple. She gasped. Somebody named DarkLordVexon came to her and helped her. It turned out that she had a terrible Maelstrom Infection, a rare infection that will turn that person into Maelstrom. Luckily, Darklordvexon helped her, and that she was happy about.

Hovawart silently cursed. Somehow, Roseann had gotten to the armory before him. He didn't think his scamners would have missed a secret passage, but it didn't matter. Hovawart sent a grenade flying toward a pile of munitions, then turned and ran.

Høvawart

Roseann turned her head and flipped towards Hovawart. She grabbed his hand. "Look," she said, holding up her other hand, the one that wasn't scared. She closed her eyes then opened them; Her hand was electric! "It is only temporary, but it is what I have to work with!" she said, "why do you want to destroy me or possibly all of the agents?"

"I'm not going to tell you the official reason, but if you want the unofficial reason, it amuses me." He jerked his hand to the side, and twisted his wrist. Roseann flipped onto her back. Hovawart deployed a blade and slashed at her.

Buck awoke in a dark room where he could see nothing. It was strange however...he could feel a strong presence nearby. "Hello?" Buck asked, shivering because of the cold chill that was spread around the dark...room, Buck guessed. "Hello there Buck. Let's get down to buisness right now. I lead the Conspiracy that plan to take over the world and we want your help. We know that you are not who people think you are so we are going to start destroying you by sending a video to everyone that is an agent in the world." a voice replied. "You can't do that. Who are you!" Buck excalimed. "Oh, just your master's master. I think you should see what I really look like though before the video is sent to everyone" the voice said. At that moment dark flames burst out of the ground around the room making everything clear. Sitting down on a dark maelstrom throne was a black ninja with a maelstrom ball shape covering him. "This is what I shall say in the video Buck".

"Hello everyone and welcome to Buck's past. You all think Buck is a secret agent from the agency of Zero but he is not. He was created by his supposed nemesis..Mr Dark who created him as a darkling. Mr Dark was using Buck as a test subject on his latest maelstrom that could use imagination against foes. Unfourtunatly for the malestrom it didn't work out and Buck became good..but he can do a lot of evil so if you see him, I suggest you defeat him".

"Video sent and dropping passenger into middle of nowhere." announced the ninja as he pressed a large red button. Buck cursed inside. "I swear I'll get you back for this somehow. People may not trust me but I'll find a way to defeaat you by myself. I swear on it!" shouted Buck as he stood up. "Doubt that" muttered the ninja as he pulled a lever that had magically popped out of the arm of the throne. In an instant a trapdoor beneath Buck opened sending him spiralling down towards the surface of the planet that the agencys were based on. As the wind caught his face it blinded him and sent him down on a destperate struggle thinking for a way to survive the fall...
