The problem with that is the fact it shouldn't be moved. Here is why.

When you write a story, you are doing it by yourself. You're not playing. You're letting other people see what you came up with. Roleplay is different. It's like a lot of people writing as they go along. Say there were five people. One was named Ann, one named Justin, the other named Jay, and the two others named Makay and Kara. Those people all created the story as they went along. They used their imagination together to make a perfect story. It's different. So roleplay is much different from a story, people say. But it isn't. It's just a lot of people making a story as they go along. And I would have put this into the Stories of the Universe Sub-forum if I had wanted to. So let it stay where it is.

So this is one of those, "Never ending STORIES" isn't it?

Buck bounced into the bouncy castle and in a flash he was past all the minifigs chatting around it. As soon as he was in the nearest shadow he could find he teloported back to base. A scond later he was infront of his friend. Buck's friend was called Bill. "So how did the mission go?" asked Bill. "Supberb" replyed Buck as he showed his friend the weapon and money. Bill was awestruck. "Wow", was all Bill could say. Buck smiled and walked away. "Heres your next mission!" Bill shouted as he pressed the enter button on his computer. Buck took his sunglasses out of his bag and put them on. Instantly all the data for the mission appeared. "So there's a traitor.." began Buck. "Don't tell the readers!" shouted Bill without hesitation. Buck shrugged and leaped out of the window.

A second later he was running and jumping over alleys and rooftops. His destination was close. Well, far away but he would manage. A couple minutes later and he had travelled about 2000 miles. His destination was in this building. An abandoned building thought Buck as he creeped in through a open window. A loud voice echoed around the room, "Will all secret agents please report to the hall". Buck ran through the door and walked casually behind all the other agents. Blingding light hit him as he entered the hall but his sunglasses protected him from it. His glasses started hacking into the agency database as he saw the hundreds of hundreds of agents sitting in seats in a vast hall. His mission was just beginning.

(Which agency is it?. Find out later)

Roseann started talking quickly to the curious secret agents that sat around the large abandoned hall. . Though she was the head of Mystic River Agency, she wasn't always stern and mean. In fact, she wasn't usually mean at all. "Something has happened and I wanted to bring it to your attention...." She began slowly. "I've recently heard from my friend in North-field, Avant Gardens, that there was an attack. About one hundred soldiers were captured and three hundred were injured. They suspect that the Paradox were in charge of the attack. However we do not pick sides. We do, however, know that the Sentinels have very good reasons and, if we do pick sides, we will chose the Sentinels." she said strongly. She suddenly snapped her head over and looked at Buck. She muttered something and looked back towards the crowd of secret agents. "As you know, the maelstrom is at large. So we must be prepared for something. Such as spies or darklings," she said, intentionally turning her head towards Buck. "Dismissed" She said. After the long crowd of Secret Agents had flooded back into their offices, Roseann noticed buck still standing there. "I don't think he realized I saw him yet," she thought to herself. She jumped down off the twenty feet high podium and landed on her feet. Her black skirt went back and forth as she walked to the database computer. She stared into it and started to type the password. A1B5782HFINO03 was the password. She didn't know that Buck had seen her type in the password. She clicked on a button that said FILE. Then she clicked NUMBER 7 and pressed PRINT. A disk came out of the computer and she held in tight to herself. She looked around one more time, locked the database and walked out of the room.

After doing this, she slowly walked down the hallway which led to her office. Her shoes tapped against the ground loudly and echoed through the entire hallway. She took a key from her pocket and stuck it in the lock. Suddenly something grabbed her wrist tightly. She snapped her head up in shock to look into a deep black face. She tried to pull her wrist away but the grip he had on her was to strong. He grinned deviously and clenched his other fist. "Why did you come here?" She asked, her head turning to her door then back at his face. Her eyes were full of worry and it was obvious she was afraid. "I think you know why," He sneered. With that, he lifted his fist and punched Roseann in the face. She dropped to the floor on her side and fainted. "Now she'll know," He said while chuckling. He turned the key in the door. He picked up Roseann and laid her in a dark corner of the room. Then he looked around for the disk that Roseann had gotten. "It's not here!" He shouted angrily. With that, he left. Roseann laid lifelessly on the ground and the disk was slightly out of her skirt's pocket.
