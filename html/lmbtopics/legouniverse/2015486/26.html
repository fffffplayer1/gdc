The monster fell to the floor, apperantly unconscious, the figure walked over and knelt beside it, he seemend to be talking to, well, no one.

"He seems familiar, do you know who he is?"

He listened to nothing for a minute.

"I see, don't worry, I'll take care of it."

He raised his hand over the monster, and an orb appeared in it, but rather than launch it, he just held it there above the Monster. Suddenly the red light started flowing into the orb, followed by Maelstrom energy from the monster. As it did the monster started changing, the wings retracted, and the monsterous face dissapeared, when the energy stopped, Mr.Dark was laying on the ground, unconscious, but no longer a monster.

The figure threw the orb, which exploded in mid-air. He rose to his feet, and that's when he saw three people, well, two people standing off to the side,the third seemend to be unconscious on the ground.

And then the figure started talking to himself again.
