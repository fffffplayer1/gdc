Buck ran after one of the soilders that had escaped. "Stop!" Buck screamed as he made an attempt to grab the soilder's wrist. The soilder quickly turned round and swatted Buck's hand away but Buck returned with a headbutt. The soilder fell down as Buck grabbed him by the collar. "Who do you work for?!" asked Buck threateningly. "The same Agency you work for. You've been helping us all along Buck. We thank you for that but now we are finished with you so..." the soilder blurted out with a wave at the end. At that moment Buck heard a click like guns reloading. He turned his head around quickly to see a group of soilders pointing their weapons at him. "We know your secret and I think we should let everyone else know. Dont you think?" sneered the captain of the squad as they all fired bullets into Buck's chest. Soaring pain went through Buck as dark smoke started floating into the air. "No, plz, you can't do this to me!" screamed Buck in terror as he felt darkness being released inside him. One glance at his hand told him everthing. His true forme was revealed. "We are the conspiracy and we are going to take over this world" the leader sneered as a helicopter touched down beside them. "HQ, with him alive." ordered one of the soilders.

Suddenly a large explosion came from the room Roseann was in. It was very bright. Bright enough to blind. Smoke flooded out swiftly. Suddenly, Roseann walked out; unharmed. Her wound on her leg had healed. Behind her was a long purple streak.... The Maelstrom! Her scar on her hand was glowing purple brightly. Her deep black eyes pierced into Hovawarts. She lifted up her left hand; the hand without the scar. She looked down at it, then back up. She nodded her head towards a lamp. It was broken. She then turned her hand towards it. A long streak of purple flew out of her hand somehow and broke the glass. It lit up and she lifted the hand with the scar. She used them both and suddenly a darkling spawned. Suddenly her eyes become normal. They didn't pierce. She looked at the darkling. The smoke had stopped and so did the Maelstrom. The darkling ran towards her and she picked a gun from her belt and shot the darkling. She suddenly sprinted towards Hovawart. "You probably didn't expect this," she said, "you can tell what it is. Nobody else other than you and I can. I know how I got it. You know how. But, you won't tell anybody because I won't let you!" and with that, she pushed him down. She just walked away.
