Roseann was sleeping in the same room as Larista, Lycius's sister. Lycius was in another room. Buck was in the room beside Larista's.

It was early morning and they woke up to the small of bacon and pancakes. It smelled delicious! Roseann sniffed. She smiled a little. She turned to Larista on the lower bunk and saw her smiling. Roseann turned her head towards the door. She jumped out from the bed and her smile faded. She walked into Buck's room and shook him. "Wake up, sleeping beauty prince!" she said. Roseann seemed like a country girl now! She saw Buck's expression. "Come on," she said, "Why don't you trust me? Lycius has been my friend since pre-school," Somehow, Roseann knew that Buck didn't trust her. "And Larista, I met her when I came to help Lycius with his math. They are very nice people, Buck. They won't hurt you," Roseann said, "And, Lycius is almost exactly like you. He got captured and infected, but lucky for him, he had just enough imagination to still be in the shape of a Maelstrom, but also enough for him to still be good." Roseann heard Lycius call, "Breakfast!" and start singing downstairs. Roseann heard the thump of Larista's feet as she ran across the large hallway to the stairs. Thumps pounded as she ran downstairs. Roseann laughed. She looked at the little garden outside. "Stay here," she said. She ran downstairs. After talking with Lycius for a little bit, she came back up, opened the window and jumped out. She flipped into the garden and picked some tomatoes and potatoes.
