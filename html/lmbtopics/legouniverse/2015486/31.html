Roseann turned. She glared back at the horseman leader again. "Sorry, I am in a bit of a rush." She leaped up and shot the horseman. He fell down to the ground, still alive, but he would be in some serious pain for the next year. Literally a year. She leaped to the top of the tree, and looked at Razer. "Hmm," she said, turning and seeing Black Widow. "Oh, no!" she whispered. Her brown hair fell down in front of her face. She shook it off and jumped in front of Razer, pulled him against the tree, and glared at Black Widow. "Get away from my brother!" Roseann hissed. When Black Widow looked over, and stepped back a little, Roseann pulled Razer with her as she went over to X. She pushed Razer back over, and glared. She threw a rope up into the trees and onto a branch. "X, climb," she said, pulling him towards the rope. "Climb up and untie that when you get up. I can just leap up." When X started to climb, she ran over to Briana. "Briana," she whispered, picking up Briana in her bird-form, "Come on," she said, walking towards the tree. "Razer, don't try that again, because what I just did was just a warning shot." She leaped up into the tree, out of sight from everybody except X. She seemed alright, until she realized something: Buck was gone.

Roseann then stepped over to X through the leaves and on the branches. She caught an arrow that was flying up through the trees. It had been headed for X. She poked her head through the branches, and glared at them. "Try that again," she said. They did. They shot at her shoulder. She caught it. She broke the arrow in half, and their mouths dropped open. "T-t-that was s-s-stee-l-l-l!" they muttered. Roseann grinned. "Don't try that again," she said. She whistled. "Army of Mystery, retreat!" she yelled. (Ever seen Lord of the Rings? Imagine the army looking like that elf army in the battle) The army disappeared, but really, they had just put on cloaking devices. They were walking away. "Alright, we need to get out of here," she said. She pressed a button, and X, Briana and herself disappeared.


They landed down on the ground. She saw X's wounds. "X," she muttered, "We need to heal these." She ran to her horse and reached in the saddle bag. She picked up a bottle of water, a cloth and a bar of soap. "Okay, this really isn't much use, but it'll work for now." She wet the cloth, knelt beside X, and poured water over X's wound. When he cringed, Roseann sighed. "This should never have happened. Why couldn't our lives just be normal, and we couldn't we just be a normal family that actually has living parents," she said. She put soap on the wet cloth, and set it over the wound. "That would have been nice. But, at least I do still have a living family member. Anyway, keep pressing down on this cloth. I have to go help Briana," she said, standing up and walking over to Briana.


When she was at Briana, she looked at how she smashed into a rock. "Briana, I know you're in there, and believe me, I know you feel like dying," Roseann said, a light and airy tone in her voice. She thought that might help. "But, other than that evil shadow look, I can almost do what you can," she said, closing her eyes. She transformed.... sorta.... into a jaguar. Only, a black one, that didn't look like a panther, but... oh, it's confusing. Anyway, she turned back into her normal self, and patting Briana. "Briana, wake up, please, you're scaring me!" she whispered. She sighed. A small tear dripped out of her eye. "If anything happens to you, or X, or Buck or Misnon," she said, tears actually flowing from her eyes, "I couldn't live." She set Briana down on a horse, beautiful and white, and a stallion. She whispered softly to the stallion and made a nickering sound. "Asrin (Az-rin), walk around gently with Briana, alright, boy?" she said. "Walk," she said. He started softly walked around. "Misnon," she called softly, "Come on out." Misnon stepped out from behind a rock. "You seriously never moved from there?" Roseann asked. He laughed. "Sorry, I thought your thingy was still going on." Roseann smiled. "Alright. Make sure Briana doesn't fall off the horse, okay?" Misnon nodded, and ran to the stallion as he stepped gently over a fallen tree.

"Oh, great, what now... a bear?" "Actually, no," Spirit wolf said, smiling at her. A black panther leaped from the woods, growling. "Oh, you've gotta be kidding me," she said. "Black Nights?!" she yelled. "For goodness sake, I thought you were legends!" she nearly screamed, stepping back. "Roseann, why are you so afraid of us?" Spirit Star said. Roseann shook her head, fear in her eyes. "If you were me, wouldn't you be afraid of a talking horse, wolf and panther?!" she yelled. Spirit star laughed. "I can't say I wouldn't." Roseann shook her head again. "Just take me back to X. All of the sudden, these woods are confusing." Spirit laughed. "That is what happens when you see us. You can signal us with this. You will be transported back now." Roseann disappeared, sent hurling towards the camp, though when she landed, unharmed.

"Black Nights?!" she yelled. "For goodness sake, I thought you were legends!" she nearly screamed, stepping back. "Roseann, why are you so afraid of us?" Spirit Star said. Roseann shook her head, fear in her eyes. "If you were me, wouldn't you be afraid of a talking horse, wolf and panther?!" she yelled. Spirit star laughed. "I can't say I wouldn't." Roseann shook her head again. "Just take me back to X. All of the sudden, these woods are confusing." Spirit laughed. "That is what happens when you see us. You can signal us with this. You will be transported back now." Roseann disappeared, sent hurling towards the camp, though when she landed, unharmed.

X noticed what happend, but it didn't really surprise him, it was hard to be surprised after everything that happend today.

Roseann was confident, sure of herself. But she had never seen this figure approaching now, even with whatever tricks Roseann had up her sleeve, X knew they wouldn't stand a chance.

Unless.... No, X didn't dare try it.

"But if you don't, he'll kill you, and your sister." X said to himself.

"Of course, if I use it, I might kill her."

X sighed and thought back, Roseann had proven she could transform, in fact everyone of them could, even X, only his were much more.... Violent than their's. And he only had two other forms, but they would have to do.

X turned to Roseann.

"Sorry,but right now isn't time to be getting mad at each other. In this form, my anger is much harder to control than it was when I was hunting you."

He looked back up at the sky, the glow was even brighter.

"We don't have much time, when he lands those other four will go to meet him, then he will come after us. So we have until then to prepare."
