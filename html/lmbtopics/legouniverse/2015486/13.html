Roseann was lying down on the ground in the middle of a field. Larista was no where in sight. Roseann groaned, rolling to her side slowly. Her eyes were shut and she wasn't visible to anybody. As a matter of fact, anything. About three hours passed until her eyes opened. She pushed her hands behind her and jumped up. Her eyes were black and piercing again. When she looked over, Larista no where, she started to get worried for Larista's sake. She started running faster than the speed of sound (I use that as a figure of speech, obviously) to find Larista. Suddenly, she stopped. "Ro--seann...." called a ghostly voice, "Ro--sann....." Roseann slowly turned around, and saw something she never thought she would see. There was a Maelstrom Horseman right behind her! He must have been following her! "Wait, Roseann, before you do anything hasty, please listen to my pleading," said the Horseman, "I am actually a young boy, about your age, trapped like this. It was a Maelstrom Horseman that infected me. Please help me!" Roseann didn't want to believe the Horseman, but she knew that there WAS something about the voice that sounded that of a boy her age. Soon she knew he was telling the truth. "Okay," she said, trying to sound steady, "I will help you. But, there are rules.
