In the NEW great tree:

An army of maelstrom horsemen advanced from the great tree. However, the great tree was now covered with maelstrom and it was impossible to get up if you were not fully maelstrom. The dark figure stood at the top watching his creations gallop across the land. Every horsemen had a shiny lance which glowed in the dark. If you looked closely at the lances you would see that they were staffs. The horsemen were advancing towards a Nexus Force and ninja army. CLASH!!. The two forces collided. While firing orbs of maelstrom at the enemy and cutting down the Nexus Force troops a force ran round the back. It wasn't until the force attacked from the back that the troops realised that the horsemen had surrounded them. The troops immediatly were cut down and the remains ran wildly about trying to dodge the horsemen.

At that moment a huge yellow ship appeared in the air with an assembly sign on the front of it. Instantly Doc Overbuild fell out of it and landed neatly on the ground. A second later the ship started to fire bullets at the horsemen. To fight back the horsemen raised their staffs and a massive shield appeared over them. The bullets bounced off and smashed into the assembly sign. The ship fell towards the darkness of the planet as quick as a missile. The horsemen charged upon Doc Overbuild and used the energy from the shield to make a massive maelstrom blast that covered Overbuild. The first stage of the plan had begun.

In the dark woods....

Roseann held tight to a horse... a horse? Where on earth did she get the horse? Oh, right, she has about six million of them. "Come on, Star Catcher, show me the meaning of haste!" she yelled. Her horse gained speed. "To the great tree, hurry!" she yelled. They were miles and miles from the great tree.... how would they get there in time? Star Catcher leaped onto a fallen tree, leaped up onto a bigger fallen tree, then leaped all the way onto the top of the tallest trees... Now I know why her name is Star Catcher. Roseann grinned, though she had a scar on her arm. It looked deep, and boy did it hurt, but she couldn't pay attention right now. Star Catcher sped up, faster, faster, faster..... Star Catcher leaped over the gap between trees. She disappeared halfway there.

What happened...

Roseann held tight to her horse. They were looking straight at where they were going.... the great tree! "Please, Star Catcher, run as fast as ever possible!" she yelled over the drowning wind. Star Catcher nodded and ran faster. She leaped right to the great tree. They were falling, falling, falling... They fell into the great tree. "Buck...." she muttered when she saw him. She leaped down. "Buck, what's wrong with you?" she asked, walking towards Buck. Star Catcher whinnied and ran in front of Roseann. "Star! Buck is..." she paused. "Buck..." Star Catcher obediently went behind Roseann, but she glared at Buck and watched Roseann carefully. She reached out her hand to Buck, but was responded with a hit in the face. "Buck," she muttered. She leaped up, grabbed her gun out of the saddle bag. Buck was charging at her. She got it ready to fire, and pointed at Buck. "No!" She yelled. She turned the gun around, pointed it at herself, and handed it to Buck. "I can't kill you. I can't!" she yelled. She closed her eyes, waiting for Buck to shoot her.
