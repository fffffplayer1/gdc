Briana whooped in delight, as the wind whipped her hair straight out behind her. "Come on, Grande!" she shouted, her voice almost lost in the rushing wind. But her mount heard her, and responded with a fresh burst of speed. Briana screamed with delight, her hair mingling with her horse's long mane. After a while, Briana sat back, and Grande slowed to ground-eating trot. The girl reached into her pack and touched a ball of fur. She gently pulled it out and laid it across her shoulders. It stretched and yawned, turning out to be a cat. She turned to see her dog, though some would call him a wolf, coming up behind her. He settled in beside her, his tounge lolling. Briana sighed, content. Suddenly, the ground shook from an underground explosion. Grande shied, and the fur along Wolf's back stood on end. He looked to the east, where Briana knew a cave system lay. "I'm sorry girl, but just one more time." She patted Grande's neck, then squeezed with her legs. Instantly, Grande burst once more into a swift gallop. They reached the cave system in several minutes, and Briana dismounted in one smooth motion. She made a quiet nickering noise to Grande, who nickered back and settled down to graze, then beckoned to Wolf. He stalked after her as she carefully stepped into the cave. Voices echoed up to her, and she soundlessly walked towards them. She rounded a corner, but stepped back quickly to remain in the shadows. A group of people were in front of her, and two seemed to be injured. A smoke cloud obscured her view slightly, but she could see a girl and three men. The girl and one of the men lay on the ground, and one stood slightly apart. She didn't recognize any of them, until the one standing by the man on the floor removed his helm and turned slightly. She tensed, remebering that night so long ago, when she had seen this very man, or machine, more accurately, outside her burning home, the very blaze that had orphaned her, and pulled a small pistol out of her pocket. She cocked it, then froze as the click echoed through the cave.
