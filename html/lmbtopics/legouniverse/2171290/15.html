Chapter twenty, Vacation Time part 1 out of 2.

Deep in a basement an unfortunate minifigure had been captured.

Black Mask Figure in a cold voice, "Tell me where it is."

Man tied to a bench, "I told you I DON'T KNOW!"

Black Mask Figure lowers a circular object that resembles as cookie towards the man. Except this was no ordinary cookie...

Man, "NO! NO! PLEASE BELIEVE ME!"

Black Mask Figure sticks the object in the mans mouth.

Man, "NOOO! STOP!!! AAAAHHHHH!"

The man slumps back to the table and doesn't move.

Black Mask Figure, "We will continue this conversation tomorrow, but for now, I must cook up a new batch."

The faction leaders were frantic, there had been twenty robberies in just one month. Even worse, it seemed to be the same people involved in all of them. The faction leaders had to clear there heads, and then Storm had an idea.

Storm, "This is just too stressful with all these robberies and such, I say we blow this joint and go on a vacation!"

Vanda, "I could use a rest bit, I agree."

Overbuild, "I could get some research done as well, I think we should. Scientist have proven that vacations make you have a longer life and help you be less stressed out."

Exeter, "I want to live longer, LET'S DO IT!"

Storm, "I know just the place."

Vanda, "NO, I will pick the spot."

Storm, "Vanda, I bet you 320,567 coins that you will like this place, and if you don't, you can pick where we go and I will give you that much money."

Vanda, "DEAL!"

The faction leaders arrive at a massive resort on the front of a pearl white beach.

Vanda, "Oh my... This is amazing... How... Did... you... find... SUCH A NICE PLACE Storm?"

Storm, "I have my ways."

*Storm holds out his hand*

Vanda, "Fine, here's the money."

The faction leaders enter the building.

Clerk, "Greetings tourist, what type of room would you all like?"

Storm, "Ocean front, four kings, all the food we can eat, three days."

Clerk, "Lets see, that will cost you 320,000 coins, but with tax it will be 320,567 coins."

Storm, "Here you go."

Vanda shakes her head, "How did I fall for that, HOW!"

Exeter, "Fall for what?"

Overbuild, "Well, it was what could be called a "Sneaky" trick. I believe that the definition of sneak-."

Vanda, "Do you want me to duck tape your mouth shut?"

Overbuild, "I would prefer it to be left opened."

Vanda, "Well then I suggest you shut down your vocal output, or as normal people say, SHUT UP!"

Overbuild, "Very well."

The faction leaders reach their room.

Vanda, "Very nice, a common room and four SEPERATE rooms so I don't have to hear you snore all night."

Exeter, "And you won't keep we awake all night with your lamp on."

Overbuild, "Look at my new invention, the sound machine 700."

Exeter, "Ooh, it has all variety of sounds, COOL!"

Overbuild, "Be careful with that though."

*Exeter turns it to a random setting*

Overbuild, "NO! That is the tone that glass bre-!"

*The glass windows and door explode*

Vanda glares at Exeter, "You will be paying for that."

Exeter glares at Overbuild, "You will be paying for that."

Overbuild glares at Storm, "You will be paying for that."

Storm opens the door and glares at a random janitor, "You will be paying for that."

Janitor, "Huh?"

*The door is slammed in his face*

Janitor, "Some people. Why the nerve!"

Storm, "That was a good way for none of use to have to pay for something."

Exeter, "I agree."

The next morning the faction leaders wake up and decide to have breakfast in their room.

Waiter, "Yes, this is room service?"

Vanda, "We all want breakfast, I would like some coffee and a your fruit platter with a biscuit."

Exeter, "I would like a-."

Vanda, "Just bring up everything on this side of the menu, trust me, it will save a lot of time."

Exeter, "How did you know?"

Vanda, "I have my ways."

Storm, "I want an omelet."

Overbuild, "I would something that is your cooks newest recipe."

Waiter looking apprehensive, "Are you sure you want his NEWEST recipe?"

Overbuild, "Yes please."

Waiter, "Okay, I will be back shortly."

Storm, "We can go down to the beach, but lets watch some TV first."

Vanda, "While, since we are waiting for our food we might as well."

*The TV is turned on*

Announcer, "Welcome to the GOLDEN BRICK!"

Audience, "WOO YEAH!!"

Announcer, "As all of you probably know if you watch our show regularly, the winner of Golden Brick wins A PURE GOLD BRICK!"

The audience cheers and sounds excited.

Announcer, "We have a VERY special guest today, the Baron himself."

The audience gasp.

Baron, "Thank you, I am here for one reason only, TO WIN!"

Audience, "BOO!"

Baron, "SHUT UP YOU FOOLS, DO YOU WANT ME TO STRIKE YOU ALL DOWN WHERE YOU STAND!"

Announcer whispers in the Barons ear, "You can't threaten the audience, it's not allowed."

Baron, "Fine."

Announcer, "And our other contestants are Amigo, Patrick, and Madia! Now they will each tell us something about each other before we start."

Amigo, "I am just a poor motel owner trying to make a humble living, I need the money for my family and motel."

Announcer, "Playing on your feelings folks, be careful, those type are always the sneakiest."

Baron, "I am trying to take over the universe and enslave all who appose me by turning them into the maelstrom."

Announcer, "That was blunt."

Patrick, "I am a ninja, I am here to show Ninja's are better than pirates."

Announcer, "Be careful folks, ninja's are dangerous."

Madia, "I'm a gangster trying to get more gold to my collection. Do any of you have problem with that?"

Random Guy, "Your just a greedy old lady."

Madia, "Unless you want to find yourself in the newspaper, I wouldn't say that to me."

Random Guy, "Why would I be in the newspaper?"

Madia, "Ever heard of the obituary section?"

The Random Guy quickly leaves and no one else says anything.

Announcer, "Well th-."

Madia, "Shut up fool, get on with the program, I don't need or care to know what you think about my speech."

Announcer, "Well anyway folks, our first test will be a test of intelligence."

A curtain rises revealing four types of food; Cheesecake, Steak, Carrot, and a Milkshake.

Announcer, "You must say which you think has the most calories."

Baron, "Why the Steak of course!"

Amigo, "Everyone knows carrots fill you up, so naturally the choice is a carrot."

Madia, "Milkshakes are the king of calories."

Patrick, "Ha, that is easy, Cheesecake is by far the most in calories."

Announcer, "And... PATRICK IS CORRECT!"

Baron, "Grrr."

Madia, "CHCH."

Baron, "AAAHHH SAVE ME FROM THE MANIAC!"

*He hides under a table*

Madia, "Heh, I made that sound with my own voice, I don't have a shotgun with me, I just was playing on your nerves."

Baron tries to compose himself, "I am of course am not afraid of you."

Madia, "Sure..."

Announcer, "The next is a competition of strength."

*A giant weight is brought out*

Announcer, "Whoever can hold it for the longest time will win."

Baron, "UUGGGHHH!"

*He lifts it for one second and drops it*

Amigo, "I think I will pass, I don't want to hurt my back."

Patrick, "HIYA!"

*He lifts it holding it above his head*

Announcer, "Can he win once more?"

*Drops it after ten seconds*

*Madia drinks a whole energy drink in one gulp*

Madia, "AARRGGG!"

*She picks it up*

Announcer, "Can she beat the time of ten seconds folks?"

Madia, "This is easy, why I use these when I'm watching TV and want to exercise."

*She starts juggling it*

Announcer, "Wow ladies and gentleman, this shows you can still be in great shape when you are elderly."

Curtain close.

Announcer, "You can view our next episode tomorrow morning, until then, have a happy day!"

Storm, "AWW, I wanted to see what happened."

Vanda, "That was creepy, we know everyone on that show except one person."

Exeter, "Well, I'm going down to the beach."

Overbuild, "I will come as well, that was a good breakfast."


Thanks for your comments everyone, they make me keep writing. I will post the ending of this in the next chapter.
~Watchful Eye of the Swords of Light
