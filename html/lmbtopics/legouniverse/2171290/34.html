Please let this through mods, thank you.

Bob VS Exeter AKA TheDuke

Announcer, "Folks, we are here because there has been a disagreement between Idiot Number four, also known as Exeter, and Bob, also know as Bobert. They are going to have a fight until they are tired COUGH COUGH, I mean, until they smash."

Exeter, "BOB, YOU ATE MY DOUGHNUT!"

Bob, "AND YOU WOULDN'T LET ME GREET NEW COMERS ANYMORE!"

Exeter, "Well why would you care, you could get the amount of money we paid you by working at a pizza parlor! We only gave you slightly above minimum wage!"

Storm from the stands, "We paid him SLIGHTLY ABOVE minimum wage!?"

Vanda from the stands, "Shut up Storm, and stop picking up the popcorn on the stands!"

Storm from the stands, "I almost have enough to fill up a whole bucket, I will sell it for half price since it's "used" popcorn."

Vanda, "That is nasty."

Storm from the stands, "Why are you on the ground?"

Vanda from the stands, "What do you mean? I am on the stands."

Storm from the stands, "WOW, you teleported up here, that was creepy..."

Announcer wearily, "Now back to the program. What are your weapons of choice Bob and Exeter?"

Bob, "A basic sword!"

Exeter, "MY SWORD!"

Announcer, "Let the round BEGIN!"

Bob, "YOU WILL REGRET THE DAY YOU BATTLED ME EXETER!"

Exeter, "Ha, you have a BASIC sword."

Bob, "So what?"

*Bob smacks Exeter with his sword*

Exeter, "Why is the numbers 1+1+1 appearing over my head? Hmm... WHO CARES, ARG!"

*Exeter smacks Bob with his sword*

Bob, "HA, a zero is coming over MY head."

Exeter, "I'm bored with my sword, time to use its special power..."

*He hits a button on it*

*A giant beam of imagination comes out of the sky blowing Bob to pieces a leaving a giant crater*

Exeter, "Ooh, 500,000,000,000 is appearing over where his head used to be... hehe..."

Announcer, "Uh... Looks like Exeter won..."

Bobs mother, "That was cheating, poor Bobert didn't have a chance against a laser... BOOHOO!"

She begins to cry.

Vanda, "Poor women, I feel so badly for her..."

Storm, "Well, another one off my insurance policy list... hehe."

Vanda, "That's a terrible thing to say!"

Storm, "I know, but I just heard my monies clatter... that makes me feel better about Bob being smashed."

Vanda, "I bet no one will go to your funeral when you get smashed."

Storm, "I don't care, all my money will be going to the grave with me, solid gold coffin, inlaid jewels!"

Vanda, "Yeah, and your tomb will get robbed."

Storm, "It will also have a twenty million coin security system in it."

Vanda, "Never mind."

Exeter, "I won, I won, I WON!!!"

Bobs mother, "YOU TERRIBLE PERSON!"

*She kicks him in the knee and runs off crying*

Exeter, "Boy, what's up with her."

Vanda, "You smashed her only son, that's what!"

Exeter, "Come on, he will just rebuild."

Storm, "She is probably crying thinking of all the money he lost when he smashed."

Vanda, "She isn't thinking of that at a time like this!"

*Bob rebuilds*

Bobs mother, "THINK OF ALL THE MONEY YOU LOST, SHAME ON YOU!

Vanda, "Or not..."

Storm, "To celebrate, I will take us all out for dinner!"

Exeter, "That's a great idea!"

Overbuild, "Sorry I'm late, did you win?"

Exeter, "Yup, now we are going out for dinner."

*They arrive at a restaurant*

Old Hag, "What can I serve ya?"

Vanda, "Uh... What do you have?"

Old Hag, "Chicken raw, chicken fried, chicken baked, chicken grilled, chicken baked and then grilled, chicken raw and then baked, chicken baked and then raw..."

Vanda, "Uh, how do you have chicken baked and then raw?"

Old Hag, "Do you really want to know?"

Vanda, "Not really."

Exeter, "Mmmm, give me twenty baked chickens!"

Vanda, "I want a cup of water."

Storm, "Giveme a chicken grilled."

Overbuild, "How many calories do these have in them?"

Old Hag, "What are calories."

Overbuild, "They are the-"

Vanda, "He will take a chicken baked."

*The hag arrives with their food*

Exeter, "YUM!"

Vanda looks at the twisted lumps that are supposed to be chicken, "I'm glad I got a cup of water."


If you didn't notice, this is the normal faction leaders, not the evil ones.

~Icaroth
