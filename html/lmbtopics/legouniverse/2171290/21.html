Chapter twenty-two, The Plot

It was a quiet day at the Nexus Tower. The faction leaders were just sitting around doing nothing. Storm was staring at a gold coin (it had already been a hour), Vanda was looking at a book about the Maelstrom, Exeter was eating some doughnuts and watching a movie, and Overbuild was tinkering with something.

Overbuild, "Ah, finally, a normal peaceful day."

Vanda, "We haven't had one of these in I don't know how long!"

Storm, "Money... money... money... money...... mo- *snore*"

Exeter, "I think Storm hypnotized himself with that coin! HAHA!"

Vanda, "SSSHHH, be quiet, this is a peaceful day."

Guard, "Um... There are two people outside who claim to be the parents of some people in the Nexus Force."

Vanda, "Tell them to talk to the clerk."

Guard, "They were very insistent to talk to YOU."

Exeter, "Who cares, NOT ME!"

Vanda, "Well that was obvious."

Exeter, "Uh... What do you mean?"

Vanda, "Your sentence was obvious."

Exeters eyes bulge, "OVERLOAD OF INFO! *His eyes glaze over and a piece of drool comes out of his mouth* Doughnuts, doughnuts, overload, doughnuts."

*Vanda slaps him*

Exeter shakes his head, "What happened?"

Vanda sighs, "You blacked out because your brain can't handle information."

Exeter, "Oh.

The Guard clears his throat.

Vanda, "FINE, but they better not take long."

Mother, "Hello, we are here because we were supposed to get a paycheck for our children since they are enrolled in the Nexus Force."

Storm wakes up suddenly, "WE WON'T GIVE YOU A SINGLE PENNY YOU THIEVES!"

Mother, "WHY I NEVER!"

Vanda, "What is wrong with you Storm?"

Exeter, "See, I'm not the only one Storm."

Overbuild, "SILENCE!"

Storm, "Nothings wrong with me Vanda, but I can't say the same for you..."

Overbuild, "WHY AREN'T YOU LISTENING TO ME! I SHOULD LOOK EXTREMELY SCARY AT THIS MOMENT!!!"

Exeter starts laughing, "You look like a clown with anger management problems! Haha!"

Mother, "I am not standing here to be ignored, I want the money we should be getting."

Vanda, "What are your childrens names?"

Mother, "Joe and Bob."

Storm, "Your childrens names are very... generic..."

Mother, "Are you implying that I DON'T HAVE CHILDREN!"

Father, "WHY HOW DARE YOU. We have their birth certificates."

Storm, "This is a real birth certificate... hmm... BUT, this picture of your "children" is the same picture that came in MY WALLET WHEN I BOUGHT IT!"

Mother, "Why... They are wallet models, that is why. They are the top wallet models, very famous."

Storm, "Well then why does this other wallet with them on it show a picture of them in a COFFIN?! THEY ARE DEAD!"

Mother, "UUUURRRGGG, FINE! I don't have children, but I NEED money!"

*the father throws a table at Storm*

Storm, "What tis wrong with you people!"

Vanda, "Security."

Oath in an extremely deep voice, "What can I do master?"

Vanda, "Lock these two up for the night."

Oath, "Yes master."

Father, "AAAHHH, A MONSTER!"

Mother, "I'm outta here!"

*They run out of the room with the Oath chasing them*

Vanda locks the door.

Vanda, "NO ONE WILL BE BOTHERING US NOW!"

The faction leaders relax again.

Overbuild, "What do you think about that story Miss Murphy told us?"

Vanda, "I don't know, we should research it."

Exeter, "I hope it isn't true."

Vanda, "Why?"

Exeter, "Because if it was, we would have to go looking for this really powerful person."

Storm, "I will research it some."

The faction leaders start researching to see if the story could be true. But far away, powerful forces are at work.

Madia, "Master, the plan is almost ready to be initiated."

Cloaked figure nods.

Yea Old Hippie, "I helped as well. All we need is the last ingredient and all will be well."

Cloaked figure nods again.

Madia, "I promise, we WILL get what you need."

Cloaked figure, "Soon you shall witness my true form. Get the ingredient, GO!"

*The figure vanishes in a swirl*

Madia, "Come, we don't have much time."

Her and the Hippie leave the cave they are in.



Sorry for it being a bit late, hope you liked it!
