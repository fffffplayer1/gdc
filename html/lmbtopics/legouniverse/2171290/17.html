This next chapter is the idea of fastracer. I will continue part two of the other chapter, but wanted to do this one.



Chapter Special edition, The Double room.



The faction leaders had decided they would visit Amigo's motel once more, especially since they had liked their stay there last time with the slight exception of the Baron attempting to assassinate them that is.



Storm, "Here we are."



Exeter, "Wow, there is a lot of cars in the parking lot."



Overbuild, "36 to be exact."



Vanda, "Well, what are we waiting for, let's get a room."



Amigo, "Welcome to Amigo's Mot- Its YOU PEOPLE!"



Storm, "Yep, we are back."



Amigo, "You almost destroyed my motel last time!"



*Overbuild hands him a check*



Amigo, "Oh, this covers all the damages... Why thank you amigos, I will be happy to have you again."



Vanda, "So what type of room can you give us?"



Amigo looks at his computer.



Amigo, "Well, I'm afraid we only have one left... And..."



Storm, "Well if you have one left, let us have it!"



Amigo, "Well, I will warn you that this room in connected to another with other people in it by a door that doesn't have a lock on it."



Exeter, “A door that doesn't have a lock? Oooh.”



Vanda, "That's fine, I'm sure the people will be considerate as we will be to them."



Exeter, “Don't expect me to not turn the Brick Rap down just because of some people who are near us.”



Vanda, “Oh lord, not that again. Oh, thank goodness, I burned your CD before we left so we don't have

to listen to it.”



Exeter, “I have fifty back up copies I packed. A true Brick Rap fan comes prepared for the haters!”



Vanda shakes her head in disgust, “I should have know.”



Amigo, "Very well, if you want to. Here is the key to your room."



The faction leaders set off towards their room.



Storm, "Well, what are we going to do?"



Vanda, "Let's relax."



Exeter, "Is there a doughnut shop nearby?"



Overbuild takes out a small computer, "According to this there is one 2 miles down the road."



Vanda, "Why did you tell him Overbuild? Now he will be begging to go there for the rest of the trip."



Exeter, "THAT'S NOT TRUE!"



Vanda, "Yes it is."



Exeter, "Can we go to the doughnut shop, I'm tired of telling Vanda I'm right."



Vanda, "You just proved I was right Exeter, your so pathetic."



Storm, "I hear the people in the room beside, listen."



Francis, "Herald, I'm so glad we can have a peaceful vacation for once. No phone calls, no work, and none of those mental people. This will be heavenly!"



Herald, "Wha-? Oh, yes, sure dear."



Francis, "Did you have your hearing aid turned off when I was talking to you Herald?"



Herald, "Oh, of course not honey."



Francis, "Well, you know the true reason we are here, we must go see Madia, I have the package."



Herald, "I am setting up a system that will alert us if there are any intruders in our room listening."



Francis, "Excellent!"



Back in their own room the faction leaders were shocked.



Storm, "It's those oldies AGAIN!"



Exeter, "I think they are stalkers!"



Vanda, "Exeter, I can guarantee you NO ONE would ever stalk you."



Exeter, "Really? I feel much better now."



Overbuild, "I have a special invention to listen in to their conversations where ever they are!"



Storm, "Like in the movies?"



Overbuild, "Exactly!"



Vanda, "How will we put these trackers on them without them knowing?"



Overbuild, "Simple, tonight we sneak in and place the bugs on them while they are sleeping."



Vanda, "You know we could get arrested for this?"



Exeter, "Come on Vanda, we ARE the law."



Storm, "Still, we could get arrested. You should have listened to me when I suggested we become despots."



Vanda, "I'm not a DICTATOR and don't want to become one!"



Exeter, "Could have fooled me."



Vanda glares at him.



*Six hours later when Francis and Herald are sleeping*



Storm, "It's now or never."



The faction leaders slowly open the door.



They see both Herald and Francis laying in bed snoring.



Storm, "Now just one step forwa-."



*He trips on a carefully placed trip wire*



*A net comes down capturing him*



Vanda, "Wha-!"



*The bed behind Vanda flies forward blasting her into a chair in which duck tape is fired tying her to the chair*



Exeter, "DOUGHNUTS!"



*He eats a doughnut*



Exeter, "I feel funny."



*He falls over*



Overbuild, "You won't get ME!"



*The door closes shut extremely quickly knocking Overbuild out cold*



Herald, "SNORE, me like money, YAWN."



Francis, "Wonder if we caught any intruders honey? We will check tomorrow."



In the morning Herald and Francis wake up to find themselves tied to their beds.



Francis, "HOW DID YOU ESCAPE OUR TRAPS!"



*Overbuild shows them his robotic arms*



Storm, "HA, we have caught you!"



Exeter, "YOU STOLE THE PAINTING!"



Overbuild, "I have called the sheriff, he will put you in jail."



Vanda, "So what do you have to say in your defense?"



Francis, "YOU IDIOTS! WE HAVE A PAINTING WE BOUGHT AT THE STATE FAIR IN THAT BAG!"



Herald, "DO YOU WANT ME TO SUE YOU? I am the CEO of Brickchairs, the finest wheelchairs on the market. Trust me, you don't want to make me your enemy."



Storm, "What are you going to do Herald, bribe the judge with a wheelchair?"



Herald, "You have no idea what you are saying. My wheelchairs are like the... the... limos of wheelchairs! I wouldn't be surprised if I bribed the judge with one and he TOOK it."



Vanda, "Here is the painting to show he is lying and who is telling the truth."



*Vanda pulls a painting out of a bag that shows a cow on it*



Vanda, "Uh..."



Storm, "HA, IT'S THE.... That isn't the one at the museum is it?"



Exeter, "Why does it have a cow on it?"



Herald, "Because OBVIOUSLY the painter liked cows."



Exeter, "Do you like cows?"



Herald, "NO, I HATE COWS!"



Vanda, "Hmm, then why did you buy one unless..."



*She pulls the painting out of the frame and flips it over revealing the priceless painting from the museum*



Exeter, "I KNEW IT!"



Overbuild, "Your going to be staying in jail for a long time."



CHCH, "I don't think so, STAND ASIDE!"



Storm stares in horror as the minifigure walks into the room.










ROFLCOPTER! This story is amazing. The Leaders and Baron always fought, and the Leaders fought each other, but I love how the oldies aren't just oldies anymore. They have purposes in this Universe. STEALING PANTINGS THAT ARE JUST A CANVAS WITH A GREY LINE!!!!

Chapter Special edition, The Double room part two


The faction leaders stared in horror as the figure moved out of the shadows.

Amigo, "Thank you for moving out of my way so everyone can see my maraca band. AND 1 AND 2 AND A 3!"

*Mexican music starts playing*

*The band starts singing and shaking their maracas*

Storm, "What a relief, I thought it was Madia."

Herald, "AMIGO, HELP! UNTIE US!"

Amigo, "When I am done with my song I will."

Storm, "STOP NOW!"

Amigo, "Uh, why?"

Storm, "Do we have to pay you or give you a tip for this?"

Amigo, "You don't HAVE to, but we would ap-."

Storm, "Okay, you can continue."

Herald, "UNTIE ME NOW!"

Francis, "AND ME TOO!"

Madia, "All in good time my friends. CHCH!"

Vanda, "It really is Madia, GET HER!"

*Madia throws a smoke grenade*

Exeter, "COUGH, COUGH, this is nasty!"

*The smoke clears showing that Francis and Herald are gone*

*Amigo's band continues*

Vanda, "CALL THE COPS AMIGO!"

Amigo, "Let me finish my song first."

Overbuild, "We did put the tracking bugs on them, they are in that car over there. We should be able to catch them in a car chase!"

Vanda, "Who can drive that fast though?"

Storm grins at them all.

Vanda, "I told you this was a ba-!"

*Storm floors it and Vanda flies into the front windshield*

Vanda, "OW! When we are done you will pa-."

*Vanda flies into the side of the car as Storm takes a turn at full speed*

Storm, "Were you saying something Vanda? Harhar, I LOVE DRIVING LIKE THIS!"

The faction leaders car was a old beat up pickup truck, while Madia was in a Lincon Town car.

Exeter, "We're gaining on them!"

Madia, "Curses, they are gaining on us!"

Herald, "I have a few tricks up my sleeve, let the handicap ramp down!"

Madia, "That would be suicide, were going 80 miles per hour!"

Herald, "Ha, my wheelchairs don't top until 220. I told you their amazing!"

Madia, "Fine."

Francis, "Be safe honey, drive carful." (yes I spelled it this way on purpose)

Herald flies off the ramp but only falls back slightly, his wheelchair wheels a blur.

Overbuild, "Herald is on his wheelchair and is falling back to attack us! Evasive maneuvers!"

Herald falls even with the faction leaders car.

*He takes out a pistol and shoots at their tires with it*

Storm, "YOU HAVE DONE IT NOW!"

*Puts the Brickrap in the CD player on max volume*

CD Player, "THIS IS THE BRICK RAP... BOOM *vibration* BOOM *vibration* TIS IS DA BRICK RAP... BOOM *vibration* BOOM *vibration*!"

Vanda, "That is terrible music."

Herald, "AAHH, MY HEARING AID!"

*He flies off the road into a nearby ditch*

Exeter, "That isn't good for his health is it?"

Vanda, "What do you think?"

Exeter, "Well, when I was younger, I jumped into ditches for fun. So I don't know."

Vanda, "Finally, that explains what's wrong with you."

Storm, "We are right behind Madias car!"

Madia, "Time to end this once and for all!"

*She fires her shotgun at the hood of the faction leaders car*

*The engine explodes*

Storm, "OH NO! WE ARE LOSING SPEED FAST!"

Vanda, "Do something!"

Storm, "Well, we are desperate soo..."

*A grappling hook is fired attaching to Madias trunk*

Madia, "ARG!!"

*There car rams into the back of Madias because she slowed down*

*Loud explosion sound*

The faction leaders get out and do what they do best, fight. (okay, maybe not what they do BEST)

Vanda takes out her katana's and attacks Madia.

Madia, "You are asking for it!"

Exeter, "THE POWER OF BODYMASS!"

Madia, "WHAT THA-!"

*Exeter lands on top of Madia with a thud*

Madia, "I'm going to feel that in the morning... Ow!"

Exeter, "SURRENDER!"

Francis, "GET OFF HER YOU OAT-!"

*Vanda grabs her and Overbuild duck tape her up*

*Five minutes later*

Vanda, "We have you all tied up, no we will be calling the police."

Madia, "The popo can't deal with ME!"

Exeter, "Who are the popo?"

Vanda, "It's what gangsters call the police."

Exeter, "I thought it meant an old person. Hmm, guess I was wrong."

Overbuild, "We should be cautious, they may have a back up plan."

Madia, "You bet I do!"

Storm, "Thanks for telling us you have one."

Madia, "It won't matter, you can't stop it."

Storm, "Wanna bet?"

*A large helicopter approaches out of the clouds*

Vanda, "Don't worry, that's a Nexus Force transport Madia, to take you off to jail!"

Madia, "Wait until you see MY ride."

*They hear a rumbling sound and see a pink bus coming down the road*

Yea Old Hippie, "HIPPY! I'm here Madia, don't worry, I WILL SAVE YOU!"

Madia mutters, "He could have taken the jet instead of the pink bus..."

Exeter, "This is Exeter to helicopter, see that vehicle, shut it down!"

*The helicopter flies towards the bus*

Yea Old Hippie, "HIPPY!!! I HATE TECHNOLOGY! ESPECIALLY THINGS THAT FLY AND ARE MADE OF... METAL!!!!"

Madia, "Good job, you took my advice and didn't get a doctor."

Yea Old Hippie, "THAT'S RIGHT, HIPPY!!!"

Vanda, "Whenever he says the word, HIPPY, it chills me to the bone."

Exeter, "Me too, anyone have a doughnut to make me feel better?"

Storm, "Some money would make me feel better."

Vanda, "Shut it, both of you."

*The pink bus fires a TV off a homemade catapult on the back of the bus*

*CRUNCH*

Vanda, "That went straight through the window and knocked the pilot out cold! He has good aim!"

*The helicopter crashes in a nearby field*

*The pink bus stops right in front of Madia*

Yea Old Hippy, "Stand down, or else, HIPPY!"

Madia, "I would do as he says, you haven't seen him in one of his fits, have you?"

Exeter, "His fits?"

Vanda, "Ignore them, DON'T LET HIM TAKE MADIA FROM US!"

*The Hippie starts playing a flute*

The faction leaders begin to sway to the music and suddenly fall over.

*The Hippie releases Madia and they both get on the bus and start driving away*

Vanda wakes up slightly to see the bus driving away at top speed, "WE WILL GET YOU MADIA, IT ISN'T OVER YET!"

Madia, "YOU'RE RIGHT ABOUT ONE THING, IT'S NOT OVER UNTIL I HAVE MY REVENGE ON YOU!"

Chapter Eighteen, The Baron is Bored


The Baron sat on his throne of depressing darkness looking at the wall.

Baron, "This is very boring."

Stromling, "Yes sir!"

Baron, "You agree?"

Stromling, "Yes sir!"

Baron, "I feel like I'm talking to a robot."

Stromling, "Yes sir!"

Baron, "SAY SOMETHING ELSE!"

Stromling, "Yes Sir!"

Baron, "Strange, you said the same thing, but it sounded different."

Stromling, "Yes s-!"

*The Baron picks up the Stromling and chucks him out the window*

Baron, "Ah, the sweet sound of silence."

*5 hours later*

Baron, "The nice sound of silence."

*7 more hours later*

Baron, "Silence."

*5 more hours later*

Baron, "SOMEONE SAY SOMETHING!"

Advisor, "Uh... I like... .... ...."

Baron, "Your fired!"

Advisor, "But sir, it isn't my thought I can't think of anything to talk about!"

Baron, "Who's is it then? Are you implying that it is MY FAULT!"

Advisor, "Heheh, NO, of course not, your greatness!"

*Bows before the Baron*

*Baron smacks him in the head with his cane*
