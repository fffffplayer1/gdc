Sorry for taking so long to write this chapter. It goes along with the one previous to it. Mods, LET THIS THROUGH, PLEASE!

Chapter twenty-three, The Imagination Nexus

The faction leaders decided to show off the security of the Nexus Tower to boost everyone’s moral. They had a planned event, but little did they know that at that very moment two twisted minifigures would try to get something... at any cost.

Crowd, "Yay, WOOT!"

Vanda, "Welcome to our first event in the Nexus Tower. We will be showing off how powerful our security is today. We want you to ALL know that NO ONE can break in and take anything."

Overbuild, "Let me start. First off, we have a quantum class 6 laser. This can take down a whole Maelstrom ship, of course unless it is made out of zerakium or cloudium. It can sense ships through any type of radar except class 8 stealth technology...."

*2 hours later*

Overbuild, "And this potted plant may look harmless, but it really is a-."

Vanda, "YOUR SPEECH IS OVER!"

Crowd, "Snore, Your over!"

*Cheer goes up*

Exeter, "Well... One of our defenses is called a sword. See, its sharp on this end, but on this end it isn't. We swing it at people and they get hurt. Its made out of something hard... and shiny. And we have something called armor. It protects you from swords... It is also hard and shiny."

Vanda rolls her eyes and shakes her head.

Storm, "Well, I have cameras set up all the place to guard our gold. We also-."

*Storm notices someone taking two breath mints*

Storm, "ONE MINT A PIECE EVERYONE! WE AREN'T A FAST FOOD PLACE!!!"

Guy who took two mints, "What is your problem, come on, I took TWO mints."

Storm, "Lock him away!"

*Guards drag him away*

Storm, "No THAT'S democracy in action."

Crowd, *GASP*

Vanda, "Eh... Ignore what the other three leaders said everyone, and listen to my speech. You see, they have some... shall we say mental problems."

Storm, "I DON'T HAVE MENTAL PROBLEMS!"

Vanda, *throws a penny out the window of the Nexus Tower*

Storm, "MONEY!!!"

*Grabs the emergency parachute and jumps out the window*

Exeter, "What floor are we on?"

Overbuild, "Floor 890."

Exeter, "That's really tall isn't it?"

Overbuild, "Well, it depends if you are looking at it from a universal or world prospective. If you are looking a-."

Vanda, "Time for my speech, so you two can be quiet."

Exeter, *Eats a doughnut and listens*

Vanda, "Well, our best defense is th-."

*The entrance door explodes*

Vanda, "WHAT THA!"

Madia and the Yea Old Hippie walk in.

Overbuild notices that they are staring at the imagination Nexus behind the podium they are standing on.

Madia, "Think you have security fools!? HA, I will show ya some security!"

*She takes out two shotguns and blast two windows out*

Crowd, "AAAHHHHH, RUN FOR THE DOOR!"

*The crowd leaves leaving just the faction leaders and security to guard the Imagination Nexus*

*The security guards pull out machine guns and start firing them at Madia and the Hippie*

*Madia and the Hippie dive under a table avoiding the bullets*

Vanda, "What is it with you two people!?"

Madia, "We need something, BADLY!"

*Madia fires both her shotguns taking out two security guards*

*The Hippie does a black-flip over the table and punches a guard in the face*

*Vanda slices both of Madias' shotguns in half with her katana*

Madia, "You have the nerve to do that to my shotguns!"

Vanda, "I can and I will!"

*Madia takes out a dagger and starts fighting Vanda*

Meanwhile Overbuild and Exeter are in fierce combat with the Hippie.

Hippie, "YOU WILL NEVER WIN!!! HIPPIE!!!"

*Exeter jumps on the Hippie pinning him to the ground*

*The Hippie bites Exeter*

Exeter, "AAAHHH, I WAS BIT BY A HIPPIE!!! HE IS PROBABLY RABID!!!"

*Hippie sees Overbuilds robotic arms*

Hippie, "TECHNO HATRED!!!"

*Overbuild blast the Hippie against the wall*

Madia defeats Vanda and Madia and Overbuild face off.

Overbuild, "I won't let you take the imagination Nexus Madia!"

Madia laughs, "Looks like I will just have to force you to let me take it ya fool."

*The building shutters and the wall explodes revealing the Baron*

Baron, "Oh, looks like most of my work is done for me today."

Madia, "YOU!."

Baron, "You no longer scare me you old crow."

Madia, "URG!!"

*She charges at him*

*The Baron raises his hand she is blasted backwards and is knocked out*

Overbuild, "You don't have to do this Baron, please..."

Baron, "Oh, I have been waiting for this moment for a LONG time."

*Overbuild flies against the wall*

Baron, "Haha."

*He raises his staff to break the imagination Nexus*

Storm, "NOOOO!!!!!!!"

*Storm tackles the Baron*

Baron, "Wha tha-!"

*Storm brings a nearby piece of metal down on the Barons head*

Baron, "AURGH!"

*Before the Baron can blast Storm off Storm brings a chair leg down again and again on the Baron until he teleports away*

Baron, "YOU WON'T WIN NEXT TIME STORM!"

*He vanishes*

Storm, "Hehe, I'm pretty good at close combat."

*Madia wakes up and runs out of the room with the Yea Old Hippie*

Vanda, "Oh no! Did the Baron get the imagination Nexus Storm?"

Storm, "Nope, I stopped him."

Vanda, "Your a hero! You saved the imagination Nexus!"

Overbuild, "I am surprised you did, you never acted like you cared about it that much before."

Storm, "Oh, you thought I did it to save the imagination Nexus?"

Exeter, "Of course that is why you did it! Why else would you have done it?"

Storm, "I did it because the Baron was going to break the imaginations encasing, glass is really expensive these days. It would have cost a fortune to buy glass to build a new Nexus."


Meanwhile Madia and the Hippie report their findings back to their master.

Madia, "We were unable to be successful master, the Baron messed us up, otherwise it would have been successful."

Hooded Figure, "I am not pleased. You have one more chance to prove yourselves to me. I have sensed pure imagination in caves deep under the Ebubo Mountains. Find the source and bring it back to me!"

Madia, "Yes master, we won't fail you again!"

Hooded Figure, “You better not...”

*Hooded figure vanishes in a swirl of his cloak


So did you like it. Did you see how I tied that in with your chapter idea Void?
