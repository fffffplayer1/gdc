Chapter Seven, The Laboratory

The faction leaders went into Madia's house and sat down, Madia to Vanda's disgust and Exeter's delight put down a plate of cookies on the table.

Madia, "What do you need Storm, need some help, or are trying to find something?"

Storm, "Well, we need to stop the Baron, he's the head of the Maelstrom."

Madia, "Well of course you want to stop him! Who would let that maniac continually be on the loose is beyond me. I mean he steals, kills, has a giant army of maniacs, and more."

Storm, "That sounded like you just described yourself."

Madia, "I'm not like that! Sure maybe I'm a little... rough sometimes but not like him."

Storm, "Well never mind about that, we need information on where the Baron is located at."

Madia, "You think I would know that? I have heard there is a spy organization down the road one hundred miles. They claim to be a dry cleaning company, but their not."

*Exeter takes a cookie and eats it*

*CRACK*

Exeter, "AHHH, MY TOTH OH TIS URTS."

Vanda, "I told you not to eat any."

Exeter, "Haha, I'm was just joking, it didn't hurt my tooth at all, it may have cracked it but it didn't hurt it, its actually really good."

Vanda, "How could have I fallen for that?"

Exeter, "Maybe your getting slower like me."

Vanda, "Lets not think about that horrible possibility."

Overbuild, "Back on topic, thank you for your info Madia, we will go to the spy organization place as soon as possible."

Madia, "Glad to have helped."

The faction leaders left the house and went back to the motel.

Exeter, "Are we going to have to go undercover?"

Vanda, "Well, unless you plan on charging in there and defeating everyone in there, yes."

"We will have to get some disguises and old clothes," Vanda said.

Exeter, "Something doesn't sound right about that sentence."

Vanda, "We will have to get some disguises and old clothes."

Exeter, "That sounds much better for some reason."

Storm, "AHH, those cheap people, they haven't replaced the soap, breath mints, or toilet paper yet!"

Overbuild, "You are taking the TOILET PAPER!?"

Storm, "Oh course, paper products are in high demand."

The faction leaders put on disguises that make them look like local's. They then take a taxi to the laundry mat.

Overbuild, "We are here."

They walked in slowly.

Manager, "Hello, may I help you?"

Exeter, "Yes, is there a secret spy organization run in the back room that you are aware of?"

Manager, "WHAT!?"

Vanda, "You idiot Exeter, it was supposed to be undercover, now we have to knock this guy out."

Storm, "I will," he jumped over the counter punching the guy in the face knocking him out cold."

He also took all the money out of the cash register, and the mans pockets when Vanda wasn't looking.

The four faction leaders walked into the back room.

Exeter, "I'm scared, I'm scared, I'm scared, I'm scared, I'm sca-."

Vanda, "WHAT IS WRONG WITH YOU EXETER, you are suppose to be the brave one here, now snap out of it!"

Exeter, "Okay, but let me do my motivational tapes, I can do it, I can do it, I can do it, I can do it. I CAN DO IT!"

Vanda, "Are you quite finished now?"

Exeter, "YEAH!"

Vanda, "Good, now we need to go into that room right in front of us."

Overbuild, "I agree, its made out of metal, and a laundry mat wouldn't have anything it would need to protect."

Stromling general, "Don't move unless you want to be covered in bullets."

The faction leaders turned around slowly to see a stromling with a machine gun flanked by around ten others."

Vanda, "How could we be trapped so easily?"

Stromling general, "I will be second in command once the Baron see's I have captured all of you, HAHAHA!"

Vanda then had an idea, it was their only hope.

*She tosses a doughnut at the head stromling and a piece of gold at another one*

Stromling general, "Ha, you must have thought this was a grenade when you threw it, because a doughnut and piece of gold won't stop me."

Exeter, "DONUT!"

Storm, "GOLD!"

Stromling general, "What is wrong with those two?"

Exeter, "GIVEME THE DONUT AHHH"

*Exeter and Storm charge at the stromlings so crazily half of them drop their machine guns from shock*

*One minute later*

Exeter, "That was a good doughnut, Mmm."

Storm, "This is a nice piece of gold."

Vanda, "And I had to motivate you to save yourself, wow."

The faction leaders went into the room with the metal door and downloaded all the information onto Overbuild's USB chip. They then quickly left and went back to the hotel.
