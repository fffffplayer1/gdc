Chapter Twelve, The Psychiatrist

The faction leaders wanted to start the competition right away, however, few know that the faction leaders have a psychiatrist because of their extremely stressful jobs. The ones who know think there's more to it than that...

Vanda sounding angry, "Great, I just got off the phone with our psychiatrist, he wants to talk to us before we start the competition.

Storm, "I hate our Psychiatrist, our insurance better be paying the bill!"

Exeter, "YAY, THE PSYCHIATRIST IS COMING THE PYHCATRIST IS COMING!"

Vanda, "Oh lord, not that again."

Overbuild, "I have mixed feelings, on one hand I-."

Vanda, "You can tell your "mixed feelings" to Fred, our psychiatrist."

Overbuild, "Fine Mrs. Grumpy, I will!"

Vanda, "You actually like him Overbuild?"

Overbuild, "Well, he isn't "terrible" but he isn't that great either."

Vanda, "That's an understatement."

*KNOCK, KNOCK*

Vanda, "He's here, here goes a perfectly wonderful day."

Fred walks in smiling, "Well look who it is, my four favorite patients."

Vanda, "For the worlds sake I hope we are your ONLY patients."

Fred, "What was that Vanda."

Vanda, "I said this better be quick."

Fred, "Oh, don't worry, it won't be long."

Exeter, "FRED! OH BOY OH BOY!"

Fred, "Oh, your here as well... I had forgotten about... you..."

Exeter gives Fred a hug.

Fred, "Okay, okay, you don't have to hug me."

Exeter, "But you said on page thirty five of your book it's good to hug people."

Fred, "Oh, did I... Well, that books out of date from this moment forth."

Exeter, "Oh, well your other book sai-."

Fred, "Forget about my books Exeter."

Exeter, "Okay."

Vanda, "Hurry up, we don't have all day, now WHAT do you want to talk to us about before we do this competition."

Fred, "Ah, I almost forgot."

Storm, "How much do you charge per hour?"

Fred, "Uh, 500 coins per hour, why?"

Storm, "No reason." He looks at the clock and mutters something.

Fred, "I am here because I want to discuss how YOU feel about this competition."

Vanda, "I feel like I'm going to win and beat these other three leaders."

Exeter, "NO YOUR NOT!"

Storm, "That's right because I will win!"

Fred, "Calm down everyone."

Vanda, "Why should we?"

Fred, "BECAUSE I SAID SO... Sorry I mean, because auguring is bad for you."

Vanda, "Yeah right."

Fred, "Now, lets just assume the Paradox faction won, how would they be treated from then on?"

Vanda, "They would be honored and well known."

Fred, "But what about others that would be jealous, wouldn't they be teased by the other factions."

Vanda, "Hmm, I never thought of that."

Fred, "What do you think of it Exeter?"

Exeter, "I'm going to win it for the brave and powerful Sentinels."

Fred, "I see what everyone’s going to say about this competition, so let me change the subject."

Storm, "Hurry up, its already been TWO HOURS, that's ONE THOUSAND COINS!"

Fred, "Okay, what do you think of Exeter after our little talk about being nicer to people Vanda?"

Vanda, "I think he is a oaf, a brute, and the least intelligent minifigure this side of the galaxy."

Fred, "It sounds better than what you thought of him last time, KEEP UP THE GOOD WORK."

Overbuild, "What did she say last time?"

Fred, "I don't think you really want to know."

Overbuild, "You are probably right."

Fred, "Storm, lets see how you are doing on your obsession of money, let me give you a sample and see how you do."

Storm, "I'm ready."

Fred, "You find a Diamond Necklace that belongs to the Queen of France, but was lost. Now, what do you do with the Necklace."

Storm, "I would take it back to her house to replace it in her treasury."

Fred, "Good."

Storm, "Wait, I wasn't done."

Fred, "Oh, you were going to do something even nicer, good for you."

Storm, "I would give the guard the piece, but when he put it back in the vault I would be recording the code so I could come back reenter it later taking everything out of the vault. I bet that would give me a lot more money than just keeping the Necklace."

Fred, "Never mind, you haven't made any progress."

Storm, "Good, that's how I like it."

Fred looking tired, "Exeter... How have you been doing on the diet I gave you?"

Vanda, "If you gave him the diet last week th-."

Fred, "Vanda, I will let Exeter answer me."

Vanda, "Fine."

Exeter, "GREAT, I am totally resistant to food temptations now!"

Fred, "Well, to test you I brought some food with me."

*Fred places a carrot on the table*

Exeter, "I'm not even phased."

*Fred places a piece of fresh bread on the table*

Exeter, "I can resist easily."

*Fred places a steak on the table*

Exeter's mouth waters.

*Fred places ice cream on the table*

Exeter, "MMM, I WILL RESIST!"

Fred, "Only one more thing left."

*Fred places one of Uncle Joe's chocolate covered homemade donuts that is soft on the inside, but crispy on the outside with a strawberry on top.*

*Exeter's eyes bulge*

Fred, "Are you hanging in there buddy?"

Exeter, "MUST.... RESIST.... THE.... DOUGHNUT...."

Fred, "You are really improving Exeter."

Vanda winks at the other faction leaders, "You know, they just discontinued that doughnut, so this must be one of the last ones that will EVER be made."

Exeter, "AHH, DOUGHNUT HERE I COME!"

Fred was holding the doughnut as Exeter came bowling into him.

Fred, "AAHHH, HELP ME FROM THIS MANIAC SOMEONE!"

Exeter grabs the doughnut and eats it in one bite.

Exeter, "YOU CALLED ME A MANIAC!"

Fred, "THATS RIGHT, I'M SICK OF BEING NICE TO YOU!"

Vanda, "Fred, you know he has anger management problems right?"

Fred, "No, I didn't."

Exeter, "AAARRGGG!"

*He picks up Fred and throws him out the nearest window*

Exeter, "That felt good."

Storm, "Thank you so much."

Vanda, "I also agree, I have wanted something like that to happen to Fred for a long time."

Overbuild, "That's terrible, we are on the two hundredth floor, he probably didn't survive!"

Storm, "That saddens me greatly."

Overbuild, "Well why are you still SMILING!"

Storm, "We don't have to pay him a thousand dollars now."

The faction leaders decided they would go ahead with the events, even though their psychiatrist didn't agree, or should I say, their late psychiatrist."

Sorry for posting it late at night again. I hope you like it.

Chapter Ten, Brickmart

The faction leaders were currently living in an apartment until the second Nexus Tower was finished. They decided they would buy furniture and other things for their apartment at the Brickmart.

Storm, "Here we are."

Vanda, "I still think we should go to a more "refined" shopping center."

Storm, "NO! Brickmart has the BEST prices and will match the competition, we should shop here."

Overbuild, "Well, it couldn't get less "refined" this."

Exeter, "I don't get whats so bad about it, they even have a BrickWay built in it."

Vanda, "BrickWay? Come on Exeter, I can think of tons of other places I would rather eat at."

Exeter, "I can't think of tons of places I would rather eat at."

Vanda, "That's not new. You can't think of much of anything in the first place."

Exeter, "That's not true Vanda, ask me anything that you don't think I would remember."

Vanda, "Normally I would, but I don't feel like standing out in this parking lot for an hour."

Exeter, "HMTH, you insult me Vanda, I could remember anything in less than thirty minutes now."

Storm, "QUICK, the sale will be over soon, lets start shopping."

Exeter, "Fine."

The faction leaders walked up to the door.

Exeter, "OOH, I love automatic doors, I will see how fast this one is!"

He runs at the door.

*CLUNK*

Vanda, "See that OUT OF ORDER SIGN Exeter? Know what it means?"

Exeter, "Oh, well, in that case lets use the other door."

Overbuild, "Lets."

They finally get inside and notice that the workers have badges with stickers and also have one that says, "The customers always right."

Vanda, "What should we get first?"

Storm, "Candy."

Vanda, "Fine, lets get it over with."

Storm, "I love candy, lets see, I want a Million Brick Bar, Chocolates coins in all colors, pirate shaped chocolates, pirate shaped cookies and that's it."

Vanda, "And ninja shaped ones too."

Exeter, "I want that big bag with 3000 pieces of chocolate in it."

Vanda, "That cost a fortune Exeter!"

Exeter, "No it doesn't, I know I'm right this time because the bag says Economy size, so HA!"

Vanda, "Forget it, take the bag."

Exeter, "For once I'm smarter than Vanda, I must be dreaming."

Vanda, "That's the only place you will be smarter than me in."

Overbuild, "Okay, next is furniture."

Storm, "Ooh, this bed is nice."

Exeter, "I like the giant chair."

Vanda, "I have already bought us furniture."

Storm, "What do you mean?"

Vanda, "I bought it at the Bath and Brick, they have stylish furniture."

Storm, "WHAT, THAT MUST HAVE COST YOU A FORTUNE!"

Vanda, "Who bought the pure gold telescope with diamond lens and jewels all over it?"

Storm, "Uhh... but... that was... okay fine you can have the furniture."

Vanda, "I better be able to."

Overbuild, "Next is computers."

Worker, "Hello, what type of computer are you looking for?"

Overbuild, "Do you have a 12 core here?"

Worker, "Uh... We don't even have a four core..."

Overbuild, "Fine, how about a Brickpod?"

Worker, "Oh, here is one."

Overbuild, "I will take it."

Worker, "Very well, here it is."

Joice, "Herald, lets get a Brickpod for Bennie’s birthday."

Herald, "Okay, I would like one Brickpod please."

Worker, "I'm sorry sir, but we just sold the last one to the man over there with that group of people."

Joice, "We have to get them to give it to us, its poor Bennie’s birthday!"

Herald, "I'm sure they are civilized folk and will give it to us.

Joice, "Wait a second, HERALD, their those wackos who made us leave the restaurant!"

Herald, "Oh my, Joice, its Bennie’s birthday, we can't let him down."

They walk over to the faction leaders.

Joice, "Overbuild, I heard the clerk call you that, can you please give us your Brickpod, our grandson's birthday is coming up and he really wants one."

Overbuild, "Why of course you can."

Exeter, "NO! Overbuild, its those oldies!"

Vanda, "It is, she smacked me with her purse! Don't give it to them Overbuild, they are bad people."

Joice, "We will do whatever we have to do to get, won't we Herald!"

Herald, "That's right honey!"

Joice, "And they also took our CREDIT CARDS REMEMBER!"

Herald, "It was the one that looks like a pirate that did that, LETS GETEM HONEY!"

Vanda, tried to tazer Joice but missed.

Joice, "You won't be getting me young lady! PEPPER SPRAY!"

*Joice sprays pepper spray all over the faction leaders*

Exeter, "AAHH, MY EYEBALLS ARE ON FIRE!"

Vanda, "I AGREE!"

Overbuild, "I love my glasses now."

Storm, "LUCKY YOU, BUT I WISH I HAD SOME TOO!"

Overbuild knocked the pepper spray out of Joices hand.

Herald, "HOW DARE YOU TOUCH MY WIFE YOU ROBO FREAK!"

*Herald hits Overbuild on his mobility scooter*

*Overbuild crashes into the video game display breaking the glass*

Random kid, "FREE VIDEO GAMES EVERYONE!"

A bunch of people start picking up video games.

Exeter, "AARG, I hate both of you!"

*He stabs his sword in the mobility scooter causing it to partly blow up*

Joice, "YOU BROKE THE FIVE THOUSAND DOLLAR MOBILITY SCOOTER!"

Herald takes his cane and unscrews the bottom off it and takes the top off as well.

*Puts a blow dart in it*

*A dart hits Exeter in the neck*

Exeter, "YAWN, hmm, cupcakes, mmm..."

*Exeter falls over and starts snoring*

Herald, grabs Storms wallet and him and Joice storm out the room with the Brickpod in hand.

*Thirty minutes later at the check out*

Cashier, "According to all the damage you caused your bill is thirty thousand dollars."

Storm, "No it isn't."

Cashier, "Sorry sir, it is."

*Points at the Cashier*

Cashier, "What?"

Storm, "Your badge says the CUSTOMER is ALWAYS RIGHT, we owe you fifty dollars."

Cashier, "Uh... I don't think tha-."

Storm, "Do you want me to report you to the better business association?"

Cashier, "Okay fine... you owe fifty dollars."

Exeter, "I'm going to have to do that at Crispy Cream next time I go there."

Storm, "See Vanda, I just saved us thousands of dollars."

Vanda, "For once, I am glad you know how to be cheap."

Storm, “And whats really funny is we bought a thousand dollars worth of items really!”

Vanda, “YOU MEAN YOU CHEATED HIM ON OUR ORIGINAL BILL AS WELL!”

Storm, “Why shouldn't I if I can?”

The faction leaders take their goods back to their apartment.

I hope you liked it.

This is incredible! I would have laughed out loud if I weren't afraid I would wake someone up! Also, I have noticed that in previous chapters, Storm, Exeter, and Typhonus have been really funny, and Vanda fits in well with Exeter's stupidity and Storm's cheapness. However, Overbuild hasn't been quite as funny [but he has been improving in the last couple of chapters]. If you want to keep him how he is, that's fine, but if I may contribute my $0.02, it might be even funnier if you made him have all sorts of useless inventions with really complicated names and such. For an extreme example of this, Google search "Club Penguin Cheese 3000 Comic." He could also be the sort of guy who's an incredible genius, but has very little common sense. For example, he might say something along the lines of "Oh no, the Maelstrom has taken over the universe! What are we going to do?! None of my inventions are going to do any good here, the nuclear-powered toaster, the cheeseburger-flavored lawn mower, the time machine, the automatic carrot peeler..."
