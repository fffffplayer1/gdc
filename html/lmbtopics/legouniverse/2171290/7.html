Chapter Nine, The Rebuilding

The faction leaders were watching the news, and for once it was good.

Announcer, "The Maelstrom has been forced to leave Nexus Square by a variety of attack methods from the local's."

Exeter, "Yay, we can go back now and eat at Uncle Joe's again!"

Vanda, "Is that what your really looking forward to?"

Exeter, "YES, and I'm not going to have them use the low fat whip cream and butter, I'm going to have the real deal!"

Vanda, "Well, I can't wait to get back to studying the Maelstrom, I can't wait to look through the shadow orb."

Overbuild, "It's terrible, have you forgotten that the Imagination Nexus has been lost!"

Storm, "About that..."

Overbuild, "What about it?"

Storm, "Well..."

Overbuild, "Out with it, it's destroyed, nothing can change that."

Storm, "It's not destroyed."

Overbuild, "LIES, I SAW THE MAELSTROM BREAK IT OPEN, IT'S LOST FOREVER DON'T TRY TO GIVE ME HOPE!"

Storm, "I took the real imagination and replaced it with a chemical that looked like imagination."

Overbuild, "YOU DID WHAT!"

Storm, "You heard me."

Overbuild, "I have been studying this for YEARS, and your telling me it wasn't PURE IMAGINATION!?"

Storm, "Yep."

Overbuild, "WHERE IS IT?!"

Storm, "I sold it o-."

Overbuild, "YOU SOLD IT ON EBAY?"

Storm, "No, Amazon, I got ten coins for it."

Overbuild on the verge of tears, "You sold the most valuable thing in the universe for... TEN COINS!"

Storm, "No, I only got nine when tax was taken out."

Overbuild, "AHHHHH!"

*He picks up Storm with his robotic arms and throws him out the window*

*He breaks a table in half and rips apart all the things around him*

Max, "Amigo, you are ripping my motel apart, STOP RIGHT NOW!"

Overbuild, "You mean all this JUNK, get out of my way or I will break YOU!"

Vanda, "Calm down Overbuild."

Exeter, "YOU THREW THE CRISPY CREAM DOUGHNUTS OUT THE WINDOW!"

Overbuild, "THATS RIGHT YOU BIG BABY! Go cry in a corner."

Exeter, "Vanda, he's being mean to mean, make him stop!"

Storm, walks back in, "Your paying the bills for all this broken stuff Overbuild!"

Overbuild, "YOU!"

Storm, "On second thought I should probably leave for a while."

*A TV is hurled directly where Storm's head was seconds before*

Vanda, "Okay, I have had enough of this."

*ZAP*

*Overbuild falls over*

Vanda, "I love tazers."

Exeter, "Let me try!"

*ZAP*

*Vanda falls over*

Exeter, "Yay, all the doughnuts for myself."

*ZAP*

*Exeter falls over*

Storm, "Har har, I get all the money now."

*ZAP*

*Storm falls over*

Max, "Hello, is this the police?"

Police, "Yes, what do you need."

Max, "I have a couple of crazy people who need to have a night in jail to clear their heads."

Police, "Be right over."

The faction leaders woke up to find themselves in a single jail cell.

Sheriff, "You will be staying here tonight."

Overbuild, "No thanks."

*Rips the bars out with his robotic arms*

Sheriff looks scared, "On second though, you can leave town and I will let you go now."

Vanda, “I just be you will.”

The faction leaders head to Nexus Square by rocket.

A bunch of random people, "Hey, their back, THE FACTION LEADERS ARE BACK!"

Exeter, "THATS RIGHT PEOPLE, WE DEFEATED THE BARON FOR A WHILE!"

Vanda, "More like an old lady did."

Overbuild, "We will construct a new Nexus Tower, better than ever."

Storm, "And our loyal citizens will fund it."

Vanda glares at Storm.

Storm, "Of course we will as well."

The construction of the second tower had begun.

This is the end of the mini episode, now I will go back to doing random things, sorry if I moved a bit quickly, but I want to get back to random stuff for a while.
~Watchful Eye of the Swords of Light
