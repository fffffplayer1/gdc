Chapter Eighteen, The Baron is Bored


The Baron sat on his throne of depressing darkness looking at the wall.

Baron, "This is very boring."

Stromling, "Yes sir!"

Baron, "You agree?"

Stromling, "Yes sir!"

Baron, "I feel like I'm talking to a robot."

Stromling, "Yes sir!"

Baron, "SAY SOMETHING ELSE!"

Stromling, "Yes Sir!"

Baron, "Strange, you said the same thing, but it sounded different."

Stromling, "Yes s-!"

*The Baron picks up the Stromling and chucks him out the window*

Baron, "Ah, the sweet sound of silence."

*5 hours later*

Baron, "The nice sound of silence."

*7 more hours later*

Baron, "Silence."

*5 more hours later*

Baron, "SOMEONE SAY SOMETHING!"

Advisor, "Uh... I like... .... ...."

Baron, "Your fired!"

Advisor, "But sir, it isn't my thought I can't think of anything to talk about!"

Baron, "Who's is it then? Are you implying that it is MY FAULT!"

Advisor, "Heheh, NO, of course not, your greatness!"

*Bows before the Baron*

*Baron smacks him in the head with his cane*

Baron, "I'm so bored I'm going to call in a psychiatrist."

Stromling psychiatrist, "What do you want to talk about... Timmy."

Baron, "How did you know people used to call me Timmy?"

Stromling psychiatrist smiles, "I am supposed to know everything about you Timmy."

Baron mutters to himself, “Creepy, I didn't think stalkers were after me.”

Stromling psychiatrist, “What?”

Baron, "What is your name?"

Bob, "My name is Bob."

Baron, "That's a very common name isn't it?"

Bob, "Yes it is Timmy."

Baron, "HOW CAN I NOT BE BORRED!?"

Bob, "Calm down Timmy, take it one step at a time, baby steps."

Baron, "I control the LARGEST ARMY IN THE UNIVERSE AND YOU WANT ME TO TAKE BABY STEPS!?"

Bob, "Deep slow breaths, that's better."

*Baron picks up a chair and throws it at Bob*

*It bounces off an invisible force field*

Baron, "YOU ARE ON THE OTHER SIDE OF A FORCE FIELD!"

Bob, "Heard, you were having emotional difficulty so I have had you confined to your room until your condition improves."

Baron, "Let me our or ELSE!"

Bob, "Sorry, but my duty forbids me to, I will be in charge until you feel better, don't worry."

Baron, "Guard, lock up Bob and let me out, THATS AN ORDER!"

Guard, "Sorry, but he currently outranks you."

*Baron blast the shield with dark energy*

Bob, "Temper, temper."

*The shield breaks*

Bob, "Uh oh."

The Baron advances on him.

Bob, "Uh... hi there... you are perfectly stable right now."

Baron, "You are about to find out how UNSTABLE I CAN BE!"

Bob, "Lower you cane please."

*Baron sets his cane on the ground*

Bob, "That's good, thank you."

*Baron takes out a RPG with a flamethrower attached to the front*

Bob, "NOO-!"

*A pile of ashes is where Bob used to be*

Advisor, "Are you still bored?"

Baron, "Not anymore."

Advisor, "Must have been a really great psychiatrist!"

Baron, "Very entertaining."

Advisor, "Where is he?"

*Baron holds up a jar with ashes in it*

Advisor, "Oh... that kind of entertaining..."

Baron, "Bring in the interior designer this place is too cheery!"

Advisor, "YES SIR!"

*The adviser hurries away*


What did you think of it? Is it funny or lame?

Bob: NOO!
Advisor: Are you still bored?
Baron: Not anymore.

Chapter Nineteen, The House of Art

The faction leaders were wanting to do something different to vary up their day, so they decided to visit an art gallery, it had ancient and modern art.

Exeter, "We are here!"

Storm, "This is such a waste of time and money, why can't we wait until the galleries 50th year party when we get to go in for free?"

Vanda, "Because, this gallery JUST OPENED!"

Overbuild takes out a calculator type device, "According to my wonderful invention, we would have to wait 49 years, 7 months, 8 minutes, and 45 seconds before we could go to the gallery if we listened to Storms plan. In addition, there is only a 23% chance that we would all be alive at that time."

Vanda, "Storm, let me simplify Overbuilds statement for you. We ARE going to go to the gallery NOW."

Storm looking angry, "Fine."

Tour Guide, "Do you need someone to show you around?"

Vanda, "Yes, thank you."

The faction leaders walk towards a large painting that shows a giant tower made out of bricks in detail.

Exeter, "WOW!"

Storm, "Amazing, how much did this sell for?"

Vanda, "This must be made by one of the great artist."

Overbuild, "There is a 45% chance that this is a fake."

*Tour Guide glares at Overbuild*

Overbuild, "I have been known to be wrong."

Tour Guide, "Well Mr. Storm, this was donated to us, but at one point sold at action for..."

Storm's mouth waters slightly.

Tour Guide, "Two hundred and fifty coins."

Storm, "That's all it sold for?"

Tour Guide, "Yes."

The faction leaders and tour guide go to another part of the building that has modern art.

Tour Guide, "Ah this is the most famous painting we have."

The faction leaders stare at a piece of white canvas with a streak of gray on it.

Vanda, "What is it?"

Tour Guide, "Its called the Darkness and Light."

Exeter, "That looks like what I did in the 1st Grade, boy I should have kept that and sold it to you."

Tour Guide, "FIRST GRADE, many have tried, but none can come close to this painters skill in modern painting."

Vanda, "You would have to be pretty terrible to come close to THAT."

Tour Guide, "You people have no appreciation for the arts, especially modern art."

Storm, "How much did this sell for."

Tour Guide, "We got it as a donation, but at one point it sold for around 50 million coins."

Storm, "FIFTY MILLION COINS!"

Tour Guide, "Yes."

Vanda snorts, "The buyer must have been blind then."

Overbuild, "Why would a blind buyer want to buy a painting?"

Vanda, "It's a JOKE!"

Overbuild, "Oh, hahaha, I believe most people laugh three times in a row for a joke they consider moderately funny, is that correct?"

Vanda takes deep breaths.

Suddenly there is a loud explosion and the wall behind and near bye painting explodes. Several figures dressed in black ski mask run in.

Black Ski mask, "Don't move and no one will get hurt."

Exeter, "What happens if we move?"

Black Ski mask, "You get hurt."

Exeter, "Why?"

Black Ski mask, "BECAUSE WE WOULD HURT YOU, YOU DUMBO!"

Exeter, "Why?"

*Vanda smacks Exeter on the head with a near bye vase and he crumples*

Vanda, "Sorry about him, he isn't the brightest grape in the bunch."

Black Ski mask, "Yeah we know."

Overbuild whispers to Storm, "How could they know, they haven't met us before?"

Storm, "I don't know, it's a bit creepy now that you mention it."

Tour Guide, "YOU BROKE AN ANCIENT VASE!"

Vanda, "Want me to hit you with the other vase, if not, SHUT IT!"

Tour Guide looks mad, but doesn't say anything.

Black Ski mask, "All of you, go over there and stand, and don't move."

*Five minutes later*

Vanda, "I think its safe to move now."

Exeter gets up and rubs his head, "Good, because I'm hungry."

Storm, "I wish I had stolen the painting before they did."

Vanda, "That's a terrible way to look at things."

Storm, "It's a realistic way."

Overbuild, "You of all people are FAR from being realistic most of the time."

Exeter, "Shouldn't we alert the security?"

Vanda, "That's the smartest thing I have heard you say all year."

Exeter, "I agree."

Security Guard, "What happened?"

Tour Guide, "People in black ski mask came in and robbed our most expensive paintings."

Security Guard, "What's this on the floor?"

Overbuild picks the object in question up, "It is a cookie."

Vanda, "That doesn't make any sense, why would a robber have a cookie with them?"

Exeter eats it, "This taste familiar, I HAVE HAD THIS COOKIE BEFORE!"

Chapter Nineteen, The House of Art

The faction leaders were wanting to do something different to vary up their day, so they decided to visit an art gallery, it had ancient and modern art.

Exeter, "We are here!"

Storm, "This is such a waste of time and money, why can't we wait until the galleries 50th year party when we get to go in for free?"

Vanda, "Because, this gallery JUST OPENED!"

Overbuild takes out a calculator type device, "According to my wonderful invention, we would have to wait 49 years, 7 months, 8 minutes, and 45 seconds before we could go to the gallery if we listened to Storms plan. In addition, there is only a 23% chance that we would all be alive at that time."

Vanda, "Storm, let me simplify Overbuilds statement for you. We ARE going to go to the gallery NOW."

Storm looking angry, "Fine."

Tour Guide, "Do you need someone to show you around?"

Vanda, "Yes, thank you."

The faction leaders walk towards a large painting that shows a giant tower made out of bricks in detail.

Exeter, "WOW!"

Storm, "Amazing, how much did this sell for?"

Vanda, "This must be made by one of the great artist."

Overbuild, "There is a 45% chance that this is a fake."

*Tour Guide glares at Overbuild*

Overbuild, "I have been known to be wrong."

Tour Guide, "Well Mr. Storm, this was donated to us, but at one point sold at action for..."

Storm's mouth waters slightly.

Tour Guide, "Two hundred and fifty coins."

Storm, "That's all it sold for?"

Tour Guide, "Yes."

The faction leaders and tour guide go to another part of the building that has modern art.

Tour Guide, "Ah this is the most famous painting we have."

The faction leaders stare at a piece of white canvas with a streak of gray on it.

Vanda, "What is it?"

Tour Guide, "Its called the Darkness and Light."

Exeter, "That looks like what I did in the 1st Grade, boy I should have kept that and sold it to you."

Tour Guide, "FIRST GRADE, many have tried, but none can come close to this painters skill in modern painting."

Vanda, "You would have to be pretty terrible to come close to THAT."

Tour Guide, "You people have no appreciation for the arts, especially modern art."

Storm, "How much did this sell for."

Tour Guide, "We got it as a donation, but at one point it sold for around 50 million coins."

Storm, "FIFTY MILLION COINS!"

Tour Guide, "Yes."

Vanda snorts, "The buyer must have been blind then."

Overbuild, "Why would a blind buyer want to buy a painting?"

Vanda, "It's a JOKE!"

Overbuild, "Oh, hahaha, I believe most people laugh three times in a row for a joke they consider moderately funny, is that correct?"

Vanda takes deep breaths.

Suddenly there is a loud explosion and the wall behind and near bye painting explodes. Several figures dressed in black ski mask run in.

Black Ski mask, "Don't move and no one will get hurt."

Exeter, "What happens if we move?"

Black Ski mask, "You get hurt."

Exeter, "Why?"

Black Ski mask, "BECAUSE WE WOULD HURT YOU, YOU DUMBO!"

Exeter, "Why?"

*Vanda smacks Exeter on the head with a near bye vase and he crumples*

Vanda, "Sorry about him, he isn't the brightest grape in the bunch."

Black Ski mask, "Yeah we know."

Overbuild whispers to Storm, "How could they know, they haven't met us before?"

Storm, "I don't know, it's a bit creepy now that you mention it."

Tour Guide, "YOU BROKE AN ANCIENT VASE!"

Vanda, "Want me to hit you with the other vase, if not, SHUT IT!"

Tour Guide looks mad, but doesn't say anything.

Black Ski mask, "All of you, go over there and stand, and don't move."

*Five minutes later*

Vanda, "I think its safe to move now."

Exeter gets up and rubs his head, "Good, because I'm hungry."

Storm, "I wish I had stolen the painting before they did."

Vanda, "That's a terrible way to look at things."

Storm, "It's a realistic way."

Overbuild, "You of all people are FAR from being realistic most of the time."

Exeter, "Shouldn't we alert the security?"

Vanda, "That's the smartest thing I have heard you say all year."

Exeter, "I agree."

Security Guard, "What happened?"

Tour Guide, "People in black ski mask came in and robbed our most expensive paintings."

Security Guard, "What's this on the floor?"

Overbuild picks the object in question up, "It is a cookie."

Vanda, "That doesn't make any sense, why would a robber have a cookie with them?"

Exeter eats it, "This taste familiar, I HAVE HAD THIS COOKIE BEFORE!"

Vanda, "Are you saying what I think you're saying?"

Overbuild, "According to my data, it would make sense."

Storm, "It couldn't be."

The faction leaders are shocked at their discovery.
