Please let this through, thank you.

Chapter One, TAXES

Deep in the Nexus Tower the Faction leaders sat around eating dinner.

Storm, "How are our finances going?"

Overbuild, "WE ARE RUNNING OUT OF MONEY! I can't finish my death laser of doom until we get more!"

Vanda, "I don't care about your stupid laser Overbuild, it won't help us win this war. Also, Storm, you better not buy anymore gold... or else!"

Storm, "You don't scare me, PURPLE LADY!"

Vanda, "Are you implying I'm infected you no good pirate!"

Storm, "Use your head, or has it been infected in the Maelstrom!"

Exeter laughs his belly shaking his purple bathrobe so it bounces up and down, "Hahaha, good one Storm. I like that, the Purple Lady."

Vanda, "Do you want a fist in your face Exeter?"

Exeter rolls his eyes, "Please, like you could hurt ME."

Vanda smiles wickedly, "Fine, fight me one on one."

*Exeter jumps on top of Vanda*

*Loud cracking sound*

Vandas' face is contorted in pain, "YOU IDIOT, MY BACK! GET OFF OF ME!!!"

*Overbuild picks up Exeter and throws him across the room*

*Loud crashing sound*

Exeter, "Look what you did, you threw me into the money safe."

Storm looks angry, "You broke the safe by sitting on it!"

Vanda, "That thing is made out of titanium!"

Exeter gets up brushing himself up, "Well I can't help it if COMPANIES don't make things like they used to."

The faction leaders sit back down and continue their dinner.

Guard comes in and bows, "My wonderful dictators, I have news that the money you requested isn't available."

Storm, "GET OUT OF MY SIGHT YOU NO GOOD GUARD!!"

Guard, "Yes dictator number 2."

Vanda, "If you don't I will personally make sure you are my next test subject."

Guard, "Yes dictator number 3."

Overbuild, "And I will chuck you out of this window!"

Guard, "Yes dictator number 1."

Exeter, "And I will sit on you!"

Guard, "Yes idiot number 4."

*He leaves*

Exeter, "I have a feeling I was somehow insulted just then, but can't pin point it."

Vanda laughs, "That's not new."

Exeter looks at her suspiciously, "Are you making fun of me?"

Storm, "SHUT UP AND LISTEN!"

Overbuild, "What is it? I need to get back to making the death laser and nuclear teddy bears that are in my workshop."

Storm, "I know how to get us all the money we need and more... without having to take a penny out of our own pockets."

Vanda, "Hmm... we don't loose anything but get something in return? Tell me more."

Storm, "Easy, raise taxes!"

Exeter, "What are they currently at?"

Overbuild, "30% is what they are currently at."

Storm, "Well, why not raise them to 300%?"

Vanda, "I know our citizens are stupid, but even THERE not that stupid. If they had to pay that much, they just wouldn't work at all."

Storm, "Fine, how about 99% percent."

Overbuild, "Hmm... they would have 1% to work for."

Vanda, "That is fair, we can use the rest of the money for our own projects."

Storm, "Its settled then, let us announce it!"

Charity rep, "I am here for the Poor Minifigure Association, would you kind sirs and ladies wish to donate some money."

Storm, "No we don't, but we will be taxing you."

Charity rep, "But this is all the money we have!"

Vanda, "Good, we won't have to find all the other people who have it."

Charity rep, "Bu-but!-"

Vanda, "Guard, check how much money he has."

*Guard checks*

Guard, "He has two thousand coins."

Overbuild, "Let's see, 99% percent of two thousand is..."

Exeter, "Forget the stupid calculations, I can approximate!"

*Exeter takes all the persons money and gives him one coin in return*

Exeter, "Thank you, have a nice day, NOW GET OUT OF MY SIGHT!"

Charity rep, "BUT SIR I MUST PRO-!"

Storm, "Guards, remove him from our presence."

*Guards drag him away*

Exeter, "Good, now we can announce the law publicly."

*They all appear on TV*

Vanda, "Wonderful people of the LEGO community. We have a slight increase of taxes. Nothing major, just from 30% to 99%. As you can see it isn't that major."

*Thousands of rioting minifigures are outside the Nexus Tower within five minutes*

Exeter, "See, they are here to thank us!"

Storm, "Well why do they have pitchforks and swords then?"

Overbuild, "THEY ARE TRYING TO REBEL AGAINST US!"

Vanda, "Ha, they can do nothing to us."

*The door explodes and tons of angry minifigures pour through*

Mob, "WE DEMAND YOU STEP DOWN AS OUR LEADERS!"

Overbuild, "You fools. You are no match for us."

*He fires the death laser*

*Nothing happens*

Storm, "Why isn't it working?"

Overbuild, "Hmm... maybe I put the batteries in the wrong way?"

Vanda, "Time for plan B. EVERYONE! We were just joking, so you can all calm down and leave. We would NEVER tax you 99% for your income. Everything is fine."

*The riot leaves muttering*

Exeter, "See, I intimidated them so much with my looks that they all left."

Vanda, "NO YOU DIDN'T. If anything they were scared by how ugly you are."

Storm, "We will find a way to gain more money! I promise you we will!"

Overbuild, "This was a very eventful dinner."

Exeter, "It isn't over yet."

*He stuffs some bread and butter in his mouth*

Vanda, "Use a fork, you don't won't too look like a barbarian."

Exeter, "How do you know?"

Vanda, "Well... I assume you don't want to."

Storm, "I love these gold forks."

Overbuild, "They are plastic."

Storm, "WHAT! I BOUGHT THEM FROM THE DOLLAR TREE JUST BECAUSE I THOUGHT THEY WERE REAL!"

Vanda, "Plus, they are made in China."

Exeter, "They break too easily!"

Overbuild, "The fact that you are stabbing the table instead of you food with it could be the reason."

Exeter, "I am? Oh, thanks."

Overbuild, "When I take over the world I won't have to put up with you people anymore. I will have wise scholars and thinkers in my court!"

Vanda, "Yep, and they will have to put up with YOUR stupid ideas."

The faction leaders finished eating and went off to bed.

So what did you think of it? I think it will get better as I go on. And don't worry, I will go back to the original after a while of doing this.

Chapter Two, Breakfast for Four

While the four faction leaders may have disagreed on many things, there were two things they agreed on all the time: Breakfast is the most important meal of the day, and Uncle Joe's Donuts and Coffee is the best place to have breakfast at. Because of this, they ALWAYS had breakfast together at Uncle Joe's Donuts and Coffee. And this morning was no different.

Exeter walked into Uncle Joe's restaurant with the other faction leaders. Sarah the waiter came up to them.

Sarah smiled, "Well look who it is, the four faction leaders, our most loyal customers." She took them over to where they normally sat at, but she found an elderly couple sitting their instead of it being empty.

Exeter, "Excuse me, but this is where we normally sit, would you mind moving?"

The Elderly lady looked up and then looked at her husband, "Did he just say something to us Herald?" "I can't tell, you know how our hearing aid has been acting up lately."

Exeter, "I did say something to you, I asked you to move."

Joice, looked up at him again, "Why the nerve of him, he wants us to move Herald, young people these days!

Herald, "Why don't you go sit somewhere us and let us eat in PEACE!"

Vanda, "Exeter, lets all just eat somewhere else, there’s plenty of tables in here."

Exeter, "NO, it is OUR seat, we have the right to eat there, we are faithful customers, they are just some one time out of town people!"

Overbuild, "The logical course of action is to either make them move, or eat at another chair."

Vanda, "Don't give him any idea's Overbuild, he probably will now try to make them move."

Exeter, "Making them move is a great idea, OLDIES, THATS RIGHT YOU TWO, MOVE IT!" "This is our table, so you have to either leave, or pa- AHHH!"

Before Exeter could move the, Herald was smacking him with his cane, you better not call US oldies, and you went to far when you threatened us!"

Exeter fell over from being smacked in the head by the cane.

Joice, "You show em honey, don't let them boss us around!"

Vanda tried to stop Herald, but was smacked by Joyce's purse when she tried to stop him.

Storm managed to slip the credit cards out of Herald's and Joyce's pockets while the fight was going on.

Herald looked angry, "We are leaving this crackpot town Joyce's, all the people who live here are a bunch of goons. Without further ado, they both left.

Exeter, "Uhh, oooww, that cane hurt when it hit my head."

Overbuild, "Well at least they have left now, lets eat!"

The four faction leaders sat down and the waiter came back out.

Waiter, "I heard some noise back in the kitchen coming from out here, is everything okay?"

Vanda in a sarcastic voice, "Never been better."

Waiter, "Okay... What would you all like for breakfast today?"

Exeter, "I would like Uncle Joe's super duper pancakes with raspberry, blueberry, and maple syrup on it. I would also like you to put one forth cup of butter on that and smother the whole thing in whip cream. Oh, and I almost forgot the ten pieces of bacon fried in barbecue sauce."

Vanda, "Exeter, that is terrible for your health, that’s like a heart attack on a plate, I bet it has over two thousand calories in it!"

Exeter looked a bit dejected, "Fine, make it light whip cream and use "I can't believe its not butter" instead of butter, but make sure to put one cup of it instead of one forth since its the healthy kind."

Vanda looks at the ceiling, "That’s not what I meant."

Vanda looks at the waiter, "I would like a onion donut and black coffee please."

Overbuild smiles at the waiter, "I would like some of your chocolate donuts and some chocolate milk."

Storm grins at the waiter, "Giveme some of your silver dollar pancakes, with your million dollar sauce on it and don't forget the bacon.

The waiter writes down everything, "It will be out shortly."

Storm, "How are the repairs coming to the tower after that missile that hit it?"

Overbuild, "Well, we can't start repairs since my workers went on strike."

Vanda, "Why did they go on strike?"

Overbuild, "Well, since the enemies on Crux aren't as rich anymore, they depend more on their salary.

Vanda, "What about all that money we made from hitting Crux with the missile?"

Overbuild, "The heat from the explosion melted the coins."

Storm, "WHAT!!! ME MONNIES ARE GONE!"

Vanda, "Well you just robbed the bank when the tower was hit."

Storm, "I didn't rob it, I was just... making sure everyone was okay."

Vanda, "Yeah right."

Waiter, "Here is your food, enjoy."

The faction leaders ate their food and then decided they would have to go back to work.

Exeter, "That was an excellent dinner, I will get that again- HUH AAAHHH!"

*Exeter falls off his chair*

Vanda, "Quick, get the EMP heart attack machine and use it on Exeter!"

*Vanda uses it and revives Exeter*

Vanda, "I told you it was going to give you a heart attack."

Exeter, "It was still worth it."

Vanda shakes her head, "I don't know whats wrong with you Exeter."

Exeter, "Nothings wrong with me."

Chapter Two, Breakfast for Four

While the four faction leaders may have disagreed on many things, there were two things they agreed on all the time: Breakfast is the most important meal of the day, and Uncle Joe's Donuts and Coffee is the best place to have breakfast at. Because of this, they ALWAYS had breakfast together at Uncle Joe's Donuts and Coffee. And this morning was no different.

Exeter walked into Uncle Joe's restaurant with the other faction leaders. Sarah the waiter came up to them.

Sarah smiled, "Well look who it is, the four faction leaders, our most loyal customers." She took them over to where they normally sat at, but she found an elderly couple sitting their instead of it being empty.

Exeter, "Excuse me, but this is where we normally sit, would you mind moving?"

The Elderly lady looked up and then looked at her husband, "Did he just say something to us Herald?" "I can't tell, you know how our hearing aid has been acting up lately."

Exeter, "I did say something to you, I asked you to move."

Joice, looked up at him again, "Why the nerve of him, he wants us to move Herald, young people these days!

Herald, "Why don't you go sit somewhere us and let us eat in PEACE!"

Vanda, "Exeter, lets all just eat somewhere else, there’s plenty of tables in here."

Exeter, "NO, it is OUR seat, we have the right to eat there, we are faithful customers, they are just some one time out of town people!"

Overbuild, "The logical course of action is to either make them move, or eat at another chair."

Vanda, "Don't give him any idea's Overbuild, he probably will now try to make them move."

Exeter, "Making them move is a great idea, OLDIES, THATS RIGHT YOU TWO, MOVE IT!" "This is our table, so you have to either leave, or pa- AHHH!"

Before Exeter could move the, Herald was smacking him with his cane, you better not call US oldies, and you went to far when you threatened us!"

Exeter fell over from being smacked in the head by the cane.

Joice, "You show em honey, don't let them boss us around!"

Vanda tried to stop Herald, but was smacked by Joyce's purse when she tried to stop him.

Storm managed to slip the credit cards out of Herald's and Joyce's pockets while the fight was going on.

Herald looked angry, "We are leaving this crackpot town Joyce's, all the people who live here are a bunch of goons. Without further ado, they both left.

Exeter, "Uhh, oooww, that cane hurt when it hit my head."

Overbuild, "Well at least they have left now, lets eat!"

The four faction leaders sat down and the waiter came back out.

Waiter, "I heard some noise back in the kitchen coming from out here, is everything okay?"

Vanda in a sarcastic voice, "Never been better."

Waiter, "Okay... What would you all like for breakfast today?"

Exeter, "I would like Uncle Joe's super duper pancakes with raspberry, blueberry, and maple syrup on it. I would also like you to put one forth cup of butter on that and smother the whole thing in whip cream. Oh, and I almost forgot the ten pieces of bacon fried in barbecue sauce."

Vanda, "Exeter, that is terrible for your health, that’s like a heart attack on a plate, I bet it has over two thousand calories in it!"

Exeter looked a bit dejected, "Fine, make it light whip cream and use "I can't believe its not butter" instead of butter, but make sure to put one cup of it instead of one forth since its the healthy kind."

Vanda looks at the ceiling, "That’s not what I meant."

Vanda looks at the waiter, "I would like a onion donut and black coffee please."

Overbuild smiles at the waiter, "I would like some of your chocolate donuts and some chocolate milk."

Storm grins at the waiter, "Giveme some of your silver dollar pancakes, with your million dollar sauce on it and don't forget the bacon.

The waiter writes down everything, "It will be out shortly."

Storm, "How are the repairs coming to the tower after that missile that hit it?"

Overbuild, "Well, we can't start repairs since my workers went on strike."

Vanda, "Why did they go on strike?"

Overbuild, "Well, since the enemies on Crux aren't as rich anymore, they depend more on their salary.

Vanda, "What about all that money we made from hitting Crux with the missile?"

Overbuild, "The heat from the explosion melted the coins."

Storm, "WHAT!!! ME MONNIES ARE GONE!"

Vanda, "Well you just robbed the bank when the tower was hit."

Storm, "I didn't rob it, I was just... making sure everyone was okay."

Vanda, "Yeah right."

Waiter, "Here is your food, enjoy."

The faction leaders ate their food and then decided they would have to go back to work.

Exeter, "That was an excellent dinner, I will get that again- HUH AAAHHH!"

*Exeter falls off his chair*

Vanda, "Quick, get the EMP heart attack machine and use it on Exeter!"

*Vanda uses it and revives Exeter*

Vanda, "I told you it was going to give you a heart attack."

Exeter, "It was still worth it."

Vanda shakes her head, "I don't know whats wrong with you Exeter."

Exeter, "Nothings wrong with me."

Chapter One, TAXES

Deep in the Nexus Tower the Faction leaders sat around eating dinner.

Storm, "How are our finances going?"

Overbuild, "WE ARE RUNNING OUT OF MONEY! I can't finish my death laser of doom until we get more!"

Vanda, "I don't care about your stupid laser Overbuild, it won't help us win this war. Also, Storm, you better not buy anymore gold... or else!"

Storm, "You don't scare me, PURPLE LADY!"

Vanda, "Are you implying I'm infected you no good pirate!"

Storm, "Use your head, or has it been infected in the Maelstrom!"

Exeter laughs his belly shaking his purple bathrobe so it bounces up and down, "Hahaha, good one Storm. I like that, the Purple Lady."

Vanda, "Do you want a fist in your face Exeter?"

Exeter rolls his eyes, "Please, like you could hurt ME."

Vanda smiles wickedly, "Fine, fight me one on one."

*Exeter jumps on top of Vanda*

*Loud cracking sound*

Vandas' face is contorted in pain, "YOU IDIOT, MY BACK! GET OFF OF ME!!!"

*Overbuild picks up Exeter and throws him across the room*

*Loud crashing sound*

Exeter, "Look what you did, you threw me into the money safe."

Storm looks angry, "You broke the safe by sitting on it!"

Vanda, "That thing is made out of titanium!"

Exeter gets up brushing himself up, "Well I can't help it if COMPANIES don't make things like they used to."

The faction leaders sit back down and continue their dinner.

Guard comes in and bows, "My wonderful dictators, I have news that the money you requested isn't available."

Storm, "GET OUT OF MY SIGHT YOU NO GOOD GUARD!!"

Guard, "Yes dictator number 2."

Vanda, "If you don't I will personally make sure you are my next test subject."

Guard, "Yes dictator number 3."

Overbuild, "And I will chuck you out of this window!"

Guard, "Yes dictator number 1."

Exeter, "And I will sit on you!"

Guard, "Yes idiot number 4."

*He leaves*

Exeter, "I have a feeling I was somehow insulted just then, but can't pin point it."

Vanda laughs, "That's not new."

Exeter looks at her suspiciously, "Are you making fun of me?"

Storm, "SHUT UP AND LISTEN!"

Overbuild, "What is it? I need to get back to making the death laser and nuclear teddy bears that are in my workshop."

Storm, "I know how to get us all the money we need and more... without having to take a penny out of our own pockets."

Vanda, "Hmm... we don't loose anything but get something in return? Tell me more."

Storm, "Easy, raise taxes!"

Exeter, "What are they currently at?"

Overbuild, "30% is what they are currently at."

Storm, "Well, why not raise them to 300%?"

Vanda, "I know our citizens are stupid, but even THERE not that stupid. If they had to pay that much, they just wouldn't work at all."

Storm, "Fine, how about 99% percent."

Overbuild, "Hmm... they would have 1% to work for."

Vanda, "That is fair, we can use the rest of the money for our own projects."

Storm, "Its settled then, let us announce it!"

Charity rep, "I am here for the Poor Minifigure Association, would you kind sirs and ladies wish to donate some money."

Storm, "No we don't, but we will be taxing you."

Charity rep, "But this is all the money we have!"

Vanda, "Good, we won't have to find all the other people who have it."

Charity rep, "Bu-but!-"

Vanda, "Guard, check how much money he has."

*Guard checks*

Guard, "He has two thousand coins."

Overbuild, "Let's see, 99% percent of two thousand is..."

Exeter, "Forget the stupid calculations, I can approximate!"

*Exeter takes all the persons money and gives him one coin in return*

Exeter, "Thank you, have a nice day, NOW GET OUT OF MY SIGHT!"

Charity rep, "BUT SIR I MUST PRO-!"

Storm, "Guards, remove him from our presence."

*Guards drag him away*

Exeter, "Good, now we can announce the law publicly."

*They all appear on TV*

Vanda, "Wonderful people of the LEGO community. We have a slight increase of taxes. Nothing major, just from 30% to 99%. As you can see it isn't that major."

*Thousands of rioting minifigures are outside the Nexus Tower within five minutes*

Exeter, "See, they are here to thank us!"

Storm, "Well why do they have pitchforks and swords then?"

Overbuild, "THEY ARE TRYING TO REBEL AGAINST US!"

Vanda, "Ha, they can do nothing to us."

*The door explodes and tons of angry minifigures pour through*

Mob, "WE DEMAND YOU STEP DOWN AS OUR LEADERS!"

Overbuild, "You fools. You are no match for us."

*He fires the death laser*

*Nothing happens*

Storm, "Why isn't it working?"

Overbuild, "Hmm... maybe I put the batteries in the wrong way?"

Vanda, "Time for plan B. EVERYONE! We were just joking, so you can all calm down and leave. We would NEVER tax you 99% for your income. Everything is fine."

*The riot leaves muttering*

Exeter, "See, I intimidated them so much with my looks that they all left."

Vanda, "NO YOU DIDN'T. If anything they were scared by how ugly you are."

Storm, "We will find a way to gain more money! I promise you we will!"

Overbuild, "This was a very eventful dinner."

Exeter, "It isn't over yet."

*He stuffs some bread and butter in his mouth*

Vanda, "Use a fork, you don't won't too look like a barbarian."

Exeter, "How do you know?"

Vanda, "Well... I assume you don't want to."

Storm, "I love these gold forks."

Overbuild, "They are plastic."

Storm, "WHAT! I BOUGHT THEM FROM THE DOLLAR TREE JUST BECAUSE I THOUGHT THEY WERE REAL!"

Vanda, "Plus, they are made in China."

Exeter, "They break too easily!"

Overbuild, "The fact that you are stabbing the table instead of you food with it could be the reason."

Exeter, "I am? Oh, thanks."

Overbuild, "When I take over the world I won't have to put up with you people anymore. I will have wise scholars and thinkers in my court!"

Vanda, "Yep, and they will have to put up with YOUR stupid ideas."

The faction leaders finished eating and went off to bed.
