I promise the next chapter will be your idea Void, but I need to do one about the Baron. But the next one WILL be your chapter idea.

The Barons Seer, and the FUTURE...

The Baron sat on his depressing throne of darkness and scowled as the nice looking sun rose above the horizon.

Baron, "I hate the dawn, it is so... pleasant. Ug."

Stromling, "We could blindfold you master."

Baron, "I have a feeling that was an insult..."

Stromling, "Of course not master, just a suggestion."

Baron, "Well, I have a suggestion for you..."

Stromling, "What master?"

Baron, "GET OUT OF MY SIGHT UNLESS YOU WANT TO BE ON THE UNEMPLOYMENT LINE!"

Stromling, "You mean I can quit!?"

Baron, "OF COURSE YOU CAN!"

Stromling, "FREEDOM, FINALLY! YEEHAW!"

*He runs out the door singing*

Baron sighs, "You can't get help like you used to..."

Stromling Seer, "You wanted to know the FUTURE master?"

Baron, "Yes, when will we take over the Nexus Tower?"

Stromling Seer, "I will attempt to find out... The FUTURE is very mysterious and hard to read... It will be very-."

Baron, "YOU BETTER GET SOME RESULTS! I DON'T PAY YOU TO TWITTLE YOUR THUMBS ALL DAY!"

Stromling Seer, "I... See... URG... Mmm... Doughnuts.... Whip-cream.... Exeter.... Gold.... Diamonds.... Storm.... Pieces of junk.... Weird looking person.... and.... AND.... All done."

BARON, "AND WHAT!!!!!"

Stromling Seer, "I could have sworn I heard someone just shout your name..."

Baron, "What is wrong with you? Predict the future, or I will smash you!"

Stromling Seer, "Let me take out my crystal ball."

Baron, "Well hurry it up."

Stromling Seer stares into the crystal, "Oh great crystal of Wal- I MEAN of Wisdom, show me the future."

Baron, "Why does it have a cord coming out of it and why did you put a smoke machine on the floor?"

Stromling Seer, "It isn't a cord, it is a talisman to channel... the... magical power. And that is a... um... Spell, not a machine."

Baron, "Well then why does the Crystal ball have a label that says made in China, and how could something that big be a choking hazard for small children..."

Stromling Seer quickly removes the label, "Oh, this... THIS is a fortune cookie... I MEAN the piece of paper INSIDE a fortune cookie."

Baron, "I can see your future clearly right now."

Stromling Seer, "What is it master?"

Baron, "I see that you will ALSO BE ON THE UNEMPLOYMENTLINE!"

Stromling Seer, "I SEE YOU BEING SMASHED BY... ME!"

Baron, "Wow, you are a sad little man..."

*Seer throws the Crystal Ball at the Baron*

Baron, "Ha, you think that can hurt ME?"

*Loud explosion*

*Baron falls over*

Seer, "I never would have guessed placing C4 inside my crystal ball would end up saving my life..."

Stromling Guard, "Is everything okay in there?"

Seer, "Stay where you are, we are using explosive massages on the Baron. It is his new... therapy, doctors orders!"

Stromling, "Ooh, can I try it!"

Seer, "Sure."

*Opens up door and chucks C4 out at the Stromling guard*

*Loud explosion*

Seer, "Happy?"

*Silence*

Seer, "Hehe."

Baron, "OW, what happened?"

Seer, "Don't move or ELSE!"

Baron sits up, "What?"

*Seer smacks him with a vase*

Seer, "I told you not to move!"

Automatic security system, "The special safety code has not been entered today, alarm will sound unless entered or the Baron destroys the system. Number of times this has been done, 345, number of systems bought, 345."

*Seer chucks the computer out of the window*

Automatic security system, "Number of times system has been bought, 346- CRUNCH!"

Seer, "Hehe, I love destroying public property."

Baron, "YOU!"

*Seer flies into the wall as the Baron blast him*

Seer, "Haha, I flew into the wall BEFORE you blasted me. You missed me, you missed me, you-!"

*Crunch*

Baron, "Like my knuckle sandwich? I thought so."

Guard, "Should I take him away."

Baron, "Yes."

Guard, "We are currently making Bob tell us information. Should I bring him to you so you can question him."

Baron, "Please do."

Bob, "Please, don't hurt me!"

Baron, "Oh, we won't physically."

Bob, "Okay, I can deal with it mentally."

Baron, "Really? It is time for some movies..."

Bob, "Please, not Dora the Explorer, or Elmo, or Barney, or-!"

Baron, "Ha, those are nothing compared to the movie I have selected... It is LONG, MWHAHA!"

Bob, "What is it?"

Baron smiles evilly, "The Sound of Music."

Bob, "NOT THE SOUND OF MUSIC! THAT IS THE MOST BORRING TERRIBLE MOVIE EVER!"

Baron puts it in, "Here it is."

*They start singing*

*Bobs cellphone rings*

Baron, "Stupid guards, they don't even remove your cellphone from you."

Cellphone, "Ring, RING, RING, RING!"

Baron, "Hello? Whoever this is, we have taken Bob prisoner, and don't try to rescue him because he is in the heart of the Maelstrom."

Bobs Mom, "YOU HAVE TAKEN HIM HOSTAGE!"

Baron, "Who are you?"

Bobs Mom, "I AM HIS MOTHER!"

Baron, "In that case, I will hang up."

Bobs Mom, "THAT IS IT!"

*The cellphone explodes while the Baron is holding it*

Bob, "Wow, my mom rigged my cellphone to blow up."

Baron, "She won't be able to save you, we will continue the movie."

Guard, "Sir, the Crux Bus has been spotted..."

Baron, "Who cares."

Guard, "And there is an intruder."

Baron, "Who cares, they will be overwhelmed by numbers."

Guard, "They have outside the blast doors and setting off a number of bombs."

Baron, "Well get rid of them."

*Silence*

Baron, "Guard?"

Bobs Mother, "LET MY POOR BABY GO YOU BAD MAN!"

*She punches him in the face with her hand that has ten rings on it*

Baron, "OW! You old hag, you will-!"

Bobs Mother, "Did you just call me a... HAG!"

Baron, "YES, I DID... YOU HAG!"

*She shoves the Baron against the wall and sprays pepper spray in his mouth and nose. She then slams him into the ceiling hitting him with spinning kicks and finally takes out a taser and holds down the button causing him to light up like a Christmas tree*

Bobs Mother, "I'm not done yet!"

*Censored due to moderation*

Bob, "Oww.... You got him good mother!"

Bobs Mother, "Yes I did. I will let him go now."

*She lets him go*

Baron, "AAAAHURRERERE, LET ME OUT OF HERE! WAAAAWWWWW!"

*He runs away weeping and screaming*

Stromling Counciler, "Whoever you are, thanks! I just got my job back, FULL TIME!"

Bob and His mother leave and the Baron has never bothered Bob since then...
