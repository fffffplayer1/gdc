The faction leaders are in Technoshop. Overbuild is looking at a big PC.

Overbuild, “This PC is really neat. It has a CD-ROM, a DVD-ROM, and an OR-ROM!”
Vanda, “What the brick is an OR-ROM?”
Overbuild, “Overbuild Rocks-ROM.”
Hael, “Hardy har har.”
Exeter, “I’m hungry.”
Vanda, *facepalm* “Overbuild Rocks-ROM? That has to be the stupidest-“
Exeter, “I’m hungry.”
Vanda, “Oh, go jump in a lake.”
Exeter, “Can it be a hot cocoa lake?”
Vanda, “OH, JUST GET A BURGER OR SOMETHING!”
Exeter, “But I can’t afford-“
Vanda, “GET HAEL TO GIVE YOU SOME COINS!”
Hael, “NOW WAIT JUST A-“
Exeter, “GIVE ME MONEY!”
Hael, “NOO!”
Exeter, “YES!!”
Vanda, “BREAK IT UP YOU TWO! WHILE YOU WERE FIGHTING I FOUND EXETER SOME MONEY SO HE CAN GET OUT OF HERE AND BUY A HAMBURGER!”
*Exeter goes and buys a hamburger*
Exeter, “I want a hamburger with mustard and ketchup and relish and chili and cheese and relish-“
Hamburger Man, “You already said “relish”.”
Exeter, “Whatever. Just give me the hamburger.”
HM, “Okay.”
*Exeter eats the burger and returns to Technoshop, where the rest of the faction leaders are about to leave.*
Vanda, “That’s a relief.”
Exeter, “Let’s go!”
HM, “YAY! I got a Legory jackpot ticket! I think I will use it to-“
Random guy, “A LEGORY TICKET! GET IT!”

Cont.
