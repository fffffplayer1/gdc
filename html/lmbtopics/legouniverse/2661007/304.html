*Another paper airplane comes out of the portal, except that due to a severe folding fail its nose is bent, so it ends up spiraling into the ground very sadly*

 

(Last chapter was actually only on page 302. Yay?)

 

~Chapter 25~

~Part 3~

 

 

Yithion's reminiscing was interrupted as one of the baristas yelled the mythrans' names. Or rather, badly mispronounced versions thereof. Nonetheless, it was recognizable, and the flustered employee handed them two large drinks before hurriedly grabbing the next coffee and calling for the next customer.

 

Anmithrayy took a tentative sip as they navigated their way through other tables. "Wow," he said, eyes opening wide, "this is amazing... in a weird-tasting way." He took an enthusiastic gulp.

 

Not about to be left out, Yithion tried his. His face puckered up oddly.

 

"By the stars, this stuff is strong. These minifigures must have stomachs of iron." But he took another couple of sips anyway.

 

They had just made it back to their table and were about to sit down when an angry voice exploded from behind them.

 

"A CAPPUCCINO?!" someone shrieked. A minifigure with short brown hair was holding a cup, shaking it at the flustered employee, who looked even more flustered now. Globs of hot cappuccino were spilling everywhere as the minifigure waved it around. "I didn't order this! Where's my extra large, extra chocolate chocolate milk with chocolate-flavored straw?!"

 

"I'm sorry," the employee said, distraught, trying to dodge the rain of coffee, "I must have mixed up the orders! If you hand that cup back to me I'll do my best to sort this all out!"

 

"There goes Choco Leit again," someone near the mythrans sighed. "I don't know why he get's so particular over his food. He's such a nice guy otherwise."
