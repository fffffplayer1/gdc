Peter the Pizza originated from the days when LU was still open. One day I met a player called OrionMadix while farming apes in Gnarled Forest, and he was one of my first friends on LU. We were almost always on at the same time, we played together almost every time we were on. Eventually I introduced my brother to him (under the guise as an in-game friend, not a sibling) and we made a really awesome trio. We even dubbed ourselves the Chaos Crushers. Some time later SuperLegoLarry also became really great friends with us and we ended up referring to the four of as the Ape Busters, since we farmed apes for the (really hard) achievements in Crux Prime so frequently. It was OrionMadix who came up with Peter the Pizza. One day he just remarked that he saw a flying slice of pizza. (Who is, by the way, invisible to everyone but him. Peter has been described as purple/beige/greenish, if I remember correctly. It was three years or so ago.) Anyway, for some reason I decided to act as though I were terrified of the thing. It stuck.

 

 

And that is how Peter the Pizza was born. *Looks around to see everyone having fallen asleep*
