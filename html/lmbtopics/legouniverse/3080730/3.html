Mission one: The Darkitect's new weapon.

Chapter seven,

Location: Caldera Mar, Crux Prime

"Yeeeeeeehaaaawwww! That makes three dragons!" Max B Falcon shouted as he put down his Serratorizors, "We make an awesome team!" Dextron turned off his Wormholer and said,"What loot did you get Max?"

"Crux Armor and a Miners Helm. You?"

"Spike Hammer!"

At that moment Dextron's cellphone rang. "Hello?" he said, and Vanda's voice answered, "Hi, Dextron. We were able to extract the unknown substance in the sample you brought us."

"What is it?" Dextron asked.

"It is a new chemical, so we named it QX700."

"How does QX700 effect the maelstrom?" he asked.

"QX700 acts like a steroid. It increased the maelstrom's ability to infect and corrupt by almost 100%."

"Do you know where the QX700 is stored?"

"Yes, it turns out the outpost I sent you to had a secret lab. We have sent a team of Paradox space marauders and Assembly engineers to destroy the lab and all the QX700. Thanks to you, a disaster has been avoided."

"So when is the next mission?" Dextron asked.

"We will let you know as soon as we have one!"

Dextron hung up. "Another mission completed," he thought as he went back to smashing dragons with Max.


This marks the end of "Mission One"........ But just wait until you read #2!!!

Mission two: Missing, code name: At Large.

Chapter one,

Dextron was in Nimbus Station when his phone rang. It was Hael Storm. "Hello?" Dextron said. "Oh, matey, please come and see me as soon as ye can!" a bewildered voice yelled, "Me best team 'as gone missin'!"

"Right," Dextron replied and hung up.

Dextron sped to the launch pad, pulled out a rocket and blasted off for Nexus Tower.
When he landed, he gulped down a quiksickle and made it to Hael's office in less than fifteen seconds. Hael was waiting for him. "Oh, good, yer 'ere," he said. "Ye got yerself a mission."

"What's wrong?" Dextron asked.

"A week ago I was a'looking through me telescope when I spied a new planet chunk. I decided to name it Sylven, for all of it is forest," Hael replied. "I sent me best team, lead by Dark Ranger, me best captain,to scout it out. But that was a week ago, and I've not 'eard from 'em since."

"So you want me to go and find them?" Dextron asked.

"Not alone. Ye will need a team," Hael replied. "When ye have organized a team, report back to me. Oh, and one more thing, matey. When ye choose yer team, make sure to include one 'o them Assembly engineers and one 'o me Adventurers."

Dextron walked out of Hael's office. He knew who was going to be the engineer,
but he had no idea as to the rest of the team.

Mission two: Missing, code name: At Large.

Chapter two,



Dextron called Max B Falcon and asked if he wanted to go on an "Extremely dangerous mission." Max agreed at once, and they met in the Sentinel war room. Dextron asked Max if he knew where to find an Adventurer who would agree to go on his mission. Max said, "Of course I do," and raised his voice. "HEY, LISTEN UP EVERYBODY!" to which everyone in the crowded room turned and looked at him. "WE NEED A VENTURE LEAGUE ADVENTURER TO GO WITH US ON A VERY DANGEROUS MISSION! YOU MAY NOT COME BACK FROM IT ALIVE!" he yelled.

Everyone looked at each other. Then one Adventurer stepped forward. "My name is Sharpie," he said. "I'll volunteer."

"Thank you, Sharpie, welcome to the team!" Max said.

"Ok, that makes three of us, but we should have a fourth," said Dextron.

"Lemme handle it!" Max yelled. "WE HAVE ROOM FOR ONE MORE TEAM MEMBER!!!!" he screamed. Immediately a grim-faced Samurai stepped forward.

"My name is Raidian, and I would be proud to be part of this team," he said.

"Welcome to the team Raidian!" Dextron said as he shook Raidian's hand.

"THE TEAM IS FULL! YOU MAY GO BACK TO YOUR LIVES NOW, PEOPLE!" Max screamed.

"Thank you, Max," Dextron said, "Team, we need to report to Hael Storm for briefing."

Mission two: Missing, code name: At Large.

Chapter three,


"Ah, I see that ye have organized a team," Hael Storm said as Dextron and his team walked in to his office. "And I see that ye have the Adventurer and Engineer that I asked for. They will 'ave special tasks."

"Let me introduce the members of the team," Dextron said. "Sharpie is the Adventurer, the Samurai is Raidian, and the Engineer is my good friend Max B Falcon."

"Say, Max, what does the "B" in your name stand for?" Sharpie asked.

Max's face turned red with embarrassment. "Um, I can't tell you that," he replied.

"Why not?" Sharpie asked.

Max's face turned redder. "It's a ridiculous name; you would all laugh if I told you."

"Okay, you don't have to tell us," Sharpie said.

"Hael, please brief us on the mission," Dextron said.

"All right," Hael replied. "Mates, ye are about to travel to an uncharted planet chunk about a hundred miles off Gnarled Forest. The object of yer mission is to find Dark Ranger and his team o' Daredevils and Adventurers. They went missin' about a week ago. The last transmission that we heard from 'em stated that they had landed in a clearing and were beginning to map the area.

"The planet chunk is mostly forest," he continued. "Sharpie, I will need ye to make a map of any locations ye discover. Max, ye will build a communications array wherever ye land, and it will keep your team in touch with Nexus Tower. The communications array comes with a remote communicator, so that even if ye are a hundred miles from the array, ye will still get a signal."

"Raidian, ye are a Sentinel, so ye will watch the team's back. There is no telling what could be on Sylven."

"Sylven?" asked Raidian, confused.

"That is the name I gave the planet chunk," Hael replied.
"I think it's time ye started on yer mission. Go and pick up the kit for the communication array from Dr. Overbake, but wait, ye will need the coordinates to get to Sylven." Hael gave them the coordinates, and they picked up the kit from Dr. Overbuild. Then they made there way to the launch pad, set their rocket coordinates for Sylven and blasted off.
