Mission two: Missing, code name: At Large.

Chapter eight,
Day 4 on Sylven.
10:05AM


Dextron woke to the sound of Max snoring. "Max!" he yelled, "You were supposed to wake us up early!" Max woke up with a start. "What?! Oh! Sorry, I must have dropped off," he said sheepishly.

"Fine watchman you are, sleeping on the job," Dextron said, "Lucky for us the apes didn't come back, you let the fire die down!"

"Well, at least we are up," Sharpie said. "We need to hurry up and find the lost team before the apes attack us again."

"Let's try the flare gun tracker thingie," Max said. "I have it in my backpack."
Max rummaged through his backpack and pulled out the flare gun. "Is there a button or a switch to activate the tracker on this thing?" Max asked.

"Here, give to me," Sharpie said. Sharpie took the flare gun from Max, then opened a small door in the handle and pushed a small blue button. The flare gun started beeping, then a robotish voice said, "F1v3 m1l35 345t" (five miles east).
"Cooooool," everyone said. "So, five miles east I suppose," Dextron said. "But first I need to call Hael and tell him what is going on." Dextron flipped the switch on the remote communicator. "No signal," it said.

Mission two: Missing, code name: At Large.

Chapter nine,
Day 4 on Sylven.
10:11AM

"Uh oh," Dextron said.

"Wussup?" Max asked him.

"The remote communicator has no signal," Dextron answered. "Which could mean two things: either we are not high enough to get a signal, or the communications array is down."

"Let's hope it is the former," Sharpie said.

"Whaddaya mean by 'the former?'" Max asked, confused.

"It means that I hope we are not high enough to get a signal," Sharpie replied.

"Oh," said Max.

"We're going to have to get on with our mission without reporting too Hael," Dextron said.

"Oh, well, we best get it over with," Raidian said while rising from his resting place.

"Are you sure that you don't want to rest a little more?" Dextron asked Raidian.

"No, I feel much better," Raidian replied.

"Well, then let's get a move on," Dextron said.

So they packed up their gear and plunged into the forest. They took the precaution to carry lighted sticks, so if they saw any apes, they would be safe.
"How many miles 'till we reach the lost team?" Max asked.

"According to the flare gun, it should be about five miles," Sharpie replied.

The forest was horribly tangled, and leaves covered the ground. Sometimes one of them would fall into a hole that was hidden under the leaves and have to clamber out. All in all, they made very slow progress.

After about three miles of walking, they came across a creek. The water proved to be very good, so they sat down and filled their water bottles. It was getting late, so they set up camp and lit a fire. After they ate supper, they turned in. Raidian insisted on keeping watch.

Chapter ten,
Day 4 on Sylven.
4:00PM

Location: Ape-infested treetops,

-Dark Ranger's team-

"I'm telling you, we are being sought," Dark Ranger said to rank three DareDevil Lieutenant Treader. "First your flare gun tracker (boy, that sure freaked out the guards!), and now those apes."

"Yeah, they look like they had a bad day," Treader replied. "I have never seen an ape sneeze, but, boy, when they sneeze, they sneeze!"

"Well, I don't see how anyone could get us out of this cage," a rookie team member remarked. "Not ta mention dat we is 'bout a hundred feet off da ground," another added.
"Say, boss, what do you think the apes are keeping us here for?" Treader asked.

"I have no clue," Ranger replied. "But whatever it is, it can't be good."


Day 5 on Sylven.
8:00AM

Location: Somewhere in the deep dark forest,

-Dextron's team-


"Wake up! The apes are upon us!!!" Max screamed at the top of his lungs. Dextron and Sharpie shot out of their sleeping bags and grabbed their weapons. "WHERE!?!?" Sharpie yelled. Max burst out laughing. "You should have seen your faces!" he said while laughing.

"Where is Raidian?" Dextron asked.

"He went walking early this morning," Max replied. "He woke me up and told me to keep watch till he came back," he added.

"How long ago was that?" Sharpie asked.

"Oh about three hours," Max replied.

Mission two: Missing, code name: At Large.

Chapter eleven,
Day 5 on Sylven.
9:23AM

Location: Ape-infested treetops,

-Dark Ranger's team-


"Hey, look, boys!" Dark Ranger said excitedly. "Those apes have brought us some company!"

Two big apes climbed up to the cage, one of them holding a struggling minifigure.
The apes untied the door to the cage and flung the minifigure in, then tied the door shut and climbed down. The minifigure was a Sentinel Samurai. He looked around at the team. "You are Dark Ranger's team, I presume?" he asked.

"Yes, we are," Dark Ranger replied, stepping forward. "And who might you be?"

"My name is Raidian. I am part of the team who was sent to find you," Raidian answered.

"So, there was a team trying to find us," Treader said. "How many teammates did you come with?"

"Four, including myself," Raidian answered.

"So, do you guys want to escape now, or tomorrow?" Raidian asked them.
"Uh, sure we can escape," Treader said sarcastically. "All we have to do is jump more than one hundred feet and hit the ground softly."

"Oh, come on, guys. This cage is made out of vines, right?" Raidian asked.

"Right," everyone answered.

"All we need is something sharp. Those apes took my weapons, as I see they did with yours, but they did not take this!" As Raidian said this, he reached into his boot and took out a small, razor-sharp dagger.
