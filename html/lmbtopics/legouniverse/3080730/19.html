Chapter nine, part one.

At the top of the ladder, Paradoxal dropped the bee in the bottle, then threw the bottle in the container. He quickly closed the lid and slid down the ladder. The bottle froze in place, halfway to the bottom of the container. Then it exploded. The container turned into a seething mass of bees, vibrating with the sheer sound of the buzzing.
But just then they felt the ground tremble, and purple mist rose around the camp.

The bees, who had been lazily flying around, seemed to go crazy. Dextron had ordered his team to stay together if they were attacked, so they all bolted to the bunk house and readied their weapons. Krag ordered his Engineers to line up, then he pulled out a pair of Serratorizors. All the Engineers equipped their wrenches and magnitizers.
Heimy and Krag took 30 Engineers and placed the rest of them under Dextron's command. The mist now began to take shape. "Max!" Dextron yelled. "I need you to shoot your bees at any large clump of Darklings. We will keep them off you while you reload!" Max nodded and backed away from the wall a bit. Suddenly, the whole camp was surrounded by Grumpy Darklings.

Part two,

Dextron had the Engineers in his command use their magnitizers to keep the Darklings away from his wall. Max shot his bees at a nearby hoard of Darklings, then used his blaster while he waited for the bees to return. Suave had not been wrong when he said to leave the bees out as security. They swarmed everywhere, completely obliterating whole crowds of Darklings. Dextron saw that they would win if they could keep the wall, so he jumped on top of it and began shooting the enemy with his pistol. If a Darkling got too close, he would yank out his swords and stab it before it could whack him with its hammer.

Sharpie continuously called down lightning and slashed the Darklings with his Elite Force Blade. Suave held off a hoard of Darklings until Max could launch a beehive at them, then drained a notion potion and went back to the wall. Raidian and Paradoxal fought together; if one got knocked down, the other would take his place. After about thirty minutes of battling, the Darklings seemed to give up. They all disappeared into mist, leaving the battlers sitting and breathing heavily.

"We whupped 'em!" Max exclaimed, flopping on the ground.

"Yes, we did," Dextron said, "but, Max, please finish that weapon!"

It was getting dark, and the team was hungry. They had skipped lunch, and now it was 7:15 PM. They unpacked their food and ate it silently, except when Sharpie started eating a stinky fish. "Hey, it tastes good!" he said when they commented. Paradoxal put a hand to his mouth and stood up. "I think I'll go take a walk," he said in a sickly voice. After the meal, they all turned in. Even Raidian went to bed. They knew that if they were attacked, the bees would warn them.
