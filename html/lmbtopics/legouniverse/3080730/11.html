The ship had the Assembly emblem painted on the front. Its shape was circular, and it had massive thrusters on each side. A huge crate hung below. Dextron assumed it held their supplies and the bricks for the fort.

"Wow!" Paradoxal exclaimed. "That is one huge ship!"

"It is our new drop ship," Heimy explained. "Kragonius designed it, so we named it after him."

"Heimy, I keep telling you: call me Krag," Kragonius said irritably.

"So, Krag, how long will it take us to arrive at our destination?" Raidian asked.

"The outskirts is about fifty miles from here, so it should take us about an hour," Krag replied.

They boarded the drop ship, Krag's Assembly Engineers had already boarded and were waiting for them.

Part two

About an hour later, they landed in a valley surrounded by a mountain range. The drop ship landed near the edge of a cliff face and set down the crate, along with the teams. Krag led his Engineers to the crate, then Krag pushed a button on the front. A large door opened. The crate was packed with all sorts of boxes, and the Engineers started unloading the boxes at once. Krag's team consisted of 40 Engineers, all of them Rank three and extremely talented. Max felt right at home as he helped to unload.

At the far back, after about four hours of unloading, Krag said it was time to unload the main components of the fort. These were packed in huge boxes, too big, in fact, to carry, and were placed at the back of the crate. But the Engineers knew what to do in this kind of situation. Krag lined up ten of them in front of a box, then he gave a signal. The Engineers held up their magnetic devices.
