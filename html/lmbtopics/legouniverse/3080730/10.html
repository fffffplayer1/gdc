Mission three: Crypto Base.



Chapter two,



Upon landing in Nimbus Station, Max bolted for Nimbus Plaza as fast as his legs could carry him. Dextron landed a second later, followed by Sharpie and Raidian. They followed Max into the Plaza, finding him talking excitedly with two people in business suits. Max saw them and beckoned for them to come. "These are my good friends Paradoxal and SuaveAbleCat," Max said. Dextron, Sharpie, and Raidian shook hands with Suave and Paradoxal. "It is an honor to finally meet you, Dextron," Paradoxal said. "Max told us all about your last mission, and now he says that you could use us in your new mission."



"Yes, I think that you would come in handy," Dextron answered. "We are going to the outskirts of Crux Prime to protect a team of Engineers, who will be building a small base to gather research."



Paradoxal and Suave stood open-mouthed at this statement. "When do we leave?" Suave asked after a few seconds. "Tomorrow afternoon," Dextron replied. "Make sure that you bring your most powerful gear; we have no idea of what lies in the outskirts," he added.



Max departed with Suave and Paradoxal. Max went to Nexus tower to buy his Inventor gear, while Paradoxal and Suave went to buy some supplies from a vendor.



"Which gear are you guys taking with you?" Dextron asked Sharpie and Raidian.



"I will be wearing my DareDevil gear, with the Nunchuks of lightning and Elite Force Blade of Lightning as secondary weapons," Sharpie replied.



"I shall take my Knight gear," Raidian answered.



"What about you, Dextron?" Sharpie asked.



"I will be wearing my signature spy gear," Dextron replied, "special flexible armor, duel katana, pistol, and black cape. That is the gear I am most familiar with."



"Spies are awesome!" Sharpie exclaimed.



After about thirty minutes, Paradoxal and Suave returned with several bags. The bags contained provisions and armor polish. They were both wearing the Faction gear. Paradoxal was wearing his Paradox Sorcerer outfit with a Doomslicer, Suave had his Sentinel Space ranger armor. They sat on a bench and talked while they waited for Max to return.



About an hour later, Max arrived in his Inventor gear. "I feel awesome in this outfit!" he said as he tried out his gear. "Watch this!" Max created a small robot with his imagination. It hovered around a bit before it auto-smashed. "That is my makeshift buddy!" he said. "Now watch this!" Suddenly, blue electricity appeared around him, forming a force-field. "And this!" he held up a portable bee hive in his left hand, then, using his blaster, he shot the bees out of their hive. The bees spread out and buzzed around for a bit before returning to the hive. "That could come in handy sometimes," Max said. "The bees are trained to attack Maelstrom, and they are extremely dangerous to a crowd of stromlings!"



Dextron stood up and said that he had a meeting that he had to attend. He left them chatting behind him and walked to the Nexus Tower launchpad.



"I say, Max," Paradoxal remarked after Dextron had left, "you really have got us in over our heads this time!"



"Aw, come on!" Max answered, "it is only a little trip to the outskirts. What could happen?"



"A little trip?!" Suave exclaimed. "We may be out there a week or more!"



"True," Max replied, "but who cares? It will be an adventure!"

Mission three: Crypto Base.



Chapter one



Dextron, Raidian, Sharpie, and Max B Falcon reclined on the beach at the pirate's camp in Gnarled Forest. They had just finished giving battle to the Admirals at Brig Rock, and now they rested and chatted. "I say we did very well today," Max remarked. "I got a ton of Faction Tokens. In fact, I think I have enough to buy the Inventor gear!"



"How long have you been saving up for that?" Dextron asked him.



"Oh, about a month," he answered.



"Mmphm thish shtuff ish good," Sharpie said while munching on some hardtack biscuit. "Do you guysh want shome?"

"I've heard that it tastes horrible," Raidian said, "I would rather not."



"Same here," Dextron said, standing up. "Well team," he said, "I have another mission, I am allowed to take anyone I want. Do you guys want to join me?"

"Awesome!" Max yelled.

"Count me in," Raidian said.

Sharpie swallowed his biscuit and said, "Where are we going?"

"I have been commissioned to protect a group of Assembly Engineers," Dextron replied,

"we are going to the outskirts of Crux Prime, where the Engineers are going to build a secret base for research," he said.

They stood in stunned silence. "No one has ever returned from the outskirts," Sharpie said.

"Cool!" Max yelled, "When do we leave?"

"Tomorrow afternoon," Dextron replied.

"Perfect! I have two friends who want to be on one of your missions," Max said, "their names are Paradoxal, and SuaveAbleCat," he continued.

"I guess they could come along, we may need all the help we can get," Dextron said.

"Yes!" Max said excitedly, "I'll go tell them to get ready!" Max sped over to the Nimbus Station launch pad and Blasted off in his Assembly rocket. Dextron rolled his eyes and followed him.
