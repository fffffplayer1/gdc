Ok! We have made it to thirty pages! Also, I have written 15,936 words, and 72,312 characters during this story. The best I have ever done is 1000 words an hour... I have never written so much in my whole life! Ok, enough of that, LET'S HAVE A NU CHAPTAH!



Chapter four. Part one.

Dextron ran all the way back to his apartment for two reasons. The first was to throw off whoever was watching him, and the second was to get his weapons. He donned his spy gear, swords, and pistol before unlocking a chest and bringing out an assault rifle. He was not taking any chances with Jason. All day and all night he was watchful. The next day, he made ready to meet Duke. Then at 9:00, he left, grabbing a bite to eat at a local restaurant. Duke was at the launch pad when Dextron landed. Duke pretended not to notice Dextron as he landed, and Dextron realized that Duke wanted him to do the same. Jason could be watching them even as they stood. Duke started walking in the direction of Nexus Theater, and Dextron followed aimlessly, wandering in and out of shops along the way.

Part two,

After a bit, they reached the Theater. Duke stalked through the alley that led to the back, and Dextron followed him, keeping in the shadows. They reached the end of the alley and walked into a storage house full of props and costumes. Ropes hung from the ceiling and boxes were pulled around, and there, in the middle of it all, stood Jason Koboi. He stood with a look of glee in his eyes. Sitting next to him, gagged and bound hand and foot to a chair, sat Micle.

"Welcome!" Jason started, "I have been waiting for you. It would please me to trade this boy for something, rather of value."

"And that is...?" Duke asked.

"It is actually very simple," Jason answered. "It involves a gun, you, and me."

While Duke and Jason were talking, Dextron had been stealthily climbing the boxes around them, and now he made ready to spring if Jason made a move.

Part three,

"Do you remember," Jason was saying, "six years ago on Crux Prime, when the Nexus Force was fighting off the Maelstrom to reclaim Sentinel Point Zeta?"

"Yes," Duke answered.

"And do you remember a certain recruit who saved your life?"

"Yes..."

"And when the Maelstrom had all but destroyed us, you left that recruit even as he lay dying at your feet!"

"But I didn't know!" Duke barked back, "I thought you were dead! We had no choice but to evacuate everyone!"

"Everyone, but me!!" Jason snarled, "Well, you can see that I survived, and I am here to say thank you!"

As Jason said this, he yanked a pistol from his belt. Dextron leaped, but a second too late. A shot rang out, and Duke was knocked off his feet. Dextron landed and rolled, unsheathed his katanas and charged Jason from behind.
