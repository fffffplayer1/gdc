Mission 4: Rex Redwulf.

Chapter one, part one.

"Ha! You have failed; I have smashed over half the guard, and you are powerless to stop me! I will get Duke, even if it costs the lives of all the Nexus Force!"

Dextron woke up in a cold sweat. It was morning, and the nightmare was over. Ever since his first mission, he had had this nightmare. He struggled to put the past behind him, but he could never shake the memories. It was five years ago when he had first faced Jason Koboi, AKA Rex Redwulf, as a rookie spy. The criminal had blown up Red Blocks and had tried to take the life of Duke Exeter twice. It was only by Fred Oseer, Dextron's trainer and mentor, that Jason Koboi was finally put behind bars for life. Jason was dubbed as "Criminally Insane" and locked in a padded cell, seeing only the guards.

Part two,

Dextron got out of bed and stretched. He took a quick shower, brushed his teeth, and dressed. He decided to take a walk to Nimbus Plaza to see what he could get for breakfast. The Plaza was busy as usual with hawkers, recruiters, entertainers, and soldiers for hire. Dextron bought a Buttery Croissant and sat down on a bench by the sea to eat. After eating, he looked up and saw a knight walking towards him. The knight held a piece of paper out to Dextron and said, "I have a message from the Faction Leaders." Dextron stood and took it. It said, "Please come to the meeting room at 10:00 sharp, Nexus Tower. This message will self-destruct." Dextron quickly balled up the paper and hurled it into the water near him. The paper exploded, throwing some fish into the air and completely drenching a few pedestrians. The messenger walked off, leaving Dextron to wonder about the meeting... That and how to explain his actions to the dripping wet people.

Part three,

After escaping from the drenched pedestrians, Dextron flew off to Nexus Tower in his stealth rocket. He wandered around for about an hour, then, at 9:59 sharp, he took the elevator to the meeting room. He was surprised to find ten armed guards at the door. The guards were expecting Dextron and let him pass. Dextron strode into the room. A round table sat in the middle, surrounded by high-backed chairs. Four figures rose to meet him; they were the faction leaders.

"Right on time!" Vanda said as he entered. "Take a seat."

"Jason Koboi has escaped prison," Duke explained as Dextron sat down.

Dextron gasped. "How is that possible?"

"He had a confederate among the guards," Duke answered. "They apparently switched clothes when the guard came to bring him supper! We really need security in our high-security prisons, that's for sure!"

"But it is worse then that," Dr. Overbuild said. "Jason Koboi has kidnapped my son!"

Chapter two, part one.

"You have a son?!" Dextron asked incredulously.

"I have two sons," Overbuild replied. "The eldest, Wreckx, is an Adventurer, and the youngest, Micle, has been in boarding school for a long time."

"Ah... Uh, I never knew this..." Dextron said, giving the doctor a confused look.

"Wreckx is always off somewhere, and Micle never wants to leave his school. He is a genius and does very well in his studies."

"Ok... Which one was kidnapped?" Dextron asked.

"Micle was kidnapped."

"How did it happen?"

"Last night, Jason escaped the prison and made straight for my son's school. He broke in and kidnapped him, then apparently got away in Micle's Rocket."

"Did Jason leave any clues as to where they are?" Dextron asked.

"Yes," Hael answered, "the scoundrel left a note sayin' that if Overbuild wanted to see his son again, Duke has to meet him, alone and unarmed, behind the Nexus Theater in Nexus City, at 10:00 PM tomorrow."

Part two,

"But if Duke goes, Jason will kill him!" Dextron exclaimed. "He has always wanted to...ever since the Crux Prime incident...."

"Yes, and that is why we want you to go with him," Vanda said. "We want you to follow Duke and protect him if need be."

"Ok," Dextron said, standing up, "I will be at the Nexus city launch pad at 9:45 tomorrow."

He shook hands with the leaders and left the room. He decided to get some taxes done at home, and he blasted off for Nimbus Station. He landed and walked towards his apartment. After an hour of trying to do his work, he sat back. He couldn't stop thinking about Jason Koboi. He decided to take a walk to clear his mind. He set off in the direction of the local park.

After walking for a while, he became aware of someone following him. He kept seeing the same baseball cap behind him wherever he walked. He walked around in circles before he was convinced of it. A corner was up ahead, so he took it and stopped. The person kept going, and Dextron grabbed him and twisted his arm behind his back in a grip of iron.

Part four,

Dextron's brain whirled. Why was Jason watching him? Could he know about the meeting?

"Where did you meet him?" he asked after a moment.

"The Vertigo Loop race place."

"He is here..." Dextron thought, "but how?! He has Micle to watch..."

"Come on," Dextron said, "we have an appointment with the police department! By the way, what is your name?"

"Uh... John Smith..."

Dextron looked at him unbelievingly.

"Oh all right... It's Super Shadow Ninja."

Dextron took Super to the local police station and gave him to the chief. The chief and Dextron were good friends, so he just told the chief to keep Super there until he wanted him. The chief asked no questions and put Super in the cooler. After he saw Super locked up, Dextron left the police station, but he stopped dead in his tracks at the door. Taped on the inside were the words, "I am watching you."
