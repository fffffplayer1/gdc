Chapter 8. Part one,
Dextron crouched behind a trash bin at the Nexus City Helipad. A large cargo helicopter was there, and a pilot was making final checks at the controls. Suddenly, a figure wearing dark glasses walked up to the copter and boarded. The engine started, and the props began to rotate. Dextron sneaked up to the helicopter and fastened a rope to the bottom of it. The machine lifted off the ground and started for the coast. Dextron held on to the rope for dear life as the helicopter whizzed at a tremendous speed. After about five minutes, Dextron looked down and saw that they were over open ocean. Above the roar of the engine, Dextron heard a loud thump from the cabin, then the doors to the copter opened, and Jason Koboi was outlined against the sky. He was carrying a small suitcase tied to a very heavy rock.

Part two.
Jason shouted an order to the pilot, and the helicopter climbed high up into the sky. It stopped at about 1,000 feet. Jason laughed aloud, as he began to push the rock to the doors. Dextron landed at that precise moment. Both katanas were out in a flash, as he cut the rope on the suitcase. Jason yelled and drew a pistol from his belt, but Dextron twisted a katana, and the pistol flew from Jason's hand. The criminal snarled and yanked out a golden sword from a sheath, but he did not attack. Instead, he darted to the side and kicked the suitcase toward the open doors. Dextron dived and caught it just as it reached the brink. Then he jumped up and stabbed at Jason, but Jason dodged and brought his sword down at Dextron's head. Dextron fended off the blow with his second katana, as he dropped his first one and pulled out his pistol. He fired, and Jason grunted and slumped on the floor. The pilot, hearing the shot, spun around in his chair and stared at the sight. Dextron pointed the pistol at the pilot and said, "Take us back to the city!"

Part three. 
The prostrate figure of Jason suddenly kicked at Dextron's legs. Dextron toppled to the floor, his pistol flying out the door. Jason got up slowly, holding his hand tight to his side. 
"I will have my revenge!" he screamed hoarsely as he moved towards the suitcase.
Dextron stood up and grabbed the criminal, but Jason fought back, kicking and punching with his free hand. A moment of grappling, and then Jason tripped on the rock, falling flat on his face. Dextron lost no time and secured the rope onto Jason's leg. Jason appeared not to notice. The criminal grunted, then, seeing the suitcase, he pushed the rock with all his might. Before Dextron had time to stop him, he pushed the rock out the door. Only then did Jason see the rope tied to his leg. But it was too late. The rock's weight pulled him out the door, and, with a despairing shriek, Jason Koboi plummeted to the sea. The pilot goggled at the door where Jason had been, then he looked at Dextron. Dextron looked back at him, and the pilot turned the helicopter around.

Part four.
Back at Nexus City, Dextron let the pilot go, then opened the suitcase. Inside was a small bottle with a clear liquid. He sighed  and walked to the Nexus Tower Launch pad.He arrived at the hospital and walked up to he nurse at the desk. She didn't even look up as she jerked a finger at a sign on the desk. It read: "Third Floor Fifth Room.  " Dextron smiled and took the elevator. At the room, he was greeted by one of the doctors. 
"Do you have... it?" he asked haltingly.
Dextron nodded and produced the bottle.
The doctor beamed. "We are in debt to you, Dextron, I don't know how to thank you..."
But Dextron was already in the elevator. 
After a few days, it was clear that the Duke was healing. He was able to walk within a month and resumed his duties after three months. Jason Koboi was never seen again, and he was thought drowned in the endless ocean. Dextron had many other missions, but he always deemed this one the most important.  
Thus ends mission four. And this mission ends all missions, as I will no longer be writing. I hope you enjoyed reading my story!
