"Destiny?  No need, I knew it from the second I touched drarka for the first time, my duty is to be a dragon rider and protect those who are lesser than me, and besides you must of forgot your training, all minds feel different, even if protected. You could of easily found out it was my mind if you did not just let the hat intercept my mind..." said The Shadow trailing off. "Oh I almost forgot, I have a gift for you." said The Shadow, while handing over a egg shaped package. "Do not open it here or it will cause you problems in the future." warned The Shadow. *Ignore the following if you wanted Wiz to find his destiny.* Suddenly The Shadow murmured a word, and CRACK.All four of them were instantly surrounded by blue light, than with another CRACK, it was over, and they were standing outside of The Shadows mansion. "Follow me." The Shadow commanded. They walked into a stable like part of the mansion, but in larger proportions than a normal stable. "Now open it." The Shadow commanded. Wiz carefully opened the package to find a white egg with gold lines that looked like they were drizzled on. Wiz slowly reached out to touch it, Right away it started to crack. Out came a golden dragon hatching, Wiz tapped it and at first regretted it, It felt like he had submerged his hand in ice cold water that tingled and snapped. It stopped as soon as it started, and on is palm was a silver circle, He suddenly felt like there was a whole new galaxy around his mind, and he felt anothers mind, and at the command of The Shadow he let it in, and met his dragon. "You are now like me, a Dragon Rider." The Shadow told him, and so he was, he felt like he would never be the same. "I will explain more later, drarka will take care of him, follow me to the main part of my mansion." The Shadow Told Wiz, and motorbiker.

It's been a long time...
