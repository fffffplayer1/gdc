Really sorry Rose. Although it's good that you're still willing to continue the story and all.

Flashback:

Buck wrapped his shinobi hood around his head, and leaped into battle. Instantly, his two swords were out, the moon rays gleaming in the reflection. Buck cut across, meeting another sword that was aimed towards him. A quick kick and his first opponent was down. Three maelstrom then surrounded him, but with two swords, Buck was able to defend himself from all sides. One went down with a kick, and then the earlier stromling was disarmed and smashed. A block that ended up with Buck's swords making an x shape allowed him to stab the two stromlings in the legs, smashing them. Hearing a faint noise behind him, Buck spun round and met the grunt's sword with his own, following up with stabbing the stromling Only then did a large ape-size stromling stomped up to him. The monster slashed it's sword down on Buck, but Buck dodged it easily. In a flash, Buck was halfway up a tree, but that was quickly cut down. It then became a game of jumping from tree to tree,l until Buck was lost in the trees. He appeared behind the monster, and leaped towards it. The monster turned round, it's sword swinging right at Buck. With added momentum, Buck flipped over the sword by a hairs breadth, and landed on the monsters face. A quick slash and the monster fell apart into purple bricks.

Flashback:

The building was covered in flames as Buck stood there, watching in dismay as the enemy army walked away. He had failed, his school that he had studied in....destroyed. A large hissing arose behind him, and he turned slowly, to see two large red eyes staring out at him.
End....

Buck walked along the edge of the camp, expecting someone or something to leap out of the undergrowth. He put his hands on his two weapons, and glanced around quickly to make sure no one was watching him. Right after, he sprinted off silently into the forest.

Buck wore his shinobi gear that he had earned from paradox. It was as padded as the paradox rank 3 shinobi gear, but looked entirely different. He looked like a ninja, sprinting through the shadows. His eyes glanced up to a large building on his right, and he quickly decided to climb it to get an overall look on the area. Using the piping that lined the corners of the house, he clambered up as fast as a lightning bolt. Once he was on the roof, he edged his way along the side of the building, scanning his nearby surroundings. It was only then that he noticed a figure on the ground, obviously in pain. Buck climbed down slowly, making sure he was behind the figure, so they couldn't see him. Once he had got close enough, he recognised the figure to be Roseann. He creeped along the edge of the building, and saw the face of the figure. It was Roseann. Worrying that he might of seen him in his shinobi gear, he scurried back up the building, not caring if he made any level of noise. Roseann might of seen him, but hopefully not recognised him as this was what he done in his free time. Roseann may be wounded, but Buck decided he had to take off his Shinobi gear first before he could help.

A figure watched Buck leap into the trees, and they watched him take his shinobi gear off, revealing his real identity. The figure leaped into the trees without making a sound, and crouched like a hawk, waiting for it's prey. This figure had the shinobi gear on as well, and the figure's eyes followed Buck wherever he went. After about ten minutes of watching, the figure noticed Roseann, wounded badly. Prey. The figure quickly closed the distance between his prey. From the eyes and the only parts that were not covered in gear, people would easily recognize it was a boy.The boy landed a few metres away from Roseann, and sheathed his two swords.
"You are my prey, and I am your hunter" the boy croaked out before slashing downwards towards Roseann's head.
A pulse of darkness missed the figure's head by a fraction, causing the boy to stumble backwards. Looking around, the boy raised his weapons, ready for the next attack. Another pulse of darkness smashed into his back, sending him flying down the street, but the boy quickly regained his footing. Impatient for his kill, the boy raced towards Roseann for the final blow. Just as the boy stabbed downwards, another sword got in the way. The boy glanced sideways to see another shinobi dressed like him. Quickly, the boy used his other sword to try and smash the minifigure, but the minifigure leaped backwards. The boy instantly guessed it was Buck in the gear, and stood there, waiting for him to charge. Buck did charge at the boy, and both clashed with their swords. A heated battle began, none gaining any wounds or wounding the opponent. At one point though, the boy found a weakness in Buck's fighting style, and was able to kick Buck onto the ground. Buck leaped up again, but by that time the boy was already thrusting his sword towards Buck's chest. Buck was barely able to dodge the blow, but then had to quickly block the next strike. The sword got through Buck's defense however, and ripped off his mask. Buck's face was exposed.
"Good job, Keith" Buck whispered.
The boy stumbled back, who was obviously Keith. Buck slashed at Keith with his sword but Keith managed to dodge it. Keith then kicked towards Buck but Buck slashed at Keith's leg, causing him to stop. Buck stabbed forwards with both of his swords, but Keith jumped over him. Buck glanced behind him, and met Keith's sword with one of his own. Keith took the chance, and managed to kick the off-balance Buck
onto the ground.
"We'll meet again Buck, and if you interfere, I won't hold back"
Keith then leaped into the trees, escaping. Buck watched Keith go, and then collected the swords he had dropped when he was kicked, as well as getting his helmet back.

Buck carefully put Roseann over his shoulder, and started to jog back to the camp, hoping that it wasn't too late.

Okay. This is a preview sorta of future events in the book (at least, my parts. )

Hovawart makes Rose go insane -

Roseann was sleeping softly, lying with her hand over her chest on the couch, a soft blanket covering her, the warm fire crackling softly. Enough things to make the world seem not so bad. But it wouldn't stay that way for long. Hovawart silently stepped over to Rosesann, grinning so sickly that anyone would crumple at the sight of it. He put his hand on her forehead, saying, "Rosie, you don't look so good." Roseann, of course, wasn't awake, after such a traumatizing event she was in. "But you should be dead. That's what my intention was, after all." Hovawart pulled his hand from her forehead, looking down out her, his grin turning to a frown. "But we all have to make exceptions, don't we?" He walked to the window beside the couch, and looked out at the snow gently falling (NOTE: Yes, when she was hurt it was winter, but it was a non-snow-day) down to the Earth. Hovawart glared out, feeling like melting it all, making the world dark. "You always destroy my plans, Roseann. And you do know that makes me angry, don't you?" he asked to the unconscious Roseann, turning his head to her, sick smile returning. "But I have a plan now... something that won't fail." Energy started cackling in his hand as he walked back over to the front of the couch. "It won't kill you, no, but it will have some other, hmm, interesting effects." He put his hand on her forehead again, energy shifting from him to her. Hovawart pulled his hand away, and grinned even wider and sicker. He knelt down next to Roseann, and said, gently enough you could almost believe he meant it, "Sweet dreams." He stood up, and walked towards the wall. A black and red swirling portal opened in front of him, right at the wall, and he walked in, the portal closing behind him. Just then, X opened the front door of the house. "Rose?" he asked, walking to his sister, checking her pulse. "What's wrong with you? Your pulse... it's random... it keeps changing. What happened to you?"


Roseann is insane -

Post-can-no-explain-this. A little summary is this: She is rather evil acting at some points, and at others she's afraid and imagines things, so you'll have to deal with a crazy person for a while. That pretty much covers it. OH! And she also is amnesiac. As in, she el-has-no-clue-about-any-secret-agent-stuff.



Roseann becomes un-insane -

Roseann knelt down next to X, her fallen protector, lying with a fatal wound in him. For reasons she couldn't explain, she was scared. But not for herself, for X. Feelings she didn't think she had for anyone, love and care. She didn't know why she was so worried about him, because she hardly knew him. The cyborg that had hurt him was standing over at the throne of metal. He was grinning horribly, in such a sickening way. He stepped down the cold, reflective metal stairs. He walked towards Roseann, his eyes filled with hatred and anger. "What did you do to him?!" she asked weakly, standing up, tears welling in her eyes, and her arms shaking. "What does it mean to you?" he asked in a sick, cruel way. "Just tell me!" she cried, shivering. "I don't believe I want to," he said, frowning, and running to her. He grabbed her and threw her to the side. He walked to her, and pulled her up by her neck, and slammed her against the wall. "You, Roseann, have foiled every one of my plans. I'm sick of it, you know. So now, your brother has payed the price, and soon, you will too!" Roseann had a pure look of shock in her eyes, glancing first at X, then back to the cyborg. "W-what? B-brother?" she strained, heart beating faster. "Yes, Brother. And you know something, Rose? He's gone! Just like what I did to the rest of your family, you are going to get smashed right now!" Roseann looked shocked, memories starting to flood back to her. The pain and suffering she and X went through as children, all of their family taken from them. All of this time, believing it was the darkitect. What he did to Briana's family, and apparently hers. What he did to Briana, what he did to X...
Roseann's eyes turned red, and they had a look of pure anger and hatred in them. Then they faded to black, her normal black eyes. Roseann shrieked in a voice unlike her own, filled with hatred and anger, "Hovawart, you are going to pay!"




Okay, yeah, Roseann is so awesome. I think Hovawart might start running for the hills soon. Yes, I know that her parents had been said to have been killed by the big Darkitect, but I decided to change that, considering that is the only thing that could kick her memory back in.

She sounds angry. But you can't no what happens until we get there!!!

Roseann looked shocked, memories starting to flood back to her. The pain and suffering she and X went through as children, all of their family taken from them. Roseann's eyes turned red, and they had a look of pure anger and hatred in them. Then they faded to black, her normal black eyes. Roseann shrieked in a voice unlike her own, filled with hatred and anger, "Hovawart, you are going to pay!"
