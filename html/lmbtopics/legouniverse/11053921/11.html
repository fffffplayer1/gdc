CHAPTER 14.3: Corrupt

 

-Zaelash-

 

The Darkitect realized his army was losing.

 

"I will NOT leave here without corrupting that nexus!"  He shouted. I prepared for anything he was going to do. He walked up within 20 yards and shot a blast of deadly pure maelstrom from his scepter. It was going to hit the nexus.

 

The shot was going to fast for me to stop it, apparantly Awesome had the same prediciment.

 

"NO!" Awesome cried out as he lunged trying to stop it. Everything felt like a slow motion scene in a movie. Sluggish. I heard a crack, as someone was being smashed in slow mode. I turned expecting to see Awesome being smashed, but nothing prepared me for what I saw.

 

Awesome stood there, covering his mouth in disbelief. I heard a wisper in my head. I just wanted to gaze upon its beauty...  I saw the stromling in front of the leak, on a knee and holding its chest, as if it had a broken heart. 

The stromlings chest area broke open as he crumpled to the floor. A shiny object rolled out... a Crystal. His Shard.

 

He wasnt dead yet but he was about to if we didnt do anything. I shed a tear. I thought I was super awesome, but that stromling trumphed me in one go.

"Awesome," I told him, "Fix the leak. You still have enough Imaginite left to make the exposed pipe infracturable."

 

He looked at me, nodded and went to work.

 

"STOP HIM"  the Darkitect yelled, but there was no more maelstrom to help him out. I stepped foward to face him. 

"You cant defeat me... you can only prevail for a short period of time! I will destroy you easy"  The Darkitect smiled. And I smiled back.

 

"Maybe. But not only am i the Most awesome bat lord ever, but I am also smart." Then I pulled out a flash bang and threw it at the ceiling. It crumpled and clappsed on the Darkitect. His had shot through the rubble. Awesome stood by my side.

 

"Done. now who needed stopping?"

 

"another time then"  the darkitect said then dissapeared. My radio buzzed. Z13 spoke.

 

-Hey Zae! I got a way for you to get back!-

CHAPTER 15.1: Break down

 

-Dino-

 

I was being chased down. The four horseman were on my dino tail.... not that i have a tail. I tripped over a rock and the four horsman stood above me. They raised their bladed Lances for the smash. I closed my eyes tightly.

 

I heard gunfire. What? I opened my eyes to see wisps of smoke, forming the shapes of the horseman. They were gone. I looked up only to see a silhouette of a minifigure standing on the hill. An orange emblem on his shirt. I could not see the face.

 

"Who are you!" I cried out. Any minifigure would be enough to make a difference in defending the Nexus. He nodded, turned, and walked away.

 

"Wait!" I ran after him/her. I got over the hill and they were gone.

 

-Crack-

 

I heard the noise of the ground breaking. I looked behind me and saw a dark figure in a cloak and top hat.

 

"You.... Leave before we destroy you"

 

"Thats not how I remember you. You were once very happy and making 'funny' jokes all the time to your friends..."

 

"But...."

 

" Yes I know why you changed. You worry for your new family. Months of having no threat to the tower made them grow on you hasnt it. You have come to want to protect them at all cost. But we both know that cant happen. I wont destroy you right here right now. You have foiled much of my plans in the past..."

 

"YES! AND I WILL DO IT AGAIN! LEAVE ME ALONE!"

 

" Feisty aren't you? As I was monologing, you will not die here. But your spirit will. Your confidence shall crumble-"

 

I didnt wait for more of this barf colored brick to continue on. I let loose my arrows. The Darkitect stood there as they went harmlessly through him.

 

"What....."

 

"You cannot hurt me. But i will hurt you in more ways than one. As i was getting to, your dirty inturupter, is that Your friends are leading me to the leak. Your little Hero watching the tower is trying to save you, but is walking into a trap. Crushed yet? because ther is more."

 

"GO AWAY!"

 

He dissapeared. His laugh still hanging in the air.

You... Leave before I destory you," I said and raised my weapons.
