CHAPTER 7: INVASION PLAN

 

alone, and determined, i reach the tower. i dont have any orbs left because i had to use them to get the power for each launch pad back on line. the status isnt good. maelstrom have surrondend the base of the tower. it looks like they were getting into single file just like in a elementary school. what could they be doing? then it hit me.

 

"oh no." i wispered to myself. "no noo! not now! i need time!!"

 

the portal to the foot of the tower, and to crux prime, usually guarded from both sides heavily, are wide open. the auto turrets that used to be manned, are not, and were overwelmed, even on auto. i needed to shut down the portal. all of them. if not, the whole place would be invaded, and i would be smashed. forever.

 

"gah!!!" i ran quickly to the nexus, stored some imagination, and ran to the portal. i quickly started to bang on it with my weapons but it was made to withstand that. how could i turn this off? this can NOT be happening! i thought the would try something... i dont know, uh, not as smart. theres no way those stromlings are this smart! i pulled out the imagination and started to build. hands blue. i built a small device i hope could overpower the devices program to always stay open.

 

just then a stromling came into the room.

"GAHH!" i screamed and smashed it. one hit. i used the rest of my imagination to build a wall to block the portal. it wouldnt hold them for long. i plugged in my device, and started typing rappidly. i was an engineer not an inventor. i didnt have the skill fully, but i managed. the wall broke and stromlings filled it. luckly for me, thats when i finished it. the portal closed. there was about 30-60 stromlings in the room. thats a terrible guess because i immediatly went to hitting them with my weapons. one tried to make a run for it, to touch the nexus. because one touch is all it takes. i smashed him with a long ranged weapon.

i stopped them for now. but something made them that smart. and it wont stop here.
