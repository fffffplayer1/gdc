CHAPTER 12.3: DETERMINATION

 

-Z13-

 

They all stimutaniously tilted their heads. Their red eyes turned yellow, I looked up in the clouds and i saw two purple glaring eyes that, if they owned a mouth, would be laughing. 

 

They raised thier weapons, and charged. I grabbed my Daredevil blasters. i knew i couldnt get away from this many elite Maestrom. They would CRUSH me. Just as i raised my guns to fire, a wall of blue pure light shot in front of the maelstrom and held them back like a force field. I stood in amazement, what is going on!?

 

Then a figure walked out of the force field, he/she was completely blue, glowing, and half transparent, with a glowing spark in the middle. He/she didnt have a mouth, but it did have two glowing blue eyes. It looked at me and spoke in my mind. At least i think it did, or else i just went crazy.

 

"This is not where you will perish. You still have much to do, and much to learn. Some things close to you have been and will be destroyed, but you cant let that make you sad, or discouraged. I cannot stay here long, he will find me. But remember this: your role may not seem as great as I, or anyone else has made it sound, but what you do will be important. Dont be mournful of your losses, you will be comforted, and you can, and will acheive what you were born to do. Now to save your friends you MUST clear all maelstrom from nimbus station. Go."

 

He waved his hand and my rank two dardevil gear turned into rank 3 dardevil gear of a unique color of green with special yellow and light blue highlights. He nodded and disapeared. I smiled.

 

Then that smile immediatly turned to panic as the force field dissapated and the Maelstrom surged foward, with Hate and absolutley no fear in their eyes. I spun my new twin blasters, and started shooting the front line down. I blasted a hole through them and ran to the very center of nimus station, they surrounded me, and i smiled. No more childish behavior. This is real, and i have a job to do.

CHAPTER 13.1: Night before maelstrom

 

-AWESOME-

 

Ten minutes to find this stupid leak isnt nearly enought time when your looking through the entire Quadrant Gamma. Five minutes in, and the radio's died. That was problem one. Problem two? if it wasnt easy to geuss, it is maelstrom. Everywhere. It took me a minute to go ten feet. I was contiuously fighting to avoid being smashed. Then all maelstrom seemed to disapear. Did i really defeat all of them?

 

I sat down for a break. I was running out of time. Zae should have stayed instead of Z13. If we ran out of time for the portal, at least Zae could dissarm the Maelstrom death portal guarding the entrance (it could only be deactivated from one side). But i told Z13 to stay because i didnt want him hurt. He was too young to be out here fighting, i knew his little secret of his action figures he keeps under his pillow, and his extreme love for the comic series Dazexus- maelstrom smasher extrordinar! I didnt think he was ready for this.

 

I wandered the suddenly barren quadrant Gamma. I suggested splitting up to Dino before the news of "TEN MINUTES! HURRY!" because i assumed something like that might come up.... and guess who was right! haha! But no matter how much i wanted to take command, Dino was more experienced than me, and seemed to know more. She may not know it, but we have been looking up to her more for leadership since the training inccident. I dont know why...but she seems to handle things well...especially since she seems to have lost all her quirkyness. She used to laugh and have fun but something about her has changed...

 

I felt my heart beat harder...no...thats not my heartbeat....without warning a dragon landed in front of me. Of course this just had to be a dragon i knew quite well. Her majestic purple wings, her gorgeous yellow eyes, her terrifying teeth...that breath definitly smells like her favorite snack!

 

i pulled out my weapons and looked the beast in the eyes, and spoke.

 

"Hello butterscortch"
