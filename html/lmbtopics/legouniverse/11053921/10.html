CHAPTER 13.2: REMATCH

-Awesome-

 

I pulled out my weapons and spoke.

 

"Hello butterscorch."

 

The maelstrom beast stood in front of me, wings extended. Her eyes were filled with pure hate, and eagerness to smash. We once battled in crux prime. It was an epic fight concluding in her getting smashed, but she didnt stay that way for long. She was NOT happy at all.

 

I prepared for any attack coming my way. It was EXTREMELY hard to fight with a resperator and tank of oxygen on your back. I had managed before, because i could fight those enemies standing still. With Butterscorch, I would have to keep moving around trying to avoid her wide ranged attacks.

 

I charged the beast, expecting her to bite, or flap her wings, but she did not. Instead she decided, "what better way to destroy this guy than blast fire at his face right?!" Which was so not cool, no pun intended. I leaped sideways, and landed on my back, using all my muscles (which was almost none) to get up with that tank on my back. Butterscorch was harder here for some reason. I kept dodging attacks, and getting very little blows in, which was NOT like me. In a minute, butter had me almost pinned. I saw one option. There was a ledge into a pit behind her. I needed to push her back. I used my imagination to summon a turrent, and blasted at her wings, so she wouldnt be able to fly. She stumbled backwards roaring in anger up against the ledge. She knew what was happening, and even though flightless, she used her wings to maintain balance. I needed her to fall in off that ledge so i could have the high ground, but she wasnt budging. I took out a flash bang, and tossed it at her feet. The explosion caused the ground around her to crack, and she made the mistake of looking down at the crumbling cliff side. She has to fall down there. I charged her, and pushed the beast off the ledge, she fell down, and I layed on the cliffside, breathing heavily. Then the ledge crumbled and i fell into darkness.

CHAPTER 13.3: Cave of Imagination and Old Friends

 

-AWESOME-

 

I landed on something rough. It was butterscorch. She got up with a furious look on her face. The plan didnt work out. I wanted her trapped down here, not me. There was no way i could climb the cliffside back up and get back to the portal in time. To add to the already difficulty leve 10/10, there was a dragon that needed smashing, making the total score of dificulty level 13/10.

 

I picked up my weapon to fight the monster. She was already injured enough that she should be half the difficulty to destroy than she usually is. I looked around in the cave, the walls were scattered with a light blue crystal with a slight glow, lighting the cavern. Imaginite, but i have never seen it in this quantity at a mine before.

 

The dragon either didnt see it, or didnt care, because she snapped at me. Little did the dragon know (or little did she care) that i could gain imagination from the crystals if i broke them, and with this amount? Taking this dragon down would be as easy as pie. Chocolate creme pie. I took out my Pea shooter, and blasted imaginite on the walls. The room filled with energy. Butterscorch looked around all confused. I put down my blaster, raised my hands, and concentrated. Blue smoke whirled around butterscorch, randomly forming chairs, hammers, tables, and weapons, then I imagined it forming a Giant hammer, and i looked up.

 

"good bye butter." Then i swung the hammer and smashed butter to pieces. I looked at the mess, kinda satisfied. now how to climb that cliff, i wondered. Then a wave of drowsiness washed over me. All that imagining drainded my strength. I couldnt climb that cliff. Then i looked over my shoulder and something caught my eye. Two red eyes glowing in the dark. But these didnt look dangerous. I walked up slowly, and the creature came into focus.

 

There was a stromling. And his T-shirt said "Attack, Get smashed, Repeat"

CHAPTER 14.1: Everlasting Darkness.

 

-Zaelash-

 

I woke up in darkness. This just isnt my day. No fun in Omicron, and then i fall into this lousy pit which there doesnt seem to be any dangerous thing to fight so i can bring back an awesome story about how i heroically- 

 

CRASH

 

The noise echoed throughout  the cavern. I looked around but saw no danger.... In fact, I couldnt see anything. I wandered around shuffling my feet with my arms swinging slowley to avoid anything that may be in the way. This is quite embarrasing, hopefully no one was watching with night vision goggles.

 

"What the...?"  a head was purple smoke swirling around. I took a step back, and heard laughter. Faces flashed all around me. Enemies of past, family members, people i know, and people i knew. Past events projected like videos on purple cloth. Images of battle, showing those who survived, and those i never saw again.

 

"WHAT IS THE MEANING OF THIS!" I screamed out to the darkness. I got no reply but laughter. I started to panic. Was I ever going to get out of this darkness? I kept walking hoping to find an exit.

 

Then a dot of light shined at the far end of the cavern, The laughter faded. I walked toward it. As I got closer, i realized the light was coming from a mini waterfall flowing from an exposed pipe. The liquid was glowing blue.

 

The leak. The whole reason we came outside in the first place. I approched it and stood about 10 yards away. I noticed something there. Someone there. A stromling was sitting on the floor watching the liquid Imagination flow into its little pool. His red eyes big and wide watching, with head tilted. It was the stromling from the Nexus tower, the one with the T-shirt, who didnt touch the Nexus when he had the chance.

 

I approached the stromling and I heard a wisper. I looked over and Awesome was standing in a corner. He opened his mouth to talk but a laugh cut him off.

 

 then the same laugh filled the cavern.

 

"You have lead me right to it. congrats."

CHAPTER 14.2: Cave Battle

 

-Zaelash-

 

Maelstrom filled the cavern. Me and Awesome back toward the leak as the hoard of enemies advanced. T-shirt stromling looked wide eyed and tried to hide behind awesome, was he scared?

 

"ah, you again." The darkitect formed in the cavern as four times the size as a normal minifigure, "How dare you not touch that imagination when you had the chance little stromling! You will be sorry when this battle is over."

 

I had no clue what that was all about. Apparantly this stromling was in trouble. This was a nice stromling, and i was determined to protect it AND the leak at the same time. Awesome hand glowed blue and waved it over at our trackers. They burst to life after having malfunctioned a while ago. I looked at the screen. We were in quadrant X. How did that happen? I didnt walk that far did I? And Awesome came from Gamma. Thats a little ways too.

 

At least now we understood why it said quadrant X. The leak was underground. And now the underground was filled with Maelstrom monsters and two lone defenders. The maelstrom surged foward to attack, Awesome slipped off his book bag and pulled out big chunks of blue imaginite, then smashed them on the ground. I understood what he was thinking (more proof that Omicron stinks: Gamma had imaginite. Omicron stinks, lets face it.) Most minifigures know if Blue imaginite is smashed, you can use the imagination it releases as a boost of imagination you can use, it can help in building stuff, but most of all, battle.

 

I slammed my bat staff sending a shockwave through the enemy lines. I looked up and felt the power the imaginite was lending me. Live bats shot out from my staff attacking enemies. Awesome built his turrent and started firing at the maelstrom. Each shot was coated in imagination from the imaginite and bounced everywhere, destroying maelstrom on contact.

 

The battle was looking like it was going well. Then the darkitect made his move.
