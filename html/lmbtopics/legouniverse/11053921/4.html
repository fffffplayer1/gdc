CHAPTER 9.2: MAZE

 

"Ok then" I said,

 

"your not glad about us, are you" Zaelash started to say, "You think your better than Zaelash the ult-"

 

"ZAE. BE QUIET." Dino yelled at Zae.

 

"ITS ZAELASH TH-" Zae started.

 

"Ok i thinks thats enough Zaelash the blah blah" Z13 said, "Im sure hes happy, just confused."

 

"Ya. What Z13 said." I told them. "So what is this Maze of Imagination?" Dino spoke up.

 

"There is not just one floor to the nexus tower. We are on one of the top floors. Everything below is just emty floors, but your imagination is so strong there that the walls will move to your will. It is designed to keep Maelstrom out, we can bend the walls and add traps, so the maelstrom never get to this floor."

 

I then had a flashback of the first attack.

 

"They found another way. But I stopped that. There next attack should be through the ma-" something cut me off. An alarm. Blaring and making noise.

 

"Well speak of the darkitect, they have broke through the front door and have entered the maze" Z13 said.

 

"How do we control the maze?" I asked.

 

"We cant. Not from here. We need to enter it." Zaelash said.

 

"Lets go. Z13 with me, Zae, you go with...Whats your name?" Dino queen asked.

 

"AwesomeFearsomeNinja, Assembly Engineer, friends call me Awesome" I stated.

 

"ok then. Zae, with Awesome." Dino completed her sentence. We ran into the stairwell to the maze below. We all took out walkie talkies, and went two different ways. Mission: find the maelstrom intruders and make sure they dont make it to the top. Luckly, our Nexus 9's had maelstrom locaters.

 

"Lets make like gandalf in a bat lord outfit..." Zaelash said, "THEY SHALL NOT PASS ZAELASH THE-"

 

"Enough." I said. We were approching some stromlings. They were on te other side of the wall. I imagined the wall dissapearing and it obeyed.

 

"cool" I said. Stromlings were right there. We cut through them easily.

 

"Theres some more down this hallway" Zae pointed out. We ran down the hallway, and ran into a wall that wasnt there to start.
