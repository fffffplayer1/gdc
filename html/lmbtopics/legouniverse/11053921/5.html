CHAPTER 9.3: PATH TO VICTORY

 

"That wall wasnt there before!" Zae started to say before another wall shot up from behind us. 

 

"Its changing. I didnt imagine this, did you?" i asked

 

"No." zae pulled out his Nexus 9 "Yo Dino! did you imagine some wall changes?"

 

-*ksshk* Ya, why? *ksshk*-

 

"Walls just sprang up all around us." I reported. Z13 spoke up,

 

-*kshk* its obvious. We are changing the walls to where we need them. So our changes will disrupt your changes. This will be harder than we *kshk*- 

 

The device stopped. They were under attack. But we got the general Idea. our destined routes may cross. We had to be careful. Me and Zae ran through the new hall and came up to some spiderlings. While in the middle of the batte, a wall sprang up, shooting Zae and a spiderling to the floor above. 

 

"Zae!" i shouted. But he couldnt hear me. I couldnt focus on getting the wall back down because i was to busy fighting. it looks like i was on my own. Another wall came down and more enemies filled the area. I fought back the best i could. but i wasnt doing well. Soon the Floor dissapeared and every enemy including me fell 5 floors. We landed right infront of the front door.

 

I would have been smashed on impact but i had just enough time to imagine a giant fluffy...stuffed unicorn plush to land on? Weird. The Enemies were flowing through the door and surround me. I imagined the door closing and it did. I sat down and just imagined. Traps, Pit holes, Walls, i started imagining just so i could destroy as many as i could. They noticed their numbers dropping and attacked. Not good. I couldnt think while battleing. I was going to be smashed, and with the amount that would come through the door once they opened it, i would stay smashed for a while. 

 

Then  a wall disapeared and Z13, Dino, and Zae burst in, Z13 and Dino started fighting while Zae went for the door. What is he doing? 

"dont just stand there in a-MAZE-ment! fight!" Dino yelled out. I took my weapons and started smashing.

CHAPTER 10.1: Smashable

 

I saw myself in the nexus room. Maelstrom everywhere. We were battling to the fullest. Z13 fell. A dark figure stood tall. And laughed. He threw he into a wall

 

I sat up straight up in bed. What a nightmare. I look over at Z13's bunk. He is alright. Dino was at the door on guard. We stopped the invasion in the Maze. Zae turned the front door into a paradoxal portal, if any maelstrom entered, the would be smashed, with the same energy the are made from. Dino looked over.

 

"Whats the matter?" She asked.

 

"Nightmare. Z13...he fell, there was a dark minifigure." I explained breathing heavily. Dino looked concerned.

 

"Did he have a Top Hat. What did his face look like." she asked. I froze....I wasnt sure about the top hat...but his face.

 

"I have seen him before. He had a Skeleton mouth, and glowing red eyes... But i still have no idea who he is. He was at the Battle of AG. He told me that I thought that i had won." I answered. Dino nodded, looking like she knew something i didnt.

 

"come with me." She said, "I need to show you something." I followed her to the paradox section, where we would get smashed and built back with no pain at all. she led me big portal in the main room.

 

"Have you looked in yet?" she questioned.

 

"Ya, but i didnt see anything"

 

"Try it now." she told me, with that face. I looked in. The whole room disapeared, and i was standing on stones in a vortex, purple smoke whirled around me. That same face flashed everywhere. Then he stood there. Him. The same skeleton face in my dream, advent gardens, and in the spider cave. He had a top hat, a cape, and a septer of maelstrom. I felt something inside getting crushed. I blacked out.

 

I woke with Dino over me. She spoke.

 

"Thats what we all see at one point. Did you feel it?"

 

"Something inside...was...breaking." I said.

 

"Inside us all, is a shard of imagination. You think you can always get rebuilt. But if it breaks, you will be smashed forever. We need to be careful. We cannot let anyone die."

CHAPTER 10.2: TRAINING

 

Me and dino Just talked it out, traded stories before the boys woke. They escaped from the evacuation differently. All three of them were on the gnarled forest ship. They each told a friend to say "here" for them for role call because they needed to go to the bathroom. Then they walked of the ship unnoticed.

 

"Why are you two out here?" Z13 asked.

 

"Discussing todays activities" Dino stated in a firm voice.

 

"Oh. Ok" Zae said with a smile. We headed over to the sentinal training area. Zae pulled me aside.

 

"So WHAT were you talking about?" He questioned. I flat out told him the truth.

 

"We were exchanging stories on how we escaped evac" I told him. He jus nodded and walked toward the training area.

 

"I LOVE THIS!" Z13 said in a high pitched squeal. Dino smiled with a sinister look on her face. She was up to something.

 

"Step right up and show me what you got ZaneZX13" Dino said smirking.

 

"Its just Z13." he replied and stepped up. The game began. A Dummy popped up. Z13 shot his twin blasters at it like crazy. The dummy stood there and finally got smashed. By the time he got to two the timer buzzed. Z13 was sweating.

 

"What was that! My record is like, at least 10!" Z13 complained. I knew that Blue Moon was a Daredevil as well, and she never got below 10 in rank two gear. Something was up.

 

"I switched the level to extreme." Dino said raising her eyebrows, "You will face tough enemies guarding this tower and you need to know what the most efficient way of taking them out is. Awesome, your up."

 

I went to the changing room and put on my best attacking gear. I came out and Bombed my training exersise. Only 5? My record was so much higher than that. Zaelash went next, of course all batlord style and got 7. This was harder than we thought. Then dino came up. She put on the rest of her sentinal samurai gear except the helm, and demolished. the training exersise. Her record? 23. Insane.

 

Z13 stepped up again. but no dummy popped up. Instead it was a stromling.
