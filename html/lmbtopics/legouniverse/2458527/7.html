Chapter 5 - Vortex of Chaos

There we were. Me, SuperSentinel, Fullo Mee, and Relly Strange were all standing on one of the many small chunks that led into the dark, shadowy, and worst of all, chaotic vortex. The chunks were not too small, but they were about moderate-sized, about half the size of Butterscorch's platform. They seemed to extend onward as a pathway to the heart of the Maelstrom. We all took off our faction gear to avoid any suspicion. The further we went, the darker and more purple the skies around us became. It was then that we faced our first obstacle, a Stromling guard.

Guard: Who are you? Why are you coming into the lair of the Darkitect?

Me: Uhh, we are um... the Darkitect called us! He, um, wanted to speak to us about something!

Fullo: Uh, yeah, that's it!

Guard: Hmmm. How do I know you're not Nexus Force recruits in a holographical disguise? Let me see your arms.

We all showed him our arms. Of, course we were infected, so it wasn't a hologram.

Guard: Hmmm. They're purple, they feel like stromling arms, ok, you may pass.

We all went onward. I had a feeling that considering how smart that guard was compared to other Maelstrom creatures, there would be more in store for us than a few easily smashed enemies or dumb guards. I suddenly felt the urge to turn back and betray Bob. I knew this feeling. But it was stronger, more urging. I tried to resist it, but I barely could, for the feeling was stronger every time. And I wasn't the only one, Relly, as we all expected wastrying to turn back so much we were almost dragging him. SuperSentinel was walking slower and slower and almost going backwards as we advanced, and Fullo was the only one who kept us moving onward, but even he had those dark feelings. We finally pushed back the feeling and continued on, going faster than before. Then, in the middle of this chaotic, dangerous, and scary world, there was something that, if I wasn't on the most dangerous mission ever, would have made me laugh. We saw a stromling that said, in his stromling tone, "You look like your quick on your feet! Wanna do a foot race to the Darkitect's Lair?" Relly really wanted to say yes, and, as it was not going to be an obstacle in our journey, we all agreed, and went on the race...

Oliver Sudden's Maelstrom Alter Ego!!

Yeah. I thought I might make a joke in the middle of all the suspense.

Chap 5.2
We raced. And we raced really fast. We jumped over maelstrom rocks, we dodged floating maelstrom chunks, and we, well we just went around the maelstrom trees. Unfortuantely for me, Fullo Mee, the adventurer, was faster than me, but I wasn't gonna let him to the finish line first. I pulled out a quicksicle I had saved up, and ate it. I zoomed faster than Fullo, and by now Relly was in the back. I had only one person left to bypass, my own clone, SuperGeniusSentinel. Because we often thought the same, he too had pulled out a quicksicle and sped past th others. We were going at the same speed, and we each tried to think of a way to pass the other. We were very close to the Darkitect's Lair now. And then, we both made a move, but the moves weren't the same. Or at least, the main idea was the same. SuperSentinel put on his faction gear and created a ravencloud guardian to attack me, and I created a turret. But, both of our plans backfired, literally. They were shooting US! We ducked under some big maelstrom rocks, and waited for the creations to fall apart. They did, and we raced on. But we then lost the race to Relly Strange, who somehow sneaked past the creations while we and Fullo Mee were distracted. We were there. We had arrived. And at one of the most feared places in all of the Lego Universe. We were standing directly in front of the Darkitect's Lair...

There we were. We stood right in front of the dark, shadowy, dangerous, and chaotic place the Darkitect called home. Strangely we had not had the "evil urge" for quite a while. It was as if the Darkitect wanted us to come, but why? The moment we stepped foot inside, a Maelstrom guard stopped us.

Me: SuperGeniusCreator!
SuperSentinel: SuperGeniusSentinel!
Fullo: Fullo Mee!
Relly Strange: Relly Strange!
Guard: Oh... it's you. Come in.

That almost freaked me out. He let us in because of our names? Something was not right. I knew it. And I could tell the others could too. We were led to the throne of the Darkitect. I was getting more scared than ever. We stood there, but no one was on the throne. But then, the Darkitect appeared in a poof a Malestrom. He then spoke,

Darkitect: I believe you are the group of stromlings trying to get uninfected. Oh wait, there's more to it than that. You want to DESTROY ME!
Me: Uh... y-y-yes.
Darkitect: Well, I believe you won't be defeating me, instead, you will be HELPING ME! MWAHAHAHAHAHAHAHAHA!

Fullo: What-t, is it-t?
Me: THE GOLDEN UBER HAMMER!

I reached into my backpack using all my strenght and pulled out the hammer which the mythran had given me. And with it, I tried to hit the Darkitect. But the Darkitect pushed me away, and I felt that all hope was lost, but then...

Bob: STOP, DARKITECT! NOW YOU WILL FALL!
Me: BOB?!? YOU'RE BACK!
Darkitect: WHAT?!? BOB! YOU SNEAKY DOUBLE-CROSSER! I SHALL DESTROY YOU!

Darkitect: Do you really think you can stop me? You'll never defeat me!
BrickMania: Maybe not alone, but perhaps with the help of an entire legion of Mythrans I will!
