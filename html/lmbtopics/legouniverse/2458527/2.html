Chapter 2.4
"...my name is Relly Strange, or at least that's what I used to be called."
Me: So, why do stromlings smash Nexus Force recruits?
Relly: I don't know... we just feel evil, we have this... feeling.
Me: Can't you fight it?
Relly: No, it takes control of us and makes us do things.
Me: You mean... the Darkitect controls stromlings?
Relly: Yes. Hold it for a sec, gotta go smash that recruit over there.
He jumped over and smashed a recruit and returned.
Me: So, about that feeling, what else do you know?
Relly: It becomes stronger and stronger until it's in full control.
Me: Oh no...

Chap. 2.5
I had to get to NT disguised as a Paradox before the Baron took control of me. But what about others? Other stromlings would be left behind. I asked Relly a question,
Me: Do you stromlings want to join the Nexus Force again?
Relly: Yes and no. The Baron controls us now. How can you help?
Me: I think Vanda can un-infect us.
Relly: But how will we get past the recruits?
Me: I know who can help.
I ran to Wisp and came back with marauder suits.
Me: Take this Paradox suit!
Then I realized a flaw in my plan to take them to NT. "All Stromlings vanish and tons of Paradox's come." Bad idea...

What would be a better plan? I had to think for a moment. I had an idea! I put on the Marauder suit and ran into the Sentinel Camp.

Me: Then it's time I showed you something.
