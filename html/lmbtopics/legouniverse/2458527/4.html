Everyone but Relly had pets, so he had to go up the monument instead of using the pet bouncer. At first, our pets ran away from us, but eventually they gave in and we got to the AG Launch Area...

Chap 3.4
By the time we landed in Nimbus Station, I had just about recovered from drinking that notion potion. We were just about to head over to Nexus Jay when suddenly some minifigures started attacking us. I felt the urge to start smashing them all, this time the feeling stronger, but ignored it again. Relly, being a full stromling was trying to hit the minifigures, but thanks to my chain was once again restricted from doing so. To dodge their attacks, I jumped sideways... right into Wenn Wuzzit's Time Machine! Relly was pulled in with me, and Fullo Mee and SuperGeniusSentinel jumped in with me.

Now, I would like to make it clear now, how exactly I have a "clone" named SuperSentinel. Well, every minifigure in Lego Universe can have up to 3 clones, one for each faction. Each one has similar looks or characteristics to the first minifigure, which, in this case is me. So, SuperSentinel, being my Sentinel clone, is in many ways, very similar to me, including characteristics and appearance.

As we traveled through the time vortex, We saw, as expected, a chicken that became an egg, an old man that became a baby, and a lot of different clocks all floating around. When we finally stopped traveling, we ended up back in Nimbus Station, several years ago. A troop of stromlings came and passed by us, not attacking us as they would recruits. Then some stromling mechs came. We smashed them. Then some more stromlings, we let them pass. We continued to smash enemies. Spiderlings, Mechs, Ronin, apes, everything except Stromlings, Stromling Pirates, Hammer Stromlings. There was no way I was going to smash a stromling unless it was never a recruit, like the Stromling Invaders on Crux which were made of pure Maelstrom.
Of course, when I had seen Murgle Blotch, I was hesitant. Should
I smash this guy? He was one of the meanest stromlings there were. But then I remembered something I'd learned from Duke Exeter before: "Murgle Blotch was always mean. He willingly joined the Maelstrom." I decided that since he was an extremely dangerous stromling, and that he joined the Maelstrom on purpose, It was okay to samsh him. And that's what we did. He was at first very confused at why Stromlings were trying to smash him, and for a while didn't attack us. Eventually he decided to start fighting us. But it was too late for him. With one more hit from our weapons, he samshed.
After a while, we decided we should go back to present-day Nimbus Station and that's what we did...

Chapter 4 - The Maelstrom Spy
The stromling-voiced person looked at us. He wasn't purple like a stromling, but he had eyes that glowed purple, and hands with Maelstrom energy around them. It was Bob. Bob the Maelstrom Spy. I was shocked. I'd always joked about Bob being a Maelstorm spy, but I didn't think he actually was.
Me: What do you want?
Bob: I need you to help me. The Darkitect plans to rule the Universe by starting an army of stromlings with Nexus Force gear to defeat the Nexus Force once and for all.
SuperSentinel: Why trust you? You're a spy for the Maelstrom! You put tracking devices on recruits! You spy on everyone!
Bob: I suppose I should tell you the full story...

"Long ago when I was young, I fell in love with Sky Lane. She, however was only intersted in Epsilon, a tall, young, and handsome man. Dr. Overbuild, my younger brother, came to cheer me up. I then had a master plan. I pretended to have a broken heart and the Darkitect came to me. He offered me immortality if I would work with him. I accepted, but I was fooling him. I only pretended to put tracking devices on others. I was really cheating the Darkitect into giving me the ultimate power, being able to use both the Maelstrom and imagination. You see I can use both imagination and Maelstrom, and I'm only partially infected. But the Darkitect has found out I've cheated him. He's out to get me. I need your help to defeat him, only a stromling can make it past the many areas of security to reach... the Darkitect's Lair..."
