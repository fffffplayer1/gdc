Chapter 7 - Where?
We were returning from Nexus Tower, disappointed and annoyed. The Maelstrom's Leader, or the Hooded Man, as I preferred to call him, could be practically anywhere. Dr. Overbuild was told what had happened, and he was saddend and worried... but then again, he always seemed a bit worried. Dr. Overbuild led us to to the Venture League room, the area with the big holographic map. Dr.Overbuild pulled out a remote from his pocket and pressed a button labeled "Full Map". After pressing it, the map changed to a bigger map, with the areas labeled. There were tons of planet chunks on the map, some space stations, and this unusual snowy comet... oh wait, that was Frostburgh.

Overbuild: This is a map of the entire known and charted Lego Universe, as you can see, here is Avant Gardens, and here is the Venture Explorer which is still miraculously flying in circles, and-
Me: He look, it's YouReeka!
SuperSentinel: Ooh! It's the castle world!
Overbuild: Excuse me, but will you please sit down and listen? Anyway, we are going to send people everywhere to find the leader, and once he has been found, you will go there. You will be assigned to search Forbidden Valley.
Me: D-dd-Did you say Forbidden Valley? But that place is so big and confusing! I don't really wanna-
Crash: Come on, Super! I'll be with you all the way!
Bob: As will I!
Dino: Yep, all of us will!
Me: *sigh* Ok, I'll go, but I really hate that place... too many pits, and dragons, and that scary vision I had when that ninja guy told me that Baron was controlling the Maelstrom, and all sorts of scary stuff-
Everyone except me: OK, WE GET IT!
SuperSentinel: Sorry.
Me: Hey, why are you saying sorry?
SuperSentinel: To confuse you of course!
Me: Oh yeah, I can do a lot better job that that! y6tybhered
SuperSentinel: That's not confusing, that's banging your head on the keyboard!
Me: How did you know I was doing that?
Max Falcon: Ugh! Can you guys just be quiet for once and stop making silly puns?
Me: Eh... no.
Dino: This is going to be a loooong day...

Epicness! Awesomely funny chapter! I wonder what happens in FV...

-Crash, the adventurous Venture League-er
