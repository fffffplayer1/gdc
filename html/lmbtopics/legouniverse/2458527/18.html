Yes, i know it's been practically a month since my last chapter, but here we go! Now where was I... oh yeah, we were heading into Forbidden Valley...

Chapter 8 - Dragon Attack

We were all traveling in our rockets to the Great Tree in Forbidden Valley. WE knew what dangers lay ahead in the most mysterious and scary chunk of Planet Crux. Even Crux Prime could not match this place's dark and scary mysteries. When we landed at the Great Tree, we expected to see some ninja masters, some ninja vendors, and some other ninja stuff. But when we turned away from the launch pad and over at the tree, we saw a very frightening and horrible sight. The Great Tree was under attack by hundreds of Maelstrom dragons, ronin, Maelstrom horsemen, and even some Maelstrom Ninja!

Me: Guys, do you know what time it is?
SuperSentinel: Three o' clock?
Me: No, it's time to...
Crash: ATTACK!!!!

We all started attacking the Maelstrom together in a huge fight. It was 1,000 Maelstrom vs. a small team, but armed with our rank 4 gear (or rank 5, I didn't really keep track of what rank it was), it was a somewhat fair fight. However, the Maelstrom was very overwhelming, and it seemed like we wouldn't be able to clear the place of the enemies. Suddenly, Max Neutron had a plan. He pulled out his pet dragon, Inferno, and used him as a distraction to the Maelstrom. While they were distarcted by the roaring pet, we ran past the Great Tree, smashing all Maelstrom in our way. Inferno caught up with us, hundreds of Maelstrom chasing us. We planned to get past the Maelstrom force field, which you could only get through if you had a Maelstrom-infused ninja hood. So, that's what we did! We put on our Maelstrom-infused ninja hoods and ran and ran. Eventually we made it through the force field, and the ronin and horsemen couldn't follow. However, the dragons flew over the force field, and continued to chase us. Several dragons were better than several dragons and hundreds of Maelstrom, I thought, and I decided to go offensive.
I shot my main weapon at the dragons, and it hit one of them, dealing some damage. Dino used her powerful weapons against the dragons, and they roared angrily. Max Neutron rewired my Gadgets extreme, and the electro-field that would normally be around me, shot up as a laser beam at the dragons, dealing a lot of damage. The battle continued, and one of the dragons smashed, and I picked up all the Maelstrom bricks that dropped, knowing a certain someone would need them. We continued fighting until only a few dragons were left, who soon retreated.
We continued on to the Paradox Refinery, hoping that it would be in better condition than the Great Tree. When we arrived, we were relieved, as it was still standing. However, the place was under attack by tons of Maelstrom. Brick Fury, was preventing the from getting too close, though he seemed to be weakened. The ninja masters were there too, they had probably retreated from the Great Tree. However, it looked like they needed help, as the dragons were getting closer. Max Falcon was first to reach the refinery, and began attacking Maelstrom like crazy. Crash and Max Neutron arrived second, and me and the others arrived third. I gave Brick Fury the Maelstrom bricks, and using them, he was replenished with power and began to fight like never before. I used my Inventor helmet to create 3 Make-shift buddies, who all went and smashed ronin like crazy. Numb Chuck used his out standing ninja skills to fight two dragons at once, and everyone else fought like Lego maniacs, and the Maelstrom began to lose the advantage.
Then it hit me, where was Bob? He had disappeared again, and the last time I saw him was while we were fighting at the Great Tree! ...The Great Tree! Of course! I suddenly realized Bob must have stayed to continue fighting! I had a feeling he wasn't going to be okay alone, and I ran out to save him. I was pursued by a lot of enemies, but a few ninja vendors helped me make it through. When I arrived at the Great Tree, I saw Bob, tired as 100 year-old minifig. But I didn't see any Maelstrom! Well, except for one infected newbie minifig, who had apparently gotten lost. I gave him a map an d sent him to Nexus Tower, where he would be okay at the Paradox Stromling Daycare Center. (hehe!) I then ran to Bob, and he said:

Bob: *yawn* amazing what you can do with a basic shortsword... with a 100 charge-up damage, of course! *yawn*
Me: One of these days I'm going to find out how you do all these insane things!
Bob: Hehe!

Chapter 9 - The End... of the Search

Me and Bob decided to make sure the Maelstrom didn't try to return to the Great Tree, so we pulled out some spare bricks and built several weak, but effective turrets. Bob drank a UBER Notion Potion for some energy and was ready to run back to the Refinery. (How and where did he get an UBER Notion Potion? I wish I knew.) We figured that the turrets would be able to hold off the Maelstrom long enough for the Ninja masters to be able to return there.

Back at the Refinery, we found that things were only getting worse. A hundred dragons were attacking the place... and the worst part was... these were no ordinary dragons... they were Maelstrom INVADER Dragons from Crux Prime! Bob immediately set to work on building something... I wasn't quite sure what, though. I too, began building something, but I knew what I was building. I was building a large turret to use against the invader dragons. When I finished it, I turned it on and it began firing madly at the invader dragons. It was strong, and it was dealing some heavy damage, but unfortunately, one of the dragons blasted a large fireball at it, and it exploded. I tried to build a new one, but I soon realized I was too low on bricks to complete it. That was when I turned around and saw what Bob was trying to build... a giant laser cannon! I immediately set to work on helping him, while firing my Inventor weapon at the Dragons at the same time. Max Neutron soon began helping, and within moments the laser cannon was complete. Bob jumped into the control area, and aimed the laser cannon straight at one of the dragons' heads. He fired, and the dragon was badly hurt. It fell down onto the battle field in front of Brick Fury, and he finished him off. Dino threw a large sword at the Dragon, and it screamed in anger. Crash built a bouncer, and bounced himself high enough to land right on top of a dragon, which he soon smashed. The battle raged on, and we were winning again. Soon there was only one dragon left, who soon fell down and smashed. It looked like it was over... but we were wrong. Soon an entire legion of Maelstrom Invader creatures began to attack. We fought like never before, but they were too overwhelming. "Where were all these creatures coming from?" I wondered. The Maelstrom creatures continued to overwhelm us, and I was getting tired. I bean to wonder if this battle would ever end, as more and more Maelstrom creatures came.
Then, out of nowhere, a minifigure wearing a jetpack flew over the dragons and smashed them like I would smash a Stromling Mech in Avant Gardens... or in other words, very easily! As it turned out, it was Nexeus! The same mysterious minifig who saved me and my friends before! Dragon after dragon, ronin after ronin, with the help of him, we defeated every single Maelstrom creature that came within 300 feet near us! was about to ask Nexeus a few questions about himself, but he flew off almost immediately, and soon I saw a rocket leaving Forbidden Valley. He had left again, and in my mind, "Who is he? Where is he from? Why is he so secret?" kept popping uop in my mind. But seeing the victory over the Maelstrom we had just won, I put away those questions and ran to my friends to celebrate our victory.SuperSentinel and I high-fived, Crash shouted "YEEEESSS!!!", and Bob simply clapped and smiled happily, with a smile that was older than the Futuron Astronaut Colony on the Lego moon... which was founded around 1980 or so... that's one of the first years that minifigures existed!
But enough talk about Bob's smile and Futuron, we had a mission. We continued our search, smashing all Maelstrom we came near on our way. Soon we had searched everywhere... except one place. And that one place was one of the scariest places in all of Forbidden Valley... the Dragon's Lair! We knew what we had to do, and we hurried onward there. By now the Ninja Masters had returned to the Great Tree, and Brick Fury was doing his usual Maelstrom smashing. We hurried to the entrance to the Dragon's Lair and all looked at each other.Seeing how much Maelstrom we had to fight earlier at Great Tree and at the Paradox Refinery, we feared that what we were about to face would be much worse, so we all bought some power-ups from the ninja vendors before going in.
Then we made the move. We entered the Dragon's Lair, all of us holding our weapons tight. When we reached the inside, we soon realized that all our fears had been right. Instead of just Burnshout, Torchblight, and Blastbreath being there, 50 invader dragons and an army of Maelstrom was there also. We knew we were no match for such a large army and decided to get out of there immediately and get help. But unfortunately for us... one of the Maelstrom creatures had locked shut the entrance! We were seemingly doomed, as the dragons circled us and surrounded us. And then, it happened. We saw the most scary thing in the Dragon's Lair ... the Hooded Man... also known as the true ruler of the Maelstrom!
He was dressed in dark purple clothing, including a tattered dark cape and a black-purple hood, that shadowed his face. All you could see of his sinister face were two red eyes that had a very evil look to them. He laughed with a low voice, and looked as if he was about to do something to us... something horrible...

I tried to build a new one, but I soon realized I was too low on bricks to complete it. That was when I turned around and saw what Bob was trying to build... a giant laser cannon! I immediately set to work on helping him, while firing my Inventor weapon at the Dragons at the same time. Max Neutron soon began helping, and within moments the laser cannon was complete. Bob jumped into the control area, and aimed the laser cannon straight at one of the dragons' heads. He fired, and the dragon was badly hurt. It fell down onto the battle field in front of Brick Fury, and he finished him off. Dino threw a large sword at the Dragon, and it screamed in anger. Crash built a bouncer, and bounced himself high enough to land right on top of a dragon, which he soon smashed. The battle raged on, and we were winning again. Soon there was only one dragon left, who soon fell down and smashed. It looked like it was over... but we were wrong. Soon an entire legion of Maelstrom Invader creatures began to attack. We fought like never before, but they were too overwhelming. "Where were all these creatures coming from?" I wondered. The Maelstrom creatures continued to overwhelm us, and I was getting tired. I bean to wonder if this battle would ever end, as more and more Maelstrom creatures came.
Then, out of nowhere, a minifigure wearing a jetpack flew over the dragons and smashed them like I would smash a Stromling Mech in Avant Gardens... or in other words, very easily! As it turned out, it was Nexeus! The same mysterious minifig who saved me and my friends before! Dragon after dragon, ronin after ronin, with the help of him, we defeated every single Maelstrom creature that came within 300 feet near us! was about to ask Nexeus a few questions about himself, but he flew off almost immediately, and soon I saw a rocket leaving Forbidden Valley. He had left again, and in my mind, "Who is he? Where is he from? Why is he so secret?" kept popping uop in my mind. But seeing the victory over the Maelstrom we had just won, I put away those questions and ran to my friends to celebrate our victory.SuperSentinel and I high-fived, Crash shouted "YEEEESSS!!!", and Bob simply clapped and smiled happily, with a smile that was older than the Futuron Astronaut Colony on the Lego moon... which was founded around 1980 or so... that's one of the first years that minifigures existed!
But enough talk about Bob's smile and Futuron, we had a mission. We continued our search, smashing all Maelstrom we came near on our way. Soon we had searched everywhere... except one place. And that one place was one of the scariest places in all of Forbidden Valley... the Dragon's Lair! We knew what we had to do, and we hurried onward there. By now the Ninja Masters had returned to the Great Tree, and Brick Fury was doing his usual Maelstrom smashing. We hurried to the entrance to the Dragon's Lair and all looked at each other.Seeing how much Maelstrom we had to fight earlier at Great Tree and at the Paradox Refinery, we feared that what we were about to face would be much worse, so we all bought some power-ups from the ninja vendors before going in.
Then we made the move. We entered the Dragon's Lair, all of us holding our weapons tight. When we reached the inside, we soon realized that all our fears had been right. Instead of just Burnshout, Torchblight, and Blastbreath being there, 50 invader dragons and an army of Maelstrom was there also. We knew we were no match for such a large army and decided to get out of there immediately and get help. But unfortunately for us... one of the Maelstrom creatures had locked shut the entrance! We were seemingly doomed, as the dragons circled us and surrounded us. And then, it happened. We saw the most scary thing in the Dragon's Lair ... the Hooded Man... also known as the true ruler of the Maelstrom!
He was dressed in dark purple clothing, including a tattered dark cape and a black-purple hood, that shadowed his face. All you could see of his sinister face were two red eyes that had a very evil look to them. He laughed with a low voice, and looked as if he was about to do something to us... something horrible...

DUN DUN DUN!
