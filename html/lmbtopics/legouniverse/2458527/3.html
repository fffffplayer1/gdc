Chapter 2.2 The Way I Was Infected

They just charged at me. They started hitting and and scratching me. It was a terrifying experience. I wanted to run. But I was cornered. They also pinned me down. I couldn't find a way to get out. Then- total blackness. I felt a dark feeling- like I wanted to ruin and destroy my friends. A black surrounding came around me. Then a ghostly face stared at me. It was the Darkitect. "YOU CANNOT ESCAPE! YOU ARE UNDER MY CONTROL NOW!" As I looked around, there were tons of innocent Paradox Researchers, defense mechs, pirates and admirals ("DON'T YELL AT US, YOU GIFFY! WE WANT TO BE FREE FROM YOUR MAELSTROM THINGY!" An admiral yelled. A pirate screamed, "I WANNA SEE CAPTAIN JACK KNIFE AGAIN!!!!!!! I MISS HIM!!!!!" The Darkitect just yelled at him back "CAPTAIN JACK KNIFE IS NO LONGER CONTROLLING YOU! I DO, NOW!!!!) I looked around me. They were all purple, like the Stromlings in real life. There was also five dragons. They looked afraid. They tried to breath fire, but all they breathed was a purple-like fire. There was Stromling Apes, too. They just banged their fists against the ground, and screamed their own ape language at the Darkitect. He then looked at himself. He expected himself to be a normal Stromling, but he was a different kind. He looked like an infected Nexus Force recruit, which he was. His helmet, purple. His armor, purple. His pants, and weapons, purple. "HA HA HA! YOU WILL MAKE A NEW KIND OF STROMLINGS, MY MINION! I SHALL CALL THEM "STROMLING RECRUITS!" HA HA HA HA!!!!!" His laugh was not really much of a laugh at all. In fact, it sounded more like a grunt. An admiral came up to me. "This is the place where the destroyed enemies go! I was a Stromling Admiral in Gnarled Forest once, the Darkitect told me, but I got destroyed by a Rank Two Inventor. His name was SuperGeniusCreator. He then sends us back into the worlds where our Stromling forms belong, obviously, as Stromlings.
