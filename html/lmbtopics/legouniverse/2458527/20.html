‎07-11-2012 03:21 PM

I guess that since my readers are back, I'll post chapter 10! And mods, I know it is long, but please approve it! I took me over an hour to write!

Chapter 10 - Epic Maelstrom Battle
The evil hooded figure raised his hands and struck Maelstrom lightning at us. Fortunately, he missed. He shot more lightning at us, but we managed to dodge each strike. Bob used his modified basic shortsword and smashed several dragons, but the Hooded Man just kept making more. I tried to fire my weapon at him, but he just disappeared in a puff of Maelstrom fog and reappeared elsewhere in the cave. We were outnumbered 1,000,000 to 1, and it looked like things weren't going to get any better. The Hooded Man began to fire huge blasts of Maelstrom at us, and along with the flaming breath of the dragon invaders, it caused us to be unable to defend ourselves much longer. I knew from my former experience that getting hit by one of those Maelstrom blasts would turn someone into a stromling instantly, and the fact that we were in the middle of a Maelstrom-infected area didn't make it much better.
All hope was lost, and it seemed as if we had lost... but then, the most surprising thing happened. Bob put away his weapon and pulled out a... walkie-talkie! The Hooded man was slightly confused, and was about to strike Bob when all of the sudden...
BOOOOOM!!!
The ninja masters burst through the door, and began smashing the Maelstrom invaders as if they were just a house of cards. But they weren't the only ones who arrived... one thousand Sentinel Rank 3 Knights came pouring in, smashing the Maelstrom like crazy. Paradox troopers came in, along with a legion of Assemblies. Bob speaked into the walkie-talkie once more, and a piano fell on the Hooded Man! I almost laughed, but this was a serious situation, so I held my laughter inside. We were no longer outnumbered, just evenly matched. And so that's when we all said:
ATTACK!!!
The Hooded Man's legions of dragons were being destroyed almost instantly. Mythrans came in and began to use their Uber Hammers and Jetpacks against the Hooded Man, and his anger grew red hot. No matter where he reappeared in the cave, tons of Nexus Force soilders were there. The Maelstrom was losing, and the Hooded Man did not have enough time to create more dragons. Then, in desperation, he disappeared. According to what Max Neutron had learned from a imagination science book, he could not reappear anywhere that was further than about 1,000 bricks away. He also could not reappear anywhere that was imagination-filled. This was the same for all Maelstrom creatures who could vanish and reappear. Of course, there aren't very many Maelstrom creatures who can teleport, so the theory had not been proved... until now.
Bob received a call on his communicator that the Maelstrom leader had reappeared near the ninja force-field which was supposed to protect the Paradox Refinery from Maelstrom Horsemen. The one who had called was none other than Echs Ray, who had stayed at the Refinery along with Brick Fury while the many Nexus Force troops rushed into the Dragon's Lair. Bob quickly called several Assemblies together and they built several large bouncers.

Bob: Quickly, onto the bouncers!
Me: Ok!
SuperSentinel: Let's go!

Me, my friends, and several others bounced on the bouncers, which caused us to fall on another bouncer, which bounced us to another one and another, eventually leading to the force-field. Other Nexus Force troops were there too, barely holding off the Hooded Man. By now, the Dragon's Lair was free of Maelstrom, and tons of the Nexus Force troops there began rushing to help us, while others stayed and built defense turrets. The Hooded Man had already destroyed the force-field, and tons of Horsemen came rushing through. Soon more Nexus Force backup came, this time in the form of Nexus Battle Rockets. The Hooded Man was more angry than ever, and fired pure Maelstrom energy at everything he could, but he was being overwhelmed. Soon, another spaceship came... and who should be in it but the Four Faction Leaders themselves! Hael Storm piloted the large spaceship, and Duke Exeter and Vanda Darkflame were standing at it's side, waiting to jump out and attack, and Dr. Albert Overbuild manned the special controls. I noticed a large hole in the side of the ship, and then that hole revealed the ship's identity... it was the Venture Explorer!
Bob stood in awe for a moment, and then smiled. Bob pulled out a few bricks and immediately built a large bouncer and bounced up onto the ship. And who do you think was standing where he landed? It was Sky Lane, who winked at Bob, and Bob winked back.

Sky Lane: Welcome back to your favorite spaceship, Bob!
Bob: Thank you, Captain Sky Lane. It's nice to be back again. Attention, everyone! The Venture Explorer's Captain and her co-pilot have returned... AND NOW WE SHALL CHARGE INTO THE HEART OF THE MAELSTROM AND SAVE THE UNIVERSE!!!!!

Sky Lane and Bob hurried to the controls and began to turn on the turbo while the Faction Leaders jumped out and began fighting like Lego maniacs. Bob and Sky Lane then flew the ship straight into the Maelstrom dragons, firing a large imagination cannon at them. The Hooded Man soon realized teleporting anywhere would be useless, and fired his most powerful Maelstrom powers at the Nexus Force troops. hundreds of troops had already destroyed the Maelstrom portals from which the horsemen were coming from, and only the Hooded Man and a few troops of Maelstrom remained. Bob received a call on his communicator from Wisp Lee that the Maelstrom in Avant Gardens had finally been cleared, and the Maelstrom caves were finally under control. Captain Jack Knife called him and told him that most of the Maelstrom there had also been cleared, and Sherland Powers called him and told him that the Maelstrom mining by the skeletons had finally been stopped on Crux Prime. Bob pulled out a megaphone and announced the good news, and the Hooded Man was so angry it looked he was going to explode.
No matter how much firepower the Nexus Force troops threw at the Hooded Man, he shileded himself with his Maelstrom powers. Even the powers of the Mythrans was not enough to stop him. Then, a certain figure stood still, and looked at the Hooded Man, as if he knew how to stop him. That figure was Baron Typhonus, and in the middle of the battle he just stood, staring at the Hooded Man.

Baron Typhonus: I made a mistake when I created the Maelstrom. I tried to bend the rules and create a chaotic being that would obey my every command. Along with the Spider Boss, I created something else, I created the very thing that contains all the power of the Maelstrom... you, the Hooded Man. There's something I've learned from that experience... I've learned that chaos causes only destruction, not creation. I made a mistake, and I'm going to fix it! ...Duke Exeter, Alebert, Hael, Vanda? I hope you will remember me by what I am about to do...

Baron Typhonus charged at the Hooded Man with all his strength, with an imagination staff in his hands. The Hooded Man fired his Maelstrom energy at the Baron, and the Baron fired imagination at the same time, and with the combined of all the Nexus forces, the Nexus Rockets, the Mythrans, and the Baron...

BOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOM!!!!!!

A large explosion pushed all of us to the edges of Forbidden Valley in a blinding light! It was so large, it spread throughout the Crux System! When it was over, we looked around and saw something many had not seen in a long time. There was no Maelstrom on Forbidden Valley! Soon the faction leaders heard calls from Avant Gardens, Gnarled Forest, and Forbidden Valley, all saying the Maelstrom creatures there had suddenly been uninfected or instantly smashed! Even Crux Prime was suddenly completely Maelstrom-free. All the stromlings in Nexus Tower had suddenly been uninfected! We soon noticed something that was just as amazing. All the parts of Planet Crux were coming back together!
Someone was missing, however. Baron Typhonus had apparently smashed when he charged at the Hooded Man.

Bob: Well, it looks like the very same person who started this mess fixed it.
Overbuild: Yep, it looks like he did.

We all looked around, and were all silent for a moment...

About 5 months later, In the middle of the newly constructed Nexus City, there stood several silver plaques, each with the name of a Nexus Force hero. Among them stood a plaque with the name "Baron Typhonus" written on it. It had an inscription on it that read: "A true Nexus Force hero". Vanda Darkflame and the other faction leaders stood there, and smiled.

Me: Well, Nexus Alpha Team, it looks like our work here is done!
Crash: Yep, we've done it!
SuperSentinel: We answered the call!
Me: We saved imagination!

THE END!

Epic chapter! Are you going to write another story, or is this the end?

-Crash, the adventurous Venture League-er
