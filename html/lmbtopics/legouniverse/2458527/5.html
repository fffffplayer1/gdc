Chap 4.2
I looked at Bob. The Darkitect's Lair?!? If there was one thing I feared the most, it was the Darkitect. He was the leader of the Maelstrom, and most dangerous being in the Lego Universe. I wanted to get uninfected first, but he had said that "only a stromling can get through the many defences." So, I had a choice. Get uninfected or defeat the Darkitect. And, I chose. Defeat the Darkitect. I could get uninfected afterwards. Then Bob said:

Bob: Here, I'll help you all the way to Crux, but when it's time to go to the Darkitect's Lair, I'm afraid you're on your own. Good luck, and take this. Use it only when you really need it.

He handed me a Imagination bomb. Dr. Overbuild had once told me that they were very powerful, and very difficult to make. WE hurried to the launchpad, and Fullo and I reconfigured the settings on our rockets so they would take us to Crux. Bob, SuperSentinel, Fullo Mee, Relly Strange, and I, were about to journey to the heart of the Maelstrom. And what a journey it would be...
