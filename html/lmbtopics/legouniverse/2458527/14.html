Chapter 5 - The Discovery of Dissapointment
Me, SuperSentinel, Dino, MaxFalcon, Max Neutron, Crash, and Bob all set out for the Violet Vortex. We were equipped with powerful jetpacks, and Incomplete but functional Rank 4 gear. Before leaving, I tried out a few of the Rank 4 Invnetor skills. One was the shoulderpads' Gadgets extreme with extended time. I tried it, and it worked quite well, but it recharge. Max Neutron attached some special batteries to make it work. Another skill was the improved robot creatin helmet which made a robot that had had a damage combo of 4+4+1. It was supposed to be five at the end of the combo, but it had a glitch. My beehive surprise was pretty cool, as it now had more bees and a charge-up. My main weapon had an awesome damage combo of 4+1+6, which was supposed to not have a 1 but instead a 5, but it too had a glitch. The shirt and pants were more comfortable and were heavy duty, causing my total armor health and imagination to be, along with the imagination bricks and life points I had gotten from achievements... 15 health, 25 armor, and 60 imagination. I felt pretty much invinsible, minus the fact that some of my gear was a little glitchy. Everyone else was enjoying their partially glitchy gear, and we were all ready to set off for the vortex when, someone tapped on my shoulder. It was Nexeus. He simply handed me a hammer and flew off. And it wasn't just any hammer, it was a Golden Uber Hammer!
We then took off our rank 3 shoulderpads and put on our jetoacks, and flew onward...
When we arrived, we began running, or flying I should say, towards the old Darkitect's Lair, we ran and ran and ran and then... "HOLD IT FOR A MOMENT!" someone said. "You look like you're quick on your feet! Want to race to the old lair... and get me out of here?" I almost laughed crazily, it was the same stromling from last time I was there.

Me: Sure! And of course I 'll get you out of here, you wanting to go to Nexus Tower?
Quikon Yorfeet: Oh, yes! My name is Quikon Yorfeet, I want to get uninfected!
Me: Sure, but we haven't quite figured out- *boom* -sounds like they blew up something at the Nexus Tower Lab again. As I was saying, you'll need to the uh... Paradox Stromling Care Center as they have not been able to build a machine to uninfect minifgures yet.
Quikon: Ok! Do you have a jetpack to spare?
Crash: I do! Here, now just fly that way to Nexus Tower. I'll call Vanda Darkflame. *dials phone number*
SuperSentinel: I had no idea you had a phone.
Crash: I didn't, Max Neutron built me one!
Neutron: Want one?
SuperSentinel: Sure! *gets handed cell phone*
Falcon: Enough chit-chat, we're supposed to be investigating the lair, so-
Me: Let's race! Say, who has a horse? *everyone raises hand* Ok, then let's ride them! Cya! *races off*
Dino: Oh no you don't! *races off*
Neutron: Time to try out my mini-racecar! *races off*

We all raced onward top the lair. It reminded me of last time, only this time was even even cooler! Max neutron sped ahead of everyone with his mini-racecar, but I wasn't going to lose to him. It was time for plan b. I pulled out every one of my pets and pulled out a wagon model. With a few extra gears and studs here and there, I quickly built a lightweight wagon pulled by the power of all my pets, and a imagination engine in the back, as well. I sped ahead of Neutron, but then someon else passed me, Crash! Crash had built a high tech hovering spy vehicle powered by a IMAGINE-777 ENGINE, currently the most efficent imagination engine built, designed by Dr. Overbuild himself. He sped ahead of me, as well as Max Falcon, who had fed his horse tons of quicksicles. SuperSentinel passed me also, but it seemed his vehicle was starting to slow down. Dino riding her extremely fast pony and Max Neutron in his mini-racecar were the only ones behind me. I knew it was time for plan c, plan all-engines-go! I fed my pets quicksicles, built a lighter imagination engine, And attached two rocket engine parts to the back of my now super-powered imagination wagon. Iturned them all on, and I sped ahead of everyone except Max Neutron and Crash. We all were going at the same speed, looking at each other with faces that seemed to say "I WILL WIN." Then the climax came, and we passed the finish line... in a three person tie! Dino came right behind in second place, with Falcon in third. Oh, and in a smaller verison of my unusual racing vehicle was superSentinel in fourth, who unfortunately didn't have enough bricks to get his racing vehicle to work right. We were at our destination. And then I realized something shocking, where had Bob gone? Just when I was getting woried Bob hopped out of the now in ruin maelstrom lair.

Bob: What took you so long? I've been waiting 30 minutes!
Me: WHAT?!? How do you get here so fast?!?
Bob: I took a shortcut, it looks like you took the longcut.
Me: *sigh* So, found anything?
Bob: I have only bad news.
Me: Dun, dun, dun...
Crash: What is it?
Bob: I have found traces of super concentrated Maelstrom that have led out of the lair and died out streching onward into space. Unfortunately, from all I have discovered, the Maelstrom's leader could be anywhere in the Lego Universe.
Dino: Oh no...
Me: Dun, dun, dun...
Falcon: Quit the special effects, Creator.
Me: WHAT!?! NEVER! Dun, dun. dun...
Bob: *sigh* Let's get back to Nexus Tower...
