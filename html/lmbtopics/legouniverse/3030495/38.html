The Legacy of Night Flash- Chapter 5.2

When I walked inside, it looked exactly the same as we left it. Slick, white walls, creepy jail cells with Correllians growling inside of them... I wondered why the Prison hadn't been touched. I walked down the hall towards where the screaming was coming from. As I walked, I heard muttering inside the cells. "Heehee. He's here. Hehhehh." I shivered with fear. The Prison had suddenly gotten colder. I kept on walking. "EAHHHH! EAHHHHHHHHHH!" The screams were getting veeery closer. The hall was... well, BORING. Nothing special happened. No ambush. No Mythrans. I finally reached a left turn that led me to 2 brown doors. I opened them, of course. "Ah, welcome to the party, Night." What I saw was a horribble sight. I was now inside a huge room, with a huge "bucket" of acid. The group were hanging by thin ropes over it. Faine stood next to it. A small radio on a little table was playing Opera. "YOU..." "Ah-ah-ah! If you attack, they go down!" He held up a small knife. I pulled out my pistol to be safe. "Don't be such a party pooper, Night!" He flicked his wrist, and I screamed in pain. My pistol fell to the floor. "I'll let your friends go if you allow me to retrieve the potion to revive the Queen." "NIGHT! DON'T DO IT!" Screamed the group. But I had a plan. "Okay... Have it your way, Faine, but your feeble attempts at ruling the Universe WILL be stopped!" He smiled. "Alright..." He pressed a small button on a remote. BOOM! "Ta-ta!" He dissappeared. Suddenly, the group (Including Nalia) fell to the ground. "NIGHT! WHAT DID YOU DO?!" Said Seven. "I have a plan." BANG! "But first, we need to get outta here." "Why?" "'Cause the whole Prison's going boom." BAAANG! The doors behind me burst into flames. I picked up my pistol. I looked up at the cieling. Brick. Walls? Brick. "How do we get OUT..." "Watch." Said Seven. He got a small drill-like device out of his pocket and started drilling a hole in the brick wall. In about 1 minute, there was a hole for us to escape. "GO GO GO!" He screamed. I wondered how that drill had made such a big hole... We ran out into the open where the remains of Betsy were. I looked behind me, and watched the Prison explode. Jakk, Seven, and Nalia stared at me. "Where're Ned and Dwight?" Asked Seven. "At the mansion, hopefully..."
"It appears the game is over before it even begins."

The Legacy of Night Flash- Chapter 5.3

"So, what's your brilliant plan?" Asked Nalia. "Find a way off here. I'm guessing Faine went to Junker Mountain to- OW!" I fell on the ground. Behind me was a beat up Sullavix, with his gas mask still on. "Why.. does... this.. HAPPEN?!" I yelled as I got up and kicked him in the shins. I grabbed him by the legs and started swinging him repeatidly on the ground, and heard his legs starting to break. "LEAVE... ME... ALONE....!" I shot him in the back as he laid down on the ground, and he went completely still. I ripped his gas mask on. "WHY ARE YOU FOLLOWING ME?!" "Because..." I gasped. His face was lined with scars. His lips were pure red. His hair was spiked up and colored purple. "Because you need the TRUTH!" He gave a nice ol' right cross to the face. I shrieked in pain, grabbed his wrist and twisted it. Nalia ran over, punched me in the face, and shot Sullavix with a dart. "Dang it... You will know... the truth... You will meet more enemies..." And with that, he passed out. I looked at the group. "Bring 'im to the mansion?" They nodded.
***

Luckily, the mansion had NOT been burnt down. I had to lug Sullavix all the way to the mansion, which was a serious pain. The mansion, actually looked the same. We opened the huge doors and walked into the bright kitchen. We all sat at the kitchen table, and waited for Dwight and Ned.
"It appears the game is over before it even begins."

The Legacy of Night Flash- Chapter 5.4

An unconcious Nero Sullavix laid on the floor by me. We waited. And waited. And waited. "Well-l-l, boys, it's been fun." Me, Seven, and Jakk looked at Nalia as she ran out the mansion doors. "Wonder what she's doin'..." I muttered. "She's probably got a way to get off Pandora." Said Jakk. We all looked towards the doors. "RUUUUUUUN!" We ran out the doors. And, of course, there she was. In a gunship. Flying away. ''NO! DON'T LEAVE US!" "Don't worry! I'll distract Faine! If you come with me, then we'll all just be blown to smithereens! Plus, I would poison you, 'cause I just NEED to get back to my roots!" And with that, she flew away. We sighed. After awhile in the kitchen (again) Ned and a tired Dwight came down the stairs. "Umbraros left us for dead. Also, we got a nice little Correllian who we're gonna interrogate.'' I said. At this, they sat down with us, and Sullavix started to stir. I pointed a knife at his throat. "Tell us what you're trying to achieve." He laughed. "Oh, achieve? I don't want to achieve anything. I want to tell you... THE TRUTH." I yelled in frusturation. "STOP SAYING THAT!" He suddenly went quiet. "Yeah, that's right. Who're you working for, huh?" He muttered something unintelligible. "WHO. ARE. YOU. WORKING FORRR?!" He looked up at us, who were all standing up now. ''A group.'' "A GROUP? OKAY, THAT TELLS ME A LOT!" "I will tell you the truth... about everything." "WHAT TRUTH? WHAT IS THIS 'TRUTH?' JUST TELL ME! EVERYTHING YOU KNOW! EVERYTHING ABOUT THIS 'GROUP,' AND WHY YOU'RE FOLLOWING MEEEE!" Everyone looked at me. I was in a rage again. "I work for a large group of minifigures and Correllians alike. We call it the Resistance. We want to restore the Universe to its former glory... Just LOOK at it now! And I know all-l about your little tantrums, Flash. In your dreams, you see Typhonus, and you let Typhonus slowly start to POSSESS YOU! That would not do... You chose wrongly! I needed you to capture me, so I could TELL YOU ALL THIS! THE RESISTANCE WILL PREVAIL! AHAHAHAHAHAHAHAHAHA!"
"It appears the game is over before it even begins."
