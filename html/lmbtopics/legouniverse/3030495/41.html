THE FIRE SPREADS SNEAK PEEK (To sustain your hunger and SPOILLEEERRRS!)

I looked all around us. Destruction... Avant Gardens... once a peaceful place, now a battlefield. The god (or should I say terrorist) had destroyed everything. Their armies of Resistance were shooting everything... Everything was lost. Seamus? Whearabouts of him were unknown. He was presumed dead. Seven? MIA. The buildings collapsed. I struggled to my feet. "Gotta get up, Flash..." I told myself. But somehow I couldn't. I felt like we had lost. I laid on the burning grass, armor on my back. Suddenly, I saw a shadow, moving towards me. "This is the end," I thought. "Ah, Commander Flash! Nice to see YOU'RE alive." It was Seven, holding up a beat up Seamus. "SEVEN!" I ran towards him, ignoring the falling pieces of metal that fell from the old Paradox shelter. "Commander, hush up. WE GOT SOME RESISTANCE TO TAKE CARE OF."

Anyone see this?

The Legacy of Night Flash- Chapter 5.7

"Attacked you?" Miller looked very troubled. "Yeah, attacked me, mostly. He wanted me to know the 'truth.' He gave me a choice, but I apparently chose wrongly. He then tried to smash us, but he claimed that he was working on his own." Miller rubbed his chin. "Oh, and his name was Nero Sullavix." "Nero Sullavix... truth... on his own..." Miller looked up. "I... can't seem to figure it out. What I can tell you is that the Resistance is evil. They need to be stopped. But right now, they don't matter. The fate of the Universe is in your hands. Now, go. I can lend you a ship." I was dumbstruck. How did he know we needed a ship and we were on a quest to save the Universe? He apparently noticed our puzzled looks. "I know many things... that, and I have cameras all around the City." Oh... that was why. "Uh... okay, Mac. See ya!" Seven smiled. So, he led us down the ladder and outside and to the backyard. There, in the backyard... was a ship. But not just any ship. A ship... that looked very similar to the Dominion. Except better. It had the tower. The sleek, black surface. The door that led inside the Den. I was expecting something worse than this. "Uh... I'm taking a look inside." SO... I ran inside, through the door.
"It appears the game is over before it even begins."
