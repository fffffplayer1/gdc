The Legacy of Night Flash- Chapter 3.8

The waitress sat us at a little cobblestone table, gave us menus, and asked us: “What do y’all want for a drink?” I looked at the “drink” section of the menu and told her: “Topsicle Pop, please.” She looked at Ned. “And for you, kind sir?” “I’ll have a Pit Pat Smoothie, please.” “Thank y’all! I’ll be righ’ back, don’t you worry.” She walked into the kitchen. “Well, Ned…” The Mythran’s stomach rumbled. “Right.” I mumbled. “What was that?” He asked. “Nothing. Hey, Ned, could I go-“ “Sure.” I ran up the wooden ramp, into the store room, and went inside the “Men’s” room.
***
Before walking back to our table, I started washing up. I looked at myself in the mirror. My fedora was rumpled and burnt. All of my clothes were ruined. I hoped Dwight, Seven, and Jakk bought some nice clothes… I strided back to our stone table and sat down in the wooden chair. My Topsicle Pop was sitting on the near right of my side of my table. It was in an old-fashioned glass cup, with white foam and rainbow colors in the glass. The Pop looked amazing. I took a sip, and I suddenly noticed how low on supplies we were. “Ned,” I said, as I put down the drink. “We don’t have many supplies. We’ve got a lot of money, so we should get more than just clothes after this.
Then we’ll go find Helpton.” He nodded, and took a sip of his Pit Pat. His smoothie looked amazing, too. There was a silly straw in it, with chopped up bits of candy and fruits inside it. The color turned out to be a purple/red. The waitress returned, and asked what our orders were going to be. “Could we have a couple more minutes, please?” I asked. “Sure thang, hon.” She walked away, and I started looking at the “lunch” section. Hm… The Carrot Top Special. I could picture it in my mind. A large plate of the different carrot types from all across the Universe, mixed with sweeteners and meat… Not good enough for me. Coal Fried Chicken. Chicken from the planet of Frex, heated on coals and comes with Crackle Fries… That sounded good enough for me! The waitress returned as if on cue, and I ordered the Coal Fried Chicken, Ned ordered the Figgy Ade.
"It appears the game is over before it even begins."

The Legacy of Night Flash- Chapter 3.9

It took quite awhile for the waitress to return. After about forty-five minutes, she came back with our food. I hungrily chowed down on the Coal Fried Chicken. The thing Ned ordered, Figgy Ade, was like a jello-substance, with Figgy meat inside it. (Figgy meat is a vanilla flavored meat, that's very tender.) "Hoo, that was GREAT food!" I said, slamming down my fork. "Same here-" Suddenly, a robot- a Hubber, I guessed- Walked in with two others behind it. The ones behind the leader looked like a normal Hubber, but the leader was silver with real eyes and spikes on its back. It was dragging Billy Helpton. The Main Hubber threw down a very hurt Billy, and started firing at customers. "Must. Defend. Master." It blasted. "FAINE!" I screamed. Ned started throwing blue energy out of his hands into the robots, but it did no good. Suddenly, Helpton got up and cut the drone Hubbers' heads off. "Y'all ain't hurtin' nobody!" He yelled. I ran over by my friend and hit the Main Hubber. THAT, was a mistake. "OWWWW!" I screamed. "6-0-1, fire at will." The robot said. BANG! There was a rumble in the Diner. "EVERYONE, YOU HAVE TO-" Dwight and the rest ran in, holding Hubber heads. "Miss me?" He asked, with sarcasm and an annoyed face. Seven started throwing mini-grenades at the Hubber. Jakk used his club wisely. (Yep, they all had new weapons AND shopping bags in their hands.) Very easily, the robot was defeated. But... "I OBEY." About one-hundred Hubber drones walked in, and pointed guns at us. "Uh, new plan... RUN!" I yelled. Me and the group, clothes, weapons and all, ran out the Diner, Hubbers trailing behind. "LEAD THEM OUT! OUT OF THE AREA! ONLY THE MAIN HUBBER COULD TAKE DOWN THE FENCE WITH THE PRESS OF A BUTTON! I have his fence-button in my pocket. LEAD THEM OUT!" I pressed the fence-button, and the fence went down. The group just made them follow them, quickly ran back inside the Hub, and I pressed the button again.
"It appears the game is over before it even begins."

"YES!" Screamed the minifigures of the Hub. I ran back to the group, which were standing by the fence. "That was quite an ordeal!" Huffed Ned. "Yep. We got some clothes, food, and weapons for you guys." Said Jakk. "But I think we should leave, very soon.." Everyone nodded in agreement. " I think I'm ready to try on some new clothes." I said, under my breath.
***
After we had gone inside a random changing room in a shop, I wondered at my clean clothes. I still had my dirty fedora on; but the rest of the clothes were different. I wore a black and white striped shirt that had the symbol of the Hub on it; a corn stalk. My pants were clean black shorts, and my new weapons were some guns, a Shark Tooth Spear, Bronze Converters, grenades, and others. Jakk wore the same thing he had when we first met him; he said he liked his normal outfit. Seven had a camo hoodie, with some plain beige pants. Dwight and Ned still wore the same things. "Well," I said, standing in front of a rickety cottage. "This is a nice 'pit stop.' " "Yes, but what about those Hubbers? They apparently work for Faine." Said Seven. "I'm guessing we're safe for now." I said. Suddenly, Billy Helpton ran towards us. "Hey y'all! I got ya a ship. Y'all gonna stay here for 'while?" He asked, out of breath. "Well, Billy, we're kinda rushed on time. Where's that ship?" As if on cue, a small, cozy ship landed. It looked like an old fashioned rocket- except it was bigger. There were blue stripes and corn-stalk symbols on it, and writing on the side that read "Betsy." "HOO, BOY, I CAN'T WAIT TO TRY THIS THING OUT!" I yelled.
