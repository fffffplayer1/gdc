The Legacy of Night Flash- Chapter 4.2

I froze in my tracks. "What's wrong-" But Seven saw it too. At least a dozen red eggs were hatching something sinister. I was about to say "RUN!" but strangely my vocal cords wouldn't work right. The reason for that is because I was looking at small tentacles bursting out of the eggs. Everyone stood frozen on the spot. 12 creatures burst out of the eggs. They all had small tentacles coming out of their heads, huge claws, and hunched backs. Suddenly, a minifigure came striding out of the facility to start petting one of the Hunchbacks. "I am proud to represent Miss Umbraros." The minifigure's hair was straight up, and had a rounded, purple face. His clothes were old-timey, with buttons and a tie. "My name is Mundo Jecarachi. And I will be the cause of your DOWNFALL!" The Hunchbacks responded by running towards us. "NOW'S A GOOD TIME TO RUN!" I screamed as I pulled a sword out and stuck it up one of the beast's chest. It fell to the ground. "GREAT IDEA!" The rest yelled in unison. I quickly smashed another Hunchback that was about to tear Dwight in half. "Thanks." He muttered. ''No prob. LET'S GET TO UMBRAROS, THOUGH!" We bounded up the path, the beasts tearing after us with Jecarachi in the lead. Jakk ripped one of the Hunchback's tentacles off and whipped it in the face. "Nice one, Jakk!" He nodded, and, soon enough, we ran through the glass doors of Umbraros's lab. For some reason, Jecarachi stopped, and shook his head. We watched him run back through the glass doors. "PHEW!" We said. The lab looked... hm, how should I put it... depressing? Creepy? Cobwebs hung everywhere. A carpet that had a stain on it that looked strangely like blood. A chandelier laid broken on the cold floor. "Let's get a move on." I said, slightly shaken. We went inside the elevator, pressed the button that would lead us to Nalia Umbraros, and braced ourselves. When we opened the elevator doors- "Ahhhh! You MADE it. I thought you wouldn't even be ALIVE, but I can still work with that.'' Nalia was setting in her musty office, at her table, on a computer. My mouth gaped open. Why didn't she just... GET RID of us, right here, right now?
