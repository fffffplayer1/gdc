The Legacy of Night Flash- Chapter 5.5

Sullavix fell silent, so I threw him across the floor, fuming with anger. I turned around to face the group. I was slowly being possesed? I doubted that. But... what if that was true? I didn't know if there even WAS a "Resistance!" "What should we do with him?" I asked, pointing towards Sullavix. ''Shoot 'im." Said Seven. So I did what he suggested. "Now, what?" ''Get off Pandora, like you said, Night. But before that, we may need to find this 'Resistance'...." After a long time, it was settled: We would find as much information as possible about the Resistance. "Let's go!" I said. So we ran out the doors of the mansion. Everything looked the same outside, nothing special, so we kept running. (We were following Seven.) "Where we going?" I asked. "You'll see." It seemed to drag on forever. Finally, we came to a stop at a rickety shop. A sign next to it said "The Steamer's Shop." Steamer's Shop... That sounded familiar... but why? At first, my impressions of the shop were little. But then... I noticed it looked a bit steampunk-y. It had many rusty gears turning attatched to it. Old automatons were littered around it. Also, old machines were in front. "Let's go inside.'' "Wait! Seven, why are we here? This could just be another trap." I said. "It's not. An old friend of mine lives in here. He should be able to tell us a bit about the Resistance- he practically knows everything about Pandora.'' He smiled at us, and knocked on the wooden door. "Who is it?'' "Greg!" "GREG?" A smiling old man opened the door. He had a dirty, torn coat on, with goggles and a wrench in his hand. ''It's been so long!"
"It appears the game is over before it even begins."

The Legacy of Night Flash- Chapter 5.6

''I know, Mac!" Apparently, the old man's name was "Mac." ''Oh, Greg! Excuse my rudeness. Come inside!" Mac beckoned to us, so we all walked inside the shop. I was still troubled about 2 things: How I had seen Steamer's before, and the fact that I might be slowly being possesed by Typhonus. Anyways, we walked in. The shop was veery small, and an old rickety bed was carefully placed in a corner. There was a metal, dirty, greasy counter in front of us, with many mechanical devices and such laid on it. There was also a wooden ladder, that apparently led to the "house" part of the shop. We were in a sideways line, and Mac closed the door behind us. ''So... My name is Mac Miller. And you're Greg's friends!" He smiled warmly at us. "Now, we should probably get upstairs and settle down, eh?" "Anything you want, Mac.'' We went up the ladder, and stepped into the "house'' part of the shop. There was a fireplace, a small kitchen, some rocking chairs, board games, and small little robots, scattering at the sight of us. "Please, sit down!" Everyone settled down by the fireplace, and Miller sat in a rocking chair. "So what brings you here, Greg?" ''Oh-h-h... That's a long story. But... since you know Pandora so well, do you know anything about the Resistance?" Miller's smile faded. "The Resistance...? The ones that think they can restore the Universe...? They have made so many minifigures lose so many relatives... They've been quiet for quite some time. Why do you wish to learn about them?" I looked at Miller. "One of them attacked us."
