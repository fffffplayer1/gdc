The Legacy of Night Flash- Chapter 1.1

"Hey, Nighty. How're things?" I looked at her, getting ready for the thing that I have anticipated. "Guess you're good, then." She kissed me. I blushed, and turned away. My girlfriend, Rean, was in terrible danger. All because of the one and only, Night Flash, mostly known as Nighty. We were sitting in a room. A blank, white room, with nothing in it besides two small chairs. It felt like we were in a prison. "Rean. I'm... sorry." "Don't be. You didn't cause this and you know it-" "OH YES, I DID!" I snapped. The walls of white seemed to whisper, "You are doomed.'' I decided to look at Rean, and truly noticed what she was wearing. A black jumpsuit with purple pants, and her hair was braided along her smooth, pale back. ''Look, Night, all minifigures make mistakes. ESPECIALLY me and you. And listen, will you?" She gave me a snotty look, so I listened. "It's not your fault you caused that wreck. It's mine and Shock's.'' I stared at her with horror, a gaping expression on my face. "Y-you? YOU caused the wreck? At Junker Mountain?" She nodded, answering my question. Then I noticed the glass door was slightly cracked. "I can get us out of here, Rean. Just watch." I told her before she could retaliate. I pounded against the clear glass three times, and it finally broke into dozens of small, sharp pieces. ''NIGHTY! I sure hope no one heard that..." "The Junk-guards probably did. Let's get the brick outta here.'" I took her hand in mine, and we ran down the white, spotless hallways. As we ran, I noticed ANOTHER thing. Tons of minifigures were trapped in a similar cell, like ours. But they were being punished. Badly. We kept running until we reached the door. "Ahh. Time for some fresh air." I opened the door, and saw two guards waiting for us. I punched them in the face and knocked them both out. "Go! Let's go!" I screamed. Junker Mountain is a peculiar place in the Universe. A mountain made up of scraps and junk, and the Mythrans decided to make it their own personal place. To plan. To scheme. To scheme about how they are going to wipe out the minifigures, and the Mythrans will rule the Universe. Inside the place, there are catacombs, command centers... All spotless and clean. In the bottom of the wretched place, there is a throne. A throne where a cruel ruler sits... We ran by the piles of metal and food, and kept running until we saw a path down the mountain. That's when I heard her scream.

"Always remember I am a hunter." -Captain Night Flash
"It appears the game is over before it even begins."
Monster Hunter extraordinaire, DC comics fan, gamer.
"I AM the meat." -Deadpool

The Legacy of Night Flash- Chapter 1.2

"REAN!" I turned around rapidly to see a Junker-Mythran holding a Venture League pistol to my valued Rean. "Nighty!" She screamed as loud as she could, making her vocal coards shake. "So, this is the girl that 'accidently' destroyed TWO of our Command Centers. Sir Faine is most... unpleased. If you don't surrender, the girl will be smashed." The nasty Mythran sneered. My action was immediate. I punched the wretch in his masked, blue face. He also immediately dropped Rean, and decided to fight back. "You dare punch a Mythran? WE are the reason Typhonus is smashed, NOT YOU! We are the reason you are still alive. Maybe, that was a mistake!" Before he could reach his pistol, I threw him off the Mountain. "Rean, let's GO!" We continued making our way down the heap of endless junk, tripping on scraps as we went. Ripped up books, metal, rotten food... And... A minifigure? "Night, wait. I think there's someone laying over here!" I ran to the right of the steep mountain, away from the perfect path made from the Mythrans, and towards Rean. "What's wrong Rean?" "LOOK!" I looked down, and shuddered. A minifigure, helpless and VERY badly hurt, was laying under a heap of ripped pages that all said "Failure." "Ugh... I... failed." The helpless minifigure looked like he had been thrown off a mountain... "Sir, we are gonna get you out of here." I kindly said to the stranger. Rean picked him up, and slung him over her shoulder. "Let's keep moving, Night. We should get back to the Goldlands." Ah, the Goldlands. My homeland. In space. After a few, long hours off endless "Failure" papers, we made it to the bottom. "Oh, no..." Sighed Rean. My mouth gaped open. This is the explosion. Where, apparently, Rean and Shock destroyed one of the Command Centers. A huge crater replaced it, and everything was burning around it. Luckily, our ship, the Dominion, survived. The Dominion is more of a smaller ship, but nice and comfy. A small ship with a needle-"watchtower" attached to it. That is the control room. In the control room, there is an elevator that leads out of the watchtower and into the "Den." The Den of the Dominion is, really, my home. There are three beds, one for me, one for Rean, and another for a stranger. The beds are in the very back of the dome shaped room. In the front, there is a kitchen table and things that are in a regular house. Except, most of the Den is black and, well, my favorite color, aqua. "Wow, Rean. Let's get inside the Dominion. NOW." "Right. I'll nurse the stranger in the Den, you navigate the ship. Got it, sweetheart?" "Got it. Let's get OFF this planet, into the Goldlands!" I went up the elevator into the control room, set the course for the Goldlands, and "blasted off."

"Always remember I am a hunter.'' -Captain Night Flash

A blank, white room, with nothing in it besides two small chairs. It felt like we were in a prison. "Rean. I'm... sorry." "Don't be. You didn't cause this and you know it-" "OH YES, I DID!" I snapped. The walls of white seemed to whisper, "You are doomed.'' I decided to look at Rean, and truly noticed what she was wearing. A black jumpsuit with purple pants, and her hair was braided along her smooth, pale back. ''Look, Night, all minifigures make mistakes. ESPECIALLY me and you. And listen, will you?" She gave me a snotty look, so I listened. "It's not your fault you caused that wreck. It's mine and Shock's.'' I stared at her with horror, a gaping expression on my face. "Y-you? YOU caused the wreck? At Junker Mountain?" She nodded, answering my question. Then I noticed the glass door was slightly cracked. "I can get us out of here, Rean. Just watch." I told her before she could retaliate. I pounded against the clear glass three times, and it finally broke into dozens of small, sharp pieces. ''NIGHTY! I sure hope no one heard that..." "The Junk-guards probably did. Let's get the brick outta here.'" I took her hand in mine, and we ran down the white, spotless hallways. As we ran, I noticed ANOTHER thing. Tons of minifigures were trapped in a similar cell, like ours. But they were being punished. Badly. We kept running until we reached the door. "Ahh. Time for some fresh air." I opened the door, and saw two guards waiting for us. I punched them in the face and knocked them both out. "Go! Let's go!" I screamed. Junker Mountain is a peculiar place in the Universe. A mountain made up of scraps and junk, and the Mythrans decided to make it their own personal place. To plan. To scheme. To scheme about how they are going to wipe out the minifigures, and the Mythrans will rule the Universe. Inside the place, there are catacombs, command centers... All spotless and clean. In the bottom of the wretched place, there is a throne. A throne where a cruel ruler sits... We ran by the piles of metal and food, and kept running until we saw a path down the mountain. That's when I heard her scream.

"Always remember I am a hunter." -Captain Night Flash

The Legacy of Night Flash- Chapter 1.2

"REAN!" I turned around rapidly to see a Junker-Mythran holding a Venture League pistol to my valued Rean. "Nighty!" She screamed as loud as she could, making her vocal coards shake. "So, this is the girl that 'accidently' destroyed TWO of our Command Centers. Sir Faine is most... unpleased. If you don't surrender, the girl will be smashed." The nasty Mythran sneered. My action was immediate. I punched the wretch in his masked, blue face. He also immediately dropped Rean, and decided to fight back. "You dare punch a Mythran? WE are the reason Typhonus is smashed, NOT YOU! We are the reason you are still alive. Maybe, that was a mistake!" Before he could reach his pistol, I threw him off the Mountain. "Rean, let's GO!" We continued making our way down the heap of endless junk, tripping on scraps as we went. Ripped up books, metal, rotten food... And... A minifigure? "Night, wait. I think there's someone laying over here!" I ran to the right of the steep mountain, away from the perfect path made from the Mythrans, and towards Rean. "What's wrong Rean?" "LOOK!" I looked down, and shuddered. A minifigure, helpless and VERY badly hurt, was laying under a heap of ripped pages that all said "Failure." "Ugh... I... failed." The helpless minifigure looked like he had been thrown off a mountain... "Sir, we are gonna get you out of here." I kindly said to the stranger. Rean picked him up, and slung him over her shoulder. "Let's keep moving, Night. We should get back to the Goldlands." Ah, the Goldlands. My homeland. In space. After a few, long hours off endless "Failure" papers, we made it to the bottom. "Oh, no..." Sighed Rean. My mouth gaped open. This is the explosion. Where, apparently, Rean and Shock destroyed one of the Command Centers. A huge crater replaced it, and everything was burning around it. Luckily, our ship, the Dominion, survived. The Dominion is more of a smaller ship, but nice and comfy. A small ship with a needle-"watchtower" attached to it. That is the control room. In the control room, there is an elevator that leads out of the watchtower and into the "Den." The Den of the Dominion is, really, my home. There are three beds, one for me, one for Rean, and another for a stranger. The beds are in the very back of the dome shaped room. In the front, there is a kitchen table and things that are in a regular house. Except, most of the Den is black and, well, my favorite color, aqua. "Wow, Rean. Let's get inside the Dominion. NOW." "Right. I'll nurse the stranger in the Den, you navigate the ship. Got it, sweetheart?" "Got it. Let's get OFF this planet, into the Goldlands!" I went up the elevator into the control room, set the course for the Goldlands, and "blasted off."

"Always remember I am a hunter.'' -Captain Night Flash

The Legacy of Night Flash- Chapter 1.3

It's harder than it looks, you know, to drive the Dominion. "Rean, how ya doing down there? Is the injured minifigure okay?" My voice blasted through the intercom in the Den. "Yeah, he's getting better. I got his name, too." "What is it?" "Jakk, right?" The minifigure bobbed his head up and down. "Alright. We got a crew, now. Me, Jakk, and you, sweetie." I said, with sarcasm on the tip of my tounge. I was navigating the Dominion closer and closer to the Goldlands. But I knew, on the way, we would probably meet unexpected company... I saw through the glass window in front of me many, many beautiful things... Large frigates, the constellations... A gate? Yes, the gate. A huge, golden gate was staring us right in the face, with a blue "substance" in between it. The Gate to the Goldlands. "Rean, we've finally made it!" All of us cheered, including Jakk, who was healed. The Dominion went into hyper-drive and pulled us through the Gate. Finally. We had made it. TO MY HOME. All around us were large space-houses floating near golden constellations, hence the Goldlands. Pink bursts of clouds were up above, and minifigures could be heard having fun inside their houses. It's time to ask this "Jakk" some questions, I thought to myself suspiciously. I went down the elevator and walked up to Jakk, who was wearing some teared, brown rags, and had blonde hair. His pants were untouched, and they were red. "So, Jakk, what happened back there? At the Mountain?" "Well... it's a long story-" "TELL ME!" I shoved him against the wall and punched me in the eye in retaliation. "Guys, guys!" Rean stepped between us before we could do anymore damage. "Look, guys, if you wanna know what happened on the Mountain, they were... testing me. Testing me to see if I could be their personal slave." Suddenly, we saw an unfamiliar ship docking ours. A Mythran-ship.

''Always remember I am a hunter." -Captain Night Flash

The Legacy of Night Flash- Chapter 1.4

"THERE'S SOME MYTHRANS BOARDING! EVERYONE, HIDE!" I screamed. I didn't know where Jakk and Rean hid, but I hid IN the elevator. Since there was a window in the elevator, I peeked out of it to see what the Mythrans were doing. Their ship is a long, huge ship with a needle point at the front. A bridge came out of the ship and attatched to our "door" to the Dominion. "Sir Faine, should we enter?" A small, feeble Mythran asked a hooded figure. "Yes." The Mythran threw back his hood, and, yes, it was Faine. He looked like any other Mythran, except with a long, dark crimson cloak and hood. Also, he was a very muscular Mythran, and had black eyes. So it was, indeed, Theed Faine, the leader of the monstorous Mythrans. The group of savages closed in and barged through the door, unaffected by space. "Ah. THERE is the girl. And, look, a little friend." The nasty Faine picked Rean and Jakk up and threw them against the wall. I heard the disturbance, and went outside the elevator to their rescue. "THERE is the boy. Get him. They will be arrested for their acts of rebellion!" A second after Faine said this, his group of jailers attacked me. I shot two of them with my pistol, and they fell smashed on the Den floor. "Stop attacking! Let me talk to him..." Faine walked slowly up to me, and clutched my face. "Look, 'little rebel,' the Mythrans are trying to help. It's been ten years since I destroyed the Maelstrom, and the Mythrans decided to give you peasents a chance. You destroyed one of my loyal servants. You and your girlfriend obliterated some Command Centers on the Mountain... But! We believe in SECOND CHANCES. You pledge loyalty to us, you won't be smashed. Deal?" He gave me a smug look on that blue face, covered with a black mask. "Hm... Let me think-" I punched him on the ground, and shot all of his team. "YOU surrender." He laughed hystericaly, and suddenly, his eyes turned bloodshot. Faine blasted blue energy into me, and the last thing I remembered is Rean getting knocked out.

"Always remember I am a hunter." -Captain Night Flash
