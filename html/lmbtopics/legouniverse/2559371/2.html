November 19, 3:00, Maelstrom Tower Beaches
Chapter 6: Patching the Pieces together
I crawled under one of the beach spikes, hoping this awful scene was just a nightmare, one I could awake from. As I was hiding, I saw gruesome scenes. One of my fellow comrades had his right arm and leg blasted off, and he was walking slowly towards me, when a Maelstrom bullet pierced his head and toppled him over, never to wake again. I curled up into a ball, where old memories started to flood in. I remember playing with my brothers and sisters, and the warm smile of my mother’s face. Remembering these memories gave me renewed strength, and I cued my comrades to charge, at the foul Maelstrom. As we charged, a bullet pierced my leg, and I fell down, screaming in agony, and succumbing to darkness.

November 20, Forest of the Faithless
Chapter 8: One False Step
I woke up on with my leg bandaged like a mummy, as I recall only yesterday that I had a battle wound. Wait, this place doesn’t seem familiar…, it’s probably a forest, one that I’ve never been instructed to know. “It’s the Forest of the Faithless.”, said one my commanders, Sergeant Tofus. “This forest used to be lush, and green, but now, it’s just a barren wasteland with swarms of Maelstrom here awaiting some hapless adventurer.” I mentally agreed, and the Nexus 554th pushed on. We didn’t have a map to guide us here, so we just walked in circles around the cursed forest. We then set up base camp around some kind of purple tree, and then we suddenly heard a buzzing noise. “Did you hear that?” said one of the soldiers. All of a sudden, a swish could be heard, and we looked at the soldier who asked the question, with his head and arms cut off, and infected with Maelstrom. Stromlings started sniping us from all directions, and a commander told us to take cover, before he was silenced by a bullet. We were being ambushed.

November 21, Forest of the Faithless
Chapter 9: Help from an Unlikely Source
We were being ambushed from all directions, and stromlings opened fire without mercy. The soldier right next to me was being ripped apart by Maelstrom bullets, one piece at a time. The Nexus 554th was being demolished by stromlings, and there was nothing I could do about it. I tried to take aim, but before I could load my gun, I was pierced and blinded by a bullet, and I suddenly felt intense pain in my right eye. I fell backwards, and covering my eye, as the agonizing pain swept over me. It seemed that all was over, until, a sudden burst of light pervaded throughout the forest. Everywhere, stromlings started running around in circles, then to be smashed by an unknown source of light. I looked up, and it was a miracle. The Nexus Air Force brought us support, and I could see one pilot saluting to me. I saluted back, hoping that he would see it. Now, what should I do… The only thing I can do now, is to bring back and reunite the Nexus 554th into an army that would be unstoppable.

END OF PART ONE

November 23, Plains of Despair
Chapter 11: Crossroads
“That town,” I said, “That town is what are we are going to take back.” I knew there were many helpless villagers just waiting to be infected and smashed by the Maelstrom. Or even worse, they would become the Maelstrom themselves. I pondered over this, when a soldier, Private Sebastian, spoke up. “I don’t want to go there and get smashed! There must be some way we can go back!” he said. I held and aimed my revolver at him and said, “You either come with us and die a hero, or leave and live as a coward in shame. Your choice.” The soldier sighed, and reluctantly agreed to come with us. “Let’s take back Chambois!” was all that I said. “Fight on!” A large cheer came from the regiment, and we marched towards the village where we would meet despair and certainly… death.

November 24, Chambois
Chapter 12: Face to Face with Death
Entering Chambois sure scared the wits out of everybody. For one thing, we didn’t know if stromlings were taking aim at us right this very second. When we entering, a minifigure’s head was on top of a sword, warning us to not to even take a single step on here, and the head had the symbol of the Maelstrom on it, the Baron’s grin. The Nexus 554th marched triumphantly in, only to find a few villagers were scrambling around and others crying over numerous bodies of villagers, most of them already showing signs of infection. One particular villager rushed me and knocked me down before I had a chance to react. He said, “They’re coming. They’re coming to get us, and you. Particularly, you.” “What do you mean?”, I asked. The villager pointed west, and I turned my head slowly, only to find uniformed stromlings marching toward us, each with a gun capable of mass destruction. What was even more disturbing, was that numerous stromlings were maneuvering Maelstrom Tanks, Panzers and Tigers, that would blast even the most armored minifigures to bits and pieces.”That is what is coming.”, the villager said. Then he stabbed himself in the chest with a Maelstrom infected knife, and he was smashed in front of my face spontaneously. “No!”, I shouted! I then heard the clattering of the dreaded Maelstrom Death Machine, a machine invented by the Baron himself that infects figs instantly, against the pavement of the road to Chambois.”Nexus 554th!”, I shouted! “Hear me!” The whole regiment looked at me, awaiting for orders. “We’re going to attack that Maelstrom army,” I pointed,” Head on!” “So get in position, aim and ready your weapons, and get ready to give the Maelstrom a punch in the face!”, I ordered.”To arms!” Every soldier pulled anything he could fight with, something that would aid him in a fight with a stromling. “Fight for Imagination and freedom!” “Fight for what you believe in!”, I shouted at the top of my lungs.”Attack!” We rushed towards the Maelstrom and charged at it with the fury that would be unequaled in the Universe.
END OF PART TWO

PART THREE-Vindication
November 24, 3:00, Chambois
Chapter 13- Faith
Perspective of Lance Corporal Tooth
I was ordered to attack the Maelstrom from above, and I first thought I had to become a bird. Then I realized I was ordered to become a sniper, smashing Stromlings in secrecy. I climbed to the top of the Town Church, and I hid right next to one of the windows, portraying some mysterious minifigure standing ever victorious over a bunch of stromling pieces. Anyways, I loaded my Elite Long Barrel Sniper Rifle, and starting picking off stromlings after stromlings. “Bless my heart,” I muttered, “and bring justice to all those who bring evil.” I smashed a stromling with my Sniper Rifle. “Vindicate all those who have perished in battle against the forces of evil,”. I sniped another stromling, smashing it instantly. “And bless those who have brought justice to figs.” “Fight for Imagination and for what you believe in.” I then looked down, and saw a Maelstrom tank with its barrel aiming directly at me. It was a Maelstrom Panzer. “Panzer Tank!”, I shouted. “Run!!” The last thing I saw was an inferno specifically for me.
Main Character Perspective
I hid in a ditch, along with 14 other men from my regiment, the only ones that were remaining. I then a huge BOOM, and I looked up where the building where Lance Corporal Tooth was sniping from was completely demolished by a tank missile, and there was no sign of Tooth ever returning. That was one less helpful minifigure that could fight in this deadly battle. When one Maelstrom tank arrived, we all threw Big Ones, super powerful firecrackers that detonated within 5 seconds. One minifigure was too late to throw his Big One, and ended up being spontaneously combusted right in front of the regiment’s eyes. When a Maelstrom Tank, the dreaded Panzer, was finally blown up, with its driver stromling smashed, we heard the rattling of the dreaded Maelstrom Death Machine, or MDM. One foolish soldier decided to charge the MDM head on, but the result was horrifying. First, the soldier was instantly smashed at the touch of Maelstrom breath. But then, he was rebuilt into a stromling, responding,”Die Nexus Force! For the Death of Imagination!” We had to smash the soldier immediately, or else he would infect us all. I silently prayed that this battle would be over, and I would be spared from the horrors of this battle. I slipped into unconsciousness, and Private Turbo said,”Lieutenant Choa! Wake up! W a k e u p!!!!.....”
