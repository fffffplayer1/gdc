November 05, 2011
Chapter 1: The Invasion of Crux Prime
I remember when I was first drafted for the minifigure army of the Nexus Force. We protected Imagination from the foul beasts known as the Maelstrom. I also remember how melancholy my family was when I was leaving, knowing that I would never see them again."Don't fear", my mother said. "I know we would meet some day again." Then suddenly, a large burst of cannonfire had been fired! "Today is the day we fight for Imagination! Today is the day we destroy the Maelstrom! Today is the day we will obtain peace in the Universe!" shouted one of the commanders. "Fight on!" was all that I heard. I was then lost in the battle of Crux Prime, and I sunk into darkness.

November 05, 2011 2:00 PM, Crux Prime
Chapter 2: Lost
My regiment and I fought our way through the dense forest of Maelstrom beings. The soldier next to me fired his Long Barrel Blaster in a stromling’s face, instantly smashing it, while I slashed with my Serratorizer through waves and waves of stromlings. It seemed that we would win this battle quickly. Then, everything went wrong. When I looked back, I saw nothing but the pieces of figs, the ones of my regiment! All of sudden, I was clubbed by some obscure Maelstrom object. I fell to the ground, seeing stars in the sky. All I heard was the guttural voice of a stromling reporting,”Commander! We got one of those pesky Nexus Force figs!” I was then dragged into oblivion.

Chapter 3: Captivity
Perspective of a Prisoner (not the main character)
When I woke up, I was suddenly in a room filled with...Maelstrom. All around me were stromlings of all kind. I then realized my hands were in chains, and were chained onto a metal wall. When I tried to move, a stromling admiral used his whip to keep me in place. “This was supposed to be a dream”, I thought.
The only thing I wished for… was freedom. Freedom of life and Imagination. Suddenly, a hole burst through the wall and Nexus Force Soldiers started pouring in.

November 07, Maelstrom Concentration Camp
Chapter 4: Liberty
Main Character Perspective
Our regiment, the Nexus 554th, fought our way into the evils that befall us in one of the Maelstrom Concentration Camps. We were quick to move, smashing any Stromling guard that blocked our way. However, there were exceedingly amounts of stromlings in this camp, so the only chance we had was to destroy this camp slowly, one stromling at a time. It took several days, but we finally came through the camp, with the remains of stromlings everywhere. We broke into the cells, and freed all of the prisoner figs… except one. We searched for him everywhere, but when we finally found him, his head was on a spike, a lifelessness expression. I sat down and cried. It was my father.

November 19, Maelstrom Tower Entrance
Chapter 5 part 2: M-Day-The Assault
My regiment and I were deployed onto the beaches of Maelstrom, where the sand was entirely purple, and spikes arose from everywhere, each with a fig’s head on top to warn us not to come here or face despair. It seemed that there was no Maelstrom on this beach, so this would be an easy takeover. Suddenly, a purple bullet whizzed by me and hit the soldier that was right next to me. He was instantly smashed. A barrage of bullets started to pour onto us in an onslaught, and some of our men, well; they were just instantly smashed at the touch of a bullet. “Hold your ground men!”, an officer shouted, before he was hit by a bullet and smashed. I crawled behind a spike, looking up as fig heads looked at me in pity.
