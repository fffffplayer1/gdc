November 24, 5:00 PM, Chambois
Chapter 14: Smashing-The Old School Way
Perspective of Private Turbo
Lieutenant Choa had slipped into unconsciousness, and there was no sign he was coming back soon. I knew I had to take this matter into my own hands, while the Maelstrom was blasting us with tank shells and mortar shot, specifically designed to destroy minifigures in a second. We were bombarded with tank shells for a grueling amount of time, but suddenly, the bombardment stopped. We all were confused why the Maelstrom would stop trying to kill us, and a soldier grabbed his binoculars. “Look Turbo!,” the soldier said.”I can’t believe my eyes!” I snatched the binoculars from the soldier, and I too, gasped. Swarms of Stromlings came toward us, each equipped with a weapon, any kind of weapon that could infect us. Some carried guns and old musty flintlocks, while others fought only with a sword attached surgically onto their hands. Whatever they were, they were certainly terrifying. One had his rib cage showing purple bones, and his purple interior. Another Stroming had half of his face metal and the other pure Maelstrom. And, the most terrifying, all of the stromlings had an infector machine strapped onto their backs. This meant that we had no chance of making this battle through without one of us being infected. This was not going to be an easy battle, that was for sure. “Charge!” I shouted. “For Imagination!”
We rushed the Stromlings with astonishing speed, and demolished all that stood in our way. A stromling marksmen tried to shoot me with a crossbow, hoping to find a lucky shot. He stood no chance against a minifigure’s weapon. I jabbed my Fantastic Pilum into the stromling, and the stromling went out like a vanquished light. I then heard a boom, and saw burn marks where a group of minifigures were fighting. I ran as far as I could to disrupt the Maelstrom Tanks aim, but I ended up tripping over a grenade wire. Five, Four, Three, the machine said. “No!!!”, I yelled, hoping that the machine would stop. Two, One, the machine said. “Ahh!!!”, I shouted. The last thing I heard was a huge burst of explosive flame that engulfed what was left of me.
Perspective of Lieutenant Choa
I woke up hearing a burst of tank fire. I remember I had slipped into unconsciousness, when suddenly, a stromling appeared out of nowhere wielding a double-sided Maelstrom Axe. He swung it around, narrowly missing my head. I counter-attacked, slashing out with an Elite Force Blade of Knockdown I picked up from a fallen comrade. I slashed across, revealing scarred purple bones across the stromling’s chest. The stromling stepped back, when he was supposed to be smashed, and rasped a few lines. “The Maelstrom grows stronger with every smash. Smash to the death, or face humiliation.” I recognized this stromling, whose name was the legendary Talli Reeko, who was an almost invincible stromling. I agreed.” Let’s have some fun with smashing.” I said. I whipped out my wormholer and starting fighting for my life.
The nine most terrifying words in the English language are, ‘I’m from the government and I’m here to help.

November 24, 7:00 PM
Chapter 15: Regards
Perspective of Sergeant Tofu
We were pushing the Maelstrom soldiers out of Chambois when we suddenly heard a huge explosion occurring somewhere where a group of three privates were fighting. I took a squad of 5 men, and all we saw were burn marks and a few dismembered body parts. We looked around, to see if there were any other survivors, and then one soldier signaled me to look at his binoculars.”Holy Brick! What the heck is that”?!?!” Sure enough, it was Lieutenant Choa fighting some kind of stromling that seemed… different. But we had to destroy it since it looked like Lieutenant Choa was in for a lot of trouble…”Get me a blaster!”
Perspective of Lieutenant Choa
Talli Reeko was proving to be really a pain in the pants to fight. He had knocked over my Wormholer and infected it with Maelstrom, using it as another chaotic tool. He kept slashing and striking rapidly, so I had barely any time to strategize any plan to smash him. After 10 exhausting minutes of battling, I had finally made my mistake. I toppled over on a rock, and Talli’s Infected Wormholer was upon me. “Die!”, he shouted. I closed my eyes, hoping for the worst. When I opened them, Talli was gone, and I smelled something burning. I looked over, and there were the remains of Talli…or so they were. I ran over, seeing the mechanical ruins of Talli. A minifigure climbed out.”Hey.” I asked.”Do ya need any help?” The minifigure suddenly pulled out a gun, pulled me close, and pumped 3 shots of Maelstrom into me. I fell down, confused why an uninfected minifigure would try to smash me. I then looked up, expecting the worst kind of face on a minifigure I could see. I was proved wrong. It looked like a face that seemed familiar, horrifyingly familiar. I squinted my eyes, and sputtered out,”Brother? Gamer?” Gamer rasped out,”Yes… I am your brother, Choa.” He turned around. “The power, the grand power….is at last attainable…”

P.S. Gamer was short for CarbonVictoryGamer, my old name.

Part 4: Stay With Me
November 24, 8:00 PM
Chapter 16: Why?
Perspective of Lieutenant Choa
Why? I could not understand it. Why would my sweet, loving brother turn to the chaotic Maelstrom as family? It was impossible. But, it was done. “The Maelstrom is power,” Gamer said,”And people like me enjoy power. Power…is…necessary…” There was no choice…but to kill my brother. It was too much a pain to bear to watch my brother Gamer become infected, so I had to kill him. I put up my Serratorizers, pointed at Gamer, and said, “You’re going down.” Gamer summoned purple fire in his hand. “Let’s see you try.” He started to bombard me with Maelstrom fire, while I stood behind my shield, wondering.
Perspective of Private Intrepid
Me, Sergeant Tofu, Private Ghost, and all of the other Nexus 554th soldiers stood in awe, watching Lieutenant Choa face his brother to the death. I, personally didn’t believe that Lieutenant Choa would try to kill his brother, but I guess, it was just family business. I thought about my own family, with my kids and my wife, especially my wife, rooting for me. I also thought about my brother Turbo—wait, never mind. I had stay focused on the shot I was aiming for, which was one of those brothers fighting to the death.
Perspective of Lieutenant Choa
I was crying and fighting at the same time, since Gamer was the only family member I ever had, ever since the Maelstrom broke into our house and infected all of us, except my father, Gamer, and I. I discovered my father had been slain only a few months ago, in a Maelstrom Concentration Camp. Now, I had just witnessed my brother was infected. But I had to show no remorse, or I too would be infected with fear. I deflected a burst of purple fire, and swung my Serratorizers around, repeatedly. I didn’t even know where I was swinging at. Then, I hit something metallic, assuming it was some kind of mechanic junk. It wasn’t. When the dust finally cleared, I saw something that I would mourn over. I saw the body of a minifigure, not just any other minifigure. I looked down me, and I saw the hand of a minifigure grasping my leg. Then I looked down at the minifigure’s face, and it was covered in tears. It whispered,” Why?”
