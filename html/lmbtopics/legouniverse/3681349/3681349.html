remember my "diary of the Darkitect" ? Well it was hard to write a diary and make it funny, so I am now changing it to a script comedy.

Darkitect: Minions! Come here!
Stromling #125: Why?
Darkitect: Because i'm your master, and you should obey me!
Stromling #368: I don't want my master to be a villan. The bad guys always lose in every movie!
Darkitect: what about Dispicable me?
Stromling #368: what, he didn't succed in stealing the moon did he?
Darkitect: but... but... anyways, I am not a bad guy
Stromling: #125: you tell us that every week! (see entry 3 of my last comedy FMI)
Darkitect: and it's always the truth
Stromling #368: even though you tell us a different story every time?
Darkitect: ...
Stromling #125: well? Explanation?
Darkitect: I went into an alternate universe
Stromling #368: oh... of course! Sorry master, good bye! *runs off*
Darkitect: wait! Come back here! Oh well, #125, come here!
Stromling 125: I just remembered, I have some things to do you know and... *runs off*
Darkitect: ugh! I have to think of some way to win back their loyalty. I can't rule the universe without them...
later that day...

Darkitect: Minions! I have a contest
All Stromlings except one: OOOOOO!!!
Stromling who didn't say 'OOO!': *playing video game* Gotta beat my high score, boom boom boom! Yeah! Oh- what? *Turns around to see everybody staring at him* uh- where were we?
Stromling #983: we were at the part where everybody said 'OOO!'
Stromling who was playing video game: oh, okay. *starts playing video game again*
Darkitect: minions! I have a contest
All Stromlings except one: OOOOOO!!!
Stromling who didn't say 'OOO!': *playing video game* gotta beat my high score, boom boom boom! Yeah! Oh-what? *turns around to see everybody staring at him* uh-where were we?
Stromling #983: we were at the part-
Darkitect: Silence! as I was saying, I have a contest
All Stromlings except one: OOO-
Darkitect: Silence! The rules are, you have to prove I'm a bad guy in 5 seconds or else you have to consider me master and a good guy for the rest of youre life.
*a thousand hands shoot up*
Darkitect: uh- you
Stromling who the Darkitect picked: You're mean, you destroyed and infecte innocent minifigures, you discintigrated more than half your stromlings for no goo d reason, you- *Stromling gets discintigrated by the Darkitect*
Darkitet: the five second are up. Nobody thought of a good reason. Sorry.
Stromling who played video game: yeah! I beat my high score!
Stromling #983 *whispers to Stromling next to him* revolt tonight, pass it on.
to be continued...
