*Nadika opened her mouth, but couldn't find any words. Instead, Karunax spoke first in a tone that completely contradicted the anger in his eyes. It was almost frightening.*

 

"You know, after all the Kane-Ra I've been dragged through in recent times, I'm actually really glad I get a moment of closure; an opportunity to set things straight with you and myself."

 

*As he spoke, he advanced on her, filling her with a sense of dread. This had not gone according to plan. She had been deceived. She tried to move again, but it was no use. Karunax knelt down in front of her and pressed his two front knuckles against her damaged midsection, forcing her to cough and sputter violently. She almost blacked out from the harsh mix of breathlessness and pain, but oddly, he stopped pressing so hard, and she noticed the pain was rapidly going away. She looked down, and saw that he was actually healing her. Both relieved and confused, she looked back up at his face, and still saw only anger. Or maybe it was frustration? Rather than questioning his motives, however, she decided to cut right to the chase as soon as she could speak again.*

 

"Brother, please... this... this isn't you... "

 

"Isn't it? You're not my sister, Toa Nadika. Not anymore. You've been replaced."

 

"But The Anonymous... "

 

"... Is an ally of the Brotherhood. He freed me from Pain and healed my mind. Believe it or not, when he transferred himself  from you to me a few hours ago, I let him in willingly. While I let him control my form for his own ends, he spent the entire time conversing with me in the mental realm. He helped me learn about myself by unlocking many things. I remember everything now. I remember the good times you and I had together with our team, the laughs we shared. I remember all the battles, all the victories and failures... I even remember your death. And after remembering everything we'd been through... I learned that I really don't care about any of it. Or you, for that matter."
