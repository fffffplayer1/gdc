"Whoa there, buddy! What did I tell you about threats? If you want to work with us, it needs to be based off mutual respect. Or else," he slides his coat back again, tapping on the side of one of his blasters.

 

Voidarix stands to the side, arms crossed as he watches the argument unfold. Again, into the breach... what had he gotten himself into?

She hisses. Her voice turns colder as her eyes close, her face dissolves into energy, forming a blank sheet of hematite. Her voice is smoother and more alluring. "I have waited for so long. They will all be eradicated." She grins ferally.

 

Her voice cracks as she returns to normal. "I apologize. I have...not been myself lately. Yes." Her face returns to it's normal self, as she turns to Devoix. "You however said nothing of threats, and I had not threatened you. My point is, I want the remains. I want the final blow. The final blow is not their destruction. It is the Feeding. It is beautiful, and I will teach it to you."

 

Her face softens as she takes a quick peak in their memories, enough to get an idea of how the Great Beings manipulated them. "You poor.." She whispers softly. "Yes. Come. We shall work together. I will teach you both the ancient arts, both of you!" She whispers with excitement. Her wings flutter as she twitches. "The Feeding is a beautiful ceremony. Of course it is not as beautiful as The Rejuvenation, but it is second to it!" She sighs.
