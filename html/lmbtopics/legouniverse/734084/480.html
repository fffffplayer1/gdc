Density Control: Another one of my favorite powers for its usefulness. Density Control allows Makuta to alter their molecules' compactness, making it closer together to be harder and tougher to resist taking physical damage or to make them spread farther a part to allow them to phase through other matter or to be quicker and faster. It also allows them to control the density of other objects as well. Though the power has some draw backs as they harder they make themselves the slower it is to move and the looser, the weaker. Please note that phasing through energy is not really possible as it will only hurt you more in a phased state than a solid one. I don't know if Makuta can phase through energy as well, but some shape shifting would need to be invovled if one tried. I also don't know if this power is losted when switching bodies.

 

Disinigration: Why do the Makuta get so many good powers? This power allows Makuta to break down things on a molecular level, turning even the toughest materials to dust.

 

Dodge: This power makes Makuta able to dodge anything, nothing shot, swong, or thrown can hit them with this power even with a Sanok. Makuta would be great at dodge ball, but two Makuta teams would never beat each other if they could instantly dodge despite perfect accuracy. 

 

Elasticity: I'm sure everyone knows this power very well thanks to people like DC's Plastic Man, Elongated Man, and most famous, Marvel's Mr. Fantastic. The power to stretch long distances, increase size, and some shape shifting ability makes it a very useful and dangerous power. Lost when in a different body.

Explosion/Shattering: This power doesn't create as clean as a mess as disinigration, but can be used from farther distances than the other power. It is called both Explosion and Shattering because that's what it does, it can cause explosion or things to shatter to pieces. This power by either vibrating on object until it falls apart or causes the object's molecules to become unstable, resulting in an explosion. Not matter how it is done, it can be great for distracting the enemy or maybe even tickling them.

 

Lightning/Electricity: Another power of the Makuta's limited control over Lightning. It allows Makuta to create a deadly electric field to fry enemies or to trap them. No it is not the same as Chain Lightning at all, since it is a field, not a bolt that goes from one person to another.

 

Fear: The Fear power works similar to Anger, which it is a  physchic ability that makes the fear emotion of the brain override everything else. Makuta can cause even Toa to run away in fear or have them freeze in terror. Power is kept when in another body.

 

Fire Resistance: An obvious power, it allows Makuta to be naturally resistant to heat. No, their power of Adaption couldn't do the same thing. It might come close, but it can't make Makuta as resistant to heat and fire attacks as this power. Otherwise their Adaption power might have saved several Makuta from being harmed by mutating chemicles.

 

Gravity: I really wonder whether to classify this as limited power over Gravity or not. Seeing as they can only alter Gravity's affects, it must be limited since the can't create new gravity. Anyways this allows the Makuta to increase or decrease gravity's pressure on an object or person. From crushing Toa into a fine powder to launching them into space, the power to alter gravity is a great one.

Heat Vision: One just has to know Superman's powers to understand this one. Heat Vision is a power to shoot beam of concentrated heat from you eyes. These beams are at very high tempatures and can melt or boil many things. However it doesn't even need to be a beam as it can cause the molecules of an object to rub against each other really fast until the melt or ignite by just looking at them. Because it deals with heat, Toa of Fire could redirect this power or even cool it off all together.

 

Hunger: Hunger allows Makuta to drain energy from living things and people. They have enough power to drain even Toa dry.

 

Ice Resistance: Ice Resistance makes Makuta impervious to ice and the cold. Because of this, it is difficult to cause ice to build up on Makuta even at very cold tempatures. Luckily, this power is lost when they are in another body. The difference between Adaption is the same as Heat Resistance.

 

Illusion: Another of the many psychic powers of the Makuta. Illusions come and go in many different uses, but mainly projects a very realistic image into a person's or creature's mind, making them think what they're seeing is real. As SprinkleOtter knows, I could go on and on about illusions, but I'm sure most understands its uses.

 

Insect Control: This gives the Makuta the power to control insects' minds and have them do whatever they not. Although bugs are consider Rahi in Bionicle, they are still consider two seperate animal kingdoms. Thus there is a seperate power to control animals.
