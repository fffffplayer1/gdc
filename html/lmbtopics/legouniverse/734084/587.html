False.

All Makuta are, by nature, geniuses and are generally extremely arrogant about it, and in the case of Charix and Mutran it was to the point that they considered other beings such as Matoran to be expendable pawns.

In addition, all Makuta shunned their inner light for darkness.

 

Gorast was a vicious, violence-obsessed Makuta who enjoyed fighting and destruction.

Icarax was power-obsessed and wanted control of both the entire Brotherhood as well as the entire world.

Antroz thought similarly to Icarax and believed that all Toa should be squashed in order to insure that the Makuta rule supreme.

Miserix is the second closest to not being a bad guy, and he was revenge obsessed to the point of being willing to destroy the entire Matoran universe just to smash Teridax. He was also rather violent, impatient, and destructive, as can be seen in his agreeing to the methods used to end the Matoran civil war(burying the fighting armies within the Metru-Nui Archives).

 

The various fan-made Makuta represent this rather well:

Karunax, who was formerly just an immensely violent juggernaut of destruction, but has since then gained control of his rage, though he is still very strong and violent.

Inharax is both an egotistical scientist and a pyromaniac.

Charix is also an egotistical scientist, who has a bit of an obsession with being considered the top genius of the Brotherhood and making the universe more orderly, in the way he wants it to be.

My old character Anranok was a warrior who also believed in bringing order to the universe, but by crushing all opposition beneath him.

Sidorak12814 created Rularx, an immensely arrogant, pompous, egotistical genius with just about every character flaw there is, who fully admits his flaws and loves himself for them.
