Artidax seethes.

 

"Heretics, all of them!" he bellows. "My mission is being spurned by those misguided fools, my crusade slandered by the ignorant! I would bring my brothers into the light, but Zeytran would not yield. Under my guidance, the Brotherhood would--"

 

"The Brotherhood would be cheated a rare specimen for study."

 

"Who--!? Argh!" Artidax turns and is blasted in the chest by a ray. Lomois steps into the light, grinning.

 

"Most interesting, that ray should have disabled you instantly. No matter. Simply a matter of-- URK!"

 

Artidax uses elasticity to sucker-punch Lomois, doubling him over. Rising, he leaps forward and smacks him in the jaw.

 

"FOOL! Did you think mere toys could stop me? I am the unbridled power of our race. I am the future that-- argh!"

 

Lomois fires his ray again, driving Artidax back.

 

"Fall. FALL!" He holds a continuous beam until Artidax uses dodge to escape. Lomois soon finds himself attacked from behind. His foe places Lomois in a chokehold.

 

"Now, you will be destroyed. Pray that you will find mercy where I will show none."
