@Macedonia

As you enter the fort you are surprised by how light it is inside. If you were expected a dark, sinister look you would have been dissapointed. The light comes from several windows in the walls and a multitude of strange ice crystal clumps which hang on the walls and seem to act like light stones. You are led down a short, main hallway with multiple doors lining the walls. One door with a high arch stands at the end of the hallway and the company advances through this. The next room is dominated by a large spiral staircase that looks almost like it partially consists of ice crystals. As you advance up you notice that the stair is is constantly reflecting and focusing light so that the entire passage is lit with a dazzling light.  After a little while you arrive at the end of the stairs and find a wall containing a door with a similer high arch to the one downstairs. You exit through the door and enter another short hallway almost exactly like the one downstairs, except for the fact that this one has pictures hanging on the wall. The pictures range from old images of the fort on Destral to images of scenic views from around the world. This hall has only one two doors. One in the right hand side of the wall and another at the end of the hallway with the same high arch that you saw before. It is through the right hand door that you and your company are lead through. You emerge into a huge room filled with shelves full of books. Small signs are plastered to the side of each row of shelves with precise information concerning the books contained on that respective row. You can just barely see a large circular area  without any shelves in the middle of the room. The floor of this area is made up of the same ice crystals that you noticed while going up the stairs.  A few chairs, couches, and tables occupy this area. It looks like an pool of cold water in the midst of a jungle of book shelves.
