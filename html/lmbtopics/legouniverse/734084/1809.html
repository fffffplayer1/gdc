"Speak your offer, Excidia."

"Whoa, whoa, whoa! Voidarix, you've been part of this deal for less than five minutes- what makes you think you're suddenly the spokesman?"

Deviox pushes Voidarix aside, storming towards Excidia, "Repentence? Redemption? You honestly think you can try and threaten us just because we used to work for old Mr. Metal-Arm? If you want to join the Conquest, you're going to need to offer something a bit more than what we already have. As of now, we have close to one million widgets, minus the cost of a new coat, and one of the most technologically advanced facilities on the planet. That's about as close to elysium, peace, and bliss as anyone around here is going to get. So, do you have anything actual to offer us?"

She grins, delighted. "I'm so glad you asked me."

 

"I can offer you power. Devestation. I am one of the most powerful beings in this universe. I am a Goddess of Destruction. I have control over the Four Apocalyptic Signs, for they are bound to my will. I have ancient powers predating the Core War, in which shattered this planet. I was there." She hisses.

 

"When those, those elemental lords, were born, that was when our war officially ended. Not when they became Elemental Lords, but when they were born. The Great Beings ravaged us, until only I remained." She hisses. "I can offer you justice. And..."

 

She chuckles.

 

"I can teach you, Kaon Science. You have the mental aptitutde, I should think, it's just a matter of discipline and being able to. Great Beings can detect when Kaon Science flares up, and yours would be unstable, as it is new and raw and young." She smirks. "Have we got a deal? We both want the Great Beings gone. It's help to both of us." She purrs.

We have been betrayed. Our trust, misguided. Our faith, put wrongly. Have they no shame? But we will live on, my brothers. We will strive forward, we will endure. We will ascend. Free of limitations, free of fear.
