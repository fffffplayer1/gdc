"Look!"

Maoni looks down at her feet, then at the matoran, then at her team. Suddenly she loosens a magnetism blast at the team, felling them.

 

"I am a Toa, shadow or not. And a Toa's duty is to protect the Matoran!"

 

The Toa of Magnetism herds the matoran into the portals, and as her team begins to rise, she gives them one long look, then disappears through the Portal. 

 

The Devices cease functioning, and the 3 hagah curse Maoni's name.

 

But not a single Matoran is left in Destral.

All the guards take out Maoni and athe portal machines. Really. If I had access to a computer ealier this wouldn't have happened.
