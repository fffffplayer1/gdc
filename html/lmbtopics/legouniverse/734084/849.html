*Korvohk chuckles quietly as a fullfilling grin crosses his face. He takes on a satified air, knowing he has brought Karunax down to what he really is, or so he assumes*

 

"Ah, but that's what makes the world go round, Karunax. Everything changes. What do you lead, what are you a part of? Well, I'll tell you... You lead the remains of what once was the proud Brotherhood of Makuta, who were once proud, wise, and merciful, cunning. You're part of something that died before it even begun, and now look at them."

 

*He gestures to the collected members*

 

"The de-moralized scientist, broken and soft on the inside, unsure of where his aligience lies."

 

"The brute, rash, ignorant, and uncareing for whoever he points the sword at, including his own bretheren."

 

"The advisor and warrior, lost amongst his own world, forgetting what his true purpose was, now only a hollowed out shell of the ideals and truths that came before."

 

"And you, Karunax. The last being standing... The last firm point for the brotherhood to look to, and your foundations tremble... You're so tired of the bickering and fighting that you feel it dragging you down. Exhuasted to the brink of insanity. The lone giant, just trying to seek solitude and justice for an age long gone..."

 

*Korvohk sighs*

 

"You need to ask yourself, who they," he agains waves his hand in a sweeping arc towards the members," who they truley pledge their loyalty to. You? Or themselves?" 

*Karunax looks back up to meet your gaze, his eyes filled with hatred and defiance. He knows your words are true, yet he will not accept--or at least show--defeat of any kind.*

"Leave. You've made your point. Now get out."

-Makuta Karunax, Leader of the Brotherhood-
