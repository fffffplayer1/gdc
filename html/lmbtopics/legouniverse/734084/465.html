Well, I suppoes it's time I introduce my new character.

 

He's not the leader, not because he's too weak to take leadership, not because he's too blindly loyal to plot for it, but because he no longer gives a Karzahni about who leads. He will willingly leap into any situation at all, no matter the danger. Maybe it's because he's been through worse on several occasions and lived. Maybe it's because he doesn't care if he is permanently defeated.

And to top it off, nobody knows him, no one recognizes him.

 

Name: Makuta Horez

 

Mask: Kanohi Dromerix. The mask has the ability to speed up or slow down the vibrations of atoms to cause phase change, like melting or freezing.

 

Weapons: claws, glaivesword. The glaivesword is a protosteel glaive with a long, double sided blade. One blade extends further then another, so he could use it to do things such as remove an annoying Makuta's arm with it.

 

Height: 8 ft. 9 in.

 

Appearance: somewhat spiked, red, silver, and black armor. Has an intimidating presence.

 

Traits: Horez is better then average at using his lightning, chain lightning, heat vision, invulnerability, and plasma powers. The reason for this being that he almost solely uses those powers.

Horez generally fights without using any but the above four powers and his phsyical prowess alone. In comparision to my two previous Makuta used, Horez could win in a no powers fight. Against both of them at once.

 

Storywise, he once fought Icarax in a no-powers fight, for three days straight. The battle ended when over a dozen Makuta demanded they cease fighting, before the makuta fortress ceased existing. Also storywise, he mysterioulsy dissapeared when on a mission, and no explanation has ever been found as to what happened.

A man laying down His life for His friends, Your sacrifice has spoken, You gave everything And "I love you" could not be said a better way.
It is finished! It is done! He is risen!
