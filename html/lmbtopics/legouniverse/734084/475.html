You completely ignore him. You tell him, this battle is over, all damage incurred and such is cancelled, have a good day.

 

In the case of Alienduck, he hasn't done anything that severely effects me in the end. He's scratched a mask, that's it. But if he decides to continue with it, I will tell him good day and goodbye.
