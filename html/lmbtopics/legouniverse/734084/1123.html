@Argh: OOC: It wouldn't be ridiculous, but I agree that it would be unfair. I'll stop doing that.

 

Quite possibly, since your arguments are only ever biased against me.

 

I think that's where the "everyone can't beat everyone" idea comes into play, not simply because one character is more powerful than the other, but because a specific character might lack any of the means necessary to harm another specific character. I say "not because one is more powerful than the other" because it's entirely possible for it to work both ways, where neither character can harm the other.

 

I can see how it would be a downgrade, but just out of curiosity, why do you think it is?

 

"Karunax is the meta" is opinion, not fact. They made their characters, and I made mine. I did not create Karunax with the intention of topping anybody, but with the knowledge that no-one else had already taken the special traits of strength and super-armor for themselves to create a juggernaut-based character. I jumped at the opportunity because it was, at the time, unique. I also wasn't the most creative fellow approximately seven years ago, being an 11-year-old newbie to online forms of roleplay. Nostalgia is a selfish feeling, and it's why I don't want to change Karunax. It's not, however, the reason I won't change him. The reason I won't change him is because I have yet to see a thoroughly legitimate explanation on how everything here comes back to me as being my fault. It's never been a problem before a year or two ago, and so far it's just been overly-competitive users, bias, peer pressure, and laziness that present opinionated arguments. 

 

Let's face it: this is a completely opinionated debate, so a consensus will never be reached until one side willingly bends to the other's will. This is a subject best dropped, as it will only flood topics with eternal spam.
