@Aidant

 

As any character in this Roleplay, you also get to have Kanohi Masks (Makuta only get two; Toa can have up to six). There is a long list of Kanohi to choose from, and it is too long and too much to explain in order to post it here. Some examples of masks are:

 

Mask of Strength

Mask of Agility

Mask of Speed

Mask of X-Ray Vision

Mask of Levitation

Mask of Invisibility

Mask of Teleportation

Mask of Water Breathing

Mask of Shielding

Mask of Flight

 

There are dozens more, but hopefully this helps you to get the point. They're extra powers, to add to the rest of your arsenal. Sometimes, you can also make up your own masks, provided they're not too super powerful.

 

Also, you can use your imagination when creating your character! For example, Kronil is a gas cloud (technically a Makuta) who somehow has all the Kanoka Disk powers. Sombak has the Greatsword, and sword with many powers. Bakud, a Matoran, has the ability to time travel (although it's kinda OP[Over Powered], if you ask me.). My character, Nexus, is friends with a few Toa. So, feel free to use your imagination!

 

Lastly, you can have more than one character, if you want. However, they cannot be in HQs that fight each other. For example, you could have a Matoran and Toa character, but not a Toa and Makuta character. The general limit to characters is 4, but it's not in the rules. Usually, extra characters are used only if you want to fight in two different HQs at once. Normally, you don't use them together. It's not really fair. But again, it's not in the rules.

 

And lastly, there is pretty much only 5% canon here. You can do basically anything you want.

 

Aaaand to end this long, lengthy, probably boring post, keep in mind that we don't really do much fighting as a team here. We do have leaders, but they're mostly just for when we get attacked in our own HQ. Otherwise, you may go around and do as you please.
