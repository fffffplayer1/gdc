Three Toa trudge across the huge desert. Well, two of them do. The other is walking, but every now and then flickers for a moment.

 

"I really hate this place," Certrix says.

"Yeah. I mean, sure, Stone and stuff need a biome too, but this is insane. At least Le-Bo-Ga is bearable to non-Air or Water or Jungle," Bothrok agrees.

 

Certrix glares at him.

 

"The bugs? Avser, find anything?"

 

The pure white Toa stops flickering for a moment to respond.

 

"Erm... No. I might have seen a city, though."

"Then go over there and see if anyone has a better mode of transportation."

Avser doesn't look too happy about this, but complies and zooms off at subsonic velocities.

Avser comes back and after a quick mental conversation the three depart.

     In orbit, a bright flash surrounds Oculus Magna. When it fades, the space around it is filled with a plethora of robotic warriors. The odd thing is, they're aiming toward the space station, not away from it.

     A moment later, they open fire. The station's defense systems render it exceptionally resistant, but the adaptive weaponry it faces quickly wears down the metallic walls.

     A series of flashes dots the cloud of attackers as telepods are sent from within the space station, spewing forth similar warriors to counteract the attack. The battle turns to chaos as each one questions which is friend and which is foe, firing upon everything in sight.

     Hunks of metal fall from the sky, burning up in the atmosphere.

     A single unique robot suddenly vanishes from the commotion with a pale blue flash.

3 weeks ago

Grand Sandstone pillars, gleaming rock. Vines entangle the walls, keeeping it together. Sand falls down like snow, not getting in their eyes but simply delicately falling. Two soil walls open up. The hematite banners gleam, showing a strange symbol.

A cluster of thorns with a circle of minerals around it, and emblazioned inside the cluster is a large, jokerman font, is a uppercase Z made out of sand. Many Matoran of various types, Earth, Stone or Plantlife stand, guarding diligently. Vorox and Zesk roam freely, as Skrall and Rock Agori do as well. Toa of Earth, Stone and Plantlife also wander, and some Toa of Sand (Special Vorox).

And upon a grand celestial throne, vines, soil, stone and sand lay an elegant figure. The Woman In The Cave. The Earth Mother. The Consort to Fire. Her armor gleams, the onyx magma crusts, the golden sand, her rocky armor and her soil skin. Her long braids of hair, flowing freely.

Sulfuras gasps, for She had been more beautiful than even before. Her radiance shone as he bowed lowly, his face stern yet awed by her beauty. "Lady Zemlya, Gaea, Terra, you have many names but none compare to Your radiance." Sulfuras murmured. And she had chuckled, her soft voice echoing through the cavernous depths.

"Flattery will get you nowhere in my court, Sulfuras." Zemlya responded. "So, Ackarne has requested my presence?" She hummed. "He abandoned me because he was incompetent......Vulcan will fail, I know him well. He is planning to return us to the Elemental Lords, but he will fail." Zemlya said, her eyes twinkling in mirth.

"My lady, The Man In The Volcano ha-" Sulfuras began for Zemlya to cut him off. "Does he force all of you to refer to him as that?! By his titles, by those? Even my subjects, they at LEAST USE MY NAME." She raged.

"Y-yes. We cannot use His true name. But he has requested your presence at The Volcano." Sulfuras began. "Hmmm.....very well. It will be good to see Ackarne again. Then I will tear him asunder."

They depart to The Volcano.
We have been betrayed. Our trust, misguided. Our faith, put wrongly. Have they no shame? But we will live on, my brothers. We will strive forward, we will endure. We will ascend. Free of limitations, free of fear.
