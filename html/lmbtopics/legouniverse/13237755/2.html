*The Voidwalker fazes into existence in the sky above the fallen second GSR. At first, he still appears as a silhouette shrouded in a swirling purple substance. But with a rippling pulse that shocks the surrounding air, the energy vanishes and his features are visible. Looking down upon the gigantic construct, he notes all the repairs his counterpart had performed on the outside, as well as the work he'd begun on the inside.*

 

"So intricate. It would almost be a shame to tear it a little here... "

 

*The areas that had been sealed with Hardlight glow a bright purple and begin to widen, breaking open the cracks in the GSR's shell and causing massive cave-ins.*

 

" ... And a little there... "

 

*Its gigantic arm separates away from its shoulder, striking the ground with enough force to cause a good-sized tremor.*

 

"Perhaps some more internal-... "

 

*A red glow appears around him, and he is suddenly wrenched around and pulled through the air. He sees The Uncreator flying up towards him.*

 

"How dare you."

 

"You left it alone, so I took advantage."

 

"You are already a great nuisance, then. Most unwise."

 

*A purple glow appears around The Uncreator.*

 

"Mmhmm."

 

*The next second, both entities are blown away from each other, spiraling violently through the air. They both manage to quickly straighten themselves, and charge back at one another without an instant's hesitation. The red streams of light decorating The Uncreator's body flare brighter, and, halting his charge, numerous javelins of HL appear before him and rocket at his opponent, tracking his movement. The Voidwalker unleashes a powerful shockwave of his own HL to deflect them, but they punch right through and end up reaching their target.

 

He visibly slows and withdraws as the javelins tear into his form, and with a flash of blue light, TU appears right in front of him and throws his open hand right into his midsection. TV experiences the insanely powerful impact from the front, while a very similar one crushes from the back.*
