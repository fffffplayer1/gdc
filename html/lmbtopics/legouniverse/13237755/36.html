Somewhere, in the sands of P-O-O-B-Wahi, a lone Nuurakh slinks foward. It's heavily battered, worn down from hostile environment, the most notable damage being to the Vahki's left eye, leaving a dark, empty socket, the light inside no longer functional. It carries a hastily made notebook, with noticable damage to the pages. This Nuurakh is Insanity.

 

Months of social isolation had done a massive blow to Insanity's optimism. After all, it's hard to be carefree and whimsical when you're trying to stay alive. He couldn't even talk to Mis-File; he was lost due to a Rahi breaking his left eye and doing some damage to his hardrive. Now, Insanity twitches madly, looking around...until he sees something he hasn't seen in a long time. A city.

 

Civilization.

 

Insanity grins like a complete and utter madman, having long forgotten how to properly smile without looking like a lunatic.

 

"Yeeeeeeeeeesssssssssss...I'll go in, and then, and then, I-I'll talk to people, and do things, and...and..."

 

Insanity pauses, a new, completely irrelevant thought having come up in his head that, to him, seems like the discovery of the ages. Obviously, the Makuta were responsible for all his pain. They turned the Vahki evil, so he had to hide. They created the Rahi, including the one who attacked him, so obviously they were to blame. He was also pretty sure they were responsible for him not going into any civilized areas despite the fact that he could've gone in easily without any trouble. He didn't have any reason behind the last one, but clearly the Makuta were responsbile for it, right?

 

"...and then I'll make the Makuta pay..." Insanity mutters with a psychopathic grin.
