Chapter 28

 

Luke paled, and quickly ran from the room while Intrepid gritted his teeth and grumbled, "Follow him and look what you did."

Silently Jay ,Katie, and Intrepid followed Luke to the ground floor lobby, which was surprisingly empty except for them.  Through the large windows, they could all see the dangers outside.

 

There were living purple skeleton thingys ripping their way out of the ground and growling.  In their claws were either sharp blades, glowing chainsaws, or huge hammers.

 

"I never thought I'd see one type of Stromling again." Intrepid moaned. "But all three?  This will be fun."

 

And then the battle between two war veterans and a huge army of five hundred plus evil Maelstrom Stromlings began.

 

After charging into the swarm of Stromlings and slashing, hacking, and grinding their way through hundreds of Stromlings did they realize that the army was growing, even though it was pretty evident already by the way the museum windows were broken and the whole front half of the lobby was overrun.  Intrepid and Luke were covering behind a row of desks and tables and were blasting away at the Maelstrom mass with.... drills.

 

When the first desk exploded, Luke sighed and said, "Alright this is it we lost."

 

"Fine then." Intrepid agreed. "But we're not going down without getting Jay and Katie to safety- wait, where are they anyway?"

 

Indeed, they were gone....

 

....from the lobby.  Instead, they were on the roof kicking a group of Paradox Mechs in the heads until they turned on. "PRDX-2 0NL1N3." "PRDX-7 0NL1N3." "PRDX-12 0NL1N3." "PRDX-9001 0NL1N3." "PRDX-4 0NL1N3- 0H L00K, 1T'5 Y0U 4G41N."

 

"NOW GET DOWN TO THE LOBBY AND DO YOUR JOB." Katie screamed at them, and obediently the mechs clanked their way to the open door hatch down the steps through the hallway into the lobby and began shooting everything.


Everything.

 

And the museum went up in flames.

Chapter 29

 

From the large hills overlooking Avant Gardens sat Intrepid, Luke, Jay, and Katie huddled around a fire.

 

"How did this happen...." Jay said softly, staring down below them at the Stromlings that ran wild, rampaging throughout the gardens but not smart enough to venture through the trees into the major population areas that colonized the rest of the Avant Gardens planetoid.

 

"You did it." Intrepid replied.

 

"WHAT?  WHAT DID I DO?" Jay screamed.

 

"I'm no scientist or Paradox researcher, and my theory is as follows." Intrepid told him. "When you revived that cute Spiderling-"

 

"Cute?" Katie interrupted, disgusted. "That thing was ugly."

 

"-it somehow awakened up all the Stromlings that have been smashed and since had their pieces scattered around under the ground of Avant Gardens." Intrepid explained. "So they all surfaced and this is what happened.  We smashed so many back in the war that they haven't even finished coming up."

 

Jay gulped. "But what about the rest of the Nimbus System?  Will the Maelstrom come back there too?"

 

Intrepid raised his eyebrows. "Who knows.  No one really understands the Maelstrom, but according to the news that Luke is pulling up," - he jerked a thumb in the direction of Luke who was peering at the screen of a boring black laptop - "only this section of Avant Gardens is in trouble."

"Did everyone get out safely?" Katie asked.

 

"I can confidentally tell you yes." Intrepid answered. "Minifigs are very smart, but of course I'm smarter since I'm awesome."

 

"So, what are we supposed to do?" Katie pressed. "We're supposed to 'defend Imagination' but how?  And how did that Overbuild guy even know to build us because the Maelstrom would come back?  It seems like we caused it to come back, at least here."

 

Luke screamed. "Breaking news!  The Maelstrom have landed at Guppy Forest, Favorable Valley, and Nexus City!"

 

"In answer to your question," Intrepid said to her, "There is nothing for you to do.  At all.  Null."
