Chaptah 37

 

Like 14 years before, Avant Gardens had a large Maelstrom presense within its east side.

 

Unlike 14 years before, Avant Gardens had a massive Maelstrom presense everywhere but the Launch Area.

 

From not so high up in the sky, Jay and Katie watched from a forcefield window in the floor as Avant Gardens unfolded underneath them.  They passed over some purple mountains and within a minute were all of a sudden over the Paradox Research Lab.

 

"Alright, let's do this!" Intrepid announced, about to stand up and leave the controls to Mara who sat in the copilot seat.

 

It was a good thing he didn't, because a loud explosion would have rocked them all across the interior of the C-17 if they hadn't been suffocating under their seatbelts.  A Maelstrom missile tore through the fuselage, but since they were quite low to the ground they weren't at risk of losing oxygen.  Still, half the cargo plane was on fire and they were spinning in an uncontrolled dive towards a forest.

 

"GIRONEMO!!!!!!!!!!!" Luke screamed as they veered into a forest of trees and faced impending doom.

 

....

 

At the last moment before impact, everybody epically jumped out and survived, though the whole team was now falling through a mess of branches and leaves.  Scattered fires from the crash threatened to spread and engulf the rest of the forest, but that wasn't their main problem as they landed on the ground.

It was the approaching Maelstrom army.

 

"Eat this, you evil ugly thingies!" Luke yelled as he, Intrepid, and Mara swung out Wormholers and fired hundreds of thousands of rapid-firing LAZARS upon the onslaught of hundreds of Stromlings, Mechs, and Spiderlings - a standard Maelstrom patrol.

 

Within seconds, more than hundreds of Stromlings had exploded in small puffs of purple smoke.  But they kept coming, and would keep coming....

 

Doom was impending.

- Fleet Captain Talmid of the USS Talmidon III, flagship of Minuteman Division in Star Fleet and the United Federation of Planets.
