‎02-11-2013 06:56 PM

Eyeing Intrepid's predicament, Typhonus Jr. giggled, "You can stay there." and then turned to face ten homing missiles that impacted with him with ten pretty explosions.  The Katana of Doom flew from his hands in a random direction that happened to be directly at Jay, and imbedded itself in the ground mere inches from the tips of his feet, causing a mini earthquake.

 

"Yikes!" Jay scrambled away from it, just missing the tendrils of Maelstrom lightning that emitted from it as it shot from the ground and returned to Typhonus Jr.'s hands.

 

More missiles headed itowards Typhonus Jr., but this time he simply held out his hand and the missiles came to a dead stop in mid-air, before turning around and streaking towards Shirley, who had launched them.  A cloud of green marble-sized thingies intercepted the missiles, stopping them.  Mara, wielding two Pea Shooter Mk.2s then turned her weapons fire to Typhonus Jr., with dire results.  The peas just bounced off him.

 

That left Luke, Jay, and Katie.  Spinning his Elite Cleaver, Luke charged Typhonus Jr., jumping and weaving from side to side to avoid the Katana of Doom's lethal lightning bursts.  Then the sword fight of epicness began.  Instantly, their swords were locked together, turning it into a battle of strength, with only the stronger minifigure able to emerge victorious.  And tired from hours of fighting the Maelstrom army, it was obvious Luke wasn't going to last very long in this position.

 

Jay and Katie swung at Typhonus Jr. from behind, who quickly dove to the side and rollled into a crouch.  Then he double-jumped towards them and flying-kicked Luke to the ground.  His sword cut the air right in front of Jay's and Katie's faces, but before they could strike back he backflipped a few feet and jammed the Katana of Doom into the ground.

 

The world shook and rumbled.

Chapter 47

 

They had probably been knocked out, since in what felt like only seconds Jay and Katie were sitting in a dark room so dark they could barely see even five feet ahead of them.

 

"Now just where are we?" Katie said, and as if in response, several lights flickered on, illuminating the massive auditorium they were in.  Smack in the center was a large circular platform on which they stood, and around them were rows and rows of seats, housing Stromlings, Mechs, Spiderlings, Apes, and Dark Ronin.  And in a box room elevated above the crowd, was Typhonus Jr. himself.

 

"Welcome!" he announced. "To my humble abode."

 

"Why have you brought us here?" Jay called weakly.

 

Typhonus Jr. sighed. "I'd have expected you to be smarter than that, given all the hype about you.  The entirety of the Maelstrom Dimension is filled up with talk about 'The Figdroids', who will destroy the Maelstrom once and for all."

 

"'Maelstrom Dimension'?" Katie questioned.

 

"'Destroy the Maelstrom'? I thought were were just defending Imagination." Jay stammered.

 

"One does not simply play the ignorant here." Typhonus Jr. threatened. "In every which way you are completely under my control now, and nothing you can think of doing can stop it, or my master plan.  You have no power here."

 

"How about we give them a sample of our master plan, Master?" a 'high up' Dark Ronin wheezed.


"Excellent idea," Typhonus Jr. agreed, and he pressed a button on his control board.

 

Two circular sections on the center platform shook and proceeded to sink into the ground as elevators to some place below the floor.  For a long minute, they clanked and clattered their way into the underground caverns of the Maelstrom's Humble Abode, before coming to a stop for five seconds, and then clanking and clattering their way up again.

 

"While we wait," the Dark Ronin cackled, "How about we ask 'The Figdroids' some questions?  Like, what would happen if you went back in time and smashed your past self?"
