Chapter 50

 

  "I only said it for drama," said Intrepid hastily before Jay opened the door so he and Katie could jump out of the cramped cabin and resume escaping on foot.  Intrepid jabbed a red button on the dashboard and the trunk popped open, (yes, the Higher Performance C5 Z06 has a trunk and the Normal C5 doesn't! Where's the logic?!) unveiling an array of three oversized crayons aimed at the

  "Futile is resistance," a voice blared over a loudspeaker from the ROFLCOPTER. "Lower your boarding and prepare to be shielded."

 

  "Stop trying to sound cool, 'cuz you're NOT!" Intrepid cheered, and punched the red button again.  Two of the oversized crayons (which were actually missiles in disguise) shot out of the trunk and streaked towards the ROFLCOPTER.  Before its AI Pilot could run an evasion code, the ROFLCOPTER was effectively grounded.

 

  The third oversized crayon's launch mechanism failed and it exploded in the Corvette's trunk AKA weapons bay, which knowing Intrepid Fusion Eclipse it probably included more lethal weapons of mass destruction within its walls....

 

....

 

  The combined explosion of cannon shells, cluster bombs, rockets, parachute mines, homing missiles, particle beams, tesla coils, and severely underpowered atom bombs resulted in Intrepid, Jay, and Katie being blown out of the flaming wreckage of twisted fibreglass and alluminum all the way to the highest most floor of Nexus Tower in Nexus City.

 

  Intrepid smashed on impact.

 

  "Grrrr, I lost hundreds of coins." Intrepid grumbled as he rebuilt, scrambling around to collect some coins that had appeared out of nowhere.

 

  "Forget it, LOOK!" screamed Katie, pointing out the window at a massive army that will be revealed in Chapter 51.
