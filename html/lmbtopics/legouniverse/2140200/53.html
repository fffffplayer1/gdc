The goons grabbed Hawk, Seymore and M. restraining thier arms behind thier backs. Seymore's notepad fell to the floor. M just stayed calm, as if planning something. The goons led them over to the area where the other cyborg stood.

"Sawfist. I've caught some agents spying on our operation. Shall i dispose of them?" Spyclops asked blankly. His motonomic voice echoed in the room. Sawfist frowned and looked at seymore. "Well you're a regular snooper, news man. You at least need to be taught a lesson."

"Sawfist. My question."

Sawfist grunted and checked the clock. "We don't have enough time to get rid of them and cover up the act. We'll just have to bring them with us." He groaned.

"Is that necessary? It doesn't seem logical." Spyclops objected.

"If it isn't then I don't know what to do about it." He shrugged. "Let's go fellas."

The goons started to lead Hawk, Seymore and M outside to the helicopter transport. Hawk tried to wriggle free. He whispered frantically to M. "M! Come on M, help up get out of here! I know you're strong enough to break free!"

"I'd advise you to stop squirming and stay calm." M said, not making eye contact. Hawk was baffled. "Resistance will get you hurt. If we go with them they will bring us right to Dr. Inferno."
