The Ballad of Luke - to be played on the flute (Ballads are usually on a flute, right? Anybody play one?)

 

This is the tale,

the tale of Sir Luke,

of when S.A.M. the spam

gave us all a spook!

 

S.A.M. looked innocent at first,

a simple personage of annoying humor,

he haunted Luke's postings

naught but a muttering rumour.

 

But then, lo!

We were surprised so,

when his true colors at last he did show.

 

Nay, they were not blue,

as we were led to believe

they were darker, true,

the colors of an imminent thieve.

 

He seized the account of lukey3449,

Raving, "It shall be mine!",

Sir Luke did suffer,

degraded to the mere Mr. Puffer!

 

But he would not be held down,

and brought the punishment back around,

capturing S.A.M. in the midst of an attempt to confound

the other knights on their own story grounds.

 

Now S.A.M. is locked up tight

In a glass cage (to let in light)

Glaring mutinously at Luke the knight

from day to night,

feeling furious for being put right.

 

This is the tale,

the tale of Sir Luke,

of when S.A.M. the spam

gave us all a spook!

 

No longer are we worried about S.A.M.

and no one can condem

Luke for putting him in a vault

Even though, technically,

It was all Luke's fault!

 

 

The End
