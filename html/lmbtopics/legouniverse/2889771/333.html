Twenty-seven, right? I can never remember.

Well hello there, my fellow knights! Fancy seeing you here. Now, I know I haven't been on in like a week, but I have a really good reason: It was me and my brothers 13th birthday, and we we're going to california for a week, mainly for Disneyland, but we also went to Legoland, (Legoland has a 78$ pizza & pasta all you can eat buffet. Or, it was that much for 3 adults and two kids. ) and California Adventure Park, which is also part of Disney. And it was AWESOME. I really loved it, and I'm really happy my parents took me (And my brother and my sister. ) there. I won't really go into detail about my favorite ride or anything, because some people might not remember Disney if you've ever been there, or if you've even been there at all... So, I had a LOT of fun there, and the place we stayed at was nice. It wasn't a hotel, actually, these people have this small house LITTERELY 9 minutes from Disneyland, and they let people rent half of it for a bit, It was a really nice place, but their neighbors we're a BIIIT weird: So, they are older people, but they had this one weird quirk: They wouldn't let people step on their grass. Now, the grass wasn't even nice grass, it was full of weeds and actually not very much grass there. So, once I went out to our car to get something, which was on the driveway of the peoples place we we're staying at, and the old guy was sitting in a chair, not ON his grass, but on the tiny little pathway inbetween the grass, and when I walked out there he waggeled his finger at me. That was the only thing that happened like that, but I thought it was funny. But even his tiny DOG doesn't walk on the grass. I mean, it's a DOG. How they trained it to do that I will never know. We also went to the beach on two of the days, a different beach each time. OKAY NOW I'M RUNNING OUT OF CHARACTERS PART TWO COMMENCE!

Bad placement of this? ) So, my sister found this kind of big hermit crab down by the water, which was like 50-100 yards down the beach, cause it was REALLY low tide, if thats a thing. So we left our place with our towels and stuff, which conveiniently had a chip bag set out. So, we all went down to look at the hermit crab, and in litterely a minute, my brother heard this commotion and looked up: It was a litteral SWARM of like 30 birds on the towels and stuff going for the chips.
