m579 wrote:

Hey guys! A couple of things:

 

What does PNG stand for? I've heard it refrenced by Talmid and others.

 

And, I'm planning to read up on the KOTOS stories, where should I start?

PNG is a file extension for pictures.  I think you meant PCG, which stands for Phoenix Command Group, it was a faction in a Star Trek roleplay from the Creation Lab, which has since moved on from LEGO.com. The whole roleplay started about the PCG, which fought to honor the good, defend the weak, and make everything right that the Federation and the rest of the galaxy did wrong.  It was a very noble group of great friends and great builders.

 

There were other factions as well, like the Minotaur Space Imperium, Martian Empire, the Scorpian Attack Force, and of course the Federation. I was Star Fleet, and quite a radical Star Fleet captain at that, with my own unorthodox-style ship that gets blown up all the time. Outside of RP we were all friends and often we collaborated together with writing stories, building mega starships, and just doing great things. Sort of like what us as the Knights of the Old Speech do now.

m579 wrote:

Hey guys! A couple of things:

 

What does PNG stand for? I've heard it refrenced by Talmid and others.

 

And, I'm planning to read up on the KOTOS stories, where should I start?

I see you have already read the Additional Manuscripts, so I recommend reading Perpetual Doom. It's our only (so far) completed multiwriter based solely on the KOTOS. It's..... big. Really big. But it's the backbone of all the rest of the KOTOS stories, and it's pretty good, if I may say so while being one of the participants. After that you might want to read Scattered Ashes, it's sequel, but there's only a few (epic) chapters of that so it should be a snap in comparison. Perpetual Doom, Scattered Ashes and the Additional Manuscripts are the only KOTOS-based story topics, though most of the Knights are accomplished authors with some great indepentant stories under their belt if you are ever looking for some new reading material. Ace and FFFFFplayer (the Shadows) both have LU stories going currently, both are great, though FFFFFplayer's is farther in. I am also kind of the only reader on either, besides the mods.
