Hi Everyone! I have lots of stuff to ramble about this time, unlike last time. I am, after all, being graded. First off, my minifigures. Anyway. Did you guys see the new LEGO Movie Forum? I can't wait for the movie!! I've been making references to it for months. XD Do you guys have a favorite character? Benny is my favorite. Also, I came home after petting a dog today- my cat was extremely disturbed. I let her smell my hand, and her tail started expanding. I just fed her. She's fine now. The funny thing is, she acts like a dog. Except for when I'm doing my schoolwork. I was studying for a test today, and she just laid right down on top of my papers in front of me. So I spent study time petting her. XD

 

Out of characters. I guess that's the end of RR -2. *Imagines failing grade*
