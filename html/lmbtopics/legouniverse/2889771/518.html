But that got me thinking, what are your favorites movies? I know we all have 'em, so why not say it?

 

Mine go a little something like this: 

1: Starwars Series (The entire thing, I just love all of them so much )

2: Treasure Planet (Not only is it a parody of one of my favorite books, it just does a great job visually and story-wise)

3: Tigger Movie (Yeah, I know. But, it is the only movie I have ever seen that has made me cry. Ever.)

4: Muppets: A Christmas Carol (Muppets and Christmas Carol. It just works together so well.)

5: The Lord of the Rings Trilogy (Love the books, and I love the movies as much too)

 

These are my top 5, not in order, but does that really matter? They're good and classic in my opinion, and I think it'd be fun to know what you guys like to watch
