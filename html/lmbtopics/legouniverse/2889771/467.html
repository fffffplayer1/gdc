Now it is here!  I made this episode relatively quickly actually.

Day 1:
I started taking pictures, working well into the night until I got tired and went to sleep.

Day 2:
I took more pictures, added special effects to them, and assembled them all along with sound effects into Windows Movie Maker.  I also added fade and wipe special effects which I use for phasers and warp effects using Windows Movie Maker.  I then saved the file as a video, put it back in Windows Movie Maker, added music, then saved it again as another video.  I ought to make a video showing how I make animations....

Day 3:
I got my friends to record voices with me.  We recorded 20+ lines and I added them to the latest video file.  I then saved again, then put it back in, and split it into two files each smaller than 5 Megabytes.  I converted them to .mov files and uploaded them to the LEGO Videogames Gallery and now here the download links so you can watch Talmid Show!
