I hate to dredge up sad memories, like the closing of LEGO Universe.  That hit pretty hard.  Just before LU closed, I found another MMOG based on my favorite game series, Need for Speed World by Electronic Arts.  The sad thing is, that game which I've been playing since 2011, now it's closing too.

 

So, if I'm not posting as much lately, that's one more thing to blame it on.  I haven't been feeling too good lately, some not nice things have been happening in my life lately and this game shutting down is another not nice thing.  But, I have some nice things to say too!

 

I've been interested in "remastering" the original Talmid Show episodes, Episode 1, 2, and 3.  Episode 1 is simply the greatest, since its Episode 1, so that has priority.  Episode 1 is also in one way the worst quality episode of all of Talmid Show, since it was saved in a very low quality video resolution.  But last week I found the original screenshots from Episode 1, so now I can make it again and save it in better quality.

 

As part of remastering it, I'm primarily thinking of redoing the voice acting, primarily for Talmid, Dave, and Brad.  Back then my voice was a lot higher, so I had to use an audio editing program extensively to make Talmid sound like an old man, not a little kid.  Now he sounds like a teenager.  Either way he still sounded pretty messed up in those early episodes.  If you watch Episode 1, 2, or 3 and then watch anything from Season 1 Episode 4 onwards, the difference is significant.

 

Also, being the first episode, I didn't have much experience and technique sorted out yet, so there's a lot of microphone noise and stuff.  Dave, even though his voice sounds pretty much the same, his lines suffer from micropone noise, so I'd like to rerecord those.

 

Brad has been voiced by three different people.

 

However I want to keep Captain Andrea's lines because as a consequence of my deeper, natural Fleet Captain Talmid voice, I can't do her voice anymore....
