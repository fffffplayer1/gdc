JAMMAS ON The Peculiar Enchanter, and the Exiled Princess!

 

Jammers
Fancy intertwining way is my vote.  So then if we do what Jonna suggested, I suppose Peragrine, and my evil bad-guy agent are the only people I need to inform you two about.

You all know Peragrine, (well enough) so I don't need to post a bio about him. But I will take this moment to reveal my new bad guy! 

Maneya Kull, Rank 3 Valiant Paradox Sorceress.

 I could not remember if we had ever done any other Paradox Rouges other than Space Marauderers, so I went to LU wiki to make something more original. I personally never played a Paradox character, but LU wiki gave me the knowledge nessesary to write like I have.

Maneya Kull is not your average Sorc though. I am still working on her inner personality, but gernerally she despises people for thier weakness and insecurities, and veiws tehdude as the only person she has met with more backbone than her. Thus her loyalty to his cause, and her belief that he alone is 'worthy' to rule. She has specialized in more mystical powers, interrogation, and tracking, than in pure firepower, like most of Paradox.  That is not to say she is incapable of mass destruction though. This edited Excerpt shows some of her natural abilities.
Jammers

Maneya Kull swept through the leaves of the forest. She did not slow. The signs of travel were evident enough, and the light she followed still glowed sufficiently. Her Rouges followed at a heavier, slower, more unsure gait. Though they did not show it, she could feel their sparks fighting a pathetic urge to rest. She caught snippits of their thoughts:
[...]

The Sorceress refocused her attention to her front. She tilted her head, and strained her eyes. Her spell was wearing off; the glow was diminishing between the shrubery. 
[...]
Her tracking spell renewed, she continued along it's shining serpetine length. A shine that only she could see.
