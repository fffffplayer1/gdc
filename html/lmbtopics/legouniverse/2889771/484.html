     As the Knights of the Olde Speech jubilantly celebrate the return of one of their highly valued members and also Founder of their noble Order, something was approaching. Sir Talmid was chatting with Sir Thingguy filling him in at what he had lost in his absence; Lady Jonna was writing down lyrics for her welcoming back song; Sir Shard was preparing the buffets; other Knights were doing other things; Sir Thingguy was dancing. The atmosphere was quite light and laughter sounded again in the halls of the KotOS Castle.

     But then the something came. Immediately, the lights went dimmer and the atmosphere chillier. The smiles on the faces of the Knights faded. They all approached the windows to see what it was. A Cookie-Class Star Destroyer was hovering next to the Castle of the Knights.

     "Oh no! Not him!" Lady Jonna exclaimed not wanting to believe it "Did he have to come now?"

     The Star Destroyer landed and out of it came... the Supreme Duckz Commander. The man was wearing a velvet black suit and had a black top hat upon his head. He was holding a scepter. He had a mischievous grin on his face. He was quite fancy, one had to admit.

     "Stop him!" Sir Shard called "We can't let him in!"

     "We both know we can't keep him out if he want to get in!" Sir Talmid replied sadly.

     With one wave of his scepter, the gates of the Castle opened widely. The Supreme Duckz Commander walked in... with style, though. Soon, he was with the previously cheerful company.

     "Sooo, I've learnt that Sir Thaddeus Nathanael Thingguy the Second is back! I came to see it myself!"

     "What do you want?" Lady Jonna asked angrily.

     "Thingguy cannot stay neutral! He'll have to side with either of us! I came to ask where his allegiance lies!"

     "You can't take him on your side!" Jonna answered.

     "Poor Super Duck! Didn't Talmid side with me? Shall I remind you he's Duckz Vader!"

     Suddenly, you notice that everyone is duckz! Except for the SDC (and maybe Shard).
