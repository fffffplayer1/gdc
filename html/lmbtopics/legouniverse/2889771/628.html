The names Stirling and Peragrine were the names of my two main characters in the best game I have ever played, LEGO Universe.  I was a beta tester, for about a week, and I played it nearly every day, with my sister Jonna, who joined me after the game opened. There were others too, such as OrionMadix, Percevel, and SuperLegoLarry.  But L.U. was not to last. With the official announcement of it's shutdown, I left the game, without saying goodbye, or setting anything in order. A decision I regret to this day.  However, after the shutdown of L.U. I was lead to the Message boards where I was a silent observer for some time.  Eventually, Jonna began posting her story, Imagination Infection, here, and I was the ambiguous "Brother" in the backround of her posts...  But eventually, I began posting as well, and later (with our parents permission) revealed myself as the mysterious "Brother." 'Mysterious no longer.'  All things considered, everyone took it rather well, and I am always humbled when I reflect on how they have welcomed me. Or rather, my alter egos, Stirling and Peragrine, as I always try to post as one or the other. And now I am taking part in the Massivly Multi-writer Story (MMWS) "Scattered Ashes"  !!! I am overjoyed.
