There were a TON of really cool minifigures in the bags, like old Star Wars figs, a Professor Snape fig with a glowing head, an alien from Mars Mission (I think we'll name it Talmid JK!) and some ADU figs which Strider used to update his Space Ranger minifigure. It's going to be a lot of fun to build. The other set was a 2009 LEGO Star Wars anniversary set, and it hasn't been opened either. I'm not sure why the thrift shop taped the boxes if they were already closed in the first place.

Jammers on! You know who only (also Jonna and Strider, but be careful, Voldemort's manners are a bit a rusty)

                “Lady Charlotte! I have a question I would like to ask you!” Ruby said entering the room.

                Firelash and Squid were playing chess in the corner and John was watching them. Lady Charlotte was studying some manuscripts on her armchair. They were not in the drawing room for the public, of course. They were in a room where there was no danger of being seen. The three other Nexus-Forcers did not happen to be in the room.

                It had been a week since Ruby, John, Squid and the Nexus-Forcers arrived in Calibus Tower. Grongringro had left with a digging party before that and he had not returned since, so they hadn’t seen him. But there had been a warm reunion with Firelash.

                “What is it, Ruby?”

                “There was something bugging me concerning you all this time, but I couldn’t quite put my finger on it. But now it came to me. I remember looking at a family tree of the royal family once and saw that Queen Amelia, Jonna’s mother, had a sister Charlotte. Are you Jonna’s aunt?”

                “Indeed I am”

                “Why did you not tell us?”

                “I saw no reason to it. It wouldn’t change anything for you”

                “Why did Jonna never know about you? I’m sure she’d be more surprised than I am to hear that”

                “I don’t think I am the most qualified person to answer this question. Maybe you should ask Matthias, if you ever see him” the Lady sounded somewhat bitter saying that.

                “Milady, there seems to be a man wishing to see you downstairs” a manservant announced walking in the room.

                “Who is he?”

                “Apparently, he calls himself Lord Brocktree”
