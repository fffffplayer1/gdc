Ninjago_Builders:

Weapons: He has rank three Space Ranger armor, gadgets on his arms which have many different attachments, a grammar sword, and a correction shield. In case you do not know the Space Ranger’s powers, I will list them for you.
Space Ranger gun: Shoots a wide blast that can hit more than one enemy, though doesn’t go too far. If you know how far the Space Marauder’s gun shoots, than the Space Ranger’s is about the same. Think of the Space Ranger’s gun as shooting a wide bullet in the shape of a cone that’s the length of a minifigure.
Space Ranger head gear power: Creates a black hole that stuns enemies.
Space Ranger weapon charge up: Creates a shield around him that protects him from a certain amount of damage.
Space Ranger left hand weapon: Calls an orbital strike that makes a circle a little smaller than the size of an Assembly armor charge up around the nearest enemy or destructible. Blue arrows circle around the target for a few seconds until a blast hits the center of the circle. The closer enemies are to the blast in the center of the circle, the more they are damaged.
Space Ranger rank three shoulder pad ability: Makes him float up into the air in slow motion with his back arched as if electricity were being shot into his back. Then, missiles start coming out from him and the slow motion stops, the Space Ranger drops back to the ground and the missiles come down around him, damaging any enemies.
Four piece bonus: When you run out of armor it shields you from a certain amount of damage.
Five piece bonus: You get one piece of armor every time you smash an enemy.
Personality: He has a good sense of humor, and often makes sarcastic jokes. He is more serious in battle, though. A lot of times he can be a bit critical when someone messes up. If you shoot a bullet at him, he may do a back flip over it and let it hit the wall behind him, or he may just tilt his head slightly and let it wiz right past him. He prefers stealth and speed opposed to strength. One of his greatest moves was where he was battling an admiral invader on Crux Prime, and he was shooting it with his Space Ranger gun, when it drew back its arm to throw its chain and shoot “The rings of doom.” He waited until the admiral was about to shoot, and then flipped over its head and called and orbital strike from behind the admiral while it was still shooting where Ninjago_Builders used to be. (Admirals can’t move while they’re shooting their canon.)
