UltraDuck paced a snow-covered rooftop in the heart of Duckopolis City, Duckland. It was cold, and even his advanced suit was having trouble keeping him warm as the cold winds blew across the city.

 

As he looked across the city, he saw lights everywhere. Ducks were in flocks shopping and going about thier day happily, enjoying  the holiday together.

 

UltraDuck had never really celebrated Duckmas, but now that the thought crossed his mind, he started to reconsider. So he went home to his house to further consider the matter.

 

All along the way he saw carolers, groups of friends, and most of all he saw joy. He sighed. He had never really had friends, until he met all the other Duck heroes, and Gizzy, who of course sometimes wanted to eat him when his food bowl was empty.

 

Then an idea struck him like a bolt of lightning. He was thrown to the ground, electricity buzzing through his feathers as he realized it was a bolt of lightning.

 

He picked himself back off the ground and trudged home, thinking about this new idea.  He would call over all his friends to his house to celebrate.

 

Over the next few hours, he set up all kinds of decorations, from trees and candles to elegant wreaths and beautiful lights. After he was done, he put one present under the tree for each of his friends. That's when he realised he had a lot of friends.

 

UltraDuck called all of his friends due to "urgent matters". Usually that meant some national threat.

 

In just minutes they arrived, looking very formal at first but then relaxed and surprized.

 

And so UltraDuck and his friends spent Christmas singing, opening gifts, and chatting together.

 

The end.
