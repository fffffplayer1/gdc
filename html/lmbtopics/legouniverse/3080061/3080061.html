Okay the main character is going to be Cog Blammo. For those of you who don't remember Cog was the Paradox Vendor guy.
Chapter one:
Cog was on his way back to Nimbus Station after battling on Crux Prime. He was just setting his rocket on the launch pad when he heard a cry for help he turned his head and saw Admiral Flogmore attacking a Sentinel Space Ranger. Cog grabbed his Wormholer and leaped at Flogmore with a flying kick right in the face, Flogmore went stumbling backwards, Flogmore jumped right to his feet and started firing at Cog.
"Okay, I've had enough messing around with you!" Said Cog Blammo as he leaped into the air throwing his EMP bombs all over.
"Well that was easy." said Cog as he walked to grab the loot that the admiral dropped.
"Yes! A Jester Hat!" shouted Cog. "I can get at least 300,000 for this in Nimbus Station." Cog thought.
Cog set his rocket up on the launchpad and was off to Nimbus. When he arrived he found the usual crowd of people. Some selling some buying and some begging.
"Why do people have to beg so much?" groaned Cog as he headed to Nimbus Plaza to sell his Jester Hat.
Just as Cog was about to announce what he was selling he saw Wen Wuzzit. coming towards him.
"I wonder what he could want." Thought Cog, as Wen began to approach him.
"Hello Cog! Haven't seen you in a while. I was wondering if you had a little spare time on your hands, you see the Battle of Nimbus Station portal broke down and I need a little help fixing it. Mind helping me? It shouldn't take too long." said Wen.
"I guess I could help." replied Cog no knowing what he was getting himself into.

Okay that's the end of the first chapter hope you liked it. I posted it at midnight so there might be a few mistakes.
