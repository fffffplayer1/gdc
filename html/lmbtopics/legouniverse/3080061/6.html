Chapter 19
"Okay, we need to find a way out of here." said Duke.
"We are not going back the way we came in. And there's only one way out of this room. And that is down that hallway, so lets go." replied Hael, as he began walking down the hallway.
"This place gives me the creeps." said Duke, as he swatted at cobwebs.
"I can see light up ahead it must be an exit!" shouted Hael.
"Quiet, I hear something." said Duke.
"What is that noise? It sounds like someone starting a lawn mower." said Hael, as he quietly walked into the room.
"The giant!" shouted Duke quietly.
"It appears to be asleep. The exit is right over there, as quiet as you can lets walk towards the exit." said Hael. "Almost there."
"Spider! get it off me!" yelled Duke, quietly. as he yanked the giant spider off him and threw it.
"Um Duke. Watch where you throw spiders. You just hit the giant with it! said Hael.
"Run!" yelled Duke.
The giant stumbled to his feet. He was at least one hundred feet tall. He looked down and saw Duke and Hael running out the exit.
"The giant just made a new exit!" yelled Hael, as he turned to see the giant running towards them at extreme speed.
"Baron, start the ship up now!" yelled Duke, as he neared the ship.
"Duke hurry up!" yelled Hael, as he jumped into the ship.
"Help!" yelled Duke as the giant reached down and grabbed Duke.
"Baron, get the ship off the ground and fly it right into the giant!" ordered Hael.
The ship roared to life and flew towards the giant.
"Do not run into this time around hit him on the next pass!" yelled Hael.
"Hael, someone is opening the door in the cargo bay." said Doctor Overbuild.
Hael ran to the window and saw someone leaping out on top of the giant.
"That guy is crazy he cannot take that giant on by himself!" said Baron.
"That is why we are going to help him." replied Hael, as he ran to the cargo bay.
"Doctor Overbuild you stay here and pilot the ship me and Baron are going to jump out on top of the giant on the next pass!" yelled Hael, over the intercom.
"Ready Hael?" asked Baron.
"Ready as I will ever be." replied Hael.

Hael continued to slash at the monster. Duke brought a blast of imagination down on the giant. The monster reached down and grabbed Duke, but Duke broke free of the monster's grip, he got on the monster's shoulder jabbed his sword into the monster's back, and slid down the monsters back. With his sword in him all the way down. Just then Hael noticed the mysterious person who had jumped out of the ship earlier. The person got a machine gun out and started firing at the giant.
"We cannot defeat him! Retreat!" commanded Baron.
Doctor Overbuild saw the hopeless battle going on from the ship. He revved the ships engine up and began flying towards the giant. He flew it straight through the giant. The giant let out a loud moan and collapsed, on the ground.
"That is the last we should see of him." said Baron.
"Where is the mysterious person that helped us? I want to thank him for his help." stated Baron.
"He is gone. When he saw Doctor Overbuild coming at him in the ship, he took off into the desert." commented Duke.
"Doctor Overbuild landed over there. We better go see if he is all right." suggested Hael.
"Overbuild, are you alright?" asked Baron.
"Yes, I am alright. The ship isn't though." replied Doctor Overbuild.
"How bad is the damage?" Duke asked.
"Pretty bad. It should not take too long if we all work on it though. said Doctor Overbuild.
"Then what are we waiting for? Lets get started." ordered Baron.
