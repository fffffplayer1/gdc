‎07-30-2011 10:34 AM

Conclusion:

Thericlaw, a novice Toa of Air, was captured by the Dark Hunters and was used to complete the so-called "Omega Project", killing him in the process. Bohrok-L was created and after a few minor skirmishes with the Toa and Order of Mata Nui, Bohrok-L revealed that he was only one of many Bohrok-L clones. Toa Hraxas Kaita Lirik, Avkazot Nuva, Toa BTL, Toa LM, Jgenal, and a few others defeated the Bohrok-L clones. Toa Hraxas Kaita Lirik finally went solo against the final Bohrok-L and battled it in an epic duel to the death. Using his own powers and improvising with the things around him, he created a single shard of crystalline protodermis. Then he charged it up into a fist-sized sphere and sent it hurtling into the last Bohrok-L, killing it. The story concludes with the fact that after killing Bohrok-L's krana-kal, Toa Hraxas Kaita Lirik's infection completes, causing him to transform into a form of darkness.

Thank you all for roleplaying! And.......
