authors note: Jonna (or to a few who don't know her as well, Samlooploop) gace me permission to write a complete Paradoy of Imagination Infection. Here it goes...

Tala Sneaky was your average Avant Gardens minifig; she smashed Stromlings that she came across and walked around collecting coins when no weak Stromlings were nearby. She didn't smash Stromlings as much as other minifigs though. She had seen Newbies and other Minifigs go down after only two seconds from the last time they were smashed, which was enough to convince her to pick her fights wisely. And that is precicely what she did. Right now though, she had found a very weak Stromling and proceeded to chase him with a vengance!
She grumbled when the Stromling continued to evade her, plumeting her with rights and lefts, she had to smash him!
'Yikes, this one is powerful,' thought the frightend Stromling, fighting for his little Maelstrom life.
Suddely, Tala tripped over something in the ground and faceplanted with an audible thump.
"Yay!" yelled the Stromling, and, seeing he couldn't destroy this minifig, zig-zagged away at top speed.
"Ow."
Tala sat up with a grumble about how she was about to break her recomrd for most Stromlings smashed in one day. She looked at the place where she had tripped, and saw a sort of ball sticking three fourths out of the ground. It wasn't that big, just a little smaller than a Minifig's head. The ball was a grayish color, with swirls of blue and purple. Intricate carvings crept all over it, and it almost seemed to glow ever so faintly.
"Is this what I tripped over? Huh," Tala murmmered quizicly, slowly reaching forit. She picked it up, curious.
big mistake, Tala gasped as pain shivered through her arm, flowing into the rest of her. The ball began to glow for certain now, a light blueish color. she felt her whole body go limp without warning, and she fell over, unconcious.
------------------------------------------------
Tala opened her eyes. Had she been sleeping right in the middle of the battlefield? How long? Why had no one smashed har? She looked up at the sun. It couldn't have been that long. She got up, looking for a Stromling to smash. There was one some ways off, so she set off in that direction. "Hey you, Minifig! Come help me smash that Stromling over there," she called out to an idle Minifig. It turned towards her, and actually began attacking her. Other nearby Minifigs did that too. "AH! Not me!" She yelped, runniong to a clump of trees. She dove in, bewildered. "What were they thinking?!"
Tala paused. She felt funny... itchy all over. Reaching up to scratch her forehead, she stopped with a squeak. Her hand was purple, instead of a right hand she had a right claw. She was wearing a torn up purple shirt. Red pants. Sticking up hair, all purple. Instead of falling gently around her face it stood straight up. Tala stared in shock at the Stromling that was her.
"I... I've been infected!..."
She passed out.
---------------------------
How do you like it Jonna? Should I stick to this style or make it a little more different than the original? And by the way, believe it or not, I did not use any copy and pate during that chapter.

"What!" Yelled Stevor, pushing down the other Stromling.
They continued fighting (Stromlings have terrible manners) as they left the room.
Slowly, Tala remembered everything that happened, including the ball. She was in a 'Hospital' whitch was really an old tree in Gnarled forest.
Stevor walked in, his clothes torn up... it took Tala a moment to realize that all Stromlings had torn up clothes.
"So, how are you feeling miss? He asked as politely as a Stromling could, (whitch wasn't very polite).
"Uh... I, um... pretty well..."
"You must be experiencing slight Maelstrom distruption- brain damge! Not to worry, I'll explain everything! It's my first dayon the job you know so I'm kinda excited! A Stromling Mech found you and brought you here. -here being the gnarled forest Stromling hospitak- He said you were in some bushes at Avant Gardens. Can you remember your name?"
"...Kinda-"
"Hmm. Uncommon name. Not that anything is bad about it of course." He added. "Anyways, you seem alright. We haven't had much buisness lately, but this hospital is kinda small. The exit is right there. That Stromling who found you paid two silver coins before he left. He said 'Take care of her. And if you do, I'll pay any extra expences.' so I suppose you won't have to worry about money."
Tala was surprised. Not even Minifigures had helped her when she was in danger.
Stevor once again gestured to the Exit, then left to tend to some more Stromlings. Tala tried hard not to pounce on all the helpless Stromlings. She noticed a room where a half infected Stromling twist and turn on a bed, a Stromling trying and trying to infect him. One Stromling sat nearby, listening to another Stromling say "I don't know, it may be too late now..."
Tala arived at the Exit. There was nobody to hold the door open for her, but manners couldn't be expected by a Stromling. Tala looked around. What was she gonna do now? She was infected and in the middle of Gnarled forest. "Vanda can probably fix me" she thought. But this was a golden oppertunity. The knoledge she could learn for the Faction leaders could turn the tide of this war...
"For Nexus Force," She decided firmly. She would learn the secrets of her enemys. And then she would turn the tide of this war.
