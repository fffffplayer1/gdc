Chapter Four: Books and Bad guys

‎10-09-2012 09:26 PM

YAY! New chapter!!!


Tala ran up to Medic steampipe, the Dis-assembly leader. At least, she tried to run. It was kind of hard to run as a Stromling and she ended up tripping and falling. She got back up again. 'I'd like to join Dis-assembly," she giggled- er- growled, strangely excited. It was weird being excited.Wait, she was a minifigure. why was she so excited to join the Maelstrom, her enemies? These thoughts began to confuse her, making her feel a lot less excited. But Medic didn't seem to notice. He did sort of a Stromling-smile. At least, I guess it was kind of a smile.
'It's always nice when another Stromling joins us! so which pack do you want? Destroyer, Demolisher, or Un-builder?" He asked, trying to be kind. Tala didn't know what the difference was between them, so she picked randomly.
"Um... Demolisher please," she said. Medic looked at her strangely. "I mean- Demolisher" it was going to be hard for her to forget her manners. she realized that Stromlings almost never used manners. Medic tried to smile again, 'Kinda shy huh? don't worry, you'll warm up soon enough! Once those stinky Nexus Force recruits start pouncing on you you'll be begging to have a good team!" tala had trouble stopping herself. Nobody called the Nexus Force stinky.
"Here's your gear!" Said Medic, handing her the gear. Tala didn't leave.
"Um... is something wrong?' Medic asked her.
"Huh? Aren't you supposed to give me a book?" Tala asked.
"We can't read"
Tala could have facepalmed herself. She was surprised that nobody suspected her yet. She walked towards Dis-assembly tower. Sitting down on a rock she looked around, trying to see what people did here. Stromlings stood in the middle of the clearing, chatting, growling, and trading information. They all seemed so happy. Tala figured it wouldn't last long. Once they got on the battlefeild their wouldn't be anything to be happy about. Or so she thought. a Stromling in green clothes came over from the Pirates vendor, Magna Bumpingdent, and started to sweep the Dis-assembly pathway while half whistling and half growling. How strange it was to Tala! Her thoughts drifted off, pondering how in Gnarled Forest a Minifigure got here.
that ball... the ball... what happnened to it? Tala nervously looked behind her, as if the ball was right there, ready to turn her into something worse. when she saw that there was nothing there she began to think again. Why was she, of all Minifigures, infected? Was it a coincidence? That ball. Maybe it-
Tala winced as the Stromling that was sweeping began to whistle an extremely loud note. That, combinding with the growling, was so annoying that Medic Steampipe had closed his shop for a break. Now the minifigure was trying to slash the broom around like a sword. Tala couldn't take it anymore.
"What is wrong with you?!!!" she yelled, running over with her hands on her hips. the minifigure just smiled and and said 'Oh, the usual' as he attempted a pole vault. It didn't go very well. He splatted upside-down on one of the booths.
"I'm a daredevil!"
he fell off the booth and onto the ground face-first. tala cocked er head to the side
and blinked. Stromlings were strange. The Pirate/Daredevil got up.
"Hi!" My name is Percygrin!"
Tala nodded and replied, "I'm Tala"

Percygrin and Tala decided to team up in Forbidden valley. They heard that there was some trouble there. Walking to a Maelstrom-launcher, Tala wondered with amazement how she was supposed to use it.

"Um, what is this?"

Percygrin looked at Tala. 'It's a launcher. You enter your card, and a vortex appears, flying you to forbdden valley."

Tala looked at the launcher once again. 'and- um- what happens if you don't have a card?"

Percygrin smiled. "Don't worry, you can use mine." As he said this, Percygrin placed the card into the machine, and a vortex appeared. Percygrin jumped in, followed by Tala.

It was a long journey through the vortex. Tala looked in her backpack for something to eat. Then she gasped. Sitting at the bottom was the ball, no longer glowing. Tala almost screamed but she quickly reached out to touch it. Nothing happened. dissapointed, Tala placed the Ball back in her Backpack. she would look at it later. right now, she had to learn the Maelstrom'ssecrets. No matter what the costs.
