Toa Team: Hauden Nuva

 

Name Derives From: A combination of Iden, 'spirit', and Hau, 'shielding', with the suffix of Nuva, meaning 'new'.  The name translates to 'the new protectors of the spirit'.

 

Goal: Prevent Inharax from taking full control of his body before Mata Nui can respond.

 

Team Members: Demaron (Toa of Sonics, Leader), Fesur (Toa of Iron, Deputy), Vonai (Toa of Lightning), Fanos (Toa of Magnetism), Sukan (Toa of Plasma), Batudon (Toa of Gravity), Bohak (Toa of Plantlife) and Cenisa (Toa of Psionics, Third-in-Command).

 

History: After meeting each other again, Demaron and his team set out to reawaken Mata Nui from his slumber.  Having received the Scroll of Preparations in Metru Nui and been gifted the Heart of the Visorak only to discover that the Dark Hunters had stolen the Staff of Artakha, they left for Odina and then New Xia once they had found the records pointing to its sale.  It was at this point that they first discovered the nature of their universe via Lightstone message from Blarkrok.  Having decided to engage in battle with the Makuta directly to halt their plans, they flew there and laid waste to Destral (after which they first met Kapronan) - only to find an that an unwanted passenger called Cold Steel had the ability to shut off Elemental and Kanohi powers and wipe memories.

 

Continuing with the Staff of Artakha, the Hauden bumped into the Makuta invasion of Metru Nui.  Realising that the battle was hopeless, they fled to the Toa Headquarters - which had been converted into a Brotherhood fortress.  Suddenly facing a substantial proportion of the Makuta's armies, Cenisa sent out a Psionic signal to all of the Toa in the universe requesting aid.  This resulted in the largest gathering of Toa in the history of the Matoran universe in one place, and the Brotherhood forces withdrew.  Only then did the Hauden learn that Mata Nui was dead, causing Kapronan to use the Ignika - using his last remaining energy, he transformed them into the Toa Hauden Nuva.
