Demaron, Toa of Sonics

 

Weapons:

 

Sonic Staff: a six-bladed spear-like weapon that can be wielded with one or two hands and focuses Demaron's Sonics powers.

Sonic Bolt Blaster: a compact blaster that uses miniature sonic explosions to launch bolts.  Does not draw on the users elemental energy.  Fires one of two types of bolts: a piercing variety, with an incredibly sharp point and streamlined design to slice through even the toughest protosteel, or a Sonic variety, which detonates a Sonic shockwave on impact, to knock enemies out and blow them backwards, as well as doing small-scale explosive damage.  Bolts of the same type reload automatically, but the different types have to be switched manually.  Can be placed on Demaron's back while Sonic Staff is in use two-handed use.

 

Background:

 

Many know that, in the time before time, six Toa of the main elements were placed in the Codrex, the heart of Mata Nui, in order to reawaken him if he should ever succumb to some cruel fate, each named for their elemental prefixes.  What they do not know is that eight Toa of the secondary elements were also created, who were to protect Mata Nui from ever succumbing.  The leader of this Toa Team was Demaron, a powerful and intelligent Toa of Sonics.  He and his team defended Mata Nui for a while, but all but him were wiped out in the Great Cataclysm by the massive earthquake that struck.  Since then, he has wandered the Matoran Universe, bitter at the loss of his friends and allies, but never forgetting his original mission.  He aids any good cause that he can find, but always remains distant from anyone, believing that separation protects him from loss.

 

Personality:

 

Before the Great Cataclysm, Demaron was an enthusiastic and intelligent leader and fighter.  Now he is a lot colder, but more experienced, and greater than ever before.  Occasionally some of his old joy shows through, but he often tries to suppress this.

 

Suva Masks: Rode, Faxon, Pehuki, Kiril, Akaku, Mask of Healing
