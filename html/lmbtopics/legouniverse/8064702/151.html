(Guess what I've been playing way too much of? >.>)

 

Name: Hokru

 

Species: ???

 

Mask: No discernible Kanohi, but he wears a VERY ornate mask.

 

Element: ???

 

Weapons/Tools/Defenses/Abilities: The strange, tall being seemed to carry a dark aura around him, as those who were near him would feel a strange chill. His only physical weapon seemed to be a large staff/mace with a long wooden handle encasing a Protosteel support wire. Higher up, the weapon itself was the leg bone of a large, straight Kane-ra Bull, multiple sharp Protosteel spikes pierced through it to make the weapon itself extra lethal.

 

Alongside his weapon, Hokru himself was a very experienced alchemist, but due to his lust for knowledge regarding the subject, he had accidentally infused himself with a number of masks, giving himself a number of their powers. The following masks are:

 

Tryna, Iden, Elda, Mahiki, Mask of Scavenging, Mask of Clairvoyance, Rau, and the Mask of Fusion.

 

Appearance: Hokru was quite tall – a little bit taller than your average Makuta – though this could be attributed to his massive mask that was the size of a large shield. Speaking of which, the mask was a very ornate craft that extended far above his head, seemingly crafted from the parts of multiple fallen Rahi. It was a rough 'V' shape, starting with the head of a Gukko fitted into a more practical thing to wear. Higher up, the wings of said Gukko were made into a headdress with wooden frames holding the Rahi parts together. The mask was topped with the horns of a Kanohi Dragon. Gems were also scattered about it that often glowed for some reason.

 

Backstory: Will be explained ICly.

"Oh, his food is fine. Just...make sure to get the 'boneless' meat."

Dinner on the rails! Or...off the rails...whichever works.
