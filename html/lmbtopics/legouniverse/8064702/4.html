I might as well post my character's profile.

 

Name: Horez

Species: Makuta

Alignment: Brotherhood of Makuta

Occupation: Brutal warrior.

Powers: All of the Makuta powers. He is extremely proficient with plasma, heat vision, and the two lightning powers.

 

Kanohi: Mask of Psychosis. Inflicts severe, but temporary, mental problems on people within a small radius. Examples include insanity, hallucinations, paranoia, inability to focus or concentrate. Effectiveness is determined by proximity to Horez, and problems can become permanent if the person was already unstable or is exposed for a very long time.

 

Tools: Glaivesword. A protosteel weapon with two connected, curved blades. One overlaps the other, and the weapon can be used as a sword, axe, or falx.

 

Abilities: Horez never evolved beyond a body, and thus can effect and improve his body through training.

He has extreme reflexes, surpassing any other Makuta.

He is physically weaker and less durable then most Makuta, but as a Makuta is still extremely strong and durable.

Horez is an excellent fighter, having spent much of the last 90,000 years of his life honing his skills to the extreme.

He has a natural resistence to powers that effect him physically, a result of having minions repeatedly use paralyzing, power-draining, and other negatively effecting powers on him so he can resist and grow stronger.

Horez has trained and developed mental shielding as well, a part of his training as well.

 

Other notes: Horez tends to use his powers in unusual, excessive, and creative way.

Continued from my previous post

 


History: Horez was created over 100,000 years ago with the rest of the Makuta species, and was assigned with the task of creating Rahi to fill the Matoran Universe with. Although he was fully capable of making Rahi, he only ever made a few, and oddly enough the few he made were generally of the more helpful and peaceful kind. The primary reason, although no one else ever figured it out, was that he felt like just some simple creation, making even simpler things. In the eyes of his creators, he imagined was about as complex and intelligent as a Rahi was to him.

 

Eventually, this spurred on a search to see if anyone in the Matoran Universe knew how to create the Makuta or had the materials to do so, in the hopes of tracking down the mastermind behind the creation of him and his species. He left with an announcement to the brotherhood, and a personal warning to Icarax, to watch his back and be wary of his fellow Makuta.

 

With a great deal of effort, and luck, he found the antidermis pool. Before he could do anything further, he came under attack by a member of the Hand of Artahka, who had been assigned to follow him. The soldier of the Hand found herself outclassed, and in a desperate manouvre, opened a dimensional portal to swallow up Horez. His dodge attempt knocked them both into the Antidermis pool. When the two emerged, a portion of Horez's essence and some antidermis had fused with the soldier, partially turning her into a female replica of Horez. Before her mind was completely replaced by the new mind inside, she threw herself into Horez, knocking them both into the portal.

 

The new dimension he was propelled into was one of utter madness, full of emptiness and floating, circular islands. What he saw and did there, aside from fight as a gladiator, he has not and will not say.

He seems to have encountered darker versions of great heroes, from Gurthang to Mata-Nui, and other unspeakable horrors, that have forever changed him.
