Main timeline: Gara was just an average sonics Matoran, just an average joe. Until one day, he got stuck into a Muaka encounter. Just before he was mauled, a bright flash occured, and blew away the Muaka. Standing there, was a Toa with Red and Black armor. Gawking, Gara asked who he was. The Toa answered, "Trynyx, and I need your help." When Gara asked why, the Toa explained that he was from the future, where a plague had gotten rid of all sentient life. (Which was partly true, since the creatures that everyone became could not be condsidered sentient.) He then explained that Gara grew up to possibly be the greatest Toa of his time in the future, before the plague got him too. So, he came to train him with a genetic device that allowed Gara to copy his opponents appearance. Gara asked why he needed this, and then Trynyx told Gara that he needed to capture a picture of all sentient life in his current time. When he was done, Trynyx would return and use the genetic DNA stored in them to revive all the life in his time. Trynyx trained Gara in all the martial arts he knew, to help him in all sorts of situations. During his training, Gara went from innocent to rock-hard, and overall, was serious almost all the time. When Trynyx was done, he left back to his own time. What Gara didn't know was that Trynyx had secretly installed a 'genetic copy' function into the belt that would only activate when Gara had, indeed, collected all of the DNA it required. It would create a perfect duplicate of all in Gara's time, and not only that, the clones were immune to the virus, so that when it spread, they could fight back and become the people of Trynyx's time. However, when Trynyx got back, he discovered something horrible. While the same events DID happen, they fought it off WAY quicker, but the virus had spread to them. However, instead of turning them mindless, the clones went power-mad, and smashed all the originals.

 

Trynyx was never heard from again.

Speices name: Tehrrahk

 

Size: generally anywhere between 1 and 80 feet, but most are 20 feet

 

Appearance: 4 legs that stick out sideways like a small lizard, large dragon wings, and a massive stinger tail that is larger than the body.

 

ABILITIES AND TRAITS:

Tehrrahk are mechanical mayhem that can auto repair themselfs; they also have incredible speed and strength.

Can turn invisible, controls silence, and each have one of the most advanced cloaking devices in the matoran universe.

Tehrrahk have powers of withering and decay.

 

Tehrrahk use their claws, stinger, and snapping jaw. All of which have the withering effect.

Tehrrahk can envelope their stingers with witherflame, giving the wither effect to its victim. 

 

Tehrrahk cannot be possesed by Makuta when Cold Steel is exerting his will over them or just when he is nearby.
