Name: Zenila

 

Title: The Botanical Maiden, The Matriarch of Botany

 

Species: Makuta...Plant...Smashed Toa..Glatorian......thing

 

Element: Plantlife and a weaker than most Makuta's Shadow

 

Kanohi: The Ketilon, Mask of Purity, Cures, secondary of a Hau

 

Appearance: She has many shields on her body. In this form, she's meant to be extremely durable and safe. The many shields on her, on every limb and covering her make her much slower, but durable. Ultimately, two shields held in place by an unknown force hover on her sides, able to almost completely envelop her, with hundreds of smaller tiny bits also held by the unknown force forming the places not covered, when she goes into sphere mode. Moss is little, instead her favoring leafs, as they sprout all over her and her shields seem to resemble bushes or shrubs or trees. Her face becomes a simplistic miru.

 

Personality: Although held at the same ideologies with plantlife, she is far less aggressive. Where Zenila in Attack Form would utterly annihilate a city like Metru Nui for simple kicks, Zenila in Defense Form would likely be the defender. She is much more peaceful, seeking a less destructive method to achieve her goal.

 

Special Ability: She, due to her deep bond with plantlife, cannot have her plant-based powers shut off

 

Tools: Lovely Lily is a shield that can fire missiles that, the after-effects increase vegetation production by 70%! A fabulous tool for economic growth...or for your local Plant wielding tyrant!

 

Prideful Amaryllis is, actually, a plant! A lovely flower, it is often mistaken for the Opal Rose, or Zenila's prized Orchid and or Lotus. But this lovely plant is very, very endurable. Very strong and endurable, it is flexible enough too. It's strength exceeds that of most protosteels. Prideful Amaryllis is a razor shield hovering near her back.

 

 

We are all doomed

 

luckily, this is the usually more prominent one, except for Zenila: Peace Form, which is next!

 

and yes, the Amaryllis is a real flower which can mean Pride

Name: Emarcee

 

Species: super-intelligent Glatorian. AKA "Great Being."

 

Powers: Has incredible imagination, and thus, is capable of creating all sorts of gadgets and cool tech.

 

Element: "Waffle Power." Though strictly speaking, this is a power, Emarcee abuses it as if it were an element. "Waffle Power" is indistinguishable from your regular old shadow bolt, is usually waffle shaped, and is usually teal in color.

 

Appearance: Imagine an astronaut suit... Okay. Paint it teal. Now fashion it completely out of metal, and make it closer in appearance to what a knight would wear, but still space-travel worthy. Now make it ridiculously ornate, with teeny tiny glowy glyphs. This is what Emarcee looks like.

 

Weapons: His only weapon is a thin metal rod, that can extend two large blades of protosteel, specially designed to be cool looking, rather than completely functional.

 

 

Bio: He, along with other Agori and Glatorian that were called "Great Beings" helped to create the GSR, and nearly everything in it.  Of note among his prolific creations are the stunning fjords, that, if his work was being rated, he'd most certainly recieve an award of some sort. When the GSR was completed, Emarcee took it upon himself to monitor it from the inside. The nanotechnology had been performing as planned, and all was going smoothly. Until...

 

The creation had taken it upon himself to create. This was most unexpected, and in fact, was quite inspirational to the "Great" Being. The Makuta were the most interesting being that Emarcee encountered, and you can probably guess what he did next.

Name: Toa Derv
Species: Ta-Toa
Powers: Fire, some degree of Plasma
Kanohi:
Mask of Implausibility:
A custom-built Kanohi made exclusively for trolling. The Mask of Implausibility causes implausible things to happen. For instants, Derv could do that whole offscreen teleportation thing or punch out a Makuta. In general, it tends to ignore pesky things like physics and powers in favor of the Rule of Cool. The mask is, however, totally unable to help Derv do anything that is plausible, realistic, or possible normally. Even its creator, Zul, does not fully understand the way it works. If he did, it would likely stop working. The mask is easily defeatable by simply removing it from Derv's face, particularly without him noticing.
Personality:
Derv is fairly easygoing and is a lot more like a Le-Toa then his actual element. He enjoys watching others try and fail to figure out his mask.
Weapons: Derv's armor contains several vents and an elemental converter that allows him to access Plasma, at least for projection and absorption. This serves the purpose of letting him fly, and is compact enough through GB tech that it does not hinder his movement. He also has two plasma swords that interface with the armor to project two solid blades of superheated plasma.

 

Name: Zul
Species: Formerly a super-genius Glatorian known as a Great Being, now a disembodied conciousness inhabiting the Mask of Implausibility.
Personality: Very annoying. Very. however, he has become fond of Derv, and will do his best to protect his charge
Backstory:Zul personally created Derv, the Mask of Implausibility, and Derv's armor. When the GSR was launched, he projected himself into the mask in order to find out. In the mask, he can speak to Derv mentally, as well as access his senses.
