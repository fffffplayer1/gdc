What can I say? I make MOCs and when I make MOCs, people get hurt

 

Name: Wow, I'll have to come up with a better name than "Illidak" later

Species: Toa/Makuta fusion. (isn't EP just wonderful?)

Element: slightly limited Plasma, severely limited shadow

Powers: Plasma (able to use the kraata power of plasma at no expense to his regular elemental power), electricity, fire, heat resistance, anger, fear, poison, hunger (cannot use the power he's absorbed), chain lightning, corruption, heat vision.

Kanohi: Kakama. This kakama has been permanently fused to his face and to boot the EP fusion left him with a quarter of its regular power. As an additional limiting factor the kanohi can only be activated on a cooldown. The kakama is shaped like a jutlin.

physiology: He has no natural eyes and instead uses unliving antidermis that has been permanently fused to his face to see. He is able to see just as well as he would have if he had regular eyes, but has the added benefit of being able to sense nearby Makuta. The antidermis is dead though and is incapable of telepathy. He has above average agility and strength thanks to both the fusion and his prior training. He has wing-light appendages that allow him to perform the previously-thought-to-be-impossible double jump technique which up until now has been limited to video game physics.

Colors: red with gunmetal gray and black.

Weapons: Two protosteel warglaives

Personality: No matter what he is always serious and accomplishes tasks with efficiency and and priority. He hates having to take a break from a hunt to protect innocents, and would rather be chasing after Makuta than fighting their minions if at all possible. He grossly hates Makuta to an extreme point, but has the discipline to unleash this hatred constructively. He's not social, and in fact is a bit of a loner. But nonetheless a valuable ally to have. He has a habit of making sure people are prepared.

Backstory: Please refer to the LEGO.com gallery description

Apparently, even if I translate it, I can't use a foreign language in my signature.
