Behavior:

Megawhatt's exist to have "fun," which in their case is to cause trouble wherever they go. While they cause a lot of damage, Megawhatt's do not seem to be evil, and in fact are not openly hostile to any species except prey, merely lacking any self-control or sense of the danger they may be causing.

Megawhatt's will openly attack if they are provoked.

The Megawhatt's language consists of a series of high-pitched squeaking and chirping noises. That is how WILD Megawhatt's behave. The queen of each colony is the oppisite, she loves to play, but she has MAJOR self control, to the point of having 100% self control.

Name: Thep Khufan

Plural: Thep Khufans

Habitat: Anur Khufos. A desert like island with small oasis's scattered and a small rain forest. They thep Khufan dwell near a oasis, most live by Lake Cotumba. It's the biggest village. Anur Khufos is the largest in the anur system. It's special because Anur Khufos has the MOST Corrodium, even more so than Anur Phaetos. The Thep Khufan, due to being created near the most corrodium developed the best resistance to it.

Appearence: Most males look like benmummy or snareoh from the original series but with blue glow instead of green. The females have the purple glow. Some look like Snareoh from omniverse, with either blue glow or purple depending on gender. Some look like Kuphulu from omniverse, or the other Thep Khufan's from the ben 10 wiki's thep khfuan page.

Abilities and powers: Regeneration, space(vacuum) surrvivability, stretchable limbs, limited shapeshifting(limited to adding or removing limbs), enhanced strength, enhanced speed, enhanced durability. Due to being the most resistant to corrodium, there limbs(bandages) are even block the radiation completely. Allowing them to carry it, and even eat it! They also have telepathy, elemental shadow(toa level/makuta level), plantlife at turaga level and even sonics!

Behavior: They are GENERALLY peaceful, they have a immense dislike of anything to do with volcanos. They are generally harmless, however if provoked enough will not stop attacking untill that who provoked is eliminated. If insulted they get upset and attack for a apology.

Predator: None like all Anur system creatures

Prey: Pretty much anything the size of a turaga or matoran or smaller.

 

 

*shortened for space*
