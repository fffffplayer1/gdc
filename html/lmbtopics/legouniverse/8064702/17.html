Name: Veneras
Alignment: Moral Justice
Gender: Male
Appearance: Veneras is adorned in the traditional white armor of the Ice Toa, accented with ice-blue elements, including a long caped that flows like the blizzard. His armor is detailed with glowing blue details and runes, inlucind the details of his Kanohi Hau. He is not particularly tall for a Toa, though not as short as an Onu-Toa. He carries a war-staff detailed with ancient markings and inscriptions. Each end of the scepter features thin blades
that run along it's edge.
Weapons: Veneras carries a white scepter, with brightly glowing blue inscriptions of Protosteel. Upon contact, the scepter channels Veneras's energies, freezing anything in reach into hopeless desolation. 

Powers and Abilities: Veneras controls the ice and cold of the world. He is energies draw the heat out of his target until it has frozen completely. As he walks, the very ground freezes beneath his feet.
Traits: Veneras is brilliant far beyond most, a philosophical thinker among the Toa, obsessed with the philosophies of Truth and Justice. As a Toa he is sworn to protect these moralities. As an ice-toa he is especially cold to those who do not act in justice and truth. He is very perceptive, always able to discern the truth from the lies. He is very argumentative, which renders him more talkative than traditional Ice-Toa. He spends much of his time in Ko Koro, contemplating. He hopes to think through every situation as to always act morally.
Biography: Veneras was a scholar in the fortress of Ko Koro. His desire to preserve Justice lead to him being chosen as a Toa. Historically, Veneras fights alone as he is not part of any toa team. He roams the island of Mata Nui. In times of peace he watches the matoran, and teaches them the virtues of Truth, Justice, and Morality. In times of internal conflict, Veneras has served as Arbiter and Judge. However, when anything threatens the safety of the matoran, Veneras fights with all the ferocity of a warrior.
