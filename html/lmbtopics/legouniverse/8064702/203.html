So, in other words, destroying this guy might actually cause even more damage then if it was intact.

Basic Model

 

Construct: Robotic Infantry

Power: Speed Kanoka- and Telekinesis Kanoka-derived mechanisms for full body control

Communications/Command: Directed remotely by Matoran strategists via Telepathy Kanoka

Intelligence: Psionics Kanoka-based combat-orientated A.I. mind; non-sentient and incapable of independent thought other than selection of attacks in battle; Biomechanics Kanoka-based sensory receptors

Shield: Shielding Kanoka-based enclosing shield

Structure: Protosteel armour; entire structure enhanced and preserved by Regeneration Kanoka and Strength Kanoka

Primary Weaponry: Twin dual-function arm-blaster/projector are able to launch Speed Kanoka-based kinetic energy blasts that severely dent or break opponent's armour (passes through most shields since blast is not physical object and does not actually do harm in itself, instead transferring kinetic energy to an object it touches) or project a Stasis Field (morally-neutral Kraata power copied and used via Emulation Kanoka-based technology)

 

Types

 

Name: Stalwart

Variation: Twin claw-blades attached to side of arm-blaster/projector; Weaken Kanoka-based technology eases armour-piercing

 

Name: Harrier

Variations: Twin claw-blades attached to side of arm-blaster/projector; Weaken Kanoka-based technology eases armour-piercing; jetpack allowing nimble flight; finned lower legs for control during flight; slightly thinner armour for reduced weight

 

Name: Slicer

Variations: Blaster/projectors moved back to forearms; scythe with Weaken Kanoka-based technology eases armour-piercing; additional Strength and Speed Kanoka-based technology built into arms for more powerful swing

 

Name: Juggernaut

Variations: Only one arm-blaster/projector (weaker Stasis Field); Strength Kanoka-forged spinning drill-mace attached to end of other arm; additional Strength and Speed Kanoka-based technology built into mace arm for more powerful swing

 

Name: Sentinel

Variations: Rotating spear with arm-blaster projector; shield with drill-boss and Strength Kanoka.
