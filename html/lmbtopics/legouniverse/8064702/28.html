Name: Jilnt
Element: Air

Weapons: A kohlii staff like weapon that focuses one's elemental energy into a sphere.

One night after a stunt that involved a fader bull and a large boulder, a traveling toa offered him a stone. He said that he had heard of Jilnt's "adventures" in his time in the village and had seen potential in him. With a flash of light the small matoran was gone, and in his place was a toa. Jilnt is reckless, although he seems to be attempting to be more responsible since his transformation.

Name: Kerlu
Element: Ice
Kanohi: Mahiki
Appearance: White and pale blue armor.
Weapons: Twin protosteel scimitars.
Bio: Kerlu was more or less a pirate as a matoran, although an honerable one. After a successful raid upon a ga-matoran treasure hunting boat, he came across an interesting stone. There was nothing special about it, but Kerlu just felt there was something... special about it. A blinding light enveloped him and when it ended Kerlu had changed from a dirty (and slightly smelly) matoran, to a toa in shining white armor. He was overjoyed, and also torn between his old ways, and his newfound responsibility. His sense of honer won out, and after docking his small ship at the nearest port, departed to make some makuta very, very annoyed. Kerlu may seem overconfident and at times foolhardy, but is truly a strategic genius, possibly rivaling some makuta scientists.
