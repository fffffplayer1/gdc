Name:Texxidos [Tiks-eyd-us]

 

Race:Makuta

 

Mask: Name of the mask is unknown and it serves only as decorative thus having no powers (The look of mask is equal to head of Vortixx, long with that everlasting sinester grin on it)

 

Powers:Texxidos is Makuta who dwells deeply in to the knowlage of fear and agony.It is better to not live long enought to reach his grasps.Aside being very intelectual person ,Texxidos is also a person of wast strenght....dispite being very thin in statue

 

Weapons:a Staff that is used by Texxidos is Symbolic Icon of his might. Alongside Staff Texxidos also owns Pair of Katana shaped blades with gold and red colors. The Blades are very tought and normal blades have very low possibility of shattering them....however stronger or elemntal weapons can compare or even outrank the power of these swords.((Obviously i don't incloud legendary weapons only from the fact these swords are not strong enought to compare to them.))

 

Personality:Texxidos tend to be very serious, lacking any kind of Humor....it is no secret he enjoys some of more "brutal" actions.Also being very careful in nature...taking light steps near stronger members of the Brotherhood.(small note...don't let Texx see you are mentaly or physicaly weak...he is known for taking brutal acts on weaker.:-)))

 

Backstory(why don't you come and ask him....wait..better not to...no now i will quit joking)) Little is know about Texxidos past to outside of brotherhood...and not even in the circles of it...however some of older Members might renember this insignificant member from early ages of the brotherhood during the Six Kingdom Incident...amd meybe even his disappearance shortly after.((ask me if you want to know more about this character's past OOCly...however ICly you wont know much...if you are not some of the more powerful members of Makuta brotherhood))
