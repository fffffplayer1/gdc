‎03-01-2016 12:33 AM

I'm calling this new set of things called Crysona's. Ya know, CRYStalia, persONA? Get it? Okay I'll just stop now...when The Composer thoroughly trashed Crystalia, Crystalia split herself to escape. One of her got smashed. The other half went to the remains of the Frozen Palace(snow banks) and went into a deep slumber. This was not good cause then she got divided and stuff.

 

 

Name: Amare 'Love'

 

Species: Makuta

 

Element: Earth and limited Shadow

 

Mask: Olmak and a Mask of Allure(it works similar to a Mask of Charisma, but it doesn't care about morals)

 

Tools: Amare, if forced to ever even utilize a weapon, has an adoration for the Scythe, which she is extremely proficient in

 

Personality: She's very...prideful. She believes that the world should scrape at her feet for her being in her presence, but she doesn't flaunt it around like others might. If you want to know anything else about her. Just guess, her name makes it pretty clear. Amare is also very slow to anger, capable of enduring for centuries before blowing up.

 

Special Ability: The Mark of the Vampire. She can literally feed off of energy, even latent energy. If absorbed from a distance it is less potent, making physical the best. The tricky thing is with this, is that if she sinks her fangs deep enough, she can inject her Antidermis into you, giving her control, to an extent.

 

Appearance: Amare is thin with heels on her feet. She's got fangs. She looks like Crystalia otherwise, just remove wings in general.

We have been betrayed. Our trust, misguided. Our faith, put wrongly. Have they no shame? But we will live on, my brothers. We will strive forward, we will endure. We will ascend. Free of limitations, free of fear.

Name: Thronian.

Is: Toa of Furniture and an Altverse version of Trainian

Element: Furniture. Yup.

    Masks: Mask of Comfort(Induces comfort in a target, to the point they don’t want to move or fight.), Mask of Tripped Starlight(Distorts a target’s sense of sight, giving them a headache and heavily dizzying/disorienting them.), Mask of Tracking(Allows Energy Hound-esque tracking abilities.), Mask of Sensory Aptitude, Ruru, Pehkui.

    Tools: Battleaxe, Element rifle(Basically a rifle-shaped device designed to channel elemental energy supah effectively.)

    Appearance: Red and Black-armored toa with brown eyes. His armor is rather artistic in it’s design, curling here, arching there, and so on.

    Other: As Thronian is an alternate universe parallel to Trainian, his backstory is almost entirely the same up to the point he was experimented on by the Makuta, except he is more obsessed with furniture than vehicles. At that point he gained the ability to create and manipulate furniture rather than vehicles, and also became a toa in the process. The Makuta of his universe believe him to be the product of energized protodermis, as one wearing a mask of Mutation was unable to disable his abilities.

 

Name: Trainian

Species: ‘Fixed’ Le-Matoran, exposed to Brotherhood experiment. FLEBE for short.

Powers: Vehicle creation and control.

Weaponry: Pulse bolt Generators. Woo.
Mask: Powerless Miru, noble form.

Appearance: ‘Fixed’ matoran with light green armor, orange eyes, and a darker green mask.

Other: Trainian was created during an incident where some Kraata of Confusion got loose in a makuta lab. This caused the makuta to do strange, seemingly pointless experiments and not log anything. As such, Trainian’s powers are a mystery even to the Makuta who granted them. Trainian is rather obsessed with vehicles of all sorts, given they go fast. As such, some theorize his powers are a variant of Rhotuka powers.

 Name: Tono-kala Jaza’vakra. ‘Tonok’[Toe-knock] for short.

 Is: Altverse In-Toa

 Element: Tools.

 Masks: Kanohi Ajtazt, mask of instructions - Allows him to formulate a set of instructions on how to best do the task at hand in his mind. Sanok, Kanohi Astrana, mask of the stars - Allows him to summon ‘falling stars’ - which are in truth just superheated, gravity-affected fireballs - on his opponents. Pakari, Kanohi Avezon, mask of reflections - Allows Tonok to use any reflective surface as a portal to any other such surface.

 Tools(lol): Nail gun(Very literally), blade-edged shovel.

 Appearance: Blue and silver armored toa. Strongly resembles glatorian, but this is a solely aesthetic effect. As such his armor looks different from your average armor, almost as if it had been carved from bone. Eyes are blue.

 Other: In an alternate universe, A GB broke her hammer while working with protosteel. Fed up with breaking tools and running out of screws and nails and having to go get tools she forgot and such, she decided to make a toa, the first toa of tools, to remedy this. Soon the Agori found out and wanted such toa for their own universes, and the GBs decided ‘Why not? Surely no harm will come of it. After all, they only control tools.’

     ...So they made more In-toa and sent them out to the villages. These toa could not only create and manipulate tools, but anything a tool needed to perform it's function - Nails and screws for instance. This also allows them to create Thornoline, a flammable liquid fuel distilled from Thornax and used in chainsaws, so these toa have little trouble finding jobs. Matoran of their element are exceptionally good at carpentry and similar professions.

Name: Metaxi[Met-axe-ee]

Is: Altverse female Lo-Toa Hordika

Element: Silk.

    Masks: Kanohi Vistorayn, Mask of the Seamstress - Allows the user to apply the energies of a nearby mask or disk to cloth or similar materials, either making the cloth use it or making the wearer gain it’s effect. This mask has been fused to her face by visorak mutation, but she can still use a very limited form of it’s effect to strengthen webbing she creates.

    Hordika tools: Mantis blades. For their appearance, take Keelerak mandibles and put them at the end of her arms, Praying Mantis-style. She can channel her element to fire webbing(Or other things under her element if she so desires) from the tips of the blades.

    Appearance: Keelerak-taur toa hordika. Due to extreme amounts of visorak venom, Metaxi’s entire lower half looks like the body of a Keelerak. The part above that has surprisingly remained mostly humanoid(Bionoid?) aside from the arm-blades and face. Her color scheme is dark and light green.     

    Other: Once upon a time alternate universe fashion-obsessed agori begged the GBs to pamper them with the finest silks and linens. Fed up with their constant cries for moar betterer clothing, they made the Lo-toa to deal with their fashion demands. Having led a decidedly non-violent life of creating clothing and blankets for agori, most were pretty helpless when their village was attacked. As such, Metaxi was easily captured and mutated in a Visorak attack. Shunned for her mutated appearance, Metaxi wandered and eventually became friends with an iron glatorian mercenary who knew as well as she what it was like to be shunned for what one was.

    Due to her intense mutations, Metaxi has a variety of visorak abilities; She can fire elemental Silk spinners in addition to Keelerak acid and paralysis ones, she can do buzzsaw acrobatics and create visorak webbing + venom, and lastly can skate on water almost as well as a Boggarak.
