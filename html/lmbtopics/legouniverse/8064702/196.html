Name: Korgrax

 

Species: Skakdi of Psionics

 

Powers: Psionics, obviously, which he can use without the aid of another skakdi, plus small amounts of all other elements, excluding light and shadow, but he needs to work with other skakdi to use them.  He has access to all known, and some unknown, vision powers.

 

Appearance: Over a head taller than other Skakdi, dark blue skin and armor with gold highlights.  Very powerfully built.  Has a third eye on his forehead, but it is impossible to see when it is closed.

Weapons: Various small, sharp objects which he directs with telekinesis, kept in pouches on his belt; Two long spiked chains which he can use expertly even without telekinesis, worn like a sashes from shoulder to hip, forming an X across his chest.

 

Equipment: Elemental reducer - A small device hidden beneath his armor, with wires that reach throughout his armor.  This device will cancel about 95% of any elemental attack used on him.  However, while it is active he cannot use elemental powers either, even with another skakdi.

 

Backstory:  Mostly unknown.  All he can remember from before a decade or so ago is being experimented on, but that is all.  He doesn’t know who, why, where, when, or how.  He hopes that by joining the order he will find out.

So... there was that big nasty legion of demon rahi that were created some time ago, so... yeah. That happened there's a whole army of them and they've been trapped in another dimension, as a stroke of luck, they've been able to communicate with one single Skakdi through use of a specially modified olmak. And now that the Skakdi have been turned into monsters, the demon rahi are coming to assure dominance. There are several breeds of these rahi, all with a different purpose.

 

 

Name: Conflagerators. AKA "Great balls of fire."

Description: Meteor-like rahi that turn into golems upon impact. Originally created by a mad Brotherhood scientist in an attempt to create living weapons for use by the Makuta. These rahi became part of a legion of demon rahi that were banished into another dimension... until now. These golems are on fire, have huge strength, and cause painful burning just by being near them. A punch from these things bruns badly. The demon rahi plan to use a network of olmaks to send conflagerators onto essential targets.

 

Name: Fell-shapers.

Description: These creatures are responsible for the procreation of all demon rahi species besides itself. Originally created by the same mad Brotherhood scientist who thought it was a good idea at a time. They were intended to save Makuta labor by memorizing all the specific virus strains needed to produce new demon rahi. These creatures live in the dimension to which they were banished and will stay there for all eternity. Alone they have no method of defense or combat beyond creating new rahi. They are essentially brains hooked up to computers and vats of the ingredients necesary for rahi production.

 

Name: Fell-vanguard.

Description: The fell-vanguard are a species of these demon rahi that were intended to serve as foot soldiers. They are responsible for taking standard issue swords and slamming them into the enemy. They're expendable but not entirely easy to destroy.
