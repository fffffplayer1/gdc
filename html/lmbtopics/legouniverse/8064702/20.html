Name: Unpronouncable in the Matoran tongue

Alias: Gurthang, the Avenger of Mata Nui!

Affiliation: Mata Nui (currently in line with the Brotherhood)

Race: One-of-a-kind!

Element: None

Mask: None

Appearence: I have a MOC I'll upload to the gallery

Tool(s): Natural tools such as claws, razor wings, talons, and retractable blades in his wrists.

Power(s): Gurthang is incredibly strong, agile, swift on foot and in flight, and his armor is able to survive an impact with a planet. He is able to go without air for a full year and can survive absolute Zero Kelvin and a supernova. His body is built for space travel and as such he can thrive in a vacuum and enter into a planet's atmosphere without bodily harm. Gurthang wields a strange form of energy which is akin to the Silver Surfer's Power Cosmic, which he can manifest as blasts from his hands or as another vision attack. He possesses a wide variety of vision such as x-ray, laser, impact, heat, telescopic, and spell-binder. He also has acute hearing. Gurthang also has complete control over his molecular stability, but cannot effect that of others.

Personality: Gurthang is a very wise, intelligent, and courageous warrior, dedicated to the cause of Mata Nui. He speaks in a much older dialect of Matoran (which I represent as olde English) which can get confusing. His is the direct approach when it comes to battle, prefering to get in close and rip his enemies apart with his claws and talons. He has a strong sense of honor and of what is right and wrong.

Weakness: Gurthang's stamina does not last forever and can be worn down if his opponent is smart enough to stay out of reach. His great hearing means that he is vulnerable to some frequncies and could potentially be brought down because of this. His sense of honor also implies that he can be baited into a fight.

Backstory: To be revealed in time.

 

I just noticed that Gurthang is a name found in Children of Hurin, a book by J.R.R.Tolkien. Did you know that?
