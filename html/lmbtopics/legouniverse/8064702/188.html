Name: Prometheus

Alias: The Makuta of the South

Species: Makuta

Special Power: Prometheus is more intelligent than any other Makuta in existence(it isn't OP. It's not taken.)
Favorite Powers: Prometheus favors Silence, Shadow, Chemeleon and Speed/Dodge as well as Accuracy; anything stealth related

Appearence: While physically smaller than the others in this Quintet of Havoc(the other members being Helios, Selene, Arachne and Epimetheus), Prometheus makes up for that with his sheer cunning and intellectual superiority. He uses sheer, and cold intellect to win. Prometheus's armor is colored Dark Blue, Dark Green, as well as Black. Prometheus's eyes are a strange color in that he has no eye color, his eyes are devoid of any color, reflecting and unwavering.

Weapons/Tools: Prometheus utilizes a variety of weapons. He has a Lava Sword and a Gauntlet in the other. His favored weapon is the Cannon of Epimetheus. Crafted by Epimetheus, the Creator, the Cannon is equipped onto Prometheus's back, or his shoulder, sometimes the hip too, depending on what mode it's on. When firing Cordak's, it's on the hip, when shooting Elemental Blasts it's on his shoulder, when in Final Nexus Mode, it's on his back as a final assault.

Name: Elgaex the Wanderer

Species: Agori

Powers: Why would he have any?

Weapons: The Mechlynx suit is about all he has.

Backstory: Elgaex was the beta tester for one of the GB's first implementations of one of their first Protodermis prototypes. After he was given it, the GBs wanted to mass-produce it, but were forced to step up the GSR program and didn't have time. He was giver the suit , as the GBs had no use for it.

 

Name: Mechlynx suit

Species: Robot

Appearence:

THe Mechlynx is a foot taller then a Glatorian and a bit stockier. It is silver in color.

Powers:

THe Mechlynx has a matter transmuter that can take raw material, such as sand, and convert it to fuel, munitions, food, water, and nanites that run teh suit's autorepair and healing function.

THe front of the suit has two large retractable vacuum scoops for getting material.

THe suit automatically provides for all teh bodily functions of the user, providing nutrients and water and recycling waste matter via the matter converter. THe nanite systems can provide medical support in teh event of an injury.

THe sides contain two missiles, and teh shoulder has a launcher that fires explosive pellets. Both types of ammo are gradually replenished by the matter converter.

Weaknesses: While the suit is strong, it is very heavy and slow.

 

THe suit is made of a strong metal alloy, which usually has comperable strength to Glatorian weapons. Damages can be repaired by absorbing material

Name: Epimetheus

Alias: The Makuta of the North, The Creator, The Forger, The Crafter, The Maker, The God Makuta

Species: Makuta

Kanohi: Epimetheus uses a Pakari, Hau and Kakama.

Special Power: Epimetheus is more skilled in creation than all other Makuta, dubbed "The Creator" for his skills, he created the renowned Cannon of Epimetheus

Favorite Powers: Weather Control and Elasticity

Weapons/Tools: Epimetheus has a...plethora to say. On his back he has a disk that can turn into a mobile forge-station, he also has a Warhammer. A Cordak Launcher attatched to his foot, and he can turn his head into a rifle/cannon. His forge also holds several weapons, Blades, Launchers, Shields etc.

Personality: Epimetheus is rash, arrogant and pompous. A loudmouth, Epimetheus isn't afraid to say what he thinks. But he isn't stupid, he knows when to stop talking. He also has a habit of calling people Freaks.

Other: Epimetheus is on god terms with Arachne, as they both enjoy making fun of Prometheus. Epimetheus hates Zeytran, as well as Inharax and Karunax and Rahia and Voidarix and Daryia and Viperax and just about over half the Brotherhood.

 

 

 

 

Name: Selene

 

Alias: The Makuta of the East

 

Species: Makuta

 

Special Power: Selene can turn her armor into a crystalline structure that is nearly impervious to conventional harm, the process is painful and Selene rarely uses it, it also requires absolute concentration making Mental Assautls effective(this cannot be shut off)

 

Favorite Powers: Shapeshifting, Kanohi usage, Telepathy

 

Appearence: The same hieght as Helios, Selene is clad in Dark Blue, Black and Silver colors. Selene wears a Mask of Intangibility

 

Weapons/Tools: Selene makes use out of her Scythe, she also has a Rhoutuka Launcher in her back(said Rhoutuka is a Rhoutuka of Disruption, causing any and all powers in the victim to go haywire)

 

Personality: Selene is cold and intellectual. Very sadistic and cruel. She delights in pain, especially psycological warfare, peering into minds for material to use
