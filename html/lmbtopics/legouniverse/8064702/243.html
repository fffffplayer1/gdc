Project: Iron Dome

New Xian science has facilitated teh construction of large towers equipped with a trilayer Hau shield. The system works as follows:

A large dome is projected with three layers of Hau shielding: The first is a much weaker shield. Anything more then a punch will get through it.

The second is a hexagonal lattice that confines unstable Iron energies.

The third is a significantly stronger shield capable of stopping an above-average Lightning blast.

When something penetrates either layer, it disturbs the Iron energies in that particular segment, causing them to instantly crystallize into a Protosteel plate that is difficult to break through.

 

Project: Key

Form: An armband teleporter with a substantial difference. When activated, it creates a microscopic Olmak portal to the destination for just an instant. Before the portal closes, the device teleports the user through it and to their destination. The device is also capable of interdimensional travel, and can be keyed for a quick panic button that sends the user to a preset location.
