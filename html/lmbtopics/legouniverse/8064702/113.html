Due to legal issues concerning everyone's least favorite toa, I have finally come up with a solution to the age old problem concerning my first character.

 

Name: Blarkrok

 

Self proclaimed titles: Leader of the toa shelei. Mata Nui's right hand.

 

Species: Toa derived inside a makuta set of armor.

 

Element: None (thinks he's an av-toa but he's not). Gains lightning when he's exceedingly mad, like if a teammate of his is heavily injured.

 

Kanohi: Olmak, Garai, Kualsi, Kiril, Mohtrek, reverse avsa (only works if the target has had their moral light already forcibly altered).

 

Powers: Immunity to shadow. Immunity to attacks affecting his mind. Impossible to make him mute because he's annoying as karz when it come to giving speeches on morallity. Able to mimic certain av-makuta kraata powers using any lightstone he finds, which he somehow is able to find a lot of them, though he does run out from time to time.

 

Weapons: A sword. A normal sword that looks cool but isn't special or anything. A shield that fires a rhotuka that created a morally inverted clone of its victim, forcing anyone hit by it to fight themselves (clone only lasts a while). A lightstone crossbow that fires the lightstones he modifies. Think of Hawkeye, but without 100% accuracy, and lightstones not arrows.

 

Colors: Black/yellow/golden. Black/red when enraged.

 

Appearance: Gallery is to date still.

 

Backstory: An abridged version is on the gallery as well.

 

Fun fact: He thinks he's helping Mata Nui but he really is just an idiot running around with a sword
