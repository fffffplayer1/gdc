Anger/Peace

Mind reading/Telekinesis

Confusion/Understanding

Sleep/Alertness

Avata make 'pure' masks, which amplify moral light in the wearer as well as inducing loyalty toward their makuta.

 

 

Level 1;

Peace: Generally calms nearby creatures

Courage: Causes feelings of certainty in nearby creatures

Purity: Leaves a trail of clean ground behind it and is healthier than your average Avata

Regeneration: Seals passages behind it to block pursuers

Creation: Thinks up creative ways of escape

Stamina: Can slither on despite xtreme hunger

Truth: Incites feelings of honesty in nearby creatures, sees through weak illusions

Telekinesis: Instinctively uses telekinesis on itself to make itself more agile and quick

Illumination: Naturally becomes invisible in daylight.

Speed: Incredibly fast, but easy to dislodge and weak to physical harm

Understanding: Strengthens sense of direction and orientation in things it touches

Alertness: Can stay fully awake and alert for long periods of time, needs only short amounts of sleep

 

 

Level 2:

Peace: Nearby presence soothes shouting matches to friendly conversations

Courage: Manifests trust and eliminates paranoia in nearby beings

Purity: Soothes nausea/other minor illnesses on contact

Regeneration: Repairs minor damage to metal or structures on contact

Creation: Forms the simplest of traps to slow pursuers and can fashion impliments to escape cells

Stamina: Can slither on despite exhaustion and gives energy to small rahi on contact

Truth: Sees through decoys + bait

Telekinesis: Deflects and throws projectiles

Illumination: Illuminates the area around it

Speed: Able to wrap itself around a creature and amplify it's speed

Understanding: Makes nearby small-minded creatures less confused and can vaguely understand pursuer's speech

Alertness: Contact eliminates grogginess, speeds reflexes
