Name: Certhax

Species: Vortixx

Affilitation: OoMN (What's left of it)

Kanohi: Great Faxon
Abilities: Certhax is immune to mental intrusion (Unless you're Tren Krom or the GSB) and trained in several forms of combat. He is the kind of martial-arts-master secret agent master. He mosly relies on weapons and combat skills. Also, he is extremley skilled and versatile with his Kanohi Faxon.

Weapons: Certhax is equipped with a ton of weaponry. First is the Rhotuka of Kindred, which fits his mask. Expecially dangerous with Visorak powers, it copies the Rhotuka of Rahi. He is also armed with cutting-edge Xian tech, including several PDing bolas, a rifle-like weapon that fires high-powered, explosive energy bullets, grenades of light and shadow energy, and a Katana of both elemental Fire and Plasma Energy.

Backstory: Certhax was a Vortixx laborer and weaponsmith. His life was pretty generic until he went up the mountain. Generically, the female partner he had ruthlessly left him behind when he needed help, leaving him to die. She did not see him on the way back down, and assumed him dead. A week after her successful return, Certhax came back. He spent a week on the mountain. Sadly for him, he was still just another manufacturer, but he realized his true potential. The Order of Mata Nui got him out of there, and let him bring along a few of the weapons he manufactured. He was later recruited by the Order of Mata Nui. After establishing himself as a talented member of the organization and becoming skilled at using a Faxon he got from the ol' Vault, he went undercover for a long time. During this time, OoMN HQ was destroyed. Then, he returned...

Personality: Certhax is intellegent, but does not see himself as a mastermind, instead as a soldier. He maintains a strict loyalty to the Order of Mata Nui, even if there isn't anything left of it. He is morally gray and will not hesitate to smash an opponent. Nonetheless, he is against the Makuta in every way possible.
