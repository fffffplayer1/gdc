Blarkrok also have a secondary element.

 

Secondary element: Dragon

 

What it does: Nothing like it has been seen in the Bionicle universe, whenever an elemental attack by anyone is used, it fades into the background and recycled into draconic energy, a red lightning that is just about as powerful as any other element. Useful for that one makuta who can absorb your light attacks...

 

Mechanics: When driven to his limit (say, when he encounters an enemy that can absorb his light attacks) Blarkrok goes into an enraged mode, where his eyes glow bright red and his armor changes color to jet black. He cannot use any of his weapons in this state, but he does gain the ability to shoot this red lightning out of his hands. While it is made of recycled elemental energy, it has no elemental effects on the opponent, but it causes damage all the same.

 

Too OP? Feel free to comment on balance issues, I need a way to defend myself from Crystalia and Cruitible after all ._.

Apparently, even if I translate it, I can't use a foreign language in my signature.
