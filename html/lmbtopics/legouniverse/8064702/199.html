So, I took a look over the last Voidarix profile I made and realized it's become a bit dated since I added him to the fray. Might as well update it.

Name: Voidarix

Race: Makuta

Mask: Kanohi Ammox, mask of weapon creation

Main weapons: An axe that has access to energy as well as the elements.

Backstory: Voidarix was a general in the Brotherhood many millenia ago, his strategic prowess matched only by his ruthlessness. He discovered an incredibly technologically-advanced city and decided, as all power-hungry people do, to plan a raid on it. It was mostly successful, until he discovered an axe buried in the rubble. Upon touching it, a being invaded his mind and took control. Seeing his precious army being shattered by the forces of the Brotherhood, he seeked Voidarix's help. When he refused, he shattered Voidarix's mind and stripped away his sanity until he didn't have the mental capacity to fight him. Then, using the power of the ancient weapon, he drove the Makuta out of the city, but not before most of his army was destroyed. To onlookers, Voidarix had apparently betrayed the Brotherhood, and he was banished. Alone with him for centuries, he was slowly brainwashed into becoming his loyal servant. Now, he has a new plan- gather the strongest beings on Spherus Magna to rebuild his army, starting with the Brotherhood. The story of Voidarix has all but been forgotten, and he was able to easily slip back into their ranks. Will he succed, will Voidarix ever regain his honor? Find out next time, on Dragon Ball Z!

Additional powers: He will occasionally offer help to Voidarix, using his ancient knowledge and mental powers to aid him in battle. Voidarix is also incredibly agile, thanks to centuries of forced training by him.

Here's a batch of weapons I developed in secret before I left. 

 

Name: Raptor

A medium sized strike fighter in the shape of a jagged wing. 

 

Size/appearence: Four Toa Metru laid length wise gives you the length of the craft. Its width is one Toa Metru.

Has a sleek, smooth appearance, and an all around light brown color. Flatish, with tapering wing tips.

 

Abilities and weapons: An answer to Xian super sonic craft, the Raptor is capable of achieving super sonic speeds, and is buit with a sonic weapon positioned in the middle of the ships rear and between the crafts four engines. The sonic weapon is employed in creating a burst of sound intended to hurt organic enemies, shake solid objects and overloud mechanical audio receptors.

 

The craft has a single plasma burst cannon positioned at the center of its wide nose, and has three exterior underside grooves for attachable weapons.

 

 

Name: Dread Wing

An emmense, flying wing comparable in size to a Mk II Ironclad. Dead black in appearence, it is flatish, and has a jagged wingspan at the rear.  

 

Abilities and weapons:

 

The craft has a total of twenty four interior weapon bays inside its wng which are divided into two groups which are positioned on either side of the crafts wing span.

Three flak turrets are positioned on top of the craft, one on the middle, and the other two further out to the sides. 

The craft has the unique ability of dimensionally traveling to the shadow zone via Kahgarak spinner inspired technology, allowing to pop in and out of prime, and shadow dimensions at will.

Name: Dread Wing Fighter craft.

A releatively small and lightwieght flying wing. Dead black and with jagged wing span.

 

Size: slightly smaller then a Raptor, but with mostly identical shape other then its dead black look.

 

Weapons and abilities: Capable of super sonic speeds and outfitted with two internal weapon bays which sit towards the front of the craft. 

Possesses shadow warp tech and shadow shielding tech (essentially a shield generator using elemental shadow and warp tech)

 

Note: My Dread Wing craft were developed in secret by my own IC forces and unique skill sets. The technology used to create them would have to be studied by outsiders in order for them to copy it.

 

Name: Visorak/automatic Deployers

Essentially robotic suits for Visorak to wear to enchance their combat ability. Each Deployer is designed to attach to the underside of a visorak, with the visorak putting its legs into flexible sleeves which end in retractable pointed drills. The suit includes a top weapons dock which is attached to its rear. When a visorak gets into the suit, the weapons dock will swing up and latch down on the Visorak's natural spinner launcher. The weapons dock has a konaka disk launcher attached to its top and gains power from the visorak's own natural spinner launcher. Drills are activated by increased pressure applied by the visorak themselves.

Several unique types of Exo Toa were developed a while ago.
