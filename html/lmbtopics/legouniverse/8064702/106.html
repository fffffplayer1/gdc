(This is going to be my last profile for a while, I swear.

 

Name: Orokk

 

Species: Krekka's Species (Whatever it's called.)

 

Mask: Kanohi Kakama (He's one of the few of his species who has the mental capacity to use Kanohi Masks.)

 

Weapons/Tools/Abilities: Massive Warhammer (Energy bolts can be fired from the end.), Earsplitting Battlecry

 

Appearance: Orokk is clad in the gunmetal grey armor of a gladiator, his armor dented and scraped from countless battles in the arena. Crimson eyes blazed behind a dark Kanohi Mask of Speed that had been gifted to him in the arena as a reward for his wins, although the mask itself was just as damaged as the rest of his armor. On his shoulder he carried a massive Warhammer – a lethal weapon in the hands of an expert. Physically, he was about as tall as Makuta Teridax – if he existed in this universe – and had similar physical capabilities.

 

Backstory: Orokk spent the first part of his life working as a slave in the cold, damp mines of Stelt. Many had tried to escape the mines, but they had always been caught and exposed to the cruel wrath of the slavers. Eventually, he was given the opportunity to fight his way to freedom in the arena. After many, many battles, the promise of freedom came nearer and nearer. However, it had turned out the arena masters decieved him, and he was cast back into the mines from whence he came. Enraged at this, he attempted to reach out to the powers of the mask he had been given in the arena, and after a few weeks of attempts, he finally managed to tap into the powers. Vibrating his body in such a way that he would pass through the metal easily, he started a full-scale rebellion that eventually led the liberated miners to the docks where they scattered out unto the sea. Only a handful of them – including Orokk – had survived. On that day, Orokk swore vengeance on his captors, knowing that one day he would return to Stelt, and he would make them pay... the ultimate price.
