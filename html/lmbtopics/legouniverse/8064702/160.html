Name: Karzov

 

Species: Bota Magnan Vorox

 

Position: Leader of the Vorox

 

Weapons: Karzov generally carries a short handled bladestaff (or a long handled Katana, if you please ) and a steel greatbow, with which he is deadly accurate.

 

Armor: Karzov wears decently standard Vorox armor, with the exception of him having much more black and Gunmetal, rather than tan. On his helmet there is a large crimson crest marking him as Vorox leader.

 

Bio: As a member of the Sand Tribe, Karzov fought as a general in the Core War, and was stranded on Bota Magna after the shattering. He pieced together a tribe of surviving Vorox and Zesk, forming the Bota Magnan Vorox. But contrary to his prime universe counterpart, Kabrua, Karzov made peace with Foros on Bota Magna, and the pair of settlements traded in uneasy peace (Similar to the Glatorian-Skrall interaction). Though the Vorox being a much more aggresive species have often planned of taking Foros, Karzov has restrained them until the time is exactly perfectly right.
