Some basic troops for The Administrator's Armies.

 

Name: Munifex

Appearence and Tools: Imagine the Blank Hero Drones, except with Cordak Blasters and three heads

 

Name: Centurion

Appearence and Tools: Generally similar, they are of Toa Inika height and build. There is one for each element, coming in the colors of that respective element, each slightly modified. They generally all look like Toa, and each type all have shields

Fire: Resistant to Heat and Flames - Swords

Ice: Same as Fire except with Cold and Ice/Frost/Snow - Bows and Arrows

Water: Able to breathe underwater for extended periods and swim gracefully - Tridents, Harpoons and Missles

Air: No need for Oxygen, faster than normal and can fly/glide for long distances - Staffs

Earth: Able to see in the dark(not as well in light), and stronger than the others - Gauntlets and Claws or Warhammers

Stone: More resistant to damage than the others - Axes and the occasional Cordak

 

Name: Hydra

Appearence: Think of a Snake. Give it legs on the front and back, then give it 2 more heads, and you got this Hydra in a nut-shell

Powers/Tools: Aside from the typical, basic numbing Poison, they have Claws and Sharp Teeth. Also, sometimes they have maces or a spike cluster at the end of their tail

 

Also, the Hydra won't appear until later....like, until I've got a lot of Centurions and Munifex.

We have been betrayed. Our trust, misguided. Our faith, put wrongly. Have they no shame? But we will live on, my brothers. We will strive forward, we will endure. We will ascend. Free of limitations, free of fear.

Name: Concept

Species: Robot

Personality: He has been imprinted with a copy of Proto's consciousness, and has Proto's basic knowledge/skills, but not his memories.

Powers: As Concept can switch out bodies through Project: Warehouse tech, the profile is divided into three here.

1:

Appearance: A slightly-larger-then-average Nektann with black and grey.

Powers: A three-way Seletu-Biomechanics hookup that allows him to mentally command Xian forces, as well as receive mental messages. He cannot be psionically attacked or read through this due to a firewall on the Biomechanics part.

He has teh standard Nektann abilities of Flight and Self-Repair, along with Intangibility, Teleportation through the Xian network, and Kakama abilities.

Weaponry: None

2:

Appearance: A mechanical version of Proto with an eyepiece

Powers: Same Biomechanics-Seletu hookup, Rode, self-repair, Mahiki, Adaptation.

Weaponry: Twin pop-out cannons on the forearms that release precision Intangibility and Molecular Disruption.

3:

Appearance: Large Steltian with faceplate

Powers: Seletu-Biomechanics hookup. Adaptation, Pakari, and 3-tier shielding system. Tier 1 uses Telekineses and Crast to try to redirect the object. Failing that, a Hau dome is erected. If that doesn't work, Concept is pulsed with Limited Invulnerability.

Weaponry:

Hidden Nektann guns on each wrist, a massive battleaxe that can shrink to fit in a forearm compartment. It can be pulsed with most energies, including Shapeshift to transform it into a Warhammer.

Name: Praetor Maxima

 

Species: Makuta-Machine Fusion

 

Appearence: Imagine a blank Hero Factory Drone. Then make it gold and purple, with a purple cape with gold runes on it. Then give a giant freaking sword. Then enlargen it to Brutaka Size. Then add spikes all over it, and you've got Praetor Maxima in a nut-shell. Oh, and a wide array of other weapons hidden in pockets around her that can shrink.

 

Personality: Bubbly, Care-Free and Silly.

 

Powers/Abilities: Praetor Maxima has the 42(41) Makuta Powers as well as Teleportation. Oh, and add the ability to channel those powers into Eye-Beams. Praetor Maxima is also extremely strong, not as strong as say Karunax or even a Noble Pakari, but still adequetly strong. Her speed is somewhat lacking, but her defense is excellent.

 

Kanohi: Felnas which can be focused into Praetor Maxima's weapons

 

Backstory: Formerly known as the Makuta Toxica, she was captured by The General for The Administrator's experiments. It worked. Praetor Maxima is highly developed and sentient. Despite being derived from Toxica, she has none of Toxica's traits or qualities.

 

Other: Question, Keplers. Could I have Praetor Maxima have her Runes Glow and then use her Makuta Powers while claiming it's Magic? It's essentially what "Conjurer" did.

 

 

 

Name: Praetor Maximo

 

Species: Toa-Machine Fusion

 

Appearence: Lanky, Small and Fast. Perfectly describes him. He's got long arms and legs, allowing him to leap and run long distances. He's about the size of a Toa Metru. He's Gold and Purple like Praetor Maxima, but he has no cape.

 

Personality: Solemn, Serious yet Impulsive

 

Powers/Abilities: Standard Powers to a Toa. Praetor Maximo has the Element of Lightning. He can focus that into his Knives, Morningstar or Pistols.

 

Kanohi: Kualsi, Olmak, Kakama, Hau and Pehkui

 

Backstory: Once the proud Toa Eclipse, he lost his team in a battle with a group of Centurions, in which The Administrator personally captured him and turned him into Praetor Maximo
