The toa fails to understand exactly what the rahkshi is trying to convey, but realizes it can't speak. He drops the rahkshi and gestures to the dust and rock around them.

"Draw."

The Rahkshi uses its staff to draw the crude image of a building – a rectangular thing with windows, really – and draws a Rahkshi head. A dotted line goes from the Rahkshi's eyes to the building.

Iota scans the plain and realizes the rahkshi were watching them. He melts out the other rahkshi with heat vision.

"Leave, now, and don't come back."

The Rahkshi do not follow Iota's order. He is not a Makuta.

 

The whistling now associated with interceptors can be heard.
