Told by: Jonna

We decided to attack the old camp at night. Rover was to be our scout. "Yar, pirate scout!" whispered Rover. We headed along towards the enterence. We just arrived inside, when we saw a sniper. I went over to the unsuspecting sniper, and then Rover took out a Big One and "BAM!" wmet the sniper. "Why did you do that?" I shouted! "Sneakin's for ninjas" replied the pirate. We heard the battle cry of the corrupted sharpshooters, and fell into the heat and fury of battle. Our group was split in half, five of us blocking the enterence to the completed monument. The rest of us, myself included, fought the sharpshooters and the newly- arrived Maelstrom forces. It was intense, so intense that you could see and feel it. It raged on for almost an hour before the Corrupted gained the upper hand. All 10 of us were grouped into the middle, at gun- and sword-point. "This is better than breakfast" exclaimed the pirate. "But, like breakfast, I can find a way out." At that moment, Rover told a guard( gullable at that) that a huge clear diamond was behind him. Our guard turned, and Yamm, seeing his chance, rushed and tacked him. "That was what the charming pirate was supposed to do! shouted Rover, and with that, we commenced to push back and defeat the evil forces. I do recall, that I was on my back, when a sharpshooter pointed his gun to my face. Rover, seeing the event, rushed in and sliced the guy. "Don't lose your head" he said. In the corse of an hour, the camp was clear. We celebrated, until we saw a huge light from the caves, along with a blood-curdling scream. We sent Rock, Wolf, and Yamm over to investigate, and hope that Roman was still alive.

Told by: Wolf

We walked into the cave. I had spent many a day farming here, but it was unlike the old caves. There was a purple ooze dripping from the rock above, and the spider remains we saw were as tall as the spider queen. "I don't like it here" whispered Rock, summing up all our thoughts. When we arrived at the main cave, we greeted a nasty discovery. The old leaders, using the imagination we fought to protect, enlarged the cave and made it an airfield as well. "Oh my..." is all I could think, and then, we all remembered Roman. We slid into the camp, and before Duke could turn his head to see us, we ducked into a tent. Inside we saw Roman, tied to a chair. "Your alive?" we asked, once we freed him. "Most of the spiders have left to go start new nests." was his reply. "We need to assault the gas tanks in here to render this base inoperable." said Roman, and we agreed. For some massive stupidity of the leaders, they locked Roman inside a weapons depot. We all grabbed some of the RPGs and rockets, and went to work. The corrupted was powerless to us, and we managed to destroy everything but the take-off strip in about 20 minutes. Then, the leaders called down a helicopter, called the Paradox Koi. They went into it, and then Vanda yelled "The emergency bombs will go off in on hour! Hahahahah!" The helicopter then took off, and then Yamm came from out of no where with a harpoon. He took aim, and lodged the arrow into the Paradox Koi. "Climb the rope I attached!" shouted Yamm, and we did so. We climbed into the Koi. Little did we know that this was the start of a huge battle, leaving four of the eight contenders to die in a burning wreck...

Told by: Rock

Our climb up, was long, but peaceful. We can't say that for when we finally climbed up into the Paradox Koi. It was like a small city there! A military city at that. We saw maybe 13-ish small quarters, holding 12 Marauders, each. We had the unfortunate luck to find out the hard way. As soon as we boarded, all personal on board were alerted. Rome had just finished contacting Rover, when they ambushed us. "Get down!" he yelled as we saw a fire-cracker fly straight towards the Paradox forces. Needless to say, they were all defeated. "Take these, you can use them" said Yamm, who handed all of us a electric dagger, elite blasters, and a dozen fire-crackers. He said that he had been looting the air-strip while we were fighting. "You could have given us these BEFORE we got on here." I said. But before Yamm could get in a come-back, Rome said to move towards the main control room. We all went along, knowing that there, we would find the "leaders" and take revenge for their crime against all, and avenge the deaths of millions of minifigs. We battled our way through the helicopter, and for every corrupted soldier we smashed, one of us said someone's name. It was a small way if getting back. We soon reached the control room, and found that no one was there. Then, out of the darkness, someone said "Fools, you should have gotten smashed with the rest." Then, all of the "leaders" stepped out of the shadows. They had their weapons drawn, and their evil, purple eyes said it all. This was a fight to the end...
