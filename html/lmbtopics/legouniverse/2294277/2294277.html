‎10-22-2011 07:35 PM


It was a day I will never forget. We had an upcoming meeting discus the growing threat of Maelstrom. But before I go on, let me introduce my self. I am RomanMan, a rather independent fig, and Duke's assistant. Duke had me carry a message to Hael, something involving missiles. I searched around the tower, gracefully hovering some few thousand feet below Crux. I was unable to find Vanda, so I went back to Duke. I was going through the Assembly halls, when I heard voices in the balconies. I went up there to investigate. There I saw all four leaders, getting large, sacks of money. Bribe money.

Fortunately, I managed to get close enough to see the figure who handed out the bribes. It was Mr. Ree. I was appalled. I only managed to hear a few parts to what they were saying. All I heard was something about "Corrupt, Soldier, End, Nexus" I was unable to hear the rest, for I saw the Duke glance right in my direction.

The next day, I was detained enterence to the Paradox area, for the championships. I asked my good friend, SSROCK101, to switch gear with me. He, being an independent fig to, gave me the gear. I just stood in the main area, watching Mr. Ree. He appeared to be very... paranoid, and he watched everyone with caution. I did hear him tell Vanda to not lock the Orb tonight. It appears that he has dealing here, and now, so do I.

That night, I was hidden, in one of the various disguise boxes, when I saw Mr. Ree go up to the Orb. Suddenly, I heard a loud, raspy voice ring the cold air in the room, saying, "Open it, Ree" Ree did so. Then, in a huge purple flash, came the one, Baron Typhonus, the leader of the Maelstrom, telling Ree that "Tomorrow, the plan will be executed. The Nexus Force, and the Universe, shall be mine!"

I walked over to the meeting hall for the Sentinels. Duke was there, and all of the Sentinels in the universe. We crowded around the hall, waiting on what was to happen. Then, all of a sudden, the Duke shouted in a voice not his own "Corruptors, attack!" Then, suddenly, several Space rangers appeared, who looked like Stromlings in faction gear. Then, they opened fire on the crowd. There were screams of pain as minifigures died, while others were corrupted into the Maelstrom ranks. I managed to fight off some, And then I started to run. I heard ugly, gasping shouts as the Nexus force was shattered. I did see one other fig who got onto his rocket, but came down a huge fireball. I was guessing that this was the worst, but then I heard the sound of lost power, as the tower's lifts that kept the thing afloat, we shorted out by Paradox. The tower was hurtling towards the grind when I saw some figs manage to escape. At that time, an adventurer came out, and shot me. I suddenly felt a huge force take me over, and at that point, my world went out like a light, leaving me and others, to burn in the falling tower of what used to be the hope of the universe, now a huge missile, soon to be exploded on Crux.
