 The team was frantically looking for Cobra for the time when they couldn't find him.

"I wonder where he could be?" Python asked.

"Cobra just wouldn't disappear overnight!" Legion said.

"I can't find anything!" Plasma said.

"Weird, there are burn marks, even though there's nothing here." Battleaxe thought.

"I can't find anything. It's weird how every place has, like, no imagination. It feels like there's less and less." Bots' said.

"We made a mistake. The maelstrom isn't above ground, it's below." Vamda said.

"I thought yer wer right forr that." Hael said.

"The launchpad is up ahead. We should go to Forbidden Valley." Duke said.

"We can't. There's a huge storm coming. We should find shelter." Overbuild said.

"There should be some caves by the vendor shacks." Python said.

"I remember seeing caves there." Battleaxe said.

"Well? Are we going, or not?" Legion asked.

"Let's go!" Plasma said.

They reached the caves just when the storm was starting. ZZZZAAP! ZZZAAP!

Lightning bolts streamed across the sky like a firework show.

"I'll start the fire." Plasma said. ZZZZAAPP! ZZZAAP!

Light from the fire now filled the small cave.

"This storm shouldn't last long anyway." Overbuild said while warming his hands.

"At least this cave gave us shelter." Legion said. ZZZAAP! ZZZZAAP!

"Good night, everybody!" Bots' said.
