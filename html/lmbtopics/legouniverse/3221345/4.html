Chapter 1.4

Although incredibly fast, Luna wasn’t the first one to react. Ziggy was. By the time Luna had whirled around to look at the grizzled face of the man behind her, Ziggy was already leaping into the air. With a blur of green fabric, Ziggy’s foot flew upwards, smacking Captain Nicholas in the face.

The Nexus Force man staggered backwards, clutching his face, right below the eye. The man behind Luna looked at Ziggy. His eyes then danced over to where Captain Nicholas was kneeling on the ground. He let go of Luna, like he didn’t realize what he was doing, and shuffled over to Nicholas.

Ziggy smiled a wide grin that didn’t seem to fit his face. He was going to say something which started to sound like ‘Ziggy…’ but Luna grabbed his hand and pulled him away.

“I’ve counted,” Luna says, running forward with Ziggy. “It takes about thirty seconds for someone to realize something’s wrong and come help their Nexus buddies. This means we need to get moving.”

“Okay,” Ziggy said. His legs were quite lanky, compared to his short torso, so he was soon bounding ahead of Luna. “Hurry up! Ziggy doesn’t want to be caught!”

“Aren’t you…Ziggy?” Luna questioned.

“Yep!” Ziggy said, starting to bounce higher in his stride. So high, that Luna feared he would stop running and hop up and down.

“Then why do you keep saying Ziggy?”

When Ziggy didn’t answer, Luna quieted and started to slow to a walk. Although Ziggy followed, he didn’t slow down, he just completely stopped running. Tipping forward, like a scale, he tumbled to the ground, just reaching around a corner of an empty building.

“Who are you?” A man screeched as Ziggy disappeared around a corner. There was then a loud yelp.

Luna followed, wondering what kind of stupid trouble he was in. It seemed like she knew Ziggy too well, even though they had just met. She gaped. There was Ziggy, sitting at the feet of two other men. One was wearing a slightly faded Nexus Force shirt, which was not tucked in and messy. The other was wearing a gray shirt and dull red pants. For a reason Luna could not fathom, the Nexus Force person was sitting on the other man’s shoulders covering his face with his hands. Cautiously, he spread his hands to peek out at Ziggy. He was…scared?

Ziggy looked up and smiled sheepishly. “Oh, uh,” It’s like he just noticed that one of them is from the Nexus Force, “Ziggy will just be going now.” He stood up, brushed off his pants awkwardly, and was about to take off running.

“Hold on,” the Nexus Force man said, climbing down from the other man’s shoulders. “You’re not Nexus. Neither are we.”

Not my best chapter. Not my worst either
