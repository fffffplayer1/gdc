5 minutes later...
Vanda: LOOK OUT FOR THAT STOP SIGN!

CRASH!
Vanda: *Gets out of car* What is this building wer'e in?
Duke: Ooh, look! An avacado! Hey look, it even has some kind of metal pin in it!
Hael, Vanda, and Overbuild: THATS A GRENADE! DON'T DROP IT!
Duke: *Jumps from them all screaming and drops the grenade*
Hael: Uh-o-
BOOM!
1 minute later...
Duke: Is everyone okay?
Hael: Yes.
Overbuild: Yes.
Vanda: No.
Duke: She's okay.
Vanda: Where are we? *gets up and brushes herself off*
Duke: An Army tank place!
Hael: An Armoire.
Overbuild: A weapons storage and containment unit for military use.
Vanda: Duke, Why did you call it an army tank place?
Duke: Because there's an army tank!
Hael: Oh-no!
Vanda: Oh-no!
Overbuild: Oh-no!
Duke: Oh-Yes! Army tank fun time!
*Duke gets in army tank and it immediatly flips on it's side, Duke gets out, and walks away, then Tank blows up and Duke runs away*
20 minutes later in nexus tower...
Vanda: Duke, why did it blow up?
Duke: I saw another metal avacado and took out the metal pin and threw it inside the tank.
Hael: Uh-oh! There's a grenade on the floor!
Duke: METAL AVACADO! YAY! *Takes out pin*
Hael, Overbuild, and Vanda: AAAAAH!

MAH PIGGY WILL DESTROY THE EVIL AVACADOS!!!!

Vanda: YOU DON'T GO IN A TORNADO DUKE STOP!

Vanda: Eww...

Ninjago Trainee: *Sees a person in red pajamas (supposed to look like ninja clothes) yelling to Sensui Wu about jumping in to a tornado* Do you know him?
Sensui Wu: ...No. Now you have to go to Jay, the Ninja of Fire, and learn from him the secrets of the most powerful type of Ninja.
Duke: I do?
Sensui Wu: I wasn't talking to you!
Duke: Oh. Did you see me doing spinningitzugo? Did you? Huh? Did you? Did you? *Jumping up and down like a little kid*
Sensui Wu: You didn't learn it, Duke. There's no such thing as spinningitzugo. You really did invent it.
Duke: But it works! Watch!
Sensui Wu: It's not going to work, I'm telling you. *turns around to talk to another Ninjago trainee*
Duke: Fine then. *Turns in to tornado and sucks up all the skeletons outside and smashes them*
Kodo Pandaheart: Yay, we can finally build that Mini-golf course!
*Skeletons sprout up from the ground and do what they always do*
Sensui Wu: Duke! Do that tornado thing again!
Duke: I can't.
Sensui Wu: Why not?
Duke: I forgot.
Practically Everyone: *Facepalm*

Duke:you need help with that? *slaps practically everyone*

Chapter 44
Duke: *Sitting on couch being lazy* I'm bored.

Hael: That's an idea. It happens when you think.
Duke: Wow! My idea is in there! It won't come out! *Burp* Okay, there we go! Wait now I forget.
Hael: Quiet, I'm watching tv. Here's a show I like! *Puts the remote in his pocket* HAHAHA! That was funny!
*Show ends and starts playing a soap opera that has been going on for about 100 years*
Hael: GAH! This is BOORING!
Duke: Isn't there anything BETTER on?
Vanda: *Walks in the room* You two are such kids.
Duke: We are NOT kids!
Vanda: Fine, fine. I'm going in the kitchen to get some lunch for myself.
Duke: Ooh! While you're there could you get me some chocolate milk!
Hael: *Jumps up and raises hand and keeps doing little hops* ME TOO! ME TOO! And a bendy straw?
Duke: Hey, how come YOU get the bendy straw?
Hael: Because I'm cooler than you.
Duke: TAKE THAT BACK!
Hael: Make me!
Duke: YOU WANT A FIGHT!
Hael: YEAH!
*Duke and Hael walks forward towards each other angrily, then start fighting by looking the other way and slapping the air in front of them*
Hael: *Gasp* Truce?
Duke: *Pant* Truce.
*Duke and Hael sit down and keep watching tv*
Duke: Change the channel!
Hael: I can't, I can't find the remote!
Duke: That's why I should have the remote!
Hael: I wouldn't lose the remote if you didn't keep on being annoying, by asking for a bendy straw all the time and stuff like that.
Duke: YOU'RE the one who asked for the bendy straw!
Hael: Duke! Do you realize what the tv is doing to us?
Duke: Programming our minds with dumb commercials?
Hael: That, AND driving us apart! We're FRIENDS! This tv has driven us apart and made us fight! You know what we have to do?
Vanda: *Standing in the doorway* Wow, you two have learned a lesson, that tv is bad for you and you should be outside more.
Hael: What? No, the lesson is we have to find the remote.
Vanda: Of course it is. You know you could just WALK five feet and change the channel on the tv by pressing the button.
Duke: There are buttons on the tv?
Hael: WE WILL TURN THIS WHOLE BUILDING UPSIDE-DOWN IF WE HAVE TO TO FIND THE REMOTE!
Vanda: It would be less work to just walk and press the button on the tv.
Duke: WE WILL FIND THE REMOTE! WOO HOO!
Vanda: Are either of you hearing a word I'm saying?
Hael: What?
Vanda: Never mind.

Chapter 21 Dimensions

*Overbuild is working in his lab, creating some other CrAzY invention*

Overbuild: And add one more ounce of ecnuo....done!

Duke: *Runs in room* Hey Overbuild what are you doing?

Overbuild: BE CAREFUL-

*Duke knocks over Overbuilds flat-screen tv*

Overbuild: NOOOOO! THAT WAS MORE IMPORTANT THAN ANY OF MY INVENTIONS!

Duke: Oh, did it have some kind of super complicated formula on it or something?

Overbuild: No, my favorite tv show comes on in FIVE MINUTES!

Duke: Oh sorry. What is it?

Overbuild: This machine? It's a boring detector. Whenever something is boring around it it vibrates. The more it vibrates the more boring it is.

Duke: I meant what was your favorite tv show.

Overbuild: Oh. Professional golf.

*Boring Detector starts humming*

Duke: Hey look your tv wasn't broken, it just fell on its side!

Overbuild: Oh good. I'll turn on the show now, since its been five minutes.

*Overbuild turns on the tv and turns the channel to professional golf*

Overbuild: OH! He almost got a par!

*Boring Detector starts vibrating so much the table shakes*

Overbuild: Eh I'm tired with this already. *Turns the channel to the weather and the Boring Detector starts vibrating so hard the glass vials in Overbuilds lab start shaking*

Overbuild: This channel does not contemplate to my intellectual complexity!

*Boring Detector hums more*

Overbuild: I'll just change the channel to paint drying, split the screen so it also shows the golf channel, split it again to also show the Advanced Algebra show and the Hour-Long Infomercial show!

*Boring Detector vibrates so much that Overbuild has to yell to be heard*

Overbuild: I WILL ALSO CHANGE THE CHANNEL TO SESAME STREET!

*The whole Tower starts shaking because of the Boring Detector*

Overbuild: I KNOW WHAT I WANT TO WATCH! THE SOUND OF MUSIC!

*When Overbuild changes the channel to The Sound Of Music, the Boring Detector can't stand it anymore and EXPLODES, exploding the Tower! Then there are pieces of the walls and ceilings of the Tower in a slow motion explosion, so it looks like a huge ball that got exploded from the inside, and all the people inside Nexus Tower are in the explosion also. Then the explosion just stops. It freezes, pauses, time stops, however you think of it. People walking on the sidewalk close to Nexus Tower stop and their jaws hang open in amazement from the sight of an explosion paused*

Duke: *Scared* Overbuild? What just happened?

*Overbuild and Duke are just hanging in the air, holding on to nothing, confused*

Overbuild: I have no idea!
