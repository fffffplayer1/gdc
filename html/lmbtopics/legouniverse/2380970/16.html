Chapter 25 The return of Whiskers

Vanda: Hey Duke, I haven't seen Whiskers for a while. Where is he?

Duke: Uh-huh. *Playing Y-Box on the tv*

Vanda: Your pet lion is MISSING!

Duke: *Not paying attention to Vanda* Whatever you say.

Vanda: How can I get his attention? Hm...A BOMB!

Duke: Whatever.

Vanda: A PET FROG!

Duke: I'm trying to beat my high score, Vanda!

Vanda: *Sigh* FREE DONUTS!

Duke: WHERE!?

Vanda: Where is Whiskers?

Duke: I don't know. I'll call him with this dog whistle.

SCREEEEET!

*Whiskers jumps through the floor and lands in front of Duke*

Duke: Good boy!

*Tosses Whiskers a bone, which he swallows whole*

Vanda: That can't be good for him.

*Whiskers throws up the bone and it hits Vanda on the forehead*

Vanda: EW EW EW LION SLOBBER!

*Vanda runs to the bathroom to wash the lion spit off her forehead*

Whiskers: *Stomach growls*

Duke: Sounds like you're hungry!

Whiskers: WOOF!

Duke: That was weird. What do lions eat? I KNOW! CROCODILES!

Overbuild: *Walks in the room* Lions do not eat crocodiles.

Duke: This one does! See you in a few hours, I'm going to the zoo with Whiskers.

Overbuild: WAIT! I HAVE TO TELL YOU ABOUT MY NEW INVENTION! It is the disguise-o-matic-3000. You wear it as a collar and it disguises you!

Duke: Why does it have 3000 at the end of it?

Overbuild: It took me 3000 tries to get it right.

Duke: Can I borrow it?

Overbuild: Sure.

*Duke goes to the zoo and puts the collar on Whiskers, disguising whiskers as a house cat*

Zoo guard: Why are you bringing a cat to the zoo?

Duke: To see the crocodiles!

Zoo guard: ...well that's kind of an answer...Go on ahead.

Duke: Why should I stand on a head? Whose head?

Zoo guard: Ahead, not a head.

Duke: Why isn't the head a head?

Guard: Ahead in what?

Duke: I don't know what the head is in. But why isn't he a head? I mean, we're calling him a head, so he must be a head.

Guard: I'm confused.

Duke: People do seem to get confused after talking to me. Have fun with your head!

*Duke walks in the zoo with Whiskers*

Duke: Oh, here are the crocodiles!

*Whiskers starts drooling*

Duke: Not yet, Whiskers. Not yet. ....Okay now you can eat one.

*Duke throws Whiskers in to the crocodile pit. A crocodile tries to eat Whiskers and tears off the collar. At once Whiskers grows in to a lion. Whiskers then yawns and walks away, jumps out of the crocodile pit, and buys an ice cream bar from a terrified vendor*

Duke: Huh. Well I guess he doesn't need a crocodile to eat.

*Whiskers runs away*

Duke: WAIT WHISKERS COME BACK!

To be continued...




To be continued... I hope it's soon!

Chapter 25 Part 2
At Nexus Tower...
Duke: WHY DID HE HAVE TO RUN AWAY?
Hael: Well, he probably got bored of you and didn't want to be your friend anymore.
Duke: WAH!
Vanda: HAEL! Don't say things like that! You know what a baby Duke is!
Duke: HEY! Oh I miss Whiskers so much! It's been 23 minutes and 22 sec-23 sec-24 sec-
Vanda: ALL RIGHT WE'LL FIND HIM! Duke, what does Whiskers like?
Duke: His explosive squeaky chew toys...*Sniff*
Vanda: Good. *Picks up the explosive squeaky chew toy* We'll just walk around Nexus City and squeeze the chew toy so Whiskers can hear it.
2 minutes later, somewhere in Nexus City...
Duke: Here boy!
Vanda: Come to the squeaky toy, Whiskers!
Squeak!
KA-BOOM!
*Vanda's face is scorched from the explosion of the explosive squeaky chew toy*
Vanda: Why would any pet like AN EXPLOSIVE CHEW TOY?!
Duke: I have no idea. HERE WHISKERS! GOOD LION!
Hael: OH THIS IS HOPELESS! We've been walking around Nexus City forever, working hard just to get a lion that isn't here!
Vanda: For your information, we've been here 3 minutes, and you've only been eating a box of donuts!
Duke: Why does he eat the box and not the donuts?
Vanda: What?
Duke: He's eating a box of donuts, but the donuts must taste better than the actual box. Or is the box glazed?
Vanda: The DONUTS are glazed!
Hael: No they're not!
Vanda: That's not my point! Duke, listen to me. People don't eat boxes of donuts, they eat the donuts.
Duke: But you said Hael wasn't working and he was eating a box of donuts. You didn't say he was eating donuts.
Vanda: I meant he was eating all the donuts in the box!
Hael: Actually, now I'm done.
Vanda: WOULD YOU STOP INTERRUPTING?!
Duke: Where's Overbuild? He could help us look for Whiskers.
Vanda: I don't know. The last time I saw him was at the zoo, by the tiger pit.
Meanwhile, IN the tiger pit...
Overbuild: * Surrounded by growling tigers* Please do not consume me, tigers, A.K.A. Panthera Tigris. My flesh and organs would be pleasing to your gustatory senses, and I may cause you indigestion.
Tiger: Growl!
Overbuild: HELP!
Zoo worker: Don't worry, I'll knock out the tigers one by one with this tranquilizer gun!
ZIP!
*Tiger catches the dart with two of his claws, then throws it back at the zoo worker very fast, and the dart sticks in the zoo workers arm*
Zoo worker: *Dizzy and swaying* Did you know...that these tigers...are actually...ninja tigers...*Falls over*
THUMP!
Overbuild: Okay, maybe we can work out a deal?
*One of the tigers looks at Overbuild as if to say, "Here's the deal: We eat you, and you don't have to worry about us eating you anymore."*
Overbuild: A compromise?
*Tiger looks at Overbuild as if to say, "We rip off one body part, your head, and after that we let you live"*
Overbuild: None of these deals are going to end with me alive, are they?
*Tiger shakes his head no*
Overbuild: AAAH!
Meanwhile, at Duke, Vanda, and Hael...
Duke: HERE, WHISKERS WHISKER WHISKER WHISKERS WHISKERS!
Vanda: Listen, this isn't getting us anywhere. Why don't you put a bunch of papers on lamp posts and stuff saying lost lion?
Duke: OKAY!
*Duke makes posters then rushes back to Vanda and Hael, (Hael has a new box of donuts) and starts putting the papers on lamp posts*
Vanda: *Reading the paper* 'Lost, my lion Whiskers. Nice, barks at the mailman, likes explosive squeaky chew toys. Responds to, "Here's some steak!" but not to his name, unless I call him. If I call him, he will respond to me. But not you, unless you are me, and if you ARE me, who am I? But you aren't, are you? Anyway, if you see my lion Whiskers, please call me on the cell phone I am about to buy. The number will have 7 digits, and my phone will probably be blue, or green, or black, or one of the colors. Oh yeah, I almost forgot. Whiskers has a big mane, a tail, he is tan in color, and has a snout. He also has long teeth. Please call me at one of the cell phone numbers.' That isn't helpful to anyone.
Duke: Hey guys, look at this new phone I have!
Vanda: Good. Now could you squeak one of these explosive squeaky toys for me?
Duke: Sure!
SQUEAK!
Vanda: Why isn't it exploding? Give that to me.
*Duke hands the toy to Vanda. Vanda shakes it, then holds it up to her ear and shakes it*
KA-BOOM!
Vanda: ARGH!
Meanwhile, in the tiger pit...
Overbuild: Please don't eat me tigers! Look! Here's some steak for you to eat instead! HERE'S SOME STEAK!
Meanwhile, at a beach 250 miles away...
*Whiskers is relaxing on a reclining chair when his super great hearing hears a sound. The sound comes across highways and beeping trucks, through a forest full of chirping birds and snoring raccoon's, across a meadow that has bugs in it that are crawling, across another busy highway, past a rock concert filled with pebbles that people cheer at, and finally makes its way to Whiskers. Whiskers could hear each of those sounds, but specifically hears Overbuild shouting, "HERE'S SOME STEAK!"*
Whiskers: WOOF!
*Whiskers jumps out of his recliner and runs all the way to the tiger pit that Overbuild is in. The ninja tigers have Overbuild cornered and are slowly closing in on him. Whiskers lands in front of Overbuild and recognizes him as a friend (Although Overbuild doesn't and is more scared). Whiskers stares threateningly at the tigers*
Tiger: Rooooooaaaaaaarr!!!!
Whiskers: ROOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOAAAAAAAAAAAAARRRR!!!!!!!!!!
*Whiskers roar is louder than the roar of a T-Rex and the ninja tigers run away from Whiskers, yipping in fright like puppies*
Overbuild: WHISKERS YOU'RE BACK!
*The sound of Overbuild shouting "WHISKERS YOU'RE BACK!" makes it's way across the street, around a house, and all the way to Duke, 3 blocks away*
Duke: WHISKERS IS BACK!
*Whiskers hears Duke saying that he's back. Whiskers and Duke are so happy to see each other that they both run towards each other, Duke with his arms outstretched, and they try to do that slow motion hugging thing like in Madagascar, but Duke and Whiskers jump to hug each other but Whiskers is so big and fast that he knocks Duke over*
Duke: Ouch. WHISKERS YOU'RE BACK! YAY! Who wants an explosive chew toy?
Whiskers: ARF!
Duke: Catch the toy!
*Duke tosses the explosive squeaky chew toy to Whiskers and Whiskers catches it, squeaks it, then throws it with his mouth at Vanda, and the toy explodes in Vanda's face*
Vanda: AARGH! *Walks off angrily*
Duke: Let's go to Nexus Tower, Whiskers!
At Nexus Tower...
Duke: What should we do now, Whiskers?
*Whiskers yawns and curls up in a spot of sun and falls asleep*
Duke: Good idea!
*Duke starts playing videogames*
Vanda: After all that searching, you're not going to do anything with your pet lion Whiskers? No playing fetch, no tossing him these explosive squeaky chew toys?
*Vanda shakes the explosive squeaky chew toy when she says "no tossing him these explosive squeaky chew toys?" and it explodes*
KA-BOOM!
Vanda: *Her hair is smoking* I'm going back to bed.

Chapter 26
Duke: *Playing videogames* WOOHOO! I BEAT MY HIGH SCORE!
Vanda: *Walks in room* That's great! What score did you get?
Duke: Two!
Vanda: ...Your high score is now two? That means that before your high score was one.
Duke: *Gasp!* How did you know my exact high score before I broke it?
Vanda: That sentence sounds really weird, and probably isn't what you meant to say.
Duke: Well, when I'm in a party the only thing I need are a wombat and some rope!
Vanda: That doesn't make any sense.
Duke: I don't have to make sense, I just have to flamingo! Hey, that's fun to say! Flamingo. Fla...mingo! Fla-ming-o! Oh come on, I just forgot what a flamingo is. Well, back to videogaming!
Vanda: Well, I'm going to my Paradox lab to experiment with this new explosive element I discovered. If you hear an explosion, tell someone that it was me and I probably need help immediately.
Duke: Got it.
*Vanda leaves and Overbuild comes in the room*
Overbuild: Duke, you have to exercise more, eat healthier-
KA-BOOM!
Overbuild: What was that?
Duke: Dunno.
Overbuild: Well, anyway Duke, you have to exercise more.
Duke: WHAT IS IT WITH ALL THESE CHAPTERS ON EXERCISING?! HAS THE AUTHOR RUN OUT OF IDEAS OR SOMETHING!?
Overbuild: What on Crux does that mean?
Duke: Sorry about that. Someone had to say it.
Overbuild: Well go on outside! Eat some vegetables!
Duke: No.
Overbuild: Yes.
Duke: You will?
Overbuild: I will what?
Duke: You'll eat vegetables?
Overbuild: No! Why'd you think that?
Duke: You said eat some vegetables and I said on and you said yes so that means that you'll eat vegetables right?
Overbuild: No. It meant that you will!
Duke: I don't wanna.
Overbuild: You have to!
Duke: I saved your life, remember? When the Baron went cuckoo and tried to smash us with a spider?
Overbuild: THAT WAS YEARS AGO!
Duke: But I saved you from the lightning.
Overbuild: But you got rebuilt! The same would have happened to me!
Duke: But I destroyed the Maelstrom with my shield!
Overbuild: That doesn't make any sense.
Duke: I don't have to make sense, I just have to flamingo!
Overbuild: EXERCISE!
Duke: AAAH! *Jumps up and clings to the ceiling* Don't DO that!
Overbuild: Then exercise!
Duke: No!
Overbuild: Exercise!
Duke: No!
Overbuild: Exercise!
Duke: Okay.
Overbuild: Exer-oh. Good! Take Whiskers for a walk.
Duke: But HE doesn't need exercise, does he?
Overbuild: Yes!
Duke: But he doesn't play videogames. See?
*Duke points to Whiskers, who is playing a videogame*
Duke: Fine, we'll exercise, but not outside!
Overbuild: Uh-oh.
*Duke and Whiskers jump on couches and all the furniture in the room*
Duke: HEY WHISKERS! WATCH ME DO A CANNONBALL! CANNONBALL!!!!!!!
*Duke jumps off a table and does the cannonball thing and he lands on the couch, then it goes down, then bounces Duke up on to the ceiling*
Duke: Ow...Whiskers you try it!
*Whiskers jumps off the table, cannonballs on to the couch, and it flings Whiskers up and Whiskers lands on Duke on the ceiling, then both fall off and on to the couch*
Duke: That was fun!
Hael: *Runs in room* Did someone say cannonball! WOOHOO! Be right back!
*Hael dashes away then returns dragging a cannon with him*
Hael: REAL CANNONBALL!!!!!!!!
*Hael lights the fuse on the cannon then puts his hands over his ears*
KA-BOOM! KLSH!
*The cannonball broke a window, then falls down to the sidewalk next to Nexus Tower. The Baron is planning a sneak attack on Nexus Tower, when the cannonball lands on his head and pounds him in to the ground, leaving only his head sticking out*
Little kid: *Skipping down the street holding a balloon. Little kid suddenly sees the Barons head and doesn't know it's still attached to a body*
Little kid: AAAAAAAAAAAAH!
Baron: Hey, I'm not THAT ugly you know.
Little kid: *5x louder* AAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAH!!!!!!!
Duke: Hey, what's going on? *Sticks head out window and falls out, pounding the Baron deeper in to the planet. The Baron falls for a really long time, and sees pianos and cupboards floating around him, then the Baron lands on the floor*
Baron: Ow...that was REALLY weird. Hey, what's this?
*Baron picks up a bottle that says, "Drink me"*
Baron: Okay. I've never had a bottle tell me to do something that turned out bad. Actually, I've never had a bottle tell me anything at all. Oh well.
*Baron drinks the drink and shrinks really small*
Baron: *Really high voice* WHAT THE?! Hey look an elevator!
*Baron takes an elevator that goes up to the room in Nexus Tower that Duke is in*
Baron: HEY DUKE! HELP! DOWN HERE!
Duke: Hey, how did you get small?
Baron: I HAVE NO IDEA! MAKE ME BACK TO NORMAL!
Duke: I don't know how.
Baron: DO IT NOW OR I WILL DESTROY YOU LIKE THE TIME I TRIED TO DESTROY YOU WITH THE BIG SPIDER!
Duke: You can't threaten me.
Baron: OH YES I CAN! MAKE ME BACK TO NORMAL OR SUFFER MY WRATH!
*Duke steps on the Baron*
Baron: ...ow...
