Sorry people. I meant to make a story yesterday @ 1:00, but then the sun miraculously came out and we went to play tennis until like 7:30. I'll make it today @ noon if I can. Meanwhile, I'll make a little mini-chapter for your patience. I'm just going to start off with some funny little ideas I thought of and see where it leads me. Did you know that I don't know what the end, middle, or most of the beginning of a story is going to be like? For example, 'camping' started off as 'road trip' but then it led in to camping, and I changed the title. That's why some of the chapter titles don't match the story. Here's the little mini story.
Vanda: *Walks in room* Hi Duke what are you doing?
Duke: YUM! EATING CHOCOLATE-CHEESE BALLS!
Vanda: Those seem nasty.
Duke: They are. Want one?
Vanda: No thanks. You would need to be magic to make those taste good.
Duke: MAGIC! I'LL BECOME A MAGICIAN! OR BETTER, A WIZARD!
Vanda: Same thing.
Duke: TO THE MAGIC SHOP! *Turns around and crashes in to the window*
*Duke goes in the elevator, then goes to a magic shop*
Duke: Hi!
Magician: *Mysterious voice* Hello.
Duke: How much does it cost to become a wizard?
Magician: It is free, if you can pass the tests. Otherwise, it is 2,000 coins.
Duke: Okay! What are the tests?
Magician: Is magic real?
Duke: Yes!
Magician: How does magic work?
Duke: Magic!
Magician: Congratulations. You have passed the test. Take this wizard top hot and put it on and you will have all the powers of a wizard.
Duke: I don't see a hat.
Magician: Oh. Wait, it's in my desk somewhere.
*Magician opens a tiny little desk drawer in his desk and reaches in, his whole arm up to his elbow going in to the desk that is only 4 inches deep*
Magician: Hm...deeper....
*Magician reaches his arm deeper in to the tiny drawer, and deeper, then he sticks his head in and calls out*
Magician: HELLOOO? ADARMU-KIYAN-SHAMSAL!
*Magician pulls his head and arm out of the drawer and a top hat flies up out of the drawer and the magician catches it*
Magician: Here you go. *Flicks his hand and the top hat disappears and is on Duke's head*
Duke: COOL!
To be continued...After the next camping chapter ends...

Chapter 22 Part 2

Vanda: I just wanted to try out the primitive cave man talk. It wasn't fun.
Overbuild: I just invented something else! It's a firecracker!
*Firecracker explodes and a portal to another dimension appears*
Alien: *Looks out from the portal* Hello, minifigures! We come in piece! We would like to learn about your technology!
Vanda: Oh good, I needed something to throw away these candy wrappers in.
*Vanda throws a bunch of garbage in to the portal*
Alien: OW! HEY! STOP IT! I'M LEAVING!
*Portal closes*
Overbuild: Why didn't you talk to the alien?
Vanda: Oh, portals appear around us all the time, remember?
Overbuild: Oh yeah.
Duke: More donuts?
Vanda: What?
Duke: No more donuts. I don't have any more donuts. They're gone. There are no more. The box is empty. The donuts don't exist anymore. They're gone. I'm sad. I can't eat them anymore. *Sniff* So sad...WAH! WAAAAAAAAAAH!
*Vanda hits Duke on the head with a hammer*
Duke: WAAAA-ooh...
THUMP!
*Duke is knocked out*
Vanda: Finally.
*Suddenly Hael runs in to the camp from the forest, hands over his head, screaming*
Hael: AAAAAAAAAAAAH!
Vanda: What's wrong?
Hael: I'TS SMOKEY!
Vanda: Smokey the Bear?
Hael: WELL HE AIN'T SMOKEY THE CHIHUAHUA!
Vanda: You're running in circles now. And since when do you say ain't?
Hael: I DON'T KNOW!
Duke: Chiwawa! I like that word! Chiwawawawawawawa!
Echo: wawawawawa....
Duke: COOL! ECHO!
Echo: COOL! ECHO!
Duke: I AM DUKE!
Echo: I AM ECHO!
Duke: What did you say?
Echo: Nothing.
Duke: Good.
Overbuild: That is impossible.
Echo: Who cares? *Slaps Overbuild*
Overbuild: HEY! NO ONE SLAPS ME!
*Overbuild jumps at the Echo and tackles him. The Echo responds by moving THROUGH Overbuild then kicking him in the back, making Overbuild land in the dirt on his face*
Overbuild: Of course, being sound, you can go around and through me.
Echo: MWAHAHAHAHA!
Duke: GOTCHA! *Grabs the echo by the leg*
Echo: Hey-what? Let go!
*Duke ties the Echo to a tree*
Echo: ARGH! Wait I'm sound. So I can go through the tree!
Overbuild: NO YOU CAN'T!
*Overbuild traps the Echo in a soundproof box*
Echo: ____*_____=shouting but you can't hear it* _____
Vanda: TECHNOLOGY SOUNDPROOF BOX!
Overbuild: WAIT NO!
*Vanda throws the hammer at the soundproof box but the hammer bounces off it and in to the sky, out of sight. Everyone is looking up at the sky*
Hael: Won't it come back down eventually?
*Everyone but Duke takes a step back*
Duke: Why did you move?
*The hammer falls on Duke's head*
Duke: Ooow....
Vanda: Wow. Well, time to go in a canoe and go fishing!
Hael: WOW, HAS IT BEEN 4 WEEKS ALREADY? OH WELL, LET'S GO!
*Hael starts to walk to the RV to leave*
Vanda: No, it's only been one day.
Hael: *Stops* Rrrgh....ALRIGHT ALRIGHT WE'LL GO FISHING!
Vanda: That's the spirit! Besides, I thought you LIKED being outdoors, in nature, peaceful, hearing the birds singing.
Hael: I do, but when you say it like that it sounds all girly. I KNOW! I'll explore in the forest-WAIT I'M NOT DOING THAT AGAIN!
Duke: *In the canoe* Come on I want to catch a fish! Now how do I do it?
Vanda: *Climbs in the canoe with Duke* First you get a fishing pole...*Hands a fishing pole to Duke* Then you swing it backwards like this *Swings it backwards* Then SWING it forward, in to the water!
Duke: OKAY!
*Duke holds the fishing pole by the hook then swings the pole back, then swings it forward, accidentally letting go and throwing the fishing pole in the water*
Duke: Did I catch one?
Vanda: No.
Duke: I SEE ONE IN THE WATER!
Vanda: Okay, calm down, don't scare it away...
Duke: Can I have you hammer?
Vanda: It's still lodged in your head.
Duke: Oh yeah. Thanks.
*Duke takes the hammer out of his head and leans over the side of the canoe at the fish, then starts trying to hit it with the hammer*
Duke: YAY!
SPLASH! SPLASH! SPLASH!
Vanda: *Facepalm* That's not what you do, Duke.
Duke: DIE FISH DIE! I WANT FOOD!
Vanda: DUKE!
*Duke freezes*
Duke: Uh-huh?
Vanda: Don't attack the fish with a hammer!

Chapter 1. An average day
Duke: meow meow meow meow, meow meow meow meow, meow, meow, meow meow, meow meow meow meow...
*Duke was sleeping, and then alarm clock goes off*
Duke: STAY AWAY! *Hits alarm clock with his sword and it gets sliced in half*
Duke: Oh, I hate it when that happens. *Walks over to closet and pulls out an alarm clock that was among hundreds of other alarm clocks in his closet*
Duke: I won't be needing this anymore. *sniff* Good-bye, my day old friend. *Tosses broken alarm clock out the window, where it lands on the Darkitect's head, who was planning a sneak attack*
Baron: OooOoOh, what happened?
Duke: Oh, the air conditioner is broken too. *pushes it out the window, where it also lands on the Baron, who runs away back to his castle, and hides under his bed all day*
Vanda: I've been hearing smashing electronics out my window. Duke!
Duke: Can't talk right now, I'm installing a new air-conditioner.
Vanda: YOURE PUSHING IT TOO FA- oh never mind.
CRASH! be-OP be-OP! be-OP! *Car alarm*
In the kitchen...
Overbuild: This is the perfect invention, the Automatic-Toast Butterer 1000!
Ding!
Overbuild: Ooh, good! My toast is ready! Now to put it in to the toast butterer 1000!
VVRR! *spread, spread, spread* CLUNK! BRBRBRTGDP- plooh!
*Automatic-Toast Butterer 1000 spits out shredded pieces of bread, and a chunk of butter*
Overbuild: Uum...*writes in marker over the title of his machine*
Overbuild: There! My machine works perfectly!
Duke: *walking in to the kitchen* Hey, nice invention! 'Toast-shredder and butter maker 1000!' Does it work?
Overbuild: Let's see! *Puts in toast*
spread,spread,spread, calculating, 74893927...DING!
*A slice of toast pops out, perfectly buttered*
Overbuild: *Facepalm*
Duke: Looks like it doesn't work, Overbuilding.
Overbuilding: My name's Overbuild.
Duke:...Could I have the toast?
Overbuild: Here. *Hands Duke the toast, which then falls to peices, just like the first toast, and then the toast shreds ooze out butter*
Duke: HEY! Looks like it does work!
Overbuild: I guess, but who would eat tha- Oh never mind. Use a napkin, Duke.
Duke: Okay!
Overbuild: DON'T EAT IT! *Facepalm*
Duke: This is so good, I'll eat the machine so bread will taste this good in my belly!
Overbuild: *Facepalm*
Duke: You need help with that? *Duke slaps Overbuild*
Overbuild: WHY'ED YOU DO THAT?!
Duke: I was helping you do this, *Slaps Overbuild again*
Overbuild: RRGH!! *storms off*
Hael: *walks in to the kitchen* Nice pirate ship.
Duke: It isn't a pirate ship, it's a peice of butter from Overbuild's machine.
Hael: I mean the painting on the wall.
Duke: You painted that!
Hael: That's my point, I admire the artist so much, I think you should meet him and shake his hand!
Duke: Where is he?
Hael: Never mind. By the way, where's the machine?
Duke: I ate it.
Hael: ...why?
Duke: The toast shreds from it tasted so good I wanted to always have that taste in my belly.
Hael: *facepalm*
Duke: You need help with that? *Duke slaps Hael*
Hael: OW! Why'ed you do that?!
Duke: I-
Hael: Never mind.
Overbuild: I'm back!
Duke: Hi.
Overbuild: I have a brand new machine1
Hael: What does it do?
Overbuild: It can scan someones brain to tell what their IQ is! Let's try it on me!
*Overbuild scans his brain*
Invention: IQ: 1,000,000!
*Scans Haels brain*
Invention: IQ: 80!
*Scans Duke's brain*
Invention: ERROR! You are supposed to scan a person's brain, not a rock!
Duke: What does that mean?
Overbuild: ...Nothing.
Duke: Ok. HEY LOOK A BROKEN AIR CONDITIONER!
Overbuild: That's not broken!
CRASH! be-OP! be-OP! be-OP! BRING!
Hael: What was that BRING noise?
Duke: My alarm clock.
Vanda: *staring in the doorway* I'm going back to bed.

This is even better than Four Fools!

*Window washer is trying to wash a spot off the window*
Window washer: I can't wash this spot off the window!
Vanda: That spot is on the other side of the window. The only way you can wash it is if you take the window out and take it in here and wash it.
Window washer: That's too much work.
*Window washer leans out the window and tries to wash but a sudden gust of wind bounces against the tower and knocks the window washer to the ground*
Vanda: *Runs to the window and looks down* Uh-oh. *Walks away casually whistling*
Overbuild: I SAW THAT!
Vanda: ACK!
Overbuild: So I decided to make a machine to fix it! I'll make an invention to control the weather! Watch!
*Overbuild twists the dial to hail and Hael is outside and hail lands on Hael and Hael hails the hail then a bunch of hail pieces land on Hael and bury him in it*
Overbuild: Now I'm going to put this weather machine on the windowsill of this open window. If it fell in to the wrong hands that would be horrible.
*Overbuild and Vanda leave and a gust of wind that somehow came from the inside of Nexus Tower blows the machine out the window*
Duke: *Standing on the sidewalk next to Nexus Tower* What the- *Weather Machine falls in to Duke's hands*
