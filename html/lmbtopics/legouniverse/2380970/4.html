Chapter 12 Duke's Mother
Duke: *On the phone* Yes Mom. I know. Yes, I brush my teeth EVERY day! What? OF COURSE I HAVEN'T BEEN PUSHING AIR-CONDITIONERS OUT THE WINDOW, MAKING ONE HIT THE MAYOR! What? One DID hit the Mayor? By Nexus Tower?! No no no, that wasn't ME, it was...someone else! Why did the picture in the Newspaper show me then? No, it was someone else who... Looks like me, yeah! It was someone else who looks like me. What? The alarm clocks in your house are missing? Well, it's not like I brought them home because mine got smashed by my sword! Did I mow the lawn? Yes. Did I brush my teeth? Yes. Stop repeating all the questions you ask me?
Duke's Mom: *At home* Why did you say all that? I haven't said anything, but that was what I wouldv'e asked you. How is your lovely *Angrily* fighting other people *Happily again* faction going?
Duke: It's going good.
Dukes Mom: It's doing WELL.
Duke: Whatever.
Dukes Mom: Say, It's doing well.
Duke: *Sigh* It's doing well.
Dukes Mom: Why do you fight your friends dressed in purple? Your Faction really shouldn't be about fighting, it should be about learning, like your friend Overbuild.
Duke: *Sigh* I have to make a Faction that fights, not one that studies!
Dukes Mom: Well I'm coming over then, to see how your faction is going compared to the others and to see if you need help with anything!
Duke: No, really Mom, you don't have to-
Duke's Mom: Of course I do, you're my son!
2 hours later...
Dukes Mom: I'm HE-RE! OH DUKEY! WHERE ARE YOU?
*Everyone laughs when Dukes Mom says Dukey*
Duke: *Rushing over* I'm here! Please don't call me Dukey again, please!
Dukes Mom: Okay then, Dukesy-pie!
Duke: Please, Mom, please don't call me anything that's not my real name! Not in front of all my Faction members!
Dukes Mom: Now where is your Faction? Is it this way? OH MY WORD, THAT GUARD SHOULDN'T BE SLEEPING ON THE JOB! WAKE UP, WAKE UP!
*Dukes Mom shakes the sleeping guard and wakes him up, just for him to fall asleep again*
Dukes Mom: No matter, we should go to see where your faction is now, Dukesy-Pie!
Duke: Fine. Here it is!
Dukes Mom: Oh my word, this is horrendous!
Duke: It's what?
Dukes Mom: Horrendous! That bouncer/fan thing! Someone could get seriously hurt on that! And after they could have been hurt, no rail on this balcony! What if someone fell off! This place needs a makeover!
Let's just skip through all the decorating and work that Dukes Mom did to make the place look just like home, and just see what happens after it's done.
Dukes Mom: There! Just like home!
Duke: Please, I can't let the walls stay pink! And also, I can't have all this fancy china and ceramic things up on shelves!
Dukes Mom: Nonsense! Now let's see how you're doing with outside, in Crux Prime! Such a wonderful name, isn't it? Let's see, here's the button, OH WOW I'M BEING TELEPORTED!
Duke: WAIT UP!
*Duke rushes over to teleport to Crux Prime to help his Mom stay alive*
At Crux Prime...
Dukes Mom: Well, I say! This place is EXTREMELY out of proper maintanence and care! The gray ground, heaps of force field parts lying around, and HOW ON CRUX DID YOU MANAGE TO GET THE SKY PURPLE! But, at lease all of your lovely purple friends are out here to help you clean this place up and restore it to it's natural splendor!
Duke: They aren't my friends, Mom.
Dukes Mom: Well, they should be! Nothing is wrong with them, sure, they are purple, but that must just be some kind of crazy thing that you young people do! Go on, play with them, something nice and calm, like painting pottery, or Truth or Dare, or maybe even Hide and Seek! Actually, please don't do Hide and seek, it is very much to dangerous, you could get lost, or you could catch a cold in this weather!
Duke: Mom, you don't get it! THey aren't normal anymore, they're Maelstrom, they would tear us to peices if they got the chance!
Dukes Mom: Oh, please, don't exxagerate, maybe they might not let you in to their nice group of friends at first, but then they'll be kinder, and realize their mistakes about you, and realize that they should be nice to everyone, and according to the book, "How to completely control your childs life and help them get along with others" you should make friends whenever there is an oppurtunity! You also shouldn't stay outside after 4 in the afternoon, and you should eat a nutricious breakfast, like the kind I eat, and someday you will be healthy and strong!
Stormling: LOOK! PERSON IS CLUELESS! ATTACK HER!
*Stormling tries to attack Dukes Mom, but then she grabs him and twists his arm backwards, making him stop*
Dukes Mom: Run quietly and safely, dear, and don't let the bed bugs bite when you go to bed at the reccommended time of 7 in the evening!
