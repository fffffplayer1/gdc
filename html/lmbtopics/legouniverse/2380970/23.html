‎07-24-2012 09:30 AM

*Hael lights a match and tosses it into the fire pit. The match burns out. Hael tries again. The same thing happens. Hael repeats it until there are no matches left*
Hael: WHY WON'T IT LIGHT! Hey I have one match left! *Lights it then blows it out* Nah, I'm never gonna be able to light the fire with only one match.
*Hael tosses it behind himself and the match lands in some wet grass (from the rain) and it lights the grass on fire and threatens to burn Nexus Tower down, but at the moment it's just a medium size flame growing fast*
Hael: REALLY?! REALLY?! *Stomps the flames out* IT REALLY TAKES MORE THAN THREE BOXES OF MATCHES TO ALMOST START A CAMPFIRE, BUT I TOSS A SINGLE MATCH BEHIND ME INTO WET GRASS AND IT ALMOST STARTS A DANGEROUS FIRE?! HOW DOES THAT WORK?!
Six boxes of matches, two lighters, three bottles of lighter fluid, and 4 entire newspapers later...
*Hael has a medium sized fire in his fire pit*
Hael: YAY I DID IT!
*Hael sits down and looks at the fire and pokes it with a stick a few times. At first he is excited, but the he realizes nothing exciting is happening. 5 minutes later the fire goes out*
Hael: ARGH! I give up.

‎07-24-2012 09:31 AM

*Hael puts the lid on the fire pit so it doesn't get rained on and goes into Nexus Tower smelling like smoke, which for some reason reminds him of an MMOG he used to play that ended on the last day of January this year. Hael goes inside and sits in a chair, bored. Then he sees smoke through the window. He goes outside and sees the smoke coming from the fire pit. Hael opens the lid and sees a hot fire. Even the logs were on fire and burning steady*
Hael: YAY I GOT A FIRE STARTED AND GOING! ...Except I don't really want to make a fire anymore...I'll put it out!
*Hael gets the hose and sprays the fire. The fire goes out for a second, then flares back up, like nothing happened. Hael filled a plastic swimming pool with water and dumped it on the fire. The fire goes out for a second then flares back up, again. Hael gets a vacume-gun from Overbuild's lab that makes a vacume in a small area for a small amount of time. Hael points it at the fire and shoots it, hoping that having no oxygen will make the fire go out. The fire does go out, then when the vacume fades away, it flares back up again*
