Chapter 46 Part 2

Overbuild:  I'd rather not go on that ride...

Hael:  Why not?

Overbuild:  It's too...boring for me, yeah it's boring.

Hael:  Oh okay, lets go on this ride then*points to a ride that says "speed of light!"  which is shaped like an infinity symbol *

Hael:  They say it makes you go faster than the speed of light!

Overbuild:  That's impossible!  Even if it were, it would send you through time!

Random person:  Wow that ride was crazy-I can't wait to go on that ride!  It's going to be awesome!*goes on the ride*

1 minute later...

Same person:  Wow that ride was crazy-I can't wait to go on that ride!  It's going to be awesome!

Overbuild:  *Stops the person*  Did you just do the same thing twice?

Person:  No, this is the first time I did it in this one.

Overbuild:  This what?

Person:  I've said too much.  *Vanishes when Overbuild blinks*

Overbuild:  What just happened?

Hael:  They say it makes you go faster than the speed of light!

Overbuild:  If you're already repeating yourself, that means we've already going to have go on it!

Hael:  What?

Overbuild:  I don't know, it's impossible to talk about time travel without confusing someone!

Hael:  It'll be fun, who cares if we make a paradox or something?

Overbuld:  Fine.

2 minutes later...

Hael:  See, wasn't that fun?

Overbuild:  Yeah I guess.  What day is it?

Hael:  Monday.

Overbuild:  I KNEW IT!  WE TRAVELED THROUGH TIME!

Hael:  Actually, you just forgot what day of the week it was.

Overbuild:  Oh.

Vanda:  *Walks up to them*  Hey, what are you doing here?  We left a week ago.

Overbuild:  I KNEW IT!
