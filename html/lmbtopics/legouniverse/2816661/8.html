Embrace the Illusions

Chapter 10, Searching for the Sun

The laughter quickly subsided, leaving us cloaked in darkness alone. I looked around, as my eyes slowly adjusted to the dark. All I saw were rock walls, but no way of escape. Suddenly I heard the ‘Clunk’ of a blade hitting rock, and looked over. Force Thunder was hitting the rock with his sword, but to no avail. “What are we gonna do now?” complained Melian. “You tell me.” I shot back “Maybe you could try think like the rest of us.” “Rest of us?” countered Melian with a smirk “I don’t see you doing anything.” I glared at her, but bit back my comeback and walked over to a wall. “Need help?” came a voice next to my ear, causing me to jump and pull out my weapon. “I’m afraid that won’t help.” The voice continued “You see, or maybe you don’t, I am a ghost. Solid things like that can’t hurt me.” As the ghost said those words, it appeared before me. The ghost had spiked brown hair, and was wearing white shirt and pants. “Ummm..” I said smartly “Where did you come from?” “My name is Dashboard, and I come from a parallel universe.” He replied “My universe was destroyed by the Nexus imploding.” “And why would you have to destroy imagination?” Asked Brocktree. “The Maelstrom was to powerful. It was the only way to save ourselves.” “Wh-” started Melian, but was cut off by Force by clearing his throat. “We really should get moving, and try to find a way out.” “Yeah..” Melian muttered “We should.” Our group walk onward, all the way to the cave. By now everyone seemed to have realized that we had been abandoned, and there hardly any hope of ever reaching the surface.

Once we had reached the cave, most of use sat down to rest. Only Brocktree and Sharpie remained standing. “Hey,” Melian said suddenly “ How about we give our team a name?” “Well, our original name was supposed to be Alpha 1281” Said Sharpie “But that name doesn’t really seem too.....you know, meaningful.” “How we rename it the Elite Shadows, ES for short.” I said. “Not really, though I do like the ‘elite’ part.” Said Melian. “How about to Imagination Elites?” asked Dashboard. “No,” I said “There are too many things with Imagination in them already, take the IFB for example.” “How about Elite Dragons?” Asked Force. “Perfect! ED for short right?” Exclaimed Melian. “Yeah.” Replied Force. “Hey, look at this!” Brocktree said suddenly. “I can see light!” What he said made us all jump us quickly and race over, no matter how tired we were. And indeed, as we looked up, we all saw a ray of glowing shadow slipping through the crack. We all grabbed our weapons and began hitting the rocks, trying to reach the sky. Finally, after a fierce swing at the rock from Brocktree’s sword Verminbane, the rock crumbled down, and formed a path to escape. Dusted streams of shadow spilled into the cave, casting a dark light throughout the room. One by one, we climbed to the surface, breathing in the clean but sooted air. There we stood, the newly formed group, the Elite Dragons. Together, we would travel worlds, cross time, destroy and heal. All to save Imagination.

Chapter 10!!!!!
I apologize profusely for not posting anything for many weeks. I've been really busy with my life, and haven't had any time to write. The beginning is a bit lame, but it gets better near the end. I tried to give everyone the same amount of 'Page time' (like screen time, only for books), and I hope I got everyone's personality correct. I know Melian seems a bit rude, but ill make her nicer later. Oh, and if your wondering about the name 'Elite Dragons', and think its a little lame... well here's a little bit of history for you:

A long time ago, I met two other people while play in a dragon battle. Only of them was Jaylight, and the other I forgot (sorry '3'). Anyway, after finishing, we decided to form a team, and called it the Elite Dragons. I was the Leader, Jaylight way the plan maker, and the other guy was the Fighter. The Elite Dragons were a great team, and I even made a base for them. Sadly, a few months before the end, Jaylight left and the other guy never came on. Thus the end of the Elite Dragons.
