Chapter 27: Far from over.

Well, Three days after the battle. Rose has gone missing, My sister is in a coma and I had lost the golden numbchucks. Worse of worst, Jaysen got away even though we destroyed all of his ships, His army and even his " Home ". My " Brother " and other "Sister" ( Who I never heard about before or remembered. MAN How big is my family? Whats next? Hael storm is my grandma?) I was pretty much friendless other then Levi. I hate to admit it but I was pretty sad.

I sat at the building station at the Nexus tower. I stared off into crux.
" First Jazmin.." I mumbled, " and Now Rose and my sister.." I flipped my golden dagger around in my hand. It has been glowing lately, I don't know why.
" Its not your fault ya know." I looked behind me to see Levi.
" Whats not my fault?" I asked staring back at Crux.
" Them going missing and you sister going into a sleep." Levi blinked.
" Rose and Jazmin are my fault. I should have stayed with them and not run off." I muttered.
" Jazmin is my fault " Levi said.
" how? you said she locked you in a closet. What were you going to do? Knock her out and lock her up instead?" I blinked at him as he sat next to me.
" No." Levi said, " what Confuses me is why she did that.."
" Yea. She should have knocked you out it would ha- Wait a second.." I said. I realized something.
" Ok this is going to sound crazy. What if Jazmin wasn't taken by the skeletons?" I wondered.
" What? What are you saying...?" Levi asked.
" What if she..." I trailed off.
" Ran Away?" Cole's voice made me jump.
" Master Cole.. Hi.. Thanks for sneaking up on me. After All this I needed a good scare." I said sheepishly.
" Jazmin wouldn't have ran away right?" Levi wondered.
" She might have.. Jazmin had a brother..." Cole mumbled.
" What? There was an ice student before Levi and after Um.. Whats his face?" I blinked.
" yes. he was Jazmin's brother. His name was Mathew." Cole blinked.
" What happened to him?" I asked.
" Smashed." Cole sighed.
" Why would this want to make Jazmin run away?" I asked. Cole looked very sad.
" I don't know. . . " He said. My dagger glowed hot. Why did this give me the feeling he was lying? I sighed, I would ask him but I knew he won't answer.
" We are never going to see Jazmin again.. Are we?" I mumbled.
" Mostly likely not" Cole moaned.
" I won't have the same for Rose. " I said standing up.
" We don't even know if she is alive!" Levi cried.
" Oh yes we do." I said and held up my golden dagger.
" Dagger. Return to me if Rose is alive." I said and threw the Dagger down to crux. We waited. there was a sparkle of Light and the Dagger was back in my hand.
" Rose is Alive.." I said, " This is Far From over. . " . . . . .

Chapter 28

I was leaning against the rock, my leg hurting badly. I had a throbbing (literally) feeling that my leg was broken. Every time I tried to get up, my leg seemed to fall out from under me. I put my hand on my head, and pushed against the ground. I lifted myself up with my other leg, and limped towards the way out. But it was impossible to get out of there with a most likely broken leg. I grabbed the Numbchucks of lightning and the swords of fire, and limped to a different rock. It was sticking out of a cliff, and it seemed like a perfect seat or bed. I sat down, and heard a voice. "Well, you got Drayden hurt badly, you must be a good fighter." a voice came. I shook my head. "Not unless I want to," I replied. It laughed. Dorkitect appeared in front of me. "You did nothing to help us... why were we supposed to help you?" I reasoned. At one point, I had liked him... there had been something about him that... that I recognized. Now, he was rude, as expected. I wanted so much to snap at him, but I couldn't do it. "I can help," he said, extending his hand. I laughed. "Yeah, right," I muttered. He nodded. "I really can.... as long as you don't call me Dorkitect." I shook my head. "Don't count on it Dorko." He sighed. "Fine," he muttered, and disappeared. I walked to the edge of the cliff. I stared down, into the endless river of maelstrom and evil. "This is my chance to break free," I sung softly. It was part of my favorite song. "Everything's depending on me, and if I keep tryin' I'll be on top of the world!" I continued. "Where I can see everything before me, reaching up to touch the sky, on top of the world." It described how I felt. I wanted to get out of here... break free. I knew that a lot depended on me, not all. It mostly depended on Diana, but a lot depended on me... like if she would get the numbchucks and if the sword of fire was safe. If I keep trying, I'll get out, and get to them. I'd feel alright, as if I was on top of the world... I'd see everything before me, reaching up to touch the sky as if the stars were wishes granted at every moment... I felt as if the song was telling me something, but I couldn't tell. Oh, it was stupid... songs can't tell anyone anything. I just needed to get out of there.

I told Gallen that I would help him in the morning since it was getting late. I sat in my room, which other then Levi's was the only one in full repair. " Tomorrow I have to go find the Dragon shrine.." I whispered to my dagger. I really don't know why I talk to it. It just felt right.
" You are going to help me right.." I asked quietly. I closed my eyes, the Dagger buzzed in my hand. I heard a faint sound, " yes. . ." I opened my eyes and blinked.
" Huh...?" I looked over at Rose but she was still asleep.
" Weird." I thought.
" Whats weird?" a small voice asked.
" Who's there? " I asked.
" Me." The Voice said.
" Me who?" I blinked.
" The Dagger of Truth. " The Voice said again. I looked down at my dagger, it now floated in my hands and glowed much brighter. so bright I hadn't noticed the candle had went out.
" You speak.." I breathed, " Ok so my sis has the Sword of Time and I have the dagger of truth.. "
" Yep." The dagger said, " Question."

" Jazmin put me under a spell. So instead of the holder taking control of the army she would." The Dagger said.
