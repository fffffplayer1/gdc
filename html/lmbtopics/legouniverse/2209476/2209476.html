" Skeletons had it." i answered.

Rose nodded and ran off to get master Zane.

" Man.. I dont want the nexus force to come." I complained.
" Hey, Maybe we will make new friends." Jazmin said.
" But we will have to make new weapons.." I mumbled.
" Oh be quiet. You know you will love the new weapons and ways to smash maelstrom." Jazmin countered.
" But they will change our ways!" i sighed.
" The Skeletons changed and now we must in order to defeat them." Jazmin said.

Chapter 1 (My part, though... they're the same, but different places)


"Master Zane," I said, holding my hands together, bowing. "Sensei is taking you and I with him to Nexus Tower to... to get the recruits and commanders... there is a new type of skeleton here, a skeleton with Maelstrom bones, er, swords. I'm not sure which to call them." Zane nodded, walking down to me. "I sense you aren't sure about the Nexus Force coming here, are you?" I sighed. "Is it that obvious?" Zane laughed. "More obvious than you think, Rose. Come, we'll set out with Sensei as soon as you are ready." I nodded. "Okay, let me talk to Kai," I said.

I walked down the pathway, ready to spinjitzu my way over the bridge. I heard Kai yell something. I didn't spinjitzu, I just ran. "Kai!" I yelled. I ran into the room, and Kai had fallen backwards. A skeleton leaped out of the window, causing the glass to shatter into a lot of little pieces. Kai was sitting on the ground, no obvious wounds, but he must be hurt to have yelled like he did. "Rose," he said, panting, "It was a skeleton. It snuck up from behind, and I wasn't ready for it, so it... it nearly infected me." I nodded. "Kai, you know Maelstrom was here now?" Kai nodded. "Yes, I knew. I was coming to tell you," he said. I realized he was right: He was in the middle of the path. "Well, I'll get you to the Ninjago hospital," I said, trying to lift him. After many tugs, groans and pulls, I finally picked him up. "Okay, Rose, you know this probably is terrible for you," he said. I nodded, breathing heavily. "I know, but you need the hospital," I said. He nodded, but I could tell he was trying to make himself lighter (By the way, that never works) and making it easier for me to carry him. I shook my head at him, and walked over the bridge. When I got to Sensei Wu, and he saw Kai, he ran over. "What happened?" he asked. I gaved Kai to the Ninja's running to us. "A skeleton jumped him, and infected him," I said. He wasn't literally infected, but that was a term we used for, "Hurt badly by a maelstrom weapon." Sensei nodded. "We'll leave for Nexus Tower in the morning. Rose, you get some rest, that couldn't have been easy," he said. I nodded. That night, I slept well.

The next morning, we were off in rockets. I used the auto phone built into the rocket to talk to Zane. "Rose, they aren't as bad as you think," he said. I sighed. "Zane, I don't really like Nexus Force. You know that!" Zane sighed. "Alright, I know. But, you can't always be so negative when somebody says or does something you don't like," he said. I nodded, though he couldn't see it. "I know," I said. We landed the rockets at Nexus Tower and walked down the path towards the Sentinels. "We'll tell Duke Exeter first," said Sensei. I sighed. "Alright." When we arrived, Duke Exeter seemed surprised to see us. "Sensei, to what do I owe the honor?" Duke said, leaping down. Sensei laughed. "Funny, Duke. But, we have a problem." Duke turned his head. "What is it?" Sensei sighed, and his smile turned to a frown. "This," he said, holding the weapon wrapped in cloth. "Maelstrom at Ninjago?" Duke asked, taking the weapon. "Afraid so," Zane said. Duke turned to him. "Hello, Zane. I could barely see you back there!" he said. Zane laughed. Believe me, Jay, Kai and Cole had a hard time making him laugh... that was a classic in Ninjago. "And who is this?" Duke asked, pointing at me. "This is Rose, Rosalina, Roseann, whichever you prefer. Her full name is rather long," Sensei said. He glanced around. "In fact, I can't even remember her full name." Duke looked at me. "You do, though, don't you?" he said. I nodded. "Would you mind telling me?" I shook my head. "No, I don't mind. My full name is Rosa Erisina Cormodo Sapranis." Duke blinked. "Okay, that is pretty long," he said. I nodded. "So, how do you like Nexus Tower?" he asked, looking straight at me. No, I don't want to answer that question. I sort of pretended I didn't hear, and glanced around the room. Sensei leaned over to Duke, and whispered, "She doesn't really like the Nexus Force... she's never been anywhere but Ninjago and, she's quite paranoid..." Duke nodded. "Alright," he said. "Well, I'll alert the others," Duke said. "You can all walk around if you want," he said. Sensei nodded. "Thank you," he said.

After awhile of walking around (And complete bordem) we got a message saying Vanda wanted to meet us. "Alright, let's go," Sensei said. I was in back, considering I was the low one of the group... though the group was only made of three people. Vanda greeted us warmly, but she seemed worried about something. "You heard about the weapon, didn't you?" Sensei asked. Vanda laughed. "Yes, but you knew I would. Anyway, what do you need help with?" Sensei looked over. "We need a lot of people there... the skeletons have mostly disappeared, and the grounds bare, but a few days ago, Rose got ambushed by a group of them, and she was lucky enough that Cole was watching over her. And yesterday, Diana saw that skeleton and his weapon... we knew something bad would happen, but not bad enough that ten skeletons would ambush a young girl, and enough that they would infect their soldiers with maelstrom and give them infecting weapons, used to infect people such as Diana. We need reinforcments, Vanda. If we don't, Ninjago and all the apprentices might be captured and used against what they are supposed to do. Lord Garmadon and the Darkitect have teamed together, apparently, and we need to stop that. Together, they are very strong, and that is bad." Vanda Darkflame stared at him. She blinked. "Alright," she said. A Paradox Agent walked up to Vanda Darkflame. She turned, whispered a little to him, and turned back to us. "This is Levi. He is a Paradox Agent, and he will come with you," she said. I shook hands with him. We both gave each other a mean look, but when Zane and Sensei shook hands with him, they smiled. There was something about him, I just didn't like. But, I'm paranoid, so how would I know who to trust and not to trust? "This is Rose, Levi. You've met Sensei and Zane already, but not Rose. She is a student of Sensei's." When Vanda saw the mean looks we were giving each other, she added, "And, please be nice to her." I blinked. "Alright," Levi said.

Later, we were standing near the launchpad back to Ninjago. "What element are you training in?" I asked. He glanced over. "None," he said. I spun over. "NONE?!" I said harshly, glaring. "I thought every Nexus Force member had to train in at least one element!" I said. He shook his head. "Nope." I sighed. I walked to the edge of the launchpad, staring over the edge. "I want to go back," I mumbled. Zane walked up. "Rose, I would like to train him," he said. I turned. "Really?" I said, my eyes brightening up. "Yes. It's hard to survive in Ninjago without Spinjitzu, and if he doesn't like it, I'll hang him by his toes in the rock garden," he said. I laughed. "Very funny, Zane," I said. Well, at least he was going to be trained in Spinjitzu, thanks to Zane.
