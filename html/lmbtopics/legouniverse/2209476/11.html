Chapter 35

I grabbed my pack. I had slid it onto my back, and had filled it with everything we might need. "So what are we doing again?" I asked, turning to Diana. She didn't respond, but kept going. I raised an eyebrow. "Alright?" I said, shaking my head. Have you ever gone to battle Frakjaw? Well, for some reason, that music was playing right now, and it was scary. "Hurry," Diana said solemnly. "Fine," I muttered, and caught back up. Suddenly, the world turned black. As in, everything around me was black. Soon, purple sparkled swirls covered the black as night walls, and a scary, female laugh, deep though, filled the room. I turned around, and saw the Darkitect... or was he? "What do you want?" I said, strolling around the black room. "Oh, I'm not the Darkitect," he said. I turned around. "What?" I said. He morphed into a more female shape... oh ninjago... he had morphed into... Jazmin! "Jazmin??" I squealed, running to her. I hugged her, though I had no clue what she was like now. "Rose, I missed you too, but..." she said, chuckling, but her laugh had a sad touch in it... sort of like she was about to do something she'd regret. "Jazmin, how'd you.. why... what happened?" I asked. Jaz's eyes looked down, and I sat down in the black world. "Mind powers... Mathew.... dead," she muttered, tears filling her eyes. I blinked. Who was.... Mathew..... "Wait, the student before me?" I questioned. She nodded, sniffling. I hugged her, hoping she'd feel better, but when I pulled back, her eyes were red, and her hair was straight. That was new. "Jazmin...." I muttered. She grinned. "So now I'm going to do something else," she said. The black walls disappeared, and I was sitting on the grass. "Diana!" I yelled. She turned to me, and ran down. "What is it?" she asked. I shook my head. "Jazmin is the one doing this!"

Chapter 36: Crux Minor
(This CHapter is in the perspective of Gallen)

Diana and Rose came rushing out og the caves. They both had fearful looks in their eyes and were out of breath. "We were just about to leave" I said. "Lets go."

We boarded the ship, all of us. Me, Levi, my mom, Diana, Rose, the four masters (carrying the four weapons of Spinjitsu) and Jaysen and Kyra. Their brothers stayed behind to watch over the monestary. We took off, me at the controls. "So, where are we goin" asked Kel'ina "Is it to where the Dragon Shrine is?"
"No. Before we go there, there is something I want to do. A threat I want to get rid of. The Androids." I began to tell them their story. "Jaysen's army being defeated is an important event in my timeline. MAny new technological advances are established thanks to the andords' remains. Most of them good, and some of them evil. Like the androids. Using materials, weapons and data found in the old android remains, and some info on their greatest threats, like us, the androids were created. They took forms to blend in in the Universe, so no one would notice until it was too late. They are virually invinceable."
"How many are there" asked Levi.
"Three, androids 17, 18, and 19. 17 looks like a pretty girl with blonde hair, eighteen is a handsome boy with a scarf and black hair, and 19, well, 19 is different. 19 can use advanced scanners to look into youre mind, and chose a form that will confuse you, someone from youre past."
"Alright, so that doesnt answer where we are going."
"To a small planetoid orbiting Crux Prime called Crux Minor. On it, and evil scientist named Doctor Rachotec builds his evil machines. He was actually one of the Nexus Force's top dog scientists, until he was expelled for crazy experiments. His prime motive was for the androids to destroy the Force, but the first thing they destroy is the creator himself. The androids will come online in a matter of hours. I want to stop them before they do."
"Good plan" said Diana "But what if we are to late?" I was silent for a moment. Then we hope we can hold them off long enough for us to start the ship and go. We are no match." She gulped.

We landed on Crux Minor about an hour later. We rushed towards the building, and I hoped we werent too late.

Chapter 36: The Androids

We rushed into the complex. I didnt waste any time with technology, I just burst the door down. We rushed inside to see a man, cluching a remote and standing in front of four pods. He didnt seem surprised to see a bunch of ninjas blast open his door.
"So" he said "It is true. A group of ninjas would come to stop me." He was talking like he knew we were coming. Like someone tipped him off.... "Its over Rachotec" I yelled.
"No, it is you who have failed their objective. He sneered and pressed a button. Three of the pods began to open. "No" I yelled, but it was too late. Three Androids marched out: 17, 18, and 19. "So youre description was right" said Kel'ina as she began to adress the Androids "17, blonde, not unnatractive , zebra pattern shirt and maroon pants. 18, black haired, handsome, red scarf, a red shirt and jeans. And that brings us to 19." 19 looked just like a regular explorian bot. His spotlights stopped at Kel'ina's eyes and stayed their for a moment. Then, his image flickered into a tall, well built young man with long brown hair and several scars. She and Diana gasped. I recognised him. It was my uncle, the Golden Graceful Legend. Android 17 turned to Dr. Rachotec. "Good morning, doctor. How are you today?" The doctor looked surpirsed.
"So, the new programming works. You are obidient." 18 snickered. "Undoubtably, Doctor. We are at youre command." His eyes (unnoticed by the doctor) slowly moved to the remote.
"Good. Now, for youre first task, destroy these pesky ninjas!"
"Ill do that" said 18 "But first-" His image seemed to blur, and then came back into focus. When it did, he was holding the remote. Dr. Rachotec gasped.
"You-. Give that back this instant!" 18 examined it. A mock expression of shock appeared on his face.
"Oh, but good Doctor, youe surely werent going to use this to shut us off, were you?"
17 joined in "Yes doctor, but why? Surley our new programming wouldnt allow us to betray you, now would it?" 18 crushed the remote.
"ANDROIDS, I COMMAND YOU TO STAND DOWN!" 17 continued to mock him.
"But doctor, youre genious can only be adressed as such. Why, anything designed by you coldnt possibly fail! Like obediance programming. If I didnt have that, I might do this!" She leaped at him with alarming speed and picked him up with one hand. She held him in the air like a prize.
"Pu-pu- PUT ME DOWN!"
She giggled "AS you wish." Her hand glowed, and the doctor screamed. He fell to the ground: in three sprate pieces. Levi looked like he was about to faint. Kel'ina had balled up her hands into fists. 19 turned to the final pod. He spoke wth Legend's voice. "Now, now now. What could be in here?" The Androids moved over to the pod. "No" I yelled. "It cant be!"
"It is" said 17. "Apparently, Dr. Rachotec designed another Android. How wonderful!"
She was right: the doctor had built a new one. This wasnt possible. In the future, there were only three- these three! The pod had a number 20 on it. I clenched my hands into fists. An explosion of light erupted in the room and my clothes grew their dragon patterns as I went into DX form. "No" I cried.
"I wont let you awaken another Android! Not as long as I can help it!" I place my hands together and began to charge a blast of electricty. "Lets get outta here" cried Levi. Everyone besides me exited through the entrance. I released my blast at the chamber, and flew after them.

We all made it outside, just in time. The entire comlex exploded, destroying half of Crux Minor. I came out of DX form in front of everbody. "Well" I said. "Its over. Now lets go find the Dra-"
"Look" cried Rose. I turned around. The smoke was clearing. Inside it, a small rock remained, still here due to Crux Minor's gravitational pull. On it, stood 17, 18, 19, and 20's pod, all without a simgle scratch. "Now" I cried. "WERE POWERLESS!" 19 pressed a button on the pod and it began to open, and 20 stepped out. He had a red mohawk, yellow armor, and a pair of jeans. He had aclam, silent expression.
"Now" said 18 "Well be using youre ship to leave." Everyone got into battle position. If we were lucky, like I had said, we might be able to escape. Alive.
