Chapter 0.5: Banished

"Ninjago!" shouted Kyra as she started up her fire tornado. Jaysen smirked. He curled up into a ball and started to spin. Lightning enveloped him as he shot forward, right into Kyra's tornado. He bounced off her like a rubber ball and she fell out of the tornado. Kyra sat up and rubbed her head. "How did you..." Jaysen interupted her speach with blasts of lightning from his hands. "Ha ha ha" laughed Jaysen "Not so weak anymore, am I sister." She drew her sword "Talk is cheap" she said" Lets do this." He cracked his whip. He lashed out at her, and the whip wrapped around her sword. He pulled, trying to get it out of her grip. She pulled as well. And she won. Jaysen lost his footing and was pulld towards her. The but of her sword hit his head and he was knocked out.

When he woke, Kyra was walking away and Sensei Wu was announcing her the winner. "No!" He jumped up and zapped her with lightning from his hands. "Enough" shouted Wu. "You know better than to attack an unaware opponent. It is dishonorable. Come with me." Sensei began to walk towards the storerooms. Jaysen followed.

"Disrespect, anger, cowardness" said Wu as he paced back and forth. "All traits of a weakling. A weakling who is not prepared. Therfore I deem you unfit to take the Ninjago Trials and become a Master. "But Sensei" interjected Jaysen "All the others are taking the triasl. Its unfair." "I know" said Sensei. "And that is not yet the worst of my news. I hereby banish you fromplanet Ninjago." "WHAT!" yealled Jaysen "YOU CANT-" "Yes, I can." Jaysen roared, activated his whip, and leaped at Wu. Sensei looked up, staff glowing, and poked him in the stomach as he came forth. He blacked out again.

When he came to, he found both hands chained to a rock at the egde of Ninjago. The masters, his siblings, the rest of the ninjas, and Sensei Wu stood in front og him. "Master please, stop this" he yelled. Jay looked down at his feet. "I am sorry" said Wu "But you have forced me to do this." Kyra began to cry. Wu slammed his staff on the ground, and Jaysen's chunk of rock began to drift into space. "Youre dead to me Jay, you hear me. DEAD TO ME!" Those words would haunt Jay for the rest of his life.

Chapter 10 (This should make more sense... I was tired when I wrote the other one! )


I was lying on the bed, my face at the end of the bed instead of on the pillow. I sighed, thinking of the day. "First, a dragon makes Levi and I fight each other, probably could have killed each other. Second, we meet, 'Nexus Jay,' a Nexus Force leader type guy... ugh. Third, Celestia doesn't really give any information on Nightmare Moon," I thought. I shook my head. Moonlight streamed in through the one-way window on the side of the room. I got up and walked over, seeing the Nexus Force recruits running around, grabbing drinks, buying food, and grabbing their swords or guns. They have no clue what they're getting themselves into... but it's got to be better than fighting skeletons. They're just about three times harder than maelstrom. In between the edge of the indoor window sill and the window, it was about three feet wide and five feet high. I pulled myself up, and sat down. I looked outside, still watching the recruits. I sighed. "Okay, Lamo, show yourself," I murmured. A chuckling sound was right beside me. Suddenly, Garmadon appeared right beside me. "Amazing, isn't it? The Darkitect is a lot easier then my skeletons. I guess adding his maelstrom made things easier, though," he said. I sighed, and turned my head over, staring at him. "Alright, creepo. I'll make you a deal. You leave us alone for three days, and I'll consider asking Diana not to light you up like christmas lights," I said. He laughed. "I'm not leaving," he said. I shook my head. "Well, you have to. Don't you need a beauty sleep?" I asked, smiling. He laughed. "Funny," he said. I shook my head angrily, and stared straight into his bright, red eyes. "Lamo, just leave, alright?" I said, turning back towards the window. "Fine, but I'll be back soon," Garmadon said, disappearing. I sighed, leaped down, and got on the bed. "Kai, come soon.. I don't wanna deal with Lamo anymore," I muttered. I fell asleep.
