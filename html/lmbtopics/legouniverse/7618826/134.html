Kratark steps into the arena. And judgementally points at Zeytran, in order to prove his absolute dominance.

Zeytran looks down at Kratark with pity. "You're tiny." He points out. He shrugs. "Wouldn't be much fun." He then shapeshifts into a more...managable size, still a full head taller than Kratark but not as ridiculous.

"You're nothing but an arrogant braggert. I shall put you in your proper place."

 

Kratark flourishes before leaping high into the air, unfurling his wings as he prepares to descend upon Zeytran at rapid speed, which he does, the axes are likely to hit Zeytran.

He opens a portal in front of him at the last second, sending Kratark stumbling into the floor. Zeytran dashes towards Kratark, much faster than usual, and swings with his Buzz-Saw Shield.

Kratark's axes smash into the ground, and he slides across the floor for half a second before he figures out what just happened and quickly manages to teleport back onto his feet, a few feet away from Zeytran. He notices a jagged scratch in his shoulder and gives Zeytran a hateful stare, the effect of which is aesthetically amplified by the kraahkan-shaped mask Kratark wears.

 

"That was a cheap trick. A REAL Makuta would've allowed his foe to break himself upon such a weapon, you insult me with your mercy."

 

Smoke envelops Kratark as he disapeers into the shadows: leaving behind several wraith-like apparitions that swing scythes at Zeytran. A voice seems to come from everywhere.

 

"Be right back. I'm buying popcorn. In the meantime, you can fight these shadow constructs I created."
