What a Fascinating Place Part 1

‎11-27-2015 01:25 AM

 

The multiverse is a strange place. Many have differing views on how it functions. Some don't even think it exists at all, that it's a massive simulation concocted by bored deities from outside known reality. Others are content to know that they just don't know.

 

But let's take a step forward, shall we? After all, the multiverse is a big place. Let's look in a very specific point in all the chaos. A dying universe, devoid of life, quickly breaking down into base material and being reabsorbed into the eternal cycle of creation and entropy. Most of the stars in this (rather small) universe had already burned out, and most remaining matter only consisted of stray photons and neutrinos.

 

Except here.

 

Here lay the gravestone of this dead universe; a cube-shaped planet, stripped of life and an atmosphere, slowly crumbling away. Its denizens once knew it as Crux. Before that, in the universe directly preceding this one, it was known as the Crux System, comprised of small fragments like Avant Gardens, Gnarled Forest, Forbidden Valley, and Nimbus Station. It had been the crux (heh) of much strife in both universes.

 

But, it seems, these violent realities now lay in eternal peace.

 

This silence is shattered when two figures materialize in a flash of light, in direct orbit around Crux.

 

"An odd sight it is to see this place quiet, eh Dialogue?"

 

"Aye, Exposition, it is. It seems that this, the center of our troubles for a long, long time, is now finally... silenced."

 

"Not entirely. You forget that echoes of this place exist in its reborn self, just 'down the way' if you will. The new Universe 0550.00 has experienced an... odd anomaly concerning that."

 

"Really? Lord White had demanded my presence elsewhere during the culling process and the subsequent reconstruction. What is this anomaly?”

 

“Call it muscle memory. The inhabitants are following certain… preset patterns from the last iteration.”


“That may be a problem. Any of the big players resurfaced?

“So, why did you bring me here? Was it to dismantle this?”
