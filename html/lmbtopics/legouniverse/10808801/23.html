Name: Kale

Occupation: Grand Admiral for the Crux Empire (Nonexisting, for now. Might add this to the RPs.) Head of Honors, Elite, and Guard divisions.

Appearance: Brown eyes, black hair, 6/2,( 2 inches above normal minifigure height, in minifigure inches), scar on his left cheek, scar on his chest (hidden). Armor that allows him to move swiftly, but great protection. Cloak to hide most of his identity.

Weapon(s): Blade as tall as half a minifigure, small hilt, disk dividing the blade from the hilt.

Race: Cruxian, minifigure (Cruxian meaning a person related to the first minifigures on Crux, not the explorers, though.)

Description: Kale is not normally angry. When Hyperion uses some mix-ups in his sentences, though, that's when he gets mad. He must take up as leader, as Grand Admiral of the Crux Empire. It's stressing, though. He invented the Koi-7, made from the Koi blueprints with a few major upgrades.

Faction: Crux Empire, currently involved in the Race, a thing I made up that is not in LURP, until now.

Warring With: Nexus Force, (In- Storyline, By the Way,) Nexus Empire, (Basicly civilian Nexus Force).

 

I'm posting the next parts of the bios next bio post. Just a basic overview of his only-known-to-me past, if I have the chance, I'll post a story about him and his friends, featuring a lot of trilogies that I'm planning!
