‎09-14-2014 10:27 AM

"Ha! You think that you can defeat me!"

"Quite so!"

Wiz grins again and then electricity is conjured out of his hands hitting Pseudus. But even though Pseudus is jettisoned back and the electricity passes through his body, he is actually laughing.

"Do you think you can hurt me with that? I am beyond physical damage!"

"Of course not! I'm just distracting you!" Wiz now was smiling from ear to ear "You see I have been preparing some very special magic all this time! Goodbye!"

Suddenly, Pseudus was gone. Kate, Santa Claws, the Ranger, the Mythrans, but not the soldiers, were back instead. Wiz had cast a spell to clear his mind. This normally was a simple spell, but Wiz prepared it all this time, so that it would become stronger. He was right that this spell combined with the knowledge all this wasn't real was enough to break the Illusion. Now, he had to make the others see him, too. Pseudus was still concealing him. He quickly teleported in front of Kate. Pseudus couldn't react fast enough and Kate saw him for a milisecond. This was all it needed for Kate to be able to break the Illusion.

"You're alive!" Kate exclaimed with tears in her eyes and hugged him.

Wiz placed his forehead on hers, so that they could still see each other.

"I found out about my lost memory, Kate!"

"Really! How did that happen?"

Wiz did not respond. He decided to show her. Minifigures (the living ones) are made of cells like us. There is a theory that says that their cells are like teeny tiny bricks. It seems logical. So, when little bits of Wiz's body started detaching, moving and going somewhere else, it seemed like his cells were changing positions. But they probably were chunks of cells, rather than cells themselves. As many bits did that, Wiz started to change. Even his clothes seemed to change. At one point, his sunglasses vanished and his eyes glowed light blue.

"If you are to survive, do it in style!" a familiar voice was heard like an echo.

And there was another minifigure there: Strange Odd Shadow!

Kate stares at Strange Odd Shadow not able to believe it. Wiz Ardon had just transformed into Strange Odd Shadow, just before her eyes. This looks so surrealistic that it seems more like an Illusion. But Illusions are not supposed to be surrealistic. They have to look real or they wouldn't be successful. And Kate also wants to believe that Shadow was still alive.

"You're awfully quiet, Kate, for someone who just witnessed their friend's resurrection -or at least what looks like it! aRE You sure you're alright?" Shadow says with a wide smile.

"How?" the wide-eyed Kate managed to say "How are you alive?"

"Now, that's more like it! I'm going to explain everything! Let's go back to the night of that battle against Chaos. I used all of my Imagination to destroy Lord Panda. Well, not exactly! One Imagination Orb was not used. I did not spend it, because it was not needed and so I intended it to join your Imagination and soften your pain. Unfortunately, but fortunately in long terms, this did not happen. You see, the Mythrans like people who sacrifice their Creative Spark to help others. They think that it shows a good soul. That's why they don't wish for them to be gone, but give them a gift for their sacrifice, when they can. They bring them back. They used that one Orb to rebuild my body around it and recreate my Creative Spark. All that was invisible to everyone of course. Once they had finished with me, they explained that if I wanted to go back, they would have to change me and that I would forget my old life. They asked me whether I was willing it give my past up. I agreed. Some sort of cave or tunnel then seemed to appear next to us, though it might have been there forever and I hadn't noticed it"

Kate thinks. All of this seems logical. Not only are some parts of it good explanations of some things like what was happening with Wiz's memories or why for a second that device that tracked people's Imagination showed her that Shadow was in Necrum Forest; but also some others just seem so logical spoken by Shadow. However, there is a very weird thing that makes it all seem very very true. That scene in the darkness with the voice was the exact same dream Kate had saw when she was in Pseudus's manor.

"And what about Wiz?" she finally asks.

"What of him?"

"Will he die now that you have returned?!" Kate asks with a concerned voice.

Shadow laughs "I feel honoured you feel so concerned about me, even my alter ego! But haven't you realised it yet, Kate? Wiz Ardon and Strange Odd Shadow are the same person! Wiz Ardon is just a side of me, even my magic existed before I became Wiz; I just didn't know I was magic to use it! I can even switch to Wiz's body if I want. I'm Wiz as well as I am Shadow! But it really feels so good that even in the face of your beloved's you are concerned about me?"

Kate laughs. The realisation of what has just happened and the fact that Wiz is not lost made her cheer up. She sees it, too, now that Wiz has a lot in common with Shadow.

"But it doesn't count! You are the same person with my beloved!" Kate says still laughing.

Shadow laughs, too. This moment neither of them could feel happier.

"Anyway, I think we're in the midst of a battle! Would you care to say to those Mythrans they're battling thin air! I don't think they can see or hear me or anything!" Shadow says after a while.

Once he said that he heads for Santa Claws whom he hits on the head with an ice saw he produces from his backpack. Santa Claws, who is real, and cannot see Shadow despite being Pseudus's servant, does not try to avoid the hit and falls down unconscious. Meanwhile, Kate talks to the Mythrans.
