‎08-14-2014 02:16 PM

After searching through the dense Jungle, Aron and his friends came to a large, seemingly unnatural clearing.

 

Rehna said, "What could've caused this?"

 

Aron smiled, "A crashed ship. Come on, there might be something salvageable in there."

 

They walked along the large clearing, and soon arrived at a large, orange and white hunk of metal, that looked like the remains of some space cruiser.

 

Akia asked, "What is it?"

 

Aron, who knew immediately what it was, stretched out his hand, pulling the metal bricks towards him, and opening a hatch at the bottom, "It's the Gigawatt, my cargo ship. There must be somethings in here we could salvage. By the looks of the ship, it'll never fly again, but we might be able to find some low-level vehicles or large ammounts of bricks."

 

The group walked inside, and they investigated the ship. They opened boxes, finding a bit of armor for their respective Factions, and a whole lot of seemingly mismatched bricks.

 

Corona, after picking up a greatsword and some knight armor, said, "Alright, what do we do with all of these?" She pointed at the bricks.

 

Eryx steped forward, and extended his staff, concentrating on the bricks in his mind. Suddenly, all of the bricks were enclosed in a strange blue mist, and quickly assembled faster than their eyes could comprehend. When it was finished, the creation was a rather large hover device, enough to seat the six of them, with two blades on the front and two cannons in the back. "That's what we do with the bricks."

 

Everyone climbed into the vehicle, and sped away, looking to find any signs of minifigure life amongst the world.
