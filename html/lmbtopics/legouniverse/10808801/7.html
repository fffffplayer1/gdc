Sentinel Point Alpha

Options
  • Mark as New
  • Bookmark
  • Subscribe to RSS Feed
  • Highlight
  • Print

‎10-06-2014 07:23 PM

A completely normal Minifigure wakes up in a rocky area with jungle surrounding. He's on top of a rock just balancing on the side of a giant pit with Lava in the very bottom.

"Hmm... uuuugh... five more minut-- wuh...woAH?! HAH HUH SORRY WHAT HELLO?" Normm says, immediately jumping off of the dangerously balanced rock onto dirt, solid ground.

 

A very odd hilly area is behind him with jungle spreading out over it. In front of him, a large ravine with volcanic lava bubbling at the very bottom. To his right is a large fragmented rock wall with dirt and vegetation spread over it. To his left is an opening into a clearing. The clearing, as Normm walks into it, is revealed to be on a tall hill overlooking a valley. In the valley, a beam of pure imagination can be seen shooting up into the air.

 

"Well then, i'm on Crux!" Normm said aloud, and then continued: "What happened to the Maelstrom dragons and all that sweet loot? Wait, Maelstrom... if I'm on Crux, where'd the Maelstrom go? I liked smashing baddies! What's the use of Sentinels now? Wait... is this ravine... Caldera Mar? And this clearing must be... Sentinel Point Zeta! Over there is Studburst Pass and... Aura Mar... Maelstrom Quarry... oh my gosh!" Normm paused his thought for a second, overwhelmed with excitement. "TEMPLE OF THE FIRST BUILDERS! RIVENDARK CANYON! THIS MAKES A LOT OF SENSE!" he continued, aloud.

 

Normm began setting up a base of operations in the former Sentinel Point Zeta, which he now named Sentinel Point Alpha. He named the Hill that the point was on "Duke Hill", then he took out some bricks and started building a Mech to protect the area.

 

Name: Normm Adler Dagget

Faction: Sentinel

Faction Kit: Samurai Valiant; Knight Valiant; Space Ranger Valiant.

Alignment: Neutral Good.

Appearance: Spikey brown hair, bushy eyebrows, blue eyes, smirky, Sentinel Samurai Armor w/ cape attached, Bow across back, Katana in Holster, Valiant on back, Helmet and daggers tied to his belt. He has a few scrapes and bandages, too. 

Sergeant Able Stark // Olde Frabjous Builder

Sentinel Point Alpha was coming along nicely. The jungles teeming with dangerous wildlife had been held off by Normm's Mechs. He was currently creating walls that could fit together to make a perimeter. A lot of stuff seemed to be happening all around the temple area in the valley below, but he didn't really notice.
