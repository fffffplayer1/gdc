‎08-11-2014 01:27 AM

 

Before you stretches the immense planet Crux! A perfect cube suspended in orbit around distant triple suns. How does this all work, you might ask. And you'd be asking the wrong question, I'm afraid. This cube defies all understanding as it is an artificially made planet. The power of a reactor buried deep within the core holds the entire assemblage together, breeding a wonderful six sided world. Life springs from within, and runs wild on the surface. Some might call it an unspoiled paradise, even though it is artificial in origin.

 

Each side of the planet holds several different continents twisting into random shapes. Lush jungles, scorching deserts, oceans, teeming with life, grassland savannas stretching out before the eye, and every other biome imaginable link together to form the sides of the cube.

 

OOC: This is a location topic for Crux, as it once was, and is today. As per the agreement on my topic “A Uni-Planetary Proposal” all characters from the old LURP have been moved by The Continuum to this alternate Crux. While there will be new locations on this planet, old installations are completely gone.

 

This the planet of Crux is as it was before the explosion seen in the LU opening cinematic. By The Continuum's doing, the shattered world has been pieced back together. All LURP is going to take place on one of Crux’s six sides here, putting emphasis on land battles and combat as seen in the game. Each side will be home to a landmass/biome/noun, with the intent of the factions present growing and moving around a bit as the roleplay moves forward.

 

This topic will serve as a general overview of the planet itself, sort of a global rundown on what is happening. Specifics for each faction and other locations on Crux itself will be in various subtopics created later. For example, a Tarbucks Coffee topic might launch a planet wide advertising spree here, but all their business will be done in the Tarbucks Coffee topic.

 

More information in the next post.

OOC: Good, it got up. Now for more information.

The IC reasoning behind this is as follows. We, the citizens of LU, haven’t been kind to our home. We’ve been messing with wormholes and the Chaos Source, slinging forces around all the time, and generally not preserving the health of the Universe.

 

The Continuum is a group contrived by LL and Barcha some time ago, who oversee the multiverse and keep things running along smoothly. (Their agent Antagonist played a part in The Codex and the events afterwards.) So it would be logical that they would need to fix up LU, as it were.

 

So some time in the very recent past, they went ahead and did some time trickery and moved the Universe back in time or something, I’m not sure, but basically, they put Crux back together, did away with all of the other planets and such, and plopped us minifigures on it.

 

I really regret not putting the usual level of thought into this, but time constraints. That’s the story though. There wasn’t any sort of announcement or anything, things just sort of happened. Characters will begin waking up here on Crux, with no recollection of what happened or who did what, or where their home, vehicles, or any of that went.

 

This IC mystery will likely be a plot at some point in the future. But for now, we should focus on getting things set up here. Any organisation that wants a base will have to make one themselves. There is no first builder tech or temple, at least for now. Once everything is established, that will make for another plot.

 

There’s an OOC agreement not to have outer space as a central part of the RP, so try to avoid that.


I can’t think of anything else that needs to be said. If you have an issue with any of that, or an idea on how to improve things, bring it up. The reason we did this was to make LURP a healthier place.

The murky darkness of not-being swirls around Bucky. He drifts silently in the abyss of non-existence.

But there... a light!

He moves towards it, eager to start being a thing again. Into the light!

Soreness. Now there’s a feeling, that’s something. That means that there’s something to feel sore.

Green. There’s a color now, that implies sight!

Rushing water. That’s a sound! Bucky definitely exists at this point.

But boy, he’s not in good shape. He props himself up, on what seems to be grass. He rotates his head around for a look at the surroundings.

There are some trees. Good. More trees. Trees everywhere. Must be a forest. Oh, there’s the water. Little stream. Hm.

Bucky props himself up against a tree, and notices a couple other minifigues lying nearby. He takes inventory. He seems to have everything that he had at his last memory, including his messenger bag, cape, and there’s his staff, lying on the forest floor. Adequate to defend himself, he figured, assuming these minifgures are in the same state he is.

He stumbles over to his staff, and leans on it, watching the two other people. He would need to gather his strength before doing anything much.

 

OOC: I suppose this constitutes my new character introduction. Bucky is a big guy with a colorful past and many talents. His defining characteristics are mastery of Dark Imagination, long, fantastic hair, commanding presence, and superior leadership skills.

 

Oh, and I must credit LL for helping me create this topic. He's much better than I with the descriptive words, he was a huge help. Props to LL.

Above Bucky, an orangeish red ovoid falls from the sky, streaking fire and debris. As it gets closer the shape of a ship is clearly seen. It is the Glory. 

 

It crashes into the ground directly next to Bucky, flattening trees for about half a mile, and leaving a smoking crater. A panel begins to rattle, and eventually gives, leaving a hole in the bridge of the ship. A small, red and gold plated podium floats out, with a small glowing red humanoid figure floating above it. As it gets closer to Bucky, it is revealed that the figure is wearing a DRE officer's uniform.

 

The figure, Deady, salutes Bucky. 

 

"Dark Ring Empire Ringworld Pilot A.I. Z417 Mark III Codenamed Daedalus reporting for duty Sir!" 

 

He puts his hand down.

 

"Emperor Kat, it is good to see you. The idiot Ive been working for lately has made me yearn for the days of glory even more. I am afraid to report that DRE forces have taken a massive blow due to the loss of Ringworld. I have tried my best to honor the Glorious Ring, but these are truly desperate times, sir." 

 

He looks back at the remains of the ship. 

 

"Also, sir, we have a... strange guest on board. He should be coming shortly."
