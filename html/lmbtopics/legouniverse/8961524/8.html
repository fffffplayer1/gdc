legojrod wrote:

Apocalypse stands in his ship, wondering what he could possibly do to rebuild his army.

 

Suddenly, a familiar yet different Voice comes from behind him, "You contemplate, my former Master."

Apocalypse turns, and sees Uproar. He says, shakily, "M-malintent? Impossible! You've brought back the Maelstrom?!"

 

Uproar laughs, "I am Malintent No more, I am now Uproar, one of the leaders of the Maelstrom. Now.. You wish for an army...I can give it to you. Granted, they won't be as destructive as your old ones, but if I extract just enough Imagination from my stromlings...I can create almost perfect replicas of your Chaos Soldiers. They'll be limited to the Magic we have now...but it's a start. So, what do you say? Come with me? Or sulk over your lost forces?"

 

Apocalypse ponders this. Malintent was once part of his own consciousness, but he was not to be trusted. But...Apocalypse had no other options. He said, "I accept. Take me and the Other Heralds to your HQ."

 

Uproar grinned, "Then I ask you to unleash Chaos creatures on the Vanguard Outpost. They're trying to beat back my maelstrom there. Once I create your taskforce, then they will work on destroying them all."

 

Apocalypse himself laughed, "That's it? Uproar, you ask for so little. But I shall do what you wish."

 

With That, all the Chaos Heralds, their ships, and Apocalypse himself got Envealoped by Purple Smoke, and disappeared.

 

)Uproar(

 

~Chaos~

 

*Weeks later,Bricky Romer is scanning the area for traces of Uproar and Apocalypse*

"hmmm...Dec would find this interesting"

*the scanning machine beeps,and shows the current location of Uproar*

"time to go"

*Bricky Romer gets in his ship and leaves*
