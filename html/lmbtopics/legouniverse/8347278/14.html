Upon the bridge of The Golden Meridian, one of Bucky’s commanders reports.

 

“Sir, Chaos forces have just landed on the planet.”

 

Bucky raises and eyebrow and responds, “Bring them up on screen, visual.”

 

A live feed of the Chaos Heralds appears on screen, being broadcast by one of the Hands on the surface from a ways away, using the zoom capability to get a clear shot. No audio, though, just Destruction’s mouth moving, and then the creatures appearing, before an explosion blocks the angle.

 

“Hmm. So there is, in reality, Malestrom-Chaos cooperation. I was hoping there wasn’t, to be honest. But, eyes on the prize, this could be an issue. Alert our troops and the Nexus Force, we need to deal with these guys using extreme caution. Make sure all of the NF soldiers are aware and know how dangerous these guys are. I’m going to have a little... chat with them.”

 

Some time passes. Not a lot, but more than a moment and less than a while.

 

Down on the surface, Bucky springs into being, next to a squad of Hands, the closest group to the Chaos Heralds. He confidently crosses the clearing, before coming face to face with the Heralds.
