Bucky reaches into his bag, and pulls out a bottle. Looks pretty normal, as bottles go. Brown tinted glass, cork in the top, no label. A liquid of some kind swishes around inside. Bucky removes the cork, and takes a peek inside. Seemingly satisfied with what he sees, Bucky winds up, and pitches the bottle beautifully in front of them.

 

As the bottle spins through the air, the clear liquid inside spills out, onto the hostile grass. At the end of its arc, the bottle smashes onto the pavement of the main street. Bucky snaps his fingers, and a small fire springs forth in his hand. He takes this fire first to the grass on his legs, burning away the roots. Then he reaches forward, and ignites the liquid.

 

The small spark in Bucky’s hand starts something much larger than itself, as huge orange flames burst from the group to reach up into the sky. Licks of flame dance merrily for all of fifteen seconds, before the entire blaze abruptly comes to a halt, leaving a very charred, but otherwise fine path to walk on.

 

“Nothing quite like fire, eh?”

 

The group continues on, walking single file down the charred strip, before reaching the main road, just before the second bridge. Bucky takes this opportunity to look around.

OOC: Ardour and Chronia are just ahead, are they not?
