A rocket touches down upon a hill in Necrum Forest. Out of the rocket comes a man, a man many know as Cerise. He proceeds to take up residence in Necrum by building a cabin right in the middle of the forest. He stays there for around three months, presumably doing nothing. However, in reality, he is training himself in the art of DI, learning to forge, cut, weld, and smelt nearly everything thinkable. He builds an expanse network of tunnels underneath, tunnels that would seem like a labyrinth to anyone else. And, at the end of his stay, he leaves much more dangerous than he'd been when he'd arrived.

Just dangerous enough to take on a former deity.
