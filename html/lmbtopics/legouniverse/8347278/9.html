Once they stepped through the portal, Wiz and Kate would find themselves in a strange, hollow cave. The Sky was a deep, imagination blue, but you could faintly see the outline of a shell, almost like a large rock,  around the outside. From where they stood, they could see that the center of the strange planet, a glowing blue core of superheated imagination. It was not pure, but it sure was beautiful.

Eryx stood infront of them. His true form was now revealed to both of them (As I described previously) And he held a wooden staff in his hand.

The Trio seemed to be ontop of a floating stone platform, jagged, and natural, like an asteroid. Eryx extended his hand, and a bridge of stones were pulled from their orbit around the center and formed a bridge, leading to a large platform with what looked like a monastary on it. Below this chunk, five pillars of imagination infused stone stood, going from the core of the planet to the monastary.

Eryx said, in an echoing voice, "Come, Wiz and Kate. I must introduce you to the high council."

Eryx taps his staff on the ground, and the stone platform moves towards the monastary. Eryx responds to Wiz's question.

 

"The Pillars of Serenity. Here, some of the last of my people reside. We believe in keeping the peace of the worlds in check. From Crux, to Earth, to ones in Galaxies your kind has not even seen yet. Our reach goes far and wide. This monastary allows us to see into many billions of planets, and the state of their evolution. And we have done this for many eras...Come, now, we approach."

 

The Stone platform halts, and Eryx walks onto a large, grassy area. However, the grass is imagination blue, and large, strange plants are all around. Tending to them, is a strange looking minifigure, with many tentacle-like appendages longer than his own body. He looks at the guests, and starts speaking to Eryx. They speak in a strange tounge, one similar to the ancient language of Chaos, but less...harsh. Their speaking sounds almost like elvish as well.

 

Translation:

 

Eryx: I see you tend to the plants as always, Gerin

Gerin: And I see, Eryx, that you are still picking up strays. What purpose do these minifigures serve? And why have you brought them here?

Eryx: The male, he is infected with the chaos curse. He holds great importance to this world. He and the girl. Even they do not know how important they are.

Gerin: But do they know who we are? And if they did, would they run in fear, or faint in shock?

Eryx: They do not know. They do however, work for the Nexus force.

Gerin: *Scoff* The Nexus Force? A group of Minifigures who think Mythrans are the most powerful. But...I see where you come from, friend. Lead them in. I shall let the council know you are coming.

Eryx: Thank you, my friend.

 

With that, Gerin walks off, his tentacles retracting with a mystical flash.

Eryx smiles, "Come on. We wouldn't want to keep them waiting."
