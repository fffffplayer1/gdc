Bucky watches from his command chair while explosions unfold on the planet’s surface noiselessly.

“It’s fantastic just to watch this unfold,” he says.

 

“Indeed sir, but we should probably do something.”

 

“I… suppose. Let’s start by getting our forces on the ground.”

Down on the surface, one of the hands ducks down behind the log barrier they’ve put up, and removes a small device from its chest, which it throws to the ground. The device unfolds and expands, into a Vortex housing similar to the one outside the police station on Giovanni. A vortex appears within, and Hands begin to troop out of it. They begin to fortify their position, using bricks from the Vortex to construct a bunker around themselves.

 

Back up on the bridge, a bird’s eye view of the situation is displayed in front of Bucky. He can see the Maelstrom and Nexus Force fighting in the city, and his troops, bunkering up a little ways off. He turns to Dr. Matoi, with a raised eyebrow. She returns the look with a huge grin, before discarding her lab coat and disappearing into the bridge’s Vortex without a word.

 

Bucky turns back to the map. With Matoi down there on the surface, he need not worry about that aspect of things. So he turned his attention to the air.

 

“I want air superiority. Launch Lance wings four through eight, maintain a zone of control above the main fighting, and prepare to assist.”

 

(Lances, for those unfamiliar, used to be the DRE’s go-to fighter craft. I made a post once, as Bucky, showing them off to the other DRE members, and explaining their capabilities. These ones today, though several generations later, maintain the same role. Heavy fighters, with huge guns. I could go into more detail about anything, if anyone wants.)

 

Lances pour from The Golden Meridian’s hangars, before assuming formation and entering the atmosphere.
