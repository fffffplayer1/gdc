Suddenly, a piece of masonry crashes in front of him and he wakes with a start. Climbing out of his niche, he grabs his pistols and goes over to where the leaders of the camp have set up. 

 

He sees Dr. Matoi, and asks her what is going on, and at that moment notices the rather large Chaos beast climbing over the wall, firing cannons at a group of Adventurers, who were valiantly trying to keep it at bay with their crossbows. 

 

Rushing over, he tosses two DCCs at the beast, immobilizing it. Then he jumps over it, leaving the wounded beast for the VL troops, and looks out at the battlefield, seeing a thin line of Chaos creatures breaking through the thick melee. They were headed straight for the castle. 

 

"We've got a bit of trouble guys."
