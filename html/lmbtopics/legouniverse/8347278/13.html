‎06-07-2014 11:49 AM

Bucky opens a comm channel to the Nexus Force ships. (Like, all of them, all the factions, on a conference call or whatever.)

 

“Hailing all Nexus Force ships. This is BuckyKat of The Golden Meridian. I see you, too, have been called in to deal with this Maelstrom threat. I would like to propose temporary cooperation between us to more efficiently go about fighting our common enemy.  With your permission, I will begin supporting your troops on the ground, if you safeguard us from any orbital threats.”

 

Bucky then opens another, separate comm channel, to Sparrowhawk, on the surface, through one of the three original Hands. (When I say separate, I mean it.)

 

“Sparrowhawk, Bucky here. I don’t know how much you can see from there, but there’s a bit of a battle unfolding here. The Nexus Force has arrived, and has begun battling the Maelstrom. Behind you, I’m sending down additional Hands. They’re making a forward outpost on the ground on which you stand. I’m also sending Matoi. You and she are in charge of operations on the surface. For the time being, hold your position until we’re established, then prepare to lead the Hands into the city (or village, whatever we’d like to call it). I’m sending air support, and asking the Nexus Force for cooperation. Matoi will inform you of all further developments.”

 

Matoi, sans-lab coat, now stands on the surface, smiling this crazy grin. She reaches to her belt, and pulls off a pair of bright red scissors. She takes one part in each hand, and whips them apart as they increase in size. Separated, each scissor blade is huge, like a pair of dual-swords, and indeed, that’s how she uses them. She gives them a spin and a flourish, cracks yet another grin, before putting them on her back.

-BuckyKat, The Kingmaker

Bucky used the Maelstrom vial for research, and, as such, knows what makes the Maelstrom tick, and that sort of thing. So Bucky’s anti-maelstrom weaponry is quite good at what it does.

OOC: I’m making the following post to illustrate how Bucky’s forces are assisting the NF troops on the ground. I’m not trying to control anyone else units, just giving the story a bit of color. (I’m going to wrap up the village battle so that we can all get onto the main attraction over at the castle.)

 

Down in the village, the battle rages on. In one area, a Sentinel Samurai is facing an eerily similar opponent, a Maelstrom Manipulated Samurai. They spin, performing the deadly dance of blades. Slash, counter, back step, lunge, slice, parry. They seem to be at impasse, skills evenly matched. But then, in a dirty trick, the Manipulated Samurai delivers a maelstrom blast from one of his hands. The Sentinel falls to the ground, sword arm wounded by the blast. His sword clatters away, and the Manipulated Samurai stands above him, preparing to strike the last blow.

 

Dink.

 

 The sound of a small energy bullet smacks against the side of the Manipulated Samurai’s helmet, succeeding in nothing more than tilting his helmet slightly to the side. He stops. Adjusts his helmet, and turns his head toward the source of the blast to see who was so rude as to interrupt.

 

WHAZZAAHHHhhhh....

 

The sound of a second shot, hitting the Manipulated Samurai smack in the face, and blowing its head clear onto the floor. Its body slumps to the floor, smashed.

 

The Sentinel slumps back to the floor, relived. The last thing he remembered before he passed out were some silvery white minifigures loading him into a stretcher.

 

Similar instances occur all over the village. Bucky’s forces methodically sweep over the area, helping the Nexus Force soldiers drive the Maelstrom out, while rescuing any hurt soldiers or villagers in the process. The Lances clear the skies so that a transport from the Meridian can land, which all wounded minifigs are loaded onto and evacuated to orbit.

-BuckyKat, The Kingmaker
