Leaving the Space Ranger behind, fast asleep by protected by two turrets courtesy of Ben, the group followed Freya's lead.  Thankfully the tunnels in their particular area were not as badly changed, and they soon arrived at the mouth of the cave system.

And near the mouth was a large, Maelstrom chasm.  On the world viewed from the sky, it looked like a deep, wide gash infected with Maelstrom energy, and it was exactly that.  And from it, Stromling Pirates, Banes, and all sorts of things emerged after being infected.

"We're supposed to drop the purifier into there?" asked Chase, the area dark because of a swarm of Banes overhead.  "Seems like a perfect disaster."

"It's the plan, stick to it Psycho," said Exceedra.  "Remember the plan..."

"Yea, smash them all and clear the area!" shoued Psycho as he pulled out his flare guns and ran out into the field, considerably wider and more packed than Ghost thought.

"You idiot, get back here!" shouted Freya as Chase started to smash the enemy.  "Brick, now what?"

"Plan B: follow his stupid, brazen lead," replied Exceedra as he brought his shield and sword to bear.  He jetpacked into the area and then came crashing down on a Stromling Admiral, immediately rolling and taking out three pirates in one swoop of his sword.

Freya and Ben were close behind, the third and fourth wheels of the group it seemed to them.  Elite started to dash from enemy to enemy, with three down but not fallen before she got to a fourth.  Cowboy in the mean while started to try to seal up the chasm somehow until help came, but his imagination and available supplies would only carry him so far.

Meanwhile, Psycho was jumping on and off of Maelstrom enemies, blasting others nearby while doing so.  One of his flares missed and caused the swarm of Banes to set off a chainreaction that started to destroy them all.  Everyone quickly shielded themselves using whatever was available to them to prevent being infected.

Then things started to go well.

Right now though, the Alphas--the squad name Psycho came up with and Ghost allowed--were focused intently on making it through.  They did not care if they made it back, only if this world was saved because of their actions.  Even Elite, the rookie, was smart enough to know this; her already steeled resolve considerably hardened.

As he and his allies moved against the tide of the enemy, Exceedra realized that the corruption was growing thicker here, the enemies tougher.  There must be something like the Spider Queen nearby, he realized.

Suddenly, beneath his feet, the ground gave way and he started sliding downward, eventually landing in an underground rapidly flowing river.

"Ghost!" shouted Ben over the comms.  "Are you..."

"Worse?!  Are you truly so easy?" asked the Hydra.

"Either my grammar is bad, and it isn't, or you don't understand; I'm bad news for you, more than I was for the Spider Queen!" shouted Exceedra as he jetpacked up and thrust his sword through one of the Hydra's heads.  However, the wound closed quickly.  Right...the legend of the hydra goes that it can be defeated only if one of its fangs is thrusted through its center head.  How the heck am I going to cut off one of its fangs?  Exceedra quickly tried again, jetpacking all over the place, but he got knocked away by one of the heads and into the cliff edge.

Letting out a sharp exhale of breath, Ghost fell quickly but used his jetpack before it was too late.  The Hydra in the meanwhile had submerged and hid beneath the waters in a place where it could not be hit, leaving the Banes and the pirate legends to deal with the lone minifigure.  Exceedra, however, was not as lonely as they all perceived him to be.  From the north, three gunships, including one with an overcharged purifier, arrived and started to clear the skies.

Meanwhile, Exceedra quickly dealt with the pirate legends, parrying their attacks and laying them to rest once more, though he did not know if that rest was a final one.

As he shielded himself from the falling Maelstrom matter caused by exploding Banes, the Hydra resurfaced right under one of the dropships, crushing it with one of its maws.  The other two quickly flew away and began opening fire.

That gave Ghost time to run up to the Hydra and try to again sever one of its fangs.  This time it worked, and he quickly thrust it onto his sword as the beast screamed in fury and began to try to blast him or crush him.

Dashing from place to place, Exceedra saw what set the center head apart from the rest; a third eye and what looked like a crown of coral.  Using his jetpack, he went right into the maw of the creature.

There, he thrust the fang up into the roof of the mouth.

"If someone's going to drop that purifier, they better do it now!" he shouted into his comms as he flew back.  One of the dropships quickly complied, opening its lower doors and dropping the purifier right onto the Hydra.

On contact, the Hydra breathed its last and fell into the waters, never to rise again.  Also, with the imagination in the area and without a commanding presence, the portal closed.  This time however, it shot straight up into the atmosphere of the planet chunk.  There, it opened a portal in the sky that started to draw everything in the immediate area and anything that was Maelstrom in origin away.  Knowing that he did not want to get trapped in whatever was on the other side, Exceedra grabbed onto the side of a dropship as it flew his way.  When he looked into the cockpit, he saw Chase piloting it, and he looked at his leader as if he did not recognize him.  Exceedra just motioned for him to keep flying.

As he looked toward where the Hydra had fallen, he could see all sorts of Maelstrom matter, be it liquid, energy, or infected beings, dragged up into the portal.  But also, in a roughly two mile radius, everything was being taken away.  So long and strong had the portal been open.  What was pulled away, even the ground and soil that left a wide pit with an open bottom, became known as Hydra's End.  Some even said that its screams could be heard long after.

"Dang man," said Psycho over the comms.  "I've done some pretty crazy things, but facing the Hydra pretty much alone and then grabbing onto this gunship?  That's something I would not have done."

"That makes me wonder and hope if you have just the slightest bit of common sense left in you," replied Exceedra, seeing Psycho bang his head on the control panel.
