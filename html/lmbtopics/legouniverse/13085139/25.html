"A brave statement, little girl, but a weightless one; you're not even a Plainslander," said the Honor Guard.

"Well then, you didn't notice my friend here?" replied Eliza, hoisting her missile launcher and aiming at him.

The ship's commander only turned his head a little bit, unsure of what she meant.  However, he got the meaning shortly afterward, right after she fired a missile and before it hit him.

The projectile impacted harshly, exploding in a shower of sparks and imagination and sending the Honor Guard into the Maelstrom core, destroying it and causing the power to fly away.

All around the group, the Maelstrom enemies backed away and ran off, knowing something bad was about to happen.  "Darn--I was going to enjoy the fight for once," said Arianne, putting her rifle away.

"There'll be more to enjoy," said Exceedra as he took a charge that he had saved from the outpost back on Crux Prime and threw it at one of the reactors while Eliza fired a cluster of missiles at the others.

The reactors started to explode immediately, but not before the Alphas could run out of there toward the portside of the ship.  "Chase, Jason, I hope you've got an exit for us!" shouted Exceedra.

"Sure do boss," said Chase, several explosions and other sounds in the background.  "The force sent the whole dang fleet here, and they're doing a good job; by the way, we've got a Talon inbound."

"Music to my ears Psycho!" said Freya as she threw a fan of knives toward some incoming enemies.

"How's Jason?" asked Eliza, firing a missile down the corridor at some pursuing Maelstrom minions.

"I'm fine, just feeling a little green...and yellow...on the inside," replied the Inventor.

"Better hurry up here though boss, 'cause that Talon's imminent!" said Chase.

"You try moving through these corridors, Chase!" shouted Ben.  "Then you've got a right to gripe!"

"Well he'd have it easier seeing as how he's got jetpacks and guns," added Arianne.

Song used: All or Nothing by audiomachine

 

The six Alphas sprinted through the corridors, somehow finding their way back to the train-filled hallway.  All inside of it however, there were massive explosions as the ship started to explode.

"Eliza, get a train going ASAP!" shouted Exceedra.

"No need for that, boss!" replied Chase on comms.

"In blazes...right," said Freya as she and some of the others wondered what was going on.  However they stopped when a Talon gunship swooped in through the massive hole up top.

"Better get in right quick," said Jason.  "There's not much time left!"

"Yea, like no one noticed that!" shouted Exceedra as the Talon approached.

"Wait," said Freya, timing the approach of the gunship second by second.  "Jump into its bay...now!"

Just as she said that, the Alphas jumped forward as best as they could, landing in the troop bay.

"Welcome aboard!" said Jason.  "Next stop, anywhere but here!"

"Then anywhere but here!!!" shouted Exceedra to him and the pilot.

With that, the Talon flew off, leaving a horde of angry Maelstrom minions brandishing their weapons or shooting at the fleeing gunship.  Exceedra got a moment to give them a casual salute.

"Feeling lucky much?" asked Arianne as the Talon exited the Maelstrom command ship.

"I'm feeling lucky everyday; you know that, right?" replied Exceedra as he hugged her.

They then, along with the Alphas, looked at the sky battle all around them.  Gunships and fighters were wizzing by, flak and rounds were exploding, and ships broadsided or rammed each other.

"Happy birthday to me!" said Chase, almost stepping between Arianne and Exceedra.  A quick over the shoulder glance by both of them stopped him dead in his tracks, as did a grab by Freya.

"I think happy birthday to all of us," said Freya as the Talon sped away from the rapidly deteriorating Maelstrom flagship, which exploded in a shower of purple and orange moments later.

"Halleluiah to living another day," said Eliza.

"Rounds are on me tonight!" shouted Mariah.

"Take another step, and another step..."--Steven Curtis Chapman

"Wha...wait, rounds are on you?" said Ben as he stepped back.  "Uh...did we just get infiltrated by a Maelstrom spy that doesn't know what to do, even to save its life?"

Mariah laughed as she holstered her bow and took off her hat, allowing brown, neck-length hair to roll down.  "No you didn't, it's just me being outside of combat."

"Something tells me we're going to have a lot of getting to know one another," said Exceedra.  "Anyway, now that the mess back there is back there, time for introductions!"

"Oh lord, now we've got eight Alphas...isn't that a bit crowded?" asked Jason.

"Crux's sake Jason!" said Arianne, lightly punching him.  "The more the merrier!"

"Right...well, I'm Jason.  You've already met Exceedra.  Arianne's his fiancee and the Space Ranger..."

"Woah hey now!" shouted Exceedra and Arianne.  "You hold in your horses a moment will ya?"

"Well, with the way you look at each other, the two of you might as well be engaged," added Freya.

"Good grief, now I've got my own squad to worry about," said Exceedra, breaking some laughter loose.

"Right...the Daredevil's Chase--the craziest one of the group as you well know," said Arianne, Chase looking at her from the corner of his eye.  The Space Ranger responded in kind.

"I'm Eliza, the one who keeps having all of the visions and the one responsible for keeping this squad alive and technically ready," said Eliza, the fifth introduction.

"My goodness; I feel secure and at home already," said Mariah after all of the dizzying introductions.  "And yes, my name is Mariah Dune and the nickname's Grappler...for the record."

Outside, toward Nexus Tower, an assembled host of minifigures awaited the Talon at the outside landing pad.  "Get ready Alphas...looks like we're getting a warm welcome," said Exceedra.
