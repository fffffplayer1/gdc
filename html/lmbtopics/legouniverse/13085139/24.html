‎12-21-2015 07:08 PM

Song used: All or Nothing by audiomachine

 

"The blazes are they up there for anyway?" asked Mariah.

"Comms to command, what else?" replied Eliza.

"Sorry, stupid question," said the Adventurer.

"Hey, we all ask them at one point or another," replied Exceedra as he deflected a salvo of Maelstrom rounds from Dark Mechs on a nearby gunship that swooped in.  Eliza and Arianne quickly dealt with it, sending it into an oncoming train.

Off in the distance, a station was approaching as was a wall; at the station were several Stromling Honor Guards, all waiting for the Alphas.  "Excuse me, y'all," said Exceedra as he walked up to the train's front and boosted away.

Upon landing, the dumbfounded Honor Guards were smashed or sent flying far away in the airless hallway.  Even in the vacuum however, their screams of fury and surprise could be just heard.

"Let's go, forthwith!" said Exceedra, taking the lead along with Ben and Freya down into a dark, powerless, airless corridor.  "FLIR on, and watch your corners.  Jason, Chase, sit-rep."

"An absolute mess up here boss; wish you were here!  Really!" replied Psycho over some dim fighting noises.

"We're going through the portal again, but a shield's raised around the ship; we should be fine," said Jason.

"We'll know soon enough won't we?  What about comms though?" asked Exceedra.

"None at the moment, but likely once we're through the portal," replied Jason.

"Good enough; keep fighting up there," finished the leader of the Alphas as he continued leading his group through the hostile hallways, still dark and airless even though the station door was closed.

Behind him, Ben, and Freya, the hallway suddenly lit up as Arianne, Eliza, and Mariah opened fire on several Maelstrom minions, visible even without FLIR because of their menacing yellow eyes.

Just as suddenly, the hallway regained power, air, and pressure, allowing the Alphas to ditch the FLIR vision and life support systems.  Arrows along the corridor pointed towards the back, near the engines.

"Come on, hoof it!" shouted Exceedra as he, Ben, and Freya charged forward, clashing with and overcoming a wave of Dark Stromlings, Spiderlings, and Ronin.  Behind them, Arianne, Eliza, and Mariah held back the other wave.

"At least the lights are on now so I can see where and what to hit!" shouted Freya as she cut down a Dark Ronin while throwing a fan of knives out, striking down several Dark Stromlings.  "Shame there's always more of them..."

"Grappler, can you see now, with your lack of a visor?" asked Arianne alongside Mariah.

"I could see even the dark, thanks...Blizzard?" replied Mariah as she shot an explosive bolt.

"Yes, and I'm just checking to make sure," finished Arianne as she used one round to take out three Dark Stromlings.  The round however ricocheted in the corridor, taking out more Maelstrom minons.

"I think my eyebrows just got shaved off over here!" shouted Ben as he used his magnet to smash several Dark Ronin.

"I think that round bounced off of my armor!" added Eliza, indicating a dent in her chest piece while firing a missile.

Suddenly the ship shook and roared for a few brief seconds, about the same time that it took for the Alphas to get through the portal the first time.  "Jason, Chase, are you okay?!" shouted Exceedra.

"We're good boss, the ship's shield protected us; maybe the portals aren't Maelstrom in origin," replied Jason.

"Good being relative!  Still taking a beating up here, but we're getting a signal from command," added Chase.

"Make sure you tell them about everything so they get the whole dang fleet up here, Imagination rounds only I think!" shouted Exceedra as he smashed several Dark Spiderlings with a single slam.

"Boss, how close do you think we are to the reactor?!" shouted Mariah just as the group arrived at a circular door infused with Maelstrom energy.  "Actually, never mind," she added as she turned around.

"How are we going to get through?" asked Arianne.

"Easy enough for me," said Exceedra.

Song used: All or Nothing by audiomachine

 

The Praetorian, covered by his accompanying five squadmates, put his shield on his back while concentrating and raising his sword.  The blade started to glow blue and swirl with imagination, dimming the presence of the Maelstrom.

Yelling while looking at the locked door, Exceedra plunged his sword into the plating of the ship.  For a brief moment nothing happened, but then veins of imagination grew toward the door before erupting and blasting it open.

"Mama mia!" shouted a Dark Stromling with scythes and blasters as he looked on.  "He's a demon, run for it!"

"Blazes man, there's only five of them!" shouted another, right as he was reminded how dangerous the five were.

With the horde contained at the rear, Eliza and Exceedra were the first to run into the reactor room, a massive spherical chamber with four power supplies forming a square inside.

Three paths stretched into the middle, snaking through the formed square and to a sphere of Maelstrom energy at the center.  Right by it was a massive Honor Guard Stromling.

"So you are the mongrels who have ruined my ship!" it shouted, brandishing two ball-and-chain maces, one per arm.  "Now you will pay the price for such an act!"

"Ruined your ship?" asked Exceedra.  "Hello!!!  This place was a wreck even before we got here."

"Yea, so how about you give us thanks, 'cause honestly the place looks better," added Arianne.

"The two of you...you're the ones who are responsible for smashing Kevin.  Exceedra and Arianne, if I remember the reports on you correctly," said the ship's commander.

Behind them, the path was blocked by a swarm of Maelstrom minions as were the other paths.  The only free space was on the wide circular platform in the center.

A chill went down everyone's spine, especially Arianne, Ben, and Exceedra, when the admiral referred to the two Praetorians by name.  "Kevin wanted you so badly," it said.

"Well, he didn't get us," said Ben.

"And neither will you!" finished Eliza.
