Chapter VIII: Old Roots Part 77

Options
  • Mark as New
  • Bookmark
  • Subscribe to RSS Feed
  • Highlight
  • Print
  • REPORT POST

Thursday

Song used: Velocitron by TSFH

 

Eliza pulled the reverse thrusters on the Crusher, essentially putting the brakes on.  The centripital force launched Freya, Jason, and Exceedra out and into the breach of the enemy ship.

Jason remained standing, immediately firing his minigun and swarm missiles at a swath of Maelstrom minions.  Exceedra and Freya rolled for a little bit before bring their swords up.

"Someone run some scans, tell us where to go!" Exceedra shouted as he used Chaosbane and Battleborne to engage two Honor Guard Stromlings.

"You are in an outer hallway, looks like a maintenance area," Ben replied.  "The hole continues for a while and opens up right in the middle of the ship."

"Well that makes things easier by miles," Freya replied, looking down the path of the MAC round.

"We still have to get to the center!  Come on!" Jason shouted, moving down the entrance path.

"I give the orders around here, remember?!" Exceedra shouted, having defeated the Honor Guard Stromlings.  He began to cut, slice, and spin his way through the Maelstrom hordes.

At the end of the path was a purple glow that was slowly intensifying, and a low-pitched hum was building up in tandem.  "Once we're there, I'll begin the detonation process," Jason said.

"And then we'll high-tail it out of here as if we had something to do yesterday!" Freya replied, cutting at several Stromlings with her katana and a shadow wave.  "And watch the fireworks."

Jason was at the front, his bullets, missiles, and rockets tearing and ripping the horde apart.  Close behind him were Exceedra and Freya, keeping flankers away from the bomb.

Please hurry!  The ship is at full stop and is opening a hatch at the bottom!  We're clearing the AOE just in case of the worst imaginable, and evacs are in motion, Maxwell texted.

"They should have evac'ed anyway!" Exceedra replied.  "And what do you think we're doing?!"

"Please...boss," Mariah said, her voice weak.  "It's my home...please save it...not another loss..."

"Take another step, and another step..."--Steven Curtis Chapman

Chapter VIII: Old Roots Part 78

Monday

Song used: Velocitron by TSFH

 

"We're working on it, Grappler," Exceedra replied, ending the lives of a few Stromlings.  "Just hang in there."

"What the heck else can we do?" Arianne said through the comms.  Exceedra felt a little cold suddenly.

The Praetorian snapped to just in time to duck under a Mech's arm, pushing aside the fact that his girlfriend was hurt.  I have to finish here, and then I can focus on what's happened, he reminds himself.

"We're nearly there!" Jason shouted as he still charged forward, weapons blazing.  Behind him, a Stromling de-cloaked and lashed at the bomb, stopped a little short only by a knife throw from Freya.

A few sparks flew from the bomb, but it was still intact and ready for use.  "Thanks for the save, Elite!" Jason shouted.

"Anytime!" the Shinobi replied, taking the left side behind the charging Inventor while Exceedra took the right side.

Soon, they were out in the middle of the ship, where thousands of Maelstrom minions swarmed.  Most of them still had to run down several flights of ramps and stairs, but still many were already on the floor.

"Glory to the bold, as they say!" Exceedra shouted as the three Alphas continued the charge forward, slashing or firing at the enemy.  Nothing could stand in the way for long, but something stopped Exceedra.

"You are a fool," the Darkitect's voice said, darker and colder than before.  "You've walked into a hive."

"Yes, we have," Exceedra retorted, still fighting the enemy alongside Freya and Jason.  "Willingly."

"And that's your problem...you don't know what you've gotten yourselves into," Typhonus said as two Spider Queens dropped from the ceiling, landing in front of a glass cylinder of Maelstrom energies.

"I don't have the time for this!" Exceedra shouted, running forward at both and unleashing imagination arcs at their legs.  The hits were direct, bringing the beasts low enough to where he could end them.

That was when their limbs grew back and anew.

"Aw come on...seriously?!" Jason shouted.

"Take another step, and another step..."--Steven Curtis Chapman
