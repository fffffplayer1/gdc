‎12-14-2015 10:35 AM

Song used: Slipstream by audiomachine

 

"Easy does it, Ari," said Exceedra.

The Stromlings milled about, securing their vehicles and making sure that the piles were too.  More than once one of the Alphas had to slink away or cloak themselves somehow.  Mariah was able to find a breathing mask.

"We're all good, take us away guardy!" said a Stromling right next to a cloaked Chase.

"Fine, fine!" shouted the guard--Honor Guard to be precise as Exceedra saw him.

"Take us away to where...and how did Stromlings learn to speak?" asked Eliza.

"Blazes if I know either of those two, but neither is any good," replied Exceedra.

Then, the island lurched before moving away farther into the Maelstrom mass.  Crux Prime slowly became fully visible, with all of its frayed edges and floating satelites, before in an instant the Maelstrom cloud shrouded it.

"Thanks for the mask...Elite, right?" Mariah said in the comms.

"Yes, and no problem; being a Paradox I'm fine in here," replied Freya.

"So what do you think's on the other side boss?" asked Chase.

"Again, blazes if I know, but it's nothing good," replied Exceedra.

Still, the island floated through the Maelstrom cloud in all of its chaotic glory, with fire and lightning storms and random asteroids flying by and sometimes impacting.

"If we get hit, I'm going to ask for some recompense Exc!" Ben whispered into the comms, his voice harsh and thin at the same time while he hid under a tank.

"Take a chill pill, at least we can do some sabotage once we get wherever these curs are going," replied Exceedra.

Suddenly, a massive humming and blast of wind was heard all around the Alphas as a portal came into view.

"Yup, Eliza and I were right...portal," said Arianne.

"Brick this, on me!" shouted Freya as she ran to the center.

Emerging from their hiding places and taking out the surprised Stromlings and the Honor Guard, the Alphas made it to the center of the island, where Freya created a shield of Maelstrom to protect them from the portal's influence.

"Take another step, and another step..."--Steven Curtis Chapman

The trip was arduous and taxing, especially on Freya, who had to be sustained by the imaginations and wills of her squadmates.  What felt like an eternity was only a few seconds in reality.

Still, the cloud lingered, but the Alphas knew they were not anywhere near Crux anymore; the storm was more intense, Maelstrom transports were flying about, and there were islands everywhere.

"Only five seconds through the portal...thanks for saving us, Freya," said Eliza as she looked around.  "Oh no..."

"You're welcome, and what?" asked Freya through ragged breaths and while she was on her knees.

"Guys...did anyone know the Maelstrom could build ships?" asked Ben with Eliza

"Well it stood to reason they could with all of this...why?" asked Exceedra in response.

Eliza was about to give her response when the island the Alphas were on broke through the cloud and into a massive opening, wherein everything was like a cavern and the cloud the cave walls.

In this opening, a whole fleet of Maelstrom ships, from fighters to transports to frigates to destroyers and finally to battleships rested, all apparently finished and ready to serve their sinister purpose.

Apparently the Alphas had also been noticed, as all of the ships powered up their weapons and every airborne enemy came falling at them.  The group instinctively walked backwards behind Exceedra.

"Running's not going to help us," he said, not drawing his shield and sword.  "Brick this, over the edge, now!!!"

As he started running everyone else just stood there, wondering what he was doing; Ben got it immediately though.

"The Crusher!  Everyone get to the edge and jump off; if you have a part of the original Crusher, use it!" he shouted.

With the enemies closing in rapidly, the Alphas ran to the right edge of the island and jumped off with Exceedra.

In unison he, Ben, Freya, Jason, and Chase pulled out their parts and constructed the gunship just as the island got blown to smithereens.  Then, they flew off.

"Take another step, and another step..."--Steven Curtis Chapman

Right on their tail were the dozens if not hundreds of fighters and other airborne enemies, and all around them was the destructive storm of fire and lightning.  To their front they had the massive fleet.

"Well, only one thing to do now!" shouted Eliza at the controls, Ben in the gunner's seat.  She piloted Crusher right at the looming fleet, which was firing its weapons at the Alphas.

"Are we really doing this?!" shouted Mariah.  "Isn't this a bit...insane?!"

"Insanity is the name of the game!  Man battle positions!" replied Exceedra.

Quickly, as Eliza piloted the Crusher into the midst of the enemies and evaded all hostile fire against all odds, the Alphas assumed positions all around the tested gunship.

"Ah-hoo-ah!" shouted Chase as he got on the dorsal turret up top and blasted a swarm of Dark Drones apart.

"Why did I agree to come here???" asked Mariah as she got on a side turret on the lower level and fired away.

"It could be worse; you could have joined us when Chase was really insane," said Freya as she got on the lower rear turret.  Meanwhile Exceedra and Jason manned the side turrets up top and Arianne got on the other lower side turret.

"Everyone hang on and blast like a bat out of the darkness!" shouted Eliza.

"Like we're not already?!" retorted Chase as he shot a missile down.

Meanwhile, Prophet had piloted the Crusher right between two destroyer-type ships that were charging their main guns.  Once they had, she boosted and pitched up, causing the ships to fire into each other.

"That's the way to do it!" shouted Ben as he used the chain gun and forward missiles to shoot apart a few gunships and fighters on an intercept course.  They were eerily similar to Nexus ship of the respective design.

"Leave it to the Maelstrom to make weak copies of our designs!" shouted Exceedra as he fired into an approaching fighter, knife-like in appearance.

"Hey, at least they're destrucible!" shouted Chase.

"Amen to that brother!" replied Jason.

"That hurts my feelings you know!" shouted Freya as she blasted a fighter apart and into a nearby frigate.

"Ben, you might want to be a little more careful with your words next time around!" shouted Arianne.

"Yea, or I'll come after you just like I'm going after these curs!" shouted Chase as she shot another missile.

"And then you'll force me to kick you below the waist!  Just focus on surviving right now!" finished Exceedra.

"Okay, just let me off the hook for Crux's sake guys!" shouted Ben as he shot two fighters down.

"After you and I resolve our dispute after this whole fight!" replied Freya, shooting a gunship apart.

Eliza continued piloting the Crusher through the battlespace, constantly pitching and rolling to narrowly miss a missile or a swarm of Dark Banes.  Meanwhile, the rest of the Alphas shot their best, almost never missing.

That was when all of their visors went dark and time slowed as Prophet once again lived up her nickname.  In her vision they saw a massive ship with sinister knife-like protrutions, a raised bridge, and a ramming head.

"My word...they're going to try to ram the tower!" shouted Exceedra as he snapped out first and blasted a gunship out of existence.  "Freya, get on the holotable, scan the area, and find that ship so we can board it!"

"Board it?!" shouted Ben.  "Are you crazy?!"

"Well someone's gotta do it!" replied Exceedra.

Still the firing continued from and to the Crusher while Freya scanned the wide area.

"Dead center of the whole fleet, a few miles out from here!" she shouted a second later.

"Then that's where we're going," said Eliza.

"Oh Crux," said Mariah.

"I'm not sure the destruction of this ship will be enough to destroy the rest of the fleet out there anymore!" shouted Exceedra as he snapped out of the vision first and cut down a jumping Spiderling.

"I have an idea about that, actually," replied Chase as he jetpacked up to the roof, only to fall back quickly as Banes, Drones, and Dragons filled into the hallway quickly.  "Bad idea actually!!!"

"Very bad idea too!" replied Mariah as she shot an explosive bolt into the cloud of enemies, destroying a good deal of them.  "Unless you're harder and more armored than you look!"

Chase only cracked a grin inside his helmet and looked at his squadmates; Freya and Exceedra knew immediately what he was going to do.  "I'll see you outside everybody!" he shouted.

Just then, he turned his armor's hard-shield ability on and jetpacked out again, this time getting past the swarms of enemies and securing himself outside.  "He's insane," said Mariah.

"It's his middle name actually, and besides; he's made it this long," said Freya.

"This is not withstanding," said Jason as he used a nearby explosion to launch himself toward the hole and past all of the enemies.  "I'll help him out on top, you guys hit that reactor!"

"And for the record, their nicknames are Psycho and CrazyDoc, respectively," added Arianne.

"I'm still wondering how I got involved in this group, and with such crazies," said Eliza as she shot a missile toward a platform of Dark Mechs.  "And if you're like me, you always will!"

"You can relax though, Mariah!" added Exceedra as he cut down several Dark Stromlings with guns and twin blades.  "They're the only crazy ones in this group; the rest of us are calculatively sane."

"What is that supposed to even mean, Exc?!" shouted Arianne as she sniped a rail off, taking a train with it.

"It means that the rest of us look insane, but we're actually very sane," said Ben.  "Did I hit it on the bud?"

"A hundred percent!" replied Exceedra.
