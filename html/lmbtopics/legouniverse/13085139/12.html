Eliza seemed petrified, still looking on but doing nothing as though there were nothing to do from the start.  It was Exceedra who showed her otherwise.  Jetpacking right into the circle of Ronin around the two Venture Leagues, Ghost dashed through two, sending their remains into other Maelstrom Ronin.  Close behind him were the other Alphas, though Eliza still stood at the fringe of the fray.

It was now that Exceedra was getting the idea that she had seen alot of bad things in her visions, and that she had the thought of nothing could be done to alter the future.

"Prophet!" he shouted at her, raising his sword and cutting down on a Ronin behind him.

Galvanized to action, Eliza ran into the fray, firing a rocket from her launcher.  This time however, it separated into several smaller ones that took out several Ronin--without taking out her squadmates.

"Now that is something!" said Chase as he shot a Ronin at closer range than he should have.

Meanwhile, the two minifigures got up and were resuming to fight alongside the Alphas.  However, during a brief lull in the fight, Exceedra shouted at the two to get their cans back to the Great Tree.

Not willing to argue with a legend, the two Venture Leagues hauled it back to the place from where they came, leaving the Alphas to deal with the Ronin and now Horsemen and Banes.

As Eliza blasted at a Ronin with her pistol, she had another one of her visions, though this time it was about to happen.  She could see Exceedra about to be hacked down by a Ronin that materialized behind him.

Aiming down the sights of her pistol, Prophet blasted the Ronin just as it emerged from behind Ghost, who turned around a moment late.  He nodded his thanks before bashing another Ronin into the sky and into a Bane.

Meanwhile, Jason was running along the cliff walls with Chase, blasting the enemy from above.  However, they were easy targets for the Banes, which Ben took care of by setting up an anti-air turret.

"Take another step, and another step..."--Steven Curtis Chapman

The den had been blown open to be a cove and to reveal the borders of the Maelstrom itself almost touching Forbidden Valley.  There were also three portals open, each guarded by one of the three dragons, who were now infected.

The floor had also been redone; in the cove, there were islands on which to cross from place to place.  The three largest islands were the ones with the dragons and portals.  Strangely, there were gongs behind them.

"We need to split up," said Exceedra, testing his jetpack and drawing his shield and sword again.  "Freya and I will take the center.  Ben and Eliza, take the left.  Chase and Jason, take the right.  Let's get it done."

Leading the way forward, Ghost took the Alphas to a central island, where they were greeted by Maelstrom Ronin and Ninjas.  Undaunted, the squad of six easily dispatched the threats and then split up to take on their dragons.

Jumping up onto their island, Exceedra and Freya were nearly blasted to oblivion by the dragon, who was ready for them.  From the fireball came Maelstrom Ronin.

"How do you stop that which is not supposed to be alive?" asked Elite to her leader.

"Put them back in the ground, six feet under," replied Ghost with a grin.

Meanwhile, the other two groups were facing similar resistance; thus far the dragons were content not to be involved.  Instead, they protected their portals like a mother bird protects her eggs.
