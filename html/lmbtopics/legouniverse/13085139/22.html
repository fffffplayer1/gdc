A few seconds later, the hailstorm stopped, though she and Exceedra were buried beneath the debris.  The leader of the Alphas resolved it by using his jetpack to boost through a large chunk of rock.

The two landed near their ATV, which had escaped unscathed yet again.  "Do you do that sort of thing all the time?" she asked, brushing herself off and checking to make sure everything was fine.

"Yea, of course I do," replied Exceedra, coughing and taking off his helmet, which was damaged.  "What about you?  And why are you so quiet compared to several other minifigures on the field?"

"I do mainly recon and some covert ops, and I'm quiet 'cause, unless there's something to be said, I don't believe anything should be said," Mariah replied, checking her crossbow and wire.

"That last part?  You and me both...for the most part," Exceedra finished, checking his shield and sword.  Just then, his helmet's comms crackled and barely audible speech could be heard.

"Alpha Actual, please respond for Crux's sake!" shouted Arianne, her voice rising and cracking.

"This is Ghost, I'm A-okay and my tower's down," replied Exceedra.  The other Alphas sighed and exclaimed a bit.

"Thank Mythranious," said Ben.  "I'm betting you know our tower and the other are both out?"

"Yup, and I wouldn't have if it weren't for a new friend I made," replied Exceedra.  "So, are we set to move on the wall?"

"You bet boss," replied Chase.  "My group and Ben's are already near the wall."

"Your group?" asked Arianne.  "I'm sorry, I thought that as Praetorian I was the leader.  Don't make me kick you..."

"Okay!  Lock it down you two; I'd really hate to reprimand you both when I get over there," shouted Exceedra.  "Okay, actually Chase, I'd be just fine throwing you under the bus."

"Oh for Crux's sake, you're a real comfort boss," replied Chase as the other Alphas laughed.

"Now that was righteous retribution on you, Chase!" replied Freya.

"Seriously, lock it down now; I'm en route," said Exceedra.

"You mean we," replied Mariah

"I'll take a look at the hoverboard too please," said Jason.

"The good ol' times are about all the time for you it seems," said Freya.

Exceedra simply drove off the plateau's edge past the group.

"And he's my boyfriend..." said Arianne as she looked on from her overlook.

"Well, nothing more to it!" said Eliza on the hoverplatform.  "Let's go!"

On the shattered ground between the split plateau and the looming wall, Exceedra was driving at a fast rate, using the thruster packs to get over the rifts in the small islands of rock.

All around, he could see the frayed edges of Crux Prime and the Maelstrom's fringes just waiting for an excuse to lap over the rocks.  For some reason, it had not found one yet.

However, the Maelstrom defenders on the walls now had a reason to try to shoot Exceedra.  Suddenly, many windows on the wall were opened, and from them protruded guns capable of being heavy cannons or machine guns.

"Oh locusts," said Exceedra as he saw it all.  "Guys, where's my blasted covering fire?!"

"Don't worry, you're there to get the enemies that try to get me!  Ben and Jason are there to..." Exceedra started.

"You know, I'm feeling a bit green," said Chase.  "But I'll make up for it later, you hear me?!"

"Can you please just all focus on your job for Crux's sake?!" shouted Exceedra as he used the thrusters to evade a missile coming at him.

The other Alphas quickly obeyed, Eliza moving the hoverplatform along faster, Mariah and Arianne shooting faster, and Jason and Ben laughing

Next to Exceedra's head, a Maelstrom bullet impacted into the pillar.  The Praetorian quickly veered around and planted his shield while he continued to wire the explosives.

"It's a little hard to do when you are taking a long time to decorate and the house owners want you out!" he shouted, his shield deflecting the projectiles back at the enemy.

"I'm already running, but how would I have had too much company?" he asked, running through the hall and finding a door out.  Just then, several Honor Guard Stromlings poured into the hall.  "Never mind, just found out," he said.

Taking a grenade from his belt for a situation just like this one, he left it at the door and ran away, slicing and bashing through more Stromling Gunners.  When the grenade went off, it collapsed the door and took a few Honor Guards out.

"Thanks for the warning, and are you guys still tracking me?" he asked, finding a window opened and not sealed shut.

"Yup, we are," replied Jason and Ben.  "Stand back from the window, we're gonna try something...unique," said Ben.

"Oh locusts," said Exceedra as he ran back and braced his shield.  A moment later, the two tanks fired on the window a the same time, blasting a large hole through it.

The Praetorian quickly ran out through the massive hole which was already mending itself.  Behind him, a few courageous gunners opened fire but missed.

In his visor's hud, the timer on the charges read a few seconds from detonation.  To his left, he saw Eliza, Chase, and Freya get out and start running.

On the plateau, the other four were still firing as their squadmates ran away from what was going to be a large explosion--and large it was.

A few seconds later, as the four hid in the rifts, the explosives went off.
