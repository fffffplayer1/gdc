‎12-01-2015 04:59 PM

Song used: Outpost by TSFH

 

Exceedra charged quickly, boosting right into a group of Dark Mechs and destroying them.  He then engaged the other enemies, the other Alphas and minifigures providing support from the outpost.

"Anything else going on?!" he asked as he blocked and attacked the horde, using whatever he could with safety.

"Nope, other than the worms trying to surprise us!" shouted Eliza, firing a missile at a dragon over Exceedra's head.

"Thank goodness for visions," said Arianne, shooting two Dark Apes with the sniper rifle.  "I have a signal from an IIR platform...where should I target for effect?"

"Anywhere but on me!" replied Exceedra.  As he spun around, cutting several enemies down, he saw Arianne still aiming her rifle.  Then he saw the laser moving.

"Does that rifle have a built-in designator???" he asked just as a round hit to his left, sending debris and Maelstrom minions flying in varying directions.

"I rest my case!" Exceedra replied, his ears ringing but the rest of his body still working all right.  That was when he got tackled by a Spiderling and gazed into its yellow eyes.

That was when Freya and Chase got into the fray, getting the enemies away from him.  "Are you okay boss?!" asked Elite as she shadow phased to Exceedra.

"I'm fine," replied the squad leader as Chase hovered overhead, shouting as usual and going right into the hotspots, guns and grenades sounding off.

The others still supporting them from behind, Freya and Exceedra charged right into the thick of the fray again, Arianne still coordinating IIR strikes close to them.

"For the Nexus Force!" he shouted, the other minifigures repeating the shout right after him.

The fighting continued for a few more minutes, with no one on the field but Exceedra, Freya, and Chase.  At the end, the wave of enemies had been bested.

"Where are you all going?!" shouted Chase as few enemies retreated.  Several more could not and were eliminated.

 

"Well, this seems familiar," said Ben.

"Too familiar," replied Eliza.

"Take another step, and another step..."--Steven Curtis Chapman

As Exceedra, Chase, and Freya stood on the fields, they recalled how similar the Second Battle of Nimbus station was.  A chill ran through all of the Alpha's bones as they thought of the fact.

"Familiar or not, we made it through," said Exceedra, boosting into the outpost with Chase and Freya not far behind.

"Yes...but I get the feeling we're marked somehow," replied Arianne, jumping from the tower and landing with assistance from her boot boosters.  "And if this type of fight is to be commonplace..."

"It won't be," said Exceedra, putting his hands on his soulmate's shoulders.

In the bunker, Ben shouted in frustration, sending a few choice words rolling out of the bunker.  "Dang...I thought I was the bad mouth," said Chase, walking forward with the other Alphas.

"And Eliza, what happened to you helping him out?" asked Jason.

"Let's just say he pulled a guy and said 'I can handle it!'" replied Eliza.

"Ben doing something like that?" asked Arianne, putting her hands to her chest.  "I'm shocked!"

Only she, Eliza, and Exceedra laughed.  "Well, he did have a thick head sometimes," added Ghost.

Inside, they found Ben futzing with the wiring and controls, not having any success.  "Don't worry, I got this!" shouted the Engineer.  He shortly afterwards shoute some more choice words.

"Let the woman give the machine her touch," said Eliza, walking forward and pushing Ben out of the way.  As he walked away, hands on his head, she went to work using her direct neural interface.

Within seconds, she had everything repaired and working with the portal.  "Command, this is Alpha Actual; portal on our side's green.  Copy?" said Exceedra.

"Solid copy," said Exeter.  "I thought we lost you.  We're sending some Engineers through first."

The Engineers from the Assembly quickly arrived and ran outside, repairing and adding onto the outpost.

"We've got everything from here on out Praetorian," said Exeter, soon walking through the portal himself.  "And you have a mission to finish--so get it done."

"Take another step, and another step..."--Steven Curtis Chapman

"So what's the play boss?" asked Chase, checking his boosters and pistols.  "We gonna go after the wall or what?"

"Yes, but not first off," replied Exceedra, leaning on the table in the command bunker.  Exeter was nearby, watching as more Nexus Force soldiers from the four factions poured through the portal.

"First off, we've got to hit the flak towers; destroying the wall means nothing if this place isn't secured.  It also won't help our flyboys one bit if we leave those towers up," continued Exceedra.

"So what?  You suggest that we break up into groups and hit the towers?" asked Eliza.

"It won't be the first time," replied Ben, standing beside Prophet and whispering in her ear.

"So we hit the towers, leave search and rescue for others, and hit the wall and find out what's behind it?" asked Jason.

"Exactly," replied Exceedra, pulling the map up.  "We've got one near the wall on a mountain peak, another that's floating in mid-air east of us, and another near the norther end of...what is it, Caldera Mar?"

"The extension of it that comes up from under the ground, yes," replied Freya.  "Tricky place to assault."

"At least no one is set to assault Caldera Mar itself, only a tower that is on a mountain near it," said Arianne.

"Correct on that account.  Now, Arianne, Chase, and Freya will assault the northern tower.  Jason, Eliza, Ben, you'll hit the southeastern one.  I'll take the one due east," said Exceedra.

"So then why are you still standing around, Alphas?" said Duke, still getting the command center set properly and admonishing the Space Ranger lieutenant.  "Get on it!"

 

Song used: Kingbreaker by audiomachine

 

The Alphas ran outside, finding that small hover tanks had been assembled as well as other vehicles and new additions to the outpost.  The Engineers had been busy.

Blizzard, Psycho, and Elite ran to one while CrazyDoc, Prophet, and Cowboy got in another.  Exceedra simply got on an ATV, getting used to the controls and some guns on the front.

"Let's ride!" he shouted.

Exceedra got out of the outpost first, riding onto a drawbridge and then out to the east.  Behind him, the other two groups of Alphas rolled out slowly, the tanks ready for action.

"Good luck to all," said Jason.  "And bad luck to the ugly Maelstrom about to have some metal surgery!"

"All right, secure all comms," said Exceedra, scanning the debris-strewn hills, flatlands, mountains, and sky for any danger.  Off in the distance was a floating island with fortifications on it.

The Alphas obeyed, keeping comm chatter to a minimum.  Who knows if the Maelstrom can intercept our messages, and what dangers can be hidden by noise? wondered Exceedra.

Soon, he came to a chasm in the middle of his way with no way across.  Cranking the handles, he turned around to the left and drove on a perilously narrow pathway near the chasm.

"Blizzard calling in; encountering heavy resistance en route to the target!" shouted Arianne over comms.

"Prophet here, and we've got the same issue; they really don't want us near the towers!" said Eliza.

"Hey boss, how's it with you?!" shouted Chase, the main gun of the tank firing and being heard even off of comms.

"Actually I've got nothing here, other than doing some daredevil driving," said Exceedra.  "Typical luck it seems."

Just then, one of Crux Prime's random lightning and ash storms started up.  The comms scrambled and all speech became garbled.  "Blast it!" shouted Ghost, nearing a tunnel at the end of the path.

With nowhere left to go, the Praetorian drove in, the path illuminated by the ATV's headlights and more of the mysterious orange crystals.  They should be a-okay though, he reassured himself.

Using GPS built into his helmet's custom visor, Exceedra found his way out of the tunnels and onto the other side of the chasm.  That was when he heard the gunfire and explosions.

Not long after, he rounded the corner of a mountain and saw a crashed dropship.

Around it were several Maelstrom minions, all moving forward

Okay, in the next part we're going to get introduced to the next Venture League person.  Provided no one else gives me a bio, here's who'll be introduced:

Name: Mariah Dune (Grappler)

Backstory: Mariah was born on a desert, canyon-riddled world known as Dunesland, her local area known as Brick Mesa.  Even before the Maelstrom invaded, life was tough for her and her family, who were among the first to colonize the Crux remnant.  The family had a monopoly on oil and geothermic energy in the area and were often critizied by the left-leaning inhabitants of the area.  As a result their house often had eggs thrown on it and some irrate visitors and phone calls.  Furthermore, no private tutors were willing to teach her.  As a result, she had to go offworld every day to a school in Nexus City.  Even there however, she was bullied; "Little Miss Overlord" she was called as she was to inhert the family business when her father and mother passed away.

She did not bend under the pressure however, but instead grew taller and straighter.  At an early age she was said to posses a wit sharp as a sword and a strength that would make a guy cry.  Overall, she overcame the stigma assigned to her and made a name for herself, eventually becoming an advocate for more jobs for the unfortunate.  Everything changed when the Maelstrom arrived however; now the young woman is fighting in the Venture League, still keeping the nickname of Grappler because of the way she dealt with bullies.

Gear: She is a Tier 3 Adventurer with a grapple hook and wire instead of the whip.

Skills: She can traverse hard terrain easily and is expert at recon and in the matter of crossbows and whips.

Description: Mariah has a tan complexion, slightly taller than normal, hale, short brown hair, and yellow eyes.  Her voice is also accented.

Characteristics: She is a-political, preferring the gray areas most people do not see often.  She is also quiet and reserved on the field but friendly and sarcastic on shore leave.

Song used: Kingbreaker by audiomachine

 

Exceedra floored it, driving right toward two Dark Apes and firing twin chain guns on the ATV.  The rounds found their mark and thensome, smashing more enemies behind the apes.

"Ho!  I can get used to that!" shouted Ghost as he kept driving and shooting at the enemies trying to get to the dropship, inside which there was a person shooting crossbow bolts.

Eventually however, he got too close; a Dark Spiderling knocked the ATV over, sending Exceedra flying off.  The Praetorian quickly rebounded however, boosting onto it and smashing it while sending its legs into other enemies.

Ghost then started boosting forward into enemies, smashing them with either the brute force of a shield bash or with the elegance of sword play.  All the while, someone was still shooting from inside the dropship, not saying anything.

Within a matter of seconds, the whole area had been cleared.  Exceedra wiped his sword and shield clean using a rock with some fabric on it before walking to the gunship.

From the ground behind him however burst another of the Maelstrom Worms.  It looked at the lone minifigure for a moment before retching up several Dark Stromlings and Spiderlings.

"Now there's gross!" shouted Exceedra, shielding himself from a lightning bolt and redirecting it at the worm.  It smashed instantly, leaving him to contend with the other enemies.

Within a matter of seconds, he had dealt with that threat and again started walking toward the gunship.  Again he was stopped when a terrible shriek pierced his ears.

Exceedra turned around and looked up, seeing a cloud of Dark Banes descending on him.  That was when whoever was in the gunship saved him by shooting an explosive bolt into the approaching swarm.

Again however, another worm broke through the ground, this time in the gunship's blind spot.  Ghost boosted forward at the head of the beast, grabbing onto its jaws before planting his sword in one of its eyes.

Now, who the blazes is inside? he wondered.

Introducing Mariah Dune!

That was when he saw a rocket flying off course from out of nowhere toward the gunship.  Exceedra boosted over to a blocked door and, shield forward, bashed the door apart and from inside, someone limped over.

"Come on, come on!" he shouted, eventually going in and grabbing the person before jetpacking away.  Just as he did, a stray missile that was supposed to hit the floating tower came and destroyed the gunship.

Behind a piece of debris that was sturdy enough to serve as cover, Exceedra now tended to the sole survivor of the gunship crash.  With time on his hands, he now observed the minifigure in greater detail.

"My...aren't you a lucky one?" he asked a female Tier 3 Adventurer from the Venture League, though he got no response as she was unconcious.  "Come on, this is still a battlefield," he continued, giving her a stimpack.

Immediately her eyes opened and she sat up.  "What the...where am I and who are you?" she asked, shrugging and slapping his hands off of her before crawling backward, her crossbow aimed at him.

"Easy ma'm!" shouted Exceedra, raising his shield.  "I'm Praetorian Ghost, so please lower the crossbow."

She did so, still looking at him and slowly lowering her crossbow arm.  "The Ghost?" she asked.

"One and the same," he replied, putting down his shield and taking off his helmet.  "Now if you'll let me tend to your wounds, I'll send you on your way back to base while I go take care of one of the flak towers."

"I'm supposed to take care of the one due east of here," she replied, her country accent now becoming more noticeable.  "I don't suppose you're taking volunteers?" she continued, getting her crossbow.

"Fine by me I suppose," Exceedra replied.  "The real name's Exceedra Norell by the way."

"Thanks for coming my way, living legend.  My name's Mariah Dune, aka Grappler, age 27," she said.

"I didn't ask for all of that, but nice to meet you."

"So...can you please patch me up before we go?"

Exceedra quickly gave her a draught of a potion he carried, her wounds mending swiftly and body functions returning to normal.  "Now that that's done, let's go; the Maelstrom won't give us an invitation," he said, helping her up.

The two quickly ran over to the ATV, somehow still intact, flipped it over and gunned it to the location of the floating tower.  Once there, they hid their vehicle behind a rock and looked up at the floating fortress.

"Not gonna be easy," said Exceedra, using his visor to scan the area.  "My jetpack can only carry my weight that far, and just barely at that.  At least there isn't a wall...just some guns and a command center."

"Not a problem, I've got my way up," said Mariah, bringing out a grappling wire with a hook at the end.  She attached it to a special bolt in her crossbow and fired it, the hook latching onto the edge of the floating rock.

"I'll see you up there," replied Exceedra, noting her yellow eyes seemed to turn gold and bright.  He also finally noted she was slightly taller than normal, had tanned skin, and a hearty complexion.

"You will, and stop gawking please," Mariah said, holding onto her crossbow as the wire retracted and brought her on up.  Exceedra followed close behind, quickly overtaking her and waving on the way up.

Once he landed on the ledge, he quickly threw a Stromling on watch duty over the edge, but not so fast as to not see its eyes go wide.  Mariah pulled up right after, quickly putting the grappling equipment away.

Right next to them, a Dark Mech's round impacted.  Several more Maelstrom energy projectiles followed from Spiderlings, Dark Stromling Shooters, and Dark Drones.  "Keep up!" shouted Exceedra.

"I won't have to," Mariah cooly replied, aiming down the sights of her crossbow and taking out three Spiderlings with one shot.  Exceedra boosted into the front of the fray, smashing several Stromlings.

"Get the guns off!" he shouted.

He had no response other than her nod.

Mariah started running along, shooting enemies like clockwork while she single-handedly got her grappling wire out and used it to swing around the throng of enemies encamped on the island.  Nothing could reach or hit her.

On the ground, Exceedra was bashing his shield into enemies in between uses of blocking and using his sword to send them back to their unholy realm.  Battlebourne seemed to gleam a silvery-blue hue against the chaos.

"Come on now!" shouted Ghost, his foes backing away from him.  "I'm sure it won't be that bad to fail!"

It never took much for the Maelstrom to be goaded into action, and the enemy once again charged at him.

Off to his south, as he looked on while Mariah got her wire onto one of the dome-shaped command center's antenna, he saw a large explosion where the other gun station should have been.

As he turned around again, this time to his northwest, he saw another explosion; the other groups of Alphas have done their jobs.  Now to do mine, he thought, cutting several Dark Stromlings into oblivion.

"You've got charges or something, right?!" shouted Exceedra to Mariah, who was running along the dome with her wire still anchored to the top.

"Yes I've got charges!" she replied, shooting an explosive bolt into a trio of Dark Spiderlings.  "I just got to plant them in the island itself!"

"In the island?!  Should I have taken a look at your head or something?!"

"I'm just fine, you're the one who's not adventureous enough!"

Exceedra only grimmaced in mock pain at the words while he slid out of the way of a Dark Spiderling's leg.  He then bashed it into a Dark Ape, smashing both.

"Well, get a move on it!  The other two bases are already cleared and my squad is waiting for me at the wall!" he again shouted to her, still fighting.

Meanwhile, a group of Dark Banes started closing in on Mariah while she was reloading.  As she turned around and looked on, Exceedra sent a crescent of imagination toward the Banes, destroying them.

"Take another step, and another step..."--Steven Curtis Chapman

At the same time, Mariah dropped into a hole in the command center, vanishing from sight.  Exceedra could only hope she knew what she was doing and keep on fighting, his foes thinning out but still trying to take him out.

That was when a small Maelstrom meteor struck the island and as another Nexus missile went off course.  What the brick is causing the missiles to miss? Exceedra wondered as he looked at the small impact crater.

From it came four of the strange Stromlings, the Honor Guard Stromlings.  Another meteor soon followed, with more landing.  He knew that while his skill was great, he could not hold out against the numbers.

At the same instant, Mariah exited the command center through the same hole that she used to get in.  One look at Exceedra was enough for her to know to get off quickly.  She threw her wire, wrapping it around him.

Just as the Honor Guards started to charge, the Praetorian turned tail and boosted off of the island, the Adventurer holding on for dear life as she sailed just above the dangerous Stromlings who were trying to get their enemies.

Just as they got off, Exceedra put on the air brakes to catch her in his arms.  He then continued falling as Mariah brought in her wire.  Above, a swarm of Dark Drones launched, heading for them with guns blazing.

"Clench up, this landing's gonna be hard!" shouted Exceedra, using his armor's shock systems to dispurse his landing energy into the ground, creating several cracks and a small crater.

"And please bring your shield out!  Charges are set to explode..."

Now she was about to say when the island exploded in a roar unlike any sound both heard before.

A massive purple and orange fireball sent parts of the island and indeed parts of the enemy flying in several directions.  Exceedra brought his shield up, protecting him and her from a barrage of debris.

Rocks, metal, and whatever else smashed against his shield while he and Mariah remained safe.
