OOC: There are two types of energy, potential energy (that is stored in some substance) and kenetic energy (energy in motion usually manifesting itself as flames or heat.) Given the right amount of energy (heat) you can release potential energy from whatever substance it is stored in and turn it into kenetic energy. For example, wood. Wood contains large amounts of potential energy, all you have to do to release that energy (in the form of heat and flames) is to somehow strip away the matter containing the energy (our prefered method is to burn the wood). What Charix did, was formulate a chemical reaction which would react with the molecules making up your body by breaking the substance down and releasing the energy in the form of heat and flames. Of course, to prevent the reaction from taking place in his own body Charix created the chemical reaction with a few extra chemicals in order to stunt the reaction, he simply removed these extra chemicals while in the process of sending the reaction to you. There was also a precuationary measure in place should the attack go wrong. Once inside Tarxann's body the reaction began working, slowly at first, but gradually as more energy was released it grew stronger and consumed more matter.
