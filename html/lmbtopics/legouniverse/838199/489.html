Dwalin22k wrote:
ok thanks! could someone give me a bio example?

Here's the things you should fill out:

 

Name: Bionicle names aren't like English ones, here's some examples (so you know how wierd they are): Jaller, Takua, Lewa, Pohatu, Teridax, Reidak

 

Species: optional, I think half the characters in this RP have made-up species, you can also be a mutant or hybrid. Here's some of the species:

Toa: Pretty much the "hero" species" they need their mask and have their own HQ, but the order accepts them (in fact I think the canon leader is a Toa),

Makuta: The "evil" species, they are one of the first species and created most of the Bionicle animals (which are called Rahi). They are actually just a gas in armour, and they all have the element of shadow. They are very unlikely to join the order, or any other HQ except their own for that matter.

Skakdi: They lack elemental powers (I could be wrong) but they still have powers. Skakdi are generally vicious and violent, even to each other, and are good fighters. They have a large "grimace" that shows alot of their teeth, and a visible spine.

Matoran: matoran are simple villagers that have no powers and can't wear masks of power. They are also about half the size of most other species.

Remember, you can always make up a race.

 

Mask: Most species can wear masks, and atleast one needs a mask to keep their power. Most masks have a power, such as: Night vision, underwater breathing, mind control, levitation, shapeshifting

 

Element: optional, the most common elements are: Fire, water, ice, air, rock, earth

 

Weapons: Most medieval weapons exist, as well as some more futuristic weapons such as disc launchers (fires discs at the enemy, I think some discs also explode) and cordak launchers (mini-missile launchers)

 

Powers: You can have pretty much anything here, as long as its not OP

 

Sorry if I missed anything, it was late when I wrote this.
