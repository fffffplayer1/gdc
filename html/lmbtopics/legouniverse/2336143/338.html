ALRIGHT, let me get this straight.
The guys at the beginning of the Cinematic Trailer ARE MYTHRANS.
I would be fine with Bane being the "Chosen One" or whatever as long as it doesn't ruin the chosen storyline and he isn't OP... but neither are true. He claims he can destroy any ship, blow up anything, conquer any HQ, and fight far more experienced Roleplayers and beat them.
Well there's something y'all Rebellioneers () need to know about this Roleplay. You aren't a supeyou begin. Not when you are a newbie and not for a while later.
You have to gain your power, and let fair Roleplayers agree with everything OoC.
I'm also talking to all newbies in the Nexus Force, Maelstrom, and Grand Council.
Wanna know why Høvawart was so powerful? He was VOTED the most powerful, and has been here longer than almost all of us. Wisholen also EARNED his honer. He had a "Will you please help me, I'm new to Roleplay" topic. Alpha_Cheesewheel also started a newbie, but look at him now! Same with crazyguy, Fire_Type, bjcoffins, and even Lindel and michealyoda!
I used to distaste almost all newbies for that. Making an HQ the moment they join and obliterating everything.
But I was wrong to do that. Because I know that everyone, even the oldest of us, needs to change.
We can't be OP, and we can't be instant superpowers. I know the Rebellion has been here for... well... about 2 months, but many have been here 3 YEARS! And the new people call them OP when they've EARNED everything! Don't you think a 300 page HQ ought to be allowed to have a bigger fleet than a 30 page HQ? More members, a more powerful ruler?
Think about it...

Well, about the being able to win against anything: I can. Anyone can, assuming that they are willing to try and to make a decent character. Besides, I never said I could single-handedly destory a star-ship, at least not without severe side effects to myself. Also, i agree: bigger HQs CAN have a bigger fleet. That isn't the issue. Also, finally, I am not any more powerful than before I added that part of my storyline; that was strictly for backstory and saying, in the most public and attention-grabbing way possible, that I will not lose. And how does having some long-dead Mythrans speak from the grave through my voice and claim that some evnts occured long ago ruin the storyline? If I can, I will see if I could alleviate that in the next bio I write.
-Bane
