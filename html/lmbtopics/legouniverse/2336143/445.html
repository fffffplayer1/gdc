JAMMERS ON PEOPLE WITH PERMITS ONLY! VOID EMPIRE HIGH-RANKING OFFICIALS ONLY! NO IC OR OOC READING! READERS THAT HAVE NOT BEEN ALLOWED WILL BE REPORTED! THIS IS TOP SECRET OF THE VI9 SPECIAL OPERATIONS DEPARTMENT, FILE RATED X. DO NOT READ ANY FURTHER IF YOU DON’T HAVE A PERMIT.










Plan to eliminate the Nexus Force
Top secret of VI9
Recorder: Max Evons, head of VI9 and supreme chief of VEID








To the person reading this:

The NF has been a threat to the safety of the Void very much and before I might be forced to give my life over or go away from LURP I will formulate the master plan to eliminate the NF.
1: eliminate the Rangers.
2: destroy ARA once in for all.
3: destroy the GNR topic. Then the NF will start to run out of topics.
4: Destroy the Faction HQs.
5: destroy any other opposing HQs.
.
These are the steps to destroy them. But this is said in a simplified way. the NF or CO wont be destroyed easily so I have formulated battle plans and such.
Here is what the GC side looks like:
The Void Empire, largest of the GC HQs
The EotA
The Maelstrom HQ
The paragraph below is for full pass holders only. If you have a part-pass you skip that paragraph below and go to the next paragraph.

and secretly, the OOGC.
The HQ Black Order of the Mysticlis,
And the Order of the Crystals.
These are HQs that are willing to help.

Now, we are coming to the battle strategies. The Void’s army is organized into 10000 legions, working together with the fleet with the same number. The EotA army is divided into 5 parts, the Fright Knights, the Islanders, the Time Warriors, the Alien Army and the Desert Kings. The Maelstrom is divided into soldier types, such as stromlings and spiderlings. There are leaders for each soldier type. Now, to the “Destroy the Rangers” plan: they have a shipyard and a base. Their base is sort of a planet chunk and they have the Redeption as their flagship and some others. Now their base must be found with an Evilian Buoy Map: Evilian energies can “echo” over the universe and when they find signs of imagination they can give out a “respond echo” to the map. It shows how much imagination and tech is there and a shape will turn up if it is part of any HQ. in this case if a triangle turns up with the imagination rates (the imagination rates must be above 10 to be sure they are the Rangers) it is their base. Now, when the forces arrive they must get through shields because that must be how their base is defended, no? Well to get through them I have formulated a plan in a way nobody ever thought about: underground. TPR-978 drilling ships must try to get through the shields and TPD-977 drilling vehicules must go underground and get inside. The TPDs must carry cannons, soldiers and war vehicules and such to bring down the shields and destroy the base. That’s how to destroy the Rangers. For the ARA they are in ships. Once again, the TPR-978 ships must play an important role: they must get through the shields and destroy the base by drilling through their engines and command center. But before ramming them the ships must ram the shield generator. After that the command center or the engines or whatever you want. For the GNR topic, it is actually harder: we must cut through the borders. But this must actually be easier: we can just go over and start blasting at the nearest and weakest space station near and destroy it, thus starting to break in. we can just start destroying anything in our way, such as cities, space stations and fleets. Now, the challenge of this is destroying Nimbus Station. We can employ the same strategy at the Rangers and how there are more civilians we can use them as hostages or such and the NF will fight less tougher against us, and the remaining forces that haven’t attacked can stay and destroy the civilian ships escaping. The Faction HQs will be the same as destroying the GNR topic except there are no borders. The opposing HQs would be the CO, and it would be the same as the Faction HQs. So I guess that was all!



Max Evons
