*A sleek black colored ship drops out of warp and lands in the Black Diamonds docking bay*

Several guards with the symbol of the Emperor come out of the ship and form lines on either side of a black carpet that was laid out. A hooded figure walks out of the ship. Hatred, spite, and power radiate from him. Under his cloak a glitter of all colors can be seen. Under his hood, a masked face can be seen. His eyes appear like to flames. He stands straight and tall, those around him feel dwarfed by his presence, but the reason for this is something more than his height. He wears a cape on the back of his cloak that is black with silver trim, while his cloak is black with gold trim. He walks past his gaurds and enters an elevator. It takes him up several floors until it finally stops.

He enters into a massive chamber so large that the ceiling and walls in front and to either side of him can't be seen. He walks towards the middle of the room where a massive throne is at. The throne is made of a strange black wood that is covered in onyx. Their are horns about the back of the throne, ivory covered in silver, studded with black diamonds.

The figure sits on the throne, suddenly all monitors in the Void Empire show him sitting there. He looks up and speaks.

*Transmission starts*

I have returned my friends. . . I, Emperor Zolmar has returned from the Void once more. I am very glad to be able to say that the Void hasn't lost power with my absence. This is due to some of my most faithful followers, and I have decided to give them all special titles to set them apart from all others in the Void. These people are Krill, Soranos, and Max Devon’s. They have been loyal to the Void from the beginning. Krills new title will be Viceroy of the Void Empire. He is second only to myself, and rules in my place for me. Soranos will be the Master Tactician. He will be directly in charge of all ground assaults and battle plans we have, this includes defenses. Max Devon’s is the Director of Void Intelligence. He finds out what we need to know to win our battles and manipulate our foes. All these positions are higher up than the original head positions. The Empire is in your debt. Thank you for your service.

*Transmission ends*

~Emperor Zolmar
~Watchful Eye of the Swords of Light
