Emperor Zolmar appearance:

He wears black robes trimmed with silver that seem to absorb light into them. His armor consist of black thin plates fused to his skin and a purple hood with symbols on it. His armor contains magical rune inchantments (first builder magic) and also technological abilities. His weapon is a midnight black sword that has several gems in the hilt. However, the sword is made so that it breaks apart to be a whip with spikes on it. The spells placed on it allow it to penatrate most armor easily. In his other hand he has a staff that allows him to use Dark Imagine abilities. However, he rarely fights himself, but perfers to have others do it for him.

Elite bodygaurd:

These consist of 12 elite gaurds. They are powerful warriors able to fight and shapeshift. They wear golden armor and golden helms. Their swords and shields are also inchanted.

The Panther

Story:
Thousands of years ago, when the first builders controlled the universe and creativity rained, there was a very good government in place. No one questioned it for centuries, however, a First Builder by the name of Zolmar, was power hungry. He worked his way into the government and became very powerful. He attempted to become head of the government, but wasn't elected. He found this was because his opponent had rigged the votes. This enraged him beyond belief, he raised an army of monsters and minifigures and attacked the government. It was a close battle, but they drove him off. However, he laid a curse on them before they got rid of him, and said they would all die out by the end of the century. Sure enough, a strange sickness struck them, and they all died. This was when minifigures from other galaxies came. Zolmar wanted to rule them, but he had been confined to the Void. Most people now don't know what the Void is, even though they have seen it. The Void is the Rivendark Cannon on Crux Prime. Recently a team of Explorers journeyed there and awoke Zolmar slumber. He wishes to wreak havoc and rule the universe by whatever means needed. The Void Empire is on the rise.
