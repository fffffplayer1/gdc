OOC: I'm going to admit, the Void is basically defeated. Fastracer won't like this, but after Krill left, Kangmin and Shadowsword sorta became enactive, well, it was done for.

So one out of these two moves will go through depending on what NPC law goes through. If NPCs are banned then plan B will go through. If not, plan A will go through.

Plan A: *The Void portal in Void space grows in intensity and size*

*A message goes to all the Void cruisers*

"Retreat to the Void portal, it is time to leave this dimension for now. . ."

*The entire Void fleet (with the exception of Krills Harbringer which is hidden somewhere) warps to the Void portal leaving the galaxy with no trace of them*

Plan B: *Xerath watches from the Void*

"The Rebellion and Nexus Force have stopped my plans. . . But they will regret it. If I can't have this dimension I will leave my mark and all shall remember it! They wish to have us leave? Oh, we will leave, but not before causing the biggest disaster in the history of their universe!"

*The entire Void Fleet enters attack formation, all cruisers, ships, from the many hidden hangers group up*

*The Void Fleet attacks all the planets ravaging them with great cost to their own ships, much of the time recklessly attacking without regard for their own losses. The planets the Void inhabbited were completely destroyed removing all of their technological advancements from the Universe while many of the GA's labs were targetted and destroyed as well. Nearly all minifigures were destroyed in the coming battle. However, when it ended, there were a small remaining group of minifigures, some battle hardened soldiers, others, families. The Void, was gone and null from the Dimension of LU and the portal was permanently sealed, never to be opened again*

OOC: Basically, this would explain why there are no more NPCs. They were all smashed in the war, the Void would also be completely destroyed. What do you people think of it?

~Icaroth

Plan A would probably happen, but I think Plan B explains it best! Great idea. The Nexus Tower is the single thing undamaged.

-Buzzsaw

Also, if plan A happens, the Void would eventually return, if plan B happens, it is HIGHLY unlikely the Void would ever return. Either way, time to bring out the Wise Old Man and the Yea Old Hippie.

~Icaroth
