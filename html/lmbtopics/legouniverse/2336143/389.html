It has given me valuable time to prepare for a new era of the Void Empire. -Several lightyears away from the black hole, a massive subspace distortion appears- - A huge ship emerges from the portal- One of the First Builder's crowning achievements. I present to you the new base of the Void Empire, the Vanguard.



Size: It is about 1.5 times the length of an average planet, but slightly smaller in diameter than the Black Diamond



Shape: The Vanguard is capable of sublight speed about that of an average Void supercarrier, courtesy of the massive engine ring on the back of it. It is an oval in shape, with large ridges arranged in a + pattern as viewed from the front. It is completely Void black, except for the ominously glowing red engines.



Weapons: The Vanguard is a vessel of war, designed to obliterate all enemies of the First Builders. It is fitted with titanic main cannons capable of blasting planets to pieces within minutes, and is bristling with other armarment, including high-yield particle weapons, mass drivers, missile launchers, CIWS, spatial distortion cannons, plasma accelerators, and many other secret weapons. It contains hangars capable of housing the entire Void fleet with room to spare, and even some First Builder ships...



Defenses: The Vanguard has a shield that, while still incredibly strong, is weaker than that of the Diamond. Its main defense is a very thick, extremely efficent, energy-recycling armor.
