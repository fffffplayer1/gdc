Emperor Zolmars new flagship:
OOC: Please note I started this about a week or so ago, it was the jammer I made adressed to my self. People may now read that jammer if they wish.

Name: Hand of the Void
Size: I'm honestly unsure, however, it is around 5 times a long as a supercarrier is. (The Hand of the Void will be the largest actual ship in the fleet)

Appearance: The head of the ship is made to look like a dragons head somewhat. The back of the ship is slightly wider than the front, but not significantly. There are multiple towers on it as well as spike like structures on it. It is black colored.

Defences: The hull of the ship is made out of a strange material that is extremely tough and durrable. This material also will repair itself rather quickly if damaged. This material can block most physical blast and repair itself quickly, however, lasers will penatrate it. Underneath this layer is a material that is able to absorb energy like lasers, etc. It can turn this energy absorbed into energy for the ship to use. The shield itself is extremely strong mainly because of its high regeneration rate. The bridge and some other parts of the ship have addition shielding and protection from chemical attacks that might get in if the shields failed.

Weapons: The top of the ship is litterally covered in Void beam cannons that are automated. On the bottom of the ship is multiple rocket lauchers. They range in size and can fire any rockets found in the Voids weapon inventory. The side of the ship has a couple hundred railguns that fire physical shells. There are also several large cannon type contraptions on the side that fire much larger physical shells. The most iconic weapon on the ship however is the Pure Void Energy beam cannon. Inside the dragons mouth on the front of the ship is a gun capable of firing a blast of Pure Void energy. The gun was modified by Zolmar himself allowing it to fire the Pure Void Energy. The gun in this area is the most shielded part of the ship.

Power source: A stabalized Void Portal is used for the ships power source, giving it near limitless power. However, firing the Pure Void Energy cannon takes so much energy that no other weapons can be fired at the same time.

Note: This is a plot-twist, so the large amount of posts is legit.
***

*I hunched figure clad in a spidery, black cloak and magically enforced moldavite (Real mineral) armor walks through a shadowy hallway, every step seeming to make plants grow from the metal ground, just to wilt and turn to ash a few seconds later.*

*Soon, the person arrives at a completely destroyed room, wrecked with a large hole punched through it.*

*The man seems not to care, just walking forward to a bundle of what looks like armor.*

"Death is eternal, is absolute. But there will come a day when the sky falls, the stars blink out, when fires and extinguished and lamps lose their power. There will come a day with the dead shall live, and the living... shall die."

*From the unfathomable darkness of his cloak, the aphotic person retrieves a disc, a disc that is glowing such a bright gold that its contrast to the dark room is blinding.*

"It is eternal, absolute..."

*The disc begins to glow even brighter, seeming to burn with an inner, sun-like fire.*

"But there will come a day when the sky falls..."

*The artifact floats away from the man's hand, several meters above him.*

"When the start blink out and fires are extinguished."

*It begins to spin, round and round, sending beams of blinding gold ocsilating about the room.*

"When head lamps lose their power..."

*The entire room is engulfed by shimmering flame, and what seems to be chaos and Imagination begins to swirl around the armor.*

*The armor begins to put itself back together, and a strange, translucent, sapphire floats from the disc and into the armor.*

"There will come a day when the dead shall live and the living... that die."

*The armor, now with someone obviously in it, takes a tentative step forward.*

"Welcome back, my old friend!" I say, seemingly floating forward and replacing the artifact- the Sun-Disc- withing by cloak.

*Emperor Zolmar nods my way, flexing his fingers in gratitude.*

"Once again, the Void shall prosper. May all your foes be destroyed- may the dead live and the living die."

*And with that, I, Emperor Nargaroth, turn on my heel, walking back the way I came.*

*The very air seems to tremble, then shatter like glass, revealing a portal.*

"Farewell, Zolmar."

*Nargaroth steps through, and the 'shards of glass' come back together, leaving to trace that he was ever there.*

~Emperor Nargaroth~

*Zolmar looks around the room and watches as Nargaroth vanishes*

"Finally, free from that dreaded existence and back into the realm of the living..."

*Zolmar raises his staff and a portal of Void energy erupts in front of him*

*Zolmar walks through the portal and into the main conference hall on the Vanguard*

*Those seated in the room gasp in shock as he walks through the portal*

"I have returned my subjects... And soon I shall find out those of you who were faithful to me.... And those who weren't...."

The people in the room still in shock begin to whisper to each other and realize this was no trick, they could feel Zolmars power and controlled wrath coming from him.

After a few seconds silence one of the imperial officers decides unless the whole group of them want to get smashed for being traitors, they better do something fast.

He suddenly yells, "Hail Emperor Zolmar!"

After several seconds of deathly silence after his statement, the rest of the officers in the room yell the same thing in, echoing the previous statement.

Zolmar stares around the room, his eyes glittering with malevolence.

"You shall all live to serve me another day."

The officers salute, and then preform the fastest meeting on record and nearly run out of the room alerting the rest of the Empire of the development.
