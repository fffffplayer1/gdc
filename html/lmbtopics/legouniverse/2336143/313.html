*A massive ship drops out of warp*

I have decided to lend assistance since you seem to be in grave danger. As you probably remember, I left the Void sometime ago in this ship. I'm now ready to assist you.

Specs on Xerath's Mothership;

Size; I don't know an exact size since I'm not sure if we scaled ships sizes down yet, however, it is large enough to carry a few hundred large ships and several thousand fighters.

Weapons; It carries Ion cannons which can shut down other ships. It carries thousands of rockets from small missels to powerful warheads. It has several thousand automated chainguns which can damage ships thats shields have been dropped. The most powerful weapon on it is the Void cannon on the front of it which can cause huge destruction due to shields having no effect on this energy type. The reason for this is that since this energy is from another dimension, it doesn't follow LU laws of nature. However, in fairness, I will say that after two uses, the rebels or whoever I use it on will find out how to modify their shields to stop it. The ship also has standard laser weapons.

Defense; This ship doesn't have a shield, instead, it has extremely durable battle armor which is highly resistant to lasers and impact. So to do much damage to the ship, you have to focus your firepower on one part of it. Also note that the Bridge has extra protection and it is impractical to attempt to take it out unless you can do so from the inside. Their are also anti missel systems that can take out missels before the hit the armor dirrectly. In general, missels do more damage to the armor than lasers. The ship has organic systems that will repair themselves over time as well.

I think that's all for now.

~Xerath
