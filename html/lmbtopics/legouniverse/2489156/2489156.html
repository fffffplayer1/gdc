Chapter 1: The wish
~20 years after the war~

I sat on the couch listening to my father tell me about his adventures in the nexus force. My eyes not leaving his face as he spoke.
" Well Now, When I first saw this kid come in, He was ragged, his hair was messed up and he looked very tired. I felt sorry for him when he had let out a long sigh as he completed his test to join a faction. Now, about a month after this kid joined the Nexus force, he was rank three and had earned his tittle as one of the best warriors. He soloed the Great Butter scorch and-"
" Yea yea yea! You told me this yesterday! What else did he do?" I said, cutting my father off. My father gave me a small smile.
My father was the great Nexus Jay. He had fought and trained new recruits in the Nexus War. I love listening to his stories. I liked listening about the great adventures Hael storm went on, too the wonders of Vanda Darkflame's studies. I always dreamed of fighting Maelstrom and becoming a hero. It was too bad the war was over and there was no maelstrom in sight.

" Well. He saved my life once." My father said, " I could tell you that one but its a little scary"
" I don't care. Tell me" I said, leaping up and making my short green hair fly in my face.
" Ok well.. I was standing in the Plaza Like I always do. But out of no where, a Maelstrom dragon flies in. This Dragon wasn't very smart, because he landed right in the plaza, " My father said, "And Everyone in the plaza started to attack it. I pulled out my gun and fired at that thing. Which upset it. The monster blew fire at me. Now, I wasn't as quick as some of the other soldiers so I was sure as BBQ. But then the Hero leaped in the way of the attack, and TOOK the hit for me!"
" Yes this is scary alright" I muttered. listening to someone saving my father's life wasn't that exciting. I liked the stories of wild adventures to the heart of the maelstrom, breathe taking battles, and daring choices. Not Some unlucky day for my father.
" well it was scary for me" My father said crossly. He stood up and looked at me.
" I think we have had enough story time right now, Quiver," And with that, My father left the room. I let out a long sigh and went into my room. I flopped down on my bed and stared at the plain white ceiling.
" I wish I could have been a hero. " I mumbled to myself. I turned over and looked in the mirror. I stared at myself. My green hair all a mess, my black eyes stared back at me.
" I'd be a great adventurer.. Able to snip a stromling from five feet away," I yawned, "Maybe one day.. Maybe one day.." and with that I fell fast asleep. . .. .

Chapter 2: Ore finds his crystal

~30-some years earlier than chapter 1~

"Watch out for the mechs!" I heard someone shout. I turned to see a maelstrom mech towering over me, gun at the ready.
"Why do I seem to attract these things?" I growled jokingly, flipping behind it and stabbing it with my sword. It fell to the ground with a THUD, and exploded.
"I think they like your shiny silver hair," one of my teammates joked.
"Yeah, yeah..." I said, crouching over the fallen remains of the mech. I was able to snatch a few screws and bolts from the mess before I had to move on. I don't know what it is about it, but I think they might come in handy later. Something tells me I should've been in Assembly instead of Venture League sometimes.

Today I'm part of a team mapping Avant gardens, looking for ways up mountains and safer ways to the launch Annex and such. I'm the only rank one on the team of mappers, so I get teased a lot. I've never made a big discovery.

As I climbed up another rocky cliff, I heard the leader of my team shout "Hey guys! Look at this!"
We scrambled over the rocks to where she was standing. She was pointing at a crevice in the mountain, just big enough for me to squeeze into.
"If you don't mind, Ore..." she said, pointing to the cave.
"Fine..." I cautiously crawled inside. The walls were glowing faintly green, giving my Buccaneer gear a nice luster. I felt the walls as I walked past. They were damp with dew. A bat flew overhead, away from the green orb ahead of me.

"CAN YOU SEE ANYTHING?" I heard from back at the entrance of the cave.
"JUST A LIGHT!" I shouted back, and kept going forward. The light was coming form a small green crystal on a necklace. I picked it up and put it on. It was cool on my skin, and made my gear look really cool and shiny again.

Then it exploded.

I was knocked backwards through the cave, blasted through the wall, and over into the battlefield, where I blacked out. When I awoke, I was in the Sentinel camp with a doctor standing over me.
"Oh, hello!" He said, helping me up. "Looks like you didn't suffer any major injuries. Lucky you. Get back to the field now, soldier."

"Ugh..." I moaned walked back over to the battlefield when the crystal (which was still around my neck) began to glow brightly again. There was a sonic boom sort of sound, and a flash of light.

When it cleared, there was a green-haired girl, no older than myself, at my feet...

Chapter 3: ten years back in time

When I awoke from my nap I was confused to find myself laying on grass. I was even more confused to hear the crashes of battle. I opened my eyes to see a purple minifig which stared at me with an evil grin. My eyes went wide and I let out a scream. A quick flash of movement from above me and the Purple monster smashed. A hand was held out for me. I quickly grabbed it. A young Boy helped me up.
" Um.. " Fear ran through my body when I looked around. I was in a green field. It looked almost peaceful, not including the fact of the on going crashing rockets and minifigs locked in deep battles with purple monsters. I turned back to the boy, who was busy smashing a robot thing that had gotten to close to us. I watched his fancy sword attacks, followed by an easy snipe of the his gun. I blinked when I realized he wore pirate-like gear.
" Where am I?" I asked the boy.
" Oh, so you speak," The boy said with a little smirk, " Avant Gardens." I looked around even more confused. Avant Gardens was a wild life park! Not some battle field.
" You have to be joking." I breathed.
" No, I'm not," The boy said. I wanted to see another smirk but his face remained serious.
" But- But- But," I couldn't hold back my shock. Had the Maelstrom attacked again? But they were all gone!
" The Maelstrom is back??" I squeaked.
" What do you mean Back??" The boy asked. I was about to answer but the boy was striked in the back by shot from a robot. He flipped around and helped up his gun. The gun glowed faintly and a trident shot out of the gun, forking the robot. It crashed backwards, falling into bricks.'
" it isn't safe here. Follow me," The boy said, grabbing my hand and pulling me off to a small strong hold. I stared around even more confused. This was where the wild life center was.
" what happen to the wild life center??" I blinked.
" What?" The boy stared at me.
" WHY IS EVERYTHING SO DIFFERENT?" I cried.
" What do you mean? Its been like this since the war started!"
"YEA AND IT WAS CHANGED WHEN THE WAR ENDED!"
"WHAT?"
" THE WAR IS OVER WHY! IS THERE MAELSTROM HERE?"
" THE WAR ISN'T OVER!"
"WHAT?"
" Why the brick do you think the war is over??"
" Because it is!"
The boy stared at my in total shock and confusion.
" but it isn't over! it only started ten years ago."
" What.. but that means.. But the war has been over for twenty years..."
" You are from the future???"
" What year is it??"
" 2000"
" You have to be joking.."
" I'm not."
" I went back in time.."
" Wow.."
" Wow? I HAVEN'T EVEN BEEN BORN YET!" panic ran through my body. How did I get back in time? I was freaking out. I wanted my father. I wanted to be home. I wanted Avant gardens to be a park again. Tears ran down my face.
" Don't cry please" The boy said, patting me on the shoulder, " My name is Ore."
" Ore? I'm Quiver.." I said quietly.
" Hard day?" A strong looking Minifig walked over. He held a few maps but looked at me with caring eyes," I know your first day can be hard.. and I see you missed your Visit with bob.. No matter, Ore, right? Would you take her to Nexus Jay please?"
"Yes Sir" Ore said. My heart skipped a beat of the thought of seeing my father. Would he even know who I am?
Ore led me up a path, where we leaped onto a big fan. The fan pushed us up to a higher part of the cliff. My eyes went wide when i saw the monument. It was still that old one of Bob and that skeleton thing fighting each other.
" Huh.. The monument hasn't be blown up yet.." I commented, remembering the story of how the maelstrom blew up the old monument.
" The Monument blows up? Don't tell Rusty that.." Ore snickered.
"Why? Old Rusty likes the new one much better." I blinked. The old builder had often given me tours of the new monument, which was a large statue of the first four explorers.
I was shocked out of my memories when I saw Rusty steel. First of all, he was young, and had no gray hair like he does in my time. He also had a fancy hard helm. In my time he didn't wear a helmet. When we came up to him, he was busy screaming at the birds. This must be why Rusty has such a cracked Voice later. When he faced us his deep voice surprised me.
" WHY HELLO!" He pretty much yelled at us.
" Hey Rusty, need us to scare a few birds for you on the way up?" Ore asked.
" Yes, that would be nice." Rusty muttered, giving the birds a crossed look.
" You don't like birds?" I asked blankly.
" Not at all!" Rusty shouted, " and i never will!"
" You say that now, " I snickered only loud enough for Ore to hear. Ore Led me up another path which climbed the tall monument.
" What did you mean by that?" Ore asked me as soon as we were out of ear shot.
" Rusty is a bird watcher in my time, " I giggled. Ore let out a loud laugh.

We reached the picnic area pretty quickly. I was happy to see this place wasn't much different then my time.
" Hello there you two!" a young woman greeted us as we neared a launch pad.
" Hello Sky lane, Can you give, Quiver here a Rocket and thinker helmet?" Ore asked.
" WHAT?" I yelped. I stared at Sky lane, my eyes wide. She wore a biker like out fit, with a tattoo and green high light. I couldn't believe what I was see. Her helmet sat at her feet, glowing with imagination.
" YOU CAN'T BE- " I had to catch myself. If I told her She was my mother or told Jay he was my father I might mess things up. I quickly calmed myself down and gave my very young and surprised looking mother a small smile.
" Umm I mean, Hi, can I have my stuff please?" I said, trying to hide the fact my heart was pounding in my chest.
" Well yes," Sky Lane said with a smile and handed me a weird helm and a model packet. I quickly walked over to the rocket building area that Sky pointed too.
" Open the Model packet," Ore told me. I open the box with a surprise of large size models popping out of it. I almost dropped the pieces.
" Careful" Ore blinked.
" Yea yea now what?" I asked, feeling kinda awkward since i was holding three big rocket pieces.
" Put on your thinker helm" Ore said.
"How?" I muttered. I placed the rocket pieces down and pulled out my thinker helm from my bag which I had no idea where came from. I put it on and everything looked very blue. I was about to ask what to do next but when I moved my hands, the rocket pieces floated up and clicked together.
" Whoa," I said, pulled off my thinker helm, which disappeared into my invisible bag, followed by the rocket shrinking and also disappearing into my bag as well.
" why do everything do that?" I asked.
" Be glad it works, I'm surprised you even have a imagination bag, without imagination." Ore said.
I gave him a small shrug.
" So um.. I don't actually have to fly my rocket do I?" I said nervously as we walked up to the launch pad.
" Nope. Its auto drive." Ore answered, placing his rocket on the launch pad and blasted off. I copied him, placing my new rocket on the launch pad (Which appeared in my hands as soon as I stepped close enough to the rocket launcher) and blasted off to wherever we were going. . . . .

Chapter 4:

The fact that Quiver came from the future was mind-blowing. Part of me thinks it's just an act, but that crystal I found convinces me otherwise. I don't know what I'm in for now, but something tells me it's going to be a lot of fun.

Quiver had a shaky landing in Nimbus Station, but she managed to avoid a lot of damage.
"So where are we going now?" Quiver asked, looking around curiously.
"I guess, since you aren't in a faction, we should take you to see Nexus Jay." I responded, taking her down the path. She jumped at the mention of his name.
"Ummm... yeah. Ok, I g-guess s-so." She stammered.

'Hello there!" Jay said as we walked up. "How can I help you today?"
"Quiver here needs to join a faction." I answered.
"Quiver..." Jay thought. "That name sounds... familiar. Oh well. Do you have Nexus Force approval?"
"Uh..." Quiver blinked. "Sorry, what?"
"No, she doesn't..." I answered for her.
"I suppose that you could get the approval here," Jay answered. "Head down that path to your left, and you can start-"

He was cut off when a shadow drifted overhead, blackening the sky. Thunder clapped, and an evil laugh filled the air. I looked towards Nimbus Plaza, where a giant ball of maelstrom energy was forming. The ground under it proceeded to explode in a column of fire, and a giant maelstrom dragon clawed it's way out, burning down buildings. Minigigures started attacking it, but there just weren't enough of them.

"Oh, this isn't good at all...." Jay mumbled as he pulled out his gun. "Hey, Quiver was it? Change of plans. Go choose a faction. Now."
