Chapter 10:

I woke from my unconsciousness to a blast of foul breath. I sputtered and looked up, eye to eye with a pink colored maelstrom Admiral.
"Ah, he's awake. It's always so much more fun when they are awake to scream." He said with a devilish smile.
"Who are you?" I asked, while looking for a way out of this situation is was in, but couldn't find anything useful.
"Why, I be Admiral Flogmore, Maelstrom captain, five-time fish eating champion, builder of many fine ships-"
"Yeah, yeah, yeah. Get on with it!" An impatient stromling pirate yelled.
"LET ME FINISH MY MONOLOGUE!!" Flogmore yelled as he whirled around and smashed the pirate with his cannon-arm. "As I was saying..."
I twisted my head around, and saw Quiver standing on the jail platform above me. I motioned for her to shoot the pirates holding me, but she was empty handed.
"...And master torturer!" Flogmore concluded. "Now, prepare for infection!"
He leaned in close and touched my shoulder. Pain shot through my whole body, and I doubled over. My vision started going blurry and cross-eyed, and I could see my body was glowing a sickly purple. Right before I fell down unconscious, I heard "HI-YAA!" from somewhere above...

Instead of falling asleep or becoming a mindless zombie, I found myself floating up and out of my body as a spirit. I saw my body was being dragged away by Quiver and a few Ninjas back to the pirate camp, while the pirates fended off the rest of the maelstrom.
I floated over to the camp after watching the battle for a while, and was shocked at what I found at the camp. My maelstrom alter-ego was on a rampage, trying to destroy the camp. . . .

Chapter 12:

I saw Quiver drop my body and fall down unconscious. She rose up out of her infected form just as the two stromlings ran away to somewhere else.
"Wh- What just... happened?" Quiver asked, looking around.
"Your imagination spark has been released," I answered. "And now our stromling selves are on the loose."
I looked back at Nexus Jay, who was gathering a search team and some paradox scientists to disinfect us. They ran into Brick Annex, with us following. The two stromlings were trying to destroy the little statue, but were easily subdued by the few members of the team. The paradox scientists used a vacuum to suck the maelstrom away, and Quiver and I drifted back into our bodies.
I sat up and scratched my head. "Thanks, guys,” I said to the disinfection team.
“Don’t mention it,” they said, walking off.
We turned to Nexus jay. “Gnarled Forest has been badly infected!” I reported.
“The Ninjas and pirates seem to have it mostly under control, but you never know…” Quiver added.
“We should send them some backup,” Jay said. “Go report to the faction leaders and tell them what you saw there. I’ll get some more minifigs to back up the pirates.”
“Will do!” I saluted Jay and ran with Quiver to the Nexus Tower launch pads, where we blasted off….

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

“….And so then Jay told us to tell you,” Quiver concluded.
“Aye, tht’s not good at’all…” Hael mumbled. “Admiral Flormore shoulda been trapped on Crux Prime… How did he end up in Gnarled Forest?”
“I’m not sure. Should we go back and look around for a… I don’t know, what should we look for?” I asked.
“I guess a rocket or somthin’.” He thought.
“Will do, sir!” Quiver and I saluted, and went rocketed back to Gnarled Forest…
