Chapter one: Rising from the wreck part 1.

Jo-jo awoke from his wrecked Hinterlander. He must have been knocked out for hours! Yet it seemed like only ten minutes since he left Nimbus station. And yet here he was back on Earth. "It's good to be back home." smiled Jo-jo standing up. He was back to his old life now... no more fighting maelstrom monsters. No more "You have to report to the Nexus tower at 8 AM sharp". No more... everything! He was free!

Jo-jo had been ordered to fight for the nexus force along with many other people. He had been sent aboard the Venture explorer, like everyone else... but of course it HAD to be when HE was on that the maelstrom struck the ship. He was one of the few people who had escaped and had landed on Avant gardens. Later he joined the Venture League, became a Daredevil. Many years later, he had a nightmare in which Earth was destroyed and he was the only one who could have saved it... but he was busy on Forbidden valley. When he awoke, he vowed that he would run away back to Earth and NEVER have anything to do with the nexus force again.

And now, he was here. Jo-jo breathed in the nice, maelstrom free air, and started walking.

Note to reader: When I said "Hinterlander" I meant "Astroscout"

Rising from the wreck part 2.

After about two minutes he stopped. Where on earth was he? Literally. It wasn't like he could just WALK BACK to LEGO city. He had no idea of which part of the planet he was on.

He walked back to the Astroscout wreckage. It was a sad sight. Smoke emitted from the crunched up engine panels in front of the cockpit. The rocket boosters were dented and on fire. The windscreen was all smashed and just everything on or in the green rocket was wrecked. Jo-jo went to the boot of his rocket. Or what was left of it. Thankfully his possessions and other gear were still intact... but then again... most of his luggage were one made of one LEGO piece.

Jo-jo closed the boot's lid and sat down, back leaning against is old rocket. The sun was setting. He looked to his left and noticed the mouth of a cave. "That'll be a good place to spend the night." he thought to himself. So he dragged his rocket into the cave. He then opened his boot and took out his gear... his Daredevil rank 3 gear... in both colours, his Rutcarver, his Buccaneer r3 gear (original) and his Broadsider, Adventurer r2 gear and slither striker and his thinking cap and his Hinterlander race car... as well as some emergency food tins.

Chapter two: Ryo part 1.

Soon, Jo-jo had made a pretty good temporary home in the cave. It had a kitchen area with all his tinned food in, a bedroom, protected by the Astroscout wreck, in case any minifig or animal should want to attack. And last but not least, an area for all his nexus gear that he had brought with him... as well as his Hinterlander.

Jo-jo stood back to admire his work. He sighed a sigh of relief.

Two days, later he was still living in the cave. Jo-jo went to the kitchen area to get breakfast. But then: "I've ran out of food!" he cried. And so he had.

So Jo-jo went outside and walked around. While he was walking, he saw a boat with a fishing rod and bait inside it. "Brilliant!" he thought.

The boat had been sitting there for at least 5 months, so there would be no harm in borrowing it to catch some food. So Jo-jo pushed it to the nearby sea, got in, picked up the oars and set of to deeper water to fish in.

Ryo part 2.

About an hour late, out at sea, Jo-jo had caught nothing. "Maybe, I wasn't cut out to be a fisherman." he sighed. He kept his line in the water for a few more minutes and then he brought his line back up. The bait was gone! So the evil sea creatures had pinched the bait without tugging the line... so Jo-jo never caught a thing.

Jo-jo sighed again and lay back in the boat. He looked to his right. Then he got up, rubbed his eyes and looked again. There was another boat on the horizon... in bad condition, but better than Jo-jo's. Then Jo-jo noticed something else... the boat was heading straight for him! And the person on board didn't seem to notice. Jo-jo picked up his oars and rowed towards the other boat. Soon he could see the owner of the other boat clearly. He had Black hair, top and fringe brushed to the left. He had bushy eyebrows and a blue t-shirt and leather jacket as well as grey trousers. The guy seemed to be staring into nothingness.

Then, the to boats collided. Jo-jo yelped as his boat was over turned. He quickly run up the steep sinking deck and landed on the other man's boat with a thud. "OY!" yelled the other minifig, "What 'cha doin' on ma boat?" "S-sorry." Jo-jo stammered. "It's just that our boats collided and my boats sunk."

Ryo part 3.

"That's all right." sighed the other minifig... "I guess I'm just a bit panicky and stuff... I've been like that a lot recently." Jo-jo sat up. "That's fine man, but, I was just wandering, why do you feel like that, and what are you going out here?" "Well..." said the minifig, "It's kinda a long story."

"Years ago when I was a kid, me and my cousin were best buddy's, you know, we always played together, went on adventures together, go in to a lot of accidents together and gave each other funny nicknames. The one I remember that my cousin gave himself was "The tester". In fact, I and him called him that so much that I forgot his real name... really! I still can't remember it now."

"So what happened then?" asked Jo-jo.

"Nothin' much really." said the minifig, "We just went separate ways as we got older and we never saw each other again... but two days ago, I caught a text on my mobile from him... it was asking me to try and find him in this area within two months... with out him seeing me... I mean... I have to try and find him, stealth-fully, and kidnap him... but he's tryin' to do the same thing to me... just like when we were kids. But the thing is that he was supposed to give me clues and stuff along the way... but my little sister, aged 15, stole my mobile 'cos she didn't want to spend all her coins on one for herself... and declared that it is now HERS! I've never seen it again since."

The minfig stared into nothing again looking kinda sad for a few moments and then came back into to it all and spoke again. "So what about you?"

Ryo part 4.

Jo-jo looked up at the sky, closed his eyes and spoke.

"I was your average guy in LEGO city, for my whole life... and then when I was 22 about, I found this orphan, all alone in the rain. So I adopted her and my life seemed to get better since I was almost able to relive my kid days with this kid, but then came LUPC. That is: Lego Universe Protector Conscription. Any adult minifigure aged 21 or up was forcefully put on the Star Ship Venture Explorer to be flown to Nimbus Station, part of the ancient exploded planet of Crux, to serve in the Nexus force army."

"You were one of the nexus force guys?!" exclaimed the other minifigure. "I thought that that was just some epic made up story... you're one of the legendary legends!"

"Well, I guess you could say that," said Jo-jo, "some optimistic troops said that... but most of us saw our lives being thrown away like garbage. The recruiters said that it was just for one year, so I was able to get a place for my adoptive daughter for one year. So then we boarded the S.S Venture explorer. But on the voyage, the maelstrom hit the ship and we had to escape to Avant gardens. We finally made it to Nimbus Station after many battles and I later joined the Venture League faction."

"When me and my cousin were kids, we used to pretend to be that faction." said the other minifig.

Ryo part 5

Jo-jo sighed again. He didn't no whether he was getting annoyed with the minifigure's constant interrupting or not... 'cos it kind of brought him back to reality.

Jo-jo continued his story:

"A one year job became a job for life. I had been conned! But they kept saying it was for the greater good of the universe. But what about me? After five years of smashing maelstrom in the Nimbus system, I really started to worry. What about my daughter, whp had been left at a good place... for ONE YEAR ONLY! What had happened? Was she safe or not? As well as that, life was still bad anyway... sure there were some fun bits... Hael Storm and Albert Overbuild were good people to hang out with. Not to mention the Raiders of the lost band, led by IndianaBonesSulfrix9 and his non band friends: Baron Doom, Sgt Indy and DoomaLot. And Frostburgh was tons of fun and Moonbase got be every time with it's low gravity.
And then I went through a lot of amazing things, seeing the Nexus tower being completed, the discovery of the long lost fabled starbase of Club station Alpha which was lost and caught up in the asteroid field during the original Crux explosion. But apart from that, life was a complete drag... so after 5 years of Nexus force service, Me and the band RotLB escaped the Nimbus solar system... and the entire galaxy that it was in! And now I'm here, trying to find food just so that I can survive until #I get home... where ever that is."

The minifig was stunned... "That was some story." he said, "I thought it was all glamour and fame and bliss in your line of work, but I didn't EVER think it would be like this."
Jo-jo gave him a sad smile, got up and said."My name's Jo-jo by the way." The minifig got up and said," OK, Jo-jo... I'm Ryo."

And from that moment on, Jo-jo and Ryo became the best of friends.
