People of the mountains part 4.

Later, at about 3 AM, Jo-jo and Ryo had gone back to the cave. "That was some party, man." said Ryo, balancing on his stuffed belly. Jo-jo looked the same... it wasn't so much food, (Which they had had a lot of anyway,) but more air from all the talking and singing.
Jo-jo settled into a sleeping bag that, he had bought from a village shop a few days previously. Ryo got into the one he had brought with him.

They were both very sleepy, but Jo-jo had just one thing to say. "You know I've told all about why I'm back here and ev'rything, right?" "Sure thing... but cha seem so happy now, even though you're lost." said Ryo. "Yeah," said Jo-jo, "I know it's weird but, even though my life back here is probably gone for good... I just can't feel sad in the slightest." "Why?" asked Ryo, sitting up. "Because..." said Jo-jo, thinking, "you know Crux and what it became? Well after travelling through all those small, strange planets, it's so reliving that,... this world is round." Ryo heard this, but drifted off to sleep before he could say anything.

Jo-jo settled down again and fell asleep.

But dark things were afoot...

People of the mountains part 5.

Jo-jo opened his eyes. There was a strong smell of smoke, and his eyes stung. He sat up. The camp fire was out... but then again, though the Venture League daredevil, it is morning. Well, it had been morning when they had got back to the cave, but Jo-jo meant that the sun had risen and was shining... but behind the caves, so the light was dim. Jo-jo got out of his sleeping bag and cleared out the ashes from the fire place. It was then that he noticed that Ryo was gone.

*****************************************************

The forests good be a lonely place. That's what Jo-jo kept thinking that as he ran to no particular destination, hoping to bump into Ryo.

He stumbled on a rock and fell flat on his plastic face. He was trying to get back up, when a rock hit him on the head. Jo-jo didn't get knocked out, but that sure did hurt! As he got up to look where it came from, another hit him. "Heh, heh, heh!" A laugh was heard from with in the trees.

"Ryo?" asked Jo-jo.

*****************************************************

Ryo was walking on the mountains, with a fake beard and hat on. He had bought them in the village and was going to play a practical joke on Jo-jo.

He walked on when he noticed something. A figure running and climbing up the tallest mountain in the entire mountain range! "He's crazy to do that without mountain gear!" gasped Ryo. He ran to the mountain to see what would happen.

People of the mountains part 6.

After about an hour, Ryo made it to the top. The minifig had Black hair, longish. He was just in a t-shirt and jogging trousers. "Crazy, just crazy." Ryo kept thinking to himself. "OI!" the minifig yelled, "Wha' cha doin' on this mountain? You know it's off limits?!" Ryo was surprised to say the least. "Then how come you're up 'ere then?" replied Ryo. "HA!" the other said, "there's a lot you don't about for your age old man." Ryo felt insulted, but then he remembered he was wearing an old man disguised. He said nothing. He looked at the climber. He was about 18 years old. Something about him was vaguely familiar. Could it be?

Feeling brave, Ryo spoke. "What's your name, sonny?" he asked in what was his idea of an old man's voice. "Just call me... the TESTER!" the tester said cooly. Ryo's eyes shrank in his head. So it WAS his long lost cousin! He was about to reveal himself as Ryo, when he remembered about his quest... to kidnap his cousin without his cousin knowing before it was to late.

Ryo walked around the teaster slowly, then he jumped at him and blindfolded him, gagged him and blocked hi ears and carried him of, back to the cave.

People of the mountains part 8.

About 45 days after the Tester and Captain rock met Jo-jo and Ryo that, during the darkest hours of the night, when the four were asleep, a shadow crept towards the cave. Jo-jo had his sleeping bag on a rock shelve now, a bit further into the cave, on the right side to those outside the cave, with his feet facing the cave mouth. Ryo was on the opposite side, but not on a shelve and he had the top of his head facing the cave mouth. The tester was in the same position, on a semi-low rock shelve just above Ryo. Captain rock was sleeping just behind the remains of the evening's fire.

The shadow neared the mouth of the cave that the four friends called home and entered, silently. The tester was actually the only one awake... well... JUST awake... more half asleep anyway... but even in his state, he could hear a noise... he could hear even the most silent thing on the planet... he could hear a feather landing on the ground... and he heard the shadow figure.

He was wide awake now, but pretending to be asleep. The shadow came nearer... it had guesses what the tester was doing. At last, the tester leapt up, picked up his staff, and moved into a defensive position and waited for his opponent to strike.

People of the mountains part 9.

The shadow moved towards the tester. The tester, dropped his staff, and gasped... he had seen this minifigure before... he had met him a fortnight ago, when he was mountain climbing with Ryo, Captain rock and Jo-jo. "Sensie?!" "Aye, it is me," said the sensie, "wake up your companions, I have urgent news for the four of you!" Soon, the other three were awake and sitting around the now relit fire, waiting for the sensie's news.
"There has been some strange activity going on in the forest of doom." the sensie said," I have over heard pirates talking about a new weapon... and something about '5 new troops'... and this isn't just something that is none of our business... they kept talking about' how it will put an end to all ninja FOREVER!"
The four just sat there, shocked, the ninjas who were their best friends, could soon be wiped out of existence. Captain rock spoke; "So-so, what do we do?" The sensie, who was standing up, picked up his bamboo staff, and pointed it at the captain. "YOU and your teem-mates, must venture deep into the forbidden forest of doom, find out what exactly their weapon is, and put an end to this." Now, it was Jo-jo who spoke; "Don't you have some secret weapon our defence that was designed for this sort of thing?!"
"Much to learn in the way of the ninja," said the sensie sadly, "we do not even know WHAT the weapon IS, or WHO the '5 troops' ARE." "Yes," said Ryo, "but don't cha have somethin' that was just designed for anything that your normal ninjas and weapons couldn't handle?"
"Actually, yes," said the sensie, in a 'This one has much potential' voice, " A long time ago when I was about 18 years old, I made FIVE special super weapons. I locked them up in an indestructible safe, but even with my years of memory, I've forgotten the code... and I can't break it open because of it's indestructible design... which is why YOU FOUR must go to the forest of doom... and you must leave... tonight!!"
There was only about 2 hours until the sun would appear above the horizon, and the sky was already turning blue, so the four backed their bags and began their perilous quest.

Chapter five: The forest of doom part 1.

Jo-jo, the Tester, Captain rock, the Sensei, Ryo, Hosproga and Hikaru were standing above a valley in the forest of doom... with the sensei and his two sons horrified. "What is it?" Ryo asked the Sensei. "We have walked right into Samurai territory." "But why did you lead us here, and then asked all surprised and horrified?" asked the Tester. "We have never known the Samurai base of operations in all the years of war... it's just like them to make an alliance with the Pirates!" "So, uh..."" said Ryo, nervously, "let's head back to the forest and go look for pirates." He turned around and was about to leave when he felt something grab his arm. "Not yet." said the person, who owned the arm. It was the Sensei, still looking and turned towards the Samurai village down in the valley, "We need weapons, and a map... only the Samurai's can provide, though we are enemies." Ryo gasped, Jo-jo froze and the Tester passed out. Only Captain Rock wasn't affected. "Let's go then!" he said getting ready to run. "Are you insane?" hissed Hosproga, "You don't go around yelling at the top of your voice in enemy territory." "Sorry." said Captain Rock.

Soon, they were sneaking around the village, in the shadows, looking for an arsenal and a map.
