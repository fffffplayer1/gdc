Chapter 6.1: Out of the Frying Pan and Into the Fire

 

Dwight took a sip of his Paradox Power potion. It tasted like liquid chocolate-chip cookies. Sadly, that was the most interesting thing going on. Everyone else was doing something to help build the new ship, but they’d insisted that he rest. Bother.

 

The Sharpened Shield was a marvel of engineering, using pieces from the Halberd and also bricks from the Property’s vendor system. The Team had built a small frigate, with above-average armor and weapons capability. Treem had even installed some prototype energy shield generators, his own design.

 

The interior was more like a luxury cruiser than a warship, since the Team had a lot of downtime flying from place to place. The lounge had two stereo systems, leather couches, and a fridge. The pilot’s cockpit, which Treem called “The Den”, had state-of-the-art control configurations. The pilot’s chair was a massage recliner, too, which was a bit dangerous since Treem might fall asleep while flying. He swore he’d stay awake, but the other Team members continued to joke and pester him about it. It was just fun to see what his next excuse would be.
