Chapter 18:

 

Joshua Saythe and his search party are on the hunt. They find the slaves' tracks near Talan's hut, then they follow them northward, using the light of dawn.

 

It is about noon when they stop for a short break. Joshua sends scouts ahead to continue searching while the rest of the party ate a noon meal and a few men set up a tent forJoshua. Before long the scouts return and report.

 

The first to arrive is already inside Joshua's tent, and we can hear nothing from inside. They talk in clandestine secrecy as if someone is watching them. After about a minute or two, the scout walks out and calls the next scout in. This continues until Joshua is finished with the last scout.

 

Walking out of the tent, Joshua exclaims,

"Alright boys, luch is over! Grab you gear and head out; the scouts have found something!"
