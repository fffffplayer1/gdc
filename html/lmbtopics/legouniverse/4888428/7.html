Chapter 12:

 

The heat of the day is beating down mercilessly on Talan.

 

Turning, turning, never-ending truning the pump under the watchful eye of Jaze. The sun sank lower in the sky. Turning, turning. A bell's tone rang and echoed throughout the fields.

 

Jaze spoke to Talan.

"I's be goin' fer dinner nar, but I's will be goin' to watch yew tew. Don't lag nar!"

 

He stode off in the direction of the Hugo's masion.

 

'Now is the time,' Talan thought.

 

While rounding the far side of the well, Talan inquired about the other man's identity.

 

"My name is Talan. Yours?"

"Prince Nashua."

"Prince? Of where?"

"Prince Nash O' Swordic."

"I've never heard of Swordic."

"Tis' a land of the far north. I was in the heart of Amythra when Shahyaar and his army came along. My country will be next, but I must not allow it to happen!"

 

Talan looked closer at Prince Nash. He was young, maybe of sixteen years, with dark hair and a vengeful glare. Talan knew that he was determined to protect his people no matter the cost.
