Okay, here's an update on my situation:

I have finished the rough drafts for probably at least six chapters (Chapters 42-47). The reason I wanted to finish them was because the first of these chapters semed to go off into another tangent, which surprised me, and I didn't want things to crumble like they did with Praxer in the back alley. This is all stuff I have said before, but now I will tell you more. I started writing chapter 42 to remove a couple of loose ends, but then by the end I realized something had changed during the time in training: because the Major trained them separately, and honed their skills to the point where they almost rivaled his own, they were no longer operating as a team. I was originally going to have a week pent in team training after their individual training, then later decided against it, and then decided I'd need at least SOME after the end of Chapter 42 hit me like a brick wall. So I decided they're just going to spend a single day, with a single exercise, in team training.

Si the foundation for the next chapters wereset, and I just needed to write them. I decided that each kid would have one chapter from their perspective before one, final encounter covering all four. (By 'perspective' I don't mean I switched to first person, I mean how I like to emphasize one character at a time; Jonathon's perspective is always covered by the Major.) However, the first two of these chapters (Praxer and Axolc) passed by relatively quickly, and then the final confrontation turned out too long, while the two chapters in between were decent length. Thus, editting may take longer as I might have to extend or condense certain chapters in order for this to come out smoothly. However, the first chapter (the one that started this whole mess) only needs standard-issue editting, as it is already an okay length. But keep in mind that even standard-issue editting can take entire days at a time.

So that's that. I am very, truly, deeply sorry I have not been able to get any chapters in for o long, but I wanted to make sure I could pull this off first. The good news is that, once I finish editting, I should be able to get all six chapters in at once, like I did with the dream sequence. This also hits two birds with one stone, because 5 out of 6 of these chapters are action sequences.

So there you have it. Now I have some work due tomorrow I need to do, but I'll still be here to see what you have to say. Please forgive me for taking so long to get this stuff done, and I hope you enjoy the chapters!
