majorq10hero wrote:

Two Updates:

 

[...]

 

2) Today I resolved that I will finish this story, which is one reason I compiled that list.  I have had my doubts about this in the past, but honestly... I owe it to you, and to myself.  I've had the whole story planned out from the beginning, and I know exactly how it will end, and I really want to be able to write that part.  If I made it this far, I can make it to the end.  Plus, it's just so much fun. 

 

Also, as I was going back through the topic to write that table of contents, I was amazed at how good I used to be at posting chapters in a timely manner.   Though I have improved in many ways since then, in this case I should really try to do a better job of living up to my former self.   I confess that, though life is hectic and stressful right now, sometimes it does come down to just laziness.   So I've also resolved to be better with that.

 

Now, in order to follow through on all of these, I'll need two things: One, I'll need readers to be as active as ever: keep the bushes to a minimum, and whatever you do, please don't say, "Wow, that chapter was worth the wait!" because, as much as I love hearing that, it sends the wrong message to my head.   Two, I'm going to need a fresh outlook on this project myself, so that I can make it my #1 priority again.  Basically, I need a fresh start.  And I've decided to start by declaring this day the official rebirth of Crux Origins!

 

Sorry if this seems so sudden, but I've been thinking about this a lot lately, and I need to let it out now.  Please hold me to these promises.

But... but... I like my bush. It's got a trampoline inside.

 

Very well, I shall cease my silent reading. *Kicks bush over a cliff* I'm glad you've resolved to finish this story!
