‎04-27-2012 09:00 PM

Greetings. I am Valdorum, President of Gallifrey.

For thousands of years, my people, the Time Lords, have mastered the ability of time travelling with ancient space vehicles known as a TARDIS. Hidden in the Kasterborus Constellation in the deep fringes of Space, Gallifrey lies far beyond the vision of powerful satellites. When you descend into the atmosphere of the planet, you can see the surface covered with high mountain ranges and vast wastelands. What lies on the lifeless terrain are the planet’s highly sophisticated cities, inside giant domes that protect them from the harsh environment. Herein dwell the Time Lords and their society. The domes have a few metal doors around the perimeter, but they haven’t been opened for generations. The only way for a Time Lord to enter or exit is using is a TARDIS to materialize or de-materialize at will. On the other hand, Gallifrey is also inhabited beyond the cover from the domes. In the wilderness live the Shobogans, a group of Time Lords who rejected society of their fellow Time Lords and withdrawn into solitude. Here, under the leadership of Nesbin, they watch out for their own interests as well as, when required, those of their planet.

But inside the domes, the cities possess Universities, City Councils and other buildings that our cities require. The buildings are like giant cathedrals with hallowed halls and ceilings held together by marble pillars. The most important city of them all is the Capitol City. Home of the Gallifreyan Parliament, High Council and me.

In the Parliament, each representative rules a city. Every 3 representatives serve under an administrator, who rules a province (the 3 cities their representatives rule) and every 3 administrators serve under a cardinal. Each cardinal represents the three founding Time Lord families: the Pyrdonians, the Arcalians, and the Patraxes. Finally, the Imperial Chancellor. Elected by the Parliament from the High Council, this guy observes the Parliament and represents the votes to the High Council. In the High Council, there is the Lord of the Capitol City (who rules the Capitol City), the Lord of Defense (Who is in charge of defending Gallifrey), the Lord of Justice (kinda like a judge) and then the Lord of High Council (who observes High Council). At the head of this huge body is the Time Lord President (that’s me). The Parliament, the High Council and I live in the circular building called the Panopticon, where all matters of state, law, government and administration are settled with general elections. In order to win an election, a group of voters in the Parliament must have at least a 75% majority of the vote. If there was as majority below that number like 60%, a recount will be initiated.
Of course, with all this conflict spreading throughout the universe, the Time Lords have an elite organization that is stationed in the Panopticon that intervenes in case there is a serious crisis. It’s called the Celestial Intervention Agency. With permission of the Time Lord President, the agency dispatches agents to settle the matter.

But of all of the wars we’ve witnessed, your war in LEGO Universe was the most shocking one the Celestial Intervention Agency every experienced. After hearing about your little Malestorm problem, we secretly dispatched Time Lords in LEGO Universe to help the Nexus Force. Later, we have discovered (what we Time Lords call it) ‘A Rift’. It was a giant wormhole that connected LEGO Universe with Earth. There was no doubt that the Malestorm will use the Rift to invade your planet. So, by the end of January 2012, we helped the Mythrans close the wormhole with a Time Shift Stone. We have never heard of Lego Universe ever since.

Here is the rank chart of our society:

Time Lord President: (Me)

High Council: Lord of High Council (1)
Lord of Justice (1)
Lord of Defense (1)
Lord of the Capitol City (1)

Gallifreyan Parliament: Imperial Chancellor (1)
Lord Cardinals (3)
Lord Administrators (9)
Lord Representatives (27)

Celestial Intervention Agency- Commander-in-Chief (1)
Captains (indefinite)
Agents (indefinite)

Normal Classes: Engineers, Guards, Apprentices, Scientists, Explorers,

Only a few deserve to possess the title ‘Time Lord’. Do you have what it takes to be a Time Lord, to protect those in need and possess enough skill in travelling in space and time? Join us…. If you dare….
Grand Master of the Nexus Crusaders and LEGO Universe Veteran
