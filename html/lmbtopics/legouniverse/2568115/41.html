I really can't believe I'm saying this...
I mean, I really can't. But guess what?
It turns out, I'm actually going to have room for one-or-two characters at the start of book three, and I figure it wouldn't hurt to let people post their bios now, if they like. YES, I am now accepting characters! Well, most of them will be declined, as I only have room for one-or-two, but still.

HOWEVER: this is not all celebration. At this point, I have VERY HIGH standards as to which characters I will be accepting, and I will NOT settle for characters who do not meet my standards if no one else posts. Very few characters will pass, and the ones that do will have to face competition before they are finally accepted.
Now, I can't tell you all of my what I want and do not want to see at this point, as a written list would only hinder judgement, but there are some things I can say.

One: The character must NOT be overpowered. Special abilities, such as Fire Elemental powers, and other stuff like that are fine, but nothing that makes the character too strong. I will NOT accept the master of the maelstrom, a personification of a natural force, an insanely powerful supersoldier, etc. I don't want LordBrockTrees and I don't want Lord Ss.

Two: The character MUST have some quality or defining characteristic that sets it aside from previously established characters. A character won't see much attention if there is already a character in place that fills their plot role better. This is meant as a precaution to keep the character from being set aside as a minor character, as has happened before.
However, the sender of the bio should be willing to allow the character to become a minor character IF NECESSARY. Hopefully, this will not happen, but I would appreciate flexibility if it does.

But above all, the character must impress me. These are simply guidelines to help characters be approved. The most important part is that the character has to be a good, no, EXCEPTIONAL character.

However, I've just realized a problem: Zatorak, Sharpie, and Major are the only ones regularly reading, so I probably won't get very many bios. Ah, well.

So, here's how it will work:

Starting now, any characters can be sent. Senders will recieve a JAMMERS! message telling them whether or not they qualify, and if they don't, why not. Qualifications will, for the most part, not be rule-based but judgement-based.
Qualifications will end at the posting of the second chapter(not counting the prologue) of Book Three. At that point, NO MORE characters will be accepted! This is to prevent the problems that have happened before, with people posting bios long after the part I needed characters for has passed.
At that point, I will choose one or two of the qualified characters to be fully accepted for Book Three. Results will then be announced, and the character(s) will appear in the story.
I repeat, the deadline is the second chapter of Book Three! I really don't think I can stress this enough...

Reading but not posting is an art.
