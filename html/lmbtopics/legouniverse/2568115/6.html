Part 15: A Decision Made
The report from Agent Draconius had been… interesting.
Warnoglee.
The Commander never thought he’d hear that name again.
Warnoglee.
Rikerone had to get out of there.
Warnoglee.
The man-eaters were being reborn.
Warnoglee.
The most dangerous and evil maelstrom creatures in existence.
WARNOGLEE!
The Commander jolted upright as if he had just woken up from a nightmare. In a way, he had. He read through Draco’s report again. There was a secret laboratory at the site of the race, but it didn’t make sense. Typhonus could just as easily operate that laboratory without having to hold a racing tournament around it. His thoughts turned to Rikerone. She was far too headstrong and stubborn to survive a Warnoglee attack. Agent Storm had dropped contact with him, and he suspected that Storm was going rogue. The Commander found that ironic. It had to happen at some point, though. Storm had always been on the line between Imagination and Maelstrom. It seemed he had crossed that line.
The Commander admitted to himself that he only liked Riker because of her parents. Tom and Rachel Sprite had been good agents. The best. Hazel, or Rikerone as she called herself, had their skills but not their mental discipline. The girl was a volcano waiting to happen. It had shocked him when she became a Daredevil; her whole family had been Assembly Summoners. Even HappyDoodle42, who was too young to join a faction, wore a wizard hat! Yet Riker became a Daredevil. A good one, too. A number of the N.F.I.A.’s operatives used Rank 4 gear, but somehow Rikerone, without the thousands of resources they had, had reached Rank 5. As far as the Commander knew, a Rank 6 kit could rival the Faction Leaders in power. Then there were the secret 4th faction kits… LordBrockTree’s Hero was the only one he had ever seen, and that was just from Draco’s visor, which displayed whatever he saw back at HQ. He decided that if Golden Boiling Storm had gone rogue, then he had no choice.
“While I do not want to send another agent into the Race, do that I must. Is Agent Crossfire ready?”

---Rikerone the Wild-Rider---

Part 16
The Rank 3 Knight oversaw two workers moving a barrier to the entrance of the experimental part of “The Ultimate Race”.
“Move it little to the left!” he said. He suddenly heard engines. Loud engines. He turned just in time to see a girl riding a green skull bike heading towards him. That same girl crashed the bike through the barrier. Actually, she didn’t even seem to notice it was there. He yelled after her,
“You rabble-rousing young punk! I’ll straighten you out yet!”
“Many have tried that. I’m one of them.”
“Who are you?” the knight asked.
“Draconius. Duncan Draconius.”
“I’m ChampionVictoryPixel.”
“Bronson Jarl’s son?”
“Pre-pare ta get smashed!!!!!”
Draconius left, as people often do when hit on the head with a Power Jouster. Five exclamation marks. Whoa.
Rikerone was in the process of enjoying her new bike when she lost it. To be specific, she had just been yanked off of it by an invisible force. A female Inventor was levitating her bike above her, and looked at Riker haughtily.
Hey, I know you,” Riker said, “You’re Raven, right?”
“I am called that,” the mysterious girl responded, “But to most I am known as Crossfire.”
“What did you do?”
“You mean this?” Crossfire tapped her helmet. “Rank 4. Telekinesis. Enjoying using the ultimate weapon, were you?”
“The WHAT?”
“Valiance. Don’t tell me you didn’t know what this is!” She thrust back her head and laughed into the sky. Riker was angry about Crossfire having taken her bike, frustrated because she couldn’t get it back, and enraged from Crossfire laughing at her. And as Rover will tell you, NEVER make the Queen of Thieves that mad. Rikerone had a closely guarded secret, one that even Happy didn’t know about. Crossfire was too busy laughing to notice the clouds gathering behind her, just as they had when Golden Boiling Storm walked onto the racetrack. She had been afraid then. These, however, were storm clouds. Rikerone thrust her head to the sky. It was time to make it rain.

---Rikerone the Wild-Rider---
