Part 22: Rise of the Warnoglee
Draconius had gotten away, but that didn’t matter to Hazel now. She viewed the racetrack from the LIONHEART, and did not like what she saw. Huge maelstrom lobsters were attacking the racers and audience, and the minifigures were losing. Their pincers took down several minifigures with each hit, and any that were eaten by this new kind of maelstrom creature didn’t rebuild. Furthermore, Nexus Force weapons didn’t seem to have any effect on them. “Rover,” she said, not at all sure of what she was doing, “Get the ship close to them, but not in the range of their claws.”
“Roger that, Riker!” Rover turned to operate the wheel, and found a Rank 2 Buccaneer with half-pants (the kind that keep you from moving your legs) in his way. “Who are you?” Rover asked him.
“I’m Captain Walter, the greatest pirate that ever lived! This is my ship, you’re on, matey!”
Rikerone sighed and picked up Walter with one hand. “If you’re the greatest pirate that ever lived, why are you Rank 2?”
“Well, I- er, you see… AAAAAAAAUUUUUUUGHHHHHHHHHH!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!”
Rover saw Rikerone come back without Captain Walter and he said, “You threw him off the side, didn’t you?” Rikerone looked at him with a giant grin on her face. Rover then proceeded to steer the ship towards the maelstrom lobsters. Once the prow was level with one of the beasts, a blast of flame shot out of the lion figurehead’s enormous mouth. It really should have at least gotten the creature’s attention.
“RIKERONE!!!”
She turned, and couldn’t believe what she saw. An army of Rank 3 Knights was charging toward the maelstrom, and at its head was a familiar figure in blindingly golden armor.
“LORDBROCKTREE! YOU SURVIVED!”
"Of course I survived! Did you think something like that would kill me? It’s dangerous to fight the Warnoglee alone! Take this!” He tossed a golden orb toward her, and at her touch the RUTCARVER MKII was rebuilt.
“But… you have Verminbane…”
“Rikerone, that bike is no more Valiance than the Verminbane is, and no less. The original was lost years ago. However, its maker made more.”
One of the Knights crashed into the Warnoglee with a custom built car, and started an endless stream of repartee against his silent foes. “You call THIS maelstrom!? I remember back on Crux Prime, we had REAL maelstrom there! Why, in my day…”Needless to say, the Warnoglee were not prepared for ChampionVictoryPixel.
Yet even with LordBrockTree handing out Valiances, Champion confusing them, and the thousands of Nexus Force warriors who had arrived, hundreds of each kit, they still couldn’t damage the Warnoglee. Rikerone was trying to find their weakness when she saw another cloud of maelstrom on the horizon. At the center was a warrior armored by a black hole, his armor was so black it seemed to become one with the darkness. She saw Golden Boiling Storm kneel before the newcomer, and heard a single sentence from him: “We have unleashed the Warnoglee, Lord Orion.”

Part 21: Lionheart
Rover’s Hinterlander had spun out of control when he was hit by Draconius. He barely survived the crash, just to see Draco in pursuit of Rikerone. No one was going to do that. No one. Rover activated his dash ability and ran toward them, but they were too far away. Instead he used his super jump ability to reach the top of a building, attempting to gain a temporary aerial advantage. He saw Draconius aiming his blaster at her, and heard snatches of their conversation. Your majesty? Was Draconius, or Vax as he now called himself, joking or did he actually think Riker was some kind of princess? What was it he called her? Hazel? Rover knew that Rikerone couldn’t be her real name, but he hadn’t expected it to be Hazel. He stood up, first checking that the wind was right for dramatic effect. Vax’s made himself the villain here, he thought, show him a valiant hero and he’ll lose his resolve.
He leaped down, swinging his sword at Vax, but to his surprise it bounced off of his blaster. Rover suddenly remembered to keep up the playful chatter that was expected of him.
“How’d it do that? A Rank 3 Buccaneer cutlass is supposed to cleave through almost all pieces of armor, the exception being the Knight gear, it can’t make a scratch in that.”
“Fool, I’ve had mine modified,” Vax said as he lashed out with his foot, knocking Rover down. He shot the Pistol and Cutlass out of Rover’s hands, but Rover responded with a similar kick, wrestling with Vax for control of his guns. Suddenly Rover’s lion pet King leaped onto Vax, tearing his helmet off. He had black hair and eyes of the same color, and Rover had the feeling that he’d seen his face before. Vax knocked Rover down again, and threw King off him.
There was a single shot.
Rikerone saw the flash of blue shoot from Draco’s gun. She saw King smash into a million pieces. First my parents, she thought to herself, then LordBrockTree, now King. Always those I didn’t expect to get smashed. She was overwhelmed by a desire to destroy Draconius, Vax. But she couldn’t stop him.
However, she thought, I know who can.
She hesitated. Why do I hesitate? Her every instinct screamed at her: Because he’s a Buccaneer! A pirate! It doesn’t matter, she decided at last. It doesn’t matter. She activated the magic inside of her, channeled it toward Rover. He shone with a blinding light and, when the light subsided, he was very different.
His pirate hat had doubled in size, and was combined with a skull mask that hid his face. His bandolier had gained a crossbones design, and his pirate coat had become black, as well as his hat. His cutlass was almost too big for him to hold, and gained a bejeweled gold hilt. His pistol was now a full-fledged cannon, which levitated beside his left hand. She had upgraded him to the first Rank 5 Buccaneer.
Suddenly a colossal pirate ship appeared, flying in the air. Its figurehead was a stylized lion. Rover pulled her onto it, and they sailed on the LIONHEART.

Part 23: The Queen’s Army
Rover steered the LIONHEART toward Orion, while Riker drove the MKII above the crowd of Warnoglee. She hadn’t tried it before, but it didn’t surprise her that it drove on thin air as easily as it did on land. There had to be a way to defeat the Warnoglee. To say that Hazel was angry about this sudden lack of power would be an understatement the size of a blimp. Rikerone tried to control her emotions. If Samurazors didn’t hurt these things, a lightning bolt probably wouldn’t either. What was their weakness? Everything had one. Finally she noticed something. The Nexus Force warriors fought in groups of the same faction kit, not intermingling.
“Power, building, exploration, and science are all just different aspects of imagination...” she said to no one in particular, “The Warnoglee are so heavily corrupted, they can only be smashed with PURE imagination!”
Suddenly she knew how to defeat them.
“ALL TROOPS SPLIT INTO GROUPS OF FOUR! ONE FACTION EACH MEMBER! COMBINE YOUR WEAPONS!”
A Ranger, an Inventor, a Daredevil, and a Marauder heard her, and aimed all of their weapons at the same target, with the Daredevil charging up his. They shot at the same time, and the Warnoglee smashed like maelstrom are supposed to. The other troops started to get the message as well, and used the same tactic. She yelled orders to push forward, drive them back.
She had heard about the laboratory from Draconius, and she knew that, unlike regular stromlings, the Darkitect could only make so many Warnoglee. Although there were hundreds of them, they were finite in number. In fact, Draconius had only seen…
Draconius. She tried not to think about him. But the harder she tried, the more she remembered. She put those thoughts aside. There would be plenty of time to feel depressed after the Warnoglee were wiped out. She saw four lights in the distance. Surely that couldn’t be…
A siege engine. A pirate ship. An Exo-Force-style mech. A shadowy dragon.
The Faction Leaders had arrived.
She turned back to the maelstrom cloud. Storm and his Punishers were making trouble for Rover. She accelerated the MKII. It was time to finish this.

Part 24: The Face of Orion
Part 1
Usually the RUTCARVER MKII is somewhat intimidating. When its machine guns start firing, the target should at least respond. Yet Orion did not. He seemed slightly amused. He reached out with his right hand, and a tendril of maelstrom energy struck Riker, knocking over the MKII. The LIONHEART appeared behind her, and Rover unleashed a blast of his Rank 5 cannon. Again, no effect. Rikerone was getting tired of invincible foes. What was with this guy? She knew he was Draco’s father, but unlike Draco, Orion appeared to have maelstrom completely at his disposal. She charged at him, but was knocked back by another chaotic tentacle. Orion laughed, and removed his helmet.

Rover now knew where he had seen Draco’s face before.
Draconius was the son of the Darkitect.
Part 2
Golden Boiling Storm knew he had to act while the Baron was distracted with the girl. He stood at the center of the cloud of maelstrom, holding up one of the Valiances that LordBrockTree had obtained. At his touch it turned into five shadow orbs, which circled around him, drawing power from the maelstrom… There was a flash of energy, and one of the orbs froze solid and fell to the ground, shattering. Storm turned to see his second in command holding his own Valiance.
“Solid Frozen Warrior. So you are the one who disrupts my plan. So be it.”
Part 3
“Get out of here, Rover!”Rikerone yelled above the roar of the storm.
“What about you?”
“I’m going to try to stop Typhonus!”
“AND HOW TO YOU PLAN TO DO THAT?”
Another blast of maelstrom knocked Hazel down. She realized that the Baron was right. She didn’t know how to defeat him. The Darkitect thickened the storm, tossing Rover and the LIONHEART out of it. Rikerone was trapped in the center of the storm. She charged at the Baron, but he swatted her aside. He disappeared in an instant, reappearing behind her and knocking her back down as she was getting up again. She lacked weapons, but she wasn’t helpless. She hit the Darkitect with a roundhouse kick, but his corruption had made him as hard as steel and Riker only hurt herself. “I need a weapon,” she thought to herself.”
A weapon…
She had upgraded gear before, that was no problem. But this… she was trying to create something out of thin air. She didn’t have time to think about whether or not it was possible. She just had to do it. She focused the magic inside of her as she had done so many times before, but this time, instead of letting it flow she compressed it together, gave it physical shape in her hand. What she held was a golden sword, long and slender, with strange runes on the side like Verminbane. She swung the sword, and found it was so sharp it cut through time and space, leaving a hole in the dimension. She pointed the tip of the sword, and a beam of pure imagination shot from it, disintegrating the maelstrom it hit.
This was the true Valiance. The Sword of Imagination.
She charged at the Darkitect with it, then… she thrust it behind her, and heard a satisfying scream.
Part 4
Rover was aboard the LIONHEART arguing with one of the Faction Leaders.
“Sir, the Darkitect himself is in that cloud. If we strike now, we can destroy him. We have to get in there and rescue Riker!”
“If your friend is battling Typhonus, she’s as good as dead,” Duke Exeter’s gaze was firm, his decision absolute, “We can’t afford a frontal assault against him.”
“Look- wait, what’s that?” He pointed to the cloud of maelstrom, which was going out of control, slowly expanding… just out of it… a legendary bike, ridden by a Daredevil.
Rover, LordBrockTree, SolidFrozenWarrior, and the Faction Leaders all yelled in unison,
“RIKERONE!!!!!”
Part 5
She rode like a storm, but the maelstrom surge was relentless. The MARKII responded easily to Riker’s touch, but it could not outrun the maelstrom. It was a race. It was an epic race for survival.

The ultimate race.

Just as the maelstrom was about to reach her, she and the bike along with her started to glow with a golden light. Her now useless Daredevil armor was replaced by golden armor and cape which shone with light, charged by pure imagination. Her headgear as well… “I’m wearing a crown, aren’t I?” she thought to herself, too busy steering the RUTCARVER to check. “I always hated crowns.” She knew what she had to do. It was time to press the special button.
