I'm actually going to fill you, fearless reader, in on my writing process here. I almost always know the general outline- they go here, do this, etc. Most often, I have some events planned out and some not. In some cases, though, I have to do something on the spot because I realize that what I had previously planned won't work.
For example, Book Two was originally going to end with Rikerone being trapped inside the ENTIZONE, having split into two seperate beings. I think I like the new ending more, and I hope that you, fearless reader, will as well.

Zatorak was one of those cases- his demise came from a realization on my part: That Zatorak was an interdimensional time-traveller. That made it very hard to believe that he would have met Hazel, much less been her partner. So, I decided to use that incident instead to help develop the 'Frezija' plotline. After all, if the Frezija itself believed it was Zatorak, what other forms might they take?

The question now is; What will happen in the core of the Entizone? You'll have to find out, fearless reader! Just as soon as I finish the chapter...
