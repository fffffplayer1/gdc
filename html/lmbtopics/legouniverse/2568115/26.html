Part 17 'Proper Respect'
Frezija had known it was coming but she had asked anyway. She knew the danger, but she just didn’t want to sit around Nexus Tower being useless. Frezija held Ms. Sprite in awe. She had been exceedingly surprised to learn that the Valiance Leader was only a few years older than herself. Furthermore, she owed Ms. Sprite. Still, she didn’t see why the Faction Leaders would keep restricting her, when there had to be a way…
A sudden thud brought her back to reality.
It had come from Ms. Sprite’s office.
*************************************************************************************
Ow. My head. The problem with the Dreams is that, not only do they really break up conversations, but I also can’t exactly control how I land very well. I slowly picked myself up…just to see Frezija rush into the room.
“My lady!”
Oh no. Not this again. Frezija cannot resist calling me by some kind of honorific. “DON’T call me that! Whatever you call me, don’t call me THAT! No “Your Majesty”s, either!”
“Sorry, My La-I mean, Ms. Sprite!”
“Look, just call me Hazel, okay? Just because you’re in my faction doesn’t mean you can’t call me by my name.”
“With all due respect, Ms. Sprite, I think that it DOES mean that.”
‘With all due respect’… Frezija is so respectful it drives me crazy. I just KNOW she’d curtsy if I’d let her. Curtsy! The most diabolical invention known to minifigures! But she would do it! She would do it if she thought it showed ‘proper respect’ to ‘her Lady’!
Insanity. And she wonders why I don’t let her do field work.
Have you ever met someone who is so polite it drives you absolutely crazy? That person is Frezija.
“Are you okay, mistress?”
Mistress, now? Ugh. “I’m fine. I just dozed off for a second.”
I was very grateful when Frezija left. We’re so much alike, and yet so different… Of course, I remember back when I used to insist on calling my father ‘sir’. It drove him crazy. Just like Frezija drives me crazy…

“Hazel.”

“Hello, Duke.”
