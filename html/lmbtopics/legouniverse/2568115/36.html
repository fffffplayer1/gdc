Part 26: The Voyage
Couldn’t this thing go any faster? If I had my bike, I could just-
No, actually I couldn’t. Sure, I could get there faster, but then what? It’s not a multi-minifigure transport, and what if the LIONHEART is broken? There’d be no way to bring all of us back. Charging in like a maniac wouldn’t help here. Ugh!
I suddenly stopped myself from punching the wall. The point of taking this transport was to not be noticed. That was also why I was using my Daredevil gear instead of that ridiculously shiny special armor.
I am glad to be using my old armor, though. I almost feel like myself again.
*************************************************************************************
Hiding in the freighter’s hold, Frezija waited. Something had clicked in her brain, like someone had just flipped a switch. It all made sense now. The war between the Nexus and the Maelstrom, the Darkitect, the Rikerone, the future. Frezija knew why she had made by the scientists now. She understood. And she knew what she had to do.

She had to summon the Volom.
