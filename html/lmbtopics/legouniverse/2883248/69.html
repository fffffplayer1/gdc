Bird: "Welcome to my world, my friend. I am actually not this bird. I am someone you know, but if I revealed myself, you would turn away. I know you have many questions. But I am here to answer only one of them: "who are you?" And by "you," I mean you, Slicer the Vorzakh.*

Slicer told him that he was under arrest on the order of Makuta Torix. Slicer suddenly realized why he knew this place. He felt sick when Trona lowered his hood and showed his mask.
