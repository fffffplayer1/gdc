LEGO Universe story: Project Super Nex

 

Chapter 146

 

They were transported swiftly to the cafeteria. Delicious, exotic smells filled the air. Cooper said, "If I were a dog, I would be drooling right now." Penelope threw him a odd look. They saw tables with big plates of food that were familiar and also some that were completely new to them, such as trafkas. Mr. Huron told them that a trafka is a layered bread pocket that holds different meats and spices. Penelope, Wyatt, and Peyton were all willing to try the new foods out, while Cooper, Corbin, and Gene, were a bit more skeptical, and stuck to the foods that looked familiar - like roast chicken and steaks. Once their plates were filled, they ate so quickly as if they hadn't eaten for weeks. "Mm...thftt...so....mm...good...," Gene managed to make out as he chomped on a piece of steak.

 

When they were done, they rubbed their bellies with satisfaction. Mr. Huron said, "Now, we need to go to the library to meet the miners to discuss the plans with them." The library was enormous. Tall, vaulted ceilings with windows illuminated the rooms and rows and rows of large bookcases filled every nook and cranny. Mr. Huron said, "Ah, there they are." He led them to the group of twenty-two miners who were talking lively amongst themselves. Mr. Huron introduced everyone, and Corbin asked with curiosity, "What devices do you plan to bring for this mission?" The nearest Lunatarkian miner, whose name was Exparto, said, "We will be bringing our trademark drillers. They will be able to drill the rocks with the proper force and pipe the protana to the outside. We will have some of the miners stay outside so they can make sure that the materials are piped out properly." Then Gene asked, "Will we have any communication devices?" Exparto nodded, and pulled out six small earpiece devices and handed them to the minifigures. "They can also be used to contact this temple, so we can transfer any information about what we find in Boraker."

Chapter 147

 

Then another miner, who went by the name of Gydar, said with a warning tone, "You need to beware of the dangers in Boraker. It is certainly more safer than the other mountains that can be found around here, but it still has its flaws. There are a few native creatures that can be less- than-friendly, although if you know how to interact with them properly, they will not harm you. There are also earthquakes that can cause the tunnels to collapse." Wyatt asked, "Shouldn't there be safety measures against that?" Mr. Huron nodded and answered, "Yes, that's why we have these." Exparto pulled out a medium-sized device from a toolbox next to him. It was a silver tube with a gray orb inside the middle, and a small computer screen built onto one end. He explained, "This device contains a moldable metal that can be sprayed onto walls. When the metal solidifies in a few seconds, it will make the wall very stiff and stable. This will make it so that the cave tunnels will not cave in. Most of the time."

 

Gene asked to see the tube, and turned it over in his hands. "Now this is cool!" he exclaimed.
The group continued to discuss their plans for the mission for the next couple of hours. Gydar went over safety and emergency issues and the minifigures listened intently. They were going to go on their adventure tomorrow and into the tunnels of Boraker. They would have to get out enough protana to be able to stop the Maelstrom, and it would all be piped out of the mountain into large containers. Then they would transport the containers back home through the teleportation device that they was used to get to Lunatark. When Wyatt got back to his room, he felt very excited about the expedition, but was also nervous as well. Hopefully, the Maelstrom would not figure out where they were or what they were doing. Chapter 148

 

It was nighttime at LEGO Universe and most everyone was fast asleep. Most everyone included the Maelstrom. The base was buzzing with activity. Baron Typhonus walked along a walkway set above a training area, observing the army sparring and using different Maelstrom weapons. He came to a door and pushed it open. There sat Naazang in front of a computer, busily typing away. "What are you doing?" Typhonus asked. Naazang stopped typing and said, "Look for yourself." He saw several outlines of the Maelstrom beings, such as the Stromlings and the Maelstrom Apes, with purple lines radiating away from them. Naazang said, "The Super Nex virus will be used to lead us right to Wyatt Cortone."

 

"The virus causes its organisms to create unique energy signatures that can be tracked fairly easily. One cannot hide these signatures, no matter what kind of technology one uses. Therefore, if we use the proper device, we will be able to track Wyatt's whereabouts. There is one flaw, but we can work around it. The device that I have can track the Super Nex organisms more easily if they are in the same dimension. Otherwise, it will be harder to acquire the information. Since Wyatt is in Lunatark now, it's taking a little longer. But rest assured, he will be found." Typhonus replied, "Even if we find his exact location in Lunatark, we have no way to get there!" Naazang answered with a satisfied grin, "Aha, well you see Typhonus, I have some contacts within Lunatark that will be able to help us with that."

There were other sentences that could be heard, but they were too fuzzy. Both Naazang and Typhonus could hear certain familiar names, such as Wyatt and Penelope. Typhonus, with his eyes transfixed on the screen and his ears straining to hear every word, said excitedly, "There he is! That's Wyatt!"

Chapter 150

 

Naazang nodded with excitement and said, "See? I believe that they are in a meeting with the Lunatark residents." The screen flickered, and then a fuzzy image appeared. When Wyatt woke up the next morning, it took him a few moments to realize in wonder that they were starting the mining expedition today. He was nervous and excited about the mission, and glad to finally get the cure for the virus. When he got out of bed, he realized that he hadn't contacted Dr. Folson. He was probably concerned about them, so Wyatt called him with his dimensional phone that Xavier gave to everyone. Dr. Folson didn't answer, which Wyatt found minutely disconcerting. But he shook it off and got ready. The group went to eat a hearty breakfast and then met with the miners, following them inside an oval transportation pod that would take them to Boraker. As the pod approached the mountain, the group could see many small caves along the mountain's base. Expartos explained, "We'll use the most recent cave that was drilled for the last mission, since that will be the most stable." When the pod reached Boraker, they walked out and Expartos pointed to one the closest one and said, "We will start there."

 

When they went inside, they saw many veins of glowing orange minerals embedded in the cave. They trailed all along the walls and gave much needed light to the dark cave. A few of the workers were spraying the metal material on the walls to keep them from collapsing on them. There were a few small, horned, lizard-like creatures who were scuttling across the ceiling, but these, Expartos informed the group, weren't dangerous at all. Penelope asked, "How far until we get to the protana?" Expartos explained, "About one mile underneath this mountain. Then we will be able to find large protana deposits. After that, we will transport them to the carts, so we can them outside." Indeed, when the group got about one mile underneath Boraker, there were some rays of yellow that were coming from the other end of the tunnel. They began to traverse more deeply into the tunnel and were getting more excited about the protana.

Chapter 152

 

They finally reached the end of the tunnel, and there was a massive amount of the glowing yellow protana lying in the bottom of a small ditch and they emitted shining rays of light. Expartos unloaded a few small buckets from the supplies. He gestured towards the protana and said, "This is what we've been looking for. Everyone gather round!" One of the miners explained, "We are going to attach these mechanical arms to the buckets and then suspend them with metal rope, maneuvering them carefully into the ditch. The mechanical arms will be remotely controlled to collect the protana. Then the buckets will be pulled back up and loaded onto these carts." As the buckets were filled and the carts were loaded, Wyatt felt a sense of excitement. He looked over at his friends and could sense their relief that things were going as planned. Expartos was now activating the rail system for the carts to move out and they began to clunk their way up the tunnel. When the carts got outside, the three members contacted Expartos by radio and one of them said, "The carts got outside safely. We're going to work on filtering them now."

 

Meanwhile, the rest of the crew were still underground, continuing to gather the protana, when suddenly, there was a slight shudder that vibrated the length of the entire ground. Gene asked in a wary voice, "What was that? Was it an earthquake?" Not looking concerned at all, Expartos shook his head and said, "Don't worry. Those kinds of tremors happen a lot here. A natural earthquake is rare in these parts." This put the group at ease, and they continued working. But then, a larger shudder shook the ground, making one of the carts fall over onto its side. Wyatt looked around and asked cautiously, "Expartos, are you sure that this is safe?" The miner now had a concerned expression on his muddied-up face and he replied tentatively, "I have to admit, that tremor was larger than normal."

The shudder ended very quickly, but the clicks continued to echo in the tunnel. They looked around as the clicks suddenly got louder and then abruptly stopped. He tried pressing the button over and over again, but nothing was happening.

 

"My communicator won't turn on. I can't talk to the other members." Wyatt checked his device and discovered that his wouldn't turn on, either. LEGO Universe story: Project Super Nex

 

Chapter 156

 

The tunnel was caving in, as large rocks broke from the soil and began to plug up the path.
Everyone made a mad dash towards the exit. Expartos made it out first, followed by the rest of the Lunatark miners, and followed closely by Cooper and Penelope. Bringing up the rear were Corbin, Peyton, Wyatt, and Gene. As Wyatt and Gene neared the exit, a violent blast shook the tunnel. The soil was imploding around them. In desperation, Wyatt used his super strength and hurled Gene through the end of the tunnel. The second after Wyatt made this move, more rocks fell and completely caved in the path. There was a huge amount of dust that came up from the final collapse of the rocks, and everyone stood back. As Corbin and Penelope helped Gene up from the ground, they all looked towards the cave. As the debris settled, they strained their eyes, looking for Wyatt.

 

But he wasn't there. "WHERE is he?!!!" exclaimed Gene. Penelope and Peyton were sobbing and Cooper and Corbin looked shocked at the sudden loss of their friend. Expartos gave a saddened sigh as he placed his hand on Gene's shoulder. "I am sorry for this. Wyatt - he seemed to me to be a special being." Another loud blast echoed though the mountains. Off in the distance, by the water's edge - something large was coming towards the shore. It appeared to be a submarine that was now lodged up on the banks, looking like a beached whale. Expartos whispered ominously, "The Terraras are coming." Cooper turned to Expartos with a frown, and said, "Well that doesn't sound good. What are you talking about?" Expartos shook his head and replied, "They are a ruthless, greedy clan. Troublemakers to say the least. Be careful - they are not to be trusted."
