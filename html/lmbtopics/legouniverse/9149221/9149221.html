LEGO Universe Story: Project Super Nex

 

Chapter 1

 

Inside Nimbus Station, there was a huge crowd. They were waiting to hear what scientific achievement the famous Dr. Grant Folson was going reveal. As he stepped up to the microphone, he waved to everybody and said "Hello, citizens of LEGO Universe!" Everybody clapped their hands and Dr. Folson continued. "I must say I'm very excited to reveal this discovery. My team and I have been working for the past four years to perfect this creation." While he was talking, he pressed a button on a small remote and hologram popped up. The hologram showed a group of microbes that were glowing blue. "These are viruses that have been genetically modified for what I like to call Project: Super Nex." Dr. Folson said. "My team and I were able to birth viruses that had the powerful Imagination inside them. When these Super Nex viruses are into the all members of the four Nexus factions, they will give great abilities to the minifigures. Minifigures will be stronger, faster, smarter. But there is another power that I consider to be the most important!" 


Dr. Folson pressed another button on his remote and a second hologram popped up. It showed a minifigure who was already injected with the Super Nex virus in a controlled experiment. A blue mist started growing around his hands and then, he started building a massive tower out of nothing! Dr. Folson said excitedly "As you can see, the virus also endows the great gift of creating objects out of nothing! Tomorrow, more people will be injected with the virus and if all goes well, in a few weeks minifigures in the four factions will be given these amazing powers!" Everybody cheered, excited by this discovery. But as Dr. Folson was giving his speech, a mysterious person was watching this through a purple mist. "Ha! Those viruses won't be doing good once I get my hands on them." Then he called to somebody, "Naazang, are the weapons prepared yet?" Naazang growled "Yes, my master."

Project Super Nex

 

Chapter 2

 

Inside Dr. Folson's research facility, Dr. Folson and his research team were preparing for the next day's experiment. The lab was buzzing like a busy bee nest with everybody preparing the viruses. Dr. Folson called out to his closest assistant Wyatt Cortone, "Wyatt, I need you to prepare the next set of Super Nex viruses!" "Coming," Wyatt said. He was about carry a set of beakers when there was a loud sound, a combination of crashing and booming. Wyatt went to the source of the sound, near the front entrance and discovered something absolutely horrifying. There was a whole group Stromlings, Dark Spiderlings, Dark Mechs and Stromling Admirals ready to attack.

 

Back in the main lab, an assistant was helping Dr. Folson when Wyatt came running back into the lab screaming, "Maelstrom!!!!" Following him was a Maelstrom army, indeed! Dr. Folson sounded the alarm to alert the Nexus factions of this emergency. He called out to everybody, "Grab the Super Nex viruses and evacuate the lab immediately!" Wyatt ran to a medium-sized case of the viruses when five Stromlings surrounded him. Luckily, he always kept his Dyna-Mighty gun with for situations like this and was able to blast apart the Stromlings quickly.

 

Wyatt grabbed the case and went outside to transport it to a lab truck. Then he went back inside to grab another case when he saw a Dark Mech run in and grab Dr. Folson! "Help me!!" Dr. Folson yelled. Wyatt was aiming of the robot when another Dark Mech shot the gun out of his hands. Wyatt ran to grab the weapon, but a Stromling got to it first. The Stromling started shooting at Wyatt, but Wyatt was able to run fast enough to get to the basement. He knew that there were more extra weapons down there, but can he get them fast enough to save Dr. Folson?

Chapter 3

 

Wyatt was able to find a box of weapons in the basement, although they were all prototype designs built by Dr. Folson. Most of them were had never been used before so Wyatt would have to be careful when using them. He grabbed a backpack and stuffed the prototypes inside. Then he ran back upstairs and discovered that there was no Maelstrom enemies inside the lab but there was also no Super Nex cases. "Oh no..." Wyatt said to himself.

 

He ran outside to discover that everybody in the army was carrying a box of Super Nex viruses and running towards a purplish-black tornado. Wyatt needed to try to retrieve those boxes! He was running towards two Dark Spiderlings when he saw the Dark Mech carrying Dr. Folson. Wyatt immediately pulled out a gun from his backpack and shot out a metal hook attached to a rope made out of super-strong fibers. The hook attached to the Dark Mech's left leg and then the hook pulled automatically, taking the robot with it. After the Dark Mech slid to a stop next to Wyatt, he got a sword out of his backpack. The sword was made out of an impervious metal alloy that Dr. Folson created. Before the Dark Mech could react, Wyatt stabbed the robot's torso effectively shutting it down. Wyatt helped Dr. Folson to his feet and Dr. Folson said graciously "Thank you very much, I don't know what would've happened to me if you didn't step in." "You're very welcome" Wyatt responded. "But you better get back inside where it's safer. I'll try to hold them off." Dr. Folson nodded in response and ran back to the main lab.

 

Wyatt turned just in time to see two Stromling Admirals race towards him! Wyatt grabbed his sword and used it to knock the first Admiral's sword out of his hands. Then Wyatt slashed the Admiral into pieces and did the same with the second Admiral. But then, three Stromlings and two Dark Mechs surrounded him. Could Wyatt escape from this?

LEGO Universe story: Project Super Nex

 

Chapter 4

 

Wyatt grabbed for his hook launcher, but when he did that, there was a loud cracking sound inside the invention. "Come on, not now!" said Wyatt. He dropped it and got another prototype out of his backpack and aimed it at a Dark Mech. It was red and ejected explosive fireballs. But when Wyatt pressed the trigger, the gun become really hot; it was malfunctioning. Wyatt instinctively threw it, and it landed on the other Dark Mech's right foot. The Dark Mech gave out a weird, growling yell and was hopping around trying cool down its foot. The other Dark Mech then ripped off Wyatt's backpack and threw it away, leaving Wyatt with no defense. The enemies were about to attack when bolts of electricity shocked them. Wyatt looked around and saw that the Nexus defenses had finally come!

 

The minifigures were battling the Maelstrom army and the Nexus war planes were whizzing by,
giving aerial offense. Soon enough, the remaining remnants of the army had been defeated but more than half of the army had disappeared into the purple tornado, which had dispersed by now. Only 8 out of the 90 Super Nex cases were saved. When Wyatt ran back to the lab, he saw what a mess it was. Beakers were broken, tables had been thrown around and there were holes in the ceiling and walls. Wyatt saw Dr. Folson sitting on the floor, hands covering his face. Wyatt walked over to Dr. Folson and asked him "Are you okay?" Dr. Folson responded "No, I'm not okay. Because of this robbery, we can't complete the experiment tomorrow!" They stood there for a few seconds thinking about this tragedy. "Why do you think they stole the viruses?" Wyatt asked. "I don't know." Dr. Folson answered.

 

If only they knew what the viruses were going to be used for...

Chapter 6

 

Wyatt woke up the next morning with a groggy feeling. He decided to go jogging in Nimbus Plaza to beat the grogginess. He boarded his rocket and launched to Nimbus Plaza. Wyatt was going to continue jogging when a purple mist appeared on the road ahead of him. There was a strong wind growing around the mist. But a few seconds later, it suddenly stopped. The mist dissipated to reveal 5 Maelstrom minions that looked like they could have been Stromlings.

 

Except these creatures were very transformed.

  

Wyatt was able to see that these "Stromlings" had 3 eyes, 2 extra arms with missile launchers replacing the hands, and they were about 6 feet tall. Each Stromling also wore a belt. Attached to the belt was a small blue ball. Wyatt didn't know what that was, but he knew one thing. These new Stromlings were not things he wanted to battle. He immediately turned around and ran as fast as he could, warning every minifigure he passed by about this new threat. As Wyatt was running he saw one of the Stromlings climbing on top of a building. When the Stromling saw Wyatt, he prepped his missile launcher and fired it at Wyatt. Wyatt turned around just in time to see this and jumped out of the way into a bush. Then the Stromling jumped down causing everybody in that area to panic and run away in a frenzy. The Stromling's right hand started forming what looked like a hammer. Then the Stromling raised the hammer into the air, but before he could smash it down Wyatt threw his sword at the Stromling's hand. This caused the Stromling to yell and drop his hammer. Wyatt then grabbed his sword and the hammer hoping he could get to his rocket in time.

After the Stromling landed next to Wyatt, he looked up to see that the blue ball was going to fall on him. Chapter 8

 

As the blue liquid absorbed into Wyatt's blood, it surged throughout his entire body. Wyatt first felt a tingling sensation. He felt completely normal. But as one of the Stromlings raced towards him, Wyatt gave a powerful jump and got about 3 extra feet over the 6 foot creature. He landed behind the Stromling and gave a powerful punch to the creature. The attack sent the Stromling flying into a building, where it disintegrated into pieces.

 

The 3 Stromlings that were left, started running towards Wyatt. He put his hands up and shot blue bricks at them. This sent them to the ground, but they were not destroyed. One of the Stromlings got up onto their feet, created a bat, and started racing to Wyatt, ready to swing. But Wyatt formed his own bat, and before the creature could react, Wyatt gave it a hard whack, completely destroying it. The last 2 Stromlings looked completely terrified, and didn't even attempt to battle Wyatt. They simply disappeared in a purple mist. Wyatt stood there for a second, thinking about his newfound powers. "How did I get these powers," he thought to himself. He created a rocket so he could fly back home. He would definitely have to tell Dr. Folson about this new development. When Wyatt returned home, he went immediately to the research lab. Dr. Folson whispered to himself, "Incredible." Wyatt stood there for a few seconds and finally asked, "What do you think?" Dr. Folson replied, "This is incredible! Although I am not sure what the source of your powers are --- ," Dr. Folson stopped suddenly and said, "Wait a second..." He was staring at the wall behind Wyatt. "Hello, anyone there?" Wyatt asked curiously.

Dr. Folson said, "Come with me." He pulled Wyatt towards the stairs.
