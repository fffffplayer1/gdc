LEGO Universe Story: Project Super Nex

 

Chapter 117

 

"WHO are you?!" bellowed Greere. Gene didn't answer. He took the disc from Wyatt's hands and pocketed it. Then he took out his Sharpoise and shot three balls at Greere. But Greere ducked backwards and the projectiles hit a Skeleton Trooper and two Blacksmiths. They all fell to the ground, unconscious. Greere got back up and took out a long, black hammer. He raised it up into the air, then brought it down on the ground, causing a shockwave. They all fell to the ground, bracing themselves and trying to remain steady. Wyatt got up first and shot energy spheres at Greere. But he leapt to the side, avoiding the shots. He then ran to Wyatt and tried to attack him with his hammer, but Wyatt ducked underneath the swing and grabbed the long handle. He wrestled it away from Greere and smacked him squarely on his chest.

 

The blow sent Greere reeling backwards, and he fell face-first onto the rocky ground. Everyone turned off their Holo-Guises and Wyatt handed the crystal to Penelope to put in her satchel. They all looked forward at the exit that led out of Camp Ribspreader. "We need to get there in order to rocket out of here!" Wyatt said as he pointed to the exit. They started the dash to the exit, but two Marksman and a Commando raced in front of them, blocking their path. "You're not going anywhere!" the Commando threatened. He was getting his bone scythe readied, and the Marksmen were prepping their bows and arrows. Then a Marksman aimed his bow at Penelope, and shot two arrows at her. But Penelope swiftly deflected them with katanas she pulled out from her backpack. The metal arrows fell on the ground with a clang, and Penelope ran towards them. One of them tried to shoot more arrows at her, but she deflected them again with katanas. Then she jumped upwards and landed right on top of one of the Marksmen.

By Edmund Burke: "The only thing necessary for the triumph of evil is for good men to do nothing."

Chapter 121

 

When the disc was placed on top of the spot, it clinked against the wooden lid. There was a buzzing sound, that gradually grew louder and then suddenly stopped. He picked it up and he said, "This is it!!" Everyone gave whoops of celebration. Wyatt turned the piece around in his hands. He saw that both ends had symbols that looked like tropical trees with long, broad leaves. Wyatt turned to Penelope. "Can you hand me the other pieces, please?" Penelope nodded and took the pieces out from her bag. She handed them to Wyatt and he took them gently, cradling them in his hands.

 

They all waited for a few moments, expecting a reaction. But there was nothing. They assumed that when they found the crystals, something magical would happen. But nothing was activated. The crystals lay quietly in Wyatt's hand. Gene said, "That's strange. What do you suggest we do now?" Wyatt didn't say anything; he simply shook his head. He decided to give all but one of the pieces back to Penelope and as he held the last piece, he moved the disc closer to it. As he moved the crystal towards the disc, there was some kind of force that Wyatt could feel. It was like the disc was pulling the crystal, or vice versa. Wyatt released the crystal and saw, stunned as the crystal floated to the disc. Then it stuck onto the rim tightly in a vertical position. Gene said, "Okay, well that's something."

Chapter 127

 

Wyatt and his friends were transported through the portal. There was nothing but blackness around them. They strained to press their cuffs and activate their Enviro-Exos. As time went on, a swishing sound could be heard, and then it turned into a buzzing. The blackness gave way to a sliver of light that appeared from a small gap at the other end. They walked towards it cautiously. When they were a few feet away, the light became so unbearably bright that they had to close their eyes and cover their faces entirely. They felt an energy, pulling them in - and there was a minute burning sensation on their skin. Then suddenly, they felt that the light had lessened dramatically, and opened their eyes to an incredible sight. Around them were many blue mountains that rose high into the air. There were cavernous valleys that surrounded the mountains. Shining blue crystals could be seen gleaming from the ground around the team.

 

This part of Lunatark seemed to be desolate except for a few animals that the minifigures had never seen before. One of the animals had the head of a parrot, but the body of an ape, with striking face markings. This animal looked at the group for a second before turning around and running away. In the distance, there was some sort of a city in the horizon. Tall skyscrapers pierced the sky, and around the tips of the skyscrapers were little pods that flew around them. Wyatt looked around, breathless at the beauty of it all. Corbin asked, "So this is Lunatark?" Wyatt nodded. "Yes, it is." Peyton said in amazement, "This is all so beautiful.
