They looked at each other and Gene stammered, "We were at Crux Prime, in the Maelstrom Quarry. That Nuckal there was very brutal." Greere narrowed his eyes. "Oh...really." For some reason, he was having a strange feeling that something was wrong but he couldn't put his finger on it. He then walked away to a group of nearby Catapults, but he was watching them closely.

 

They could sense this and Peyton walked over to Wyatt. She whispered, "What are we going to do? We can't dig up the portal piece with him watching us like a hawk. We're going to get caught!" Wyatt nodded. But he had an idea. Before asking the other's opinions about it, he yelled, "Hey, Greere. You need to come over here! There's some kind of whirring sound!" Greere was alarmed by the warning and came rushing over. "What do you mean? Where is it coming from?" Wyatt pointed to the ground in front of him and said, "Right here. Wait...it seems to have stopped. It was really loud before. That's not good at all." Greere asked, "Why?" "Because there could be some kind of hidden weapon. Or something entirely different. But either way, it could be dangerous." Peyton followed along and said, "Yeah, I heard it, too. I think we should really check the area out." Greere sighed, then said, "I suppose you can dig up the area here. But nothing else!" Wyatt simply nodded and started digging with a shovel.
