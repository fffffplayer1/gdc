LEGO Universe story: Project Super Nex

Chapter 193

"Ever since their last clash, Tyrobe has never appeared again in Lunatark. It was officially concluded that the global organization resorted to selling off their technology to other illegal clans. It is hoped that someday, Lunatark will be able to end their devious schemes." The Hastir's presentation ended and the hologram showed a new menu of sections, withholding more valuable information. Wyatt and Peyton stood still for a moment, as frozen as ice statues. They were both filled with rushes of fascination and astonishment from the detailec data they just learned. Then, Peyton said with amazement, "Wow, that's... It's amazing how far back the history of Tyrobe goes with Lunatark. A century or so, it seems. Or even longer." Wyatt nodded, equally stunned. "Now, we know much more about group. But the thing is, if Lunatark wanted to deter their enemy's progress so badly, why aren't they letting us assist?"

Peyton thought about this question before answering, although her voice had an unsure tone to it, "Perhaps they could have had bad experiences with partnering up with foreigners before. Especially if there's tricky people, like Tyrobe, existing in this world. That would probably make them a lot more wary of teaming up with us." Wyatt replied, "But they have joined with Terreras before. That's what it said in this document. And with the distant behavior of Zlytann..." He stopped talking to carefully piece together his next statement. "I just feel like there's something else. Another element to the puzzle that we're missing." Peyton tried to think of a response, but couldn't - a ruminative expression remained in her amethyst eyes. Eventually Wyatt said, "Well, let's just keep looking through the rest of these documents. We have to find out all we can about Tyrobe." Then the two minifigures devoted themselves to intently studying the rest of the Tyrobe-revolving Hastirs for the rest of the afternoon.

Chapter 194

While Peyton and Wyatt were busy documenting Tyrobe intel at the Lybri, Cooper and Penelope were unveiling their own eye-catching discoveries. Cooper was in the cafeteria in the Lunatark Temple, which had a surprisingly large variety of food after all the destruction that had been caused. There was still some loud construction noise in the room and large amounts of smoke billowed in from defunct machines. Cooper was sitting in the back of the room, sitting down to a hearty meal. There were several plates of large cuts of meat, which he thought would be very filling after the exhausting experience he just went through. He had also squirted several types of sauces on top of each cut, mixing all the sauces together to create unsightly food glops. He was halfway done with the second plate when Penelope came through the entrance. She quickly got her meal and walked around, looking for a free table. Then she witnessed Cooper in the back of the room, and she reluctantly walked over to him.

She said simply, "Hey, Cooper." His head raised up and when he saw Penelope, and he gave a simple nod, mutedly acknowledging her presence. Once Penelope got an eyeful of the food that he had, she said with a teasing tone, "Wow, what are you doing? Seeing how many gallons of food you can gorge in before you toss your cheese?" Cooper dramatically rolled his eyes and defensively replied, "I just went through a very, very strenuous battle. I need to refill my energy stock." Penelope sarcastically rolled her eyes in retort, sat down at the table across from Cooper, and sniped, "I don't know how you can stand to eat that mess." "The same way I've been eating the stuff you've been making," Cooper answered sarcastically. He was referring to the many dishes that Penelope had made in the past for stakeouts and other types of missions.
