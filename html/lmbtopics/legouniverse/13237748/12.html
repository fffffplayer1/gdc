The Fateful Crossroads (Final)

‎05-09-2016 04:10 PM

... No... not all...

 

Still rather sharp, even when broken. I had come to know the Ur-Anonymous likely better than he knew himself, and you are simply a lesser piece still in need of time for development. Every blow we exchanged, I slipped a part of myself into you, carefully undetected. It is how I could read your thoughts, it allowed me to prepare your mind for collapse, and it enabled the disability of your warp drive. I forced you to commit, and by the time our quarrel reached its end, all I needed was a decisive blow. It was over the instant I delivered my first kick.

 

... I... no...

 

I can offer praise to your resilience, however. I was sure I ended you the first time, and after you came back, I needed to start my intrusion all over. But time was never on your side, however much you wish it were.

 

... Nnuuuuuhh...

 

Oh, and one more thing before the reality of your incompetence truly settles in: I will be taking back my Datai. All of them.

 

The Composer and his opponent both disappear in a flash of blue light, reappearing in the space on the opposite side of Spherus Magna, facing the sun. He draws what little remains of The Anonymous closer to him, and says aloud two simple, blunt words:

 

"Future. Wasted."

 

Drawing back, he throws his dissolving counterpart as hard as he can through the void, rocketing him out of sight and straight into the distant gargantuan cauldron of fire that is Solis Magna.
