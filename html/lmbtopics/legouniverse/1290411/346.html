These sort of stats are incredibly misleading, especially in this universe. On a 1-10 scale, Inharax's Mind would be 15, and Karunax's armor would be a 30.

 

These stats need to be considered: An average score is probably a 7 or so, no? And stats change per species. A 7 for Matoran is more like a 3 for a Toa. For Agility, Le-Matoran have close to a 10 whereas Onu-Matoran have a 6 or 5. Strength is reversed.

 

Also, these stats mean little depending on how the character is played.

 

Better ways to give out stats are by using a description for the vehicle, especially since no one else uses these stats.

 

Just for fun, here's Inharax's stats from both Makuta and (Ta-)Matoran standpoints.

Speed: 8 (wings 9)

Armor: 7 (same for everyone but Karunax)
