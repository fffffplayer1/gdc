I think you are calling him that because you are not skilled enough to defeat him, and are insisting that anything that can beat you is OP. And what do you mean by that last sentence? "You are one Matoran." So, wait, you're saying it in that way, and I think what you mean is this: If you are in an HQ with lesser-powered characters, and with less characters, you are not as good a person, or roleplayer, or your opinion doesn't matter. 

 

All Bakud's powers are, when you break them down, is:

  1. Teleportation: When he knows where a place is, he can teleport there as long as he can reach it on foot some time or another.
  2. Retroactivity: He can make retroactive moves, but none that can totally obliterate an enemy before he meets them, as it would create a paradox, and is limited to sabotage, which can be done with some Makuta powers.
  3. Future Sight: As long as he can reach something or teleport to it, he can witness it. He cannot witness higher-security events or Jammers.
  4. Duplication: He can create more selves, yet is limited by his patience. He cannot do the same thing over and over again any more than 3 times, and prefers to mix it up.
  5. Armor Piercing This isn't even a power, and is just his weapon.
  6. Durable Armor Nor is this.
  7. Flight, limited Kanohi powers, and Weapons don't count on Makuta, and all there powers are only seen as one thing, instead of "Limited Intangibility" and "Increased Desnsity" it's just "Density Control," etc.
  8. What else?

Then there's the Makuta's unlimited unheldback array of abilites they shamelessly use. This includes prominent features:

  1. Limited Invulnerability
  2. Teleportation
  3. Density Control
  4. Sleep

Combine with brute force and such and it's OP in my book.
