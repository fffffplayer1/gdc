Name: Bakud
Species: Matoran
HQ: Matoran HQ
Element: Gravity
Mask: Mask of Rebounding somewhere from lv. 1-6
Armor: Handcrafted Genetic Armor
Weapons: Genetic Sword: Runs based off genetic code, which lets it absorb materials and apply and de-apply them to the sword or armor at the users will when the tunneler genome is applied. Alongside with that, he made a function to prevent others from using it, which uses some of the users inner power on themself positivley or negativeley, amplifying it to however it needs to be, and as Gravity is basically related to time, it lets him time travel. I will make this quick: Say there's a Makuta with a mask of time duplication, he can teleport, and has accuracy, like, say, Bitil. Replace 5 of the powers with seeing the future, another 5 with stable time loops, and another 5 with the sword's absorbing properties, another 5 with the benefits of absorbing disks, and another 5 with the sword's sharpness, and take away all the powers other than the ones mentioned. That's basically the powers Bakud has.
Additional Powers: None, really. He's crazy and defenestrates people as his calling card, but never in roleplay.
Natural Abilities: Insanity. Usually terrible at normal fighting methods unless skills are enhanced by sword when synced  up with himself. Also he was apparently a Quantum Physicist before, which sorta explains all his temporal knowhow.
