The Bossman: What?! Dustbins of Doom are highly classified items of Document Destruction!! Mere Minifigures are not authorized to own one!

The Bossman: Arrgghh! *Stalks off into a corner to sulk, muttering about his authority being undermined*

 

Anyway, everyone gets a Dustbin. Prisims are placed in strategic locations, so rainbows criss-cross everything. My Brainstorm Machine is in a corner, smoking ominously, and occacionally spitting out a (charred) new idea. The floor is made of hard cherry candy, so whenever you feel the urge you can topple over to lick it. (Note: You must put on anti-bacterial shoes before entering) One wall is made of tree bark, another made of bookshelves (Only good books on there), another made of s, and the last one carbonized Chpters. ABSOLUTELY NO CLOCKS ARE ALLOWED. I also have a suit of Dica's gear at my disposal. (No one touch it!!)

 

Last, but not least, a life-sized thedude-punching bag.
