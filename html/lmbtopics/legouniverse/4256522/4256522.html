Chapter 1 - Just a normal day, right?

 

It was another day in LEGO Universe. The location was Nimbus Station. Bob was playing chess with himself, Nexus Jay was selling faction drinks in his new vendor booth he bought... and SuperGeniusCreator was just taking a walk. He looked up at the sky... it was evening time... or was it morning? He couldn't tell, becuase Nimbus Station was always like that. It was just another happy, normal day...

 

Meanwhile, a mysterious figure looked at a checklist. It had many different things on it including "The Final Battle in Ninjago", "The Battle of Nimbus Station", and "The destruction of the first Death Star". The list was labeled: "EVENTS TO CHANGE". Listed as number one on the list was something that would frighten Super if he knew about it... "The escape of the Nexus Force recruits on the Venture Explorer" The figure grinned an evil grin... and laughed...

Chapter 2 - The Detour That Took a Detour

 

DATE: October 26, 2010

LOCATION: Nexus Force Starship, Venture Explorer

DESTINATION: Nimbus Station to train Nexus Force recruits

 

"Look out!" shouted Bob, "we've headed into a Maelstrom storm!" The Venture Explorer, carrying hundreds of Nexus Force Recruits, had founf its way into a bit of a problem. The ship's engines had begun to leak imagination, which attracted the Malestrom's attention... so now the ship was under attack. BOOOM!!! ...that was the sound of one of the walls of the Venture Explorer exploding. "We need to evacuate to Avant Gardens, Bob!" said Sky Lane. "But what about all the minifigs?" said Jett Moonshot, who was guarding the hyper-sleep-pods in which tons of minifigs slept. "We'll have to wake them up and get them out of here!" said co-pilot Bob. And so they did. Tons of minifigs were awakened and began unlocking their imagination and buildign rockets. Everything was going just as it happened... until suddenly, a mysterious figure teleported onto the ship and... threw a dagger at Bob... smashing him. "NOOO!!!" said Sky Lane and Jett Moonshot. "Who did that?" said Jett. Then, the mysterious figure jumped over to Sky Lane. "Going to get rid of me? You'll have to fight me first!" Sky Lane pulled out a sword and tried to fight the mysterious figure... but she failed, and also smashed. He then teleported back, but forgot about Jett. He then awoke all the minifigs and helped them escape to Avant Gardens...

Chapter 3 - The Monument That Was, But Wasn't

 

DATE: Same as Chapter 1, 11/26/2012

LOCATION: Nimbus Station

 

It was another day in LEGO Universe. The location was Nimbus Station. Jett Moonshot was playing tag with Vector Longview, Nexus Jay was selling faction drinks in his new vendor booth he bought, and SuperGeniusCreator was just taking a walk. It was just another happy, normal day... or was it? Super felt as if something was wrong... something... something... but what? He then felt the srangest urge to travel to Avant Gardens.

And so he did. There, he ran over to the Avant Gardens monument. It was not Bob fighting a Maelstrom enemy as you might remember if you were there, but it was a statue of Bob, Sky Lane, and Jett Moonshot. Bob and Sky Lane were holding out swords, fighting a maelstrom creature, while Jett was leading minifigs to safety. At the bottom of the monument, there was a plaque which read: "In memory of Bob and Sky Lane, who bravely steered the ship that brought the Nexus Force recruits to Avant Gardens and wouldn't surrender when evil came to stop them, and of Jett Moonshot, who bravely led the minifigs to safety when Bob and Sky Lane were gone."

Super knew something wasn't right. Even though he remembered it always being there... at the same time he felt it shouldn't be there. A strangely familiar sentence appeared in his mind: "Sorry about the bumpy ride!" ...someone said that to him once... but Super couldn't remember who... he knew something was not right...

Chapter 4 - The Watch That Does More Than Tell Time

 

DATE: 11/30/2012

LOCATION: ???

 

"Master, I have been able to destroy Bob and Sky Lane..." said the mysterious figure to his master... another mysterious, dark figure.

"And what about Jett Moonshot?" said the master.

"Jett..." said the mysterious figure, "who's Jett-"

"You fool!" said the master, "You didn't destory Jett Moonshot? ...all those minifigs survived, then!"

"Well," said the mysterious time traveler nervously, I could always go back and-"

"No you can't," said the master, "because you can't use the time-manipulator watch to travel to a place you've all been to!"

"I can't?" said the time traveler. "What now?"

"Now..." said the master, "YOU WILL BE DESTROYED!!!!"

"NO! Please no!" said the time traveler, "If it hadn't been for the fact the watch only allows you to stay in a certain time for a few minutes, I would've gotten that Jett!"

"Fine," said the master, "I will give you one more chance... but if you fail..." The master pointed to an arena with 3 roaring monsters in it.

"Oh, I will not fail you, master," said the time traveler, "Oh, I won't..." The time travler laughed and thought to himself, "One of these days, I'LL be the one giving commands... but first I need a good plan..." The evil time traveler pressed a button on his watch and teleported to number 2 on his "EVENTS TO CHANGE" list... "The Battle of Nimbus Station."

 

DATE: 11/30/2012

LOCATION: Avant Gardens

 

Super had returned to the monument. He still thought of that phrase: "Sorry about the bumpy ride!" He couldn't stop thinking about it. He felt as if a change had occured in... time! That was it! Super ate a quicksicle and rushed to the Nimbus Station rocket launchpad. When he arrived he zoomed over to Wenn Wuzzit, who didn't seem to be happy.

"Something is out of order," said Wenn Wuzzit with a shocked expression, "Something's... not right."

"What's wrong?" inquired Super.

"Time is wrong." said Wuzzit, "And I fear it is about to get wronger..."

Chapter 5 - Defeat at Nimbus Station

 

DATE: Sometime before October 2010

LOCATION: Nimbus Station

 

It was a happy day in Nimbus Station.  There were a bunch of minifigs dancing around welcoming everyone to LEGO Universe, and everything was all happy and fun... not. The Stromlings hiding behind the cardboard minifigs turned around... they saw Maelstrom reinforcements coming, and knocking over all the buildings and smashing stuff. The sky turned purple with Maelstrom, tons of Maelstrom dragons, Grumpy Darklings, and Dark Spiderlings all running around crazy. Then, suddenly, the sky partially cleared, and 4 minifigs appeared. Duke Exeter, Doctor Albert Overbuild, Captain Hael Storm, and Vanda Darkflame... the leaders of the Nexus Force. The opposing sides looked at each other a moment... and then an towards each other. It seemed like the 4 faction leaders were outnumbered, until thousands of minifigs began rushing towards the Stromlings. It was the Battle of Nimbus Station.

The Nexus Force was winning... or so they thought.

 

DATE: A while before the above events

LOCATION: Nimbus Station

 

"C'mon!" shouted SuperGeniusCreator, "Let's show these baddies who will stay the victor of the battle!" SuperGeniusCreator, holding an Uber Hammer, rushed towards a Dark Spiderling and smashed it. He had travled back in time using Wenn Wuzzit's time machine to prevent the Maelstrom from changing past events. And he was succeeding... but not for much longer...

 

The evil, mysterious time traveler teleported into Nimbus Station. He had to act quick. He took out an Uber-gun, which, although more powerful then an Uber Hammer, required a recharge time of 10 minutes. He knew he only had one shot, so he decided he'd use it well... He was behind a bush, waiting for the perfect moment to strike. The 4 faction leaders drew near. He had only 30 seconds left before he would be teleported back. Then just when they got close enough so he couldn't miss...

 

SMASH!!!

 

The faction leaders were no more.

Chapter 6 - Empire of Chaos

 

DATE: 12/3/12

LOCATION: The Great Tree, Forbidden Valley

 

It was a danger-filled day in LEGO Universe... filled with danger, smashing, Maelstrom-fighting, & more danger. SuperGeniusCreator was defending one of the last places not yet taken over by what was known as the Empire of Chaos, the incredibly large army of Maelstrom. Only a few other places remained untaken: the Pirate Camp in Gnarled Forest, the unfinished Nexus Tower which could never be completed due to all the Maelstrom attacks, & Red Blocks Concert, which was no longer an amphitheater... but a Nexus Force base. The leaders of the Nexus Force, Mardolf the Orange, Sherland Powers, Capt. Jack Knife, & Wenn Wuzzit, had all showed up at the Great Tree to defend it from a heavy attack by the Maelstrom. There were no factions, and all the gear the Nexus Force had was custom-made by the Nexus Force soldiers themselves. The Maelstrom had developed heavy-armored Maelstrom tanks, helicopters, and jets, and the Nexus Force continued to get smaller and smaller.

Super felt something wasn't right. He felt as if none of this should've happened. And Wenn Wuzzit felt it too. Super took a break from the battle to replenish his health, and Wenn Wuzzit sat down by him. "Wenn, we both know time has somehow been changed," Super said, "but how do we fix it?"

"I don't know." said Wenn, "My time machine I invented was destroyed during a Maelstrom attack, so until I can build another one, we won't be able to fix what has happened."

Super tried to remember time before it was changed. He thought and thought, and he remembered something...

 

DATE: Late 2011

LOCATION: The Venture Explorer, before time changed

 

"Now that you're awake, we've got to get you out of here!" shouted Jett Moonshot

Super was in a new world. He'd just been awakened out of hyper-sleep, and he was now in LEGO Universe! He began rushing over to... Bob?

 

...Super remembered Bob, and from there remembered his entire life in the original time line.

Chapter 7 - Hidden Identities

 

DATE: 12/4/12

LOCATION: The Great Tree, Forbidden Valley

 

SuperGeniusCreator now remembered all his adventures that had now never happened... but they did, but now they didn't. It was a little confusing to remember 2 different realities, but Super managed to keep everything in order. He remembered when he turned into a Stromling and defeated the Darkitect... but in this reality that never happened. He knew now what had happened to time, but he didn't know how to fix it...

 

DATE: Same

LOCATION: ???

 

"Master..." the evil time-traveler said, "The faction leaders are all gone."

"Perfect," said his master, "Soon, the entire Nimbus System will be in our control."

"Indeed," said the time-traveler, "And LEGO Universe WILL BE MINE- ...I mean ours"

His master looked at him suspiciously. "Now, we must destroy the Darkitect."

"Why?" Inquired the time-traveler.

"Because I know he doesn't want to share power." said the master

"I see..."

"Now go, and destroy him!"

"Yes, master..."

 

But the time-traveler had other plans. "Now's my chance," he whispered. He pulled out his Uber-gun and aimed it at his master, who was now walking away to plan evil scemes. "I'm tired of being a servant." he said... BOOM!!!

 

"Did you really think I didn't suspect that you would be betray me?" said his master, holding up an Uber-shield he pulled out the moment he heard the sound of the Uber-gun.

"WHAT???" said the time-traveler, "I thought I'd gotten rid of you!"

"Think again," said the master, "NO ONE BETRAYS DOCTOR DOOMSDAY!!!!" He leaped forward at the time-traveler about to destroy him with a purple staff.

But the time-travler dodged the attack, snatching the purple staff from Dr. Doomsday.

"Don't think that I don't know how that staff works," said the evil time-traveler, shooting an extremely powerful ray of Maelstrom, "After all, it is mine."

"But that's not possible!" Dr. Doomsday shouted, "You would have to be-"

"The Darkitect?" the Darkitect grinned evily.
