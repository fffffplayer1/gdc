Dear LEGO
Congrats on the new mmo LEGO mini figures. You have been doing so well with having family friendly toys and being the number one toy industry. Your one down fall is listening to your fans, your children, long term fans, LEGO fans. LEGO Universe was the one game that kept us all together and created the best possible environment ever. Yes you keep saying you'll never re launch LEGO universe, but really is that the right thing to do?!

Many people connect with LEGO universe, I was skyping my friends and they mentioned LEGO universe I got so excited and showed all my items I kept from magazines and such. You've created Heaven for all Imaginationers, and now its gone.

Minecraft took over the gaming industry but is slowly falling down, we all see that. LEGO universe is expensive to run, and many of the forms say mainly LEGO was unprepared to run a full server, especially mmo based. The easiest thing you could do is sell the rights to a gaming company, at least your fans would be happy.

The game would now have so much hype and players, also increased advertisements and different platforms for it. Starting a kick starter cam pane and doing more research and you already have the game can help. Also with the game, easily fortunes could be made from it with created sets of legos straight out of your own game! The "perve prevention" would still have to be enforced, instead it could be warnings, if you do, you get banned, this would also prevent players from doing it and prevent them from doing it again

Please lastly just put up a poll, to see how many people loved LEGO universe, even if it means scrapping an extra 5 bucks to play, millions of LEGO fans would be thrilled to have it back.

Thanks LEGO for (hopefully) reading this.
I had a great time in LEGO Universe

My adventure shall go on

Hi everyone,

 

We understand that LEGO Universe still has many dedicated fans. It was definitely an awesome game and we loved it too!

 

Unfortunately we must remind you all that LEGO Universe has been permanently retired. It has now been years since the game was closed and we hope that you are able to move forward. We are so sorry to disappoint you all, but we will not be bringing LU back. It is absolutely closed permanently.
