My eyes widened behind my goggles. I couldnt dodge it! It was coming at me too quickly. I braced myself and shut my eyes tightly.

There was a loud clang.

I dared not to, but i opened my eyes to see mech's arms stretched in front of my face, sheilding me from the claw that had sped towards me just a moment before. "Thanks mech." I breathed.

I looked up to see a maelstrom-infected ovebuild. I gasped. "N-no..."
"Oh yes." He said, his claw-ish hand was squeezed in mech's grip. "I think you will learn to love me better in this forme thanyou did before, sweetie." He had an evil grin and his goggled glowed purple instead of blue.
I stared. "W-what?!? No I wont!! You've been infected by maelstrom, so i have to change you back!" I yelled.
He didnt like the sound of that and brought his chainsaw down on mech's arm, freeing his hand. "Change me back??" He laughed as if this were a joke. "Dont you see? It's better this way! If you cant see, ill just have to show you!"
He lunged at me.
"G, NO!" Uncle otto and rare both yelled.
Mech blocked maelstrom overbuild's chainsaw, and i found my self in close combat with the man i once loved.
