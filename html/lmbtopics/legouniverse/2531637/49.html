Chapter46:

I stood there, staring ahead, not moving, shocked that Rare had yelled at me like that. "I..." I trailed off, my words unable to finish themselves into a sentence. Erron stood by. I looked at him and lost myself. I almost forgot that he wasnt Rare. "uhm...something wrong?" Erron asked. "You're looking at me weird." I shook my head.
"No, im fine!" I said, but still thinking of Rare. "Erron, can you find rare? Please?" I asked.
"Sure i can." He said, then frowned. "But shouldnt i stay here? Id' be leaving you behind with maeltrom overbuild there...."
"I have mech. I'll be fine. Just please find rare."i pleaded. Erron nodded and ran off. I turned back to overbuild. He sat there, pinned against the wall by uncle otto's mech. I sighed and reached to stroke his forehead. Uncle otto stopped me and held my arm back. "Don't touch him." He warned. "His level of maelstrom content is very high. Its too dangerous."
"Fine." I muttered, and knelt down on the cave floor near overbuild, ignoring the persisting pain from my side. Maelstrom Overbuild just stared ahead at me in silence. Finnaly, i spoke. "Albert, you remember being a faction leader dont you?" He didn't answer. "You remember the inagination nexus right?" That got him stirring. He struggled to move and said.
"The nexus is destroyed. Imagination is doomed! The maelstrom will rule all!" I took out a bottle of imagination potion. .
"Would you like me to pour this down your throat? I sugest you talk nice." Secretly though, i didnt want to hurt him, but maybe just a little...if i had to...

I heard a faint rustling in the distance. I stayed curled up, hoping that whatever was approaching would swiftly end me. Instead, a hand clamped on my shoulder. "Leave me be, Erron." I snarled, my tears still blurring my vision. Erron got in my face, forcing me to look him in the eye. "I said," my voice growing louder, bolder, angrier, "LEAVE ME BE, ERRON."

he just stared at me with flaming blue eyes. "First, explain your little episode in the cave." "Is a robot forbidden to want time alone?" I asked. Erron responded with a sharp remark, "I think this was about more than alone time." I sighed. It was, but I would never tell a complete stranger what I couldn't tell the neice of my own creator, or even my creator. "You want to feel emotions, to be a Minifigure." I grumbled back at him. "And maelstrom are injured by imagination, cars can't run without gas, and the wheels on the bus go round and round." Erron glared. "I knew it. Besides, it's forbidden. Robots like us are never going to feel emotion." I stood and growled. "Maybe, maybe... I DON'T WANT TO BE A ROBOT  ANYMORE!" Erron looked stunned, and immediately reacted with, "why?" I had to tell someone, eventually. " Because," I said, "I... I love her!" I said, sulking back to the cave.

Chapter 48:
Uncle otto gasped. "You're not really considering that as an option are you?! Its too risky, my dear!" He exclaimed.
"I know. I was just wondering!" I thought about the risks and dangers of that method. It WAS dangerous, but what else could i do?? I had to change him back. I couldnt allow him to be possessed any longer. I frowned nervously and thought over my other options.
"What?" Maelstrom overbuild gave an evil grin. "You arent SCARED are you??" He chuckled. Ok, that was it. Definitely a bad idea. But...there wasnt any other way! An imagination potion wouldnt be strong enough. It would only cause him pain, and the maelstrom would reinfect the rest of him a moment later! Buti couldnt just leave him like that, and i wasnt going to smash him. No way! Rare had just tried to. Smashing him would be wrong and it wouldnt solve anything.

I used my imagination potion to protect mech's claws in the liquid. Mech held overbuild back, squishing him up against the wall. I took a deep breath. Was i really going to do this??

An answer came with a shout from the opening of the cave. It was rare
