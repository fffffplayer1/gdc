Chapter 66. (Still in Rare's view.)

"What is it?" Gwen asked. " It.. I.... They.. they're coming. right now... for m..me." I stammered. " Who?" Gwen asked. I got up, " The maelstrom. Baron's created an army of me." Gwen seemed to grin evilly. " That was his flaw." she laughed, still acting evil. suddenly a portal opened, and a robot just like me, but without the missing arm, stepped out. Soon dozens were in the room. two had wrestled G to the ground. a menacing figure with a black top hat came in. " I see you found the truth." He said. "Allow me to reward you." My arm regenerated, beaming with maelstrom. " You won't infect the nexus." "I know. I came for you." I transformed my uninfected arm and aimed it at the baron. " You LIAR!" A calm reply came back. " Do I lie?" A robot with an electric whip came out from the others, walking toward G. " He will fry her to death if you don't come with me." " Fine." I rested my lazer cannon. then lifted it back up and blew the head off the torture-bot. " Come, General Rare." I sighed. I looked back at Gwen sadly, and then went into the portal.

I felt a tear trickle down my face as I walked through the dark hall following the darkitect. I soon saw a balcony. " Behold, the downfall of nexus tower!" I walked to the balcony and looked down. " So many... the nexus force will be outnumbered..." The darkitect laughed. "They only take orders from you." Darkitect handed me a piece of paper. I slowly unfolded the paper, revealing the commands. " Arc 8!" I shouted. the bots turned and faced the left. " Trans 67!" they turned their hands into blades like the average stromling. " PIN 5!" the bots returned to their normal position and converted their arms into claws again. I shoved the note into my pocket. " They may be useful to defeat the nexus force, but they are inferior. They lack feeling. they have no heart." I grumbled. " Well, you don't have a heart." A voice said, coming from the prison hall. I darted over to the hall as Darkitect walked to his throne room. I found myself face-to-face with Baron Typhonus, behind bars. " I may not have a physical heart, but I experience emotions, that's what makes those bots inferior." " I know... but you have little to no chance of having any heart after seige on nexus tower. and it will begin tommorow." " But-" " I'm sorry. your efforts have failed." I sighed." There must be a way to stop him... And there is."

Chapter 70.
I marched with the soldiers across crux prime, thinking all along. " I know how to stop the army, but I can't do it. There must be another way..." " We're here troops." Darkitect said. I looked at him, actually grinning. " Ready for demolition?" Darkitect asked, returning the smile. " Yes... Yes I am..." The whole army charged in, greeted by landmines and missiles, the cries of battle from both sides, and the sight of the imagination nexus. But that was not all, no. the mythrans equipped the gear of the first builders, and charged forward, smashing any stromling or robot in their path. But most were infected, and turned on their own brothers, this time smashing any nexus force member in their path. Gwen was fighting the spider boss and darkitect at the same time, putting up a good fight, but loosing some of my robots were attacking the spider boss as well. " Mach 7! aim at spider general! FIRE!!" the spider boss moaned in pain, yet the lumbering beast knocked out my units with a simple stomp. mech grabbed the spider general's leg, and ripped the beast in two. I saw the darkitect running toward me. " Rare! We lost the spider boss!" " I know." I said. " Focus all fire on the nearest enemy unit!" Wait... what was I thinking!! The nexus force was Good! No, they're just a roadblock we're about to destroy. but! they're the defenders of imagination! No, They're trouble. THEY.ARE.GOOD!! I pushed a green button on my wrist while the darkitect was next to me, and all the robots exploded, I knew I was to self destruct to, but I was taking darkitect with me. I dove and tackled him, and then... Everything went black.
