Chapter 9: Avant Battlefields

Options
  • Mark as New
  • Bookmark
  • Subscribe to RSS Feed
  • Highlight
  • Print

‎04-15-2012 09:35 AM

Something was amiss between this place, so I decided to check out the two faction leaders.

Doctor Overbuild was fumbling with some kind of contraption. I walked towards him and asked him what it was.

Overbuild spoke up. “This here is a wonderful machine we have!” He had a mad grin, and looked like he just had a gallon of Super Sentinel Soda. “It’s able to fix anything! Watch!”

Overbuild turned on the engine, and I expected it to work. Instead, it fluttered with a sad pewpewpew….and exploded into bits. “See?”, he said. “It works!” He grinned wildly.

“Yeah….”, I murmured. “I guess so too.”



All of a sudden, Cosmic spoke up on the overhead. “We are approaching Avant Gardens now. As you can see, Avant Gardens has—wait what the? What the brick is going on down there?”

I could see what Cosmic was talking about. Instead of the lush, green world it was supposed to be, the planet was torn apart by war. Everywhere, the Nexus Force was confronting the Maelstrom. And it didn’t look like the Nexus Force was doing too good. A Maelstrom Discharge was planted right in front of a battalion, and the battalion got blasted into oblivion. When the dust cleared, a squad of stromlings arose and charged towards the battalion next to them.

I pointed towards a green spot amongst the bloody battle. “Land there!”, I yelled to Cosmic. She responded immediately. We quickly landed unnoticed into that green spot. “Come on!”, I yelled towards Doctor Overbuild and Hael Storm. Then, we all evacuated the ship, hoping it would stay unnoticed. Unfortunately, we got more than we expected. A fireball was hurled towards our ship, and the somewhat wonderful rocket was blasted into Ship ‘n Bits.

Stromlings started massing around us. I implanted my katana into one stromling and shook the other with pure lightning. I bashed their heads together and they crumpled like recycled paper. I looked over at Cosmic. She kept smashing stromlings with her smoking gun and a knife like it was a Smash Buffet. Hael was just standing there, when a stromling came up behind him. “Watch out!”, I yelled as I made my way towards him.

Suddenly, he grabbed a cutlass and slashed and hacked the stromling into Maelstrom pieces. “Who says I can’t fight?”, he asked. I grinned. Meanwhile, Doc Overbuild was a walking bowling ball, with a drilling gun in each hand, and in each of the 4 tentacles behind him. Soon, every stromling within 100 yards of us was either obliterated or smoking dead. We had to find protection fast.

Cosmic had an answer. “Beck Strongheart! Let’s get to him!”

We traveled through a sea of Maelstrom towards the Sentinel Base, and we say stromlings just ganging up on the soldiers. I felt furious, as these were the people that took me in as a fighter. I floated up in the air, and discharged massive amounts of electricity. Arcs of Imagination jumped from one stromling to another, frying them on at a time. All that was left were a surprised looking soldiers and a relieved Beck Strongheart, along with the remains of some 500 Stromlings.

Beck was the first to speak up. “Thank goodness you’re here!”, he said. “Yeah,yeah. We get the point.”, Cosmic replied. “We’ve been stuck on this planet because all of a sudden, armies of stromlings appeared out of nowhere and just charged towards us.”, Beck said. “We’ve got only like 500 men left while have to face against that.” Beck pointed towards a giant purple splotch in the distance. “It’s that what we’re worried about. We have—wait, what’s that noise?” Out of nowhere, a loud SCREEEECH could be heard.

A Dark Ronin, more evil, more dangerous, more Chaotic, flew out of the sky and landed on the ground. He raised his sword, and rallied even more stromlings to his command.

“Grim Daisho.”, Beck spoke in awe, most likely fear. “I should’ve known it was he who was leading the stromlings.”

Grim Daisho walked towards us, along with stromlings, and cackled at me. “I think he’s beckoning you to fight him.”, Beck said. “I think he wants a good swordfight.”

“Well then!”, I spoke. “I’m going to give him one heck of a fight.” “Wish me luck!”

I charged out of the camp, and towards Daisho. He raised his sword, and sent his stromlings towards me.

I raised my hands, and thought. Meanwhile, stromlings started shaking, as their souls were starting to be absorbed by me, until they were fully drained of power and crumpled onto the ground.

I unleashed my fury towards Grim, as a burst of Soul Energy was blasted towards him. Daisho blocked the incoming blast like it was nothing, and directed the Soul Energy away. He straightened his sword and waited.


“Let’s go.”, I said, as I charged towards him, and grinning at the same time.
