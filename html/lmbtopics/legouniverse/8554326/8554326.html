"I can't go back to yesterday because I was a different person then.”

 

When I looked at myself in the mirror, I saw a reflection of the past, and all the hardships that came with it. The triumph and failure, the joy and grief; all this could be seen in the past. But I was a different person back then. Like the phoenix, I was reborn from the ashes of my former self. My past was burned away, yet memories still remain with me. I want to set fire to it, to end the poignant reminiscences I have.

 

I wanted to change the world, but I have found that the only thing one can be sure of changing is oneself.

 

I want to end it all.

It's me from the past, twenty-five years ago. I try to wave hello, but he only blinks back at me, shuddering.

 

I have to go now. They're coming. Fast.

I hope I make it through the night.

Cool topic! Now that I think about it, my character is like Jowe, to some degree. They both have to deal with a painful past. Anyway, here's one about Talia written from her perspective, after the end of the story she's in. (So there's spoilers if anybody ever wants to read Imagination Infection)

 

 

Diary entry #3

 


Everything is so warm here. So much different from Avant Gardens, Crux Prime, even Nimbus Station. They all had a chill in the air I'd never noticed before. Riina says the chill is from the Maelstrom; that it's permiated the air so much it's left a stain in the very atmosphere. Not so here, in the Mythran City, where no Maelstrom has ever tainted the startlingly fresh air- well, except me.

 

I saw a specialist today- he was supposed to have studied the Maelstrom and it's effects on Mythrans and Minifigures alike. He took a look at some scans on me. It's no use. There's nothing they can do.

 

I don't know what I was hoping for, going there. That maybe that he could erase the Stromling still left in me, or... something. But he can't. I was made a Stromling, and if they take out the Maelstrom inside of me, I'll just smash.

 

It's hard being half-Stromling. Sometimes I go into fits of chaos- I just want to destroy stuff. Tear it apart. Decimate its existance. Anything. Something to satisfy the monster caged under this delicate wrap of blue glass. The monster I was and will never truly not be. Yet I'm also half-Mythran. I've been learning things I've never dreamed of: spawning objects, using Imagination to move things I can't move by myself, change my features. I've been starting to do orderly things, like keeping a diary and cooking. There's hope- oh, there's certainly hope. Someday the nightmares and fits will end, and I'll be free of the last strands connecting my mind to the Maelstrom. For now, I will simply enjoy my new life here with the Mythrans. Riina invited me over to her dinner party tonight. I can't wait to see her again.

...○•Jonna•○...

.....○•Odd for God•○.....

this sounds like a cool topic! here's a diary from the point of view of my character, haroldosaur, during his time in captivity in Dr Overbuild's lab following a deadly confrontation with the Darkitecht . Overbuild is trying to figure out the cause of Harold's maelstrom powers, because Harold is not letting on...

 

Day 7

 

more tests. will they ever end? all they talk about is maelstrom, maelstrom, maelstrom. I'm utterly sick of it. can't they talk about anything normal? like what the weather is like? I don't actually know what it is, stuck inside this lab. or what's going on outside. the maelstrom could have won and I wouldn't know. 

 

Plue is in here as well, being studied. no minifigure has ever come into direct contact with the darkitecht as far as I know. at least my powers could protect me. she wasn't so lucky. I saw her yesterday. no change in her condition, unconcious and fighting the infection from inside.

 

then I was shunted to the testing chamber where they stuck robitic arms onto me and monitered everything as if it was important. I don't really know what happened concerning my powers. something to do with a spiderling, my head... I don't really remember. it's all a blur. next thing I know, I have an alter ego with an attitude problem and some kickbutt maelstrom abilities. 

 

should I try to escape? 

 

Yes. 

 

Can I?

 

no.

 

why?

 

the corridors are gaurded by inventors with lasers at every turn. all the checkpoint are secured by gun turrets and security cameras keep watch 24/7.

 

It's not me I'm worried about. It's plue. despite what the inventors say, I don't think they're going to cure her any time soon. it's up to me, and we are strictly seperated at almost all times. 

 

but If I wish us to live, I need to find a way to escape. 

 

I must think.

The Maelstrom Minifigure, Knight Of The Old Speech, Time Lord.

Here's mine from my story, The Journey of Exceedra--Revised Edition

 

The guy a lot of people remember is, for all intents and purpose, no longer in this realm.  He has been for a long time.  I just call myself Ghost, for that's what I am; a shade of what once was a part of the world of Plainslands.

 

I've been forced to survive on my own world, now dominated by the Maelstrom, sneaking around and ambushing small groups of the minions of chaos.

 

I remember when I was able to walk around freely, without a thought or care for the Maelstrom.  Every day was the same; tending to the herds, harvesting the crops, or going out on patrol among a few minor surprises and some other common occurances.  Sure I knew that force of chaos was there and that one day it may come to my world.

 

I just didn't expect it would come so soon or suddenly.

 

So here I am, three years stuck on this now accursed rock.  If somehow, someone reads this, my name is Exceedra Norell, last minifigure alive (or was) on Plainslands.

 

I have to stop now, there's too much to write and not enough time...

"Take another step, and another step..."--Steven Curtis Chapman
"Writing takes on many shapes; story writers open up portals to new worlds..."--me.
VM, AKA Exceedra, Level 36 Sentinel Jack-of-all-trades
