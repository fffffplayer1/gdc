The maelstrom started to approach. We thought it was just weak invaders, but we saw not just stromling, pirate and ronin invaders, we also saw mechs, spiderlings, admirals and horsemen. And we had barely made any progress!
"Don't know if we can make it out of this one." Said X.
"How about we do what we did last time, except I use the rope?" Said Sarah.
"That's a great idea!" I said, putting away my valiant weapon and getting out a rocket launcher. I shot a rocket at a rock, turning it into a hook shape. Sarah got out a rope, flinged it up to the hook and swung up to the top of the hook shape, tied it up, jumped down and swung to the top. I was last. When I got down, a blue lightning bolt from out of nowhere hit me. It didn't hurt, but it gave me a vision. A female pink dragon with a purple fire on her head and me. The dragon striked by turning the fire on her head into a horn and striking me, sending me up into the air. I came down... in peices. I was smashed. I awoke from the vision, looking around me.
"Are you okay?" Said X.
"Yep. I just had a vision when the blue lightning bolt hit me." I said
"What was it?" Said Happy.
"It was some wierd, non-maelstrom dragon smashing me." I said, scared by that vision from the blue lightning bolt...

A male black dragon, talking in a girls voice said "Yep. I just had a vision when the squirel hit me." And give up on finding a way to stop me from doing this!

"Who are you?" I asked.
"I am Chopov, skeleton of Earth," Said the first one.
"I am Krazi, skeleton of Lightning," Said the second one.
"I am Bonezai, skeleton of Ice, and this is Frakjaw, skeleton of Fire. Our mission is to protect the Darkitect." Said the third one, pointing at the fourth.
"Hold on, guys." Said Zentrox, appearing out of nowhere.
"What?" Said Nero.
"I'll teleport you out of here. I'll also take on these guys. This is the last help I can give to you." Said Zentrox. Suddenly, we appeared in the Barons castle. Roofless. Barely any light. It was creepy.
"Welcome. Let us have some tea before we fight." Said the Darkitect.
"Baron, I know ye make great tea, but why don't we just smash these scurvy dogs?" Said Admiral Flogmore.
"I know, it's just me, my son, and Butterscorch are like, no longer evil, despite being infected." Said Baron. "And unlike those two, I am getting weak and old. Nickycool, Kowwkoww, Admiral Flogmore, Lord Garmadon, you four will replace me." Those four shared a happy look...
