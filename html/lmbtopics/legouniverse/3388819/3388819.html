Prologue

One day, two mythrans were talking.
"Zentrox, I would like to talk to you about something." Said a mythran.
'Yes, Redflare?" Said Zentrox, one of the remaining mythrans.
"I would like to reveal the secret to that boy." Said Redflare in a very calm voice.
"Okay, but... You should tell him that." Said Zentrox, who was acting lazy at the time. Redflare turned into his minifig form, but was smashed by a stromling. Zentrox decided it was best to stay in mythran form until he reached Nimbus station, and he ran to the launchpad as fast as he could.
"Life is a highway, full of speed bumps and red lights, all night long."
