"X, go to gnarled forest and offer a peace treaty with the pirates." Demanded Vanda.
"Right on, Vanda!" Said X, a Rank 3 Shinobi and one of Vanda's best agents.

Back to me...
Xale made us split up. The Paradox took on one dragon, the Assembly would take on another, and the Sentinels would take on the third.
"Okay guys, let's make this quick!" Said Dynamo. We all attacked the dragon, but it wouldn't go down.

The Assembly were doing good.
"We're doing good! Keep it up!" Said Bubsey. Just then, the dragon breathed fire at them.

Sentinel had destroyed their dragon already.
"We did it!" Said Tense...

X left Forbidden valley. One hour later, he returned to Vanda.
"What are we doing for them in return?" Said Vanda. I couldn't hear what he was going to say, but I could see. Hael Storm suddenly jumped into the scene and kicked him down. Vanda and Hael pulled out their swords and started fighting. I helped X get up, and he thanked us. But we had a bigger problem: The dragon.
"Prepare for destruction, dragon!" X said, being very brave and reckless. He attacked with his katanas. It hit, and it did damage, but the dragon did not seem damaged. It quickly blasted flames at X, making him fall over.

The Assembly were beginning to lose.
"We need help. NOW." Said Nero. Just then, Nickycool and Kowwkoww came into the scene.
"Finally! Hope!" Said Bubsey.
"Not so fast," Said a young stromling who was riding the legendary maelstrom dragon known as Butterscorch.
"Who are you? Shouted Happy.
"I am Kexon, son of the Darkitect," Said the young stromling. "I'd thought I'd go by and enslave these two sentinels!" Butterscorch blasted Nickycool and Kowwkoww with a fireball, knocking them unconscious. She then swooped down low so Kexon could pick them up. He picked them up, and flew back into the sky. We tried to attack him, but he was too fast...

"Finally! Chapter!" Said Bubsey

"Just a few minutes ago, we learned that Baron had a castle. Are you heading for that?" Said Dr. Overbuild, out of nowhere.
