"You shall regret opposing the power of the maelstrom!" Said the horseman. Just when he said that, a girl in Assembly engineer gear swooped down with her wrench! Unfortunately, it wasn't smashed.
"What? I thought that would've smashed you for sure!" Said the girl.
"Guys, this is Happy. I met her right when I was created in a lab." Said Skoorb.
"How did you get created? I never met a minifig who got created in a lab." I said.
"A spider jumped into a test tube, and boom. Then Happy found me. So basically, I was created by accident." Said Skoorb.
"Guys, I'll fight the horseman. You go fight the ronin!" Said Happy. I rushed into a ronin, and quickly smashed it.
"You don't know what you're doing, Happy, do you?" Said the horseman, blasting Happy with Maelstrom.
"I do," Said Happy.
"Surely one would know better than fight me one-on-one?" Said the horseman.

Meanwhile, the people in Venture were at the great tree.
"Hey guys, Me and Adventurous thought of an idea." Said Sarah Lane.
"OK, what have you thought up of?" Said Rambling.
"We'll try to build a shelter, and once we're done, we an put our gear back on," Said Adventurous...

"What? I thought I would get two chapters instead of one!" Said the girl.
