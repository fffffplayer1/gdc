New CAHPTAR Part! We're ultra-close to the end of the book now! Also, a while ago I decided the Nether is back! So check that out too! In fact, I should have a chapter up when this is approved.

 

Meyer was still Khin until a few days after the end of the battle. He kept himself occupied with dagger practice in the Sentinel area and helping out with any rogue Maelstrom found in the vent systems.

His dagger skill wasn't as good as Vanda/Vareh remembered from the times before Khin and she become Meyer and Vanda. She and the other Faction leaders decided that the Stromling sent him back to before he was too proficient with his dagger.

"An' a good thin' too!" Hael said. "The lad nearly smashed me poor parrot once!"

Jammers
Did I mention writing dialogue for Hael is difficult? You need to get all the pirate-y contractions and wording in there.

Khin's dagger skill dropped drastically about two days after the battle. "I think I'm turning back into Meyer," he said to Vanda. "Watch."

He went over to the Sentinel training area and took a few quick slashes at the dummy. He missed the first strike, the second barely slashed the dummy's arm, and the third sent the dagger flying from his hand. He turned to Vanda with a miserable expression.

"Looks like it," Vanda said grimly. "I suppose this will be your last day before Meyer regains consciousness."

"I suppose," Khin said. "You've changed. A lot."

"You haven't," Vanda said. "You're just as annoying as I remember."

"Maybe you haven't changed as much as I thought."

"Maybe."

They laughed for a second, then Vanda said, "Go get Meyer's staff. Let's see how well you can fight with that."

They fetched the staff and Khin grasped it, and Vanda was amazed that he held it nearly exactly like Meyer did. He swung it at the dummy and smashed it in one hit.

An Admiral dummy popped up, and he knocked its cannon right off of its torso, then jumped and smashed it into pieces.

He turned around and dropped the staff.

"Don't worry," Vanda said hopelessly, her face saddened.

I was scrolling through the 3 previous books and decided that I should write a PaReminisce for Paradox 3.

 

Book 3 - Paradox 3: The Search for Extreme

 

Immediately after arriving back at Nexus Tower, the minifigures - Vanda, Meyer, Thenso, Yonder and Kangroo - separate and begin searching the Universe for Extreme. They are unsuccessful until Thenso, Yonder and Kangroo find Cerberus Xenos - the place where Extreme had been stranded for months.

Their Talon is caught in its eternal lightning storm, their engines and computers are scrambled and they crash to the surface. After receiving a garbled transmission from them, Vanda and Meyer follow them and share a similar fate.

Meyer sees a light and summons a Dragon to help him get to it. Thenso has had his limbs knocked off, and reassembles himself. He sees Vanda's katana nearby and walks over to it.

Without knowing it, Meyer and Thenso have seen the same thing and meet each other. They see another light in the distance and decide to take Vanda's katana and carry it, however it is extremely heavy.

Meanwhile, Duke Exeter takes Kangroo and a squadron of his best Talons to Cerberus Xenos. These Talons are modified and are not damaged by the lightning. They land when they see an exhausted Kangroo lying on the rock, with an army of Maelstrom on the horizon. They pick him up and speed up their pace toward a light in the distance.

Meyer and Thenso find a cave where a magical Guardian, Shade, explains that Vanda must be the new Keeper to keep balance in the Universe. Yonder stumbles into the cave, disoriented, and Extreme leaps out at the four from the shadows in happiness.

Duke arrives with the Maelstrom army is on his tail, and then chaos erupts as Meyer leaps into the main cave. Suddenly the old Keeper appears and explains the Shade that he had stored his last strength away for a moment like this. He throws everyone out of the cave, makes Shade a minfigure and then gets to work erasing Chaos from the cave.
