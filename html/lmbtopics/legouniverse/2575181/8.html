‎03-01-2012 01:18 AM

The minifigures trudged on. The path was becoming steeper and harder to walk on, and the trees were thinning on either side of them.
Overbuild was mournful. His Engineer, Summoners and Inventors had been mostly smashed, and the few that had survived ran, being smashed on the way or infected. There were probably only two or three left, all injured. Or maybe they were all smashed.
Yonder had some memory loss. He'd knocked over Sutoro, then been knocked out himself. The next thing he knew, a golden shinobi was fighting a huge army as the others ran from it.
Extreme was mourning the loss of his beloved Wormholer. He was holding his double-rocket launcher, his Blaster in its holster at his belt. He'd removed his damaged helmet and left it behind. If anyone came along, they would know of the sacrifices that had been made at the path - Sutoro's, Overbuild's, everyone's. And the battle that had been fought, lost, won, lost, then won finally.
Thenso was weakened with his newly-repaired Imagination Spark. He was gaining energy and losing it each step he took.
Shade felt purpose for the first time since he felt his position as an immortal Guardian. He could live on as a guardian to Vanda, to Paradox and fighting the Maelstrom which had taken over Sutoro's lost soul and bent it to its will, making Sutoro into a beast trained to attack him. He would honor Sutoro, and fight to avenge him.
Meyer was supercharged. Vanda was no longer trying to kill him, and a weight he'd been holding for years had been lifted from his shoulders as he'd finally told Vanda the truth.
Vanda herself was thinking. About alternate courses of time. If they hadn't left Sutoro on Crux Prime. If Vanda had killed Meyer. If Meyer hadn't loved her. If they'd never met. If Sutoro had not released his spirit after his physical form was destroyed...
Her thoughts went on. And as they did, the minifigures got closer to the city of Tek. Watchful eyes observed them from the trees, from high above, from the city, even, as they got closer.
Then they dispatched a squadron of Teknites.
~ Brickmaster7946

The basic footsoldiers of the Tekuillien Maelstrom, Teknites used to be simple law enforcement officers. Now they used their weapons against the minifigures.
They approached carefully, slipping to trees and near the fallen rubble from the Maelstrom's initial seige of the city. The minifigures reached their pistol range. The first took aim and...
Exploded in a shower of bricks.
The minifigures on the path, shaken from their thoughts, looked up and saw the Teknites aiming at them. The Teknites jumped, their cover completely blown. A gunshot sounded, and a Teknites yelled out, a laser blasting it's eye. It smashed.
Extreme unleashed a salvo of rockets at a few Teknites, smashing them. The unknown shooter took another shot at the Teknites, picking off yet another.
The battle was over in a matter of seconds, with Extreme's rockets and the mysterious sniper. Vanda called out, "Show yourself! We are members of the Nexus Force, here to-"
A voice echoed, "Nexus Force?"
"Yes," Vanda confirmed.
A small hole in the wall of Tek fell from it's spot, then landed clumsily on the ground below. It was a fake piece.
In the wall was a minifigure holding a rifle. He didn't look like a warrior - just dressed in a white hoodie and black pants. His hair was wild, brown and spiky.
"I'm Coyote," he said. "Welcome to Tek city."
