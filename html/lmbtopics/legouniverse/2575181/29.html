Paradox, though without a leader, still fought bravely. Space Marauders with Wormholers and Shinobi held the front lines, while Sorcerers summoned Spiders and Thunder Clapped anyone who got close.
Back on the bridges, the Sentinels were getting close to finishing off all the Maelstrom on their bridges, but the small band of Paradox had only got halfway down their bridge. While progress was being made, every second they were delayed reaching the Nexus, more Maelstrom were smashing Nexus Force and getting closer to the Last Shard of Pure Imagination.
Most of the Maelstrom were Teknites and Elites, but every once in a while a higher-ranking monster would appear from the mess of Tekuilliens. One such monster, this one a small creature with a large helmet, and a very sharp-looking glowing red blade in its arm, leaped over the heads of its allies, managing to evade all of Coyote's shots. As it got close, Vanda swung at it, but using Vanda's blade to gain momentum, it flew into the air, and came down upon Meyer, blade raised.

Assassins hung from the roof, holes were blasted in the floor, dropping unaware Stromlings into lower levels, the walls were covered in Maelstrom splodges.
Slicing through Stromling after Stromling, Khin never broke a sweat. His dagger cut through the Maelstrom like a hot knife through butter.
Slowly, the Nexus Force cleared through the room, smashing everything in their path. Then they broke into the Venture League area.
As Extreme revved his Wormholer to begin firing again, there was a roar from above.
Then, suddenly, hundreds of Rutcarvers dropped from the sky.
