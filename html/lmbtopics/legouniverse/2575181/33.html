CAHPTAR Part 2

 

The Nexus Force had won.  The battle was over. The Tekuilliens had been defeated, the Darkitect's plan foiled yet again, and the Tower saved from a huge invasion.

The victory had not come without loss, of course. Fallen minifigures lay on the ground, some revivable, but for some it was too late. Maelstrom had infected others, most of which were treated with a notion potion.

Jammers
Notion Potions can only be used to uninfect very recently infected minifigures, and cannot be used on Maelstrom creations (they have been made by the Darkitect). Some must be treated with Super Notion Potions depending on how strong the infection was.

Though the Maelstrom were defeated, the forces on Crux Prime's battlefields below had been bolstered. Some Tekuilliens were even there. Bronson Jarls was sitting next to a turret, smashing Stromling, after Stromling, after Teknite.

The other members of the Factions had the joy of cleaning out and repairing Nexus Tower. Maelstrom were smashed everyone, and purple goo stained the stainless brick flooring, which astonished Doctor Overbuild. Some bricks even had to be replaced, since they kept trying to attack minifigures.

CAHPTAR Part 3

 

The Nexus itself had remained completely untouched during the battle, much to the pride of all the minifigures who had defended it. It gave many people the nostalgia of entering the Tower for the first time after it had been built.

Coyote got used to being around the Nexus Force members during this time, but refused to join the Force itself, preferring to stay seperate. He did, however, agree to perform any missions they offered him - for a price, of course. He  argued that a sniper needed to purchase more ammunition after a mission, and a bit of profit never hurt anyone.

Shade was commended for leading Paradox during the Tekuillien attack, and was granted a specially crafted longsword from Sentinel's blacksmiths and Paradox's enchanters.
