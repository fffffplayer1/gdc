Vanda and Duke talked at the edge of the paused battle. The Sentinels and Paradox eyed each other warily, and Coyote had his eyes on Duke, his rifle ready.
"Duke," Vanda said. "First: Why did you attack us?"
Duke was silent for a moment, then said, "You have betrayed us! The Nexus Force is in peril because of what you have done! The Tekuillien scientists were with you in the plan, I am sure. So we came here to put an end to this treachery, starting with them!"
"Why the Tekuilliens? Surely they did not go to attack you, did they? You opened fire on innocent people, Duke?"
"Is that what Sentinel is about?!" screamed Yonder.
Duke opened his mouth to speak, but then he seemed to doubt his answer and instead muttered, "No, I suppose not." Waiting a moment, he added, "But you have betrayed us! And for traitors there is no mercy!"
His expression victorious, he knocked Vanda to the ground with his arm and drew his sword, and began to swing it.
Then a bang echoed around the camp, and Duke gasped, then fell backward, his sword falling from his hand as Coyote swiftly reloaded his rifle.

‎06-28-2012 05:09 AM

The Sentinels raised their weapons and Samurai aimed their bows at Coyote, who in return put his finger on his rifle trigger.
"Oh, please," Thenso said. "Enough! Lower your weapons, everyone."
The Sentinels wavered a bit, but only a few put down their weapons.
Standing back up, Vanda said, "Lower them! This dispute is over."
Several more Sentinels dropped their weapons, but still, most didn't move.
"Don't make me start it again!" Vanda threatened, slipping one of her katana from its sheath.
This time, the Sentinels lowered their weapons.
"Okay, Duke, time to talk," Vanda said, sheathing her katana and walking over to the minifigure.
Duke groaned and looked up. "He shot me!" he said. "I could've been smashed!"
"What you you planning on doing to me?" Vanda said.
"You have a point," Duke said, reaching for his sword.
Extreme stepped in and kicked the sword toward Yonder, who grabbed it up and morphed his arm into a Stromling's, rendering the sword part of his arm.
"Not getting that back just yet," Extreme said, his Wormholer still revved.
"Fine," Duke said. "What do you want to know?"
"Why do you think we are traitors?"
"Stop trying to deceive us, Paradox!" Duke said. "We know what you've done!"
"What did we do, Duke? Tell us!"
"The Stromlings, of course! You conspired with the Tekuilliens! That's why we attacked them. You sent the Stromlings at-"
"Stromlings?" Vanda asked. "What are you talking about?!"
"The Stromlings you sent to attack Nexus Tower! You've betrayed the-"
"You fool!" Vanda exclaimed. "We didn't do that! It was the Darkitect! He wanted us to intercept the transmission all along - he wanted to make sure we weren't at the Tower when he attacked!"
"What do you mean?" Duke asked, puzzled.
"Operation T-Minus was all for nothing! The Tekuillien Maelstrom attack has already begun - the Darkitect deceived us!"
Duke's eyes widened. "Soldiers! To the Talons! Get to the Tower immediately!"
He stood and grabbed up he shield. "I'm so sorry, Vanda," he said. "I just thought..."
"Don't worry, Duke," Vanda said. "We need to save Imagination right now."
Duke nodded and raced off to one of the Talons, Yonder giving him his sword on the way. Paradox assembled in one of the Talons.
"Time to save the Universe again!" Extreme said. "No big deal."

The landing platforms were stocking up the main siege force, three dropships at a time were deploying troops there.

Duke landed his Talon right on top of a squad of Teknites, jumped out and began dicing up Tekuilliens. The Sentinels cheered and did the same.
"The third platform hasn't got any Talons on it," Yonder noted.

As they came in to land, Extreme shifted his Wormholer into a combat position and said, "Here goes nothing."

As the Talon circled above, firing on the dropships, clouds of smoke began to fill the platform as Extreme laid more carnage.
"I'm not being left out," Thenso said, drawing his wand. He too jumped onto the platform, landing and creating a blast of Maelstrom energy. Yonder followed him down, morphing into a Stromling and slicing apart Tekuilliens.

Meyer summoned Maelstrom at the Tekuilliens, and Vanda drew her glowing katana, then jumped into battle.
