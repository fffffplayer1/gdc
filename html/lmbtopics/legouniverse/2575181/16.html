The Tekuilliens' plan had bombs to be set up in five locations around the city. One would be in the lab itself, and the others at the base of key buildings in Tek city.
"We need to be careful," Coyote said. "Ratchen may not be the only traitor. Keep your eyes on the explosives at all times."
"Of course," Zevius said. "For maximum security, you will have poessession of all five bombs. I will stay here and prepare the portal for evacuation."
"Let's go," Meyer said.
***
The minifigures had set two of the five bombs. Approaching the third site, they saw a flash of purple from a nearby tower. Coyote immediately drew his rifle and fired at the shot, blasting the creature in the eye. It exploded into Maelstrom smoke.
Before anyone could speak, a grinding noise filled the air. A section of nearby ground slid open, and Teknites began pouring out.
"Move!" Extreme yelled.
Meyer summoned several Mechs, then joined the others dashing towards the bomb site. It was a low building, only a few stories tall, but much wider and longer than other buildings. The doors opened on it and several infected Tekuillien assassins jumped out, but Extreme tore them apart with his Wormholer.
As they entered the door, Coyote sniped two more from an opening elevator, then Thenso Thunder clapped the approaching waves of Teknites outside. Yonder morphed into a Stromling and leapt at the door panel, slamming the button and stabbing the controls.
The doors sealed and the wires were fried.
"We made it," Vanda said, sheathing her katana. Yonder transformed back into a minifigure, and Coyote lowered his rifle.
"However," Thenso said, pointing out a window, "we still have about a few thousand Teknites pounding around outside.
In the windows, all that could be seen outside were Teknites, covering the ground everywhere. They were still coming out of the hole in the ground, surrounding the building and bashing on the walls.
"How are there that many?!" Meyer asked. "Surely there weren't even that many people living in Tek city."
"Perhaps," Yonder said, "but maybe... the Darkitect has been cloning his soldiers."
