Chapter 43 part 1:
"Wake up, meester wizard."

Ninja's eyes refused to open.

"WAKE UP, MEESTER WIZARD!!!!"

Ninja awoke, only to find himself tied to a girder on the ceiling of Nexus Tower. Bonezai's face was in front of his. "WHAT THE-?!" He yelled(You'd yell, too, if some skeleton was screaming in your face while you were tied to the ceiling). "Good, you're awake." Grinned Bonezai. "Now, in case you deed not know, I am Bonezai, deh awesomest skeleton in de universe, yah." Ninja struggled to get free while Bonezai rambled on: "I wanted you to be awake so you'd be alive when I smash you, yah. Because smashing people ees more fun when they know they're going to be smashed." Ninja responded, "Can't I just rebuild myself?" Bonezai's grin vanished. "Ummmm... Well..." While the skeleton was thinking of a response to his question, Ninja continued to push the ropes. Fortunetly, Bonezai was apparently bad at knots. With some effort, Ninja finally broke himself free. He fell to the floor, landing on his feet, and ran out of Bonezai's sight. He sighed with relief. He turned to head back to his friends when he came face-to-face with Blastbreath the dragon.

Chapter 44 part 1:
"OPEN FIRE!!!" Yelled Hael Storm. Another barrage hit The Darkitect, one that contained more Firecrackers, bullets, lasers, and anything else you can think of in a lifetime. Hael had assembled all the other minifigures in LEGO Universe, just as Duke had told him to. Everyone was here now- Furious Urufu, Epsilon Starcracker, Heimlich Stewblaster, you name it.

With a fighting force like this, Hael was sure they could bring The Darkitect down.

Another barrage struck their target. The Darkitect suddenly fired a blast of Maelstrom at a third of Hael's fighting force, infecting them. "Blimey." Whispered Hael. The new Stromlings suddenly turned on their former comrades, and fighting off Stromlings while at the same time trying to fight something else was difficult. Just then, however, several minifigures leaped out and began driving away the intruders. At the same time, several others stood in for the figs that had been infected.
The Darkitect tried to fight back, but his strength was draining. "KEEP FIRING, LANDLUBBERS!!" Shouted Hael. The minifigures continued. Finally, The Darkitect collapsed. "CONSTANT FIRE!!" Hael gave the final order to finish him off. He didn't last 5 seconds- Suddenly, with a great roar, The Darkitect's body turned into balck smoke, which then drifted away.

The assumed master of chaos was defeated.

Well, let's see... I think I have a few tips:

1. Read. You can't write a good story if you don't read a good story.

2. Write down your ideas before posting them. That way, if you've written a bit that you actually don't want in your story, you didn't just send it out for the whole world to see.

3. I think you already know this, but remember that this shouldn't be a game(Exception for Formic's "The Nightfall Incident"). Try to make it sound as less like a game as possible.

Chapter 44 part 2:
The Darkitect's master watched from his lair the destruction of his slave.

Typhonus had blown it. The plan failed with his eternal smash, and he knew as soon as the Nexus Forcers drove out his minions, they'd improve the security so that they'd never be able to launch another attack again. The Master was furious. Because now he had to go do it himself.
Great creature he was, he heaved himself out of the pit he lived. Confused by the stir, Spiderlings poured out of the pit with him. The Master simply shook them off. His weight was so huge that he accidently pushed the stone path out of it's place in the Vortex, causing it to become destroyed by the surrounding Maelstrom. The Master didn't care. Once he destroyed Nexus, everything that has Imagintation- which was nearly everything- would be corrupted. His army would no longer be restricted to boundaries. Once out of the Vortex, he turned himself into a great cloud of Maelstrom- something very few creatures were capable of doing- and flew out to deliver the final wave of destruction, one that nothing could stand.
