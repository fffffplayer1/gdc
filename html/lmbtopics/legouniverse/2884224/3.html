Chapter 17: The Ravine
Nexus Jay blandly told me where the Launchpad to Gnarled Forest was, then went back to whatever he was doing on his notepad. I excitedly ran over to the Launchpad- which was behind the Venture League Faction Vendor- and took off. I landed on a lush world, full of overgrown trees and other wildlife. I talked to the Launchpad Guard, then set off to explore. It was so nice in Gnarled Forest, I almost couldn't believe that there were dangerous Stromlings lurking. Suddenly, as I was walking, my foot gave way to nothingness. I caught myself just in time. As I looked down, I realized that I had almost fallen over the edge of a huge chassis. It was peppered with large platforms, but they seemed so far apart. Below was a huge lake. I gulped, then began looking for a way to cross. I noticed there used to be a rope bridge, but something had completely destroyed it. I looked at the platform nearest to the edge where I was standing. 'Oh no,' I thought, 'There has to be an easier way-' But there was no easier way.
I had to jump.
Bracing myself, I stepped back a few paces for a running start, then ran. As I passed the edge I tried to jump, but unfortunetly it was too late. I fell and fell and fell, until I smashed in the lake. I rebuilt where I had started. I tried again, and missed again. It must have taken me 15 tries before I finally reached the other side. "Yesss!" I said to myself. I smashed a pile of stuff in front of me, then proceded down a dark corridor.

What I didn't know was that my troubles weren't nearly over.

Chapter 18: PIRATES!!
At the end of the coridor was a barrel. Several minifigures were standing around it. 'What are they doing around a simple barrel?' I wondered. I walked up to it. Suddenly the lid opened and out popped a minifigure head. "Y-Y-Your not a pirate zombie, are you?" He uttered. "Um, nooo..." I said. "Phew! That must mean you're a minifigure! I'm Toby Squidbarrel. You see, we have a bit of a problem over here. Look yonder!" He said, pointing out into a small field. There was a big pit in the middle of it, and surrounding it were strange new Stromlings. Most of them looked like regular pirates. "I want you to git out there and smash some of 'em, then come back to me." Toby finished. Now, if I wasn't wearing my new gear, I would have been a little hesitant to go out and fight them. But I was desperate to give my gear a try. I rushed out and began attacking the new Stromlings. One of them noticed me and began lumbering in my direction. I built my new turret, which immediately began shooting the pirate. Meanwhile, I went after the pirate myself, and thanks to my new wrench quickly smashed it. I began to go around smashing other Stromling Pirates, as they were called, when I accidently crashed into another minifigure. "I'm sorry, I didn't see you there." I apologized. Suddenly I thought I recognized the minifigure. "Hey, aren't you that girl from Nimbus Station? I think Mardolf said your name was Darby...?" The minifigure- a Rank 1 Venture League Daredevil- recognized me as well. "Hey, I remember you too! Um, I wanted to apologize for my friend Ninja's behavior. He has really been looking forward to getting his Faction." She noticed my suit. "That looks cool." Said Darby. "As does yours!" I responded. Our converstion was broken up by a Pirate of whom we quickly smashed. "Hey, um, your name was BizzareNerdyLegend, wasn't it? Anyway, do you want to be on our team?" She asked. "Sure!" I said. Then, "What's a team, by the way?"

Chapter 19: APE!!!
A fter Darby explained what being on a team meant- working together, basically, and sharing the loot- I agreed to join her. Her friend EvilSlimyNinja happened to be there, too, but I can't say he was happy to see me. He never thought very highly of Assembly, Darby explained to me. Ninja was wearing a Rank-1 Paradox Sorcerer suit. After we had our fill of smashing as well as finished our missions we headed off to see a pirate named Aaarthur Aaarbuckle. As we proceded down a long corridor, a Rank-1 Sentinel Space Ranger suddenly bolted around the corner, screaming, "HEEELP!! IT'S THE END OF THE UNIVERSE!!! GIANT MAELSTROM CREATURE!!!" As he was running, he crashed into Ninja. "Watch where you're going!" he called after him. "Giant Maelstrom creature?" I muttered to myself. Suddenly something hit me with huge force, instantly smashing me. Once I rebuilt I saw what hit me. A giant infected ape was sitting right there in the middle of the path. The ape threw another boulder, but I dodged it ran over to Ninja and Darby, who were crouching behind a tree. "What should we do about it?" Darby asked. I suggested we could try going around it, but that seemed highly unlikely. Meanwhile Ninja just stared at the ape. "Imagine all the loot I could get outta that thing..." He mumbled. Suddenly he took up his weapon and charged the ape. "NINJA!!" Darby cried. Obviously he got smashed. He came back to us after rebuilding himself. "Ow." He said. Then, "I know how to smash these things, for the record. I was just showing you how not to do it." Then I got an idea. "Hey! This probably sounds crazy, but what if we tried to smah it? After all, It's gotta be smashable, right?" Darby and Ninja looked at each other. "I was... just about to suggest that." Said Ninja. "Well..." Said Darby, pulling out her Daredevil Flareguns from her Backpack. "I don't know what else we should do."

Chapter 20: Brig Rock
The ape was tougher than we expected. Aside throwing boulders, the ape also beat the ground, which knocked us back. We never did get the loot from that ape, though. As we were fighting, a Rank-3 Sentinel Samurai came and finished it off with 3 swipes with his sword. After Ninja yelled at him, we found Aaarthur Aaarbuckle, who eventually asked us to head down to Brig Rock.
There were hundreds of Stromling Pirates and Admirals. We got a mission from someone asking us to smash some of the Admirals. So we went out to fight. I found an Admiral right away, which began to run towards me. Suddenly he planted his anchor in the ground, and before I knew what he was doing, he blasted me with his cannon, knocking me back several mini-feet. I got back up and charged him. He threw his anchor down again, but this time I gave him a few good blows. Eventually I at last smashed him. I then went off to smash some more. Once I had smashed my Admirals, I headed back to the same minifigure who had asked me to smash them to recieve my next mission.

Sorry for the boring chapter. I'm trying to hurry up and get to the exciting bit to my story...

Chapter 21: Nexus Tower
We finished up our missions in Gnarled Forest. Basically, we found a mad gunman who was actually a monkey, and succesfuly smashed a Stromling Ape. I couldn't believe all the goods that came out of it! My share of the loot included an Anchor and a Notion Potion.(Yeah, I didn't help out much.) We didn't get any more missions, so we all decided to head back to Nimbus Station. On the way there, Darby wanted to know more about me. I told her about the Venture Explorer incident, and what it was like to confront the Spider Queen. "Me and Ninja wanted to go and fight her," Darby said after I mentioned the Spider Queen. "But there was actually a line at the Launchpad of minifigures wanting to fight the Spider Queen. You wouldn't BELIEVE how long it was." About the time I finished, we arrived at Nimbus Station. "C'mon, maybe Jay has somethin' for us," Waved Ninja. Nexus Jay DID have something for us: The offer to go to Nexus Tower.
"The Launchpad is down by the Vertigo Loop Race Place. Once you get to Nexus Tower, find Nexus Naomi and she'll tell you where to find th Faction Leaders." He instructed us blandly. We hurried off. I wondered what Nexus Tower was gonna be like, and what it was gonna be like to meet Dr. Albert Overbuild, in person! I loaded my rocket onto the Launchpad, and took off.

Chapter 22: Nexus Tower, part 2
As we aproached Crux Prime, I squinted at the glowing blue beam, growing as we got closer. The beam shot out the top of an enormous tower. Nexus Tower. I looked below towards the base and saw the unsightly surface of Crux, its edges poking out like glass shards. I heard about the grounds there- I looked forward to fighting there. In fact, I was so mesmerized by the whole planet chunk, I nearly wrecked my rocket again on the Launch Bay. Darby and Ninja had already landed. "Is that what you call driving?" I heard Ninja mumble under his breath. Darby poked him with her elbow. "Come on, let's go inside," Said Darby. "I'm just DYING to meet Hael Storm." She said that last bit with a sort of dreamy look in her eyes. She ran out, followed by me and Ninja.
The first thing we noticed was an enormous hologram of the Nexus Symbol, surrounded by the Faction Logos. We walked up to a hologram of a woman in front of the larger hologram. "Hello, and welcome to Nexus Tower." She spoke in a soft voice. "I am Nexus Naomi, your guide to Nexus Tower. Would you like to meet your Faction Leaders?" We all shouted "YES!" at the same time. Ninja bowed his head awkwardly. "If you would like to meet your Faction Leaders, follow the Logos of your appropriate Faction." I looked to my right, and there was a line of holographic Assembly Logos hovering down the hall. "Wow, look at-" I turned to where Ninja and Darby were, but apparently they had already ran off to meet their Faction Leaders. So I proceded to find my own.

Wow, this is my best chapter so far! We're getting closer to that twist I mentioned earlier...

Chapter 23: The Faction Leaders... and THE ORB OF HORROR!!
I had a blast. Dr. Albert Overbuild was very kind and gentle, though he seemed to be worried about something.(I later found out Overbuild was afraid of pretty much everything[Sorry, Overbuild.].) I did a few errands for him- whole-heartedly, of course- and then he asked me to visit the other Faction Leaders.

I think the first one was Hael Storm. First thing he did, right off the bat, was ask me to find his Compasses, which were all on top of the walls around the Vendor area. Took me about 15 whole minutes to get them all. He asked me to do some other things. I'm not saying I didn't like him, I just thought he could have given me a better greeting.

The Paradox Lab was creepy. Vanda Darkflame fit right in.
"Um, hello?"
Vanda swung her infected katanas a bit to close for comfort in front of my face. "Sorry," She said, quickly retracting her katanas. "I like to show off."
She asked me to do a few other tasks, just like the others did. But one of them was the very worst.
"Do you see the purple orb over there?" She asked.
"Uh... Yeah...?"
"I want you to gaze into it. Do not be alarmed by the scene that's set before you. To fight the Maelstrom, we must show no fear."
How comforting.
I hopped down from the platform and walked over to the orb. I looked into it. I was just to say "I don't see anything", when all of a sudden, I felt myself falling. The Paradox Lab faded, and was replaced by a deepening shade of purple...

Next thing I knew, I was surrounded by Maelstrom In fact, I was IN the Maelstrom. I was standing on a floating path, suspended in the massive tunnel of Pure Maelstrom. I thought I saw the form of a minifigure in the distance...
I didn't have time to think about that, because just then a terrifying face swooped in at me. As I stood back up, it suddenly got dark. VERY dark. There was a purple glow in the distance, and in front it was the same minifigure form I thought I saw earlier. His dark sillouhette disappered, and then reappeared, only closer. A couple of balls of Maelstrom Fog circled around the path. The sillohette got closer and closer until he was right in front of me.
He being The Darkitect.
The balls of Maelstrom Fog hurtled towards me. Just before they made impact, I heard the figure speak.
"You'll return."

I yelped and flew backwards, crashing into a Paradox Guard. "Hey! Watch where you're going!" He snapped. I looked around me; everything was just as it was before. I was relieved. I got up, apologized to the guard, and went back up to Vanda to tell her just how awful the experience was to me.

But if only I had recalled The Darkitect's words...

Chapter 24: Duke Exeter
"So, how did it go?"
Me, Ninja and Darby had all gathered back together to talk about our visits. "Vanda is awesome." Ninja said. "She's awesome. Her place is awesome. And that orb was super awesome." I shuddered. Ninja looked at me. "You were SCARED of it? It was just a vision! It didn't really happen." I decided to change the subject. "Hey, has anyone met Duke yet?"
"I was just about to do that."
"So was I."
"Well, let's go see what he's like!"
Duke Exeter's place was grand. Minifigures of much higher rank were trying to sell whatever they could to make a few U-coins. Duke Exeter was on a platform above.
Duke held in his hands the massive sword that I had seen him holding on so many advertisement posters. In his other hand, he held his great shield to match the proportions of his sword. He greeted us with a smile. "I'm guessing your own leFaction Leaders sent you?" We nodded. "Well, as you already might have figured, I'm Duke Exeter, commander of the Sentinels. And now that your here, I could use your help..."
When he said "help", he meant run all over the Universe smashing enemies for an object Duke wanted us to get. Once we gave him the Dark Ronin helmets and lances, he asked to get an anchor from a Stromling Admiral or Ape. As we left, we heard him call out to us, saying, "You go do that, and when you come back, I think you'll be ready for the surface of Crux Prime."
"Crux Prime"?
I was wondering whether I should be happy or nervous.
Nervous, probably.

OK, if I'm starting to bore you, I only have about 2-3 chapters more, and then we get to the EXCITING bit!!!
