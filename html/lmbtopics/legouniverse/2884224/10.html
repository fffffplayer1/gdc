Chapter 45 part 1:
Me and Monkey were running down to the Venture League section when we heard huge noises. They came from the heart of Nexus Tower. Where Imagination was.
"Uh-oh!" I said as we ran down to where the noise was coming from. It was worse than bringing down 3 dragons simutaneously. What we saw was The Darkitect pitifuly fending off attacks from hundreds of minifigures. Monkey pulled out a Rutcarver. "Dang it! Why didn't anyone tell me they were bringing down The Darkitect?! Come on, before they get all the loot!" I wasn't sure that you'd get loot outta The Darkitect, but I followed anyway. By now, The Darkitect had collapsed under the force of the barrages. "CONSTANT FIRE!!" Hael yelled over the roar of the gunfire. Monkey used a Fig on Fire to plow through the croud so he could at least deal him a few blows. That's when he blew up.
A great roar knocked back surrounding minifigures. Black smoke rose from The Darkitect's rapidly vaporizing body, then, he was gone.
I got up. The radiation had drained my health to a dangerously low degree. Nevertheless, minifigs cheered. That's when suddenly Darby ran into me. "Legend!" She cried, embracing me. "Where were you?!"

Meanwhile, the rest of Team Elite joined our group. "Well done, guys!" Said Awesomeme. He was beaming. He turned towards Monkey. "Who's this?" He asked me. I answered, "A friend." Suddenly the Twins came running up. "Guys! We can't find Ninja!" Said one. "Yeah! We can't find him anywhere!" Suddenly, a new sound was heard over the victorious shouts of minifigures.

Chapter 45 part 3:
The new sound seemed to silence all others. It was a faint, thundering sound. It grew louder and louder as whatever it was neared.

Meanwhile, Hael was grinning at all his Venture League-ers, as well as anyone else who could thank him with joyous shouts of triumph. He turned around to meet Duke. "Yo-ho-ho, me heartie! We did it! We smashed the... blasted... uuuh..." He stopped as he observed Duke's furious expression. "Why didn't you tell me you were smashing The Darkitect?!" He growled. He didn't like it when someone got to something first. Especially something this big. "You told me to get reinforcements, landlubber! What else was I supposed to make 'em do? Stand there until they got your say-so?" Duke facepalmed. "Well, I'm sure you could have at LEAST told-" He and Hael stopped as they realized that all the minifigures were becoming quiet. Listening, they heard the same thunderous noise. They looked at each other. Mouthing a truce agreement, both of them ran out to see what the commotion was about.
The sound came from near the Launch Bay. Once Duke and Hael got there, they gasped. The sky outside was shaded purple by an ominous cloud of Maelstrom. Among the many minifigures, Vanda and Overbuild stood gaping. The cloud decsended just outside Nexus Tower. A portion of the cloud drifted away to reveal the most terrible thing any minifigure could imagine. They merely stared in horror. Only Duke had enough courage to utter its name.

"The Spider Lord."

Chapter 46 part 2:
The creature stood on huge, lanky legs, which contributed to the bulk of its height. The cluster of glaring yellow eyes went as far back as the sides of its massive body. Mandibles dragged the air into its mouth, which was surrounded by long needle-like teeth. An arsenal of lethal-looking weapons topped its massive back. The creature was much diffrent from when Duke had seen it last, but the shape was vaguely recognizable. Apart from the above descriptions, it seemed to resemble the Spider Queen. Even the Maelstrom was afraid of the massive beast, and Stromlings were going as far as blasting holes through Nexus Tower's walls and leaping through them, thus plummeting themselves to their smash.
The only creatures that remained were numerous Dark Spiderlings, which scuttled over to their ultimate master. We all looked up in horror. The creature did nothing for an agonizingly long while but stand there, eyeing each minifigure through its numerous eyes. It blocked the exit to Nexus Tower, which meant we couldn't escape.

We had to stay and fight. We had no choice.

Doc had slunk away to find a hiding place for himself, and several other figs were getting the same idea. Duke sighed and drew his sword. Vanda, realizing what he was doing, gripped his arm. "Don't do it! That thing will smash you- forever!!" Duke sighed again and turned towards her. "A true leader must lead by example." He gently pushed off Vanda's fingers and marched off.

Chapter 46 part 2:
Duke equipped his helmet, sword, and shield. Without him even having to call them, several other minifigures equipped their best gear and followed him. The Spiderlings were itching to lunge at the minifigures, but the Spider Lord willed them to stay back. Awesomeme44 and his team was among those to go forward. "Should we go?" I asked Monkey. He nodded. "We gotta at least say we tried." He pulled out a Rutcarver, and I my Rank 3 Summoner staff. Then we marched.
The Spider Lord eyed the approaching army. He waited until just the right moment. At one point, Duke charged, still followed by his little army.

The moment was now.

Suddenly it started stomping on Duke. "ATTACK!!" He cried while at the same time dodging the creature's massive legs. The minifigures opened fire, but to their great surprise and horror, the projectiles only pinged off of its body, corrupted. The monster hissed and kicked Duke aside. He crashed into a wall, then went limp. Me and Monkey continued firing at it when a gun began to pull itself out of the creature's body. "WHAT'S THAT?!" I asked over the roar of ammunition and attacking Spiderlings.
"WHAT'S WHAT?!"
"THAT!!"
"DO YOU MEAN-"
Monkey didn't get to finish the answer. Nor was it neccesary. Because it was answered when the Spider Lord fired a barrel of Maelstrom at it's attackers.

Chapter 47 part 1:
A HUGE amount of Maelstrom poured from the Spider Lord's gun. The walls caved in upon us under the force of it.
For me, there was nothing but confusion. Walls falling. Minifigures screaming. Maelstrom everywhere. The Spiderlings clicked and charged at us while we were distracted. I was sent flying into a wall. A hissing sound rose from my arm. It was covered in purple stuff. And the purple stuff was spreading.

I was being infected.

I felt too weak to gasp. All I could manage was the feeble word, "Help..." Suddenly I felt myself being dragged away. "Probably some Stromling making sure I get infected thoroughly," I thought gloomily until I saw through my fading vision some figure pulling out a bottle and pouring it onto my arm. My vision began to return as I saw one of the Paradox twins standing over me, holding an empty vial of something I had never seen before, marked with the Paradox eye.
"You alright?" He asked. He himself had a purple blotch on his chest, and by the way he acted and sounded I could tell it hurt. I managed a nod. "What... about you?" I asked. "I'll be fine." I could tell this was a lie. The blotch had spread to his shoulders and waist. Suddenly I looked around. As the Maelstrom Smoke cleared, I could see the Spider Lord had left. "The Spider Lord- Where is he?" I asked. "Probably... to Imagination. You gotta go after him." He responded. I drew back in horror. "Me?! But I'm just a... I don't have the right..." He cut me off. "There'll be... others with you. If we... don't... do something, it... will be all the worse for... us." His strenghth was draining. Aparently he didn't have another bottle of stuff. I drew in a breath. "Alright, fine. I'll go." I turned. "It's the right thing to do." At last, after thanking the Space Marauder, I ran off to meet my destiny.
