Chapter 6 part 2:

The one with the cocoon laid down its cargo before its master, an ancient coal-black skeleton draped in a tattered black cloak. The figure drew a katana from his back and slit the white capsule. An angry, orange-bearded minifigure adorned with a pirate captain’s hat peeped out of the hole. “What’s the meaning of this?!” He shouted. “If you think ye can capture Captain Jack Knife, then ye be sadly mistaken!!” Jack examined the figure before him. “Wait a second... Ye be the skeleton who attacked Duke!!” The Hooded One nodded. “So he went and told all his little friends, didn’t he?” He turned towards the spider. Shiundai the spider bowed and turned to aid in the construction of the new base.

With one quick slash The Hooded One opened the cocoon, so Jack could step out. “Where’s my crew?!” The pirate captain demanded. “What’ve you done with ‘em?!” Mactarus slid his weapon back behind his cape. “You mustn’t worry about them,” He coaxed. “They’re in good hands with me.” But Jack refused to be calmed. “That doesn’t answer my question! Tell me what you’ve done!!” The Hooded One ignored his threats and casually took up his staff- The one he had forged out of Nexus itself for the Siege of Nexus Ka. “Are ye even listenin’ to me?!” Jack asked ever more irritably.

Chapter 7:

2 DAYS LATER

Legend’s POV

The engines of the Venture Royale, the latest addition to the Nexus Force’s transport fleet, seared the landing pad of Nimbus Station. Minifigures cheered from a safe distance.

Meanwhile, the other Venture-class cruisers- The Venture Eagle, the Venture Naval, and the Venture Unity- descended with a roar upon the three other landing pads. The massive passenger cruisers, after having traveled innumerable thousands of light-years, rested for a minute on the pad, as the Cruxians* waited anxiously for the arrival of the former recruits. Then, with a hiss, the massive doors of the Venture Royale opened, followed by the doors of the other cruisers. Minifigures cheered, as the Nexus Force Recruits stepped out.

The first one out was a sturdy minifigure, about six feet two inches. He had large bushy brows and a circle beard. But it was the iconic spiked blue hair that made it obvious to everyone who it was.

 

It was me, the legendary BizzareNerdyLegend.

Chapter 7 part 2:

Fireworks shot up and exploded high in the air, celebrating my arrival. Minifigures cheered harder than ever. I was followed by many other minifigures, of all different sizes, strengths, and hair colors. For some, this was their first time in Nimbus System, and they marveled at the new world they were stepping into. Meanwhile the other cruisers unloaded their passengers, who were greeted warmly by their own joyous gathering.

I began to fight my way through fans and eager news reporters to get to the podium. This was a reminder to me of how famous I was. I noticed two minifigures, a girl and boy, pushing their way through the crowd, calling and waving eagerly. At first I thought they were just more really excited fans, but as they got closer I began to recognize them. I grinned and pushed my own way through the crowd. “Ninja!” I called. “Darby! Over here!!” The three of us finally caught up with each other, and the paparazzi, having a sudden consideration to our privacy, began to back away to give us our peace.

Darby’s long black hair was braided, while Ninja’s hair hung loosely over his face, just like it always had. Ninja held out his hand, grinning. “Man, we missed you! How’s it going?” I eagerly took his hand and gave it a vigorous shake. I was about to answer when I heard another voice call our names from behind us. We turned around to greet Aron4 and Awesomeme44. Eventually LoudShockingMonkey caught up with us, too. We all proceeded in engaging each other in conversation, as other recruits, former and newbie alike began to do the same.

Chapter 7 part 3:

Eventually the Four Faction Leaders rose to the podium. Every eye turned towards them as Duke walked forward. He recapped the minifigures on the current situation: How he had met The Hooded One, how he and everyone else had learned his origins, and the mysterious seclusion of Gnarled Forest. Then the rest of the Faction Leaders joined in, explaining the new gear that was to be given to every recruit. We all listened intently.

***

Bob hadn’t been himself lately since learning of Mactarus’ return. He just didn’t feel like there had been anything to smile about recently. As he stood listening to the Faction Leader’s speech, he fondly recalled their youth together, when they were just young boys, playing games in the bright afternoon sun. How depressed he had been when Mactarus angrily shut himself up inside his hut after confronting the Nexus Officials about taking over the Nexus. Bob had warned him that the answer would almost absolutely be no, but he had ignored him.

Then there was when he returned years later, and tried to take the Nexus by force. He remembered Figmentia’s out-of-breath warning of the advancing Toshik. He had decided that he was the only one who really understood him and had made up his mind to confront him.

Suddenly Bob had an idea. They knew where Mactarus was hiding with his army. Bob looked out towards Shival. He was listening intently to Overbuild explaining all the new creatures Summoners could summon. Shival would not approve of what he intended to do. He didn’t approve of it the first time either. Bob drew a long breath and slunk away from the crowd. Once clear of eyes, he prepared to transport himself to Gnarled Forest. If I could confront Mactarus once, he thought to himself, Then I can do it again!
