SPIDER QUEEN
I had finished up the assigniments Rusty had given me, so I headed back to Beck Strongheart. "Beck Strongheart." I saluted. Beck looked at me and said, "Good! You're back. Your next assignment is to go down to the Spider Caves and speak to Melodie Foxtrot." My heart jumped. The Spider Caves!? I had never been down there before. I went down to a cave opening on the Battlefield. It was dark. There was obviously Maelstrom. I went in. After descending a short tunnel, I came to an open space. There were Stromlings, Stromling Mechs, and a hideous creature I had never seen before. In the distance, I saw a woman standing on an elevated surface. I figured she was Melodie Foxtrot. I hopped down, and was instantly attacked by an army of Stromlings. Unfortunetly for them, I had my trusty spear with me. They were no match for me and my weapon. I continued my path to Melodie until I caught the attention of one of the new creatures. I struck it with my spear. No smash. I struck it a second time. Still no smash. A third? Fourth!? The creature decided it was his turn. It charged up its weapon and fired at me. A pain sharper than the Stromling Mech's went through me. I felt like I was exploding, and then everything went black.

Instantly, I came to again. I thought I had died, but I was still alive. I had heard of minifigures who could rebuild themselves after being smashed, but I had never really believed them. I was invincible. Of course, there was one problem: I had rebuilt at the cave opening, where I had started. I sighed. This might take a little longer than I expected...

I love my Sentinel Shield. I got it from the same Vendor that got me the Breastplate of Armor. The Sentinel Shield was how I finally got past all the Stromlings, Mechs, and Spiderlings.(That's what the big creatures were called, by the way.) Yes, I got shot, but the force field that was projected from the Sentinel Shield protected me from any Maelstrom. I finally made my way over to Melodie. I got worried, however, when I saw that she was wearing blue- the Sentinel's color. 'Please, please, PLEASE don't yell at me!' I thought. I puffed myself up, and said, "Melodie Foxtrot." I also threw in a salute. Melodie looked at me and said, "Oh, hi. Did Beck send you?" I sighed with relief, quitely. "Yeah, in fact, he did send me. He said that you'd give me my next assignment." Melodie responded, "Well, I DO actually have a 'chore' for you to do for me. I'm sure you've met the Dark Spiderlings by now, haven't you?" I nodded. "Well, there are a whole bunch of 'em down there." She pointed down a dim, torch-lit corridor. there seemed to be purple web strewn upon the walls. "Yeah... So what?" "So I want you to smash 5 of them." I thought I was gonna be sick. 5 Spiderlings!? those guys seemed invincible!! "Uh... Maybe you'd need help smashing... Oh I don't know... A few Stromlings, perhaps?" I said, trying to ease myself into something easier. Melodie shook her head. I gulped. "I can understand your opinions on them, however. That's why I have some of these..." She held out 4 suits: One was green and grey, another was black and scarlet, still another was blue and silver, and the last one was orange and white. "These are Trial Faction suits," said Melodie. "I have here Venture League, Paradox, Sentinel, and Assembly Faction Suits. They'll help you smash the Spiderlings more easily." She smiled. "Which one do you choose?" They all looked powerful, but I chose the Assembly suit. It was my dream to become Assembly. I put it on. I had way more Shields, Imagination, and even Lives than I had previously thought possible. "Wow, you look awesome!" said Melodie. It was true- I DID look awesome. I was about to go crunch some Spiderlings, when a question popped into my mind. "Hey, Melodie?" "Hm?" "No offense, but- why aren't you fighting?" Melodie's smile faded. She looked around, then whispered, "I'm afraid of spiders." I shrugged, deciding not to make a point out of it. With that, I marched off to grind some Spiderlings.

Chapter 15: Ninja and Darby
"Oh! Sorry," I responded to whoever was behind me. The minifigure who spoke wasn't wearing much. His haircut wasn't to fancy either. "I need to see someone." He explained. "And I'd like to do it fast. I've been waiting FOREVER to get on the Parodox." He walked past me, followed by a girl. "Please don't be so impatient with him- we don't even know who he is!" I decided since they were heading in the same direction, I might as well follow. Mardolf the Orange happened to be an Assembly wizard. He had a long beard, a hat, and a staff with an Imagination Orb sitting on top. "I knew you were coming." He smiled. "Let's see here... You must have been sent by Nexus Jay. I believe your names are BizzareNerdyLegend, EvilSlimyNinja, and Darby." 'Ok- THAT was creepy,' I thought to myself. "I suppose you three are looking for something to do. Go and build a rocket using these parts, then come show it to me." He gave us each a selection of rocket parts. There was a Rocket Building Platform nearby, so we went over to that. I didn't think I needed another one, but considering my driving skills, I thought I might as well, just in case. Once I finished my rocket, I went back over to Mardolf. "Good work! Now, go back over to see Nexus Jay. He'll tell you what to do next.

*********************************************************************************

The rest of the day went on simuaraly(I know I spelled that wrong.). Nexus Jay was apperantly so caught up in whatever he was doing, he didn't even remember who I was.
"Nexus Jay?"
"Hm?"
"It's me, BizzareNerdyLegend?"
"Sorry, doesn't ring a bell."
"I'm the one who doesn't have a Faction yet?"
"Oh yeah, I remember you now."
After that, he told me to go see some guy named Johnny Thunder. And when I came back, he forgot who I was again. But after I reminded him, he told me exactly what I had been waiting to hear.

Chapter 16: A faction at Last!
Granted, there was no chorus singing when Nexus Jay said the words. He only said in his usual I'm-bored-out-of-my-skull tone, "Now that you've proven yourself, you're ready to get your own Faction. You can choose from the Sentinels, the Paradox, the Venture League or the Assembly. Now please, *yawn* hurry up and take your pick." Nevertheless, I couldn't believe it. A Faction at last! I immediately chose Assembly. Nexus Jay yawned again, then said, "Alright. Now, your Faction Vendor is over there. You'll need Faction Tokens to buy your gear, though. I think there might be some in your Mailbox." Then he went back to his doodling. I rushed over to the Mailbox and picked up a bag with the Nexus Force symbol on it, and then I ran over to my Faction Vendor. His name, I think, was Meldric Steamvalve. "Well, hello there!" He spoke in a gleeful tone. "I believe you're looking forward to getting you new gear! Alright, I have 3 options for you: Engineer, Summoner, and Inventor. Which one do you choose?" I decided to pick the Engineer suit. I paid my Tokens and U-coins, read the book, and then put my gear on. It consisted of a shirt, pants, a wrench, and a hard hat. I looked at it. I felt so much stronger. I noticed a button with a picture of my hard hat on it. I pressed it. A bunch of bricks fell in front of me. Using Quick-Build, I discovered that they built a small turret. "Nice!" Said Meldric. "Why don't you go show it to Nexus Jay over there? He might even send you off to try it out!" With that last bit in mind, I hurried over to Nexus Jay. Obviously, he was still doodling. After reminding him who I was, he gave me 2 options. "You can either go to Nexus Tower and see Imagination Nexus, or Gnarled Forest to fight powerful enemies. I thought about it, then deciding to save the best for last, I made my decision.
"Gnarled Forest."
