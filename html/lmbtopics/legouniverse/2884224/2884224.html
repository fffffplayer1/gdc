Suddenly the whole ship was thrown into chaos. Everyone trampling each other, trying to get to the nearest Escape Pod. It was loaded in 5 seconds, then it took off. "It's GONE!! The Escape Pod's GONE!!!!"
"WHAT!?"
"PANIC TIME!!!!"
It was true: the Escape Pod WAS gone. It must have been blasted off by the Maelstrom. In the confusion of it all, I had lost my friend. "LoudShockingMonkey!? Where are you, LoudShockingMonkey!?"

We all jumped onto the thing and were launched upward. Once I got up, there was Sky Lane, just like Bob said. She was telling everyone how to escape. "Alright, alongside you guys, the Venture Explorer is carrying rocket parts to Avant Gardens. We can use those to build an escape rocket. You'll need three parts- a nosecone, a cockpit, and an engine. Once you have your parts, go over to the blue circle on the floor and put this on..." She handed us each a strange -looking helmet. "Once you've finished your rocket, take it over to the escape chute and head to Avant Gardens. It's closer than Nimbus Station. Now, hurry!" We all lept down and gathered our parts. Then we headed over to the blue circle that Sky Lane had mentioned. I put on my helmet. Suddenly all the noises were drowned out by... Nothingness. Pure nothingness. I reached into my Backpack and pulled out my nosecone. It must have shrunk somehow going into my Backpack, but once I pulled it out it suddenly blew up, full - size. I'm not sure what startled me more, that I suddenly found myself holding a giant nosecone, or that it seemed completely weightless. I dropped it into its designated position, and it stood suspended in the air. Same deal with the cockpit and engine. Once I finished my rocket, I took my hat off. The noises suddenly flooded back in. I rushed my rocket over to the escape chute and deployed it. "Now," I said, "How do I turn this thing ooooOOONN!!!!!" Suddenly I was whisked away by my rocket. Apparently it didn't want to wait for me to to figure it out.
