I believe I am sure that this is the only story topic that reached 20 pages before the story even started! I have to say, however, that this was an easy/clever way to gain many readers before the story even starts (even if this was not your intention)! The title draws people to see what kind of story will this be. Then, they sign up because they have nothing to lose and because it seems a little interesting. Then, the prolonged wait until the story starts makes people think that the story will be worth the wait or at least that it must be worth it. Finally, when the story starts they read it because they are interested and because their heroes are involved. In that way you gain many readers before the story even starts!
