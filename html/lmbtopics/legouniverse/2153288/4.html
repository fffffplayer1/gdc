The Battle for Lego Universe

Chapter 13
Vector Longview woke with a start, hopping up and looking around. Then she remembered: The binoculars. She ran over to them and looked at Epsilons post. The Ranger wasn’t present… he was probably on another eradication mission. She must have dreamt… er… nightmare’d Epsilon being attacked by a bald Stromling. Now that she thought back on it, she laughed. “Boy, am I silly or what?”
She turned, noticing that no one was there to listen to here. “Yep, I’m silly.”

A while away, in the Sentinel Encampment, things were incredibly hectic. “I WANT A THOROUGH ANSWER TO WHY EPSILON IS GONE!” Beck Strongheart was waving his map of battle strategies around like a baby with a blanket. “HE SHOULD HAVE REPORTED TO ME!!!”
“Ummm… sir?” Theo Balfour stepped tentatively forward, addressing his leader.
“SALUTE!!!!”
The Sentinel Grunt hurriedly did so, and then proceeded with his report. “Rocket Jet 3 of Air Squad 17 has crashed into the remains of the Paradox Research Facility, doing maximum damage, smashing the pilot, and allowing the last Spiderling held in captivity to escape. Apparently, it hauled Prime Maelstrom Cube and Arachnid Researcher Wisp Lee into the Spider cave, unnoticed by us… this was recently found out because we found infected hand marks in the ground, and a trail through the Maelstrom goo.” The Grunt then looked down, avoiding Beck’s eyes.
“What, soldier?”
Theo lifted up Epsilons helmet. “I’m afraid Sir Starcracker has been-“
“SMASHED? EPSILON SMASHED!!! HOW CAN THIS BE!!!!!????”
“We… don’t know… “
Fitz Vanderbuild then piped up. “I could swear that I heard the roar of an Ape, along with gunfire and missiles!”
“You were asleep, on the ground with the Guard!” Beck snapped, and Fitz practically curled into a ball. I haven’t seen you this angry since that Stromling came into camp and started riding on the Dobermans…”
“AGH! Don’t remind me of that time!!! And anyway, of course I’m mad; we have practically been completely wreaked by whoever smashed dear Epsilon!”
Fitz managed a small salute before fainting, falling onto the grounded guard.
“Melodie, Kit! I want you two to keep an eye out in the Spider Cave!” Beck roared, and the Sentinel Woman nodded, running towards the entrance of the cavern immediately. Kit Wanderware, on the other hand, grumbled that there was “Too much noise”, but when the Sentinel General gave him “The Look”, the Venture League Scout hurried away, rolling his eyes under the windshield of his helmet.

Chapter 14
Bob looked up from the edge of the Spider Cave. He was leaning on the rundown rocket launch pad to Block Yard, yawning. Ahead, looming over the swirling of Maelstrom below was a tall building that rose high into the top of the cavern.
“Ha, I knew that would be that Spiders next base! Used to stay right here, now stays in a practically ancient Paradox Research Facility… in fact, it’s living in the FIRST ever one! Can’t believe these Nexus Force dummies!”
From the Facility, Bob heard a shriek too bloodcurdling for a normal Spiderling. “Well, here I come!” He said, flexing his plastic, clamp-like fingers jumping off the edge of the cave.

Melodie sat on a rock in the Spider Cave, bored. She kicked a little pebble down and sighed. Sentinels were warriors… not waiters! Below, a Mech spotted her and aimed its blaster… just before it fell to the ground, a dagger in its eye. Melodie hopped down to retrieve it, and she pulled her weapon out just fast enough to parry a blow from a Stromling. The creature swiped a few more times, slashing violently, but the Sentinel just kicked it into the wall, causing it to break into pieces.
“I can’t believe there hasn’t been any action since the eradication mission… I didn’t know the Maelstrom where such cowards!” Melodie muttered to herself, doing a back flip and landing on the rock that she had been sitting on a few hours earlier.
Someone poked her on the back, and she spun around, spinning her daggers wildly in her hands… just to hear a soft CLINK as the knives clanged against a spear.
“Why are you so paranoid?” Kit Wanderware sighed, sidestepping and making Melodie fall over onto her face with all her weight.
When she got back up and dusted herself off, she began her tantrum. “WHY CAN’T YOU LISTEN?! I TOLD YOU NEVER TO FREAK ME OUT, JUST LEAVE A LOUD SIGN THAT YOU ARE COMING!”
“That would be against my scout training, and you are making too much noise!”
But the Sentinel didn’t hear him… instead, she screamed.
“WHAT!?”
“I-I-I br-broke a nail!!!!!!!!!!!!”
“Oh shut it, you are made of plastic and you don’t have nails!”
“Okay then, I WOUNDED MY IMAGINATION!!!!”
“Oh boy…”
But suddenly, their argument was interrupted by a low rumbling coming from the depths of the cave, followed by the screeching of a hoard of Dark Spiderlings.
Kit readied his spear. “I believe we are gonna have some company.”
“Yep, I think that, for once, you are right…”
“Okay platypus, race you into the cave to get a head start!”
“OI! DON’T CALL ME PLATYPUS!!! IT’S ENOUGH THAT EPSILON CALLS ME THAT!”
But the headstrong Venture League scout was already charging into the mouth of the rocky, torch lit “hall”.

Chapter 15
Melodie didn’t even have time to move before the first wave of slimy arachnids crawled into view, screeching with their strange, eerie, and utterly bloodcurdling chatter.
“Sshhhrrrrrraaashhhhh! Shhiiiaaaathssss! Rrraaatthhhhhhhhh!!!”
One of the ugly, multi-eyed critters blasted an infected web at the Sentinel, but Melodie was fast, parrying the blow and rolling beneath the bug, stabbing it with both her daggers. The creature leaped upwards in pain, holding on to the rocky wall and began firing whirling orbs of energy. Another Dark Spiderling rushed towards her, slashing malevolently with some unnatural, sharp utensils that reverberated through the air with a skin crawling ZING. But Melodie sent the freaky ZING back at it, chucking her twin spinning daggers into the creature’s eyes. The corrupted arachnid’s legs wobbled under it as it fell to the ground, reducing to a pile of bricks.
Melodie rocketed for her weapons, but a well aimed shot from the Spiderling on the wall sent her crashing back, right into yet another of the gruesome beasts. The creature raised one of its shadowy legs and stepped on her. The Sentinel grabbed for her belly, losing her air and gasping as she reached for the nearby daggers. She finally grabbed it, weakly tossing it at the creature’s leg. The limb fell off, and the Dark Spiderling looked at what had happened quizzically, before it fell apart. Shocked, the firing Spiderling hopped down, spinning towards Melodie… just to have its bricks sprayed onto the rocks below. But three Spiderlings didn’t equal a hoard, and more were rushing in by the second. Soon there were almost twenty dozen “pairs” of eyes staring evilly at Melodie.
She backed away, wielding her now almost blunt daggers menacingly. The Spiderlings proceeded. And then she heard a scream… a scream that came from Kit’s mouth…
The Dark Spiderlings crawled onto the walls like a swarm of never-ending shadows that kept stepping over each other, creating a parting, a path that led to the hallway where the Venture Scout had boldly charged. And there, Kit Wanderware’s helmet rolled into view, without a body or a head to wear it.
Far away, deep in the cave, a monstrous screech like she had never heard before echoed through the cavern, and massive footsteps could be heard…

Chapter 16

Melodie stared as the helmet rolled to her feet, her jaw the shape of a porthole. How could this have happened? Why had Kit been such a fool? Why…? But her thoughts were interrupted by a stalking Dark Spiderling, followed by ten more… the Sentinel readied her twin daggers. But the crawling, four-legged arachnids were also interrupted by… a scream…
“MELODIE! OH MY GOSH! THIS IS CRAZY! WE GOTTA GET OUTTA HERE RIGHT NOOOOOOOOOOW!!!”
“Kit?” She whispered, shocked, as the helmet-less Venture scout came rocketing through the cave opening. “Wha?”
“THE SPIDER QUEEN IS RIGHT BEHIND ME! BUT SHE IS AN INVADER! NOT NORMAL, I REPEAT, NOT NORMAL!!!!!!!!!!”
And sure enough, a bright, reddish purple, over-sized Spiderling came crashing through the opening, screeching wildly, its six eyes glowing in burning, scarlet hatred. The creature roared, firing a wave of multiple, infected, homing fireballs at Kit and Melodie, and sending them spinning to the hard, rock ground.
Kit Wanderware was the first to hop up, waving his spear around frantically. “I sure do hate that Spider Queen… it almost smashed me before and now it’s come back… TWICE AS STRONG!”
Melodie, in the meanwhile, was shivering, pointing her daggers at the threat and softly, but incessantly whispering to herself. “This isn’t a Flying Stromling Pig, this isn’t a Flying Stromling Pig, this isn’t a Flying Stromling Pig…”
The Dark Spiderling made a circle around the two Nexus Force soldiers, blocking their only escape. They seemed to be chanting in their horrid voices.
“Sshhhrrrrrraaashhhhh! Shhiiiaaaathssss! Rrraaatthhhhhhhhh! Sshhhrrrrrraaashhhhh! Shhiiiaaaathssss! Rrraaatthhhhhhhhh!!!”
Kit stopped down to pick up his fallen helm, his long scar glinting in the purple tinted light. As he put it on, he murmured, “I thought Avant Gardens was meant for newbies… they should rename it Arduous Gardens…”
In a flash, he set himself on fire, the blazing, orange flames scorching the ground beneath him. “Let’s fight fire with fire, shall we?” He charged, oscillating his dagger-like spear so that it seemed nothing but a propeller, aiming for the smash.
Melodie soon gather her wits and rushed forwards, slashing at any Spiderling that dared get into Kit’s flaming path. The Spider Queen Invader fired another wave of fiery orbs, just to have them blocked as the Sentinel flew in front of scout, blocking them, and then rolling out of the Venture League’ers way. The arachnid quadruped pounced upwards, holding on to the ceiling, upside down, just to find Melodie climbing up rabidly with her glinting knives. It leaped back down, knocking half its children away, and sending Kit sprawling to the ground. But the Venture scout hopped back up, missing just one black glove, and took a massive leap, spinning around in a wild arc before landing on the Spider Queen’s back, piercing it with the spear. The monster wobbled under its weight, before Melodie hopped to the ground, smashing it.
The two warriors hopped to the ground, wiping off sweat and growling at the hundreds of Spiderlings. But the creatures just chattered, not worried at all.
“They must have a master much more powerful than an Elite version of the Spider Queen…” Kit bared his teeth at the thought.
And soon, his hypothesis would be confirmed… very soon…

Chapter 17

Two great warriors stood to face their new foe- a creature that would spark the beginning of the end of the Universe- their names were Kit Wanderware and Melodie Foxtrot. They didn’t know of the misfortunes following, the terrors that awaited… they couldn’t have dreamed of the war to come… all the smashing… But they couldn’t stop it, for no matter how horrid, history with barrel ahead like a wrecking ball, crushing everything in its never-ending path.
Kit spun his spear with a start as he heard footsteps. Someone… or something, with softly walking towards them. And what they saw next was a nightmare: Bob… they man who had been smashed in the capturing of the Venture Explorer, a man who’s last sight had been an overwhelming mass of swirling corruption… at least that was what Assembly Inventors had come up with while studying the infected starship. But, obviously, this was wrong.
“Ahh, my old pals! How do you do!?” Bob opened his hands cheerfully.
Melodie gasped, running for Bob, her arms also opened wide, until-
“BANG!”
Bob was sent spinning backwards, crashing into a wall with a sickening crack. Melodie leaped back in shock, stripping over her own feet and falling over to the ground. As she looked up, she saw Kit’s helmeted, scarred face with pursed lips. He was holding his spear in his right hand, and had a single, smoking Flaregun in the other.
“WHAT ARE YOU DOING!” The Sentinel hopped up frantically, waving her daggers around madly. But Kit just pointed at Bob with the gun.
The kind, plain Minifigure was now emitting a pungent purple aura, his blood red eyes glowing evilly.
“No… no way…” Melodie held tightly to her spot-less weapons, sweat dripping from her plastic, multicolored hair.
Bob laughed and immense, evil laugh and fired a gigantic purple fireball from his palm. The whirling, fiery object missed a quick Kit by millimeters, slamming into the rocky wall and smashing about two dozen spectating Spiderlings. The hole was quickly covered by twenty four more arachnids. Bob stopped laughing, knowing that he had met “a challenge.” He raised both his hand, pulsing Maelstrom energy crackling from each, and produced two infected whip-like things.
“CRACK!”
The malevolent weapons where swung in almost every direction, whipping at the valiant-but incredibly frightened-Minifigs. One slashed Kit in the back, sending him rolling to the rocky ground.
“NO!” Melodie rushed forward, stabbing Bob with both daggers. But he just smiled with an unseen mouth and released a blast of Maelstrom energy, sending her flying backwards. He then raised his hand.
“One bullet, one big mistake…” He was about to shoot the ammo that he had just absorbed from Kit’s deadly Flaregun shot. “Who will go first…?” He turned to Melodie.
But, all of a sudden, flames erupted from behind Bob, sending him rocketing upwards, his behind of fire. In his place was Kit, his gun and his spear held tightly in his clip-like hands. Sadly, in midair, Bob turned onto a whirling ball off shadows, and flew lightly back to the ground, at the mouth of the “hall.”
“Fine then… say hello, to the beginning of the end…” Bob slowly vanished, getting carried away by the stale breeze. At right behind where he used to be, at the mouth of the shadowy cavern, eight gigantic, monstrous, blood-red eyes stared into the two hero’s faces. An eerie, disembodied voice seemed to come from everywhere:
“Finally, I can show myself to the Nexus Force, finally, I can wreak havoc on the universe!” A strange machine that looked like a cement holder on a cement truck’s inside suddenly grew a violent purple, and a purple, ever morphing smoke came whispering through the cave, turning into faces, into hands. “Run, insolent Minifigures, or face the wrath of the Spider Boss!”
