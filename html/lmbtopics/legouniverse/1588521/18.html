The Journey Of Vanda Darkflame- Chapter 45
"Doc! Hael!" said Duke as him and Vanda shook their hands. The four faction leaders had not been united since the Maelstrom was pushed out of Nimbus Station. That was... at least 20 years ago, maybe? It had been so long. "Welcome aboard the Venture Smash!" said Hael. "After the Venture Koi was smashed and the Venture Explorer corrupted, we made this one." Vanda looked around. She was in what seemed to be a diner. Beyond that was a larger version of the Venture Explorer's bridge. "Sit down, we'll get you something to eat!" Doc said. Vanda gladly accepted, but she wasn't hungry or thirsty, due to the fact she secretly ate and drank. She felt horrible. All of her greatest friends had been corrupted. But, once again, she had been rescued by a ship. She looked out of the window. They were passing out of Avant Gardens. The infected Venture Explorer stood gleaming below them. She felt bad for poor Bob.

Doc returned a few minutes later, empty-handed. "I took our order for food", he said. "What are we eating?" asked Vanda curiously. "Hot dogs", said Doc. Vanda's stomach ached. Of course, he chose exactly what she had just eaten, but said, "Great!"

Soon enough, their waitor arrived with the food. He handed them their food. But, of course, after looking at Vanda and Duke, he double-took himself. "Hey, you two are faction leaders too! I heard about your journey. And, I was just wondering... mind if I TAG along?" Vanda couldn't help but agree. She nodded her head. "Thanks! My name is Indy Lord!" he said. Vanda had a small question for Doc. "Where are we going?" she asked. He replied, "Crux Prime."

The Journey Of Vanda Darkflame- Chapter 44

Sky was safe, but where was Wisp? She hoped that he was okay. Sky seemed to be reading her mind. "Wisp didn't make it, Vanda," she said. "I'm so sorry." Vanda did not answer. She felt so ashamed, but this time she knew that there was no way of saving him. That wasn't her only problem. Maelstrom electricity was zapping there way towards them. "We need to get out of here," she said. There was no way out. They were on the roof of a gift shop, surrounded by an entire army of Maelstrom. And from the looks of it, the Maelstrom had torn aparf the launch pads. Yeah, there was no way out. They would have to get infected by the Maelstrom sometime. They couldn't stay there forever. She heard her stomach ache. Wow, she hadn't eaten for a while. She took out her bag and took out a few hot dogs, along with a Paradox Power Potion. She secretly muched and drank her way through t as the others chatted. Suddenly, they felt a jolt. The gift shop wobbled for a second. Well, this was the end. Vanda hoped that something strange would happen, and something strange happened indeed. A massive ship was flying above them. The Venture Explorer, possibly? No, much bigger. And immediately a rope ladder dropped down for them to climb. "Let's go! Its our only chance!" shouted Vanda. She began to climb, and Duke followed. Sky was about to get on as a Spiderling jumped up and infected her. She fell over, but came up in the form of the Stromling. Tank, worried by the Maelstrom, pushed them both off. He was about to come on the ladder when Vanda saw a terrible problem. The ladder had begun to rise above the ground! Eventually, it got so high that Tank could not even jump close to it! Duke shouted, "Tank! No!" But it was too late. They were pulled into the ship where they greeted a luxurious room, along with Doc Overbuild and Hael Storm.
