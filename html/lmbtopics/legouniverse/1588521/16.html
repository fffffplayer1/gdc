IThe Journey Of Vanda Darkflame- Chapter 40
The scarlet in Tank's eyes dimmed back to their normal black, and the purple glow on his skin faded to yellow again. Vanda and Duke gave a sigh of relief. They didn't have any time to rejoice, though. The Maelstrom cloud was getting larger. This was extremely like the Gnarled Forest and Forbidden Valley. The Maelstrom was plotting something... but what? Duke suddenly grabbed her arm and pulled her to the Sentinel Encampment. A Maelstrom lightning bolt hit the ground where she was previously standing. "Thanks", she said. He nodded. Tank seemed to be acting normal again. That was very good. Vanda hoped that they would be safe here. A brown-haired knight walked up to them. "Beck!" exclaimed Duke. "Its been so long!" "All the better", he replied. "Now who is this?" He pointed at Vanda. "Oh, this is Vanda Darkflame", said Duke. Beck's yes widened, and he nearly toppled over. "THE Vanda Darkflame?" he asked. "The leader of the Paradox, famous ninja, and former assisstent of... Baron Typhonus. Your master is now leading the Maelstrom army!" Vanda nodded. She looked at the cave that led to the peaceful monument of Bob. Suddenly, Sensei Wu rushed out of the rocks. A loud noise seemed to echo far behind him. "They're coming!" he shouted, and he was struck by a Maelstrom lightning bolt coming from the cave. A glowing purple form of himself appeared instantly. Sensei Wu had been infected by the Maelstrom. Everyone began to scream at the sight of the Maelstrom warrior. Suddenly, the noise in the cave grew louder. Out came thousands of Stromlings. The knights drew their weapons, but they were too late. Stromlings touched almost all the knights, who rose into more Stromlings. Soon enough, the only un-infected people were Vanda, Duke, Tank, some Sentinel girl named Maddie, and Beck. They drew their weapons just in time.

The Journey Of Vanda Darkflame- Chapter 41
The Stromlings closed in. Thousands of more pounded out of the cave, cornering the small group. Maddie was the first to fall. Her glowing blue daggers were flung out of her hands. She was immediately sucked into the everlasting crowd of Stromlings. Vanda heard a muffled scream as the girl was infected by the Maelstrom. "Up here!" shouted Duke. He was on top of a pine tree. Without hesitation, the others followed. Beck was the last to climb up. He began to make his way to them, but he broke one of its branches and tumbled to the ground. The swarm of Stromlings gladly infected him. "NO!" Duke shouted, but he was too late. "We need to get out of here", said Vanda. But, wait, where was Wisp and Sky? She hoped to the Nexus that they were safe.

There seemed to be no way out of the mob. Dark Spiderlings and Stromling Mechs filled the encampment. The sky was full of Maelstrom clouds, and the glowing face of the Maelstrom was upon them. The dense purple mist seemed to be among them, as well. Vanda suddenly had an idea. She looked at the wall with the bus on it. "Guys, follow me!" she said. She flipped onto the wall and onto the bus. The others followed, but with fear. The Stromlings growled as they saw their enemies escaping. On the other side of the wall was a rocky ledge. "Climb!" she shouted, and they did. They began to make their way to the tunnel. Rocks crumbled down, but that didn't stop the fearless minifigures. They climbed their way to the wall of the rocky tunnel.
