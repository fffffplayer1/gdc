The Journey Of Vanda Darkflame- Chapter 19
Vanda had no weapons, the Baron and his warriors were coming for her. She got back up, and ran to the left where the bright golden light was. She was getting awfully close to it, and couldn't stop herself. A collision made her vision fuzzy, and everything went black.

Vanda awoke to a sensation of drinking hot water... really hot water! She opened her eyes. That wasn't water... that was lava. Vanda spit it out, and her mouth needed something cold. She screamed for water as her tongue went numb, and there was another black out.

She awoke in an open area. She sat up and opened her eyes. She was in some sort of dojo. A old man was sitting next to her in a chair, drinking tea. "Hello", he said without looking at her. "I am Sensei Wu. You, my friend, are Vanda Darkflame, leader of the Paradox faction and skilled ninja." Vanda was speechless. Sensei Wu, if that was his name, was wearing what looked like a large and long cone on his head. He had a long white beard, and he wore white robes. Where was Wisp? Where was she? Vanda looked around. The dojo-like area was a tower on top of a large bamboo building. Vanda knew this man was crazy. She jumped off the tower instantly, and Sensei Wu rushed after her. She realized that a path of floating stones led to the building. She made it out of the place's gates just as they shut. The land where the dojo was surprisingly was sunny. Vanda ran into the dark area, and found that it was the mountain where she had fallen off. And Wisp had been captured by the Baron, and was tied up.
Chapter 20 coming soon!

The Journey Of Vanda Darkflame- Chapter 7
Upon leaving the Nexus Hospital, Vanda encountered the same crowd of minifigures. Being the quiet and mysterious person she was, she pushed right through the crowd again. Instead of going to the Forbidden Valley's launch pad, she strode toward the Gnarled Forest's. She took out the rocket she had always known, climbed into it, and was launched in the direction of the Gnarled Forest. Vanda did not realize that she just started a journey that she would never forget.

Vanda had only been to the Gnarled Forest once before. If she had remembered what it was like, she would have not been surprised to see a dense rain forest. How would she find Wisp here? Her rocket landed on the metal deck, and she walked down the ramp to the moist ground. A minifgure greeted her politely, but Vanda ignored him. She pushed on through the trail.

The trail ended in a rock crevice. Vanda stepped through, only to find herself standing on the ledge of a rocky cliff. Vanda looked up. An enormous yet so beautiful scenery stod before her. Darkflame had to treasure this moment. Several rope bridges with minifigures climbing across them swung from side to side of the cliff. At the furthermost point, several waterfalls came together to form one gigantic one. Just as she came back to herself, a Maelstrom gorilla came from behind her and shoved her off the cliff.
