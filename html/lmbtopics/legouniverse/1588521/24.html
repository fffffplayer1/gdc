The Journey Of Vanda Darkflame- Prologue
Vanda flipped from rock to rock, searching the Forbidden Vally. The Maelstrom had recently penetrated its entire forces, so everyone needed to be cautious. She shifted past a Dark Ronan silently, and then with one quick slice of her ninja blade, the Maelstrom warrior fell into a heap of dust. She glanced at the Maelstrom Dragon cave. The dragon kept inside of it roared. It was lucky that the Maelstrom had not freed that yet. Suddenly darkness filled around Darkflame. She looked into the sky, and then she saw it. The purple face in the sky, representing the center of the Maelstrom was glancing down at her. Suddenly there was another roar. "No", said Vanda. The dragon was enormous, and the Maelstrom had freed it from its cave. Heroes ran out the cave, screaming. Several Dark Ronans surrounded her, and even worse: the dragon was looking in her direction. Vanda Darkflame, leader of the Paradox was surrounded by the Maelstrom. She only had one sword to defend herself, which was not enough for defeating all these Maelstrom warriors.

The Journey Of Vanda Darkflame- Chapter 50
"We should get some sleep before attempting the challenge", notified Duke, "because it would be much harder to do it at this rate. We haven't slept well for a long time." Vanda couldn't disagree. Fighting Butterscorch seemed almost impossible, as the center of the Maelstrom greatened her strength. Vanda laid down on a rock and shut her purple eyes.

That night she had the oddest dream. It seemed as if she woke up from her exact sleeping position. She did, even though she still laid down on the ground. She looked at her hands. They were glowing transparent blue. She was herself in a Mythran form. Suddenly, a different Mythran appeared in front of her. It had a smiling face as it waved at her. It was Bob. "Hello, my little Vanda!" he said, still smiling. "Bob!" she shouted. "What happened? Are you okay?" At the hearing of the two questions and said, "Alas, no. After being dragged from the Venture Explorer, the Spiderling that I was cast upon infected me. But I come to give you a message, brave Darkflame." He pulled a scroll out of thin air. He began to read, "The Mythran Council gives Vanda Darkflame an urgent message: We warn Vanda Darkflame and Duke Exeter not to proceed the mission of smashing the dragon. If they do, one of them will be changed forever!" He let go of the scroll, and it dissolved. "Vanda", he said, "Please do not listen to the Mythran Council. They do not know your doing this for the fate of the universe. And I must tell you one more-" His form began to dissolve slowly. "You can meet me in Caldera Mar in my new form. But be warned! We will be on different sides of the battle this time...." He dissolved, and Vanda woke with a start.
