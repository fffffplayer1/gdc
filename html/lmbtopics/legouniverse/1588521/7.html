The Journey Of Vanda Darkfoame- Chapter 23
What had she just seen? It seemed like ages ago did she see her memories. She knew, but she couldn't think of it. These people were right, she had lost her memory somehow. And she needed to get it back. But no matter how hard she tried, or how hard she thought, the memories would not come to her. She was named... was it Vacuum.... Vacuum CardGame? No, what was it? Ugh, she was getting sofrustrated with herself. She began to pace as she thought, the three people glaring at her. She didn't realize how far she was pacing, though. She walked up and down the hills, the three people still following her. Suddenly, a chunk of rock broke on the ground, and Vanda fell right through it. She screamed as the three people leaped in to help.

Vanda awoke to darkness. What happened? It was all a dream... but she couldn't wake up from it. Now she was awake. The dream involved Wisp, Exeter, and Sky. She pushed that thought aside and moved her foot around. It hit something solid. "Ow!" gasped a voice, which was all too familliar. "Sky, is that you?" Vanda asked into the darkness, which replied, "Yeah I'm here Vanda. So are Exeter and Wisp. Wait, VANDA! You remember!" Vanda got up, and looked for a spot of light. She opened a rock, which revealed the air above. Light shined in, and she could clearly see her three dear friends: Duke Exeter, Sky Lane, and Wisp Lee. They all seemed surprised that Vanda was oky. They retold the story of Vanda's memory loss, but Vanda quieted them when she heard a thud. A rock had just fallen. Suddenly, a massive Maelstrom dragon, bigger than any one in the Forbidden Valley flew out.

The Journey Of Vanda Darkflame- Chapter 24
The dragon whipped its tail furiously, and shot fire out of its mouth severely. Instantly, the cave had been set ablaze, and the four minifigures took no time to try to escape. But the dragon was not only powerful, but also intelligent. It thrashed one of its claws into the exit. The light should have gone, but the fire was dense enough to see. Suddenly, the light went to its maximum level, due to the same spinning light Vanda had seen before she lost her memory. Out of it came Sensei Wu. He did not attack the dragon, but re-opened the exit. Sky, Vanda, Duke Exeter, and Wisp climbed out in just enough time before the dragon zoomed out. She was so graceful yet dangerous. She flew away and landed far off, but on the same planet as they were. Sensei Wu used his golden transportation and went in the direction of his dojo.

Upon walking back to the Sentinel Camp, Wisp asked, "What should we name it?" Everyone seemed confused except Wisp and Vanda, so he continued, "You guys. The Paradox are the faction of researching Maelstrom warriors. If we find a creature truly remarkable, then we give it a name. You'll have to admit that dragon was not only abnormally large and destructive, but also beautiful." Vanda nodded her head in agreement, and then she said, "I agree. I'm the leader of the Paradox, and I decide that we name it Butterscorch. It scorched that cave, and Butterscotch is a name for a pet. Combine them, and you have Butterscorch." Duke Exeter was on the verge of laughing, and Sky had let out a small giggle.

The Joueney Of Vanda Darkflame- Chapter 25
"Its not funny!" Vanda shouted. She ran away from them. Tears streamed down her cheeks. The three others tried catching up with her, but she was too quick. Once in the Sentinel Camp, she pulled out Wisp's rocket and placed it on the launch pad. The trio was walking into the camp. Vanda smacked the launch button, but the launch pad malfunctioned on her. It started smoking and sparking, and then five seconds later, it blew up. The others were slammed out of the camp, and Vanda spiraled into the heart of the Maelstrom uncontrollably. "Help!" she called down to the planet, but she seemed out of sound range. She was heading right into the center of the Maelstrom. Purple mist surrounded her, this time so dense that she could not see five feet ahead of her. She waited for whatever signal nature would give her. Suddenly there was a scraping metal noise, and Vanda set foot on land. She could not control her actions. The density of the Maelstrom presence had made her one of its own. Vanda tried to fight it, but she couldn't. So this was her fate. She walked across the floating purple rocks to an open area. In the middle of it stood a throne, and on it sat the Darkitect himself, Baron Typhonus. Suddenly, Vanda's eyesight went red, she felt a chilly feeling upon her, and she was pushed out of her own thoughts.

The Journey Of Vanda Darkflame- Chapter 26
Vanda could not move. She was a small thought in the darkest corner of her thoughts. All she could do was see and hear. Baron Typhonus said to her, "Well, well. If it isn't our little guest, Miss Darkflame. You were a fool to come here, weren't you?" A voice that was clearly not the true Vanda's said, "I can serve you well, master." He scratched his head, and then replied, "I will put you to the test. Eliminate Wisp Lee, and I will let you serve me." The Maelstrom-Vanda nodded, and she walked away silently.

The search partie's rocket had just arrived, and Vanda had witnessed it. Out came Wisp, Sky, and Exeter, all wearing protective suits to not be infected by the Maelstrom. Wisp was observing the place. He said, "Look at it. This is pure Maelstrom. No one could survive here without these suits on. I wonder how Vanda... hey, look, she's over there!" He pointed to her. She could not do this. Wisp was her friend, not her enemy. The trio was already looking suspicious of Vanda, and Exeter said, "Vanda, you don't look so good. Are you okay?" Maelstrom-Vanda replied, "It was foolish of you to all come here. I must destroy Wisp Lee for my master. If not, he will destroy ME!" "Vanda, this is not you!" exclaimed Sky. "Snap out of it, Vanda! Typhonus did something to you here. The presence of the Maelstrom, its too much for you to handle. We won't let him destroy you, just don't fight..." It was too late. Maelstrom-Vanda took out her steel katana, rubbed it with her two hands, and pointed it at Wisp.

The Journey Of Vanda Darkflame- Chapter 27
Sky and Exeter were ready to fight. Sky took out her two flareguns, and Exeter drew his glowing sword. Sky had set the flareguns to stun. Maelstrom-Vanda accepted the challenge. She aimed for Exeter first. She jumped into the air and sliced down at him, but he kicked her backwards. She got up and turned for Sky. With one slash of the blade, Maelstrom-Vanda knocked the flareguns out of her hands. The normal Vanda needed to stop this, but she didn't know how. As she was about to chop Sky, Exeter jumped in and deflected her blade. But he landed on the ground. Maelstrom-Vanda seized the oppurtunity to get at Wisp. He backed away to the edge of the floating rock they were on. Far below stood Crux Prime. Maelstrom-Vanda looked down at it. Wisp knew what to do, apparently. He tripped Vanda and caught her katana. However, he accidently tripped her too hard. She tumbled down to the planet below. The regular Vanda heard Wisp cry, "No!" She hit the ground with a thud.

Maelstrom-Vanda woke up in the same place where she had fallen. Luckily, she had not been smashed, but she was still Maelstrom-infected. She got up, and realized that Wisp Lee still had her katana. In the distance, she could make out the Sentinel Camp. The search team's ship had just landed. That was where Wisp was. With no effort, Vanda charged toward the camp at full speed, knocking out minifigures along the way.

The Journey Of Vanda Darkflame- Chapter 28
Maelstrom-Vanda reached the Sentinel Camp. Screams erupted from minifigures' mouth at the sight of the Maelstrom warrior. She looked around, and spotted Wisp. She ran to get a good hit at him, but he had a weapon of his own. He pulled out the same needle he had to make Vanda fall asleep, but this time it had a shining blue substance... pure imagination that had been retrieved from the Nexus Of Imagination. He jabbed it into her arm, Vanda felt the same chilly feeling she had when the Maelstrom had overwhelmed her, her vision blurred again, and Maelstrom-Vanda was ejected out of her. Vanda felt weakened, but Wisp supported her. She felt almost as if she was still infected by the Maelstrom. But it was all over.

The Venture Explorer had been revived recently, and it was ready for takeoff. Two brave minifigures had found Bob and the other Escapees who had stayed in a camp in their emergency rockets. Crux Prime was getting ready to close, for it was right in the Darkitect's territory, which was too powerful for the Maelstrom. The team had decided to let Sensei Wu come on the journey back to Nimbus Station, for it had been many years since he had, and because there were too many Maelstrom for him to handle. Vanda and her friends had finished packing up the Sentinel Camp and were putting it into the Venture Explorer's storage compartment. Everyone boarded the massive ship.
