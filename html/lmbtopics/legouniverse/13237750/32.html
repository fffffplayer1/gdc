Bochondrius is not having a good day. There are plants... EVERYWHERE! So much so that a giant plant is now chasing him, destroying EVERYTHING in its path. Which only strengthens the bo-Toa's fear of flora.

 

"AAAAAAAH!!!! THEY'RE OUT TO GET ME!!!!! HAAAAALP!!!!!"

 

Suddenly, and without warning, the plant shifts shape and becomes an adorable little kitten, purring at Bochondrius before falling apart in a mess of roots and leaves.

 

"Wha- what is this foul chloromancy!"

 

Suddenly, the kanohi botridax was in Bochondrius' face. It was the mask of making plants talk.

 

"Ohaider! Heehee HA! Having plant problems today I see?"

 

Bochondrius involuntary winced at every erratic move the Toa before him was making. He of course had to ask, "Who- who are you?!"

 

The reply was just shy of overlapping with the question, "Borachion! B-O-R-ACK!-I-ON! Toa of plantlife and professional Makuta Huntard, at yer service or lack of thereof... what's with the felnas weirdo." His tone suddenly became much more deadly as some vines began growing near Bochondrius, "CAAAAHOOOOOOOOTZ?????!!!!!!!"

 

Murky was just on the razor's edge of losing balance and falling into Borachion's vines. And he couldn't tell which would be worse: Being cut apart with those dangerous looking warglaives, or being touched by plants. He couldn't take it anymore and just yelled out, "That's it! I OFFICIALLLY HATE Karzahni! YOU KNEW THIS SICKO WOULD BE AFTER ME! YOU KNEW IT!"

 

Vines eerily wrapped and coiled around Borachion and he slowly went up and over Bochondrius' head as he said, "Ah? Blaming this on Karzahni then? HA! How cute. No Toa would wear such a mask, you MUST be a Makuta! You know what I do with those, hmm?"

 

Murky had now fallen over and Borachion's vines had him in their slimey grasp. Murky started screaming, which didn't really help his case. "Oh what a baby! I haven't even found the match I'm going to use once I get past that armor of yours!" The screaming of course, continued.

"No no no, that was clearly you."

"Well it clearly wasn't me, and monsters like that don't just appear out of nowhere!"

 

"They don't?"

 

"Of course not! Why, it takes a Toa of plantlife far more talented than I in order to- oh..."

"No Makuta could of created such a monstrosity. It must have been you."

 

"But- I detest plants!"

 

"I know you do. But you better change that before you strangle yourself to oblivion. Plants aren't a game mister! If you don't learn to control your powers as a bo-Toa, you won't survive 10 seconds on your own in a place like this."
