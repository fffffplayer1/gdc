2 weeks ago

 

Leres waits.

Procella gestures for him to do something.

Leres looks really confused.

She frantically begins gesturing, trying to tell him to talk to Atem.

Leres nods.

 

"Uhh... So... Atem. Even if it was a good idea to start another war, you'd run afoul of the Mangai."

"Who are these Mangai you speak of, Ambassador of the Seas?" Is the sharp response.

 

Procella timidly swirls a snake of water around Atem, before it enters him, to try to calm him.

"An absurdly huge Toa team. Each of its members would have little trouble leveling a city on their own."

Procella looks at him with pity. "Leres, I alone, despite being the weakest Elemental Lord," Protests from Atem. Procella ignores. "I alone could level Metru Nui. I am the first Toa of Ice, the first Toa of Water. Atem was the first Air and Lightning Toa. Zemlya was the first of Plantlife, of Stone and of Earth. Even Ackarne was the first of Plasma and Fire. We have allies in Atero Nui, servants of our armies."

 

"Procella, you shouldn't so easily share our secre-" Atem starts.

 

"I trust him, Atem. He is my own ambassador, my representative." Procella finishes, cutting him off. "Leres. Go to Atero Nui, and bring back Toa of Air, Lightning, Water and Ice. Show them this."

 

She shows two symbols, one of them is that of the ocean with fractals of ice glittering around it. The other is a Tornado with a stylized lightning bolt in front of it. "They will recognize it. They will come."

We have been betrayed. Our trust, misguided. Our faith, put wrongly. Have they no shame? But we will live on, my brothers. We will strive forward, we will endure. We will ascend. Free of limitations, free of fear.
