"Cerberus Xenos."
The words echoed in Duke Exeter's mind. The sentence formed.
"Last contact from Cerberus Xenos."
The search parties for Extreme had been last contacted on Cerberus Xenos. Communication between Thenso, Kangroo and Yonder had been lost just after they'd entered it's atmosphere. Vanda and Meyer had gone after them, also losing comunication.
Duke stood, thinking, for what seemed like hours to him. It had only been a few moments.
"Sir," the Sentinel officer prompted, breaking into his thoughts. "What should we do?"
Honestly, Exeter wasn't sure. For the first time, he wished he wasn't in command, didn't have to make all the decisions, didn't hold the lives of minifigures in his hands. But he did. He made up his mind.
He looked up. "Prepare Bravo. We're going in."
The officer nodded wordlessly, hinting no emotion, and hurried off to carry out his orders.
Duke Exeter's brain buzzed. The Sentinel command center was full of Sentinel officers, walking around, contacting troops on distant worlds, sending reinforcements, preparing attacks...
Normally, Duke would marvel at the organization and swift actions of his soldiers. But now, it only gave him a headache.
He had a feeling today would be a devasting day. Either for the Maelstrom, or the Nexus Force -- or both.

Extreme coughed and limped towards the nearest cover, a rock. His leg was injured and his Wormholer had been badly dented and the firing curcuits destroyed. He sat down behind the rock.
Just in time.
A lightning bolt crashed down a few meters away, followed immediately by several clashes of thunder. Rain poured down even more heavily, of course with more lightning.
Where is this place? Extreme thought, looking around. Even he couldn't be unlucky enough to end up here. It was a barren planet, for an obvious reason: there was an endless storm there, with lightning striking the same place all the time, deafening thunder rumbling, and rain bucketing down endlessly.
It was Cerberus Xenos.
***
As soon as Vanda, Meyer and Thenso arrived back at Nexus Tower, they organized a search for Extreme. Duke Exeter gave them two Talon Dropships, and Kangroo and Yonder volunteered to come along.
They created a plan: Vanda and Meyer would go in one Talon, and Thenso, Kangroo and Yonder would go in another. They would split up and search a planet each, and meet up every week and report their findings. If one found Extreme, they would immediately contact the other group with a long-range transmitter and set a nearby rendezvous point.

Thenso and Meyer gasped in astonishment as the saw him stagger into the cavern. A tattered gi covered him, and his face was covered in grime and dust. A dented katana was sheathed at his belt.
"F-Forgive me," Yonder said weakly, collapsing to the ground. "I couldn't control it... it beckoned me, it's call... irresistable..."

Extreme could barely contain his urge to jump out of his hiding place. Everyone was here! Almost - where was Kangroo?
But he couldn't stop himself. Without meaning to, he leapt from the shadows, pouncing towards the group of minifigures at the entrance of the cavern.
They gaped as they saw him charging toward them, then smiled and began talking happily.
"EXTREME!" they yelled. "Where have you been?!?!"

‎01-26-2012 12:44 AM

These characters have gone through alot together - from saving Nexus Tower, being flung to the edge of the Universe, nearly smashed, fighting through massive armies, and much more. I've decided to make a revision/character description of each of them.

Book 1 - Paradox (Saving Nexus Tower)
Vanda Darkflame volunteered to venture into the deepest Maelstrom territory on Crux Prime with Sutoro Hatto and Meyer Muckrake. They flew there in a Talon and fought Dragon Invaders before running into a Space Marauder named Extreme. They smashed through the ranks of a huge army before Sutoro fell prey to a deadly Maelstrom infection. He was blasted aside by Thenso, a Sorcerer. They cured Sutoro, but he was unable to recover quickly and they left him in order to go on and try and find help for him.
Instead, they found a Nexus Force outpost and were urgently teleported to Nexus Tower, despite arguing that they should go back for Sutoro. They arrived at the tower and found everything backwards - Nexus Naomi was a solid minifigure, the holograms solid, the speed pads slowing you down. They saved Bullet Mullet from Maelstrom "real-ograms" and went into the training area to find Achilles Plutarch fighting off hordes of Maelstrom. They managed to destroy the spawner and then went on to find Hael Storm and his Venture League allies infected.
They fought him, and during the battle Trek Furino blasted Vanda Darkflame in the back. They managed to inject most of the Venture League pirates with Imagination, disinfecting them.
Then they found Rutger Hemoglobin fighting off Maelstrom in the Paradox vendor area. They helped him and Mr. Ree came out of one of the teleporters. He told them of undescribable things in the main room and led them in. There, they fought the monsters and, as Vanda Darkflame was injured and her Imagination spark nearly destroyed by a Maelstrom soulsapper, Meyer managed to revive her with the remains of her spark. They then destroyed the Paradox eye in the centre of the room, therefore stopping the flood of monsters into the room.
They exited the area and went into the Imagination Nexus Room, where they witnessed the Nexus turn into Baron Typhonus himself, who then summoned the Spider Boss. Hundreds of minifigures flooded the room, fighting the Spider, then defeated it. Typhonus, his plot foiled, withdrew himself from the Nexus and back into the Maelstrom Core. Dr Overbuild then located a Maelstrom-Infected Brick that had been planted in the Nexus and removed it, returning the Tower back to normal.

Profiles

Vanda Darkflame - Leader of Paradox
A mysterious warrior, Vanda Darkflame leads the Paradox faction. She is thought to be a ninja princess, but no one has any proof. She wields twin Maelstrom katanas and normally stands in the Paradox area in Nexus Tower. She is faithful to her friends and will fight for whatever she believes in, whether others approve or not.

Meyer Muckrake - Paradox Guard
Meyer is a guard in Nexus Tower who volunteered to go with Vanda on her mission on Crux Prime. Meyer generally uses his staff as a weapon, but also has a handgun by his side. What he looks like is as much a mystery as how he manages to stand in one spot for so long - he is concealed in battle armor that only shows his eyes.

Sutoro Hatto - Paradox Gear Supplier
Sutoro volunteered, along with Meyer, to go with Vanda. He is a vendor in Nexus Tower, selling gear to Paradox members. He enjoys seeing minifugres running off to test out their new gear. He is a Shinobi and his skin is white. He claims it is because of the low light he gets in the room, but no one really believes him.

Extreme - Space Marauder
Extreme is a battle-hardened Space Marauder. He enjoys logn afternoons of tearing down line after line of Stromlings with his beloved Wormholer. He found Vanda, Meyer and Sutoro on Crux Prime after being stranded there for several days, alone.

Thenso - Sorcerer
Thenso is often thought of as an advisor, but in reality, he can hold his own just fine in a battle. He does, in fact, know quite a bit and can outwit almost anyone in an argument. He often calms down Meyer when he gets impatient or angry - which happens quite a bit, but seemingly only when it's about Vanda Darkflame...?

Background characters

Nikolai Gammapulse: Nikolai was at the Crux Prime outpost and teleported the main characters to the Tower despite their arguments about leaving Sutoro behind.
Duke Exeter: Exeter gave Vanda the task of going to Crux Prime. He helped in the fight against the Spider Boss.
Trek Furino: Trek shot Vanda in the back while he was infected. Uninfected, he begs forgiveness from Vanda, who gladly forgives him.
Baron Typhonus: Typhonus noticed the small energy from a Maelstrom-infected Brick in Nexus Tower expanded it's power, corrupting the entire Tower. He morphed the Nexus into himself, and sent the Spider Boss to fight the minifigures. After its defeat, he escaped.
