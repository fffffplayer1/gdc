How did you even find a love spirit?"

Kolokvar holds up his hands in protest.

 

"All very good questions, my good fellow, but ones for another day. These are the shattered personas of the makuta in that casket. I will take the casket to my chamber. As for the 'Crysonas' , try not to antagonize them, or disturb them, and let me know if they antagonize or disturb you. This whole situation is less than desirable, but there's no way around it."

"So you want to try to re-merge these - these 'Crysonas' - into your ally?"  Teron shakes his head.  "Not my area of expertise.  I'm sure that you know what you're doing, though, so I'll just stay out of your way and organise the army."  With that, he turns and departs.

Umbrax vanishes into his shadow form, dispelling the illusions he had set up and following Teron undetectably, as only a shadow flitting across the ground....

Umbrax suddenly becomes immobile, as a stasis field is thrown up around him.

 

"I thought I felt something following us." 

Unfortunately, it is nigh-impossible to freeze a two-dimensional object, and Umbrax has flitted out and is standing on a rooftop, out of shadow form, in the blink of an eye.

"Catch me if you can!", he shouts, diving behind the building, Mahiki shapeshift already active.

Sadness shoots out whips of Water, dashing towards Umbrax.

 

Love turns, and slams her fists down on the ground, spikes of Earth shooting up, before flying at Umbrax. The other Crysona's dash, their elements doing something.

All they see are the normal citizens going about their business.
