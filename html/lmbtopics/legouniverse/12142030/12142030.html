I think that it was high time that the village of the Earth and Jungle Tribes on Bota Magna - which I have named Foros - was created!  Nobody else seems to have claimed this, so I think that I will.

 

Quick bits of information: the Earth Tribe's main profession is farming, and most Earth Agori here will be dedicated to that purpose.  The village's numbers will be great, since it hasn't had any major threats to contain its population (unlike those of the dangerous world of Bara Magna); the Agori don't tend to interfere with the biomechanical dinosaurs that are native to the planet.  Interestingly, however, the Glatorian of Foros consider bringing down a dinosaur to be a great feat of courage and fighting skill, and they tend to measure their fighting ability based on the number and strength of the beasts that they have brought down; the Agori don't mind this, because it clears space for them to safely gather resources and use the land, and because they can reuse some of the parts of the dinosaurs' armour.  They have about the same technological level as Bara Magnans.  Due to the fact that they have lived in isolation for so long, they have no need for walls or other defences, though Glatorian still train as warriors in order to battle the dinosaurs.

 

I claim the right, as topic creator, to lead Foros.  I will make my profile on the 'Profiles!' topic soon!

A_RNN wrote:
OOC: Logically, Bota Magnan tribes would have technological levels similar to those of Le-Koro.

OOC: Why?

Whereas Bara Magna was a very harsh environment, both for machines and people, Bota Magna is not, being similar to Le-koro's environment. Bara Magnans had little reason to assemble machines out of natural things, both because few things were suited for it, and because it would break down quickly. In Bota Magna's case, however, vines, wood, and etc provide ample materials for simple machines, and the jungle is much less harsh for vine/wood contraptions that the desert, what with its scorching heat and sand, would be.
