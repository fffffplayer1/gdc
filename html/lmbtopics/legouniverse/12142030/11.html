"See what you can do. I'll help you in 3 hours."

Teron looks at Karzov, sighing.  "Well, I suppose that we'd better go to speak to our people first.  Given the battle, I doubt that the two groups will be getting on well.  We don't want any riots."

"Of course, says the Vorox."

 

as the two walk, Karzov asks, "What do you think of him?"

"I can't say that much - I only just met Kolokvar, and anything else that I know comes from the Guard.  This whole situation is probably fairly odd for both of -"

 

Just then, Bota Magna's orbit causes Bara Magna to come into view.  Huge streams of red light appear to be emanating from two vaguely humanoid figures, and explosions of titanic force rock the world.  Teron stands there trying to make sense of it.  "I don't suppose you're familiar with any of that, are you, Karzov?  Or am I losing my senses?" he asks, casually.

Karzov looks on shocked

 

"Not in the slightest. It's as if the Great beings have returned!"

"I think that uniting our people may have to wait," says Teron.  "This is probably the sort of thing that Kolokvar would want us to tell him about."  Teron begins running back to the Makuta.

The Pair finds the Makuta staring in the direction of bara Magna as well. He holds up his hand.

 

"You don't need to say it--universes are clashing!"
