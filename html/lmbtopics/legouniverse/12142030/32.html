Boomer2k wrote:

OOC: Okay I think I get most of it. So what now?

OOC: Well, you have some options.

 

Presumably you want to make a character of some sort.  There are, as you've seen, three tribes here: the Jungle Tribe, the Earth Tribe and the Sand Tribe.  Within those, there are two options for each: Agori (or Zesk) and Glatorian (or Vorox).  If you roleplay as the latter, your character will have power over their Element - if you want to have your character be able to create earthquakes, raise sandstorms or grow giant, carnivorous rooted minions, that's your choice.  However, playing as a character without special abilities can be rewarding as well - I'm doing exactly that in the newer BIONICLE Roleplay with the villager Dekos, who's currently being instrumental in the increasing influence of the Stone Tribe by building an army.

 

Next, village structure.  Currently, power in Foros is held like this: Kolokvar is the ultimate leader but he delegates most of his tasks to Teron and Karzov.  Karzov is currently in a dictatorial role over the Vorox and Zesk, while Teron's power is limited by the Hall Council, a group of Earth and Jungle Agori elders which can veto his decisions - I would imagine that roleplaying as one of them would be enjoyable.  Alternatively, any member of Foros can join up as a member of the army - Zesk serve as front-line infantry (think of a Zerg Rush) (protected by the new Zesko Suits, of course), while Landcrusher tanks are each piloted by an Earth Glatorian and a Jungle or Earth Agori, Treeclimber mechs have Jungle Glatorian to control them and Sandstorm fighter aircraft are flown by Vorox.
