Chapter 1: Goodbye, Destiny.

 

Darkeye and Evamio rushed out of the inn, a hooded figure with a schythe sppeared out of nowhere. The figure suddenly turned the schythe into a green orb and but it away. 

 

"Who are you?" Evamio asked.

 

The figure un-hooded himself. All they saw was a green wisp.

 

"Say goodbye to Destiny, and yourselves." The wisp said.

 

The wisp turned into a minifigure and blew Darkeye and Evamio up with green magic so quick that they couldn't identify him.

 

They appeared in Nexus Tower, but it was different, not blue, not purple, but green.

 

"What did that stupid wisp do?!" Darkeye yelled.

 

"He blew us up, you have eyes?" Evamio asked.

 

"No we're in Nexus Tower, right? It's green!" Darkeye said.

 

The minifigure appeared.Darkeye tried to get to it, but something was stopping him.

 

"You obviously want awnsers. So I'll give you awnsers. Number 1, The tower is green because blue isn't imagination, It's fear. Just so you know, Green is imagination." The unknown minifigure said.

 

"Who are you?" Darkeye asked, now calm.

 

"I am imagination, I am the guardian, watching over this world. I am Kswerni." Kswerni awnsered.

 

Darkeye and Evamio sat down.

 

"One more awnser, you are ditting on Overbuild's Dog's Newspapers." Kswerni exclaimed sarcastically.

 

They hopped up off of the newspapers. Evamio giggled.

 

"Now if you don't mind, I've got a universe to save." Kswerni finished, then flew off.

 

So, what do you think?

Chapter 2: Just a Soul-Stealin' Brickface

 

Kswerni was heading towoards the Treebrick Island, no one has been there for 400 years. Until now he hasn't detected any movement with his powers. The only movement detected besides now were the animals, but this, this was different. he detected another being with his such powers. He guessed Qythra, the guardian of light, but that couldn't be right, she was in another dimension, protecting over the other non-guardians. Finally he guessed Xadus, the guardian of lies and truth. Kswerni wondered if Xadus turned on the other guardians as Frimos did.

 

"Why would he be there?" Kswerni asked himself.

 

Kswerni started detecting smashed giants, he couldn't let that go to waste, he needed to take the creation spark, or soul, for short. Kswerni reached the giant's corpse of huge minifig parts and his schythe started absorbing its creation spark. Kswerni detected minifigures walking around, theese were the ones he was looking for, he slowly smashed them all one by one.

 

"Well didn't find Xadus. I'll find him, so, two more minifigs.... I can deal with that." Kswerni said to himself.
