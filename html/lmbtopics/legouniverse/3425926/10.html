Chapter 7: Not one, But Two

 

 Darkeye took the hood off of his head and scooted the thing covering his face down. His blue hair flopped down, making a more natural look. Darkeye grabbed his wrench and flung it up into the air, and caught it. He continued the process.

 

"How did my dad manage to brainwash you so much?" Darkeye asked.

 

"I dunno. He just kinda, did it." Evamio replied.

 

"Sounds like him." Darkeye said.

 

They walked off, into a portal leading to the real world. It had changed, like someone had been there. Evamio spotted some minifigure parts, and someone there! They dashed over there. The person was Dr. Overbuild.

 

"Doc Over! Wake up!" Darkeye yelled at him.

 

"Darkeye, you have to stop him," Doctor Overbuild told him.

 

"I know, my..." Darkeye replied.

 

"No, not him... Tom, Tom Xadus." Overbuild interupted.

 

"Him... Where is he?!" Darkeye asked.

 

"The, ruins of... Siraque" Doctor Overbuild awnsered.

 

Doctor Overbuild was slowly coming apart. After a while he was smashed. It was very sad, he was the most creative person you could ever meet.

 

"We have to find Vanda and Bizzare, now!" Darkeye ordered.

 

It was going to be a long night.

Chapter 8: Under and Under,

 

Bizzare held out the object, Darkeye had been gone for three days now.

 

"It's not gonna work you know." Vanda said to Bizzare.

 

"Why?" BizzareNerdyLegend asked.

 

"Because Darkeye made it! He always pranked me like that, ALWAYS." She awnsered.

 

Darkeye and Evamio came running into the inn, BizzareNerdyLegend flicked out his staff. Then he made a creature with his Assembly powers.

 

"Calm down! He's not brainwashed anymoe!" Darkeye yelled.

 

Bizzare sheathed his weapon, but still was ready to attack.

 

"Get to the ruins of Siraque, it's east from here. Don't ask why you need to distract someone." Evamio told the two others.

 

"Got it." Vanda replied.

 

After hours of searching, they found the ruins. But it was low underground, very low.

 

"Well, how do we get down there?" Bizzare asked.

 

"The fun way." Vanda Darkflame awnsered.

 

She jumped off a ledge, and grabbed onto the floor at the end of the jump and climbed up.

 

"How am I supposed to do that?" Bizzare thought to himself.

 

Then he saw a pole, he kicked it down and build a little grabber. Then jumped off the edge. When he got to the end, he swung the object up, and climbed p the ledge.

 

"See, you CAN do it.' Vanda said sarcasticaly.

 

BizzareNerdyLegend had thought Vanda and Darkeye were all dark and scary before, now, he had just felt like he read the fan-made book of them.
