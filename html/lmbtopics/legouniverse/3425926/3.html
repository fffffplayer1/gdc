Chapter 3: How is this guy your dad?!

 

 They woke up, feeling a rough grip on their legs. "Welcome, survivors... You have escaped my plan, to rule LEGO Universe." The heroes heard. "Dad?!" Darkeye yelled. The villan showed his face. "THE DARKITECT IS YOUR DAD?" Everyone yelled, except for Darkeye and BizzareNerdyLegend. "Dude, how are you not mad?" Nytefury asked "What time is it?!" Darkeye interupted. "Eleven fifty nine!!!!!" Legend yelled loudly. Then Darkeye turned into a stromling. He sprinted up the stairs and held his dad hostage. Darkeye was behind the Darkitect and hinged the blade to his neck. "Let him go!" Darkeye heard. "Evamio..." Darkeye whispered. "Then, if you want him free, win the game."Darkeye continued  in a rude tone. "Riddles? At least it will be fair." Evamio said. "Me first, A tree moves slowly, but not it's place." Darkeye exclaimed. "I give up." Evamio said after ten minutes. "Fine! He will be free, but you must show us the way out." Darkeye yelled in victory. Darkeye let his dad free, then Evamio showed them the way out. "I have a plan." Darkeye whispered to everyone. Darkeye pushed Evamio out of the spaceship. Making him the only one who could breathe. Then Darkeye smashed all of his survivor friends, collected the parts, and jumped out of the ship. Landing on his Block Yard
