Chapter 4: Lying the truth

 

After Darkeye landed, he set all of the parts of the minifigures in seperate places. Then, he climbed up the mountain at the end of his land. After climbing, he meditated. Then all of his friends came in their normal state. "What was that?!" RadicalJetpackPanda asked angrily. "Look, I can explain... Wait, Bizzare, did you try to smash me?" Darkeye asked and said at the same time. "Yes" BizzareNerdyLegend replied. Darkeye remembered his past with him. "Impostor." Darkeye said under his breath. Darkeye shot him with his Paradox Space Marauder gun. A fog of Maelstrom came from the imposter as he was smashed. "We need to go back to Advant Gardens to find him." Darkeye ordered "No, just me we can't risk all of us." He continued. Then he blasted to Advant Gardens to find the real BizzareNerdyLegend.

Chapter 6: Evamio's Deception

 

Darkeye looked at the visitor with a frowned face. "Vanda, do you expect me to break into the mueseum at LEGO City again after that bad of a failiure?" Darkeye asked. "Not only me."  Vanda replied. A figure walked into the scene. "Ev... Wait! Where are my friends?!" Darkeye yelled. "Look, they're fine. It's me Evamio! Remember?" Evamio replied as he came near the mailbox. "No! This is a recipie for disaster! We've tried this stuff before, and blew up the whole Statue of Overbuild!" Darkeye refused. "Listen, I wont do that on purpose again." Evamio said. Then Evamio dissapeared and knocked out Darkeye. He woke up in a rough prison with Vanda Darkflame and BizzareNerdyLegend tied to him.

Chapter 7: Smarts are Strength

 

BizzareNerdyLegend tried to escape. "Don't do that." Darkeye whispered. Then Vanda Darkflame spotted something. "Over there! A..." She screeched. Darkeye knew what it was. Then Evamio showed up. "Solve these tests and you will be free. But you only have five minutes. Clock's ticking!" Evamio informed with an evil smirk. Darkeye waited two minutes. One of his eyebrows fell. Darkeye caught the hair with his knee, and blew it into the mysterious space. The ropes broke and they fell into a pit. They were surrounded by walls. "Easy!" BizzareNerdyLegend yelled. He stepped on a pressure plate resting on the rock hard floor. One of the walls opened. "Stay there! Don't move, we will find a way to get the door staying open!" Darkeye ordered. The two of them ran through the open space. Vanda spotted a rope. She and Darkeye pulled the large rope. "Get over here!" Darkeye said as Bizzare ran over. They were at the final challenge. "Vanda the Vampire!" Darkeye yelled as a gate opened. Evamio was waiting outside.
