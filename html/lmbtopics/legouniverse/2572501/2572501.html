Welcome, my guests. I am Bane, The Knight of Two Identities. This HQs sole goal is to conquer all others; if they do not join us, they shall be destroyed! Now, if you desire to join this mighty campaign, please read the rest of this script:
The command structure is as follows:
Supreme Leader- Me
Battle Advisers/ Guards- 4 open
Specialty Leaders- 1 for each specialty
Specialty Troopers- infinite
General of the Main Army- 1 open
Soldiers- infinite, can be broken up into battalions at the GMA's request( I will refer to the general of the army as such)
Naval General: 1 open, also in charge of the as-of-yet-unconstrcuted flagship of our space fleet
Naval Commanders: 1 per battleship
Naval boarding troopers- infinite
Pilots- infinite
The following specialties, each with a description, can be chosen if desired, but I, or the specialty leader must approve all specialty trooper profiles. the GMA can approve normal troopers:
Magicians- self-explanatory, anything to do with conjuring, summoning, creature control, and so on.
Technicians- Weak in battle, but are invaluable. They build bases and ships, repair anything, and can produce weapons and other gear. They also are the researchers who can develop new technologies.
Advanced weapons expert- If lasers and jetpacks are your thing, then you should sign up as an advanced weapons expert.
Demolitions exxpert- ideal at destroying vehicles and bases, these guys are experts in rockets, bombs of all kinds, and generally making things go BOOM!
As I think of new specialties, they shall be added to the list. Once this topic has been created, my bio will follow shortly. Positions come on first come, first serve, and you must have a detailed bio to apply. Also, if a weapon hasn't been built, or would be OP to use right away, I will tell technicians to invent it.
-Bane,The Knight of Two Identities
