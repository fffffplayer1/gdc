Pure Imagination, was a legend of the mythical First Builders. Any time before them, is the even more mystical time of the Creators. Pure Imagination, was used for evil at one point, and created the Maelstrom. The evil brother of Pure Imagination, Dark Imagination, also became known to the universe. But, the Creators had always anticipated something going wrong. Zolmar. Baron. Etc.

Several Rangers techs examined the latest test results from a fragment of Pure Imagination acquired from Assembly... And cross-referenced them with the latest test the Paradox had done, that is, before the Void destroyed their space station...

The Maelstrom not only had some sort of pattern, the purest form of Maelstrom had a molecular structure, visible, and not chaotic as most.

They cross referenced Pure Imagination... And the resemblance was strange. Immediately, they realized that neither Maelstrom, nor Pure Imagination were fully elements, they had a strange series of molecules only in their perfect forms. DNA? But somehow, they were missing data, atoms, gooseeggs

Imagination, was uncomplete. Pure Imagination held keys that no one, had ever discovered. Maybe this was a safeguard the First Builders put in, or some strange occurrence.

After several years (four months in roleplay, that's when I first got the idea), a special formula was made. A formula that could allow Pure Imagination to adapt, and change in whatever this fundamental way may be. Several Assembly techs first tested it on a small fragment of Pure Imagination... And discovered,

That Imagination, is more powerful, that ever before...

OOC: Before some yells OP, hypothetically, you could try this with any element. Though for the Maelstrom it might not work right. And I'm willing to let anyone try it with any element, just you'll have to steal it first This might also mean that hypothetically, adding DI may make a giant explosion, or Pure Imagination more powerful. I'll let Bane decide since its his move.

BTW Bane, when do you want that support?

While there is some reaction, I'm afraid Dark Imagination only corrupts Imagination, similar to the process of Maelstrom. So the thing Bane was gonna set off is... now a giant pillar of red stuff.

That's a great backstory. I appreciate the time and effort you and your fellows put into this plot twist.

Thanks for the complement!
