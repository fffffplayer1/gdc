1 flagship
2 large cruisers
0 small cruisers
100 dropships
500 Interceptor class starfighters

Land Forces Report:
The missing base has forced most of our land units to be of limited use, although KK scientists are working on methods to allow Punisher tanks to fire from the hangar as heavy turrets.

DARTs: 50
Punisher tanks: 20
Mobile command centers: 2
Snails: 20

Personnel report:
Active members: 2
Potential members: 2 discovered, but with no posts on here, is closer to 0.
Clone troopers: 600
Clone Spartans: 30
Clones specialists: 20 per class
Clone pilots: 500

Currently, I hereby authorize the promotion of Ruby to magician leader for her attempts at locating new members, as well as showing no signs of spying or traitorous acts against the KK.
JAMMERS OFF!!!!

Ok, now that that ridiculously long report is done, I have assignments for everyone while they are twiddling their thumbs aboard the Freedom. Dimenscionado, you are to be in charge of several scientists looking to make Ruby dragon armor. This armor should have breathing apparatus and the ability to expand at least, although adding some high-tech weapons is recommended.

Ruby, in addition to the search for members, you are to try and track down a pure sample of Maelstrom. Demon is too unstable and chaotic for the kind of power we need.

Also, KK scientists are in the process of designing a new land vehicle as well as cheaper in-atmosphere craft. Hang in there.
