Transmission from KK Heavy Cruiser A3:
Greetings, warriors of the Kingdom. As you may know, Bane recently was smashed by Void forces at an undescribed interstellar location. However, as he will not return for at least a few days, and because he cannot be everywhere, I have come, at he and the ancient Mythrans' command, to aid him in his quest. I am Commander Storm, and allow me to show you who I truly am:
Name: Commander Storm(OoC: Obviously.)

Affiliations: Former member of both the Maletsrom and the Nexus Force, newest member of the KK(OoC: Not in LURP literally, see the backstory below.)

Description:
Normally wears a deep, royal purple cloak that covers his enitre body and weapons. Without the cloak, a suit of very high-tech armor is revealed. Said suit is very simlar to the KK's preexisting Spartan Clone MK ll Armor, but provides superior protection with far less bulk. This armor is a brighter purple color with hints of maroon and electric blue at the joints and smaller pieces. The visor is blue, but is not transparent. Much like Bane, nobody has seen his face since the tragic incident that ended his career in the Nexus Force, with the exception of those who were killed immediately after seeing him. Walks with the aid of a staff capped with a purple, malevolently-glowing jewel, but can actually walk perfectly well without it.

Powers and weaponry: In addition to his vast knowledge of Malestrom and Nexus Force comabt capabilities( And minor knowledge of Void tech, due to combat experiences.), Storm posesses powerful technological weapons and many chaotic, dark powers.

Tech-based weapons:

Elemental Rifle: This is Storm's ranged weapon of choice. Channeling Storm's own chaotic energy, it can act as a flamethrower, an EMP cannon, Maelstrom blaster, freezing ray, etc.

Plasma pistols: Mementos from Storm's time in the Force, these guns work in tandem to deliver plasma-based pain to Storm's enemies. Aside from use as pistols, they can be linked together and formed into a sniper rifle that fires slow but very powerful shots.

Staff powers:

The Staff's capabilities are numerous, but almost all of the powers revolve around chaotic energies, natural or just downright evil. They can range from storm magic, volcanic attacks, Maelstrom power, and a few oddball ones listed below.

-Shadow Cloak
This involves Storm literally becoming a lightning-quick shadow. Perfect for a stealth attack or just getting around faster.

-Imagination Burst
During his time in the force, Storm picked up on how to channle existing Imagination, though he cannot create it. Channeling it can create powerful blasts, although, if done wrong, these can hurt Storm severely.

-Terror Pulse
Using the power of chaos against weak-minded minifigs, Storm can send them into sheer panic. Uusally best against NPC characters, as actual RPers are much stronger mentally and more courageous.

Personality:
Years of fighting and being under the control of the Malestrom have left Storm cold, claculating, and able to keep his emotions controlled. He is a warrior of the mind, and he doesn't forget it when he promises revenge. However, there is... something, beneath the facade, that few ever see.

History:
Long ago, during the first Maelstrom-Nexus Force War (I am referencing LU events here.), Storm had been captured during a battle with the Baron, who had sensed his power. He had been infected and forced to smash many minifigs, and was known as Vargas the Tormented because he always seemed to be fighting an inner turmoil when he smashed his foes. However, one day, he was caught in a blast of Imagination from the Duke's blade, and was partially freed, as something protected him for some reason. Although he regained his shape and his mental control, he was still infected. Shunned by all he met for this, he donned a cloak and fought as an assasin on the side of the Nexus Force, where he received the pistols and special rifle. However, his secret was discovered, and the four original leaders exiled him out of fear of his power, for he possessed nearly as much power as the Baron by having that Imagination mixed in him. He wandered, and, after stealing his suit of armor from the Void's facilities( Which was corrupted by his power as well.), took missions as a bounty hunter. That was how Storm was, until he heard a voice seeming to call him to the Kingdom, and that, my friends, is where we are now.

Well, my comrades, that os my past. I can say that, although I still hate the Maelstrom, I have nothing against the current leaders of the NF, and am ready for battle. As the Void have aided the Maelstrom, I now consider them my foes, along with the GC. Now, we will give them a reason to remember our names. This is the hour, this is our final effort, it is time for us to finish this fight. Now, we must aid the Crimson Order and defeat the Void forever more. For Bane!
-Commander Storm

Thanks!
