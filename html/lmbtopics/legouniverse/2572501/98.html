*Incoming Transmission from Sabre X15, Unknown Star System, approximately 3 years post-Maelstrom creation.*

"Storm, are you still there? I need assistance, for it seems a new foe has risen against me. His crystals, forged of Imaginite, drain the power of IMagintation itself. You are the only Maelstrom wielder left that can aid me. I need you to rally a small army and push forward to end Barcha V2 and then out to Coordinates 9-30-10. Yes, I know that is beyond the rim, but the destiny of the true KK, not Ruby's, lies there. Good luck. Oh, and if you see two Banes, don't panic Good luck, Bane out."

Alright, men, time for battle. You heard the man; we are finally leaving for war, FORWARD UNTO DAWN!!!!
(Troopers) HOO-RAH!!!!
* A handful of heavy cruisers and ligh cruisers, with a total of troops to be determined at a later date, begin to form over Salvation.*
Ruby, remember that I have almost as much power as Bane. I am his commander, so I wield as much power as you, who is also a second-in-command that took power when Pane left to seek his own power. My men hear me, but I will not take more than a pettty number of troops. Enough to bring the heat to the Final Cold, and nothing more. Good luck.
* The fleet accelerates to lightspeed, passing out of KK territory.*
-Storm
P.S. I knew there was a way out with peace, as long as I was clever enough to remember. I did, and now I have a Maelstrom character who can corrupt the Imaginite with ease, rendering it under Storm's control. Yes, you heard me right. those crystals, mine at last!

-the small, single Crystal that has been following you sputters-

I am sorry, but all orders must come from User 01. As you have destroyed one of our Users, all commands from Subject >, registered as Bane, will be ignored. You will be assimilated.

Umm, you aren't a KK soldier, last time I checked. Troopers? Remember that concussion missle Bane was working on? Blast him to pieces!
*In mid-warp, the Freedom unleashes a missle on the shard, causing it to shake itself to pieces.*
-Storm
