Are you an enemy or not? One answer gets you destroyed, the other doesn't.

~ Let's build the World.

State your HQ of origin and rank in said HQ. If you are an enemy, I hope you will enjoy the brig. If you be an ally, then we will have our medics look you over and send you home.
-Bane

Are you imagination? Then I can do a fair 1-hit defeat.

If Imagination and Dark Imagination annihilate each other, then you are as vulnerable as I. Besides, like in Poke'mon, what is the victory worth if you really on advantages of one power or type over another, instead of power and tactics? If you truly wish to be powerful, then you should skip the OP moves and simply have a fai duel, one that forces you to your limits. Even in real life, I know that this is how people improve themselves, and, if you wish to succeed in eventually unseating Hov or Zolmar as the most powerful RPers( Without using OP moves, Eythm, or Dark Imagination.), then you will need to be the best you can be. ( Gah, too much philosophy) Also, if I recall rightly, all minifigs possess at least small levels of Imagination within their bodies, allowing them to move and interact with building. Wielding Dark Imagination would then, in theory, kill the user. Also, I control darkness and Imagination( At least in my dark sided form, which happens against foes I have very dark emotions against, while my light form is a hero-based one where my positive emions and determination shine through.), so how hard could Dark Imagination be to control? And I have a shield against that type of move; too easy to counter. So, I say, at the Void, post your bio already and quit the weak trash-talk. If you say I am all talk, i could say the same to you. And I have been plotting; my sister has been driven bonkers by my constant LURP chatter.
-Bane
P.S. That may be the second-longest LURP post I have ever made. The longest, I think, was the first post on this topic.

Nah. I store DI in Eythm, making it DI Eythm which will not effect Imagination. And also, you do NOT have an energy null shield as it is a non replica item.

When did I say I have an energy null shield? Also, I can counter Dark imagination easily; a simple Imagination blast will cause a detonation as the two react.
@Hov:
Dude, that old excuse for RP doesn't exist anymore. Things are different than they were a year ago. Besides, I would have done it your way, but the M.I.T.'s original betrayal when my HQ first launched into battle has forced me to train myself and unleash every power I can. Also, what new powers do you mean? All I have is Imagination( Which gives me various magics.) Darkness, and Light. The latter two are more like extensions of my emotions into the physical world, so there isn't an issue.
-Bane

Who are you to say if there is an issue or not? If people belive there is an issue, tbere IS an issue. Thats what makes it an issue. If you belive you do not have an issue, that's all well and good, but its not for you to decide if you have an issue. An issue is a problem to other people. You dont get to decide if you are annoying others.

-BuckyKat
