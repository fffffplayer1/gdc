*Approaches Bane*
A duel, you vs me, I win. you leave the Void and its allies alone, you win, you get a free teleport into the Void's HQ, unharmed, do as you wish once your inside. Do you accept?

*Pulls out two handed sword


Ooo...

And as soon as I'm done with him, Bane, your next.

Legowarrior, I should advise you to back out of this now. Bane does not have a good track record of being fair in duels. With the firing upon onlookers, and calling in troops to fire upon his opponent.

-/•\- Iønus -/•\-

Excuse me, but it isn't exactly fair to consider me bad in duels when I have dueled only three times.
-Krill: He fled the hangar.
-Prof. 1: Called in a fleet of Sword ships.
-Prof. 2: Had an OP suit using every powerful energy we know of, and then called in a fleet of ships from.. the Swords, again.
Ok, fine, I admit I cheated the second time around, but only because the Professor himself is known as a liar and a cowardly cheater.Sorry, but you aren't worth my time.
-Bane

I'm not exactly sure what happened with Krill, but I was there for both of your duels with the Professor.

First time- Before the duel started, you attacked Bucky because the Professor called him up to the control box.

Second time- You said as follows at the duel: "Do you really think I would treat a traitor fairly?" And you called your spartan clones to fire upon him.

Professor Shadow is not a liar and a cheater. He is actually one of the fairest and kind RPers in LURP. He has more of a sense of honor and dignity than you've ever shown to possess. But that, is an argument for another time.

-/•\- Iønus -/•\-

I agree, I've never really seen Prof. do anything mean or traitorous. Bane, are you still mad at the fact you fired on HIS allys and he fired back? That was a long time ago...

(~*~Ɍṵᵬẏ~*~)

He is. I don't mind. If he chooses to continue to fight me, so be it. I plan not to attack him anymore. I can't really do much from my new planet! On my new planet, nothing works. Nothing. Well... pretty much nothing. Everything there is so rainy. And dripping with moisture. It's a giant rainforest! Which means you need an airlocked building to have electronics...

~Professor Shadow

Excuse me, but the betrayal was YOUR fault; you failed to alert me that your allies were participating in the battle, and who they were. You do realize that you are single-handedly responsible for one of the most tenacious enemies the DRE will ever know, right? And don't worry, I will vaoprize the rain... along with everything made of matter within the base.. Or I could finally conclude Project GR with a test run.
-Bane

*Yawns*
Bane, vaporizing things isn't always the way to go. As you just saw, 152252 agreed with me, some of the stuff around here is pretty wacky. I'm surprised, we've been agreeing more lately. I don't build superweapons (just supply the Void with the means to build them ) like giant plasma cannons or massive death rays. I take advantage of things already there, like Imagination! I can think up some pretty crazy stuff to do in duels with that! So... I feel bad that vaporization is your only option. And I'm sorry I betrayed you, better communication is in order for next time, eh? I apologize for that, I hope you accept my apology. But I'm not in the DRE, read their whole set of pages, I haven't joined. I don't even know if I've posted on their topic. The Swords kicked me off their world, that's why I'm on Monsoonus. I realize your point, you certainly are tenacious! So I'm glad I've inspired a passion in someone, good or bad. I'm really not sure which one it is, but when you turn to vaporization to vent your passion...

~Professor Shadow
