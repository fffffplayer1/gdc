Ben pulled all the harder when he saw all the fallen rise from the ground, with nothing in their eyes.
"Oh my..."
The risen figures were getting closer, and some were sprinting towards him. Their eyes displayed nothing as they leaped towards Ben. He was nearly there. All he had to do was get to Roseann and the others, and him and Buck would be safe, or safer. However, just before he could do that, a wave of fallen figures ran infront, blocking off the route. The figures infront were nearly upon them now, and Ben dropped Buck in terror. Buck slid to the ground and lay there, not moving.
"I'm so sorry Buck..."
Ben, filled with fear, turned and ran in terror. The figures were coming on all sides now, and he saw that they were making a straight path ahead, and at the end of it, the figures were closing the exit. Ben sprinted as fast as he could, as he got closer and closer but the gap got smaller and smaller. There was only a crack of a gap left now, and Ben leaped as far as he could towards it. He crashed into the forms of the fallen, and fell to the ground as arms snatched him from every direction. In a few seconds, all he could see was hands grasping all over him to increase the relentless grip that restricted Ben's movement. He felt himself falling, falling, falling...

Yonder saw the risen dead flying towards him. In that one small moment, he realized that all hope was lost. There was nothing they could do... except for one thing.
He tugged on Briana, "Stop."
She stopped her progression towards Whisper and began to hover.
Yonder stood up on her back raising very slowly. He walked to the edge of Briana and looked down.
He whispered, "This... is for Nexus Force."
Reluctantly, he dropped his body off of Briana and began falling towards the ground gaining a large amount of speed.
The minifigures below gasped in surprise and horror. What was he doing?!!
Time seemed to freeze as he fell. Finally, after what seemed like an eternity, he hit the surface and sunk several feet into ground due to the impact.
A shriek from a female voice was heard. Vanda Darkflame pushed to the front of the crowd of minifigures who had seemed to have paused their fighting. Tears raced down her eyes shocking most of the Nexus Force members. Vanda had never been spotted this emotional.
She kneeled down beside the hole that Yonder had made when he fell. She stared down into it wondering if there was a purpose he had done this...

Suddenly, a bright, purple glow shot out of the hole. The minifigures surrounding it were forced to squint their eyes. A glowing minifigure then rose out of the hole and blasted up into the air.
For a moment, Vanda gazed in awe and confusion. Then she realized... it was Yonder!

Yonder tackled Whisper with unmerciful anger. With his inner Maelstrom power that he had contained for so many years, now completely unleashed, his strength was now equally matched with his opponent's.
"Fight me!" Yonder demanded, "Let us end this now!"

Whisper laughed. "End? You cannot end me fool. For every light there is a shadow. I am the shadow. You may destroy this physical form, but I will take on another. As long as there is light, there will be darkness. As long as there is life, there will be death. As long as there is joy, there will be those who work to bring misery."

Dark energies swirled around Whispers hands as he continued. "But all of that is rather irrelevant, because soon, you will no longer draw breath."

With that, he unleashed a massive wave of shadow energy, which smashed into his foe with devastating force.
