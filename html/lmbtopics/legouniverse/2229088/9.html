((Finally, back to the action...))

X was standing ontop of one of the few remaining skyscrapers, stairing intently at the sky. He was searching for something, but he couldn't see it.

A strike copter suddenly appeared from behind the clouds, it firied two missles at X, who simply teleported next to the helicopter, and put his hand on it.

The next instant the helicopter exploded in a huge ball of energy. X barely flinched, he just keptstairing at the sky.

Then he saw it, a dot on the horizon at first, but as it got closer it took on a shape, the shape of a plane, a very big plane.

"About time, alright then. Let's get this battle started."

X closed his eyes and teleported, whe he opened them again, he was standing ontop of the plane, right above what appeared to be a hatch.

"Knock knock, can I come in?"

X raised his hand, and blasted the hatch open. He dropped into a cargo bay, as boxes and crates were being tossed about by the wind that was now blowing through the cargo bay. Two giant mechs were positioned at the other side of the room, and they were active.

"Good, they're expecting me. I hate dropping in un-expected."

The mechs raised their guns, and opened fire. X charged forward through the storm of bullets towards them.

"That the best you cna do? Watch this!"

X raised his hands, and created two energy balls. He tossed them both at the mechs, and in an explosion that shook the whole plane, the mechs were destroyed.

"Alright, now, where are you hiding?"
