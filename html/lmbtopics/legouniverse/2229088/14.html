She leaped up onto Sprendon, and his wings outgrew again and he flew up into the sky. Sarah held on for her life, but Roseann just sat there calmly. "You chose Rockstar, huh?" Sarah said, still clutching Sprendon's reins. "Yes. But I can't sing," she said. Sarah turned her head to Roseann. "What?!" she whispered harshly. "I just can't... I'm not good," she said. But really, Roseann's voice was very beautiful, but she didn't let on.

"Hide here, and don't answer anybody's calls unless they are mine. Got it?" Roseann asked Sarah, dropping her off at the point Sarah told her to. "Alright," Sarah said. Sprendon leaped up, and flew back towards Nexus Tower. Roseann started humming something, one of her favorite songs from childhood. "Sprendon, I'm not gonna sing," she said. Sprendon shook his hood, and pretending to stomp his hoof. "Yeah, I know, but..." she muttered. Sprendon flapped his wings harshly, and they soon reached Nexus Tower.

"Yeah, I know, Sprendon, I should," she said, heaving a sigh. "But I can't sing well," she muttered. Sprendon shook his head as she led him down the halls. "Just do any song, any song," he seemed to say. "Yeah, but what?" she said. He shook his head. "Alright, better Idea, how about I make something up?" she said. Sprendon seemed to leap, and she held his reins tight. "Whoa boy, alright, I get it," she said. Sprendon ran down the hall to the stable, as Roseann walked to her room. "Alright," she said, sitting down on the bed. "Time to start writing," she said.

"It's getting harder to stay awake, and my strength is fading fast, you breath into me at last," she sung. "Here, right here, right now, right now, stand my ground and never back down, I know what I believe inside, I'm awake and I'm alive." She shook her head, throwing the papers down. She collapsed onto the bed, her head in her hands. "I just can't, I just can't!" she muttered. A small tear streaked her face. Her head shot up, and she knew what she was going to do.

"Alright, X is master singer. He does Here, I say right here, he says right now, I echo that, I say stand my ground and never back down, and he says I'm awake and I'm alive. Yeah, that'll work!" she said confidently. "Alright. That's only one song. Time to write again. Alright, how about something called... Saving me?" she questioned herself. "Yeah!" she said. Later, she would say she got stir crazy, but she couldn't help it. "Yeah! Okay, um, X is gonna sing this one... um... alright! Here!" she said. She started writing down the words. "Prison gates won't open up for me
On these hands and knees I'm crawlin'
Oh, I reach for you
Well I'm terrified of these four walls
These iron bars can't hold my soul in
All I need is you
Come please I'm callin'
And oh I scream for you
Hurry I'm fallin', I'm fallin'

Show me what it's like
To be the last one standing
And teach me wrong from right
And I'll show you what I can be
Say it for me
Say it to me
And I'll leave this life behind me
Say it if it's worth saving me.....

Yep, he can sing that one!" she said. She started leaping up and down, and Sprendon burst into the room. Sweat was trickling down his forehead, and Roseann was snapped out of her crazy moment. "Sprendon, what happened?!" she yelled. Sprendon whinnied in a painful, soar throat. Roseann looked up, and saw police chasing her poor horse. "Is there a problem?" she asked, stepping to them. Her hair started glowing with purple, and she seemed to grin. "The horse was fighting innocent people. We need t- AHHHH!" Roseann had started fighting them. Her clothes turned back to secret agents, and her hair was blond, and her mask was on. She fought them. She knew they were lying about that... Maelstrom were terrible liars. "Got a problem with him now?!" she muttered, kicking them halfway across the room. Two maelstrom policeman ran up from both sides of her. She switched her head from side to side, pretending to be clueless, and leaped up, causing them to crash into each other. She grabbed the other two policeman's hands, pulled them up, and jumped down, kicking the two guards, causing them all to tumble down. A few people had gathered to see the masked person fighting them. "What is that person doing?!" some people yelled. She saw a policeman from above her, who was real, because her hair glowed blue when she looked at him. "Got a problem with the horse now?!" she asked, kicking them again. She knew she had to prove to the people she hadn't just beat up security, somehow. So she grabbed a spray that was attached to her belt, sprayed them, and they turned into stromlings, groaning on the floor. She disappeared, and walked away, transformed into the singer, and ran back, without the cloaking device. "What happened!?" she shrieked, stepping back, putting on her horrified face. "Some guy in black clothes came and beat this maelstrom up!" they all said. Thank goodness, they hadn't known she was a girl. She ran back into the room, pulled Sprendon to the corner, shut the door, locked it, and put imagination into him. She picked up her phone. "Guys, you need to come to Nexus Tower NOW!!!! It's worse than we thought... this battle isn't just for the faction leaders anymore, guys... it's the battle for Nexus City."

X put his hand to his forhead, and spoke.

"I would really love to help you Roseann, but I have a problem he...... Huh?"

Suddenly X felt something, and looked down at one of the few remaining mountains, a strange glow emenated, completely ignoring Nemisis X started flying towards it.

"Where are you going coward? Stand and fight!"

Nemisis firied a blast that hit X square in the back, sending him flying to the ground, but X climbed out of the crater, smoke swirling from his armor, and contiuned walking towards the glow, Nemisis stared, not sure what to think.

"Alright, do whatever you trying to do, it won't help you."

X walked up to a cave inside the mountain, and staired at it, the light glowed from it, and suddenly a blast of energy struck him. Nemisis staired from his vantage point in the sky. He knew he should fly down and slaughter X while he seemend to be hypnotised, but a sickening had come over him, even overpowering his dark power. It was a horrible feeling, and even though it didn't affect his power, it made him feel weaker than his original power.

As the beam faded from X, he seemend unchanged, and for some reason, Nemisis felt compelled to ask.

"X, are you ok?"

Nemisis had to slap himself, why had he asked that? It was his job to destroy X, anything that hurt him was good, right?

The glow from the cave had faded, X slowly turned, and suddenly teleported in-front of Nemisis, and it was obvious he was changed. His hair was purple, with energy crackling through it, and Maelstrom glowed from his armor. But even more disturbing was that now his eyes were completely purple, and glowing, and a long, purple scar ran across his face. The Scarstrom had returned.

"Ah, so this is the power my brother has always kept hidden, I wondered how strong he really was."

Nemisis staired, now that X was close to him, he felt almost terrified, he couldn't understand it, his power seemend to have been surpressed.

"Who... are you?"

X laughed.

"Allow me to introduce myself, I'm X's brother, Mr.Dark. Unforntunatly for him, he didn't realize two thing. One, I never perfected the cure for Scarstrom, so it always remained in him, and Two. He never realized, I could possess anyone who has Scarstrom infection, and after my previous self was destroyed by a hero want-to-be, I was reduced to me energy form. Thankfully, your little fight allowed me to find him. And with his powers, and my own, this world is about to fall to the Maelstrom. Starting with you!"
