Jyk reached into the bag, and pulled out a grenade. "Sweet!" he said. Jyk threw it at the ceiling over Svart's head (Who was distracted trying to burn through Speedy's fire proof wings.) the grenade exploded, causing rocks to fall and bury Svart. "And thats how to beat a dragon!" Jyk said "By the way..... WHAT IN THE UNIVERSE ARE YOU GUYS DOING DOWN HERE!!!"

What? There is no ceiling. We're outside.

No, we,re in a cave!

Oh well in that case...

Yonder cheered, "Great job, Jyk! Let's go."
He began to turn around to walk away when suddenly, he heard a rumbling sound behind him. He quickly spun around to face the sound.
What he saw made him gasp. The pile of rocks which had fallen on the Dragon was falling apart and shaking.
After a few seconds, the Dragon's head shot up from the pile with the rest of its body following it. It spit a blast of fire at Yonder as soon as it had the chance.
Before Yonder had time to react, the blast hit him scorching his body in flames. He fell to the ground screaming in pain. He rolled around trying to make the fire die.
He looked up at Briana and Jyk with fading vision, "HELP ME!"

Horrified, Briana swiftly changed into another dragon, this one silver with scattered gold scales. She breathed a stream of ice at Yonder. The flames that enveloped him melted the ice as it came near, dousing him in tepid water, and putting out the flames. Then she turned to Svart and breathed another stream of ice, this one bigger, at him. Shards stabbed at him, but broke on his scales. One chunk, bigger than the rest, hit him in the neck and sent him flying backwards. While he was stunned, Briana turned to Yonder. "You okay now?"

I'm back. Anyway, Briana, bookworms, horse girl, whatever you like being called, I may be getting two horses at some point in the near future! I can't believe it.


Roseann looked over at Yonder, and thought hard for a second. Suddenly, she looked up, pulled something from her saddlebag, and threw it towards Yonder. They were imagination gloves, something that may just help Yonder. Before she could say anything else, Sprendon fell out from under her. She fell to the ground next to him, air knocked out of her. Her eyes blurred, and she looked around, seeing everyone fight the dragon. "I'm further away than they are, so thank goodness, they didn't see that. I've got to go help Alyson!" she muttered, regaining her breath. She leaped up about twenty seconds afterward, and Sprendon got up with her. She leaped up onto him, slapped his reins and kicked, tightened her legs, and Sprendon reared and galloped.

Galloping through the trees with bright green leaves, Roseann lifted her watch to her face. "Alyson, do you hear me?" she yelled. Fuzzy sounds came from the other side. But a voice made through, and it was Alyson's. "Alyson! Do you hear me?" she yelled again. "R-r-r-r-r-rosean-n-n-n, i-it's L-l-Lloyd!!" came a strained voice. Roseann's eyes lit up with anger and pain, and she stared off. "He's back!" Alyson whispered in a little voice again. Alyson was about Roseann's age, but just a little older. Sprendon started galloping faster, too, because Alyson had a mare that he liked... a lot!

Roseann leaped off of Sprendon, and walked down a path. She pressed herself into a wall, and a secret door opened. She looked inside, and saw nothing but wounded girls. You see, Alyson was Roseann's best friend AND leader of the Black-Star agency, a secret agency formed of ONLY girls. Roseann heard a bit of laughter behind her, and she pulled her mask back up. She turned around, and saw Lloyd, leaning against the wall with Alyson's arm in his tight, cold grip, with her trying to fight against him. "Lloyd," Roseann began, walking towards him slowly, "Let her go!" She threw a dagger towards his hand. He lifted his hand, but still gripped on Alyson. Alyson disappeared, knowing that this fight really did belong to Roseann. Alyson had just transported somewhere else to fight her own enemy. Lloyd looked over, laughed, and turned back towards Roseann. "Knew it," he said. "Alyson was the perfect bait!" Lloyd leaped in the air, his knives ready to slash. Roseann leaped back, one of the knives hitting her leg and cutting it. Roseann fell back towards a wall, and groaned. She looked up at Lloyd, and said, "Well, Lloyd, smart trick, but where is your father whom I also dislike?" Lloyd laughed evilly at Roseann's remark, and said, "Oh, that guy?" Roseann stared directly at him. "Yes, Lloyd Garmadon!!"

X's eyes suddenly opened.

"What?... Oh, must have fallen asleep."

He got to his feet and looked around, everything seemend normal. But he felt that something was wrong, but he could place what.

Then something drew his eyes over to the battlefield, well, actually ABOVE the battlefield. A small, glowing, black orb hovered there. Suddenly purple energy started flowing from the craters into the orb, which grew and changed into the form of a minifigure. It resembled a mythran, only black, with red eyes.

As it touched the ground, more energy started flowing into it. It simply stood there until all the energy was absored, examined itself, and spoke.

"I hate absorbing left over energy, makes me feel like a scavenger. Prefer to get it directly from the source."

It's voice was strange, sounding very proper, but there was something else in it. X just couldn't place it.

The figure looked around for a second, noticed X, and what appeared to be a smile crossed its face.

"X! I was wondering when I would have the honor of meeting you again! Though you have changed quite alot since I last saw you. How have you been old chap?"

X sighed under his breath, of all the people to show up. Did it have to be this guy?

"Great, just great. What brings you all the way out here? Ran out of people to destroy back home?"

The figure laughed.

"You make it sound like I'm a heartless killer! No, nothing like that. I just detected a large amount of energy being used here and thought I would drop by. Looks like I missed all the fun though. Pity, been awhile since I've seen a jolly good fight."

"Yeah well, it was just... A family matter. Nothing to concern you, Shadow."
