‎10-05-2011 12:49 PM

Yonder stood on top of a tall skyscraper looking at a view of a fleet of helicopters approaching Nexus City. At first he thought they were Talons because they were difficult to see considering the fact that he was looking at them with the sun directly in the background, but he quickly realized that they weren't.
He was puzzled on what they were. He had never seen any vehicle like that before. Suddenly, a few blasts of energy shot out of the tip of one of their cannons. Yonder jumped out of the way just in time. The blast exploded right where he had been standing.
"What the!" he yelled.
He leaped off the building to avoid more blasts. The landing on the ground was safe. He looked up to catch a quick glimpse of the helicopters swooping down towards him. People walking down the sidewalks of the city casually doing their daily routine screamed and ran around in panic.
Yonder grunted," This has to have something to do with the Paradox's newest breakthrough."
Cars screeched and crashed into building breaking into barber shops and cafes. Yonder did the best he could to avoid getting hit by them. Even worse now, the helicopters were deploying green armored soldiers onto the ground.
Yonder charged up his Wormholer and shot off a couple rounds of it at a group of soldiers. It caused an explosion on the ground and sent the soldiers flying. Yonder raced over to the grapple on the ground leading to one of the helicopters. He began climbing up it as quickly as he could.
A rotary cannon popped out of the bottom of the helicopter and shot at him. He simply swung to the left and avoided the blasts. For his next move he leaped off the grapple rope and grabbed the cannon. He clung to it dangling from a deathly height from the ground. The cannon spun wildly trying to shake him off, but he came to no success.
Yonder swung himself up to the ledge of the helicopter and pulled himself into the vehicle.
Inside the cockpit, the pilot gritted his teeth in anger. He swung the helicopter to the left trying to shake his opponent out of the vehicle. This only allowed Yonder to move up to the windshield of the helicopter. He put his face up against the glass and grinned making more angry fury grow inside the pilot. The pilot swung to the right this time, but Yonder held on firmly.
Once the shaking stopped Yonder pulled of the windshield and grabbed the pilot by his shirt.
"Let go of me!" the pilot screeched in terror as Yonder moved to the edge of the helicopter.
They were his last words. Yonder dropped him and he was smashed when he hit the ground. Yonder climbed inside the cockpit of the helicopter and closed the windshield.
He turned the vehicle towards the other helicopters and shot a few missiles at them. They exploded.
Yonder laughed," Too easy!"
He began flying down the streets of the city. Over the loud sound of the helicopter's engines, he could faintly hear mini figures on the streets cheering him. Suddenly, three more helicopters flew out from behind a skyscraper. They turned down the street heading directly towards Yonder's helicopter.
Yonder was about to let off a few more missiles when he heard his Nexus Force Comm. Link beep. He looked down to it. The word EMERGENCY flashed on it.
He answered it," Hello?"
"Soldier," the voice of Duke Exeter's sister, Amanda, replied on it.
"Yes ma'am."
"The faction leaders have been kidnapped!" she told him," Our Secret Agency is working on getting them back right now. They are under attack... they need your help."
