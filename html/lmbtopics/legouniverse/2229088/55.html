PART 1

Briana leaped over fallen soldiers, and ran towards Roseann. She had gotten the message to come, so she had made sure to NOT make Roseann angry. Not to say she wasn't already, however. "Briana!" Roseann yelled when Briana reached her. Briana smiled and nodded, trying to shout over the noise. "This hardly seems like a fair fight! We're winning!" she yelled happily, clapping her hands. Even Roseann had to smile at this, for Briana knew that Roseann was like her sister. "Yes, thanks to whoever just turned the tides!" Roseann said, running towards Briana. But before she reached her, something blasted the midle of them, forcing them both backwards. When they both recovered their vision, they looked up to the direction the blast had come from, seeing something they both had thought, or mostly hoped, they would never see again.

Hovawart

PART 2

"Don't tell me you believed I'd never return," Hovawart said, grinning evilly. And it made Briana sick. "We could only hope, but then again, you never give up, even though you know you'll lose! Good idea, wrong side, buddy!" Roseann yelled, lifting her fist in the air. "Oh, Rose, you never change," he muttered darkly, barely audible. "Likewise," Roseann replied. Briana managed to get to Roseann, but only by transforming to a wolf. When she reached her, she transformed back to human again, glaring up at Hovawart. "Oh, hey Briana, didn't see you there!" Hovawart yelled to Briana, making her even angrier than she had been before, which was extremely angry. "Don't talk to me like it's nothing, Hovawart! You destroyed my family and my life, do you think I will listen to your jokes!?" she yelled, though it was nearly screaming. Hovawart looked rather annoyed, and he came down to the ground. "Actually, I was hoping you would, surprisingly. Would be a nice thing to tell you before-" Hovawart was stopped mid sentence, and he turned, seeing Buck run up to Roseann, looking rather worried. "Roseann, I heard something extremely loud come from this direction! What was-" Buck started, but stopped as soon as he got there, seeing Hovawart. "No..." he whispered. Hovawart grinne evilly, and said, "Why, hello there, Buck!" Buck growled at his remark, and said, "What do you want?" Hovawart pretended to look shocked, and said, "Why, I was about to say! That is, before you so rudely ran in!" Buck looked even angrier, but kept quiet. If looks could kill, Hovawart would be a pile of mush, because the expression on Briana's face made it obvious she was ready to short every one of his circuits with a bucket of water. "Anyway, Briana, it would be a nice thing to tell you," Hovawart started again, flying up into the air, and readying his guns, before he continued, "Before I destroy you."

PART 3 (switch it Roseann)

Everything in the next few seconds seemed to happen in slow motion to them all. As the blast hurled towards Briana, she looked up, eyes wide in horror. Roseann shrieked, or screamed, it was hard to tell which, and ran towards Briana. But Buck caught her arms, pulled her back as the blast hit Briana straight in the heart. A bright flash of light, and a deafening explosion happened all at the same time. Buck dragged Roseann to the ground, shielding her from the metal that was flying towards them. When the smoke cleared, all that was left of the spot Briana was standing in...
was concrete. "NO!" Roseann screamed, tears falling from her eyes. All at once, the world seemed to crumble. Dark clouds appeared, and Roseann couldn't tell why, but it didn't help her mood. "NO, NO NO! Briana, please don't be dead!" she screamed, getting herself free of Buck's grasp. She ran to the spot Briana had been standing, only to see a piece of paper there. Roseann was oblivious of the fact that Hovawart was close to her, laughing so darkly and horribly, it itself would defeat an enemy. She picked up the paper slowly, her hands shaking, and her eyes blurry. She unwrapped the piece of paper, and wiping the tears from her eyes slowly, she read.
"Playground school bell rings again
Rainclouds come to play again
Has no one told you they're not breathing
hello, I'm your mind, giving you some one to talk to,
hello

If I smile and don't believe
Soon I know I'll wake from this dream
Don't try to fix me, I'm not broken!
Hello, I'm the lie, living for you so you can hide, don't cry

Suddenly I know I'm not dreaming
hello I'm still here, all that's left of yesterday..."
Roseann let the paper drop slowly... because her friend, someone who was to young to die...


had died.

Buck lay there, staring at the ground infront of him. The metal from the explosion had stabbed into his back, and he felt himself fading. Fighting to stay awake, he opened his eyes fully, examinin his surroundings. A few meters away was the rubble of a building, but at least he could use the rubble as a support for himself. Slowly but carefully, as not to hurt himself even more, he crawled towards the rubble. It seemed so far away, down a large tunnel as black started to seep into his vision. " Have to....stay awake.." Buck muttered to himself trying to encourage himself, but this didn't work. The rubble was so far away now, only a dot in the distance. He reached his arm slowly out, although he knew that he would not reach it. However, to his suprise, his hand touched the rubble and his vision shifted backm into focus in a moment. While leaning against the rubble, Buck examined the destruction, and saw hundreds of smashed minifigures on the ground, and a few intact ones, but who were also lifeless. While scanning the corspes of both sides on the ground, he noticed a figure that he seemed to recognize. Ben.
"No!" Buck screamed at the top of his lungs as he forgot about his pain and sprinted over to his brother. His eyes were black, and showed nothing. He found his hand stroking hid brother's hair back, and noticed tears rolling down his cheeks. He was devastated. thought of Mr Dark. He had done it, and he would pay for it, whatever he tried to do to stop him. Mr Dark wouldn't get a chance for a next plan, for by then Buck would of destroyed everything he had everything that he held precious to him. His work would be destroyed, and everything he had ever worked on would go up in flames. Buck's eyes burned in fury, and it was just then that his eyes drifted over to the back of his hand. In a second the moment was gone and astonishment rippled through his eyes.

On the back of his hand was a small white dot, where his skin were either black or grey, there was a tiny bit of white. He remembered something that he had heard before, "with no scarstrom, you are doomed, and you will die instantly once the last drop of scarstrom is gone". Fear shot through him, and the metal in his back burned more intense than ever. In an instant, his vision was black....
