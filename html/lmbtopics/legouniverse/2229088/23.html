Jyk landed at the bottom of the hole. "Ow, that hurt!" he said as he got up painfully and walked over to a boulder and leaned against it. When Jyk leaned against the boulder a tunnel opened up in the cave wall! "Okaaaay that was just a little weird." Jyk said walking towards it. "HEY GUYS, I'M GONNA EXPLORE DOWN HERE A BIT, DO YOUR STUFF! I'LL CATCH UP WITH YOU!" he yelled, as he walked down the tunnel. After a while he reached a light and the source of the light was a huge glowing lake of maelstrom radiation! "Well this is one weird day." Jyk said.

As Jyk walked around the radiation lake, he saw a light coming from a tunnel. "Well look, another light." he said as he walked towards the tunnel. When he reached it, he saw a sword laying on a stone, and there were minifig bricks and weapons all around it, he took a shoulder bag off of the ground and put it on. Jyk walked over to the sword and picked it up. At the same time he picked it up the cave shook and a huge, ugly, maelstrom dragon walked towards him. Jyk sighed "This is just not my day."

As the Dragon was about to blast a fire ball, Jyk heard a voice from above him," Need some help?"
It was Yonder. The Space Marauder landed on the Dragon's head. The beast furiously whipped his head around trying to shake him off, but it came to no success.
Once the enraged animal had calmed itself, Yonder double jumped off it and landed beside Jyk," Don't worry. I've easily handled these things before."
Little did he know that this wasn't any ordinary Dragon...

"Yonder! Jyk!" Briana screamed. She had followed Yonder, and had seen the dragon. And, having read multiple legends about dragons, she knew who this was. "Get away!" She quickly shape-shifted into the dragon form of before, and said, "It's Svart Skugga, the fabled dragon who spread terror and destruction many years ago. Then he dissappeared for so long that it was believed that he died, but he simply retreated, though no one knew where. It seems that he came here. It also seems that he is not in the best of moods." Rearing up, Briana waited for the other dragon to make a move.
