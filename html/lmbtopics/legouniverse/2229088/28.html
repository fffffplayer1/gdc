Back...


Roseann shook her head, and groaned. "Ugh, my head...." She reached up to her head, as it was throbbing. She looked at her hand, and though there was nothing, she could tell she had bruised it. Sprendon was lying on the ground. Roseann leaped up. She ran over to Sprendon, and helped him up. He whinnied, saying he was alright, nuzzled Roseann, and got down again for her to get on. Her leg felt numb and weird, so she had to get on like that. Once she was on, Sprendon got up, and stood. Roseann turned his reins, and slapped them a little, kicking a little. Sprendon turned at a high speed, and ran back to go to the battlefield.


Roseann waved her hand over her face as Sprendon galloped to the field. Her hair turned back to blond, her mask appeared, and she leaped off of Sprendon when they appeared from the woods back at the battlefield. She stopped quickly, and said, "Why do I not see anyone?" Sprendon whinnied, saying through the Whinnie, "I don't know, Roseann! But I don't like it..." Roseann nodded, and said, "I feel it, too..." She started walking towards the gate. She lifted up her leg, the non numb one, and kicked the gate. It shook, and fell down. She walked through the main street of the city. "What is it with Nexus Village?" she muttered. She turned around as she heard something crack, and said, "Who is there?!" loudly. She turned around slowly, back to the street. As soon as she heard it crack again, she started running. She leaped over a car, slid on the sidewalk, and leaped up on top of an apartment building. Roseann had extreme skills, considering she was the leader of the largest and best Secret Agency in the Universe. Sad thing was, being the leader of the best Secret Agency in the entire Universe meant you had people trying to get you all the time. But she honestly didn't care. She kept running, and hid behind a small wall on the roof. With her head hurting, and her leg numb, she couldn't fight very well. But when she heard someone else on the roof, attacking the person following her, she looked. It was Callie, her friend in the Agency, one of the higher ranked agents. The person punched her, forcing her against a wall, causing her to be knocked out, and captured, thus disappearing with the transporter that was used by whoever the evil person was. Roseann got very angry, and leaped out at the person. The person deflected at the surprise attack, and Roseann got even more angry. "You do know who I am, right?" she said angrily. The evil agent laughed. Roseann suddenly attacked him, and forced HIM against the wall. He leaped up behind her, and she kicked back, he grabbed her leg, she leaped over him, and punched him. He let go, and stumbled. She used one of her most powerful kicks to get him against a wall, unable to attack again. "If you're only dreaming, why do I hear you screaming?" she asked. She left him there, jumped off the building, and said, "Now to find the others."
