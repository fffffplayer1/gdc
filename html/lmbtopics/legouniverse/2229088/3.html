The silver figure was now an object of intense interest for Briana. After watching him change, she longed to know more about him. So instead of mounting Grande, she transformed into a tall gray horse. Misnon raised an eyebrow but mounted his horse anyway. When they started off, Briana observed the newcomer for a moment, then began to run beside him. "Hello." She said. "I couldn't help but notice that you shapeshift." She waited for him to respond.


-Horse, Crazy Daredevil

Swarm glanced over at the horse who, a moment ago, had been a girl. "So do you," he replied. After a moment he continued. "It's useful, especially when ones natural form stands out. I've found that it is often easier to blend in, go unnoticed. Sometimes safer, as well."

Briana went quiet. She had never attempted her natural form. Her parents had always told her to only use it in times of dire need, because of the raw power it contained, and the energy it took to maintain. Feeling nervous, she fell back, running alongside Misnon. He reached over and tugged her mane, and she switched him with her tail. She continued to watch the figure as she ran. Then she caught a fragment of Buck and Roseann's conversation. Buck was saying that the newcomer's red eyes could spell trouble. In shock, Briana tuned out. So that was what he had meant by his natural form standing out! For now she wouldn't accuse him of anything, but he was definitely one to keep an eye on, not unlike Hovawart. Briana wondered where he was now.

Can I join? I just got on the messageboards, and I wanted to look at stories... then I saw this, thought it was interesting, and clicked on it.... I love secret agents! Here is a profile and first post


Name: Star
Age: Fifteen
Appearance: She is a shape shifting horse, who can talk... not only that, her age is exactly that of a human.... AKA, fifteen in her years are the same as in humans. She is a pretty, paint mare, with bright blue eyes and a soft mane.
Likes: Horses, books, reading, fighting, nature, shifting between wolf and horse
Dislikes: Darkitect, Garmadon, Evil shapeshifting creatures



Her hooves thudded against the ground, and the warm sunlight beamed onto her back. Behind her she heard shouts, and grunts. She looked behind, and saw ten ronin running at her. She ran faster, and weighed shifting to a wolf, or staying a horse. She took wolf. She thought hard, and suddenly, she changed into a black, sleek wolf. She turned around, and growled at the ronin. The Ronin stepped back in surprise, and held their swords in front of them. They gathered themselves together, and started to run to her. As they neared, she leaped over a log, and into a small creek. The one place maelstrom couldn't go: Imagination water. She galloped, water splashing on her face. She ran onto the other side of the creek, far from the ronin. She had lost them. She turned back into a mare, and trotted along. She heard voices, but they weren't raspy and dark. They were worried, she thought. She guessed that there were two girls and a couple of guys. She galloped to where she thought the sound was coming from, and skidded into a stop, then reared. Right in front of her was a girl on a black horse. Beside her, two guys stood, who must have been talking to her. Behind the girl, there was a silver horse with red eyes, and a tall grey horse, and another guy on another horse. She stepped back, struck with fear. Were these creatures trust-worthy? The only people she had known before were girl stromlings and guy stromlings, so she had no clue real people exsisted. She turned, ready to gallop, but stopped. She looked at the two horses in the back, walking freely. She stepped back again, then ran through them all to reach the two horses. "Where are we? What is this place? Who are they? Help!" she said in a scared tone. She waited for an answer, hoping they would respond quickly.
