Roseann leaped out of the window, strung her whip on a pole above her, and pulled up. She had come up with a valid excuse to leave the tower for awhile, so she was leaving. Sprendon flew up to her, and she pressed her wrist's button, and her clothes changed back to Secret Agent. She leaped on Sprendon. He flew down to the path, and hid his wings. Roseann held tight as he galloped back towards Nexus City.

Still, the agents were outnumbered by the green-armored soldiers, and Roseann shook her head in dismay. "I need Dakota," she muttered. Dakota was a Ninja, with brown hair and green eyes. (Dakota is modeled after me... in my ninja costume yesterday at my Tae Kwon Do class) Her suit was black, black gloves, black shoes, and black hood. She had a blue mask, and blue armor, along with a ninja strap crown on her head, and she had gold decorated swords in their sheaths, attached to her back. "Donne-moi le Dakota!" Roseann said to her phone. Dakota spoke mostly French, but she did speak english. (Kidding, I speak English, not French, but I know it) Suddenly, a girl, Dakota, road in on a black horse with glowing blue eyes. Roseann smiled as she saw Dakota ride in on her horse. Dakota pulled out her double blades, and cross them in front of her. She struck them out on either side, slashing two soldiers. She leaped up, as her horse reared and ran into other soldiers. Dakota leaped up, and kicked a soldier on his head. She backflipped down, and slashed at two soldiers, and leaped up as they charged for her, causing them to knock each other out. She ran towards Roseann, and her horse leaped next to her, and she leaped up onto her horse. "Nouveau temps record, (New record time)," Roseann said. "Peut-être, mais c'est ce que vous voulez parler, Roseann? (Perhaps, but is that what you want to talk about, Roseann?)" Dakota responded. Roseann laughed, and Sprendon turned and ran for one of the leaders. Dakota followed close behind her, her horse's hooves thundering against the ground. Roseann slashed at the guards, as Dakota leaped up onto the building, and got ready to ambush the leader if Roseann needed help. Roseann ran to him, and they got into a furious sword fight. "You fight well," said the leader. Roseann smiled, and said, "It's a working progress!" They fought for awhile, but the leader knocked Roseann down, and held the sword to her. "Last words?" he said. Roseann smiled, and said, "One. Maintenant! (Now!)" Dakota leaped down, and kicked at the leader, knocking him against a wall, and as he struck with a sword, she bent backwards and ducked, leaped over, spun around and kicked him. She leaped up, landed behind him, and kicked him. She grabbed his arms, twisted them around to his back, and he yelped. She kicked him away, and Roseann started punching him. Dakota battled with ten guards as Roseann beat the living Maelstrom out of the leader. Roseann finished, and Dakota walked over, and said, "La haine de vous déranger, Roseann, mais votre frère semble avoir été repris par M. Dark. Nous avons besoin de le faire sortir de cette transe, ou bien, nous sommes tous d'achat des pierres tombales. (Hate to bother you, Roseann, but your brother seems to have been taken over by Mr. Dark. We need to get him out of that trance, or else, we're all buying headstones.)"
