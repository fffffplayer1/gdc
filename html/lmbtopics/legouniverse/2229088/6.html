Yonder was just able to dodge another missile by swerving his helicopter to the right. He regained control of the joy stick and continued flying.
Soon he flew outside Nexus City and down a natural land. Eventually, he began nearing a cliff. He saw several other mini figures scrambling around it. He prepared to land near the area.
Suddenly another missile hit the helicopter. The ship shook violently knocking Yonder's head into the right side of the windshield covering the cockpit. The spot that his head hit shattered leaving about a two inch sized hole.
Yonder rubbed his head on the spot that was hit. He felt something soak into his glove. Blood. He brought his hand back from his head and put it down on the controls of the helicopter.
His cut was bad. Even worse, his right wing was covered in flames. He continued doing the best he could to dodge more missiles, but it was very difficult. Eventually, he had to give up. A missile hit his right wing again completely tearing it off.
The helicopter spun around wildly. Yonder tried to take control of it with the joy stick, but he wasn't successful. His plane hit the ground, tumbled over a few times, dragged across the dirt for a few seconds, and finally stopped right in front of the Secret Agency squad.
He breathed heavily relieved that he had survived the crash. He laid back in his seat and blacked out.
(Do whatever you want with him. When I awaken, I'll take it from there.)

Swarm acted almost without thinking. Leaping onto the cockpit of the crashed chopper, he used his palm lasers to cut a sizable hole in it. Dragging the pilot out, he examined the nasty wound in his head. "Just as well you're unconscious. You won't feel this," he muttered. As he placed his hand over the cut, the nanobots he was made of spread over it. They rapidly stitched the wound, and when Swarm removed his hand, the bleeding had stopped. Lifting him up, he carried him clear of the wreck, then carefully laid him down. "He's going to have quite a headache when he wakes up," he said quietly.
