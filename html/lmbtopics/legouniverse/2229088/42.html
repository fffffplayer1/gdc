Yonder sat up and moaned. What had happened? The last he remembered he was jumping off Nexus Tower after Roseann *. He had no idea what took place after that.

He looked at the surface around him. It was all hard, flat stone with several rocks jagging out of it. Some were just a few feet away from him. How he did not land on them he did not know. The Mythrans were surely watching over him.

Several cracks that streaked across the surface divided it. Bright, purple light probably from Maelstrom energy danced out of the cracks.

With all of these details around him plus the noises of Caldera Mar's bubbling heat and dragon wings flapping in the distance, Yonder was sure he was on Crux Prime.

He was sore all over from the fall. His back and arms were aching. His legs were numb restricting him from standing up when he attempted to.

Roseann had been right. He had landed on Crux Prime. He just had not expected it to be as painful as it was.

How long had he been asleep? What had happened? Where were Sprendon and the Faction leaders? Were they alright? Where was Roseann?

Yonder pressed a button on the communicator on his wrist and spoke into it, "This is Paradox Agent Yonder. Can anyone hear me?"
