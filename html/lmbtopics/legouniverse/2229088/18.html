Jyk heard an explosion near the gates of nexus city so he started running to it. When he got near the gates, he saw a whole bunch of minifigs and one was floating in the air not to mention he was purple. "Ugh just what my day needs, a floating infected minifig, bricks." Jyk aimed his blaster and fired.

Both the wormholer's blast and the bolt from the blaster hit Mr.Dark, but he barely flinched.

"Ha! I have a few powers my brother didn't, like the ability to absorb energy attacks so the do little to no damage."

Mr.Dark raised his hand, and a glowing sphere appeared in it.

"Here! Catch!"

Yonder leaped in the way. In mid-air his hands started to glow purple.
"I'll take that," he said snatching the energy sphere with his purple hands.
He then threw it back at Mr. Dark. He knew it wouldn't do much, but it would distract the mini figure for a few seconds.
With his spare time he leaped up and grabbed X by the shirt and swung him around a bit. Then he dragged the infected agent over to Briana who still lay on the ground in pain.
"Listen to me, X!" he said kicking him onto the ground right by the Dragon form of Briana, "This is your sister! You would never betray her, would you?!! Now, I know there's still good in you. Release yourself from this horrible infection! I know you can! The Universe depends on it!"

((Wrogn again, Briana is Roseanns fried, but Roseann is my sister.))

Mr.Dark shook his head quickly, then laughed.

"Not going to work, I'm control the scarstrom inside of him as well, meaning I retain full control until it is removed. And guess what, The only antidote was on my old body, which that fool Buck conviently destoryed. So sorry."

Mr.Dark started flashing, and suddenly a huge explosion sent everyone flying. Mr.Dark hovered above the newely formed crater, with Yonder still holding on to him.

"Oh, are you still there? I.... I...."


A loud wolf howl suddenly interrupted Mr.Dark, and X's pet wolf came running up. Mr.Dark stared at it for a second, the turned and punched Yonder, knocking him off.

"Excuse me, it seems I have a dog problem, be right back."
