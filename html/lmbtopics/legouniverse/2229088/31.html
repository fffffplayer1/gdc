WHAT THE HECK?! Well, on that strange note... I'm gonna write about the Third Book. Or first post of third book. Whatever.




*JAMMERS ON! AUTHORS ONLY!!!!*


First post of book Three.
Oh, and you CAN have the other characters say stuff in this book, as long as it's not REALLY far. Sorta like what Briana said to Rose.

Roseann walked towards water's edge. The lake air was blowing her hair back behind her, and the water stilled. She sighed, bent down, and picked up a shining river rock. She turned around, and walked back towards the camp, setting down the rock. "This is an awfully long break," Briana said. "We've been here for months now! Ever sinc-" "Briana, don't say anything about Nexus City... we're disguised now, and we have to keep it a secret. You know that is a risk we can't take..." Roseann said, her voice stern. Briana put up her hands and said, "Okay, Rose, I get it. What's wrong?" Rose shook her head and said, "Ugh, sorry, I got really on edge for a minute. I've.... gotta go do something."

Rose walked along the path, her skirt swishing side to side. She heard the birds chirp, and though she'd normally enjoy it, she didn't seem to. Something made her sad, or whatever. Everyone had gotten through the battle of Nexus city, but she was still sad. She was now twenty, and that was funny, considering you have to be twenty to get out of collage... at least. And she was leader of a Secret Agency, not to mention the biggest one! It was strange, but it worked. She turned around, and she shivered. She turned her head, and dark storm clouds of the color Purple started rushing towards their camp at a fast pace. "Oh no...." she muttered. She started running with her head still turned, and then turned her head back to the front. She started running even faster than before, and when she got back to the camp, she yelled, "Everyone! Get in the woods in the tents! NOW!" The Maelstrom started covering the camp, and they looked up, thinking, "Oh no..." They all got up, and ran to the woods. Rose ran behind them, and said, "Keep going! I'll get the horses!" She turned a little, and ran towards the horses. Sprendon was rearing and bucking, trying to get the others free from the log. Roseann ran next to him, and pulled at the reins, and got him free. He started kicking the log, and Roseann started undoing the ties. Star, Grande and Runner got free. She sent them with the others. The maelstrom kept creeping over the campsite, and purple lightning struck the ground. "You'd better hold off your storm, dude!" she yelled up to the sky, knowing who was watching. Surprisingly enough, it wasn't the Darkitect. But we'll get to that part later. She pulled the rest of the horses free, and they ran towards their owners. Roseann leaped on Sprendon, and he started galloping.



*JAMMERS OFF!*

Shadow got up off the ground as Jyk left.

"Well well X, giving orders now are we?"

X shrugged and started wlking towards one of the doors.

"You know X, I can sense you really don't like your werewolf form, do you?"

X turned to face Shadow, his fur slightly bristiling.

"What do you think?"

Shadow smiled and nodded, and that's when X saw it, for a brief second, Shadow's eyes flashed bright red, and then went back to their darker color.

"I was just asking X, no need to get upset about it." Shadow said, smirking as he walked past X.

"He's up to something, but what..." X thought. He then simply shook his head, and ran behind Shadow.

Shadow put his hand on the door, and energy started flowing through it. The door suddenly opened, revealing a brightened hallway.

"Your friend probally hasn't gotten those cameras down has he? Oh well. I think you know what to do?"

X nodded, and his whole body started shaking, faster and faster until he was moving so fast he couldn't be seen. Shadow also dissapeared, btu X knew he was still there, just in energy form.

X walked down the hallway, noticing that Nexus Tower seemend alot less noisy than normal. As he walked, he looked around. They were on a level of the tower he hadn't seen before. But he figure it would be easy to get to where they were supposed to go.

They walked a little more, until coming to a teleportation station. X walked onto it, Shadow appeared right next to him. If he was in energy form when he was teleported he might get disspearsed.

A flash of light, and they were standing on a more familair level of Nexus tower. X had managed to stay invisible through the teleportation, and Shadow had immediatley gone to energy form when they arrived, but strangely there was no one there.

X closed his eyes and used telepath to talk to Shadow.

"Since when is Nexus Tower desserted?"

"Good question." Shadow replied. "Hold on, I'll check that terminal."

X coudl sense Shadow moving, he went over to a Nexus Naomie terminal, which seemend to be offline. It suddenly started sparking, and the blue light appeared, but that was it.

"Hmm... Seems that the 'Faction Leaders' ordered most of the Nexus Force soldiers out of the tower. Except for a few left to help guard them."

The terminal powered down. And X sighed.

"Good, that makes our job easier."

He started walking again.

"So which Faction leader we go after first?"

"If you want my advice," Shadow said, "We go after Vanda Darkflame, that imposter has control of all the Paradox research. We should take care of her first."

As Shadow was talking, X saw it again. His eyes flashed a bright glowing red, but it wasn't just that, the way his eyes flashed, it reminded him of something, but he couldn't quite place what.

"I'll have to be careful, whatever Shadow is planning I have to be sure I don't fall for it."
