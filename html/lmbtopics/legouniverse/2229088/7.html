((Man, X really needs to learn to fly faster.))

X stopped in mid-air, hovering over Nexus City. Flames burned from all around as Agents, Soldiers, and strange purple monsters all fought it out. It was a three way fight for the city, and it was impossible to tell who was winning.

"Well, as if this day couldn't get any better. What am I supposed to do now?"

A smile crossed X's face, a smile he hadn't had since he transformed. His red hair stood straight up, and energy started crackling through it, as well as through his armor.

"Wait, what am I doing? Floating here above the city, trying to think of a plan?! Why bother?! There's a battle down there, and I'm going to it!"

X surronded himself with energy and blasted towards the city.

Two soldiers were backing a small group of civilains up against a wall, laughing. Their laughter was cut short by what sounded like energy crackling, they turned around, but didn't see anything.

"It's a shame they're not looking up."

X crashed into the ground, sending a wave of energy in all directions. The wave hit the soldiers, shocking them (Literally) and sending them flying. A patrol of soldiers down the street heard the crash, and turned to look. What they saw was an orb of energy, then nothing.

X rubbed his hand, stairing at the crater his energy orb had left.

"Didn't know I could do that. Pretty cool."

He felt something bounce of his armor, he turned to find one of the civilians holding a gun, his hands were shaking as he held it at arms length.

"Y...you get a..away f..from us m...monster!"

X sighed and raised his hands, which accomplished nothing but caused the man to fire another shot, which again, bounced off.

"Ok, you've managed to waste two bullets on me, way to go hero."

X teleported beside the man and grabbed the gun, he pulled it out of his hands, threw it into the air, and firied a beam. He watched as the gun exploded, then turned back to the man, who was backing up.

"Look, if I was going to destroy you I would have already, I'm here to help. But I can't do that with you shooting at me, so I figured you wouldn't need the gun anymore."

The man continued backing up, he grabbed a hammer that was layingo n the ground and held it in self-defense, the rest of the civilians huddled together. X again, sighed.

"Ok, I get it. It's hard to trust a guy with glowing red eyes who is wearing grey battle armor. Fine, I get it... But you do need to get out of the city, I can help if you trust.. .huh?!"

X turned in-time to get a scratch on his battle armor, one of the purple monsters decided he would make a nice victim.

"Wow, you scratched my armor. I'm impressed, even Layab couldn't do that. Let's fight."

The monster leaped at X, it's sharp claws extended. X teleported one inch infront of himself just as the monster struck, meaning the creature seemend to pass right through him. It whirrled, hissing.

"Youuuuuuu willllllll dieeeeeeeeeee!"

X leaped into the air and hovered as the creature leaped again.

"I will die huh? Well, I guess we all will sometime. But I'm going to let you in on a little secret."

X raised both his hands, and a huge orb of energy appeared in his hands. He hurled the orb at the monster, and in a blinding flash, the orb exploded.

"Your going before I am."

As the light faded, X smiled, the monster was crawling onto it's feet, it was hurt, but not badly. X was glad, he was anxious for a good fight.

The monster leaped at X, who raised his hand and blasted the creature into the street. But once again the creature go back up, and suddenly teleported next to X, and kicked him, sending him flying to the ground. It then raised both of it's hands, created a giant orb, and launched it at X, who barely had time to teleport out of the way. The monster hovered in mid-air, then raised it's hand and started firing energy beams.

"Why you little copy cat. " X said, this creature was using his moves. "Glad I didn't use anything REALLY powerful on it. We could all be in-trouble."

X surrounded himself in energy, and flew into the air, the monster did the same. X smiled, he might have a plan.

He flew, then suddenly stopped, and reversed directions, the monster did the same. It was then X realized something, the monster was faster than him.

"Well shoot....."

The monster quickly caught up to X, and collided with him in mid-air. Because they were both covered with energy, the result was an explosion of energy that, because they were both high in the sky, could be seen from around most of Nexus city. But it wasn't for the simple fact that there was alot of explosions happening.

Xmanaged to keep himself from falling, and he soon realized he wasn't hurt. He smiled, the same couldn't be said for his opponent. The monster was panting, it's energy had faded, and it barely had enough power to stay in the air.

"See, never try to duplicate my power! Goodbye, you were a good opponent!"

X raised his hand, and firied a huge blast of energy. When it faded, the monster was gone.

"Now, I need to find another one of those monsters! I haven't that much fun in a fight in forever!"

"Well, this city has gone to pieces..."

X hovered high above Nexus City, watching as below the soldiers were being beaten by the agents, but it was the monsters that were proving to be a concern. They were now hunting in packs, and a few had started using energy attacks. X didn't know what they were, but he did now they wanted to destroy everyone in this city.

"Yeah, like I'm going to let them do that."

X saw the others, fighting a group of monsters. X was about to go help, but then he felt a tug, a power he was sensing....

The next instant X was struck from behind, sending him tumbling down. He managed to turn himself as he fell, and saw a green glowing sphere heading towards him. And then he hit the ground.

The sphere hit X's landing spot asecond later, causing a huge explosion of energy. X watched as the blast devastated a whole city block.

"Wow, I'm finding teleportation to be extremely usefull, I don't think I could have survived that.."

X turned and saw a group of monsters heading towards him, well they were until he teleported. He was not in the mood for that kind of fight.

X had to catch his breath, his wind had been knocked out of him by whoever it was that had attacked him. As he knelt behind a half destroyed wall, he sensed something above him.

X rolled out of the way as a lazer obliberated his hiding spot, he tried to look up to see his attacker, but he only caught a glimpse before having to dodge again.

"None blind sides me like that!"

X closed his eyes, and teleported behind his attacker. As he prepared to take care of this new threat, he noticed something. The figure was wearing dark green armor, with a symbol of fire with a hazard side in the center. The figure was wielding a sniper rifle, which he had been shooting at X with, he also seemend to be carrying more weapons than most armies. X knew this figure.

"Nemisis?"
