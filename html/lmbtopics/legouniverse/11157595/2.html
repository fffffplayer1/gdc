More voices were heard and they didn't sound friendly. "Who's tha--" Rosette was cut off, Normm put his hand over her mouth. "I have a bad feeling about this." He breathed.

 

As if on cue, Paradox space marauders stormed the area. It couldn't be heard, but Shinobi were quietly circling the forest of stone, securing the area. They couldn't be heard by a soul but they could hear everyone else. A nasty looking Space marauder with scraped up armor walked into the ship, escorted by two paradox grunts. Normm and Rosette hid in back of the ship behind the giant door, keeping ever so quiet...

 

Normm felt like his breathing could be heard from a mile away. The leader of the band of Paradox ruffians(who were enjoying their work of scrapping the place a little too much) had walked into the cockpit and stuck a little chip or something into a slot, and then took it out a minute or two later. "Alright! Move out!" Their leader yelled. Normm was incredibly relieved because he knew he had probably just seen something he shouldn't have.

 

SQUEAK! Rosette made an awful loud sound, the nervousness getting to her. "Wait, what was that?" A space marauder jeered. Normm again heard his breathing as the loudest thing in the world, it could of popped his ears as far as he was concerned. "Probably nothin', Danger." calmly replied another soldier. Normm calmed down and whispered "Whew..." to himself.

 

"Alright... we're done here." The leader of the Paradox squad could be heard talking. "Now trash the place." he continued. "What? No! No no no no no! Oh boy!" thought Normm, scared with good reason. Suddenly bolts of plasma started hitting the hull of the already in bad condition ship, and something caught fire. There was going to be an explosion from the imagination tanks any second now, and Normm and Rosette weren't getting out without either being spotted or being smashed to pieces...

"Grab onto one of those extra Imagination tanks!" demanded Normm. "Okay! Okay!" responded Rosette, quickly. It crash-landed in an empty battlefield of Maelstrom on the edge of the planet chunk. A surprising distance from where they were. Normm hastened his pace as fast as he could and got to the crack, the Stromlings laid off chasing him. Normm sat Rosette against a rocky wall and tried to get her to wake up. It was too late... she had been smashed.

A long long time ago, a child was tempted, another was captured, and a woman mourned her loss. This, of course, all started with a man. A certain unnamed Baron, that is.

 

This Baron amassed an army of chaos under his secretive control. He whispered to the injured, the weak, and the lost. He looked into dreams, and brought to life nightmares. He infected the universe with mindless chaos, but his mind was the only one that could think of it's own volition in this maelstrom.

 

He needed more cerebral power if he was going to advance his plans.

 

Meanwhile, a dedicated Paradox researched the maelstrom that this Baron created, paying little to no attention to raising her children, leaving her husband in the Assembly faction to raise them. But one fateful day, two of her children vanished.

 

She hated herself for not paying enough attention to them. She decided to work even harder to make up for the loss she had experienced. Her entire family was affected. Her only son and daughter left decided to join the Nexus force, so they could fight their anger away. The family was torn apart, and the Paradox researcher fell into her desperate work-- thinking that somehow, someway, the Maelstrom had been involved in this horrible chapter of her life.

 

She decided to find some way to destroy the Maelstrom once and for all, to smash, conquer, tear, break, and do whatever she had to so that she could destroy it...

 

---

 

Bleu Brikk had been observing the happenings around him extremely closely. He had been writing his findings on a small notepad with an imaginite pencil.

"An Imagination tank approx. 600 - 750 studs away was spotted flying through the sky, landed approx. 30,000 - 50,000 studs away." Careful thought was put into these measurements. 600 studs could be travelled within an hour or so, but 30,000 studs away was days. There were questions to be asked. What was an Imagination tank doing that far away? And were those minifigures attached to it? "w/ Minifigures" he wrote, vaguley sure.

"I have to get out of here." Normm said, blocking out his distressing thoughts. He looked up from where moments ago a breathing, living minifigure sat against the wall. In the pass between rocks a large valley full of odd skeletons were to his right, and further down on the right of the slab wall was a large volcanic underground with floating bits of rock splattered everywhere. The pass ended leading to a familiar sight, a small figure Normm could only just barely spot: A Sentinel crest.

 

Normm ran as fast as his little plastic legs could take him, this was his chance to run away from all the fear and terror of Crux Prime. He ran right down the pass but was stopped by a Sentinel with a machine-gun like gun in one hand and a sword in the other, both were various shades of blue, and he was wearing a chestplate.

 

"Hey kid! You need to talk? I just saw your friend over their get smashed... tough stuff, kid. Where'd you come from?"

"The Venture Fever. It was attacked, and we escaped. We weren't very fortunate landing, and i'll have you know a lot of us recruits-- and some Sentinels-- are way out there in unexplored areas of this planet chunk!

 

"I'll send a dropship or two out there to go looking for them. Looks like you just had quite a studburst there." the Sentinel said to Normm. "Studburst?" Normm asked. "It's slang for shock related to a smashing." the Sentinel replied.

"Well, i'm not in shock. Now just get me to where I'm supposed to go: Nimbus Station." Normm demanded.

"Alright, then..." replied the Sentinel.

"Just go over to the launchpad! It leads to Nimbus Station." the Sentinel said over his shoulder. "Seeya!" he continued, walking forth into the pass.

 

Normm stayed quiet and ran over to the pad to put his Rocket on it. He did so without a second glance, and flew off to Nimbus station to join a faction.

It was all a part of being in the nexus force.

Normm tackled the leader of the pack, the one who pushed him, and a dog-fight ensued. Normm let out all of his anger on a total of 4 of the troublemaker's followers who were trying to fight him off.

It turned into something more depressed, laid back, and out of tune with the world around him.

 

"What's the matter kid, you got Studburst or something? Kid? Hello?" the Grunt wondered. Normm was sitting idly with tissues in his nose and bandages on his head and knuckles. It was going to be a rough day until he could tune out his anger issues.

A relieving sound was heard as Normm, Bleu, Al, and Rosette were rebuilt.

"That was so cool!" shouted Normm.

"What was that thing?!" Bleu shouted in unison with Rosette.

"It was the Spider Boss! We smashed the Spider Boss!" Al cheered.

"No, it wasn't the Spider Boss." Bleu retorted. "How could we have defeated it? We're not even rank one." continued Rosette.

 

Normm, Bleu, Al, and Rosette reported to Beck Strongheart and received Sentinel approval. In fact, Beck gave them a reward for surviving as long as they did! They all cheered and scampered off to Nimbus Station so they could join a faction. Normm, Rosette, and Bleu had already gained the Assembly and Venture leauge approval, but decided to wait for Al to catch up. "What's taking him so long? I don't intend to wait any longer!" Bleu snorted. Bleu ran up to Nexus Jay and was assigned his faction.

 

Normm and Rosette's head were turned right around to Bleu, annoyed.

"Remind me to have a word with him later. Now it's just you and me, Normm-- Normm?" said Rosette as she looked back at where Normm was sitting. He had followed Bleu's footsteps and got a head start. "Wait! Hey-- that's... ugh." Rosette went to catch up with Al in Race Place and tell him the bad news.

 

"They did what?! Those...! Ugh!" Al scoffed. It was a really uncaring thing to do as a friend, so naturally Rosette and Al had reason to be angry. What was worse is Normm came to show off his Samurai gear.

"Why did you do that?!" shouted Rosette at Normm, Al sadly slumping away to gain Assembly's approval.

"Um... Faction...?" Normm said in a very clueless way that made his IQ seem to be a whopping one.

Rosette whapped the back of Normm's head and stormed off. "Hey!" Normm yelled in frustration.

 

Rosette teamed up with Al to help him gain his approval, leaving Normm in the dust. Bleu seemed to disappear as well and Normm was all alone. "I guess I was kind of a jerk..." Normm said to himself.
