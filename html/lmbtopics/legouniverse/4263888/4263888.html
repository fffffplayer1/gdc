Epic Heroes

1

A one-in-a million Nexus Force Recruit Got Into a one-in-a million sleeping pod, and what are the odds, that that pod was cracked, and that that recruit was... Not tired. MaceMetalVortex Got in, the pod, and a bully the size of a mech cracked his BFF's pod, SlyTripleCobra, and chuckled as he walked away. "Aww, SLY!! HugetheHammer just cracked you pod!!" Mace" Mace whined. "Mace, it's NO BIG DEAL!! I'll get through!" he grumbled. "Sly, it's a three year Journey! You CAN'T Just GET through!" Mace argued. "I'll Deal wi-" "No, Sly, You switch with me, RIGHT N-" SlyTriple cobra ended the conversation  by closing the hatch and activating the sleeping gas. Sly  hopped  in his pod. This was going to be a long three years...

 

CRASH! SLAM! SMASH! BOOM! Mace woke up to a Shaking Ship, Only A few minifigs woke up and, they, acting in panic, just ran around screaming. Mace dragged sly out of his pod. "WAKE UP!" Mace gritted through his teeth. Sly came-to and yawned, then jumped up and ran. "Follow me, Mace!" He followed, confused.Glancing out the window, he saw the malstrom was eating up the Venture Long (The ships Name) And Spitting out malstrom dragons. An poor minifig fell off the ship, grabbed a pipe and dropped his backpack full of gear, and an extremley large dragon came out.  Mace looked at the dragons name. "Butterscorch, I'd hate to meet him!"

 

Hope you readers like it!

 

 

 

 

Josi

" Sly's Pod was bashed as it fell into doom.  Sly ran towards trip, Grabbed his hand and leaped across the gap and barely grabbing the edge of the remaining ship. Mace Hurriedly pulled him up. Epic Heroes: Book 1: The Awkward Squad

 

"Uh, Hi-"  Mace's head flew across the room, and his torso fell off his pants, smashed. "HAH! No one cane survive my fist!!" Fist yelled. Mace's torso slid under Fist's legs and his pants leaped  over him as they landed on top of each other, as the torso picked his face up a placed it back on top, all in a cloud of Imagination. Mace ducked another punch and, tripped over one of Fist's goons. Fist, a dagger in hand, raised his hand  for the final blow. A blade grew out of his stomach. Fist toppled over to reveal a decked-out sly, holding A sword, forged out of a fallen star. "Here, Mace. You need this!" He said as he offered the sword to our hero. Mace, still reluctant, looked at him. "What will you use?" "The twin blade!" He said as pulled out an identical sword. "But this one, Mace, is  forge out of a nova!"

Trip looked up, finding a sword in his hand. "Looks like we got triplet blades!" They looked sheathed their weapons and continued to look for survivors. 

 

The mystery man followed the brave three. He had stalked them long enough. It was time to introduce himself. He pulled out a golden sword. This was going to be fun.
