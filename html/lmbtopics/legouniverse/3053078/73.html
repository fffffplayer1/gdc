Alien Vengeance

Chapter 6 Part 1

The Battle

 

Qartal, Kraken and Helena left the beach, their teams following them. Kraken was a Rank Three Space Marauder and Helena, a Daredevil.

“Hello, Qartal. Haven’t seen you since the first battle.” Helena said while they walked.

“Oh, hello again.” Qartal replied.

“Yo, Qartal, nice job on getting second in command.” Kraken said.

“Thanks but you should have got it, you’re a higher rank.”

“Ranks are nothing compared to skill.” Helena said. “You were probably picked because you pretty well in the last battle.”

“Yes.” Agreed Kraken. “I was mainly helping in the command room at Nexus Tower during that battle. Hey, you, give me that.”

 Kraken called to one of his soldiers who passed him a Wormholer.

“Right, I’m setting up my team. See you two later.”

A minute later Helena left too.

“Good luck.” She said, putting on her helmet over her black hair.

Qartal nodded and led his team of thirty soldiers to the edge of the Ravine.

“This is where we’re going to form a defensive line. We’re not going to let any rebels past.” He told his soldiers. Qartal formed three rows, with ten soldiers in each one. The rows were one behind the other. Qartal had put those with ranged weapons in the back two rows and those with close range weapons in the front row. Qartal himself stood in the middle of the second row, bow ready.

Chapter 6

Part 2

 

Around seven helicopters passed over them and into the distance.

“Looks like they’re heading for the Maelstrom Trench. Shall we go after them sir?” Asked an Assembly Summoner.

“Nah. Captain Cecilia can deal with it.”

“Nexus Dropship!” Someone called.

“What’s that doing here? We’re not expecting any backup.” Qartal muttered.

The dropship landed and several minifigures came out, firing guns. Three of Qartal’s team fell but the rest reacted fast, firing back at the rebels. Qartal fired two arrows, both of them hitting their targets. Two rebels remained and they ran back to the dropship.

“Don't let them get back to the dropship!” Qartal shouted.

His team charged, chasing after the rebels. They had almost reached the dropship when there was a deafening explosion and Qartal felt himself being hurled backwards. He smacked his head against a tree and his vision blurred for several seconds. In those seconds, a rebel charged at him, sword in hand. Qartal grabbed one of his daggers, unsheathed it and swung wildly. He missed because of his bad vision and the rebel raised his weapon looking triumphant. Two gunshots sounded and the rebel dropped to the ground.

A  Rank Two Adventurer stood a short distance away, a rifle in his hands, whip at his side.

“Need help, sir?” He asked.

“Thank you.” Qartal rubbed his head and sheathed his dagger.

His entire team were smashed apart from him and the Adventurer and the dropship had departed.

“Seems like we ran into a load of mines.” Qartal said. “Let’s get over to the Maelstrom Trench and help out there.”

Chapter 6

Part 3

 

The two headed over the bridge as more helicopters flew over them. Qartal pulled out his bow and readied an arrow. They reached the Maelstrom Trench to find a full scale battle taking place. Two rebels ran towards them. Qartal fired the arrow and the Adventurer fired two shots. The Adventurer then ran off and engaged enemies in close combat. Qartal fired more arrows and hit more rebels. Eventually he ran out of arrows and he ditched his bow and quiver. Drawing his sword and a dagger, he charged at a group of rebels. Two swung with knives, Qartal parried both strikes and drove his blades forward. Another rebel lunged with his rifle. Qartal dropped his weapons, grabbed the rifle and twisted so the rebel let go and fell to the floor. Qartal shot the rebel and spun round and shot another who was about to shoot him. Qartal snatched up his dagger, threw it in the air and caught it by the blade. He then hurled it  at another rebel. Qartal spun round, blocked a punch, grabbed a sword from the rebel's other hand and slashed forward. Stopping to catch his breath, he surveyed the battlefield.  Captain Cecilia’s team had dealt with most of the rebels in this area. The remaining ones were running towards Brig Rock. Helena saw him and came over.

“Reckon we should follow them?” She asked.

“Yeah. Kraken’s team might need a hand.”

They headed after them, soldiers following behind them. Oddly, Kraken wasn’t there. Just ten or so rebels and Nexus soldiers battling. Then they heard several explosions from the beach and ran there to see what was going on. Kraken, Captain Jack Knife and several pirates and Nexus Force soldiers were scattered all over the beach. There were three craters in the sand made by canon balls.

“What’s going on?” Qartal asked Kraken.

Alien Vengeance

Chapter 7 Part 1

The Tyranny of Vexvol

 

Qartal, Kraken and three Assembly grunts flew over the sea in a dropship. They were rapidly approaching Captain Jack Knife’s ship.

“We’re going to drop on to that ship.” Kraken explained to the team. “The pilot will fly us over and we will jump. Make sure you hit the ship’s deck and don’t fall in the sea.”

Qartal opened the dropship’s side door as they approached. In less than ten seconds they would have to jump.

“Five seconds...” Called Kraken. “And ju-” He stopped. “Actually don’t jump! Look out!”

Three helicopters came out of nowhere, firing at the dropship.

“Close the door! Close the door!” Kraken yelled.

Qartal pulled it shut as the pilot did a barrel roll to avoid bullets coming from the helicopters.

“We’ve flown right into an ambush.” Qartal groaned, getting up from the floor.

“Yes and we need to take out those helicopters.” Kraken replied. “You got anything we could use here?” He asked the pilot.

“Crate in the back, contains rifles and some explosives.” The pilot swerved to avoid more gunfire.

One of the grunts grabbed the crate and pulled it open to reveal three rifles, and a rocket launcher with two missiles. The pilot swerved another time as Qartal grabbed the rocket launcher. Kraken and an Assembly grunt pulled open the door as Qartal finished loading the first missile.

“Let’s hope I can aim this properly.” Qartal muttered.

He fired. He just had time to see his missile hit its target before the dropship spun upside down again.
