Chapter 30
Xeryon’s Memory 3
5th October 2011
8:47pm
Nexus Tower

I parried a soldier’s attack and thrust my sword forward, smashing him.
“Vorox, how is the bomb coming along?” I yelled over the sounds of the battle.
“Almost done. I just need a few more minutes.” Vorox yelled back.
I engaged a couple more soldiers in a sword fight. I saw Terraz nearby. He dived forward and tackled a Sentinel. He aimed his assault rifle at the downed soldier and pulled the trigger. More soldiers ran at us.
I signalled with my hand and four of my assassins armed with bows fired simultaneously. Most of the soldiers fell and the remaining began a fight with the archers.
I stabbed a soldier and quickly flipped round to block a strike from another. I knocked his next attack sideways and stabbed and him now that he was undefended.
“Job done, the bomb is primed.” Vorox shouted.
He picked up the bomb and ran off down a corridor. He returned a moment later. He ran to our escape helicopter.
“Alright. Let’s move! We’re leaving.” I shouted to my assassins.
I had arrived with ten, excluding Vorox and Terraz. There was eight left. The remaining Nexus Force soldiers had been defeated and my team ran for the helicopter. Terraz and I were about to follow when I heard a shout.
“Going somewhere?”
I turned to find a black cloaked minifigure. He held and katana and his free hand was glowing purple: Maelstrom. This had to be Elite, the Darkitect’s son. Terraz had told me about him. He had a Paradox grunt bedside him.
Elite hurled himself at me. I just had time to see the Paradox soldier engage Terraz in combat before I found myself thrown to the floor. Elite swung his blade downwards and I barely had time to block. I rolled and got up. For the next few minutes we repeatedly slashed and blocked. Until Elite pulled back and slammed his fist into my face, his hand still glowing with Maelstrom. It felt like I had been hit with a rock. I had time to dodge before he attacked again.
Out the corner of my eye, I saw the Paradox soldier shoot Terraz. My friend collapsed, dead. Then the bomb went off and I ran for the helicopter.

Book Two: The Four Kriken
Chapter 31
29th December 2028
6:36am
Krikeya

"We're out of food and water." Xeryon announced.
"We'll have to find that temple by tomorrow." Dextron added.
Everyone set about gathering their weapons and a few remaining supplies. Five minutes later they set off.

7:19am
Krikeya

A little while later they found themselves in a forest of some kind. The trees had orange leaves.
"How much longer?" Tarip complained.
"We've only been walking for about forty minutes." Arran muttered.
Something snapped behind them.
"What's that?"
A Kriken stood behind them.
"What do you want?" Yakow asked it.
"Your life." It replied, then dived at him.
Yakow fell to the ground. The Kriken drew a blade and raised it to stab him. However, Exceedra grabbed the Kriken and threw it off. Dextron shot it.
"Come on let's move. That shot probably alerted any more nearby."
The group hurried on. After about two hours they reached the end of the forest. Some Kriken were waiting for them.
