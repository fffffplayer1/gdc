Book Two: The Four Kriken
Chapter 27
29th December 2028
2:36am
Krikeya

“I wonder... what under that.” Zedez said.
“Let’s take a look.” Yakow replied.
They both dived into the lake of Kriken goo. Yakow was immune to it and Zedez’s armour protected him.
Under the surface was just like normal water.
“You can breathe under here?” Asked Yakow. Like most Kriken, being immune to the goo also let him talk in it.
“Yes. Armour gives me ability. Also seems I can... talk too.”
“Let’s keep going down.”
They swam deeper. Eventually Zedez saw a shadow. It moved and they followed it. A couple of minutes later, they lost it.
“I’m sure it went into that cave.” Yakow said.
Zedez quickly used his armour’s visor to scan the area.
“There’s heat traces.” He stopped for a moment. “Under sand.”
Yakow slowly swam forward and poked the sand with his rifle. There was a movement underneath. The sand suddenly flew upwards and a massive orange tail emerged. Zedez got out the combat knife he’d taken from a Bio-Armour 2 owner and Yakow readied his rifle. An orange snake, about fifteen metres long swam out of the sand.
“Another of our leaders.” Yakow said.
The snake lunged for them.


Sorry this is short. The battle will follow in the next chapter which will be up in a couple of hours.

Book Two: The Four Kriken
Chapter 28
29th December 2028
2:40am
Krikeya

Zedez swam aside, barely avoiding the sea serpent’s jaws. He slashed with the knife, the blade shattered on impact with the snake’s skin. The snake swung its tail round and knocked Zedez backwards. He noticed its tail had three large spikes on the end. Yakow fired several shots, each with no effect on the snake. Zedez quickly recovered and aimed his assault rifle. He pulled the trigger.
Nothing happened; the water had jammed his gun.
He threw it down and attempted a punch. The snake dodged out of reach. Zedez was no weapon less apart from a pistol. But he wasn’t going to use that in case the water had affected it as well. He turned invisible and swam behind the snake. Yakow fired more shots. Zedez jumped on the snake’s back and grabbed it round the neck. It threw him off and came over to him. It opened its mouth to bite him.
Zedez looked into the snake’s mouth. The back of its mouth was purple. Zedez pulled out his pistol and, praying that it would work, fired several shots into the snake’s mouth. The snake screeched and fell back.
“Yakow. Grab tail.” Zedez yelled.
“Why?”
“Just... do it.”
Yakow swam forwards and grabbed its tail. He began twisting it round. Zedez swam over and helped. They pulled it to the snake’s face. Zedez activated strength mode on his armour and kicked the snake’s jaw. Its mouth open to scream and Yakow stuck the snake’s tail in it.
The snake screamed as the spikes on its tail hit its weak spot. Then, just like the T-Rex and the Scorpion, it exploded into green and orange goo.
Another leader down.
