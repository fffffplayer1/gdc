Book Two: The Four Kriken

Chapter 11

23rd December 2028

6:00 am

Nexus Tower



The Tower was totally different. It was now red with large banners hanging from the roof. Each banner had a big red ‘K’ on it. Exceedra and Yakow silently approached the door. There were two Nexus Force soldiers standing guard.

“This is stupid.” Said one guard. “Why is Duke making us serve these aliens?”

“No idea. But I think someone should smash him.” The second one said, getting out a can of soda.

“Seriously?”

“Yes.”

“I guess. He obviously doesn’t want many friends.”

The two guards walked out to the launch pad and looked over the railings and down at the city. Exceedra and Yakow used this moment to walk into the Tower and duck behind a crate placed in the main corridor.

“Alright.” Whispered Exceedra. “We need to get to Duke’s office. Only smash traitors. Anybody who is still loyal to our universe: spare them.”

Yakow nodded. He was holding his pickaxe and had his bag of tools slung over his shoulder.

The two run forward and ducked behind another crate. There wasn’t may guards around. Next up they had to go past the Sentinel security checkpoint. They walked through one of the gateways, immediately setting off an alarm.

“Run!”

They charged forward towards Duke’s office. Quickly, they opened the door and went in, shutting it behind them.

“To what do I owe the honour?” Asked a voice behind them.

They turned to find Duke Exeter and several guards.

Book Two: The Four Kriken
Chapter 12
23rd December 2028
6:03 am
Duke Exeter’s Office

“Honour is the only thing you won’t be getting, Kriken clone.” Exceedra said.
"So, you worked out that I'm not the real Duke."
Duke's clone had no emotion in his voice.
"Yakow here told me he'd heard about Duke doing weird things. I knew my faction leader would never execute his cleaners or make up some stupid rules. I had also found documents belonging to the Nexus Force. They were torn apart but I managed to work out what they were explaining. Clones."
"Well done for working that out. Now you can take that with you to the grave. Destroy them."
The four Sentinel grunts standing with Duke raised their rifles, ready to shoot, when one of them yelled out in pain. Yakow had pulled a wrench out of his tool bag and had hurled it at one of them. He was wearing no helmet and was knocked out instantly. While, the other three were distracted, Exceedra sprang into action. He swung his Long Jouster in a wide arc, taking out the two nearest soldiers. He then fired at the remaining one.
Clone Duke cursed before vaulting the desk and running out of the office. Exceedra and Yakow gave chase.
"Security!" Screamed Clone Duke.
Security was not what they expected; Zedez appeared from nowhere and walked over to them.
"Zedez." Said Exceedra. Elite had told him about Zedez.
Then, behind Zedez, five more appeared. Each one held an assault rifle with a pistol and combat knife attached the the metal holsters on their legs.
"More clones." Yakow muttered.
"Not clones, Kriken. We're elite Nexus Force soldiers. Each of us equipped with the Bio-Armour 2." The leader replied.
"Bio-Armour 2?"
"An improvement to the Bio-Armour 1. There's only fifty of these around, constructed for us by Dr. Imfen. Allow me to show you one of the improvements."
And with that, he slashed forward with his combat knife. That was a loud clang of metal against metal as Exceedra blocked the strike that was inches away from hitting Yakow. The other five opened fire. Exceedra and Yakow dived behind a wall.
"We need a way out of this!"
"That you do."
One of the soldiers had come up behind them, invisible. Yakow struck with his pickaxe. The axe slammed into the soldier's armour and snapped in half.
"Yakow, it's no use. Run."
The two of them ran, bullets smashing into the floor behind them. They charged through the checkpoint and to the launch area. They found what they were looking for.
