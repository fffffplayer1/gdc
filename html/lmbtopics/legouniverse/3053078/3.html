Book One: The Virus Spreads
Chapter 5
23rd November 2028
3:12 pm
The Skies

‘Osllo! What on Crux do you think you’re doing?’ asked Arran.
‘Dancing.’
‘Why?’
‘Because dancing on a plane is epical!’
‘Rank Ones.’ Muttered Arran. ‘Always thinking they’re cool.’

They had been in the air for less than two minutes when Osllo had started acting stupid like he always did. Arran went into the pilot’s cockpit.
‘How long ‘til we’re down?’
‘I’d say about five minutes.’ Replied the pilot.
‘Look! A rare silver eagle.’
‘Where?’
The pilot left the controls for a moment and the plane went falling out of the sky.
‘AHH!’ screamed someone from the plane’s back area.
The pilot quickly grabbed the controls again and brought the plane back up.
‘Never, ever, ever, ever, ever, ever, ever, ever-’
‘I get the point.’
‘Yeah, just don’t do that again.’
‘OK.’
‘Ever.’
‘Got it.’
‘Ever.’
‘Alright!’
‘Ever.’
‘SHUT UP!’
‘I’m just saying: never ever, ever, ever, ever, ever,-’
‘Oh for the sake of your life, shut up!’ Arran yelled, pulling out an automatic pistol.
Arran returned to the back of the plane to join his team.
‘What happened just then?’ asked Kandem. ‘We were trying to play chess, but then all the pieces went flying off.’
‘Oh, it was just the pilot trying to be a joker.’
‘I HEARD THAT, ARRAN!’ yelled the pilot.
‘Seriously?’ asked Arran. ‘You heard that?’
‘Yes!’
‘How, may I ask, over the sound of then engines did you hear my speaking?’
‘Look, guys. You mind shutting up?’ asked the co-pilot over an intercom. ‘We’re coming over the drop zone. Get you parachutes ready. You’ll need to jump in two minutes.’

The six Nexus Force troops headed over to the plane door.
‘Are we gonna survive this?’ asked Kandem.
‘You probably won’t.’ Tarip had come over. ‘Look at your pathetic Rank One armour. My and Arran will survive with our Rank Three gear but you Ranks Ones and Twos have no chance at all.’
‘OK, guys you’re clear to jump.’
‘I’M GOING FIRST.’ Yelled Tarip.
‘You sure?’ asked Arran. ‘I thought you were scared of dangerous things.
‘Are you kidding me? I’m a Daredevil. Doing dangerous things is my JOOOOOBBBBBBB!!!!!’ he yelled diving out of the plane.
‘You forgot your parachute, you idiot!’ yelled Osllo.
‘C’mon. Let’s go.’ Said Arran, strapping on his parachute and jumping.
Kandem, Osllo and the other two shrugged and jumped after their leader.

Book One: The Virus Spreads
Chapter 5
23rd November 2028
3:18 pm
Nexus City

Tarip was still falling. Arran couldn’t believe that he’d actually forgotten his parachute. There had to be a way to save him from death. Arran quickly formed a plan in his mind.
‘Hey, Quan! Throw me that rocket launcher I gave you in the plane.’ He yelled at a Rank Two Shinobi.
‘Sure!’ Quan called back. He pulled a rocket launcher off his back and hurled it down to Arran.
Arran reached out and grabbed it from the sky as it fell past him. As he caught it, it dragged his arm downwards. Just what he wanted. The RPG’s weight pulled him down through the sky faster. A was rapidly falling towards Tarip. After about ten seconds of free fall he reached Tarip. He deployed his parachute and grabbed Tarip’s arm. Behind him the other four deployed their parachutes.

A couple of minutes later Tech Squad landed in a field.
‘Thanks for your help, Corporal.’ Arran said to Quan. ‘I’ll make sure you have a promotion when this is over.’
‘Yeah, I agree.’ Said Tarip.
Quan nodded his thanks. The team threw off their ’chutes and regrouped.
‘What now?’ asked Tarip.
‘Well we need to think. If your submarine crashed and you managed to get out, where would you go?’
‘Pizza Hut.’ Suggested Osllo.
‘Um, no.’
‘Well, I’d go and search for supplies.’ Said Quan.
‘Exactly. They’ve probably gone to the massive shopping mall in the centre of the southern area.’
‘Brilliant. Let’s go.’ Said Corporal Fern, a Space Ranger.
Tech Squad began moving across the field. There were traces of orange and green goo in a few places.
‘I wonder what this is.’ Said Osllo, reaching out to pick some up.
‘DON’T TOUCH IT!’ yelled Tarip, grabbing his Osllo’s hand and pulling it away from the goo. ‘I think I know what that is. If I’m right, then we could be doomed.’
‘Tarip? What do you think it is?’ asked Arran.
‘Well, I’m not sure if you’ll believe me, but I think it’s alien goo.’
Osllo and Kandem burst out laughing.
‘No seriously.’
‘He could actually be right.’ Noted Fern. ‘I’ve studied space. There was always an unidentified planet about three thousand miles away from our universe. I don’t know what it’s called, but it’s home to the Kriken Empire.’
‘Kriken Empire?’ asked Quan in disbelief.
‘A race of beings named The Kriken. There are stories about them travelling to other planets and infecting the planet’s inhabitants with a type of goo.’
The whole squad had been listening to this in awe.
Tarip continued the explanation.
‘The Kriken somehow transfer the goo to another being. After three hours, if you’ve not been treated the goo gives you a virus. This virus isn’t curable. If you get it you might as well accept that you’re never going to be yourself again. The virus lasts for a week or so and at the worst part of it you’ll become a Kriken.’
‘So,’ Realized Arran. ‘If the Kriken are in our universe...’
‘It can only mean that they’ve come to infect us and take over.’ Finished Kandem.
Arran cursed. This was unbelievable.
‘C’mon. Enough stories for now. Let’s do what we came here to do.’
Tech Squad set off through the field once again.

A little while later they reached the city.
‘At last.’ Muttered Quan.
‘Into that house now!’ said Arran.
The team dived through a door and threw themselves to the floor. Two rebels walked past the door. Arran pulled out his gun.
‘Anyone got a silencer?’ He asked in hushed tones.
‘I have.’ Said Tarip. ‘But it’s for a sniper rifle.’ He tapped a rifle that was strapped to his back.
‘Give me it anyway. I’ll see if it fits.’
Tarip passed him the gun silencer. Arran attached it to the pistol’s barrel.
‘It fits.’
‘Good.’
Arran got up and slowly walked to the door. He looked round and pulled the trigger. The rebels collapsed at the bullets hit them.
Arran came back in.
‘Nice one, Captain.’ Said Osllo.
‘Thanks.’
‘Outside!’ said Kandem in an alarmed voice. Arran looked round. There was a helicopter. The pilot had seen them. Arran quickly slammed the front door shut.
‘Everyone get to cover.’ He said.
Kandem and Osllo dived under a table. Tarip, Fern and Arran ducked behind a sofa. There was a burst of gun fire and the window shattered.
‘Where’s the RPG?’ asked Arran.
‘Quan had it last.’
‘But where’s Quan?’
They heard a flushing sound and Quan came running down the stairs.
‘Quan? Where have you been?’
‘Sorry, sir. I was using the bathroom.’
‘Give me the rocket launcher quickly!’
Quan passed it to him.
Arran smiled at the sight of a heavy weapon. ‘Let’s take this piece of brick out of the sky.’

Book One: The Virus Spreads
Chapter 6
23rd November 2028
3:49 pm
Nexus City

Arran fired. The missile blasted through the window and hit the side of the helicopter. The helicopter was ripped apart into four pieces. The pieces were hurled away in different directions.
‘YEAH!’ yelled Kandem. ‘TAKE THAT YOU FOOLS!’
‘They didn’t even see that coming.’ Quan celebrated.
Just as they were starting to relax, Corporal Fern ran over.
‘They’re coming from round the back.’ He said urgently.
‘They’ll get no further.’ Said Tarip, pulling out his flare guns.
‘Tech Squad! Defend this building!’ shouted Arran.
‘Yes, Captain.’ Osllo saluted. He may act stupid a lot of the time, but Osllo was always serious in battles.
The first rebel ran inside- only to be shot down by Tarip. Three more ran in, two carrying knives and one holding a machine gun. Arran dealt with the gunner as Kandem and Osllo engaged the others in a sword fight. Rebels continued to come in. After a few minutes they started using the front door too.
‘We can’t hold out forever!’ shouted Quan, dodging a knife and thrusting his katanas forward.
‘You’re right! But what can we do?’ Replied Arran, slamming his wrench down on a rebel’s head.
Tarip flipped round, shot a rebel, ducked a blade and kicked upwards. He turned again and fired point blank at a rebel who was standing next to him.
‘Any way we can get back up?’ He asked.
‘Fern had the radio transmitter.’ Said Quan. ‘Fern get over here!’
Fern ran forwards. On the way towards them he shot a rebel and hurled a grenade out the window.
‘Yeah? What is it?’
‘The radio transmitter. You got it?’
‘Aw, man! I left it on the plane.’
Quan cursed.
‘Well that’s not good.’ Said Arran. ‘But we have to keep holding out. Osllo and Kandem need help.
He wasn’t kidding. Osllo’s sword had been broken apart and he was running around dodging knifes and bullets. Kandem had picked up a gun and was behind cover, trying to find a moment to look round and shoot.
Fern, Tarip and Arran readied their guns. Quan gathered up a few shurikens.
‘Now.’
Quan threw the shurikens as the others opened fire. The rebels were killed instantly.
‘Nice work, team.’ Arran commented, kicking a body with his foot.
Their victory didn’t last long though. A window upstairs smashed.
‘I’ll check that.’ Said Kandem, running up the staircase.
There was a yell and Kandem was thrown down the stairs.
‘WHAT THE NEXUS?!’ shouted Fern.
A strange creature came down the stairs. Its whole body was bright red and tough looking. One hand was a giant crab claw. Three blue eyes were positioned in the middle of its face. It had no mouth.
‘It’s...’
‘A KRIKEN!’ yelled Fern.
The Kriken leaned down over Kandem’s downed body.
‘No!’ It was Osllo. He charged forward and kicked the Kriken in the face.
The Kriken looked at him. Then it picked him up and threw him into Quan. Fern and Tarip opened fire, aiming for the Kriken’s head. The shots had to effect what so ever. It punched Fern with incredible strength, sending him to the floor. It turned round and swung his foot, sweeping Tarip’s feet away from him. It turned on Arran next.
Arran raised his gun, only to have it knocked out of his hands. The Kriken threw him to the floor. It stood over him with its claw in his face.
‘Go on. Kill me.’
The Kriken looked at him. It raised its claw. Arran closed his eyes. This was the end. The claw came down. There was an inhuman scream. Arran opened his eyes. The Kriken was surrounded by purple energy. Not just any purple energy. It was Maelstrom. Something he hadn’t seen since he was a kid. The Maelstrom seemed to be draining the Kriken of its strength. The Kriken screamed.
‘Now, Xeryon!’ yelled a voice. There was a gunshot and the Kriken collapsed.
Arran looked up. Two people stood in front of the dead Kriken. One wore a black hooded cloak and a sword in a sheath on his back. Maelstrom energy curled in his hands. Arran recognised him as Elite. The Darkitect’s only son. Sixteen years ago Elite had defeated his father and destroyed the Maelstrom entirely.
‘Elite.’ Said Arran.
‘Yes. It’s me. Ever thought you’d meet the one who destroyed the Maelstrom?’
‘I doubt he ever did.’ Said the second person.
He wore a white cloak and body armour. He held a rifle and had a sword in a sheath at his side.
‘Xeryon? But aren’t you an enemy of the Nexus Force.’
‘I was.’ Replied Xeryon. ‘But I’ve changed. Noticed my helmet?’
Arran looked up. Xeryon was wearing a Rank Three Sentinel Samurai helmet.
‘Wow. I can’t believe I’m actually meeting you two.’
‘Look, I know you want to talk. But now is NOT the time.’ Said Elite.
‘He’s right. That crashed helicopter will attract a big crowd.’ Xeryon told him.
‘C’mon let’s get out of here. Wake up your squad.’ Said Elite who had noticed the downed soldiers.

A couple of minutes later everyone was ready. They gathered up all the supplies they could find and walked out into the street.
