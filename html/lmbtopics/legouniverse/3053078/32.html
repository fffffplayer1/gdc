Book Two: The Four Kriken
Chapter 14
23rd December 2028
6:16 am

The Space Ranger stepped forward, bringing out his gun. He aimed and fired several shots at Duke’s clone. Clone Duke was hit by all o the shots but one. He fell back. Everyone was silent for a few seconds, and then Clone Duke jumped back up and launched himself at the real Duke. The Kriken followed his lead. The group of Sentinels began fighting the Kriken with the other Nexus Force troops caught in the middle. Exceedra and Yakow tried to make their way over the real Duke to assist him. However, it seemed the Kriken didn’t want them to. They would have to fight their way to him.

6:25 am
The Rooftops
Xeryon had seen what was going on. He had been watching TV when the news flash came on, interrupting what he was watching. The streets had been closed off and there were Kriken stopping anyone from entering the area. But they had made one mistake. They’d forgotten to keep a watch on the roofs.
Xeryon ran forward and hurled himself into the air. At the last moment he reached out, grabbing the roof’s edge of the building opposite. He pulled himself up and continued forward. A couple of minutes later he arrived at the scene of battle.

6:26 am
Nexus City

The Kriken sweeped its claw downwards, taking Arran off his feet. The Kriken picked up a discarded plasma rifle and aimed at him. Its clawed tightened on the trigger. Xeryon fell from a nearby roof and landed on the Kriken.
“Xeryon.” Arran said, getting up. “You’re never far away when there’s a fight are you?”
“I’m not missing a fight like this. Two Duke Exeters battling.” Xeryon replied, unsheathing his sword.
“Hey, Xeryon nice drop!”
“Dextron, how’d you get here?”
“I know stealth too. I am a spy after all.”
Tarip came over and noticed the two new arrivals.
“Great! Backup! Let’s smash some aliens.”
“Oh yeah, Xeryon. See if you can figure out what this thing does. All it seems o do to me is give me weird dreams.” He held out the crystal he’d picked up in Imfen’s office.
“Alright.” Xeryon took it.
A couple of minutes later, while fighting Kriken, Xeryon began to feel tired. He was still holding the crystal which was glowing. A couple of seconds later he collapsed. The crystal was still glowing.

For this chapter I've taken the fight scene in the first chapter of "Xeryon- Warrior or Assassin?" and improved it a little.

Book Two: The Four Kriken
Chapter 15
Xeryon’s Memory 1
29th December 2012
8:37 am

I was almost at the end of my first year in prison, just another nineteen to go after this one. I was in the prison cafeteria queuing up for breakfast when I heard the shout.
“Navarro!”
I turned and saw someone standing there. Somebody I did not wish to share a prison with for the next nineteen years. Standing a few places back in the queue was my old enemy, Vor, a big man with a small amount of knowledge. I remembered back to last night when some new people had arrived. Vor must have been one of them.
“What do you want, Vor?”
“I want my five grand back.”
“Well, you aren't going to get it here. They took everything off me when I was arrested.”
“You're still gonna pay.”
“No thanks.”
Vor stepped forward and swung his fist at me. I blocked it and threw him aside. Vor crashed into a table and fell over.
“WHAT ARE YOU JUST STANDING THERE FOR?!” He screamed at some of his friends. “GET HIM!”
Two of his friends charged at me. One tried to execute a kick. He grabbed his foot in mid air and hurled him into a wall. The other minifigure swung his fist in an uppercut. I blocked and brought my knee into his stomach.
Vor screamed in rage.
“GET HIM!”
But the rest of his friends were too scared to fight me after seeing what I’d done to the others. The prison guards were doing nothing. There were just staring in awe.
“You're going to die right here, Xeryon!”
“Bring it.” I replied.
I was pretty confident even though Vor was noticeably bigger than me. Vor attacked. I pushed his hands aside and kicked him in the face. He yelled and punched again. I grabbed his hand and twisted it behind his back.
“NO! PLEASE!”
There was a loud snap. I let go of him. He rolled around on the floor holding his broken arm and screaming.
And then the guards finally reacted. Two of them grabbed hold of my arms and dragged me away from Vor.
“The captain ain't going to be happy about this, Xeryon. You're going to be in a lot of trouble.” One of them noted.
“Sounds good.”
They led me back to my cell.

Book Two: The Four Kriken
Chapter 16
23rd December 2028
6:30 am

Xeryon awoke. He’d just had a dream about his past. He could remember it quite clearly. He looked at the crystal. It had stopped glowing.
“Was it the crystal that made me fall asleep or something?” He asked himself.
He noticed something else: All the fighting had stopped. It looked like the Kriken had retreated. The only people left fighting were surrounded by a crowd of minifigures. Xeryon went over to take a look. Duke was fighting his clone.
“Why is nobody helping Duke Exeter?” Xeryon shouted.
Exceedra saw him and explained.
“Duke has asked us not to get involved. He wants to defeat his clone by himself”
“Exceedra? What happened? I thought you were dead.”
“I’ll explain later.”
Xeryon turned his attention to the two fighters.
Duke slashed forward with his sword as Clone Duke raised his shield in defence. Clone Duke stabbed forward while Duke’s sword was knocked aside. Duke raised his own shield a second too late. Clone Duke’s sword slammed into Duke’s side. Luckily, he was wearing his battle armour and he was only knocked back.
“NO!” Yelled a Samurai.
He fitted an arrow into his bow and fired. Clone Duke span round with incredible speed, grabbed the arrow and hurled it back at the Samurai. The arrow hit him in the face, taking his life instantly. Duke slashed forward repeatedly. Clone Duke expertly blocked each strike. Then he pulled his sword back and swung forward, sending Duke’s shield flying out of his hands. Another strike shattered his armour. Duke tried to counter his clone’s next attack but he couldn’t. His sword split in half and he was kicked over.
“Victory is mine.” The clone said, laughing.
“No. Everyone knows you’re a fake.”
“Perhaps. But the Kriken Empire is in control of your useless Nexus Force. It will never be revealed to them that I am a fake.”
“You just told us yourself!” Xeryon yelled.
Several minifigures laughed. Clone Duke yelled in anger.
“You think you’re funny, do you? I’ll make sure you die straight after this idiot.”
He turned his attention back to Duke.
“Any last words?”
“You can go to-”
There was a distant rumble, then an explosion. Something slammed into the ground. As the smoke cleared the object became seen. There were several gasps of disbelief. Standing in the street was a creature. It was forty feet long and twenty feet tall. Its massive hind legs had four large claws each. Its arms were smaller but with equally deadly claws. Its mouth had a lot of teeth. Standing there in 2028 was a bright orange Tyrannosaurus Rex, another of the five Kriken leaders.
