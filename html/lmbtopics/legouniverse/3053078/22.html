He had two katanas sheathed on his back and a pistol holstered at his side. The other wore what looked like all the rank three Sentinel faction kits put together. It consisted of a Knight’s helmet with a Space Ranger’s visor, Samurai shoulder pad with Space Ranger tank on the back. He also wore a chest plate combining the Knight’s and Space Ranger’s armour. Finally the shirt and trousers were the Knight’s combined with the Samurai’s.

‘I’ll tell you what’s going on. We’re all going to die.’ Xeryon replied.

‘We’re all gonna go BOOM!’ Elite added.

He slammed his palms together, creating Maelstrom for effect. The Sorcerer screamed and ran round the other side of the tank.

‘Actually, I think that might be true.’ Said the first minifigure. ‘I’ve been spying on the Kriken recently and they’re building up their armies.’

‘Dextron? Is that you?’ Asked Xeryon.

‘Yeah. If I’m not wrong, you’re Xeryon right.’

‘Yep. Good to see you again.’

‘You two have met before?’ Asked Elite.

‘Back in 2012.’

‘So, you say the Kriken are building their armies?’

‘They’re getting stronger as we speak.’

‘Hard better then.’ Elite noted.

‘That’s why, we’ve got people like this guy fighting with us.’ Replied Dextron.

He waved a hand at the other minifigure who came over.

‘I’m Exceedra.’ He nodded to them.

‘Nice armour.’

‘Thanks. I call it the Master Kit.’

Book Two: The Four Kriken
Chapter 6
3rd December 2028
2:16 pm
Planet of Kriken

A tank shell exploded blasting several Kriken apart. A few Nexus Force soldiers were also caught in the blast. Thousands of Kriken and faction grunts were locked in combat together. After the Nexus Force had arrived, the Phark had retreated in fear. The battle had started five minutes ago and it was expected that it would last hours. Many of the tanks had successfully been dropped but a few had failed parachutes. They had smashed on impact with the ground. At the sidelines, Elite was fighting alongside Exceedra. Exceedra was fighting with a weapon Elite had never seen before. It seemed to be a Powerjouster and a Space Ranger gun put together. Exceedra stabbed two Kriken with the end and swung the weapon round, shooting at the same time. Three Kriken taken out at once.
“I was right.” Said Elite. “Your gear is awesome.”
“Yeah. It should be awesome. It’s worth about one hundred grand.”
“Whoa.” Elite parried a Kriken’s attack and hurled his sword. “So, how’d you end up here?”
“Same way you did, I expect. I was fighting off invaders. Then they all suddenly left and a minifigure came running down the corridor and told me that we were attacking this planet.”
He fired a shot at another Kriken and finished it off with the blade.
Elite reclaimed his sword and launched himself into a series of attacks at the Kriken.
“How long d’you think this battle will go on for?”
“Ah, a couple of days at the least.”
Exceedra said, flipping out of a Kriken’s firing range and shooting at it.

About a minute’s walk away, Xeryon and Dextron were fighting together.
“Just like old times, huh?”
Xeryon stuck his sword into a Kriken with one hand and hurled three knives with his other.
“Yep. Just like old times.”
Dextron lunged with both katanas. Two Kriken had their armour shattered.
Dextron sheathed one katana quickly and pulled out his pistol which he used to shoot the Kriken while they were still surprised.
“You’re even better at combat.” Noted Xeryon.
He slammed his elbow backwards, winding a Kriken. Spinning round, he smashed the Kriken with a single slash.
“Thanks.”
Dextron smashed the armour of a bigger Kriken and fired seven shots at it. It stared for a moment and lunged. Dextron dodged it and Xeryon leaped on its back, dagger pointing downwards.
Overhead, helicopters and planes fought with Kriken spaceships. The backup for the spaceships seemed endless. A building to the left caught Xeryon’s attention. Spaceships seemed to be emerging from it.
“Duke should know about that.”

2:29
Planet of Kriken

“Alright, Xeryon, you have permission to destroy it- but be CAREFUL.” Said Duke.
“You know me, sir. I’m always careful.”
“DUCK!” Yelled Exceedra.
He and Elite charged in a took out two Kriken who were about to shoot Duke. Dextron and Xeryon.
“Oh and Xeryon.” Said Duke. “ These three can go with you.”
He motioned to Elite, Exceedra and Dextron.

2:36
Planet of Kriken
Spaceship Launch Area

They’d had to fend off around twenty-eight Kriken on the way. Eventually they made it.
“So, what now?” Asked Elite.
“We destroy that thing.” Replied Dextron.
“Alright. We have a few sets of explosive. Elite and Exceedra you head inside to set them up. Dextron and I will keep guard out here.”
Elite and Exceedra headed through a door and into the build. They went down a dark corridor.
“I hear something.” Exceedra whispered.
They pushed open a door and walk into a room. Elite silently shut the door and listened as footsteps went past.
“We ended up in the right place.”
The room was full of fuel barrels.
“Quick. Set up the explosives.”
The two of them set to work setting the bombs up.
“What are these on the wall?”
“Just documents. Strange, they’re written in English.” He glanced at them briefly and then forgot about them as Exceedra spoke.
“We’ve got a problem.” Said Exceedra. “The fuse timers only go up to fifteen seconds.”
Elite cursed. “Then we’ll have to be quick at escaping. You ready?”
“Yep.”
“Alright start the timers now and RUN!”
As soon as Exceedra started the timers, they crashed out through the door and hurled themselves down the corridor. Five seconds passed, ten seconds, eleven, twelve, thirteen, fourteen and finally: fifteen.
The explosion threw Elite off his feet. He looked around for Exceedra but couldn’t find him.

2:38
“Where are they?”
“There’s Exceedra.”
“I don’t know what happened to Elite. The explosion threw me outside.”
“He’s there now!”
Elite was slowly limping towards them.
“Elite, you OK?” Asked Xeryon.
“Don’t trust yourself.” Elite said before collapsing on the ground.
“What? Elite? What are you talking about?”
“Goodbye.” Said Elite.
“What? Wait!”
But it was too late. Elite closed his eyes for the last time. A few seconds later, his body melted away into the air.
“And that’s how the Darkitect’s son dies.” Said Dextron.
“Rest in peace, Elite.” Xeryon said.
There was another explosion and the remaining three were thrown backwards. Xeryon groaned as he felt burns on his arms and chest. He looked up; a silver eagle was coming towards him. Then his vision blurred and everything went completely black.
