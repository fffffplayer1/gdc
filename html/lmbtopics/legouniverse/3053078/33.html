Book Two: The Four Kriken
Chapter 17
23rd December 2028
6:36 am

The T-Rex stepped forward and crushed a news reporter and his camera man who were trying to film it.
“Long live the Kriken Empire!” Screamed Duke’s clone.
The T-Rex looked at him for a second then bent its head down. The creature grabbed Clone Duke and its mouth and crushed him with its jaws. It spat a couple of pieces out then turned its attention to destroying anything that moved. The remaining soldiers began attacking at in an attempt to stop it.
“Yakow, give us some info on that thing.” Exceedra said.
“You may know this already but that thing is one of five powerful Kriken. Those five Kriken are our leaders, or those four now. One of them, a scorpion has already been defeated.”
“Yep that was Zedez’s work.” Xeryon said. “Do you know what the other three look like?”
“There’s a giant wasp, a sea serpent and the final one is the Kriken Empire’s prime commander. I’ve never seen him so I don’t know what he looks like.”
“Alright, let’s make it our mission to destroy all of the remaining leaders.” Dextron suggested.
The four charged and then stopped. A group of giant yellow beetles were crawling towards the T-Rex.
“Some Phark soldiers!” Yakow said. “Looks like they’ve come to attack us again. We barely held them off last time they attacked. The Sentinels who had been attacking the T-Rex now turned their attention to the Phark as a battalion of Kriken arrived. The battalion included two Kriken assault soldiers. Heavily armoured Kriken with missile launchers mounted on their shoulders, the type that had been involved in the assault of Nexus Tower. The battle had jut got a whole lot bigger.

Chapter 18
23rd December 2028
6:39 am

Yakow needed a weapon. None of the tools in his tool bag would do. Before he could look for one, a Phark jumped on him. They both fell to the ground.
The Phark hissed at him. As well as being fluent in both Kriken and English Yakow could also speak the Phark’s language and therefore understood what it was saying.
“You die Kriken.” It hissed.
“I’m fighting against the Empire.” Yakow replied.
“Liar!”
“Get off me.”
“Never! You are my enemy and I will destroy you.”
“No. You are my ally.”
And with that, Yakow spat at the Phark. The green and orange goo hit it straight in the face. It fell back and rolled over. Two minutes later it was now bright red and had large claws.
“Hello, fellow Kriken.” It said to Yakow. “What are our battle orders?”
“Attack the giant T-Rex.”
“OK.” It charged off.
“Yakow, what did you do to it?” Exceedra had noticed.
“I infected it.” He looked sad. “I hate doing that. But there was no other way. I had no weapons.”
“Come on let’s defeat this T-Rex.

6:43
Nexus City

The battle was starting to annoy Xeryon. Each time he got near to the T-Rex one of the assault Kriken would blast a missile at him. He needed to take them out. He looked to his left and saw a bus parked on the road. That was going to help him.

Book Two: The Four Kriken
Chapter 19
23rd December 2028
6:44 am

“Tarip, Arran, I need your help.” Xeryon said to the two.
“What is it?”
“I need you to distract that assault Kriken.”
“Alright.”
Xeryon ran for the bus. He hurled himself through the doors and climbed into the driver’s seat. Thankfully the keys had been left in the ignition. He started the bus and drive forward. Picking up speed, he turned and drove towards the assault Kriken. Xeryon reached out and smashed the window next to him and dived out. The bus kept on going, straight into the assault Kriken. It stood no chance and was pushed into a building and was crushed as part of it collapsed.
That left just one more assault Kriken. It looked as though Dextron was going to deal with this one. He jumped on its back. The Kriken tried to throw him off. Dextron twisted its arm round so it was aiming at the T-Rex. The Kriken struggled and tried to pull its arm back round. And then Tarip yelled something. The Kriken didn’t understand him but it could tell by Tarip’s tone that it was an insult. The Kriken fired. Tarip ducked and the missile went straight over him and hit the T-Rex. It roared in pain. Dextron twisted the gun round again so it faced him. The Kriken fired. A second before it fired the shot, Dextron twisted the gun so it aimed at the Kriken’s head. Then he jumped off. The T-Rex was next. Having defeated the other Kriken and Phark attackers, the group of Sentinels now focused on the T-Rex. Yakow and Exceedra charged forward. Exceedra with his Long Jouster and Yakow with a short blade collected from a dead Kriken. They simultaneously slashed at the T-Rex’s right leg. It roared as is leg when limp and it fell slightly. The Sentinels took their chance and swarmed the creature. Tarip, Arran and a few Space Rangers fired their guns at it. Dextron and Xeryon also, Xeryon using a discarded assault rifle. The T-Rex roared and tried to kick away its attackers. It was no use. The Tyrannosaurus collapsed. A moment later the helicopter approached over head. A figure jumped from it and landed on the downed T-Rex delivering the final blow. It exploded into green and orange goo, much like the scorpion had done. The second Kriken leader had been destroyed.
