Book One: The Virus Spreads
Chapter 8
24th November 2028
2:26 am
Power Station

Zedez aimed the shotgun and fired. The shot blasted a Kriken to pieces. He swung the gun round, hitting another Kriken. He pulled the trigger, killing it. Zedez continued forwards. He had seen something strange going on here. He had learned about the Kriken from a bunch of rebels. He had also learnt how to kill them.
‘Prophet what are you doing?’ asked Imfen over the radio transmitter.
Zedez was going to have to tell Imfen the truth. He wasn’t Prophet. But that would mean he would have to speak.
‘I...’
Three years ago on his nineteenth mission, Zedez had taken a bullet to the neck. He had survived but it had done serious damage to his voice box. Even now, speaking that one word hurt him.
‘What?’ asked Imfen.
‘I’m not... Prophet.’ That was all he could manage.
‘Who are you then?’
‘Can’t explain.’ This really was hurting his neck.
‘You can tell me everything when you get to my office. Just hurry up.’
‘Little busy... right-’ he winced at the pain. ‘Now.’
He ran towards the lights he’d seen earlier. He kicked open a door, switched guns to an assault rigle and opened fire. Everyone inside was killed- or they would have been if it wasn’t for Elite’s quick reaction. He had gathered his energy and created a Maelstrom force field.
‘Prophet!’ Yelled Xeryon. ‘You almost killed us.’
‘Oh you’re not... Kriken.’
‘Prophet?’ asked Elite. ‘That’s not you is it?’
Zedez shook his head.
‘I can... explain later.’
‘Well, it’s good to have another ally here.’ Arran said.
‘Anyway. What are the Kriken doing here?’ asked Tarip.
Zedez shrugged as there was a clang in the next room. The group ran through and found themselves in a giant room. There were reactors everywhere. Kriken stood at each one.
‘Are they spitting at those reactors?’
Indeed they were. A Kriken spat out orange and green goo. It hit a reactor and melted in.
‘I know what they’re doing.’ Quan said slowly. ‘But I only hope I’m wrong.’
‘What?’
‘They’re transferring the virus to the reactors that supply electricity for Nexus City. If we don’t stop them, they’ll infect the whole city.’
‘Then let’s stop them!’ yelled Elite.
He unsheathed his katana and run forward. Arran and Xeryon followed with Quan and Fern close behind. Tarip provided cover fire while Osllo and Kandem sat down and played cards. Xeryon dived on a Kriken’s back and stabbed a dagger into its back. Elite sent blasts of Maelstrom around the room. Arran opened fire at Kriken as Quan engaged them in melee fights. Tarip aimed and shot down a Kriken who was trying to sneak up on Quan.
Meanwhile Zedez was scouting around, invisible. He walked up behind a Kriken and grabbed it round the neck. It struggled in his grip while he pulled out a pistol and shot it in the left of its back. Another ran up to him.
‘Power mode active.’
Zedez swung a punch. It hit a Kriken in the face and sent him fly backwards. He walked up to it and aimed his gun. The Kriken made a strange clicking sound.
‘Translating.’ Came the bio-armour’s voice. ‘Translated.’
The Kriken had said: ‘You have no hope. Our virus has been improved. Now it takes only two minutes to infect a being.’
Two minutes had passed; it was too late. Doors at the back of the room were thrown open and around fifty Kriken came in.
‘Now would be a good time to retreat.’ Arran said to Elite.
‘Yes, you’re right. Let’s get out of here.’
They began to run. The Kriken followed.
‘Try and get past this!’ Shouted Elite.
He concentrated and three small cracks appeared in the ground. A Stromling Admiral crawled out of each crack.
‘Stromlings.’ Said Arran. ‘Ain’t seen them in a while.’
The Admirals were quickly overwhelmed by the Kriken.
‘Hmm, they’re tough enough to beat admirals but they can’t beat THIS!’
He slammed his palms together. Maelstrom energy rippled across the air. There was a purple flash and a Maelstrom dragon stood in front of the Kriken.
‘BUTTERSCORCH?!’ Kandem yelled.
‘Yep. Let’s give her some back up though.’
He repeated the action three more times, summoning Blastbreath, Torchblight and Burnshout.
‘BEAT THIS, KRIKEN EMPRIE!’ he yelled at the Kriken. ‘Now we run.’
The Nexus Force members turned and ran through the doors and out of the Power Station.

Book One: The Virus Spreads
Chapter 9
24th November 2028
3:01 am
The Seas

Leftenant Nero stood on the deck of a battleship. His team, Hunter Squad had been tasked with searching the ocean for any evidence that Vamor Squad could still be alive. They had had little luck so far. All they had found was a few scraps of floating metal.
‘Sir, we’ve got something on the radar.’ Said one of his troops, running over.
‘Show me.’ Replied Nero.
The two of them ran into the captain’s room. There were several objects on the radar. All of them heading for the ship.
‘What now, Leftenant?’ asked the ship captain.
‘Destroy all unidentified targets.’

Nero went back out onto the deck. He could see the objects now. They were space ships.
‘What the-?’
The ships opened fired. Nero threw himself at the floor. Missiles flew over him, smashing into the ship.
‘Abandon ship!’ someone screamed.
Kriken jumped from the spaceships and began boarding. Nero pulled out his Buccaneer’s pistol and fired at a Kriken. The Kriken returned fire with a plasma rifle. Nero ducked and fired again. The Kriken ran up and kicked him into the sea. He hit the water with splash. The ship was burning. He had to get away. He began swimming. Something hit him in the back and he blacked out.

Leftenant Nero stood on the deck of a battleship. His team, Hunter Squad had been tasked with searching the ocean for any evidence that Vamor Squad could still be alive. They had had little luck so far. All they had found was a few scraps of floating metal.
‘Sir, we’ve got something on the radar.’ Said one of his troops, running over.
‘Show me.’ Replied Nero.
The two of them ran into the captain’s room. There were several objects on the radar. All of them heading for the ship.
‘What now, Leftenant?’ asked the ship captain.
‘Destroy all unidentified targets.’

Nero went back out onto the deck. He could see the objects now. They were space ships.
‘What the-?’
The ships opened fired. Nero threw himself at the floor. Missiles flew over him, smashing into the ship.
‘Abandon ship!’ someone screamed.
Kriken jumped from the spaceships and began boarding. Nero pulled out his Buccaneer’s pistol and fired at a Kriken. The Kriken returned fire with a plasma rifle. Nero ducked and fired again. The Kriken ran up and kicked him into the sea. He hit the water with splash. The ship was burning. He had to get away. He began swimming. Something hit him in the back and he blacked out.

Book One: The Virus Spreads
Chapter 10
24th November 2028
2:47 am
Fields

There was a roar from inside the power station. Next second the four dragons came flying out. But they were now bright red and had bigger claws.
‘I don’t believe this.’ Groaned Elite.
‘The Kriken infected your dragons.’ Xeryon said, almost laughing.
‘It’s not funny.’ Arran said. ‘We’re doomed now. We obviously can’t fight Kriken and Maelstrom dragons combine together.’
‘You’re right.’
‘Over there! There’s some vans.’ Pointed out Kandem.
They ran to the car park and got into the three vans. Xeryon, Arran and Tarip took the first. Kandem, Osllo and Zedez took the second. The third was taken by Elite, Quan and Fern. They drove out and across the fields. Soon enough the dragons had been left behind.
‘That was easy.’ Said Tarip.
‘You shouldn’t say things like that.’ Advised Xeryon.
‘Why not?’
‘Because if you say ‘That was easy’, after something bad happens more bad things will happen.’
‘Nobody believes that, man.’ Arran said as a bullet flew through the window.
‘Told you.’ Said Xeryon.
‘Where did that shot come from?’
‘The side.’
Three bullets hit the back of the van.
‘Coming from two directions now.’
Tarip kicked open the back doors and opened fire. He hit the driver of a car behind the van. The car swerved off the road a crashed into a rock. Xeryon put his rifle out the window and ended the life of the one who was driving the other car.

3:50
Cliff Edge

The three vans parked and the team regrouped at a cliff edge.
‘We’re too late to stop the Kriken’s plan.’ Said Elite. ‘That, we can be sure of.’
‘So what now?’ asked Quan.
‘Now we-’ he began before being interrupted.
A rebel dived at him, grabbing him around the neck. Elite slammed his elbow in the rebel’s stomach, making him fall back. Xeryon brought his gun up and shot the rebel in the shoulder. Xeryon picked him up and held a knife at his neck.
‘Why are you here?’
‘To kill you lot. You’ve caused us trouble.’
‘Have we? All I recall is killing one of your mercenaries.’
‘Yes. That is trouble for us.’
‘Where is your leader?’
‘Why would I tell you?’
Xeryon punched him in the face.
‘Tell me.’
‘It’s down there.’ He pointed to an apartment building.
‘Thank you. Now leave.’
He threw the rebel to the ground. The rebel got up and ran. But on the way he tackled Quan, causing him to fall off the edge.
‘You’ll pay for that!’ yelled Tarip.
He shot the rebel seven times in the back. Each flare causing the rebel fatal damage.

3:58
Apartment Building
Later they had made their way to the apartment building.
‘So, who goes in first?’ asked Arran.
Without replying, Zedez pushed open the door and walked in. The corridors were clear. They ran up some stairs.
‘I hear... inside.’ Said Zedez.
Elite nodded.
‘We move in.’
Xeryon readied his rifle. Elite kicked open the door. They went in, as Xeryon fired a shot. It was only when they got inside that Arran was aware that two shots had been fired.
The rebel leader sat behind a desk. In his hands held a gun. He had fired as well and Fern had taken the bullet. He collapsed. The rebel leader turned and dived out of the window.
‘So long!’ he called out.
Zedez jumped after him, followed by Xeryon. Elite started after them.
‘Hey wait!’ yelled Arran. ‘What do we do no?’
‘Meet us back at Nexus Tower.’
Zedez, Xeryon and Elite began to chase the rebel leader.
