Occasionally, I will write some chapters to explain the unexplained things that have happened throughout The Virus.

 

Explaining the Unexplained

Chapter 1

The Traitors Are Defeated

23rd December 2028

6:20am

Nexus City, Secret Location

 

Overbuild, Tarip, Arran and Zedez stood around a table.

Overbuild was explaining something. “As Arran and Tarip successfully stole blueprints for the ‘Bio-Armour 2’ I have been able to improve Zedez’s armour. His strength enhancement has been improved only with his visor. We have also learned that the Bio-Armour 2 has a weakness to plasma. There are currently six traitors wielding this armour and forty-four other sets to issued out.”

“Sounds bad.” Arran muttered.

“It is. So here’s the plan: Sergeant Tarip and Captain Arran will head into the city and cause some commotion so that nearby Kriken soldiers are distracted.”

“What about us?” Zedez asked.

“We.” Overbuild brought out two Kriken rifles and threw one to Zedez. “Are going to take out these traitors.”

 

Ten minutes later. Zedez and Overbuild were stood at the Nexus Tower launch pad.

“Okay. The traitors are sent to personally guard Duke Exeter.” Overbuild said the words with a tone of anger. “I don’t know what’s wrong with Duke, but I’m going to find out. Let’s move.”

The duo headed inside and towards the Hologram map of Crux Prime. This map was no longer used. They headed towards a door close by. One of the traitors stood guard.

“Hey, you!” He shouted at Zedez. “Where’ve you been? You are supposed to guard Commander Exeter at all times.

Overbuild saved Zedez from thinking of a response by blasting the traitor with the plasma rifle. Although the traitor tried to activated defence mode to make his armour stronger, the plasma smashed straight through it.

Zedez hid the soldier behind a wall just as Duke came out of the office.

“NO! Stay there. I do not need help with this.” Duke shouted.

Overbuild ducked out of sight while Zedez cloaked himself. Duke walked straight past and his office door slammed behind him. Zedez and Overbuild went over to the door.

“Use your visor improvement to find out how many there are.” Overbuild said.

Zedez brought up the area map on his visor.

Tag enemies. He thought.

Five red dots appeared on the map. The map shrunk and went to the bottom corner of his visor.

“Five.” Zedez said.

Overbuild kicked open the door and they ran inside, firing. Four enemies fell. They went inside.

“Where’s the fifth?” Asked Overbuild.

“Here.” Snarled a voice.

Someone invisible grabbed Overbuild and threw him to the floor. Zedez quickly activated his visor’s heat vision and was able to see the remaining traitor. Zedez fired and hit him.

“Thank you. And it looks as though we’ve found the other sets of armour that were going to be given out. We should destroy them.”

Overbuild brought out an explosive charge and put it on a bunch of crates. They headed down the corridor and Overbuild detonated the explosives. A second later, there was another explosion, down in the city.

“I hope that’s not Tarip and Arran.” Overbuild said.

The duo ran for the launch pad.

Alien Vengeance

Chapter 14 Part 1

The Jammer

 

The sky filled with explosions, helicopters and dropships hit in the air and falling like giant missiles, soldiers of either side defeated in battle, the Nexus Force's retreat. Images of the battle from five days ago filled Cerise's head. Under his command, one hundred rebels had invaded and taken over Nimbus Station. They had succeeded in their next step towards controlling the Nimbus System. Rumour was that the leader of the rebelion wanted his soldiers to move on Nexus City soon. Cerise wasn't going to miss that for sure.

 

It was after midnight when Eagle and Hawk climbed from the freezing water and onto the shore of Red Blocks. They were here to investigate. Something had been blocking Nexus Force communications for the last day. Falcon and Raven were currently in Gnarled Forest. Crouching in the shadows, they moved past the stage and up towards Nimbus Plaza. They ducked behind a rock as a patrol of eight rebels passed, then they ran past what used to be the faction vendors and to Vertigo Loop.

"Down. Quick." Whispered Eagle.

The duo quickly lay down near the car-fixing area, hidden by shadows.

Several minifigures walked by.

"No. We will not close down the race track. We'll keep it open and have weekly races." Came the voice of Cerise Arsolis.

"But why?" Asked another voice.

"Because I like car racing. That's why." Cerise replied, simply.

"Okay, sir." Said another voice.

"Now, I'm off back to Avant Gardens. Keep on guarding until 3:00 AM and then some others will come and take your shift."

A minute later, there was the sound of helicopter rotors and then they faded after another minute.

Hawk and Eagle got up and clambered up into the hills. For this mission, they had brought extra gear. Along with their pistols and daggers, they had a sniper rifle equiped with a night vision scope, night vision binoculars and some explosive charges.

When they reached the top, Eagle spoke.

"Right. Let's get searching for the source of the jamming."

Chapter 14

Part 2

 

Eagle brought out the binoculars as Hawk began assembling the rifle.

“Alright.” Said Eagle. “Nothing interesting where we’ve just been. Just a couple of guards.” He looked through the binoculars towards the Brick Annex. “But this area looks more interesting. There’s two guards in the hills, clearly guarding something. They’re manning search lights. We’ll need to take them out before advancing over there.”

“On it.” Hawk said.

He crouched and looked through the rifle scope. Holding his breath to steady his hands, Hawk fired and eliminated one of the guards by the searchlights. Hawk snapped the rifle bolt backwards and fired again.

“Targets down.”

Eagle and Hawk climbed down from the hills and made their way to Brick Annex. They made sure to use any cover they could find to hide themselves on the way. When they arrived, there was only one guard and he was easily dealt with.

“And there it is.” Eagle pointed at something.

It was a fifteen metre tall metal structure with three aerials at the top. At the bottom of the structure was a control panel. Eagle set up the explosives on the panel.

“Time to go.” He said.

Eagle and Hawk dived into the water and swam for ten minutes. Eventually, they reached the boat that they had left floating in the water before they arrived at Red Blocks. They climbed aboard and Eagle detonated the explosives. The jammer blew apart  and the pieces flew in all directions. Hawk picked up the boat’s radio transmitter.

“Nikolai, come in. Do you copy?”

“Loud and clear. Nice work guys. Mission accomplished.”
