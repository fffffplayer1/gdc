Book One: The Virus Spreads
Chapter 15
24th November 2028
5:36 am
Nexus Tower

Elite pulled his katana from the body of a Kriken. The battle had been going for ages. It seemed that every time he defeated a Kriken, it was replaced by thirty more. The Kriken had powerful reinforcements consisting of twenty heavily armoured aliens with rocket launchers mounted on their shoulders. Only Xeryon had managed to defeat one and it had taken him half an hour.
Elite looked for Xeryon now, to find him slashing quickly at multiple Kriken. Xeryon’s expert fighting skills were what kept him alive. He’d only been defeated in battle twice. Once by Elite and another time that he wouldn’t talk about.
‘We... can’t hold out... much longer.’ Zedez said, coming over.
‘Yeah. We’re all going to get smashed if we keep fighting much longer.’
Duke Exeter seemed to be thinking the same thing.
‘PUT THE TOWER ON LOCKDOWN!’ He yelled.
Metal doors began closing over each entrance to the Tower. Several Kriken were crushed by the doors. As they were closing a missile blasted through. The explosion was deafening. Elite heard a faint scream as the missile hit a wall. The doors closed and locked. Several Sentinels ran towards a control area as Elite got up from the floor. A few Kriken remained inside the Tower but they were quickly finished off by Arran and Tarip.
‘Force fields activated, sir.’ A Space Ranger told Duke.
‘Good. That should hold them off for a while.’

A couple of minutes later, Elite, Xeryon, Arran and Tarip standing with the faction leaders. The Venture League had been under the command of Sky Lane ever since Xeryon had smashed Hael Storm back in 2012 during a prison break.
‘The four of you have been chosen for a special mission.’ Overbuild said.
‘To avoid unfairness we have chosen one minifigure from each Faction.
Elite: Paradox,
Xeryon: Sentinels,
Arran: Assembly
and Tarip: Venture League.’ Vanda explained.
‘I do not approve of the choice for the Sentinel Faction, Duke sir.’ Sky Lane said, giving Xeryon a cold look.
‘Let me guess.’ Said Xeryon. ‘It’s because I smashed that idiotic pirate obsessed with treasure, isn’t it?’
Sky gave him an angry look.
‘I think Sky and Hael were the world’s greatest friends.’ Elite told him.
Xeryon coughed, hiding a laugh.
‘Or the world’s greatest idiots.’ Suggested Arran.
Tarip dropped his flare guns. They broke apart.
‘Oops.’
‘You four! Stop messing around!’ Duke yelled.
‘Fine. But get on with the mission briefing.’
‘Alright. The Kriken have set up a camp down in Nexus City. There is an apartment building right next to their camp. Your mission is to place explosives inside the apartment and detonate them to drop the building on the Kriken camp.’
‘Sounds fun.’
‘Yes it does. And to make sure you don’t do anything stupid, Xeryon, Sky Lane will be going with you.’
‘WHAT?!’
‘You heard me.’
Xeryon cursed.

7:37 am
Nexus Tower

The five walked down to the tower basement. Elite remembered this place. It was a prisoner holding area. A crazy Shinobi traitor named Viper had locked him down here once. The door hadn’t been able to withstand Maelstrom energy however. This had been before Elite had discovered about his father being the Darkitect. He had had no idea how powerful he was.
They went through a door and down a narrow, dark corridor. A couple of minutes later they emerged in Nexus City. The secret exit originally came out to the surface of Crux Prime. But since the Darkitect’s defeat, Nexus City had been expanded to where Crux used to be.
They located the Kriken camp easily enough. Duke had shown them a map of the area and had told them that the camp would be near to the city block that surrounded the base of the Tower. Sky handed Xeryon a backpack full of explosives.
‘Put them in the apartment’s basement.’ She ordered.
‘Who put you in charge?’
‘Well, I have the most authority here. You, Darkitect’s son, go help him.’
Elite and Xeryon headed inside the apartment followed by Arran and Tarip a few minutes later.
‘Sky Lane gave us a lecture on why it’s not funny to dismantle each other’s Faction gear.’ Arran said, putting his wrench back together.
‘Here.’ Xeryon handed everyone two sets of explosive. ‘We should put two on each wall.’
‘Good idea.’ Agreed Tarip.
They set to work fixed the explosives to the four walls. As they finished a high pitched scream came from outside.
‘What the...?’
Elite run up the basement stairs on out into the street. Sky was being attacked by a Kriken.
‘Oh great. A Faction leader who can’t even fight. Just great.’
‘C’mon let’s detonate these bombs.’ Said Xeryon.
‘Sky’s got the detonator.’ Arran pointed out.
They saw Sky running away.
‘WHERE ARE YOU GOING?!’ Shouted Xeryon.
‘I’m getting out of here.’ Sky shouted back.
‘No chance, coward.’ He said.
Xeryon pulled his rifle off his back in aimed at Sky. He pulled the trigger and shot at Sky. She screamed and collapsed. Another shot at the Kriken. They ran over and got to detonator from her.
‘Get to a safe distance.’ Said Elite.
‘Wait! Help me!’ Sky cried.
‘No. You were going to leave us, so no we’re going to leave you.’
Xeryon, Elite, Arran and Tarip ran a few metres away. Xeryon flicked the switch on the detonator. The apartment exploded. It fell sideways, crushing the Kriken camp. Sky was caught in the explosion.
‘Mission accomplished.’ Xeryon said.
‘Yeah but you’re gonna have to tell Duke that you got Sky Lane smashed.’
‘I’m sure he can wait a few years to find out.’
The four turned round and walked back to the Tower.
