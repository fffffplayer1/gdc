Vamor Squad

Chapter 15

 

They didn't know where the criminals' base was but luckily Max had been sent the coordinates. The criminals had a small compound consisting a two buildings and a garage surrounded by a massive metal wall and a big gate. It was located a little way outside of Nexus City. Max landed a few metres away and the squad got out.

"How are we going to rescue the civilians exactly?" Max asked. "Our dropship will only hold six passengers and two pilots."

"I suppose we should probably take out the criminals first and then radio back to the Tower for backup to help the civilians." Kaan suggested.

"Right. I'll be waiting here if you need to get away quickly."

 

Round that back of the compound there was a small storage room guarded by one criminal. Ruby brought out her sword and dealt with him before he had time to shout for backup. The team moved inside the stoorage room and found a staircase leading upwards. They emerged on the wall surrounding the compound. They quickly ducked out of sight of the guards and headed towards a tent set up near the garage. Inside the tent was a map. The map showed that the prisoners were being held inside the basement of the second house.

"Alright," Kaan said. "Here's the plan. Cerise, I want you to place explosives on the walls of this compound. We're going to blow it up as soon as we have all the civilians out. Zedez, Ruby, take down the gate guards and open the gates. The rest of us will rescue the prisoners and lead them to the gate."

 

Zedez and Ruby used their sword and dagger to take down the three gate guards and they then opened the gate. Cerise came over a minute later.

"All done." He said.

Suddenly, Yelov and Vendetta came running, followed by several men firing machine guns at them. Kaan wasn't with them

Vamor Squad charged through the open gates and over to the dropship. A bullet hit Cerise's detonater and the explosives went off. Zedez felt a pain in the back of his neck and everything went black.
