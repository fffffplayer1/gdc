Book One: The Virus Spreads
Chapter 11
24th November 2028
4:03 am
Beach

Nero awoke. He was lying in sand. His first thought was that he was in the desert. Then he heard the sea. He got up and looked around, seeing that he was on the beach near Nexus City. Nero checked his weapons. He had lost his gun in the battle but he still had his cutlass. He climbed up the steps into a street. That’s when he heard the gunshots.

4:05
Streets

Xeryon ducked a knife and thrust his sword upwards. He swung round and smashed another rebel. A rebel swung the butt of his gun, as Xeryon raised his sword. The blade clashed again the gun. Xeryon kept the rebel busy trying to hold off his sword while pulling his rifle off his back. He pushed his sword forward, sending the rebel backwards. He pulled the trigger and a bullet was sent into the rebel. Without looking, he pointed the gun behind him and sent another rebel to the floor.
A little to his left, Elite we fighting three rebels. One of them lunged at him with a knife. Elite blocked the strike and kicked the rebel backwards. He thrust his sword at the second one. The third backed off and aimed his gun. A blast of purple energy dealt with him. The first had recovered. He punched Elite in the face- well, he tried too.
Xeryon had come up behind him with a dagger and smashed him.
While all this was happening, Zedez was in defence mode, trying to destroy a tank that the rebels had stolen from the Nexus Force. After just forty five seconds, Zedez’s bio-armour ran out of energy to power defence mode. Strange. It had lasted way over five minutes last time. Zedez ran forward and took down a soldier. Once he was on the floor, Zedez got his shotgun and shot the rebel. He checked his energy: it was full again. He switched to power mode and ran towards the tank. He swung his hand upwards, hoping it would work.
It worked. Zedez’s hand smashed into the tank and sent it flying upwards. I crashed through a house wall.
‘Unbelievable.’ Muttered Elite. ‘How’d you convince The Prophet to give you his armour anyway?’
‘Prophet is dead.’
‘How did it happen?’
Zedez explained the best he could. He was hard to speak with a damaged voice box.
‘He will be missed.’ Said Xeryon after Zedez had finished explaining.
‘C’mon we’ve got to get back to Nexus Tower and meet up with Arran and his squad again. We need a plan to help Dr. Imfen too. Last I heard, his office was under attack by rebels.’

Book One: The Virus Spreads

Prologue



6:34 am

23rd November 2028

Undersea



Six minifigures sat in the submarine. A Space Marauder, three Space Rangers and two Daredevils. Their mission was simple: help a group of scientists and fighters - led by Dr. Imfen- who was holding off against rebels in Nexus City. For the Space Marauder, Sgt. Zedez this was just another mission in his career.



‘This could be tough. I here it’s an alien invasion.’ Said one of the Space Rangers.

‘You actually believe that sort of rubbish?’ asked a Daredevil. ‘This is strictly a minifigure crisis.’

‘Approaching target in ten minutes.’ The submarine captain’s voice rang out over an intercom. ‘Get your weapons and diving gear ready.’

Zedez grabbed his Wormhole as there was a rumble somewhere outside.

‘What the brick was that?’ muttered the first of the Space Rangers.

A moment later there was a loud explosion and Zedez was thrown off his feet. He got up slowly and looked around. The submarine was on fire.

‘We’ve been hit! Abandon ship! Repeat, abandon ship!’ yelled the captain’s voice.

The six Nexus Force troops ran down a corridor and turned left down another. They reached door. A of the Daredevils grabbed the handle and pulled.

‘It’s jammed! Give me some help here.’

Zedez and the other Daredevil charged at the door, smashing it open. In the next area the water had flooded everywhere. Zedez dived in and swam towards a part of the submarine that had been ripped off by the explosion. Zedez felt himself falling. His Marauder armour was too heavy to swim in. A pulled off his helmet and shoulder pads and threw them away. He ditched his Wormholer next to them.

The other five swam ahead and Zedez followed. They swam out of the gap in the side. Now they were in open sea. Zedez felt a pain in his chest and realized that he needed air very quickly. He swam after his team. He surfaced, coughing. He gratefully breathed air into his lungs. He knew that they had had a close escape back there.

Things were about to get worse.

‘Alex!’ one of the team yelled. ‘Where are you?’

‘He’s gone; Zedez was the last one out.’

‘Well at least we’re safe.’

How wrong he was. The team saw a shadow crossing over them. Some sort of spaceship. Without warning it opened fire on them. Zedez threw himself under the water to avoid the shots.

Zedez surface again. His team were lying dead all around him. The ship opened fire on him and he felt himself get hit. Everything started to blur. He saw a minifigure standing over him, holding a joint anti-armour weapon. He fired the weapon as everything went black for Zedez.


Crazy story!
