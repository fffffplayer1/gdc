kendal7716 wrote:

I have many hero moments.

 

My first happened when I did my first solo dragon battle in Forbidden Valley. I must admit, I was always afraid of doing the dungeon solo. One day, however, I was going to do a dragon battle with another guy, but he left me. After spending a while thinking, I decided to try it. I was a Rank 3 knight back then, so I decided I could do it. After a lot of fighting, I finally managed to defeat the first dragon. But I didn't stop there. I went on and defeated the other two dragons, feeling unstoppable. Solo dragon fighting became one of my favorite things to do after that day.

 

Another happened when I was on cavalry hill in forbidden valley, destroying maelstrom horsemen. That was my favorite place to farm for loot. One day I decided to see if I could defeat a total of 50 maelstrom horsemen without smashing once and without any help from other players. I went on smashing horsemen after horsemen, strategicly escaping to regenerate health and armor. I was unstoppable. When I finally managed to achieve my goal, I felt amazing.

 

My third hero moment in forbidden valley happened when LEGO Universe was only open to the people that pre-ordered the game. I was a rank 1 knight back then, and I not only became the first rank one to ever go to  forbidden valley, but the first to fully complete the main questline there. I know it says that forbidden valley is for rank 2 and up, but I proved the game wrong. Heh heh.

 

I loved everything about LEGO universe. I could go on for hours talking about my best moments. It was an awesome game, but alas, it ended. LEGO achieved a major goal with their first MMO. If only it could come back...

Wow! Sounds like you were someone the maelstrom wouldn't want to mess with in-game. Great memories!

My finest LU Hero Moment?.. GotGriffin and me, in full Rank 3/Valiant Faction gear, taking down Horsemen on Cavalry Hill, during the final few hours of LEGO Universe's existence. We got into a flawless rhythm soon enough... I think we must've fought on for half an hour, and in doing so, we assisted countless newcomers to FV. It felt pretty epic to be that big of a help, and it was a spectacular way to end my LU journey.

 

There are a couple other herioc times I can recall... Max/Forest and I slashing our way through Gnarled Forest, clearing a path for lower-ranking players... Snow (Assembly), New (Sentinel), RareHypnoticCat (Paradox) and myself (Venture League) absolutely destroying the BoNS mini-game (The fact that we were each from on the of the four different Factions made it that much cooler)...

 

... And there was an instance where I was being a hero in a different way: I was helping my sister figure out a mission on her own LU account. ... No other time or place in history, other than that day, were the words, "Okay, now, you need to go up the stone staircase and get the gun from the monkey." uttered.

Finest Hero Moment for me had to be taking on Butterscorch in CP...solo.  It took me a lot of running, potions, and what-not.  Using my Space Ranger rank three gear, it was relatively easy for me.  As I finished him, a group of rank threes came by, and they said "There's no way you beat him alone!"  I just replied "Yes way."

 

Second finest:  Solo BONS, made it to the end in Knight 3 gear...and alternating between Space Ranger 3 as well.

 

Third finest:  Free to Play in Avant, made it to level 22 in 11 days, and made it through to wave 22 in Survival.

 

That about sums it for me.  I made it to level 36 on my character, Exceedra, before LU shut down.  Fond memories...but those are all they are now.
