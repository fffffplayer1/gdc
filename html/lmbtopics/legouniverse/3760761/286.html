‎02-27-2014 04:04 PM

Nexus Force Personnel, Faction Leaders, and Bane only. Please no unauthorized IC or OOC reading.

 

Jammers
This will likely be my final message to the Nexus Force for a while. I know I've "left" already, but there are a few things OOC that need to be said. To begin with, I am placing Turbo and Barbossa in charge of the defensive systems of the Nexus Tower. Hopefully you two will be able to handle things in my absence. The second thing I want to say is that the Swords of Light should not be underestimated. Primarily I'm referring to Bucky and Barcha. This is only a hunch, but I believe that Bucky is planning some sort of plot twist involving White Imaginite. For all intents and purposes, the Swords of Light can do whatever they want to with it, which leaves us vulnerable. Therefore, I hereby order the removal of White Imaginite from all secure and critical areas, with the exception of several shield systems and WI based cannons in secure storage facilities. White Imaginite should not be considered a reliable counter to Dark Imagination or any other energy. Therefore, Pure Imagination is of an utmost importance to the Nexus Force. Since the Rangers discovered a way to amplify its power, it has become a fairly good counter to Dark imagination and it should be used as such. Now, to the threat posed by Barcha. Barcha has some phenomenal technology known as the Crystal Rose which is some sort of self replicating intelligent crystal weapon. I'm thinking that some classic plasma glassing might be effective against it. In addition, I believe that a multipurpose electromagnetic jammer might mess up whatever system the crystals use to communicate, but that is nothing more than speculation on my part. Anyway, this turned out being longer than I wanted it to be. Be aware of these threats and prepare accordingly. FOR IMAGINATION!


~Commander Scott "Buzzsaw" Norman ~ Eris Redbeam ~ Nijki ~ Nap O. Leon

"Honorable soldier, antisocial scientist, mischievous smuggler, tactician, and innocent bystander. That's me."
