-Antagonist's voice echoes throughout the tower-

I think now would be a great time to introduce the rules for the upcoming tests. I don't want to drop you into the middle of such a fun game not knowing the rules!

1. Everything I do to test you must be dealt with. You can't ignore, change, or manipulate what I do to make it easier on you. It is a test after all.

2. You may not quit once you have started. You must complete the tests before I leave you alone.

3. You must let me do whatever I want to test you. As I have said, I won't do anything overtly cruel, impossibly challenging, Et Cetera. Everything I test you with will have some way of being overcome. Impossible tests are fair, are they?

4. You cannot y to harm, hurt, injure, or smash me. I don't think you could even if you tried, but that isn't the point of these tests anyway.

5. Monsieur Darek is my main contact and test subject. I will only listen to him when it comes to changing tests, unfair things, or what have you. I will hear other's comments, but I'm not obliged to actually do anything about them.

There are a few other stipulations and other such items. but those don't need to be said right now.

-Antagonist

Indeed, I accept your "challenge." Please set a time, and I will do my best to accomodate. If you don't mind, I would like to be the first test subject. If you choose to take Gameking first, however, I understand.

~ Monsieur Darek

Good to hear! Your first test starts now and will last until I decide you are finished and ready to move on to the next section. To borrow from one of my favorite Test Proctors, this will be a cooperative testing situation. The entire Nexus Force is to be involved in this, as such, teamwork is not optional.

-sirens, alarms, bells, and whistles begin to sound-

I noticed a disturbing trend here in the Nexus Force. You all seem to have departed from your original charter and have begun to weaponize Imagination. For this test...

-snaps fingers-

All your Imagination fueled/powered/etc weaponry, defensive equipment, communications devices, guns, engines, warp drives, lasers, reactors, coffee makers, or anything else remotely relating to Imagination has been erased from existence by yours truly! Now, in order to be fair, I've replaced everything with its closest non-Imagination relative. That beautiful Nexus, however, has not. Everything will be returned at the conclusion of testing.

All of your starships, flagships to fighters, have had their tanks drained to allow a one way flight to... Hmm.... Gnarled Forest sounds nice and twisted, so that is their range. All fuel enrichment/procurement facilities are offline, so you can't just get more.

Now, for your goal, you must prove to me that you are worthy of "Defending Imagination" and all that rhetoric the original Nexus Force was founded upon. Remember the rules! Punishment for breaking them is... most unpleasant. Your Proctor should be along within 24 hours to administer the first challenge. Best of luck!

-Antagonist
OOC: So, yeah! Testing time! Please don't try to circumvent what just happened or exploit loop-holes. I would love it if you just rolled with these punches and worked through the problems instead of cheating and trying to change reality. Have fun and remember to brush your teeth!

I would like to take these tests. It stinks, however, that my iron-magnesium blue-imaginite-tippied shurikins are now just iron-magnesium shurikins. However, I will have to make due with that... good thing those rifles I ordered are electromagnet-powered!

-Buzzsaw
