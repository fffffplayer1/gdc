He brushes the thought aside.  The tower security was top notch, as he had seen from all his visits there, and it wouldn't be so easy to sneak in.  But he knew that if an attack was coming, it wouldn't be long until it came.

 

He thinks he hears footsteps from down the hall.  He begins to move towards them hoping to find Vastus.  He finds his hand drifting towards his pistol holstered at his side.  He mentally scolds himself for being paranoid, but does not remove his hand.
