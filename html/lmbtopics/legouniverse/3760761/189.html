Hey, all!

 

I've just started a new topic, the Institute of Non-Permanent Player Containment.  The point of it is to provide an entertaining alternative to being smashed.  Next time you find yourself in an almost unescapable situation, just say, "Take me to the INPPC".  Then, your character will be sent to the INPPC topic and put into a containment area.  You then have two choices:  1. Stay in your containment area for the three day limit or 2. Attempt to escape.  The entire topic will be designed to find that balance between being fun to escape from and being fair to the people who sent you there.  Hopefully, you'll give it a try.  If so, have fun finding creative ways to escape!
