Ardour is walking in the hallways of the Nexus Tower. After Shadow was smashed, he has been quite depressed. When he had started his journey towards the Tower ti join the Nexus Force, there was nothing he left behind. The Maelstrom had taken him everything he had. After he joined Strange Odd Shadow was the first person he became friends with. It was neither a very long nor a close friendship, but for Ardour Shadow was important. His being gone was just one more blow to Ardour. It was more evidence that things could never be good. He was never very cheerful and he was rather pessimistic lately, but now he truly was depressed. Even the victory against Chaos couldn't cheer him up.

Right now, he is going to meet Sarah and Magical. He wants to see whether they learnt the news. They have been quarelling the whole time after the Fall of the Beast. Sarah is blaming Magical for what happened to Shadow. That's why Ardour thinks they might have not even noticed that things have changed. And he's quite right. He soon finds them in a room still fighting. However, seeing them fighting he decides he is not in the mood of talking to them, turns around and starts running.

Ardour keeps running. He can't stop, because he needs to get away. The actual reason he left is that when he saw the two minifigures fighting, he started getting angrier and angrier and his final thought was: "I need to smash something! Maybe them!"

Now, the minifigure's anger combined with his fear makes him almost lose control.

Smash! Smash! Smash a thing! Smash everything! SMASH! he thinks.
The last Smash he had actually shouted.

I'm losing control! I must put an end to this! he manages to think in between to his other more destructive thoughts.

And now he has come at one of the many balconies of the Nexus Tower. It was the only thing he could do. . .
