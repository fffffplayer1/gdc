Still shaking after the teleportation, Tyler doesn't even notice the leak of the Imagination Nexus.

"S-scared? E-ehm, no. I am rather shocked."

 

Eventually he shakes off this terrible experience and listens to all the given information. Pondering deeply about this problem, Tyler looks at the displays, where he can see the recorded images of the Chaos heralds visiting various places of the Crux sytem and eventual battles with them.

 

Focused on these records, Tyler only says: "Greetings!" to the newcommers and waves roughly in their direction. He pays no more attention to them, so it is very likely he wouldn't remember their names.

 

After gathering all of the possible information, a knowing smile appears on his face:

"Of course the imagination and chaos seems to be innert to each other. It is because imagination itself is a process. An operation that allows us to form an Idea by merging the all-possible variables of chaos into a strictly specified shape. It is  the process in which we forge chaos in order to create."

Hoping to get the attention of others, Tyler continues to explain:

"Therefore chaos can not destroy imagination, for it is like destroying itself. And neither can imagination defeat chaos, for it is based on it and without chaos, there could be no more imagination. What we need to seek first is a ballance." 

 

 - Tyler the Flat - Elder Summoner - Bearer of the Golden Brick -
