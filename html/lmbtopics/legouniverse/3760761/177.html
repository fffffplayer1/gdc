OoC: Note that I have an expiremental GravGun a mile wide on the underside of the Voyager. Said GravGun can project a gravitational field of seven Gs, in a mile-wide beam. Meaning that, if I were to hover over NT and project the beam, Nexus tower's weight would be increased sevenfold.

Let's do the math. Let's say that each floor of the tower is 3 tons. That's:

6,000 x 200 (the amount of floors) = 1,200,000 pounds. If all of that is supported by the first floor, then that's 1,194,000 pounds on that floor. 

That's a lot of weight.

Now, if we increase the gravity above the tower sevenfold, that's 7x1,194,000=13,958,000 pounds of weight on one floor.

Meaning a collapsed tower. Have fun preventing my attack!
