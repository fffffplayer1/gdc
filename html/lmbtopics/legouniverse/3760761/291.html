“I like to think I’m pretty interesting, yeah. I don’t consider myself a villain, though. The way I see it, I’m BuckyKat, and I do what I want. At the time, the formation of the DRE was what was best for the people around me, and by extension, me. When it stopped being a good thing, I did away with it, and went on my way. The Codex was very much like the situation we are faced with today. If I had let the Chaos Source go about its business, it would consume the Universe. So I stopped it. A long, long ways away, Hov was sitting in his lovely space-conditioned airship with his new scientist friend. He was busy tinkering with a new little construct, but he felt a slight mental twinge. Ah, yes. I forgot about that.

 

Hov turned to his scientist companion. "Say, Mr. Engineer whose name I have not yet bothered to ask! One more realm-jump, alright? We are being summoned to a peaceful meeting. As sad as it is, the blasting will simply have to wait."

He caught his unnamed comrade by the arm and broke a rune sequence to the side. There was a flash of burning pain as the body converted to energy, the essence being tossed far, far away...

 
                                                                                .     .     .

 

The two of them were recomposed in the Nexus Tower, right outside the room where, according to Hov's brief mental scan, the meeting was being held. Hov gestured towards the seats around the holoprojector. "Take a seat, Mr. Inventor. This meeting'll probably be amazingly boring, but showing up and appearing polite is important."

 

Hov walked over, waving to Savos and Bucky, before sitting down in a chair. Ugh. This thing is terribly uncomfortable.
He pushed the chair aside, reached into his cloak, and pulled out a small little object. He proceeded to place it on the floor, draw a long metal rod from his belt, and tap the object smartly. There was a brief spark that illuminated runes on both items. The small object grew in size, becoming recognizable as a chair. A leather recliner, in fact. It kept growing until it was a suitable size, whereupon Hov tapped it once more. He sat down, kicked out the foot-piece, and relaxed. Much better.

OOC: This speaking bit is gonna be a bit long. Bear with me. Grizzly style.

 

A small crowd of influential people have gathered in the War Room now. They chat and mutter, maintaining an uneasy peace with their usual enemies. If your character hasn’t properly arrived, you can either consider them already here, or enter quietly. Bucky has taken to the podium, and now addresses the crowd. His voice is deep and reaches every corner of the room. The conversation fades immediately.

 

“Ladies and Gentlemen. Before we start, I have a few things to say. First of all, if any of you attempt to exploit this gathering to further your own agenda, or move as if to harm anyone, I will deal with you using extreme prejudice. I realize that a number of you have weaponry on your person; however, none of it is enough to prevent me from rebuilding you into a tasteful scarf. You have been warned. With that out of the way, please hold all questions until I ask for them, and no flash photography. Thank you.”

 

With that, the lights dim, and the starmap comes back up. Bucky can be seen through a veil of stars, and continues to address the group, and I’m going to emphasize the deepness of his voice once more.

 

“Fellow citizens of LEGO Universe. Today, we are faced with yet another threat to our very being. In the past few hours, the outskirts of the galaxy we call home has been taken over by a new, invading force. These invaders plan to take over the entire galaxy, and it is only we who stand in their way. If we do not stand together now, we will never stand again. Thus, I have gathered you all here to share my knowledge, discuss, and create a plan for dealing with these invaders.”

Bucky gestures to the starmap. As he mentions locations, they glow for a moment, for emphasis.

 

“As you can see, all of these small, outlying outposts have been taken over by the invaders in the past few hours. This is the first step of their plan. I have worked with these invaders in the past, and know how they operate. They call themselves the [Bucky produces an unnatural series of bubbling and clicking]. Unfortunately, that’s not pronounceable in Minifig, so instead of constantly butchering their name, from here on out, we shall simply refer to them as The Invaders.”

 

“For a bit of background, The Invaders are just that. Over many years, they have spread from their homeworld, conquering everything. They take the recourses and technology of conquered races, and use them for their own. Their empire spans many, many worlds, and their armada is second to none. They use primarily what we would call biotech. Living machines, and that. They always follow the same, deadly efficient plan when invading. First they scout it out. Find who lives there, if anyone. Decide if it’s a good idea to attack. Then they outfit their fleet against those they’re going to invade. Change the shielding to properly counter the weapons used there. Outfit themselves with weaponry the locals don’t know how to deal with. Then they go ahead and take the outer reaches of the area they want, as they have here in the last few hours. Block any reinforcements, close all trade routes. Establish a beachhead for themselves. Then, and here’s the important bit, they create an opening. Be it a literal hole in the defense, the removal of an important figure in the local government, the sabotage of some important system, they do whatever they can to create an opening or distraction. Then, with that advantage, they sweep through and establish total control.”
