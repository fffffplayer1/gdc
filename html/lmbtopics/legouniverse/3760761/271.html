"Fine. Hostages no more then. DESTROY THE PLANE-" He is cut off by a large white fist slamming into his face.

 

Apocalypse looks at the figure, and for the first time in milenia, showed a look of true and utter fear, "C-c-creator..."

 

The Glowing white Creator stood above Apocalypse. "You destroyed me once, Apocalypse. You endanger my Universe with your schemes and your tricks. I cannot allow you to persist in this form" (Using this color because white font would be useless )

 

A glowing white staff appeared in his hand and he held it over Apocalypse, "From this moment, I banish Apocalypse, and all of his followers and ships, to the form of Bricks and minifigures!" He thrusted it down, and a bright flash temporarily blinded all who witnessed the sight.

 

When the light had cleared, A minifigure was in Apocalypse's place. He wore red robes, much like Apocalypse did, and his crown was the same. All the Chaos ships had turned to a super-plated titanium as well. The minifig looked down at himself in utter despair, "NO! How could you have done this to me?!"

 

Wisdom was not in the place he was before, he was standing, minifigure size, infront of Apocalypse's Minfigure form. "You and all of those who are affiliated with you will turn into minifigures. With the abiltiy to smash and reform. Infinitely. So you can never truely rest for your crimes."

 

Apocalypse was envealoped in White light, "Now, I'm going to give you a chance. You and your entire base and fleet will be transported back to its base, which will now be a system of asteroids I custom made so that your frail brickly bodies could breath. You will retain most of your powers, but none of your chaos will truely work ever again. This is your eternal punishment." Apocalypse was gone.

Wisdom turns, and faces the masses. He grows to full size and tells the Balancers, Nexus Force, and other allied forces, "Today you fought well, minifigures, You fought with the diligance and courage any of my first builders would envy to this day. Unfortunately, I must purge the Wisdom element from you. Now that Chaos can be defeated by your own means, you no longer need this element."

Suddenly, all of the Wisdom in Crux is replaced with Imagination. "Should it fall into the wrong hands, I would not know what to do. As for the Planet Magneote..." The red glare disappears, as all of Chaos' soldiers are gone, and the planet is put in a stable orbit around Crux's Sun. Wisdom smiles, "And for his generosity, and caring nature, I leave you with the immortal being who summoned me." He disappeared, leaving Aron hunched over, exausted, in his Assembly gear. Slowly, the Chaos vortex was overwhelmed by a white light, disappearing.

 

OOC: Alright. Time for me to explain. I wanted to truely end Chaos at this point, but when more people joined the HQ, I felt that it was unfair to them to just get in and get kicked out because Chaos was destroyed. So, I did this. Now, Chaos is on the same level with everyone else. Wisdom no longer has any affect on him, but conventional things do. And now that he isn't as powerful, I don't want to see Anti-matter Missiles headed straight for the Chaos HQ. Now I have no way to counter that. And that would become more OP.

His being gone was just one more blow to Ardour. It was more evidence that things could never be good. He was never very cheerful and he was rather pessimistic lately, but now he truly was depressed. Even the victory against Chaos couldn't cheer him up.

Right now, he is going to meet Sarah and Magical. He wants to see whether they learnt the news. They have been quarelling the whole time after the Fall of the Beast. Sarah is blaming Magical for what happened to Shadow. That's why Ardour thinks they might have not even noticed that things have changed. And he's quite right. He soon finds them in a room still fighting. However, seeing them fighting he decides he is not in the mood of talking to them, turns around and starts running.
