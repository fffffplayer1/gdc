OOC: Hello, Nexus Force! With your graces I have a new character to introduce here. I'd love to just jump in with his introduction below, so without further ado about nothing:

IC:

"Where are you, men and women of courage? Where are your spines?! Each and every day we continue to fight the Maelstrom menace, and we never gain a foothold or advantage againt them. And yet you still place our trust in those that call themselves our leaders."

It was a hot summer day, with thermometers registering in the triple-digit range across the planet. Outside of the Nexus Force University of Technology a man is dishing out fiery oratory to an audience of enraptured students.

"We don't need the diversity of four factions, we need the solidity of an organized government that can defend its people. Our military is made up of young people, much like yourself, with little to no prior training. If our enemy feeds off chaos, how fat will he be after devouring a group of unorganized militia? We need leaders who will train and direct soldiers to defend not just them, but you and I as well. We need forces that won't just **bleep** and jab at the behemoth, but can slay it completely."

He pauses to take a sip of water from the plastic cup on his podium and to wipe his brow before continuing in a more somber tone.

"There are those who call me a militant, a warmonger, hateful, and even a zealot. If my desire to rid ourselves of our enemies makes me those, so be it. I would rather die than be a man in whom passion for freedom does not burn. Some of you here will probably balk at my fervor, or shrink from my conviction. But search yourself, can't you see these things to be true? The Nexus Force will not save us. The Nexus Force indeed cannot save us. If we are not willing to do what it takes to destroy the Maelstrom, we deserve to become part of it."
Myers-Briggs Type: Extroverted, Intuitive, Feeling, Judging.
The Guiding Light of the SoL.

"The only thing necessary for the triumph of evil is for good men to do nothing."
