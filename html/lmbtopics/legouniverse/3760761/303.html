OoC: Well, since Barcha's plotline went inactive, I shall suppose that somehow they got out of that situation or that they never got stuck in it.

 

IC: Ardour and Chronia walk in one of Nexus Tower's many hallways. Chronia is using her screwdriver thingy to check something around her.

"What are you doing?" asks Ardour.

"Oh, you see after I arrived in this time and space I forgot to check whether The Devourer is around! So, I'm doing it now!" Chronia answers cheerfully.

"And who's that devourer guy?"

"Well, he's my arch-enemy! In one of my past incarnations, I can't really remember which one though, I bumped into him and we sort of found each other to be disagreeable. He's been chasing me ever since. Many of my regenerations have been caused by him. He is a creature unknown to me -and you know how rare it is for a Time Lord or a Time Lady not to recognise some being- which has the power of absorbing the powers -and not only- of others. I must say he has gotten pretty strong!"

Whoever that guy is, thinks Ardour, it doesn't sound like someone I'd like to meet.

By that time Chronia has almost finished sonicing the place when someone exclaims: "What's that?!"

They turn around to see Sarah Darkon, the friend of Strange Odd Shadow.

"What do you mean?" Chronia asks with some unexplained excitement and curiosity appearing on her face.

"The screwdriver!" the girl explains "What sort of screwdriver is it?"

"Oh, interesting that you ask! You see, it's not the typical sonic screwdriver! Not even a laser screwdriver, which also has been produced at a time. It is a Sonic-Laser-IB Screwdriver. IB stands for Imagination Beam. This screwdriver has all three functions! Why  do you ask?"

"Well, I would have recognised the design anywhere, even though it's slightly altered. I can't believe you have one! You see, the IB screwdriver was designed by my friend Shadow!"

"So?"

"Shadow has smashed, but he never finished that design!". . .
