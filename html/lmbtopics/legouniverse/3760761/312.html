In a little back alley, a long way away from any NF scanners or something, a DI Vortex opens up, and a figure walks out quickly. The Vortex closes, unnoticed. 

 

Emerging into the street, the figure is revealed to be a Rank 3 Engineer, complete with the wrench, magnet, helmet, shoulder pads and everything. He walks calmly down the street, nodding at the random NF marines as he passes them. He approaches the Tower, and as he walks up to the door, he starts fidgeting lightly. 

 

He enters his code into the keypad, revealing him to be Engineer Ambris Raptorum, found in brick annex as a youth, trained as an engineer until he was fifteen, when most records of him stop. But, the computer reads his profile as still active, even fifteen years since his last NF mission. If a guard were reading it, it would seem odd, but the computer only cared if it said "registered" or "unregistered". 

 

So the legally still member of the NF Ambris Raptorum walks calmly into the ground floor of NT. He passes a terminal, and a hologram of Nexus Naomi appears. 

 

"Your helmet's computer seems to be carrying a foreign technology, Ambris. May I examine to see if there is a threat?" 

 

The man makes a stumbled "shhhhh!" noise, and quickly touches the terminal with his hand, sending a pulse of DI through it, wiping its memory of the last thirty seconds. He walks away. 

 

Under his sound proofed helmet, he whispers.

"Deady, you need to disguise yourself better. That would have blown the entire thing."

"Feh, this subtlety is driving me crazy. But fine, I'll hide myself better."  Said the red light in the back of his mind.

 

Sparrowhawk continues walking towards the elevators.

Located on floor 49 of Nexus Tower, protruding from the south side lies Rocket Pad 021. It is an unremarkable rocket pad, three rocket landing pads on a platform. Standard stuff. Used all the time by Nexus Force agents on their way to world beyond. But today, Rocket Pad 021 receives a special visitor

.

*A rocket approaches Rocket Pad 021.*

 

The rocket isn’t anything much, just another standard issue rocket. Pointed nosecone, four tailfins, black and yellow color scheme, streaking along like nobody’s business. It identifies as Rocket 1550BF, which, according to the database, is a rental rocket on loan from the Valley Base in Gnarled Forest. Everything is in order, no red flags, no suspicion. *Said rocket lands on Rocket Pad 021, and a masked pilot exits.*

 

The pilot of Rocket 1550BF exits their rocket, and puts it in their inventory.

 

They are wearing an ambiguous black jumpsuit with gloves, and a black motorcycle helmet with the opaque visor down, completely obscuring their features.
