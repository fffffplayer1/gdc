NEXUS TOWER!

Thanks to the very generous Mythrans, we've been able to return to normal. Our new tower is roughly the same height, however, the landing area is actually on the lower section of the tower, overlooking a gorgeous view of the pure lake country. Here's a list of the levels of the tower:

Nexus Tower Level 1 - Lobby
Level 2 - Game Room (A place to do mini-games, eat, goof off, etc. )
Level 20 - Paradox (The Paradox Level of Nexus Tower.)
Level 21 - Sentinel (The Sentinel Level of Nexus Tower.)

Level 23 - Venture League (The VL Level of Nexus Tower.)
Level 100 - Rangers (Special Rangers floor.)
Level 6 - Shield Generator (NO ACCESS ALLOWED.)
Level 3 - Communications Center
Level 99 - Artificial Skydiving Room (self explanatory...)
Levels 24-26 - Assembly Creation Lab
Level 76 - Venture League bungee jumping.
Level 15 - Garden
Level 200 - Venture League Observatory
And more! (Please create one on LEGO Digital Designer so we can add it!)
