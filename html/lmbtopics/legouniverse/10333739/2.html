IC:

 

"Ladies and Gentlemen, I have yet another exciting update.  This brand new particle, which the boys in the lab have named, "Veratanium", has been shown to fully react upon contact with the Imagination particle, annihilating them both.  I'm sure most of you grasp the magnitude of this discovery, but allow me to explain it entails."

 

"As mentioned before, all of the Substance are fundamentally based on ratios of matter, mental focus, and Imagination particles, with a few exceptions.  Remove any of those, and the Substance is either impotent, or incredibly difficult to control.  If, for example, all of the Imagination particles were removed from an Imaginite molecule, the Imaginite would become useless.  The same applies for Maelstrom, Chaos, and perhaps even Dark Imagination, though we have yet to test the latter.  The point is, any pure Substance can be neutralized if exposed to an equal proportion of Veratanium."

 

"Now, before anyone raises a fuss and accuses me or my staff of attempting to conquer all of existence with this new substance, allow me to fully demonstrate our good will.  We have been working aroound the clock producing as much of this substance as possible, and will be distrubuting it to anyone who wishes to pick it up at Space Station Renati.  Simply stop by, inform us of how many tons you will require, and we shall give as much as possible.  Unfortunately, it requires special particle separation machinery to produce, but we shall be posting the, er, 'recipe' on the Holonet within a couple of weeks nonetheless."

 

"And for the naysayers who are still accusing us of ill intentions, allow me to restate our philosophy: We only wish to bring peace to the universe.  If we can neutralize the horrendous weapons these Substances have become, we shall be one step closer to that peace.  Thank you.  I shall take questions now."
