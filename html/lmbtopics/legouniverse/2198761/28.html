Okay, the Bio:


After the events told in Lord Brocktree occurred (read the book ), Brocktree decided that the life in Salamandastron (again, read the book) was not for him, and decided to go and explore once more. After wandering for a long period of time, he found the Nexus Force, and quickly realized that it was what he had been searching for the whole time. After joining the Sentinels, he rose quickly through the ranks, until he achieved the momentous position of Adviser to all Faction Leaders. As a gift for his excellent services, Brocktree was given his best weapon, a massive double-edged broadsword named Verminbane.





When not aiding them, he prefers to either help rookies in Avant Gardens or pounding various dragons into the ground.





Characteristics: Brocktree is tall, and usually quiet unless his opinion is asked for. He is incredibly brave, and will always help a friend in need. His strength as well is above the par, allowing him to easily lift his massive weapon. He suffers from a disease known as the "Bloodwrath", (once more, read the book) which renders him impervious to pain, and gives him super-human strength, although while he is possessed by it he has no mercy, and will destroy anything that stands in his path, unless the something is extremely close to him. The Bloodwrath only comes upon him in extreme combat situations though, or when a friend is threatened. He is a highly skilled swords-man, and can easily disarm any foe that comes near him. He also brooks no insolence towards him from any creature.





Extra information:


Listed in Top Ten fighters of the Universe.


Only currently known Minifigure to suffer from Bloodwrath.


Sword is made of Adamantium, therefore almost indestructible.


Also keeps a long range sniper rifle slung across his back.





Fears:


Death of friends because of his actions, and harming a friend while in Bloodwrath.





War-cry:


Either "BLOOD AND BONES!!!!!!!!!!!!"


or "EULALIAAAAAA!!!!!!!!"





Known enemies: Wild warlord known as Ungatt Trunn.





Well, I hope you like it! If you want the FULL bio, then as said, read Lord Brocktree, by Brian Jacques.
