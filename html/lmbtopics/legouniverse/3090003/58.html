‎06-12-2013 04:51 PM

 

James noticed my distraction, and asked, “Silver? Are you sure you’re all right?”

 

I gave him a weak smile; too weak- and answered, “I’m fine, James. Trust me, I’m...fine…Thank you for the gesture, by the way. The candles were a nice touch. But…you know we can’t, don’t you?”

 

“Can’t?” James stammered, “Silver, we can’t what?”

 

“You know,” I said, moving my arms around, “All of this. I’m glad you tried, and I wish it could be another way, but we’re not even the same species; you’re a titan, and I’m… well, whatever it is I am. And…”

 

“And? Silver?”

 

When I looked at him again, I did my best to make sure he didn’t see any tears on my face. “James…Do you…do you remember what you found out about my…condition, before the Time Shaper hit us? That I’m going to…expire, just from the effort of keeping myself together?’

‘ I…oh, I didn’t want to actually say this…I had Ailment do some calculations, and… James… I only have about three months left.”

Silver's POV: The Tears of Avlakyli Part 10

Options

‎06-12-2013 04:52 PM

 

I had seen James in many types of turmoil. I’d seen him attack my father in rage, I’d seen him in anguish over his lost sister. But I’d never seen him as I did right then. James seemed to age a thousand years in a single moment.

 

“I…” he said, desperately trying to find something to say, “Oh, Silver… I already lost you once, I can’t… I  can’t lose you again!”

 

“I can save you!” he said, clinging to a last string of hope, “Silver, if I can give you your body back before then..I can stop all this…”

 

I shook my head, sighing. “James, please… You’ve been trying to wrap your head around that formula since this happened in the first place. There’s no way you can finish it in time. We can’t do it the Jason Armos way anymore, James. There won’t be a miraculous cure. No heroic rescue.”

Silver's POV: The Tears of Avlakyli Part 11

Options
  • Mark as New
  • Bookmark
  • Subscribe to RSS Feed
  • Highlight
  • Print

‎06-12-2013 04:54 PM

 

“Heroes to the end!” I yelled, throwing my arms up in hopelessness, “That’s what I said before, wasn’t it? Not anymore. I can’t be a hero now. I can’t be Avlakyli. Not at the end.”

 

“Silver!” James reached out to try to calm me, “Please, don’t leave me like this. Not like this. If you know you’re…If you know you only have a few months left…why are you trying to close yourself off from me? Wouldn’t you rather live your last months to the fullest?”

 

I gave him a weak smile and tried to dry my tears. “I wish I could, James. But I can’t. Because anything and everything I do now will affect everyone else after I’m gone, and because…I don’t want to hurt you.”

 

“Hurt me? Silver…”

 

“James…when I’m gone, you need to carry on in my stead. It will be up to you to stop Black Knight. Please, promise me…promise me you won’t let my passing keep you from doing what you have to. That’s why I can’t be close to you, James. Because I don’t want you to grieve over me. I just want to say goodbye.”
