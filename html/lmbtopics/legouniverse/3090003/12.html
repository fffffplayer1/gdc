JAMMERS ON! Grievesmad7 ONLY!
Silver's secret name is "The Guarding Dark".
JAMMERS OFF!

JAMMERS ON HEROES TO THE END WRITERS ONLY
OK here is my plan. Silver uses James' secret name to stop him. James' dreams continue to be spent in communication with Horius. When Silver releases him Horius fuses with James. I will write a special Horius' POV : My host .
When Silver , S and Sharpie try to find a cure they realize that Silver finding out the secret name of Horius ( Born from Chaos ) can give her the extra power to put me back in control of my body. However since no mortal can posses more than one secret name , Silver has to give up knowing James' secret name using the spell ______ I give you back your name. ( Note : the _____ was in place of the name ( not secret ). ) However Silver gets injured in the spell. James tries everything he knows but realizes that a healing spell containing her secret name is all that could save her. James is forced to use telepathy to ask her. As he does he sees the entire life of Silver and doing so not only discovers her secret name but realizes from seeing how much S cared for Silver that pirates are not all bad. James heals Silver and agrees to let them stay in Titainia until they find a new home and reveals that he loves Silver. The Titans , Khione ( Froststar ) and Mag Mel ( Darkstar )
take the carnage as a declaration of war. The war rages and the Nexus Force also show up. StarClan accepts Lupa's ( Wolfstar ) alliance and fight. Khione is thrown from the throne ( get it ) and Silver takes over in her place. Mag Mel is also given the boot.
Zatorak fights and ( since he is a bad guy and if Zatorak allows it ) gets smashed.
END OF BOOK 1
JAMMERS OFF
With some alterations ( tell you later ) TURBOBUILD might make it in book 2.

YEAH! I like it! Now those are plans I can work to!
