( Or Zeth as he was then known ). After NightFlash defeating Mora's Termite-tron which threatened to devour his home planet , Shival strips Mora of her childhood by transforming her into adult acrobatic fighting expert , Morgana ( Mor-gah-na ). Morgana forces Reene ( Spelling? ) into spying for Shival in a desperate attempt to reagain her childhood. Shival reveals that he will only turn Morgana back into Mora if she captures Z.E.T.H. so Morgana unleashes the Mutant Mythran to buy time but in mid-capture of Z.E.T.H. , NightFlash returns so she is forced to call on her big sister , Toxica to bring it back to life. But bigger. Morgana captures Z.E.T.H and releases Goradon. She is then returned to being Mora. NightFlash manages to destroy Goradon but his ship takes heavy damage and he crashes on the Junkpile ( It was called that , right. I need to brush up on the story a bit. )
I need your approval to start the story known as Zeth so can you tell me if you approve this idea ?

Ҟαʂℯў ℣ℴℓτℯҟ

Lol, Night is the main character. The story takes place in a first person view, so he's the one who "narrates." The trilogy is set in the future of the Universe when the Maelstrom was destroyed by the power-hungry Mythrans. (This is what happens before the first book which I am currently writing) The Mythrans decided that they were all-powerful because they destroyed the Maelstrom. Or so they thought... Greg "Seven" Cattivo, gun for hire, was sent on a scouting mission with his team "The Grays" on Avant Gardens and that's where he found the barely-alive SPIDER QUEEN. The Leader of the Mythrans, Theed Faine, wanted to take the glory so he froze the Spider Queen (FROZE, not smashed) and smashed all of The Grays. Seven escaped, but no one found out that Theed Faine had disbanded the team that had gone through many hardships for the Universe. Then the Mythrans became... slightly evil. They tried to make the "bad" minifigures their slaves, and thats where Night comes in.

He was imprisoned on Junker Mountain. SO, that's the time period before the Trilogy. Basically, the first things that happen are these: Night and his girlfriend (Not a HUGE relationship, very small) are imprisoned for an accident they accidently caused, break out, and *covers mouth to stop talking* Ah, that's better. Don't wanna give away TOO many spoilers, DO I?
