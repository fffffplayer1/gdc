Chapter 2

 

The boat continued on through the water.
"I didn't know you had a sister." I said to Elite.
"Step sister." He corrected. "Same mother, though the Darkitecht wasn't her father. She's a few years younger than me."
I thought for a moment before remembering that Elite was 36.
"They argue just about every second." Dextron informed me.
"She's just jealous of this."
Elite held out his palm and a small purple flame flickered for a second before dying out.
"Actually my powers are virtually non existent now that my dad and the Maelstrom are gone." He said, looking disappointedly at his hand. "It's for the best though, I suppose."
Twenty minutes later we arrived at the end of an empty stretch of Nexus Beach and left the boat.
"We're just south of Nexus City. Don't let anyone see you." Dextron said.
"There's nobody around to see us." I observed.
"You made it." Someone said behind us.
We spun round, Elite's hand going to his sword, then he relaxed.
"No need to get hostile." The newcomer raised her hands.
She was dressed similarly to Elite, though her hood was down revealing shoulder length black hair which was streaked with a reddish brown colour. Unlike Elite, she looked friendly and like she wouldn't snap at you for the slightest joke. Then again she hadn't been through what Elite had, being the son of the universe's greatest threat.
"Freya. How many times, don't sneak up on me." Elite grunted.
"Whatever." Freya turned to me and smiled. "You must be Xeryon." She held out her hand.
I shook her hand, realising that I was nearly a foot taller than her.
"I need to know whether I can trust your husband and his men." I told her.

Chapter 2, part 2

 

Freya looked puzzled. "Husband? Since when was I married?"
I noticed Elite smirking and so did Freya. She turned to her step brother.
"What did you tell him?" She asked, looking annoyed.
"Nothing." Elite replied.
Freya turned back to me. "What did he say?"
"He said you were married to Numb Chuck." I said.
Freya punched Elite on the arm.
"No I'm not married. Numb Chuck isn't even around anymore. He left a few years ago on a spiritual journey or something." Freya said.
"She does have authority in Forbidden Valley though." Elite said rubbing his arm.
"Yeah. Numb made me head ninja before he went." Freya agreed.
She pulled out a small touch screen device.
"A phone?" I asked.
"No, you've been out of it for a while Xeryon." Dextron said. "Its a teleporter. Created by the Nexus Force. They've been experimenting with new tech."
"This is actually the only functional one in existence." Elite added.
"How did you get hold of it then?" I asked.
"I... ah, borrowed it."
Freya tapped the screen a few times and a second later there was a flash and I had a strange feeling of falling before everything became clear again and the hard ground sent jolts through my legs. We had arrived in the middle of a village. Ninjas dressed in black, red or white outfits walked about their business. They stopped and turned towards us before bowing in Freya's direction.
"Welcome back, Elite." One of them said to her.
Freya smiled while Elite scowled.
"Still stealing my codename I see." He grunted.
Elite and Freya argued as Dextron led the way forwards.

The Paradox refinery has been decommissioned and Freya actually has her own personal quarters in there. Its also where the ninjas meet up to socialise as the main room has been turned into a sort of cafe."

Chapter 3

 

Freya's POV

 

A few hours later I was leaning against a wall, arms crossed and watching Xeryon and Dextron practise with katanas. Xeryon's appearance matched what Elite had told me over the years. He was about 6 foot tall and muscular. He had a black buzzcut and a short beard. Clearly his sword skills had declined as Dextron was nearly always parrying his strikes. A few minutes later two ninjas clad in white approached me.
"Zero is here!" One of them gasped, out of breath.
Dextron and Xeryon looked over.
"Zero? Here? He must know." Dextron said. "We need to hide."
"I know I do but why do you?" Xeryon asked.
"Zero isn't the biggest fan of me. I'll explain later but it's to do with the fact that I pulled off a heist with you."
"Oh yeah."
The two of them ran behind the refinery building. I looked to the two ninjas.
"Act like I'm teaching you." I said.
The ninjas brought out their swords and stood as if they were about to fight. That's when Zero arrived. He walked purposefully towards us. He was dressed in the full R3 space marauder gear, helmet in his arms and pistol holstered at his side. His ligt brown hair was medium length with a combed back fringe and he had a scar from the middle of his left cheek down to his neck. He was accompanied by two other space marauders, these two had their helmets on and- worringly- their Wormholers in their hands.
"Freya." He said.
"Zero."
"I was on a mission earlier today."
"Oh really?"
"My team and I were at a certain resort. We were searching for Xeryon Navarro."

Chapter 3, part 2

 

I had a feeling I knew where this was going. Unfortunatly I was correct.
"But when I got there Xeryon was nowhere to be found." Zero continued.
"Unlucky for you. I doubt he was even there I'm the first place. Nobody's heard from him in years."
"I didn't see Xeryon but two of my men said they were attacked by some faces I'm sure will be familiar to you."
I already knew who he was talking about.
"They matched the description of your brother and your friend Dextron. My men told me they were with an individual that looked suspiciously like a certain wanted criminal I know." Zero's voice had lost its friendly tone.
"Look, I've got no idea what you're talking about. Elite and Dextron have been here helping me train my ninjas. I'm not a master like Numb Chuck so I need help. Perhaps since you're so obsessed with trying to find people no one's heard from in years, you could try to find him instead of Xeryon who is obviously long gone."
I couldn't resist adding that last bit and I got some satisfaction from the look of annoyance that crossed Zero's face.
"Don't think for a second that-"
"Sir." One of his guards interrupted. "VO is interfering again. We need to leave."
"Fine!" Zero shouted in annoyance. "We're not done Freya. I'll be back."
And with that the three of them walked away. I ran a hand through my hair and sighed in relief.
"Close one huh?" Elite said appearing next to me. "I saw him arrive and had to hide."
"Too close." I agreed. "We won't be so lucky next time."
Xeryon and Dextron emerged from behind the refinery.
"What's VO and why is Zero so desperate to find me after all these years?" Xeryon asked.
"Time to fill you in on what's been happening." Dextron replied.

Five minutes later we were all sat around a table in the cafe area. It was a medium sized place with about twelve tables.

Chapter 3, part 3

 

Okay." Elite began. "A couple of years after you disappeared, that's three years after the Maelstrom War the NF decided a council should be in place to take charge of the Nimbus System so one was elected. We now have a president- Alfred Overwell. And he runs the council of ten people. They have ultimate power over us all. It was fine at first but in the last five years they reduced the NF significantly and they've brought in a private force to do military work for them. The NF is essentially sitting on the sidelines with only a few squads who still operate regularly. Nexus Tower is still operational though."
"You're not going to like the next part." Dextron said quietly.
"Great." Xeryon muttered.
"The leader of this private force is none other than Vor. Hence the name VO, Vor Ops." Elite continued.
"You're kidding? How did that clown get out of prison? I altered his records when I escaped. He should be in there until 2078."
"Yeah, about that. Do you even know what Vor was arrested for?"
"No. Why does it matter?"
"Because Xeryon, people generally don't go to prison fo decades just for stealing a car."
Xeryon was speechless. "Oh" he said simply.
"How do you know Vor?" I asked.
"He was in my mercenary group. But he tried to sell us out to a rival and we walked into an ambush. I exiled him and met him again in prison. Hard to believe he's running his own PF now."
"And to tell you why Zero is so eager to catch you, he originally gave up his search but later he was reprimanded by the council for his failure to bring you to justice and he's been out for you ever since." Added Elite.
"As you can tell the council are not nice people." I said.
"I want to investigate VO." Xeryon announced. "I want to see what's going on for myself."
"I'm in." Said Dextron. "I've been wanting to do so for a while now."
"It'll be just like old times." Xeryon nodded.
"I'm in too." Elite and I said simultaneously.
We both smiled at that.
"So, that shopping list I was telling you about...?"
