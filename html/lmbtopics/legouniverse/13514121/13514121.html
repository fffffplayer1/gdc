Kra Koro Tech:

 

Shadow shielding-

A generator producing light to heavy volatile shadow energy. The energy is projected around the unit through a serise of tiny wires ending in projectors. The wires channel the energies which are then projected a short distance in front of the unit being shielded. The shield, when projected, is a continuous flow of volatile energy; any rupture in the stream will result in the channel being interrupted, and failing. The energy is well capable of disentagrating most solid objects on its highest setting, and any energies that come into contact with it will experience a structural break down. The unit being shielded can usually manipulate which wires channel the energy, thus allowing them to maipulate which areas of the ship are shielded.

Shadow Shielding Mk II-

Most shadow shield generators incorporate Kahgarak inspired field of shadows projectors. This enchanced shielding requires substantially more energy to operate, as in order to project a stable one way opening to the shadow zone, a steady flow of energy must be maintained. If the energy is shut off, the opening will fade slowly before closing.

The shielding essentially surrounds the unit with multiple, stable, one way rips to the shadow zone. The one way nature allows the unit to send attacks through the rips, but incoming attacks will enter through the opening and enter the shadow zone. 

Depending upon the amount of energy available, a MK. II shadow shield can project a shadow signal spike at an area or target of its choice. The generator will  shoot Kahgarak energies at light speed to a target, and the energies will open up rips to the shadow zone in the space it travels through. When the signal spike is used, observers will see a thick streak of darkness shoot forward like lightning toward the targeted area. The darkness will be the visable rip to the shadow zone. Anything caught in the path of the shadow spike will enter the shadow zone, and will be trapped when the spike dies away and vanishes. Most units will not have the energy required to use a shadow spike.

Product of Idrisk research

 

Hunger Shielding-

A generator making use of wiring to channel hunger energies through a unit and project them a distance from the shielded unit. The hunger energies can be projected quite a distance from the unit when neccessary. All incoming force/energy that makes contact with the energies will have its force/energy, drained and rerouted into the units system. This often means that the unit can use incoming attacks to bolster its own weapons, or shields.

 

Molecular Disruption Shielding-

A generator making use of wiring to channel molecular disruptione energies through a unit and project those energies a short distance around the unit. Generally only used on immobile fortifications due to the amount of power needed to sustain the shield. Once agin, areas being shielded can be changed and manipulated. Once a molecular shield circut is breached it will fail in the area it was breached. Long recharge and restart times are typical for this generator.

Dread Wing family of flying wings.

 

Dread Wing:

 

Appearence- A Massive, dead black flying wing.

Size- Emmense. Akin to a flying fortress. Slightly larger then an Ironclad Mk I warship.

Weaponary- The Dread Wing possesses few dedicated exterior weapons systems, instead allowing room for interior weapons to be stored and operated from within the craft.

 

There are, however, three exterior flak turrets positioned on top of the Dread Wing. One in a central position, and the other two further off to the right, and left of it. 

 

Interior weapons.

A Dread Wing will generally be outfitted with a total of twenty four interior cannons. These cannons will be split into two groups of twelve, which are placed in long, connected rooms spanning the forward right, and left sides of the Dread Wing. The forward facing right and left sides of the Dread Wing have openings through which each cannon can fire through. The openings are closeable, and the cannons, once rolled up, are capable of locking into position.

 

Characteristics.

 

A Dread Wing will have rooms for storage mostly in its rear and middle rooms. The storage will range from quarters for organic troops (these rooms generally towards the center of the craft) and hangers for assorted supplies or tech. The very large power generator is positioned in the very middle of the craft, providing constant electrcity to power the crafts functions. The Shadow shielding generator is positioned slightly off center in the ship, and a little ways behind the power generator to the left.

 

The Dread Wing has six engines protruding from its rear.

Shadow Wings:

 

Appearence-

Smaller relatives of the Dread Wings. Moderately sized black, flying wings with jagged rear wing tips.

Size: Wing is slightly wider than three Toa Metru lying lengthwise. It is about one Toa metru from front tip to rear. A very wide, flat and sleek like ship.

 

Characteristics-

Possesses shadow shielding. The craft itself acts as a shell for Kraata to operate within. Making use of embedded interior Kraata tubes, one craft can host up to two kraata, giving it the inate abilities of those Kraata. This design makes the craft a direct extension of the pilot.

The craft possesses twin engines which can be enchanced by kanoka speed energies.

 

Weaponary-

A Shadow Wing possesses twin needle cannons capable of firing off rapid fire sprays of shadow blast energies. The Wing also has two embedded weapon tubes which can host a variety of specific weapons.
