Part 1

 

Speech felt a bit awkward among all those minifigures. Not only was he Maelstrom, but he also was of no use. Or at least that was what he thought. He had now sat at a shadowy corner almost invisible and unnoticed even if one looked directly at him.

"Interesting!!!" someone next to him said, scaring and surprising him.

Speech turned around to see who had talked and was completely surprised by what he saw. A Mythran was standing in front of him, with his gentle light blue eyes looking at him. Speech looked at the other and was shocked to see that they hadn't even notice the light blue shining creature.

"They can't see me!" the Mythran said with his gentle and beautiful voice "I'm only visible to you at the moment!"

"Who... who art thou, kind sir?" Speech asked trying to talk politely as he usually did.

"Just a Mythran! Name's not important!"

"What... what dost thou seek here?"

"Well, a Mythran friend of mine was sent to see what damage had caused that crazed Chaos man: Pandemonium!" said the Mythran with his eyes showing disgust, as he said the name of Pandemonium "While he did that, he told us that he saw a civilised and talking Darkling! I bet that there couldn't be such a thing and now I have come to see whether you are real or not! It seems I owe Fantas 10 Imagi-coins!"
