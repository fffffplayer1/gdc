Name: Neo

 

Appearance: Neo has black hair. He have 2 suits. The 1st 1 is : Green Tunic with brown belt, black cape which can absorb weapon blasts (up to an extent), light leather armor,  , and under my tunic, rare light but extremely strong grey armor. My 2nd suit is the Assembly Rank 3 Summoner Suit. A third uniform 

 

Weapons: Unifrom 1: custom spear which can shoot ice bolts, and 2 imagination blasters, and 10 shurikens Uniform 2: Rank 3 Summoner Staff, ability to summon monsters Unifrom 3: Katana, 2 Shurikens of ice, twin daggers.

 

Personality: Has a normally cheerful and upbeat personality. Neo's flaw is that he's afraid of Maelstorm Dragons, which smashed his friend, and if he thinks he's in a desperate situation, will run, retreat, or surrender.

 

History: Used to be an Assembly Rank 3 Summonder, then, after LU (the game) ended, Neo joined the LU Survivors. There Neo learned to be a splendid and accomplished pilot, even though he was already good.  When the Survivors went inactive he took a break from the Maelstorm, but now has decided to return. Is now a Rank 3 Summoner.

 

Skills: Isn't a well fighter, but Neo can use his staff to heal himself quickly and can summon monsters to protect himself. Also a ninja,  he's stealthy and can easily go unnoticed. Neo's greatest skill however, is a pilot

 

I'm also a member of the Rangers.
