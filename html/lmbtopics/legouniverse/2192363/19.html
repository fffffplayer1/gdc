Chapter 80: Arrival at the Temple

“Shadow!” Magical, Katie and Sarah exclaimed together “You smashed the Darkitect! He won't chase us anymore”
“No, he's not dead. His spirit stays alive as long as the Maelstrom exists. That's why I have to go to the Pieces of the First Builders' Temple. If I am right his plan is the Maelstrom Vortex to throw Corrupting Lightnings at there. There's a crack at the Temple's western side from which the lightnings can pass. So, I will go there and stop them, if I can” Shadow told them.
“We will come with you!” Magical answered.
“No, you shall not!” Shadow disagreed Once when I was at the Battle of Nimbus Station I found a cave. The cave was called 'The cave of Chaos Recreation'. I searched for it in the Nexus HQ and found out that this is the place where some very strong Maelstrom Beings, such as the named ones, are recreated. The room next to this is another cave like that one. I'm sure that the Darkitect will appear in one of those two. When they first appear at the caves, the Maelstrom Beings are in a weaker form that looks like a ghost of the Being. If you smash it, at that form it will never be recreated again. Sarah and Magical stay in that one. Katie, go to the Castle Kingdom take Dragon with you and go to Nimbus Station. Search in your F-phone's map for its location. Once you have smashed him, send help. I don't know if I will be able to stop the Maelstrom on my own. If my calculations are right he will be recreated in about two hours. Good luck”
“Ok” they all agreed, although they didn't want to leave Shadow alone.
Shadow pushed a button on his ice saw handle and it became a crossbow again. He threw an arrow with a rope on the room above, so that he and Katie would climb there, because they were sure they wouldn't be able to pass through the hall again. They climbed the rope and went upstairs. They found a window and jumped out of it. They fell out of the Maelstrom Tower, but didn't hurt themselves. They took their rockets and after Shadow built a Launch Pad they took off.
They passed through the clouds and kept flying. They were going as fast as they could and after half an hour they reached the Pieces of the First Builders' Temple. They said “Good Luck” simultaneously and then Katie left. However, before Shadow got in a Maesltrom Lightning came and passed through a crack. Shadow passed through another and saw what was happening.
The lightning would normally fall into space and would not corrupt anything. However, to prevent this F-F-G was there and was directing the lightning. He had already corrupted a lot of Mythrans. Shadow took his back up shield and landed.
“Four-Faced Guy, stop this madness!” Shadow shouted.
However, F-F-G just moved his hand and Shadow flew away and hit a wall. Shadow didn't know what to do, apparently the Darkitect's Servant wouldn't let him even to approach him. Then, suddenly all his pain left. Something was touching him on the forehead. He looked up and saw a Mythran. Pictures were passing before his eyes, but they were like a language. And it was saying:
“I am sorry, Strange Odd Shadow that I can't talk to you to your language, but Mythrans do not speak any language. They just use pictures. Shadow, you are our hope. We, Mythrans, have power, but we can't use them to fight, because fighting is a kind of chaos. However, you can! Unfortunately, sometimes the only way to fight chaos is with chaos. This is one of the minifigures' role. Protect the Imagination, protect us, protect the Universe”
The Mythran stopped and went to his “seat” and waited there. However, F-F-G had seen that. So, while Shadow was standing up he directed the lightning towards him. However, when the Mythran had touched him, he had given him Imagination, as it always happens if a Mythran touches you. So, when Shadow, dizzy by the too much Imagination, raised his arms the lightning chnaged direction and hit the Black-Robed-Man. That was his end.
However, this wasn't the only result of Shadow's too much Imagination. As he had raised his hands the pieces of the temple started to connect with each other until it was like the Temple was never split. However, the lightning of the Maelstrom that was still coming hit what used to cover the Corrupted Imagination Nexus, destroying it and uncovering the Corruped Imagination. . .

Chapter 81: At the Cave of Chaos Recreation Part 2

“ENOUGH!!!” he shouted and with one move of his hands Dragon flew away. Then, some rocks left the ceiling, floated in the air for a moment and then moved towards them. Both of them smashed incoming rocks each one with his weapon, but one managed to hit Katie throwing her on the floor. She tried to stand up, but didn't manage to do it. Those rocks were something more than just rocks. She looked up and saw the Darkitect above her. He was smiling evilly. Katie couldn't see Dragon around, but she hoped, he would come. But, he didn't come and the Darkitect, after taking off his glove, touched her on the neck. It was just terrible, feeling her cells being destroyed. She couldn't do anything and she would probably smash in a minute. Then, her neck started being infected. This made things even worse. Before the Darkitect's touch was too hot, but now due to the infection she felt both like she was being burnt and like she was buried in snow. Afterwards she felt she was fading. She felt her mind starting to sleep and her soul being reduced. Her heartbeats were becoming more slow. She closed her eyes. She was leaving this universe forever...
Then, suddenly her pain stopped. Her mind woke up, her soul became normal, her heart beat normally. She opened her eyes ans, although everything was blurred, she realized that two humans were above her.
“Dragon?” she whispered weakly.
“No, I'm not Dragon, but I can help, too” someone with a bass voice answered.
Suddenly, her sight became normal again. Saber Jetpack Cyborg was standing there. His sword was colliding with a purple sword that the Darkitect was holding. Katie tried to stand up once more, but was unsuccessful again. Cyborg was having difficulties to block the Darkitect's attacks. She searched for her weapons and found out that her flamethrower was close enough to grab it, as she did. As Katie was launching fire more Knights emerged. However, it was still difficult to beat him. The Darkitect kicked Katie and her weapon left her hand. He threw up fire once more and two Knights caught fire. Then, he released some more of the Maelstrom Mist which caught fire from the two Knights creating an explosion, too. This caused the ceiling to collapse at several places. Some rocks fell on the Darkitect, but they just smashed leaving him as he was. Then, Admiral Flogmore and Butterscorch, the Dragon appeared from the depths of the cave. Most Knights went to the Butterscorch thinking it was more dangerous than the Darkitect.
“Things can't go worse” Katie thought. However, she couldn't be more wrong. The ceiling, the walls and the ground cracked and purple lava came out of the cracks. Most Knights, being terrified, left the cave. “We're doomed” Katie thought and closed her eyes again.
She kept hearing the sound of the battle until suddenly she heard the roars of the Butterscorch. Thinking, because of the echo, that the Dragon Invader was next to her she opened her eyes. What she saw was far different from what she expected to see. Magical Heroic Dragon had returned and he had just stabbed Butterscorch on the neck just behind the head. Flogmore was escaping. Cyborg's left hand was infected, but he was okay and still was fighting with the Darkitect. No other Knight was in sight.
Dragon approached them because Butterscorch had passed out. “Sorry I'm late!” he said and then did the weirdest thing that anyone could imagine he would do: he hugged the Darkitect. Katie stared at him, with bulging eyes. Then, she felt the desire to laugh, but at that time the Darkitect screamed from pain. The Imagination Iron was hurting because it was containing Ganium while he was made of Gunium. He, then, was stabbed by both Knights a lot of times.
“If you though that a snake could beat me, you're really mistaken. Even if it was huge” Dragon said.
“You will regret this!!!” the Darkitect responded “I'll punish you!!!”
“How will this happen if you are smashed!” Dragon laughed at him.
“I won't stay that way for long!!!” That was the Darkitect's last words before he was turned into dust. . .

Chapter 81: The battle of the Temple

Things were bad. Just when the Corrupted Imagination was released, Maelstrom Beings of all the kinds came out of it. Shadow shot lots of them with his crossbow, but until he smashed one, ten more had appeared, so that soon the temple was full of them. The normal Mythrans were stopping the Beings from leaving, but couldn’t do anything else. The Beings reached Shadow, so he used the Ice Saw version of his weapon.
He smashed two Stromlings with one move, but three Stromling Pirates appeared. Shadow hit them with his Saw, but weren’t smashed. Before he could hit them again, two Maelstrom Scorpions appeared and one Maeltorm Snake coiled around his leg. Knowing he couldn’t beat them all with the saw he threw a Huge One, a mini-bomb like the Big One, but better. Only the Snake survived from those beings and Shadow cut his head with his saw, although not before it bit him. Some Dark Ronins came and they hit him on the head. Shadow kicked the one throwing it on the ground, spiked two of them and disinfected the rest with a Disinfecting Grenade.
More creatures came. Shadow fought many kinds of them. Some Admirals hit his arm with their hammers. Some Maelstrom Knights stabbed him at the stomach. He smashed some Horsemen and Riders. An Ape hit a Mythran trying to escape, but the only thing that happened was to be disinfected. Some Stromling Gorillas (they’re like Stromling Apes, but twice as big) threw him some Hammer Stromlings, breaking some of his ribs.
Suddenly, the Maelstrom Beings all screamed. They panicked and started running chaotically. If they crashed one of the Beings would smash the other. At that time Shadow found the chance to smash many of them with arrows, because it was like they forgot him. He knew what had made this happen. It was their leader’s end that made them panick.
“Thank brick, at least we won’t see him again, but I don’t think they’ll see me again either” Shadow said, because the Beings had started calming down.
Then, a big Maelstrom Dragon appeared and roared.
“Oh, brick!”
The Dragon flew and landed in front of Shadow. The other creatures afraid of it didn’t approach. The Dragon opened its mouth and purple fire came out of it. Shadow protected himself with his shield, but from the great heat, he almost threw it. The Dragon charged at him and bit almost ate his leg.
“NO!” Shadow yelled and when it opened its mouth wanting to eat his leg, Shadow threw a poisonous arrow on its eye. The Dragon raised quickly its head belching fire. Shadow launched more arrows, but the flakes of the Dragon were like armour made of steel.
In the meantime a Dragon even bigger than the first one came out of the Corrupted Imagination. This one attacked with its breath of fire the Mythrans. It managed to make a passage through them and left. A lot of the other Beings followed it, before the passage was closed once more.
As the Dragon was moving uncontrollably Shadow hit it on the stomach where there were no flakes. Furious the Dragon attacked at his left hand with its mouth. Shadow heard it chewing his shield. He felt warm blood scrolling on his left arm and great pain. However, he had no time to have a look at it. The crazy Dragon was attacking again with purple fire. He caught fire on the shoulder.
Then, Shadow did the only thing he could do. He put a special arrow on his crossbow. At the one side it had a light blue sign of the Nexus Force and from the other a green capital F. Both signs were shining. As the Dragon opened its mouth again, Shadow threw the arrow in it. A huge explosion of light blue, purple and green fires occurred. The flames reached him and then nothing. . .

Chapter 83: At the Hospital Part 1

Shadow woke up. He felt his whole body aching. He could hear something coming from far. He thought it was like singing, but he couldn’t hear well. He opened his eyes. Everything was blurred. He could see two green and two shining light blue little circles above him.
“What’s that sound?” he asked weakly as he saw that.
“SHADOW! You are awake! I’m so happy! All this time we thought that you might die!” he heard Katie’s voice talking.
“Kate, are those two little green spots your eyes?” Shadow asked.
“What?! Yes!” Katie answered “Shadow, we did it! We smashed the Darkitect! We would have smashed, but Cyborg, who suspected that we were doing something wrong, followed us and saved us! Maybe we should consider to put him in the team. When he is not so strict he is a good guy”
“Kate, don’t talk that fast. You’re making me dizzy! More than I am normally at least” Shadow told her.
“Sorry!”
“What’s that sound?” Shadow asked again.
“What sound? Oh, that! They’re Christmas songs! It’s Christmas! Did you know?! After all those days I had really forgotten the date. Then, yesterday when I brought you here I saw all the decoration and realized what day it was” Katie said.
The facts of the previous day started coming to Shadow’s mind and then suddenly “The Mythrans! The Temple! What happened?!”
“Calm down, Strange Odd Shadow. Everything is under control” another calm voice said “The explosion you created contained Maelstrom, Imagination and another kind of power I don’t know what it was. It created the separation of the temple, the disinfection of the Mythrans and the smash of the Maelstrom Beings. I have to say that you were lucky that your friends were there when the explosion occurred. You would have died if it weren’t for them”
“Dr. Overbuild!” Shadow exclaimed “What are you doing here?!”
“I’m assuring you and your friends are okay!” Overbuild said “I have to say that your friend Kate was the less injured although, she had a curse. And when you came here you were a mess, the worst of all”

Chapter 83: At the Hospital Part 2

“My friends!” Shadow remembered “What happened to Sarah and Magical?!”
“We’re okay, Shadow! And I wondered when you would remember us!” Sarah’s bored voice said.
At that time Shadow’s eyesight got better and he noticed that Sarah and Magical were behind Kate and Dr. Overbuild. Sarah’s hand was infected and Magical had scratches and cuts all over his body. They were wearing white clothes of the hospital and so did Katie.
“Sarah! Your hand is infected!” Shadow exclaimed.
“Look at your own mess, Shadow! Besides I was pretty worse when I came here. Almost half my body was infected. The Disinfecters did what they could, but the Infection in my hand was so deep that it will always have a Maelstrom Aura. However, don’t worry! It’s Infection of zero grade. It has no infecting power. Your hand, however, is worse than mine!” Sarah explained.
Shadow looked at his left hand and saw something he wasn’t expecting: “It’s missing! Where’s my hand?!” he exclaimed
“It was already missing when we found you” Katie said.
“Oh my brick! The Dragon must have eaten it with my shield!” Shadow realized “That’s why I felt blood scrolling after it attacked me!”

However, some of them didn't forget it and when they heard it again and saw who it belonged to, they instantly knew who he really was. But this happened lots of years later, so we won't speak of it now.
Once the voice stopped talking, panic prevailed. Shadow heard people screaming and running. They thought that the last day of the Universe had come. Actually, that became a joke and when people overreacted about a bad situation the others used to say “You act like the Day of the Voice came!”. However, it certainly wasn't the end. On the contrary it was just the start of much pain and danger and many adventures. From the words of the Darkitect the Nexus Forcers figured out many things, like that the Darkitect had been smashed. That's why since then many people celebrated the 24th of Endecember* as the Day of the Darkitect's End. However, Shadow didn't celebrate that day, because he knew that the Darkitect had not been smashed forever. . .

*Endecemer= The 13th month of the Crux' calendar. The Crux System and the Crux Planet before it exploded had a calendar with 13 month. Each month had 28 days, except for September, which had 29, for unknown reasons. Every leap year, August had also 29 days. In Crux they celebrated Christmas at the 25th of Endecember instead of the 25th of December. That's why the incidents of those chapters are happening at the Endecember instead of December.
