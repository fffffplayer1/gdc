Chapter 7: Off to the next mission! Part 3

 

          “Where were you?” she asked before sipping some orange juice.

          “I visited Hael Storm to take a list of the missing people since the Great Battle! I thought we might check their properties as well!” Shadow replied.

          “That’s all good, but shouldn’t we wait for the results of Sarah’s examination before we go and check all of these properties!”

          “We should! That’s why, for now, we are visiting a very particular Block Yard property! I forgot to mention I also met Nirwell and had a talk with her! Unfortunately, her doctor found us before she could tell me the whole story, but she will soon! Besides we’ve got enough clues to continue our search!” Shadow said and described to Kate Nirwell’s story.

          “Don’t you think we’ve had too much of properties these days?” Kate asked.

          “Yes, I do! And that’s one more reason I think the two cases are connected!” Shadow responded.

          “You mean Ebasha’s and Nirwell’s?” Kate enquired “That there is someone who systematically abducts people and destroys their properties?”

          “It is an assumption!” Shadow told her “By the way, I think we should take Magical with us! We might meet someone aggressive!”

          “Okay! On another note, Shadow, you can still talk to me! You know, about the Great Battle!”

          Shadow avoided her eyes and an awkward silence followed.

 

          Magical was snoring loudly. Really loudly! It made Shadow think that Sarah must have complained about this at some point!

          “Magical, will you wake up any time soon?!” Shadow almost yelled in his ear while at the same time pushed him on the shoulder.

Chapter 7: Off to the next mission! Part 4

 

          “Wait, what? What…?!” Magical was surprised “Did they assault the Northern Tower?!”

          He looked at Shadow for a few seconds. Then, at Kate. Then, at the room.

          “So, what is it?” he asked after making sure he was in the Nexus Tower and yawning.

          “Perhaps, you would like to join us in our next mission!” Shadow suggested.

          “Why don’t you two wait outside while I get dressed, then let me eat something and I might consider your offer!” Magical told them.

          After Magical was ready, they headed for the launch pads. Shadow filled in Magical in what they had done yesterday and the morning. Then, they were back in their rockets. In the months that had passed since Shadow joined the Nexus Force, rocket-travelling had not become less unpleasant for him and that made their constant travelling from one place to another all the more annoying.

The end of the Block Yard area also constitutes the end of the Nimbus System on that side. Next to it is the Zephyr Asteroid Field, an area unexplored due to the many accidents caused in it. Chapter 8: In the property farthest away Part 1

 

          The three minifigures landed on the property. Contrary to most Block Yard properties, that one was dark, with little light reaching the place. The three friends walked cautiously towards the interior of the planet chunk. They found what seemed like a mansion.

          There was a pool and a garden full of beautiful flowers, bushes and some trees. The building itself was a structure of fine architecture, perhaps three storeys tall and wide enough to contain quite many rooms.

          “This garden desperately needs a gnome or two, don’t you think?” Shadow commented in whispers tongue-smiling.

          “It would certainly cheer the place up a bit!” Magical replied.

          They walked towards the double leaf door of the villa. Shadow got something from his backpack. It looked like a penknife and a key at the same time. He placed it on the keyhole and after some struggling with it the sound of unlocking was heard. The door slid open and they entered. Kate coming in last did not close the door; they might have to leave hastily and a closed door would be a problem.

 

          The interior of the house was even darker, with the only light coming in from occasional windows. They were even more cautious this time and even tried not to make the planks creak. Every time it happened –and it was way more often than they’d like– they stood still trying to listen to any movement.

He passed amongst the rocks with ease, while his pursuers were moving clumsily through the dangerous asteroids. The chase continued, but the minifigure was getting further and further away.
