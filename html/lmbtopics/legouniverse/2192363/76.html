Chapter 1: The start of another day Part 1

 

          Strange Odd Shadow woke up from yet another nightmare. He was having these a lot lately. Lately being the last seven months. In the start, his nightmares mostly consisted of stuff that happened to him in Yorkanton City and during his first adventure in which he confronted the Darkitect as well as scenes of pain of others which the Darkitect had shared with him.

          Now, however, these nightmares were becoming rarer and rarer. They had been replaced much by scenes of the Great Battle that had occurred one month ago. These weren't always the real ones. He would also see things that could have happened in it. There was also a really horrifying one where Captain Brickbeard, Cyborg, Quantum, Dracula and all that had sacrificed for him in that battle turned against him and accused him. Of course Quantum and Dracula had survived in the end, but this didn't matter in his dreams.

          There was also another matter that troubled him in his sleep. It was the return of the Darkitect. Shadow couldn't imagine what he could do now that he was back. Perhaps, there would be a repetition of the original chase that had acquainted him with the Darkitect. And this time they couldn't win since the Leader of Maelstrom had gained the ability to possess bodies. Every time he was smashed, he could just possess a new body. Related to that, was also the infection of the Imagination Shard that he had found in Deadly Desert (scenes from which haunted him, too).

Chapter 1: The start of another day Part 2

 

          But there was also another dream he was having. To someone else this might have seemed frightening, too, but to Shadow it was a relief from the other dreams of his and felt somehow relaxing by its nature. It was a memory of these moments he had passed in a two-dimensional-like state. After the Great Battle, Shadow had spent much time to find out what this was. Shadow thought it might be related to his power to camouflage in the shadows. He knew that Superians' abilities could grow and develop. This might have been a development of his power.

          Shadow got up. He was alone in his bed. Kate, his old friend from Yorkanton whom he had married, had left already. She always woke up early this time. She had found her long-lost sister during the battle in the Maelstrom Lair, Maelstrom's base. She had saved her and the other prisoners and they now were protected by the Nexus Force. However, after the long period of her imprisonment little Anne was quite scarred. Kate was trying to take care of her as better as she could. She got up early to be there when the girl would wake up.

          Shadow had found out something else interesting about the girl. Her long imprisonment there was very peculiar. Few people lived so long in the Maelstrom Tower dungeons. They all were smashed or infected eventually. Shadow had studied Annie's DNA and had found she was also a Superian. Her ability was that she was impossible to infect. This showed how arrogant the Darkitect was. He wanted so much to infect every Superian, which allowed him to take their powers, and to bend down a force that resisted to his infection that he even wanted to infect an uninfectable person.

          Shadow dressed and then got out of his room. He walked through the living room and reached the door. He was out in the corridor when his F-phone rang. It was Quantum.

Chapter 2: A phone call in the midst of the day Part 1

 

          Shadow was working for most of the morning and noon was approaching now. His arms moving quickly, the bricks followed his directions. Shadow did not accept help from other Nexus Forcers in this project, because he wanted to keep the designs of the FFFFF Tower secret; have some aces up his sleeves, in the case of an emergency. The only reason he confided in his fellow members of the Squad was that he needed their help to complete the project in a reasonable time. And perhaps some more trust he had in them. But even from them he concealed some stuff that he built on his own.

          Despite their being so few people relatively to how many people usually work in projects like these, the Tower was being built quite fast. It was Imagination that helped them so much. Thus, half an F stood there.

          It was at that time that Shadow’s F-Phone, which was part of his helmet, rang. It was so sudden and unexpected that Shadow jolted. Yet, being at the edge of the half-built Tower, he almost fell to the ground from a pretty high point. It was only for Adam’s quick reflexes that he avoided a painful smash that would need some time to recover from.

          After restoring his balance, he answered the call. The voice of Magical Fire Monkey, Shadow’s best friend came to his ears.

          “Hey, Shadow! Today’s the first day of my vacation from Castle Kingdom! I don’t think you’ve forgotten?” Magical was saying.

          “Of course not! I have arranged to leave the worksite earlier today!” Shadow replied.

          “Good! We have a whole week to enjoy one another before we both get back to what we’re doing! So, I was thinking perhaps we could go to the Avant Gardens Survival!”

Although she physically looked eighteen years old and few of her mental functions had grown on their own, she mostly seemed to behave like a ten-year old.
