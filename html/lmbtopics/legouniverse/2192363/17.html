The damage that the battle created was big, but not unfixable. Putting down the fire, stoping the infection and rebuilding the walls wasn't an easy job. So, after two hours of work, both Sarah and Shadow were exhausted. Katie was driving as fast as she could, so they would reach Castle Kingdom more quickly. Magical stayed where he was, so he would strike back quickly if another spaceship attacked.
Now they had done more than the three quarters of the distance. While Shadow was hoping that they would reach their destination without any further trouble, they saw a huge world that had nothing more than golden sand.
“Deadly Desert!” Shadow whispered calling the world with the name they had decided that fitted to it “We are close!” he continued loudly now. They flew above it but did not go very close, afraid of what could be there. As they left it behind, Shadow heaved a sigh of relief.
“OH! The pilot and the other woke up and started screaming and trying to free themselves” Sarah told them as she entered the cockpit “They won't stop for anything”
They kept travelling and finally they saw a gigantic world approaching them. It was bigger even than Deadly Desert; surely the biggest world in Crux System. Castle Kingdom was just as they left it. After flying above some valleys and a forest they reached a valley they knew that hid the seven Castles that were in war. As they continued, they passed above a Maelstrom Castle and through all their bombs on it. The Maelstrom Catapults answered to the attack but when they did this, it was already late. The rest of the travel was peaceful until they reached the central circular castle with the huge tower rising in its center.

“Katie, do you know how to land?” Shadow asked then.
“No, idea!” Katie answered without a milimetre of her face showing the least panic. Before Shadow could respond or even scream, the spaceship started losing height. Shadow closed his eyes and after a minute the whole spaceship was shaken as it hit the ground...
Shadow fell down and he felt warm blood streaming on his face. The sound of medal creeping on the ground and colliding with buildings showed that the spaceship was still moving. Afraid that he would fall again he stayed on the floor. He had still closed eyes when he heard an explosion. He didn't know where it had occured because he had lost his bearings. After that he heard more explosions around him and he realized that it didn't matter where they occured because they just occured everywhere.
Now, the speed of the Venture Explorer was very slow and a little after it stopped moving. Shadow opened his eyes, got up, and looked around to figure out what the damage was. With the first glance it was sure that the spaceship was too destroyed to be repaired. The roof and the walls had collapsed in many places, the floor had holes, fire was everywhere around and pieces of rock and of medal had smashed the Contol Board and other parts of the Traveller.
Shadow had a cut on his right cheek and a couple of bruises on his hands, but otherwise he was okay. The others hadn't his luck. Katie had many cuts and bruises on her face and a piece of the roof had fallen on her left foot. Magical had bruises all over his body and his arm was a little burnt. Sarah had also bruises and a deep long cut on her leg.
As they looked at each other and at the spaceship screams were heard from outside and then a loud voice “No! This will stop now! Who's in there? Who dared to come here that way and destroy all those buildings?!”
Shadow recognised the voice. It belonged to Panagis. Instead of an answer, he walked towards the hole that was the closest to the place where Panagis was heard and got out from it. Knights with spears had surrounded the spaceship. Panagis was standing between two of them and was looking at the several holes waiting someone to get out of them.
“YOU!” he said as soon as he noticed Shadow with his eyes looking at him with hate.
“Me” answered Shadow calmly.
“Why did you do this?” asked Panagis still angrily.
“Our sources informed us that Dragon was seriously injured in a battle”
“'Us'?! Who else is with you?! And did you have to destroy the half of the castle just so you can meet a friend of yours?! Are you crazy?!”
As an answer to his first question Shadow's fellows appeared behind him. Sarah was supporting Katie whose ankle must had been broken by the piece of medal that had fell on it.
“That's your answer, Panagis. About the destruction of the castle, I can't say we're the best at landing a spaceship. And no, sir we're not crazy. Now if you excuse us we have other jobs to do” Shadow responded and with that he passed through the knights with the spears who didn't attack him because they waited orders from their leader who was speechless. . .

Chapter 70: At the Sanitarium

Shadow and his friends were at the Sanitarium. They had had some of their healing consumables, so their injuries were just a bad dream. They were standing around a bed where Magical Heroic Dragon was lying. They had just explained him their story after they arrived Nexus Tower.
"You didn't have to destroy the half castle just to see an injured minifig!" he was saying.
"Well, I couldn't let one of my friends being in danger and me being away" Shadow answered. He then put his hand in his backpack and searched for something. After a minute his hand appeared again holding something like a cellphone. It was an F-phone.
"I know, but you put you lives in danger and made Panagis very angry. And you'd better not make him your enemy. He has a way to beat any of his enemies" retorted Dragon.
"Well, he shall find his master, if he opposes me. And besides, rules are to be broken; if it for a good aim of course!" Shadow reretorted.
"You have to admit it's difficult to beat Shadow" said Magical "Don't forget that he has achieved to escape from the Darkitect twice already!"
At that time the subject changed and they started talking about the Darkitect and the Maelstrom.
"...Yes, they've become stronger than before" told Dragon after an hour "For instance, yesterday we attacked a team of Maelstrom Knights believing that we could beat them easily, but suddenly we found ourselves seperated and almost beaten. I think they're smarter, too. Thank god that that minifig, Myrror came and saved us. It is said that he is the apprentice of Mardolf the Orange..."
Then, they talked about wizards and magic and the subject kept changing like that. They talked until a nurse came and said they should leave. . .

Chapter 71: The Spy

'The Fellowship of the Five Fs' was in its room sleeping. They were using the same room as the previous time they came to the Castle Kingdom.
They all were dreaming except for Katie. Shadow was dreaming of strange machines that no one else could understand how they worked or what they did, so it wouldn't be interesting since we wouldn't understand what was happening. Magical was dreaming of him being between two huge dragons in a cave. However, this dream was out of topic, so we won't talk about it.
On the other hand, Sarah's dream was both interesting and not out of topic. It began as a regular weird dream. Magical told her that she had splotched his favourite sword and now it was useless. Then, Katie and Shadow magically appeared and Katie said that Sarah owed him something and she suggested to give him two thousand ice saws.
"But I don't have any ice saws!" exclaimed Sarah confused.
"I can give you the ice saws," Shadow told her "but you will have to find me a Maelstrom part of my mother" He, then, showed her his mother's infected hand.
And as Sarah looked at it started growing like her eyes were zooming in. After a minute the only thing she could see was the flame-like aura of the hand. And suddenly Sarah was in it... She felt like she was passing through walls and suddenly she was in the shade.
When her eyes got used to the darkness, she noticed that she could see pretty well although everything was slightly blurred. She was in a circular big room which seemed to be purple. The only thing in the room was a throne αt the opposite of hers. A man with a black tuxedo was siting on it. He had a staff; his red eyes and his purple face were shining- his face just slighly but his eyes a lot more. He was the Darkitect... In front of him there was another person that was stooping. The only thing that made her notice him was that his black robes, cape and hood were even darker than the rest of the room. Next to her was standing a Stromling Admiral. The Admiral and she seemed to guard a door that was between them. Nobody attacked her. Actually, nobody seemed to notice her. Not like she was invisible; they just didn't care about her.
The dark minifigure was saying something. Sarah stretched her ears to hear.
"...gdom, today. I think we could chase them there. Your forces are very strong there, Master" Sarah heard him saying with a voice that seemed familiar to her.
"And what makes you think that this time we will be successful. Your traps at the Gnarled Forest and the Huge Desert didn't work and my forces are strong there, too" the Darkitect ansered angrily.
"This time it will be different. I'll be there, too" the minifigure replied with some fear in his voice.
"This does changes the things, but I'm still not s-" the Darkitect started saying but stopped. He was looking at Sarah "Tetraprosopus, it seems we have a visitor" His voice wasn't angry anymore, it had a sarcastic tune.
Sarah knew that this was a dream, but she was terrified by the idea that the Darkitect had discovered her. The dark minifigure turned around to see her and Sarah figured out why she thought that his voice was familiar. Instead of a face he had a white creepy mask that had a terrified look. However, the mask vanished and a smiling version of it replaced it.
"Are you playing, Sarah?" he said and then a purple flash covered everything and she woke up with a great pain in her head. For a second she thought that she was still dreaming because the white grinning mask was still watching her. Then, she realised that she was back in the castle and that the Darkitect's servant had just followed her.
"Sleep" he whispered with an authoritative tune and Sarah did so, because she couldn't resist to his magic "Tommorow, when you will wake up you will not remember any of these" Afterwards, the Four-Faced Guy disappeared leaving just a little black smoke. . .

Chapter 72: The Darkitect's Servant's Arrival

The next day, when they woke up, Sarah didn't remember anything about her dream. However, the magical appearance of the Darkitect's Servant wasn't unnoticed by the rest of the people. It seems that the magicians of the world had put a spell on the castle, so no magical invasions happened. Once F-F-G appeared an alarm sounded.
The myserious night event was the only thing that everyone, from the gate guards to the vendors, talked about all the next day. However, that was not the only surprise for the four friends. The second surprise came about the noon. Shadow and Magical were at the top of the Northern Tower and were watching the Maelstrom Forces that were reconstituting after the battle of the day before yesterday. They were still far and few but Shadow believed they would be below the wall with a great army. That was what always happened. At that time someone put his hand on Shadow's shoulder and said happily "Shadow! Magical! What are you doing here?!"
Shadow and Magical turned around and found themselves in front of a smiling white creepy mask.
"Four-Faced Guy! We came here to see a friend, but what are you doing here?" Shadow answered enthusiastically.
"Have you ever heard of the Black Knight Castle?" he asked and then continued without expecting an answer "It's the castle that's now ruined. Well, the Maelstrom for some reason doesn't go even close to its ruins although this was what destroyed it. I want to learn what keeps them out of it, because we could use it to defeat the Maelstrom or at least banish it to somewhere else. I'm going to go there today. Would you like to come?"
"Of course!" Shadow replied, who was excited with the idea, without however suspecting that this could be a deadly trap. . .
