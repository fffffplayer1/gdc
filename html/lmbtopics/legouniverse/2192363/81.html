Chapter 12: A chapter for which I didn’t bother finding a title Part 1

 

          “Namandel, stay there hidden somewhere! And probably call for some backup! If they come for the property, you should follow them!” Shadow was saying.

          He had just seen the change of status of the particular property where Namandel was to “Normal”. Just after that, Namandel had called to notify him personally! Shadow hoped someone would indeed show up in the property, so that they could get more information.

          Meanwhile, he had also been informed of Adam’s and Namandel’s attacks. They had not been the only. Three more members had been attacked, one of them, one of the newest, smashed! And that made Shadow worry more for Maelstrom rarely attacked in properties. And now there had been five attacks in a day. And Shadow did not think that they were random nor irrelevant.

          Now, he was once again typing furiously on a computer trying to find a property or a person related to all this. It wasn’t long before he was interrupted again. This time it was Doctor Overbuild!

          “Hello, Shadow! Happy to see you’re at work!” the man said while his automated wheel chair brought him near the young man.

          “Greetings Doc! What brings you here?” Shadow replied not even looking at the man.

          “Actually, you! That drawing you sent me! I searched for that man! We found many shots of him and videos as well, taken from some surveillance cameras we have placed in some spots” Overbuild explained.

          “Really?! Where is he? Did you capture him?” Shadow asked excitedly this time turning to look at him.

Chapter 13: In the mysterious property Part 1

 

          “Do we really have to do this together?” Sarah complained for the umpteenth time.

          “For the millionth time, it could be dangerous there and it wouldn’t be wise if only one of us went!” Magical replied “Not that I like it of course!”

          Strange Odd Shadow had discovered a property to match his requirements. He had called Magical and Sarah to send them on a mission to investigate it. Now the two minifigures were heading to the particular Nimbus Isle with their rockets.

          “I know, I know, but couldn’t Shadow pick someone else?!”

          “I guess he knows something more to send the two of us!”

          “What is there to know? I don’t think you’d do anything properly, so why send you in any important mission anyway?”

          “You know it is your skills, the ones you supposedly have, that are not necessary to this mission! On the other hand, if we need to fight anyone or anything you wouldn’t like to be without me!”

          “Yet, fighting is the only thing you can do! Oh no! You can also open jars!”

          “Mock me as you like, but if I leave you alone expect to be kidnapped like these people! I don’t think you’d like it too much! But I think they’d like it even less! They wouldn’t be able to stand you!”

          As the two people continued playing ping pong of insults, they passed over Nimbus Station. Sarah looked at the people below having a good time, going about their business, ignoring the fact that something important might be happening at the moment. She looked to the edge of the world, where in the blackness of space she could see the little chunks of Crux looking less and less like stars and more like what they really were. They soon would be there.

Chapter 13: In the mysterious property Part 2

 

          The Nimbus Isle property that was a suspect happened to be one of the chunks nearest to Avant Gardens. That was one more hint for its being related to all of this. The computers of their rockets recognised the world as they approached the borders of the Nimbus Station territory.

          “Let’s start descending!” Magical said interrupting their arguing.

          “You don’t need to tell me what to do!” Sarah protested.

          “Don’t I? If I let you, you’d continue going on till you reached Yorkanton!”

          “Haha! Very funny! You think I’m the one with less brain of the people in these two rockets!”

          “Well, it’s certainly not me!”

          “You wish! You’re not even compared to a-” Sarah was interrupted by Magical.

          “Wait! What’s that?!” he asked worriedly.

          “What? Have we started to fe-” this time Sarah stopped on her own; she was feeling it, too.

          Their rockets were being shaken, harder and harder as they approached the property. It was like something wanted to stop them from going on. Perhaps something did! Or someone…

          Yet, the two of them continued on! They were both too stubborn to stop in the first difficulty. Even as the shaking started getting dizzy enough to make them feel like Shadow every time he rode a rocket. The shaking did not stop for some time. However, in the end, with one curt move the rockets shot towards the ground of the Nimbus Isle.

          Sarah started immediately trying to change the rocket’s route and saved herself from crashing just the last moment. On the other hand, Magical’s reflexes were not as quick and his rocket crashed, while he managed to jettison himself out of it.

Chapter 13: In the mysterious property Part 3

 

          “That’s so Gnarled Forest!” Sarah exclaimed recalling their other rocket crash during their first adventures. That time it was the Four-Faced Guy messing with their rockets. Who was it now?

          Magical almost smiled at the noting of the déjà vu-ness of the situation, but he remembered it was Sarah he was with.

          “Let’s just continue!” he said and moved towards the inner of the property.

          Her rocket had dug slightly into the ground and Magical was not waiting for her, so she decided she could take care of the rocket later.

          The property contained some sort of mansion much like the one Shadow, Kate and Magical had investigated. It wasn’t easy to break in, but in the end they managed to get through the door. Inside, the building was also similar to the other one. The two minifigures researched it for long and examined ‘every inch of it’. However, for long they found nothing. It actually seemed like it had been long abandoned. They did not split up at any moment of their investigation for fear of facing something separately less efficiently (or not efficiently at all) than if they were together.

          In the end, Magical collapsed on the sofa of a big living room in the centre of the ground floor. He was sure there should be something they could find there, but they couldn’t despite all their efforts. Sarah started walking across the room from one side to another and then back to the first one. They were both trying to think of what they hadn’t already thought. Yet, after a while, Magical’s thoughts were distracted and thinking this or that he ended up focusing on the sound of Sarah’s steps. And while he didn’t do it for any particular reason, after a bit he noticed there was something peculiar. And then something clicked in his mind!

Chapter 13: In the mysterious property Part 4

 

          “Walk back there again!” he pointed to a particular spot on the floor.

          “What?!” Sarah was looking at him as he had finally started showing the signs of madness she was always suspicious he had.

          “Just do it! I want to see something!” Magical replied with excitement.

          Sarah obeyed, but she still had the same look.

          “Ha! Just I had thought of it!” Magical exclaimed “It must be hollow there!”

          Sarah hit her foot on the floor again and she noticed the hollow sound, too. She almost congratulated Magical for having one good idea in his life, but she didn’t give him even that, she just said “Oh yes! You must be right”

          With no patience to see if the floor opened in some particular way, Magical just grabbed his longsword and stabbed at the spot. It went through and after some more times, Magical had smashed the whole thing opening a hole for them to get in.

          “You do realise that this could have caused something bad? It still may have!” Sarah found the chance to criticise him.

          “Just get in!”

          Inside, they found more interesting staff. After climbing down a metal ladder they found themselves in a circular hall with several doors. One led to a big room with many chairs and a pedestal. It seemed like a place where people of organisations of any kind met. One led to a room with two metal doors they couldn’t open. It seemed like they were cells. Another door led to a lab. Unfortunately, no signs of what kind of experiments were worked on there were left, but still there was an eerie atmosphere there. It was generally eerie with an odd silence down there.

Chapter 13: In the mysterious property Part 5

 

          They were still surveying the lab, which was quite big. Magical was still looking at some chemistry sets to see if he could find anything more, but Sarah moved slightly further. The end of the room was there and there was one more door there. Sarah opened the door and found a long corridor that seemed to lead to utter darkness.

          “We should totally check this place out!” Sarah told Magical.

          The latter looked at her direction and saw the open door leading to blackness. He thought it seemed much more suspicious than anything else.

          “No, wait! Let’s finish with this room first!” Magical responded and went to look on another table.

          “Come on, Magical! We’ve already finished here and if we are going to find something it will be there!”

          “No, Sarah! I’m not going just yet and we shouldn’t split up!”

          Sarah looked at Magical. Then, she looked back at the corridor. She had a bizarre look, as if the darkness had some charm on her.

          While Magical continued he didn’t notice anyone coming. Yet, something else warned him: his superian power suddenly became suspicious of activity that could lead to a battle. He always seemed to sense when fighting was coming; like the first time they’d met the Last of the Black Knights… or the Great Battle of course.

Chapter 13: In the mysterious property Part 6

 

          Magical turned around to the surprise of two minifigures who were trying to sneak near him. They were wearing black clothes that covered as much of them as possible, they were holding daggers and pistols and did not look friendly. As they were taken aback, Magical had the chance to hit first. The one closer to him felt the rage of his sword on the face and chest (not the cutting side) and was flung to the opposite wall. The other one looked at his comrade and then fearfully at Magical. The said man charged against him, but having recovered the minifigure dodged his attack. Meanwhile, the one who was hit was unconscious. The minifigure fired his pistol, but Magical swirled and the bullet hit on the shield on his back. The attacker took the opportunity and charged against Magical placing his dagger near the man’s throat. However, Magical kicked him on the reed and his opponent being distracted he managed with one hit to disarm him of his dagger. The black-clothed enemy found a chance as Magical’s sword was still moving due to the momentum it had gained. His free hand grabbed Magical’s neck and started squeezing. The Sentinel drew his other sword, but with the pressure on his throat he found himself struggling to move properly. But Magical refused to give up and finding the strength to do so he raised his leg and kicked his opponent on the belly. The next moment the stranger found himself lying on the floor with Magical on top of him threatening his throat with one of his swords.

          “Now you shall tell me what all of this is about!” Magical ordered.

          However, instead of talking he watched the person get white as a sheet and smash. It was like he was poisoned. Or as if he had poisoned himself… Magical had heard of people who would even give their lives so that their cause was not hurt.

Chapter 14: A new development Part 1

 

          “I told her! I told her not to go there! But she wouldn’t listen to me!” Magical exclaimed in despair.

          His eyes were looking worriedly moving fast and suddenly. He played with his fingers like people do when they are nervous. He was pacing up and down and if Shadow had let him he might have repeated the same things many times. His tone indicated how worried he was.

          “Wait a sec, Magical!” Shadow protested “What happened? Where is Sarah?”

          Magical had just returned from the mission to which he’d sent Sarah and him. Sarah was not with him and Magical was in that state. Shadow had been anticipating some good news, but now he got anxious. Kate was with him at the moment and she didn’t look more relaxed after seeing Magical.

          “That’s what I’m telling you!” Magical said slightly frustrated with his mind apparently not clear.

          He soon calmed slightly and narrated what had happened. At the point when he mentioned Sarah’s having vanished, Shadow’s expression got bleak and Kate’s larynx produced some exclamations. Then, he reached the point when the rocket took off.

          “I immediately ran after them, but they were way ahead and as I approached the launch pad the building exploded behind me! The shockwave knocked me further and I was generally distracted for a moment. Yet, when I did get on my rocket and after the rocket of the other minifigure I did manage to spot it despite the distance. It flew away from Nimbus Station by Avant Gardens and finally reached the Zephyr Asteroid Field. There, I completely lost it! I couldn’t save her, Shadow! Now they have her! What will we do?!”

Chapter 15: In the Asteroid Field Part 2

 

          Kate had known she couldn’t change his mind and so she didn’t try. Shadow was very mulish some –or many– times.

          Now, the three of the four original FFFFF members were piloting their rockets to save the fourth. Despite Shadow’s worries, part of him felt pleased that it was them working together again. They often got separated nowadays.

          The Zephyr Asteroid Field did not consist of actual asteroids. They were just chunks of Crux that did not come from the surface. They varied in size and shape, but what they all had in common was that there was no life on them and that there was no level surface on them. The field had a spherical shape and had about the same diameter as the Nimbus System, but theoretically there could be something inside it. That was what they were going to find out today.

          In the beginning, they were just flying their rockets maneuvering to avoid the chunks. However, about half an hour after they entered the field, something started firing them. The first hits, the ones that surprised them, were on Kate’s rocket. Luckily, Shadow, in knowledge of the danger of the Field, despite not knowing its origin, had prepared by strengthening the rockets’ shields and weapons. Even so, Kate’s rocket was slightly damaged and it almost hit an asteroid.
