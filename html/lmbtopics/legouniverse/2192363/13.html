Chapter 50: The room underground and the new neighbour

"Why are we going underground?!" Shadow asked.
"As Cyborg said, they want to keep you away from the battle" Platinum answered.
"What!!! We are at the perfect place for battle and we won't fight at all!!!" Magical exclaimed.
"I know that you are very good fighters but explain that to the council. I'm sorry" Platinum responded.
They reached their floor and got out ofthe elevator. Platinum led them to the fourth room at the aisle. The aisle was almost completely dark. The only light source was a small lamp at the end of the aisle.
They passed the door, Platinum said that he would come back in an hour and he left with Dragon. The room was big enough for them and a fifth person. It had five beds, five chairs, one table, two desks, one fridge, one sofa and one television. Magical sat on the sofa disaffected. Shadow, as soon as he had placed Katie on a bed, took a chair to one of the desks, sat on it and started building something. Sarah sat on a bed and turned on the TV.
Ten minutes passed and suddenly someone opened the door.
"I was waiting for that moment three months! And finally I have neighbours. Hello, my name is Turt Wig Train and I'm your new neighbour! I live in room -23! What are your names?" said enthusiastically a minifigure that came in.
"Well, my name is Strange Odd Shadow but you can call me Shadow" Shadow told him awkwardly.
"Ι'm Sarah Darkon"
"I am Magical Fire Monkey and she is Katie Pirate" Magical said pointing to Katie.
"Great! Did you hear that some minifigs found that place without telling them!" Turt asked them.
"Yeah, actually that minifigures are us" Shadow replied.
"OOOH! So, I guess they don't want you to be at the surface?" Turt said.
"Yes" Magical answered.
"Then, who wants to go upstairs secretly" Turt told them with a mischievous smile. . .
