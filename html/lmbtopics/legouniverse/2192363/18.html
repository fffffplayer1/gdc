Chapter 73: Among the Ruins Part 1

Sarah growled. They were waiting at the Southern Tower for the Four-Faced Guy. Shadow and Magical had told the girls about his plan and they had found it exciting, too, although Sarah wasn't very excited about the arrival of F-F-G for reasons unknown even for her.
"Shadow! We've been waiting here for over than an hour! F-F-G is not going to come! Shall we leave?" complained Sarah.
"He will come, Sarah! Something must have happened. Just wait!" Shadow tried to reassure her.
Shadow started thinking of the possibilities of what could have happened and he looked around. The sun was about to set although it was just 6 o'clock. The reason for that was that the Vortex of the Maelstrom was at the West, so the sun was hidden behind it a lot before it would normally.
As Shadow looked at the Vortex, he started thinking of the Maelstrom. He thought of the fact that when people said people they could mean the Force of Maelstrom, the Vortex of its and just the Maelstrom beings. 'However,' he said to himself 'those may not be so different'. He, then looked at the middle of the Maelstrom, where the two evil angry eyes were located. They looked like they were looking for something and their glance seemed to be looking at the Castle Kingdom. They reminded Shadow something and then he realized they were exactly like the Darkitect's eyes, except for being purple.
When Shadow tried to look somewhere else, he realized he couldn't take his eyes from it. And as he was staring at the eyes, a great fear came and sat in his heart. He lost all his hope that they would beat their enemy and he became miserable. A sudden desire to hide somewhere where those evil eyes wouldn't be able to find him came upon him and he would have screamed if he wasn't frozen from terror.
And suddenly an unexpectable voice came and made him to cohere from a terror that he thought he would never conquer. It was the voice of Katie and it seemed to be heard from far far away or like something very fat was between them, so in the start he didn't notice it. But when he did, he realized it was frustrated.
"SHADOW! WILL YOU EVER WAKE UP?! SHADOW!" she was saying.
"WHAT?!" Shadow shouted surprised and still afraid of what happened before.
"Shadow! I've been shouting for a minute before you wake up. You were looking at the Maelstrom with a very frightened face! What happened?" In the start Katie sounded a little angry, but when she finished talking her voice had a little fear.
"I just looked at it. And then I just could not take my eyes off of it. Then, a great fear came upon me and I froze" was Shadow's explanation about what happened.
"Well, then we shall not look at it, so this doesn't happen to us, too" Magical talked "Can we eat something? Waiting here made me hungry"
"Here, have an Imagination-Enriched Sandwich. Your hunger will vanish at a second" Shadow offered and took a sandwich from his backpack.
Silence prevailed again and Shadow looked around once more, but trying to avoid the Maelstrom. He gazed at Katie and a silly smile formed on his face. A force, greater even than the Maelstrom’s one, drew his glance to her. Sarah looked towards him and Shadow tried to hide it, but it was too late. Now the smiling one was Sarah.
At that time F-F-G appeared at the door next to Magical and said “I’m so sorry that I’m late, but the guards here don’t like me very much” Sarah opened her mouth to say something, but Shadow gave her one of the strict looks he rarely gave, but no one wanted to see it, so she changed her mind.
“How are we going to go there?” he asked.
“We will teleport” the Darkitect’s Servant answered sharply.
“If we have to” Shadow said disappointed.
They all gathered around F-F-G and grabbed his hands. They waited for some seconds and then suddenly they disappeared leaving only some black smoke.

They found themselves surrounded by black ruins. They looked around and saw that everything was built of the same black stone. There were many buildings that had been built by great architectures in an era that had passed. The buildings had domes on their roofs and pillars around them, the streets were paved and in the centre there was a palace that had domes, pillars, deformations, statues and a surrounding protective wall. It was all beautiful, but it was destroyed. The domes had holes, the pillars had broken in the middle, the cobblestone was cracked, the statues were headless and the walls were ruined.
“Wow! Nigrium! It’s a pity that this place was destroyed! It must have been very beautiful in the ages of its acme” Shadow sighed.
“Yeah, it can be found only here” F-F-G replied referring to the black stone, Nigrium.
“Isn’t it weird that the Maelstrom destroyed it, but now it doesn’t even come close?” Magical wondered out loud.
“Yeah, it is beautiful but it’s also depressing with all this black” Sarah ignored him.
They walked for a little. In some places, flowers and grass were growing through the cracks of the plaques of the flagstone.
“We will go to the palace. I believe what we are looking for will be there. It is barely damaged” F-F-G announced. And it was true. The main damage of it was a big hole at the biggest dome of it. The others agreed and they kept walking until they reached the Inner Wall that surrounded the palace. They passed through a crack and came into the yard of the palace. A huge staircase was there and leaded to a metallic two-ply gate. However, the one ply was kinked and fallen. Shadow counted 2053 steps till the top. They passed the ruined Gate and found out that the interior of the palace was a maze made of hallways and rooms. They searched in many rooms, but they didn’t find anything interesting. In the end, after some hours they found another gate just like the other, but not ruined, at the end of the hallway that started at the first gate. Shadow felt that something strong was in there. He had felt the same feeling twice more when he was close to the Last Shard of Imagination Nexus, but at those circumstances it was more weakened.
They pushed the plies of the Gate and it opened overly easily. Inside they found a big circular room. Although there was a big hole at the dome that was used as a roof, the place was pretty dark. There were three more gates, which together with the fourth one they entered from created a thinkable cross. The room was completely empty except for a little podium with a small table on it in the centre.
On the table there was a sphere made of crystal. It seemed to absorb and create light at the same time. It seemed to be clear and transparent, although you couldn’t see through it. It seemed to have great depths in it, but it was about the size of a minifigure. When they saw it, everyone knew it was what they had came for.
“Let’s take it and leave” F-F-G told them with a greed in his voice that they didn’t recognize.
“And what will happen to this place?” Shadow asked him “The Maelstrom will destroy it!”
“Who cares? It’s already ruined! However, if we take it we will be able to win this war!!!” F-F-G responded.
“Wait!” Magical exclaimed “Someone’s coming” He, then, run to the Gate that was opposite to the one they had entered from and put all his weight on it like he was trying to stop someone from opening it. Then, a sound was heard like someone hit the gate and at the same time the plies opened and Magical flew at the other side of the room. . .

Chapter 74: The Story of the Black Knight Part 1

A minifigure got inside from the now opened gate. He was wearing the black panoply of a knight which was made of Nigrium, too. He was a Black Knight. The Knight drew his sword and jibed its blade towards them as he was mumbling “I‘ve been expecting that someone greedy would come to steal the Power within the Crystal. And they brought the Traitor of the Knights with them”
“Who are you and what do you ask, Black Knight?” Shadow asked him with imposing voice.
“The question is who are you, thief? However, I’ll say that: I’m the Black Knight or the Last of the Black Knights. The name that was given to me when I was born doesn’t matter anymore. It has no meaning that could represent the truth anymore. So, my name now is Nigerequitemultimum*. That is my answer to your question” the Black Knight spoke with a weird accent.
“Hello, Nigerequitemultimum, Last of the Black Knights” greeted Shadow “My name is Strange Odd Shadow. We have not come to steal anything from here. Even if some of us may have been charmed by the power of some things here” He looked at F-F-G “the aim of our trip was to discover what kept the Maelstrom out of here. And I think we’ve found it!” he showed the sphere in the centre.
“Hello, minifigure who calls himself Impar Alienos Umbra*. Your words are well put together and it seems you know how to talk gently. And I would have believed you, Umbra if you didn’t bring the Traitor of the Knights with you. However, it’s true that you’ve found what you were looking for. It’s called the Power within the Crystal, or The Neutral Sphere, or The Orb of Neutrality, or the One that Nigerequitemultimum won’t let the thieves take” the Knight talked and then moved, reached the centre and reached out his hands protectively, in front of the table.

[*Nigerequitemultimum= The last of the Black Knights (Latin) Impar Alienos Umbra= Strange Odd Shadow]

Chapter 74 Part 2
“It’s interesting to learn what this sphere is, but you Nigerequitemultimum referred to a traitor and said that he is one of us. Tell us who you that is a traitor and why. Because I can’t answer to your words without knowing all the facts” Shadow replied.
“The Traitor of the Knights is the one with the white mask and the black robes” Nigerequitemultimum started “I call him The Traitor of the Knights now, but in the past he was the Black-Robed-Man. The Black-Robed-Man used to be friend of all the Kingdoms of this place. He helped them, although he never gave an advantage to one of them, so it could win that war that started long before we were born. My father was the Nigerrex*, the King of the Black Knights, so he was one of the few that had access to this room on which you are now. One day he showed this room and what it contains to him, because he trusted him very much. The Black Knights were supposed to protect the Neutral Sphere, not use it. However, the Traitor wanted to use it. ‘To end this war forever’ he said, but Nigerrex disagreed. The Black-Robed-Man left that day and didn’t return until the very black days, when the planet cracked and exploded and the Maelstrom came. That was the first time that all the Kingdoms decided to make peace, so they could fight a common enemy together. All except for us. The Black Knights were always very proud and never made alliances with others. One day, our spies informed us that a huge Maelstrom Army was coming to destroy us. The Nigerrex sent 4000 Knights and he went with them. That day our forces collided with theirs. 1000 Riders came from the Kingdom of Viridi*, our enemy from very old times. At that battle both kings fell and 4999 Knights were leaded to death or infection. Only one Knight of ours survived because he was sent by the Nigerrex to the Castle, so he would inform of what happened. The next night the Maelstrom arrived in front of the Outer Wall and the battle here started. Their army had variety, but it was mostly made of Infected Knights. There were Infected Black Knights, too, those who are call Dark Knights now. At that time the Traitor appeared again. In the start he helped and managed to stop the first waves of their forces. However, when they broke the third Wall and got into the Castle and our Captain retreated with the alive Knights, women, children and elderly in the Palace where the Inner Wall protected them. There he transformed in a raven when he was in front of a window and three arrows were coming towards him and they hit the Captain instead. Then, he abandoned us. After a little they broke the Western Gate of the Palace and got in. When I heard that news, I instantly ran to the Room of the Sphere. Surely, the Maelstrom would like to have the Power within the Crystal. However, they never came. A bomb hit the dome and made a big part of it to collapse, so I watched the battle through the hole. I expected them to come but after some hours I noticed they were leaving. They were not retreating; they were just leaving after they destroyed the force of the Black Knights. And I was the Last Survivor of them, just because I tried to protect the only thing the Maesltrom was afraid of”. . .

[*Nigerrex= The Black King (title-Latin)
Viridi= Green (The Southern Kingdom of the Knights)]

Chapter 75: Three times happened, three times bad Part 1

While Shadow and Nigerequitemultimum were talking, the others were just hearing and waited to see what would happen. However, they woke up from their ‘lethargy’, when suddenly F-F-G shouted “That’s ridiculous! I would never do such thing!”
“Your anger only shows that you would do this” the Black Knight said calmly “And I know more about you, Traitor of the Knights. You have betrayed all of the Fallen Kingdoms of the Knights”
“F-F-G, what is the meaning of this?” Magical asked. His face looked horrified, but also confused “Did you bring us here to kill us?”
Before the Black-Robed-Man could talk, something really unexpected happened. The Knight, who was standing in front of the hole of the dome, was suddenly hit by a purple lightning. He fell down and passed out. Shadow, terrified of what could have happened, ran at the hole, put his shield on the front and looked down. Through it, he saw a figure standing in the darkness of the night at the yard of the Palace. The figure was wearing black clothes, and his face was purple. The Darkitect had found them again…
The Darkitect raised his staff and launched a Maelstrom Lightning from it. The lightning hit his shield, which left his hand and flew into the room until it hit the wall. Shadow yelled, threw an arrow that hit his enemy and exploded and then he jumped.
Shadow landed on the grass with his feet and his left hand barely feeling any pain. He pushed a button on his crossbow and its bow parts folded and got into the ice saw part. His weapon was an Ice Saw again. With it he charged at the smoke from the explosion from which the Architect of the Dark appeared. Their weapons collided and purple and light blue sparkles came out of them. Shadow and his nemesis kept fighting like that. Shadow managed to hit the Darkitect on his leg, but he hit him on the stomach and the head. After the second hit Shadow fell on the ground; he wasn’t built for the battle. Shadow closed his eyes and got ready for the third, and possibly the last, hit. However, the only thing that happened was to hear a clang. He opened his eyes and saw Magical having connected his two swords into an X protecting Shadow from the Darkitect’s hit. Behind them, Sarah and Katie were fighting with the Darkitect’s Servant, who was holding a scythe with black handle, and silver blade.
Shadow got himself up and attacked to the Darkitect to help Magical. As their enemy tried to hit Magical on the arm and Magical blocked, Shadow hit him on the head before he could avoid it. Shadow hit again, but this time he dodged the attack. However, Magical spiked him on the stomach with both his swords, throwing him on the ground. They then both tried to hit him, but when their weapons touched him they were electrocuted and flew away. Shadow fell on the wall of the Palace and hit his back.
“You idiots think you can beat me!” the Darkitect said “I’ll show you now” he then raised his hands at the height of his shoulders and threw lightnings to them. Shadow knew that the lightning that came from the staff, which hit him, would be stronger. And it really was; Shadow remembered the pain that he had felt in Gnarled Forest, when he was captured by the very same person. Compared to the pain he felt that time, it was nothing.

Chapter 76 Part 2
