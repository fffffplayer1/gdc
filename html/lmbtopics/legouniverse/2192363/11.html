Chapter 31: Frostburgh

Shadow remembered the last time he had seen his mother. He felt sadness because of what had happened to her and anger because he remembered Rusty Steele. He really hated him.
When he returned no one yelled at him because they were all used to the fact that he was leaving all the time. He didn't tell to the others what happened. He wasn't at a very good mood which affected everybody else, too. So, after they watched F-F-G creating a raven and making it to attack the maelstrom beings they left (F-F-G took the raven with him). They stayed at Quantum's property for the night.
The following day they returned to Nimbus Station and set off for Frostburgh. They used some special rockets where you could sleep. Frostburgh was about twenty hours away. Although you could sleep the travel was very tiring so when they reached they went to a hotel to sleep.
When Shadow woke up at ten o'clock he had a very good mood. He went to the kitchen where he found Magical, Quantum, F-F-G, Lemon, Sarah and Glittery having breakfast. He took a toast and started eating.
By twelve o'clock everone was gathered.

Chapter 32: Bored not building

In the seventh day in Frostburgh Shadow said to the others "You know, Frostburgh is great but I'm a little bored not building anything".
"Come on Shadow, we have holidays. Just relax. Besides you made the snow-thrower 1.0 and 2.0" Glittery answered.
"It seems you don't understand assembly and especially me. Building is my life. I cannot live without that" Shadow answered.
"I would like to have my gear. That would be incredible" Magical said enthusiastically.
"Yeah and what are you going to do with it. You always leave your weapons and fight with your fists" Sarah said.
"That's not a bad idea. This could also be something like the symbol of the FFFFF team" Katie told.
"I can make gear for you but there is one term: You have to design it on your own. Also, I need my lab" Shadow said smiling "There's no other way that it could be really yours".
"That's okay. I totally agree" Katie answered. Shadow felt happy she agreed.
"I think I will just stay here and relax" Lemon said "I'm not going to ruin my vacations"
"I'm sorry guys but I think that Lemon is right. We all need a break and one week is not enough" Platinum told.
"I think that it would be nice to have my gear. However, I'm not going to travel for twenty hours just for this" Quantum said.
"Whatever, If the dumb one takes his gear then I want, too" Sarah said and pointed to Magical.
"You will some day pay for all these insults, you stupid!" Magical answered "I will never understand why she is in the team!" And they started fighing(as always)
"SO, WE FOUR GO TO THE FFFFF BASE AND YOU FIVE STAY HERE" Shadow yelled trying to be heard.
"Wait! I want to come, too. However, I don't want any gear" F-F-G said "Plus, I can teleport you there in a second"
"Okay!" Shadow answered cheerfully.
"Shadow, F-F-G really gives me the creeps. Do we have to have him in the team?" Katie whispered.
"I know, he gives me the creeps, too but he is very powerful. It's better to have him in our team" Shadow answered.
So, after ten minutes the five friends gathered and they teleported.

Chapter 33: At the FFFFF Base

Shadow and his friends were at the FFFFF Base. Teleporting was even worse from using a rocket. It was like you were becoming dust and then again a minifigure just in another place."Awesome! Let's do this again!" Magical said.
"You find everything awesome, you dump" Sarah said.
"OH! Is this going to be happening all the time?" F-F-G asked.
"Actually, they were fighting much more at Yorkanton City" Katie informed him.
"So, let's get to business. It's time you drew your gear. You better use your thinking hat" Shadow said and then sat on a sofa and started reading a magazine about the Evolution of Technology. After twenty minutes Katie came and gave him a paper with the drawing of a minifigures and some notes explaining what the weapons could do.
"Well I'd better get started" he said happily that he would start with Katie's gear.
"Can I have your thinking hat? I want to be quicker and I have lost mine" Shadow asked.
"Sure!" Katie answered.
Shadow went to his lab and started building. His hands were moving so fast that you almost couldn't see them. Half an hour later everyone had put their designs on a table. Th better drawing was the one of Sarah's which was very scientific and the worst was Magical's which was most like a smudge.
Shadow was rying many things. For instance he was mixing several chemicals (creating a lot of explosions) to put in the weapons. Actually, he was so busy building that he wasn't leaving even for lunch. So, Sarah who was the best cook of the three of them used to cook. However, the fifth day he heard an explosion coming from upstairs. He quickly went there and saw smoke coming from the kitchen.
"What happened here? Ususally the explosiond occur at the rooms where I am!" Shadow said. He then heard Katie telling "I just wanted to help you to cook! I'm so sorry!".
"WELL, I HAVE NEVER SEEN MINIFIGURE COOKING SO BAD. EVEN MAGICAL COOKS BETTER THAN YOU!" Sarah yelled.
"Did she just said what I heard?" Magical told. After that Shadow took an extuinguisher and tried to burn out the fire.
Except from that there wasn't any other remarkable event. So, after a week Shadow had finished and called the others. It was time for them to see their gear. . .
