Chapter 3 Part 6

S.O. Shadow went to Strongheart to report about my mission. After telling him, Strongheart gave him his reward and then said "The Sentinels approves you to be in the Nexus Force but you will need the Paradox approval, too. Go to Starcracker he will send you to the right Paradox guy".
"Okay" S.O. Shadow answered and then left. Just in time Quantum and Platinum came. "Lemon went to Ninjago Monastery because he was bored waiting" Platinum said to Quantum still a little angrily.
"Oh, hi S.O. Shadow. Platinum will stay with us 'till we reach Nimbus Station" Quantum said.
"Where are we going?" Platinum asked.
"To Epsilon Starcracker" S.O. Shadow answered "But there's something else I want to ask. Strongheart mentioned something about Sentnels and Paradox. What are they?"
"You want to become a Nexus Force member but you don't know about the factions" Platinum said like S.O. Shadow have told that he doesn't know what a hand is.
"They are two of the four factions in which Nexus Force is seperated. The Assembly faction which consists of construction specialists and critical thinkers who drive back the power of the Maelstorm through problem solving and creative building, the Sentinels who are in their element in the thick of a fight;armed with inventive weaponry, they are renowned for using their wits, creative fighting moves and team skills to battle those who wish to destroy imagination, the Paradox whose members closely study the Maelstrom power in an effort to find the evil within it; they believe that the battlefield is no place for honor and use whatever means are necessary to achieve victory and the Venture League whose members are a composition of adventurers, daredevils and explorers who want to get to the bottom of all of the mysteries filling up the Universe.
The name of the Venture League brought back a memory of when he was ten years old:
It was summer and he was running to his house. He had just learnt that his mother had returned from her journey. Three months ago she had left to join an organisation named Nexus Force. He reached his house. His parents were at the garden discussing about something. He heard his mother saying "...Nexus Force is awesome. There are four factions. I'm in Venture League, Nexus Force' explorers..."
"S.O. Shadow, are you okay?" Quantum asked.
"What?"he answered while he was dragging out of his thoughts.
"You were looking at nowhere for one minute. Are you okay?" he responded.
"Yes, I'm fine I was just thinking of something" he said.
