Chapter 34: Smithier 2.0 Part 7

 

          “Veeeerrry funnyyyy! Ssssooo, you wooouldn’t mind ussss checkiiiing?” that voice was very odd, alien; it seemed like a totally different creature than human was speaking.

          “Just don’t make a mess, please!”

          “Shadow, look!” Magical was pointing to a window.

          “Good idea!” Shadow whispered.

          They opened the window as quickly and as silently as they good and they got out. Magical had a little problem passing through for it wasn’t wide enough, but in the end they did it. Magical closed the window and they started running away, but making sure they weren’t seen inside the house.

          “Shall we take off?” Magical asked.

          “No, if we do, they’ll see we came here and Smith will have trouble!”

          “What then?”

          “I have an idea!” Shadow put his hand in his backpack and searched for something, all the while running; he got something that looked like a motorbike, but instead of wheels it had two circular flat surfaces that emitted a white light; it hovered above the ground “Hoverbike! Ride on!”

          They mounted it and Shadow put in motion. It was pretty fast. The black cloak Shadow was wearing and his hair waved in the wind.

          “Why did we not use this before?”

          “We couldn’t have or we didn’t need to! We either used our rockets instead, the ground was not appropriate –it doesn’t work well over sea– or we were indoors”

          “Very well!”

          “Now, we will get away from Smith’s house and take off then!”

Chapter 35: Arrival at the Lake City Part 1

 

          “AAAAAAH!”

          “AAAAAAAAH!”

          “AAAAAAAH!”

          Shadow and Magical were the only ones who could hear their not so calm exclamations as they almost hit a floating rock, as it is impossible for sound to propagate through space.

          Of course it was not for no reason that they almost flew into a rock. Soon after they took off, their two chasers had reappeared attempting to catch up with them. Even though they were far enough and couldn’t aim well, the two Nexus-Forcers still piloted their rockets like mad.

          “Shadow, there’s a world over there! Do you think it might be Lakum?”

          “If it is, I just hope they won’t follow us there” Shadow commented.

          Lasers shot past them as they approached the planet chunk Magical had spotted.

          “Do you think we could get rid of them some other way?”

          “I don’t know! We can’t outrun them and I don’t how well fighting them would go. Their rockets seem to have better weapons installed”

          “All we need is a trick! You certainly can outsmart them!”

          “I’ve already told you, though, that I can’t find any ideas that would help us!”

          The world did not seem to be Lakum City. For one part, there was no part of the Makreia Sea –which is actually a big lake– next to which the city originally lay. For another, there was no sight of any city. The world actually mostly contained fields where they could see minifigures working.

          “Not the right one!”

          “Are you sure we’re going the right way?”

          “I think! Most of the still uncharted area is towards that direction, so Lakum City must be somewhere there!”

Chapter 35: Arrival at the Lake City Part 2

 

          As they passed by the world –making sure not to be seen so as not to cause any stir or have any more people chasing them–, they looked over their shoulders at their pursuers more than once. They had escaped those two minifigures’ grasp many times until then, Shadow thought. They had to do it once more. But how could they do it? There’s no place to hide in space! Or is there?

          “Magical! I just had a thought! It seems I, too, can miss the obvious. Since I can use my power on objects I touch, too, perhaps I could camouflage my rocket. The space is dark, isn’t it? The stars might cause some problems, I don’t know what effect they’ll have, but it’s better than nothing”

          “I knew you could do it!” Magical cheered.

          “Yeah, but you’ll have to jump on my rocket! That’s not the best idea!”

          “Well, if we have to! Coming near you now”

          Magical drove his rocket in order to place it side by side with Shadow’s.

          “Wait for me to extend the rocket’s artificial mini-sphere” Shadow said referring to the term mini-atmosphere.

          Shadow pressed buttons like a maniac; it did not seem like an easy thing to do. Finally, after a few minutes he stopped.

          “Okay! I think I’ve done it! I’ve merged our mini-spheres” he announced.

          “You think?!”

          “Oh come on, now! It’s not like we have any other choice! Just jump!”

Chapter 35: Arrival at the Lake City Part 3

 

          Magical looked at Shadow with a deadly look for a second, then sighed and got up. He stepped on the side of his rocket and, trying not to look at the void of darkness below him, he jumped. As he did so, he could not help screaming a little, but in the end he landed into Shadow’s rocket, safe and sound, if a little hurt by the impact. The other rocket started shrinking and it returned in Magical’s backpack.

          “Are you alright?”

          “I think so!” Magical said while changing his position to be more comfortable.

          “Then, let’s do it!”

          Shadow started channeling his power to the whole rocket and it disappeared for the eyes of those who would look at them.

          “Now, let’s try a different route, so that they lose us!”

          Shadow drove his rocket out of the straight route they’d been flying on for some time, so that their pursuers would actually lose them. Sure enough, after a while the projectiles ceased raining all over them and looking over his shoulder, Shadow couldn’t see the two rockets.

          “Well, that’s better! Magical, can you take the handles, so I can concentrate on this?”

          “I’ll try!”

          Magical tried to squeeze his way in the front of Shadow and with a lot of effort he managed to do it without throwing anyone out of the one-man seat.

Chapter 35: Arrival at the Lake City Part 4

 

          “Any idea on what direction we should take?” the man asked.

          “Not anything different than before!” Shadow replied.

          “I’ll just go this way then!” Magical said and continued towards a random direction.

          “As good as any!”

          They passed a few worlds as they went by and every time they located a chunk of Crux they would hold their breath to be disappointed when they examined the surface (if there was any). As they went on, Shadow could feel his power draining slowly, but steadily. Despite that, he was trying to mentally keep track of all the worlds they’d seen and the distances between them.

          Then, suddenly Magical pointed out he had seen the lower part of world. Above them and to their left and front there was a point where the stars disappeared and which was brownish. Magical raised their rocket, so that they could fly above the height of its surface in order to see what it looked like.

          In one instant, they were amazed. They were faced with the sight of a very beautiful city. Surrounded on three sides by the cyan waters of the lake reflecting the light of the sun, the city was a mix of shining white of marble and green of beautiful trees. It was a city built in a particular way and not randomly and with certain architecture. That was Lakum City. . .

  1. Book 1: Strange Odd Shadow: Picking up where you seem to have left off, being chased by Maelstrom Scorpions, the fellowship reached the First Builders' Temple in the Desert and they sought sanctuary there, while they also searched for the piece that was needed to build a launch pad. A storm rid them of the scorpions and they actually did find the piece they were looking for. After the storm, they tried to leave, but the appearance of the Darkitect stopped them. Quick action allows them to escape and a little farther away they set up a lauch pad and take off with their rockets. Soon, they approached another world. Flying over the vast Medieval world, Castle Kingdom, they found, they landed inside a besieged castle. Soon, they discover that due to the danger this world holds it is being kept secret to most of  the Nexus Force (their not having permission to be there being a problem). There, they meet the cheerful Magical Heroic Dragon and the strict Saber Jetpack Cyborg while they also find Platinum (learning that he and Glittery are some of the Castle Kingdom fighters). They are sent to an underground room and meet their neighbour Turt Wig Train. Turt secretly helps them to get  to the surface and explains to them that Castle Kingdom is split in 7 kingdoms, 3 Nexus Force controlled, 3 Maelstrom controlled and a ruined one where there is no one anymore. They meet Dragon and when a battle begins they learn he is the leader of the Iron Riders, who are the elite Knights of the Castle Kingdom. The minifigures get into the fight and Shadow saves Cyborg, but is recognised by him. After that, they were locked in their room and Shadow sort of made friends with Rad Laser Lava, the person who brought them food.

To be continued.
