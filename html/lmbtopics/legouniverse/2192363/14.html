Chapter 51: Iron Riders

They were at the Square of the Knight, the busiest place of the Castle. Around the Square there were many stores and stalls where minifigures where selling parts of panoplies, bricks, books and other things. The castle had a round shape and was seperated by two vertical with each other walls in four sections: the northern, the southern, the eastern and the western. Square of the Knight was in the western section.
All of them except for Turt were wearing cloaks and hoods, so they wouldn't recognise. When Sarah had asked if this would not seem odd to others, Turt had answered that a lot of people had adapted this style, so it wouldn't be something odd.
Turt had also explained them that there were seven (known) Castles in the world. The biggest Castle was the one in which they were and the central one. Six Castles were around this one. Two of them belonged to the Nexus Force and the three were infected by the Maelstrom.
"What happened to the last one?" Shadow had asked when Turt had stopped talking.
"Oh, yeah! There are only abandoned ruins there. We don't know why the Maelstrom hasn't infected it but as far Nexus Force is concerned, we cannot reach it because Maelstrom is between our Castles and the ruined one" had been Turt's answer.
At that time they were staring at a huge statue of a knight which was in the centre of the square. As he kept looking at it, Shadow started walking but he came across someone and stumbled on him.
"I'm so sorry! It's all my-" Shadow started saying but was interrupted: "Shadow!".
It was Dragon...
"What are you doing here?! You shouldn't be here!" he exclaimed.
"Turt brought us" Magical replied.
Dragon opened his mouth to say something but he didn't manage to because the sound of some bells was heard. Although Shadow hadn't ever heard that bells before, he knew what it meant: it was an alarm for battle...
"Great!!! A fight!" Magical said loudly.
"You don't understand! It's dangerous! You must go to your room!" Dragon exclaimed.
Suddenly, a loud voice was heard all over the Castle: "ALL THE IRON RIDERS, GATHER TO THE NORTHWESTERN TOWER!"
"What are the Iron Riders?" Sarah wondered.
"They are the elite of the Castle Kingdom. There are 20 of them: 13 Sentinels, 4 Venture League, 3 Paradox and none Assembly. The panoply of these Knights-Riders and of their horses is made of Imagination Iron, a compound of Iron and Imagination. This material is one of these few that cannot be infected and is very difficult to be damaged" Dragon responded as he changed panoply to a silver shining one and started walking towards the Northwestern Tower.
"Are you an Iron Rider?" Shadow asked.
"Actually, I'm their leader!" Dragon answered and then left running. . .

P.S.: For those who have read the few chapters of "The last battle for Ninjago": I was the one that came up with the idea of the Iron Riders. My friend just liked the idea and put it to his story, too. For the record my friend is Magical Heroic Dragon.

Chapter 52: The Nexus HQ

As they watched Dragon leaving, Magical said "So, are we just going to sit here or are we going to the battle?".
Shadow, who was still surprised by the fact that Dragon was the leader of the Iron Riders, looked at him and answered with one word "Battle".
"YESSS!!!" Magical exclaimed.
They followed Turt who agreed to take them with him.
"The bells that are ringing are from the Northwestern Tower, so the battle must be somewhere close to it" he explained.
As soon as they passed the wall and came to the Northern part of the Castle, they started hearing the sounds of the battle. Then, suddenly, Shadow told "Finally!".
"What happened?" Sarah asked.
"Have you ever heard of the Nexus HQ?" Shadow replied with a question.
"Yes! It's a database that gathers all the information about Nexus Force, Imagination and the Maelstrom" Turt said.
"Exactly! Well, when I was making my gear I hacked it and put all the information in the F-phone I have embodied in my helm. When I start-" Shadow explained but was cut by Turt: "What you hacked the Nexus HQ?! Do you know that this is illegal?! What will happen if they catch you?!".
"They won't!" Shadow continued "I'm a very good hacker. And you didn't let me finish. When I started searching the information, I realised that there was a secret part that needed a password to search it and finally after so many days I found the password! The most weird thing is that the password is For a better future. Let's see what creatures we can find here: Maelstrom Knights... Maelstrom Riders... Maelstrom Catapults... Maelstrom Archers... Dark Knights... Maelstrom Dragons... and Maelstrom Warlocks. That's interesting!". . .

Chapter 53: Time for battle

"Let's go!" Magical exclaimed "I can't wait!".
They approached the outer wall and the closer they got the louder the sounds of the battle sounded. They reached a stairway which they immediately started climbing. When they arrived at the edge of the staircase the sounds were louder than ever.
Almost immediately, they were attacked by dozens of maelstrom arrows. Shadow sticked his shield out and Magical blocked them with his swords. Turt just dodged them while Sarah went behind Shadow.
Shadow didn't wait any second to strike back. While he kept his crossbow under the protecton of his cloak, he started launching arrows to the archers.
Then, a wooden ladder appeared in front of Magical. He, although he usually wasn't very clever, during battles he was acted like a genious. He waited until the Maelstrom Knights had climbed to the edge of the ladder and then pushed it, so when the ladder crashed all the knights smashed.
Meanwhile Sarah was smashing knights with blasts from her sword. As she looked down she could see thousands of soldiers, so she wasn't throwing to anyone special. Turt, following the steps of Magical, was throwing ladders to the ground.
They were all going very well until the catapults were put ino action. Bombs were falling around them creating craters and shaking the wall making them lose their balance and falling. Shadow put some very special arrows in his crossbow that as soon as they touched the catapults, they were put in fire.
"Wow!" Turt admired.
Then, something weird happened. A silver shining triangular shape went out of the wall and started smashing the Knights, Archers and Catapults that were in its way. When Shadow looked better he realised that they were the Iron Riders. Shadow realised why those Knights were the elite of Castle Kingdom. Their power and virtuosity was incredible. Even Magical wouldn't be able to defeat one of them.
Afterwards, one minifigure appeared near to them that made Shadow to worry. He pushed Magical at his arm and pointed discretely to the minifigure.
"Cyborg!!!" he exclaimed but Shadow quickly closed his mouth.
They told it to Sarah, too and they agreed to leave slowly but when they tried to materialize their plan the wall cracked into two where Magical was standing making him fall. However, the last second he grabbed from an overhang. Shadow threw him a rope and with the help of Sarah and another minifiure he didn't know, they pulled him up.
However, by the time Magical was saved Maelstrom Knights had invaded the wall and the one leading was huge. Magical immediately emerged towards the huge knight although he was shorter than him. He grabbed him, lifted him and with superhuman strength threw him away. After that he started slicing the rest of the Knights. Soon reinsforments arrived and helped the hero. Between of those who came was Cyborg. Suddenly, a Knight emerged towards him but Shadow got in his way and smash him. However, while he jumped to save Cyborg his hood left from his head revealing his identity. . .

Chapter 54: Imprisoned!

Shadow was staring at Katie angry. They were at their room locked. Cyborg instead of thanking him for saving his life, had yelled at him and had sent them to their room where he had locked them. Turt had been put to availability because of breaking the rules.
Shadow was staring at Katie sitting on a chair in front of a desk and hoped she would wake up soon. He then continued building what he was building before still staring at Katie. When he finished what he was doing he put it at the side and started building something else.
This continued for long days. A minifigure with Rank 3 Inventor gear was coming every six hours to bring food. His name was Rad Laser Lava and he sometimes was staying and talked with them a little.
"You know, we could take your friend to the sanitarium, so she gets better more quickly" he said once.
"You mean Katie. No! I want her with me" Shadow answered curtly and coldly.
"Oh, come on! Some of the best members of the Building Squad are there. You can trust them!".
"I haven't heard of it" Shadow continued talking coldly.
"You haven't heard of it!!! It's the most important, popular and the knownest Squad of all. I am a member of it and as I can see you could join it, too!" Rad told him watching his hands which were working really fast.
"No, thank you! I am part of a team and as I can see it could become a squad itself" Shadow replied.
"It's not that easy, boy!" Rad said "The founder of this Squad needed a year to persuade the leaders to make the Building Squad; and he was one of their best friends!".
Shadow smiled for the first time after a lot of days.
"I bet all of my unused bricks that by the end of the year my team will have become a Squad and by the time I get 30 years old it will have become more popular than yours" Shadow responded.
"Okay, see ya in one year" Rad told him and then left. . .

Chapter 55: The Awakening

Some more days passed and Shadow started making holes in one wall. When two big holes were ready he put one of what he was building all these days in each of the holes. Τhen, he did something with a remote he had built.
Then, Magical who was worried about him asked Shadow "Ehh! Shadow, what are you doing?".
"Wait and see!" Shadow answered enthusiastically.
Magical, who was surprised that Shadow was happy, looked at Sarah who pried her shoulders showing her ignorance.
Afterwards Shadow put conected a wire to the television shouted "It's ready!!!" and Sarah asked "What's ready?".
"My fighting shouters" Shadow explained "I built some shouters, so we can fight even though we are here".
"Well, that explains a lot" Magical said "However, how can we see what happens in the battle?".
"I connected them with the television" Shadow replied.
"Awesome!" Magical exclaimed and opened the TV. Shadow gave him the remote and expalined him how to use it. On the screen, which was seperated into two, they could see the battlefield where several knights, Nexus Force and Maelstrom, were fighting.
Then, suddenly, Shadow heard a weak voice which made him really happy and that he had to hear a lot of days saying "I want to play with it". Katie was awake. . .
