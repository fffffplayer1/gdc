Well, I suppose this more concerns Buzz, but I might as well tell ya all. I've built a LEGO version of the Alcubierre War Portal launcher. I used a Bionicle spinner launcher and several other parts to build this, and it works great. I'll send it to the Creation Lab soon.

JAMMERS OFF!!!

L.S.S. Redemption Update

The Redemption was coming along well, but I hit a snag. Namely, I wasn't totally happy with the way it was going. Thus, I am currently in the process of dismantling much of the ship, taking an inventory of the parts I have, and getting set to start building again. I've decided to make the ship a little more compact, with less of a giant flat-bottomed boat look. The goal is to streamline the look, and turn it into a more space-worthy craft.

Most of you have likely seen the latest batch of pictures, but if you haven't, here's the link to them:

http://universe.lego.com/en-us/community/creationlab/mycreationdisplay.aspx?id=91f6fb3c-9ae4-4767-803e-df70d6abd808

I'm going to completely revise the center section, get rid of that horrible pancake roof, and work on maybe getting the ship to look less like a flying box. By streamlining, I'll save pieces. This will allow me to continue the ship, and make it look better.

Odds on, that's the last you'll see of the ship for about another three weeks. I hope to finish it by the end of June at the most.

Best,

~ DS
