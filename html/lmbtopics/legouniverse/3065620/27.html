I suppose you could, but for most of us, the Creation Lab is easier to use...

Actually, just follow these instructions.

1. Follow this link:

http://universe.lego.com/en-us/community/creationlab/displaychallengelist.aspx

2. Click on "LEGO Universe Gallery". That is where we upload all our stuff.

3. You will a button in the "Challenge" window that that says "Join Challenge". Click that to get to the Add Creation page.

4. From there, just browse your computer for the wanted pictures, and upload them. You can have up to 5 pictures per creation.
