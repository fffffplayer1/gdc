So? Doesn't seem to be a big concern, right? Well, guess what happened in two pages no less? Crysal joined the Riders of Pancakes (or whatever they're called) and the Kreativity Kingdom and I think the Crimson Order.

JAMMERS OFF!

-Buzzsaw

JAMMERS ON!!! RANGERS ONLY!!!

First off, I think I owe you guys a bit of an apology for being off the grid so much. School has actually been *fairly* light this week, but I've been using a lot of extra time in building. I've got a talent show coming up, and I might be displaying some of my LEGO builds. As such, I've putting a lot towards those. I'll see about posting some pics on the CL over the weekend.

Now, on to the more major topic, we definitely need to show support for them, but I don't think the Rangers can move into a fight against the Crysal shipyards, especially since they are allied with RoP and CO. Personally, I'd rather wait for Crysal to attack Ninjaboy, and then we can move in with good reason. An insult by a small HQ against a larger, better-established HQ is hardly anything to take seriously im my opinion.

I've requested that the MMRL cease their attack, at least for PR concerns, though they still have Rangers support.

Still, I'm rather thinking that the CO would be pretty hesitant to join a full-fledged war over an insult. I'll see what they think, though I'd guess by now you've probably spoken with them, Buzz. What we need to do is keep this from exploding into a massive war, at least one caused by us and our allies. We should try and avoid a WWI scenario.

That's my opinion, what does everyone else think?

~ DS, Dear Leader
