Here is a description of my personal ship:

Name: Raven A3i

Appearance:

I built this from bricks I collected in LEGO Universe. My ship looks like that of the LEGO Agents Jet set 8630. It has been repainted orange and blue and grey.  It has the Rangers symbol on its wings and fins. 

Weapons:

  • 2 blaster cannons that have pinpoint accuracy due to a revolutionary targeting system, able to shoot targets the size of a baseball from 1 1/2 kilometer away. 
  • 2 homing missile launchers
  • 2 regular blaster cannons on top the wings.

Speed/Manuverability: The Raven A3i can go up to 1150 kilometers per hour, over the speed of sound. It has excellent manuverability.

Customizations:

  • 2 extra regular blaster cannons
  • New colors and symbols
  • A cloaking device that can go 30 min. at a time before having to recharge. The Raven A3i takes 1 hour to recharge.
  • My ship has smoke canisters around the fins, which, when released, will temporarily blind anyone behind the Raven.

 

 

Reaction: The Rangers have an official symbol?
