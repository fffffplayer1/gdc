Rangers,

 

In case you all haven't noticed (actually, I know you have by your posts ), LURP is quite possibly about to enter a new era.  As such, I've been thinking about creating a new HQ, based off the Rangers, of course, but completely re-styled.  We'll still have the Redemption, Supernova, Vista, etc., but we'll have a new name, topic and look.

 

Given that we're trying to restart LURP, it would appear to make that, like Krill Mathias has done, we restart our HQ for a new generation.  However, I also know that many of you are probably rather attached to this HQ, and don't get me wrong, I am rather so as well.   That said, though, I'm of the opinion that rebooting this would do a lot of good, and allow LURP to proceed along a new path.

 

So, what are your opinions?  A captain is nothing without a crew, so if you all want to stick with this HQ, we'll stay.

Well, opinions away!
