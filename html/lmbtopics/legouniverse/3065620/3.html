Anyway, the new base is on an artic planet as you know. The planet is located within a pocket dimension, meaning very hard to enter. Pocket dimensions are known in StarWars as otherspace. I had a couple of setup ideas. Tell me what you think of this:

For the main complex, the base is made up of three cylindrical bases, errected upright and then extending several hundred meters underground. Each is connected underground through tunnels and has a hangar at the top. Each is powered by the geothermal energy provided by the planet's warm core.

In addition, the trio of complexes are located close to high peaks. Natural weather sends blustering frigid snowstorms through the mountains over the base. In several of the mountains, hangars are located which are attached to one of the main cylindrical complexes. In case of attack, the winds will provide little visibility for landing enemy ships, whereas fighters can launch from these mountains and take out any ships, or actually cause disorientation because of the conditions.

Also, I was thinking about there being two other smaller complexes.

A shipyards and supply depot in the north, where large ships like the Zenith and Redemption could dock and be refitted. Possibly even SSX.

For the third area, I was thinking a copy of the laser on the Zenith and on SSX. A hypermatter laser for anti-ship defense.

Anyway, tell me what you think!
