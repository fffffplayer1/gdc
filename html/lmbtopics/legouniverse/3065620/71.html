Welcome to the brand new headquarters of the Rangers, minifig. We were previously known as [][]The Rangers II[][], but that topic had been overrun by Maelstrom, and, as our goals have changed, we need this new headquarters topic. To fill in several plot holes, here is the official Rangers story…


“The Rangers were originally formed about the middle of the Void Empire/Nexus Force war as a mercenary group. Attacking whomever they chose and working for pay. Then, the true evil of the Void Empire was revealed. They laid siege to the Rangers planet with thousands of ships, and were barely fought off. After this, Darek Skychaser officially chose to ally the Rangers with the Nexus Force, settling his conscience by becoming a group dedicated to defending the weak, and fighting for good.

“Following a Maelstrom attack on their planet, the Rangers chose to remove the original base from this space-time continuum, and move to a new planet. This is present day.”


And that is our story, simplified as much as I could. The Rangers have actually been involved in numerous other battles, among them the Battle for the Black Diamond, the Attack on the Sentinel HQ II (not one of our prouder moments) and an earlier attack on the Void Empire.

Currently, we have several active members, more than a lot of HQs on this forum, and are very well established in the LEGO Universe Roleplay community. Our members are:

~ Darek Skychaser, also known as DS, the commander of the Rangers and captain of the L.S.S. Redemption

~ Buzzsaw, second in command of the Rangers, captain of the L.S.S. Vista and intelligence specialist

~ Jayden Crow – Captain of the L.S.S. Eagle and all-around great guy

~ Sharpie – Fighter squadron commander

Those are all the members of the Rangers who have been active pretty recently. As one can see, we have a fairly good force, especially for Roleplay.

All this said, we have indeed become fairly popular here, and plan to keep on growing here in this new HQ. More information will be posted about the Rangers soon, but for now, if you want to join, please post a bio of your characters, and I will get back to you as soon as possible.
