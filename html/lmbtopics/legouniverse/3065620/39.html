By request, I shall now present the inventory of Rangers large scale tech and units. Some stuff will be marked classified, because I don't wish to tell the absolute aspects of it.

Crystal Gravfield Trap: A highly expensive and relatively rare device that detects cloaked ships and other craft at long range by looking for fluctuations in space.

Tractor Field: CLASSIFIED.

CFDS: CLASSIFIED.

Maser Array: Most Rangers craft have otherwise rare maser arrays. Maser arrays create beams and fields similar to lasers, but send them with a much more powerful kinetic push, hitting hard on multiple spectrums. They have high maintenance, and therefore cannon be used extensively or for long periods of time in active mode. Variants of the weapon include a supermaser weapon, used on the Zenith cruisers and on some Rangers installations.

Railgun 4G: The fourth addition of the classic Rangers railgun is a highly powerful asset to the fleet. Over time, their power requirements and maintenance have been drastically reduced. Railguns use electromagnetic fields to fire high power shells for extremely long distances more accurately and more devastating than lasers. Speeds of railgun shells have been clocked at up to Mach 10.

Plasma Cannons: Plasma cannons assist the other weapons of cruisers in different roles. They are powered by plasma reactions. Update is in progress.

Free Electron Laser 3G: An FEL is an innovative electron conducting apparatus with variable control. An FEL can be configured for many uses and are good assets because of their ability to create beams from any part of the electromagnetic spectrum, from microwave fields for frying electronics or gamma rays, capable of rapidly draining shields. A 1G FEL once drained 10% of the Black Diamond's shields within several minutes. (Fairly and acceptably as allowed by the enemy)

Alcubierre Portal: An Alcubierre portal is an energy draining weapon which creates a wave which would cause the fabric of space ahead of a spacecraft to contract and the space behind it to expand. The ship would then ride this wave inside a region of flat space known as a warp bubble, and would not move within this bubble, but instead be carried along as the region itself moves as a consequence of the actions of entering the portal. Upon reaching the exit point of the portal, the object is unable to adapt to subspace, and is flung into light particles faster than light, creating a very powerful weapon, or counter-weapon. (I copied and pasted a little bit from Wikipedia by the way. The consequences of exiting the portal is from Popular Science, quoting a University of Texas study)

Slipspace Bomb: Several slipspace bombs were acquired from the Kreativity Kingdom around the time of the Third Battle of the Black Diamond.

There are more weapons that I have plans for but aren't in service.

Particle Shields: Rangers craft use Particle Shields for foreign object defense.

Electromagnetic Shields: Rangers craft use electromagnetic fields to protect against most attacks.

Retem/Liberty Class: The Liberty Class is the mainstay of the Rangers fleet. The Liberty Class provides perimeter cover for taskforces, performing roles like anti-starfighter and are designed to absorb lots of punishment, meant for the other members of the fleet. There are currently around 40 in active service.

Redemption Class: The Redemption is the flagship of the Rangers cause, filled with more weapons and so crosswired it can lose half its systems. It can take tons of damage and pour it back. Originally, two other ships of similar caliber and design were made, giving the ship its own class, but currently, only the Redemption is in active service.

Zenith Class: The Zenith Class goes against most Ranger philosophy. Most Rangers ships are extremely survivable and powerful, through a combination of speed and efficient size. The Zenith class is slower and less versatile, but has more weapons. Therefore, the Zenith Class is not the flagship class, but more of a heavy hitter. The chief weapon of each ship is a large SuperMaser, that takes an enormous amount of power and time to charge, meaning the ship is more or less unarmed, yet fully shielded during this period. Only three are in active service.

Glorious Class: The Glorious Class is a much smaller ship, a heavy frigate. The craft is designed for weapons testing and special uses. Such as attacking other craft and defending the fleet with advanced systems. The Glorious class is the only ship class made for the Alcubierre portal or the Free Electron Laser. However, flagships like the Eagle will carry Alcubierres and the Grapple Class was eventually armed with Free Electron Lasers. Around 15 or so are in active service.

Grapple Class: The Grapple Class is a fairly large cruiser, slightly smaller than the Redemption. The Grapple Class provides the central firepower in a Rangers taskforce, with scores of Masers, Railguns, and some FELs. There is roughly 1 Grapple cruiser for every two Liberty cruisers.

Snoita Class: The Snoita Class is a medium sized class of cruiser, designed for a mix of firepower and starfighters. They are the main carrier class of the Rangers. They were the basis for the larger Grapple class. Around 5 are in service.

Xesse Class: The Xesse Class Starfighter is main fighter of the Rangers and is designed for high maneuverability and firepower. It has a small pod shaped design, which allows for easy and compact storage. Roughly 400 are in service.

Notre Class: The Notre Class Starfighter is a smaller delta wing craft, designed for escort and CAS. They can also act as parasite spacecraft, deploying from ships with no hangar. They are some what lightly armed, but can evade or take most attacks from other fighters.

Mit Class: The Mit Class Transport is a freighter sized craft, designed for heavy transport.

Dex Class: The Dex Class is a special ship type, designed for dropping down Rangers if any situation. They carry heavy torpedoes or missiles for annihilating targets that might pose a particular threat.

Protector Class: The Protector Class is a special craft type made for deploying Alcubierres over short periods of time. Only a few exist and are used for defense of Nexus Force population centers in the presence of a superweapon attack. Cough Xindi Cough

Aurora Class: CLASSIFIED. Dun Dun DUUUUUNNNNN. Note the name.

Agase Class: CANCELLED OR DESROYED.

SSX: SSX is the popular designation for the Rangers specialized shipyards and battlestation. It contains extremely rare technology, that people like MIT would fall dead if they knew what it could do.

There's some other stuff, like our installations, land vehicles, and the wormhole drive. The ground vehicle stats are just like a page back or so, and most people know what a wormhole drive is.
