(This first thing in response to LT's post)

*Two gleaming eyes are seen in the darkness*



"Greetings. My name is Mechanizer, though none of you will recognize it.
I am the head of the Maelstrom. Today marks the beginning of the end.
It has begun."

*All at once several monstrous roars resound all throughout the HQ. Hulking brutes smash out of the walls, ventilation shafts, supply closets, etc, and proceed to rip the HQ apart. Any minifigures they encounter are smashed.*

I do not have a decent name for then yet, but I think I'll just call them the Plague.
The Plague are the result of the infection of the Nano-Maelstrom. They are microscopic Maelstrom creatures capable of wrenching minifigures out of their right mind, slowly tuning them into creatures if the Maelstrom. The resulting Maelstrom menace, the Plague, are like raging animals. They are not much larger than minifigures. They possess unnatural strength and durability, and surprising speed. Their skin is protected by a thick layer of solidified, energized, Maelstrom. In its Maelstrom armor, is ingrained with a metal. Such an armor is not penetrate by Imagination. At the heart of a Plague, is the core; a ball of near completely pure Maelstrom. When the armor is damaged, it feeds itself to it to repair the armor very quickly. Almost pure, it can regenerate itself through the Core of pure Maelstrom itself. The Nano-Maelstrom die in their endeavor to create this monster.

The Nano-Maelstrom got here in the first cardboard box. As they are microscopic, they are not visible to he naked eye. When the first cardboard box arrived on your doorstep, the Nano-Maelstrom emptied themselves from the box and entered into the HQ. As a large amount of Maelstrom would be quickly detected, only fifty Nano-Maelstrom were in the box.
Only twenty of the Plague have been unleashed so far.

Note that nobody in your HQ knows of the Nano-Maelstrom, only of the Plague. Act accordingly. All moves that involve the Nano-Maelstrom will be ignored.

~Mechanizer.

I know you're not going to like this MY, oh goodness I can't believe I did that!

You remember how I put the box in White Imaginite? I recommend you read up on White Imaginite on the Brick-Tech topic. It's a special extremely volatile substance that annihilates Dark Imagination and Maelstrom. Mixing the two opposite forces creates an extreme reaction, but gives neither supremacy.

This is not a joke. I'm serious. It was LL's plot twist before he left.

*or rather, a large explosion takes place above ground on the Rangers HQ, not your move*

Therefore, the only thing you did was create a massive crater on the Rangers planet. I know you're not gonna like it, but that's what my move was, and you never contested it.

-Buzzsaw

Also, using this nano-maelstrom seems to be technically a biological weapon by using your own description of it, and therefore nulled by the rules of roleplay.

1: Show me where they have been outlawed.

2: "Pharmacology any substance, as a serum or vaccine, derived from animal products or other biological sources and used to treat or prevent disease."
Taken straight from a dictionary.

1. "13.
You can have a gun, weapon, blaster or fantasy weapon. Let’s keep the weapons kid-friendly and don’t name and/or use real-life weapons, weapons of mass destruction or chemical/biological weapons."


2. "a harmful biological agent (as a pathogenic microorganism or a neurotoxin) used as a weapon to cause death or disease usually on a large scale"

Maelstrom is a living organism or else it couldn't infect living things.

I rest my case.

-Buzzsaw
