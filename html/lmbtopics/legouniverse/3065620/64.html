Ready for an update?

We've developed a whole lot o' junk recently!

JAMMERS ON! RANGERS ONLY! NO SPREADING OF INFO.

JMTM: Joint Multi Target Munition.

The JMTM is a joint system made up of bomblets capable of targeting up to 35 independent baddies in the smallest version. It can target vehicles, personnel, starfighters, or missiles. It can be carried by starfighters or fired by torpedo tubes. A single one launched from a Liberty cruiser can annihilate a squadron of starfighters.

JAMMERS OFF!

Courageous Block II:

The Courageous is having a partial upgrade, with several high power mass driver cannons, spanning parts of it, capable of smashing up heavy cruisers.

Redemption Block II: A new sensor suite has been added and new link system to allow it to get a lot of information quickly.

Redemption Class: The two Redemption class reserve cruisers have been outfitted for heavy hitting, getting a total refit and aesthetic changes. They've received several mass driver cannons similar to the ones added to the Courageous. They've also received large interplanetary missile pods.

Grapple Block II: The Grapple Class cruisers have received an update to their hangars and a new space saving design change, giving them two new batteries of Maser arrays. Also, they're receiving a large engine refitting, which will change their shape significantly.

Liberty Class Block II: The Liberty cruisers have received a new power system, giving them more power to put towards their shields.

Glorious Class Block III: Five new Glorious cruisers are being built, while the others are being updated with several new power subreactors.



~Commander Scott "Buzzsaw" Norman ~ Eris Redbeam ~ Nijki ~ Nap O. Leon
