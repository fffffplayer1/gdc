JAMMERS ON!!! RANGERS ONLY!!!



Alright, folks, I've got a couple of quick announcments to make in one of the famous Rangers updates.



First up, I've been thinking it over, and we aren't going about fixing LURP the right way. Instead of passing legislation or something, let's actually lead the way. We'll start just doing fun stuff, like running missions to steal Soranos' toaster () while fighting with bottles of ketchup (), and being friendly with everyone. We can lead the way, and make things better.



Second, I've got a bunch of pictures of my LEGO Creations which I'll be uploading to the Creation Lab soon. One's already up, and you can check it out here:



http://universe.LEGO.com/en-us/community/creationlab/displaycreation.aspx?id=8dc78051-7498-4d85-bdfa-2c41ffc02ae4&challenge&challengeid=C8F3FB2D-9B0B-4A4C-85E5-F30515314DBE



It's the L.S.S. Arrowell, what will be our new flagship. School's starting soon for me, but I do hope to have it finished by around October. It will be smaller than the Redemption, but as I said, it will be better designed and detailed.



Third, in regard to the ARA invasion, I'm giving Mistika a chance to back down before we are forced to destroy him. Personally, I'd like to try and get him to join this HQ again. I'm sure he can be a lot of fun, and I want to include everyone.



That's pretty much it for right now. I'm logging off LURP, but I might post a little more tonight. Until then, see ya'll later!



~ DS



JAMMERS OFF!!!

Well, normally we all chip in a little bit, but right now it's mostly Commander Buzzsaw who runs that department. However, one of the core elements of the Rangers HQ was to be able to build a ship of your very own to use. In fact, that was the original plan. The Rangers would operate from the starship Redemption, and use their individual fighters in battle.

If you want, it would be great if you could build a custom spaceship, in real LEGO or on LDD, and post it to the Creation Lab for us to check out. Then, you can use it in LURP.

Best,
