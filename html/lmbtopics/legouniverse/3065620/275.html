Welp, I tried to post a "Goodbye LURP Party" for the forum. As I feared, it didn't get past moderation. I would throw one here for us Rangers instead, but we would only have five days, not to mention that for the moment, there's no Rangers in sight. I was afraid no one would be around while this whole thing went down.

 

In light of that, I have one last thing I want to do. I'm not gonna spend five hours giving myself a headache making this whole speech perfect, although I may have to do some cutting because as I'm sure you all know I am one to blabber. . LURP itself may merely be merging with the LU forums, but the LU forums don't have much time themselves. They closed the Creation Lab a couple days ago, and I know for a fact they have their sites set on the MBs.

 

I joined a little after I had started playing F2P on LU. I started out in the Maelstrom HQ, until I decided to literally have my character convert to the other side. I had no idea that I was about to go on an adventure that it would pain me to leave. The time I've spent drama-fying Vastus' life (,) all the fun I've had with you guys in the process. I've had a good time with some of the nicest seeming Christian people a person could ask to meet over the internet.

 

I cannot express how thankful I am to all of you. LURP to me is a childhood memory, and it's not going away any time soon. You guys out there like Buzz, DS, Sharpie, Indy... Heck, all of you have literally impacted my life. I owe each and every one of you a lifetime supply of PDCPL ammo () for the things you've done for me by just, being yourselves.

 

Hopefully the majority of those this is addressed to out there will read this before LURP ends up gone for good. I just wanted to say thanks for everything. Vastus owes you, wherever the heck he is. .

 

Stay frosty guys.
