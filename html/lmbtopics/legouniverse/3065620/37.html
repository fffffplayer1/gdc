Alright, this is extremely important, so listen carefully. We are seriously on the clock, and have to move fast.



First, this is what is going on. The Nexus Force and Rebellion are going to attack and defeat the Void Empire once and for all. Sources seem to show the Void preparing for something big, so we have to hit them before they can hit us. Our intelligence is limited, but based on the writers of JAMMED posts, we know several things:



1. Due to the amount of JAMMED activity, the Void is definitely preparing something big.



2. In addition to the Void, BuckyKat has been seem posting, seeming to suggest that SoL is involved.



Given this, we can draw the conclusion that, yes, the Void is likely planning to invade. As such we are preparing to strike first, and strike quickly... tomorrow.



Second, this our basic plan. Fire-Type is going to lead the invasion on the Void world, while we, given our numbers, cover other "good worlds". Buzzsaw will be going with Fire-Type, since he has a special trick up his sleeve for the Void.



We, meanwhile, will fan out to other worlds, and I'll fly around some to make sure that you are holding out well. Then, when it is confirmed that the Void can no longer launch an invasion, we'll all converge on the Void, and defeat them forever.



Three, the purpose of this is for the overall good of LURP. Fire-Type and I have a vision of a LEGO Universe Roleplay based in LEGO. Duels will still exist, but fleets will not. Building will reign, and we'll hold competitions in the Creation Lab. With our words and bricks, we will create a whole new Universe, based off LEGO Universe...



Lastly, I want to give the opportunity to opt out of this mission. Yes, we would no longer have giant space battles, but there would still be plenty of opportunity for adventure, without all the espionage, treachery, and things have made LURP mirror the real world too much. I probably could have said that more eloquently, but whatever. Basically, we are just trying to put the LEGO back into LEGO Universe Roleplay, and the way to do that is to first defeat the "evil" HQs, and then to completely discard fleets. Starfighters and rockets? Sure, but nothing like what we have now.



I'll post more plans later. I hope all of you have a wonderful Sunday afternoon!



~ DS
