Ha, Ha, Ha, I knew it had to come. But my parents didn't come to action quite that fast, It took them a whole month of LU to make the one-hour decision. Good luck on your story, I like how it is turning out so far, but there are to many lose ends in the story that you should begin tying up before you lose your audience. I am not saying that that will happen, but I know from experience that if the suspense and mystery of a story goes to long unanswered, people start forgetting about it in the first place, and when the answer is revealed in the end, they don't have all the appreciation that they would have had if the secret was told sooner. I hope you will receive my advice in the spirit of a peer, and will not be offended by it.
