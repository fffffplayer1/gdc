"A Hero Rising" - Prologue

Bob loved his job. He stood there everyday in his plain red shirt and blue pants, greeting hundreds of minifigures everyday and directing them where they needed to go. Things were so peaceful on board the Venture Explorer that sometimes he forgot there was a war going on. A war that required thousands of soldiers, and every minifigure he met was just that. He had many friends on board, mostly just the pilots and instructors, since the Nexus Force recruits only had a short stay before leaving to serve in the army. Bob couldn't imagine himself doing anything else besides being a guide on this grand ship. But then there was that day. The day that changed Bob's life forever. It changed him, and it changed the way he looked at the universe forever. He would never be just plain Bob anymore, with his plain clothes and pasted-on smile, just like everyone else. No. He was different. He would become a hero.

-The Dude

"A Hero Rising" - Chapter 1

On the second day of February in the year 2011, the Maelstrom was finally able to hack the Nexus Force mainframe. In an instant, all of the explorers and faction members in the Nexus Force became glitched. They could not chat with each other; they could not use their rockets; they could not change outfits; some of them could not even move. In even stranger cases, some minifigures reported seeing sharks jumping around the main circle in Nimbus Station!

The glitches traveled throughout the universe, eventually reaching the Venture Explorer where Bob worked. With minifigures unable to use their rockets, new recruits became stranded on the ship with no weapons to defend themselves from a Maelstrom attack. The new recruits were frantic and cried for help, but no re-inforcements could reach the ship without rockets. Only one experienced member of the Nexus Force was aboard the Venture Explorer at the time of the hacking: Bob.

Bob tried to counter the hack by using his super-computer on the ship, but the Maelstrom had already hacked into the central mainframe and cut off all electronic communications. With no re-inforcements, no weapons for the new recruits, and an imminent Maelstrom assault on the Venture Explorer, Bob had only one option left...
