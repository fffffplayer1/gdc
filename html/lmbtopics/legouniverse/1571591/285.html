"A Hero Rising" - Chapter 21.4

The next few moments were a blur. All Bob could remember about those precious seconds were the appalled faces of the scout and the Mythran – and behind them, though his vision was becoming increasingly cloudy, he thought he could see Commander Canax snickering.
Bob gazed at his hands. They were completely purple, with razor-sharp claws. Even now, the dark, unpleasant scar was clearly visible on his palm.
Instantly, he thrashed his fists at the Mythran, who consequentially darted to the other side of the room (followed by the scout). Commander Canax held out his hands, which were still cuffed together. With one swift slash of his hand, Bob snapped the chain in two. At this point, he slammed his hands on the door and kicked it with all his might. It did not budge.
But he did not give up. He lunged over the metal chair and landed right in front of the scout and the Mythran. At this point, his hearing was not much better than his eyesight. He saw the Nexus Force scout's moving, but he could not hear any sound. Instead, he heard Commander Canax's voice echoing the same phrase in his head – "get the staff".
Once again, his hands flew toward the two minifigures cowering in front of him. This time, there was no escape. Bob grasped the red staff and ripped it from the scout's hands. Turning, he promptly tossed it to Commander Canax.
What happened next was the most baffling of all. Canax thrust the staff toward the wall, and a dark whirlwind began emanating from it. As it reached the wall, it created a large hole in the metal. Looking through it, he saw Nimbus Station. Not a Maelstrom-infested Nimbus Station, but a peaceful Nimbus Station – the one he had come to know and love so quickly. This brought him back to his senses. Bob turned back into regular old Bob, in his plain red shirt and plain blue jeans.
He stepped through the portal, and he never saw Commander Canax again.

-The Dude
