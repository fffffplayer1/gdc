Hael: If you knew, you wouldn't want to know.
Baron: Well fine then! Don't tell me who it was! I'll just figure it out myself!
30 seconds later...
Baron: *Gasp!* Oh, now I know why I don't want to know. Well, we might as well find out how to get back to the real world.

Hael: What? Oh, you mean computer. No, I'm not making fun of you, but that computer store is the type of place Overbuild would go!
Baron: Ok then, let's go in the other direction! *turns around to walk away, but Hael grabs him*
Hael: Don't you understand? Overbuild would know how to get out of this mess!
Baron: Oh.

Baron: Weird. Well, let's download it!

Hael: YOU JUST PRESSED ON MY FOOT!
Baron: Oops, I thought it was the mouse.
Hael: *Facepalm* Okay, Let's let it load!
Baron: What does 'ready in 53 minutes' mean?
Hael: What do you think?
Baron: Here, let's watch that thing! *clicks on 'the adventure begins here! video*
Computer: 4 explorers, traversing the reaches of space, and time, united in their seach for a unique and precious element!
Baron: HEY THAT'S US!
Hael: Weird.
53 minutes later...
Hael: It's ready!
Baron: *Wakes up suddenly* What is?! I just had a dream where we got stuck in a weird place and- OH COME ON!
Hael: Logging in... there! Hey, it's avant gardens!
2 hours later...
Hael: *gasp!* It's Overbuild!
Computer Overbuild: Hello, would you like to join the assembly faction?
Hael: Chat...*types in 'I'm the real Hael, Overbuild! The Baron's with me! We got stuck in NYC! Please help us!'*
Overbuild: Okay, by tracing your computer signal, I can tell that you are... in NYC! I'll teleport myself there! But I'm not sure if my teleporter device goes through trans-dimensional shifts!
Hael: Just try it!
Overbuild: Okay, here it GOES!
*Teleportation maching appears in front of Baron and Hael*
Baron: Where's Overbuild?
Overbuild in computer: RRGH! I made a teleportation machine that only teleports itself!
Hael: Well that's a complete waste of time. How will we get you here?
Baron: Why don't we just walk through the screen of the pomcuter and just appear there?
Overbuild: DON'T BE RIDICIOULUS! That is impossible! The screen of a computer is glass, you can't walk through it withoug breaking it!
*Baron walks through computer screen and appears in LU*
Overbuild: ACK! Oh, wow,*Hael comes too* this is amazing! It's a whole new technology! This could revolutinize-
FBI agent: I'm sorry, but civilians are not ready for that kind of technology yet.
FLASH!
*FBI agent flashes memory device, and everyone, including the FBI agent, forget what just happened*
Baron: HA-HA! I've got you now, Hael and Overbuild! Yes, I'll corrupt 2 of the faction leaders, then get the other 2! MWAHAHAHAHA!
Hael: What am I doing here? I was just harpooning sharks?
Overbuild: Where'd my teleportation machine go?
FBI agent: What am I doing here? I'm supposed to be at HQ! Where is HQ? I mustv'e accidentaly erased some of my memory with a memory eraser. Now where is HQ?
*Overbuild uses teleportation ray to teleport Baron back to his castle*
Baron: AAH! A TV! I'M SCARED OF IT! TECHNOLOGY! I should go camping...
