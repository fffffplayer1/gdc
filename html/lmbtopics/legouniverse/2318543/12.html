Chapter 28 Maelstrom Clowns
Baron: I'm so boooooooored............WHAT SHOULD I DO I DON'T KNOW WHAT TO DO!
Stormling: You could watch tv.
Baron: There's nothing on but weather ALL THE TIME! It's like the tv station is specifically targeting MY tv and changing all the channels I get and replacing it with weather channels!
Meanwhile, at the broadcasting station...
Tv Producer guy: Hi, Overbuild!
Overbuild: Hi.
Tv Producer guy: I just wanted to say, thanks for that machine that turns all the channels the Baron gets in to weather channels!
Overbuild: No problem. And stop thanking me, that was the fifth time today.
Tv producer guy: Okay...but thanks!
Back at the Baron's castle...
Baron: Hm, I COULD make a machine that finds out what someones greatest fear is, and then make that! STORMLING SCIENTISTS! I NEED YOU!
Stormling scientist #1: *pant* what do you *Gasp* need, Baron?
Baron: I need you to make a machine that finds out what someone's biggest fear is, then give it to me!
Stormling scientist #2: That's impossible, a machine can't read minds!
Baron: I WANT TO KNOW WHAT SOMEONE'S GREATEST FEAR IS!
*The two scientists leave the room, then make fliers that say, "Write here what your biggest fear is! We will use this survey to find out what everyone's biggest fear is, and then give this to the Baron!" Then only two people entered, and one said "Maelstrom" And one said "Clowns", then the scientists go back to the Baron*
Baron: Well? What is everyones greatest fear?
Scientist #1: Maelstrom!
Scientist #2: Clowns!
Baron: Which one is it?
Scientist #1: Clowns!
Scientist #2: Maelstrom!
Scientist #1: Um, Maelstrom clowns!
Baron: Is it? Which one is it, Maelstrom, clowns, clowns, Maelstrom, or Maelstrom clowns?
Scientist #1: Maelstrom clowns.
Baron: Good! Now I want you to go to your labratory and make Maelstrom clowns!
Scientist #1: You didn't give us a labratory, so we can't.
Baron: Then use the basement!
Scientist #1: A basement is not a proper sanitary and-
Scientist #2: *Seeing the Baron reaching for his discentigrator* ACTUALLY, THE BASEMENT IS PERFECT! COME ON, SCIENTIST #1!
Scientist #1: But-
*Scientist #2 drags him to the basement to make the Maelstrom clowns*
In the basem- LABRATORY...
Scientist #1: And we add a little bit of carbon monohydrate...
Scienitst #2: And I add a few pickles, some ketchup...
Scientist #1: What are you making that requieres pickles and ketchup?
Scientist #2: A sandwich.
Scientist #1: A SANDWICH?! WE'RE SUPPOSED TO BE MAKING- Well, that DOES look good...could I have a bite?
Scientist #2: Sure.
Scientist #1: Thanks! *takes a bite of the sandwich* EEW PICKLES AND KETCHUP AND- what is this, vinegar? AND VINEGAR TASTE HORRIBLE TOGETHER!
Scientist #2: HA-HA I TRICKED YOU!
*Baron comes in the room*
Baron: Is THIS, your work? A sandwich!?
Scientist #1: PLEASE DON'T DISCENTIGRATE ME!
Baron: If you give me that sandwich I won't.
Scientist #1: ...okay!
*Scientist #1 gives the Baron his sandwich and the Baron walks away*
Scientist #2: See? This is why we should be working on the Maelstrom clowns!
Scientist #1: But, you're the one who was making the sandwich!
Scientist #2: Yes, but you're the one who ate it.
Scientist #1:...Lets just get back to making the Maelstrom clowns.
1 hour later...
Baron: Where are those two scientists?
*The scientists go to the Baron, leading a dozen Maelstrom clowns in to the room with them*
Baron: PERFECT! NOW, MY LOYAL CLOWNS, GO OUTSIDE AND SCARE PEOPLE! MWAHAHAHAHA!
Clown: Okay!
Other Clown: Sure!
Another Clown: I can make a balloon dog out of a hot dog!
Baron: ...good! Now go outside!
*Clowns go down the stairs to go outside, but one who was on a unicycle, when he tried to go down the stairs, fell over and over again until he got to the bottom, then he landed on the unicycle, picked it up, then went outside*
Kid: YAY A PURPLE CLOWN!
Clown: FEAR ME! Okay, now that I've done that, heres a balloon dog!
Kid: YAY!
Unicycle clown: I CAN RIDE A UNICYCLE ON TOP OF A HOT DOG!
*the hot dog gets squished under the weight of the unicycle and clown*
Balloon Clown: I CAN MAKE A HOT DOG OUT OF A BALLOON DOG, AND I CAN MAKE A BALLOON DOG OUT OF A BALLOON!
Kid: Weird.
Balloon Clown: I CAN ALSO MAKE A LIVE BALLOON KING COBRA! AAH GET IT OFF ME!
Kid: HAHAHAHA!
Person: Ha! I'm afraid of clowns, and Maelstrom, but not Maelstrom Clowns!
Camera Clown: LET ME TAKE YOUR PICTURE! *Holds water spraying camera backwards by accident and sprays himself in the eye* GAH, MY EYE!
Baron: *Watching from window* NO NO NO NO NO! COME BACK HERE CLOWNS!
*The clowns go back to the Baron*
Baron: Good. Wait, where is that unicycle clown?
Balloon Clown: He's still trying to ride up the stairs with his unicycle.
Baron:...whatever. Now, why don't you scare people?
Scientist #1: It just occured to me that, although most people are afraid of Maelstrom and some are afraid of clowns, neither are afraid of Maelstrom Clowns.
Baron: WHY DIDN'T YOU TELL ME THAT!?
Scientist #1: I just did.
*Baron discenigrates the Scientist*
Baron: Now, what should I do with you useless clowns?
Pie Clown: HAVE A PIE! *Throws a pie in the Baron's face*
Baron: Grrr...ALRIGHT, WHAT I WANT YOU TO DO IS...is...I DON'T KNOW WHAT I WANT YOU TO DO, GIVE ME SUGGESTIONS!
Balloon Clown: Make you a balloon snake? *Holds up a normal long balloon*
Baron: NO!
To be continued...

Chapter 28 Part 2
Baron: ARGH! WHAT ON CRUX AM I SUPPOSED TO DO WITH YOU...CLOWNS?!
Balloon Clown: Make balloon snakes? *Holds up a normal long balloon*
Baron: STOP DOING THAT THATS THE SECOND TIME YOU DID THAT EXACT SAME THING! Now, what could I do with you all?
Balloon Clown: Make balloon worms? *Holds up the same normal long balloon*
Baron: *Facepalm* NO!
Airhorn Clown: Why do you keep shouting? Can't you hear us? I'll test it! *Activates the airhorn right next to the Baron's ear*
Baron: AAAGH! *Falls sideways on to the ground with his hands over his ears*
Pie Clown: YOU CLOWN! WE ALL HEARD THAT TOO!
Airhorn Clown: Yeah, but not as much as he did. *Points to the Baron*
Baron: *Gets up* Why did you do that?
Airhorn Clown: To see if you can hear me!
Baron: Alright, alright! Why don't you all give me suggestions on what to do with you?
Pie Clown: THROW PIES AT YOU? *Throws a pie in the Baron's face*
Baron: *Pie drips down his face* No.
Juggling Clown: Juggle you with the chainsaws?
Baron: What?! How could you lift me? And besides- wait, did you say you're juggling chainsaws?
Juggling Clown: Yes! AAAH I DROPPED A CHAINSAW!
Juggling Clown: OH NO THIS IS A DISASTER!
Pie Clown: Hey, it's just that you dropped the chainsaws.. What worse could happen from dropping chainsaws?
Juggling Clown: THAT! *Points with his good hand at the chainsaws that he was juggling, that are still spinning in the air, then fall, cutting part first, to the floor*
Everyone: AAAAAAAAH!
FLUMP! FLUMP! FLUMPFLUMPFLUMPFLUMPFLUMP!
Baron: How many chainsaws were you juggling?!
Juggling Clown: Seven.
Baron: HOW ON CRUX DID YOU JUGGLE SEVEN CHAINSAWS AT ONCE!?
Juggling Clown: Easy, you just get three chainsaws, hold one in one hand, two in the other, then throw one of the ones that was in your hand with an extra one up in the air, then throw the one with one chainsaw in the air then catch it-
Baron: I'M NOT HERE FOR JUGGLING LESSONS!
Juggling Clown: Really? Then why did you ask me how to juggle?
Pie Clown: Yeah, what he said!
Balloon Clown: Yeah, what he said!
Juggling Clown: Yeah, what I said!
Baron: QUIET!!!!! Now, what can I do with you? Where do people find clowns?
Balloon Clown: The circus!
Baron: I'VE GOT IT! THE-
Balloon Clown: Don't say Circus.
Baron: -LIBRARY!
Balloon Clown: Um, how about the Circus?
Baron: That works too.
At the Circus...
Baron: So, I would like to give you these Maelstrom Clowns for your Circus shows!
Circus Master: Okay! Come on, Maelstrom Clowns!
*All the Maelstrom Clowns go in the Circus train, and push the Baron in to the Circus train with them in their hurry to get in*
Baron: NOOOOOOO!!!!!
