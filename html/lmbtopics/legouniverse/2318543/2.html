Chaptr 6 The Crazy Candy Craze

The Darkitect, manaical master of mayhem and maelstrom, maliciously dropped m&m's into his mocking mouth.

Baron: HA-HA! M&MS WILL BE MINE! ALL OF THEM! *sorrounded by tons of little packets of m&ms*

Stormling: how on ear- never mind. how did you get all those?

Baron: It was easy. you know how trick or treat lasts for two hours?

Stormling: yeah.

Baron: well I just spent one hour in each time zone, then went East and time went back an hour and I had another hour of trick or treating! Or maybe I just stole it from a bunch of little kids.

Stormling: I thought that first explanation was too complicated.

*Baron discenegrates stormling*

Baron: aaah, the sweet sound of sile- WAIT I DID THIS BEFORE! I'm hungry.*rips open another half ounce packet and pours all of the m&m's in his mouth*

Baron: *shaking* more candy....*pours another packet in his mouth* moreee cannndyyy.....*greedily gobbles some green M&m's* morecandy.....morecandy....morecandy...morecandy..morecandy.MORECANDY!

Stormling: okay, thats enough saying that.

Baron:MY CANDY! *protects candy by wrapping hands around it*

Stormling: oh-no, its another of his sugar crazes. happens EVERY Halloween. OKAY EVERYONE GET THE QUARINTINE READY HIS SUGAR CRAZE IS CONTAGIOUS!

Baron: MY CANDY! oh its melting... *stuffs packets in his mouth*

Stormling: you didn't even open the packets yet! you ate the paper and chocolate! choocolatee.... yummy chocolate... I WANT CHOCOLATE!

Baron: NO! ITS MY CHOCOLATE!

Stormling: I WANT SOME!

Baron: NO!

Stormling: fine, I have an invisible candy thats the biggest ever and it tastes the best ever! *walks over to window* OOPS! I dropped it out the window!

Baron: I WANT CANDY! *dives out window and glass shatteres*

Stormling: the window wasn't even open! now I get the caaaandyyyy all to myself!

*Baron dives back through the window from the ground*

Stormling: AAAH!

*Baron and stormling start to fight, and a brochure from an ontouched bookshelf fell to the ground*

Baron: whats that?

Stormling: its a brochure of Frostburgh, with gingerbread houses and candy-cane lamp-posts.

*Baron and Stormling look at each other, then at the exact same moment run through the wall leaving stormling and baron shaped holes in the wall*

Stormling & Baron: FROSTBURGH!

Lol I love this it is very funny.  Keep up the good work.

Chapter 8 The Darkitect learns oragami
Baron: *staring out the window* I'm so bored. How can I take over the worlds? That one time I tried it lasted two hours and I was burnt and had to lie in bed for a week. *sees a paper airplane fly past, and discenegrates it* I hate oragami, its just paper and- WAIT! Is that a paper dragon?
Kid outside the window: Yeah, it's really cool, Oragami is the coolest and most dangerous thing around! *secretely high-fives a friend behind his back*
Baron: THATS IT! I'll make dangerous and humongous Oragami animals and send them through the mail to the faction leaders! *calls Oragami teacher*
Oragami teacher: Hello?
Baron: Hello.
Oragami teacher: *sigh* hello.
Baron: Hello?
Oragami teacher: OH, JUST TELL ME WHAT YOU WANT!
Baron: I want to take over the worlds.
Oragami teacher: I MEAN WHY DID YOU CALL ME- Baron?
Baron: Sensui Wu?
Sensui Wu: *Sigh* What do you want?
Baron: I want to take over-
*Sensui Wu tries to slam phone in the phone holder thing but misses and slams it in to the wall*
Sensui Wu: uh-oh.
CRACK! CRACKCRACKCRACK... CREEEAAAK
*wall cracks and cracks spread to the ceiling and other walls, and the whole building falls down*
Meanwhile...
Baron: I'll just buy an Oragami book, wait, what's that on the tv?
TV: Breaking News! The NinjaGo Monastary has collapsed! Ninjas are trying to use Spinjitzu to dig Sensui Wu out of the rubble! But they are completely defensless without their golden weapons, which were lost in the rubble! I hope the Darkitect isn't watching this, because if he came here we would all be doomed! To make matters worse, beeeeooooop.
*baron turns off tv*
Baron: I don't have time for listening to what the tv said, I need an oragami book! what did the tv say? Oh well, it probably isn't important.
*Goes to the store looking for an Oragami book*
Sandwich Maker: DON'T TURN ME INTO A STORMLING! WHAT DO YOU WANT? THIS IS A SANDWICH SHOP!
Baron: I thought this was a book store.
Sandwich maker: NO! THE SIGN CLEARLY SAYS 'this is a sandwich shop. please read our book called 'the sandwich book'' oh. I see how you could think this is a bookstore.
*5 minutes later*
Baron: OKAY EVERYONE GIVE ME A SANDWICH!
Book-store employee: this is a bookstore!
Baron: oh. what did I want in the first place? hmmmm...*30 minutes later* OH YEAH! I WANTED A BOOK ON ORAGAMI!
Book-store employee: oh, your'e still here. I forgot you were here.
Baron: WELL GIVE ME A BOOK!
Book-store employee: That'll be $9.99.
Baron: Thank you.
Book-store employee: YOU HAVE TO PAY FOR THAT SIR! oh never mind.
*Back at the castle*
Baron: lets see, *flips page* flower...*flips page* box... *flips page* AH HERE IT IS! Dragon! Lets see, right corner left... left corner right... flip over... repeat steps one and two... wait, what was step one and two? Oh well, I'll skip that. Now lets see...step four...turn the whole thing inside out out without tearing paper...
RIIIIIIIP! *rips paper*
Baron: AAAAARG! I'll just buy one!
*5 hours later*
Baron: Nothings coming. Maybe they didn't get my order.
Stormling: You have to send in three box tops and wait 4-6 weeks for delivery.
Baron: Well, I'll take care of that first part! *Rips off the top of some box-computers and puts them in an envelope*
Stormling: I don't think that's what they meant...
Baron: Come on, it ripped the envelope. Do you think they'll still mail it?
Stormling: I don't think so...
Baron: how many stamps should I put on? Well, I don't have any stamps. I KNOW! I'll use snakes! They both start with 's', right?
*1 hour later*
Duke Exeter: Hey, what's this I got in the mail? AAAAAH SNAKES! I'M TERRIFIED OF SNAKES! *Throws snakes all the way to Baron's castle*
Baron: I like this movie! *sitting on the couch, staring at a rainbow-test pattern thing*
*snakes come flying from the window and wrap around the Baron's neck*
Baron: AAACK! Oh, they returned it to sender. I don't want it! *throws snakes back at Duke Exeter*
Duke Exeter: AAAH SNAKES! *throws snakes back at Baron*...

Chapter 9 The Darkitect's Laptop
The Darkitect, master of chaos, was celebrating.
Baron: I won! I won! I won! WOO-HOO!
Stormling: What did you win?
Baron: *whispering* A laptop!
Stormling: uh-oh.
Baron: What do you mean, uh-oh? I can use technology just fine, I'll let you know! *turns around and walks in to a lamp-post*
Baron: OW!
Stormling: See? You couldn't screw in a lightbulb without calling a technician!
Baron: HOW DID YOU KN- I mean, that's not true... *fumbles with chrystal staff to discenegrate stormling*
Baron: Oh whatever, but how did a lamp-post get in my room?
Stormling: You said it was too dark at night, but you didn't want a night-light. You want some help with that chrystal staff?
Baron: Here.
Stormling: Ok... just slide this switch so it says on... and here you go!
Baron: Thanks. *discenegrates stormling for knowing about the lightbulb and technician thing.*
*walks to a phone and starts to dial laptop prize people*
Baron: I can work technology without someone always helping me. I can keep up with technology. How do I dial the number? There arent any actual buttons, just a blank black screen.
Stormling: You just touch it!
Baron: oookay...*touches it and the Eye-Phone lights up* AAAAH! IT TURNED ON BY ITSELF!
Stormling: Settle down, it just turned on when you touched it.
Baron: I think it's angry...
1 hour later...
Baron: This must be the place. That phone was practically asking to be thrown out the window and stomped on, with all that 'Slide to unlock' and 'You have One Million dollars in debt to the purple brick department'. *opens door* Hello?
*Baron looks in to store filled with beeping phones that look like flat thin boxes and sees kids texting on their Eye-Phone's*
Baron: Hey kid, how did you turn that on?
Kid: It was easy, all you do is touch it then slide your finger across the bottom and then type in the 12-digit code that they send to you via e-mail, and then you can choose from hundreds of apps and games like 'Very Angry Birds, Pak-Man, and are you smarter than a fifth grader, Live!
Baron: They didn't make phones that complicated when I was a kid!
Stormling: They didn't make phones at all when you were a kid!
Baron: *whisper/hissing* What are you doing here?
Stormling: I thought you would need help with getting your new laptop.
Baron: Okay, fine you can stay, but keep quiet. *walks up to counter* Hello, I want to get my free laptop I won in a contest.
Phone store worker: Sorry, we sell phones, your'e looking for that store across the street. *points across the street to store called 'Top Lap-tops!'*
Baron:*goes there* Hello, could I get my laptop I got from a contest?
Laptop store seller: What is your promotion code, 183704937849, or 948739407381?
Baron: Those are the same backwards.
Laptop store seller: Maybe, but what's your promotion code?
Baron: Umm... Well here's a coupon!
Laptop store seller: That's for Pizza-hat.
Baron: Do you want it or not?
Laptop store seller: ...Here's your laptop.
Baron: *In his castle* Now to turn it on! But first, to open it! *tries to open laptop and can't.*
Stormling: Need some help with that? See, all you have to do is press both sides at the same time and pull it up.
Baron: *holds both sides of Laptop and lifts it above his head* Is it on?
Stormling: No. Here let me.
Baron: Suit yourself. *drops laptop on stormling*
Stormling: oww... Okay, lets see... rggg... *pant* I can't *gasp* get it open *pant* either!
Baron: I know what to do! *calls technician on old fashioned rotary phone* See, this type of technology I can handle! *Rotary phone slowly goes back from first number, then is ready to dial the next number*
15 minutes later...
*technician dressed in a trench coat comes to Baron's castle*
Baron: Oh your'e here. Could you open this laptop for me?
Technician: That's a suitcase filled with...*pulls open suitcase* magnets!
Baron: So that's why my 'laptop' stuck to the bus on the way home.
Technician: You want to see a real laptop instead of a suitcase? Look at this! *Shows Baron high-tech laptop that can download all the files in the white house and CIA in half a second*
Baron: That looks like a pizza box covered in glitter. What's the thing with technology? It's so boring, I mean what does it do? Basically what I mean is-
*Mechanical arms comes out of technician's trenchcoat and slams Baron in to the wall and the wall cracks*
Technician: *angrily* What was that you said?
Baron: Who are you?
Technician: My name is G, and the metal arms is Mech.
Baron: Is he made of pipe cleaners or something? *Mech presses harder* Oww...
G: I'll let you go if you say that technology is great and you don't deserve to be in the presence of technology.
Baron: TechnologyisgreatandIdon'tdeservetobeinthepresenceoftechnology!
*Mech lets Baron go*
G: Here's a laptop. You can open it yourself.
*G leaves*
5 minutes later...
Baron: I can't get this open!
Stormling: Thats not a box! That's that robot that attacked you earlier! ...I think I'll leave! *Stormling runs away as fast as possible*
Baron: Uh-oh... *drops to his knee's and grovels to Mech* TechologyisgreatandIdon'tdeservetobeinthepresenceoftechnology, technologyisgreatandIdon'tdeservetobeinthepresenceoftechnology...AAAAH!
*Across the street, G smiles to herself.*
