Chapter 25 Invisible Ink
*Baron is watching tv*
Stormling: BOO!
Baron: AAAH A GHOST!
Stormling: Relax, I'm just invisible.
Baron: Tell me how to be invisible or you WILL be a ghost! Get it?
Stormling: I just used invisible ink!
Baron: Invisible ink, you say? I have some!
*Baron gets invisible ink*
Baron: Oh it's empty.
Stormling: No it's not, the ink is just invisible!
Baron: Oh.
*Baron pours some on himself*
Baron: YAY I'M INVISIBLE!
Stormling: No you're not, I can see you.
Baron: But I thought you said that it was invisible ink!
Stormling: It is, the ink is invisible, it doesn't make YOU invisible!
Baron: *Facepalm* Okay, how CAN I be invisible?
Stormling: There's an invisibility store across the street.
Baron: Where? I don't see it.
Stormling: Well, OBVIOUSLY it's invisible. Duh.
*Baron goes to the invisibility store across the street, and actually get in after crashing in to the invisible wall a couple dozen times*
Invisible Employee: Can I help you with anything?
Baron: Yeah, how can I become invisible?
Invisible Employee: Well, we have a lot of invisible invisibility products, including invisibale cloaks, invisible shoes, invisible ink, invisable glass, and our best product, The Invisible Book Of Invisibility! (Not my own idea, from another book, but who cares, it's funny, and it isn't like they have a company of it)
Baron: How can anyone read an invisible book of invisibility?
Invisible Employee: You can't, but we ordered them, and we got them, and we've been stuck with them for years.
Baron: I would like something that makes me invisible!
Invisible Employee: Okay, go visit our invisibility professor in the back room for that.
*Baron goes in to the invisibility professor's office, but not before crashing in to the wall 4 times*
Baron: Hello? Could you make me invisible?
Invisibility Professor: ...
Baron: I know you're here, I can hear you breathing.
Invisibility Professor: Fine, you want to be invisible?
Baron: Yes.
Invisibility Professor: Well, it is a complicated type of magi-I MEAN SCIENCE! You will need ingredients that are fairly easy to find though.
Baron: What do I need?
Invisibility Professor: You will need a notion potion, an ounce of happiness in a bottle, a ship in a bottle, and a soda bottle, some ice cream, and three ounces of gladness.
Baron: Some of those things aren't actually things you can get!
Invisibility Professor: Exactly.
Baron: Then how am I supposed to become invisible?!
Invisibility Professor: *Sigh* Eat this.
Baron: What is it?
Invisibility Professor: Baking soda.
Baron: Eew.
*Baron eats the baking soda*
Invisibility Professor: Now drink this.
Baron: What is that?
Invisibility Professor: Vinegar.
Baron: I think I remember something about baking soda and vinegar-
Invisibility Professor: THEY TASTE GREAT TOGETHER!
Baron: That must be it.
*Baron drinks the vinegar, and the baking soda and vinegar in his stomach chemicially react and pour out the Baron's ears*
Baron: HOW ON CRUX IS THAT SUPPOSED TO MAKE ME INVISIBLE!?
Invisibility Professor: It isn't, I just find it funny. Here is the real thing that makes you invisible.
*Baron drinks a notion potion that the Invisibility Professor gave him, thinking it was an invisibility potion*
Baron: ACK! Hey, I'm invisible!
*Baron tries to get out of the room, but can't because he keeps running in to walls, and by the time he gets out of the store the potion wears off*
Baron: AURGH!
