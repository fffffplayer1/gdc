Chapter 30 Part 2
Baron: Are you sure I'm supposed to eat a baskektball to get rid of hiccups?
Stormling: Absolutely.
Baron: Well okay....
*Baron tries to put the basketball in his mouth but can't because it's too big*
Baron: I can't do it!
Stormling: You might have to deflate the basketball first.
Baron: Okay.
*Baron deflates the basketball with a sword and then throws the sword out the window, where it hits a robot spy squirrel, and the Baron swallows the deflated basketball*
Baron: That tasted horrible!
Stormling: Well are your hiccups gone?
Baron: I think! YES! YES MY HICCUPS ARE GONE AND I CAN GO DO MY SPEECH NOW!
*Baron goes in front of Nexus Tower and prepares his speech*
Baron: ATTENTION EVERYONE! I HAVE A SPEECH TO MAKE! Ahem. I have taken over the Nex-hic! What the-hic! Uh oh. hic!
*Baron runs back to his castle to find out why he hiccuped*
Baron: WHY DID I HICCUP AGAIN?
Stormling: It seems like you digested the basketball, and it could no longer stop the hiccups from coming.
Baron: WELL-hic-WHAT CAN I -hic- DO ABOUT IT? -hic-
Stormling: We need to get something to plug your hiccups, so they can't come out of you.
Baron: -hic-Like what?
Stormling: This giant cork that I used for a giant soda bottle should work!
Baron: Since when -hic- do soda bottles have -hic- corks? And what -hic- am I supposed -hic- to do with -hic- the giant cork, -hic- swallow it?
Stormling: That is exactly what you're supposed to do.
Baron: Alright then.
*Baron swallows the giant cork, which plugs his hiccups*
Baron: YES! IT WORKS! NOW I CAN GIVE MY SPEECH ABOUT TAKING OVER THE NEXUS FORCE!
*The Baron goes in front of Nexus Tower and starts to give his speech*
Baron: ATTENTION EVERYONE! I HAVE A SPEECH TO MAKE! I have taken over the Nexus Force, and I have taken all of your faction leaders hostage, to make sure that no one attacks me! Because from what I understand, you can not attack me unless your faction leaders say so!
*Vanda walks out of Nexus Tower and sees the Baron*
Vanda: What's going on, Baron?
Baron: Um...YES, GOOD JOB, MY VANDA ROBOT, YOU ACT EXACTLY LIKE THE REAL VANDA!
Vanda: I AM the real Vanda!
Baron: HA, I MADE THE ROBOT VANDA SO GOOD AT MAKING SURE YOU DON'T KNOW SHE WAS TAKEN, THAT SHE WON'T STOP ACTING LIKE THE REAL VANDA! HA!
*Stormling whispers in the Baron's ear*
Stormling: Did you actually kidnap the faction leaders and replace Vanda with a robot?
Baron: *Whispering in the stormling's ear* No! She's on our side!
Stormling: Oh yeah.
Baron: *Doing the speech again* NOW ALL OF YOU SURRENDER, AND I WILL NOT BLOW UP NEXUS TOWER!
Person: How could you blow it up?
Baron: BY PUTTING DYNAMITE UNDER IT, ATTACHED TO THIS FUSE, WHICH IF I LIGHT, WILL BLOW UP NEXUS TOWER!
Person: I don't believe that would happen. We won't give up unless Nexus Tower DOES blow up!
Baron: OKAY, I WILL LIGHT THE FUSE NOW THEN! Let's see, where's my box of matches....*Stalling*Here it is, now TO BLOW UP NEXUS TOWER!
*Baron lights a match, then drops it on purpose, then does it again, and again, then on the fourth one finally decides he can't stall any longer and lights the 'fuse'. The fuse gets eaten more and more by the spark, until the spark dissapears underground*
Stormling: *Whispering to the Baron* Did you actually put dynamite under Nexus Tower?
Baron: No.
Stormling: That's going to be a problem.
Person: Nothing's happening!
Baron: OH JUST WAIT! YOUR PRECIOUS TOWER WILL EXPLODE IN A MINUTE!
*While all this was happening, inside the Baron's stomach the hiccups were building up against the giant cork in the Baron's stomach, and building up and up until so much pressure was pressing against the giant cork that it EXPLODED out of the Baron's mouth and richoched around the street*
Random person: Now I need to close my giant soda bottle! But how should I do that? What's big enough for this giant soda bottle?
*Suddenly the giant cork lands in the giand soda bottle, closing it*
Random person: That's better!
*Meanwhile, after the cork exploded out of the Baron's mouth, the Baron let out a GIANT hiccup and the amazingly loud sound from it echoed across the planet, which also shattered all the windows in Nexus Tower, including the glass which protected the Imagination Nexus (Which just created some more glass to protect it) and it also blasted the Baron in to the air and through the atmosphere, and out of orbit*
Vanda: *Staring at the sky where the Baron was before he went out of orbit* Well you don't see that every day.
Meanwhile, in space...
Baron: *Floating through space* Well, what am I going to do now?
