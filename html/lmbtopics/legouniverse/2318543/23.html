Chapter 38 Tongue-Twisters (Disclaimer: if you try to read this story out loud you might never talk the same for hours. Also, I got some of the more complicated tongue-twisters from the Internet, and others I thought of)
Baron: *Yawn* *Gets up from bed in the morning* I'm so tired. What time is it?
Stormling: I woke you up! Three in the morning!
Baron: Why?
Stormling: To take the time today to try to have fun tremendously!
Baron: Why are you talking weird?
Stormling: It's Annual Tongue-Twister day! A tutor who tooted the flute
Tried to tutor two tooters to toot.
Said the two to the tutor:
"Is it harder to toot or
To tutor two tooters to toot?"
Baron: What are you talking about?
Stormling: Seventy-seven slithering snakes sang silently so sadly.
Baron: WHAT!?
Stormling: ANNUAL TONGUE-TWISTER DAY!
Baron: I need a doctor to help me with all this craziness.
Stormling: When a doctor gets sick and another doctor doctors him,
does the doctor doing the doctoring have to doctor the doctor the way the doctor being doctored wants to be doctored,
or does the doctor doing the doctoring of the doctor doctor the doctor as he wants to do the doctoring?
Baron: I DON'T KNOW! WHAT ARE YOU TALKING ABOUT?! THIS IS CRAZY! ....I'm hungry...
Stormling: Crisp crusts crackle crunchily.
Baron: *Groan* Can you stop?
Stormling: Silly Sally swiftly shooed seven silly sheep.
The seven silly sheep Silly Sally shooed
Shilly-shallied south.
These sheep shouldn't sleep in a shack;
Sheep should sleep in a shed.
Baron: Are you just doing tongue-twisters that use the letter of the first letter of the last word I say?
Stormling: Usually.
Baron: Zoo.
Stormling: Umm.....
Baron: HA!
Stormling: How much wood would a woodchuck chuck
If a woodchuck could chuck wood?
He would chuck, he would, as much as he could,
And chuck as much wood as a woodchuck would
If a woodchuck could chuck wood.
Baron: STOP IT!
*Baron grabs his head with his hands and runs out the door and bumps in to another Stormling*
Stormling: Hi.
Baron: Oh this is great, you're talking normal!
Stormling: ita vero. ego sum sermo northmann.
Baron: What?
Stormling: ita vero. Est tu neccesitar consumit cenam?
Baron: WHAT KIND OF DEAD LANGUAGE ARE YOU SPEAKING?! TODAY IS TONGUE-TWISTER DAY NOT...LATIN DAY OR WHATEVER!
Stormling: Oh. Of all the felt I ever felt,
I never felt a piece of felt
Which felt as fine as that felt felt,
When first I felt that felt hat's felt.
Baron: AAAAAAAAAH!
*Baron jumps out the window*
Person: Hi! His head had hummed how many humms that humming had become dumb.
Baron: Along with you.
*Person kicks the Baron in the knee*
Baron: OW!
Another person: Ow? I can't think of a tongue-twister for that!
*Police officer drives there in a police car and puts handcuffs on the persons wrists*
Officer: You're under arrest for making a pest, of not making a tongue-twister head rest!
*Suddenly the person broke loose of the cuffs and around the rugged rocks the ragged rascal ran*
Officer: Well, there he goes with my nose to make himself froze.
Baron: AAAAAAAAAAAH! *Runs in to the distance, a fugitive from the law for no reason at all*
1 day later....
*Baron is dragging himself by his hands back to his castle*
Baron: Water....water.....must...get....WATER!
*Sweat drops from the Barons head and the Baron suddenly, very quickly, swoops his head down and drinks the drop of water*
*Baron finally reaches his castle*
Baron: Hi Stormlings!
Stormling: Aah uuh wuhtuhmuh.
Baron: What?
Stormling: Aftwer yefterdsay we hawd ar tungs num.
Baron: YAY! NOW I'M THE ONLY ONE WHO CAN TALK!
*Stormling grabs the Barons tongue, stretches it out, then tazers it, making the Barons tongue also numb*
Baron: AAAAAAAAH!

*Baron sees the tracks of the armored truck in the desert sand*

Baron: Almost...there...
3 hours later...
Baron: Any...minute...now....
4 hours later....
Baron: *Dragging himself across the desert with his hands* You know, this is harder to do than it would be to just WALK. You know that right?

Baron: Soo....thirsty...
*Suddenly the Baron sees a shimmery image in the air in front of him*
Baron: Huh?
*The image forms in to a giant glass of water, with a spout near the bottom for people to pour water out of it*
Baron: Hehehehehe....WATER!
*Baron gets up and runs to the giant glass of water, then runs right through it*
Baron: Huh? *Looks behind him at the image*
*The image blurs and turns in to an oasis and the Baron runs to a 'pond' and dips his hand in to the 'water' and puts it in his mouth, then gags and chokes and spits the sand out*
Baron: *Groan* Oh it's hopeless.
*Suddenly the Baron sees something different from the other images. He sees himself back in his castle, and the stormlings were having a party. One stormling wearing a birthday party hat sees the Baron and runs up to him and puts a fruit hat on his head, then gives him a slice of cake and says, "I knew you would find your way back! That whole interrogation thing was just a joke!". Then the image goes away*
Baron: That was weird. I wonder if it means something? Nah.
*Baron starts dragging himself back towards where he hopes is civilization*
7 hours later....
Baron: Sooo....hot...
*Suddenly another wavy image appears in front of the Baron*
Baron: Talking donut?
Donut: Yes...I am Tim the Talking Donut. *Singing an advertisement jingle* Whenever you're hungry and want a snack, come to Tim's yummy donut shack!
Baron: I always liked Tim the Talking Donut's shack...But I'm stuck here and can't possibly go to your shack to buy a donut!
Tim the Talking Donut: Don't worry! I'll take you to the edge of the desert!
*TTD picks Baron up and flies to the edge of the desert*
TTD: WEEEEEEEEEEE! There you are! *In a fading voice*Now don't forget buy a donut at my shack! *Disappears slowly*
Baron: Huh. I thought he was just a mirage!
*Baron turns around and sees Nexus City*
Baron: YAY!!!!!!!
*Baron happily runs to his castle and enters*
*Stormlings are having a party. One Stormling wearing a birthday party hat runs up to the Baron and puts a fruit hat on him and gives him a slice of cake*
Stormling: I knew you would find your way back! That whole interrogation thing was just a joke!
Baron: Thanks for the cake!
*The Baron starts walking upstairs to his bedroom. Halfway there, he suddenly stops, wide-eyed, and runs back to the stormling*
