Chapter 32 Opposite Day
Baron: I am so bored....why am I always so bored....either I'm so bored I'm talking for hours and hours and hours and hours about being bored, or I'm doing something crazy.....I shall now practice this boring time to master the art of Patience.
*Baron sits cross-legged and puts his hands on his knees, trying to meditate*
Baron: Hummmmm...............ARGH I CAN'T DO IT I HAVE NO PATIENCE!
Stormling Docter: Now you know how I feel when people realized I'm a bad docter. I don't have any patients anymore either.
Baron: ...That was so cheesy. I wish something exciting would happen....
Jesse: *Walks in room* Why don't you have an opposite day?
Baron: Good idea! Wait, how did you get in my castle?
Jesse: Got to go! *Dives out the window*
Baron: I have a major security breach.
Stormling: Which means you left the front door unlocked again.
Baron: Exactly. Now let's have an opposite day!
*Mythran appears*
Mythran: Hello, Baron.
Baron: ARGH NOT YOU AGAIN!
Mythran: I have no idea what you're talking about. Now, if you want I can make there be an opposite day, where people are the exact opposite of how they are now. Just for a day. For example, Overbuild would be dumb, Duke would be a chicken, Vanda would be a pirate and afraid of Maelstrom, and Hael would have no sense of direction. Oh, and you would be a genius.
Baron: I can tell there's some sort of insult in there somewhere, but I can't find it.
Mythran: Do you want the opposite day?
Baron: Okay.
Mythran: Okay, I will make there be an opposite day when you wake up.
Baron: I'M ASLEEP!?
Mythran: No, you're awake. I meant when you wake up from next time you fall asleep.
Baron: Ok.
*Mythran dissapears*
Baron: I want the opposite day now! STORMLING!
*Stormling rushes in to the room*
Stormling: Yes?
Baron: I need you to knock me out. Use this frying pan.
*Baron hands Stormling a frying pan, and the Stormling can barely hide his glee and excitement about being able to beat up the Baron*
Stormling: Well, if I HAVE to!
Baron: Oh, if you don't want to, I can have someone else do it for you.
Stormling: I WANT TO! *Jumps in the air and swings the frying pan over his head and clonks the Baron on the head. Seeing that the Baron was still concious, the Stormling hits the Baron again and again with the frying pan. Meanwhile, invisible, Shival and his friends are eating popcorn and watching the Baron for fun*
Stormling: JUST ONE MORE HIT!
Baron: NO IT'S NO USE, THE FRYING PAN DOESN'T WORK OBVIOUSLY!
Stormling: Well what makes you fall asleep fastest?
Baron: I don't know, a math teacher?
*Stormling gets a math teacher and the math teacher starts talking about congruent angles and how to find the radius of a triangle and talking about how in geometry, lines go on forever, almost as long as lines for rides at the carnival, and the Baron falls asleep and starts snoring*
Shival: Aw, I wish the Stormling kept hitting the Baron with the frying pan. Oh well, now we have to make it opposite day.
*Shival makes a remote appear out of mid-air and presses a button, then the Baron wakes up*
Baron: Oh my head, what happened? Was it Suruaseht mixed with Gnirts Yllis equals twenty seven and cake? Why am I saying all this smart stuff?
*Mythran appears in front of the Baron*
Mythran: It is because now that everything is the opposite for a day you are partly smart, partly dumb, because you have to have your normal memories AND the intelligence of an alternate you, which obviously are not able to be merged together without confusion. Bye.
*Mythran dissapears, leaving the Baron confused now that he knows so many long complicated words and is super smart while at the same time having the IQ of a cockroach, and less IQ than certain cockroaches. The Baron looks out the window and sees something he didn't expect in a million years*
Baron: Oh wow!
To be continued....

Chapter 32 part 2
Baron: Oh, wow! Overbuild is chasing a wasp with a butterfly net like a little kid!
*Overbuild is chasing the wasp*
Overbuild: *Skipping like a little girl* LA LA LA LA LA, I'M HAPPY! *Wasp stings Overbuild* OW IT HURTS DON'T STING ME AGAIN WAH!
*Overbuild runs away with his hands over his head*
Baron: If all the faction leaders are that weird, it will be easy to take over the Nexus Force!
*Baron walks over to Nexus Tower, and goes to the Faction leaders' office*
Baron: BOW DOWN TO ME AND SURRENDER OR FEEL THE WRATH OF ME!
Duke: AAAAH DON'T HIT ME WITH THAT PAPER!
Baron: What? This is the peice of paper I'm using for my little speech.
Duke: YEAH BUT IT'S SCARY!
Baron: Bow down to the Maelstrom and I won't hit you with this peice of paper!
Duke: OK, I'LL BOW TO THE MAIL-STORM, BUT LET ME GO TO THE POST OFFICE TO BOW TO THEIR LEADER!
Baron: What on Crux are you talking about?
Duke: The Mail-Storm. A storm of mail? Like a tornado or something? It's leader would be at the post office, right?
Baron: WHAT!? NO! The Maelstrom is an evil purple element that turns people evil and purple! It's SCARY!
Duke: It is? Who's it's leader?
Baron: ME!
Duke: DON'T EAT ME!
Baron: *Facepalm* Duke's weird even when he's being the opposite of himself. I'll go to Vanda's office.
*Baron goes to Vanda's office*
Baron: BOW DOWN TO ME OR FEEL THE WRATH OF ME!
Vanda: AAAAH MAELSTROM I'M TERRIFIED OF IT! I'M JUST A PIRATE DON'T HURT ME!
Baron: That was easy.
*Baron starts walking down the hallway to Hael's office when he bumps in to G*
Baron: AAAH DON'T HURT ME G!
G: You're normal too? Something happened, and I think everything became the opposite, like an opposite day, but I used Mech to keep Mech and I the same.
Baron: Come on, even today you're a genius. I AGREED TO MAKE AN OPPOSITE DAY TO HAVE SOME FUN, NOT BE SCARED OF G SOME MORE!
G: YOU did this?! ARGH!
*G attacks the Baron, and the Baron ducks but G picks him up and bounces him like a basketball and slam dunks the Baron through the floor in anger*
Baron: AAAAAAAAAH!
*Baron falls on the ground and sees a vending machine*
Baron: Ooh goodies!
*Baron gets up and walks to the vending machine, shakes it to get snacks out, and it falls on him*
*Janitor finds the vending machine and picks it up, and puts it back where it used to be, with the Baron stuck inside it, with the glass mysteriously not broken and back in place*
Baron: AAAH! GET ME OUT OF HERE!
Random Person: *Sees the vending machine* Ooh goodies!
*Random Person punches in a number in the vending machine, without seeing the Baron, and the vending machine's snack dispenser-screwing thing drills on the Baron's back, trying to release a snack*
Random Person: Why aren't I getting my snack? *Sees the Baron in the vending machine* AAAH! Why are YOU in there?!
Baron: IT FELL ON ME!
Random Person: Oh okay.
*Random Person walks away*
Baron: HEY GET ME OUT OF HERE!
Random Person: Sorry, I'm getting a snack from another vending machine.
Baron: ARGH! HOW WILL I EVER GET OUT OF HERE!?
*The Baron stays in the vending machine all day, waiting for someone to see him in the vending machine, but no one does, and Nexus Tower closes for the day and the lights go out, and the Baron falls asleep. When he wakes up, the world is back to normal but the Baron is still inside a vending machine*
Janitor: *Tries to get a snack out of the vending machine, but nothing happens, and the Janitor doesn't see the Baron* Hmm, this vending machine doesn't work! I'll have it sent to California!
Baron: NOOOO! NOT CALIFORNIA! ANYTHING BUT CALIFORNIA! What's California?
Ten days later, in Califronia....
Baron: HELLO! *Trying to get people to see him* I'M IN HERE!
*No one notices the Baron in the Vending Machine, and he just breaks the glass and goes to the nearest store*
Baron: Hello, do you know the way to Crux Prime?
Employee: Yeah, it's that way. *Points over his shoulder*
Baron: How far?
Employee: About 20,000 miles.
Baron: How long would it take me to walk there?
Employee: I don't know. Tell me after you've walked there.
Baron: How?
Employee: I was being sarcastic.
Baron: Oh. TO CRUX PRIME!
*Baron goes out of the store and walks in the direction the employee told him Crux Prime was at*
3 hours later....
Baron: Can't be too far now!
4 hours later....
Baron: How much farther?
5 hours later....
*Baron arrives at a store*
Baron: How far is it to Crux Prime?
Employee: I don't know, 19,000 miles?
Baron: I'LL NEVER GET THERE BY WALKING!
Employee: We sell unicycles which you could ride to Crux Prime!
Baron: Deal. *Hands the employee a thousand dollars*
Employee: We don't have change for that.
Baron: THEN DON'T CHANGE IT!
Employee: I think you've gone kind of crazy from twelve hours of walking.
Baron: How did you know I've been walking that long?
Employee: Guessed.
Baron: GIVE ME THE UNICYCLE ALREADY!
*Baron get's the unicycle, gets on it outside the store, and promptly crashes it in to a ditch*
Baron: That didn't work.
*Baron gets the broken unicycle and sets it on the counter in front of the employee*
Baron: I would like to return this.
Employee: Why?
Baron: It's broken, they made a mistake when they made it. It only has one wheel.
Employee: That's how unicycles are.
Baron: No, they have two wheels! I NEED A UNICYCLE WITH TWO WHEELS!
Employee: You could ride two unicycles at once!
Baron: Deal. *Hands the employee five thousand dollars*
Employee: Have fun!
Baron: Why?
Employee: I don't know, we're supposed to say that at this store.
Baron: Goodbye.
*Baron gets on two unicycles, one foot on one, the other foot on the other, then he starts cycling, but the unicycles go away from each other and the Baron falls on the ground*
Baron: I'll just walk there.
24 hours later....
Baron: FINALLY I'M AT MY CASTLE!
Stormling: Oh hi.
*Baron goes upstairs to bed, then is bored*
Baron: I am so bored........
*Mythran appears*
Mythran: How would you like an opposite day?
Baron: NOOOOOOO! NO!
*Baron gets a fire axe and swings it at the Mythran, and the Mythran turns it in to a peice of string*
Baron: ARGH!!!!!!!!!!!
*Baron dives at the Mythran and the Mythran blasts him backwards with an explosion*
Baron: YOU MADE THAT OPPOSITE DAY HAPPEN! YOU MADE ME WALK TWENTY THOUSAND MILES FOR ONE AND A HALF DAYS!
Mythran: Yeah, and I lost the bet on how long it would take you to go crazy. I said it would be two days, my friend said it would be one and a half days.
Baron: YOU BETTED ON HOW LONG IT WOULD TAKE ME TO GO CRAZY!? ARGH!
*Baron falls asleep from exhaustion and the Mythran makes a blanket pop out of thin air and lays it on the Baron and puts a pillow under his head, then disappears*
