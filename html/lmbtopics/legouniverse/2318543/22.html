Chapter 36 part 2
Baron: Yay I got a pet Slinky!
*Baron skips out of the toy store and goes to his castle*
Baron: Now what can I do with you, little Slinky?
Slinky: .........
Baron: The box says you can go down stairs! Let me see you do it!
*Baron takes Slinky to stairs and pushes it down and it slinks down and stops at the bottom of the stairs*
Baron: ...That's all?! THAT'S ALL!? Come on Slinky, we have to do something more fun!
*Baron trips and falls down the stairs like a slinky, head first then feet, then head, then feet, and reaches the bottom of the stairs*
Baron: Ow my head and feet...come on Slinky!
*Baron picks up slinky and goes up to his bedroom*
Baron: What can we do? I know! We can invade Nexus Tower together! But just two people, and one of them a Slinky...we will have to ROB the Imagination Nexus! At night! Hehehehehehe, hehehehehehe, MWAHAHAHAHA-
*Lightning strikes outside Barons window and there is a dramatic thunder clap*
Baron: HAHAHA-whoa it sounds like it's going to rain tonight. I'd better do it when it stops raining! Now what time is it?
*Baron checks his watch*
Baron: Two in the afternoon. And we should steal the Imagination Nexus at midnight, so how long is it until we should rob it? NO NOT A WORD PROBLEM! Do it for me, Slinky!
*Slinky slinks off the table from the vibration of the Barons yelling and slinks next to a line on the floor, to make 1o(10)*
Baron: YOU ARE A GENIUS! Now what do we do until ten? Checkers?
Slinky: ....
Baron: Yeah, that's boring. I KNOW! I will get masks for us and robber clothes for us!
5 hours later....
*Baron runs back in to his castle, soaking wet from the rain and holding a bag of black masks and black clothes for him and the Slinky*
Baron: *Gasp* I'm back! Hey where are all of my Stormlings who are usually here?
Meanwhile, in Nexus City Disco Party Place....
*Stormlings are having a party, this time AWAY from the Barons castle*
Stormling: BEST! PLANNED! OUT! PARTY! EVER! TO! GO! HERE! INSTEAD! OF! THE! CASTLE!
Other Stormling: Just say BEST! PARTY! EVER! or something short like that for full sentences besides such a long one.
Stormling: You're no fun!
Other Stormling: Oh yeah!? Watch THIS!
*Other Stormling goes up on a stage and jumps in to the crowd, and all the Stormlings back away except one dumb one, who is flattened*
Flattened Stormling: Ow.
ANYWAY, back at the Barons castle....
Baron: Eh, they're probably just fighting somewhere. OH SLINKY! I GOT THE ROBBER CLOTHES!
*Baron goes to his bedroom and finds the Slinky, then puts a little black robber mask that just covers the eyes on the Slinky*
Baron: There! Now let ME get in to my super-awesome robber clothes!
*5 minutes later the Baron comes back in the room wearing black sweatpants, a robber mask, and a black T-shirt*
Baron: Ta-da! Like it?
Slinky:....
Baron: Well that doesn't matter, because it will work! Anyway, it's only seven o clock, so lets wait for five hours!*
Baron smiles eagerly and stares in to blank space for three hours straight*
Baron: Oh FINALLY it's twelve o clock! TO NEXUS TOWER! Wait how do I get there? STORMLING!
*Stormling that was left behind walks in the room*
Stormling: Yes?
Baron: How do I get to Nexus Tower?
Stormling: Let me see...*Stormling pulls out an iPhone 19* You go in a straight line in that direction *Points to Nexus Tower* and walk until you reach Nexus Tower*
Baron: Okay!
*Baron jumps out the window, then remembers he forgot his Slinky, goes back inside and in to his bedroom, grabs his pet Slinky, then jumps out the window with his arms spread wide like a bird, enjoying the fall, until he is about to land on the street but a truck comes along really fast and the Baron hits the windshield and bounces off, back in to his bedroom*
Baron: Whoa...*teetering on his feet and dizzy* What just happened? I'll try again...
*Baron jumps out the window, lands on the road, and walks to Nexus Tower*
At Nexus Tower....
Baron: AH-HA! I'm here! Now to climb up Nexus Tower!
*Baron gets two suction cup pad things out of his pocket, puts them on his hands, and climbs up the wall of Nexus Tower, with his Slinky in his pocket. Then Baron finds a window, pulls a glass cutter from his pocket, and cuts a small hole in the window, and throws his Slinky in*
Baron: Come on Slinky! Push out the window!
Slinky:....
Baron: Fine.
*Baron cuts more holes in the glass until he can crawl inside, then he crawls inside*
Baron: Okay Slinky, we don't want any more mistakes like that, so do what I say, okay?
Slinky:.....
Baron: Good.
*Baron walks out of the room and finds himself in a hallway, then sees a Janitor mopping the floor with a bucket nearby*
Baron: Uh-oh. Hey Janitor! Go away! I am the Darkitect! I am here to steal the Imagination Nexus! Go away or I will attack!
Janitor: Huh? No way! I will defend the Imagination Nexus!
*Janitor spins his mop around him like a ninja, making the Baron slightly dizzy*
Baron: OH YEAH!? HI-YAHH!
*Baron throws a bunch of knives at the Janitor, but the Janitor spins his mop again like a weapon, and he spins it so all the knives hit the mop and do no harm to the Janitor, leaving a bunch of knives stuck in the mop, then the Janitor thumps the mop on the ground defiantly*
Baron: Wow that's impressive.
*The Janitor kicks the mop while holding it so all the knives get blown out of the mop and toward the Baron*
Baron: *Little girl scream* AAAAAAAAAH! *Ducks and all the knives thump in to the wall*
Baron: Is that all you got!? AAAAAARGH!
*Baron rushes toward the Janitor but the Janitor just kicks the bucket over, spilling chemicial cleaner on to the floor and the Baron stops*
Baron: What was that supposed to do, make me slip?
Janitor: No.
*Janitor calmly takes a matchbox out of his pocket, lights a match, and drops it on to the chemicial, which instantly catches on fire, making the Baron run away*
Baron: HOW IS THAT POSSIBLE?!?!
Janitor: And now, my final move!
*Janitor jumps and flips over the chemicial and fire, lands in front of the Baron, and throws him out the window, along with the Slinky*
Baron: AAAAAAAAAAAAAH!
*Baron lands on the sidewalk, gets up, and runs home with his Slinky*
Baron: Well, at least I have YOU as an evil friend, my little pet Slinky.
*Slinky suddenly gets eyes and stands up, somehow*
Slinky: You are evil?
Baron: Yeah, and YOU CAN TALK?!

Chapter 37
Baron: *Watching tv* I LIKE THAT COMMERCIAL FOR A TOY ROBOT!
*Baron calls the people who made the commercial*
Employee: *Picks up phone and starts talking to the Baron* Hello?
Baron: Hi, I would like that robot on your tv ad.
Employee: That will be three thousand dollars.
Baron: WHAT!? I don't have that much money! Give it to me or I will break your arms!
Employee: Will that be with a check or credit card?
Baron: What?
Employee: Will you be paying with a check or a credit card?
Baron: I WON'T BE PAYING AT ALL!
Employee: Then goodbye.
Baron: WAIT-
*Employee hangs up*
Baron: Argh. If I want to buy a robot, I'll probably need some money.
*Baron goes to the bank with a sack and a mask on and goes to the person who gives the money to you*
Baron: GIVE ME ALL YOUR MONEY!
Employee: Why?
Baron: I WILL...um...SEND IN AN ARMY OF STORMLINGS!
Employee: Go ahead.
Baron: What?
Employee: I said go ahead.
Baron: Um...okay...*Raises his arms in the air dramatically* I SUMMON A HUNDRED STORMLINGS!
*Three Stormlings walk in the Bank*
Baron: WHAT!? I SAID A HUNDRED, NOT THREE!
Stormling: Everyone else is at a party.
Baron: WHAT!?
Stormling: Whenever you leave the castle we all have a party.
Baron: AND WHY DIDN'T YOU EVER TELL ME THIS!?
Stormling: We thought you would be happier if you didn't know. We knew you would be angry if you knew and you would probably do some crazy thing like join the party saying you aren't angry.
Baron: THAT'S NOT TRUE! I'M NOT ANGRY! I'M HAPPY! I'M JOINING THE PARTY!
Stormling: Knew it.
At the party....
*Stormlings are partying*
Stormling: YAHOO!
*Suddenly the Baron opens the front doors and all the Stormlings stop*
Baron: Well? Don't stop! I'm not angry! LET'S PARTY! So what should we do first? Twister? I have a Twister pad thing in the closet in the attic! Or how about Truth or Dare?
Stormling: Are you okay?
Baron: Of course I am! Why wouldn't I be? I just want to have fun, you know, invite some friends over, stay up all night, you know!
Stormling: *Whispers to other Stormling* He needs a psychiatrist.
*Stormlings grab the Barons arms and drag him to a psychiatrist*
At the psychiatrists office....
*Baron is reclining on a recliner*
Psychiatrist: So, why do you come to my office?
Baron: Well, the Stormlings think I've gone crazy because, well, I'm the Baron and I just wanted to have some fun with them at their party-what are you doing?
Psychiatrist: Checking your pulse. Now tell me, have you had any other symptoms of sickness?
Baron: What?! No, I'm not sick, I just wanted to have some fun at the Stormlings' party!
Psychiatrist: I see. And how does that make you feel?
Baron: What do you mean?! It made me feel like I wanted to go to their party, I just told you!
Psychiatrist: I see. So when did you start feeling left out?
Baron: How did you know I was feeling left out?
Psychiatrist: Lets just say I'm a really good Psychiatrist.
Baron: Okay. Well I started feeling left out the moment I heard they had lots of parties without me-
Psychiatrist: While you were trying to rob the bank and were away other times. I see.
Baron: How did you-
Psychiatrist: Read your mind? I didn't. I'm just good at my job.
Baron: Oh.
Psychiatrist: And why did you want to do such kiddish games as Twister, or Truth or Dare?
Baron: I don't know, that's just what I did at parties when I was a kid- Wait, I didn't tell you the games I wanted to do at the party! Anyway, I don't understand why the Stormlings didn't like my party ideas.
Psychiatrist: Ah, yes, of course. They fear you as their leader and they think you never want to have fun and are always angry.
Baron: I AM NOT ALWAYS ANGRY! I'LL SMASH YOU FOR SAYING THAT! *Gets up*
Psychiatrist: *Calmly waves his hand in front of the Barons face* You are calm.
*Baron suddenly feels very calm and lies back in his recliner again*
Baron: So anyway, do you know how I can be more fun at the parties?
Psychiatrist: You could do karaoke.
Baron: But I don't like singing! I wish I could like singing though if it would make me more fun.
Psychiatrist: Okay. *Waves his hand in front of the Barons face again* You will not mind singing in front of people.
*Suddenly the Baron feels like it wouldn't be so bad to sing in front of a lot of people*
Baron: How did you do that?!
Psychiatrist: I didn't. You are now under my control.
Baron: I am now under your control.
*Suddenly G walks in the room and whispers something to the Psychiatrist and hands him a roll of money*
Psychiatrist: You will now sing Yankee Doodle at the top of your lungs for the next three weeks.

Chapter 37
Baron: *Watching tv* I LIKE THAT COMMERCIAL FOR A TOY ROBOT!
*Baron calls the people who made the commercial*
Employee: *Picks up phone and starts talking to the Baron* Hello?
Baron: Hi, I would like that robot on your tv ad.
Employee: That will be three thousand dollars.
Baron: WHAT!? I don't have that much money! Give it to me or I will break your arms!
Employee: Will that be with a check or credit card?
Baron: What?
Employee: Will you be paying with a check or a credit card?
Baron: I WON'T BE PAYING AT ALL!
Employee: Then goodbye.
Baron: WAIT-
*Employee hangs up*
Baron: Argh. If I want to buy a robot, I'll probably need some money.
*Baron goes to the bank with a sack and a mask on and goes to the person who gives the money to you*
Baron: GIVE ME ALL YOUR MONEY!
Employee: Why?
Baron: I WILL...um...SEND IN AN ARMY OF STORMLINGS!
Employee: Go ahead.
Baron: What?
Employee: I said go ahead.
Baron: Um...okay...*Raises his arms in the air dramatically* I SUMMON A HUNDRED STORMLINGS!
*Three Stormlings walk in the Bank*
Baron: WHAT!? I SAID A HUNDRED, NOT THREE!
Stormling: Everyone else is at a party.
Baron: WHAT!?
Stormling: Whenever you leave the castle we all have a party.
Baron: AND WHY DIDN'T YOU EVER TELL ME THIS!?
Stormling: We thought you would be happier if you didn't know. We knew you would be angry if you knew and you would probably do some crazy thing like join the party saying you aren't angry.
Baron: THAT'S NOT TRUE! I'M NOT ANGRY! I'M HAPPY! I'M JOINING THE PARTY!
Stormling: Knew it.
At the party....
*Stormlings are partying*
Stormling: YAHOO!
*Suddenly the Baron opens the front doors and all the Stormlings stop*
Baron: Well? Don't stop! I'm not angry! LET'S PARTY! So what should we do first? Twister? I have a Twister pad thing in the closet in the attic! Or how about Truth or Dare?
Stormling: Are you okay?
Baron: Of course I am! Why wouldn't I be? I just want to have fun, you know, invite some friends over, stay up all night, you know!
Stormling: *Whispers to other Stormling* He needs a psychiatrist.
*Stormlings grab the Barons arms and drag him to a psychiatrist*
At the psychiatrists office....
*Baron is reclining on a recliner*
Psychiatrist: So, why do you come to my office?
Baron: Well, the Stormlings think I've gone crazy because, well, I'm the Baron and I just wanted to have some fun with them at their party-what are you doing?
Psychiatrist: Checking your pulse. Now tell me, have you had any other symptoms of sickness?
Baron: What?! No, I'm not sick, I just wanted to have some fun at the Stormlings' party!
Psychiatrist: I see. And how does that make you feel?
Baron: What do you mean?! It made me feel like I wanted to go to their party, I just told you!
Psychiatrist: I see. So when did you start feeling left out?
Baron: How did you know I was feeling left out?
Psychiatrist: Lets just say I'm a really good Psychiatrist.
Baron: Okay. Well I started feeling left out the moment I heard they had lots of parties without me-
Psychiatrist: While you were trying to rob the bank and were away other times. I see.
Baron: How did you-
Psychiatrist: Read your mind? I didn't. I'm just good at my job.
Baron: Oh.
Psychiatrist: And why did you want to do such kiddish games as Twister, or Truth or Dare?
Baron: I don't know, that's just what I did at parties when I was a kid- Wait, I didn't tell you the games I wanted to do at the party! Anyway, I don't understand why the Stormlings didn't like my party ideas.
Psychiatrist: Ah, yes, of course. They fear you as their leader and they think you never want to have fun and are always angry.
Baron: I AM NOT ALWAYS ANGRY! I'LL SMASH YOU FOR SAYING THAT! *Gets up*
Psychiatrist: *Calmly waves his hand in front of the Barons face* You are calm.
*Baron suddenly feels very calm and lies back in his recliner again*
Baron: So anyway, do you know how I can be more fun at the parties?
Psychiatrist: You could do karaoke.
Baron: But I don't like singing! I wish I could like singing though if it would make me more fun.
Psychiatrist: Okay. *Waves his hand in front of the Barons face again* You will not mind singing in front of people.
*Suddenly the Baron feels like it wouldn't be so bad to sing in front of a lot of people*
Baron: How did you do that?!
Psychiatrist: I didn't. You are now under my control.
Baron: I am now under your control.
*Suddenly G walks in the room and whispers something to the Psychiatrist and hands him a roll of money*
Psychiatrist: You will now sing Yankee Doodle at the top of your lungs for the next three weeks.

Oooooh... Miss G got him again...
