Chapter 15 The Carnival
Baron: Ahh, the sound of silence.
Stormling: Silence isn't a sound.
Baron: Sure it is. ...I'm bored.
Stormling: Do you want to try to invade Nexus Tower again?
Baron: No, every time I try that alarm clocks and air-conditioners fall on me. (see an average day for the faction leaders for more information)
Stormling: Why don't you drop alarm clocks on nexus tower?
Baron: How would I get above it? I'm so bored.
Maelstrom Shouty McBullhorn clone: THE CARNIVAL IS IN TOWN! FLY TO NEXUS TOWER AND HELP SAVE NINJAGO! THE CARNIVAL IS IN TOWN! MY THROAT IS HOARSE BUT I STILL LOVE SHOUTING!
Baron: Be quiet! Can't you talk in a quiet voice?
Maelstrom Shouty McBullhorn clone: NO! THE PROGRAMMERS HAVE CAPS LOCK IN MY PROGRAMMING!
Baron: What do you mean, 'programmers'?
Maelstrom Shouty McBullhorn clone: ...I'VE SAID TOO MUCH. *turns head away*
Baron: Oookay...I know! I'll go to the carnival!
15 minutes later...
Carnival ticket person: Sorry, but you can't come in without a ticket.
Baron: I have this traffic ticket I got on the way over.
Carnival ticket person: I don't mean that kind of ticket.
Baron: I have a ticket to a movie tonight I took from someone.
Carnival ticket person: ...Deal! BOSS, I'M GOING TO LEAVE WORK EARLY TODAY!
Baron: He-he. Sucker! That movie ticket is for a row in the BACKSEAT! MWAHAHAHAHA! *Walks in Carnival* Wow, this place is AWESOME! A SIDEWAYS CARROUSEL! A SIDEWAYS FERRIS WHEEL! A spinning teacup ride? OH MY GOSH A SUPER BIG SUPER FAST ROLLER-COASTER! AWESOME!
*Baron sees someone go flying out of a roller-coaster car and fall in the dunk tank, in between the softest mattress in the world and the bounciest trampoline in the world*
Baron: HA-HA! That guy got wet! Ooh, there's another person flying out of the roller-coaster car!
*Person lands on the trampoline and rips a hole in it and slams in to the ground*
Baron: Actually, I think I'll wait and go on some of the kiddie rides! That way the roller-coaster will seem even cooler! That's why I'm doing it. Not that I'm scared or anything, HA-HA! That ride looks good!
*Gets on to a ride called 'the choo-choo express!', and rides on it at half a mile per hour*
Baron: AAAAH!
Baby on same ride: Your'e a scawedy cat!
Baron: I AM NOT! AAH THE TRAIN IS ABOUT TO TURN A CORNER!
5 minutes later...
Baron: That baby ride was boring, and so slow. Time for the Roller-coaster! *Looks at roller coaster and is about to give up when he sees a toddler in line, and decides that if a toddler can do it, he could probably maybe possibly do it sometime*
Baron: I'll go on it now!
5 minutes later...
Baron: *On the rollercoaster* AAAH I'M GONNA HURL! BLEAH!
*Throws up, but his throw up, instead of going on the person in front of him, seemed to hover in the air, then splattered on the Baron's face*
Baron: Eww, gross!
*Ride suddenly stops, and Baron lands in front of the fortune teller's booth*
Baron: That was weird. I might as well learn my fortune.
*Baron slips in quarter tied to a string and tied to his hand, and machine pulls his finger in the coin slot*
Baron: OW! Oh well, it'll just grow back.*finger grows back*
Fortune teller machine: Your fortune is...25 cents less than it was a minute ago.
Baron: *Facepalm*
Cotton candy seller: COTTON CANDY! GET YOUR COTTON CANDY!
Baron: Is it real candy?
Cotton candy seller: Sure, why not?
Baron: I'll take one please.
Cotton candy seller: Here you go.
Baron: *Takes one bite of it, and his eyes have swirls in them like a hypnotized cartoon person* SUGAR CLOUDS!
Cotton candy seller: Uh-oh.
Baron: SUGAR CLOUDS!
Cotton candy seller: Hey, no need to panic!
Baron: FOR SUGAR CLOUDS THERE IS! *Eats all the cotton candy in the cart* MORE SUGAR CLOUDS! *Gasp!* *Sees a real cloud*HUGE SUGAR CLOUD! MINE! *Chases Cloud and falls off a cliff* AAAAH!
P.S. By the way, when do you think I should start Christmas/Holiday themed chapters?

Chapter 16 Decorating the Christmas tree
Baron: Oh Christmas tree, oh Christmas tree, tell me how to work this chain-saw...To cut you down, to cut you down, to cut you down, to cut you down, Oh Christmas tree, Oh Christmas tree, tell me how to work this chain-
VRRR!
Baron: Oh, never mind.
*Baron tries to chainsaw tree down from the park, then the tree falls on him*
Baron: OW! Well, I got the tree. Wait a minute, that's the wrong type of tree! *Facepalm*
Chainsaw stormling: I'll help you! I want to be a magician and this is the perfect oppurtinuty! I, the great storm-ini, will saw this baron in half, and he will be unharmed!
2 minutes later...
Chainsaw stormling: Umm...Do you have some glue or something?
Baron: AAURGH! *Tries to discenegrate stormling but discenegrates the snow, making a mini-flash flood that sweeps him away in to the sewer*
Baron: Hey, this is way nicer than my castle!
Sewer Alligator: Argh...CHOMP!
Baron: AAAH! *Runs out of the sewer and finds the chainsaw stormling*
Baron: Stormling, I need your help! I need to borrow your chainsaw, can you give it to me?
Chainsaw stormling: Sorry, it IS my arm, I can't unattach it- OW! YOU COULDV'E USED THE CHAINSAW YOU USED BEFORE!
Baron: Yeah, but it's wet!
Chainsaw stormling: *Facepalm*
5 minutes later...
Baron: OW THIS TREE HURTS! Ok...almost got it off me...there! Now to take it home!
2 hours later...
Baron: *Dragging tree in to castle* this. is. HEAVY!
Stormling: Really, let me help. *lifts tree off the ground with one hand*
Baron: *facepalm* could you put it in the big room where we put this kinds of stuff?
*stormling puts it in there*
Stormling: Aren't you going to decorate it?
Baron: You have to decorate it!? this is harder than i thought!
5 minutes later...
Baron: Hey, heres a ball thing that looks like a candy-cane! *ball has red and white stripes* Candy-cane...YUMMY! *Takes bite out of glass ornament* OW, YUMMY candy hurts...
Stormling: of course it does, its glass.
Baron: Doesn't taste good also...
Stormling: Yeesh, just finish the tree, okay?
20 minutes later...
Baron: I'm done!
Stormling: That looks like you put one ornament on it and a plastic ninja star on the top!
Baron: That's because it is! But there's something missing...It's not infected! I'll infect it!*touches christmas tree and it comes to life and tree is about to stomp on a stormling*
Baron: MWAHAHAHAHA!
*Chrismas tree stops, looks at the baron, then grabs him and drags him in to the imagination nexus, then his house explodes and a stormling helps another stormling up on to a floating piece of house*
