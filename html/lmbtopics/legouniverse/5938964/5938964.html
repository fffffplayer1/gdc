ALERT! This story is anticipated to be a multi-writer with my friend, Whoop60. This is a prequel to The Night Flash series, and takes place after The Youngling's Quest. Enjoy!
Chapter 1
"Ah..." Where am I? I don't really know; it's dark in here. But really.... where am I? "Oof!" I fall over the hospital bed, and struggle to get to my feet. Wait.... a hospital bed? I'm in the hospital? Oh... I remember. I was with a company. Dwight Lavamonster, Dylan, some other people. We came to check out the newly named Pandora, the Democracy's capital planet; it used to be Crux Prime, ravaged by the Maelstrom War. Then... ah, my head hurts. Then, me and Dwight.... we got seperated. He got away... "Ow!" I clutch a gash in my chest, which looks fresh. I guess I haven't been out that long. But me and Dwight got seperated... he got away, me and the rest were stranded. We saw- creatures. To say the least, they were horrifying. I try to get back up, and barely manage to lift myself up by holding the wall. This injury is a pain. I study the hospital room. It looks normal. The bed is fine. I see exotic foods from the Universe piled on a table, with some weird needle. It was labled "Relief." Well, if it's relief, I want it. I take the needle and poke it into my arm. "Ah!" That hurt. But... my gash feels better. That's good. I get up and open the hospital door. "What the brick...?" The entire hospital was on fire. I gasped at the light and started running. I saw the ship docks doors, opened them, only to see more fire. How was my room protected from the fire...? Eh, I don't have time to think about that. I run down the white, blazing corridors, looking at the security cameras as I go. Was this a sick joke...? No. No, of course not. I finally reach the big, iron doors, and swing them open. I yell at what I see.

"It appears the game is over before it even begins."

Chapter 2

 

I see a minifigure; a minifigure with no face. A minifigure with a green, mass of fungi-like blobs replacing what face it used to be. It's hands were sharp and long. The creature turns it's face-well, not really a face- towards me. It stares at me for awhile, then starts running. I do, too. It was coming straight for me at an abnormal speed. Besides the fact a disturbing creature was chasing me, it was bright outside. Too bright. From staying in the hospital room too long. Why am I thinking about this? Anyways, the streets of Pandora are new. And ravaged by war. Craters are everywhere. I see tall buildings, all abandoned as I run. I must be in Pandora City. Where can I hide- oh. Oh no. No... this is bad. More of those creatures; it's a horde. I run through the streets of the City, dodging the.... monsters. I see ship docks on fire, high-tech bases.... All of them used to be beautiful. "ARG!' I trip.... NO! One of the creatures grab onto my leg, and... oh, bricks. "Grlargerj...." The monster growls, and- BLAM! The creature falls. "Go! Go!" I quickly get to my feet and run after after the other minifigure. While I'm thinking, I should study my surroundings... the tall buildings are probably capital businesses. Rich houses, diners, mansions. stores, ships.... Ships that were on fire. Boy, am I sure glad that minifigure saved me. "Hey, kid! Jump on the speeder, NOW." I happily agree and jump on the speeder, riding away from the creatures as fast as we could. I make out the minifigure's features; dark, chopped hair. Green eyes. Wearing a tight jacket and a cap. Long legs, tight jeans... was that... "Dylan?" The figure turns around. "Uh... Indy? Indiana Lord?" Dylan smiles. "Oh, yeah. Indy? Lots of stuff happened while you were out. I'll explain later; right now, we have our hands full." I laugh. "No kiddin'." Dylan's my best friend. Been best friends for awhile....

Chapter 3

 

We keep on riding through the abandoned City. Behind us, the disturbing creatures are starting to catch up; like I said, the creatures are abnormally fast. Instead of running, they kind of... shamble, but very, very fast. "WHAT THE BRICK HAPPENED HERE?!" I scream over the wind pounding in my ears. ''CHEMICALS! PEOPLE GOT CHEMICALS IN THEIR SYSTEM, BECAME THOSE THINGS! BARELY ANY PEOPLE ARE LEFT ON PANDORA, AND ALL THE SHIP DOCKS ARE DESTROYED! THE MYTHRANS CAN'T SAVE US!" Well, that's great. "OH, BRIC-!" Before I finish my sentence, one of the- Diseased, as I may call them- grapples onto the speeder. "AAAAAH!" I try to kick it off, but the only thing that happens is the Diseased grabbing onto my leg, trying to pull me off the speeder. BLAM! The creature falls off the speeder. I pull myself together and study Dylan's weapon. Nexus Pistol, Mark 42. Good. Not much ammo, though. "NICE JOB!" "THANKS!" We turn right, then left, then right... After a long time, we lose the hordes. We stop at an office building. "Well, that was close." I say. "NO KIDDING. Anyways, I've got a group in the City; we've been staying here for awhile. Been able to keep out the hordes, but... there was a security breach." Group? City? What the brick was Dylan talking about? "A group?" "Yes, a group. We've been surviving for awhile; they seem to think of me as the leader. We had barricades; ships. Some... PERSON, blew up the ships, boxes, electric fences... and ran. All the creeps got inside... we're trying to get more Nexus weapons. C'mon, I have a bag full of Nexus weapons right here. We'll take out all the creeps!" I nod. He opens the door for me. "Ladies first." I laugh. "You wish." The building looks in good shape; there are couches, food... Me and Dylan walk upstairs, and look at his group.

Chapter 4

All of the group instantly points their weapons at me. "Hey, I don't mean any trouble." I say. Apparently, the group thinks I'm holding Dylan hostage. "Put your weapons down, everyone." Dylan says with a glare. Most of the group happily puts their weapons away, but one of them doesn't. She puts a knife to my chest. "Amy, stand down." The girl, Amy, looks at me. "Who is this, Blake?" "It's my friend, Indy." Amy puts her knife on a table. I smile and tip my fedora. "Nice to meet you, too." She glares at me. Amy has long, blonde hair. Blue eyes. Ripped jeans, and a black jacket. Dylan's group has about 6 people, including me and Dylan. "Yeah, I'm Amy. I don't really tell people my last name, if that's okay with you." She continues to glare at me. Huh. What a nice person! "Anyways, Indy. Welcome to the group. This is James," Dylan points to a preteen who has crazy hair and a torn up face. "Austin," He points to a man with a hoodie and a mysterious face. "Emily," He points a girl with short, red hair, jeans, and a sweatshirt. "and Amy, but you've already met." I smile. "Nice to meet all of you. Now, I'm pretty sure Dylan here just went for a trip and got lots of Nexus weapons. But before we talk about that, what happened? What happened to Pandora?" Dylan laughs a grim laugh. "I already told you. Chemical-" "I know it's chemicals. But how did the chemicals get out? Was it a chemical that the Maelstrom used during the Battle of Pandora? Or was it a chemical-in-progress?" Austin suddenly looks up at me. His face is... Pretty scary. "I know what happened, Indy." Amy sighs. "Not THIS story again." "Let him talk!" I say. "It happened. The companies came; they came to check Pandora out. See if any war chemicals got out during the Battle of Pandora. Oh, the chemicals weren't from war; Mythrans made them." Everybody stares at Austin. "What the BRICK are you talking about?" Says James.

Dylan goes outside and starts "helping" the other survivors by taking out some Diseased. "Aw... bricks." I run down the stairs to go help Dylan and the rest of the group take out the hordes. We have about nine people left. "WE'VE GOT ENOUGH AMMO! SHOOT FAST!" I happily oblige. I start picking off the Diseased that are close to us; get rid of the major threat. I dodge one of the zombies; shoot it in the head. I look off into the distance for a split second, and see more Diseased coming in. "They're just gonna keep on comin'! WE HAVE TO CLOSE THE GATE!" Dylan gives me a dirty look. He backs right up behind my back, covering me. "Indy, we can throw the group in the horde-" "Dylan, shut up. We're not doing that. We need more people. We need more guns. These people could help." Dylan continues to look at me. "Indy, you ain't leader of the group." Apparently- AH! One of those DISEASED grab me. Dylan doesn't shoot it. "DYLAN! PLEASE! HELP ME!" The zombie tries to rip through my clothes. I struggle. One of our group turns around to my cries, and sees Dylan staring at me, fighting off the disturbing Diseased. Dylan yells all of a sudden, and looks surprised. Wha-? He shoots the Diseased, and helps me up. "You okay, Indy?" What was Dylan doing, a stage act? I suddenly realize we've picked off the hordes. What?! They agree with him?! Austin walks over and stands by Dylan's side. So does Emily. "Wh... Wha-....?"Amy tosses Dylan a mini-gun. It shoots... Energy bullets... "Okay, we don't mean no trouble.
