Chapter 11.1 :0

Wind thrashing his face like a whip, Dwight descended on Butterscorch's back to the island thathad once been known as Chantey Shantey. As the dragon swooped over the river, a massive red tentacle coiled up from the water and made a movement that could be interpreted as a jaunty wave. Dwight waved back. The cliffs surrounding three fourths of the island gave way to a waterfall that spilld into a small lake. Between the lake and the beach was a two-story modern house. It was built with hite stone and had a black roof studded with solar panels. Most of the windows were open to let in the sea air. This was where Dwight, Reyna, and their daughter Rean lived.

Butterscorch landed on the beach and Dwight flipped expertly out of the saddle. He started toward the house a few steps and then Reyna came barreling out of the doorway. She was dressed in a white shirt and blue jeans, and was holding baby Rean, swathed in blankets, in her right arm. Reyna used her free hand to scratch Butterscorch behind the ears, and then puilled Dwight into a hug. "Hi! How was the c-" She pulled back. "Something's wrong." Dwight sighed. 'I can't hide anything when she's around,' he thought dryly.

Taking a breath, Dwight said, "We have to get out of here. Now. Faine's gone mental, and he'll target you if I try to stop him."

Reyna looked concerned. "Stop him from doing what?"

"He's trying to control the Maelstrom." Reyna almost gasped, whcih meant she was terrified. "More than that, he staged the entire Pandora epidemic! Thousands of innocents robbed of their lives, just so they wouldn't notice him the last of Typhonus's power into his body!"

Reyna was mortified. "He actually went through with it?"

Dwight nodded. "I gues taking out the Darkitect wasn't enough for him. He's insane, Reyna, and he threatened to go after you and Rean if I interfere." He ran his fingers through his hair distractedly. "Plus, Indy and the others are still alive."
