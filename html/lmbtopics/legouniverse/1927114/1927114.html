Chapter 1

The Attack.

Bob was on probation. he had talked back to sky lane, and she made him mop. Suddenly, a huge blast rocked the Venture Explorer. Bob thought a meteorite hit the hull.Sky Lane walked up and said"Wow, Bob, not a bad job for bluebricksofimaginationwhatisthat?"
Bob turned around. A purple cloud had enveloped the back of the ship and was infecting minifigs left and right. "you have to get out of here!" Sky Lane yelled, whipping out a net and ensnaring a spiderling. Bob pieced together a rocket and flew out a gash in the hull.
He worried about the minifigs still on board, but his thoughts shifted to himself when a big maelstrom dragon, bright purple in front and glowing white in the back, appeared out of nowhere. All Bob could do was yell "help!' as the dragon blew his ship to pieces.

When Bob awoke, a Sentinel knight was standing over him. "What happened?" Bob asked him. "Your rocket blew up. It was AWESOME!!!" he said in an oddly high voice.
He grinned, showing sharp teeth. "Uh..." Bob stuttered. "Hey. I don't bite." he said, taking off his helmet. A brown ponytail fell out and the knight grinned again.

The knight was a girl.

Chapter 2

The Sentinel

Bob's jaw dropped. "What?" she said, putting her helmet under her arm. "Uh, nothing, I-"
Suddenly, Bob was seized from behind. He felt himself getting dragged away from the knight by a creature that vaguely resembled a minifigure with a hammer and wings. "Darkling!" Darklings are like stromlings, only created out of pure maelstrom energy. The knight yelled, raising her sword, a ferocious light flashing in her eyes. She charged, sword over her head, and launched herself at the three creatures. The first had itself chopped up, the next two fled, leaving Bob and the knight. "I'm ToaCourtney" she said suddenly. "I'm Bob." said Bob. "So, ToaCourtney-" "Call me Toa C." she interrupted. "Thank you for saving me, Toa C." "Don't mention it, I save wimps all the time!" She suddenly looked upset. "Whoa, that came out wrong. Sorry." "That's okay-" "Why are you pale?" "I stay inside a lot because-" "Computer freak?" "No, I build a lot-" "You should join assembly." "I should really get going-" "I'll escort you to the monument!" Bob sighed. this was going to be a long day.
