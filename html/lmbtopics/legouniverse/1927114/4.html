Chapter 9

Crux Prime

The cold was unbearable. Bob could feel himself being disassembled and reassembled over and over again. Finally, He fell flat on his face on rocky ground. As he got up, a ball of fire sailed over his head, destroying a pile of crates and sending bricks everywhere. "What are you doing here?" A knight tussling with a dark ronin shouted. "D-doc Overbuild sent me," Bob spluttered, "He said you needed help." "We need more help than you, kid," said the knight, smashing the ronin over its head with its own katana, "If you can get to Quatanodo, the village beyond the canyon, you can probably be of some help there." Bob took his advice and ran. He had only sprinted about a hundred yards when he felt a hard impact on his back and fell to the ground. A massive purple ape was standing over him, preparing to smash him with a giant boulder. Bob reached for his sword, but it had been knocked away. Instead, his circular hand closed around the Imaginator. He picked it up and pulled the trigger. The ape flew ten feet and crashed to the ground. Unfortunately, it got back up and lumbered toward Bob once more. Bob clicked the trigger again, but all the Imaginator did was click. "Prototypes," Bob said through gritted teeth. The ape lifted a massive arm-and fell to pieces. There was a knight behind the pile of infected bricks, who said, "Well, I never thought I'd see you again!" and took off his helmet.

The knight was a girl.

Chapter 6,

The Doc's office.

Bob walked up the stairs. He had decided to find wherever he was staying without running into someone. Footsteps started echoing from the hall, so Bob ducked into the nearest closet. He found himself in a small office with blueprints pasted to the wall at odd angles. "Ooh, thats cool," Bob said, looking at the schematics for what looked like a modified laser gun. Up in the corner, in a red pen, read Too unstable, prototype for destruction. Which, of course, sent Bob looking for it. He finally found it in a drawer, next to a can of Friendly Felix's Elbow Grease. He picked it up, hit it on his hand, and blasted a hole in the door. He looked out, staring at the flabbergasted face of Dr. Overbuild. "Well, hello, you must be the new assembly recruit," He said, opening the pulverized door, which fell off its hinges. "D-Doctor Overbuild-" Bob stuttered. The doctor just pulled the can of elbow grease out of the drawer and rubbed it on one of his robotic arms. "I thought I'd find you here. Oh, you found my prototype Imaginator. No, no, you can keep it, just remember it's quite, ah, unstable." He smiled at Bob. "Well, off you go." He shooed Bob out the door, towards a teleporter. "Now use this, I daresay they will need you there," The Doc gave a nod and a wink and walked away. Bob pressed a button, and everything went dark.

Quatanodo

He was a ninja, with a gray kimono. He bowed, and pulled down his scarf. "Well, Bob, i'd honestly hoped you would do better!" It was the Mythran.

Chapter 10, part 2

Out of the frying pan, into the fire.

Bob gaped at the Mythran. "What're you playing at?!" he said, his skin turning from pale to light pink, "I've been sucked into a disaster!" "I'm afraid you haven't a choice. Mythrans like myself are assigned to make sure potential heroes get their potential recognized. I've failed this mission many times." The Mythran, despite only having eyes, looked nervous. "...she'll be ridin' on a ilama, she'll be ridin' on a ilama... " A voice rang out from just beyond the canyon. "Here comes Friendly Felix. He'll help you out with whatever you may need," The Mythran said, starting to shimmer, "Take this, and may good luck follow you." He vanished in a flash, leaving behind a bow and a quiver with three arrows inside. Bob touched an arrow, and his hair stood up on end. "-when she comes!" A minifigure who was undeniably Friendly Felix rounded the corner. He wore a white turban with a blue gem in the middle. He carried a banjo, and strapped to his back was a drum and a backpack. "Hey, fellow traveler! What brings you to this neck of the woods?" Felix spoke in a singsong voice "Can you lend a hand?" Bob asked him. "No, but I sell anything for the traveler at a bargain price!" Bob pulled his last salary from Sky Lane out of his pocket. "I'll take a can of your elbow grease, and this hat." He pointed at a pilot's hat, "Plus, I could use some advice." Bob said, "The goods will be 250, but the advice I give for free," he semi-sang, "I have been told to give a Bob a map. Would that be you?" he asked. "Y-yes, that would be me," If he couldn't trust Felix, who could he trust? "Here. This is all I was told you need," he said. "Thank you." Bob said. He got in his rocket and he discovered it had coordinates scrawled on then. He decided to warp to the coordinates written there. When Bob slowed to normal speed, explosions started going off around him. He had come out of warp into the middle of a battle.

Chapter 11, part 1

Getting better?

Bob pulled into a loop. Whatever he expected, it wasn't this. Two Venture-class ships were engaged in battle. One was normal, with the name Sentinel Flyer on the side, the other was heavily damaged and infected. "AAHHHH!" Bob saw a dragon flying towards him. He pulled away and brought his ship to a standstill behind a chunk of debris. He saw the name of the other ship; Venture Explorer. Bob may not have liked his job, but was still upset at the state of his old place. He looked through the compartments in the pilot's seat. He found a laser, a note, and a chocolate mint. He read the note:
'Put some things on your ship. The one who asked me to give you the map told be to trick up your ship too. Best of luck, Felix.'
Bob grinned, stuffing the mint in his pocket. He pulled toward the Sentinel Flyer. The Dragon targeted him again, but this time Bob turned to face it. He pressed the button on the joystick(which wasn't there before), and an Imagination blast hit the dragon, which recoiled. Bob fired again, and again, and the dragon smashed into hundreds of peices, but not before breathing a fireball in his direction. It collided with the hull, setting it on fire. Bob pulled towards the Flyer, and, gathering speed, flew through the hangar barrier. He crashed into a wall and hopped out, several Sentinel minifigures in Hazmat uniforms put out the fire.
"Are you okay?" A Sentinel in a space suit walked up.
"I'm fine, thanks,"
"Gotta name?"
"Bob. You?"
He took off his helmet. "I'm Epsilon Starcracker. The third."

Chapter 11, part 2

The Union of Heroes.

Bob shook his hand. "It's a real pleasure, sir!"
"We we're just needing a little help. You a good pilot?"
"I think so," Bob said, "It's really been a while."
"You're goona get practice, soldier," Epsilon said, "We got a lotta trouble brewin'." He walked over to a Hazmat minifig, and had a muttered conversation. He walked back to Bob with a grin on his face.
"Great news!" he said, "Your rocket is fixable. It'll be ready in a few minutes."
"Great!" Bob said, "I'm ready to get back out there!"
"That's what you think. Now, you are going to be an escort to our best pilot, next to Sky Lane-"
"Sky Lane's alright?" Bob asked. She hadn't been the best boss, but naturally Bob didn't ill-wish her.
"Yeah, of course! Now, you're going to be an escort to our best pilot, not like she needs it, to the other ship, where you will provide backup and follow her every command. Got it?"
"Yes, of course!" Bob said, "Who?"
"Bob, meet ToaC," He brought over the one minifigure Bob always seemed to encounter.
"Hey, B-dawg! Long time no see, hur hur," she said.
"You know each other?" Epsilon said, confused.
"Uh, yeah, we've...met."
"Go figure. These are the other escorts, Chip and Pickles," He brought over the vendor and the grunt bob had seen at Nexus Tower.
"Hello," the assembley grunt, who wore a red hard hat, said, "I'm Pickles, and that's Chip." His voice was high, and soft.
"It's a pleasure to meet you," Chip said, "I hope you aren't blown out of the sky like the last guy."
Bob didn't know if he was kidding, so he just said, "Thanks. Same to you."
"Alright, Good luck to you, and an old friend of mine used to say, 'Strike first, strike fast, and get away clean*'"
The four of them blasted back into space.
