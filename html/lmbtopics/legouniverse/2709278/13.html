Chapter 11
Destination: The World Of Dark


Duke shuddered as a strong wind hit him in his face. Chris Man was still in the lead, holding a torch, lit with burning fire. Spiral stairs were somehow magically placed beneath the solid rock which Duke had already passed. He couldn't stand this place! It was so creepy! Further and further the two went, until the stairs stopped at mid- level. Now, two paths could be taken. Which direction would they go next? Duke,
starting to get a little nervous, began pacing back and fourth.
Chris Man abrubty began to read from his map, " Two paths shall be confronted. Only one will lead you to the right place. The other, will give you only the worst of the worst that you could hope for."
Duke felt a shiver run down his back. He began pacing faster, getting more nervous than ever. " Which path shall we take?"
"Either way, Duke, we will still have challenges that we must face." Chris Man frowned. " How about you pick a path." His voice seemed to change, from his normal pitched voice to a deep voice that freaked Duke out.
" How about you pick?" Duke swallowed, taking a deep breath.
" You're the leader, Duke. Choose the path you think we should go through. You know, Duke, I can't help you with everything." Chris Man glanced at Duke.
Duke began to think. Well, one path, he could hear the owls hooting. He could feel the icily chill of the wind, the darkness of a lonely path. The other path, was covered with soft, sweet smelling grass. He could hear the sweet melodie of birds chirping in the field filled with freshly grown flowers. How Duke longed to joyfully run to that path, but he knew that in reality, you had to work to earn your desires. That path seemed to easy, to just run through that path would be no work at all! The other path seemed right.
" I..I think we should take this one." Duke's fingers shaked as he said the words.
" Alright. Lead the way leader!" Chris Man smiled, letting Duke pass him.
All Duke saw was dark, sad things. Nothing was happy and cheerful walking along this path. Strong winds kept hurling toward Duke and Chris Man.
This cave were filled with thousands of bats flying around the ceiling above the two minifigures head's. They had a glossy coat that was deep black, their claws were as sharp as knifes, which could scar someone up really badly. Also, to Duke's attention, those bats were even bigger than him and Chris Man. It would be so cool if they could ride on those creautres!
" Hey Chris Man, do you think we could ride one of those bats up there?" Duke asked.
" Well, Duke. You have to be chosen to be able to ride one of those bats." Chris Man replied.
" And how long does it take before they choose you." Duke wondered.
" Some minifigures never get chosen." Chris Man frowned, unhappily.
" Oh, okay." Duke looked up at the bats one more time.
They were almost out of the end of the cave when a voice that seemed to be from someone who had lots of wisdom spoke from behind him and Chris Man.
" I see, you know much about us." A black bat hung a centimeter from the ground.
" I am Shadow. Your chosen one." He bowed his head slightly down, towards Chris Man.
" I...I am honored!" Chris Man smiled, his whole body beginning to shake.
" Please, don't thank me. Thank, Shadow." Shadow moved his black figure to the side.
A golden bat came to view. " My tribe hasn't had many minfigures tumble by lately, but there was a prophecy that we know of. Let us show it to you." The bat unrolled a scroll that was clutched tightly in his claw.
" There will be two to come
a golden and a shadow must
come before the two for
these are the chosen four meant
for the group to find and help
a world that needs saving."
" We believe you both are the ones. Never has the leader of this bat tribe ever had to leave his tribe behind because of a prophecy, but it is time for this to happen." The golden bat spoke. " I am Shimmer, your chosen one, Duke Exeter, leader over the Sentinels for a world called LEGO Universe, but we are not in need of saving your world yet.. but it is another world, Earth?" Shimmer smiled.
" Yes, we must save Earth from an evil force known as the maelstrom." Duke answered, not afraid anymore.
" We shall be going, Shimmer." Shadow looked up to his leader.
" Yes, I suppose it is time. Teka, you shall be the temporary leader while I am gone." Shimmer looked up, his eyes locking with another bat that was coming out of the crowd of bats. They met each others eyes, than they both nodded, as if agreeing to the decision.
Then, Duke and Chris Man climed up the backs of their new bats, as they flew into the darkness.


Zoo and dino ninja had been talking for hours, trying to find a way to get to this world that everybody was disapearing to. They tried to think of an idea that would actually work, but their minds seemed to be blank. Finally, Zoo thought of an idea that might possibly work.
" How about we find a Mythran? He may give us some advice." Zoo smiled hopefully.
" I don't know if that would work, Zoo. First of all, it would be hard to find a Mythran, and second of all lots of minifigures have never even seen a Mythran before." dino ninja frowned.
" Maybe we'll be the lucky ones and actually find a Mythran!" Zoo smiled.



Golden had been running for hours. She just hoped that she could find those guys that just got transported to Earth.
