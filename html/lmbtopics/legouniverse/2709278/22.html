The bats had landed for the night and were already snoring to sleep by the time Duke said aloud that he was going to take the night shift, watching for any signs of danger. Duke sighed. Of course, he couldn't forget of Golden and Handsome. That was his top priority at the moment... well besides finding a way out of The World Of Dark. He was still guessing that it had been Darkitect who had tricked him into coming to this world. Baron was always a pest around here.
Momentarily, Duke could smell something. It felt like a bad sign... it smelled like a group of at least twenty monster creatures, but they smelled familier to him.
" Wake up! Chris Man. Shimmer. Shadow!" Duke yelled shaking Chris Man until he finally woke up.
" What is it?" Chris Man asked, confused and tired.
" I smell something, rather familier." Duke frowned, mad that he didn't know the answer.
" Come on, lets wake these sleepy heads!" Chris Man joked, but Duke didn't laugh, his face looked rather series.
Chris Man began shaking the bats softly, talking to them gently in the ear. The bats began to wake up slowly, their eyes red and puffy, still tired. Then, Shadow ears perked up, alert. " Shimmer! There's a whole gang of Stromlings and Mechs! They seem more powerful than usual."
Shimmer's eyes opened wide. " We must hurry. They are headed in our direction I think."
" Too late for an escape now!" An ugly purple stromling snarled, running in attack position.
Chris Man smiled. This was going to be easy! He unsheathed his sword, drawing it upward in a swift movement, twirling around the blazing silver structure in his hand. A mech pointed his blaster at Chris Man, who reflected the blow quite easily by his fast motions. Angling his sword in the right position and slashing the silver blade in the mech, he knocked it to the ground with a single blow. It was until then that he noticed how many maelstrom were there... more than Shimmer had infered. There were at least fifty of them hidden behind each other and when the four of them knocked the first row to the ground more and more kept coming...


" The only place we haven't looked at is where the imagination is held. This is the only place left that Duke could have disapeared through...but I don't know how he did it." Vanda Darkflame frowned.
" Maybe he didn't go through a portal in the imagination. Maybe he went through something else." Zyea thought.
" Brilliant, Zyea! Maybe, he did go through a portal!" Darkflame smiled in awe of the awestruck idea.
They tryed a way to get to the "portal", but nothing happened. They tryed and tryed for hours which then turned into a day. Sad, they left the place in which Duke really did disapear to with no hope. Things didn't seem meant to be...


dino ninja smiled happily. He and Zoo's rockets had just landed in front of Pet Cove. Hopefully, the Mythran hadn't found his Grandma yet, dino ninja kept on repeating to himself. " We are here, Zoo!"
" Likewise." Zoo said, his face looking as if he just wanted to get the job done and leave.
" Hey, I don't remember that house being there the last time I went here." Zoo frowned, pointing to a wooden brown cabin facing in the direction of the landing and launching portal. dino ninja rushed forward toward the house, trying to hide his excitement and fear. " Come on, Zoo. What are you waiting for?"
" I think we should wait before we go over to that house."
" Come on, lets go!" dino ninja smiled.
Zoo hadn't wanted to move until he was sure that the Mythran was in the house with his Grandma, but he couldn't let dino ninja have all the fun!
The cabin door opened wide once dino ninja reached the right side of the house with Zoo by his side.
" Hello, come. I must talk to both of you for a moment." A ghosty voice spoke.
Zoo and dino ninja slowly walked toward the door of the house.
" Do not be afraid. I seek peace. I'm only trying to help you."
The two walked forward, not knowing what to expect...

Gresh, a very confused new member of the LEGO/ Earth Crew began to uderstand what everybody was going through: sadness for losing Kai and Epsilon, coming here with complete confusion and mainly just missing the world of LEGO Universe. He couldn't believe that they were all having really bad problems. Even Golden and Handsome of can't find their Father/Brother. That's kinda wierd, not knowing if a person really is who he says he is, and a group of four minifigures, who had landed on Earth are being chased by the Maelstrom! That would be horrible to be in danger right when you get to a very confusing world.


Zoo hesitated before walking through the door. dino ninja wasn't always a minifigure who took caution in things before doing it. Oh, well. Zoo sighed and walked through the wooden door. A huge fancy house was shown by his view. It was amazingly bigger in the inside then out. Zoo felt an invisible figure touch his shoulder, almost freaking him out of his plastic pants. The invisible ghost then became transparent and Zoo could see him clearly. The Mythran wore a white shirt, covered by a black jacket. His hair, dark brown was covered by a silk top hat.
" Welcome, my friends!" The Mythran smiled. " I see, you have found out of my visiting at Pet Cove. We, Mythrans have lifes just like you, minifigures do." The Mythran smiled.
" I see, but my friend and I weren't here for a chat...we..."
" I understand and already know what you are to ask of me." The Mythran cut dino ninja out of his unfinished sentence.
" Do you mind answering?" Zoo asked.
" The message: You will have your chance." The Mythran looked as if he were going to say more, but he didn't, instead he pretended to mess with his top hat.
" What does that mean?" dino ninja asked confused.
" I am sorry. I mustn't and can not tell you anymore." The Mythran replied. " We shall meet once again!" And the Mythran vanished from sight.
" That wasn't much help." dino ninja groaned.
" He said: You will have your chance. I think that means that we will have a chance just everybody else to go to this world." Zoo said, still not sure of his answer.


" Duster, we have to keep looking! These are our friends!" Grand cried.
" Nope." Duster stayed sitting on a tree, wanting to sleep.
" Fine, Duster. I go alone." Grand yelled madly.
" Suit yourself." Duster grumbled.
Grand rolled his eyes and began looking for Jumpy and Snark again. He had to find them. It was dark again, and the bright yellow thing disapeared, appearing with the same white thingy in the sky from yesterday's night. Grand wished someone who lived in this world could tell him what these things were...
