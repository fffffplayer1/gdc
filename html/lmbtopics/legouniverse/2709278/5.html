" Handsome and I used too, before he disapeared.

" Well, actually I never met my Mother. She died long ago. And my Father, well that was another story. He just disapeared. He never even left the house from the door, he just disapeared." Golden frowned.

" What do you mean?" Duke yelled, his body shivering with fright.

By proceeding you to this cave first before you travel means only that we warn you about the disadvantages and advantages of traveling from one world to the next. " We have changed that pathway. It is no longer avalible to a leader's advantages. Only to minifigures it is possible. Do you wish to proceed?" The voice cracked through the cave walls.
" No matter what happens, I will go." Duke yelled.

" Duke was acting really strange. I've been thinking. Duke probably had an idea of where the missing minifigures went. He always seems to know the conclusion to problems. He's probably with them right now or maybe not. He gets into trouble every once in a while, but that's the best guess I can come up with." Overbuild's eyes looked pleadingly at Storm's, begging for help to find his lost friend.
