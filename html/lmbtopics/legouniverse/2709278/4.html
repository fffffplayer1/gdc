Chapter 6


Destination: Earth


The team had been running for at least twenty minutes before they had stopped running. Golden thought they weren't going to make it, but Kai-infected had lost sight of them and had stopped trying to look for them.
" Why did it have the be Kai? Why couldn't it have been some person that I didn't know?" Tears streamed down Melodie's face. She had been crying and weeping for the past hour or so because of Kai- infected. Of course, Melodie would never call her lost friend, Kai-infected. Knowing her, she would never ever call him a name like that, but Golden hadn't really known him very well so she could accept the fact that he was infected. Melodie on the other hand couldn't. It seemed as if they weren't in danger at all. Epsilon had found shelter in a little sheltered burrowed tunnel made by dirt that barely had enough room to fit the whole group for a sleeping hut. That night, they were all getting ready for bed when Golden noticed something in the shadows of the forest. It looked like some dark red outfit gleaming darkly against the bark of a fallen tree. Could it be Kai-infected or was he not infected anymore?
" Hey guys! It's me Kai!" Kai had now stepped out of the shadows to show pale light skin on his face. By the looks of him, Golden guessed he probably wasn't infected anymore, but what if he gave himself a drink to make him look normal again and then try to kill them? This seemed suspicious.
" It's me Kai." Kai looked at Melodie and smiled.
Melodie turned her head. Why all of a sudden would he become clean and not infected? One moment, he was trying to kill them and the next, he was being so nice to them? Something was wrong here. Why am I thinking such thoughts, Melodie asked herself. No, no. He's not infected anymore! Yay! Melodie was beginning to run over to Kai when Golden grabbed her wrist.
" Melodie, don't go over there! It's a trap!" Golden looked at Melodie with big, scared eyes.
" So, there is a smart one in the group! Well. . ." Kai- infected began to reform into an ape, his body changing form to the disgusting shape of a huge monster.
" NO! NO!" Melodie screamed, as Kai- infected began banging his strong, powerful arms into the ground, breaking the mosit soil into tiny dust particles. He then began charging to the direction of the group. Melodie screamed and cryed, as she through both ice daggers into the Kai- ape's back.
" AAAAAAARRRRRRRRRR!" Kai-ape screamed in pain, giving the group time to run away from their sheltered home.
Epsilon helped everybody move, especially Melodie who was having a hard time dealing with this sad moment of depression. He ecouraged everybody on when they thought they couldn't move any longer. He did notice though that Golden didn't seem to sad about Kai becoming infected. What was that all about? Anyway, back to this
" Kai Runnaway" situation. They were burnt toast. Epsilon gave up hope.
Kai was coming closer his ape formation that helped him run and leap like a cheeta super fast. Golden couldn't believe this. They were going to be gone, and nothing would be left of them, well once Kai-infected and his teamates captured them that is.


A chill went through Duke Exeter's plastic body. The top secret meeting would be starting since it was almost midnight. He was supposed to be meeting them in Dr.Overbuild's science lab. Duke hoped everything going on with Golden and Handsome were okay. He still was trying to look for a way to find them.
" Duke Exeter!" Dr. Overbuild called, rushing forward.
" Hey. Where is everybody?" Duke looked around the lab.
" Oh, they're not here yet." Overbuild glanced at Duke. He looked tired and depressed. "Are you okay?"
" Yeah, I'm fine. Just deep in thought." Duke muttered.
" So, about Epsilon Starcracker and Melodie Foxtrot? By any chance have you figured out where they are?" Overbuild asked Duke.
" Uh... I don't know. I've been trying as hard as I could to find them. I just don't know where they are." Duke's pale face grew paler.
Overbuild felt so sad for his friend. He looked so sad. " Vanda Darkflame!" Overbuild called, walking toward the new guest, leaving depressed Duke Exeter alone standing.
" Hey, Dr. Overbuild! I've come just in time!" Darkflame smiled gently and looked over at Duke. He had never been close allies with Paradox, she usually never felt bad for him but this time she did. He looked so sad and very, very depressed. He looked as if he were going to fall to the floor. " You all right, Duke?" Vanda asked.
" Yeah, I'm fine." Duke answered tired of hearing minifigures asking him if he was okay.
Hael Storm had just come into the lab. He smiled at Overbuild. " Thanks, Overbuild for inviting us. We really do need to talk about ths problem of minifigures disapearing." Storm looked kindly at everybody.
Oh, no. The meeting was about to start. Duke half smiled, half frowned. He was never going to tell them that their missing minifugures were on another planet. Hopefully, Golden and Handsome were with them.

Duke was the first to leave Ovebuild's lab. He was actually so glad when the meeting had ended that he had rushed out of the room, even though back then, he would have been talking to Overbuild for hours. His goal at the moment was to find the energy source that would take him to Earth. He guessed the portal wouldn't be in: Pet Cove, Gnarled Forest, Ninjago, Nimbus Station. . . or any of those places, but Nexus Tower. This was where the imagination was stored and kept. The portal to Earth had to be here he just needed to look harder.


Epsilon looked behind him, the ape, Kai was still charging at them. Epsilon guessed he wouldn't want to lose track of them since he had lost them at one point already. NO, he was catching up to them! " Melodie, Wisp, Golden, Handsome, run faster!" Epsilon yelled, pushing everybody in front of him with his hands. They probably wouldn't last any longer. Kai had already gained on them. He zoomed past them and then turned his body at the exact right moment and was right in front of them. They couldn't escape now. " Pleaase, Kai. Please give us a chance. Please. We used to be your friend." Epsilon pleaded moving in front of the entier group.
" I used to be your friend, but not any more. I will give you mercy in exchange for one person in your group to join me." Kai looked at Epsilon, his purple eyes staring constantly without blinking.
" Deal." Epsilon yelled, walking in front of the big ape, Kai.
" NO! You don't have to do this, Epsilon. I'll do it . Just stay with the group and protect them!" Melodie cryed.
" No, this is my destiny. I'm sorry." Epsilon stared at Melodie, not looking at anyone else. " Do it." Epsilon, eyes still only on Melodie, began to draw a tear from his cheek just at the same moment that she did.
" Goodbye, Epsilon." Melodie cryed as Kai infected Epsilon by shooting a laser ball at him. " NO!" Melodie cryed. The now infected Epsilon then stared at Melodie with his purple, glowing eyes, then disapeared with Kai. Melodie couldn't believe it. Everything had happened so quickly and now he was gone. Gone from the life he had once shared with her as a friend. Tears sprang from her eyes like fire blazing up her body. No, no, no. This can't be happening. Melodie cryed and cryed as the night progressed.


Epsilon zoomed fastly in his new ape form. It was so much fun, riding like the wind! " Kai, this is great!" Epsilon smiled.
" Actually, my name's not Kai anymore. It's Blaze." Blaze looked back at Epsilon with a questioned look. " You seem so familier, yet you aren't."
" Well, we actually used to be really close friends!" Epsilon pictured all those moments when they were together.
" Yes, I guess we were friends at some point, but now, you can become a close friend of mine again and we'll never be apart again!" Blaze looked over his shoulder at Epsilon.
" Yeah, friends again." Then, Epsilon's mind pictured a girl with a red pony tail. She had a tear coming down her cheek, then she brushed it off. Who was she. Then he remembered seeing that same girl before he disapeared from her sight. Why didn't he stay with her? Why couldn't he have been with her for the rest of his life? He shrugged the thought off and began thinking of this new life.. this new home that he would be coming to. He couldn't wait.


Sky Lane had been just about to report something strange to Hael Storm. She saw him looking through a telescope looking lost. She was the only one who knew about the leaders' secret meeting. Maybe something was up? " Hael Storm."
" Yes... Sky Lane. How are you?" Storm asked looking dazed.
" Are you all right Hael Storm? Was the meeting okay?" Sky Lane asked.
" Ah yes, the meeting. . ." Hael Storm smiled, finally getting back to reality. " Well, Duke Exeter seemed strange. After the leader meetings, Duke is usually talking to Overbuild for hours, but he didn't. Insead, he rushed out of the room, and he was the first to leave. He's usually the last!" Storm laughed.
" Well... you know how he lost quite a few of his minifigures so maybe he's looking for something?" Melodie replied, a little confused about the Duke Exeter situation.
" I suppose your..." Hael Storm had tryed to reply, then to Sky Lane he disapeared. Everything disapeared. HIs room. His telescope. Everything. Where was he? What the...? Lane was in this new world. Green stuff was all over the place.


Oh,no. Golden noticed a girl on the ground. The whole process of minifigures to Earth was happening all over again. She couldn't stand losing people from the team, then gaining more people again. It's just getting so annoying. Golden frowned.

Chapter 1



Deep, Dark, Secret, Forest

Destination: Earth



It was misty, clouds puffed out darkly against the black sky, as rain poured roughly over trees and bushes. Owls hooted in the dark forest as wild winds twirled around.

Golden was never fond of the idea. She had wanted to walk around outside today, but it was too cloudy from the storms outside that she couldn't even see the forest even if she walked outside. It was her brother, Handsome who came up with the idea of exploring the house that they lived in.

A few weeks ago, her dad ( also Handsome's dad), left teh house. He said he'd be back, but he never did come back. Well, not yet he hadn't. When Golden's dad was around, he had overly reacted to stuff that wasn't that neccesary, like only allowing us in specific rooms. Rooms that he used for work or something, he wouldn't let us come in. Now that he was gone, Handsome wanted to explore.

" Huh. I'm so tired of this Handsome. Aren't you done looking? We've been here for hours, and all you do is look at is that lab-top." Golden groaned.

" Wait Golden, I found something! You remember how one time our dad was playing with LEGO's? Well, I found this downloaded software for some computer game called, " LEGO Universe!" Handsome was spinning the chair around and around.

" Fine Handsome. Do as you please. Just don't get us in trouble or I'll regret giving you permission." Golden sighed.

" I think it'll be to late to regret something if I get the two of us into trouble. This software won't do any harm. I promise." Handsome smiled.

" I hope you're right about that." Golden replied. Usually, when Handsome wants to do something, it always seems to turn out to be a mess. Golden didn't think this software waas harmless. She remembered her dad always coming into this particular room for hours on end. Sometimes staying here in this room for days. That was really wierd. Who knew what he did in here. Anyway, Golden just hoped this software was safe. Handsome was a computer geek. He could get passwords and usernames from any person he wanted to. Handsome hadn't clicked the log-on button yet. Golden still had time to think of what was going to happen. . .





" Anybody want to beat my record?!" Beck Strongheart had just finished his map locations and was now bragging and asking minifigures if they wanted to beat his record for map locations. Every minifig just looked at him as if he was crazy and got their attention back to what they were doing a minute ago. Just then, Melodie Foxtrot had ran with all her effort to Strongheart. He asked her the same question he had asked everyone else, thinking she would want a challenge, but se didn't. Instead, she yelled at his face. " Strongheart, do you know what's happening to our people? They're disapearing! Not just them, but even stromling mechs, stromlings, and also dark spiderlings! There's this blue light that's sucking minifigures and enemies. It's creeping me out. Did you know that Epsilon Starcracker, Wisp Lee,. . ." Melodie had disapeared also, never finishing her speech.

" And also Melodie Foxtrot has disapeared." Beck Strongheart mumbled. It wasn't long before ever minifigure in the camp of Avant Gardens were quickly running, running to Nexus Tower to tell the faction leaders that something terrible was happening.

Chapter 7


Destination: Ninjago


Jay looked sadly across the table at Zane and Cole. Sensei had refused to leave his room that day, his old, wrinkled face was very pale. He even looked a little sick. Jay still missed Kai. He was the closest friend to Kai besides the other ninja's. They barely touched their plate filled with sushi and rice. Jay couldn't stop thinking about Kai. He just disapeared somewhere and they didn't know where he was at all.
" I'll go to my room for the night." Jay frowned, leaving his untouched plate on the table.
" Me too." Cole had also gotten out of his seat.
" Me three." Zane also kept his plate on the table just like everybody else did.

Jay shifted uncomfortably in his bed. He couldn't sleep these past few days ever since Kai disapeared. He didn't want to sleep at the moment so he walked away from his bedroom to Sensei. He must been starving. Ever since Kai disapeared he never left his room and hadn't eaten a single thing." Sensei Wu? Are you okay? Do you need me to bring you some food?" Jay asked.
" No, Jay. I'm fine." Sensei's voice was so low, Jay could barely hear him. He was more sick than last time.
" Don't worry, we'll find him soon. I promise." Jay whispered into Sensei's ear.
" I hope you're right about that." Wu frowned and pulled his old body back to lay on his pillow then finally drifted off to a daydream. Jay decided to stay with him and make sure he was okay. Jay began to drift off to daydream too, his plastic body on the floor with his arms gently on Sensei's bed. His eyes still opened to the clicking of his dream.


Jay awoke the next morning to find that his body was hurting with pain. Leg cramps. Jay stopped his daydream to notice a strange field of green stuff all over the ground. What is this? He was only in Sensei Wu's bed last night and now he was in this wierd land of " Greeny Land"? What is ths? Some kind of prank or something?


Golden kicked the orange, blonde hair girl gently by her foot.
" Hello?" The girl grumbled in her sleep.
" Are you okay? We found you stranded in this forest all alone." Golden asked, already knowing the anwser. Her teamates had decided to sleep a little longer while she tryed to wake up this girl. " What's your name?" Golden asked.
" Huh? Oh, my name's Sky Lane. I'm from LEGO Universe. Where am I?" Sky picked herself up as if coming back to reality again.
" Ah, you're in a world called, Earth." Golden smiled.
" Okay?" Sky tilted her head confused.
