" I won't let you get away with this!" Vanda frowned.
Zyea's head dropped, looking at the floor covered with water.
" Even though Duke Exeter is not here, you still aren't going to make a mess and ruin minifigures from going to their beds after a long day. Why did Duke even let you become an employee?" Vanda sighed and walked away.


ZooNinja sat silently on the plastic chair writing away notes in his journal, waiting for his time to come. He had thought up every idea he could think of on how to get to this mysterious place that lots of minifigures had already disapeared to. He frowned.
" Hey Zoo!"
" What do you want this time." Zoo groaned in his seat.
" This time, I have come to tell you that I want freedom!"
" What? You know that may never happen, dino ninja." Zoo's tired face fell. He needed some sleep.
" Ever since I met you yesterday... there was something about you and me that made us alike somehow." dino ninja smiled.
" And what would that be?" Zoo mumbled, bored.
" Well, wanting to explore! Wanting to go to this new world that everybody is disapearing to!" dino ninja's voice had hope.
" Now, you've caught my attention!" Zoo smiled, leaning away from his chair, his arms up against his knees.


Each tree had a sliver of light illuminated between the branches, making the forest look a little misty.
" Duster, I hope we find them!" Grand yelled.
" I do too!" Duster yelled back.
Minutes turned into hours, still with little hope left, Grand and Duster still manged to keep running.
" Maybe they're hiding, or maybe we passed them, or maybe the maelstrom took them." Grand began to get emotional toward his lost friends.
" Oh, it's all right, Grand. We'll find them. I know we will. We just must not give up hope!" Duster looked over to his sad friend. We'll find them buddy, he thought. They must!


Handsome's legs shaked with worry. Hopefully, the maelstrom hadn't caught those guys yet. That would be horrible, if that really did happen, it would have ruined his day and make it a disaster. Even with all those worries, he still ran like the wind. He and his friends were going to find them and if the maelstrom took them, Handsome made a snarl, they were going to make sure those ugly purple monsters were going down!


Duke sighed. Another day. He couldn't wait to get to Earth. He had to see Handsome and Golden! Chris Man had set up camp near the entrance to the middle of this dark world and Duke was nearly itching out of his plastic pants to go down there!

Zyea had decided what he was going to do. He was going to put this entier employee outfit in the hands of Vanda Darkflame. He knew it wasn't good to have a leader think him as the bad side of her. He planned to do it this morning.
Where's Vanda? Zyea had been looking for her for what seemed like hours! " Hey, do you know where Darkflame is?"
" Uh...no." The guard had a smirk on his face.
" You know more than that! Why do you have a smrik on your face? Tell me. This is important!" Zyea yelled at the guard, his anger rising.
The guard ignored him. " Hello, sir. Welcome to Paradox. I hope you're having a luxurious visit!" The guard smiled.
" What is wrong with you?!" Zyea screamed.
" Problem, no do I not have." The guard smiled.
" Stop acting like minfigure Yoda!" Zyea was getting really frustrated.
" I'm sorry, young one, but I will not be able to help you answer your question." The guard then positioned himself to look straight ahead of him, not even glancing at Zyea any more.
" Well, you weren't much of a help." Zyea munmbled. This was going to be a long day...
