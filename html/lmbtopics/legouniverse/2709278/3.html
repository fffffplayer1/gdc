Chapter 5

" You must learn your talent, not by me, not by friends, but learn by yourself so you will become the hero you were meant to be." -Handsome Exeter
Destination: Earth


Handsome looked at the red ninja named, Kai. He wished he had an outfit like him. Red, blazing ninja swords! Man, that would be so cool! Handsome still felt bad about bringing all these people into the Earth world. His sister, Golden seemed pretty upset about him causing trouble all the time. Even though they were twins, he sometimes felt that Golden was way older than him. He felt childish around her, and she, he guessed, felt more like the grown-up. The one who saved the day, before her brother, in this case, he , Handsome seemed to be the one she must stop before he gets the two of them into trouble, but she always told him not to do something when it was to late. She tryed to save the world when the trouble had already happened. To be a hero, you must always be prepared, tell others not to do the things that get people in trouble before they do it.That was the lesson Golden had to learn before becoming a hero at all. She always knew the answers, and to tell it to late every time. She has to learn herself, Handsome thought. This was her tanlent, figuring things out.

Golden turned around and looked at her brother. What was he thinking in that smart brain of his. He always seemed to be the one, the smart one. He thought quickly and said witty comments that not even Golden, herself could do. She wanted to be the smart one though, the one who thinks the fastest, quickest, and always comes up with the best ideas. She always told people, including her brother and father things when it was to late, to late to tell. Like when she didn't want her brother to push the button for the LEGO Universe software, but told him he shouldn't have even though he had already clicked it and it was to late. She was worried, worried that her answers were wrong, even though they were always right. Why was she so afraid to tell people how she felt? This never accured to her before, but why was she thnking of this now, and not years back? Things were beginning to get really strange.


"Mam, there's nobody here." A stromling mech was standing straight up, after hours of standing.
" I suppose you're right. Let's get moving meches, spiderlings, stromlings. Come on, let's start walking out that door over there." It was the spiderqueen, getting her soliders ready for battle. It wouldn't be long before they found someone to attack. . .

" Epsilon Starcracker!" Wisp Lee yelled from where he was sitting.
" Lee? Here, I coming down!" Epsilon yelled using his jetpack to move toward the direction of Wisp Lee. As Starcracker landed, Lee couldn't help but become a little jealous since Epsilon had a jetpack to help him move around. Epsilon gently landed on the ground, still standing, he walked over to Lee and helped him up.
"What?! How can you walk on this land?" Wisp Lee was shocked.
" Oh, I practiced. This jetpack did come in handy. It even seemed to help me get used to the climate around here." Epsilon, a show off was now touching his jetpack like it was the most prized possession anybody could get. Lee then understood quickly. Epsilon was the first to get here. He should know more about this world then even he knew. Epsilon then showed him the steps to learning how to walk on this climate and he too began to walk nicely. " We're probably not the only one's here. Come on, let's go find the others." Epsilon started walking, followed by Lee who was walking a little bit more slowly since he was a beginner.
" Hey, is that Melodie and Kai? And who are those kids that are hanging out with them?" Lee started to get excited since more minifigures from LEGO Universe were here.
" Yeah, you're right. Come on!" Epsilon began running over to the group of friends, leaving Lee on his own since Epsilon didn't teach him how to run yet.

" Melodie, Kai, hey guys!" Epsilon yelled running over to them.
" Epsilon Starcracker? I can't believe it! It's really you! And Wisp Lee is with you, great!" Melodie smiled.
" Who are you?" Golden's head tilted, confused.
" I am Epsilon Starcracker and this is Wisp Lee." Epsilon introducing himself and Wisp Lee. " And who must the two of you be?"
" Golden, and this is Handsome." Golden smiled at Epsilon.
" Your names sound familer. I just can't recall it." Epsilon looked at the two of them with questioned looks.
" Uh. . . am I invisible or something?" Wisp glanced at the group of cheerful people.
" No, you're visible, I can tell you that." Golden looked at Wisp. His outfit was so strange. Mostly purple. Purple shirt, purple pants, purple everything!
" Should we celebrate this happpy reunion somethime later? Those purple monsters are coming this way!" Golden yelled, running away from the group.
What were they going to do? There were so many stromlings, meches, and spiderlings crawling this way! Oh, no, Golden thought. We are doomed.

Destination: Nexus Tower


"Duke Exeter!" Dr. Overbuild yelled.
Duke walked slowly over to Overbuild, deep in thought. " Hey." Duke looked up to see Overbuild watching him.
" Duke, what's wrong. You seem a little down. Your're usually not like this. Come on, tell me, what's the matter?" Overbuild waited for Duke to speak.
" I can't. My problems are my deal." Duke then looked at the ground again. Overbuild couldn't believe it. Duke would always be the one to lead them into battle, and here he was now acting not honorable, but sad and depressed. Duke told him everything. Especially when it came to him telling him his problems in life, but this time Duke didn't. They were best buds! Always in things together. Oh, well. All the leaders were having a group meeting about the missing minifigures tonight at midnight, so maybe Duke would tell him later when the meeting starts. He began to walk away, seeing that Duke wasn't going to be talking to him at the moment.
" Wait! Overbuild, I'm sorry about everything. It's just that this problem of mine, it just seems to be a problem that I must fix by myself. We do everything together, I just think this problem is my problem. I promise, after the top secret meeting, we'll have a long friendly chat since we haven't done one in a while." Duke's pale face looked a little brighter, so Overbuild agreed to the chat. Then they went on their way, Duke to his chambers, and Overbuild to his science lab. He just hoped the friendly chat would bring them together again. It would be nice to be close friends with him since they seemed to be falling out.


" You're right, Golden! Trouble is coming!" Melodie yelled, swinging her two ice daggers around and around in her hands.
" Everybody get prepared. Golden, Handsome, stay in the back since you have no defense." Epsilon yelled getting everybody ready for battle, with him and Melodie in the front, followed by Wisp Lee in the center row and the thirteen year old twins in the back. "Get ready to attack the SpiderQueen! Melodie, I remember, you're afraid of spiders so stay behind me!" Epsilon joked.
" Epsilon, I used to be afraid of spiders, but all that smashing makes a girl feel powerful!" Melodie smiled proudly.
" Yeah, yeah, yeah, I get the point. You're tryng to be a show-off." Epsilon mumbled.
" Hey, everybody gets their glory every once in a while." Melodie defended herself.
" The SpiderQueen is almost here! Guys get ready! Remember, she's not the only one we must smash, also her army of stromlings, meches and also spiderlings!" Epsilon yelled, shooting his rifle at the SpiderQueen. Epsilon was a great shot, one of the best Space Rangers around, so it was very obvious that he would have a great target at the SpiderQueen before she made her laser shoots. " WOW! You're such a good shot that you practicly shot one of her arms and made it fall to the floor!" Melodie was shocked, and also laughing.
" Melodie, why are you laughing? The SpiderQueen just grew her arm back, and she's looking more powerful before. Even her army looks more powerful!" Wisp Lee yelled at Melodie causing her to stop laughing.
" What?! She's imortal? That's impossible!" Golden felt as if she was going to fall to the ground with disguse. She couldn't bare watching the SpiderQueen any longer. The SpiderQueen was also so ugly!

The SpiderQueen had lunged forward. Melodie striking one of her ice daggers at the big creature had caused her to stop a moment from the pain of the sharp blade. Still, the spider only stopped a moment before crawling forward, toward the group, her army right behind her. A blow to the right, a blow to the left. Nothin could stop the SpideQueen from moving. Meches started shooting, causing the team to move further down into the dark forest. Stromlings began running over to the group, with their fast running feet. " You can't get away from us! Why don't you just retreat? We'll beat you all anyway." A stromling teased the team of four. Kai had disapeared somewhere the night before the battle, and no one knew where he went.
" Where did you take Kai?!" Golden yelled.
" Where did we take Kai? Well, I'm sure you'll be suprised! He's one of us now! We've already infected him! You should be glad we showed mercy ro you all and didn't infect all of you instead!" The SpiderQueen laughed a mean laugh, then she moved her spider body, to show another spider that looked just like her, but then the spider changed form and became a ninja again. His skin and also his eyes and hair were all purple.
" NO!" Melodie yelled.
" Hello, old friends!" Kai smiled. Then, he became an enormous spider, even bigger than the SpiderQueen herself, then he charged at the group, spitting out huge laser balls at them. Melodie screamed and cryed, falling to the floor. Epsilon had to drag her across the ground in order for her to move. They were going to lose, even though now, Kai was the only one fighting them or you could call him, Infected- Kai. He was even more ugly than the SpiderQueen. He even looked stronger and more powerful. Golden was also screaming. She could feel the hot drops of water fall down her cheek. Even Handsome was crying. They were all running for their life, watching sadly, the Infected- Kai make them fall. It was either him infecting them or escaping the horror that awaited them. That was their only choice. Golden guessed the answer to their problem now was, escaping the terrible picture.
Melodie cryed. She cryed for Kai and she cryed because it was their dear friend that was trying to kill them. Oh how she wished none of this was happening, and why was Handsome clutching this heavy silver thing in his hand? Why was she asking all these questions in her mind, then she remembered that Epsilon was dragging her. She realized Kai was getting closer to the two of them since Epsilon was helping her. She pushed Epsilon's hand away from her shirt, and they began running for dear life!
