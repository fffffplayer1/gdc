Chapter 18

Argon slowly blinked his eyes. His vision was blurry, his head throbbed with pain, and everytime he moved in felt like fire burst to life inside him.

Argon forced himself up and looked around, he saw Super slowly getting up as well. Super and Argon both stood up and looked around. They were in the lauchpad area of Avant Gardens.

"Ohhh my head...what the brick happened?" said Super. "I don't..." Argon felt something in his armor, he pulled out the note and found Swift's writing on it.

"What's that?" asked Super. Argon looked at the note and his face went pale. He fell to his knees and screamed at the top of his lungs. "Its all my fault!! NOOOOOOO!!!!" he sobbed and beat the ground. Super picked up the note and read it. He dropped it and walked over to Argon, tears filling his eyes.

"Argon...don't you dare say anything else. It WASN'T your fault. I don't know what else we can do..." said Super. Argon stood up and said, "I'll tell you what were going to do, we are going to find Swift end this nightmare...once and for all!" Super nodded and both Sentinels got their rockets in the lauchpad and blasted off.

The note from Swift read the following:

"I'M CORRUPTED. YOU MUST FIND ME, BEFORE I CHANGE.
SWIFT"

The End
