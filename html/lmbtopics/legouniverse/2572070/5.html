Chapter 16

"Where is Argon...he's been gone for hours!" said Super. "I know...he should have been here a long time ago, I hope he is okay," said Swift.

"You never know, something might have come up. I bet he'll be back in the morning." said Redgie. Swift looked out the room window of Nexus Tower that showed a huge view of Crux Prime. As he got ready for bed, he knew he wasn't going to sleep.

Worry soon corrupted the Sentinels. Every time sleep crept into their aching bodies and minds, pictures and images of a smashed Argon flooded into their minds, jolting them awake. Even Redgie was worried.

When morning finally came there was still no sign of Argon. "That's it. We're going to look for him." said Swift. The others agreed, so they suited up and blast off to Crux Prime.

When they arrived, they saw a strange purple lightning next to the launch pad. "Whoa! I never thought I'd see one of them before! Cool!" announced Redgie. "What is it?" asked Swift.

"It's a maelstrom portal. They're super rare! Inside them are some of the hardest maelstrom generals. If we go inside, we might find Argon." explained Redgie.

"If it means finding Argon, then I'm in!" said Super. He jumped into the portal. Then the other two Sentinels jumped in. It was like stepping through a lightning storm. A maelstrom infected lightning storm! As they were sent hurtling through a purple universe of corruption, they were hit often by purple lightning.

When they finally landed, they saw a place similiar to Crux Prime, but with a lot more maelstrom! The maelstrom was everywhere the Sentinels looked. Swift turned and looked forward and found Argon sitting in the middle of all the maelstrom. An evil smile possesed the face of Argon...he wasn't pure! He was infected!

"No Argon you----" Super was cut off when Redgie hit him and shot him with his Blaster. Super was knocked back from the impact and lay motionless. Redgie turned to Swift and hit him square in the jaw. He launched his jet pack and landed towards Argon. "Good work, Argon." said Redgie. He turned and looked towards a dark corner. "Your majesty." said Redgie.

Swift slowly looked up and saw the Darkitect walking forward. "Redgie! My number one spy, I see you have brought me a present!" said the Darkitect. His voice was smooth and calm but corrupted with chaos and disorder. Redgie reached inside his back pack and pulled out his Shard of Imagination. Argon then pulled his out and they both gave it to the Darkitect. A stromling saw what was happening and took Super's Imagination Shard as well.

"Excellent! Three down and only one more to go! Then I can create a new army of maelstrom and destroy the Nexus Force for good! Now...kill the Knight!" screamed the Darkitect. He reached up his hand and a strange light shot forth from it. The light landed on Super and he got up. Super then turned to face Swift. He was corrupted.

Swift was helpless. He looked around and found that he was surrounded suddenly by Super, Argon and Redgie. His friends...his brothers. Tears stained his eyes and looked towards his friends. He loudly announced, "For imagination...for the Nexus Force...for you!!!!" He unsheathed his sword and prepared to fight his brothers.



Hope everyone liked it!
