Maniac stood on next to a tree, near brig rock. He looked at the Ninja, preparing for the piece of information about his parents.
"The pirates have them."
"What? You said they were weaker and could not do anything, and they have my parents."
"They....are weak. I believe they don't know that your coming."
Maniac punched the tree in anger.
"YOU SHOULD'VE TOLD ME BEFORE! HOW AM I SUPPOSED TO FIGHT AN ARMY OF PIRATES?"
"They are weak. You are strong....destroy them...."
With that, the Ninja flipped away into the forest.

Maniac chased after him.
"YOU CAN HELP ME!" He yelled after the Ninja.
The Ninja didn't reply. Maniac stopped, and looked around. He realized he was lost.
He turned back in the direction he had come from, seething in anger. Where were his parents? They could be anywhere where there are pirates.....
"You hear the Ninjas broke out?" said a voice.
"Ahr! It be mad!" said a deeper voice.
"You know Brickbeards furious at you?"
"Ahr, but I be angry at him too, he be sayin' I let the Ninjas out! I better be guarding the other prisoners, they are more valuable I hear."

Maniac stood for a second...'The other prisoners..'......his parents! He looked out from the trees, and saw a pirate walking away. But who had the other person been? Wasn't a pirate...
Maniac began walking towards the pirate. The pirate walked towards a stone wall. Maniac stood there, confused, and the pirate pushed the wall. It opened. The pirate walked inside, and the door closed. Maniac ran over, and stopped. Weren't the pirates not smart enough to invent a door like that? He stood there pondering for a few seconds too long.
