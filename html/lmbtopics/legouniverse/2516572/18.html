I've decided I need to end this story. It has almost been a year since I first made it, and it hasn't gotten anywhere. One of the main problems was that I was focusing on getting a chapter posted than making it funny, as I said in the post above.
If you liked this story though, don't be too disappointed, as this isn't really the end. I am going to start a re-write, with more humor, and better written. It will most likely be posted around the same time that this one was last year, around the new year.
Something better now. If you can't wait the two months or so until this story gets reposted, I am turning this topic into a contest! One of the great things about the Nightfall Incident is the question "What would you do if the Maelstrom started to take over in the game? How would you survive the Nightfall Incident?'
Write a short story about what you would do if you were in the Nightfall Incident! Would you run as far away as possible? Would you stay to protect players who didn't know what to do? Would you log off LU and shut down your computer? This can be either in the Original Formic version of the story, or if you feel like making it a comedy, you can do it in my version. The choice is yours!
