How did I escape from the Maelstrom with malfunctioning gear? Like this:

Stromlings were everywhere.
For a moment, I couldn't believe it.
"No. This must be a glich. Or a Mythran party. Or-"
I didn't have time to finish thinking, as a Stromling Admiral Elite (a "Battle of Nimbus Station" exclusive enemy) ran straight towards me. For a second I panicked. I pulled out my Fantastic Pilum and smashed it with ease. Then I quickly jumped up onto the "safe zone" of Nimbus Plaza that was so popular in BoNS. Remembering that I had just bought Rank 4 Sorcerer gear, I put it on. I expected to look awesome, like the most powerful wizard ever.
I was so wrong.
I looked reminiscent of the "Good Wizard" set from the old Castle sets. My wand had transformed into a fish on a stick. I didn't even have a Sorcerer Orb.
Apparently, Stromling Mech Invaders just happened to decide to spawn at that moment, and the path to Red Blocks and the LEGO Club was literally clogged with Dark Spiderlings, and with them all shooting at me at the same time, there was only one thing to do.
I double jumped over the Mechs, ate a Quicksicle, and ran towards the Avant Gardens Launch Pad. I barely made it before the Maelstrom Horsemen began spawning.
When I got the Avant Gardens, I could tell something was definitely wrong with the game. The Launch Area was swarming with Stromlings, Stromling Mechs, Dark Spiderlings, Chainsaw Stromlings, and Hammer Stromlings.
I quickly ran to the Property launch pad and rocketed to Block Yard.
Much to my relief, the property was empty, as usual.
I sat back in my chair for a moment and thought.
"What's going on here? Why is the Maelstrom everywhere except properties?"
I had spoken too soon.
The Spider Queen had just returned.
