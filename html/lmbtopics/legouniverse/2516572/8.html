We edged around, trying to avoid being spotted by the figure. It was probably a Mythran, and we didn't want to be caught, and possibly muted. We were in a cave like tunnel area, and his name was obscured by a rock. Whoever he was, he was strolling along like he didn't have a care in the world. I couldn't take it any longer. I jumped out.
"Hey, what are you doing here?" Luckily, it was just another player. His name was ProphetOfDoom.
"Well, I could ask you the same thing", I replied.
"Just looking through the glitch world. I was on the MB's, and found a rumour of a Mythran meeting place somewhere around here. And guess what, I found it." He pointed to a mountain-like area ahead down the tunnel from which he had come. Near the top, I could see a swirling, blue light.
"Come on, want to have a look?" Prophet asked.
"Okay, I guess" I replied. But I wasn't so sure. I had a feeling that the name Prophet Of Doom would end up being correct.

Prophet and I continued up the slope alone. The rest of my group had gone back, tired of the glitch world, and some of the younger ones had to go to bed. So in the end, it was just the two of us, walking up the steep side, which was littered with a bunch of large boulders, which gave the mountain the look of a volcano, at least one spitting out blue imagination from the top.
A sudden movement out of the corner of my eye caught my glance, and I turned around, and saw we were being followed. The person was hunched over low, scurrying between boulder and boulder, trying to avoid detection. Very mysterious.
I turned to Prophet. "Hey, did you see that weird person who is following us?"
He turned around, and surveyed the lands behind us. It was an imposing sight, the black rocks littering the side we'd just ascended, and in the distance, Ninjago Monastery looming in a sea of skeletons. But there was no sign of anyone else.
I shrugged. "Guess it was my imagination. You know I how much of that I have."
We continued on. I still felt like we were being watched. Suddenly, I saw the figure again, stooped over a boulder in front of us. He was about to ambush us. But before I could move, or alert Prophet, he pounced.
