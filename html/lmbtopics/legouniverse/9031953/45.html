turtle3112 wrote:

hi everybody have never been on roleplay yet so could you guys and gals please help me out in bionicle roleplay

yes! First step is to chose what side you want (Toa, Makuta) second, you want to post a bio of your RPC (roleplay character). Heres a bio of my RPC to give you an idea...

 

 

Name: Kolak.

Mask: Rode, the mask of truth.

Element: Ice.

Weapons: Sword. Shield with kanoka disk launcher.

Side: Toa.

 

 

When you begin an actual battle, it is allowed to post something simple like, "I attack you". But to make thing more dramatic, we do things in a way like, "I bring my sword crashing down upon the Makuta, Hoping he could not defend against it."

 

Sometimes we add words to add even more drama to the battle, like, "A mere sword? One small sword could not take down a makuta! fool!"  and replys like, "Ah, but this sword is protosteel! The only known substance to cut Makuta armor!"

 

 

Have fun!  (or... are there any questions?)
