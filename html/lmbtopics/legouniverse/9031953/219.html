Sadly, after over two years of waging war, I have decided to finally leave the roleplay. I have, over the past half a year, grown tired of battling here. I have tried to hold it off as long as I can, however, considering the state the Roleplay is in, if I wait to actually have my characters leave IC I may never leave. Currently, the roleplay sadly feels like more of a drain to me than anything, and it's probably for the best that I retire from it. I'll still probably be lurking around the LMB's and may even return in the future, but for now, I am waving goodbye to the place where I first roleplayed and my first real social media (lolz).

 

 

@Brickme: I don't think it can be overstated how much you affected my roleplaying style in the beginning. You incited me, indirectly and probably without intending to, on constructing an army of my own. The wars we had and the preparation beforehand, before the whole year long storyline took its course, I will never forget. They were the highlight and peak of my time here, even if they were the most stressful.

 

@ARNN: You waited patiently all those years for command of Xia, and now you have it. Ye have proved more than worthy of your post.

 

@Keplers: You have been the RP Rep for almost as long as I have been here, and for most of that time you have done fabulously. Granted, you may or may not have made some mistakes(), but so do we all. I couldn't have asked for a better Rep.

 

@Nicholas: Your Personas are flat out awesome, and it's kind of cool to watch their adventures unfold. (that is, until Voidwalker decided to attack Atero Nui, seriously?).

 

@Mrcqm: You're RPing skills have improved since you've joined, as well as your maturity. You have given me some serious laughs, both in and out of the Roleplay, and I hope to get more in the future.

 

TBC...

Rest in peace, Ye Bionicle Roleplay of Olde, you will forever remain in my memory.
