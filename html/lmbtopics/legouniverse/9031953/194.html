Xian forces have been recently firing blasts of slow and stasis, and some of their forces have been shown to possess control over sonics. I can't speak for others in the rolpelay, but for my part, I tended to rationalize my own use of varied powers by simply deciding that the powers contained in kanoka, or krataa energies could all be analyzed and replicated by scientific engineers, and then stored for future use...I don't know how others have justified it, but that's how I've done it. 

Before I answer your question, I just want to say that I have changed my mind somewhat as to main character fannon abilities.
When I first joined, we had an understanding that your main character could possess the natural abilities and powers granted by its species (Toa, and with limitations Makuta), and that you could also pick one other ability for your character to be specially good at. We had a few rules, such as Toa could have up to three masks, and Makuta could have one. We also limited the strength of Makuta characters by acknowledging that, while a Makuta would have acess to all krataa powers, he would only be particularly skilled with a few of those powers (One character, for example, speciallized in chain lightning, and was particularly strong). I don't think we need to entirely eliminate sepcial attributes, but we need to keep them chained to cannon, and only allow one such attribute. Example of acceptable special attributes would be Karunax's super strong armor, a character's great strength, or a characters' abiliity to be very quick, and fast.  The fannon issue that I am most concerned about, is fannon tech in armies.
