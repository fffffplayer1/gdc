Hey, I made these guidelines.

 

  1. If you can logically defend it and prove it isn't OP, there's a chance it isn't.
  2. I was attempting to prevent "I pancake you with gravity" scenarios closely followed by "I am completely unharmed by the gravity and destroy you with Magnetism."
  3. I've seen Vahi-like/super-Vahi profiles posted in the topic.
  4. At the time I was only thing of Kanohi powers that give you the option to use any Element you want, etc.
  5. No single character should need to destroy a building in one shot. Karunax doesn't even do that.
  6. Even Macedonia was breaking it.
  7. *Coughcough* Gurthang. I was trying to prevent another doomsday plot that requires Karunax, Charix, and Vortaxx working together to dent the villain.
  8. I will look to 90% if the roleplay. They are why I made these rules, and it was thinking of very specific scenarios when I made these so be to prevent them from repeating.

My thoughts:

 

1. Something I've noticed: OP seems to quickly be met with OP.

2. This is why I very rarely use the Makuta power of gravity except in tactical and/or defensive situations. Therefore, I agree in that respect. But I still think AD has a point when it comes to Toa of Gravity.

3. Legendary Kanohi are to remain Legendary Kanohi. There is nothing like them in existence.

4. I have this strong opinion where I think only canon Kanohi should be allowed, but I doubt that will ever happen.

5. I don't see how he could without using multiple moves and/or unleashing insane amounts of power, effectively draining himself. If he punched or simply barreled through a building, chances are he'll run through it so cleanly that it won't come down unless he hit a structural asset or it was already severely weakened. Now, if it was a Matoran hut... yeah, he could bring it down with his pinkie finger.

6. I'm staying out of this one.

7. Agreed. This isn't HF RP, nor do I ever want it to be.

8. Meh, not much to add here.

The new representative and the new guidelines are set. The Mods have made these changes in order to help turn this roleplay around.
