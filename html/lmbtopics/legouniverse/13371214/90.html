"Leka, this is Krakarda, the makuta who transmitted the previous message. I know you from the reports of when Destral tried the rescue-to-protection route. You're a clever, intelligent matoran... If Mata-Nui set a matoran's Destiny in motion, then why would he intentionally create rivals? ...unless her life's purpose was to stop them if they went too far.
