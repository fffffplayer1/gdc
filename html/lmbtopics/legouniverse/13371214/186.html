- It can make anyone within 50 feet unable to access any of their powers, whether it be mask, disk (probably), natural, or other.  If masks are switched out the wearer is still unable to use them, as it is not the mask that is powerless, but the wearer.  This is not automatic, as the user of the gauntlet must decide to take a being's powers.

 

- It can block the effects of any powers within it's 50 foot radius and removes beings' powers for the entire time they are within the radius.  Unless the user of the gauntlet decided to remove their powers, once they leave the radius they can access their powers again.
