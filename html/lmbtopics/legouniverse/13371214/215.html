He waited until all the eyes were turned towards him, then continued, "Today, let us rejoice! I bring wonderful news! For millenia, I have worked towards a certain cause. Now, those of you gathered here will be the first to witness the fruits of my labor. I call it, 'the Conquest.' I hope you enjoy it!"

 

Almost immediately, several thousand mechanical soldiers seem to materialize in the outskirts outside Atero Nui, completely surrounding the city. Moving in complete synchronization, they charged towards the city walls. In their hands, large, futuristic blasters glowed a bright blue as they bombarded anything that moved.

 

"Unfortunately," he conceded, "a side-effect of this demonstration is your destruction, but that's a sacrifice I'm willing to make."

"Oh boy, more crazies," Marnd comments as he, Ignu, and Thrax run to confront the new threat. Marnd hurls a wall of stone at the robits while Ignu and Thrax snipe a few.

In the blink of an eye, Deviox is suddenly right above their heads. He throws down a small, circular device that explodes in their midst, sending them flying into the nearby walls, interrupting their attack, then follows it up by bombarding them with his twin blasters, firing with pinpoint accuracy.

 

The wall of stone is quickly obliterated by Voidarix, who is leading the charge, with a simple blast of energy, then the rubble from it is used to create several golems that fall in line with the other soldiers.
