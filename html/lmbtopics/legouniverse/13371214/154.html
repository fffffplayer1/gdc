The instant the Domain hits Sonucus, he almost goes Nova. As it is, a huge wave of SOnics spills out, devastating a few buildings and likely deafening anyone near him. Quickly bringing himself under some semblance of control, he switches to his Mask of Adaptation.

A couple seconds later, his Mask has made him able to dodge nearly anything fired at him as it restructures his neural pathways to block out the Domain's psychological effects.


 

I... I cannot...

 

*So long. It was so long ago. So many minds before his... but he still remembers. At least, he does now. It was many eons and many faces ago when he first experienced the cruel bite of real agony. Yet here it is again. Only this time, it is a different kind of pain. Back then, it was the sting of insurmountable sorrow and bitterness that made him dig out his emotions. But now, it is the shocking weight of his philosophy's denial that threatens to crush his mind to powder. He begins to question.*

 

Will... not...

 

*No. He will not deny himself. He will not deny the absolution of the Void. He will not surrender.*

 

M u s t . . .   n o t . . .

 

N  e  v  e  r     a  g  a  i  n  .  .  .

 

*His large hands suddenly lurch open, their long bony fingers outstretched, and the eyes on the palms glowing with an almost blackened shade of purple. The dark glow pulses once from the eyes, and every single one of The Voidwalker's opponents are immediately thrust into the Domain.

 

Everyone is instantly greeted by a brief crushing impact from all sides, followed just as quickly by every other ill effect of the Domain. There is no rift sphere... each combatant is now like The Voidwalker: contained inside the Domain. Only for you guys, there is no way out.

 

TV attacks wildly, rapidly materializing and launching massive stakes of Hardlight at all of you from every direction, not unlike one of those bullet hell games.*

Garadur uses dodging to avoid all Hardlight he then shifts to a mask of accuacy and fires a empty zamor at the Voidwalker.

The Old Bio RP has been struck down.....

We have been set free.

Revenge will be OURS!!!!
