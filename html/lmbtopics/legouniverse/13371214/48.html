Fesur hears Nartyez - and merges the two himself, ripping a hole in each set of armour, sending the two flying together so that the holes attach and remolding the armour into one cohesive whole.  He decides that he will use Nartyez's armour as the inspiration for this new being's appearance in the hope of influencing his character.

A strange scream errupts from both Nartyez and Zeytran. Their limbs start molding together forcefully, becoming once again whole.  Flashes of Light and Shadow errupt. Just before the fusion is finished, they seperate once more, both bodies slamming against different buildings as a Greyish color errupts. Something stands inbetween them, bubbling and frothing. The grey gel bubbles into a new form. Duka, the Spirit of Twilight, Dawn and Dusk has been born.

 

The frothing grows, as the creature takes the form of a Grim Reaper-Angel fusion like creature. The creature screams."You cannot merge once more! I will not allow this! You ask who I am? I am Duka. I am the Spirit of Twilight. I am the Spirit of Dawn. I am also the Spirit of Dusk. When Kraka and Avka split, I am the remains. To reunite them would destroy me, and that...I cannot allow. You would risk the destruction of the universe itself! Nay! The entire cosmos! I have wielded the Kanohi Ignika, and I have my curse! I can see the future! The universe will fracture! They are awake! The Ignika's destruction has opened the first barrier! The Attack on Atero Nui as well as Mata Nui's slumber has opened the second barrier! Should you reunite us, then the Third and Final barrier will open! There will be no stopping them! There is a REASON why Mata Nui and the Great Beings worked in conjunction with Arthaka, Karzahni and Tren Krom to imprison them! THEY WILL DESTROY US ALL!!!" Duka says screaming.

We have been betrayed. Our trust, misguided. Our faith, put wrongly. Have they no shame? But we will live on, my brothers. We will strive forward, we will endure. We will ascend. Free of limitations, free of fear.
