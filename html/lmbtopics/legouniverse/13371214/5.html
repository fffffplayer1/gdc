Stumbling from various corners across the city, ragged and worn out Toa trudge. Their armors are layered in grime and dirt, cracked armor and other various wounds. Kratella's mask is missing although several chunks of a metal that look like Kanohi fragments are in her face and fingers, several chunks of her armor almost...bitten out from the looks of it. She screams loudly as she trips over a rock at the start of the gathering of of the Toa Mangai. Tahlenarix collapses behind her, looking rather grey.

Nearby, a sickening crunch is heard followed by a sharp wail. Toa Konalia had fallen, and her friend Botanara could only scream in pure and utter horror as her friend was forced to freeze herself in a block of ice to prevent worse injuries. What's worse? A venemous, dangerous Rahi was inside there with her. Even more were coming, after her. Botanara had felt the least out of place with the dirt and soil in her armor, she was a Toa of Plantlife, it's natural for her. She tried to help Konalia with the feeling.

Elsewhere, a small cluster of more Toa stood, farther away from Botanara and Konalia. Fenoraknh was almost completely catatonic from his Kanohi missing, his entire left arm was missing and he had sustained several injuries. Batoraxa, Fatronis and Potran suffered the same level of wounds, they just had Kanohi. Cracked Kanohi, but functional in letting them function. Galitrexa and Levonirax were working with Sukonarax, Deytanira and Cetalia to hold off the hoard of venomous Rahi.

The only ones missing were Matrixa, Voitaria and Onurakna.

OOC: So, yeah. Stuff's boring. Need to bring these babies back. If any of you can guess what these Rahi are based off of, you get a cookie! Doubt you'll get it though..

Hellooo?

We have been betrayed. Our trust, misguided. Our faith, put wrongly. Have they no shame? But we will live on, my brothers. We will strive forward, we will endure. We will ascend. Free of limitations, free of fear.
