*Amra watches from a spot, darkened with shade in such a way that he is hidden, but not suciously should anyone spot him. He sighs and looks at his sword*

 

"What's a Toa like you, pretending to be an outcast?"

 

*Amra turns see who spoke, finding a Vorox like fellow coming from behind him*

 

"It's....it's complicated. Who are you?"

 

"A real outcast--the name's Feand."

 

"Amra."

 

"So Amra, why are you hidding? Why are you even considering using that sword? If someone is to do either of those things, it's the monster they claim me to be."

 

*Amra looks back at the sword, looking at himself in the reflection*

 

"My past, my purpose, whatever that was, is lost to me. All I ever wanted to do, all I ever wanted to know was carving tools and statues. Now I can only carve wounds into enemies of the Matoran. But they no longer need me, I have no purpose. So why live?"

 

"....I remember when I was teaching Tredixs how to fight. During one of our breaks, he asked me how I went on living without having been made with a purpose. I had one, still do, but I told him that's the crazy thing about destiny, it changes. You thought it was carving, you though it was protecting the Matoran, and it might have been those things at one time. But now you have a new destiny, a new goal to complete."

 

"What goal could that possibly be?

 

"If I had to guess? Probably the same as Tredixs is currently doing."

 

"But, he's..."

 

"Nope. He's alive, but where? Now that's what we have to find out...."
