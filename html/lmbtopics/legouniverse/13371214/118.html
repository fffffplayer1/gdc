Cold Steel, watching the sphere expand, came to a decision. Rocketing into the sphere, he planned on using his powers over  technology to simply break the Voidwalker. As advanced as the entity was, was he not nothing more than a machine to be mastered? However, this turns out nothing like he expected and he is forced to use a considerable amount of his power just to keep his body together within the swirling sphere of doom. It doesn't take him long to realize that he will never accomplish anything in this manner and, seeing the wisps that are all that is left of Leka and Crystalia (maybe lol), he has an idea. Giving up control of his body, it and his physical mind are blown away into nothingnees, leaving only his soul, his least used, most powerful, and as of yet untested weapon. Even his soul could feel the agony, the torrent of forces raging in the sphere, but his spirit was, while still developing, still far superior to nearly all other wills on the planet. For not only was it powerful, it also evolved, corrupting and consuming all that challenged it. However, Cold Steel only had begun to know this, he had yet to test it... or find it's limits.

However, this turns out nothing like he expected and he is forced to use a considerable amount of his power just to keep his body together within the swirling sphere of doom. It doesn't take him long to realize that he will never accomplish anything in this manner and, seeing the wisps that are all that is left of Leka and Crystalia (maybe lol), he has an idea. Giving up control of his body, it and his physical mind are blown away into nothingnees, leaving only his soul, his least used, most powerful, and as of yet untested weapon. Even his soul could feel the agony, the torrent of forces raging in the sphere, but his spirit was, while still developing, still far superior to nearly all other wills on the planet. For not only was it powerful, it also evolved, corrupting and consuming all that challenged it. However, Cold Steel only had begun to know this, he had yet to test it... or find it's limits.
