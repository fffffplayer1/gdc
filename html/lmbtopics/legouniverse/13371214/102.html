OOC: Considering all of The Voidwalker's special abilities in addition to the details of the standard-issue abilities available to every Persona, and considering my observations regarding this battle...

 

... I think I've fully realized just how unfair and one-sided everything about this is. TV has enough mental capacity to multitask against every opponent present, as well as reflexes that can challenge the speed of light (any fully-developed Persona can track the individual moving particles of a beam of light with their eyes), on top of a bunch of other things that add up real quick. 

 

While victory certainly isn't impossible for you guys, I fear that you'll drive your characters into certain destruction while simply trying to find a single weakness. And if I want to stay true to TV's character, I will have him take every available opportunity to obliterate his attackers as quickly and efficiently as possible. I really don't like the idea of random crutches used just for the sake of plot, which, while necessary at times, are things that I'm trying to remove as I progress my writing skills.

 

So after this, I think I will try much harder to refrain from future situations that force your characters into action against any Personas unless it's planned ahead and agreed upon. If I recall correctly, that was one of the terms I set up for myself in order to even have them in the RP.

 

And as for the ongoing battle, a few things can happen:

1. If you're already too annoyed, I can take TV away from here and give him reason not to come back, as was the original plan. 

2. I can "cheapen" the fight by giving you guys some heads-up and information along the way that may or may not influence metagaming on some level.

3. We can maybe plan out the battle's outcome, which might be more work than it's worth.

4. We can keep going the way it is. The risky, blunt, and brutal way, but the legitimate "all that the heroes have, versus all that TV has" way.
