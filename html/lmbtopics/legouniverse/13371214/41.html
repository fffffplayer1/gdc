If anyone possessed the ability to moniter energy scans, that person would suddenly notice that the whole area was all at once being scanned, thouroughly, and quickly.

 

Fifteen blurs suddenly slash across the sky, each moving to fast for identification. With slightly above super sonic speeds they split into three groups and ever so slightly dip towards the city, each group release an attack aimed at three of the most heavily equiped fortifications in or around the City. If their are no heavily equiped fortifications, then they will aim at the most concentrated groupings of Makuta forces. If their are no concentrated groupings of Makuta forces, then they will aim at all fighting groups of beings. 

 

Crash! The damage done by the attack is almost imemdiate. The projectiles fired off were to fast to be observed, but the damage is done. Whatever was targeted is smacked with a multitude of forceful and voilent explosions which possess the power to level fortified walls and release shockwaves that would throw unsecured beings and objects several yards away. Any beings or objects that are exposed to the attack find themselves unnaturally weaker as well.

 

The blurs which did the damage are gone, but they leave one other thing to remember them by. A shattering Boom is suddenly heard which pounds into eardrums, and causes every object to shiver with shockwaves.
