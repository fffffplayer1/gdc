"That's wrong, Zeytran. All...all wrong. If you truly...believed that, then. You wouldn't have hesitated to spare me. You...never would've trusted Inharax, you never would've been hurt when Inharax betrayed you...you never would've truly survived the dip in Energized Protodermis...nor would Mata Nui have blessed us!" He says, as tears stream down his Kanohi. "Zeytran, I know you cannot understand what I'm...saying now...you have utterly no moral light...let us merge once more! Become whole! It...it could work! We could be one again!" He tries as his Kanohi continues to wetten from tears.

 

A guttural roar is heard. "NO!!! No, Nartyez. We can NEVER become whole once more. I didn't hesitate to spare you, I merely wished to prolong the fun. I didn't trust Inharax! I was not hurt! That was all you! Pathetic Toa! Mata Nui did not bless ME, he blessed YOU. There's a difference, Toa Nartyez. Now....let's begin the true fun, yes?" A scream escapes Nartyez.

Fesur hears Nartyez - and merges the two himself, ripping a hole in each set of armour, sending the two flying together so that the holes attach and remolding the armour into one cohesive whole.  He decides that he will use Nartyez's armour as the inspiration for this new being's appearance in the hope of influencing his character.
