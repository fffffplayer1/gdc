LordBrocktree meandered on to his room, thinking on what Stigroy had said. There were, at that moment, hundreds of Mythrans all around him. It was slightly disturbing. He shuddered, and then thought of something else. As long as he was out here, he might as well see the names on the other 7 buildings. He look at each name plaque as he neared them

Cheerful Power Rover

Ace Magma Marvel

Mardor Aident

Sergeant Ghost Mustache

SeliaStarswift

Justice River

Luke Stormer

Brocktree recognized the names. All of the minifigures were once famous. He remembered that the other day he had seen Cheerful Power Rover awake. He wondered where he was now. It wouldn't hurt to try his room. He went up to it, and knocked on the door. His knock was greeted by, "Come on in!" He walked inwards, and saw Cheerful sitting up on his bed. Cheerful spoke, "Hi. You must be one of the other 7 'Minithrans' what a weird name! Anyways, I'm Cheerful Power Rover, but you can call me Rover." "Thanks. I'm LordBrocktree, but you can call me Brocktree." Rover smirked, "Huh. Broccoli. Nice name Broccoli!" Brocktree started to speak out in protest, but Rover drowned him out. "Hey, about your Samuraizor. Did you know that it's blade is made of platinum? That's why its so expensive, and so sharp! Oh...." Rover's cheerful face sagged. "It WAS so expensive......" "Hey," said Brocktree, "I'm upset too, but we ARE alive at least, right? " "Right... But I miss my friends..." "Yeah, me too." They sat there in silence looking at each other. Rover broke the quiet, "So.... Do you know if anyone else is awake?" "Nah. So far I think it's just you and me. Hey, do you know when you're going to train?" "I've already done it. I went with my Mythran Instructor (his name is Felious, by the way) to this big building, and we studied defense." "Cool! I just finished studying Attack. What were you taught in Defense?" "Something entirely new. You know how only Sentinels are able to block attacks with faction gear? Well, apparently, ANY faction can, you just have to use your weapons and whatnot!" "Hey! I learned something different too! When we were in the Nexus Force, remember how we would always swing our weapons in the same pattern? Well we don't have to! I completely owned my Instructor because I picked it up so fast!" Both of the Minithrans were so excited with their new skills, that two things happened. First of all, they forgot their sadness, and secondly, they didn't notice Stigroy walk into the room. He spoke to get their attention. "Well Brocktree. It appears that you got lost. I'm afraid that your room is over there." He pointed out the doorway. "And, I see that you have gotten acquainted with Rover here. Well, that's very nice. But, I think its time to go, don't you?" He pressed a button on his arm, and both he and Brocktree were whisked away. Brocktree landed inside his room, and just as he looked towards the door, it clanged shut. He wondered what had just happened.

Chapter 6
LordBrocktree was heading out the door when he paused. He turned around, looked at Stigroy, and said
"Hey Stigroy?"
Stigroy looked up from what he was doing. "Yes Brocktree?"
"How many of the other figures are awake?"
"Well, you know for yourself that Rover is awake. Besides him, there are three others. Uh... I think they are Ace Magma Marvel, Sergeant Ghost Mustache, and.... um Mario-door A-dent."
"Mario-door A-dent?"
"Sorry, I always get his name wrong. Lets see, it says here... Ah, its Mardor Aident. Is that all you want?"
"Yeah, thanks."
Brocktree headed out of the training arena, and stepped into the sunlight. He wanted to try and visit the other figures, but he knew that he wouldn't get away with it. Instead he went back to his room. Once there, he looked at the electronic screen next to his bed curiously. He had always been a techno-wizard, and he wondered if he would be able to hack it. He sat down, removed it from a holder in its wall, and went to work.
About an hour later, he sat back, his work done. There on the screen were a list of options to choose from. Most of them were unimportant, such as a list of Mythran names, IDs, and so on. But, one file caught his attention. It was labelled "Archives," and he thought that if anything on the screen had information about five years ago, that would be the place. The very first option in it was dated 4/11/11. It's title was "The Decision."
"Mythran log 4271. Today, Shival called a meeting. He stood up at the table.
"Mythrans! The Maelstrom has been pushed back! We are victorious!" (Cheers erupt) "BUT! Now that there is only a small remaining mount of Maelstrom, what are we to do with the Minifigures?" (Puzzled silence) "You know what I mean! The Universe cannot support so many figures! We must get rid of them!" (Most do not know what to think) "It is the only way! There is no other solution. We MUST do this." Figmentia speaks:
"I have two questions Shival. First of all, are you sure there are no other solutions? And if so, what is this thing we must do?"
"An excellent question Figmentia. I have come to this solution. You see, the minifigures love to fight. That is why they joined us in the first place! But who will they fight if there is no Maelstrom? Either themselves..... or US!" (Gasps of surprise) "I have a plan. We must pretend to be closing down for some reason or another. Then, to help the minifigures 'enjoy' the ending times, we will send out rare, or unreleased items to them. They will of course put these on. But." (here Shival chuckles) "But, on the last day, at the last millisecond, every item will go off. They will be bombs in disguise. None of them will survive, and we will have peace! PEACE!" (here meeting ends)
Brocktree groaned. He fell backwards onto his bed. The truth slowly sank in.

The Mythrans had smashed everyone. Everyone in the Universe, except for themselves, and the eight survivors. Mustering some strength, he looked at the next entry. It was dated: 1/31/12.
There were only a few words on it. He read:
"The plan was a complete success. There were only eight survivors."

Chapter 7

Brocktree slowly woke up to pounding on his door, followed by a voice:

"Brocktree! Brocktree, are you in there? The voice did not sound concerned, but rather angry.

"If you don't respond, I am coming in there!"
It was Stigroy. Brocktree yawned loudly and spoke in response.

"Huuuuhhh...... Uh, what? Who's knocking on my door?"

"It's the angry wolf. Run and hide. IT'S ME, STIGROY ALREADY! Wake up and come outside! You're going to meet the other waking figures!"
Brocktree sat straight up, all pretended traces of sleepiness dropped. He quickly shoved the hacked panel (which he was still holding) into its wall-holder, and then quickly prepared himself. He then walked out the door.
A very irate Stigroy hustled him towards a large building to his immediate left, and once reached escorted him inside. It was a domed structure, with strange twirling art curving around the walls. But that was not what drew his attention. Standing in a circle, were three minifigures, apparently chatting with one another. He recognized Rover, but the other two were a mystery. Stigroy, however, soon put that to rest. He shoved Brocktree forward, and said in a monotone:

"Minithrans, this is Brocktree. Brocktree, this is Cheerful Power Rover, Ace Magma Marvel, and Mardor Aident. Now start breaking the ice!" With that, he left the room.

"So uh..." Brocktree said, " Hi."

"Hey," they all chorused. Brocktree looked around nervously.

"Do you guys.. uh... think that this room is bugged? I have something important to say."

Rover replied: "Nah, we already checked before you came. We can say anything!"

"Well you aren't going to like this. You see, I hacked that electronic panel that is in my room. Do you guys know it? Good. Well, I went to Archives which were on the list of options. The last archived file, was from a Mythran meeting on the fifth of November. When they announced that the Universe was closing down. Well.... you remember all those give away items we received? They were all... all.... bombs. Shival betrayed every single figure, because he was afraid that he would loose power."
LordBrocktree's face was darkening in anger.

"The Mythrans used our trust to destroy us! They killed everyone and everything! Except us...."

The other minifigures looked pale. Rover shuddered. And gradually, they all departed to their homes.

Chapter 1

Brocktree was surprisingly calm. Ever the planner, he pondered what to do next. "
Well," he thought," first things first. Lets see how the gear is doing." Wondering why it was still intact, he peered into the depths of his back pack. The results were not completely encouraging. Except for faction gear, all of his many items were rusted away. He slowly stood up. His weak bones almost buckled from the effort, but he managed to stay that way. However, five seconds later he wished he had not. A loud alarm blazed, and three mythrans rushed into the room through a large door. One of them took out an electronic pad, and reading from it he said: "Welcome remaining Adventurer! You, and seven others like, were retrieved from Nexus Tower when the Darkitecht, through plans malicious but too complicated to relate here, blew it up. You are the first to awake from a resulting coma, and are therefore summoned to speak to out leader, Shival!" He put away the pad, and signaled the other two. They marched forward, and before Brocktree had a chance to react, snapped a pair of Mythran Issue 71-i9 handcuffs on him. After all the years, Brocktree still retained his ironic humor. "Well! This is a nice waking committee!" The mythran smiled smugly. "My friend, whether you are aware of it or not," at this point he leaned towards Brock, "you, are a prisoner." He again signaled the other two mythrans, and, setting forth in a pace which Brock in his weakened state could hardly keep up with, headed out of the room. When they left, Brocktree gasped. In front of him, the once green and growing Nimbus Station was now a charred waist land, with a hot and shining sun beating down upon him. As they continued forwards, he noticed seven other buildings resembling the one he had been taken out of. He struggled to read a plaque on one of them: CheerfulPowerRover. "These buildings must be where the other survivors are kept," he mused. All this time they had been walking, and slowly, Brocktree started to make out a large and imposing structure made of black marble and white ivory, with a giant statue of a Mythran holding a huge hammer perched on its top. He was led inside.

Chapter 1

Brocktree was surprisingly calm. Ever the planner, he pondered what to do next. "
Well," he thought," first things first. Lets see how the gear is doing." Wondering why it was still intact, he peered into the depths of his back pack. The results were not completely encouraging. Except for faction gear, all of his many items were rusted away. He slowly stood up. His weak bones almost buckled from the effort, but he managed to stay that way. However, five seconds later he wished he had not. A loud alarm blazed, and three mythrans rushed into the room through a large door. One of them took out an electronic pad, and reading from it he said: "Welcome remaining Adventurer! You, and seven others like, were retrieved from Nexus Tower when the Darkitecht, through plans malicious but too complicated to relate here, blew it up. You are the first to awake from a resulting coma, and are therefore summoned to speak to out leader, Shival!" He put away the pad, and signaled the other two. They marched forward, and before Brocktree had a chance to react, snapped a pair of Mythran Issue 71-i9 handcuffs on him. After all the years, Brocktree still retained his ironic humor. "Well! This is a nice waking committee!" The mythran smiled smugly. "My friend, whether you are aware of it or not," at this point he leaned towards Brock, "you, are a prisoner." He again signaled the other two mythrans, and, setting forth in a pace which Brock in his weakened state could hardly keep up with, headed out of the room. When they left, Brocktree gasped. In front of him, the once green and growing Nimbus Station was now a charred waist land, with a hot and shining sun beating down upon him. As they continued forwards, he noticed seven other buildings resembling the one he had been taken out of. He struggled to read a plaque on one of them: CheerfulPowerRover. "These buildings must be where the other survivors are kept," he mused. All this time they had been walking, and slowly, Brocktree started to make out a large and imposing structure made of black marble and white ivory, with a giant statue of a Mythran holding a huge hammer perched on its top. He was led inside.
