Brocktree looked around him at the other Minithrans. All of them were staring at him. He responded to the question, as Mardor tied up the Mythrans with a vine he found trailing on the ground.

"I don't know. I just saw Luke about to be killed and.... something happened. "

"Well, whatever happened, it was AWESOME!" Said Rover

Brocktree whirled on him.

"It's not awesome! I almost killed them!" With the last words he pointed at the Mythrans, who were looking at him with wide eyes.

"Well, whatever it is, I don't like it. Don't get me wrong Luke, I'm glad you're alive," said Selia," but..... Brocktree was about to kill a bunch of unarmed people! It's over though, so where do we go now Brocktree?"

"Well, there's still no way we could cross that desert, so I guess we should head back through the tunnel."

The Minithrans headed down the mountains, leaving the Mythrans tied at the top. They reached the bottom, and were about to start over to the tunnel when they heard a loud crack. They looked around, but nothing was any different and set out again. They were about a quarter of the way there when the crack came again, louder this time. Again the looked around, and again nothing. They made their way to the third quarter before yet another crack, much louder came out. Brocktree felt a sense of strong foreboding.

"Come on! Let's run for the entrance before whatever that is comes out!"

They sprinted towards it, and made it to the entrance of the tunnel. All except Mardor. He had lagged a little behind, and now was about 10 meters from the doorway. Before he had gotten another step, a final crack rang out. He looked around him in fear. And in an ear-splitting groan, the earth split open underneath him.

His leg span slowly widened as the ground beneath him shifted in two directions, leaving a gaping hole in the middle. He tried to run from the ever-widening crack but his feet slipped, and he fell into the hole. He clung to the edge of the pit with his hands, and cried out.

"HELP ME!"

Brocktree dashed towards him, and grabbed his arm, and was about to pull him out of the pit, when a large robotic tentacle came whirling out of the abyss, wrapped itself around Mardor's ankle, and started to pull in response. Brocktree was slowly pulled forward as the tentacle receded into the pit, inch by inch, with Mardor with it, and the other Minithrans were too stunned to do anything.

Mardor realized through the haze of his terror what was happening. If he didn't let go, Brocktree would be pulled down with him, and both of them would die. He looked into Brocktree's face, and Brock read what he was going to do in his eyes.

"Don't you let go Mardor, don't you dare let go!"

"Goodbye Brocktree."

And with that, Mardor let go of Brocktree's arm, and plummeted into the darkness.
