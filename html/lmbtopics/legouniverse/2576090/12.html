Okay guys, now this is the real chapter 10. Just so you know, the previous chapter is not real. Ignore it. I'll ask the Mods to delete it later. Although, one thing still stands. Instead of going to see Mardor, Brocktree is now waking up in his home........

Chapter 10

"Brocktree. Wake up."
Brocktree's eyes flew open, and he saw a Mythran standing beside his cot.
"You and the other Minithrans have been summoned by Shival to a.... get-together. Come with me."
Brocktree and the Mythran headed out the door, and made their way towards the large building he had met Shival in before. As they walked, he spoke to the Mythran.
"Where's Stigroy? Why didn't he get me?"
"Stigroy has been relocated by Shival to another planet."
The Mythran was barely paying attention to Brocktree. He walked quickly, as if he was anxious to get this over with.
"And...... why was he relocated?"
"You don't need to know."
They continued on into the building, but instead of taking the path to Shival's throne room, they walked down a long hallway into a large circular area. Brocktree saw that all 7 of the other minifigures were gathered down there. He recognized Rover, Ace and Mardor, but the rest were all a mystery to him. A Buccaneer with long, gray hair, a mysterious Shinobi wielding a long spear, a dangerous Space Marauder and an Engineer with deadly-looking Serratorizers.
The Mythran spoke in a loud and booming voice that echoed around the room.
"Minithrans!"
All of the minifigures turned and looked at the Mythran and Brocktree. Before Brocktree realized what was going on, he was shoved by the Mythran next to the group, and a large wall shot out of the ground, enclosing the pillars and surrounding them.
"Shival has decided that you are worthless to him!
And as such!
You're lives are no longer needed!"
As he spoke the last words, a large rumbling sound filled the room, and each of the pillars opened up, and Mythrans poured out of all of them.
The Minithrans stood in a circle, watching as the Mythrans surrounded them. The Buccaneer whispered to the other 7.
"I don't know what's going on, but I'm not going to die here!"
And then the Mythrans started forward.
Brocktree ran into them, Verminbane slashing through the air like lightning. It cut into two Mythrans, and they collapsed into the ground. He twirled, and slashed into another, and then jumped up into the air, smashing down upon a Mythran about to hit the Buccaneer from behind. He glanced out of the corner of his eye, and saw the Shinobi plunge his long spear into a slightly bigger than average Mythran, and, with the Mythran still on it, twirl the spear in a circle, knocking down several others. Mardor was devastating the Mythrans as he ran amongst them with his Serratorizers, causing havoc in their ranks. Brocktree then saw a group of 10, slowly advancing towards him. Time slowed down, as Brocktree felt himself dash towards them, duck underneath their weapons, and carve Verminbane in a large horizontal arc. He jumped out of the way as the ten crashed down onto the ground. He looked around the enclosure, which was rapidly clearing of Mythrans.
But then he heard something.
A terrifying roar reverberated around the room.
And the roof exploded.
Through the debris, Brocktree saw the largest Maelstrom Dragon he had ever seen, and riding atop it was the Mythran.
He shouted up at it:
"YOU USE THE MAELSTROM?!?!?!?!"
The Mythran smiled, and stared down at him condescendingly.
"But of course! Shival taught us to use power, and quite frankly, the Maelstrom is power. Did you think that Typhonus, the miserable wretch you called "The Darkitect" was destroyed as well as all of your friends and family? No! He stayed safe and sound, and in a bargain for his life, he taught us how to use and summon Maelstrom. Of course, we killed him anyways. And now, I shall do the same to you."
The Dragon roared, and a burst of flame from its fiery nostrils flew into the room. The Minithrans threw themselves out of the way, but the remaining Mythrans were not so lucky. Rover and the Buccaneer shot at the dragon with their pistols, hoping to damage it. He roared again, and batted away the shot as if it was a fly. While it was distracted, however, the Space Marauder shot its missiles and blaster at it, and the Shinobi called down lightning upon it's head. The beast whimpered, and would have flown away, but the Mythran whispered something in it's ear. It instantly changed direction, and landed on the ground, where it swiped at the Shinobi with it's claws. The Shinobi was thrown backwards, but picked himself up. The minifigures assaulted it, and while it was distracted, Brocktree crept behind it, leaped up onto it's back, and threw the Mythran down on the floor. The Dragon, realizing that it's rider had been defeated, roared once more, but before it could attack, Brocktree sunk Verminbane into it's head. The Dragon disappeared in a puff of Maelstrom smoke. Brocktree and the other Minithrans headed over to the Mythran, who was attempting to crawl away. Verminbane thudded down next to his head. Brocktree crouched next to him and whispered into his ear.
"Friend, I'd advise you to tell me what I want to know. It'll make things a lot easier for us both."
The Mythran responded.
"What if I don't?"
"Then I'll kill you and find out from the next Mythran that we meet.
The Mythrans eyes widened in horror.
"You're bluffing!"
"I'll give you 5 seconds to start talking.
5......4.......3.......2......1"
"Wait, wait, I'll talk!" The Mythran screamed.
"First of all: Where is Shival right now."
"He went to another planet!"
"Which one."
"I don't know, I wasn't told!!!" The Mythran was almost crying with fear.
"Secondly: Why was Stigroy relocated."
" Shival said that he was going to lead an attack on the natives there!"
"Where?"
"A planet called Gyfero."
"Lastly, are the Faction Leaders still alive."
"Yes, yes, Shival keeps them locked in the dungeon below here!"
Brocktree started. The Faction Leaders were actually alive? He had hoped, but....
"Listen to me now. I'm not the person who promises life and then kills. I'll let you go, but I'm not going to let you go raise the alarm. Lets see...."
He noticed a pile of rope hanging on a wall peg.
"Perfect!"
Brocktree tied him up, and gagged him with a piece of cloth from his Valiant Cape. He spoke to the other Minithrans who were looking at him and the Mythran.
"We'll introduce ourselves later. Right now, we need to go find something. Follow me."
They walked out of the room (the Dragon had destroyed the wall), and headed into the main room.

Finally! Chapter 10! Since I got such a good suggestion, my mental block is overcome so expect new chapters soon!

Chapter 11

They introduced each other as they walked. The Buccaneer was Selia Starswift, the Engineer was Luke Stormer, the Shinobi was Sergeant Ghost Mustache, and the Space Marauder was Justice Rivers. Brocktree had explained to them what Shival had done to the rest of the minifigures.

They made their way to the main entrance hall, and headed into a door with an "Archives" symbol above it. Brocktree understood most of what had happened in the previous days, and he understood why the Minifigures had been destroyed; but he couldn't understand why Shival had allowed him and the others to live.

The Archives room was gigantic. Rows and rows and rows of storage units decorated the area. Brocktree spoke to the others.

"Okay. We don't have much time, so try to find something that took place around the time the bomb went off."

The eight Minithrans searched around the room, looking for suspicious files. Around 20 minutes later, they all returned.

"Lets see..." said Brocktree, "We have a list of Mythran Casualties, we have a list of gear found, we have data from meetings.... Well this looks interesting!"

Brocktree was looking at a small file with large red letters on the front, which read:
WARNING. NO ACCESS TO MYTHRANS UNDER LEVEL A10.
Brocktree snorted. Like that was going to scare people away?
He pulled the file open..... and received a huge electrical shock that sent sparks running up and down his spine. "Well that would work." he murmured.

"Anyone here have something that might defuse this?" he asked.
Justice Rivers raised her hand.

"I tricked out my Marauder gear at the Paradox section of Nexus Tower. It should do the trick." She aimed at the file with her blaster, sighted, and fired. The file crackled and then was still. Brocktree gingerly picked it up, and closing his eyes, he gently pulled it open.... and nothing happened. Justice snorted with laughter.

"Told ya," she said. Brocktree muttered something about safety precautions, and looked inside the file. A large black screen was inside of it, and he picked it up. The moment he did so, it activated and two Mythrans flashed on the screen. One of them Brocktree recognized as Shival, and the other he had never seen before. They began to talk.

"I wish that we didn't have to record these things. I mean, what if someone managed to bypass the security on the file?" said the Mythran.

"They won't. Trust me, the first shock always scares them away." Shival replied.

"Yeah, well.... I'm not sure.... Shival, I've been wondering. Why did you let those eight live? I mean, you know that if they survived that blast they must be a cut above the ordinary. And if they are, that means that they could overthrow you!"

"Fool! Do you think I took them in out of the kindness of my heart? NO!"

"Then why?"

"People are spreading rumors. Mythrans like Figmentia, who questioned the operation in the first place, are saying that I blew up the Minifigures only to make sure that my position as king was safe."

"But you did, didn't you?"

"YES! But I don't want them to know about that! And now with the dealings with the Darkitect and then his killing, my crown is hanging by a thread! When the Minifigures wake up, I will show my kindness to them to the whole world! I will shower them with gifts, and I will train them, and then, when they are loyal to me, I will kill them all! They will be too stunned to protect themselves, and even if they aren't, enough Mythran and Maelstrom power will overwhelm them. I will cover it up by saying that they were ambushed on an interstellar training mission by Space Pirates, and that I tried my hardest to save them, but we were too late. The Mythrans will see me as a kind and good monarch, and the throne will be mine forever!"

"Are you sure it will work?"

"Tell me. Do you enjoy your position as a commander?"

"Yes Sir!"

"Well then, if you question me once more I will personally have you thrown in the dungeon for a month."

"T-t-t-he D-d-d-dungeon? With the Faction leaders?

"Yes! With the Faction Leaders. And I don't think that they'd be happy to see you...."

------------------------END TRANSMISSION------------------------------
Brocktree sat down, stunned. The Leaders were still alive? But.... it couldn't be! Thousands of people stayed with the Faction Leaders to the end! They were there! How could they have survived!

All of a sudden a loud alarm sounded in the distance, and the black screen spoke once more: "YOU ARE NOT A LEVEL A10 MYTHRAN. PLEASE WAIT WHILE YOU ARE ARRESTED."
Brocktree heard the sound of pounding feet in almost all of the many doors. But there... there was a door which was feet free! He yelled to the others.

"Come on! Unless you want to face a bunch of armed Mythrans, then let's go!"

They dashed through the door, into a large tunnel, with gaping holes in the wall. Brocktree heard mummers in the Archives room, and he set off at a sprint down the tunnel. He and the others were about half-way through it when they heard the door open behind them again, and the pounding of feet started again, intermixed with various shouts and hollers.

Brocktree saw a shaft of light up ahead. He called to the others, and they redoubled their speed..... and they ran straight into a brick wall with a skylight above it. Brocktree looked blankly at the wall, which seemed to have come out of nowhere, and then back down the tunnel, where the shouts were getting louder. He noticed a crack in the wall, and desperately felt for some kind of bomb in his backpack, but no luck. He turned to Sergeant Ghost Mustache, in his Shinobi gear..

"Hey Sergeant! Think you could call down some lightning through the skylight and hit this crack?"

The words were no sooner out of his mouth than a massive bolt of lightning came crashing down, singing Brocktree's hair on the way. When the dust settled, Brocktree saw a Minifigure size hole in the wall, and with the others following, led the way out. Gasping with relief, Brocktree noticed that the shouts and feet had stopped. He listened as he caught a few words.

"Well that settles it. No one goes out there and comes back alive. Let's go boys."

Brocktree looked around him.
And gasped.
There before him was the most nightmarish scene he could imagine.
