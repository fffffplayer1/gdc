He was there again, watching his friends fall to their deaths on the surface of Crux Prime.
He reached towards them, and they tried to grab his hand!
They were gone.

**************
Brocktree tossed and turned. The Minifigures moderating him suddenly detected vast brain activity, more than there had been for quite a long time.
**************


They were there!
Rover, Sergeant, Justice, Selia, and the others!
All seven of his old companions!
He ran towards them, shouting for joy, but then he stopped.
As one, all of them drew sinister-looking weapons, ranging from massive battle-axes to long-swords, their tips dripping with evil looking venom.
Brocktree’s face twisted in confusion.
They all suddenly dashed at him, with surreal speed.
He screamed as the first weapon came whistling toward him.

**************
Brocktree screamed. His body began to twist and turn wildly.
The doctors panicked, trying to stop it.
**************

He tried to dodge their blows, but they came too fast.
He was being destroyed by his friends.
Over the intense ringing in his ears, he heard a laugh:
the laugh of a Mythran.

**************
Brocktree bolted straight up from where he had been lying.
A moan oozed out of his mouth.

Chapter 1
The laser had damaged something in Brocktree. The few Minifigures who had experience as doctors tended to him, trying to find out what was wrong, giving him all the treatment they could.
And they could find nothing.
Brocktree had been lying in a deep coma for the past month.
-------------------------------------
The Minifigures had swiftly repopulated Avant, cutting down without mercy any Mythrans remaining on it. They built up defenses at the Launch Pads, ready to destroy any Mythrans who dared try to take the planet back.
Surprisingly, most of the green planet had been spared from the devastation of the bombs, but as they moved around the roads, they found that the Monument had been completely leveled.
*************************************

"MINIFIGURE RETURN," blared the headlines.

He breathed angrily. The Mythran News painted the returning Minifigures as celebrities, heroes, acting like they were some kind of "Long-lost wanderers, coming into their rights!"

They didn't know the truth about them.
They didn't know what they had done to him.
But he would show them.
Oh, he would show them!

The Minifigures would pay for what they did.

*************
Brocktree had finally woken up. He was cold, and he seemed very afraid.
But he was finally awake.

He had already been informed of what had happened after he had been propelled into the coma. But he had one question.

“Where did the statue come from?”
James had answered as well as he could.

“After Intrepid destroyed it and before we entered the portal, a party of Minifigures went back to the cave to try and find out. We didn’t find much, but we did find….”

He paused.

“What did you find?”

“We found Mythran tools.”

************
The Mythran walked slowly to the entrance of his apartment, located in Mythran City. The Minifigures had already taken over Avant, and he had just been informed that Brocktree had woken up.

Brocktree!

The one he dreamed of smashing!

The one whose demise he would play over and over in his head!

Brocktree was the biggest threat to his work.

But the Mythran was glad that he had survived.

It would be his hands that destroyed Brocktree.
