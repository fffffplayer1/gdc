Chapter 16

Brocktree's eyes hardened in fury. His entire mind was focused on one sentence:

Shival is going to pay.

He overrode his auto-pilot (as did the others) and steered towards Nexus Tower. He and the others communicated on a comm-link.

"Brocktree, what are we going to do once we get to Nexus Tower? It's going to be crawling with Mythrans," said Selia.

"I learned quite a lot in the years I spent at the Tower. It's riddled with abandoned halls and entrances. If you know them, you can get anywhere in the tower without being spotted," Brocktree responded.
___________________________________________________________

They quickly reached Nexus Tower. Brocktree's rocket had some extra frills, including a heat sensor, and sure enough the Tower was crawling with Mythrans. He rocketed down to the bottom of the tower, close to it's boosters. There, above them in the metal, was a small trap-door.

"This door was used for repair crews. It let them get close to the boosters so they could fix them easily," Brocktree said.

They left their rockets tethered to the door, and swung themselves in. Rover stood next to Brocktree, and flipped on a high-power flashlight.

"Standard Venture League issue," he said, winking at the group.

Brocktree lead them to a panel lying against the wall, and with a screwdriver offered from Rover ("Venture League's get lots of equipment," he whispered), he pried it open. He muttered to the rest of the group as he began to rearrange the wires.

"We can't just go willy-nilly through the Tower looking for Shival. That would double the risk of being spotted. What I'm doing here is trying to hack into the Tower mainframe, to find Shival's office. If he's there, then our job will be a lot easier. If he's not, then it should have information as to where he went.

A blue screen suddenly projected out of the panel, and Brocktree smiled.
_____________________________________________________________________
"SECURITY BREACH DETECTED" blasted out a computer. Shival laughed, high up in his office. "Thank you Brocktree... you've saved me quite a lot of trouble. Now just keep on going through those tunnels.
_____________________________________________________________________
