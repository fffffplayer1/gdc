Chapter 1
The laser had damaged something in Brocktree. The few Minifigures who had experience as doctors tended to him, trying to find out what was wrong, giving him all the treatment they could.
And they could find nothing.
Brocktree had been lying in a deep coma for the past month.
-------------------------------------
The Minifigures had swiftly repopulated Avant, cutting down without mercy any Mythrans remaining on it. They built up defenses at the Launch Pads, ready to destroy any Mythrans who dared try to take the planet back.
Surprisingly, most of the green planet had been spared from the devastation of the bombs, but as they moved around the roads, they found that the Monument had been completely leveled.
*************************************

"MINIFIGURE RETURN," blared the headlines.

He breathed angrily. The Mythran News painted the returning Minifigures as celebrities, heroes, acting like they were some kind of "Long-lost wanderers, coming into their rights!"

They didn't know the truth about them.
They didn't know what they had done to him.
But he would show them.
Oh, he would show them!

The Minifigures would pay for what they did.
