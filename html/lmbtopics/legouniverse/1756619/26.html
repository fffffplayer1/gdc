Chapter 4 >>>

BerserkFusionDude, or Fusion, as he was know to his friends, sat down at a table, after grabbing his dinner meal.
On his left was Hurricane, who was deep in conversation about battle plans with Ninewells, and on Fusion's left was EmperorFred, who was munching on a biscuit while reading a book.
Fusion looked up, and tapped Fred on the shoulder.
'Who?" Fred said, not looking up from his book.
"Fusion. I was just wondering what gear you would want to choose when Numb Chuck asks us."
Hurricane had heard, and turned around, along with Ninewells.
"Well, I sort of wanted to look like Percy Jackson..." Fred said.
Golden, who was sitting across the table, picked up Fred's book, and glanced at the cover. It was the first book of the Percy Jackson series. He then set the book back down, after reading his favorite part.
Everyone started to discuss what they wanted to have for their gear. Fusion, however, had his own thoughts.
"I didn't really like any of the weapons we trained with," he said. "I was more interested in learning... well.. magic."
"Kenjin the Wise did mention that magic does exist," Milligan, who had joined the conversation, said.
"Exactly, you are correct."
Everyone turned around, and found themselves staring at Kenjin the Wise.
"How does the magic work?" Fusion immediately asked.
"I do not know. Only Mythrans, First Builders, and those who came before them know the secrets of magic. The only problem is this: Mythrans who support humans have all been imprisoned by the Nexus Force, the First Builders and the other Mythrans would kill any of us on sight, and those who came before them are on the edges of the Universe, and many of them would kill you on sight too," Kenjin said.
"So, I guess I don't have a hope," Fusion said, dejectedly.
"That is not true. There is one Mythran who will be able to help you."
"Who is that?"
"He is a native of our world, and went to yours for a short time to help the Lego Company. The reason he is not imprisoned is because he was able to fight off the Nexus Force Troops sent to capture him. His name is Inventrius."

Chapter 4 >>>



BerserkFusionDude, or Fusion, as he was know to his friends, sat down at a table, after grabbing his dinner meal.

Fusion looked up, and tapped Fred on the shoulder.

'Who?" Fred said, not looking up from his book.

"Fusion. I was just wondering what gear you would want to choose when Numb Chuck asks us."

Hurricane had heard, and turned around, along with Ninewells.

"Well, I sort of wanted to look like Percy Jackson..." Fred said.

Golden, who was sitting across the table, picked up Fred's book, and glanced at the cover. It was the first book of the Percy Jackson series. He then set the book back down, after reading his favorite part.

Everyone started to discuss what they wanted to have for their gear. Fusion, however, had his own thoughts.

"I didn't really like any of the weapons we trained with," he said. "I was more interested in learning... well.. magic."

"Kenjin the Wise did mention that magic does exist," Milligan, who had joined the conversation, said.

"Exactly, you are correct."

Everyone turned around, and found themselves staring at Kenjin the Wise.

"How does the magic work?" Fusion immediately asked.

"I do not know. Only Mythrans, First Builders, and those who came before them know the secrets of magic. The only problem is this: Mythrans who support humans have all been imprisoned by the Nexus Force, the First Builders and the other Mythrans would kill any of us on sight, and those who came before them are on the edges of the Universe, and many of them would kill you on sight too," Kenjin said.

"So, I guess I don't have a hope," Fusion said, dejectedly.

"That is not true. There is one Mythran who will be able to help you."

"Who is that?"

"He is a native of our world, and went to yours for a short time to help the LEGO Company. The reason he is not imprisoned is because he was able to fight off the Nexus Force Troops sent to capture him. His name is Inventrius."
