Hi guys, just thought I'd take my hand at writing a comedy while working on rewriting "The Nightfall Incident". This is a semi-sequel, but don't take anything here as actually happening in the Story itself. This is like my Fake Chapters. Just a joke. So, here you go.

So, Milligan Thinks He Can Write a Comedy? >>>

"Traversing the reaches of Space and Time, four Explorers," Patrick Steward said.
"Quiet!"Shouted Milligan. "Put EMPHASIS in it! EMPHASIS!"
"Wait, what are we even doing here?" asked Patrick.
"Making a book, because Milligan here thinks he can write a comedy," Said Hael Storm.
'Get in the Koi!" Commanded Milligan. He, Patrick, Hael, and Captain Falcon, who had arrived at the last minute, ran aboard.
They blasted off, and the moment they were in the air, Milligan shouted, "Do A Barrel Roll!"
Hael did so, crashing the ship.
"Oh no!" Yelled Captain Falcon. "The Maelstrom!"
"No worries," Said Milligan. "I'll just write them out of existence!"
"Let's check their power level first!" Said Patrick.
"Alright," replied Milligan. "It's only 1006, that's not so bad!"
"Um, you're holding it upside down!" Pointed out Hael.
"Oh My Mystics!" Shouted Milligan. "IT'S OVER NINE THOUSAND!"
"I'll save the day!" Said Captain Falcon, who jumped in front of the Maelstrom.
"FALCON...," Captain Falcon began. "PAWNCH!"
He pawnched the Maelstrom, and sent them all flying away.
"AWESOME! ONE HIT KO FROM 0%!" He said.
"Well, readers, that was awesome! The End!" Said Patrick, this time putting EMPHASIS on his words.
"NO! STOP BREAKING THE FOURTH WALL!" Milligan shouted, just as he ran out of paper to write on.
