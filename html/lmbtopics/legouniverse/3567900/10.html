“Someone took her.”
“Who took her?”
“We don’t know.”
Darkitect’s face was boiling.

“Where am I?” Shauna mumbled.
“Somewhere that isn’t safe. We have to move before the Maelstrom find us.”

“ It will give you enough strength for the journey.”
The bowl touched her lips as she sipped. “ What’s in this?!” Shauna cried, the liquid sprayed out of her mouth with disgust.
You must drink it, or you will become too weak and be smashed.” The man said.
With a face that meant this drink couldn’t have been any worse, Shauna drank it quickly for if you don’t like something you always do it that way.
“It is time to go. Come.” The man got up to his feet and helped Shauna up, whom was still weak.
“We must hurry!” He said, worried.
