Evil is Not Born, but Taught or is it?


“You’re strange.”
Thorn shrugged his shoulders in reply. “I’ll take that as a compliment for my greatness.”
“Are you implying that you’re awesome?”
“Precisely, you should think better of me you know.” Thorn said sarcastically.
“I think not.” Shauna said disagreeing.
“That is an outrage to my ears!” Thorn laughed. “You will soon like me as the others did though.” He became silent.
“What do you mean…others?”
Thorn shook his head and looked out the window.

The rest of the drive was very silent and calm, once Shauna got used to the fact that this car was magical in a way that even she didn’t know.

Something that had been on her mind for awhile finally came out of her mouth. “Where are we going?” And she expected an answer from this guy, not one of those ‘I can’t tell you now, answers’.
Thorn simply replied to her annoyance, “Somewhere safe.”
“Tell me!” Shauna pleaded.
“You’ll recognize it once you see it.”
“And I’m driving to a place that I don’t even know, without instructions on how to get there?’ Shauna asked frustrated with him.
“You know where to go, that’s why.”
“And how would I know that?”
“It’s because you’re special.”
“Oh, I’m special. That’s a great answer. Look, I wanted an explanation for what is going on right now. I’m with this crazy man. In this magical car, driving it and I don’t have a clue what the heck is going on here!”
“I can’t tell you until the time is right.”
“Are you serious? I can stop this car right now and get out of here if I wanted to.” Shauna almost yelled with anger.
“Do you think I wanted this? No, I never did. I would love to tell you everything, but I can’t. My life has
been difficult and harsh. Do you think I wanted that? Do you think I wanted my entire life to be like this? NO, I didn’t! And even if you wanted to stop this car you could because you don’t even know how to break. And if you do somehow find a way to get out of this car, you’ll be leaving your destiny.” Thorn’s face was hidden from the darkness, foreshadowing sadness, and Shauna couldn’t help but feel sorry for him as she saw a tear roll down his rough cheek.

______________________________________________________________________________________


The snow rumbled through as if an avalanche was approaching. It moved rapidly in quick tense movements never stopping.

Five minutes went by, then it stopped. Something stood up from the ruffled snow. In both his hands, were a glowing fireball, one red, one purple. His face was half red, half purple. His left iris was red, his right was purple. Everything on him glowed intimidating. He was the clown.

Evil is Not Born, but Taught or is it?


“You’re strange.”
Thorn shrugged his shoulders in reply. “I’ll take that as a compliment for my greatness.”
“Are you implying that you’re awesome?”
“Precisely, you should think better of me you know.” Thorn said sarcastically.
“I think not.” Shauna said disagreeing.
“That is an outrage to my ears!” Thorn laughed. “You will soon like me as the others did though.” He became silent.
“What do you mean…others?”
Thorn shook his head and looked out the window.

The rest of the drive was very silent and calm, once Shauna got used to the fact that this car was magical in a way that even she didn’t know.

Something that had been on her mind for awhile finally came out of her mouth. “Where are we going?” And she expected an answer from this guy, not one of those ‘I can’t tell you now, answers’.
Thorn simply replied to her annoyance, “Somewhere safe.”
“Tell me!” Shauna pleaded.
“You’ll recognize it once you see it.”
“And I’m driving to a place that I don’t even know, without instructions on how to get there?’ Shauna asked frustrated with him.
“You know where to go, that’s why.”
“And how would I know that?”
“It’s because you’re special.”
“Oh, I’m special. That’s a great answer. Look, I wanted an explanation for what is going on right now. I’m with this crazy man. In this magical car, driving it and I don’t have a clue what the heck is going on here!”
“I can’t tell you until the time is right.”
“Are you serious? I can stop this car right now and get out of here if I wanted to.” Shauna almost yelled with anger.
“Do you think I wanted this? No, I never did. I would love to tell you everything, but I can’t. My life has
been difficult and harsh. Do you think I wanted that? Do you think I wanted my entire life to be like this? NO, I didn’t! And even if you wanted to stop this car you could because you don’t even know how to break. And if you do somehow find a way to get out of this car, you’ll be leaving your destiny.” Thorn’s face was hidden from the darkness, foreshadowing sadness, and Shauna couldn’t help but feel sorry for him as she saw a tear roll down his rough cheek.

______________________________________________________________________________________


The snow rumbled through as if an avalanche was approaching. It moved rapidly in quick tense movements never stopping.

Five minutes went by, then it stopped. Something stood up from the ruffled snow. In both his hands, were a glowing fireball, one red, one purple. His face was half red, half purple. His left iris was red, his right was purple. Everything on him glowed intimidating. He was the clown.

Oh no!! Bewrare the Maelstrom Infected Clown of Doom!!!
