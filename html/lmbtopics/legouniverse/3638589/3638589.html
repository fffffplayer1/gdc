Thank you for all the bios people! Now for the prologue for whole trilogy.
************************************************************************************************************
Prologue
July 13 2015
Crux prime.

A lone figure wandered the barren wasteland of Crux Prime. The only this that you can see about the figure was the chaos surrounding him. He was searching for a place unseen but seen. His destination was the skeleton mine. This place was in fact a disguise for a greater evil. For under it was what most people called "the living devil's tomb". This figure walked over to the area of the mine that had no drills or watch towers and put his foot on the darkest spot in the area. A silent rumbling eloped the area.
A big crater opened up in front of him and revealed a lava river with an obsidian walk way. The figure jumped on the path and the hole closed up behind him. In the light you could see this dark figure's face. One half was that of a normal minifig's, the other was the equivalent of a fossilized skull. Half his head had hair, which was in fact, sizzling from the heat. He was in the darkest possible armor, which consisted of obsidian armor, and black bat lord shirt and pants. Gifts form the Darkatect himself. He reached the end of the passage where the lava turned purple and fell off a cliff face, into the void which waited below. There also was a throne which had at the head, was Nexus tower with shades of purple scattered around the structure. Next to the tower were large spikes made of purple silver. All around the chair were skeletons wearing Nexus Force gear, with stromlings near the heaps of plastic bone. What was known as the vortex glared down on this chair and threatened to eat the universe. But that wasn't today's main attraction.
A figure sitting on the horrifying chair was the main attraction. One gloved hand gripping the end of the bedrock arm rest. The other hand holding a large, menacing scepter with the scariest jewel known to minifig kind, the maelstrom crystal. A purple top hat was sitting under the towering head rest. The head wearing the hat was the most terrifying face ever. Known as Baron A. Typhonis, or more commonly, The Darkatect.
“Why are you here?” The Baron boomed. His voice has the power to make any creature suffer, save the figure standing before him.
“I have brought terrible news my Lord.” He said shyly. “The wretched Nexus force, they are winning the war.”
“Well I guess it’s time to strike at the heart of the Nexus.” he boomed.
“What do you propose we do, master?” the figure asked.
“Attack their city. They will fall. Take this.” He tossed double bladed sword at his servant. “This gift will allow you to take over someone and start the battle.” They both laughed menacingly.

END TRANSMISSION

Book1: The real war begins.
Prologue.
-The Venture Explorer-
-Raw space-
_____________________________________________________________________
The wretched figure flew to the ruin of a mighty transport ship once known as The Venture Explorer. Now no one knew what it was. The maelstrom now uses this for a prison ship. The figure soared threw some pieces of sheet metal, which were being held together by pure and fresh chaos.
He landed in the middle of what was once known as the creation area. The figure walked over to the head mech and asked if he could use one the prisoners for military purposes. The mech complied and ordered for one of the most trusted Nexus Force members to be brought over to them.
In moments they brought over a battered minifigure who was wearing a red shirt and blue jeans. The servant of darkness thanked them for their service and stabbed the three mechs. The Nexus member fell on his chest and muttered in fear and pain. The unusual stromling knelt down and spoke.
“Well who might you be?” the stromling snuck in a mesmerizing tone of voice.
“Bob. Former co-pilot of this ship. Founder of-“He was cut off.
“I know the rest! What I want from you is not your information.” It said.
“Then what do you want from me?” Bob asked.
“This.” The stromling pulled out the double bladed sword and hit Bob. The stromling laughed crazily and Bob screamed in pain. In the end of it there was only Bob. He stood up and broke into laughter.

END TRANSMISSION
