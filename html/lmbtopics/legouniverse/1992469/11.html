Chapter 5, Part 2.

Traylor, Ry, and Zolzar went over to the Ranger.
"Thanks," Traylor said. "Thanks a lot for saving our plastic back there."
"I am in your debt, sir," Brickface said as he got up from sitting down. "Is there some way we can repay you at all?"
"Well, if there's one thing, what was going on that got the gunfight started up?" the Ranger said.
"Ah..." Ry began. "That's a bit too much."
"I save you guys' life and I ask for one thing, and I get THAT?" the Ranger said, slightly irritated. "Come on, it can't be that big."
"Let's just say he," Traylor said and motioned towards Brickface, "had earlier dealings with them."
"I was hoping for something a little more... specific."
"If the Ranger wants answers, we'll give him answers," Zolzar said. "It's the least we can do."
"All right, all right," Traylor moaned. Then he told him the whole story.
"Wow," the Ranger said once Traylor had finished. "So, it looks like you guys need major help."
"We've got it covered," Traylor said. He didn't want the Ranger invovled.
"Must I remind you that I just saved your guys' lives?" the Ranger persuaded. "C'mon, I'll join you guys. I've got nothing better to do since the Maelstrom's gone."
Before Traylor could protest, Ry jumped in. "You're in."
