Chapter 3, Part 2.

That was all Traylor really needed. He tore a piece a paper out of a drawer, grabbed a pen, and ran over to Brickface.
"Get drawing," he said. Brickface thought that he was a little bit demanding, but he figured that there was too much at stake to take this lightly. He grabbed both items and started doodling what he saw.

* * *

It was only ten minutes before Brickface was done. Traylor, Ry, and Zolzar took the map and looked at it.
"Now that we have the map," Ry said as she looked at Brickface, "you can do whatever, really."
Brickface laughed.
"I think not. I would actually be glad to join you three," he said. "I myself have my own vendetta against the Dark Force. It would be my dream to tear them apart."
Suddenly, something occurred to Traylor, but it seemed that Zolzar read his mind. He asked the question for him.
"You've never mentioned the name of your leader," Zolzar said. "Who is he?"
Brickface shrugged. "No one knows his name. We always referred to him as 'The Great One.'"
"Well, there's no time like the present," Traylor said, changing the subject. "If we've got the map, we'd better go."

Chapter 4, Part 1.

The minifigure known amongst his team as The Great One stood on a rocky outcropping over the famous Brick Rapids, one of the seven Universal wonders of Crux. It was a beautiful sight, with massive waves, and a lush forest on one side and a mountian- which the outcropping poked out from- on the other.
Yet the Great One didn't care anything for this.
He grasped a map in his hands. It was dirty and creased, but it was the key to the ultimate power of Crux.
He gazed solemnly into the rapids, making sure no one could see him. He was lucky. It was only him out here.
He carefully opened the map. According to the map, he and his team were about a quarter of the way to their destination, the one and only Imagination Nexus.
His loyal second-in-command unsteadily moved along a pile of rocks toward the Great One. His name was Lubjack.
"We've heard some news, Sir," Lubjack said.
"Important news?"
"Yes," Lubjack said.
"Tell me."
"Someone broke Sergeant Brickface out of prison."
The Great One almost had a heart attack. Brickface is free? He knew he should've sent someone else to do the job! If Brickface told someone about the map, and drew it out (dang his memory!), they could be caught.
The Great One excused Lubjack and thought about the situation. He needed to bring the Maelstrom back. Were they evil? Of course. But they were his ticket to power.
And he wasn't going to pass this opporotunity.

Chapter 4, Part 2.

Traylor, Ry, Zolzar, and Brickface sat down on a couple of benches, waiting for the Crux Express. It was a tram that could carry you anywhere throughout the universe. In this case, their destination was the famous Nimbus Falls.
Nimbus Falls was the largest waterfall in the universe. It was simply massive. And that was where they had to go first. Why? From there, they could start getting to the Core entrance.
A few minutes later, the tram screeched to a halt. Traylor got up and went in casually, followed by everyone else.
They picked out their seats and sat down as the tram began moving. It was weird. For some reason, Traylor couldn't help but to feel that something way more dangerous should have happened.
Then he noticed out of the corner of his eye, were four men in suits, who just got up and started moving toward Brickface.

Chapter 4, Part 3.

"Lieutenant Brickface," said one of them with a mustache. "The Great One wants you back."
"Not in a million years," Brickface laughed, still sitting.
"You don't want to make us use force."
"On the contrary, I won't do it even if you drag me across a thorn patch."
"Then we'll have to resort to the last option," the one with the mustache (I'm gonna call him "Mustache" for easy reference) said. He reached into his jacket for something.
"No!" Traylor said. This alerted everyone in his compartment, including the guys in suits.
Traylor pulled out his Rank 3 blaster and aimed it at the four minifigs.
"Get out of here."
"Since when must you get involved?" said one of the men (not Mustache).
"Back off."
"Oh, you must be the one who broke Brickface out of prison. Well, that settles it," said Mustache. Then he pulled out a small Super Dagger that had been concealed in his jacket. He turned towards Brickface and thrust the dagger at him.
