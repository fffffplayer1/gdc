... What... Happened here?

 

*A black, hunchbacked figure bends down to clench a fistful of soil. He thought he used to know this place, but it seems as though where just yesterday there was once a place of great heroes, there is today nothing but rubble abroad.*

 

*Quickly, the bipedal creature unsheathes his sword. Something did this, probably something big. In truth, it didn't matter if it was the size of three Makuta, he'd be a fool to come unprepared. Then, his eye catches a few living things on the horizon, one of them, familiar: The Toa he met seasons ago on the day of his departure from his old woodland home. He's forgotten his name, it was so long ago they met. He approaches cautiously, hoping that all is well with the Toa in the distance.*

 

Ho, Toa! I come just as always, as a friend!

 

*Destrak throws his sword to the ground in front of him, arms raised.
