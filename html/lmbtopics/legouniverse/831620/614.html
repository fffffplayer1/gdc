Karkon looked behind him it was a massive sandstorm! It swept him up! Karkon couldn't see anything! Karkon closed his eyes. Then THUMP! Karkon looked up and he was oddly on the ground of a jungle! Karkon stood up and he looked around his surroundings. He was on a island. Then Karkon heard shots of Midak Skyblasters! A matoran came running through the jungle with a Midak Skyblaster shooting it at a pack of visorak behind him!  Karkon ran up to the visorak! He pulled out his tentecles and jumped up about to strike! Then suddenly everything disappeared into nothing! Karkon landed feet first on the ground. All he could see is whiteness. Then he heard footsteps behind him. Karkon turned around it was a makuta! The makuta was all black. He looked like a shadow! The makuta had the head of one of Takadox's species! ''Hello Karkon'' said the makuta. ''Who are you'' asks Karkon. ''Who am I I am your nightmare'' says the makuta! The makuta ran up to Karkon laughing insanely! His hand covered Karkon's face!

 

Then Karkon awoke. There was something very different about him though. He had a black aura around his body and no color besides black. Karkon whipped out his tentecles. They were also black. Karkon expressed no emotion. He then walked out of his hut silently to the toa HQ. ''I have returned to destroy you all toa'' said Karkon
