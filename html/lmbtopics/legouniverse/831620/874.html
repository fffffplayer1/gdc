Demaron zooms in at incredible speeds on his Sonic Striker.  Coming up to the island, he first notices the tower, then the elemental blasts heading back and forth between the multiple beings there.  He then sees a collection of seven Toa.  They looked just like - no, they couldn't be - it is!  Demaron's old Toa team!  The Hauden!  There they were, looking at a strange being lying on the ground.  Fesur was at their head - then Vonai, and Bohak, and Batudon, and Sukan, Kenisa, Fanos - they were all there!

 

"Toa!" he cries, using his powers to project his voice towards them.  "Hauden!  My friends, allies and comrades!  Is it really you?"

 

The Hauden on the island cannot believe their ears.  This is a voice that they have not heard for hundreds of years.  They turn to what they perceive to be the source of the noise, and see a small silver and black object hurtling towards them.  It grows into the shape of a light battlecraft, which lands, revealing - their leader!  Demaron gets out of the flyer, and stops, standing stock still.

 

None of the beings can believe their eyes.  Sukan is the first to break the silence.  "Demaron?  Is - is that you?"

 

"It is I," he replies.  "So, you were alive all this time?  I thought that you were... gone."

 

"We thought the same of you, my friend," answers Bohak, walking up to him and clapping him on the back.  Soon all of the team are involved in a strange mixture of hugging, handshaking and reintroductions."

 

"Now," says Fesur after this has concluded.  "I officially return leadership of the team to you, Demaron.  What are we going to do about her?"  He points to Crystalia.
