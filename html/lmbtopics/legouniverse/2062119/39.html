I gazed at the armor that appeared in front of me. It was a combination of blue, gold and purple, and had various bumps on it that had glowing circles, which I decided to call Nodes.

"This is the Sorcerer Kit mark two. We don't have a valiant weapon, or a third level specialty kit, but it makes up for both."

He points to a less advanced version of the suit and says, "This is the level one version of the suit. The basic abilities are: Maelstrom manipulation Nodes(My thoughts: I was right about the Nodes!), Imagination rictor pumps, and a generator that runs on Imagination."

He points to a staff of red and gold, with an orb on top.

"This is the weapon, a Pulse Staff. It fires a triangular pulse of chaos, that envelops the target and traps it in suspended animation for attacking easily, though the rank 1 only traps one at a time."

My eyes ate up the beauty and technological marvel of the suit. It was like a gift from above.

"That's it. That's the kit I want."

The vendor shrugged. "If only it were that simple. You would have to take that up with Vanda, and she hasn't let anyone have it yet."

My heart sank in my chest. Not only would I most likely not be able to get the most perfect suit of all time, but the only way to get it was from my former student.

Who would probably recognize me on the spot.
