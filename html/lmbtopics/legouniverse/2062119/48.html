Crux Prime. It was a relatively quiet place. Dark, murky, and decript, yes, but quiet. That's why Exceedra enjoyed coming to this spot of the planet chunk. War had not yet come to this area, so he didn't have to worry about the dangers or noises of battle. Whenever a stray stromling came along, well, he could take care of that.
Exceedra was just relaxing in his usual position, hands behind his back, with his eyes closed, when he felt a faint tremor. He dismissed it as a very minor earthquake, or something along those lines, but it came again. And again. And again. Finally, it was a constant rumble, with louder bits every so often. He could hear it now, too.
As it became especially loud, he finally opened his eyes and sat up. What greeted his eyes was a very large vehicle about to trample him.
"Agh-ah!" He scrambled back quickly to avoid being squished.
It raced past him, before grinding to a quick halt. A hatch on the side popped open and a minifig with a pilots hat on stuck his head out of it.
"Hello there! First off, sorry for nearly squishing you. Second off, run like the Dickens!" I yelled.
Exceedra turned his head and saw a mist of purple heading towards him.
I quickly added, "Prefferably you should run in this direction! Just saying."
I wasn't even finished speaking before he leaped up and charged for the Cata-tank.

"Alright, up you go. That's it." I hauled him up as he reached the Cata-tank.
*Grunt* He was almost up, when he heard something... strange. Like and extremely loud spring going *boing*.
"What was that?" I looked down, and saw a stromling rocket towards us, and latch onto Exceedra.
"Exceedra! Your leg!" He looked down to see the stromling.
"Ah. Just a sec." He closed his eyes and started to vibrate, and the stomling below began to do the same. From the hand down, a blue glow traveled through the stromlings body. When it reached its head, brown hair replaced purple, and a mouth sprang from nothing.
He gasped, his lungs once again in effect. The minifig looked up, with an obvious expression, 'how?'.
"Mind hauling us up now?"
"Right. Of course."
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Inside, we all rumbled along. Many of the minifigs were conversing, trying to figure out what had exactly happened. I was trying to figure out what had happened with Exceedra. He had introduced himself, so I knew what to call him. I was approaching him when we heard a voice come from above. A very familiar voice.
"Unregistered attack vehicle, cease movement and surrender. Oh, these are good.'nmh'."
A smile came to my lips.
'Albert, you nut.' I thought as I raced for the control room.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
"What the brick is that thing?" G asked.
"I don't know. But it looks familiar. Very familiar. I just can't place where I've seen that before." Overbuild said. He then ate a donut, and lost himself in thought.
G sighed, and walked over to him.
"Albert, that is your fifth donut today. That can't be healthy for you."
"I know. But they are delicious! Especially the coconut flavored ones, those are divine!"
G sighed again, and looked at Mech. His claws came up in a shrug. She would have to do something about this.
As Overbuild was reaching for his sixth donut, the box was snatched from him.
"Hey!" He turned around to see the box in Mech's claws.
"What was that!?"
"Oh, nothing. I just told Mech your donuts were filled with pudding." She grinned maniacally. Mech just looked at her with blankly. And G laughed.
"Mech, you are so gullible. And Albert, you're going to get fat, so I had to take them-"
"Hello!!" A new voice startled them from below. Overbuild fell out of the chair he was sitting in, and mech threw the box of donuts into the air.
"Who or what was that?" G exclaimed.
"You know, your microphone is still on up there. Very many figs down here are clutching their stomachs and rolling on the floor." It came again.
"Who are you?" G demanded.
"Well now I'm hurt. Come on G, the monument a few days ago?"
Slowly a smile came to G's face. It quickly disappeared as a donut deattached itself from the ceiling and fell onto her head.
