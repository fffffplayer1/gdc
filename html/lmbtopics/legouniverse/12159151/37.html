Okay, I finally finished LoC, posted seven parts on LCS: Vigilantes to end my turn there, and these character bios are the next thing on my list. Oi, I feel busy. Here we go!

 

Name: Peregrin Wanderthistle

 

Species: Minifigure

 

Backstory: Peregrin's parents owned a small farm on Crux. Peregrin was rather young when his planet exploded, and sadly, he was the only survivor among his family. that he knows of. No corpse, not dead, at least in today's culture. (DARTH MAUL.) He was rescued by A terribly overworked and disorganized relief effort, dealing with as many as millions to billions of people. they dropped him off on a small planet, wished him luck and flew off. Where he simply got up and left the overcrowded relief shelter, and was never missed. He spent many years there as a homeless orphan, making his living amongst the lowlife of the area. Which comprised of the population. One day he saw a ragged recruitment sign for the Nexus Force- something about it moved him, (I actually have a expanded story for this in my mind, but to write it right would take months of dedicated time. ) and he joined Venture League with Daredevil as his main kit. A few months after seeing the sign, he met Talia and teamed up with her for a while. After she became a Mythran, he and Percevel became part of an elite squad, and were assigned the job of clearing Maelstrom from recently discovered planet chunks.

 

Personality: Need I say anything? Eternally cheerful, full of happiness, always ready to tell a joke, be it good or really truly terrible. A pretty good shot with his flareguns, he relies on quick-thinking, improvisation and using every little advantage in a battle rather than raw strength.

 

Appearance: Hobbit-ish brown hair and freckles. There's a rumor that he wears contacts.

I filled the tiny spots, but yeah! You've got the jist! MAN! Now I want to write about Perry!
