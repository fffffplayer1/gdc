Story One: Siblings.

 

All Harold could remember was the love that he had felt in that singular moment.

 

The boy, aged seven, shuffled into the hospital ward along with his father. The pair had come to greet the newest member of their family - a girl, apparently. When Harold had shown signs of reluctance. his father had gently squeezed his hand and reassured him. Truth be told, Harold had never been a fan of hospitals. Or babies. Or siblings. He always heard all about how annoying his friends' siblings were, and he didn't want one for himself.

 

Harold's mother waved her boys over, and whilst his parents approached, Harold walked slowly over to the cot and peered in.

 

In one moment, his heart was shattered and reformed with a new center.

 

Inside the cot was a child clad in nought but a white blanket. The baby didn't look particularily special - it had podgy limbs that it waved about wildly and eyes clenched shut against the harsh electric lights. But despite that, there was something that took the young boy's breath away.

 

Perhaps it was the bond of blood that the two shared, and that could never be replicated by anybody else. Perhaps it was simply the sight of those beautiful, emerald eyes opening for the very first time to stare at nobody but him. Whatever it was, Harold could feel all of his anxieties, all of his doubts and fears and trepidations, be washed away with an overpowering feeling of love.

 

Still cautious, but now for different reasons, the boy reached into the cot and plucked the baby up. Instead of crying as anticipated, the bundle of joy snuggled into her brother's chest, and Harold already felt nothing but compassion for the child.

 

As Dante watched the young girl play with her new friend, Lux, he realised that his feelings hadn't changed in the slightest, despite all the time that had passed.

Please excuse me while I go cry my eyes out.

I decided that some bios of my own would be in order. These characters, also, are written as post-story.

 

 

Name: Dante Wilder.

 

Species: Technically human... he has superpowers, so whatever you want to call him I guess.

 

Backstory: Read the previous 32-odd pages if you're curious. Basically he touched something he shouldn't have.

 

Personality: Whilst still trying to remain positive, Dante is not nearly as happy-go-lucky and quippy as he once was. Having been given memories that date back thousands of years and having seen close friends of his lose their lives in sequences of events that he feels he initiated means that he's kinda depressing sometimes. He is also a natural leader, and a prodigy when it comes to strategy and tactics.

 

Appearance: Dark blue-grey eyes, light brown hair that has a habit of defying gravity at times.

 

Theme Song: Boulevard Of Broken Dreams (Green Day)

 

 

 

Name: "Raelyn".

 

Species: Sentient magic rock.

 

Backstory: Originally created thousands of years ago by the first builders to combat the threat of the maelstrom, the gem of plasma was eventually captured by the maelstrom and experimented on, corrupted and given a concience, deemed a failure and tossed away before being found by and bonding to Dante Wilder.

 

Personality: Whilst Raelyn both acts and genuinely tries to be nice, her core consists of almost pure evil. This means that she is manipulative, annoying, harsh and occasionaly cruel. She's only really nice to Dante, due to their unique sitation. As she said to Dante himself: "I don't care - at ALL - about your friends." But she's working on it, and only uses Dante's mouth to insult Gaia about twice a day now. Which is a vast improvement.

 

Appearance: In real life Raelyn is a rough chunk of crystal about the size of a large-ish marble and in a diamond-esque shape. Inside the mindscape, Raelyn takes the form of a tall girl with very long purple hair and eyes of the same colour.

 

Theme Song: Immortals (Fall Out Boy)

The Maelstrom Minifigure, Knight Of The Old Speech, Time Lord.

a month ago

Name: Plue Abernathy.

 

Species: Human.

 

Backstory: Joined the Nexus Force at a relatively young age along with Dante Wilder and William Virtue, distinguishing herself on the battlefield with both her skills and her valiance. She is the Sentinel Faction's representative in the new Unified Force.

 

Personality: Plue is a very kind soul, and rather timid at heart. Though she has built up a rough exterior over time due to circumstances such as war, she still prefers to find a diplomatic solution to problems when at all possible. That said, Plue knows how to wield both a sword and a bow extremely well should the opportunity present itself.

 

Appearance: Long blonde hair, pretty green eyes... looks like a cinnamon role, basically. Though she could totally kick your butt.

 

Theme Song: I May Fall (Jeff and Casey Lee Williams)

 

 

 

Name: Gaia Eluciss.

 

Species: 60% Human.

 

Backstory: Nobody knows. Not even Gaia.

 

Personality: Brash and overconfident, Gaia has a both a quick wit and a quick temper, resulting in many dangerous situations. However, she is strong and skilled enough to fight her way out of every corner she gets herself into.

 

Appearance: Gaia has long, choppy, dark brown hair with streaks of purple dye in it, and green eyes. She's rarely seen without combat boots and her specially designed battle gauntlets.

 

Theme Song: I Burn (Jeff and Casey Lee Williams)

 

 

 

Name: Vector Jadewing.

 

Species: Human.

 

Backstory: Vector is a well-known member of the Venture League, with his exploits known throughout the Nexus Force. He's slain the ghost pirate of Penbrook Bay, as well as Kinga Hurl's counterpart Dankey Kang. He is now the Venture League's representative in the new Unified Force.

 

Personality: Vector, whilst in posession of an admirable sense of humour, is generally stoic and level-headed. He can be easily wound up, however, and he has a bad habit of bickering with a certain someone.

 

Appearance: Black hair and stubble, a muscular build and brown eyes.

 

Theme Song: Caught Up In A Dream (The All Insane Kids)

Story 2: Propinquity

 

Song Used: Fairy Tail Main Theme: Music Box Version

 

Harold, Plue and Will stood on the steps as a trio, overlooking the grounds and watching as students swarmed like ants out of the gate, into the embraces of their families. It took little time for Anna to find them, and she proceeded to tackle hug her sibling, sending both of them sprawling onto the floor and roaring with laughter whilst hugging each other. As Harold assisted his sister in rising, Plue's mother and father approached the group and struck up a conversation with their daughter.

 

Will watched all of this, and felt something stir in his chest. Something unpleasant. He had always been envious of his friends - more specifically, of their parents and siblings. As he observed the interactions, he knew that it was something he could never experience for himself.

 

But there was something he COULD experience.

 

As he took a couple of steps forward, his friends broke off from their familial interactions to move towards him as he rummaged in his bag, eventually pulling out the camera that he carried around with him most days. Glancing at it for a brief moment, he eventually made a desicion and handed it to Anna.

 

"I'll tell you when to take it." He told the young girl, who nodded and proceeded to stand back.

 

Will felt Harold's arm drape itself over his shoulder and pull him in closer, and he siezed the rare oppourtunity to smile - not plastered on, and not cynical - a genuine warm smile. It was something he hadn't done for a while.

 

There was a clicking sound and Anna bounded back up to Will, holding the camera out. He took it back before turning to his friends.

 

"You know, this is what true family is." He said, earning a confused look from Harold.

 

"The way I see it, you don't have to be related by blood to be family." Explained Will. "Family is anyone who supports you and cares for you. Who comforts you when you need it, and who has your back no matter what."

 

"Anyone who loves you is family."
