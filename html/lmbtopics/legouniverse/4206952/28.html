LieutenantLafayette wrote:

"Tragedy has struck! This past Friday, the planet inhabited by the militant group known as The Rangers was destroyed completely by an attack by the Void Empire. After a task force assembled by the Nexus Force was able to clear out the Void forces the planet itself was destroyed by several massive charges set to destroy the planet's crust and rupture its mantle. The causality count is the highest we have seen to date. It is even more evident that peace must be won quickly, by any means."

Lafayette moved on to the next newspaper in the large pile he found in his office. More tales of destruction and war littered the pages. A common theme followed through them all, "Remember the Rangers!" After their destruction, the Rangers became the rallying point for all opposed to the Void and Maelstrom forces. In a few years, victory was achieved.

"But at what cost." Lafayette murmured. He pats his pockets, looking for a pen. The rustle of parchment causes him to pull out the dirty paper from the last floor. All the past words were gone. It now read:

 

For I know the plans I have for you, plans to prosper you and not to harm you, plans to give you hope and a future.


"So this is your paradise.  A world free from destruction.  A world free from evil.  A world with a benevolent  Author."  The dreaded voice says from behind.

 

"Your choice is simple.  All of the most painful ones are.  You can abandon the old world for your paradise, allowing it to fall into madness and final destruction, or you can leave the paradise that is everything you ever desired in order to save a world that will never live up to expectations."

 

With a crack, a chasm opens before you.

 

"The chasm is your way out, should you chose the latter.  That is all."

 

(Lord) Barcha, Piercing Spear of the SoL

You could say he fears that, in a way.

Paradox...
