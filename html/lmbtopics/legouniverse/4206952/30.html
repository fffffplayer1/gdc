Alright, NOW here is my character's history. And Mods, I edited the history, as to not conflict with any rules now, so PLEASE pass it. It's for the dungeon and LURP in general.

 

History: Being born into royalty, he was placed with a large responsibility at a young age. His home-planet is the capital of a busy solar-system, by name of Sector 8, plagued with civil war. At the beginning of his family’s rule, the monarchy of the Hraan blood-line, a rebellion sprouted, the citizens of planets being told a lie, from the lips of the royal family’s Advisor, seeking power. The solar-system was thrown into chaos, with a short, but devastating war. The responsibility of taking action was placed on his family, and victory was almost inevitable…if not for an assassination. Aeric saw the smashing of his beloved parents, after returning from a retreat from the war. Aeric was traumatized, the responsibility of taking action in his hands. Using exemplary strategy, he quelled the rebellion, but for a high price. Vraania was nearly destroyed in the chaos, but was quickly taken back. After years of rebuilding, Aeric took to studying, the arts, and strategy, hoping to never have such a thing ever occur again. His interests led him into other systems, and even galaxies, his solar-system and galaxy advancing far in technology, creating many useful devices, weaponry, ships, shields, and the greatest discovery ever made in their system: Harmonia. The energy source has an overwhelming type of power found in it that has the ability to neutralize and/or absorb negative energy from unknown or hostile powers. It is, to a degree, a form of magi-tech. It’s other property, is it’s amazing ability to blend, or combine with machinery, being able to greatly enhance the efficiency to a great extent. Since its discovery, it has been used, and adapted with Vraanian tech, to great use and prosperity.
