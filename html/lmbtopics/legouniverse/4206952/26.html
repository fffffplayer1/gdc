*The door slams shut behind Darek, leaving him alone with just himself, a long hallway, and a headlamp*

 

"I knew I should have brought along the flamethrower," he mutters as he walks forward.  Oddly enough, his headlamp begins to dim.  "Say, I thought I changed the batteries in this thing just before we arrived here...  Oh, wait.  But this is the Eternal Codex, and I'm pretty sure Barcha's got the power to turn off a light."

 

As the light continues dimming, Darek squints his eyes to look ahead as far as he can.  "Well, well.  It looks like this hallway is just a straight run all the way through.  As long as I keep going forward, I may not even need the light-"

 

Just as Darek utters those words, his headlamp cuts off.  "Blast it, and I was hoping to at least have the light for a little longer."

 

*He continues walking forward for an indeterminate length of time, until he begins hearing strange noises.  Noises like explosions and the screams of... minifigures.*

 

"It's nothing, it's nothing," he says to himself.  "They're just figments of my imagination, I've been through this before.  Well, yes, they're real, but I can conquer them.  If I'm not afraid, they can't harm me."

 

*Suddenly, his headlamp blinks back on with a flash, almost blinding him, because the environment lights up, too, like going outdoors on a bright summer day after being inside*

 

"What in the world...?" He says under his breath.  Immediately, a cheer rises from a crowd.

 

"Darek Skychaser!  Darek Skychaser!" the crowd cheers.  Seeded throughout the groups are signs congratulating a "Darek Skychaser."

 

A band begins playing, and Darek notices that he is now on a high stage in Nimbus Station.  Seemingly out of nowhere, three minifigures walk up to him.  He recognizes them all.  Two are the private and lieutenant from the L.S.S. Arrowell, while the other is his commanding officer from Nexus Force Academy...
