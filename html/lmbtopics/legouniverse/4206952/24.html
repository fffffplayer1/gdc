This entire post is OOC and directed at the SWRPers who have found their way here:

 

For those of you who are new, general background.

 

The man presently waiting at the center of this planet is named Barcha.  Somet time ago, he stumbled across a device he named the Chaos Source.  It damages the laws of reality within its area of effect.  At present, the device is inactive, meaning that its effects manifest over a smaller area (roughly a mile from the highest point on the planet) and in  much less pronounced ways.  In its active state, it is capable of destroying all froms of order in the universe as a whole, thereby causing the universe to break down.  The goal of the heroes is therefore to reach the center before the Chaos Source comes online.

 

However, the inactive state of the Chaos Source has several effects on reality within the Codex.

 

Within the area of effect, reality behaves noticably different, and is highly built on perception as opposed to fundamental laws.  Within the Codex, the physical form is completely malleable.  However, this is one of the greatest dangers of the place.  Once changed, the character cannot exist outside of the Chaos Source's area of effect.


After finding a way down into the depths of the planet, three distinct floors stand between you and your target.  The subconscious plays with reality down there.

 

The first floor is one where the worst fears of the character manifest in physical form.  To expand on this a bit, "Worst fears" are almost never physical.  We've had a hero fleeing from the literal ghosts of his past, a hero who was afraid he didn't know his own wife, ect.

 

The second floor is a floor shere the characters are met with a Truth.

 

The bottom floor has not yet been revealed.  It is the worst.  It is the absolute worst.

(Lord) Barcha, Piercing Spear of the SoL
