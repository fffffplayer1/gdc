Humans (Minifigures, Earthlings, Man, Mankind, etc.)

This species is the second oldest known species to date, only Demons being older. Many other species are simply genetically mutated branches of Humans, like the Espers and the Ashlines.

-History-

Throughout the majority of space, the humans are indefinitely the most populous species to live. Beginning on Earth, they lived humble lives, advancing quickly with technology. Climate control systems were established to make up for the poor treatment of the planet’s ecosystem. Then, in the year 2086 A.D., the climate control systems failed, and as these had been the only things holding the planet together, it began to tear the planet apart, dooming it to a slow death. A new, post-disaster calendar was created, called A.W., standing for After World. 2086 A.D. was known as 0 A.W. Then, 21 A.D. (2107 A.D.), a gargantuan spaceship, around the size of the country of Ireland, suddenly appeared in Earth’s orbit. Government scientists found that the name of the ship was the Alisa III, and it was designed to support millions of life forms for thousands of years. The Alisa III crashed into the moon, and it’s pieces were studied and used to build a ship called the Noah, which would take a thousand humans off the doomed planet to a system thriving with life that they had found in the Alisa III’s databases, called the Algol system. These humans left on the Noah, leaving behind everyone else, in the year A.W. 71 (2157 A.D.). It clears Earth’s solar system in A.W. 74 (2160 A.D.), and no further information is known.
The humans that were left behind found a wormhole that led to an alternate area of the universe, and all the humans evacuated through this, running from the doom coming to Earth. Earth imploded on itself in A.W. 121 (2207 A.D.).
In this alternate area of the universe, the humans begin to thrive, finding a total of hundred planets the size of Earth and with the same stable ecosystem. These are called the Hundred Worlds, including many like Shaneea, Treniem, the Planet of the Guards, Gallant V, and others. There was a myth about the first world the wormhole led to, called Planet Crux. The happiness of the humans having escaped their fate was said to have created a Nexus capable of producing practically anything. Unfortunately, according to the myth, the humans were forced to leave, due to unknown reasons.
Many colony planets were founded of the Hundred Worlds, the most famous of these being Skrios, the rain planet, and Slinelatin, home of the Shadows.
After several millennia of living in peace, Baron Ricaro Typhonus began a quest for the Planet Crux and the Imagination Nexus in A.W. 5307. He recruited Doctor Albert A. Overbuild, who went by his middle name, Arthur, the space pirate Hael Storm, and the famous knight, Duke Jake Exeter. With Typhonus’ assistant, Vanda Darkflame, they found the Nexus, but Typhonus, turning out to be evil, corrupted it. Planet Crux exploded into several worlds, and the Nexus Force was formed, which began recruiting members from the Hundred Worlds and its colonies to fight against the Maelstrom. A new calendar was created here, N.U.Y., standing for New Universe Year, as the new system created by the explosion of Crux was said to be a rival to the Hundred Worlds.
In N.U.Y. 7 (A.W. 5314), -{-CENSORED-}-
-{-CENSORED-}-
In N.U.Y. 8 (A.W. 5315), many Humans were stranded on the remains of the Crux system. After Biomonsters were discovered, they formed the Agency to fight against the Biomonsters, which became a well kept secret over the years. This is all information known up to the present, N.U.Y. 486 (A.W. 5793)

-Description-

Two eyes, one nose… I think we all get the gist. Any characters entered as ‘human’ will be a part of the Agency. The majority of them a fair skinned and athletic, trained for battle.

-Naming-

Normal, average names.
Male examples: Jonathan Bowes, Peter Jackson, Leonardo DiCaprio, Matt Damon, Jackson Fleming, etc.
Female example: Catherine Elizabeth, Peggy Reeder, Ashley Schell, Maia Lawrence, Rachel Scott, Hannah Newlin, etc.
