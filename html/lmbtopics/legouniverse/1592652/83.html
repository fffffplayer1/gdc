**** SPOILERS!!! DO NOT READ IF YOU PLAN ON READING THE JOURNEY TO THE NEXUS!!! ****

The Beginning
Book One- The Journey to the Nexus

Business Tycoon Baron Typhonus, a good-humored old man, has employed the daughter of an old friend, Vanda Darkflame, a silent, intimidating character, as his assistant. After flying on the cruise ship The Hurricane, they landed down on the planet Shaneea, where they find the man they were looking for, Doctor Arthur Overbuild, a wise and very friendly man, the same age as Ricaro, at Treniem College. As Ricaro and Arthur are old friends, Arthur accepts the Baron’s invitation to join his quest to find the legendary Imagination Nexus. From there, they track down the infamous space pirate, Hael Storm, who is quite greedy, yet clever. They enlist him to help them find the famous Duke Jake Exeter. They all get on Storm’s ship, the Pathfinder, where Overbuild finds a golden square. As it turns out, Storm had one too.
Eventually, they touched down on the Planet of Guards, where it was said that the Exeter Palace was. After they had all gotten off the ship, Pathfinder exploded behind them. Vanda managed to save Typhonus, but Storm’s spine was permanently broken by a large chunk of the ship, and Typhonus began acting strange, a lot darker than usual. They went to the Palace of Exeter, where they rested themselves before meeting the Duke. While Overbuild, Typhonus, and Darkflame were dining with Exeter, Storm woke in his room, lying in his bed and paralyzed from the neck down, unable to move. A strange creature at the window of the room gave him two gifts, and told him that as long as he kept these two things, a black parrot and a bejeweled cutlass, near him, any injury he was given could neither kill him stay; anything would be healed. Of course, the creature requested a price- that Storm become its servant.
Storm refused, and sliced through the creature with his new cutlass, falling out the seventh-story window himself. His broken bones were healed almost instantly by his gifts, and after rejoining with the group, Exeter agreed to come with them and let them use his ship, the Venture Koi.
After a while on the Venture Koi and a strange encounter with the same creature Hael had met before, they found that the two golden map pieces Overbuild and Hael had fit onto a pedestal in the Venture Koi, along with a piece Exeter happened to have. This formed a map, which led them to Planet Crux. When they got there, Vanda stayed behind in the ship to keep it safe, while the other four went forward.
At the entrance to the Nexus Shrine, they met the last of the Guardians, Bobaximilucarusetdeotry. He took another form, of a human wearing a red shirt and jeans, and they called him Bob. Then they entered the Nexus Shrine. Each explorer began to get their image of what they wanted from the Nexus in their head. Overbuild himself made a robotic dog, which he named Glo. But something possessing Typhonus made him create a chaotic creature, infecting the Imagination Nexus and turning it into complete Maelstrom. Overbuild ordered Vanda to fly the ship of the planet and get safe through a telecommunications device. Typhonus was dragged into the Maelstrom.
The Shrine was blown to ruins and Exeter killed by a blast from the Maelstrom. The Venture Koi was exploded by the Maelstrom too, and Overbuild used the Imagination he had gained to cork the Maelstrom. All the backed-up energy, though, completely exploded Planet Crux, creating thousands of smaller worlds, spreading out and forming the Crux Asteroid System. Overbuild, using his newfound Imagination, put Exeter back together. Vanda, as it turned out, had set the ship to autopilot to get off the planet, technically following Overbuild’s orders. Hael Storm came to join them as Glo nudged a shard of the Nexus, still pure towards Overbuild. He used its energy to call any explorers to help form the Nexus Force.
At this time a new era system was created, called New Universe Year, or NUY. This was NUY 1.
In the Nexus Force, four factions were formed.
The Sentinels, led by Duke Jake Exeter
The Venture League, led by Hael Storm
The Assembly, led by Doctor Arthur Overbuild
The Paradox, led by Vanda Darkflame

**** SPOILERS! DO NOT READ IF YOU PLAN ON READING PARADOX SECEDING!!! ****

The Beginning
Paradox Seceding

The year is NUY 3, and all the recruits of the now huge Nexus Force are celebrating the birthday of Duke Exeter. But arguments between the four factions, mainly from the Venture League and Sentinels towards Paradox, have stirred up discontent. After a Venture League prank on Vanda, she storms in late on Jake’s birthday party. After he refuses to force the factions to stop the pranks and arguments, Vanda calls all the Paradox together, and they leave the Nexus Force, staying together in Forbidden Valley. An attempt by a Venture League Daredevil fails, and he is thrown in prison with a special task force, working for Duke Exeter. He learns that Paradox has messed with concentrations of Imagination, making so that whenever a recruit is killed on Forbidden Valley or Nimbus Station, they will reform inside of the prison.
The rest of the Nexus Force groups together, as they are certain the Paradox are ready to attack. But when a single ship comes down, Duke Exeter checks it out himself. It turns out the pilot of the ship was a dummy, with a bomb inserted into it. The bomb explodes, smashing many of the recruits around the area, but Jake is protected by his heavy armor.
Using a new invention, the Paradox teleport behind the Nexus Force, throwing them completely off their strategy. Paradoxes attacks the confused and injured Nexus Force, driving them back to Avant Gardens. Using bombs, they kill off even more recruits, until there are barely any Nexus Force members left. Arthur was also smashed in the first battle.
Paradox advances onto Avant Gardens, but they have all turned completely dark, infecting themselves with Maelstrom. The planets of the Nimbus System begin to blow up, planet by planet. The Nexus Force began a final charge, led by Jake, just as Avant Gardens exploded.
Then Jake awoke from his nightmare.
At this point, Faction Wars were made illegal by the Nexus Force.
Also at this point in time, the Shinobi, Inventor, Adventurer, and Space Ranger kits were designed.
