Chapter 2: Victini's Quest.

(View: Victini)

"Come on Star!" I called back to Superstar as we trudged up he maelstorm covered slope. I glanced over at my shoulder and looked out that the far reaches of Crux Prime. The dark waste land that was filled with lots of adorable Maelstorm. I LOVE it here!

"Victini, I'm pretty sure we aren't suppose to be doing this...." SuperStar25 murmured quietly from behind me, "going out of bounds is dangerous.... what if we smash? We might not rebuild... plus we're only rank 2!"

"Oh stop complaining! Vanda never sends us on missions so we have to go on our own!" 

"Vanda never sends us on missions because YOU always act weird and almost get yourself smashed EVERYTIME!" Star exclaimed.

"Don't be a crybaby!" I teased, "it's not that high!"

 "That's not.." Star murmured but I ignored him and began climbign the rest of the way up the slop. I stood at the top and looked around. Nothing. Just more rock. I was disappointed not to see any maelstorm. I wanted to find my sister. The maelstorm took her so I have to ask ALL the maelstorm where she is!  

For a second, my mind wandered to the days when I would play with my sister. When I'd dress up as a maelstorm and Diana would pretend to be a sentinel. Sometimes we had the greatest adventures. Other times one of us would go running to mommy because the other had hurt her. One time some of my purple paint rubbed off on Diana and she started screaming that she was infected. Which gave us the idea of painting daddy and mommy purple! We got grounded for a week but it was a lot of fun. Another time I couldn't sleep, and my sister held me in her arms and read me stories of nexus force adventures from the newspaper.

"VICTINI LOOK OUUUUTT!!!" Star inturpted my memories. I turned in time to see a large spidering marching forward. It knocked Star down the slop with one swing if it's leg. For the first time ever, I was scared. I was scared of this maelstorm.

"Help..." I squeaked.....

Chapter 3: Truth finally told (part one)

(View: Dino)

 

I am a terrible shot. I'm an even worst rocket driver. But there is one thing I am amazing at. Crashing. Everything had happened so fast. It had started as a simple mission in Crux to go and clear up some of the maelstorm. Then we found Star in a pile of bricks at the base of a mountain. Droc, who was with me, rebuilt him and me and him questioned what had happen.
"VICTINI!" He had gasped, "at the top! Spiderling..." That was followed by a scream. Droc and I looked at each other. Panic filled me.

"Droc! Rocket launcher! Please!" I commanded. Without question, bricks flew out of Droc's bag, and he arranged them into a Rocket Launcher. I placed my rocket on the launcher and blasted off to the top of the mountain. There was no auto pile here. I had to steer myself up there. I saw the spiderling over Victini.

"VICTINI!!!" I screamed and crashed my rocket right into the spiderling. Victini looked up at me in surprise. I leaped out of my now busted rocket and drove my samuria (I was in Samuria gear by the way) sword into the spiderling. It screamed and smashed. I landed on the ground panting and turned to my sister.

"Why are you up here," I asked seriously.

"I wanted to see more maelstorm..." Victini replied sheepishly.

"Going out of bounds like this is dangerous!" I barked.

"You went on a mission like this when you were rank two!" Victini squeaked.

"I had friends!"

"So did I!"

"My friends were rank 3!" I yelled. I was very angry at her. So angry I just let it all out, "why did you even join the force! You should have just stayed home!"

"BECAUSE I WANT TO SEE MY SISTER AGAIN!" Victini cried, "isn't that why you joined the force too, Dino? Didn't you join for some special reason like that!" Victini cried back at me. I froze. Tears began to run down my face. I stepped forward and hugged Victini.

"I wish..but no..." I cried...

(Part 2)

"I.. didn't join the force for some heroic reason, Victini..." I replied quietly, "I joined to selfishly protect myself. In the end, I caused my best friend and my sister to come into danger, I drove my mom sick with worry, and I've caused a bunch of other problems..."

"Dino..." Victini sniffed. Victini and I stood there in a silent hug. After a few moments, Victini pulled away and looked up at me in my face. "No, Dino! You've done a lot of good though! You saved Dark Moon and stopped some of the fighting in Sector Z!"

"Victini..." I murmured.

"You're brave! You're just like......" Victini's voice trailed off but then she said, "you're just like my sister. You're weak but you try hard for those you care about anyway. Even if you aren't any help, you still try to make things better..." At that moment, I awkwardly began laughing. 

"I'm just like your sister, huh?" I smirked.

"Why are you laughing?"

"You know.. it's not too difficult to be just like someone you are..." I said without thinking. Victini stared at me blankly. She studied my face to see if I was kidding. A bunch of different emotions few through her eyes. Tears streamed down her cheeks. She leaped forward and hugged me so hard we both when tumbling down the slope. I stopped us by digging my sword into the ground and held my sister tightly.

"DIANA!!" Victini began screaming

"Shh.. it's okay Sarah!" I gasped trying to pull us onto stable land.

"I MISSED YOU SO MUCH!" Victini cried, "Why didn't you tell me? No.. I know why. I'm sorry. I love you, sister.." 

"I love you too, sis," I smiled.  We slide down to where Star and Droc waited. Star cooed over Victini, surprised to see her crying. Droc looked at me silently.

"Did.. you tell her?" he asked as we returned to the Nexus Tower.

"Yeah... I don't think I need to hide anymore.."I replied. A moment later, Victini tackled me from behind.

"Sis! We're both in the Nexus Force!! Sis! Sis! Let's go smash Maelstorm!" Victini cheered.

"Sure, let's go!" I smiled back.
