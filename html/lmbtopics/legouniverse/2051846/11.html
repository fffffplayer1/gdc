Here is the next chapter!
Chapter 46: Probably the end.

I Gathered all my remaining friends together. It was sad how few of use there was. I turned to 7op as he walked up followed by a few of droc's friends. " Where is swifty?" i asked. 7op frowned, " Swifty.. i dont know.." Gameking and dingo walked up looking really sad. " Swifty smashed. We tried to save him but Dark Moon's lighting went right over us and hit him. . ." GameKing mumbled. Tears ran down my checks. Dark moon was going to far. She must be stopped. " Ok. I have a plan.." I said. The word plan made everyone look up. As I started a crowd of the few warriors left stood around me. " Ok.. here is the Plan. We all go up to the top were Dark Moon is. We must strike her from behind. Catch her off guard. Everyone will take there chance against Dark Moon . Most of you won't come back though. . . Three of us will sneak around back and smash her." I said. By the time i finished everyone was around me. even the faction leaders were listening. " This might just work.." Vanda mumbled. " anyone who is with me Please come with me to the rocket pads." I said. Pretty much everyone followed. Duke came up to me. " I hope you know what you are doing." He said. I gave him a nod. He turned to the crowd, " GOOD LUCK MY FRIENDS! FOR IMAGINATION!" He called. Everyone cheered and we blasted off to Ag. " Yep.. this is Probably the end." I Sighed as we landed.
We walked up the mountain to were Dark Moon stood. She was Yelling at what looked like thin air but i could tell it was Sigilyph. " Dark Moon." Lenny shouted as Droc, 7op, and I ducked behind a rock. She looked up and stared right at Lenny. " Oh look. they came to try to stop me again. How sad." Dark Moon laughed. " CHARGE!" Lenny yowled. Everyone Dashed at Dark Moon. I watched as Dingo and Gameking worked together to get close. daredevils leaped into the sky. Assembly used their abilities. But all fails. I closed my eyes as Dark Moon easily smashed everything. Dingo was smashed by Lighting. GameKing jumped in front of Lenny and took the a hit for him, and smashed. Droc's friends worked together but were smashed. Soon all that were left was Lenny and a few others i didnt know. Dark Moon laughed as she blasted away the few remaining. Only Lenny could stand his ground. my little team ran forward. Dark Moon spotted us and shot lighting at 7op who jumped out of the way and took a place next to Lenny. Dark Moon Shot another lighting blot at him. this time landing it's mark. 7op smashed. I had to hold back from screaming but tears ran down my face. I couldn't bare to watch the battle between Lenny and Dark Moon. We were almost behind her when i heard Her evil laugh. " Oh you are a strong one. Arent you?" She laughed as Lenny Took a lighting blot to his infected arm. The lighting blot didnt effect him and he kept moving. I realized that he was using his arm like a shield. Lenny charged forward but Dark Moon grabbed him. He let out a yowled, " DINO!!! NOW!!" He screamed then Smashed. I couldn't hold back now. Dark Moon had smashed all my friend but Droc. I equipped my sword and ran straight at her. " DINO NO!" Droc yelled. Dark Moon laughed at me. I closed my eyes and waited for the end. I heard a blast of lighting but in confusion felt nothing. I opened them to see droc in front of me. He Fell to his knees. " Bye.. Dino.. good luck.." He mumbled then smashed. " DROC!!! NO!!!!" I cried. I fell to my knees and dropped my sword. It was all over.. i couldn't win.. " Wah. that was a hopeless try.. Oh well. Looks like i win." Dark Moon laughed, " welcome to the maelstrom Dino" Dark Moon zapped me with maelstrom lighting but it wasnt like her other attacks. This one was meant to infect. I felt the chaos come over me.. Pain.. " its all over..." i sighed. " No." Sigilyph voice made me look up. " Dino, Remember when you slapped me? Im like the pieces!" He said, " Take my hand!" I frowned, but weakly reached forward and took his invisible hand. . . .
