Chapter 29: Old friends.

It was three days after the elevator ride. We all had taken a day of recovery then got back on duty the next. I stood at point Zeta with a shot gun. Gameking stood on the other side with a blaster. The Turrets weren't working so Sentinels had to guard point Zeta while they were being fixed. Droc and his friend Melmen had been assigned to fix these guns. Gameking sniped a stromling that came to close. I shot at a mech who had came a little to close. The mech smashed quickly.
" How long do we have to stand here again?" GameKing asked, Sniping a spider.
" Duke said as soon as our shift is over or when They finish." I answered and shot a stromling pirate. There were a few peeps out in the field. None of crux had very strong imagination. There were very few safe spots. Point Zeta was one of the safest smashing areas. Mostly because there were sentinel here at all times so if you smash and don't rebuild, the sentinels will help you. A rank one DareDevil was using " Fig on Fire" and smashing Maelstrom all around him or her ( Couldn't tell.) A rank two Paradox and assembly fought side by side. I knew the two rank 2s were Victini and her friend ( I think his name was SuperStar). The rank one looked about the same age as me. Victini and her friend were only about 11. The Rank 1 smashed and rebuilt behind me.
" Man! Those Maelstrom are strong out there!" She muttered.
" They Don't say you should have strong gear for nothin." GameKing replied, smashing an Ape with his blaster. The Daredevil removed her helm to show bright pink hair tied back in a bow.
" Kirby." I realized.
" Is this were infected peeps would be?" Kirby asked.
" Not always. Most of the Time they say on the planet they were infected on." 7op said, who had just landed from the drop ship.
" Oh." Kirby sighed.
" Why ya ask?" Melmen asked.
" Cause my best friend got infected." Kirby mumbled. Droc, 7op and I all looked at each other quietly.
" what was her name?" Gameking asked, Sniping a mech.
" Diana." Kirby answered.
" Huh.. Weird.D-" Gameking started to say but I shot him in the leg.
" OW!" He squeaked.
" SORRY!" I yelped. The other sentinels of point Zeta came to help Gameking and a different Sentinel took his place for the time being.
" Why did ya shoot him?" Kirby blinked.
" I didn't mean to. I Guess I'm better with a sword then a gun" I sighed.
" DINO I'M GOING TO GET YOU FOR THAT!" GameKing yelled.
" Sorry King.." I muttered.
Our shift ended and I went out to smash with 7op a bit. Kirby tagged along too. I swung my staff into a spider. The spider shot at me but I rolled out of the way. Kirby came from behind and did a back flip over it, using Fig on Fire. She shot the finishing blows.
" Nice attack." I said, Charging at a mech ( By the way I'm using bat lord.) with shield slam.
" Thanks!" Kirby said, " Wish Diana saw that. We used to pretend to do that move."
" Ah." I said. The memories of Kirby failing to do a back flip in her move she made up called " Flip the BBQ" , Came into my head. I leaped onto a spiders back and smashed it with a few strikes from my sword. I looked up to see Kirby Cornered by the Named spider ( Which I do not know it's name so yea..) I tried to dash over to help her but a horseman jumped in my way. I smashed the Horse men in time to see Drew, in full rank 3 adventurer land in between Kirby and the named spider. He snapped his whip and tripped the spider, making it fall on it's side. Then he shot the named spider till it smashed. He turned to Kirby, using his healing ability.
" There ya go!" He said cheerfully.
" THANKS!" Kirby said with a smile. 7op ran up to us.
" Hey Drew!" He said. We continued smashing for the rest of the Day. It was nice hanging with Kirby again. Even tho I couldn't tell her it was me. I knew we were still best friends. . .
