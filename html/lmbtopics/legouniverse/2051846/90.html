Chapter 4: What would you do if?
(View: Dino)

 

Nothing had been going on lately. Just the normal, smash maelstorm here, attempt to get my sister to go play with her friends rather then cling to me there, and more smashing. same old, same old. Staring out from the tree house of my newly designed block yard, an idea dawned on me. I pulled out my contact device and invited whoever was around over to my Block Yard. What a better way to make life more interesting then with a party!?

After a few hours, everyone arived. Klem had brought a couple of her friends whom I didn't know. Drew, 7op, and Swifty brought some drinks and pizza and began having soda chugging contest almost right off the bat. Droc had brought Major, Mel, and a girl name Cailin. Kirby and Victini brought Star. Moon and Sig also showed up with Perky. Droc and Mel built a radio and began smashing tunes and everyone had a dance off. After a few hours of partying, the bulk of the party left, leaving me, 7op, Droc, Sig, Perky, Kirby, and Klem.

"so uh.. what now?" Klem asked blankly.

"Uh.. wanna play a game?" I suggested.

"Oh! How about What would you do if?" Sig said eagarly.

"What's what?" We all asked.

"It's a game where you ask fun What would you do if questions!" He said. We all looked at him blankly. 

"That.. sounds very boring," Klem muttered.

"Well, what else are we suppose to do?" Droc wondered.

"Okay who starts then?" I asked. Everyone looked at me. Of course I got picked. "uh.7op.. what if... I ... hit you with a bus?" (Random but first thing that came to my mind), everyone looked at me blankly before 7op started laughing.

"Well! I'd probably smash!" he replied, "Okay my turn then? Klem, what would you if everyone turned into pink dancing pandas?"
"I'd go home." Klem replied so plainly everyone started laughing, "Sig, What if Perky and you switched bodies?"

"I'd smash myself." Sig replied blankly then yowled when Perky bit him. 

 

To be continued!
