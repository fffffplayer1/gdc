Chapter 31 (Part 1): I'M BACK (A title that has nothing to do with the chapter at all)

 

As we appoarched the launch pad for the nexus tower, I fell in beside Castle and eyed him blankly.

                "What is it, Dino?" He murmured to me.

                "Is it just me, or does it feel like a lot of time has passed?" I asked blankly.

                "How much time?"

                "Like two years."

                "Yeah, just you," Castle grunted and ruffled my hair, "Well, I don't feel anything, but you probably hit your head when you fell."

                "Thaaanks. Who's fault was that?" I grunted. Castle gave me a devious smirk but remained silent. Opposite Land was almost a misleading name for this world. Everything here was exactly like the Nimbus Station I was from. The grass was green. The race way echoed with sounds of buzzing cars. Minifigures in vendors called out to us in attempts to sell their products. Dino2 and Penny went ahead and launched off to the tower. Rare and Castle snorted as they watched Dino2 have a bit of a problem flying his rocket (Seems like the only real defferences between us is our gender...) Clement and Dew waited for my friends and I to blast off before following. (Jay stayed behind)

                It felt nice to be in the Nexus Tower again, even if it wasn't our orginal world. Sector Z had so little imagination that it was almost sufficating compared to the imagination filled air I was used too. In the tower, i felt filled with engery and power. Feeling much more relaxed now, I equiped a pair of bat pants, a scale shirt, and my traditional messager bag.  As if to compete with me, Dino2 equiped a similar outfit only replacing my scale shirt with the t-rex t-shirt. . .

(Part 2)

"What do we have here?" To our luck, Exeter and Overbuild were busy talking to each other in the hallway. "Hello Lady  Exeter," Dino2 said with a salute.

"Greetings," the woman said. I was taken aback by their appearance. 'Lady Exeter' could seriously be Duke's twin sister. Her hair was a lush blonde that was cute short, barely hanging pass her ears. She wore a breast plate and well armed pants. She smiled kindly at us but you could feel her strong aura a mile away. Dr. Overbuild, on the other hand, was very different from her male counter part. She dressed in a long orange dress with her fraction syblem on a necklace. Rather than a thinking helm, Dr. Overbuild had a tiara looking thing. Finally, rather than robotic arms, she had a pair of robotic wings. Dr. Overbuild seemed like an utopian queen than a builder.  At first, Lady Exeter smiled but then layed eyes on me and my counter part.

"Uh... why is there two Dinos and one is a girl?" she murmured.

"Uh, she's my alter Ego" Dino2 replied shyly.

"Alter egos?" Dr. Overbuild wondered.

"Yeah," Dew said, "they're people from a world where everyone is opposite of what they are here!"

"So.. I'm the leader of Paradox?" Lady blinked.

"Uh, no madam.. Gender opposites. I'm UltraDinoWarrior," I explained.

"Oh. Well, Okay then... How did you get here?"

"Sinking ship," Castle grunted.

"In space?" Overbuild asked. Rare nodded.

"That's not possible. Ships don't sink in space," Overbuild said, "you must have gotten pulled into some type of portal."

"So.. this isn't the bottom of the universe?" I asked.

"Of course it isn't!" Penny sneered.

"Says the one who believed us the most," Snorted Castle.

"Hey! Do you wanna start something?" Penny shot back. Rather than saying anything, Castle equiped his weapons.

"WHAT IS GOING ON HERE?" a loud woman's voice Interrupted. "Yeah, I saw this coming," Muttered. . .
