Chapter 40: The Cure ( Part one )

Vanda started flipping through the Diary. I stood in the corner and Legend had been taken off to sick bay. Mew walked around the room, looking at stuff. Once she made a full circle around the room she stopped in front of Duke's bed side.
" Hey Duke! Hello? Uncle Exeter ya there?" Mew said waving her hand in front of his fast.
" Wha.. COUGH COUGH " Duke mumbled coming too. He looked dazedly up at Mew.
" Mew..?" He mumbled.
" YEP! HOW YA DOIN?" Mew cheered. Duke's eyes went wide in surprise and he let out a scream. He flipped backwards and fell off the bed he was laying on.
" OOF! My Mew, Don't spook me like that! : He gasped sitting up.
" SIR! " I gasped.
" Mew was the.. OH! " Vanda said flipping in the Diary a bit then reading a page, " He is afraid of Surprises and nothing surprises him more then seeing family. "
" Mew is.. " I blinked.
" YEP! HE IS MY UNCLE!" Mew laughed.
" Wait.. WHY DIDN'T YOU TELL US THAT??" I yelled.
" You didn't ask. " Mew blinked.
" And did you know the Cure too?" I asked.
"No. I only knew Uncle Exeter hates a surprise. " Mew blinked.
" Hero and Thorn didn't have to die.. " I Breathed. I stood up and ran out of the room. My hair glowed brightly. . . . .

Again, great story! Still don't see why I smashed for nothing... But never the less I still think this is an Epic story!

Chapter 40: The Cure ( Part two)

I ran down the halls. Tears ran down my face.
" I can't believe I let sentinels die for no good reason!" I cried in my head.
" Diana. " The sound of my name made me jump. I almost didn't reply, I was so used to Dino.
" Who..?" I flipped around to Face... no one.
" Who's there?" I said.
" Diana. " I realized the voice was Kirby.
" Kirby?" I blinked. I walked around the corner to find her curled up asleep.
" Kirby wake up. " I said, shaking her.
" Diana, I'm right here." Kirby said from behind me.
" What?" I flipped around to face face her.
" Wait a second... " I mumbled. Kirby doesn't know I'm Diana.
" Diana.. what a weird name! I actually prefer Dino myself. " Kirby shrugged. Her pink hair turned nightmare black and fell out of it's ponytail. Her green happy eyes turn a mocking purple. Her smile, evil. Her out fit changed to old rank 2 summoner clothes.
" hate these clothes!" She muttered.
" Where is your black leather outfit?" I said, recalling what she used to wear.
" Thanks to you and my worthless brother this is all i get now. " She said crossly.
" Ah. Fits you. What do you want?" I asked.
" Aw come on Dino! How come you always let my brother talk to you?" Moon said.
" Because Sig has a heart " I answered.
" Whatever. " Dark moon rolled her eyes, " Just ask him. I used to have a heart. I mean you heard my story!"
" Well you couldn't stand the Maestrom " I said.
" If she had helped me I would have. But no! I had to pass alone. Some friend. " Dark Moon mumbled sadly. I blinked at her. Was Dark Moon sad?
" Dark Moon?" I blinked.
" UGH! Brick! you got some of your 'Kindness' on me when you rebuilt me.." She muttered.
" What?" I blinked.
" When you rebuilt yourself after the test, I stole some of your energy to rebuild myself." She said.
" what? I thought you were rebuilt when I looked at you that day after we saved the nexus and was trying to fix the leaders. " I blinked.
" No. That just gave me the strength to keep myself together. " Dark Moon said.
" Oh.." I mumbled, " what do you want?"
" Thorn and Hero did not die in vain. you will learn this soon. Also.." Dark Moon said, circling me, " I want a final battle. Just you and me. Top of the Nexus. Winner takes all"
" You want the pieces.." I Growled.
" No. Darkitect wants you. " Dark moon said, pointing at me.
" Hmm.. " The anger and sadness still burned inside me. I wanted to say deal but I knew that would be just what she wants.
" Accept. " Myth's voice surprised me but it pushed me enough to say.
" Deal. Final show down. Winner takes all. " I said.
" Ha. Till we meet at the top, Diana. " Dark Moon laughed. She held out her hand. Epic fail, I took it. We shook but pain ran through my body.
" OW! " I cried. Falling to my knees. My hand glowed lightly of purple.
" Haha. Fool, I'm as much pure Maelstrom as before. " Dark Moon laughed, " Till our Battle." And with that she disappeared in a puff of purple smoke. I let out a long sigh.
" Its time to make History, Din-" I started to say to myself but stopped.
" Its time to make History, Diana. " . . . . .

Chapter 41: The Plan.

I ran up the stairs of my house, then back down them and down the hall. I grabbed something from the living room and ran back up to my office. I was such in a hurry to plan out my battle. My thought where all mixed up though. I kept coming up with something then realized that Dark moon would do something else and so on. I whacked my head against the plastic desk.
" Why am I trying to plan? Shouldn't I do- well wait I had a plan there too.. Ugh.. " I mumbled to myself.
" Here's a plan. Go up, grabbed the piece, use the power to smash Dark Moon. " Sigilyph voice would have surprised me but I was kinda used to him popping in. Even now he doesn't surprise me that much.
" Hi Sig. " I said, writing down a few things.
" Sup?" He said, leaning against the wall.
" My brain is going to blow and I have to fight Dark Moon at the top of the Nexus tower.." I sighed.
" I heard that much. " He laughed.
" Hey.. maybe if I bring Droc and you what there with me. " I said, and started to draw out my plan.
" Your deal was just you and her though." Sig blinked.
" We didn't say anything about no one watching. " I said.
" Oh I get it. " Sig said, snapping.
" yep. Ok.. now what.." I sighed.
" Training. " Sigilyph said, equipping his bat lord gear. He walked over to the Window and leaped out. There was a loud crash and yelp. I ran down the stairs and met him around back.
" Remind me never to try that stunt again " He muttered, removing a branch from his hair.
" Ok. " I laughed, equipping my own bat lord gear.
" Now listen. " Sig said, striking at me. I blocked him with my shield.
" Dark Moon tends to fight Random. There is no plan to her attacks, its just attack attack attack. She is also weaker then your first battle with her. " He sent a furry of attacks at me, all of them I blocked. Then I striked at him, failing to lay a bow at his chest, he blocked it with his shield.
" Your best bet is to get the piece. Use it's power to over power her" Sig said. Just when he wasn't looking I tripped him.
" OOF!" he muttered as he hit the ground.
" Right. The trick is to over power her" I laughed.
" And don't ever let your guard down. " Sig said.
" Right " I mumbled . . . . . .

" Um.. Dino can you help me up before the next chapter please? " Sig mumbled.
" OH RIGHT!" I squeaked, and helped him up. . . . . . .

And now for the big epic plot.. The ending of book two.. this is The Fall of Dark Moon!

Chapter 42: To the Top of the Nexus.

It took some time to get Droc to agree to go with me. He kept saying bring Melmen or 7op or someone. It was quite an argument. Finally, I won and ran off to get Swifty to open the elevator.
" Swifty." I called, running into his office.
" yea?" He asked, while typing away on a computer.
" I need to get to the top of the Nexus." I said. He flipped around and faced me.
" Dino.." He breathed.
" All I need you to do is open the door for us. " I said.
" No. I'm coming. I actually heard a little bit of your Argument with Droc. " He said, standing up.
" Thanks Swifty" I smiled. We walked back into the nexus room and Swifty started to feel the pillars for the door.
" Wah. Where is it?" Swifty mumbled crossly.
" May I offer some help?" Blue appeared and tapped one of the pillars. It slide open into a door.
" Good Luck, Dino. " He said and disappeared.
We all walked into the Elevator. Swifty typed in the code and Droc braced himself. I did the say as soon as Swifty pressed the up button. Going up was not as bad as it was going down. It just felt like you were on one of those dropping towers. It didn't go up that fast either.
" Well. This is smooth. " I said. Just then the elevator stopped. Click. The elevator buzzed. Click. Another clicking sound and then. Click! The Elevator shot up like a rocket! it was going so fast there wasn't any gravity in the elevator! Swifty floated around aimlessly. I tried to brace myself in a corner. Droc floated upside down with his hands on his ears. Sig floated calmly around.
" THIS IS SMOOOTH RIGHT?" Swifty yowled.
" SPOKE TO SOON!" I squeaked.
" AAAAAAAAAAAHHH!!!" Droc screamed.
Clank! the Elevator stopped.
" OOF!" We all breathed as we hit the floor. The doors slide open. Like the bottom. The flooring here was clear but it flashed blue every few minutes. The top of the Nexus itself, looked like someone had turned an squid over and stuck it in a hole and have it's arms waving everywhere. Imagination flowed everywhere. It was so calm. as we stepped close to the top of the Nexus, the clear floor turned to a pretty grassy field with flowers and butterflies.
" whoa. Hmm. I wonder." Swifty said, closing his eyes. The pretty grassy field turned to the top of a roaring water fall.
" Whoa. " I laughed.
" Cool! we can control what our surroundings look liked! " Swifty giggled.
I looked around smiling. When my eyes fell on what I saw I frowned.
" Dino what is it?" Droc asked, following my gaze. Sig was staring at what I saw too. he had been quiet this whole trip. We both stared at what we came here to do. We both looked right at the Piece... and Dark moon . . . .
