Ok here is the next chapter! Sorry about the wait!

Chapter 23: The Test.

Myth led me into the nexus. At first, I wondered if we would go right through it but once we reached the centered we started walking down this long stair case.
" You must pass a test. This will show you something you do not know. It shall show you something from the past, present, and future. Good luck." Myth said. Myth looked different in the nexus. Instead of the cheerful little girl from she normally uses she was a tall girl about 18, She had bright orange hair and deep green eyes. She sorta reminded me of Kirby ( If you remember my friend) in a way. We stopped at a tall blue door.
" Remember. When you see Droc, call his name. IF you fail this test. You will not be able to rebuild and I'll have too..." Myth said trailing off. Her green eyes turned brown and she looked down at the ground.
" Just don't fail me." Myth mumbled. ( How does she look familiar in some way?)
The Door opened and Myth pushed me through. There was a bright flash of light.

When the light cleared, and I could see again. I was holding a cardboard sword, with a cardboard vest and a cardboard bow in the other hand. My sister sat in front of me. She hand purple marker all over her face and purple colored cardboard vest and claws. Her red hair was tied back with a funny looking hat on. She also wore red sunglasses. Kirby stood next to me. Her pink hair was in a pony tail, she had cardboard green as well. I took a quick look around to realize that I was in my house when I was seven.
"Huh.." I mumbled.
" Diana! thats not what you are suppose to say!!!" Kirby squeaked jumping up and down.
" OH sorry." I said, I guess we are playing nexus force. We always made Sarah be the stromling, I was always the sentinel, and Kirby was always a venture.
I started saying my silly little speech that I always told Sarah before attacking her. I felt kinda dumb doing this, yet I think every thing I'll do in this will feel dumb to me. (Now I understand why you grow up. Being seven again feels more like being two!)
Once I finished my little speech Kirby shouted charge and we both dashed up to Sarah and tapped her with our weapons. Kirby pretended to shot Sarah. Sarah attacked us too. She hit me with her purple claws. I pretended to smash.
" No!!" Squeaked Kirby.
" Snack time!" My mom called. Everyone dropped their weapons and ran to the Kitchen. I stayed behind.
" OK what.." I thought then I heard my dad talking. I sneaked into the back room where he was talking with Kriby's father, Mr Harper.
" I know I shouldn't had left the Nexus Force, Jack, But I didn't want to worry my wife." My father said.
" I know. But why pretend you hate it?" Mr Harper asked.
" I don't want the kids joining." My father muttered.
" They got a while. Don' they?" Mr Harper said.
" No. The age limit is going down. Soon two year olds will be able to join!" Dad muttered.
" If this is bothering you so much, Maybe you should go back." Mr Harper said.
" NO." My Father shouted, " I might had been the best assembly they had. I might have been a great hero. BUT I'm NOT going back. Never. "
I blinked and walked to the kitchen. " My father was in the nexus force??" I thought. We I entered the Kitchen everything changed. I was Kirby now, I had a backpack on and like always my pink hair was tied up in a pony tail. My mother ( Or Diana's in this view) was crying and Mrs Harper ( Or my Mom in this view) making pie and trying to cheer my mom up.
" He let my little girl join... Sarah is gone.." My mom cried.
" He did it to let her find Diana. Kirby is going to join for the same reason." Mrs Harper said.
" I know. But why did it have to be sarah.. poor sarah..." My mom sighed.
" She'll be alright! " I said, " I'm going to join and I'll take care of sarah. "
My mom looked up, " Oh Thank you Kirby."
" Good Luck dear, You better hurry the ship takes off any moment now." Mrs Harper called.
The area changed again as a walked out of the Kitchen. Now, I was Dino again, I was about 18, My blue hair out hanging around my shoulders my blue eyes stared at a grave. My mom was crying her head off and Sarah/Victini was sniffing.( It was Funny, Victini looked about 16, I never could picture my sis so old like that and brave like she looked right now) Kirby was patting Victini on the shoulder. Droc stood behind us. He looked 18 too, His hair was still a navy blue. He even had a Navy blue start of a beard. He stood next to a coffin, his eyes staring off into the blue.
" He died with hero's honor Dino." Kirby said.
" Bye Dad.. Thanks for letting me join the force. We found Diana.." My sister whispered.
I blinked, " Something here..." Then I got it.
I stood up and turned to droc.
" DROC! REBUILD ME!" I yelled. Everyone looked at me. The Coffin few open and my pieces started flying out. Droc looked young again I stood in the nexus tower. Droc's eyes started glowing blue.
" DARK MOON! NOOO!" Myth voice made me jump. I span around to see a shadow coming at me.
" Rebuild." Dark Moon laughed.
" Droc.. Build faster!" I screamed as Dark Moon came at me. The last pieces Flew onto my body. there was a flash of light and Dark Moon let out an evil laugh.

" Dino..?" Droc asked.
" oow.." I muttered.
" SHE'S ALIVE!!!" Tine cheered.
" how did ya do it Droc.." Drew mumbled.
" Welcome back Dino." 7op said.
" Thanks. Hey where is Sigilyph?" I asked.
" Um... In the closet." Tine mumbled.
" Tine... " I sighed, " Go get him."
Tine walked away and everyone started laughing.
" Thank you." Dark Moon's laugh echoed. I looked to see her with an evil smile standing in front of me, " Till next Time Dino." She laughed and disappeared.
Dark Moon didn't really bother me. There was something else I needed to find out. . . . .

This might be a short chapter. . . But here I go.

Chapter 24: Its in the family.

First was First. I wanted to check how my sister was doing. Of course I wasn't going to go find her. I walked to the Paradox area. Vanda was busy pressing buttons on a computer.
" Hello Vanda." I said, and saluted her.
" Good Day." Vanda mumbled.
" How is your working going?" I asked.
Vanda turned to me and laughed, " Just tell me what you came for Dino."
" Um.. Well I was wondering how Victini is doing." I said.
" She is doing find. She has been working in the assembly section with her friend SuperStar." Vanda said, " I'm quiet surprised on how skilled she is. I thought she would have some problems like you did. "
" And she didn't...?" I asked.
" Not at all. She even flew her first rocket without auto." Vanda said, turning back to her work. I was speechless. My own sister is better then I was! I didn't have anything more to say to Vanda so I dismissed myself. After taking a walk around the tower ( I needed to think...) I walked to the Venture area. I normally never talk to Hael storm, But right now is different.
" Mr Storm?" I asked walking into his office.
" aye girl! Just call me by Hael." Hael replied, " What can me do for ya?"
" Um.. Has there been any new recruits for Venture lately?" I asked shyly.
" Why do you want to know that, Dino." Drew's voice made me jump.
" Just because..." I muttered.
" Did Duke send ya?" Hael asked.
" No sir." I said.
" ARG! Stop with the sirrr and Mrrrr and blah blah blah." Hael muttered.
" Sorry S- I mean.. Hael" I mumbled.
" Arg." Hael muttered, " Sentinels are a bunch of fancy wancys.."
" What? You don't want respect?" I asked crossly.
Hael let out a laugh when I said that, " Oh. Cat got claws." He laughed.
" Can you answer my question?" I asked, " Sir." I added.
Hael faced me then started laughing again, " Oi girrrl." He laughed.
I looked at Drew and mouthed " Does that mean yes?"
Drew smiled and Mouthed " yes."
" No new recruits YET. Though there was a ship that took off today." Hael said, " If all goes well she'll landed her're by to'marrow. "
" Thank you Hael." I said and Dismissed myself.
" Surrre. " Hael mumbled turning back to a map he was making, " Quiet a girrl.."
Drew caught me just as I started towards the assembly area.
" Hey Dino. Why did you ask Hael about that?" he asked.
" Cause. I'm trying to figure some stuff out." I said.
" Aye." Drew said with a smirk.
" Venture." I muttered.
"What?" Drew asked.
" You know I have no idea what Aye means.." I muttered.
Drew just smirked at me. I rolled my eyes at him and walked towards OverBuild's office.
" Hey Victini! Whats up?" I heard Drew say. I looked over my shoulder to see my sister carrying a large box down the hall, followed by a young boy in assembly gear. They both looked almost rank 2.
" Nothing Drew!" Victini said cheerfully and walked away.
" Yep. Nothing." Her friend ( Who I was guessing was SuperStar) said.
I walked the rest of the way to OverBuild's office.
" Sir." I said walking in.
" Hello!" OverBuild said looking up from his work, " How can I help you?"
" Um.. Can I ask you something sir?" I asked.
" Go ahead." Overbuild said, turning back to his work.
" well um.." I started, " Was my father once an assembly?"
Overbuild looked up for a second then said, " Name.."
" Jack .." I muttered.
" Jack what?" Overbuild asked. My face turned red.
" Um.. Jack.. Raptor.." I mumbled.
"Ah. yes. Your Father was one of the best assembly we had." Overbuild said, " He dropped out because his Wife disliked the Nexus force. Even though Her parents were both in the Nexus force. I believe she even tried to join to. "
" Really..? What happen?" I asked.
" Her imagination spark wasn't strong enough. She couldn't used any of the gear nor even join a faction." Overbuild said, " It was a shame too. She was a fine fighter."
" Thank you sir." I said.
" Anytime." Overbuild said with a smile.
I left his office and sat down next to the Nexus. I pulled out my notes and started writing down what I had learned. " Ok. So Kirby might be joining the force... I come from a family of Nexus force agents... And my sister is way better then me.. Why do I have a feeling I took from my mom's side?" I thought to myself. I looked at my other notes then remembered the code that I needed to give to swifty.
" Sigh.. leave it to me to forget something like this." I muttered and went off to find swifty. . . . .
