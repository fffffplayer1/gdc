Chapter 22: Sigilyph's past.

I sat on my couch at my prop. The T.V wasn't on. ( i don't really care for T.V any more.. mostly because all my favorite shows were about nexus force and I'm part of it now so.. whatever) actually i wasn't doing anything but staring at the wall and thinking. " I didn't know Sigilyph was that strong...." i thought, " I didn't know he could change peeps into dragons.." I sat back on the couch and closed my eyes to think harder. " Why.. why did he do that? i don't understand.." i sighed to myself.
I must have fallen asleep, cause when I opened my eyes Droc was sitting in the chair across from me. He was reading a book and when i sat up he put it down and said, " Well morning to you." " aaww be quiet!" i giggled. " Dino. . . " Sigilyph's Voice mumbled. " Sigilyph? you there?" i wondered. " yes.." He mumbled. " Sigilyph.. why did you do that to Tine?" I asked him. I heard him take a deep breath then he answered, " Its my job to make the nexus force weak. I am the spy. Sometimes i must infected minifigs. When Nexus jay was infected that day. . . it was me. I've been doing it all.. its all me.. I used to not care, I used to laugh as i watched them infect and join us, But ever sense we started talking. . . ." He trailed off, " I've changed. . . . you are to be infected for the maelstrom. . . So we can find the pieces." I frowned, I had started to seen Sigilyph as a friend too... " Dino.. I've never seen how cold and lonely it is . . . All this time.. i was looking for a way to come great or powerful.. when i think all i was looking for was a friend..." Sigilyph Finished. " Aaww Sigilyph.. that's sweet." i thought. " But.. My master still have control over me... I have to stay and work for him till i can figure out a way to stop.." Sigilphy said, " uh? am i boring you?" i started to laugh. I laughed so hard that Droc asked me if i was feeling alright. I told him i was ok after i caught my breath. I was telling Droc what Sigilyph told me when Tine came in. " Tine!" Droc and I both said. " HI! ME-SA BACK! and who is Sigilyph again?" Tine said. "uuhh. The guy who infected you." Droc answered. Tine stood there for a second the asked, " where is he?" I buried my vision then pointed to the air where Sigilyph stood. Tine walked right over and stood right in front of of Sigilyph. Then She Punched the spot. " TAKE THAT!" there was a loud crash against the far wall then sigilyph's shaded shape appeared, Then Disappeared again. " Whoa" Droc mumbled. " Tine.. how did you?" I mumbled. " SCORE!" Tine cheered. Droc and i stared in silence, As Tine dance around. "Sigilyph how..?" i wondered. " how.. GOOD QUESTION! HOW DID SHE HIT ME?!!" He said sarcastically but i could tell he was really upset. I was about to ask tine if she had any idea how she did that but was cut off by nexus jay walking in. " sorry about busting in on your small like party. But Lenny wants to see you all." Jay said then lead the way out. We all followed him to a part of the place I've never seen before. We walked through this portal in the brick annex and appeared at a huge base. " welcome to star base. Lenny and his team are over here." Jay said and lead us to a small crowd of People. Lenny flipped around as we came up. " Lenny what do you need?" i asked. " well..." He started but never had time finish because alarms went off. . . . .

Chapter 23: Battle at star base.

We all saw what had set off the alarms. There was a huge maelstrom ship landing at star base. Lenny started to yell out orders to his team, " Blaynii and Kbrick! get the assembly ready and have those turrets up!" Lenny command, " Tine get the sentinel in the front! 7op, paradox behind them and Venture follow me!" With that he Jump up to the portal where we came in and waited with the rest of the venture. Everyone followed orders, the assembly built turrets, The sentinel made a block for the front line, And the Venture followed Lenny up next to the portal. The paradox stood behind the the Sentinel, Ready with their guns and wands. I was only rank 2 ( still...) and during these battles if you get smashed you stay smashed till the end of the battle or forever if you are really unlucky. so instead of following all the sentinel to the front. . . I ran to the back and hid in a box.
The battle began, Huge Maelstrom Monsters Dashed out from the Ship. Horsemen came out and fired at the line of sentinel. For a while, the battle was just shooting. Then the first of the sentinel line broke and everyone charged. The venture people jumped down on top of the maelstrom. I watched as Lenny landed on the back of a horseman, Knocking the rider off. surprisingly the horse let Lenny ride it. Which he did and smashed at least six or seven maelstrom with it till the horse was smashed by a shot from a turret. Next Lenny swung his infected arm and sent everything in his path through the walls or roof. The sentinel ( who seem to have put tine in lead) were fighting together in groups. If one of the sentinel in the group needed to heal, she or he would back up behind his or her partner to heal while the partner fights. I watched Tine trying to heal then her partner about to smash any second. The last blow was coming for him when one of his friends or someone jumped right in front of him and took the attack. The Defender smashed leaving the two he protected still alive. " GAMEKING!!!" cried Tine's partner ( I'm going to guess that "Gameking" was his friend) Tine pushed her partner back to heal and started Fighting. I watched her neat moves. " DivineSilverDingo Please stop crying and heal! don't make gameking's smash a waste of time!" Tine command. Dingo nodded then started to heal himself. I saw Droc fighting beside Melmen in the crowd. They both had there valiant and they smashed everything that came near them. I was startled by a stormling who was right next to my box. " agrh?" it said. I pulled a frying pan out of my bag, popped out of my box, and whacked the stormling on the head till it smashed. I watched Tine's Partner ( Dingo) Get pushed backwards into the vendor. His legs kicked around and he started screaming for help. Tine was up by the portal playing the Dragon Flute. I watched As DragonFire Landed on the maelstrom ship. Tine hopped on her dragon's back and started blasting maelstrom with Fire. Soon.. the battle was over. summoners went around and rebuilt the smashed ( Which included Dingo and GameKing. Dingo had been smashed in the vendor). Lenny was looking around for Nexus Jay as i climbed out of the box. " Hey have ya seen Jay?" He asked. I shook my head. " HEY LENNY! I FOUND JAY! LOOK UP!" 7op called. Lenny and I both looked up to see Nexus jay's body hanging out of the roof. " Oops." Lenny mumbled, Though we were both trying super hard not to start laughing ( i couldn't even keep a straight face). Jay started to kick and tried to get his head out of the roof. A random worker at the star base flew up with a Jet pack and helped him down. " LENNYTHE1ST WHY DID YOU SEND ME TO THE ROOF!!" Jay screamed. " because.." Lenny mumbled, " you got in my way?" Jay was super upset and he just left the star base. " Why did you do that to him?" I asked. " uuh.. No real reason.. I didn't actually mean too." Lenny answered. " Why didn't you tell him you didn't mean it?" I asked. " oh so i should have said," Lenny said then said in a small little kid voice, " Sorry Jay... I didn't Mean too.." I giggled at his joke. He patted me on the shoulder then he walked around and checked his Team.
Duke and Hael came to check on their men at the star base. Duke came up to me. " Overbuild wants to send you and Major on a Mission with Melmen and Droc, Meet Them tomorrow at the Tower. " Duke said then walked over to talk to Tine. I sighed then walked home to get ready for my next Mission. . . .

Chapter 24: Mission to Nexus City

I stood waiting next to Major for Droc and Melmen.When They Arrived, Major asked them what the Mission is. " The Mission is to fix the imagination tanks at Next City." Melmen answered, " All you have to do is guard us" " Kay then, Shall we head out?" major asked. Melmen nodded and we all headed to the Drop Ship Bay. I was a little worried about going to Nexus City.. What if I run into my parents? Actually when the thought crossed my mind i remembered that if my parents do see us they wouldn't come close. Also now that my hair is blue they shouldn't be able to tell it's me, But i was still worried. when we reached the Door for the Drop Ship bay, Major knocked on it three times. The door opened for us and we walked in. Few things you should know about the Drop Ship bay. First, The Drop Ship bay isn't open for everyone. In Fact you are only a loud to enter if you have a Mission or work there ( after i learned this, I figured that's why Lenny go in trouble for taking a drop ship.). The only Drop Ship open for public is the one to Crux. Anyway, back to us. Tine was in the Drop Ship Bay yelling at the leader of the Bay about keeping her dragon here. One of the works led us to our Drop Ship. " Ok.. So who is driving?" I asked. " You" Major said with a sheepish smile. I rolled my eyes then got in the back of the Ship. Major climbed in the Drivers seat and Melmen sat shot gun. Droc sat next to me in the back. We strapped ourselves in, Then Major started up the ship and we took off.
We landed at the same Drop Ship bay that 7op had ran to with me in nexus city. Only Melmen and Droc knew where this " Imagination tanks" were so we followed them. I tried to look down as we walked down the streets. " Here" Droc whispered giving me a Ninja hood. I thanked him then equipped the Hood. It had a ninja feeling to it and it made me more comfortable. Soon we reached a pie shop, Melmen and Droc walked right in. " Uuuhh.." I mumbled. " The Tanks are in here." Droc said. " oh." i said with a nod. We walked up to the back bar of the pie shop. The man behind the bar gave us all a look then asked, " What can i do for ya?" " Pie with Cream. and a Milkshake." Droc said. The man nodded then lets us behind the bar. Droc and Melmen walked through a door and me and Major stood out side of it guarding. Only at the last second did i remember that the pie man was actually one of my best friend's father. I had a best friend when I lived here. Her name was Kirby. I was so glad to have the hood on when she walked right inside the shop. " Oh so the Nexus Force peeps are here? Cool... sigh.. i wish Diana was here.. She would liked to see the tanks.. i even told her i would show her them.." Kirby said. Her Father patted her on the shoulder. Kirby walked right up to us. " So. you are sentinels?" She asked. " yup." answered Major." " cool.. Don't you have funny names?" Kirby asked. " They aren't funny but we do have.. err.. Different ones." Major answered, " Like I'm MajorBrickenstien" Kirby nodded then turned to me." your's? She asked. I knew i couldn't tell her it... She would know it was me.. I always used " UltraDinoWarrior" when we played nexus force. " uuh.. Dino" I answered. " Dino what?" Kirby asked. " Just Dino" I said. To my luck Melmen and Droc came out. " Eerr.. We are missing a piece" Droc mumbled, " and we need to get it.. FAST.". . .
