HEY GUYS! I'M BACK! WHO MISSED ME?! Anyway its back to a chapter or two a day again! ( Er.. Unless school starts up and gets in the way of that.) Anyway here is the next chapter! Thanks for all the post peeps!

Chapter 33: Down the biffy and up the mountain.

It wasn't hard to get permission for the mission. Both, Lenny and 7op signed this forum thingy and gave it to vanda, Who approved it. Swifty picked the team for the mission ( Who were: 7op, Lenny, Droc, And me) Droc, Swifty and I all waited at the docking bay for 7op and Lenny. a few minutes later, A nice size ship landed in front of us. The ship was a deep black with deep green stripes on the side. There wasn't a part of the ship that didn't have some type of weapon on it. The front was decked out with huge blasters and the back had a bomb cannon. the sides had huge zap-cannons with rocket launcher add ons. The top opened up to a huge chain-gun-bazoka. The name of the ship was painted on the top of the right side, It was called the "light-blast". I stared in awe at the ship as Lenny jumped out. " This used to be my team's ship." He said with a straight face. We all jumped in, Swifty went to the front to set the destination in the ship's GPS. Droc and I went to the back of the ship. I seat belted myself in and droc stood, holding on to the table. swifty came into the back and sat down next to me. " Hello! welcome to the Light blast! taking off now!" Lenny shouted back. " FULL POWER UP!" Laughed 7op. " WHOA WAIT!!!" Screamed swifty, Just as the ship made a huge blast upwards, Sending both droc and swifty into the roof of the ship.
