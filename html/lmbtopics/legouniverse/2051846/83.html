Chapter 33: Sigilyph? (part 1)

 

I didn't know what I was expecting when my vision blurred. Part of me expected to see this world's Sigilyph or Myth standing there. Or even to be confornted by "Dark Void". Instead, I was met with a rather familiar face looking at me.

                "DINO!" Sigilyph yelped.

                "Sig?" I blinked. 

It was Sigilyph alright. He dressed in his blue bat gear without a helmet. Strange enough his pure white hair now had a purple hew to it. He ran up to me and gave me a hug.

                "Dino! I was so worried. We're all worried! I'm so glad I could reach you..." Sig cried.

                "Whoa, whoa Sig! come down," I thought, "how are you here?"

                "Uh. well, we raided Green to save you... only to find out you had already escaped. Then 7op noticed this portal.. and I sensed you inside of it.. and... so... I uh... had Perky infect me......" He trailed off at that last part.

                "Sig! You mean you're infected?!" I gasped.

                "I'm not a thought again! I promise! and being uninfected will be easy, relax. I was a maelstorm before.. so.. I can control myself. I had too, cause.. there was no way to reach you otherwise!"

                "You couldn't had just come in here?"

                "We could've but then we would have no way out."

                "So you guys have a way out?"

                "We do. Uh.. you're not going to like it though."

                "Let me guess, infection?"

                "Well, to explain it-" Sig began but suddenly Castle's voice boomed in my left ear. I let out a yelp and my vision cleared.

                "CASTLE DON'T TALK TO ME WHEN MY VISION IS BLURRY!!" I yowled and held my ears.

"Never... repeat NEVER doubt the Blue hair!"
