Surprise chapter out of no where! JK. Here is the next chapter, sorry I' haven't wrote anything in this story for a while, I've been busy and also trying to keep up with my two mutli writer stories. So yea.. Here is the next chapter!

Chapter 22: In the Work shop

They threw us (Literally) into a big room. Before either of us could get to our feet they shut the door. The room around us looked like a workshop mixed with a house. The walls were a deep black and the floor was a grey carpet covered in oil stains. Two beds hung on the right wall. Work benches lined the left. In the middle of the room sat a pile of paradox gear and some Venture gear.
" Ugh! They aren't going to let us out till I do what they want," Castle muttered crossly.
" They are going to give me to Darkitect aren't they?" I asked.
" Eh. Green doesn't turn down offers as fast as Blue does," Castle sighed. He picked up some of the paradox gear and pulled out a sentinel weapon and started to work.
" What are you..?" I started to ask then realized he was making the Paradox gear Sentinel.
" If you want me to make you a pare, hand me some of your sentinel gear," Castle said. I handed him my knight helmet and shoulder pads. Castle started to work. I watched him pull parts off of the Paradox gear and throw them to the side, replacing them with sentinel pieces. It took him a haft an hour before he was done with the paradox gear and started making me a Venture outfit.
" Why does it take so long?" I asked. I was bored out of my mind. Castle didn't reply till five minutes later.
" Don't rush me," He answered. I sighed and took a nap.

I awoke in a dream. A very strange dream. Well it was strange till Darkitect walked in. He looked pretty crossed with me.
" Can I help you?" I asked. I don't know why i said that. I guess I meant it as an insult since it was what I said to new recruits when they came up to me but Darkitect's expression didn't change.
" To long have you escaped my grasp, Dino," He said as if that meant anything.
"And I plan to do it longer!" I said.
" You'll try. But Gang Red will hand you over," Darkitect sneered.
" Doubt that," I answered, " your Price is too low."
" Scizor works for the maelstrom! Of course he will hand you over," Darkitect laughed.
Before I could ask what he meant, the dream switched to a normal one.

Castle woke me up when he was finished (I was not surprised that I had been asleep for three hours.) I felt a little uneasy from the dream but I didn't mention it too castle. We had enough to worry about. The Pile of Paradox gear had a green glow to it.
" I thought you weren't-" I started to say but castle cut me off.
" Fakes. They'll blow up when they try to equip," Castle explained. He handed me my own pare of Venture and Paradox gear. Castle had made me the Daredevil and shiobi(Spelling). He had made himself all the kits from the two factions.
" Hey! How come I only get one?" I complained.
" Cause I didn't feel like making them for ya!" Castle answered with a smirk. I was about to hit him when the door slide open. . . . .

Sorry about all the typos! next chapter comes soon!
