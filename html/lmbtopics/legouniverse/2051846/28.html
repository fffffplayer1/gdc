Aw I'm sad on how many few guesses I got. Oh well, And No someone. Dark moon is NOT back. At least not yet.. Here is the next chapter hope you like it!

Chapter 31: Cuteness of death!

Sigilpyh led me out into the plaza. Everything was wrecked. The vendors were empty, The plaza was purple, and a light maelstrom fog covered the grove. Drew and 7op had also been assigned to save Nimbus but they stood dumbfounded staring at the creatures that ran around the plaza.
" Whats wron- Oh.." I started to asked then saw what they were staring at. There in front of us was a cute little puff ball creature. It had big round eyes and a little mouth. Two long ears stuck out of its head. It looked almost like a bunny only it didn't have four legs, it only had two small round feet. It stared up at us smiling.
" The new maelstrom monster is some girls' toy?" Drew breathed.
" Drew. You've said that like six times now." 7op mumbled.
" The New maelstrom monster is some girls' toy?" Drew repeated.
" Drew. You've said that like seven times now." 7op muttered.
" the-" Drew started to repeat but 7op kicked him, " buddy! knock it off!"
" Sorry." Drew blinked.
" I agree with Drew.. Has the Maelstrom ran out of ideas?" I blinked. I mean these things look like the toys I used to beg for when i was younger.
" They are called CuteJabbers." Sigilyph said, " They are more dangerous then butter." " ha! yea right!" Drew laughed.
" No. I'm not kidding. Watch. Go try to hit it." Sigilyph said. Drew shrugged and walked forward.
" See? " He said picking it up and tossing it around. The CuteJabber squeaked as it few and giggled when caught.
" Harmless." Drew said.
" attack it." Sig said.
" Alright alright." Drew said putting the CuteJabber down. He took out his sword and raised it to whack the puff ball. It's eyes went sad and its ears lay flap. Drew stopped his attack.
" Aw MAN!" Drew muttered, " I can't hurt this thing!"
" And then it" Sigilyph said, Like on cue The CuteJabber ear's popped up and it looked right up at drew. A flash of light started glowing in it's mouth.
" What th-" Drew didn't have time to finish before the creature shot a blast of purple light at him. Drew went flying into the brick annex. There was a loud splash and a scream that sounded something like " AAAAHH!!! SHARK!". The CuteJabber turned to us and smiled, making a cute little sound. 7op and I stared blankly as a very wet Drew stomped up to us.
" THAT THING IS EVIL!" He yelled. The CuteJabber's face turned sad again.
" Thats what it does. See, when I was still in the Maelstrom, the Darkitect had been working on these. They weren't working when tested though. Highly likely because the maelstrom who were the testers didn't have any heart. I know this cause they worked on me.. But What they do is take the strongest weakness every nexus force member has, the Caring heart. No one with a kind heart can even come close to hitting it. Once in their guard is down, The Cutejabber blast the victim. One of these things could smash millions..." Sigilyph said.
" Then how do we beat it?" I blinked.
" Is there anyone in the Nexus Force with a dark heart?" 7op wondered.
" Only rebels. And Duke won't let them out if his life depended on it." I asked.
" Dang.." Drew muttered. 7op walked over and started whacking the puff ball with his eyes close.
" Hey look! It works!" 7op said as he whacked the CuteJabber. It made a squeak every time he hit it.
" 7op. You are doing any Damage." Sigilyph muttered. 7op opened his eyes and frowned.
" It was worth the try." 7op shrugged.
" We are doomed..." I muttered.
" Maybe not." Blue appeared in front of me. Next to him stood another ninja with green hands. On his other side stood a ninja with silver.
" Aw Not more you guys!" Drew muttered taking out his crossbow.
" Drew wait." I said.
" Hello. I'm Green. Copy ninja of Hope." The green handed ninja said.
" Hello. I'm Silver, Copy ninja of Loss." The Silver handed ninja said.
" Hi I-" I started to say but Green cut me off, " We know who you are Dino. You are well known through out the Copy Ninja kingdom."
" Um.. Okay.." I mumbled.
" We can to offer help. For a price of course." Silver said.
" Well.. None of us can get rid of these.. So.. Whats the price?" I blinked, " Imagination?"
" No." Green said.
" Um.. We will tell you the Price after we have finished." Silver said, There was a hint of shyness in his voice.
" But what if-" I started but Blue cut me off, " Just go with it."
" Go a head and take care of these things please.." 7op blinked.
" Deal accepted." Green said, She turned to the open field and raised her hands. All the Cutejabbers turned sad. Silver stepped forward and Raised his hands. all the CuteJabber started to cry. One by one, They smashed. Soon all of them were gone. Blue stepped forward and raised his own hands. The maelstrom cleared.
" Done." Green said.
" Ok. Now whats the price?" Drew blinked.
" well.. Um.." Green mumbled shyly.
" Say it." 7op said.
" We want to be your friends..." Silver mumbled looking away.
" What?" I blinked.
" We don't have any friends in the kingdom. Only Blue. When Blue said he made a friend.. we wanted one as well." Green mumbled.
" Oh. Well sure I'll be your friend. " I said with a smile.
" Yea. Sure." Drew and 7op agreed.
" Sure! Come stop by my cave in AG whenever!" Sigilyph smiled.
" Thank you." Silver said and all of the copy ninja Disappeared.
" Well done." I said. A loud squeak made us all turn around.
" Aw man! They missed one." I muttered. . . . . .
