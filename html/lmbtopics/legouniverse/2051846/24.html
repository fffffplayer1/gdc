Chapter 26: First stop. The bottom.

Swifty ran off to get 7op and Droc for the mission. GameKing led Drew and I to the Nexus.
" Does anyone even know where the door thing is?" Drew asked.
" Ye- wait.. No.." GameKing said.
" Fail.." I mumbled.
" I know where it is." A voice said from behind us. We all flipped around to see one of the hologram ninjas.
" WHO ARE YOU?" I yelled sorta awkwardly.
" Hehe. Calm Down. I only wish to help." The Ninja said.
" Well... Who are you?" GameKing asked.
" YES! FINALLY!" Swifty cheered. He dashed up right to the Ninja.
" Swifty. I'll answer that later." The Ninja said.
" How?!" 7op muttered walking up, Followed by Droc.
" ITS ONE OF YOU!" Droc yelled, " You looked like Melmen!"
" Can we please stay on topic?" The Ninja asked.
" Fine. where is the door?" I asked. The Ninja walked over to one of the pillars of the Nexus. He tapped on the wall three times. A door opened.
" Only Four may enter." The Ninja said.
" Ok. So Drew, Swifty, Dino, and Me right?" Game King said.
" Sorry King. But I think we should go all four factions. And Dino has to come.. so that means Its Drew, Dino, Droc, and me." Swifty said. GameKing stared at Swifty for a few minutes and then said.
" Fine. . . I got a mission to do anyway." With that he left.
" Talk to you later!" I called after him.
" Sure." GameKing called.
" I'm going to work on one of my props. Good luck guys." 7op said and ran off.
The four of us left all turned to the Ninja.
" In. Then Type in the code. Press Down or Up." The ninja said and disappeared.
" Talk about instruct and go." Drew blinked.
We all walked into the Room. Inside looked like an elevator. There was a key pad on the right wall. And handles lined the left wall.
" Odd elevator." I mumbled. Swifty ran over to the key pad and typed in "12T0CA". The pad blinked then turned into a up arrow and Down arrow.
" Up first? Or Down?" Swifty asked.
" Down." Drew said.
" OK!" Swifty said the pressed the Down arrow.
(Ever been on one of those elevator rides? You know like the tower of terror? Well do you remember the feeling of falling? Ok well thats what it felt like in this elevator.)
There was a loud clank. Then out of no where, the elevator started free falling.
" OH MY PIIIIIIIIIEEEEEEEE!!!" Droc was able to gasp before we all went flying against the roof. We all screamed as the elevator fell. Elevator music switched on and the elevator slowed down enough to not put enough force to hold us up. ( I bet you can guess what happened next. Yes. FACE PLANT)
" Ow..." I mumbled sitting up in the now slow moving elevator.
" Why does everything have to be like this? I mean first its the dragon in the cave at GF. Then its the out house that has a built in slide. NOW THERE IS A ELEVATOR THAT MAKES YOU FEEL LIKE YOU ARE FALLING TO YOUR DEATH???!" Droc yelled kicking the wall.
" Droc calm down." I said. He sat down in the corner muttering to himself for the rest of the ride. after a few minutes, There was another a loud clank and the elevator stopped. The doors slid open. . . . .

And here is the chapter you have ALL been waiting for.

Chapter 27: The Bottom of the Nexus.

I walked to the edge of the elevator and looked out. There was pretty much NOTHING. Just open space.
" Uuh.." I mumbled, " There is nothing here." I leaned forward a bit and lost balance.
" Oh my- HELP!!" I screamed waving my arms in the air. Swifty grabbed at me ( sense he was the closest) But missed. I fell. This was the end. THUD.
" OW!" I muttered as a face planted into something.
" Oh invisible floor." Swifty breathed.
" Yea. I knew that. . " I mumbled. My body was shaking, Remember how I'm afraid of heights? Well try being afraid of heights then stand on a clear floor that looks down into the black Maelstrom space storm thingy. The red eyes stared up at me. Drew helped me up.
" Man.. This place could go for miles." Swifty muttered, looking around. He flipped around to face us then let out a gasp.
" GUYS! LOOKED!" Swifty squeaked pointing behind us. Slowly, Drew, Droc, and I turned around to face the bottom of the Nexus.
" Oh my.." Drew mumbled.
" Did not see this coming.." Droc muttered.
" THE BOTTOM OF THE NEXUS IS A GIANT PLANT???" I yelled with surprise. Yes, There it was. The Nexus came out of what looked like a plant. Sort-a like a bush in a way, Only with huge leaves. The Nexus itself came out of a flower at the top of the bush. The pelts of the flower glowed with imagination. Looking back at the leaves of the bush I realized they were infected. The trunk of the bush was glowing a mix of Blue and Purple. The elevator, Which was right in front of us, Came out of a long pole.
" Amazing isn't it?" A voice said from behind us. We all flipped around to see three telegram ninjas.
" You.." I said, " Who are you guys..?"
" Time we tell you." One laughed.
" I am Red." another one said, Taking a step forward and showed us his red hands.
" I am The Copy ninja of War. No one can beat mean in a fight." Red said.
" I." A ninja with white hands stepped forward, " am White. I am the Copy Ninja of truth. No secret can be hidden from me forever."
The last ninja stepped forward He had Blue hands, " I am Blue, Copy ninja of friendship and trust. . . . We are Copy Ninjas. We know all your secrets. We could trick your best friend or own mother into thinking we are you."
" You... you.. you..." I mumbled. . . .
