Note: Hi! hope u enjoy this story. note if u read this story first then " The Nexus force adventure" this story might give some spoilers to that story.. also all the names i use ( well not the minifig names) are fake. just to let u know.. anyway plz enjoy and read this one!

Chapter 1 : Nexus Force.


Hi my name is Diana, and i live in nexus city with my parents and my sister Sarah. Ive been having the same dream about joining the nexus force for about a week now and i know it has to mean something.. will i ever find out? nope.. see my parents .. (uhh.. the right word would be)Disapprove of the nexus force.. in their opinion the force is just a bunch of kids running around fighting purple things.... i think the nexus force is cool .. tho im only aloud to read the paper...
so here i am now. sitting on my bed reading the nexus news paper. i ran my hand through my curly brown hair. My sister ran up and down the halls. i watch the door way as she ran back and forwards. her red hair flying. i rolled my eyes and looked back at the paper. my mom came into my room and looked at me. " reading the paper again? its bed time" my mom sighed. " yea yea i know" i said and got under the coves and turned over, " night." my mother sighed and turned out the light and closed the door.i fell asleep fast.
i was in the middle of the part in my dream where the ship crash when i awoke. i looked at the clock it said "1:00" "one a-clock???" i sighed. there was a knock on the door. i jumped up from my bed and walked down stairs. i looked out the peek hole in the door to see a smiling face. i opened the door to find a boy. he had light blue hair and Paradox gear on ( well.. only the pants and shirt)... a Nexus force guy? he was rank 3 in space murder gear... i looked back up at his face. "Hello! and sorry to wake ya.. mind if i come in and crash on the couch?" he asked tho he had a sheepish smile on his face. part of me said " close the door in his face. " the other part said " let him in." i let him in. " u cant make any noise and u must be in the closet by five. " i whispered. " why..?" he asked. " because my parents dont like the nexus force! they will call the cops if they see u!" i answered. i sat down in a chair and he sat on the couch. " Im 7op, by the way" he said. " nice to meet u. Im Diana." i said. i started asking him all these questions about the nexus force and he answered every single one! he was in the middle of telling me a story about the time he fought an ape called Roo Morg, when we heard a loud crash. 7op was up and had his guns and shoulder pads equipped. " stay back." he said. i watch as the door broke open and a maelstrom spiderling ran in. i screamed and 7op shot at it. soon the spiderling smashed.. but it wasnt the last.. " go and get your family and get OUT of the house. NOW!" 7op command. i nodded and ran down the hall. i ran right into my parents room. " MOM! DAD! WAKE UP! there are maelstrom in the house! we have to get out!!!" i squeaked. my parents sat up and rubbed their eyes. " wha??" my father mumbled. 7op walked in the room with my sister who was bouncing around him. " sorry. but uuh.. we got to get out of here!" my parents got out of bed and followed 7op out of the side of the house ( he had to blast a hole in the wall cause the maelstrom was all over the living room..) my parents and sister ran one way. 7op and i ran the other. " stay here" said hiding me. i peeked over to see the maelstrom had stopped to get me. " 7OP!!!!!" i screamed. " oh man!!" he sighed and ran and grabbed me onto his back and started running again. " oh man! u are heavy!" he mumbled. " just run!' i cried. 7op pulled out a walkytalky. " calling in back up plz in nimbus city." he spoke into it. " got it 7op. first hanger plz" a voice answered back. the maelstrom followed us like it was water. it splashed against the walls and smashed right through cars. we turned into a space ship hanger mark 1. a drop ship sat waiting for us. we dived in ( sending me into the back wall) the door to the ship closed and we took off. " Lenny! where is back up??" 7op asked. " isnt one man back up enough?" The minifig driving asked. " no" 7op said with a sheepish smile." boys.." i mumbled. " who's the girl and why were the maelstrom chasing her?" Lenny asked. " Her name is Diana.. and I dont know why the maelstrom wants her..." 7op answered. i sighed and turned over on the hard melt floor of the drop ship. " LENNYTHE1ST!!!! WHAT ARE U DOING WITH A DROP SHIP????!!!" a voice yowled through the radio. " uuh.. helping 7op.." Lenny mumbled. i didnt hear the reply cause i fell fast asleep.

I awoke on a bed in a brick built house. i was confused. " where am i? where is sarah? uuh.." i thought. i remembered everything once 7op and Lenny walked in. Lenny mumbled. " WHAT?!???!!!" i yelled, " why???" " you said it yourself. your parents highly disproved of the nexus force. we even tested them. you need to be in nexus force protection. your parents would aloud that. so we lied to them." 7op answered. I stared at them both for a sec.. taking it all in. " so.. whats going to happen to me?" i asked. " well that was fast" Lenny said with a sheepish smile. (actually that was my play name i always did when my sister and i would play nexus force, so i thought it would be a good name. tho now that i think about it my parents might- oh wait.. never mind) " ok then Dino" Jay said, " lets go get you your gear! shall we?" we all nodded and we walked off to nimbus station.

" kay works for me" i said with a sheepish smile. " hey Dino, Paradox rocks" 7op said with a wink. " yea yea but venture rules" Lenny said patting my shoulder. Lenny and 7op looked at each other with sheepish smiles. i knew they wanted me to join one of their factions.. but... " I want to join sentinel" i said. Lenny and 7op both frowned at me. i gave them a sheepish smile. " Are ya sure?" asked jay. " Never been more sure about something in mah life. oh and i want to start with the samurai please." i said. " kay!" jay said. he gave me a book with the rank one samurai gear. i sat down and read threw the book then equipped the gear. the faction gear felt so powerful and my new control over my Imagination was even cooler ( actually i had spent at least an hour quick building random stuff in ag). " kay now that you are all equipped how about we go smashing in GF?" Lenny asked. " sure!" i smiled.
we headed off to GF. it wasnt that far either. " question.." i asked, " arent there wars and stuff against the maelstrom? How can we just go and smash in places?" "Yes there are battles and stuff. but there are also controlled areas, called Free Zones. there is just enough imagination to keep the maelstrom in these areas controlled. also the imagination in the air disinfects the drops so you can pick them up." Lenny answered. 7op and him stopped and showed me some dropped rares. " neat." i said. Lenny and 7op placed there rockets on the launch pad. they blasted off. i copied them placing my own rocket on the launch pad and blasted off. " wait.. " i mumbled as my rocket started up.. " I DONT KNOW HOW TO DRIVE A ROCKET!!!!" i screamed and took off....

SORRY!" i squeaked climbing out of my rocket. " see why you always move?" Lenny said with a sheepish smile as he helped 7op up. " note to self.. NEVER let a new girl drive a rocket without telling her to put it on auto " sighed 7op. " THERE WAS AN AUTO BUTTON??!!!" i screamed as i picked up the remains of my rocket ( which was now just a bunch of bricks. go fig. smash my own rocket on my first day ) " why are you guys helping me anyway?" i asked as 7op and Lenny helped me clean up my mess i made. " just never let Lenny come pick you up in a drop ship. kay?" 7op said with a sheepish smile. " HEY! i thought i was a loud to take a ship! I'm the leader of one of the best teams in the force!" Lenny said. " great..." i thought to myself, " Lenny and 7op arent my friends. they are just in trouble so they have to take care of me" i sighed and started off down the trail, followed by Lenny and 7op.
we stopped at a free area called brig rock. there were already a bunch of minifigs here. tho finding a spot was easy cause everyone moved out of the way for Lenny ( i dont think he was kidding about leading one of the best teams in the nexus force.) " ok this is easy. just watch out for the attacks and fight back" Lenny instructed. " kay " i said just as an admiral came up and blasted me right in my face. i was slammed against a wall and smashed. everything went dark for a second then i felt my imagination put myself back together. i walked back to where Lenny and 7op where smashing and picked up my dropped coins. " Good try!" 7op said. Lenny or 7op would fall in beside me and give me a quick tip. soon i had over enough tokens and money to buy myself some rank two gear. Lenny and 7op lead me to the pirate camp..
