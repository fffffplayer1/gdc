Chapter 38: The Dumpster ( part two )

Hero let out a scream as he fell through the floor. Legend made a grab for him but almost fell himself. Thorn caught Legend and pulled him back up. The sicking sound of Hero smashing echoed through out the building.
" Hero!" I cried. I tried to run to the hole where fell so I could see if i could rebuild him but Thorn caught my shoulder.
" Dino. Hero is gone. Just leave him. " Thorn said. Tears ran down my check but I just nodded.

We continued walking through the Building. Finally we reached a door. It was made of steel but badly rusted. The handle rolled around aimlessly on the floor. Thorn picked up the Handle and placed it in the door. With a small turn, the Door fell backwards with a loud crash.
" Ok.. I wouldn't had bothered with the handle if I knew it would just do that " Thorn blinked and walked into the room. The Room was pretty much empty other then the safe on the far wall. The Safe had bombs all around it. the Wires must be in the the wall of the safe or something cause there was no wires in sight.
" Ok. I'm going to unlock the safe and grab the book. Run when i say go. " Thorn said, taking the key from me.
" Right. " Legend and I said that the same time.
" One.. " Thorn said slowly turning the Key in the safe.
" Two, " He removed the key and opened the door to it. There was a loud click and a ticking sound
" THREE! RUN " Thorn screamed grabbing the book inside the Safe. . . . . . .

Chapter 39: Escape.

We all dashed off as fasted as we could. I was running faster then I've ran before. Thorn, who had started running last, was pretty fast ( Or I'm slow) he caught up to me after a minutes. Legend was tripping and trying to run behind us.
" TO THE STAIRS!" Thorn screamed and pointed to the stone steps. They were covered in plants but none of us really cared. We dashed right up those steps. Legend tripped a few times but we manged to make it to the second set of stairs before the first explosion when off. The second set were made of fine wood. We quickly dashed up these sets. We were almost at the roof when.
" OOF!" Legend mumbled as he tripped onto the step.
" Legend! " Thorn yowled and dragged him up the rest of the stairs.
" THORN WHERE IS THE BOOK! " I screamed when i realized he didn't have it.
" Oh dang it.. " He muttered running down the stairs, " GET LEGEND IN THE ROCKET! "
Just like Mew had promised, there was a get away rocket parked on top of the roof. She sat in the drives seat listening to some music. I dragged an out cold Legend into the rocket. Thorn tossed the Book at me. With quick moves I caught the book and turned back to see Thorn. He was standing on steps.
" THORN COME ON! " I screamed.
" I can't! GO! " Thorn yelled.
" WHAT? NO!" I yelled and started to get out of my seat.
" MEW! GO!" Thorn screamed.
" Oh! KAY! " She said and slammed her foot on the gas.
" AAAAAHHH!! " I screamed falling back into my seat, " NO NO NO NO! THORN!" I Screamed as we blasted away. Thorn waved us a sad good bye as we left. I looked away when the explosion went off...

Mew gave us a ride all the way to the Nexus tower. I couldn't believe that Both Hero and thorn had died. I held the Diary in my hands. We landed in the Nexus tower pretty soon, Vanda was first to greet us.
" Thank bricks you are back. Duke has been getting worse. " Vanda said, " Where are the other two sentinel?" She blinked when the three of us climbed out.
" Didn't make it out. " Mew said carelessly, " Aint surprised. No one makes it out of the Dumpster alive unless they got pure luck. "
" They died great Heroes. " Vanda sided.
" wasn't one named hero?" Mew blinked.
" Um.. Yes.. " Vanda blinked blankly. I handed Vanda the Diary and we all walked into Duke's room. . . . .

-DinoQueen
