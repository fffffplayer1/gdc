Chapter 15: The Gangs

I sat against the wall of the Blue gang ship next to my friends for what felt like days. It was only three hours though. 7op and Drew tried to talk to the adventurer. He didn't reply much at first but when they started telling jokes and being.. Them.. he joined in. Mew walked into the room after the endless three hours. She looked really grumpy too.
" Untie them." She commanded.
" What..?" The space ranger and knight had returned as well and they both looked confused.
" You heard me." Mew said, " Tank2. Go get their weapons." The knight stared at her with astonishment but followed orders and dashed off. Castle and the Space ranger untied us.
" Um. You are letting us go?" Sig blinked, freeing Perky from the cage they had put him in.
" Where are you suppose to go? You don't know where anything is. I'm just not keeping you as prisoners any more." Mew answered.
" What did Darkitect say for the price?" Castle asked.
" Foolishness." Mew hissed.
" Aw your kidding me! She is unharmed, tied up and weaponless and Darkitect offers a low amount? YOU KIDDING ME?" The space ranger threw up his hands which knocked his helm off to show deep black hair and a surprisingly kind face.
" Zero. Your helm." Mew muttered. The space ranger quickly picked it up and put it back on. The knight returned with our gear. Surprisingly, they had manged to get ALL Of our gear. ( If you remember, when we were captured we didn't have our helms on) I stuffed my helm into a pocket of my cape ( I did not know Valiant capes had pockets) and my guns into their sheaths.
" So Darkitect gave a bad price for Dino so you are not keeping us as prisoners?" 7op blinked.
" Yep" The Space ranger muttered.
" Aw man so that's why we are letting them go." The knight, who had missed the news said.
" Do whatever you want with them. But don't smash them, hurt them or should them to the escape pods or launchers." Mew said and left.
" Well, she doesn't tend to stick around much." Drew smirked.
" Well.. Let us tell you who were are so you at least know the names of your capturers " The knight said, " I'm Tank2"
" I'm Zero80" The space ranger said.
" I'm LegoCastleBuilder" Said the adventurer.
" Weren't you in the Nexus force?" 7op asked.
" We all were." Castle said.
" We had greater abilities then the other peeps in the nexus force. " Zero explained.
" But not in a way the Nexus force saw.. 'Good'" Tank said.
" What do you mean?" Sig asked.
" well.. Like castle, " Zero pointed to Castle for him to tell his own story.
" I was trying out mixing Factions together." Castle said, " Even though I was a sentinel, I got a full rank 3 adventurer kit and start messing with it so it worked for sentinels instead of venture. I believed the Nexus for should stop being for parts in a divided plate. If the Nexus force mixed their kits together..They wouldn't be weak as they are now. Duke found out what I was doing, and Hael got really mad when he heard about it. Duke told me I should just leave choices like that to them and to fight for imagination," Castle stopped for a few minutes almost as if to think then continued, "Mew found me and learned what I was doing. She told me to come with her and join her Gang. So I did"
" So that venture stuff is sentinel powered??" Drew asked.
" Yes, See?" Castle handed him the crossbow. Drew's eyes went wide as he held the crossbow.
" Whoa. That has a weird feeling to it.." Drew said, " Dino, you try" He handed the crossbow to me. I was almost as surprised as Drew. The Crossbow fit perfectly in my hand and had the energy of sentinel weapons. I handed it back to castle.
" Well, that is something.." I said.
" So are you all sentinels?" Sigilyph asked, jumping about the topic we were on before.
" Most of us yes. See Even though there are many gangs, only Gang Blue, Red, Yellow, and Green recruit for the Nexus force. " Tank said.
" That Yellow guy in gang red.. Was he a captured Yellow?" I asked.
" You mean Turbo?? Oh no, He is one hundred percent Gang red but he is an assembly instead of paradox. He is what made gang Red sky rocket.." Zero said coldly.
" The gangs each have a personality, which all depends on their Leader." Mew's voice spooked us all. She came into view.
" even if the people in the gang are kind hearted, if the gang leader is mean then they have to do mean things. Its obey the leader at all cost." Mew continued, " Gang Yellow is more destructive. They Destroy stuff then rebuild it to their use. They are the only gang with a strong power in imagination. Gang Green is rarely seen, They, taking on the venture characteristic, went deep into Sector Z. Gang red, lead by Scizor is almost heartless. They used to be more of a strong foe who knew almost everything about you. Scizor didn't like this Slow study thing and turned Gang red into a bunch of meanies. Gang Blue, is actually the kindest of them all next to Yellow." Mew finished.
" Ok.. " I blinked.
" Now, I can not take Scizor alone, and since you are here, there is no other use for you, and already manged to beat him once. You are going to help us take him down." Mew said. . . . .
