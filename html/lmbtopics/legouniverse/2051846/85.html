Chapter 37: Oh, so that's... my power.... (part 1)

 

For a moment, we all kind of just stood there in awkward silence. Suddenly, Mew started outwardly crying and pushing against the ceiling in an attempt to get down.

"I... I'm sorry!" She cried, "g-get me down from here... please!"

"What's here problem? Sig blinked.

"Uh... Mew's afraid of heights... Well not exactly Heights, she doesn't like being on unstable ground above highs or not having anything under her feet...." Tank replied sheepishly.

"So... why is she up there?" I asked blankly.

"Obviously you sent her up there. It was crazy. She just started floating up...." 7op said in a blank awe.

"PLEASE GET ME DOWWN!!" Mew cried.

"Alright alright.. Sig how do I get her down?" I asked awkwardly.

"I have no clue," Sig answered. A small smirk creeped across his face and I was pretty sure he was enjoying this. I wondered why Sig seemed to dislike Mew? This thought was quickily distracted when suddenly my vision blurred.

 

Rather than the normal everything is fuzzy stuff and only see unstable imagination, I actually saw images above people's heads. Out of curiousity I walked over and touched the one above Sig. The image glowed and suddenly I heard:

"Greatest fear. Losing friends and being alone. Deepest fear. Losing Dino. Other notable fears. Perky infecting the couch. Spiderlings. Darkitect. Dark Moon-" I pulled my hand away from the image quickly and backed up. My Vision cleared and Sig was looking at me with the weirdest expression.

"Dino?" he blinked.

"Fears. I can see fears... and create them..." I blinked.

 

"Never... repeat NEVER doubt the Blue hair!"

(Part 2)

"Fears? So you like.. find people's worst nightmares and then make them see it?" Tank asked.

"Yeah, watch," I said. For whatever reason, I suddenly understood how my powers worked. I walked over to Tank and reached forward. My hand and hair glowed purple and suddenly Tank was binded by a large purple snake. Tank screamed so loudly that everyone had to cover their ears. He struggled and screamed as the snake slowly looked at him and licked his nose with its tounge.

"OKAY OKAY OKAY OKAY!!!! GEEETT IIITTT OOOOFFFF MEEEEEEEEEEE!!!!!!" Tank yelped. I snapped my fingers and the snake disappeared. I also removed Mew's nightmare. She smacked to the floor with a loud thump.

"So.. Dino can create Nightmares?" 7op laughed.

"I'm NOT happy about this.." Mew snarled. She stood a good distance from me with an angery glare. I couldn't stop myself from smirking at the fact that Mew was scared of me. "I demand you disinfect her RIGHT NOW."

"Uh... Mew, wouldn't it be more useful to keep her like this? I mean we could learn Scizor's-" one of the soliders in the back began to say but was interrupted quickily by Mew, "NO!"

"The guy has a point. maybe I should stay like this..." I murmured. I wasn't too happy about the idea of being maelstorm but if I could use this power to our own good... Plus something about using this power more and more seemed very pleasing...

"Uh, no. That won't turn out well. Actually we only have an hour left, so let's get you disinfected," 7op said.

"An hour before what?" I asked.

"OH RIGHT! I FORGOT!" Sig gasped.

"DON'T LEAVE ME HANGING!" I yelped.

Chapter 38: Disinfection is not an easy process (Part 1)

 

So yeah, remember how we disinfected Sig all those months ago? With a HUGE blast of imagination.... Uh, we don't have that now. I personally had no clue how 7op was planning to disinfected us.

 

He led us into a large room full of pipes, tables, buttons, and computers. I felt sick the moment I entered the room. the Imagination in the atmospher was sufficating. Castle, Droc, and Rare were all laided out in tubes of imagination. I was glad to see they were all half disinfected.

"Won't dinfection beds be a little weak to deal with our types?" Sig murmured.

"Yes, they are," 7op replied with a silly grin, " you guys get the instant disinfection process."

"The what?" I blinked. I didn't know there was an instand disfection process. Why didn't they use this more often? Something bothered me about that....

"Oh no no no no!" Sig gasped and back away quickly. He turned to make a run for it but a large claw reached down and dropped him in a tank. Sig started banging against the walls in a panic.

"7op! GET ME OUT OF HERE! HEY! INSTANT DIS-" He screamed but 7op completely ignored him.

"Sorry, what was that? I Can't hear you over the of this button being pushed," 7op replied with an evil laugh before pressing a big red button.

 I watched in slight horror as Sig was blasted with what seemed like enough imagination to create a second nexus at the speed of a laser cannon. Now I knew why they rarely used this process (I stand corrected. We DO have a huge blast of imagination). Sig's screams were muffled by the sound of the motors. When the process was finished, the spray of imagination dripped out as if it were a sink that had just been turned off.

 

"Uh..." I murmured backing away blankly.
