Chapter 18- Part 2
The mechs stopped a few hundred feet from us, and launched billions of plasma balls at us. I have no idea how those things do it, but every one hit it’s mark. Gold and I were in a very large amount of pain when the mech leader, ED-902, came up to us.
“W311, w311, w311… (Well, well, well)” He buzzed. “700k5 71k3 1 93t t0 83 t43 1ucky 0n3 t0 d35tr0y y0u. (Looks like I get to be the lucky one to destroy you.)[Note: Typing like ED-902 is WAY too hard, so I’m just gonna write normally. Pretend like it’s robo-talk] “Now, you never get to fulfill the wonderful things you could’ve done with your lives, had you lived.”
“Wait…” I said. “How did you know what we could’ve done?”
“I know EVERYTHING.” ED-902 said, matter-of-factly. “I know everything that exists and makes any sense at all.”
Gold thought for a moment. “If that is the case, then time travel must not compute for you.”
“What?” ED said. “I-Uhh-That’s simple! Time travel is simply creating a half-wormhole in the space-time continuum and falling through it, traveling through multiple dimensions, not to mention avoiding the space aspect of it.”
“But how would you do that?” Gold responded, smirking. “Isn’t this whole idea a big paradox?”
“Err… Uh…” ED stammered, and sparks started flying from him. “Army? What do you think?”
The rest of the mechs computed what gold had said and began saying “Does not compute, does not compute…” And exploding.
“BBZT!! KRZZK!! DOES-NOT-COMPUTE!! PARADOX ILLOGICAL!!” ED said, and blew up as well.
I looked over at Gold. “How…?”
“Paradoxes. Computers can’t make sense of them. Don’t think about it too much.” He responded.
Black Sun and Wormhole warped back down to the battlefield. “Well, you’ve managed to defeat the army.” Sun said. “As suspected.”
I looked at him, confused.
“Drew, Black Sun and I are future beings.” Wormhole said. “We know everything that happens and has ever happened. And you being destroyed by us would be one of the things that cannot be changed.”
I thought back to the 2-on-2 battle I saw on the way down the tower. The two being smashed were Gold and I.
While I was thinking, Wormhole charged me and knocked me down. “Say goodbye to your life!” He said, raising his sword for the final blow.
I saw Gold pinned next to me, trying to figure out some way to escape. I only had one idea to save us both. I concentrated hard on my imagination spark. I felt pain course through my body, and I smashed myself.
I rebuild over on the Sentinel outpost, and hid behind a stack of supplies. Wormhole and Black Sun were very confused. Gold slipped away, and warped next to me.
“We have to stop them.” He whispered. “But they’re too strong right now. We have to go back to stop them from being created.”
“How do we do that?” I asked.
“Those two are Maelstrom copies of us,” Gold explained. “They got created when we were afflicted with a paradox.”
“We have to go back in time again?”
“Yes, of course.” He grabbed me. “First stop, September 4th”

Chapter 19- Stranger Danger
As we fell through the wormhole, I had a lot of questions to ask.
“If we go back to when I had to save myself, and we changed the course of history, then wouldn’t I have to save myself again?”
“Not necessarily,” Gold said. “The course of history is only different into the future, so the past is the same, for now. And we’re currently re-writing the future as well. Interesting fact there.”
We fell down in the bushes beside my house, which was not the ideal thing to land on.
“What now?” I asked Gold.
“We wait some more.” He answered.
Soon, I saw my not-really-far-back past self landed on the roof with Silver and Gold. It hurt to see Silver again, since our version of her was gone. The instructed the past me on what to do, and warped out.
So now, two mes and a Gold were lying in wait for the third me to come out the door. The whole Flogmore incident happened, and when the two past mes went away, I saw the small ripple in the space-time continuum where they had touched. It opened into a vortex, and Wormhole stepped out. Strangely, he wasn’t infected.
“Huh?” He said. A small wisp of maelstrom smoke came down from the sky, ready to infect him.
“STOP THAT CLOUD!” Gold yelled, raising his staff. A small group of bulpers materialized and started firing at the smoke. I called down lightning on it. It was thankfully not suited for battle, and it disappeared.
“Now let’s get wormhole somewhere safe.” Gold said, picking him up.
“Ahh! Who are you people? Put me down!” He started pounding on Gold’s back. “Lemme go! Help!”
“Shh!” Gold warned. “Do you WANT to get us all killed?”
“I don’t know.” Wormhole answered. “And why did you call me wormhole? I don’t like that name. How about Joe?”
“Whatever.” I said to the newly named Joe. “You just need to come with us for now.”
“What do I get if I come along?” Joe asked. “I want a cookie.”
“Ugh. Whatever. We have places- or times I guess- to be.” Gold said, grabbing me.
“When to now?” I asked.
“Two years ago, on August 15th.” He said. “Let’s get rid of Black Sun.”

Chapter 20- JUST when it started getting better...

Gold, Joe, and I landed on the hill above Nimbus Plaza, where the rocket launch pad to The Forbidden Valley was. I looked down onto the scene below. The Nexus Press was swarming a specific minifigure.

Gold motioned for us to slide down the hill. We hid behind the paradox vendor stall, where the vendor at that time, Madison Growl, was eagerly listening in on the conversation going on between the Press and the Summoner they were swarming.

“Gold! Gold!” They were shouting. “What was behind the attack in Crux Prime? Are you a spy for the maelstrom?”

The younger Gold replied “Get out of my way! And why would you think that I would ever answer a question like that!?!?!”

He stormed out of the crowd while the press furiously scribbled down what he had said. He started to walk up the hill to where we were standing, and another Gold appeared next to us.

“This was when I came back to witness this day again.” Gold said. “Just wait.”
The present day gold walked up to the traveling Gold and accidentally bumped into him.

“Sorry.” The present day Gold said, and kept walking past where My Gold, Joe, and I were, and rocketed to Forbidden Valley, while the other Gold disappeared.
The little ripple in time-space appeared, as did the cloud of maelstrom. I was about to destroy the cloud, but it became a giant whirling mass of maelstrom and nearly shocked the life force out of Joe, Gold and I. It then went over to the newly created Black Sun, and was about to infect him.

“We… have to do something…” Gold muttered. “We can’t lose.”
I flung myself forward using a shinobi rush, and landed next to Black Sun.

“WHAT IS THAT!?” He Yelled. “WHO ARE YOU?! WHY AM I SCREAMING?!”

“Just come with me.” I said, taking his hand and dragging him back to Gold.

“Can we go now?” I asked.

“Not until that storm is vanquished.” He replied.

“But we aren’t strong enough!” I said.

“Have you really forgotten we can bend time and space to our will?” He said, raising an eyebrow.

“Oh yeah…” I said, raising my hand.

Time stopped, and the maelstrom cloud was frozen solid. The only problem was that I couldn’t smash it. It’s a cloud. I touched Gold, and he unfroze.

“Any advice?” I asked.

“Umm….” He thought. “…No, not really.”

“So what do we do?” I said. “If we unfreeze time, it kills us. If we don’t, well that’s not much of an option.”

“You don’t need to unfreeze time!” A voice boomed through the universe. “I can destroy you now!”

A Purple Minifigure appeared in front of us. The suit, the cane, the rigid face, the evil top hat.

Baron Typhonus was here to smash us personally.

A Purple Minifigure appeared in front of us. The suit, the cane, the rigid face, the evil top hat.

Baron Typhonus was here to smash us personally.

Chapter 21- We destroy the universe

“I must say, I am impressed.” Baron congratulated us, pacing back and forth slowly. “You survived Butterscorch twice, AND turned my two best stromlings to drooling buffoons.” He pointed over to Joe, who was inspecting the ground, and Black Sun, who shouted “My name is orange!” and continued chewing on his foot.

“But that doesn’t matter, since I’m here now.” Baron continued. “And I have more power in my right hand then those two had in their whole bodies!”

“What do we do?” I whispered to Gold.

“We’re cornered.” He replied. “We can’t do much. Wait for the end?”

“We can’t do that. We’re so close to saving the future…”

“Wait!” Gold exclaimed. “We DID save the future! Joe and ‘Orange’ aren’t agents anymore! Everything should be normal!” He thought for a moment more. “So the only thing stopping us is that time is stopped.”

I started the flow of time again, and the maelstrom ripples coming from baron started to wreak havoc on the world. People were desperately trying to fight it, but they couldn’t defeat it. It was too strong, and kept coming.

“Ha!” Baron laughed. “This here is your new and improved future! Infected! The whole of Nimbus station!” He thought it over. “Actually, I would’ve liked Nexus Tower being destroyed better, but this is a start.” He pointed his staff and the four of us. “Say goodbye to your pitiful lives!”

“Now hold on just a minute!” Another voice echoed through existence. This one was much calmer, and female. Two glowing minifigures appeared next to us. They were simple, like bob, but shining the purest of blue. “This universe is WAY screwed up!” One said.

“W-who are you?” Baron said nervously.

“I am Figmenta.” The one on the left said.

“And I am Imaginatrix.” The one on the right said. “We are mythrans, direct decedents of the first builders. We are some of the beings that keep the multiverse in balance.”

Figmenta looked around. “And this one is WAY out of whack! The maelstrom infection is fate, yes, so I can’t stop that. But the distortion you all have been causing from time travel! It’s destroying this universe!”

As if on cue, a chunk of the planet below us fell away into a swirling void. Not maelstrom. No, much, much worse. The universe was imploding on itself, and that was a black hole we were staring into.

“Oh no.” Imaginatrix said. “It looks like we may be too late.”
The planet crumbled below our feet, and everyone was thrown screaming into the end of the universe…
