‎02-07-2014 02:38 AM

  Dr. Overbuild was silent as they read the rest of the words. They appeared to be instructions, but Duke, Baron, and Hael couldn’t make head or tails of it. Dr. Overbuild studied them carefully, before suddenly exclaiming that he knew where to find the Imagination Nexus.
  Hael Storm rushed to the ship’s controls and Dr. Overbuild entered in the coordinates. Duke Exeter and Baron Typhonus stood at the window looking out intently into space as the stars blurred by.
  They talked about the possibilities of what they could do with Imagination. Hael wanted a new pirate ship, one that could travel on land, sea, and through space. Duke thought about creating a castle. A castle where knights could be trained so that they could protect those who needed protection. Dr. Overbuild said he wanted a dog. “A dog?” Duke asked. “I’ve always wanted one ever since I was a boy,” Dr. Overbuild answered.
  They talked about how powerful Imagination was, and how it could be used to improve the lives of everyone in the Universe.
  Baron spoke up, “With the Imagination Nexus, we could become as powerful as kings! We could even rule the Universe if we wanted to!” Then everyone was silent, each thinking their own thoughts.
  As they approached their destination, they slowed down and held their breath. In the distance was a planet. A cube-shaped planet.
  “Is that...?” Dr. Overbuild whispered.
  “Planet Crux,” Duke said.

‎02-07-2014 02:45 AM

  They quickly brought their Golden Corners out. When the three hesitated to put the pieces together, Baron told them to hurry up before he died of suspense. Then the Golden Corners were placed on the stand, clicking together. The Golden Corners levitated and it began to glow. Suddenly, with a flash of light, a beam shot out of the blue glow and hit the planet Crux. Duke and Dr. Overbuild let out cheers and Hael let out a loud “Arr!” Dr. Overbuild came over a placed his metal arms on their shoulders. Baron stood next to them with a large smile of satisfaction on his face.

  When they got closer, they spotted it: a pillar of light shooting upwards out of a temple like a fountain. They brought the ship closer and it hovered over the temple next to the pillar of light.
  The explorers boarded a smaller ship that took them down and landed in front of the temple. They got out and stared wonderingly at the temple and the Imagination shooting out of it. They began to walk towards it. Dr. Overbuild paused to study a material outside the entrance.
  “Overbuild! Hurry up!” Duke called. Dr. Overbuild quickly put away the material to look at later and ran to catch up.
  Duke was the first up the stairs, with Baron close behind. “The Imagination Nexus,” Baron said, his voice hushed. It fountained upwards so far that they couldn’t see the top. They gathered around it, each thinking of their most desired creations. Imagination seemed to gather around Dr. Overbuild. A puppy appeared out of the Imagination Nexus and leapt into his arms. Dr. Overbuild set it down and it ran around them in circles barking. A big grin spread across all of the explorers’ faces. All of their faces... except Baron’s.

Prologue One, part 6

‎02-07-2014 02:45 AM

  He was staring intensely at the Imagination. He glanced in his notebook and quickly decided what to create. He sent his idea into the Imagination Nexus and thought triumphantly, ‘And now, at last, I will become more powerful than I could ever have imagined.’ He laughed as his creation came out of the Imagination Nexus. The other explorers backed away. Baron’s creation resembled a spider. It had four long legs that clicked on the floor as it walked. It was purple and black, and an energy of some sort ran up and down its body. It had orange eyes that glowed with a dark light.

  The puppy approached it and barked curiously. The spider lifted up its front leg and with a screech, brought it down, smashing Dr. Overbuild’s creation. The explorers stared in horror. Baron laughed louder. He sent more ideas into the Imagination Nexus. He would rule the Universe, he decided. He would smash all who opposed him, destroy their dreams if he had to. He glanced at the others cowering before his creation: a Spiderling. They would never let him control Imagination. They wanted to share it, spread it, and give it to all. But not just anybody deserved the gifts the Imagination Nexus could bring. The others had to be smashed. Baron regretted that it had to come to this. They were some of the few that deserved to use Imagination. But the only way he control Imagination was to destroy them.
  Suddenly the Spiderling turned. Its eyes bored into Baron Typhonus. The energy around the Spiderling grew darker, more sinister. The Imagination that had made it had turned into Chaos. And creatures of Chaos have no master. Too late, Baron turned to run. The Spiderling pinned him down with its legs and began to drag him towards the Imagination Nexus. Baron yelled out in fear, but the other explorers were helpless against the Spiderling.

Prologue One, part 7

‎02-07-2014 02:46 AM

  As the Spiderling dragged Baron Typhonus into the Imagination Nexus, dark purple and black streaks exploded in the once blue and white Imagination. Lightning strikes of Imagination and Chaos began shooting off everywhere and the three remaining explorers had to avoid being struck by them. Their ship high above them, which used to be hovering next to the fountain of Imagination, was now hovering next to a powerful and chaotic maelstrom. Lightning hit it again and again before it smashed and the pieces rained down on them. Using what little Imagination was left, Dr. Overbuild prevented the larger chunks from hitting them.

  Duke Exeter blocked the lightning as best he could from hitting Hael Storm or Dr. Overbuild using his shield. Then the impact of a huge blast of lightning knocked Duke’s shield from his hands and pushed him away, exposing Dr. Overbuild. Filled with sudden strength, Duke ran with all his might and leapt between Dr. Overbuild and the corrupted Imagination just as some lightning shot out. It smashed into Duke, breaking him into his individual pieces.
  “No!” Hael Storm yelled.
  Dr. Overbuild quickly put the broken pieces of the ship together and forced it over the corrupted Imagination, sealing the opening closed. Then he crouched next to Hael Storm by Duke’s LEGO remains. They were devastated by the loss of not just one, but two of their friends. Then Dr. Overbuild remembered something from one of the scrolls he read:

When a friend is lost,
When they fall apart,
Remember Imagination is life.
Don’t worry, take heart.

  With shaking hands, he used his Imagination and one-by-one, Duke Exeter’s pieces came together and snapped into place.
  Duke blinked and took a deep breath.
  “Ye be alive!” Hael shouted.
  “We thought you lost your Creative Spark!” Dr. Overbuild added.

  But while they were celebrating, deep within the planet Crux, Imagination and Chaos were building up. They clashed against each other and tried to push outwards. But Dr. Overbuild had sealed up the only exit for the two powers. They would have to force their way out.

  Beneath the three explorers feet, the planet rumbled and shook. A crack appeared down the center of the temple. Duke Exeter and Hale Storm were on one side and Dr. Overbuild was on the other. “Jump!” Duke yelled as the crack widened. Dr. Overbuild took a few steps back then ran and jumped across.

  Chaos worked its way through the cracks and Imagination churned and fought against it. Then, unable to withstand so much pressure, the planet Crux exploded and shattered. Its remains flew apart before slowing due to the Imagination that was barely keeping the pieces close together.
  The force of the explosion caused Dr. Overbuild to slip over the edge. Hael Storm quickly reached down and pulled him up. The explorers looked around them at the destruction that the Chaos had caused. White, purple, and black lightning pulsed back and forth between the planet chunks. The fragment they were on was covered in debris and broken pieces of the temple. An ominous wind blew.
  “It’s... it’s gone,” Dr. Overbuild whispered.
  Duke spotted something fluttering in the breeze. As it landed nearby, he realized it was a scrap of paper, extremely tattered and torn.
  “That be a piece o’ Baron’s sketchbook,” Hael Storm said, also noticing the paper.

  Duke carefully picked it up, trying not to damage the paper any further. He turned it over to look at the drawing and froze. The drawing was well done. Very well done. And it was realistic. So realistic that there was absolutely no doubt as to what was on that piece of paper. It was the Spiderling Baron created, drawn in perfect detail.

  It made Duke wonder how long Baron Tyhonus had been planning everything. A sudden strong gust of wind whisked the scrap out of his hands and over the side where it vanished from sight.

  Nearby, something small and white caught Dr. Overbuild’s eye. It was an Imagination crystal, rare and powerful. Such gems were almost as pure as the Imagination Nexus itself.
  Using it, the three explorers sent out a message to all the Universe. A message that would eventually bring together enemies, make heroes out of ordinary minifigures, and prove the prophesies true… or false.
