Chapter Six:The Corrupted King:
The Skywards minions were walking down a dark,dank hallway,in their new castle. If you were wondering,they are called "Srouders". The skywards minions look a bit like hairless monkeys,covered with grime and filth. And they don't like bananas. They like worms. Anyways,they were walking down a hallway,looking for someone. Who,is easy. Why,is not. The "Who" is Lord Albreack. He didn't make it in the run from the castle. Why,is that their master wanted him for his new,right hand man. Men are easily corrupted. "Listen!" one said. " He's over there!" They ran over and picked him up by his hands and feet,dragging him over rocks,scratching his back and bumping his head against the ground. They walked though grimy dungeons,dark pathways,and,finally,a grand door in front of them. Albreack's head bumped the door with a "Thump". "Come in", a voice hissed. They opened the door,scrambled into the room,and left without a word. The Skyward was still a snake,but with a demon possessing him. It now had a mind of its own. Instead of that vile,gruesome,blood-loving monster,it could think like a human. And the demon that possessed it,knew magic. The Skyward started chanting,in a way that only snakes could. A invisible force lifted Albreack off the ground,set him standing,and his eyes opened. All the of the demons thoughts began flooding Albreack's mind. He started hating all humans,hating all things,and hating all worlds. He knew that he was the demons right hand man. He knew that he wanted to destroy the known worlds,even more than he did. He was turning into a demon.
I have you attention if you are reading this, and I suppose it will stay that way until this signature ends, or you could look away, but then you wouldn't get the fulfillment of reading through this, even though it is very boring.

Chapter Seven:Cave Trolls
Three emotionally stressed kids,and one man,got out of the carriage they were in. Aragorn walked over to his horse and whispered to him "Run free, Breago. Run away from the evil that stalks this land. Be safe. Get back to the castle safely." The horse took off to the north,whinnying softly to himself. They stood in front of a steep,treacherous ,rocky mountain. "We have to get to the top of that hill,to get away from this world." "What?" said Sam,his face still stained with tears. "Another world,free of darkness." he said. He left it at that. The kids didn't know what had happened,or where they were in this world anymore. They knew that if they didn't follow him,they would probably die. They had been climbing up the hill,slipping and falling on rocks. They reached a totally flat place,with a cave in front,and bent,broken,scratched trees strewn everywhere. As the kids were about to flop down in exhaustion and fall asleep,Aragorn said,"Don't. Move."
"And keep your voices down." Just back away slowly,and stay quiet." They were surprised,but they did what he said. Then "SNAP!". Tom had stepped on a twig. He stumbled backwards,trying to regain his balance,but fell over. Aragorn cursed. "Don't mo- He was cut off by a mighty "ROARRRRR!" Everyone stood stalk still. A Troll came out. Not just any troll,but a Cave Troll. They had very bad sight from living in caves,but their hearing and smelling was astounding. It tilted his head,sniffing,listening for anything that would give its prey away. The kids could smell him from twenty paces away. He smelled like rotten meat,wet wool,and death. Bones were stuck in his teeth, He wasn't wearing anything but a dirty loincloth around his waist. He was at least twenty-five feet high. His toenails were yellow and covered in dirt. His head stopped swiveling,and pointed his head,straight towards Aragorn. RARRRR!!!!! It charged. Aragorn ducked a hand swipe with surprising speed and agility,pulling out his sword. He slid under the trolls legs,stabbed him in the back,jumped on his sword,and jumped to the trolls head,grabbed his sword,and plunged his sword into its head. Howling in pain,it tried to knock off the attacker with his hands. Then is fell over,Aragorn jumping off his head,and landing smoothly onto the ground. he grabbed his sword,wiped it on the grass,and put it back in its sheath. The kids stood there in shock. They thought Trolls were just from Fairy tails! Their minds were still trying to process what they had just seen. "You may think that all those mythical creatures weren't real,but they are." He said,as if reading their minds. "Wha- wha... what the heck?" Sam said softly. "I'll explain later. We're almost to our destination. Its almost dark. Bad things come out when your in the Forest after dark. Worse than that troll. Tom started to cry. "I just want to see my mom again" he sniffed. Jenna walked over to him and picked him up. "Come on" She said in a soothing tone. "Its going to be all right." They trudged on,Jenna still carrying a sleeping Tom. They reached a clearing at the top of the mountains. "This is the only safe place in the Forest after dark. We make camp here." He got out four bed rolls,and lay them down on the pine needles. Jenna put Tom in his,and got into hers. The kids fell asleep The sounds of the Forest echoed into their sleep. Wolfs howling,things lumbering through the Forest. Aragorn stayed up and gazed up at the stars,finding all the constellations he knew until he fell asleep.
