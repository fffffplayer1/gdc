Sorry that this took so long. Here it is!

Skyward, Chapter Ten, The Silver-Pelt Wolfs:
Everyone was up bright and early the next morning. But not all wanted to get out of bed. "Do we HAVE to move, THIS early in the morning?" Tom groaned. "Yes." Aragorn said simply. "Can't we stay just go back to sleep, and THEN leave." Tom said, quite hopefully.
"It feels like Six." "Thats because it IS Six, now get moving!" Aragorn nudged the lump with his foot. "That was my head!" He complained. "Next it will be all of you,covered in cold water!" Sam and Jenna giggled. Tom started moving, grumbling something about grumpy weird people. "I'm not weird, nor grumpy. I think you are the grumpy one." Aragorn said. Tom looked a bit surprised that he heard that from forty feet away, but quickly regained his composure. "After everyones awake and has eaten, we will move. We have to go though the Forest of Helvian, which means we have to cross over the mountains."
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
"Is there anything dangerous in those forests?" Sam asked. "No, I don't think so. The worst I've encountered was a goblin. They aren't dangerous unless they appear in numbers." He said. "What should we do now?" Asked Jenna. "We should get moving. Something might come after us. And we don't want that." "No we don't," Sam thought.
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
They were up and moving close to seven thirty. As they walked down the path, Aragorn thought about plugging his ears. Tom was talking rapid-fire, forgetting about his previous mood. "Hey! Where are we going? Are we going through these Forests up through the mountains? Hey! Look at that! A Waterfall! How cool! Does anyone have some water? I'm thirsty! When can we have a break...." "Will you just stop?!" Aragorn snapped. "Ok" Said Tom, a little deflated. As they walked closer to a bend in the road, Aragorn stopped so suddenly that Jenna ran into him. "Wait. Be quiet." He looked around, just as he heard a snap. "Oh, no." He said, drawing his sword. "Oh, how can this be happening?" A eerie silence hung in the air. Then a wolf howled. Then more, and more still. "Its to late!” He yelled. "Draw your weapons!" He just barely said that before a wolf jumped out at him. He swung his sword, chopping it cleanly in half. They stood back to back, nervously waiting, for the next to come. Silence. Then the forest exploded. A wolf leaped at Sam, before he dove to the ground, stabbing it in the back. It turned on him, growling.
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
"Scatter!" Aragorn yelled, trying to get his voice to be heard above the noise of the battle. He saw a wolf turn on Sam, but he had his own problems right now. Three wolfs were gaining up on him, waiting for him to attack. He waited for them to attack instead. He felt something come up behind him. He grabbed his sword by the hilt with both hands, and spun around in a full circle. He heard an anguished cry of pain from the wolf behind him. He had almost finished his spin when one of the wolfs behind him jumped. He was pinned to the ground.
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
Tom ran. He was scared. He didn't want to fight. He had never used a sword until yesterday. Now wolfs were attacking. "Jenna!" He cried out desperately. A wolf was tailing him. It was catching up. He turned around. His heart started thumping in his chest. If he didn't act now, he could die. The wolf jumped. Time slowed down. He saw the wolf jump, but he ducked before it could reach him. It turned around, facing him. It slashed out with his claws, but he blocked it with his sword. He jumped, slashing it on the side, and the a leg. It was wounded, and could only limp towards him. He was knocked off his feet when a wolf came charging towards him from the back. He cried out for his older sister one more time. He didn't hear a reply. The wolf jumped into the air, towards him.
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
Jenna ran up a hill, trying to get a better vantage point. She had a bow, and she could use it. A wolf ran up to her as she got to the top of the hill. She grabbed an arrow out of her quiver, jabbing it into the approaching wolfs chest. She got out her bow, and aiming carefully, she aimed at a few wolfs down on the battle field. "Jenna!" Tom called out to her. She saw a wolf come at him from the back, knocking him over. She nocked an arrow to her bow, and fired. A arrow whizzed over his head, striking the wolf in the heart. She looked for Aragorn. She saw him, right as a wolf jumped on him, trapping him. She panicked, looking for anyone close enough to help him. Then she saw Sam, the wolf gaining on him. She needed to help him. She needed to help both of them! She could only get to one in time. She ran for her brother, drawing her sword as she ran towards the battlefield.
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
Sam sliced at the jumping wolf, ducking. He knew he had to get out of here, along with his sister and his brother. But what about Aragorn? He helped them, so it would only seem fair, to help him. He slammed at the wolf with the flat of his blade, knocking it out. He ran to where Aragorn was. He saw a wolf jump onto him, trapping him. He ran at him, whacking at all the wolfs that got in his way. He ran to him, slicing at the wolf on top of him, getting its attention. He saw Aragorn, injured. By the looks of it, his arm was broken, and he had scratches all along his right leg. He lunged at the wolf, but it dodged the blow. It slammed into him, knocking the breath out of him, and disarming him. He saw Aragorn struggle to get up, trying to help him. He got onto him feet, and grabbed Sam's lost sword. He stabbed the wolf in the back, and it slumped over dead.
He dropped the sword, which Sam picked up, and tried to get away from all of the wolfs. Sam ran to help him, smacking wolfs as they came at him and Aragorn. He saw
Jenna rushing towards them. Tom was at the top of the hill, fighting wolfs that came at him. Along with Jenna and himself helping Aragorn to get out of the battlefield, Jenna helping him walk along, and Sam fighting, they just barely managed to get out of the main battle area. They looked back. Dead wolfs lay everywhere. A few injured ones tried to limp away. As they got to the top of the hill, Aragorn collapsed to the ground. "What should we do?" Jenna said, sounding a bit panicked. "We need to get away from here." Sam said. "We should set up camp here, actually. We need to wait until he is feeling better." "I agree." Jenna said, taking the pack off Aragorn's shoulders. They set up a small, waterproof hut on the side of the road, using sticks and branches to craft it. As nightfall came, Aragorn awoke. He said to Sam who was sitting beside him "Thank you, for saving my life. I wouldn't be here if it weren't for you and Jenna." He said, his voice barely above a whisper. He fell back asleep almost instantly. They kept watch that night, taking shifts so each other could sleep.
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
I know this isn't a very good chapter. I don't know why, but the whole thing seemed a bit unorganized. Sorry that it was bad!

Mods, please let this though! I know it is a bit violent, but I tried to not make it to scary for younger readers. I spent about two to three hours on this, and will be pretty sad if you don't let it though. Thank you!
I have you attention if you are reading this, and I suppose it will stay that way until this signature ends, or you could look away, but then you wouldn't get the fulfillment of reading through this, even though it is very boring.
