Why is there a Wahi for the element of Shadow

You should be asking why there's a Wahi for Ice, Sonics, Psionics, and Shadow altogether on Spherus Magna. Well, Mata Nui's dying energies were used to terraform the three planets back together, resulting in several different dedicated locations to accommodate the elemental power that had nowhere to go. They don't cover the entire planet, of course.
