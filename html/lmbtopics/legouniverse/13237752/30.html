     A toa of Gravity clings to the shadowed underside of a ledge with the power of gravity, unnoticed by Epsilon guards above. On his shoulder sits a shrunken toa of earth.

     Beside him, a toa of magnetism wearing a Suletu stands, courtesy of the Ba-toa.

     The ledge heats up to orangish glowing temperatures under the influence of a fourth, invisible toa. The glowing heat moves, silently carving a message into the rock.

     This is the spot you saw them enter?

   The toa of magnetism transmits the reply with his mask.

     Yes.

     Immediately, the toa decloaks and swaps his Volitak for a different mask. Two cylinders attached to his shoulders flare to life, blasting out jets of plasma that propel him around the ledge... And into the tunnel positioned above it. His mask glows to life as he hurls small balls of plasma at the two Epsilons guarding the entrance.

     "COME'N GET ME, TIN CANS! WOOOOOO!"

     The phrase echoes for miles around.

     The Epsilons whirl and give chase at a speed matching the toa's own, adaptive weaponry ablaze.

     The Ba-toa simply rotates his - and his two companions' - direction of gravity, spinning them around the ledge in a moment.

     The Fa-toa extends a hand, and the two robots freeze mid-flight. A flick of the hand later, and they collapse to the ground. They do not rise again.

     There may be more. Be on your guard; We don't know what we're dealing with here.
