*Many hours later, the door to the room slides open once more. The Anonymous enters and approaches the section consisting of Pain's prison. The terminal materializes, and he places his fingertips upon its screen in the correct order. The pedestal containing the prisoner rises from the floor, and he drags his fingers down slightly, causing the orange field around it to fade away. The blue orb over the pedestal flickers for a moment before glowing brighter.*

 

"You... "

 

"I am not here to hurt you this time. I have been wanting to speak with you for quite some."

 

"You think I wanna talk to you? After everything you've put me through??"

 

"Please, let us be realistic. I was the one who convinced the others to spare you."

 

*The orb laughs harshly.*

 

"SPARE??!! If you wanted to do that, you would've let me die."

 

"I doubt I could understand what you are feeling right now, but I do understand it would be better for you to not feel anything at all. When this is over, I will finally put you to rest."

 

"You really think it will be so easy?"

 

"I am actually surprised you think it will not be. But then, there are a few things about you that surprises me. How are you not subdued by grief and hate, like your counterpart?"

 

"What happened to him??"

 

"He destroyed himself. I am wondering how the same has not happened to you."

 

"You're not finding out; I'm never letting you in."

 

"Apparently not... but I do not need to be 'let in' to know certain things. It is still easy to see that you are nearly bursting with questions. Questions that, I am sure, have been haunting you for a while."

 

*The blue orb doesn't respond to this.*

 

"Questions such as, 'where are The Composer and The Uncreator', and... "

 

*He pauses. The orb finally speaks up, but its voice is grave.*

 

"Where. Is. He??"

 

"Where is who?"

 

"You know who. Mack. Mack Peterson."

"Ah... you mean The Confirmer... "

 

*The orb flares brightly.*

 

"Shut up!! Don't call him that! Where is he??"

 

"Well, now, here is a predicament. You have something I want, and I have something you want. Except, really... I have something you need."

 

"Don't play this game."

 

"I think I will, actually. Tell me your secret, or you will not find out where your friend is."

 

"He was your friend, too."

 

*The Anonymous remains quiet for a good while. The orb finally breaks the silence.*

 

"I don't know how I got any sort of control over the mess you left me with. I remember meeting this entity, and ever since then, I've not been weighed down as much by those feelings. Don't get me wrong, though; I can still feel them burning away at my thoughts. Especially rage and hate. Right now."

 

*The Anonymous still doesn't say anything. He just stands there, staring at the orb with his hollow orange eyes.*

 

"That's all I can tell you about that. It's the truth."

 

*No response.*

 

"So... now it's your turn. Where's Mack?"

 

"Does it really matter where he is? He is not who you once knew. He is one of my servants... a Datai. You know this... and he was never friends with me; The Anonymous. He was your friend."

 

"And I know you've not forgotten who you really are. 'The Anonymous' isn't your name."

 

"Irrelevant. The Confirmer is merely a tool at my disposal."

 

*The orb flares brighter than ever. The next instant, it's screaming at The Anonymous.*

 

"Are you kidd-... NO!! He was a human being with a real name!! My first and only friend! Our first and only friend!! He was the only one who ever cared about us, who ever saw us as more than just an asset or a tool! He set aside the world for us when we needed him!! Who else?? Who else did we ever have?! It was always HIM!! So how dare you?! How dare you call him a tool, when he was the one person who never thought that way about us??!!"

 

*The Anonymous is silent again. The orb sees him lower his head ever so slightly.*

 

"He is gone. Solipsil... I destroyed him."
