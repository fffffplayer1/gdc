Atem hovers at the peak of the mountains, his lieutenant faithfully standing. Thunderaan was bouncing around. "The winds bring about change, Atem!" He spoke jubilantly. "I can feel it! Buzzing...warming, it's coming!" The younger screeched, to Atem's amusment.

"Easy, Thunderaan. The Winds call to me, from the South and from the North, from the West and from the East. All other directions too, they all call to me. They have pleaded for me to reign over them once more, and I soon shall. The Winds of the Center shall begin anew."

He pauses. "Go, go find my Queen. Find where The Woman In The Ocean lay. I will go to her." He says, to Thunderaan's squeals. "Yes, Man In The Heavens!"

     Zeta chuckles.

     "That's none of your business. Get out of my facility."

     "No. If you are developing things to strike down toa, then you are, by logic, an enemy of the toa."

     The Ba-toa's extended hand glows purple as he applies his power.

     "Farewell, machine."

     He increases Zeta's weight to twice as much. Ten times. A hundred.

     But Zeta doesn't even budge. His frame does not creak in protest. If anything, he seems... Amused?

     "Toa. Did you really expect I would not devise something to defend against toa before something to attack them with?"

     He draws his sword and swings it.

     The toa of Gravity leaps back, gaining only a minor scratch, and swaps his mask of Sensory Aptitude for his other mask; a mask of Freezing. He activates it and Zeta is immediately encased in ice.

     "Didn't prepare for kanohi, did ya? Well, good."

     The toa opens the door. After he and his companions step outside, he applies his power to the ceiling. The result is a cave in. Zeta is buried beneath tons of frigid mountain rock.

     "Let's get out of here. We need to tell Atero Nui."

     "Why bother? We destroyed him, didn't we?"

     "I don't believe it's that easy for a second. He has some trick up his sleeve. He has to."

     The three toa hurl bursts of their respective elements to deal with the Epsilons as they dash down the hallway back to the entrance.
