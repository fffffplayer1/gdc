I am reapying you for what you did for me, but now I consider I have repaid you fully. If you try to threaten me again, you'll become my enemy and even though I won't be able to defeat you at least I'll harm you enough!"

"Here is the chest with one of the things you're looking for! Do not check it in here. And now I shall give you the information for the location of... the Neutral Sphere!"

He walks near the man called LPTD and whispers something to the place where his ear would be weren't it for the hood hiding it.

"Good!" said LPTD as he raised the chest that Sebastian had brought from Vanguard Outpost "You're not trying to fool me, Pseudus, are you?"

"Do you think I am so foolish to want to face your anger!"

"So I imagined! Goodbye Pseudus! I hope we meet again!" and he was gone, only with a slight golden light.

The way LPTD had said the last words was very intimidating. Even Pseudus, who always seemed to be so confident, now seemed to be afraid of something.
