OoC: While the ten-page peace has always been a bit murky, the general rule is that the HQ cannot be attacked for that period provided that it does not engage in any hostilities with other HQs. However, the exact definition of "hostilities" is undefined; my move, which was to merely observe the HQ, could be either hostile or a friendly act of learning about the galaxy. However, my definition still stands: if you attack anyone else, the peace is over, no matter what. I do not know if there are any other rules related to this that have been invented for various reasons, so I would suggest that a more knowledgeable RPer be asked, or, better yet, a moderator who is familiar with the rule. Then again, I don't recall the rule being official, so they may not be able to help you. Anyway, I hope this helped.

-Bane
