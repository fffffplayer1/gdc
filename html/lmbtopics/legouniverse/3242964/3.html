Chapter 6: Battle! Part 2

 

          In that way they reached the Mine in no time. There, they made their way to the Launch Pad fighting the Dark Spiderlings individually. It wasn’t difficult at all. They took off and reached one of the properties where a Spider Queen still dwelled.

          Then, they started planning how they would attack to smash the Spider Queen as fast as they could. That was actually their hobby. They were even holding a record.

          Nikolai walked in front of the Spider Queen, which seemed to be asleep, while the other two climbed at the rocks that separated the main area of Block Yard with the landing area. Nikolai started firing with his zipguns on the eyes of the monster.

          The Spider Queen woke up immediately, but until it realized what was happening almost all its eyes were blinded. Yet, it knew where the attacks were coming from, so it charged towards Nikolai. However, before it reached him, Melodie jumped on its head and hit it with her claws. At the same time Xeno jumped from the rocks of the opposite side hitting and destroying the gun of the brute with his sword. Then, after landing on the ground next to the Spider’s leg, he hit its leg with his sword making it stumble and fall. Meanwhile, Nikolai kept shooting it and Melodie kept stabbing it. Then, Xeno climbed on its head and gave the last hit hitting the Spider on the centre of its head. After that, the Maelstrom Being smashed into pieces.

Chapter 7: Kingdoms for Sale* Part 1

 

          Xeno, Melodie and Nikolai all looked at each other decidedly. Then, they turned to the Daredevil and said all at once: “Show us!”

          They all got in their rockets and with the Daredevil, who borrowed a Steampunk rocket from Nikolai, on the front they headed for a Nimbus Isle Property called ‘Kingdoms for Sale’. They flew above Nimbus Station and saw a most peculiar sight. Nimbus Station, the usually overcrowded place was empty. Things must have been very serious.

          They passed the peaceful world and got in the area of the properties. They quickly passed the Nimbus Rock Properties and reached the Nimbus Isles. Once they were there, the Daredevil they noticed before led them to one of them.

          “Wow!”

          “Ooooh!”

          “Impressive!”

          It indeed was very impressive. Big Ben, Taj Mahal, the Tower Bridge, the Eiffel Tower and the Statue of Liberty were all there, made of LEGO bricks. Of course, the three minifigures had never seen the actual Monuments, since Planet Earth was a very distant planet. However, that just increased the awe they felt as they saw those monuments.

          Yet, when they landed they saw that things there weren’t that wonderful. The place was full of Dark Ronin Invaders, most probably Elite and occasionally Improved. They then realized why Nimbus Station was empty; everybody was there. Most of the Nexus Force Recruits were at the first part where rockets land not to let any Dark Ronin pass. However, there were some brave minifigures, like Mardolf the Orange, fighting in the main area.

          “Let’s go!” Xeno said seeing this like a good challenge. Besides Dark Ronin were his favourite enemies.

 

*All credit to the property Kingdoms for Sale goes to EnigmaticPlastic, who built it when LEGO Universe had not closed. You can see it in the Hall of Fame.

Chapter 8: In which a property and its owner are saved Part 1

 

          “HOLY CRUX!!!” Xeno shouted afraid and started running.

          Xeno and Melodie had always a special relationship. When they had first met, they seemed to be fighting one another much and were very competitive with each other. Later, however, they became very good friends. Xeno could not afford losing her. And if the whole Eiffel Tower fell on her, she surely wouldn’t be able to rebuild.

          Xeno ran as fast as he could and quickly reached the place where Melodie lay, but the Eiffel Tower fell fast, too. He lifted the girl and threw her to the hands of Nikolai. However, now he had to save himself, too. He started running again. The Tower approached him dangerously and for a moment he thought he would smash forever. Yet, he jumped the last second before the Tower crashed on the ground and it just fell on his leg. Xeno did not hear the sound of bones breaking due to the deafening noise of the crash, but he felt the pain.

          Soon he was surrounded by three Dark Ronin, which thought that they had found some easy prey. However, Nikolai shot them, so quickly that they did not even realize what smashed them. Afterwards, Nikolai still holding Melodie rushed to where Xeno lay and after putting the girl down he helped his friend to bilk from the fallen Eiffel Tower.

Chapter 8: In which a property and its owner are saved

 

          After consuming a Hardtack Buscuit and a Strong Notion Potion, both Xeno and Melodie were good enough to fight, although Xeno still hobbled.

          Once they looked around things were very different. All the former guards of the Eiffel Tower were either knocked out or temporarily smashed. Few minifigures were on the Tower Bridge protecting it, while the Statue of Liberty was missing an arm and was on fire. The builder of the property was still on the top of Big Ben, but now dozens of Ronin was climbing it to destroy it and the builder.

          “You two go to the Bridge, I go to Big Ben!” Xeno said and left them without waiting for them to respond.

          He reached the base of the tower and started ascending on it, not climbing, but running on the wall. He drew two katanas, one at each hand and stretched his arms. As he ascended he smashed all the Ronin that got in his way. Soon he reached the top, having almost cleared one of the sides. Then, he grabbed some rocket parts from his backpack and threw them on the Ronin smashing them or throwing them down. The first side was clear.

          The builder was holding a pistol and shot any Ronin he saw, but each Ronin needed many shots to be smashed and they were coming too fast. It was obvious he wasn’t able to keep them away.

          Xeno quickly ran to the other sides and used his katanas to push away the Ronin hurling them. As the fell they hit on other climbing Ronin, which resulted many of them to fall. He then dropped more rocket parts and other heavy stuff from his backpack to hit the Ronin. The builder realising what he was doing, started doing the same. Occasionally, some Ronin managed to reach the top, but some quick strokes of Xeno always smashed them.

          Soon the Ronin climbing Big Ben were so few that the builder could handle them on his own. Xeno then looked at the Tower Bridge.

Chapter 8: In which a property and its owner are saved Part 3

 

          Xeno then looked at the Tower Bridge. The minifigures had managed to seclude the Maelstrom Beings at the one side of the Bridge, but they were still outnumbered. The vendor realized they needed help. However, he was too far to get there in time.

          That was when he thought of a great plan. He took his rocket from his backpack and filled its cockpit with all his Firecrackers, Massive Firecrackers, Big Ones, Flash Bangs, Cherry Blasts and other explosives. Then, he pressed the launch button (he did not need a launch pad, for launch pads are just to set destinations) and the rocket flew towards the Bridge.

          There weren’t many other Ronin anywhere else and the crowd that was in the small part of the property, realizing that, got into the main section of its and started chasing the remaining enemies. Xeno had saved the day. . .

          The problem was solved when Nikolai and Melodie came up there with their rockets.
