Chapter 9: In which two friends quarrel Part 2

 

          By the time Melodie asked her question, Xeno was looking at her with an annoyed look.

          “Melodie! I am not the Ronin-Slayer! This is the craziest idea you have thought!!!” Xeno almost shouted at her.

          “Xeno! All the clues show that you are the Ronin-Slayer!!! Why won’t you admit to me? To your friend?” she said with her eyes preparing to release some tears.

          “BECAUSE I AM NOT!!!” This time he had shouted and he had pushed Melodie down as he did that.

          That made many minifigures look at them. Melodie got up and looked at Xeno furiously.

          “Then, I shall leave without you! Goodbye Xeno!!!” she said and turned around leaving the crowd.

          Xeno watched her for bit stubbornly. Then, his anger was replaced with sadness, disappointment, shame, guilt, repentance, realization of what he had done.

          “Melodie!” he called with a way that revealed all those, but the girl was not close enough to hear that.

          Xeno started walking towards her, but Nikolai grabbed his arm.

          “Give her some time!” he said “Ask sorry later!”

          Xeno observed the girl as she got out her rocket and took off with it.

Chapter 11: In which Xeno becomes famous Part 1

 

          “Please! Let me through! I need to pass!” Xeno started saying as he tried to pass through the crowd that had gathered outside Overbuild’s office.

          At last Xeno managed to get through and started running, because the reporters, photographers and cameramen were following him. However, Xeno was faster –a lot faster actually– and they couldn’t chase him for long. When they lost him, they all rushed to the launch pads to go to Nimbus Station at his stall, where they thought he would be going.

          Yet, Xeno followed another path and went to Nikolai’s stall.

          Nikolai put him into his stall and asked him what happened. Xeno explained and then waited hidden. However, no part of the crowd appeared since they had set for Nimbus Station.

          Yet, displeasing news came when after a while all the channels were announcing that Xeno was the Ronin-Slayer and showed videos, photos even recordings of the conversation Xeno had in Overbuild’s office.

          Soon people started noticing him at Nikolai’s stall and came to ask him questions. A guy told him that he was his #1 Fan, but then he started quarrelling with another minifigure saying he was his #1 Fan.           “Since I realised they are important! If you wanna be someone, people must know you! Fame is the first step to the top!” Xeno’s eyes seemed to glow, something that scared Melodie.

          The girl was too shocked and generally full of many different emotions that she couldn’t find an answer. Xeno smiled even more. Suddenly, Melodie turned around and left almost running through the crowd.
