          “He kissed you?! Well, I should expect anything from him now! Before he left me at Nexus Tower, he stole Asano Naganori’s armour and caused havoc filling the place with black smoke! When it was cleared up, Xeno was nowhere to be seen!”

Chapter 17: In which they talk about Xeno and Asano Part 2

 

          “He did what?!” Melodie exclaimed in a purely shocked and surprised way with no anger in her voice at all.

          “We need to find him! We don’t know what he might do!”

          Suddenly, a third voice was heard in the dark of Nimbus Station: “Are you Nikolai Gammapulse and Melodie Foxtrot?”. The two minifigures nodded and a Rank 3 Assembly Engineer appeared.

          “Good! You need to come with me! Dr. Overbuild wants to see you!” the man said.

          “We kind of need to do something and it’s urgent!” Nikolai answered.

          “I believe that it is the same thing that concerns both you and Overbuild! Come, quickly!”

          “Okay, but we need to go the Launch Pad for Nexus Tower and people might see me and stop us!” Melodie replied.

          “Launch Pad? No, this is too urgent for rockets! We need something faster! Grab my hand and you Nikolai grab hers”

          Once they did so, the Engineer pressed a button on his watch and then everything was a blur. For some seconds, they seemed to be standing mid-air while everything around them was blurry, restless and colourful. It all ended as suddenly as it started. The two minifigures let the air go out of their lungs realising they had been holding their breaths until then.

Chapter 18: In which the battle begins Part 1

 

          Bill Shiddo looked at the Spirit Ronin who were coming closer by the minute. Next to him Echs Ray, Vapor Overcast and Boris Toobsox were standing. Even Brick Fury was there, just a few steps behind them. Still, Bill couldn’t help, but be scared by the idea of having to fight these Spirit Ronin. They had been told that they couldn’t be smashed. And even worse, they could possess them. What were their chances even against few of them, let alone all those who were coming towards them, now?

          They had to protect the Paradox Refinery. If the Ronin broke in there chaos would occur. It would be another Avant Gardens Paradox Research Facility incident. And they had enough problems without that.

          The Spirit Ronin had reached the other end of the bridge that led to the Paradox Refinery area. Bill could count them now. They were about ten. Everyone got ready to fight; only Kammy Kazei and Shu Fitts were hiding behind their stalls. But even they would fight if they had to.

          The Ronin were now halfway through the bridge. Brick Fury raised his arm cannon and fired the first time. The Leading Ronin was hit. He fell down and was pushed backwards (pushing the other Ronin, too), but when he stood up it was apparent he was unaffected. He kept marching. The next shot wasn’t as accurate. It hit the bridge, which caught fire.

          “No! Brick, stop!” Vapor quickly bellowed “You’ll destroy the bridge!”

Chapter 18: In which the battle begins Part 3

 

          Curses! The Ronin probably tried to outrage the Dragons, so that they would attack as well. And they didn’t fail.

          Indeed his thoughts seemed to be right, since soon enough four more Ronin appeared from the entrance to the Dragon Instance. They were doomed.

          Or where they? Upon approaching the Dragons did not breathe flames onto the minifigures, tried to bite them, crash them, hit them with their nails or did anything else to them. Instead, they did all of the above to the Spirit Ronin! That almost stunned Bill, who couldn’t imagine Nexus Force and Maelstrom fighting the same enemy.

          But that was the case, five Nexus-Forcers and four Maelstrom Dragons were fighting together eight Spirit Ronin, who probably didn’t wish to anger the dragons, but smash them. And then Bill hoped even more, for the Dragons perhaps could hurt the Ronin.

          But it was then things started going wrong. A smoky arm appeared on the cliff, as if someone smoky was trying to climb it. Then, more arms appeared. And then the arms turned out to be the fallen Ronin… who had managed to climb to the top of the cliff! Six more Ronin joined the ones who were already fighting.

          At that moment, Boris fell down wounded and unconscious. Bill’s attention was distracted more than it should have been. And then he felt a pain on his stomach. He folded his body in two due to that pain. It was not caused by a strike. It was something he hadn’t ever experienced before. He watched the armour of the Ronin he had been fighting fall down on the ground lifeless and then he fainted.

          They started running immediately. Soon they had reached the base of the Great Tree, the place where people could start climbing it. Ninja and Nexus-Forcers were there fighting twelve other Spirit Ronin. The two friends had no time to check out who were the fighters. They passed them quickly.

          Soon they were at Mantis Rock, but by the time they reached it the Dark Ronin were… leaving. They ignored the two minifigures and joined the battle at the Great Tree, but not against the Nexus-Forcers! They didn’t dwell on that. They expected they would have to fight there. They were glad they could go on so fast and they kept running without thinking why it was so easy.

           They got out of the Mantis Rock area, but not through any of the usual paths. They got out of the mapped areas towards the location where Asano Naganori used to be before being disinfected. Shadow had told them that that was the place from which Asano oversaw the battle the previous time. If his assumption was correct, Asano would again be there.

Chapter 19: In which Asano Naganori is confronted Part 1

 

          “Melodie and Nikolai!” Asano said with Xeno’s voice, but in a very un-Xeno way “I expected you might come!” Asano wasn’t even looking at them, his face turned towards the interior of the cave “Have you come to stop me? To save your friend? Don’t you know that your little friend is far gone? You’ll have to do with me now, because it’s all you’re gonna have!”

          Worry started growing inside of Melodie that Xeno might indeed be lost forever. That the plan couldn’t work from the very beginning. She wanted to look at Nikolai, to see whether he felt the same, but she refrained from doing so. It could give her away.

          “Actually, we are here to join you!” she replied trying to hide all such feelings and sound smug “We know our interest!”

          “Oh, really? Because your friend’s memories of you tell me you belong to the kind of people who are fools that will not see what their interest it, but fight for their beliefs!”

          “Well, Xeno was mistaken!” Nikolai said with a more convincing voice.

          Melodie smiled at that.

          “Really?!” Asano kept doubting, this time turning to face them and walked towards Melodie “So, this is no kind of trick that you might use to stop me?”

          By this time, he was just in front of Melodie, his cold eyes staring into hers. Melodie could see how pale Xeno’s skin had turned. She could feel his breath onto her nose and mouth, for they had similar height. And she was sure that if it was quiet enough he could hear her heart beating faster than ever, worried by this deadly eyes surveying into her soul! One second passed. Asano smiled, but his smile did not reach his eyes. And then Melodie punched him as hard as she could.
