Chapter 19: In which Asano Naganori is confronted Part 2

 

          Asano fell on the ground, his hands on his nose. But not before he could raise his katanas scratching Melodie’s legs. Her legs couldn’t bear her weight, but not just because of the scratches. What the other Ronin had done affected her, too. She fell, too. Now, both she and Asano were down.

          But Asano had the advantage having fallen first and so being ready to attack first. He charged at her from the ground where he was and falling onto her grabbed her neck and squeezed. But there was one thing he forgot: Nikolai! Before he could finish what he intended to do, he was hit on the back of the head with the butt of one of his fallen katanas.

          Asano fell unconscious, still on Melodie. Melodie breathed for sweet air and thanked Nikolai inside her mind. Panting she looks at him, her sight still a bit blurry and says out of her breath “Now what?”

          “Now, we take on!” said another voice.

          Melodie turned her head towards the source of the voice, at the entrance of the cave. It was Strange Odd Shadow; and Doctor Overbuild behind him. They were smiling.

          “Good job! Now, let us do our thing!” Shadow told her.

          Both of them worked with quick moves. Shadow approached the fallen minifigures. He removed Asano from Melodie, perhaps with less care than he could have used. With his next move, he stung an injection on his upper arm.

          “We wouldn’t want him to wake up too early!” he explained at Melodie’s shocked expression.

Chapter 19: In which Asano Naganori is confronted

 

          Meanwhile, Overbuild had gotten a book out and was reading something in a low voice. Melodie couldn’t hear well what he was chanting, but she thought it wasn’t of her own tongue. Now, Shadow was building something with some pieces he had found in the cave and Asano’s armour, which he had gotten from Xeno’s body. Finishing (Melodie had gotten up by now), the girl saw it was actually a Ronin’s statue. Probably, Asano’s one.

          And as the statue was built, Overbuild started reading louder. The last words of his were: “ANOT! NO! CHARETH! NI! MPOTIR!*”

          When the last word was spoken, something that looked like black smoke came out of Xeno’s body. For a second it floated in the air, as if it was trying to resist and then it entered the statue. The statue seemed to glow silver-ishly momentarily. But then nothing more happened. Melodie waited to see what Xeno would do now.

          “Don’t expect him to wake up! What I gave him is too strong for him to wake up even if you hit a gong next to him! But it won’t last much! You’ll see that he’s fine then!” Shadow explained.

Chapter 19: In which Asano Naganori is confronted Part 4

 

          Then, Shadow headed to the interior of the cave. Melodie really looked at it for the first time. She saw there was a basin filled with some liquid that looked like water. But Melodie knew it rarely was really water. Shadow seemed to do something with his hands around it and then picked it up. There was some light and the basin cracked. Overbuild picked Xeno’s unconscious body and the statue with his robotic arms up, too. And then they carried these things out of the cave.

          Melodie and Nikolai followed. They were confused, because that was not mentioned in the plan. And then Overbuild spoke and his voice was heard all over the Forbidden Valley: “We have defeated Asano Naganori! We have defeated your leader! You can see the body he took over, his rebuilt statue and the Basin of Sight that is cracked! He is defeated! And now we are coming for you!”

          Every Spirit Ronin in Forbidden Valley heard him; every Spirit Ronin in Forbidden Valley saw the objects he showed them (their sight was probably better than normal minifigures’); and every Spirit Ronin in Forbidden Valley took flight immediately. . .

Chapter 20: In which Xeno wakes up again Part 1

 

          As Melodie watched the Ronin retreat in the dark corners of the Forbidden Valley, out of the Nexus Force’s reach, she wondered how this was achieved so easily.

          “How?!” she finally asked.

          “The Ronin know that Asano is the strongest of them all. So, seeing him defeated they knew they’d be defeated, too, finally. They considered that, especially being leaderless, it was not worth trying to conquer everything if eventually they lost!” Overbuild explained.

          “But how did they know he was defeated?” Nikolai expressed the query of both.

          “Apart from seeing his statue and the unconscious body, there was also the Basin! It was an artefact made for Asano himself and so was connected to him. It could only be destroyed, if he was defeated!” Shadow replied “You guys should really be studying more history!”

          “But what was that Basin?”

          “It was an object that allowed Asano to view any area in many kilometers around him! Did you think he came to that cave just to look from the high altitude of the place? If it was so, he’d spend more time outside the cave than inside it!” Shadow said as if he was pointing out the obvious.

          “Wow! That headache’s really the worst!” said Xeno’s voice and it was really Xeno’s.

          Melodie turned to Xeno, who had just woken up and tried to run towards him, but her hurt legs got in the way. Nikolai was as excited as her to see him woken and still… Xeno!

          “Do you remember anything?” he asked.

          “Yes… unfortunately! To the littlest detail!” he answered and looking at Melodie’s leaning on the wall of the cave “Sorry!”

Chapter 20: In which Xeno wakes up again Part 2

 

          “It’s not your fault, Xeno!” Melodie comforted him.

          “It really is!” Shadow said cynically “But anyway I shall leave you with your girlfriend and your friend!”

          Shadow walked out and he didn’t seem to hear the “I’m not his girlfriend!” exclaim of Melodie. As Overbuild followed, Xeno remembered the incident in Nimbus Station and his cheeks reddened.

          As the three friends got out, too, Nikolai supporting them both, he and Melodie described him the events of when he was unconscious. Outside, Shadow was still nearby.

          “Well, at least it’s all over!” Xeno said looking at the Forbidden Valley spreading around them.

          Shadow turned and smiling responded “Over? Nothing is over! The Valley is full of Spirit Ronin! It all just began!”. . .
