As the orb returns to Bucky's hand and he andWOM, Savos, Hov, and I'm assuming Alpha prepare to fly off, a crackling DI Vortex opens in the sky, and a small, stocky ship that has a large bulge in the rear section emerges. A reinforced glass dome near the bridge area of the ship opens with a slight hiss, and a figure wearing a billowing, extremely cool looking brand new longcoat and a pair of fingerless leather gloves floats down to the car.

 

Stepping into the side door, he sits down on the back trunk, and just sits there, an attentive look on his face. He seems to be waiting for Bucky to say something. Realising that the majority of the people here have no idea who he was, he gains an understanding but slightly embarrassed look. 

 

"Sorry, I've been alone with mindless Proto-Lifeforms and a cranky old man for company inside of a ship for a month. Im still trying to reclaim my former self. It may be gone for good, I'm afraid. But I am General Ambris Raptorum of the Giovanni Armed Forces, and captain of the nifty looking beauty up there in the atmosphere. But most people call me Sparrowhawk, or Sparrow, or Hawk, or... You get it. Anyway, I'm here to help my friend, Bucky. Also to destroy the Maelstrom. See, they kinda smashed my parents some thirty years ago, left me an orphan, etc. And now I can have my closure."

 

Sparrowhawk clicks his fingers and the bulging area of the ship opens up, revealing four Vortex Generators. They all begin to spin, crackling with energy. 

 

"That, is what I call a 'Mobile Buddy Maker'. The combined concentration of DI in a close proximity generates thousands of DI Spawn per minute. And since those Generators are linked with my now enhanced neurons, they all work for me. Just tell me where to point them."

 

~Sparrowhawk

I would like to make a few slight corrections to Sparrowhawk’s post. He spoke for the group without consulting the group, and he made a few mistakes.

 

It’s true that DI and Maelstrom are non-reactive. There isn’t any chemical reaction between the two. But I’d like to compare them to metal and wood, respectively. They don’t DO anything when they touch, but they can still whack at eachother. The way Sparrow phrased it, he made it sound like Maelstrom just can’t do anything to DI, which isn’t true. DI and Maelstrom can still ‘fight’, as it were, but it’s just that nothing neat happens when they do. Each one would treat the other like any other normal bit of matter. Raw DI itself isn’t corruptible. The metal in the car is, however.

 

We would like you to explain the darkness in the air. Sparrowhawk’s method of seeing would definitely see through any normal smog, which makes your smog unique. Also, the opinion of the group is that you still need to explain why you were able to survive being erased from reality on the Giovanni topic. We have not “let you get away with it” as Sparrowhawk said. I apologize for the miscommunication.

 

Also, on a more personal note, I like to think Dark Imagination is quite balanced. That’s more of my opinion rather than a correction, though.
