Uproar seems not to react to the message, for some reason.

 

Apocalypse, however, grins, and goes to Uproar's office.

 

The Maelstrom Leader looks like a ghost of his former self, so weak he could barely lift a feather.

 

Apocalypse, on the other hand, looks strong enough to lift the whole Maelstrom HQ. He points his staff at Uproar, and a red energy flows through him, making him appear strong once more.

 

As if nothing happened, Uproar said, "Ah, Apocalypse, did you see Pseudus' message?"

 

Apocalypse nodded, "Yes indeed. You could use the neutral sphere to create a new army."

 

Uproar smiled, "Of Course. We must load the forces at once, we-"

 

Apocalypse halted him, "No. Leave the others to lead. We shall go...alone. Wouldn't want to alert the Nexus Force that we got the message."

 

Uproar nodded, and said, "I will inform my stromlings to prepair a small drop ship. Whilst we are gone, your heralds and my maelstrom leaders will lead the armies."

 

Apocalypse nodded to him as he left to prepair. Parasitic creeped into the room after Uproar left, and Apocalypse said to the insect, "Prepair. Take out the other leaders, take their places. We shall show Uproar we do not need the Maelstrom to become great. And send Destruction to keep that Veratanium substance out of production. He no doubt can use that 'Maelstrom Fighter' for a prime ammount of destruction."

 

Parasitic nods his insect head to Apocalypse, and proceeds to scuttle out of the room.

 

Apocalypse grins evilly. "You call yourself Uproar? I'll show you what a true Uproar can be..." He walks in the same direction as Uproar.
