Up until now I had been able to manipulate the MPA schedule so that we would not be discovered... but now, you two must be.... eliminated!!! *With these words, Sherry the Evil Mythran Secretary instantly equips an awesome ninja outfit and two matching katanas, turning into Sherry the Evil Ninja Mythran Secretary*

 

Barry: Jonna always said ninja were bad news. *He and Opal dive out of the way of her attacks*

 

Minifigure #1: The Mythrans are fighting!! O_O

 

Minifigure #2: *Passes around the popcorn and pulls out some lawn chairs* *All four Minifigures sit down to watch the fight*

 

*Barry, Opal and Sherry battle for a while, but it soon becomes apparent that they are seriously outmatched, even with their advanced agent training. What Sherry lacked in finesse, she made up for in raw power as they individually summoned objects to aid them in battle while simultaneously fighting hand to hand*

 

Opal: How does she have so much energy to fight? I thought that Mardolf said that transporting Minifigures was difficult for the average Mythran! *Dodges another swing*

 

Sherry: I am far more powerful than any Mythran, Agent Bubbles! Yet when I joined the MPA, despite my unfathomable skillz, they refused to let me become an agent, just because I refused to work with such ludicrous codenames!
