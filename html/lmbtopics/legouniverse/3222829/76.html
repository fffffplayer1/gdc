363 days ago, I started writing a Christmas Skit....

 

363 days later, I have decided to finish it!!! I feel bad for never finishing it last year. D: The first five parts are on page 61 if any of you want to go back and refresh your memory. I apologize for my deplorable timing.

 

Christmas Skit Special ~ Part Six

 

 

*Barry and Spencer are in the ventilation shaft, and Barry is begging Spencer to tell him what is in Phil's present*

 

Barry: Please? Pretty please?

 

Spencer: No! You have to wait to find out.

 

Barry: *Sigh* Fine. Now let's keep going. *The two of them continue onward in their search for the correct shaft for them to drop out of* *Finally, after much searching, they look down a shaft and see the Christmas tree!*

 

*Both Mythrans drop down the shaft and look around the room, which is still criss-crossed with red lasers, and notice that Opal is standing right next to them by the tree*

 

Spencer: How did you get here?

 

Opal: I tunneled over with my hair supplies. *Points at a large, dark hole in the carpet at her feet*

 

Barry: Oh. I see.

 

*Opal, Spencer and Barry immediately grab the nearest presents addressed to them and shake them vigorously, listening carefully for any tell-tale sounds* *Suddenly, Phil appears next to them with multiple shopping bags on his arms, startling Barry, who falls backward into the tree*

 

Barry: Ow.

 

Phil: Hey, guys! I was hungry, so I had Mardolf teleport me to the kitchen so I could grab a snack, then asked him to port me to some nice shops to get some gifts for you three. Here, these are for you. *He reaches into the shopping bags and hands each gift to its recipient* You can open them now if you'd like. I don't mind.

 

Barry: *Opens his gift* Oh, wow. A screaming sock monkey. *Lifts it up and view it dubiously* Just what I wanted.

 

Opal: *Opens her gift as well* Neat, leopard slippers! They're so fuzzy! Thanks!

 

Spencer: *Opens his gift and pulls something out* What is this? Some kind of fluffy, pink, fuzz-ball with eyes?!.....

Christmas Skit Special ~ Part Eight

 

 

*The four mythrans cringe in fear of whatever terrible punishment is about to be inflicted upon them for escaping their prison- but wait! Is T.B. wearing.... a Santa hat?*

 

T.B.: MERRY CHRISTMAS, GUYS!!! *Tosses a handful of red and green confetti over himself and smiles hugely in a very un-Bossman like way*

 

*The agents look at each other in confusion, wondering why they have not yet been sent to detention, and why T.B. is acting disturbingly merry, when Phil's eyes brighten with understanding and he nods his head at the clock on the wall* *It reads 12:01, one minute after midnight and officially Christmas morning*

 

Barry: Oh, phew.... er, Merry Christmas to you too, Bossman!

 

T.B.: Though I do wish you all could have contained your excitement and waited a few more hours. *Yawn* I'm exhausted. But hey, it's Christmas! Let's open some presents! *He walks toward the tree, and everyone sits around it and pass out the presents*

 

Opal: *Opens the gift that everyone else had gotten for her* Instant-drying nail polish! Thanks guys! Now I don't have to stare at them to make sure they are dry before doing anything.

 

Phil: *Mutters* Thank goodness.

Christmas Skit Special ~ Part Nine

 

 

Spencer: *Shreds up the wrapping paper on the gift that everyone gave to him* *Gasp* It's a Roger the Goat plushie!!! *Squeezes it expermentially*

 

Roger the Goat Plushie: *In a monotone voice* Hi, Uncle Spencer. *

 

Spencer: AMAZING!!! I LOVE IT!!! Way better than some pink ball of fuzz. *Glares at Phil*

 

*The door opens and Roger the Goat walks in, wearing an officer badge on a festive red and green vest*

 

Roger the Goat: *In monotone voice* I am offended that someone ordered a plush toy made in my likeness for the amusment of the Mythran who wrongly considers me to be adorable. I will now consume this affront to my existance. *Roger begins striding across the room towards a very shocked Spencer, when he steps on a large piece of wrapping paper that is spread out on the ground* *It crumples underneath his hooves effortlessly, and he plummets down into the hole that Opal had dug with an even, monotone "Woah."* *A giant crash is heard a few yards below them*

 

T.B.: Weird. How did that hole get there?

 

Opal: Uhh, no idea. *She quickly pulls two manhole covers out of her hair supply bag and puts one on top of the hole, before running back to her room to cover the other end of the tunnel with the second cover* *She then comes back and sits down next to the manhole cover* I'll let him out later. Your turn, Barry!

 

Barry: *Wondering if he even wants to know what sewer pipe covers are even doing in Opal's hair supplies, he neatly and precisely cuts open the gift he got with his pocketknife*

 

------------------------------------------

 

 

* This is a reference to Chapter 13, on page 14, where Spencer asks Roger to say "Hi, Uncle Spencer," but was denied. Interestingly, I never planned for Roger to end up in this chapter, but he kind of just ended up there.
