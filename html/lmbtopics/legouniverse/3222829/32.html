A year ago, a game called LEGO Universe closed. It was a sad thing. Many children cursed those who made the choice to do so. Some cried…. some did nothing.  And others? Well, they wrote stories about the game, the worlds they loved and knew for so long. Those minifigures they built? Well, they’re fighting on Crux’s front, while we fight here. With stories.  People like Jonna Fig, and Ninjago_Builders       (Who sadly is not here with us, he’s offline for who knows how long) and majorq10hero keep LEGO Universe alive with their stories of imagination.  And I salute those brave mini figs, and authors who keep LEGO Universe alive.

 

                                                                Thank you
