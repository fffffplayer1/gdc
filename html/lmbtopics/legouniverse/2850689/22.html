* A NF marked dropship lands on a docking bay, and a Sentinel Scout Troop Commando steps out.* The leader of the Wizard Order, the Wise Old Man, is requiested by the commands of General Fire-type, to come to the Nimbus Station (the Galactic Nexus Republic topic) and assist him on leading the NF with the rest of the faction leaders during Lindel's absence.

*There is a flash of white light and the Wise Old Man walks through the door into a conference room in the Nexus Tower*

Good day Fire-Type. It seems this shall be a full time job then since Lindel is unfortunately always out of commission. I will do what I can.

We are currently in a good position. The Maelstrom is of no threat to us, the Void is no longer on the offensive, and the SoL don't seem to be an immediate threat. I suggest we use this time wisely and rebuild and revive.

Also, are we allies with MIT still? Currently it's leader and Bane are in conflict. Are we taking sides on this conflict or shall we let it resolve itself?

There is also a matter of most urgency I must tell you about. I have sensed the other godswords have entered this universe. As you know already, I possess the Saradomin one, but if they were to fall into the hands of someone evil, it would be catastrophic. We must regain their pieces before someone else does. I will tell you more of this later, but they are given their name for good reason. Even an immortal can't survive an encounter with one.

~Wise Old Man

*Hands you a cup of coffee.* Good day to you too, Wise Old Man. We have loosened up our alliance with the MIT. We are getting more inventions and technology out of the faction research facilities now. Also, you have said my line. We MUST take advantage of this time given to us. The problem is, it seems that we are divided more than ever with the absence of Lindel. We MUST stay together, for this news of the godswords are most disturbing. We must set off at once to recover these godswords. In fact, you DO know that I have the Armadyl one? We must hurry, my friend. - General Fire-type

*Something bursts from the ground, knocking both of you back with its dark energies: A red-eyed shadow, spawn of Emperor Nargaroth*

*The shadow shriekes, and slices and the Wise Old Man with a shadowy sword. However, it misses, chopping off only a bit of the wizard's beard*

~Emperor Nargaroth~


I felt that signal from Sir Computer. *I fly to Nagaroth and use the Earthquake spell, opening a hole below you, and you fall down it.* Bye bye!

Silly you. I'm not here.
And shadows don't fall.

*The Shadow hurredly grabs the hair from the ground*

*It then vanishes in a puff of black flames*

Sir Computer, you have failed. Wise Old Man... there are some who wont allow the power to defeat a deity into your hands...

~Emperor Nargaroth~

Ah, a little friend from Emperor Nargaroth. I'm guessing you're taking my hair because you think you can do something with it. Well, I'm afraid that you just made it harder on yourself.

*The Wise Old Man raises his hands and an angelic being appears*

Find the Shadow and destroy it.

*The being vanishes in a flash*

And I already have the power to defeat a deity in my hands. I already have the sword. I'm worried about others like you getting the other swords pieces. But you just gave me a valid reason to destroy you I'm afraid.

~Wise Old Man

The speed of darkness is vastly superior to what you can throw at me... I'll have your hair in no time.
And I know that you have the first godsword. But with four... I can not let you thwart my plans, although that in itself is almost impossible.
I shall double my speed and go towards the Shadow. Then, if your servant dares battle me... you will feel real pain,

~Emperor Nargaroth~

I'm not all that I appear to be. . . You would be foolish to fight that being. Its powers come from no artificially created dimension. . . Your shadow may be faster than me, but not faster than the being. And it is no servant of mine, no, it is a friend.

*The angelic being speeds towards the shadow*

~Wise Old Man

*My H'roth and I zoom towards it at incredible speeds*

The LEGO Universe Dimension is artificial, yes. But I am not. And even if I don't get that lock of hair... You lose.

*Dozens of shadows materialize before me, and vanish in black flame*

For when the 9 Creators are revived, I shall open a portal to the Eternity Vault... and nothing will stop the wrath of the being emprisoned in there by the Prime Dimension. They're as old as the Void itself... locked away by those who feared eternal darkness. Locked away in a prison that no one could escape from... not even death.
The Eternity Vault shall open... and there's nothing you can do about it...

OoC: That's a hint for my plot twist. However, I'll tell you this: Even though you can't reverse what is to come, Nargaroth wont have a good time when the prison is opened...

~Emperor Nargaroth~

Your quite right Nargaroth. My power isn't great enough to stop you. However, you have overlooked quite a bit. . . You may know about many powerful dark secrets, but there beings of the light and of justice who are more powerful than you could imagine, yet they do not show themselves to you because of your evil ways. I do not know what this "Eternity Vault" is. But I will tell you this, no matter how powerful these beings are inside, something more powerful put them there.

*The angelic being glows scattering the shadows and revealing them*

*Nargaroth feels as if it is reading him and in an instant all evil he has done flashes before his eyes*

*The being vanishes*

Have my hair. Do what you will to this universe. But you will fail.

OOC; Interesting plot twist preview. I put this in here just so there will be "some" mysterious beings of power that aren't evil. Aka, the Void, Dungeon Masters, Enternity Vault, etc.

~Wise Old Man
~Watchful Eye of the Swords of Light
