--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The spells of each type of magic will be in order from weakest to strongest (And in most cases, easiest to hardest). They will all have a name, level of difficulty (Eg. 4D, up to 10), level of power (Eg. 5P, up to 10), level of shielding (Eg. 9S, up to 10), and effect.


»»Fire Magic««
Fire magic is very powerful, but also very hard to control. Fire magic can easily destroy most things made out of ice, and most Nature magic, but can easily be beaten by water.


Ember I — 1D, 1P, Basic Spell — Creates an ember that is capable of starting a small campfire.

Fireball I — 1D, 3P, Basic Spell — Shoots a fireball at your opponent.

Ember II — Varies — Creates fire. Power varies.

Fireball II — 2-5D, 4-6P, Intermediate Spell-Expert Spell — Creates a fireball in both of your hands. Size varies.

Fire Wave — 4D, 6P, Intermediate Spell — Creates a wave of fire that is very dangerous. It will melt almost all ice, and water can easily destroy it.

Firewall I (I know.... ) — 4D, 5S, Intermediate Spell — Creates a wall of fire in front of you. Almost anything ice that goes near it melts. Water will destroy the Firewall

Firewall II (Goes all around you) — 5D, 6S, Intermediate Spell — Creates a wall of fire that goes around you. Almost anything ice that goes near it melts. Water will destroy it.

Fire Sphere — 8D, 9S, Expert Spell — Creates a sphere of fire around and above you, but you are vulnerable below. Almost anything ice that goes near it melts, and most other objects or magic will fail to get through it. Strong Water Magic can destroy it.

Bolt of Zamerike — 9D, 9P, Expert Spell — Flames fall from the sky. It is capable of destroying forests. Expert Water Magic can stop it.

Fire Tsunami — 10D, 9P, Expert Spell — Creates a huge wave of fire. It will melt almost everything made out of ice, and only few water spells can stop it. It will destroy ENTIRE FORESTS, and is very hard to control. Only the most advanced wizards should use this.

Fire Dragon — 10D, 8P, Expert Spell — Creates a dragon made out of fire. The dragon is capable of breathing fire, and can easily melt ice. Most Intermediate water spells can take this out. It is hard to control.



»»Water Magic««
When fighting against a Fire Magic user, Water Magic is the best thing you've got. Water is by far the best against Fire magic, and doesn't have any big weak spots. Water Magic can be strengthened by electric-based magic, but electricity will hurt you when in a Water Sphere. Water is average in attack, difficulty, and control, but it makes up for it by being able to beat Fire magic and having not any big weaknesses.


Puddle — 1D, 0P, Basic Spell — Creates a puddle. Doesn't do much.

Waterball — 1D, 1P, Basic Spell — Shoots a small sphere of water at your opponent. It is not very powerful, more annoying than anything else. All this spell is really good at is making your opponent annoyed and wet.

Wave — 3D, 5P, Intermediate Spell — Creates a wave. It can be frozen by ice, and can destroy fire.

Water Wall I — 3D, 4S, Intermediate Spell — Creates a wall of fire. It can be frozen by ice, which really just makes it stronger. Most objects and magic can get through this, just not fire.

Water Wall II — 4D, 5S, Intermediate Spell — See above, but goes all around you.

Water Sphere I — 5D, 6S, Intermediate Spell — Creates a sphere of water around you. It is barely stronger than the Water Wall, but it protects you from every direction (Except the ground, that is). Electricity will harm you if you are inside the Water Sphere

Water Sphere II — 8D, 8S, Expert Spell — Creates a sphere of water around you. The water moves extremely fast, making it impossible for most things to get through it. Electricity will still harm you if you are inside it.

Tsunami — 8D, 7P, Expert Spell — Creates a huge wave of water. It can be frozen by ice, and become more powerful from electricity. It is MUCH easier to control than the Fire Tsunami, but not as destructive. Can easily destroy anything fire




Water Dragon — 9D, 7P — Creates a dragon made out of water. The dragon is capable of spewing water extremely fast and can easily destroy anything fire. The only ways to stop the Water Dragon is to defeat the caster, un-summon it, or freeze it.



»»Nature Magic (Druid)««
Nature Magic is the specialty of the Druids, but others can learn it. Nature Magic is very weak to Fire Magic, and isn't powerful against anything. Nature Magic is the weakest kind of magic, and their specialty in Basic Spells are basically tripping people. The spells really only get good at Expert level, so usually people will choose a different starter magic.


Plant Summon — 1D, N/A, Basic Spell — Creates a small plant. Nothing else.

Vine Summon I — 2D, N/A, Basic Spell — Creates a vine that can trip your opponent.

Bush Summon — 3D, N/A, Intermediate Spell — Creates a bush.

Vine Summon II — 3D, N/A, Intermediate Spell — Creates a vine as strong as wood.

Vine Wall I — 4D, 3S, Intermediate Spell — Creates a wall made of vines. Ice can make it more solid; electricity has no effect the first time but continue using electricity and it will disintegrate/kill the vines. A beginner fire spell can easily burn this.

Animal Call I — 4D, 3P, Intermediate Spell — Summons small animals to attack your opponent.

Tree Summon — 5D, 0P, Expert Spell — Creates a tree. Bigger trees are more difficult.

Vine Summon III — 6D, 0P, Expert Spell — Creates a vine as strong as steel.

Vine Wall II — 6D, 5S, Expert Spell — Creates a wall made of vines as strong as rock. Only strong Fire Magic can destroy it.

Animal Call II — 6D, 7P, Expert Spell — Summons larger animals (Eg. Bears, Wolves, etc.) to attack your opponent.

Nature Dragon — 8D, 7P, Expert Spell — Creates a dragon made out of vines and plants. The dragon is not capable of any special powers, and can be defeated by any Expert Fire Spell.



»»Ice Magic««
Ice Magic has a few unique powers. It can easily be defeated by fire, but can freeze both water and plants (Nature Magic) solid. Ice Magic can put up a very good fight against pretty much anything other than fire.


Snowball — 1D, 1P, Basic Spell — Creates a snowball. You know what a snowball is, I don't think I need to explain it any further. Can be melted by fire.

Iceball — 2D, 2P, Basic Spell — Creates a ball of ice. It's the same thing as a snowball; just it hurts a lot more. Can be melted by fire.

Ice Floor — 4D, 0P, Intermediate Spell — Makes the ground turn to ice and slippery. May cause tripping. Can be melted by fire.

Ice Wall I — 4D, 5S, Intermediate Spell — Creates a hard, solid wall of ice in front of you. It can be broken, and it can also be melted by fire.

Ice Wall II — 5D, 6S, Intermediate Spell — See above, goes all around you.

Water Freeze I — 4D, N/A, Intermediate Spell — Freezes a small amount of water.

Hail — 4D, 3P, Intermediate Spell — Causes it to hail. The bigger the object, the harder it is to do. Please do not abuse size. That means hail as big as castles (or mountains) are NOT ALLOWED.

Water Freeze II — 6D, N/A, Expert Spell — Freezes water. Can freeze up to the size of a lake.

Ice Sphere — 7D, 8S, Expert Spell — Creates a hard, solid sphere of ice around and above you. Strong Fire Magic (Or really hot fire) can destroy this. The bottom is vulnerable.

Ice Golem — 9D, 8P, Expert Spell — Creates a golem out of ice. Only Expert Fire Magic or lava can melt it.

Ice Dragon — 9D, 8P, Expert Spell — Creates a dragon of ice. Its breath can freeze objects solid. Lava or Expert Fire Magic can only melt it.


»»Light Magic««
Light magic is one of the very unique kinds of magic. It isn’t very offensive, making it not a very good magic to use in a fight. Light Magic is stronger against Shadow Magic.


Light Orb — 1D, N/A, Basic Spell — Creates an orb of light that can be thrown. It will destroy weak Shadow Magic spells. It can also light up a small room. Has no effect on anything else.

Flash — 1D, 1P, Basic Spell — Blinds opponent for a few seconds.

Light Barrier I — 3D, 4S, Intermediate Spell — Creates a wall of light in front of you. It blocks some Shadow Spells, nothing else.

Light Beam — 4D, 4P Intermediate Spell — Shoots a beam of light that will destroy some obstacles in its path. It is much more effective against Shadow Magic than Light Orbs.

Light Embue — 5D, 6S Intermediate Spell — Embue yourself with the power of light. Dark type spells aren't very effective at all and all other magic's effectiveness is reduced. It also offers protection against weak melee weapons, but not powerful ones.

Light Barrier II — 6D, 7S, Expert Spell — Creates walls of light all around you. It blocks many types of magic and objects. It blocks Shadow Magic very well

Saradomin Strike — 6D, 7P Expert Spell — Calls down a beam of light that will obliterate most targets. It sends a shockwave after the initial hit.

Gaurdians of the Light — 10D 9P Expert Spell — Summons the three guardians of the light. They are somewhat angel like and are around ten feet tall each. They are extremely powerful and hard to defeat. They are considered elite NPCs and again, can only be done by the head of each department minus the machinemage.

Light Dragon — 8D, 7P Expert Spell — Creates a dragon made of light energy, it is extremely fast compared to the other dragons, but has less power. It is capable of creating a beam of light out of its mouth that is stronger than Light Beam.
