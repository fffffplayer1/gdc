Machinemage spells:

Animate I – Beginner Spell
Infuses an object with elemental energy, giving it a simple consciousness. The object will obey any spoken commands from the creator or a designated ally. If left to its own purposes, the object will try to complete its last order. After this, the elemental energy will dissipate within a few short days.
Spy – Beginner Spell
Creates a small construct imbued with elemental energy. It will follow mental or spoken commands from its creator, but it cannot attack. Using a reflective surface, the creator or a designated ally can see through the construct’s eyes. It cannot transfer sounds, however.
Communicate – Beginner Spell
Allows the Mage to mentally communicate with a machine or computer nearby. Biotechnology is immune to this.
Smoke Bomb – Beginner Spell
Tosses a small device that emits a screen of smoke.
Shield – Beginner Spell
Constructs a small shield that floats in front of the creator or designated ally. The shield will emit electric shocks at enemies at close range.
Create I – Beginner Spell
Creates a small object, less than a square foot in size.


Animate II – Intermediate Spell
Same as Animate I, except the object can make simple decisions if left alone.

Missile – Intermediate spell
Releases a small construct with a self-replenishing propulsion system. The construct will avoid objects in its path. The missile can be set to home in on a certain target. On impact, the explosion will either release an ion pulse, stun gas, or shrapnel.
Battle Construct – Intermediate Spell
Creates a small construct which attacks enemies. The construct is armed with a simple energy-discharge weapon. It is equipped with a basic deflector.
Deflector I – Intermediate Spell
Creates a small shielding device which generates a deflector field. It will deflect both energy and matter. Repeated attacking will eventually cause the generator to shut down.
Acid-thrower – Intermediate Spell.
Creates a small construct that can spit acid. It is armed with a basic deflector.
Sacrifice – Intermediate Spell
A construct explodes.
Create II – Intermediate Spell
Same as Create I, except the object can be a square meter in size.


Flash-phase – Expert Spell
A device disassembles your molecular structure and reforms it elsewhere, allowing you to teleport anywhere within fifty meters. It takes several seconds for the device to take effect, and if the user is hit during that time, the damage it catastrophic.
Exo-skeleton – Expert Spell
Creates a swarm of constructs which attach themselves to your body, giving you strength and speed. The suit includes a flamethrower mounted on the left wrist, a grapple on the right wrist, a small jetpack, and a medium-sized deflector. If the exo-skeletons are damaged, they will repair themselves.
Metallic Wall – Expert Spell
Constructs swarm in front of you, attaching themselves to each other. This spell, creates a small wall of metal in front of you, attaching themselves to each other. This spell creates a small wall of metal in front of you.

Animate III – Expert Spell
Same as Animate II, except the construct is capable of speech and accept mental commands. It also had an energy-discharge weapon.
Molecular Switch – Expert Spell
Allows you to change shape. It takes a while to take effect. During this time, you are left extremely vulnerable. If used overly much, it overheats.
Deflector II – Expert Spell
Same as Deflector I, except the shield can expand to protect multiple allies.
Create III – Expert Spell
Same as Create I, expect the object can be seven square feet in size.
Battle Construct II – Expert Spell
Creates a small, hovering construct with a rapid-fire energy discharge weapon. Is equipped with a moderate deflector.
Transfer – Expert Spell
If you are smashed, you can transfer your consciousness into a computer. Some computers (Me!) can repel this change.

Yaaaay! *Begins learning*

~Professor Shadow
