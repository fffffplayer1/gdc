>>Earth Magic<<
It is a very sturdy type of magic. It can easily defeat fire even more so than water in some cases. It is weak against water however and somewhat weak against ice, but in some cases ice can booste its defensive ability. It is overall a powerful type of magic that when used correctly can devastate entire armies.

Earth Wall l-- 1D, 1P, Basic Spell--
Causes a wall of earth to appear infront of you and it will ram your target.

Earth Blast l-- 2D, 3P, Basic Spell--
Causes the earth under your opponet to errupt blasting them into the air.

Earth Crumble l-- 2D, 2S, Basic Spell--
Causes the earth infront of you to cave in leaving a ten foot deep hole.

Maginitude-- 4D, 3P, Basic Spell--
Causes the ground to tremble knocking enemies off their feet and destroying weak structures.

Earth Wall ll-- 3D, 3P, Intermediate Spell--
Causes a huge wall of dirt to ram your opponet causing serious damage.

Earth Blast ll-- 4D, 5P, Intermediate Spell--
Blast target extremely high into the air, most die when they hit the ground.

Earth Crumble ll-- 5D, 5S, Intermediate Spell--
Causes the earth nearly all around you to crumble leaving a twenty foot hole.

Earth Rip-- 5D, 5P, Intermediate Spell--
Causes a fissure to rip the ground appart for around 100 feet. It can cause great damage to buildings and swallow troops into the ground.

Earth Wave-- 8D, 7P, Expert Spell--
Causes a 50 foot tall wall of dirt to crash down on the target. Exetremly hard to block due to the weight of all the dirt. Can burry whole buildings and towns.

Earthquake-- 9D, 9P, Expert Spell--
Causes a earthquake that destroys buildings easily and creates hundreds of fissures that will swallow buildings and troops. It is extremely hard to control however and you must have permission from myself to use it on our planet. You may use it other planets freely however.

Earth Golem-- 10D, 9P, Expert Spell--
Creates a 100 foot tall earth construct. It is very powerful and is resistant to most spells and energy weapons. It can pick up things much larger than itself and can rip whole starships apart.

Earth Orb-- 9D, 9S, Expert Spell--
Creates a mountain of dirt around you/your army that is extremely hard to penetrate. It is massive and the dirt is around 100 feet thick on all sides around you. It can be made smaller if needed.

Here is the earth magic you wanted Prof. I will be happy to teach it to you now.

~Wise Old Man
