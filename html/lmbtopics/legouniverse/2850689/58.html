‎11-07-2014 10:44 PM

A farmer pushed his cart into town, the sun beating down on his back. Local peasants were toiling away, generally unaffected by the news of the recent chaos that had struck the kingdom. The Wizard's creations had gotten loose in the capital, apparently, and many were making a fuss about it. But not here. Here life went on, even though many sensed the feeling of stagnancy in the air, as if the world was a fire that had lost its fuel.

 

All of a sudden the sky split open. Denizens of this world were used to odd occurrences, but this, this felt... Wrong. As if unmaking itself were seeping in from the abyss. The feeling caused vertigo to many, and those who were sensitive to such things were knocked out cold. But it only lasted a few seconds, long enough to deposit it's payload. 

 

A small silver sphere fell in the middle of the road, barely missing the farmer and instead hitting his cart. There was a flash of red, and the next thing the farmer knew, his cart and the sphere were in a four foot deep crater. A figure fell out of a hole in the sphere, clutching his head. He was wearing outlandish clothing. 

 

"Oh my head... Hov, what did you do?" Sparrowhawk moaned, lying back in the scattered hay and soil. 

 

Then a hooded figure stuck his head out, using his staff to climb out of the wreckage. The Farmer gasped. It was the Wizard! He quickly kneeled. Sparrowhawk climbed out of the crater, dusted off his shoulders, and straightened his coat. 

 

"So this is home sweet home?"

~Sparrowhawk
