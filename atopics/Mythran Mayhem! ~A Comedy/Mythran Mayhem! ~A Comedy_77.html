Barry: *Groans* *Hands him his pocketknife*Just use this.

 

Phil: *Mutters* Thanks. *He cuts the ribbon, hands the knife back and yanks the wrapping paper off of his present*

*Phil squeals with joy and faints*

Opal: We couldn't find anything that said Bossman, so we just added it onto the one that said Boss.

 

T.B.: *Sniffles* It- it's- it's beautiful, guys. *Pulls out a hankerchief and dabs at his eyes, touched*

 

Mardolf: Good, now I can pass out my gifts! Feel free to open them all at once. *He hands one of the long presents to everyone, including Phil, who has woken from his brief, euphoric, comatose state*

 

*Everyone opens the presents at the same time, and reveal that they have all received- are those gardening tools?*

 

Mardolf: Hoe, hoe, hoe, everybody! Merry Christmas!!! *He laughes uproarously at his own wit*
