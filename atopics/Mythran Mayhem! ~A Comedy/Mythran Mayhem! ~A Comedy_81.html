Chapter 25 ~ Part 1

 

*Somewhere, in a very immediate galaxy, on a very small and inconspicuous planet chunk, The Bossman is frustrated*

 

TB: Agent Phone! I need a status report, pronto!

 

*Agent Phone (better known as Phil) spins in his office chair until he is facing TB, giving a salute*

 

Phil: The energy readings are still fluctuating madly on all of our Tech Support gadgets. We still aren't able to pinpoint the source, nor have we been able to find out who might be responsible.

 

TB: Hmm. My money's on Sherry- she's certainly capable of creating such massive readings.

 

Phil: I disagree. Whatever it is, it isn't her. This energy is very unlike imagination- in fact, it's unlike anything we've encountered.

 

Spencer: *Is sitting a few feet away in his own office chair, typing at a keyboard and observing multiple screens at once* Actually, we've never encountered anything except for imagination.

 

Phil: Irrelevant.

 

TB: *Sigh* *Looks around the room- namely Tech Support- where all four members of Team Tuna are using various gadgets. Or at least looking like it- Barry is actually playing Angry Duckz on a different tab, Opal is too busy being scared of live mice to pay attention to her gadget, Phil is spinning 'round 'n 'round in his office chair and Spencer has a football game playing on one screen* I knew I should have put a more experienced team on this case. *TB takes a seat in his own Deluxe Office Chair (complete with built in mini-cooler and micro-microwave) and strokes his chin thoughtfully* *He had called all hands on deck in order to investigate sudden and wild energy readings that had popped up in the MPA base* *It had been just his luck that the only team available had been Team Tuna*

 

TB: *Furrows his eyebrows and starts talking to himself* Hmm. If not Sherry... than who could be doing this? And just what type of energy are the sensors picking up, anyway? *He settles into a deep-thinking pose*

Chapter 25 ~ Part 2

 

*His deep thoughts were rudely interrupted by a huge flash of light, which temporarily blinds everyone in Tech Support*

 

Spencer: AAAHHH! I can't see! Why can't I see?!

 

Opal: AAGGH, THE MICE ARE GONNA GET ME!

 

Mouse: Help! I'm blind!

 

Mouse #2: So am I!

 

Mouse #3: Oh no! Me too!

 

*The three blind mice blunder back into their mouse hole*

 

TB: *Yells in an attempt to calm everyone* SILENCE!

 

*After a few moments the initial ruckus dies off, and the bright light slowly fades as everyone's eyes recover. However, as soon as they can see again, they are shocked to discover that a group of strange creatures have appeared in the middle of Tech Support!*

 

Mysterious Individual #1: *Blinks, looking very woozy* Where are we?

 

Mysterious Individual #2: *Rubs head* I've no idea. What color button did you press?

 

Mysterious Individual #3: I wasn't paying attention- heeey, wait a minute... this feels a bit familiar... *Looks around carefully, as if trying to remember where he's seen this place before*

 

Mysterious Individual: *Wheezes* Quuooocckk.

 

TB: *TB is totally aghast, his mouth hanging open* What on Crux are you four?!

 

*They were some of the strangest people he had ever seen* *Two of them are Minifigures, yet have unusual appearances. The first one is picking up a slightly squished top hat and looking at it sadly, while the other one has wild brown hair and begins sniffing the air while muttering something about a smell of cookies* *The other two are totally alien. Seeming to be some kind of waterfowl, the first has pink feathers and a pink dishtowel tied around her neck, and the other wears a dark and oddly-shaped helmet that hides his head entirely*

Chapter 25 ~ Part 4

 


TB: Don't expect me to do anything about it! I have enough of my own problems. I've been dealing with a crisis here, and it's bad enough without having to deal with a bunch of teleporting lunatics who illogically think they've been bending the rules of space and time.

 

Phil: *Stops staring at the Mysterious Individuals long enough to spare a glance for the gadget on his desk* *He executes an admirable double-take before speaking up* Uh, TB? The energy fluctuations are gone.

 

TB: WHAT?! You mean they went kaput and we didn't even notice?!

 

Phil: Not exactly. The readings say that there was a gigantic spike just before that bright light blinded us all and these weird people appeared.

 

Barry: *Swivels in his own office chair to face the three Mysterious Individuals and brilliantly deduces,* Aha! So you're the perpetrators!

 

Mysterious Individual #3: Quuooock. Probably, yeah.

 

TB: Why are you here? Do you intend to attack our base?!

 

Mysterious Individual #1: We weren't planning on it, though I'll keep it in mind if I get bored.

 

Mysterious Individual #2: *Glares at Mysterious Individual #1* *Stands up and shakes her wings, using her beak to straighten out her feathers* Aside from that, how are we going to get out of here? *Quack*

 


*The answer arrives with another blinding flash of light, and another creature appears!!* *This one is the strangest yet: it wears orange robes, carries a tall staff with a heavy crystal on top, and sports a badly brushed beard; oh, it's Mardolf*

 

Mardolf: Duckz Vader, Super Duck, Supreme Duckz Commander- a pleasure to finally meet you all. *Nods at them*

 

Mysterious Individual #3/Duckz Vader: Quuuoooock! Who are you?

 

Mardolf: Agent Scotty, though you may know me better as Mardolf the Orange. I'm your ticket out of here.

Chapter 25 ~ Part 5

 


Mysterious Individual #2/Super Duck: As far as I know, you can't teleport through dimensions, so how do you plan to get us back? *Quack*

 

Mardolf: *Smiles cheerfully* I won't. *He waves a hand over the crystal on his staff and murmurs a magic word. In response, a fuzzy image appears inside of it* *A voice crackles from the crystal as the images fizzles and refocuses*

 

Voice: Hello? *crackle,fffffsssshhh* Is this thing- *zzt* -working?

 

Mardolf: *Holds the staff up so everyone can get a good look at it* There's quite a bit of interference, but we can hear you well enough, Nosh. *The picture in the crystal is actually a real time video feed of a spaceship, hovering in space*

 

Voice/Alo Nosh: *Crackle* Good, good. *zzt* I've got the Aluminum Fowl *zzzt* idling nearby your location. It's a funny story, actually, Insane N_B came by andfffffffsssssshhhhhh.... *The connection fizzles into static*

 

Mardolf: *Mumbles something with irritation and hits the crystal with his hand* *A few jumbled words come through before fading out again* *Frustrated, Mardolf violently smacks the crystal into the ground, and the signal returns much clearer than before*

 

Alo Nosh: *Crackling fades entirely*...and so I said, "You push a hard bargain, N_B," and he said, "I know I do." So with a heavy heart I agreed to the terms and gave him my fresh-baked tray of pumpkin muffins, and he gave me the watch. I figured I'd better install that neato little thing right away, and now my ship is snazzier than ever, thanks to him! Even if it was a high cost to pay... *Sniffle* Anyway, I decided to test it out and maybe swing by and pick you guys up, seeing as he left you all stranded. Mardolf's there to teleport you all onto my ship, since I can't fly any closer to the MPA base. They have some nasty looking anti-aircraft turrets down there.
