*At the MPA base, Team Tuna is having a day off* *They are enjoying their time, busy with their respective hobbies*

 

T.B.: *Bursts into the room* SOMETHING TERRIBLE HAS HAPPENED!!!!

 

Barry: That sounded horrifically familiar. Isn't that what you said when we got merged?

 

T.B.: Yes. I filed that incident underneath CH-MRGD, in section 64 of the Dustbin. But now it looks like I'll never get to file anything again.

 

Phil: What do you mean, Boss?

 

T.B.: See for yourself. Here's the official announcement.

 

 

WhiteAlligator:

 

On Friday, June 10 this subforum will be closed.

 

All currently active topics will be merged into Classics main.

 

Any topic with 2,000 or more posts cannot be moved and will no longer be accessible after June 10.

 

Please post in "Talk to the Mods" in the Help & Suggestions forum with any questions.

 

 

Opal: Not another merge!!

 

Spencer: I agree it's sad, but we'll be fine, right? I mean, we thought we'd lose a bunch of stuff last time but it got brought over. It's not that bad.

 

T.B.: *Sigh* *He takes his sunglasses off, which he doesn't do very often* *For the first time, the Mythrans notice the crow's feet around his eyes, and note how downcast he is* I'm afraid it isn't the same this time, Spencer.

 

Spencer: ...What do you mean?...

 

T.B.: This time, revived topics aren't getting moved. I'm afraid... *He briefly fingers his glasses* We're not going to make it.

 

*Team Tuna slowly puts down whatever they were doing as this news sinks in* *Even Phil forgets about the level of Hungry Mungrys he was playing*

 

Barry: *Quietly* You mean this topic is going to be deleted.

 

T.B.: It's almost certain.

 

Spencer: *Stand abruptly* But you said that before too! And the revived topics still got moved! Doesn't that mean that we'll be moved too?? *He glances around desperately* Doesn't it?!?!

 

*The other four look back in despair, all of their eyes reflecting the same thing: it was over for real this time*

T.B.: *Looks at them earnestly* Listen. I wouldn't trade our time for the world, guys. We got to do a lot, more than some comedy characters got to. We got to go past the fourth wall more times than I got to count. Other characters, they don't get that. We may not have gotten the most chapters. But the ones we got were priceless.

 

*Opal slips her hand into Barry's and blinks back tears* *Phil puts his head in his hands, and Spencer continues standing, grieved*

 

*Then I walk in, a sad look on my face*

 

*Hey, guys. I want you all to know, you're some of my favorite characters, ever. Even though I archived this topic, it's a lifeless version. It's like having a photo album rather than the real thing. There's a slim chance this topic will make it through... but I don't think it will*

 

*I can't bear the thought of you guys being locked up and thrown out with the rest of this forum. So here-* *I reach into the air and open a seemingly invisible door. A breeze comes out of it and into the room*

 

*Come on, guys. Time for you to abandon ship. I know you'll miss this topic- I will too. *I smile a little* But at least we'll have our "photo album" of it, won't we?*

 

*T.B., Barry, Opal, Spencer, and Phil look around*

 

T.B.: Almost exactly two years from the last merge, too. Goodbye, LEGO Universe. *He reluctantly puts his sunglasses back on*

 

*They all follow me through the door* *Barry is the last one through, and pauses before leaving, and says to no one in particular- or perhaps everyone in particular-*

 

Barry: Thanks for everything.

 

R.I.P. LEGO Universe Forum
