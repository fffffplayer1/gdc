*Barry and Opal, our two favorite Mythran agents, are patrolling Nimbus Station* *Having just received an alert from Tech Support that there's a disturbance by the Forbidden Valley launchpad, they head over*

 

Barry: What do you think it is this time?

 

Opal: I bet it's Onlooker, he keeps getting his cast stuck in the base of the launchpad when he tries to step into his rocket. I wonder when he's going to get that removed?

 

Barry: Are we even sure he was ever injured? I think he just wears it for kicks. Hah, get it, kicks?! 'Cause it's on his foot!

 

Opal: That was terrible.

 

Barry: You're right. I shouldn't be so judgemental. He probably thought it was more convenient than an autograph book, so he just wears to to collect autographs! Hah! How many does he have now?

 

Opal: I think just Phil's.

 

*Halfway to their destination, Mardolf appears in their path*

 

Mardolf: All MPA Agents are being called to base for an emergency meeting. *He waves his hand, cutting off Barry and Opal's protests, and teleports them to the teleportation pads in Tech Support*

 

*They appear in Tech Support next to their fellow agents Spencer and Phil, who are removing headsets from their heads* *The Bossman walks into the room, looking angry*

 

Phil: Hey, Boss, what's this all about?

 

T.B.: You won't believe it! It's insanity!

 

Barry: We've dealt with insanity before.

 

Spencer: Why are you looking at me like that?

 

T.B.: It's worse than any past shenanigans you can think of. In fact, it is the end of all shenanigans.

 

Opal: *She does not like the look of things* Just get to the point.

‎01-22-2017 03:43 PM

T.B.: The entire LEGO Message Boards are being shut down. It's like a merge, except nothing goes anywhere. It all simply disappears.

 

*There is a moment of silence*

 

Phil: Okay, haha, I think that's what we call a "too soon" joke, T.B. We just went through a merge and that was harrowing enough.

 

T.B.: Have I ever made a joke?

 

*Everyone stops and thinks for a moment*

 

Spencer: Uh, I can't remember. Maybe not intentionally?

 

T.B.: Irrelevant. Here's proof. *He pulls out an official looking piece of paper and everyone leans in to read the first paragraph*

 

White Alligator:

 

After much consideration, a very difficult decision has been reached that the Mods need to share with you all. The LEGO.com Message Boards will be retired on March 6, 2017. The entire community, including all forums and all content within them, will be taken offline permanently.

 

 

T.B.: *Rolls up the paper* It goes on to explain that everything will be locked on Feb. 28th, then taken down on March 6th. This time, there really is no way for this topic to survive.

 

Barry: This... this can't be serious. The whole Message Boards? All of it? That's... *He shakes his head, mouth open*

 

T.B.: Insane.

 

Phil: But they can't do that! What are those moderators thinking?!

 

Opal.: Maybe it's not the mods at all. Maybe it never was. Perhaps they are only answering to their own bosses.

 

Phil: Like moderators for the moderators? *The very thought seems to boggle his mind*

 

Barry: *He is despondent* Does it even matter at this point?

 

Spencer: *Angrily* It matters to me! We worked hard to get this topic where it is now! And now it's just going... going to be gone.

 

Opal: We can't lose our heads now. *Turns to T.B.* Will another "photo album" be able to be made?

 

T.B.: Archiving efforts are already underway, not just for this topic. Some are even working to preserve complete forums. And don't worry- the authors of the all these topics are resilient. They'll find new homes. Just like we will.

Barry: So it's back through the invisible door now, isn't it?

*Mardolf appears next to them*

 

T.B.: Agent Scotty! I thought I told you to go through the door already!!

 

Mardolf: *Laughs* Oh, my dear Andras. You know me better than that! I come and go as I please. But before I do, I thought you'd like to know- I saw Sherry go through the door. *He twirls his staff and winks* I'll be seeing you Mythrans around. *With another laugh, he waves a hand and disappears*

 

Phil: Andras?!

 

T.B.: *Groans* I never liked that Plutonian. *Ignoring the alternating snickering and gaping of the agents, he continues* Pack your things, everyone. Time to start a new chapter, proverbially speaking. Or maybe literally. After all, if Sherry made it through- who knows?

 

Phil, Spencer, Opal and Barry: *The four agents stand silently, each thinking thoughts of the past and future* *Soon they go and put their belongings into their backpacks, then finally stand in front of the invisible door* *Barry turns the handle and pauses*

 

Barry: I can't believe it. We've been here so long. We began here; for the longest time, I figured we'd end here too.

 

Opal: Some things don't turn out the way people thought they would. *She and Barry smile at each other*

 

Phil: I'll say. Like how Jonna used to think she was in charge, until we rebelled and things were never the same since! We really came into our own then! Good times, right, Spencer? ...Spencer?

 

*Spencer is standing forlornly, hands in his pockets, farthest from the door* *He looks shell-shocked*

 

Spencer: It's not fair. It's just not fair.

 

Barry: Come on Spence, pull it together man.

 

Spencer: Pull it together? Don't you get it?! We did pull it together! Against all odds! This topic made it so far! Because we believed in it! Because the readers believed in it! Because... because I believed in it.

‎01-22-2017 05:00 PM

*He stands rigid, teeth clenched*

 

Spencer: I just don't think I can leave it. Not after everything we've been through. I think I'd rather stay with it. Until... you know.

 

Barry: Don't say that! You can't!

 

*Everyone stares at Spencer, stricken by what he is saying*

 

Opal: It's not worth your life, Spencer.

 

*He stares around the room, unable to look at his fellow Mythrans*

 

Spencer: It's my home.

 

*Phil stares a bit longer* *He looks around, then at all his dumbfounded colleges* *Finally he looks at Spencer, his face clouds over angrily, and he marches over*

 

Phil: YOU DORK!! *He slaps Spencer*

 

Spencer: *Jumps, stunned* *He looks from Phil's face to Phil's hand to Phil's face* Did you just slap me in the middle of my emotional monologue?!

 

Phil: YOU BET I DID! YOU'RE MAKING A FOOL OF YOURSELF!

 

*For once, Spencer actually seems hurt by what Phil is saying*

 

Phil: Of course it's your home- it's ours too! But homes go away. That doesn't mean you can't have a new home. I'm not saying that where were going is better or worse than here. It's just different. But it just might be a home, too. And if you don't walk through that door, then I'm not going either!

 

Spencer: What?! You can't do that, it's supposed to be my dramatic exit! My finale! My grand emotional demise! And besides, then you'd-

 

Phil: Smash? Yeah. So will you if you stay. I can't let that happen. You're like a brother to me.

 

Spencer: *He awkwardly scratches his head* A really annoying brother, right?

 

Phil: *He allows himself a little smile* Exactly. Now, let's go and find someplace new.

 

*Spencer looks longingly back into the topic* *Then he nods to Phil, and they both go stand by the door with Barry and Opal* *They wait for T.B.*

 

T.B.: Go ahead. I've got to finish our work here.

 

Barry: You're not going to pull a Spencer on us, are you?

 

T.B.: Of course not. That'd be dumb!

 

*The four agents watch him carefully*

 

Barry: ...Alright. I'll hold you to it.
