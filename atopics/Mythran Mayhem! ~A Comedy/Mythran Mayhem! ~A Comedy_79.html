Christmas Skit Special ~ Part Eleven: The End

 

 

Phil: I'm gonna go play my new game! *Rushes into tech support, where he keeps his XStud*

 

Spencer: Wait, that was all of the gifts?! It can't be! Don't tell me it's over already! *Crawls underneath the Christmas tree in a vain search* *T.B. shakes his head at him, then heads into the kitchen*

 

Barry: I can't wait to see Pluto!

 

Mardolf: It is a lovely place! So freezing and blue and freezing and desolate and freezing- much more pleasant than this exhaustingly warm climate. Plus we have great indoor snorkling lessons!

 

Barry: Uh... the snorkling lessons sound good at least.

 

Mardolf: *Hands him a brochure* When you get back, put a glowing review up on our tourist site, will you? Might help get more visitors in, the tourism industry has been floundering for some reason.

 

Barry: I am suddenly overwhelmed with the uncomfortable sensation that I am being used. *Sighs, decides to make the best of it and starts flipping through the brochure*

 

Spencer: *Pokes his head out from under the tree with pine needles in his hair* There's no more! WHY?! *Lets his face splat onto the ground and sobs into the carpet*

 

T.B.: *Walks out of the kitchen, carrying a plate of warm Christmas cookies and a pot of coffee* I made cookies! Help yourselves!

 

Spencer: *Completely forgets about his disappointment and rushes to get the first pick*

 

*Everyone, minus Phil, sits down to eat the cookies and coffee T.B. had made*

 

Mardolf: You know, I've been meaning to talk to you about something, Bossman. I think a comrade of mine may have taken a wrong turn and ended up in one of your dustbins- think I could take a peek around for him and help him out?

 

T.B.: Hmm, let's go look. *The two of them go into his office* *T.B. lifts the lid of the dustbin*

 

*Inside, several minifigures are battling over a one time-one person- ABSOLUTELY CLASSIFIED teleporter*

 

T.B.: *Quickly shuts the lid of the dustbin and exchanges bewildered looks with Mardolf*

---------------------

 

 

The end.
