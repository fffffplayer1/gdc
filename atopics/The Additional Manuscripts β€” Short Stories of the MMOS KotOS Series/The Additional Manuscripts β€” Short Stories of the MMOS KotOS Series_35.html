This is something I started writing months ago, and after reading all you guy's amazing stories, I was inspired to finish it. It's pretty short, but hey, isn't that why they're called short stories? It provides some insight into Barney's motives for helpig teh dude.

 

 

This is an excerpt from Lord Barney’s speech upon his inauguration as chief of police:

 

Oh goodie! I’m sooo glad that King Dude made me the official lawmaker. This is going to be so much fun!!!

 

Law #1: Always obey all laws.

 

Law #2: Don’t obey the laws if we tell you not to.

 

Law #3: Always look both ways before crossing the street, on penalty of smash. In the long run it’ll save lives!

 

Law #4: You have the right of free speech and you can say whatever you want. (Though you may be smashed if it’s not something we want)

 

Law #5: Sharing is caring! Make sure to “share” all valuables with the Dude’s royal treasury on pain of smash.

 

Law #6: If you manage to capture an evil KOTOS knight, you will get a million dollar reward! (Which will then be transferred to the royal treasury on account of Law #5

 

Law #7: You must sing the Barney theme song after every meal.

 

Law #8: You must watch the Barney TV show at least five hours a day.

 

Law #9: You must play the Barney theme song every night while you sleep. We will be installing security cameras in your homes to ensure cooperation.

 

Law #10: Always eat your vegetables!! These vegetables are provided by The Dude and will be served free of charge with every meal because they will make you hypnotized and submissive…oh, I mean they will make you big and strong.
