I wrote this article about one of the kingdoms mentioned in our stories, The Land of Aquila!  This is supposed to be like the encyclopedia style entry that Ninjago_Builders made about Elepharia near the start of this thread.

 

 

Geography

 

 The land of Aquila's topography is simple - it is predominantly one large plain, aptly known as The Great Plain, with one eastern forest, the Anguis Forest, and two rivers.  The Athelas Mounts (also known as the Fire Mountains due to the use of bonfire beacons for communication) to the south and an unnamed range to the east separate the land from Morcia, and the Bewitched Forest forms the border with Ankoria.  To the north is the Icy Sea, beyond which is the land traversed by the Nordeners.

 

Points of Interest

 

These include locations in and out of but relating to Aquila.

  • The Great Plain
  • City of Glasted
  • The Glass Citadel
  • The Icy Sea
  • The Bewitched Forest
  • Athelas Mounts
  • Athelas River
  • Anguis Forest
  • Anguis River
  • East-Aquila Mountains
  • The Aquilone Watchtower

 

History

 

Aquila is one of Militiregnum's Four Great Kingdoms, founded very many years ago.

 

The Rulers

 

For the longest time it was ruled with a typical monarchy system, with kingship always passing to the first son, even in the case of firstborn daughters.  Royal daughters always, however, held important positions in governance.  Members of the royal family do not retain power after the passing or replacement of contemporary kings - they must make way to be replaced by the new generation.

 

Conflict

 

Aquila has a long history of fighting with its southern neighbor Morcia.  Attempts to pass The Icy Sea proved fruitless, leaving only one other direction to go: south.  But Morcian masterminds and the creation of the Watchtowers kept the Aquilones at bay, and they never made it past the Athelas Mountains.

Continued:

 

Five hundred years before the Syntax War, there was a fifty year time period in which Morcia did conquer up to the Athelas River and built two villages and a Watchtower, and it was a thorn in the Aquilones' side that did not go unnoticed.  After several years of small operations, the Aquilones did send a large army to recapture the land and take the villages and Watchtower as their own.  It was a climactic battle, hundreds of men on the ground against the Morcians in the Watchtower, and while the Morcians were defeated, it reminds everyone how strong the Watchtowers are, and since then the Aquilones have been especially adamant to attack Morcia except once every semicentennial.

 

During the reign of Morcia's King Matthias,  Aquila was believed defeated for good when Morcia scored a tactical victory over an Aquilone invasion force.  Aquila had to sign a peace treaty and pay annual tributes to the victor.  It was a sorry time for Aquila and the worst blow to its ego yet, and many in the kingdom were tired of the conflict and lobbied for a change in policy.  Everyone was distrusful of the Royal Family.  This would add the tinder needed to ignite the Aquilone Civil War.

 

During the Syntax War, the leader of Aquila's Warmonger Party, Councilman Accipitris, was visited by thedude and convinced to attack the weakened Morcia.  Accipitris agreed and the commander of the army, Baron Perfidus, who was loyal to Accipitris, led an army of two thousand strong men, horsemen, and chariots to attack Morcia, in violation of the peace treaty.  A Morcian army lead by General Dux, sent by King Matthias to stop the Aquilones, was totally defeated.  Only Nuncius the Messenger was allowed to escape, and he became known as the "Horse-man" who could run faster than horses.

5771 Years after present.

 

To begin to understand the peculiar story of HCS and CLS, you must first understand who, and what they are. They are Zerroxians. What is a Zerroxian? A Zerroxian is an alien very similar to a human, but with a much longer lifespan, and the mystical ability to travel through time and space! All Zerroxians are born with this power, and they learn to use it as they grow older. Speaking of birth, that's a good place to start with our two protagonists!

 

"It's- Wait, they are two boys!" The doctor shouted as he held the two newborns. Now, while the birth of twins may not be too unusual to us, it is extremely strange to Zerroxians, as the birth of twins is considered a sign of trouble and war. Most twins are separated and sent to totally different households, to prevent them from ever finding each other again.

 

 

Well, HCS and CLS's mother was quite different. Because it was forbidden by law, she could not raise them herself, but she did name them, HCS's name was Marhko, which means Adventurous, or silly. CLS was named Clovik, which means serious or a strong leader.

 

She left her children together at an orphanage and they took them in, assuming that they were different mother's children. Growing up there, Marhko and Clovik were known for... Being different. by the age of five, the boys were put into a "Disciplinary school" because they kept running off when they should be in class. And they escaped from there too. For you see, when they ran off, they would go and practice their powers, and they became quite good at using them; So much so, that they could create portals and travel through space much better than anyone their age.

 

Unfortunately, that would get them into trouble quite often.
