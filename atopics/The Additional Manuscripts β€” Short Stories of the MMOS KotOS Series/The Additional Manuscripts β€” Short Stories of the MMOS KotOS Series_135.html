File 2315-A65

Moderator Powers

 

 

Moderator powers are an obscure set of supernormal abilities that Mythrans and some minifigiures wield.  Moderator powers consist of many previously unknown and completely never before seen abilities.  The few known specific abilities that have been documented are presented as follows:

 

  • Word Editing

Subjects with moderator powers can consciously change the words of any book, manuscript, or text by looking at them and substituting them with othe words of their choosing.

 

 

  • Speech Augmentation

Moderator powers also include the ability to silence, or mute, specific words and utterances from being heard in their presenece, or field of influence which may expand beyond their immediate presence.

 

 

  • Object Transformation

The transformative abilities of moderator powers extends beyond words and utterances to even physical objects.  For example, during one of the Crystalien Conflicts, Mythrans were observed transforming swords, guns, and other lethal weaponry into nonlethals such as pretzel sticks and laffy taffy shooters.

 

 

  • Locking

Mythrans have been observed summoning gigantic steel gates with an unlockable golden padlock that is suspected to be just for show - only Mythrans, or other people with moderator powers, can unlock the gates and they can do it without even touching the golden padlock.  The Nexus Force has observed locks being used to quarantine particularly dangerous Maelstrom zones on Crux Prime.

 

 

  • Telepathic Communication

It is unknown yet if this is a Mythran specific power, but scientists have monitored Mythrans' brainwaves and found that they can open telepathic communications with other Mythrans, and possibly even mnifigures.

 

 

  • "Fourth Wall" Breaking

Theorists, the type who suggest there is a fourth dimension with four-dimensional beings looking at us, suggest that someone powerful enough in the arts of Moderator Powers can even break a mysterious object known only as "The Fourth Wall."

 

 

End of File 2315-A65
