He walked with such an air of importance and had equipment that seemed so superior that the Knights knew immediately that it must be Lord Brocktree.

                Ruby was preoccupied with a knight coming from the window, John and Squid were still recovering from the explosion. Hycra shot one of the Rogues down and quickly after that he shot Brocktree. Yet, the man didn’t even try to dodge the arrow, which bounced off his armour. Brocktree, in response, drew one of the various pistols his belt carried among various other weapons and fired at Hycra. The move was so quick that Hycra barely managed to move at all to dodge the hit. Even that slight moving though saved him for instead of taking the hit to the heart, he was hit on the shoulder. He collapsed to the floor in pain.
