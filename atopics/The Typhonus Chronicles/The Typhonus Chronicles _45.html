As we trudged on in the gloomy, overcast, barren land, I noticed Daniel seemed, watchful, casting his eyes about. He appeared to be looking for something. Sometimes I would watch as he slowly fell away from me and Clay, and dig at patches of dirt for a moment. He would also walk off to the side to look under rocky outcroppings, as well as caves.
Finally, Curiousity got the best of me, and I stepped closer to him.
"Are you looking for something?" I asked.
He turned to look at me, and replied, "Mmm. Kind of..."
"Come on, you have been straggling from behind and turning over every rock in this place, you have to be looking for something."
"A regular Sherlock aren't you? Yeah, I'm looking for something."
"What?"
An annoyed look came over his face. "A while back, I was flying over Crux Prime. I had recently gotten a mission to scan the entire land mass with some wierd thing made by Overbuild, so he could make a three-dimensional map. I was being... I dunno, a Dinkus, and I dropped it down here."
"Why do you need it back?"
"Because Overbuild still hasn't gotten his map. I need to finish the mission."
I thought for a mili-second, then said, "How long ago did you get the mission?"
He shrugged. "Five, six months? Somewhere around there."
"How do you know he hasn't just made a new scanner?"
"Because, well... the last one had 'cough' a shard of imagination.''
'Oh brick' I thought.

‎06-04-2012 01:24 PM

This confirmed my fears. With the shard the Maelstrom could easily do anything they wanted. I couldn't let that happen...
"Brick!" I said out loud.
"What?" Daniel looked at me questioningly.
I sighed. "Do you know what happened last time chaos came into contact with pure imagination?" I put my arms out, "This."
"Yeah but, this is only a shard. How much damage could that be?"
"Think of this," I said, "When Overbuild retrieved a shard of the nexus, did it stay small?"
Daniel paled. "No, it... grew."
"Exactly. Which means we aren't looking for some miniscule blue light. It's either in the possession of the Maelstrom as we speak, or there is a brick of a lot of rubble covering it up."
"There was a recent mountain collapse... oh brick I am such a Dinkus!"
"What?"
He turned around to look at me. "The maelstrom has recruited a group of undead skeletons. Know what the majority of them are doing? Mining."
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
We had decided on a plan of action. We would camp out in a clearing we had found, then in the morning we would move out to find the skeleton mining pit. We still weren't sure of where we were, so that actually would be easier said than done. But we all agreed that before we got to Nexus Tower, we had to secure that shard. It might even help them to accept me, if Vanda recognized me...
For the time being we had built a tent out of bricks. Our only defenses were the MRS II, as we had all agreed to no longer use any form of violence. We didn't want to hurt Spark any more than we had already, Clay especially. At the mere mention of the Mythran he shivered. And me and Daniel would chuckle. And then recieve the stink eye from Clay. And we would chuckle some more. It was a cycle.
As we rested in our makeshift shelter, we closed our eyes, and tried to sleep. Out of us three, only Clay fell. We could tell from his snoring, the same reason we couldn't sleep.
'This is going to be a long night.' I thought.
