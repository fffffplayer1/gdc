"Typhonus, wake up!"
I was roughly shaken from my sleep by two hands attached to my shoulders.
"Ugh... I don't even remember falling asleep..."
"Typhonus wake up!" He then proceeded to slap me.
"AAuughh!!" I sat straight up.
"Why the brick are you waking me up at this hour?" I complained.
"The maelstrom is here!"
Soon after we found ourselves fleeing from the site, running like scared puppies from the coming purple menace. We had a bit of a head start, as Clay slept like a baby and was able to wake early and scout the area. He had seen the Maelstrom and woke Daniel, who then woke me.
"How big did you say the group was?" I asked Clay as we ran.
"Well, I can't spread my arms and say "'Bout yay big"! It was really large, they even had a dragon with them!"
"Oh brick. I've been saying that a lot lately!" I said half to myself.
"Why send such a large group to a barren area like this?"
I paused. "They're after me!"
They both looked at me with curiousity.
"Why?" They both asked.
I breathed in deep for a bit before answering.
"When I was part of them, they would use my head for their operations. You saw how disorganized they were, right Clay?" He nodded. "Well, that's because they need me to come up with strategies and plans!"
"Why you?" Clay asked.
"Because, I have a link to Imagination. They would get to Overbuild if they could, he came directly into contact with the nexus as well. But I'm the most vulnerable, so they will keep targeting me!"
