We were winning. Everyone was putting their all into it, and the maelstrom was slowly pulling back.
And then I heard, "Exavier! Look out!!"
I turned just in time to see a cannon ball coming strait toward me, and then everything was black.

I was floating again. Nothing around me, just emptiness. I started to panic. I couldn't be back in the maelstrom, I just had a second chance! I was about to give in to misery when I realized there was a blue light shining on me. I turned, and saw Spark.
"Spark! Its, you! Oh, I thought I was back in the maelstrom, but... Spark, what's wrong?"
He was shivering, and his eyes were half closed.
He looked up, and said, "I'm dying."
"Wha-What!?"
"You heard me, I'm dying."
"Well, yes but, why? Aren't you one of the most powerful of the Mythrans?"
"Of course. But, I am weak. At times, it feels like chaos is to strong, and that I will lose the struggle."
He sighed, then continued. "Chaos is powerful. It grows from its own energy, absorbing the evil and corrupt thoughts of all and then brings them into a cruel reality. The same is Creation, but I thrive off of the wonderful, inspirational hopes and dreams of the good in heart, and turn them into a beatiful existance of life."
I pondered on this for a while, then said, "Wait, you said 'I' thrive. What do you mean by that?"
He looked up at me, and stared into my eyes.
"My existance is directly linked to imagination itself. The Minifigs of Crux may think they are helping to defeat the Maelstrom, but they are building it up. Every time they go into battle, chaos insues, and the Maelstrom grows. Use imagination. Find a way to defeat the maelstrom with Imagination. If you do that, Chaos will go dormant, and," He paused for a moment, and said, " I may live."

A MYTHRAN IS DYING!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!! I must have failed my duty as a Mythran Guardian!
