I feel like I'm opening up some old forgotten box, tucked away in the corner of a cluttered garage. I haven't written anything for a long time, The Typhonus Chronicls(TTC) hasn't been prominent in my mind. But as of late it's begun to nag at me, and I finally remembered it while I had time to kill. The thing is, I don't know where the story is going. Heaven's no I'm not giving up on it, I just feel that it needs a large overhaul. I've grown, and I can see some things that I wish I hadn't done that mess with the story in my head. It's been over two years since I first started playing LU, and barely under two that I began to take real notice in writing. TTC was my first ongoing story that people encouraged me to pursue and finish, I'm not going to let it die.

 

Sleep for a while, maybe, but not die.

 

 

 

And just so I don't leave you on a cliff hanger, this means that I cannot definitively determine a time or a day that it will continue. I just need time and imagination to get it going again.
