The spider just shrugged, and looked up.
"What? What are you... oh...."
There happened to be a very large ship above us. Purple smoke and electricity enveloped it, few portions of the ship clearly visible. Rubble fell from far above, pieces as large as a bus seeming as small as ants. They fell, slamming into the ground with an incredible force, creating miniature craters upon the already pockmarked sruface.
"Whoa!" I yelled as one of the fore-mentioned rubble clumps nearly squashed the spider, and me along with it. This jostled my pack, spilling some of the contests, including Tripod. A tiny rock from above hit his activation switch, and he came to life.
I felt a nudge on my leg, and looked down to see a frightened Tripod. The Spider Boss just watched Tripod curiously, chittering down to us. And Tripod chittered back. The Spider Boss positioned himself directly aboved us, providing an umbrella.
I looked at Tripod.
"You can talk to him?"
He nodded in response.

Elsewhere, a certain orange caped minifig was very frightened. One hand on the edge of the great big hole in the floor, the other preventing G from falling to her doom. They swung with the vibrations of the Pathfinder, precariously close to falling.
"Augh!" He cried in pain, when the ship jerked lower, and bounced them up and down.

"HEY! We need help!" He made another attempt to lift himself and his cargo, but once again, it was to no avail.
"Stupid Donuts," He muttered.
