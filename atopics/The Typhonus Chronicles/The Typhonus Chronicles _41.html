"Y-your... your hurting him." I said feebly.
The mythran that gave me a second chance was still hurting, but he was no longer screaming. He was no longer concious either.
"Please stop.." I said, louder this time.
"Stop!!" I shouted.
Daniel stpped the rocket in its tracks, while Clay continued to shoot.
"NO! Not you!" I said to Daniel. "YOU!!"
The rocket resumed its original flight path, while Clay looked at me in complete and utter astonishement.
"WHAT IS WITH YOU!??" He yelled.

"Well this is just great!"
We had almost made it to Nexus Tower, but we fell to the ground before we could get close enough. We could see Nexus Tower, but we could see it from Nimbus station too, so we weren't even sure how close we were to it. And as of late, Clay had gone into a temper tantrum.
"We were THAT close to getting there, but now the rocket's wrecked, and we have no idea how close we are to the tower!!" Clay continued to rant.
Daniel had remained calm through this ordeal. He had just sat there, watching Clay take out his anger on me. He sat in a cross legged position, coolly watching Clay's outburst.
"What is with you!?" He yelled at me, again.
"Why did you tell me to stop shooting? I could have seen that missile and blown it into miniscule bits in an instant!!"
I cringed, wincing whenever he raised his voice. He was terribly loud, and was beginning to foam at the mouth. I half expected him to turn into a werewolf and disembowel me.
"You were hurting more than you were helping." I said weakly.
"HURTING WHAT?!!"
"Every time you destroy any maelstrom, or anything, the maelstrom just grows stronger. And imagination takes a painful blow. He can't take much more of this."
He turned around, exasperated. "You are completely nuts! You talk about imagination like it's a real person!"
"He is."
"WHAT IS WRONG WITH YOUR HEAD!!! DID THAT ADMIRAL CANNONBALL COMPLETELY SCREW YOUR BRAIN!!!! Imagination, is just a thought! NOTHING MORE!!!"
I snapped. "Imagination, is a mythran, named Spark. I hear his screams in my head every time you yell, every time you beat the maelstrom. Do you want to hear them? DO YOU!?"
And with that, I lunged at him, and put my hands on his head. Blue sparks fizzled, and his eyes rolled back into his head...
