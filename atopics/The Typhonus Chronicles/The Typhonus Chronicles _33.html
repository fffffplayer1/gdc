He was disapearing. Fading away into the darkness.
"Save imagination, Typhonus. Save me..." He said, as he drifted away.
"Wait!" I called. "Wait! Why me!!? Come back!"
I could hear his voice everywhere, calling me. "Exavier..." "Typhonus..."
"Help..." "You have to..."
I closed my eyes, the voices everywhere. "Exavier!! Help!! Help us!!!"
I opened my eyes. I was back in the Nexus walker. It had toppled over onto some unsuspecting stromlings. The voice, I could still hear it. But it wasn't coming from everywhere, just to my left...
"Exavier!! Get your brickin' butt in gear! You need to help!" Clay was shouting.
I looked at him, and asked, "What happened?"
An expression of relief came to his face as he said. "You were hit in the head, and fell over onto these guys." He gestured to the crushed stromlings.
"Your walker is fine, but you need to get back up and help!!"
I shook my head. "No."
He stared at me. "What? I-I think my hearing went bonkers for a second, what did you say?"
"I said no. Violence and destruction is not the way to go with this."
He stared at me some more. "Your saying your not going to fight!??"
"No, I am not going to fight. Not the way you are, anyway."
He turned around and yelled, "Medic!!! This guy took a blow to the head!! I think it scrambled his eggs!!"
'Oh joy.' I thought, as the doctor came running up to me.
