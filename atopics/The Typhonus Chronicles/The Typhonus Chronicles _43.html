For the next hour, we walked in not quite silence. Clay kept asking me the randomest of questions, such as "Why did you infect the nexus?" and "Where did your cloak go?" and "I your favorite color purple?" as well as "Do you know a recipe for a maelstrom smoothie?"
It went on and on.... painful.
The only sensible question was "Why are we walking?"
The reason was we had decided to stick to the original plan and head to Nexus Tower. So now we were trudging along the old broken trails of Crux Prime, hiding from the Maelstrom whenever necessary. This was near torture for Clay, since he had a natural impulse to destroy anything threatening looking. By that night he was shivering just a little, and didn't stop until Daniel gave him a pillow. He gratefully took the fluffy goodness, and screamed into it's feathery heart as loud and obnoxiously as he could.
This were his words of relief, "That pillow smelled like wax."
Such is the wisdom of Captain Clay Rando.
