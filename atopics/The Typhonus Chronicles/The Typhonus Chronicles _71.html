Dr. Overbuild tripped on yet another rock, and yelled in frustration.

"We are making no progress! I swear, I feel like we've been at this for weeks!" He stormed.

G patted his shoulder as he got up and started moving again.

"I know what you mean," She said, "Though in reality it has only been... what, one day? I dunno."

He sighed as her hand left his shoulder. They had been following the tracks made by yours truly almost non-stop, never resting, never eating, and always tripping. They had stopped to sleep for only three hours, yet their progress was still a mere fraction of mine, and they were aware of it.

"G, we are not making any progress."

"Yes we are Albert-"

"No, we are not. We've made barely ten miles at the most, and that's not counting the added distance from going up and down these blasted hills. And I haven't had my morning."

G checked over her shouler to see Mech flickering to life.

"Well, it looks like your yelling went and woke up Mech. I don't know if he's recuperated from that wierd power damper earlier, and I don't want to risk straining his servos again, so no rides from him."

"But mama-" He whined childishly.

"No buts. You get your rest."
