‎04-18-2012 05:49 PM

"So what were you up to while we were gone?" Daniel said, interupting my thoughts.
"I've joined Paradox."
Peering at the rear view mirror (Rockets do have those. At least mine anyway.), I could see Daniel and Clay Bumping their fists. I have no idea how that trend caught on, but they seem to enjoy it.
"Well great! Can we see your armor?"
"I don't have any."
I looked in the rearview mirror again to see them just staring at me.
"Oh, I fully intend to get a kit, but at Nexus Tower. I'm going to see Vanda, and ask her if I can be the first to use the Sorcerer Kit Mark 2."
"Ah."
We flew in silence for a while, staring at the constellations in the distance. There was the blue star, as constant as ever. Some speculate its a planet inhabited by people like us, but not like us at the same time.
As I gazed at space, I saw something unusual in the sky. It was a red star, one I had never seen before.
"Do you see that?" I asked my passengers.
"What?" They both said simultaneously.
I turned to the rearview mirror again, so I could see their faces.
"That red star up there, it wasn't there before..."
I drifted off as I looked past the figs in the back, to see specks of purple coming after us.
"Clay! Maelstrom!"
They both turned around.
"Oh brick."
He pulled out his weapons ready for a fight.
"No don't shoot! You'll make it worse-"
KEEeerrraaaAAABOOOOOOMMMMM!
A massive blast came from the weapon Clay had pulled out of his bag. It erupted in shades of purple with crackling electricity coming from it. I stared, horrified, as I heard the scream of a Mythran in pain, echoing through my head.
