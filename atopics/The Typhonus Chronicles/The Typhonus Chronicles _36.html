I didn't want to be any closer to the maelstrom than I had to be, but I had been a part of it, and my information would be most useful in the Paradox.
"Okay, Paradox..." Nexus Jay mumbled as he wrote on his paper.
"Right. Well then, Exavier, you are now a member of the Paradox."
I waited for something to happen, such as some horns blaring, or a congrats from Nexus Jay. On the contrary, he had gone right back to his papers.
He looked back up at me, and said, "Well, go on. The Paradox vendor is over there."
I waited a while longer. Nexus Jay, seemingly irratated, looked up again and said,"Shoo! Get out of here!"
And I walked away to the vendor, a little disappointed.

"Ah, a new recruit! Come here, and let me have a look at you."
The vendors voice was muffled by his helmet, and his lit up eye scope examined me thoroughly.
"You have quite the spark of imagination, I'll tell you that." He said.
"But I see a smidgeon of dark, a worm of maelstrom that follows you wherever you go."
I was quite taken aback at his remarks, and startled too.
"You can tell all that just by looking at me through that scope?" I asked.
He laughed out loud, and started to wipe a tear from his face, when he realized he was wearing a helmet.
"No, I was just messing with your head. A common talent among the Paradox if you ask me."
