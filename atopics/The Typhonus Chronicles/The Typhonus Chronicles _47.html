As soon as the words left my mouth a large boulder slammed down next to me. I then turned my attention to behind me, and saw something that made me seethe in anger. The others turned around to see what I saw, and their mouths fell open like fish.
'What is that?" Clay yelled.
We were looking at a catapult. A very futuristic catapult. It had a tank-like structure for its base, moving at high speeds. The catapult itself was pulled back by massive gears and high grade steel chains, the opposite end put under mounds of pressure by a length of springs. The release mechanism was a rod that was pulled out from the main stem of the catapult, which let the catapult launch the boulder to a length of at least 3 miles. It was very well designed. I should know, I designed it.
"Brick them to the pits of-" Boom! Another rock crashed down next to me.
"What was that?" Daniel asked?
I shook my head. He got the message.
"We can't 'pant' keep running like 'pant' this!" Clay wheezed out.
We were all getting run down from this. painful stuff, running for half and hour straight. It was at that moment I had a crazy notion. I remembered what happened when I had fought the Spider Boss. And I remembered Tripod was still dormant in my bag.
'Here 'pant' goes nothing.' Heh, I was even panting in my head.

"What! Why do you keep chittering?" He had my attention now. And I saw a small microphone had popped from his back.
"Oh. Well, that works."
I brought my mouth to the microphone, and spoke.
"Any and all minifigs cured, get the the big catapult tank thingy, and get the brick out of here!"
Daniel and Clay looked at me. "Would that include us?"
"Yes. Yes it would."
The next moment would find us making a beeline for the Cata-tank.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Unbeknownst to us, at Nexus Tower, we had been noticed. It's hard not to notice a big beam of bright white light erupting in the middle of nowhere.
Overbuild raced to the Sentinels Command Room. He was carrying with him a book, wrench, pen, one-by-three plate, and a donut.
As Duke Exeter turned to his entrance, he found a panting Overbuild, with an armload of assorted items. Minus one donut.
Overbuild wiped the sweat from his brow, and a smear of chocolate from his mouth.
"What 'pant' the brick 'pant' is so 'pant' important? 'Pant' I was 'pant' in the middle of something 'pant'!" He spurt out.
"Imagination. Pure imagination." Duke replied.
"Well, yes, that's obviously important, what about it?"
Duke turned around to look at his map of Crux Prime. "There was a burst of it. A big one. Outside, of the tower."
There was a large clatter behind him, so he turned to look. Overbuild's arms had dropped to his sides, plopping all of his load onto the floor.
Overbuild frantically yelled into his communicator,
"Gwen! Get down here, I need you now!"
