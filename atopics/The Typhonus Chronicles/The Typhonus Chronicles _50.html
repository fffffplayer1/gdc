I turned to the crowd of Minifigs in the room and called out,
"Alright then, the Nexus Force has come to carry us to the Nexus Tower, and if anyone here has no idea what either one of those are, raise your hand."
About three-fourths of the group raised their hands.
"Wow, a lot of you guys were infected real early huh? Anyway, the Nexus Force is a large Organization bent on protecting Minifig-kind and Imagination, the Nexus Tower is their base of operations. So now, to the ropes! Men, please let the ladies go first."
The crowd slowly moved toward the ropes, ladies first as I had requested. It used to be that I was almost never listened to. Maybe it was something to do with the goatee...
"Typhonus!"
I spun around, my train of thought derailed.
"Yes! Right, the rope."
Soon after, I reached the top of the rope, breathing heavily.
"Ugh." I collapsed on the floor with a grunt. I layed there, panting, until a shadow fell on my face. G and Albert were looming over me.
I pointed to him, "Albert, get an elevator."
I pointed to G next, "And you, c'mere, I want a hug." I was quickly tackled by a large, cold object, which I deduced was a Mech that had been plugged in for too long.
"Mech, you smell like coconuts."
