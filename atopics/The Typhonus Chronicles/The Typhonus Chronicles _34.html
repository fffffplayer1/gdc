"No, that is just a popular saying from where I am from. Haven't you ever of Great Brickin?"
He had a blank expression on his face, while the doctor checked me over.
"Oh, my." He said.
Fear started to creep into my heart.
"What? What's wrong?"
"You have a big bump on your head. The size of a stud!"
Clay slapped his forehead.
"You brick head, we all have those!"
The doctor looked at him, a little confused at his outburst, and then realization dawned on his face. They started to get into an argument on the capabilities of the doctor, talking annoyingly at first, and gradually built up to shouting. As they bickered like two children over a biscuit (sorry, some of you call them "cookies". Ridiculous name.), I snuck away into the bushes, and headed for a rocket pad.
