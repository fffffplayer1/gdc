Chapter 25: A mission in the dark Part 1

 

          “Do you have anything to tell me, too?” Shadow asked Kate.

          There was a momentary look of panic on her face. The girl opened her mouth, didn’t make any sound and then closed it again. Shadow smiled to her and there was something odd in that smile, perhaps some mischievousness. Then, Kate seemed to remember something.

          “How are we going to leave out of this System, since we have to pass through the Zephyr Field?” she wondered.

          “I thought about that! Wait a second! Jomadre! Tell me, is there some place where the Ensee keeps their rockets?”

          “Yes! Why?”

          “Can you take me there later?”

          “Yes!”

         “What are you doing, Shadow?” Kate looked worried.

          “I am going to steal some of their rockets! These guns at the Asteroid Field seemed to recognise us as not Ensee automatically. So, I’m guessing it’s something on the rockets! And anyway, I trust you to lead them and yourself safely out of here!”

          Jomadre seemed to have talked to her husband, too. She approached Shadow.

          “Valihombre want… lead you… instead!” she told him.

          “That’s fine with me!” Shadow could understand that Valihombre wanted to protect her.

          “Shadow, if you are going to steal the Ensee’s rockets, why are you fixing my rocket, too?” Kate asked, when Jomadre rejoined her husband.

          “Just in case it’s needed! And I’m going to do that after sunset, so I’m just passing my time! Go rest, you’ve had a bunch of difficult days!”

          “So have you!”

          “I don’t feel like sleeping right now! I prefer building!” he said though that was a lie; he did not want to sleep, so that he had control of things.

Chapter 25: A mission in the dark Part 3

 

          Shadow thought of the dream he’d seen. He was in his home at his Avant Gardens Property watching some television. However, out of the device the Darkitect appeared and started chasing him around the F-Tower. Shadow asked Kate for help, but the girl announced him she was leaving to fulfill her dream of becoming a cow rider. Then, Kate’s face became his mother’s face. Then, someone knocked on the door. When he opened it, Shadow was surprised to see a man wearing a red cap. The man couldn’t pronounce the words he was saying correctly for his life; Shadow also thought that if he wrote them he’d spell them even worse. The man asked him if he wanted to build a snowman with him, but Shadow couldn’t remember what happened next. He thought it included falling from the top of the F-Tower, though.

          “The sun has set! It’s dark now! Time to go steal some rockets! What did you say you want them for?”

          Shadow explained his plan to Magical.

          “Oh! Are you sure you don’t want me to come with you?”

          “Just make sure the girls are okay, will you? I think we’ll be okay!”

          “Fine!”

          “And do get some sleep, Magical, you look awful!” Shadow noticed Magical did not look in his best of states.

          “Always a pleasure to speak with you, Shadow!” he joked.

          “Valihombre, shall we go?” Shadow had on the meantime prepared his backpack.

          He did not reply, but he seemed to know more or less what he said, because he took lead and got out of the cave. Shadow grabbed his upper arm and they both merged with the darkness. Someone must have explained him and Jomadre about his power, because he was not surprised.

Chapter 25: A mission in the dark Part 4

 

          They both remained silent while they walked. That was both because they did not want to be heard and because they couldn’t communicate anyway. Shadow had his ears stretched, so that he could know whether anyone was near them. He was frightened every time his footsteps made the grass rustle.

          They did not meet anyone while they were on the hill. However, they soon entered the town. They had to be extra careful in there, because there were more chances to meet Ensee people patrolling. They saw some of them at times, but avoided discovery by staying away from the lighted areas their torches created. Sometimes, they also saw a helicopter pass over them. It was more difficult to hide from its headlight. Once, when it got near finding them, they managed to jump behind some dumpsters in time. Yet, after that it got away to search in another island and they were safer.

          Thankfully, their destination was near enough, on a neighbouring island. They passed over a bridge to the other island. It was not long before they reached a big building that did not fit with the rest. Of course, the door was close and locked.

          How are we going to get in? he thought to himself, There could be an alarm, if we broke in.

          Then, they heard voices. Two minifigures were approaching. Shadow and Valihombre hid.

          “At last, some reinforcements!” one of them said; they were looking at the sky.

          Shadow looked, too. Sure enough, five rockets were approaching. The two minifigures ran to the doors of the building –they were more like gates actually– and opened them. Soon, the rockets landed inside it. Five minifigures got out of them and walked out of the room. Shadow thought he recognised the two of them as the ones who’d chased them in the Asteroid Field and the Magma Tops.
