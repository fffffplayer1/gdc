Chapter 36: The City by the Lake Part 1

 

          Before they could enjoy the view of Lakum City for long, they felt their rocket bump on some invisible barrier. The rocket changed direction and it started flying in what would be a wide circle around the world, if they didn’t stop the rocket.

          “There seems to be a force field stopping us. Let me see if I can do something about it” Shadow said.

          He pressed several buttons on the rocket’s panel and the little screen it had showed something that seemed to please Shadow.

          “Yes. Their technology is not as advanced as the Nexus Force’s. I think I can bypass this”

          He pressed some more buttons, while his eyes glowed blue. Then, Shadow had the rocket move and it proceeded unhindered by anything.

          “Success!” Shadow cheered.

          The rocket now approached the world as fast as it could. Nearer to the city, the two men were able to take a better look at it. Though they did not know that, it was quite similar to Venice in some respects.

          Soon they landed in a dark alley. As the two minifigures stretched their muscles after the tiring journey aboard the rocket, another minifigure appeared on the end of the alley and he ran towards them.

          “Quick! Come with me. You have been seen. The soldiers of Lakum will soon come to arrest you. You have to come with me!”

          “Who are you?” Shadow asked.

          “A friend. I saw you landing and knew immediately who you were”

          “And why should we believe you?”

          “Look! If you want to be safe from the Ensee, you’ll come with me. If you want to waste time and get smashed, fine by me. I only wanted to help, though”

          “Okay. We’ll come”

          “So, why was it so important to get into a safe place?” he asked.

          “You did a foolish thing! A very foolish thing to come here! You never should have. You’ve placed yourselves in more danger than you could imagine!”

          “We’ve been through a lot these last few days. Try us” Magical replied.

          “But you don’t understand. You have come to the base of Ensee! All of the things, they did to you, until now, they shall do unto you tenfold here! This is the worst place to be for a Nexus-Forcer! Especially for two Nexus-Forcers that have caused so much trouble to Ensee!”

          “If you know that the Ensee’s base is located here, then would you also know what exactly it is?”

He’s worked in utter secrecy, so that the Nexus Force doesn’t discover him, but I think he now intends to invade the Nimbus System, too. He is evil, I tell you”

Chapter 36: The City by the Lake Part 3

 

          “There is such a place. There’s a big building on the other side of the city, where minifigures fight each other and people make bets on who will beat who.           “So, is that all?”

          “What do you mean?”

          “What?! No! You’ll have yourselves smashed, if you do that. It’s not safe to get out and going to the President is suicide! You need to stay here until we can send you out of here”

          “We’re going to the President and this is final” Shadow said.

          The eyes of the guards seemed to widen at the sound of that. The one who had spoken knocked on the door and called for someone.           “The President of Lakum will be happy to see you” he said.

          The minifigure led them to a great hall inside the mansion. There was a man sitting on a throne-like chair on a slightly elevated part of the floor at the other side of the room and a woman sitting on another chair next to him. There was an oval table surrounded by chairs some distance away from them, but no one occupied those seats at the moment.

          “Sir, these are the Nexus-Forcers” the man said to the person who must have been the President.
