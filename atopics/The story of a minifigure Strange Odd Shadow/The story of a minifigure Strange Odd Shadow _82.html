          Shadow immediately looked over his shoulder at the source of the attacks, which had momentarily stopped. On a chunk from which they were going farther and farther, he noticed two laser blasters aiming at them. He did not know whether they were robotic or controlled by someone who was somewhere else, but he knew that they then started firing him. Shadow maneuvered to avoid the fire, but he also had to dodge the floating rocks, so he was hit by some lasers. Soon more guns installed at other asteroids located them. Now, Shadow did not wonder why so many explorers had been lost in these places. Someone had made sure they wouldn’t survive. All three of them tried to stay as intact as possible, but they would often either get hit or bump on the chunks. They, especially Magical, were also attacking the blasters, but it was difficult to hit them. Magical smashed the small asteroids, so that they could pass more easily, too.

          And as if that wasn’t enough suddenly, two other rockets appeared behind them. And it seemed that the ones on them were not friendly. Trying to fight back, Magical managed to hit the one rocket sometimes making its pilot lose control, while Shadow finally managed to build something. It was only a brick wall, but Shadow released and it suddenly floated in space in front of the other rocket. It crashed on it and it stayed behind.

Chapter 16: The Investigation Begins Part 1

 

          Three rockets landed on the first world one would meet if they passed the Zephyr Asteroid Field coming from Avant Gardens’ direction. Shadow, Kate and Magical got off of them and examined their surroundings.

          They were in a world that seemed kind of empty, deserted. It was a level surface continuing for many kilometres in all directions but for behind since they’d come that way. This land of brown soil was empty of plants, people, houses, sounds… life! Only on the background far ahead of them there were some mountains. The place seemed vast, but it was an effect partially created by the emptiness since it was actually smaller than other worlds like Crux Prime.

          “Doesn’t seem very lively, does it?” Shadow commented.

          “Perhaps there could be something at the other side where the mountains are!” Kate replied.

          “We should check! I don’t think there’s anything anywhere else, if there even is something at the mountains!” Shadow agreed.

          The got in their rockets again and flew to the other side. However, after long investigation by the mountains and over the mountains they found nothing. The mountains were the end of the world, so there was no need to search behind them.

          The minifigures left slightly disappointed, but there was still hope. The area inside the Asteroid Field would be quite large, if the width of the asteroid field was the same throughout the whole of its. That would leave much space for many worlds hiding many secrets.

Chapter 17: At the Magma Tops Part 6
