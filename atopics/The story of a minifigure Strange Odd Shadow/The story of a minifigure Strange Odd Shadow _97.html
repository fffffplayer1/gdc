Chapter 38: Into the Arena Part 1

 

          Light. There was blinding light engulfing him. And extreme heat. He could feel flames burning his skin all over his body. The pain was unimaginable. He would smash.

          Vladimir Dracula the Eleventh woke up. It was the same dream as always. He dreamt of the moment of the explosion in the centre of the Maelstrom Vortex. He was terrified of that dream. Although he knew it was just the past, it was the most terrible moment of his life and reliving it again and again was very stressful.

          Vlad had played it cool in front of his friends, telling them that he had survived, because he was a vampire. But he knew better. He knew vampires and he knew what he had felt at that moment. No one, not even vampires, were supposed to survive something like that. Vlad knew he was supposed to be smashed and he couldn’t understand how he was still alive. And he wasn’t sure how he could live with that.

          Vlad looked at the sleeping girl in front of him. She had had similar traumatising experiences as well. She had been smashed twice without being expected to rebuild, but she was still alive. Only the two of them could understand how that felt. Other people thought that their being smashed and rebuilt felt like the usual smash-rebuild process that occurred in the Nexus Force’s ‘safe’ worlds. Sure, the latter was quite inconvenient and confusing, but when you smashed in an ‘unsafe’ world and for some reason rebuilt, it felt like it was real and was at least ten times worse. Maybe, that was another reason why Vlad felt like he should protect Nirwell that much. They’d been through similar experiences.

Chapter 38 Part 2

 

          Someone entered the room. Vlad looked up and saw Platinum walking in.

          “Hey, I’ve been called for a mission. Many of us actually. I thought you might want to come, too” he said.

          “I think I’ll pass. I’ll just stay here. Nirwell likes it better when I’m here, anyway”

          “Are you sure? You’ve been sitting in that chair a lot lately. Sleeping on it, even” he said looking at Vlad’s messy hair “I don’t think that’s very healthy”

          “I know, but… I think it’s better that way. Nirwell needs to be looked after and I don’t feel like doing something containing action right now”

          “Suit yourself. If you insist on staying here, who am I to disagree? Goodbye, then!”

          “See you later”

                                             ~~~***---***~~~

 

          Sarah looked at her own hands. She was being led inside a corridor that ended up in light. While looking at the light itself blinded her after being in the dark for so long, it provided her enough illumination to examine herself.

          Her left hand now matched her right one. Her right hand always had a Maelstrom aura ever since she’d been infected in the Maelstrom Lair; disinfection had failed to remove the aura from her hand, although it was not dangerous. Now, her captors had infected her other hand, too, as part of their tortures.

            Before she could look at the rest of her body, they were in an open area and the blinding light attacked her from everywhere. She squinted her eyes in an attempt to protect herself. The place was loud. She could hear the sounds of battle all around her and the sound of many people cheering, booing and chatting from farther away, but still around her. What was this place?

Chapter 38 Part 2

 

          Someone handed her a sword and a shield.

          “You’ll have to fight the others to survive” he said “It’s smash or be smashed”

          Then, they pushed her further into the big spacey area. As her eyes started to get used to the light, she realised some minifigure was approaching her from in front of her. She still, however, couldn’t see well, so she didn’t see the minifigure’s attack coming.

          The slash of the sword caused her to fall on the ground. She was not in a condition fit to fight. She looked up and finally managed to see well. She could see the fearful face of another girl. Sarah could not understand why the girl was fighting her. She looked into her mind and realised she was Nexus Force, too.

          What are you doing? Sarah asked her telepathically, while the other was raising her sword for the fatal hit. Why are you fighting me?

          The girl was so confused she did not make the hit.

          I have to, the girl thought, but Sarah wasn’t sure if she was telling her or herself. Otherwise I’ll be smashed.

          People could pretend to be smashed in order to escape!

          They tried it before, but they realised and then we were all punished.

          Did you try auto-smashing?

          How would that help? We’d just rebuild immediately and they’d catch us.

          Don’t you know you can resist rebuilding for some time?

          What?

          When you auto-smash, it is not necessary that you rebuild immediately. You just have to put some effort to it. Then, they’ll abandon your pieces somewhere and you’ll be safe to rebuild. Just wait long enough to be sure they won’t be there anymore.

          I’m not sure.

          Do it! Just before I hit you, auto-smash.

Chapter 38 Part 4

 

          Then, Sarah leaped towards the girl her sword slashing towards here. The girl was smashed, but Sarah was the only one who’d seen it had happened just a little before her sword had reached her.

          Sarah could now see she was in an Arena. Several minifigures were fighting each other in the oval-shaped sand area, while others watched from the spectators’ seats with malevolent expressions on their faces. She knew what she had to do.

          Suddenly, she was broadcasting telepathic information to every fighter explaining the plan she had devised and told the girl. It was their only hope to survive and that’s why she could feel that the minifigures were ready to take the risk.

          Two minifigures appeared in front of her, apparently locked in an intense battle. Finally, one of them, a boy with ebony-coloured skin defeated the other, but Sarah knew he had auto-smashed. Then, the boy looked at her and their eyes locked to each other’s.

          “Are you Ebasha?” Sarah asked.

          “Yes”

          “I met your brother, Namandel. He’s very worried about you” she said and then swung her sword against him and the child auto-smashed.

          She had finally found the boy they were looking for. But their job was far from over.

          At that moment, Sarah heard a portcullis sliding up behind her. She turned around to see what was going on and out of the darkness, a huge Maelstrom beast emerged. . .
