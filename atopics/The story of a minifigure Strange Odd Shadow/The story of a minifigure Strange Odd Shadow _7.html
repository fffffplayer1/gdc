Chapter 15: Meeting Strange Odd Shadow
Quantum, Lemon and S.O.Shadow decided to go to the battle of Nimbus Station.
"Oh, so you're back. Everyone is back" Wenn Wuzzit said when he saw us coming "This is great. I'll send you this date but five years ago" It was the third of November.
Battle of Nimbus Station was great fun but when Murgle Botch appeared it got wierd. First of all, Murgle Botch left from the battlefield. Then, S.O. Shadow ignoring the voices of the others he followed him. However, he couldn't catch him. Then, he saw some kind of mirror which should be magic because his reflection had a start of beard. And then it talked "Ah, Shadow you will never change. You always ignore the others' advice and you do whatever you want"
Even though he was used to wierd things, due to he had grown up in Yorkanton City, he was terrified.
"No, this is not a Maelstrom trick" the reflection said like he could read his mind " And yes, this is not a mirror. I'm just you from the future. Wuzzit sent us to the same date"
S.O. Shadow didn't have time to think because someone yelled " Shadow what are you doing here?" but the minifigure answered to his own question "Oh yeah, Murgle Botch!"
Then, a third S.O. Shadow appeared. He didn't have a beard.
"What is happening here?" the present one said.
" I was also sent to the third of November. Wuzzit is a maniac with this day because all the seven leaders appeared. Now, Shadow I have to advise you to be careful. Someone, is chasing you" the third one said.
"Shadow, you're not supposed to say what happens or what you learn in the future" the second one told " You are from my future you should know that"
"Well, sorry. At least I didn't say who chases him. Now, I have to go. There are two more riders to smash" the third one said and left.
" I have to leave, too" the second one said and also left.
So, S.O. Shadow stayed alone thinking of what happened.
