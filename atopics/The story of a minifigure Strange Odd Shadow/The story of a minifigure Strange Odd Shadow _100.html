      They were all silent for the moment, not seeking to do anything or speak at all after all that had happened. Only Lemon was holding a walkie-talkie, listening to the Nexus Force frequencies. From time to time the static would stop and the silence broken by someone's voice speaking things like “The Farmlands are secure!”, “The Magma Tops have been secured!”, “NC Agents escaped from the Arena” and “The line in the cloning facilities breaking. Need reinforcements to finish them off!”.

      After recovering from the shock of the explosion, Shadow had searched the burning debris of the exploded house, before he was picked up by the dropship. He had found two things. The first was a metal hatch he suspected led to the Nexus Chasers' underground base. He had informed the others of it and moved on. He was in no place to explore it himself. But he had also noticed something glittering a little at some point, between the lit up wooden parts of the house. He had approached and found a silver ring. It was Commander Nagnus's ring. Did that mean he was smashed? Very probable. He had bent down to pick it up. However, as he had touched it, it had been freezing cold and it had caused pain to strike through his hand. Chapter 44: The Shadow Book Part 1

 

      Strange Odd Shadow looked at the face of the man in front of him. He had thought he knew him. The face was very familiar, but the expression on it was foreign. It was that of a maniac. And Shadow couldn't help, but wonder how he could have been so mistaken about a person. But there was only one question really burning inside him. It wasn't a complicated question. It was just one word.

      “Why?” he spoke with a hoarse snarl.

      Ther person on the other side of the railings could have feigned ignorance at his question, but he decided against it. He understood what Shadow meant very well. All was revealed now, so there was no point in hiding anything.

     “The Nexus Force is weak. The Nexus Chasers hold the real power. I never cared for any one of you. I am just a very good actor. I joined your petty little group, because I thought there were some good ideas I could steal. The Nexus Chasers are the greatest power in the world and we shall destroy you! We will conquer the Universe and no one can stop us. NO ONE! And then you will rue the day you sided against us, Strange Odd Shadow. For we shall take everything you love before your very eyes and destroy it; and in the end we shall destroy you, too”

      “You do realise, of course, that you lost. The Nexus Chasers were crushed by the Nexus Force in one single day. Do you think it's us who are really weak?”

      “Our Commander is almighty and shall destroy you all. We shall rise from our ashes and when you least expect it, your whole lives shall collapse! And I will be freed in the end. I will be freed! And then our positions will change and I will be laughing! LAUGHING, I say! You're doomed! DOOMED!”

He was about to leave, but decided to turn around and say something to them. Perhaps, it would help them.

      “Maybe, it was team effort!” he spoke.

With your having read the final two chapters, I may say a few things.

 

 

About Adam Benjamin Tray and Super Fission Brick. When I first introduced the former, I did have in mind for him to be the traitor. However, I made two mistakes. I called him Adam B. Tray and I only introduced him in this book for the sole purpose of the betrayal, while what I should have done is introduce him in the previous book, then have his true character be revealed in this one. When Jonna guessed Adam's true character so easily, I had to do something. I had to be more unpredictable than predictable. So, I had Brick be the traitor instead. Which is better actually, because it's more shocking with a character you've gotten used to. But let me say I did enjoy playing with your expectations at Chapter 42!
