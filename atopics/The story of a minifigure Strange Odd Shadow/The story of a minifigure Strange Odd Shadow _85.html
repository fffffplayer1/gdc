Chapter 21: A lovely boat ride Part 1

 

          “I was just informed that you have also trespassed the Head Chunk and the Magma Tops Chunk, causing major trouble in the latter! Your punishment will be fitting!” the leader of the group told them as they walked on the central street of a town that lay on the particular island.

          “We clearly did not know it was illegal!” Shadow pointed out, but the leader yelled “Do not speak, worm!”

          They were led –Magical was being carried– at a port-like structure on the one side of the island. From the place they could see the rest of the Lonely Islands, even though it was night. The islands were close together, a few bigger ones with a lot of smaller ones among them or around them. The islands were so close together that the space in between was usually narrow enough for them to build bridges connecting them. The whole island group was a town that stretched over all of the islands.

          There were two motorboats waiting for them in the part between that island and the next. Kate and Shadow were forced on the one boat while Magical was placed on the other. Four people embarked on Kate and Shadow’s boat and two more on Magical’s. They had taken their weapons and backpacks, but the one that was holding them got on the boat where Magical was in.

          The engines of the motorboats were turned on and Shadow gulped in the idea of what they would do to them. He didn’t know what it was, but it was certainly not going to be nice. The motorboats started moving forward the one Kate and he were on leading the way. For a while, they just went on, the boats crossing the sea and getting away from the islands leaving a trail of foam behind them.

Chapter 22: Run Shadow Run Part 1

 

          “Magical! Can you go faster?!” Shadow shouted to his friend.

          The minifigures did not have their weapons anymore, but Shadow still did not like the idea of their catching up to them. Besides they did have the sharks.

          Magical looked back and saw them, too. Shadow saw fear reflected in his eyes.

          “I will do my best! But why don’t you build something to make it faster?” the young man suggested.

          “Good idea! Kate, I’m sorry, but I will have to take care of your wound later!” Shadow said to the girl.

          He wasn’t sure if she understood what he said or if she heard him. She had her eyes closed and a grimace of pain on her face. She did not respond in any way. Shadow started getting bricks out of his backpack and making them fly and place themselves on the back part of the boat. He thought of just using rockets, but the motorboat was not stable enough for a safe taking off. The bricks created some turbines while some were placed in the front as counterweight.

          Once the extra turbines started working, they started sailing faster. The Sharks and their pursuers stayed behind. They seemed to be safe.

          “This is the Base! Come in! What happened?” called a voice coming from a little radio the boat had.

          Magical pressed a button and replied “It all went by the plan! We threw them in the water! We’re now returning!”

          “Good job! We are expecting you!”

          The radio stayed silent. Magical and Shadow looked at it. That meant they wouldn’t have much time. When they wouldn’t come, they would realise something was wrong. But perhaps it was enough time for them to hide somewhere in the town. Shadow turned to Kate again.

Chapter 22: Run Shadow Run Part 4

 

          There were four minifigures on the boat. Magical smashed one of them upon landing on the boat. The two others looked at him angrily, while the third was steering the boat. The water welcomed him pleasantly. Kate had sent it. Meanwhile, Magical kicked the other and hit him with the flat of his sword on the head. He joined his friend in the cold water. The last person realised she was alone and fearing Magical or Kate would smash her jumped out of the boat herself.

Maelstrom was the enemy and they were fighting each other. Why had he smashed a minifigure? Why had they forced him to do it? And was he indeed forced? Or could he have avoided it?           The two boats left the place. As Shadow looked behind he saw some Maelstrom Sharks help the ones in the water, but they were already far away. They searched for a way in and eventually they saw the island end and another start a little further. They headed that way.

Chapter 22: Run Shadow Run Part 3

 

          Soon they were inside the strait and stopped there. They jumped from the boats to land. They were on the third island now.

Kate did not have something appropriate to hit them with and did not want to delay by searching in her backpack. She just stayed as far as she could from the coming piranhas and if she had to, she kicked them away, despite the danger of being bitten.
