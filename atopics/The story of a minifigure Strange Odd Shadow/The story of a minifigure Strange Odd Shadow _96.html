Chapter 36: The City by the Lake Part 6

 

          “Me? Ensee? That is preposterous! It seems that someone is trying to confuse you; or ruin my reputation. Either way, I may assure you that such rumours are definitely lies”

          “And how can we be sure about that?” Magical asked, not persuaded.

          “Look. I’m just trying to rule Lakum as best as I can. We’ve all been through hard times ever since we split from the rest of Makreia and I’ve been trying to do the best for my people. The Ensee, though, is evil. I don’t know how I could persuade you I don’t have anything to do with them. I’ll just say that we placed this force field around our world, so that people like them may not enter it. By the way, I could arrest you for your unauthorised arrival to Lakum. But I shall not, respecting the Nexus Force”

          “If you do not have anything to do with the Ensee, then might you explain to us what exactly it is?” Shadow asked.

          “I cannot. To survive with the Ensee ruling everything around us, I had to make some agreements with them. My mouth is shut. They could destroy us all, if I talked”

          “Are you saying that you, the leader of this whole city, are actually afraid of even talking about them?” Magical was perplexed “What’s going wrong here? Everyone is so afraid of them”

          “They have good reason” Shadow said “So, you do not have anything useful to tell us, then. If that is the case, I think we should be off”

          “If so you wish. But I will have to ask you to leave Lakum as soon as possible. I do not want trouble with the Ensee”

          “We shall do that! Goodbye and thank you” Shadow said and so they left. . .

Chapter 37: The Shapeshifter Part 1

 

          “I don’t trust him” Magical said immediately after they got out of the room.

          “Me neither, but… why would he let us go if he was of the Ensee?” Strange Odd Shadow countered.

          “I don’t know. Maybe he likes to play with his food; or he wants to maintain his reputation” Magical suggested.

          “Perhaps. But his fear seemed genuine. And if he was Ensee, he wouldn’t just tell us he can’t prove he’s not. He would have some explanation prepared. I can’t be sure –he could be a really good actor–, but I don’t think it’s him”

          “Maybe. Still I suspect him”

          “Well, we can’t trust anyone, right?” Shadow said as they got out of the doorway and then “What on Crux?!”

          “Brick!” Magical exclaimed, too.

          In front of the mansion, some sort of vehicle with a big laser cannon upon it was hovering, the cannon aiming at them. There were two minifigures aboard: a pilot and another manning the gun. They were the two minifigures that had chased them all over the Zephyr System.

          As Shadow jumped to avoid the first blast fired by the cannon, he noticed the guards were nowhere to be soon. Looking at the streets below, he saw them fleeing in the distance. Shadow got up and looked at the big crater that had been created on the marble floor.

          “Magical! Rocket!” he shouted, as he started running, in time to avoid a second laser.

          They got past the ship, but that was no help, because the cannon could spin 360 degrees. Shadow got his rocket out of his backpack, while a laser struck just behind him. The steps’ explosion caused him to be flung away. He fell back onto the stairs and proceeded to roll down the remaining ones.

Chapter 37 Part 2

 

          “Ouch!” Shadow declared, just before Magical caught up with him, grabbed him and his rocket and jumped inside it, with Shadow between his arm and ribs.

          Magical had the rocket set off just in time to avoid one more blast. He looked over his shoulder and saw the ship was following them by now.

          “This is outrageous!” Shadow complained “Doesn’t the Ensee know anything about copyrights?! This is another one of my designs. It’s the Gun-Ship. It’s got a really powerful gun”

          “That’s all very great, but how are we to escape it?”

          “Try a Big One. That ought to do the trick” Shadow suggested.

          Magical grabbed a Big One and tossed it to the Gun-Ship. There was an explosion, but through that explosion another laser was launched.

          “Jump!” Shadow yelled.

          The rocket exploded just as they jumped out of it. Meanwhile, the Gun-Ship was crashing behind them.

          “At least we brought it down” Magical said after they landed on the ground.

          They started running in a street vertical to the one they’d previously been, after they saw the two minifigures emerge out of the smokes around the crashed Gun-Ship. Panicked people were running and screaming all around them. Mothers were picking their children, people closed and locked doors and windows and everyone got out of their way. However, in between all of them they saw the blur of a person who was running towards the Ensee pursuers.

          Shadow and Magical continued running for ten more minutes changing direction every now and then. And then they realised they had lost their pursuers. They stopped in a dark empty alley near the lake to catch their breath.

Chapter 37 Part 3

 

          “What do we do now? Our last rocket is destroyed” Magical asked.

          “I don’t know. We can’t stay here with those minifigures, but I don’t see any way out”

          “Maybe, the President will give us something since he wants us out of his world”

          “I don’t think he’ll be so happy to see us after this incident”

          “Can you build something?”

          “I don’t know. I don’t think I have all the appropriate bricks to make something that will fly, but perhaps we could find some in the city. I think it’s our only chance”

          “So, where do we go now? Where would we find bricks?”

          “I don’t know. And I’m not sure anyone would sell to us after this. There goes that idea”

          For a few minutes, they remained silent trying to think of something.

          “We’d better get out of here. We shouldn’t stay anywhere for too long” Shadow finally said.

          “You’re right. Let’s go”

          They started walking, when suddenly a minifigure appeared behind them.

          “Thank brick!” the man said panting “I found you!”

          They turned around to look at him. He had short blonde hair and green eyes, he was tall enough and on the thin side. He wore simple clothes.

          “Who are you?” Shadow asked.

          “The man who just saved you. I distracted and then smashed your pursuers!” he said self-complacently.

          “And how would you have done that, supposing we believe you?” Shadow questioned him.

          “Like that!” the man said and his characteristics started changing until he looked exactly like Strange Odd Shadow, but for the clothes, which remained the same.
