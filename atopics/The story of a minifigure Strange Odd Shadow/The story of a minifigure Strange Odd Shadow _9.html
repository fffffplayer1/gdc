"Hello, Platinum"

As Shadow looked at them more carefully he realised that their gear needed instant repair and that they were wounded wherever you looked.
"Really, I haven't noticed it. I was too busy creating my gear" Shadow answered.

"Are you crazy? After all that you want to fight?" Glittery told.
"I'm a Sentinel. I can't change that"
Shadow didn't understand but he didn't ask.

Chapter 21: Nexus Tower

Shadow checked his map that was appearing on the glass of his helmet to see where Quantum was.
"Yeah, he has left from here. He is in Nexus Tower" he said. He, Platinum and Glittery were at Crux Prime.
They went to the launch pad but two guards were there.
"Who are they?" Shadow asked.
"Don't worry, they just check if you have Maelstrom with you" Glittery said.
"Please, take your maelstrom bricks and samples out of your backpacks" one of them said. Shadow didn't do anything because he had forgot his backpack.
Then, the guards checked them with a rod and then let them go.
Flying with the rocket was as unpleasant as it always was. However, it worthed it. Shadow felt stronger than ever. He was imagining a different thing per second. And then he saw it. It was just a light blue shining stripe but Nexus was the most beautiful thing he had ever seen.
A strange thing was that he smelled lemon.
"Do you smell lemon, too?" he asked.
"It probably is the Nexus. When minifigs are close to it they sense things that there aren't there. They usually are our favorite things. For instance right now I feel like drinking brick-shake" a known voice said.
"Hello Quantum" Platinum said as he turned around.
Nexus Tower was like an earthly heaven.

After walking around a little (actually a lot; you can't imagine how many minifigures there are there) in Nexus Tower Quantum said "Shadow, do you want to meet the brilliant minifig that invented the Assembly Faction?"

"Oh, Overbuild is so boring. I'm going to the sentinel area" Platinum said.
"I will look for Mr. Ree" Glittery said.
"Follow me" Quantum told.
They entered the Assembly area and then they saw Overbuild talking to about twenty minifigures. Shadow and Quantum waited until the rest of the minifigures left.
"Hello, Quantum. Who's your friend?" Dr. Overbuild said looking at some designs one of the minifigures had given to him.

"A vendor gave it to me. He said that it was your first weapon you have given it to him to give it to the successor of yours" Shadow answered.
Quantum was looking at them surprised but he wasn't the only to be surprised. Overbuild took the weapon and said " Although it has the symbol of my family it can't be it. I have this weapon right here". He showed the weapon which his mechanical arm was holding. It was exactly the same with the one that Shadow had purchased from the old vendor. Chapter 23: The Dream

Unfortunately, Shadow and the others didn't find a solution to theur problem so Dr. Overbuild left because he had work to do. Shadow decided tto rent a room and go to sleep because he was exhausted.
While sleeping Shadow saw a very strange but also terrifying dream. In the start he saw some minifigures at Avant Gardens. They seemed very happy but then everything disappeared and the onlt thing that existed was mist in a black background. Then, two spheres of light appeared. The one was purple and the other light blue. The two spheres clashed illuminating the whole place. However, the purple light conquered the light blue one. When the light left Shadow saw Avant Gardens again but there were some minifigures that had only eyes on their face and seemed to consist of Maelstrom who were walking and bringing chaos everywhere. Then, he saw himself on a throne. He was like a king. However, his eyes were looking evilly around. When the fake Shadow saw the real one he talked but with a cold and treacherous voice "Help me, Shadow, to make this real. You will be a king. Come with me"
"Never! I don't want to bring chaos or to be a king!" Shadow answered.
"What about seeing your friends again?" the fake him asked.
Shadow didn't answered. He just stared at the fake him hesitating.
"I wait your answer, Shadow" he told and disappeared in a sparkle of purple.
Then, Shadow woke up sweating. . .

Chapter 24: Avant Gardens Attack!!!

Shadow got up immediately and wore his gear. He started running until he found Quantum.
"Avant... Gard...ens in... dan...ger!" was the only thing he managed to say. Then, he made for the launchpad. He in the end reached Avant Gardens and made for the Sentinel Camp.
He then saw something terrible. Wounded, half-infected and whole-infected minifigures were everywhere.
"What happened here?!" Shadow said.
"An explosion at the Survival! Now, all the Maelstrom that was there is here!" someone answered.
He went to the battlefield and saw its chaos. Maelstrom of most of the known kinds were there and the minifigures had a hard time to smash them. He threw some arrows to the spiderlings and then decided to test one of his special moves. He aimed his crossbow to the sky and threw ten arrows.
"Rain of arrows" he whispered. After a while the arrows fell in several points and smashed some maelstrom apes and pirates.
Suddenly, ten Spider Queens, twenty Maelstom Apes and thirty Maelstrom Horsemen attacked him. It was a trap.
Shadow tried to defend himself but they were just too many for him. They hit him and he fell. He didn't have more energy to block the attacks of his enemies. He would die if a minifigure with α robe a hood didn't come and throw something that smashed the half of them and made the rest of them to reatreat. . .
