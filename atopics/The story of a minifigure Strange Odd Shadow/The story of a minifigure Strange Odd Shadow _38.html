Chapter 49: The Game Begins Part 1

 

      It was indeed very sad to leave Ron and keep going without him. However, they did, but much time passed until they were happy again. The continued fighting and now they had one more reason to fight. They would avenge for their loss. They were less than before, but now they wanted to defeat their enemies even more. Time passed Spring came and no important events happened until the ends of April.

      Shadow was at the University and a lesson of Brickology had just ended. However, as he descended the stairs to go to his home, he suddenly turned around and started running up the stairs. He had noticed some men wearing black clothes and sunglasses, just standing in the hall. They reminded him frighteningly of the minifigure that was stalking him. Yet, they noticed him, too.

      “Hey you! John Steele! Stop! STOP HIM!” one of them shouted as they started chasing the boy.

      “You'll never catch me!!! Whoever you are! Whoever you work for!” Shadow replied as he ran in a hallway.

      As he did that, he remembered a dialogue he had a long time ago with Kate:

 

      “I explored the school yesterday and found where every room was! Even the secret ones!” Katie had said.

      “What?! The school has secret rooms?! Hard to believe!” he had exclaimed.

      “Yes!” she had said excitedly “Every building like this, how they call them, I think something like Skascrepers…”

      “Skyscrapers you mean!” Shadow had corrected.

      “Yes, those! They all have at least one secret room or secret passage! Isn’t it exciting?!”

Chapter 49: The Game Begins Part 2

 

     He then remembered another day when Katie had explained to him how to find such secret rooms and passages.

      “Let's hope it will work!” Shadow told himself.

      As he reached in front of a painting, he stopped and had a look at it. It depicted a man gazing at a big light blue column. He did not find it interesting, but he quickly pressed his thumb at the head of the man. No sooner did he do that than the painting ascended revealing a dark opening in the wall. Shadow quickly got in and the painting descended again, hiding the opening.

     Shadow did not know if that was a passage or a room, but he did not dare explore it for there was no light in there. He waited with his ear on the back of the painting waiting for something to happen. Then, he heard some people running, then passing in front of him and at some point one of them saying: “Do you see him?!”

      “No! Let's go to that classroom!”

      As soon as Shadow heard the door being closed, he pushed the painting and it ascended once more. He got out and ran as fast as he could for the stairs. However, a little before he would not be able to be seen, the men got out of the room.

      “THERE HE IS! AT THE STAIRS!!! STOP!!!” one of them shouted.

Shadow descended the stairs running and then made for the door as fast as he could. When the men saw him reach the door, they shouted: “IN THE NAME OF THE MAYOR, STOP!!!”. However, as soon as they reached the door, too Shadow had disappeared.

      At the same time, Shadow was running in one of the many roads of Yorkanton City saying: “Sooo, our little Mayor made his first move! I suppose now it's my turn!”. . .

  • The Ages of Great Prosperity, it starts when the Chaos War ends. Although peace is not always permanent, that period is like the Mid-Figorian Period, but sometimes even better. It's the last known period and it is not known when it finishes.
