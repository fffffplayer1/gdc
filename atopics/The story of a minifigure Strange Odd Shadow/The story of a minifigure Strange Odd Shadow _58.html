They flew over one of the large buildings, where aircraft like those were kept. They started making circles around it not landing. Shadow wondered why they did that when he noticed that more Maelstrom Airplanes were coming. Soon seven more planes took off and together with the other three they made for the enemy’s Air Fleet. Shadow had a bad feeling about this next wave of planes.
