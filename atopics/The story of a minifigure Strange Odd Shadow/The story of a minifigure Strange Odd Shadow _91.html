Chapter 30: A usual seeing off Part 1

 

          Strange Odd Shadow was already closing the door behind him when he heard the Commander use the name he had picked for himself some years ago. So, he had already closed the door when his mind started working again. He didn’t want to go in there again. Especially now. So, he just left.

          It was very odd. How did Nagnus know his name? Probably, his people had heard it somewhere in the several worlds they’d gone to. But why had he waited till Shadow was leaving to reveal it? That was what worried Shadow more. Could he be related with the Ensee? Probably not, otherwise they would already be chased or smashed. But even with this argument he couldn’t persuade himself there wasn’t something wrong with Nagnus.

          The sound of a little bell coming from his F-phone notified him that Magical was calling. It was not the first time. He’d heard the bell a few times while he talked with the Commander. It was one of the reasons he’d left so hurriedly. He answered the call.

          “Why aren’t you answering me?” Magical bellowed, but at the same time keeping his voice low.

          “If you’ve forgotten, I was with the Commander! Anyway, have you reached the poles?” Shadow replied.

          “Yes! They are very weird. I can’t understand what their technology does, but they have these glass tubes filled with a substance that looks like thick black Maelstrom aura! Perhaps, you should have come here and I to the Commander”

Chapter 30: A usual seeing off Part 3

 

          Shadow jumped back and closed the door. Many lasers had passed through until then, but none hit them. Shadow exchanged terrified looks with Magical and then they started running. Behind them, the door opened again and the mercenaries and Ensee agents got out after the two Nexus-Forcers.

          “Let’s ride our rockets!” Shadow suggested.

          “What? In here?! Are you insane?!” Magical screamed.

          “No, desperate!” Shadow replied and he already had his rocket in his hand.

          In no time, they entered their rockets and they shot forward. Indeed the rockets were too fast for such a confined space and they were hitting on the walls all the time. Chapter 31: The Journey continues Part 1

 

          “Nobody seems to follow us!” Magical said sighing in relief a few minutes after they took off.

          “Write this down in the list of narrow escapes we’ve had the last week!” Shadow said sarcastically.

          “You decided to count this week only, so that the list is half the length?” Magical comments.

          “Come to think of it, Magical, you’re right! We’ve had so many narrow escapes in our lives. What with the Darkitect coming after, what with the Great Battle, what with the Ensee and so many other things, we’ve had enough of them for a lifetime. I wonder why everything seems to be happening to us.”

          “Perhaps, because you meddle with everything and we are stupid enough to follow you!” Magical laughed.

          “It is an idea!”

          “Remember that time we fought Chrysoclepht! It seems like that was centuries ago, doesn’t it? But it was only a year ago more or less!”

          “True! So, think of all these things we did in Yorkanton, they all seem to be from a different lifetime”

          “Remember that time when we didn’t meddle ourselves with such stuff as Maelstrom. It wasn’t as fun, but you know that time had tranquility without, you know, the constant smashing risk!”

          Shadow and Magical laughed. It was then they spotted another small world. They headed for it. Once they landed, no one was there to welcome them. Shadow was actually glad of it. That meant there was no one to try to smash them either. The two minifigures changed out of their disguises. They were of no use anymore. Besides, no one seemed to be there.

Chapter 31: The Journey continues Part 2

 

          It was a barren small world, consisting of brown soil and rock. There was a cave ahead of them. They approached the cave. Shadow remembering a part of Nirwell’s story had a bad feeling about all of this. They entered the cave cautiously so as not to make a sound. Maybe it was because they were afraid someone could be there. Maybe it was the eerie aura of the place.

          Inside the cave they found a most terrible sight. Skeletons and pieces of smashed minifigures piled upon each other filling the cave. Shadow couldn’t look, yet his eyes were transfixed. How could they have committed such a crime? The cave continued on in the background, but Shadow did not want to explore it. He knew what he would find.

          “Let’s get out of this unholy place, Magical! I don’t want to stand in here for a minute longer!”

          Magical was pretty shocked, too. As they turned around to leave, both of them wondered how minifigures could do this to other minifigures. A tear rolled on Shadow’s cheek, so as to mourn the loss of all these people, none of whom he may knew. Yet, Shadow also realised that the more he journeyed in these deadly parts, the strongest his resolve to defeat the Ensee became.

          Magical and Shadow took off again. They continued their journey in search for another world. Now, anything would be welcome, even the whole Ensee army, so as to help them forget the cave world. The smiles with which they’d landed had vanished.

Chapter 32: Two brick girls

 

          “Now, now, Sarah. You don’t have to get angry! The two of us could work together you know. You could be useful to us”

          “I would never work for you!”

          “Are you familiar with two minifigures called Strange Odd Shadow and Magical Fire Monkey?”

          Sarah was surprised by this question. Her face clearly showed that she did. Yet, the man certainly couldn’t see that. Even if there was a camera she was in complete darkness.

          “Before you start refusing, let me tell you that I can see you despite the darkness! See, I can sometimes tell what other people are thinking, too. No super powers though”
