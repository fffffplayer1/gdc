Chapter 64: Ganium and Gunium

Unable to think a solution to Sarah's problem and saying that he couldn't just sit and don't do anything but think, Shadow continued making tests on Katie.
"You are officially a Superian!" said Shadow after a while, cheerfully without having forgotten of Sarah.
As she was putting the electrodes off of her, Katie asked "What's that 'Superion' thing?!"
"Superian. It's a term that Ganaz invented-" Shadow started but was interrupted by Sarah "Ganaz again! You are totally fond of him!"
"Why not?! He's one of the best scientists of the universe's history!" Shadow replied while he was putting the elctrodes on himself "Anyway, Ganaz discovered a new chromosome in beings' brick-cells. That chromosome, the Supernatural Chromosome, mostly consisted of a chemical compound made of two chemical elements. Those two elements, also discovered by Ganaz, were Ganium and Gunium. Ganium is the subastance from which Imagination is made and what makes people think, have abilities and use Imagination. Gunium is the substance from which Maelstrom consists and what makes people become chaoic and corrupted even in the lowest levels. Each compound of a normal minifigure has 1 Gunium Atom and 4 Ganium Atoms. However, some people have another number of Ganium Atoms. Minifiugures with disabilities, genetic problems or some other illnesses usually have only 3 Ganium Atoms. Except for them, there are some minifigures that for some reason have subjected some kind of mutation. Those minifigures have 5 Ganium Atoms and are called Superians"
As he finished explaining, he had put all the electrodes on himself and started making the tests on himself.
"What do these electrodes do?" Sarah asked.
"They send some ideas to my body and then see how I respond to them. The results are shown in that screen" Shadow answered showing a screen which looked like a cardiogram.
"That's brilliant! Who built it?" Katie exclaimed.
"Actually, me" Shadow responded "Ganaz... is not the scientist you would call... recognised... and he also came from Yorkanton City, which we all know has been isolated from the rest Universe for centuries. So, how could anyone here have a machine based on his theories?"
"Can't anyone remove his Gunium Atoms, so he can be pure?" Sarah wondered.
"No, because if you do, you'll die"
Then, while thinking of all these, the cardiogram-like thing started making some things, the lines on it blasted off, then the screen exploded and the lights went off. Almost immediately, the lights went on again and everyone looked at the others with a surprised, confused and frightened look.
"Well... let's... take a sample of my brick-cells to study my Supernatural Chromosomes with another way" Shadow told them as he put the electrodes off of him.
Afterwards, Shadow dragged a little tool on his arm. Then, he put the tool under a microscope and after making some settings, he looked through it. Some minutes later he shouted "IMPOSSIBLE!!!"
"WHAT?!" the two girls shouted back.
"My c...c...compounds h...h...have not 3, n...n...not 4 but neither 5 Ganium Atoms!"
"Then, how many?!"
