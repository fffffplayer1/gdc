          Kate looked behind her. She was riding a rocket together with Jomadre. She couldn’t see the two other rockets anywhere. It was only natural. They had left before the Shadow and Magical, so even if they managed to escape she could have missed them. But had they escaped?

          Kate thought she could use her power to see where they were, but she was afraid she was too upset to control the zero gravity side effect. Then, she remembered her F-phone. It contained an application that showed other F-phones’ locations. She got hers out and started pressing the appropriate buttons. A map was shown on its screen. Even though when she moved it to the particular area, it showed a big black blank –since the area wasn’t mapped– she found two little dot-sized pictures that symbolized her two friends, as well as a picture that was for her.

Chapter 28: Space… wouldn’t it be empty without some dangers in it? Part 1

 

          Strange Odd Shadow watched Kate’s little picture on the transparent screen he had on the front of his helm until he saw it get out of the Asteroid Field at which moment it disappeared, oblivious to the fact she was doing just the same for him. He was not very worried when he saw the dot vanish, because he knew its signal was interrupted by the Field somehow.

          Shadow sighed. Action and worry about his wife and the young couple had caused him to forget his other feelings a bit, but now they painfully returned to fill his mind. A memory came to his mind of his mother announcing him that she was going to leave to join the Nexus Force. He realised that perhaps this was why he didn’t want Kate to leave him. His mother had once abandoned him –it was for the greater good, but still she hadn’t chosen her son. And again later, she had gone into the Great Battle doing most dangerous feats, again neglecting her son who might have needed her. Now, she was gone forever and Shadow knew how it felt for someone so loved to leave you and never return. The wound it had left him couldn’t but sting when he saw Kate leave, especially on such a dangerous moment.

          “Magical!” he spoke through the comm. “Did you want to go with Kate and the others, too?”

          “And leave you alone to be smashed by the first person you meet? No way!” Magical replied.

          “Don’t leave me, my friend!”

          “I won’t!”

          They remained silent afterwards. Shadow felt grateful for having Magical. Magical seemed to be absorbed in his own thoughts. Neither of them felt particularly joyful at the moment.
