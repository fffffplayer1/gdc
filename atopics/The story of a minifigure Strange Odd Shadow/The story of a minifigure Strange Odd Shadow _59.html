Chapter 21: In the Bridge Part 1

 

            Glittery made the Traverser turn right to avoid one more bomb. Yet, a missile hit the spaceship at the right wing. She pressed some buttons and many lasers were shot at the spaceships around theirs.

            “Holy Crux! We barely can move and half our repair droids are smashed!!!” Glittery said to no one in particular.

            A laser hit the starship making it shake and then it remained still, unable to move at all.

            “Holy Crux!” the girl exclaimed again “There’s no way we survive this!!!”

            “Glittery, are you there?” Brick’s voice came to her through a communicator “I am out of missiles! I only have lasers now! I don’t know wheth-”

            Yet, Brick did not finish his sentence and communication was cut off.

            “Brick! Brick! Do you hear me? Are you there?! Oh my Nexus, Brick!” Glittery spoke.

            The Traverser was shaken again and Glittery heard explosions very close to her. The girl looked around in fear. She fired again, but she knew it was just a matter of time to be fatally damaged.

            “Gregory to the bridge! Is everything okay over there?” said another recruit of the team.

            “I’m okay! What about you?”

            “Jean and I are fine, just a little bit injured! Do you know whether Brick is fine? Lasers were shot towards his direction!”

            “I think he… he may… may have…”

            “Not Brick, too! Let’s hope of the best!”

Chapter 21: In the Bridge Part 2

 

 

 

            Shadow watched the armies colliding in front of the wall. He hoped that Magical would return form down there; that everyone would. Yet, he knew not all of them could.

 

            He was watching things through the Imagination Energy Shield. He took his crossbow out of his backpack again and started shooting arrows. Then, a most incredible thing happened that all the later generations would just think was an exaggeration. Arrows from both sides were launched. And they were so many that it is said they covered the sky and sunlight could not reach the ground. Luckily for them, the Shield stopped any Maelstrom, but not anything else. Thus, any arrows that did not collide with others just stopped there and so did the Maelstrom boulders.

 

            While he kept attacking, Shadow looked around him for what would be the hundredth time. Yet, still there was no sight of Kate. While he turned his head to look at the battle below, he heard a great bang coming from the side of the river. It was the explosion of Beck, Epsilon and Arcanum. Yet, Shadow did not know what it was and hoped that it had been caused by them and not the Maelstrom. And then his train of thought reached his wife again.

            Afterwards, something much unexpected happened. There was a knock on the door and then it was opened. Glittery turned around and saw a much-scorched and badly injured Brick come in.

            “You’re still alive!” Glittery exclaimed.

            “Do you think I would get smashed that easily?” said Brick smiling, but the smile faded quickly “Is there anything to do, here or somewhere else?”

            “Go at that panel! More hands are always needed to control more cannons in such ships with little crews! But I don’t think we’ll hold on for long anyway”

            Brick did as he was told, ignoring his injuries. Not long had passed when the ship was shaken again.

            “Three front cannons not operational!” the computer announced.

            Glittery and Brick watched a Maelstrom spaceship appear from above them. They knew what would follow and stayed still waiting for their fate. They even saw a laser cannon turn around to aim at them.

Christmass Special Part 1

 

            Strange Odd Shadow shaped one of the kourabiethes into a duck. Three weeks had passed since the team had met the Darkitect and Shadow had just got out of hospital. Now they were celebrating a late Christmass due to not being able to celebrate them three weeks ago.

            Shadow had made dough for kourabiethes, a Greek version of Qurabiya cookies. When baked and sprinkled with sugar they looked like sugared buns. Now Katie and he were shaping the dough in several shapes: bells, minifigures, christmass trees, hippos, moons, stars and many others. And of course ducks!

            “Why have you made so many ducks?” Kate asked with a smirk on her face.

            “Well, back in Yorkanton City I had a tradition of making many kourabiethes of the same shape. This year I decided to make ducks!” Shadow responded smiling, too.

            “Come on! When will we open the presents?” Magical complained.

            “Patience is a virtue my friend! Besides we’re almost done!” Shadow said.

            Soon the kourabiethes were put in the oven together with the melomakarona, another traditional Greek dessert, which could be described with few words honey cookies with walnuts.

            “Where did you find about Earth cuisine anyway?” Sarah enquired as Shadow approached the other three who were now sitting around the tree.

            “I found a really old book about it in my old house at Yorkanton City. It was so old that it seemed to be from ancient times!”

            “Do you think it could be of the First Builders?” Kate asked with great interest being written all over her face.

Part 3

 

            “I really wouldn’t like to have you give me a present! I’m good with those of Shadow’s and Kate’s!” Magical said.

            “That’s all you’re going to get from me, stupid-head! Or should I say black-head!” Sarah told him.

            Yet, her smile faded when she saw her present from Magical. It was a huge sock surrounded by Stinky Fish. Sarah dropped it immediately completely disgusted.

            “You know, I got that sock from a Maelstrom Troll!” said Magical, whose turn was now to grin.

            “I’ll have my vengeance for that!”

            The other presents of Sarah were a book about Maelstrom and a Maelstrom-infused Ninja Hood. Shadow got books and Assembly stuff. In the end, Kate got a compass from Sarah and a set of arrows by Magical.

            “They are the best kind of arrows you can have! Well, maybe with the exception of Shadow’s arrows!”

That box was small enough to hold in your one hand and cover it completely. She opened that one and wrapped in tulle she found a ring!
