          Shadow woke up the next day on the bed of the room. He didn’t know he was such a deep sleeper, or that Kate could carry him from the chair to the bed. Speaking of the girl, she was still sleeping at his side and Shadow did not wake her up.

He stood up and made some hot chocolate to which he added a little bit of Imagination dust –it really is quite tasty! After drinking that and eating something from his backpack, he got out with his mind on what his plans were for this day. After the Great Battle, Shadow had a lot of free time, not really having any mission, to fill in with his own plans.

          However, before he could go searching for Ebasha, he had to meet with the others to do it together. Thus, for now he just wandered around the halls of the Nexus Tower looking for some inspiration of a kind. He was really quite fine having surpassed the phase when he barely resisted the urge to crawl in a dark corner of his home and pity himself.

          Neither Nirwell nor the psychiatrist guy saw him as he came from behind. Pushing the girl gently but fast enough, the two of them were lost in the crowd, the psychiatrist not even realising what had happened.
