Chapter 56: Not alone

Shadow had just narrated to Katie their story after the trap in Gnarled Forest.
"He is really evil!" said Katie with her weak voice.
"Yeah!" answered Shadow, who was staring at her all the time with a silly happy grimace and smelling the aroma of lemons which was coming from her. Now he knew why he had smelt lemon when he was near the Imagination Shard.
"And you don't remember anythng from the moment you passed out and after?" asked Sarah with an unusual enthusiasm in her voice. A kind of enthusiasm you feel when you discover a new mystery.
"Actually, I remember a moment when I was almost awake and I felt a really strong pain" started saying Katie "Like a lightning was hitting me and even worse. Then, I remember feeling someone lifting me and taking me away. It must have been when Darkitect captured us".
"Yes, it probably was. And the one that lifted you was me" told her Shadow.
"Really?!" exclaimed Katie "I thought it was Magical!".
While she was saying that, her cheeks and ears became red and her eyes looked towards Shadow worriedly. However, Shadow didn't see that because at the same time he looked at Magical, who all that time was smashing Maelstrom Knights through the shouters Shadow had built, with angry eyes. That happened because he unconsciously felt that Magical was stealing the girl he loved.
However, Sarah noticed everything and to break the awkward silence that ensued, she said "So, Katie do you want anything to feel more comfortable?".
"Wait!" exclaimed Katie worried that they wouldn't let her do what she really wanted "I didn't finish my story!".
"What else?" asked Magical, who had stopped fighting after he realised what had happened.
"When I was sleeping I saw a dream with Darkitect" started she "I was in a cave and Shadow was holding me on his shoulder (now that makes more sense than before). He then let me down and a Maelstrom branch caught him from the neck. Then, Darkitect appeared and said 'Hello, Katie! You know that you can help me with your... with your power*. And you will do this if you want him to live. I need to find something...', then fog filled the cave and the dream ended".
"What kind of power?" said Shadow trying to stay calm and feeling a mixture of enthusiasm that there was someone else that had a power, fear, because he realised that there might have existed a lot more than the two of them out there and curiosity about her power.
"Hide something, so I have no idea where it is and I'll show you" was her response.
Shadow did as Katie said and then told her to open her eyes, which she had closed, and that he had hidden the coin she had given to him some years ago. Afterwards, she just concentrated to the coin and Shadow could see her murmuring his words. Then, suddenly,the iris of her eyes glowed green.
For a second it was like there was no gravity around her because it seemed to Shadow that all the things around Katie, including herself, flought.
However, whatever flought, fell immediately and Katie's eyes stopped glowing.
"It is in the left hole behind the sofa you put on the northern wall to hide the two holes. Do you want me to tell you its geographical coordinates? By the way, I am surprised you kept it after so many years" said Katie.
Shadow stayed with his mouth open. She had found its exact location. . .

*It was Katie who hesitated to talk about her power, not Darkitect.

Chapter 57: The wrong use

"Incedible!!!" Shadow exclaimed.
"Do you realise that we can find anything?! A-NY-THING!" Magical said enthusiastically.
"Like Darkitect" Sarah suggested.
"Ohh! First time you said something clever!" Magical answered sarcastically but approving her idea.
"Wait a minute! Isn't the Darkitect who tries to catch us? Why should we try to find him?" Shadow asked.
"Well, it's good to know where your enemies are" Sarah responded with a strangely mischievous and kind of evil smile.
"Okay, but only if Katie agrees. Katie, what's your opinion?" Shadow told them.
"It sounds logical" she replied.
Then, she started concentrating and the same happened. Her eyes glowed and things started floating above the ground. This time, Shadow was sure because it lasted more. Among the flying objects was Katie whose hair rose, too.
However, everything changed when Katie vellicated and yelled "NOOOO!!!". Afterwards the colour of the shine changed from green to purple. She continued vellicating and started yelling.
Shadow was the first to run close to her. However, he stopped quickly because the floating objects around her started moving in circles like they were parts of a twister.
"WHAT ARE WE GOING TO DO?" Magical shouted, so he could be heard over the gale-force winds that were created by the procession of the floating objects.
"I KNOW!!!" Shadow answered.
He, then, took his crossbow and threw an arrow with a rope to one of the the objects. Then, he pulled it back with the object which was a sofa. He kept doing this with all the objects.
"NOOOT THEEEE TVVVV!!!!!!!!!!" Magical shouted when Shadow threw an arrow to the television.
"I'LL FIX IT LATER!!!" Shadow replied.
When all the objects were taken away, Sarah approached Katie, jumped to reach her and slapped her on the cheek.
“WHAT ARE YOU DOING?” Shadow yelled not because of the winds since they had died down but because now he was angry.
“I thought it would bring her back!” Sarah answered.
“Well, it didn’t, so step away!” Shadow told her sharply.
Shadow came close, too. Sarah got angry but obeyed and went next to Magical.
“You must stop now” Shadow whispered calmly in Katie’s ear.
She, for the first time, stopped screaming and whispered to Shadow “But he will-” but was cut by Shadow who responded, still whispering, “I won’t let him hurt you. You must now stop” and then he caught her hand making her descend. Eventually, her eyes stopped glowing and Katie woke up.
“It was horrible!” she exclaimed immediately “He got in my mind and instead of finding him he found me! It was like he was infecting my mind!”
“Is that even possible?!” Magical wondered.
“I believe it is” Shadow answered “A minifigure consists of three parts: the body, the soul and the mind. Our body can be infected and our soul can be corrupted, so probably our mind can be infected or corrupted, too”
“Aren’t those two the same thing: infection and corruption” Katie asked with a weak voice.
“Corruption is the process when someone or something turns into evil. Infection, as we use the word, is the process when you become a maelstrom being” Shadow explained.
“Well, smart guy, explain something else” Sarah told him “Isn’t Imagination the only thing that can be corrupted and all the others can only be infected?”
“Our soul is Imagination”
“What?!” Sarah exclaimed.
“That’s right! Isn’t Imagination from which ideas come?” Shadow answered “Well, in a minifigure level, the soul is the one that comes up with the ideas. The mind is the one that expresses these ideas and the body is the one that gives them life. This is the Process of Creation. Mardolf the Orange was the first to express this theory. He’s the second more important Assembly after Dr. Overbuild”
“How do you know all these things?!” Magical wondered jaw-dropping.
“First of all, I have the Nexus HQ in this” Shadow answered showing his helm “And secondly, when I was in Forbidden Valley I discovered a secret library in the Great Tree”
Before anyone could reply, the door opened and Rad got in saying “You have permission to leave; you will be transferred to Nexus Tower”. . .

Chapter 58: The Vow

"Come wi... What happened here?!" said Rad seeing the mess that had been created.
"We had an accident" answered Shadow worried that Rad wouldn't believe him.
"Well...okay..." he told him awkwardly "You must come with me τo swear that you won't tell anything".
So, they followed Rad although Shadow didn't think good about this. They got into the elevator where an awkward silence prevailed. The relationship between the four friends and the Building Squad member weren't exactly friendly, so none of them felt comfortably.
Finally, after some seconds they reached surface where the loud sounds of the people celebrating covered the awkwardness. In addition, Shadow's curiosity conquered his not so friendly feelings about Rad, which originated from the fact that Shadow met him when he was full of anger.
"What are they celebrating?" exclaimed Shadow loudly, so he could be heard over the cries of the others.
"Our win against the Maelstrom" responded Platinum who appeared from nowhere.
When Rad looked at him with his wondering face, Platinum replied to his wonder "I heard that they are going to do the vow. Are you sure it's a good idea. Shadow it's a little cranky".
"Tell this to the council" was Rad's answer.
"So, they didn't just gave the responsibility to Cyborg?" asked Platinum, as they passed a statue.
"Are you kidding me?! Even Panagis was involved to the case" responded Rad surprising Platinum.
"Who's Panagis?" wondered Magical.
"President of the Castle Kingdom's Council!" said Rad and Platinum simultaneously and then Rad continued: "You'll meet him right away!"
They had now reached the central building and Shadow looked at the huge tower which was above them and seemed like continuing forever. They got into the building where after walking along a hallway they entered a round room which had two semicircular tables. Ten minifigures were sitting on chairs and another one was standing in the middle.
As soon as the oaken double doors were closed by two guards, the man in the middle said “Hello, Strange Odd Shadow and co. I am Panagis McInterest, President of the Castle Kingdom Council, Leader of the Fighting Squad, 3rd Rank in Imagination Order, 4th Rank in Building Squad. You know Strange you and your friends caused a lot of trouble”.
“Actually, friends call me Shadow not Strange and you are not a friend Mr. McInterest. I barely know you” Shadow answered smiling “However, I think I know what kind of person you are. You remind me someone else I know. He is the Mayor of Yorkanton City. He is a self-seeker, who tries to gain any kind of power, although there isn’t a lot of power to gain there. Your only difference is that he is a little preservative”
“Okay, Smart pants! If you want play like this! I tried to be kind with you but there are some limits!” Panagis exclaimed angrily.
“What? I thought I was completely kind” Shadow responded with a ‘gentle’ voice smiling sarcastically.
“Sir, Shadow is a little cranky. Don’t you-” Platinum started saying but Panagis interrupted him: “Don’t care Platinum Diamond Tiger. I just want them to do the Vow and leave! Bring Mardolf the Orange!”
Then, from another door like the one they had got in the room Mardolf the Orange got inside saying to a guard: “… repeatedly said that I refuse to do such magic! I dislike it as the Maelstrom!”
“It’s only for once, Mardolf! Come on! You know that all the other wizards and sorcerers of the castle have left to chase the remaining Maelstrom Knights from the previous battle!” Panagis exclaimed desperately.
“Oh, Panagis! I expected that you would be behind all these” Mardolf replied trying to stay calm “What do they leaders say about that?”
“I haven’t asked them. They are very busy. We must not give them more worries”
“I knew that you wouldn’t have. Okay, I’ll do it. But only this time. If you ask me to do it again I won’t do it”
“WAIT!” Shadow shouted “Don’t you think you forgot something?!”
“What?!” a lot of people wondered.
“We didn’t agree to do this!”
“Come on, Shadow! Being in the best place for fighting unable however to battle, is the worst torture for me!” Magical complained.
“Yes, Shadow. We want to go back!” Sarah completed.
“OK! If you insist I’ll do it!” Shadow said condescendingly.
“Great! Let’s carry on! We have other things to do” Panagis told them happy they were almost over.
Magical went first. He caught Mardolf’s hand. Mardolf, who was holding a wand with his other hand and touched with it Magical’s hand, said “Magical, do you promise to keep this place secret?”
“Yes” Magical answered and a blue flame lighted up which was coming from the edge of the wand and made a circle around their hands to end up to the edge of the wand again.
“Do you promise that you will tell anything about this place under no circumstances?”
“Yes”
Another flame lighted up which also made a circle in a way that the two circles created two Xs where they connected.
“Do you promise that you will try to stop anyone from learning about the place, unless you are told to act differently?”
“Yes”
A third flame appeared which passed from the points, where the other two flames were connecting, creating a circle, too.
Sarah and Katie followed and in the end Strange Odd Shadow did the Vow, although he didn’t want to. When they were finished, Mardolf took him aside and told him “Shadow, as Imagination, Magic to be used right needs all the parts of a minifigure to agree to that. This time all the bodies and minds agreed to that. However, there were two hearts that didn’t want this to happen”
Shadow didn’t understand and even when they started walking away to leave, he hadn’t realized what the old man was trying to say to him.
“I’ll be watching you, Strange Odd Shadow” Panagis said a little before they went out of the room. . .

"This is awesome!" Katie exclaimed.
"I can't understand why they like it so much!" Magical said his thoughts out loud.
"Finally, we agree!" Sarah responded.
After 15 minutes they had permission to leave and took off. Venture Traveller was fast, although not as fast as Venture Explorer. After an hour they reached Nexus Tower. . .
