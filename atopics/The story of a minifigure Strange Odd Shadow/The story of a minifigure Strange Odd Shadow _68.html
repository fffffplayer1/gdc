He could see the blackness starting spreading to the whole plant. Berg was happy he managed to end the plant before his own end came. . .

However, its best feature was that all of its power was concentrated on the object thrown to. Its force would only be diffused all around, if not all of it was necessary for the complete disinfection of the object. It would be the best weapon against the Maelstrom Vortex.

            Everyone was amazed at the cube and they congratulated Shadow for that great achievement of his. However, they did not all just see that side of the story. Many thought that if Shadow tried to do that, he’d certainly be smashed seconds after he had implemented his plan, if not earlier.
