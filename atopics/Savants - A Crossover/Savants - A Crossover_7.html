Chapter 7 Part 3:

 

Song Used: Free Bird (Guitar Solo) (Lynyrd Skynyrd)

 

Grimacing, Plue grabbed Cadmus' ankle, and pulled it off her stomach, enabling her to rise. Having lost her sword, she instead attempted to attack Cadmus with her limbs, sending kicks and blows in his direction. Seemingly due to fatigue, Cadmus seemed unadept at dodging them and only tanked the blows as they came. before retaliating by socking Plue. Reeling backwards, she strained to keep her balance.

 

"Hey!" A voice called out suddenly. Plue strained her head to see Vector standing woozily upright, holding his broadsider.

 

"This won't hurt a bit!" He yelled, before firing a shot from the grenade-launcher type weapon. Buckshot peppered Cadmus' chestplate and he was knocked onto his back away from Plue. Perhaps he had run out of energy, perhaps it was the kick of the projectiles, but he made no attempt to rise.

 

"Did I mention that I was a compulsive liar?" Quipped Vector, tossing the weapon aside and allowing hmself a smile of satisfaction.

 

"I thought that was Gaia." Plue chuckled.

 

"Someone say my name?" The aforementioned deviant popped into view, holding a coil of steel cable. "What did I miss, aside from an action scene?"
