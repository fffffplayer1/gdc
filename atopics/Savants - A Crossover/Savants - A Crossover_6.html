Chapter 6 Part 1:

 

Song Used: This Will Be The Day (RWBY OST)

 

"What do you mean, you've come for the Mythran?" Jonna immediately leapt forwards, placing herself between Talia and Ivy. "What do you want with her?"

 

"Save your breath." The man interrupted. "She's one of those "strictly business" people. You aren't going to get much out of her."

 

The raven-haired newcomer turned her attention to the man, and a look of disdainful disgust formed on her face.

 

"Dante Wilder." She spat the name out like the vilest of poisons. "The Twelve of Infinite Sight are aware of your exploits."

 

"Charmed." Dante gave a mocking bow, his coat fluttering as he did so. "It's nice to see that my reputation proceeds me. Unfortunately, I don't have a clue who you are. That fine by you?"

 

"I suggest stepping aside." Declared Ivy, ignoring his words. "Currently, this Mythran is my prime target."

 

"Whilst a great deal of me agrees that this isn't my problem, I can't comply." Dante told her in reply. "If I don't stop you now, who's to say you won't come after me later? History has a way of repeating itself, and I can't just let you do whatever you want; not on my turf." He glared at her, eyes shining with intensity and power. "I suggest you walk away."

 

"Flee from your words? What nonsense." Ivy twirled her scythe. "If you insist on standing in my way, I'll just have to cut you down!" With that, she leapt towards him, weapon bared.

 

"Fine by me." Dante stood stock still, hands in his pockets, as Ivy charged towards him. At the last second, however, his form flickered and vanished.

 

"Wha-" Ivy didn't finished as Dante reappeared behind her, noncolantly raising his head. Pink beams of light erupted from his eyes, arcing towards his opponent. Ivy raised the scythe's blade and blocked the lasers, which snaked away and cleaved a hole in the cavern wall. Dante raised an eyebrow as Ivy landed in front of him.

 

"Did you think you knew what I was capable of?" Dante laughed. "Sorry, Ivy, but I think your intel needs updating."

"Oh, so you can control your power? I'm surprised. Didn't you gain it recently?"

 

"Well yeah, I guess so. I've been able to do this stuff since around the time the Nexus was overcharged."

 

"Since what was what?" Jonna could only murmer as she tried to piece together what the pair were talking about.

 

"What might interest you, however, is that I'm not the one controlling that power." Dante grinned, baring his teeth. "I can use it so well because... well, I guess you could say I've had help."

It arced towards Ivy, and this time it was she who stood motionless and let the attack arc towards her. As the flames reached her, Talia gasped. "Jonna! Are you alright!" She gaped.

 

"It's not me..." she said as the flames kept burning. "It's him."

 

"What about him?"

 

"As a Mythran, I can sense souls." She said. "I sensed this man's when we met him, and could tell that there was darkness within it. And... and j-just now, I suddenly felt that balance be flipped... now his soul is dark, with light in it."

 

"I don't quite follow... but he's on our side, remember? Besides..." Jonna trailed off as she pointed at the woman standing within Dante's flames. "She seems pretty tough to."

"Too bad you're not resistant to electrokinesis!" He chuckled. "Were you aware of the fact that fire isn't the only thing I can use to fight you?"

"I was, actually." Ivy filled in. "But I-"

 

"Oh, enough TALKING!" Roared Dante abruptly, sending another bolt of lightning at Ivy with a snap of his fingers. "It has been over a YEAR since I've been in a proper fight. That Dante just feels like he has to step away from it all, and hold me back, and it's so INFURIATING!" He raised both his palms and unleashed a slew of laser blasts towards Ivy, who either dodged the projectiles or used the metal blade of her scythe to deflect them.

 

"And... now he's referring to himself in the third person?" Jonna's eyebrow had probably risen by this point, but there way no way to verify that. "Okay, something's very, VERY odd with this guy."

 

"I told you!" Talia clasped Jonna's shoulder in fear. "It's his... his soul!"

 

"Regretting that curiosity now?" Jonna muttered, backing away slowly from the fight. She had intended to subtley move far away enough from the brawl until a sprint would be plausable, but Ivy spotted the pair moving away.

 

"No!" She bounded forward and landed in front of the two of them. "My target is not getting away from me that easily!"

 

"Concentrate on your own opponent!" Dante, too, leapt forward. Spinning around in mid-air, he kicked out and knocked Ivy to the floor, replacing her in front of Jonna and Talia.

 

"Don't worry, you two. I'm sure Dante'll be willing to keep talking with the pair of you once I finish this chick off."

 

"Why are you referring to yourself in the third person?!" Cried Jonna, not noticing Talia shaking her head is disapproval. "Aren't you Dante?"

 

It was as she said this that she realised that Dante's eyes were deep violet; it wouldn't have been an issue, except she could have sworn they hadn't been that colour earlier.

 

"Uh, in short - no. No I'm not." Dante - or rather, whoever was truly in control - deadpanned, before turning around and beginning to sink into the shadows. "But like I said, I'm sure Dante will explain once I'm done here."
