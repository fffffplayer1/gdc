Ivy, for her part, could do little but flap her hands in anger in response to Raelyn's taunting due to the sudden mutism that had been inflicted upon her.

 

Raelyn glared.

 

"Talia didn't do anything wrong to her." She declared. "I don't like it when people attack others with no reason."

 

"Well, why don't we ask about a reason?" Talia pointed out, walking up behind the two. "Perhaps the solution is in negotiation instead of violence."

 

Dante spread his arms out wide. "Thank you." He groaned.

 

Raelyn, knowing she was outspoken, folded her arms in disdain. "Fine." She muttered, before raising her voice. "Ivy, why were you hunting Talia? And you'd better give me a good reason, or I'm coming over there." To conclude her sentence she snapped her fingers, causing Ivy to gasp as her voice returned to her.

 

"You want to know why?" She spoke with equal disdain. "Fine. Mythrans like her are no better than the Maelstrom."

 

Dante and Raelyn each raised their left eyebrow, moving in syncronisation.

 

"Go on." Deadpanned Raelyn.

 

"They're malevolant beings." Ivy went on. "They showed no regard for any lives other than their own. They treated humans such as us with nothing but callousness, and they never lifted a finger to help fight agains the Maelstrom. To them, our race was no more than insects. And, as such, they're still dangerous, even though they're practically extinct. It's my duty as a member of the Twelve of Infinite Sight to either capture or destroy this Mythran before she..."

 

She trailed off, interrupted by ugly laughter erupting from none other than Raelyn, who was clutching her sides.

 

Several seconds passed, before Ivy snapped. "Are you done?!"

 

"Hahaha... wha? Uh, sure. Yeah, I'm done." Raelyn's smile faded. "Just reacting sensibly to the biggest load of hogwash I've heard in a few thousand years."
