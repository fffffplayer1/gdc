Well, it's nice to see how much people care.

 

Don't worry, I'm not salty. But while I'm here, I would still like to share some character origins;

 

PANDEMONIUM'S BANE: ORIGINS

 

Dante/Harold started off as a self insert, hence the primary name. Will and Anna are also based off real life people - my own best friend and my own sister.

 

Vectorgreendragon and Plue were two friends I made during LU, and I carred their names and avatar appearances over to my stories. Vector's personality I made up based on appearance, while Plue's personality is based on that of an old friend of mine.

 

Honestly, I have no idea how I came up with Shadow. I think I was inspired by one of the earliest ninjago episodes, where Kai fights his own shadow. The idea just kind of came to me.

 

Jacen, Gaia and Rose are all based on characters that were submitted for this story that I don't think ever got written? It was the longest character submission topic I've ever seen, and I ended up taking over two pages of notes on all the different characters. When searching for ideas for MM, I decided to check the old document to see if there was anyone with maelstrom powers I could be inspired by. Lo and behold, there was a guy called Tom who was immune to maelstrom infection, a character called Roseann who could temporarily become maelstrom (very uncreative of me, I know) and some dude called Fighterforce who could "sprout weapons from his arms, is more resistant to maelstrom and can shoot maelstrom fog, fire and lightning from his hands. His powers depend on the amount of maelstrom in the area".

 

One genderbend and a couple of main ideas later, and I had the last three PB founding members.

 

Finally, during the original Maelstrom Minifigure story, there was a character called Dilip (who's name is from a total wipeout contestant). I thought he was so cool I gave him a bigger part in the rewrite. (He snuffs it in both though.) There was also a sniper called Stewie. Stewie later became Stu.

 

And yeah, I think that's it.
