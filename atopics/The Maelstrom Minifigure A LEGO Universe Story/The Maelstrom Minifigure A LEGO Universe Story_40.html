So as I'm archiving the original maelstrom minifigure, it hit me just how little the overall story structure has changed, and how many ideas stayed with this story through a remastering and a rewrite. Okay, here we go...

 

  • Character named Haroldosaur getting maelstrom powers through a blow to the head by a spiderling
  • Characters named Harold, Will, Plue, Vector and Anna
  • Doctor Overbuild being all mad scientist and "I will run tests on ye, creature of maelstromagination"
  • Harold's actions leading to Duke Exeter's demise (Except it was originally played for laughs.)
  • Will in a scene where a protagonist hits an antagonist on the head with a heavy thing to save another protagonist
  • Harold being wrangled by the Nexus Force
  • Plue and Harold being imprisoned inside Nexus Tower
  • Someone influential within the Nexus Force being in league with the Darkitecht (Originally I created a new character called HungryKeyBrick "Key", who Celotape referenced earlier in this topic. Later I did away with the idea of Key and just made Overbuild the main antagonist for simplicity's sake.)
  • Magic rocks that blessed people with powers (No, seriously.)
  • A group of people with magic rock powers uniting to fight the maelstrom
  • A maelstrom entity inside Harold controlling him (Not originally played for laughs)
  • Will being partially infected by maelstrom
  • A signifcant protagonist who is also a Shinobi
  • A character called Dilip getting tragically smashed in the final battle
  • The protagonists and their allies making a final stand against the Maelstrom
  • Harold dueling the Darkitecht

 

Those are all the ones I could find at first glance. Maybe there are some more? Who knows.
