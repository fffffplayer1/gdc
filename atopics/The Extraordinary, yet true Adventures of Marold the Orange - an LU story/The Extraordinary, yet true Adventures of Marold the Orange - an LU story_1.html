Chapter 1: Mardolf the Orange and the Annoying Orange

 

        The Darkitect laughed. He had put all his power to create the worst monster ever. And now it was ready. There were just a few more details. He put the rock he had in his hands on a table. And then he infected it. The effects were terrible and after a few minutes even the Darkitect screamed in horror. . .

       

         Four years later:

 

It was late and Mardolf the Orange was walking in the streets of Nimbus Station. He had a tiring day trying to show the new Recruits how to build stuff like rockets and racing cars. He was afraid that the recruits were getting dumber and dumber.

          Then, suddenly he heard a very disturbing sound. It was laughter. Then, the person said with the same annoying voice "I will bring destruction through annoyance!!! HEHEHEHE!!!" Mardolf looked at the bushes and saw the Maelstrom's worst monster. It looked like an orange, but it had a mouth and two eyes. It contained a will for destruction worse than the one the Darkitect had, but it lacked power. Except for its super-catastrophic power of annoyance.

           "Who are you?!" Mardolf asked worried, because he felt that something was wrong with it.

           "I'm the Annoying Orange!" the evil creature said with a disturbing enthusiasm "Who are you?!!!"

           "Mardolf the Orange!" the wizard answered now afraid.

           "HEY!!! Both our names end with an orange in them!!! HEHEHEHEHEHE!!! And we are both orange we should become best friends! HEHEHEHEHEHEHEHEHE-" The Annoying Orange never finished that laughter, because the Magician outraged by the Orange's annoyance squashed it with his staff. And that's how the worst monster of the Maelstrom found its end. . .

Chapter 2: The Evil Clowns

 

       However, the Darkitect was watching the Annoying Orange hoping that it would destroy the Universe through annoyance. So, when he saw Mardolf the Orange destroying it, he got very furious. To take revenge he got back to his Very Secret Mystic Classified Lab at his Very Secret Mystic Classified Lair. Once more he had to smash the Bright Sign with Light Bulbs on it that had the shape of a huge arrow showing his Castle and was writing with big Neon letters "Welcome to the Very Secret Mystic Classified Lair of the Darkitect".

        "How stupid are the Stromlings???" he asked himself feeling desperation conquering his body.

        At his lab he started blending several liquids creating in the end a purple shining substance.

        "The first person that will meet me shall become the next Worst Monster of Maelstrom" he said and then he laughed evilly and maniacally.

 

        Mardolf kept walking trying to forget the Annoying Orange. However, suddenly after some minutes he heard the sound of wheels. And then about twenty Infected Clowns that were on unicycles appeared.

         "We are the Evil Clones of the Evil Clown!!!" they said altogether "And we came to destroy you!!!"

         "Great!" Mardolf said as the Evil Clowns surrounded him and started doing a circle in a row around him. The Sorcerer launched a lightning from his staff and hit many of them. However, nothing happened, excluding the Clowns' starting to laugh evilly. Mardolf started being weakened and in the end he fell to his knees.

          "ATTACK!" one of them screamed and they all turned towards him. The enchanter, with the last powers of his, jumped and the Clowns crashed on each other.

          "Our source of power!!!" they all yelled as they fell from their unicycles and then became just dust. . .

Chapter 3: The M-Rex Part 1

 

        The Darkitect angry to see that his second evil plan had failed decided to start something new to destroy Mardolf the Orange. And in the creature he created, he put more malice than ever...

 

         Mardolf kept walking. He was glad that he was safe, but he couldn't get either the Annoying Orange or the Evil Clowns out of his mind. He then realized that he was going the wrong way. Being angry he turned around and started walking the other way. However, it was then when he heard screams of minifigures coming from in front of him. And then he saw something really terrifying. It was a creature that looked like an infected Tyrannosaurus Rex, but it was twice as big, had arms that weren't small and was throwing fire from its mouth.

         "You've got to be kidding me!!!" the Mage screamed while he was running, regretting his turning around before. However, the monster, Maelstromosaurous Rex, was pretty fast and reached him quickly. The Wizard, knowing that, jumped and using his magical abilities he flew and reached the head of the chaotic creature.

          "Whatever damage I can do to it, it will be easier to avoid its attacks over here" he thought. Then, he launched some lightning with his staff, but the monster barely felt it. After that Mardolf remembered a spell that could help in that situation. When he told the appropriate words, a cold female voice like the ones the machines use was heard "Curse loading 1%"

           "WHAT???!!!" Mardolf yelled frustrated. When the beast heard his voice, it turned its head quickly making the old man slip from the monster's head to its neck and then its body where he stopped. While he was slipping he scratched the monster trying to stop. When he looked at the monster's head, he saw it was staring at him with its tongue out of its mouth... like a dog.

          "You like scratching, aren't you?" he asked and the monster nodded positively.

Chapter 4: The Maelstrom Channel

 

         "MWAHAHAHAHAHAHAHA!!!" the Darkitect was laughing evilly for about half an hour. However his evil laughter was interrupted by the voice of a fearful voice saying "EEEEHH, Sir! Could you leave now?"

         "WHAT?! WHY?! YOU INTERRUPTED MY EVIL LAUGHTER!!!"

         "Well, sir we are casting our new show 'Cooking with Flogmore' starring Admiral Flogmore here" the afraid Stromling answered and a cameraman, a bowman, several other stromlings and Admiral Flogmore entered.

         "WHAT?! SINCE WHEN DO STROMLINGS AND STROMLING PIRATES DO TV SHOWS???!!!" the Darkitect shouted.

         "Since those stupid Dark Ronin started making a television crime show!" the stromling said with some anger in its voice.

         "AND SINCE WHEN DO DARKLINGS MAKE TELEVISION SHOWS GENERALLY?"

         "Well, after losing the Battle for Nimbus Station a great amount of Darklings hid and they started getting too bored, so they started doing silly or rebellious stuff. To stop that, we created the Maelstrom Channel" the Stromling explained.

         "And what if Nexus Force finds about the channel?" the Darkitect asked.

         "Hmm, I hadn't thought of that!"

         "Whatever, I'll leave, but don't destroy anything here!" he said and after taking his staff from the wall, he left. When he was walking through the hallways, he heard the voice of Flogmore "How am I going to hold the scoop? I have no hands!!!"

         "I knew we should have taken Murgle Blotch for the job!" another stromling said.

         "NO! NO! I can do it!" Flogmore shouted "Besides Blotch is already the host of 'Talk'!"

        In the end the Darkitect got out of the Very Secret Mystic Classified Lair of the Darkitect and with an evil laughter he disappeared in the shadows. . .

       Mardolf saw his house. He had finally after all this time reached his destination. However, when he put the key into the keyhole a cold cruel wicked voice behind him said "I wouldn't do this, if I were you. Your house is full of bombs. If you open the door you will be blown up!"

        Mardolf turned around and saw the Darkitect. He was wearing the clothes that Baron Typhonus used to wear and had a similar staff, but his face was Maelstrom purple and his eyes and mouth were shining red.

         "Time to die!" he said and raised his staff. Maelstrom lightning was launched from it, but didn't hit Mardolf because he had also conjured lightning and they had now collided. Their staffs were now connected through the lightning and a spiritual battle started. Whoever had a stronger spirit would have stronger lightning, so his lightning would conquer the other's. But it would take hours for someone to win, because their spirits were equal. Understanding that, the Darkitect took a Maelstrom Big One and thew it at Mardolf, who didn't expect it and was hit. That made him fall,so that the Darkitect's lightning hit him knocking him out. . .

Chapter 6: The Terrible Tortures

 

       Mardolf the Orange woke up. He was in a completely dark room without knowing what was happening. Then, he remembered his fight with the Darkitect and screamed in agony "Where am I?"

        "Good! Good! You're awake!" the evil cruel cold voice of the Darkitect said "MWAHAHAHAHAHAHAHA!!!!!!!!! It's time for you to pass through T.T.T.!!! MWAHAHAHAHAHAHAHAHA!!!"

        "What's T.T.T.?" Mardolf asked.

        "It stands for The Terrible Tortures!!! MWAHAHAHAHAHAHAHA!!!" the Darkitect answered.

        "Oh!" the old man replied frightened.

        "BRING THE FIRST MACHINE OF T.T.T.!!! The Machine of The Terrible Electrocution!!!"

        A Stromling that had no hair came in bringing a machine as tall as the average minifigure. Two cables came out of it. The Bald Stromling put the machine next to Mardolf and put the cables on the enchanter's body. Then, it pushed a button on the machine and it turned on.

         Electricity passed through the cables, reached the wizard and... nothing happened. The electricity wasn't even tickling him.

         Seeing that the mage was relieved the Darkitect asked "Don't you feel terrible pain?"

         "No!" Mardolf answered trying not to laugh.

         "NOOOOO!!! BRING THE NEXT MACHINE!!! AND QUICKLY!!!"

         The Stromling left running. However, a little later a deafening sound was heard like something had fell and broken.

          The Stromling got in again, but without any machine.

          "Eeeehhh, sir" the Stromling said weakly "The machine broke"

          "BRING THE OTHER ONE, YOU FOUL!!!" the Darkitect yelled completely outraged.

          "Well, it broke the last time. There is no other machine"

          "AAAAAAAAAHHHHHHHH!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!". . .

Chapter 7: MOB!

 

        "It seems that we will do it the old-fashioned way. MOB!!! BRING MY STAFF!!!" the Darkitect commanded his minion angrily.

        "On it boss!" answered the Stromling. After some minutes the Stromling returned holding a staff.

        "Before you begin, could I have a cold drink?" Mardolf the Orange asked.

        "OH! I want a cold drink, too! And a donut!" the Stromling answered.

        "How did that come to your mind?!" the Darkitect wondered surprised.

        "Well, there's a fridge there saying Cold Drrrink Rrrefrrrigerrratorrr! Arrrgh!" Mardolf replied finding the whole scene comical.

        "Who put this there?!!!!!!!" the Darkitect shouted becoming angry again.

        "I did, sir! And a Stromling Pirate wrote the message!" the Stromling said.

        "And why is this?" the Darkitect tried not to cry.

        "Because it would harm the Feng Shui, if we put it elsewhere!" the Bald Stromling responded like it was saying the most natural thing in the world "Can I have a cold drink? And a donut?"

        "No!" said the Darkitect curtly.

        "Who are you anyway?" Mardolf asked referring to the Stromling.

        "He's Mob, my henchman!" the Darkitect told him "Actually, he's infected Bob. And I called him Mob, because I made clones of him and together they really created a mob. So, actually he's Mob 1.0. Enough talk, time for torturing"

        "Wait! Poisonus snake!" the Enchanter pointed at the wall behind the Darkitect and for some weird reason the Evil Being turned around. The Mage seized the chance and with the help of magic he managed to stand up, take his staff which was at the side and run for the door. At the same time, Mob started running crying "DOOOOOONUT!!!"

        He reached the door, but then he felt a hit on his head and passed out. . .
