Chapter 13: The Mail-Strom!!! Part 1

 

     Mardolf was at his home finally. Now, that the base of the Maelstrom was destroyed he expected that much time would pass before it would be ready to attack again. Even though Mardolf had just banished a most peculiar user from the LEGO Message Boards, he did not suspect that there was any evil in it.

     While he was sitting in his living room enjoying the view of his window, he heard someone knocking on the door. Wondering who it could be, the Magician walked to the door and opened. What he saw would leave anybody gaping with their mouth open for hours.

     It was a postman, but the weirdest postman ever. His skin looked as if it had been painted yellow and paint was actually still dripping from his face. His eyes were shining red. His hat did not touch his head due to his pointy hair that seemed unable to change in shape and were also possibly painted, brown this time. Finally, the postman's left arm looked like a blade to which a glove had been added at its edge.

     Mardolf gazed for a little more at the failed attempt of a Stromling to disguise itself into a minifigure postman. However, he received the letter that it was holding in its right hand and closed the door, as the Stromling Postman left.

     The Enchanter was actually curious to see what the letter contained. Mardolf broke the purple seal and opened the envelope. As soon as he had done that, α purple slime got launched from it to his face.

Chapter 16: Meet the terrible Sparkitect! Part 2

 

     In the room, there was a man who looked a lot like Baron Typhonus. He certainly wore almost the same clothes, but in white, decorated with some sparkly and silver parts.

     "Might I ask who you are?" Mardolf replied curious to see how this would continue.

     "I... am... the SPARKITECT!!!"

     There was silence. The minifigures were looking at him mouths gaping.

      "Really?! You don't know me?!" he asked disappointed.

     "I personally find ya'r name a bit ridiculous, but no, 'e don't know ya!" someone said.

     "The Darkitect was my little brother! Really, hasn't he ever talked to you about me?!"

     "Not even mentioned he had a brother!" Mardolf responded.

     "Well, Darkitect decided he wanted to destroy the Universe, but I wanted more to become an actor! I wanted my spark to shine in the world! I'm quite famous actually! But you Mardolf have destroyed my brother! And you will pay for it!"

     Before Mardolf could even start defending himself a phone rang (it actually had the ring tone of a song which sounded a lot like one of those sung in Red Blocks). Everyone started looking at each other. The phone rang some times and then Mardolf said "Well, it isn't mine!"

     The Bill-Dwellahs instantly started talking simultaneously assuring each other it wasn't their phone either. The Sparkitect finally said: "Oh, it's mine! I keep getting confused when I change my ring tone! Excuse me for a bit! Hello?" he had picked up his cell phone.

     "Yes... yes! Ahoy! Really?! Okay! Goodbye!" he hung up "I was just informed that my brother is alive and actually has become a baker... his other dream! Well, goodbye and see you later! Have to catch an audition!"

     And so he left. The minifigures kept standing there mouths gaping again. Finally, someone said "What 'as that?!"

Chapter 17: The End of Mardolf the Orange's Story Part 2

 

     Curious, he approached, not forgetting, however, Mob who could recover any minute now. The first button read "Donut Button!". The second said "To be labelled". Mardolf pressed that and suddenly a hatch opened on the ceiling letting the refrigerator he had seen in the old base fall onto the ground.

     Mardolf, having first face-palmed, looked at the third button. On it the following message was written "Destrrruction of the Maelstrrrom Button. Arrrgh!". Mardolf repeated his face palm. Why did the Maelstrom always repeat itself, sometimes becoming worse as time went on? Mardolf pressed that button and gave an end to it.

 

     Indeed peace followed after that. All of the Maelstrom was destroyed afterwards, with few little exceptions. Crux was peaceful once again. Mardolf managed at last to find peace in his home and rest. All this had been very tiring. For some reason, it seemed that everyone had forgotten about chasing him. Mardolf decided not to remind them. Now, Mardolf passed his evenings quitely, watching his favourite TV shows like "Cooking with Flogmore" and "MCIS - Maelstrom Criminal Investigative Service". Some Nexus-Forcers had indeed found out about the Maelstrom Channel just a little after the destruction of Maelstrom and it had become very popular. For unknown reasons, the Maelstom of the Maelstrom Channel had not been destroyed. Perhaps big buttons spared the people of entertainment.

     As for Mob, who also survived after the destruction, he found the Darkitect and started helping him in his bakery. Their bakery became the most renowned in all of the Crux System, if not LEGO Universe, and they lived happily ever after.
