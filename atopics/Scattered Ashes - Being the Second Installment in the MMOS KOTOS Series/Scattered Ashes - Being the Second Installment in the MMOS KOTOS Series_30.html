Chapter X (nut a number (too lazy to change to 31)) Part V

 

 

Running out of time, gotta think fast so I can be on my way, I don't have time for this... Drumr thought to himself, formulating a plan. The two started to approach him slowly, holding their swords out in case he attacked again. Drumr's brain whirled, trying to think of a way out. One of them charged, then the other. And Drumr had an idea.


As they came towards him, he planted his feet firmly in the ground before springing upwards. The one that had taken off first hit a tree head-first and groaned in pain while the other barely stopped in time. Drumr landed behind him, and swung his sword, which the enemy blocked without any apparant difficulty. The other swung around as Drumr swung his sword again at the two of them, and the stopped it with a double block. Drumr sprang back and prepared to leap in again.


They held firm and one said, "My name is Thingguy the Second, and in the name of the Knights of the Olde Speach I tell-eth you to stand aside!" That same instant, a loud thud was heard and both of them looked at each other with wide eyes, a bit confused. For not ten feet away, sprawled on the ground before them, was Drumr's sword.


"Did that guy just drop his sword?" one of the Thingguys asked.


"Looks like it. But I wouldn't question it," the other said, shrugging.


"You're Thingguy?" Drumr asked, a bit perplexed as his mind whirled with enough thoughts to drive somebody mad. I was told the Knights were dead... But they aren't...


"I'm Thingguy. He's an imposter sent by thedude," they said at the same time, pointing at each other.


"Alright..." Drumr muttered, unclear of what was going on. That must have been what they were arguing about earlier.


"Don't believe him! He's an imposter!" one of the Thingguys said, growling at the other one.


The other Thingguy replied, "Don't believe him! He's a spy sent by thedude!"
