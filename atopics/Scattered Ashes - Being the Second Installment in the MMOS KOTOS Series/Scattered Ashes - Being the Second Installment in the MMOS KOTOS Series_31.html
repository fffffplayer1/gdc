The two replied at the same time: "We were sent by Sir Talmid to find Lady Jonna!"


"That's why I'm here as well. What a coincidence," Drumr stated, picking up his sword and sheathing it.


"But how do I know I can trust you?" one of the Thingguys said, scratching his head in confusion.


"Easy. You don't know if you can. You can only ever take my word for it. But whether you trust me or not, we have a common goal."

"But who are you? We're supposed to work with you, but we don't even know your name," the other Thingguy said.
