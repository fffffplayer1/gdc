Drumr rolled to the side, just in time, as a bulky figure hurled right past him and back into the fog again.


His eyes flew open as flashbacks hit him. Two bodies on the floor. An evil laugh. Weak. In pain. Unable to stand. A fight in the dark and his fear.

 

Fear.


He dodged to the side as a kick came from behind, and Drumr prepared to strike, assuming his foe off balance. But Vladek had obviously anticipated the move, and spun around, kicking Drumr in the chest.


Weak.


The rebel was knocked to the ground, and only a swift barrel roll saved him from being crushed by Vladek's sword.


Helpless.


Drumr started to thrust his sword at his apponent, but before he could Vladek threw a mighty punch to Drumr's head, snapping something and almost completely crushing his ear. Drumr staggered and tried his best to stand, but could barely keep his legs straight underneath him.


Can't stand.


Vladek was lightning fast, not about to loose his chance to attack fiercely. He kicked Drumr in the chest, and the rebel howled in pain as he felt a sickening crunch. He fell to his knees, raising his sword just in time to stop the smashing blow. However, in doing this, his sword flew from his hand, clanging off into the distance at the edge of his vision, which he noticed was now badly blurred.


Pain.


The sword came down again, and Drumr prepared for the blow. But instead, he heard a clang, and saw a blurry Thingguy holding a sword against Vladek. He knew he was about to pass out. He faintly heard explosions and wondered what was going on as battle cries rang up around him. He felt the other Thingguy's hand grab his shoulder and start hauling him towards the camp entrance as swords clanged around them before everything went black.


I fought the demon in the dark. And I failed.

Dreams hit Drumr like a wave. He saw things that he never wanted to see again.


"Why the brick is there fog inside the tower?" Exeter yelled as the fog continued to thicken until visibility was almost non-existent.


We had just got back from our mission, me and my team... Rocker, Neon, Smart....


Drumr got an eiree feeling and decided to go check the main room when all of the sudden an explosion was heard. I know what's going to happen!


Drumr stumbled out of the room and into the hallway with shrapnel from the wall throbbing in his skin as he realized he was hit multiple times. Let me out of this dream!


Three Nexus guards were smashed upon the ground in front of him, and as he kept moving he saw something he never wanted to see.


Pain.

Suffering.

Helpless.
