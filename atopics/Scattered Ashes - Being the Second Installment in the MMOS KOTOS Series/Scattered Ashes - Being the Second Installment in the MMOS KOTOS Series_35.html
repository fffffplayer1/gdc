In the distance he could just make out two rockets. He almost tripped over himself as he rushed over to them, seeing two people standing looking at a planet. He almost fell over as he realized that that was Morcia from space.


The first was a female daredevil, who stated "Do you think he'll be alright?"


The second was a male engineer, who slung his hammer over his shoulder and said, "He'll be fine. He has new friends, he's not alone."


"Are you sure we shouldn't assist him at least some?" she asked the engineer, drawing her pistols.


"This is his fight, not ours. He will face challenges, but he will pull through as long as he sticks close to his friends."
"New friends, huh?" the daredevil said quietly, considering this for a short while.


"You are not alone, Drum. We are not with you for this battle, but we don't need to be." The engineer and the daredevil both looked his way and smiled, and he started to feel the dream fade.


His face lit up as he saw his friends standing there, looking straight at him.
