I too would like to write a "small" speech.

 

Once again, another part of my Childhood begins to crumble.  But life goes on.  Once again, I have a choice to stick it to the bitter end... or simply dissapear. It would be flawless, it would make sense, since I had just made a scheduled leave of absence anyway!...

 

But no. As most of you know, I have a personal guilt for what I did with LU and it's closing, leaving months before the servers went down.  The intense regret as I look at screenshots of my sister online at almost midnight, during those final hours. . . The thousands reveling around the Nexus. .   .      .

 

Where was I...?

 

Why did I waste it?

 

Time waits for no one. There are no second chances. You DESSERTED.

 

Well, perhaps. But here is something quite similar. And I will not make the same mistake twice. I will not lose friends. I will not simply disappear. I know that most IF NOT ALL of you will not disappear.   KOTOS is unique. KOTOS is not community Chat. It's not just Brickbook. It's not just... 'friends'  

 

We are brothers. We are Allies. We art survivors.  I can't even believe the news of the Boards shutting down didn't phase me at all. I read it and... And I knew that it would be all right. We have other means.  We have archives.

 

My only worry, which hit me a few hours later was....

 

We have no audience.  Or, rather, we soon won't.

 

With no Boards, and thus no outlet for an audience what would be the point of writing epic sagas, other than to write together? (which would and is quite rewarding, in and of itself, and I would be happy to do just that with all of you, if that is what the collective wishes! )

 

Well, a thought occured to me, reading everyone's speeches, and I'd like to propose it, simply as an idea for the future, though there is no rush. I'd just like to float it out there.

 

con't.

con't

 

As Fearless Leader Talmid suggested, we could post our stories on the Gallery, for as long as it will last, (though personally, I believe if the Boards are going down, the Galleries have a limited lifespan, in the wake of LEGO Life...

 

But that would require Visuals... For every Chapter... For ever segment...

 

And it would be beautiful.

 

And the more I thought of it, the more I KNEW... That if we wanted an audience, if we wanted to write an epic saga, and display it... I KNEW  we could do it.  As we all have varying qualities and quirks in our writing, we all have varying qualities and quirks in our sketching/drawing/painting/animation... And I know that we could, as a collective, we could be 'that old group, the KOtas, or whatever, that stuck around from some old version of the site called "message Boards", (sounds old and messy) and make some AWESOME Stuff that's great to read, and REALLY COOL art to go with it...'

 

Kotos, as always, is poised to be the next "Hero(s) Rising"....

 

And WE, the individual members, are ALL now, after this closing... We'll all be 'Veterans'.  'Vets' of the Message Boards. ' previously Vets of LU, Vets of SoLU, some of you Vets of the OLD OLD LMBS.... JEEPERS, we're Vets of Veteran status!!! Is ther something beyond Vet Status?!? Get Talmid one of those!!! We'll be 'the oldies'.   Many people will see the LMB's closing as the end of 'their time'....

 

But I.... as always, I see this as Yet another  BEGINNING!  Yet another.  And as I look into the future, I do not see an end time for KOTOS.  I refuse to see it.

 

I've said it before and I'll say it again. 

I may take a vay-cay, (everyone's entitiled to one! ) But I will always return, as long as there is something to return to, fellow knights.

 

 

One day, and I think of this often, I hope to meet you all in person, and laugh. And ya'll will probably laugh at me.... But that day is not yet. 

 

LONG LIVE IMAGINATION, INGENUITY, and INNOCENCE!!!
