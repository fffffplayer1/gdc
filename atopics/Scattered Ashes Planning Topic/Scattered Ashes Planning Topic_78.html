Though this is probably not going to be my last post, my farewell:

I am sorry to have to say goodbye to all of this, but I am one of the fortunate people who can say they will not lose the essence of these; only this means of conducting this business. And so I would like to give my thanks to you guys who made it all possible.

Tuesday

Ugh.

 

Honestly, I've never really been one for complex speeches. Neither writing, nor reading out. But, seeing as how I don't actually have to speak with this text format, and how my second home is hours away from perpetual doom (pun/reference intended) I thought I might neglect my revision to make an exception to my rule.

 

This moment, until the announcement, was never something that I thought would really happen. I guess, in my naive childlike wonderment, I overlooked the fact that everything has it's time, and everything ends. It's LEGO Universe all over again, in that regard. But this time... this time, I'm taking the opportunity to say more than "Goodbye LEGO Universe". To say something that might mean something, to someone, somewhere. I can only hope.

 

Firstly, I should probably say discuss the future of my writing; what I have planned for my own series. The long and short of it is - I don't know. Since the announcement, I have made little to no progress on pretty much anything. Maybe I'll get my motivation back later. For now though, my writing's kind of on hold. Just until I can get on top of everything. To be fair, this was a pretty bad time to write to begin with. I don't know how many of you know what GCSEs are, but mine are looming on the horizon. So... all of that's been pretty skewiff, as of late.

 

But it isn't about me. Not today. Today, it's about just saying goodbye. To a place that holds a lot of memories for me. From mid 2012 I've been here, though I was inactive from 2013 to late 2014, so that's... I don't know, at least three years? Maths isn't exactly on my mind right now, I guess. But anyways - I've had a lot of fun. Honest. I'm not the sort of person who vocalises my appreciation for things a lot, hence why I probably don't comment much aside from my own writing and question answering, but... everything that I see here, I've always enjoyed seeing. And I have a huge appreciation for the community we've built.

 

(hopefully)CONT.--->

 

 

The Maelstrom Minifigure, Knight Of The Old Speech, Time Lord.

Tuesday

This is probably my last post. I'm gonna have to go to sleep, so I'm gonna miss the last few hours of this place.

 

After the first initial shock, I persuaded myself it didn't matter much that we lose the Boards, because there are other places where we can basically do all we do here. So, I let myself forget the fact that this day was coming. I told myself there were two more months to enjoy and as the naive mind always does -with no exception- I had the idea that these two months would last forever. Even though reason or rather experience tells us time actually passes, if you think about it, people tend to think that night will never come when it is day, let alone the 28th of February when it is January. But time passes and this day has come. And it does matter that we lose this place. It was a great part of all of our lives for a long or short time (shoutout to Kal!) and all of us hate to see it go. I'm one of those people who don't like change. Even if I know that I can find my place in a changed world, too, I'd prefer the old world, because it feels safe and I'm comfortable in it. But frankly I think it's the small things I'm going to miss the most about the Boards. I'm even going to miss the character limit, a pain though it might be. It's been a part of my life for so long and it makes me sad to see it go; plus it means I can't fulfil my dream of a hundred-part Additional Manucript.

It feels surreal that today I can post this and then after I go to sleep and wake up tomorrow, I won't be able to anymore. It feels odd that in six days this place will be gone forever. I won't be able to log in and even just see what the LEGO Community has to say in this background and these colours, this style and this format. And I won't be able to add to it. Nothing new will ever come to this place.

 

It's sad. Very sad.

 

Still...

 

This is not the end. See you on the other side. I am honoured to have been a part of this community. Thank you. Goodbye. Have a great new start.
