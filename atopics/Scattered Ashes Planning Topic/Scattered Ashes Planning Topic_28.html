JAMMERS ON! Possible SPOILERS AND SUCH FOR MORCIA'S EARLY HISTORY

Jammers
I had always wondered why we didn't do mythical creatures such as dwarves, elves, and gnomes and golems and such. When I asked about it early on, ya'll brought up that we had used dragons, and perhaps one or two others, so they were around, just REALLY rare.. This got me thinking, what happened?
Well.
I had an Idea to do a (this time really short) Manuscript in a Fairy-Tale style describing an ancient hatred between Dwarves and Dragons, concerning the precious wealth of the Mountains. (GOLD, JEWELS, METALS, PROPERTY,) The Dwarves would mine too near Dragons dens, or Dragons would guard natural Gold deposits, saying they did not want Dwarves "warping" thier gold into "Filthy coins" (I also had an basis for why Dragons collect treasures, but that is more in-depth sciency/magical, and would need a whole seperate post to explain my incomplete reasoning...)

These issues lead to constant war between them, which (probably from some new inconceivable heresey,) lead to the near extinction of both races.  That is why we never hear from them in the current day. Still too busy licking thier wounds from that ancient defeat on both sides... Both entirely humbled.
