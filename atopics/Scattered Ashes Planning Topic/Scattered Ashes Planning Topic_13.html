@Jonna, fffffplayer1, Haroldosaur, and Strider:

 

Continuing the Stromling Saga... that is sort of what I want to do, although it will also be more like a fanfiction based on the Stromling Saga, since it is a sequel to Transdimensional Maneuvers too.  My LU character, Intrepid, was the main character in that, and was a minor character in the Stromling Saga, but he will probably be a main character here. For now I can think that the perspectives will switch between Intrepid and Cyclone a bit, but I'd like to focus on Cyclone and Kate.  I have written a few thousand characters and so far Cyclone has the main POV.

 

I had a great idea today about adding new characters.  One aspect where I failed with Soul Searching which we can completely forget about, is that I thought I could get somewhere with just three characters... well, that didn't work for many reasons but I will blame the lack of characters!  The plans I had for that story, however, are remembered by me and will influence this new, better story.

 

The ending of Transdimensional Maneuvers suggested a future relationship between our dimension's Intrepid and Kate.  That made for a great plot twist but is also most perplexing.  This story will explore that.

 

 

There is still a lot I need to plan with this story.  Sort of like how the makers of Star Wars 7, I imagine, had a list of DO NOTs with examples from the Prequel Trilogy, like "No Jar Jar character!" and "No boring council scenes!  In fact, blow up the council planet!" , so Soul Searching helped discover a lot of mistakes which I won't repeat.  I recently reread some parts of Transdimensional Maneuvers and The Figdroids! and noted some parts that were less than satisfactory, and make me want to revise when/if I repost those stories. Either way,

 

I'm going to try really hard to make sure this story is as epic as possible! And I will write and proofread a bit more before posting what I have.
