To Whom It may concern:

 

  An important event  has come up that will require my full attention for an extended period of time.  It is, in fact, so important, that I will not be available for three months.  It is not bad! In fact, it is very good. If all goes well, Lord willing, I will have even more free time than I do currently! And that means more writing potential!

 

But as for these next 3 months, I will not be very available, and probably won't even post. That being said, you can skip me in the writing queue if ya'll get to that before 3 months time.  If any of ya'll really need to contact me, just direct your questions to Jonna. If she doesn't know the answer to your issue she can contact me.  Jonna basically knows most of my story-related stuff. (Remember, she's my editor and all that. )

 

I'll try to keep up, and at least read, even if I don't post!  And I'm sure Jonna will keep me up to date if I don't read.   

 

Lastly, I just want to take this moment to say: For as long as I possibly can, I will always come back.

 

After making the mistake of leaving LU without saying goodbye, without seeing it all the way to the end, I will never make that mistake again.   So even if I disappear from time to time, I just want to say that you guys are awesome, and I will always come back.  This group of writers and readers is very special to me, and more than once, seeing someone's commets on something totally random (probably something about lack of chapters, ) has been enough to brighten my entire day.  

 

Legos and Duplo are a big part of my childhood, but I think I can truthfully say that the LMBs, and the folks who inhabit them, you guys, have also become a big part of who I am!  I hope to have that continue!

 

See  Read you all later!

                                     -James.
