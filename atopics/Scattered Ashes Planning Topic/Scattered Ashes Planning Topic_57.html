Darkness. Complete, impenetrable, thick darkness. Everywhere. Nowhere escapes the darkness. And not even one star to break the monotony. Space is now empty. Or rather we should not speak of space as it, too, is destroyed. There is only Void. But what Void is no one knows and no one will ever understand in the limits of these mortal realms.

 

But nay! Not everywhere. Somewhere in the full darkness, something material -something existent- lies. It is a huge space station, featuring hundreds upon hundreds of spaceships; there is even a whole armada of Cookie- and Duckz-Class Star Destroyers. So, big is this space station (though tiny it seems when compared to the vast emptiness surrounding it) that even a whole Empire of, say Duckz, could fit into it, with a full supply of crackers to last them for centuries.

 

It is there where our heroes have finally arrived, via the time elevator, and now they watch in awe the nothingness full of nothing outside the glass in front of them. Duckz walk about them, ignoring them, going about their business. None of the heroes has seen so many Duckz gathered together ever since the Supreme Duckz Commander abandoned their dimension and so they are amazed at that as well.

 

But suddenly...

 

"So, you have come then!" a voice speaks and a slow sarcastic clap is heard. "Well done. You have found me after all. It is time I should see what to do with you then!"

 

And a figure appears from the shadows, wearing a stylish suit and a fancy top hat. It is... the Supreme Duckz Commander!
