@Previous posts: This is how I think of things:

  • thedude was not able to expand his empire in the LEGO Universe due to the blockade. That's why he started building the Rhoddwr Marwolaeth, which was finished at the end of the 15-year rule (it's fifteen, not fifty JDI, we don't want our characters to be 70 years old! ). The Rhoddwr Marwolaeth, secretly built in orbit around Forbidden Valley (thedude could send stealthy shuttles through the blockade to carry his servants, thought not anything big enough to be able to attack), would be able to wipe out the Nexus Force once and for all with a couple of hits. It took thedude a long time to create, but it would allow him an easy and certain victory. After destroying Nexus Tower, Nimbus Station and possibly other worlds the Rhoddwr Marwolaeth would destroy the blockade and thedude would be ready to unleash his combined forces of Red Mythrans, Paradox Rogues and Battle Station against all of the planets of the LU. However, the Rhoddwr Marwolaeth was destroyed before it was capable of implementing such plans.
  • Meanwhile, thedude did not let his Paradox Rogues just rest. He had the Unverse Drive created and used it to send his forces to the PCG Universe. With the help of new allies, he managed to quickly get a hold of a big part of the dimension. Thus, that would allow him to have even more increased power when the Rhoddwr Marwolaeth would start operating. It is also a good hideout for thedude if ever required.
  • I'm not sure how much of Villainy is considered canon, because we had different ideas later.
  • As for the map, I think we should go with Strider, not with mental link. It just seems to make more sense. Also, I still believe that the map could only work if whole, thus not enabling thedude to use his piece.
