I'll post this here since this is where we're all hanging out so far, and I'll save my posts in Additonal Manuscripts for posting the manuscripts:

 

I have two Additonal Manuscripts ideas, one of which I've been thinking about for awhile, another I just thought about.

 

 

Story 1, working title: When We Were Six

The first chronological story would take place just after the first battle against the Paradox Rogues.  The knights are a group of six: Sir Talmid, Sir Luke, Sir Thingguy, Sir Legoboy, Sir Seton, and Sir Hycra.  They've just been made commanders of Matthias's army.  Thingguy, as we know, is looking for Jonna at this time.  The story would be about the military engagements briefly alluded to in Perpetual Doom Chapter 8.  There's a Paradox base in the Athelas Mounts which they will attack through the Ghost Passages.

 

Main characters:

- Sir Legoboy

- Sir Seton

- Sir Hycra

 

Story 2, working title: Then We Were More

This story would begin with explaining Perpetual Doom's first continuity mistake: Sir Talmid's unexplained capture by Barney.  This guy gets captured a lot, doesn't he?

So anyway, Sir Luke learns of Talmid's, Thingguy's, and Jonna's imprisonment, and they tell him to back to the Nimbus Station and recruit more Knights.

The story then is about Sir Luke's leave, and how he recruits Sir Sharpie, Sir Grongringo, Lady Ruby, and any others.  It is rumored that there is a connection between the Ladies Ruby and Jonna - perhaps this would be explored....

 

Main characters:

- Sir Talmid (shortly)

- Sir Luke

- Lady Ruby

 

 

 

As for when I should write these?  Not sure.  Whatever way, they are not to impede the writing of Scattered Ashes.  These will probably be just side projects of mine, as I try to get more writing experience which is my current focus.

 

Just want to throw these out there!
