DrumR02 wrote:

Sorry to pester with all these questions, but I think this might be my last one. Vladek might be in my chapter, and if so, how powerful should I portray him? And what powers, if any, does he have? Just wondering, needed a bit of clarification. Thanks!

 

-Sir Drumr

In the original LEGO Theme, Vladek did have a magical sword from which he could shoot red lightning/energy. He used this electricity to shoot incoming boulders out of the air, and he could also shoot the red energy at his enemies, of course.

 

He could also use the sword to deconstruct bricks and and rebuild them into something else. When using this power, he shot red energy at the bricks he was using, and also shot red energy at the location where he wanted to create the new structure. The bricks where telekinetically moved and rebuilt at that location.

 

I think it is important to note, however, every time Vladek used magic, it required some sort of magical object. When turning King Matthias' knights into shadow knights, he used the Book of Morcia; when making his army invincible, he created the Vladmask; when , he used a crystal orb; and when shooting lightning and reconstruct bricks, he used his sword.

 

However, in PD and SA, I don't think we ever had him use magic in combat.  his main combat trait is his exceptional skill with the sword. I would say that Vladek is at least as skilled a swordsman as even the best of the KotOS knights, if not even better than the best of KotOS.

 

P.S. My sources for Vladek's combat capabilities are the Knights Kingdom comics.
