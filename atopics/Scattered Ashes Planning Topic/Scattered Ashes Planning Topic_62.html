The reposting of Perpetual Doom is nearly to the end now. It's so epic that a story that doesn't go over 10 LMB pages stretched over 150 or so pages.

Speaking of the size of Perpetual Doom, I was putting several of our stories in word counter websites. The results are very interesting, because it seems that our "Short" Stories aren't exactly short. Not when compared to Perpetual Doom at least!

 

Warning: The numbers are not entirely accurate for several reasons.

I have also included some other things that the Word Counter showed - reading level and reading time:


The Adventures of the Peculiar Enchanter: 57634 words, 9th-10th Grade, 3 hours 29 min


To Return Home: 27583 words, 11th-12th Grade, 1 hour 40 min


Charcoals & Embers: 24905 words, 11th-12th Grade, 1 hour 31 min


Fall of Calibus Tower: 22987 words, 11th-12 Grade, 1 hour 23 min


The Adventures of the Peculiar Enchanter A History Lesson: 21814 words, 9th-10th Grade, 1 hour 19 min


The Rhoddwr Marwolaeth: 17927 words, College Student, 1 hour 5 min


Foes and Heirs: 16969 words, 11th-12th Grade, 1 hour 2 min


Lady Jonna's Quest: 16012 words, College Student, 58 min 14 sec


The Adventures of the Peculiar Enchanter The Ten Heroes of Malidor: 14338 words, 11th-12th Grade, 52 min 8 sec


Perpetual Doom: 10977 words, 11th-12th Grade, 39 min 55 sec


The Adventures of the Peculiar Enchanter The Beginning: 10010 words, 9th-10th Grade, 36 min 24 sec


Scattered Ashes, Volume I: 8832 words, College Student, 32 min 7 sec


Lady of Calibus: 6000 words, 11th-12th Grade, 21 min 49 sec

 

By the time you are reading this, you have probably noticed that Perpetual Doom is shorter than most of our long AMs. PD always felt longer, so I was amazed to find that it's barely longer than APE: The Beginning! In contrast to these, my longest Story of a Minifigure story is 58223 words longs.

 

In other news, kudos to Thingguy and Harold for writing College Level stories and kudos to Strider for the longest AM, yet (if you count To Return Home as one).
