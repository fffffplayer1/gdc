Yesterday was a good day. I finished an AM, started my SA chapter, reposted a bit of PD and a bit of Curse of the Ronin. Meanwhile, I got some good news concerning DLU and I found out there were new episodes to some series that I watch.

 

But life can be cruelly surprising sometimes. Like when I entered the LEGO Universe website years ago and I was momentarily happy to see a new announcement. And then I saw what it was about. LEGO Universe was closing.

 

Yesterday, LEGO decided to give me another of those experiences. When I first saw some new posts by my friends here, I thought it would be a good thing. Then, I realised the LMBs were closing, too.

 

I have been on these Boards for more than five years and have lived some crazy and amazing moments here. I have read some fantastic stories and I have met some great people. It's funny how until now my bookmark for this place is called Stories of LEGO Universe. I never changed it for old times' sake and because it was the stories that drew me here.

 

I remember first coming here, because of LU. The first thing I ever did on the boards was post several posts of ideas that could be implemented on LU. I remember idolising a user called Bumbaca at the time, though I don't remember why. I was such a newbie then. That reminds me of the old Boards when you'd use the filtres to keep track with your topics.

Then, I noticed the Stories of LEGO Universe subforum and started browsing it, searching for an interesting story to read. Little did I know then that I was defining a big part of my life for the next years. I found a story called Typhonus Chronicles and it drew my attention. I discovered a very good story that would shape some ideas for my stories very definitely, mainly concerning the four Explorers.

Through that story I discovered The Blue Haired Wonder and LEGO Destiny, also good stories. By some twist of fate, none of these three stories was ever finished. But I was also inspired to write my own story.

Continuing:

On the 12th of September 2011, I posted my first story post. At first, I had wanted to name my story A Minifig's Story, but I found there was already a story called like that, so I changed it to The Story of a Minifigure. Strange Odd Shadow's character was born. Even though, on retrospect, I find the text is of very bad quality, I still like the story of the first book. Several users found their way to my story and asked to be in it, but only Flynntron did not leave. However, I was unlucky to see him abandon the story after the second book began.

 

I started more stories. Curse of the Ronin and the Story of Mardolf the Orange, though I made slow progress on them. I also took part in the Secret Agency series.

 

While I was a loyal reader to these first stories, I did not read many more stories. I just didn't bother to take much interest in other things. However, eventually I checked out the two big stories of the subforum: Servant of Darkness and A Hero Rising. In the end, I read them both to the end, but not at first. Still, one day I got more serious with the former and read it. Through that, I discovered Perpetual Doom and the Knights of the Olde Speech and I decided to join them.

 

This opened up a whole new world of possibilities for me. I finally made some good friends in the Boards and I got to participate in one of the most hilarious and still epic stories written on these Boards, if I'm allowed the bragging. I found new stories to read like Imagination Infection, Broken Slave and the Rhoddwr Marwolaeth. I got constant readers of my stories. But more than anything I had friends. Talmid, Jonna, Grongringro, Ninjago_Builders, Thingguy, Luke, Sharpie, Fishul and later James, Harold, Drumr, JDI, Dagget_Dux and more I might be forgetting.

 

There have been good days and some bad days and there have been crazy days. With my new friends, I participated in subject wars and crazy duckz roleplaying.

 

I have had a great time on these boards and I want to thank you all.
