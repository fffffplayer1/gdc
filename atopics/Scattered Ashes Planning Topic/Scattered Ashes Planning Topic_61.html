Here goes! Naturally I made some stuff up.

 

 The Four Great Kingdoms of Militiregnum

 

  Militiregnum

Consider a world that's a medieval world in time, place, and theme.

The world is called Militregnum, on a planet that’s blue, brown, and green.

In the west are its four Great Kingdoms, ruled by kings and queens.

Aquila, Elepharia, Morcia, and Britay.

 

  Aquila

A circular garden with spiraling shrubs, with the tall Glass Citadel in its center.

An appropriate appellation is Glasted, the land's capital in the Great Plain of Aquila.

Aquila is surrounded by forests, including the Bewitched Forest on the west, and northern seas.

And the peaks of Athelas – their rocky heights separate Aquila's south and Morcia’s north.

 

  Morcia

Forests and deserts and plains with many noble and devious names.

Mount Scary, The Lost Plains and Forests of Doom, Godzilla Burger – they are the scary ones.

Calibus, Evereed, The Citadel of Orlan, and the Farmlands, most importantly the Farmlands.

Places of fear and beauty, manmade and natural, make the diverse lands in the Kingdom of Morcia.

 

  Britay

A land of sovereignly mandated science, religion, and advancement.

Bravely Britayan men and women ride the arrow of time pointing into the eye of the storm.

On an island in the sea, they fight evolution’s storm alone.

But the Ankorian Wizard’s Curse makes an anticyclone.

 

  Elepharia

The elephanters live by life’s essentials, and their God-given gifts.

Rudimentary in joy, governance, and family construct.

Fathers and mothers train their children by their lifelong traditions.

Then their grown up children train the gray elephants, in their strength they find security.

 

  The Four Great Kingdoms

Of Militiregnum these Kingdoms hail.

Kings and queens are like brothers and sisters, siblings fighting by their parents’ feet.

And sons and daughters carry on generation long rivalries.

Divided they remain only Four Kingdoms.

 

And in division they can fall.
