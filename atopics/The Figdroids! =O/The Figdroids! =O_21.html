Chaptah 34


"Go to Epsilon Starcracker!" Wisp Lee cheered.

 

"What, that's it?  Yay...." Katie said.

 

Jay and Katie dizzily zigzagged away from Wisp Lee before collapsing under some trees.  Waiting for their thoughts to clear, Jay panted, "Now just where are we?"

 

"It's obviously Avant Gardens," Katie stated. "There's the Paradox Lab Museum Place Thingy, the same trees, that large bridge which is now broken, and there are Stromlings."

 

"In a much smaller number," Jay pointed out.

 

"Yeah.... so this is the way how Veggie Works plans on getting us trained."

 

"Must be."

 

"But what is 'the way' anyway?  Everything is so real, and so different...."

 

"Simulation?"

 

"No...." Katie was silent for a few seconds, before saying slowly, "Want to know what I think Jay?  I think this is the Maelstrom War.  We're in the past."

 

May as well get that training over and done with.  Following the mission chain, Jay and Katie smashed hundreds of Stromlings, Stromling Mechs, and Dark Spiderlings; died several times in Survival; fell off the Monument even more times; and then walk all the way back to the other side of Avant Gardens.

 

...

Several days of grueling missions, leveling up, collecting and buying items later....

 

After running down the road to the launch area adorned in their best gear, passing by a guy shouting a paraphrase of "Become a member and give me all your money!" who was in turn being booed by someone who looked a lot like Intrepid Fusion Eclipse, setting their new rockets on the launch pad to Block Yard, and waiting twenty minutes while their rockets' system monitors displayed a "Travelling to Block Yard", Jay and Katie finally were ready for the ultimate test.

 

Battling the Spider Queen.

Chaptah 35

 

After those boring twenty minutes, Jay and Katie materialized at a dark, forbidding chunk of rock.  It was indeed a chunk of rock.  A small rock, with some trees and other rocks at its borders and a huge mountain at the back.  They were the only people there, except for some other guy in Sentinel clothes standing near a random mailbox and a huge purple spider, if you count the Spider Queen as a person.

 

"Well, this is it." Katie said, pulling out her Superior Longsword of Stunning and eyeing it. "I doubt this will work very well against that...." She stared at the huge purple spider.

 

"Let's check out that guy." Jay said, pointing at the Sentinel clothes guy.

 

They walked towards him, who greeted them saying, "Oh cool, I've never seen two people come to claim a Block Yard and battle the Spider Queen at the same time.  I was sent here to battle it but I failed.  Take these Trial Faction Gear and-"

 

"Yay thank you!" Katie interrupted him, and grabbed the Venture gear, while Jay got the Paradox gear.

 

"What is it with kids and guns...." Vance grumbled as Jay and Katie charged the Spider Queen with their new weapons blazing.

 

Two seconds later they were hiding behind the rocks with 0 armor.

 

"That was embarrassing," Jay panted.

 

"Indeed." Katie agreed, while Jay spawned an anvil which gradually supplied armor points to their charred gear.  Thirty seconds later they were at full health.

 

This time they planned a more coordinated attack.  During their initial attack, their memory banks had stored the data on the Spider Queen's battle tactics, which was to focus all weaponry on one target, eliminate it, then move on to the next.  Too bad her attacks were so slow, and her small brain was clouded with so much Maelstrom she didn't have much logic in her own memory.

 

Jay jumped over the rock and immediately came under fire, which he easily dodged by sidestepping two feet, while Katie began blasting away at the Spider Queen from behind.

‎01-22-2013 05:48 PM

Since their battle plan was better than the Spider Queen's, and they outnumbered the huge purple spider 2-1, it wasn't long before they forced the Spider Queen to retreat up the mountain.  Katie was just about to climb up after the Spider Queen when sudennly the rocks which they had taken cover behind only moments before exploded and out came....


Dark Spiderlings.

 

With their trial gear, they smashed the Distraction Spiderlings in only a few seconds.  After those few seconds, the Spider Queen jumped back down and sent everything flying, including Jay and Katie.  They quickly got up again and continued blasting away at the Spider Queen, who continued shooting at Jay.  They forced the overgrown spider up the mountain again, defeated the Spiderlings again, and then when it jumped down again it did something that wasn't again.

 

Turning its weapons vertically straight up, the Spider Queen fired several rounds to the cloudy sky.

 

A bunch of red circles appeared on the ground as the cannon rounds fell back to the ground, making sonic booms as they passed 700 MPH.  But what would never happen again after that was that all the red circles were centered on the Spider Queen.

 

The cannon rounds hit the ground, exploded, and took the Spider Queen with them.  And with the Spider Queen went the Maelstrom on this Block Yard.

 

The Spider Queen was smashed.

 

"Good show." Vance Bulwark applauded them as they returned. "Since you were the best -and the only- team I have ever seen take on the Spider Queen, you can keep the Trial Faction Gear.  Now all you have to do is impress me with your building skills and place down four models." He placed the models in front of him. "Models are prebuilt brick models-"

 

Katie grabbed the models, and she and Jay slammed them on the ground next to the mailbox.

 

"I'm even more impressed." Vance said. "I've never seen anyone place them outside the building area.  You truly are imaginative."

 

Then sudennly they were back at Veggie Works.
