Chapter 30

 

"But," Intrepid continued, "there is a way to make yourselves useful."

"And that is?" Jay asked.

 

"To put it simply, you can't do anything that has to do with fighting without your own gear." Intrepid explained. "You've haven't even gotten enough skill from me, but with the proper weaponry, armor, and stuff you can overlook that.  So we need to get you some gear."

"But Intrepid," Luke interrupted while patting his sword, "this stuff is vintag, there's no way to get them anymore.  And we can't part with our own gear because they're linked."

 

"'Linked'?" Katie repeated.


"Forget about that." Intrepid said quickly. "Actually Luke, there IS a way.  Though it's a very dangerous, deathly, and unconventional way. And it should give our two trainees some real training at the same time." He jerked his thumb in the direction of Jay and Katie, before proceeding to say to them, "Want to see Avant Gardens in its true pride and glory?"

 

...

A week had passed since the Maelstrom had literally resurfaced in Avant Gardens.  And at the start of that week Jay and Katie were back at their apartment outside Nimbus City.  And in the midst of that week, multiple things happened.

 

- The New Nexus Force military rose to power.

- Martial Law was declared by the New Nexus Force.

- Old veterans from the war 14 years before were back.

- Avant Gardens, Guppy Forest, Favorable Valley, Nexus City, and Pet Cove were all caught up in warfare.

- Everyone was doomed.

- AND IT WAS ALL JAY'S FAULT.  MWUAHAHAHAHAHAHAHA.

 

"It's not your fault Jay, really." Katie was trying to assure him. "It's not like anyone smart would leave a bunch of infected bricks in the middle of a museum."

 

Jay slumped deeper into his seat, and was about to retort something witty when the window next to them exploded.

Chaptah 31

 

"I apologize for all the broken windows," Intrepid said hurriedly as he lead Jay and Katie through the crowded streets of Nimbus City before stopping abruptly at an old rundown building.  He jammed a key into the flimsy door which had a faded picture of some vegetable on it and with a twist of his hand, the door swung open.  They stepped inside, with Intrepid closing the door behind them.

 

The building's interior was a far cry from its exterior appearance.  The walls were clean and whitewashed, and the dark windows outside were actually just heavily tinted to hide the stuff that littered a bunch of wooden tables throughout the brightly illuminated main room.

 

"Fascinating, isn't it?" Intrepid stated. "It's called Leek Works, established in late 2012 after the militaristic government collapsed.  All my coworkers, friends, and team members are here.  Myself, Luke, and Mara, the whole three of us." He snickered.

 

Jay posed a question. "What about that brown haired guy?"

 

Intrepid's eyebrows shot into the ceiling. "What brown haired guy?"

 

"From the Paradox Museum," Katie explained. "Back when the old Nexus Force leader guys were having a meeting.  You and some weird brown haired guy were walking with them."

 

"There wasn't any brown haired guy." Intrepid said.

 

Katie and Jay shot a glance at each other, which pretty much said drop it.  Silently, they followed Intrepid through another door into a weird room with a weird object in it that looked like a wormhole projector thing from some sci-fi movie.  At its sides doing some maintenance work were Luke and Mara.

 

"Alright, it's about time I gave you your instructions." Intrepid announced. "This is just an old wormhole projector thing thingy thingy that will, hehe, well, once Luke brings it online you just have to walk through it and follow the instructions you'll get along the way.  Super simple.  SO WALK THROUGH IT, MWAHAHAHA."

 

Then it turned on.

Chaptah 32

- Late 2011

 

All was quiet in the cabin of the Venture Explorer.  Commanding Officer Sky Lane, First Officer Bob, a random guard called Jett Moonshot, and some bored disposable orange shirts were the only minifigures awake on the whole ship.  The rest of the passengers, numbering in the infinite, were all in cyrogenic sleep somehow squeezed like sardines in some ten travel tubes near the engines at the back of the ship.  The only sound throughout the ship (asides from everyone's breathing) was the slow throbbing of the engine to a fearful tune: doom doom doom doom doom doom doom doom doom....

 

Then the forward window and the surrounding bulkheads exploded in a shower of glass and debris which were all sucked into space.

 

....

 

The Maelstrom attack had been brief, but with eternal damage to the Venture Explorer which would from then on remain in eternal, never ending doom.

 

An infinite supply of new Nexus Force recruits also flowed from the Venture Explorer.  And few people knew why.

 

Following protocal, those Nexus Force recruits had their Imagination unlocked early, were given thinking caps, built their own rockets, and blasted off the forever doomified starship to the world of Avant Gardens.

 

One such minifigure, called Quest Blake (or by his official Nexus Force name Happy Lucky Fish) was approaching Avant Gardens' eastern launch pad in his custom rocket like all the others before and after him.  But unlike all the others before and after him, he encountered an obstacle that he could never have expected ever.

 

Two minifigures fell onto his rocket in mid-flight.

Chaptah 33

 

Quest's expression looked like this O_________o for five seconds before his shock finally subsided and his voice box turned back on.  He let out a long scream of terror as he realized he had overshot the launch pad and was headed straight for the.... THE PARADOX MUSEUM!!!!

 

His two hitchhikers also realized their perilous course and jumped off seconds before impact.

 

With a mighty crash, the rocket struck the base of the building and to the dismay of a guy with funny spiky red hair who was blown away by the shock wave, a group of Stromlings wielding hammers swarmed out of the hole. "Not again!" he yelled. "Why does everybody always smash into my Paradox Lab!!"

 

Offering no help to Quest as he disentangled himself from the wreckage of his rocket, the guy - who's name was Wisp Lee - muttered something unintelligible and was about to return to his previous position when he remembered his job.

 

"Help!  I need help!" Wisp Lee screamed.

 

"Nooooooooooooooooo!!" Quest yelled. "Someone help me instead!  Get me out of this death trap!"

 

The two competed for help until Quest finally got out of his mess and stomped over to Wisp Lee, who quipped, "All of our research has been destroyed!  At least the Sentinels are-"

 

"Whatevers!" Quest grumbled. "My older bro told me about you and your annoying missions when he came back for winter vacation, I know everything about Avant Gardens and what to do already!  SEE YOU LATER."

 

He then proceeded to run towards Epsilon Starcracker the Nexus Force Scout and then get smashed by a Stromling.

 

Wisp Lee sighed and was about to take off his "Imah infected help meeeeee!" costume and get some fresh air when he noticed two more minifigures walking towards him.  So he resumed screaming, "Help!  I need help!"

 

Being good minifigures, Jay and Katie forgot about their own problem at hand and ran towards him.
