I am writing this while I am very very tired so please do not mind my numerous grammar and spelling mistakes.

 

~Chapter 51~

 

  Hundreds upon thousands of Maelstrom Monsters of every type flooded the ground two hundred floors below where Jay, Katie, and Intrepid stood.  And two hundred floors up from where the Maelstrom Monsters were was a fleet of about ten ROFLCOPTERS, all blaring out of their loudspeakers the Trololo song.

 

  "Ohhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhh, yoyoyoyo, yoyoyoooooyooooooooyoooyoo.  Hohohohohohooooooooo, yoyoyo, yoyoyoyoyoyoyoyo!"

 

  "Quickbuilds!" Intrepid screeched, and dove towards a pile of jumping bricks.  All across the floor were several piles of jumping bricks.  Jay and Katie quickly started building them, and soon an array of powerful looking rocket launchers littered their floor.

 

  The rocket launchers swiveled towards the windows which also opened up automaticaly, and homing missiles shot out of the tower and straight into each and every ROFLCOPTER, effectively obliterating them.

 

  However, not effectively obliterated were two silhouetted figures who managed to bail out of the exploding ROFLCOPTERS and launch a grappling hook through the open window.  And then be it on purpose or by mistake, no one will ever know why the grappling hook grabbed onto Intrepid and pulled him out the window into open-air, 3000 feet above the surface of Crux Prime so far below....

 

  For ten seconds, Jay and Katie stared silently, shocked, at the spot where their mentor, guide, and only friend in a suddenly changed world had stood only moments before.  Then two pairs of hands suddenly appeared over the edge of the building, and with a few fluid motions, le Maelstrom Clones of Jay and Katie stood before them.

 

  Who then made a mad rush for the center of the floor, which housed a huge glass tube spanning the basement all the way up to the 200th floor.  The glass tube in turn housed the most valuable of valuables.

 

The Imagination Nexus.

 

LE DUN DUN DUN.
