Jay and Katie stiffened as they searched their memory banks for an answer.  Instantly, popup windows started appearing over their vision, and Microsoft Sam's voice screaming error messages. "Computation of question 'what would happen if you went back in time and smashed yourself' failed.  Shutting down central processing unit to prevent physical damage. - Shutting down central processing unit failed-"

As Jay and Katie gasped and fell to their knees in Computer Death, Katie only managed to squeak out, "Flip the power switch!"

A Stromling jumped over and promptly flipped their power switches, and as Jay and Katie collapsed to the ground as they began rebooting, the Stromling hissed, "You owe me one."

Shrouded in steam, the elevators reached the floor again as Jay and Katie pulled themselves to their feet.  Rubbing his hands together in utter evilness, Typhonus Jr. announced, "The following experiment will give us an answer to everyone's favorite Ronin's question, though without the time travel part.  Such a shame." He jeered, "So Figdroids, can you defeat yourselves?"

The smoke cleared, and from the platforms stepped,
