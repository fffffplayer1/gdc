Chapter 58

 

The streets of Nimbus Grove never seemed so inviting.  Sitting in the backseat of a '12 Challenger 392 driven by Intrepid Fusion Eclipse, Jay sighed.  He turned away from the window and grinned at his sister.  In only a few moments, they would be home.

 

Home.  Ever since he and Katie had been taken under Leek Works' wing and lead the front line of the war against the Maelstrom Uprising, he hadn't given home a second thought.  Or even his dad.  He couldn't help but wonder how the reunion would go.  He knew Katie's thoughts were pretty much of the same context as his.

 

When the Challenger screeched to a screechy halt in front of the familiar brickwork house - and Intrepid cursed the brakes for squealing - a glance out the side window confirmed Jay's hopes.

 

This reunion wasn't just a happy reunion.  It was a chance for answers.

 

Intrepid unlocked the doors and his passengers tumbled out.  Standing in the front lawn was their dad, some familiar looking guy with brown hair, and another familiar looking guy.

 

It didn't take a second for Katie to speed over to Sandy Studs - the father - and embrace him in a hug.  Jay held back, and found himself absentmindly shaking Dr. Albert Overbuild's - the another familiar looking guy - hand.  He was more focused on the other guy, the familiar looking guy with brown hair.

 

"If I may introduce myself, Jay," he said, extending his own hand. "I am Fig, the second Figdroid Prototype and advisor to the Nexus Force leaders.  You could say I'm your older brother."

 

"Nice to meet you," Jay said, recalling how he was familiar.  They had seen him at the Paradox Museum, when they had been spying on the faction leaders.  He had been with them.  One more loose end tied, another loose end formed. "Wait, second?"

 

Dr. Overbuild sighed. "You know, the young man who claimed himself the new Darkitect?  Junior?"

Jay stiffened. "I'm listening."

 

"He was Figdroid Prototype #1, but he wasn't created by the Nexus Force, but by Baron Typhonus himself."

Chapter 59

 

Surprising himself with staying cool and not faltering, Jay said slowly, "So what you're telling me, I.... all of us," he waved his arm around to include Fig and Katie, who was now watching them with silent intentness, "are 'copied' from the Maelstrom?"

 

"In some ways," Dr. Overbuild answered, "but not all.  No offense to Fig, but there is one thing he lacks that we really needed to 'borrow' if you were to be true to your potential.  It was your Imagination."

 

Jay couldn't help but feel flabbergasted, and couldn't formulate a reply.  Katie came to his rescue with two syllables. "Say what?" she said to the doctor. "Repeat, please."

 

"When the weapon of Darkitect Junior was created, the logic center that Baron Typhonus personally installed went against our knowledge of Maelstrom logic, no pun intended." Overbuild somewhat explained. "It gave him the power of a natural minifigure's mind, and all of the Imagination with it." At Jay's and Katie's still perplexed expressions, Overbuild said, "Here's a hint, Imagination is what powers the power of emotion, again no pun intended."

 

"Oh...." Jay said. "Fig lacks emotion?"

 

Fig nodded. "Indeed, I do not have the particular weakness of emotion, weaklings."

 

"Hey!" Katie exclaimed, in mock offense.

 

"It was an attempt at a joke," Fig defended himself, turning to Katie. "I am not devoid of being able to sense emotion."

 

"Yeah I know, me too, somewhat." Katie said.

 

"Well, I just wanted to clear that up." Overbuild concluded. "Not even the mastermind behind the Maelstrom is without some form of Imaginative thinking.  When all is said and done, we still have a bit of Baron Typhonus to thank for helping to bring you wonderful two into the Universe." He stared at Jay and Katie each for a few long seconds. "And now it is time for us all to go home."

Chapter 60

Epiclogue

 

It was weird, Jay thought the next morning, how easy it was to return to 'normal' life.  Intrepid Fusion Eclipse, Grand Masterly Shadow, Elite Distant Tofu, all of their friends, Sandy Studs too, they had done a good job at maintaing his and Katie's anonymity throughout it all, so well that they were truly able to just pick up where they left off and get back to living the life they had prior to this adventure.

 

Their only public appearance had been to some wide eyed tourists and staff workers at Nexus Tower.  Otherwise, they were truly out of the public eye.

 

If anything thing had changed though, Jay had been promoted from bicycle mail man to ATV mail man.  As he gunned the 'well muffled' engine of a hot pink - Katie's fault - Mini four wheeler up a hill in the early dawn neighborhood of Nimbus Grove, a wave of nostalgia washed over him, heating up his heart.  He felt tears well  up in his eyes, everything was just so 'normal', and he had everyone to thank for it.

 

It.  Oh, it just felt so great to be back home.

 

 

The End.
