Chapter 23

 

Like the last night, Intrepid met up with Jay and Katie on the roof.  And this time, he was carrying a laptop bag with him.

 

"I wasted the whole of today reading your blueprints," Intrepid complained, "but then I found this in the back flap of the folder." He held up a small booklet with 'Programming Manual' written across it and waved it around.

 

Katie cringed. "Programming Manual?  You're going to reprogram us?"

 

"No way," Intrepid grinned. "It's much more simple than that." He retrieved a shiny black laptop from the aforementioned bag and turned it on, before connecting a special-looking USB cord to it. "It turns out you have a connector port here" - he touched the area behind his ear - "and if I connect this laptop to it, I can very simply check all the boxes for all of your awesome figdroid features and then you'll be awesome like mehself!"


Now Jay cringed. "So you're going to plug us into that?  Wont we get electrocuted?"

 

"Do you get electrocuted whne you drink a glass of water?" Intrepid answered with a question.

 

"Uh, no?"

 

"There you have it." Intrepid told him. "Who wants to experience awesomeness first?"

 

...

 

 

 

'Awesomeness' translated to increased processor speed, increased movement speed, increased agility, and pretty much awesome SUPAHPOWAHS!!! controlled from an awesome Heads Up Display.

 

"Wow...." Jay gasped.

 

"Whoa." Katie gawked.

 

"If it's too overwhelming, you can just switch it off." Intrepid assured them. "Thought control is awesome."

 

It worked. "Thanks," Jay told him.

 

"No prob." Intrepid said with a smirk. "So, you guys ready for the fighting test again?"

 

...

 

"CHEEEEEEEEAAAAAAAAAAAATTTTTTTTEEEEEEEEEERRRRRRRRRSSSSSSSSSS!!!!!!!!!!!" Intrepid screamed less than ten minutes later, scaring Jay and Katie so much they jumped out of the position they were in to pin Intrepid to the ground.

 

And that pretty much sums up how the test went.
