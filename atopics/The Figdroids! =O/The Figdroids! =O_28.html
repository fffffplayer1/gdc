Chapter 44

 

They came from the sky.  Bombs!  Bombs everywhere, around the fort, inside the fort, in the trees, on the Maelstrom, and the eXpL0s10nS!  Everything looked like it had a gigantic bonfire sticking out its top before they realized that no, the Maelstrom hadn't bombarded them and set them on fire.  It was the Maelstrom on fire, with hundreds of Stromlings smashing within seconds.  They were saved!

 

At least for awhile.

 

Intrepid, Luke, Mara, Jay, and Katie turned to face their saviour, and in doing so Intrepid paled immensely. "NOT YOU!" he gasped.

 

A minifigure in the gear of a Rank 7 Paradox Space Marauder stood before them in all her awe and glory. "Miss me much?" she said sweetly.

 

Intrepid facepalmed. "Get out of here, Shirley." he groaned.

 

Shirley's face turned sour. "You're mean, I don't like you anymore." Shirley pouted, and was about to turn away when Luke clamped his hand over Intrepid's mouth and interjected, "Hey wait, we could use your help...."

 

"But she's a traitor!" Intrepid whined, wrenching Luke's hand off his face. "She betrayed the F2P Legion and joined a faction-"

 

Luke's glare cut Intrepid's sentence into pieces and he growled, "Look at things from a strategic position for once Intrepid.  And I know the real reason you're being so difficult.  Family rivalry with your cousin, eh?"

 

"Family rivalry?!" Jay exclaimed.

 

"COUSIN?!" Katie yelped. "You have family Intrepid?!"

 

Intrepid rolled his eyes. "Obviously," he grumbled.

 

"How about we wrap up with the introductions," Mara suggested, "And get ready for the next batch of enemies?  I can hear Dragons in the distance...."

 

"Alright, long story short," Intrepid started, "SHIRLEY AKA SHRILL FAILED BRICK IS MAH COUSIN AND WE HATE EACH OTHER!!!!!!!!!!!!  The end."

 

It was a good thing they took Mara's advice, because a hole was ripped through the wall of trees in front of the fort, and from within stepped the most fearsome steed of the Maelstrom.

 

The Dragon Invader.

Chapter 45

 

In only a few moments, there were twenty of them in all with the other 980 someplace else.  All Dragon Invaders, and all packing fatal, lethal, and deadly speed, agility, and firepower.

 

Intrepid, Luke, Mara, Shirley, Jay, and Katie all stood with their weapons in front of them, challenging the Dragons to make the first move.  Yet they just stood around the fort, ready to obliterate them at any instant, yet instead waiting....

 

"What are they waiting for?" Katie whispered.

 

Nobody responded for the next ten seconds of nervousness, until Intrepid answered, "For a command."

 

"But that's impossible," Mara protested. "Chaos has no master-"

 

OR DOES IT?

 

And then abruptly, 19 of the Dragon Invaders took off and flew away into the clouds, leaving the six helpless minifigures to face the first one.


And its master.

 

With a swift leap through the air, a figure that had previously been seated upon the Dragon was now standing in front of the Dragon, and in front of those six helpless minifigures.  He was clad in a dark hooded robe that concealed his face, the fabric as black as a moonless midnight sky.

 

But what could be scarier, than the Staff of Doom in his hands?

 

Intrepid, Luke, Mara, and Shirley gasped.  They knew of the powers of deadly doom within that staff, and Intrepid and Shirley had been able to witness them first hand only 12 years ago, when it was in the hands of DEH DARKITECT!!!!


Shirley gasped."It's-"

 

"IMPOSSIBRU!!!!!" Intrepid finished.

 

The voice boomed, ricocheting across the forest and then coming back again. "Anything is possible," it cackled, "with the power of the Maelstrom in my hands!  MWAHAHAHAHAHAHAHAHAHAHAHAHAHAHAHAHAHAHAHAHAHAHAHAHAHAHAHAHAHAHAHAHAHA- *cough*"

 

With a flourish, deh Darkitect spun around and flung his hood down to reveal his face.  Intrepid sighed with relief.


He couldn't have been much older than Jay really.  His hair was long and brown, his face screaming determination, and his identity unmistaken. "I'm Typhonus Junior, your DOOM!"
