@Chapter 35: I know a much better way to defeat the Spider Queen (although you used a part of it):

  1. You run towards the Spider Queen "forgetting" to put on your trial gear.
  2. You attack the Spider Queen.
  3. You lose all your armour and maybe some of your life.
  4. You hide behind a rock.
  5. You smash the rock.
  6. You collect the loot that come out of the rock.
  7. With repaired armour and full life, you attack again.
  8. You repeat steps 3-7 until the Spider Queen is smashed.
  9. You celebrate.
  10. You eat a whole cake yourself.
  11. You eat three more cakes.
  12. You leave because you are still hungry and you are out of cakes.

This is pretty much how I did it when I palyed LEGO Universe (excluding parts 10-12). However, that was with my Free to play player Magical Fire Monkey. When I used Strange Odd Shadow, my first and favourite player, who was not free to play (I had bought the Game and played the free month, but then my time expired and I couldn't find a way to buy more without going in another country, something I couldn't do. That's why I created a second free to play account), it was before the existence of the Spider Queen. Then, you claimed your property when you defeated some simple enemies. From what I remember there were a lot of them. Then, while fighting, you built an elevator thingy and went up on it. There, you found an Imagination Thingy and by taking it you kicked out all the Maelstrom in your property. As you see I don't remember it very well, but it was something like that.

Chaptah 36


"Congratulations!" a voice cheered from behind them, and Jay and Katie whirled around to face Intrepid. "Over the course of a week, you've achieved Level 15, gained lots of gear and weapons, Permanent Rank 2 Faction Gear, and quite a bit of experience in combat, building, and flying rockets.  It's my honest opinion that you two are now hereby ready to assist in Leek Works' grandeous Operation Takeback!"

 

"Hold it, slow down a bit." Luke called from where he was seated at a computer console. "First we've got to give a littl background info on what's happened the past days, then we'll explain Operation Takeback...."

 

....

 

During the 7 days of Jay and Katie' absense, the Nexus Force was completely back in power and all worlds (except for Nimbus Station) were a battleground, with Avant Gardens hit the hardest.  And Operation Takeback revolved around the elite team that Leek Works was landing at Avant Gardens and striking as the heart of the Maelstrom presense: The Paradox Research Lab.

 

Operation Takeback had three major parts to it.

1) Flying the official Leek Works modified C-17 Globemaster through the thick Maelstrom air defenses to the Paradox Lab.

2) Landing at the Paradox Lab and planting bombs in the basement.

3) Escaping on the Globemaster as the 4th of July begins early.

 

It was decided that Intrepid would fly the C-17 through the dangerous Stage 1, then Mara would take over controls while Intrepid, Luke, Jay, and Katie went down.  They would blast their way through the Paradox Lab, plant the bombs at the basement, then return to the C-17 which Mara would have to land.  AND THEN THEY ESCAPE.

 

And being the impatient, hasty group Leek Works was, they would be leaving for Operation Takeback immediatly.
