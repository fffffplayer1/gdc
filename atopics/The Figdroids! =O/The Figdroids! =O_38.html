Chapter 54

 

  The second Jay and Katie stepped into the basement, the elevator doors behind them slammed shut, and the elevator itself rocketed to some other floor far away.

 

  The elevator had opened up to a dark and dank basement that wasn't very well maintained.  Like the basement at the Paradox Lab, there were random parts strewed around the room on steel tables, but unlike the former, they didn't appear to be mech parts.  They were something else, but Jay and Katie couldn't place it, because their thoughts were primarily on the feeling of forebodement and doom that came with entering this room.

 

  "Welcome," a creepy voice said. "To the end!"

 

  Brandishing a scary looking sword, Darkitect Jr. stepped out from the shadows.  He spun the sword around in front of him, each swish accompanied by a whooshing sound as it cut through the air.  His movements didn't stop upon leaving the shadows, he was walking straight for where Jay and Katie stood.

 

  The two Figdroids gave each other a brief glance that said 'Stall him'.  "By end," Jay started, "you mean our end?"

 

  Darkitect Jr. paused walking, and answered, "I thought you would have figured that out by yourselves." He smirked, and slashed his sword into a table next to him.  The sword cut right through it, steel and all. "Yes, I do mean your end, and I intend to bring it to you." He continued walking.

 

  "That's not fair." Katie protested. "You have an overpowered sword."

 

  Jr. paused again, and his expression turned into a frown. "I find it justified, there are two of you."

 

  "Yeah," Katie continued, "but you've got your equally overpowered lightning powers or whatever they're called."

 

  "True." Darkitect Jr. lowered his sword and scratched at an imaginary beard at his chin.  Whether he was feinting nonconcentration or not, Jay and Katie instanty charged towards his cloaked form and rammed him.

 

  It was probably legitimate, because he ended up on the floor, his sword clattering to the ground some yards away.

Chapter 55

 

 

  It didn't take two kicks to send Jay and Katie flying off Darky, (I'm going to call him that for now) knocking down several tables, and into the elevator shaft doors, denting them.  It took one.

 

  With surprising agility and speed, Darky was up on his feet with the sword in his hands. "Nice try," he cackled, aiming it at the metal behind them.

 

  Jay and Katie dove behind an overturned table each as lightning shot out of Darky's sword, obliterating the doors to the elevator shaft and a huge portion of the room around it.  To their surprise, another room was visible through the hole in the floor.

 

  A room full of grotesque looking Stromlings.  But instead of glowing purple, they glowed red.  They were inside stasis tubes, though.

 

  Even Darky looked sickened at the sight of them.

 

  Taking this momentary distraction, Jay and Katie jumped up from over their tables and landed on Darky again.  The sword fell from his hands and landed in Katie's, who proceeded to take a swipe at him.

 

  There was a loud explosion, from which Jay, Katie, and Darkitect Jr. emerged battered, charred, but not majorly hurt.  Except Darky was missing an arm.

 

  At the place of his left shoulder were several wires and a blackened metal joint.  With a gasp, Jay and Katie looked at Darky, then at each other, and then back. "You're a Figdroid too!" Jay exclaimed.

 

  "No," Darkitect Jr. spat. "'Figdroid' is the name used for the Nexus Force project 14 years ago.  I'm earlier than that."

 

  "Then what are you really?" Katie pressed, picking up the sword and aiming it at Darky's chest. "We're not done with you."

 

  At this, Darkitect Jr. started laughing.  First subtly, then louder, and finally an all out maniacal crazy laugh.  When it finished five minutes later, he said to a very impatient Jay and Katie, "The Nexus Force is but a band of thieves.  I truly am Maelstrom, a project of the Darkitect himself.  The Nexus Force stole my schematics and built two clone weapons to stop me.  You."

Chapter 56

 

  "We know that," Katie snapped.

 

  Darky Jr. raised his eyesbrows. "But how much do you really know?" It was pretty obvious by Katie's and Jay's facial expressions that they were interested.  They were listening. "How do you plan on stopping me?" he challenged. "If you cut me apart, I'll still be alive.  As you might have realized already, you and me alike are built to be invincible."

 

  With his right hand, he patted his chest. "Solid carbon fiber fused with neutronium.  Invicible to nuclear explosoins." He pointed at Jay, "Your face too, unless the Nexus Force has decided to go the inexpensive route, and use cheaper materials for building you."

 

  "So you're telling us," Katie continued, "you're invulnerable to all means of weaponry, conventional and unconventional.  And there's a possibility that Jay and I aren't."

 

  "Oh no, it's not just that." Darky Jr. grinned. "Which one of you is supposed to be 'older'?"

 

  "Me." Jay said.

 

  Darky's grin widened. "There you have it.  In the period of us talking, you've given me enough time to analyze the materials used in your interior frames." He pointed at Jay, "You, Jay, are like me.  Well, physically." He pointed at Katei, "You on the other hand, are built out of lighter, less durable materials.  Which means-"

 

  Lightning started to brew around Darky's fingertips, which were still aimed at Katie. "-you're not invincible, and I could destroy you right now."

 

  It would have been Jay's insticts to dive in front of his sister and protect her from the lightning, but his legs were glued to the ground shock.  Katie however seemed unfazed. "So I'm lighter?"

 

  "Yep."

 

  "Good."

 

  In the split second that followed, Katie jumped into the air, bounced off the cieling, and kicked Darkitect Jr. backwards.  A split second later, Jay's legs became unstuck, but his nerves were still messed up, so he ended up careening towards Darky and knocking him through the hole in the ground into the room below.

 

  And the Stromlings awoke.

Chapter 57

 

  The stasis tubes holding the green Stromlings automatically opened when they felt the impact of Darkitect Jr. on the floor in their midst, and they blew open, the foggy vapor inside them billowing out.  Out of the fog came the Stromlings, who in every which way just seemed wrong.

 

  The obvious wrong was that they were green.  The next wrong was that they walked extremely slowly and made extremely long, slow, growling sounds.  And the parts of them that were green was really just green slime, which dripped off of them with every step.

 

  Jay's mouth dropped open, and Katie said, "Zombie Stromlings!"

 

  Then, with immense speed and ability, they pounced on Darky Jr.  The sound of his screams mixed with the long, mournful noises of the Stromlings was absolutely horrible, and Jay and Katie could do nothing to keep it out of their ears.

 

  A flash of purple lightning and an explosion later, and suddenly the sounds of Stromlings dissipated.  Jay opened one eye, and there, standing in the middle of the room below, was Darkitect Jr. covered in the green slime, with the vapor clouds around him, smirking at Jay and Katie above. "Nice try," he jeered. "You think a little Nexus Force experiment could stop me?"

 

  "Nope." Jay and Katie said together, their composure regained.

 

  "So that was all just stalling of time?" Darkitect Jr. said, before laughing maniacally. "That's all you can do, waste time?"

 

  "Actually," Jay corrected him, eyeing the vapor clouds that was still closing in around Darkitect Jr., "I think you might want to know, but you're the one wasting time."

  "You're doing it right now," Katie quipped.

 

  Darkitect Jr.'s brow furrowed in thought, and the vapor continued to inch closer. "A little green slime wont hurt me, what the brick are you talking abou-"

 

  With that last sentence, he wasted the last little bit of time he had left.  The vapor, which was water vapor, made contact with his exposed arm circuitry and Darkitect Jr. shorted out.
