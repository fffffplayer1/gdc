Chapter 48

 

  As sudden as the Darkitect himself rising from the dead, a massive eXpL0s1oN rocked the auditorium.  Everyone except Jay's and Katie's lost their sense of hearing as more eXpL0s1oNs radiated out from the source of the eXpL0s1oNs.

 

  The eXpL0s1oN had come from a massive generator in a corner of the auditorium, which had eXpL0dEd.  Smoke poured from it, making everyone except Jay and Katie lose their sight.

 

  "Thank me later!" a familiar voice screamed so loud to be heard it was high-pitched, and the mysterious Stromling was back.  This time he had earmuffs pressed against the sides of his heads, and he was yanking at the fake sword on his arm.  INTREPID FUSION ECLIPSE.

 

  Grabbing Jay and Katie by the arms, he pulled them towards some random place.  The Figdroids had no idea how Intrepid could see through the smoke, but at least they could see enough to know that the 'some random place' was an employee door with EXIT written in bright red letters.

 

  The instant they stepped over the threshhold, they were suddenly on Crux Prime with Nexus City not so far away on the horizon and a shiny yellow Corvette very close to them in the foreground.  A whirling sound from behind caught Jay's attention, and he spun around to see absolute nothingness. "wut." was all he could muster.

 

  "Explanations, explanations," Intrepid chanted. "What we are standing in front of is a cloaked building called 'The New Maelstrom Headquarters' after we blew up the old one some 14 years ago or something like that.  What nobody except us knows is that it's right here outside civilization.  Also what nobody except us knows is that you left your I-Brick on, and that's how I could track you." Intrepid held up a pink I-Brick, and Katie snatched it.

 

  "And finally the last thing I'm going to say," he continued, "the Imagination Nexus over thar" - he pointed towards Nexus City - "is about to be stolen.  GET IN THA CAR."

Chapter 49

 

  After fifteen seconds of fire extinguishers and advanced ventilation systems, the auditorium in the New Maelstrom HQ To Replace The Old One That Was Destroyed 14 Or So Years Ago was cleared of smoke and noise.  Typhonus Jr.'s eardrums were still ringing, but he had enough composure to take advantage of the failure of unacceptable odds in the face of the coming threat to all of minifigure kind.

 

  Gesturing towards a picture of a mustachioed Dr. Overbuild, Typhonus Jr. mocked, "This man built these Figdroids as the key to defending that horrible distraction called 'Imagination'.  Then he had his assistant Sandy Studs play Daddy to them while they waited for meeeeeeeeeeee to come along and make my move.  The only problem is, after I made my move of hiding some Maelstrom Infected Bricks in the Paradox Lab, they didn't do anything except the take bait.  I don't care for Avant Gardens.  I'm taking Crux Prime.  NAOW.  MWAHAHAHAHAHAHAHAHAHA." he monotoned. "Get the Clone Army online and unleash them."

 

....

 

  It is very unwise to squish three people within a $25,000 two-seat sportscar.  Which is why the bumpy ride back to Nexus City was very uncomfortable for all three occupants of Intrepid's Corvette.

 

  "Why did you bring a two-seater!" Katie hollered to be heard over 6000 RPMs.

 

  "Because I expected to bring back only one of you." Intrepid replied.  Jay and Katie stared at each other in shock. "What, was it something I said?" Then his face turned pale as he faced the reflection of something so horrible in the rear view mirror.

 

  Very faintly heard over the engine was the ominous chanting of the song "Oh I am so glad to be home" or something like that. "Trolololololololololololololololol" whirled the horrible something, and Intrepid screamed.

 

  "IT'S A ROFLCOPTER!!!!!  RUUUUUUUUUUUUUUUUUUUUUUUUUUUUUUUUUUUUN!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!"
