Chaptah 38

 

Jay and Katie, who had been hiding in the trees for those seconds of minutes of epic fighting decided to get to work.  Jay glanced at Katie, who winked at him.

 

With a gulp, Jay swung out his own Wormholer (courtesy of Leek Works) and firing it straight into the middle of the Maelstrom, double-jumped from his perch and landed just to the left side of the army, sucessfully diverting their attention from trying to infect Intrepid, Luke, and Mara who needed to reload their own Wormholers.

 

After that long runon sentence, every single Stromling set its glowing red eyes upon Jay.  The Stromling Mechs' eyes not only glowed, but shone laser beams into Jay's face.  Then the lead mech stepped forwards, and Jay groaned.

 

"PRDX-4 F4C14L R3C0GN1T10N T35T R3SULT5 R3C0GN1T10N 0F F1GDR01D J4Y.  H3LL0 C0US1N." Paradox Robot Defense X-4 monotoned. "N0W PR3P4R3 T0 D13."

 

"Oh noes!" Jay screamed as all the mechs opened fire on him.  He intended to use his super figdroid legs to leap five feet into the air, but ended up bumping his head on a low hanging branch.  He grabbed it and held tight, avoiding the first stream of Great Balls Of Purple, but the Stromling Mechs recorrected their aim and their next stream of Great Balls Of Purple hit him straight in the chest.

 

He fell flat on his back to the ground, unhurt except that all the wind was knocked out of him.  However, he recovered superquickly and dove behind a rock for cover, barely missing the next stream of Great Purple Balls.

 

But being shot at by every single mech was his idea.  Because they didn't notice Katie sneaking up behind them, and by the time they did half their Maelstrom Patrol had already fallen from the immense fire of a Wormholer.

 

By that time, their teammates were back in the fight and with the added firepower of Intrepid, Luke, and Mara, the Maelstrom Patrol was defeated.

 

But they had more to do.

Chaptah 39

 

The lobby doors slid open and five silhouettes stepped inside.  Since it was a night operation, there was no illumination at all within the walls of the Paradox Lab.


Except for the long red lasers that signified the arrival of more Stromling Mechs.  The sound of mechanical footsteps grew louder with each passing second, and then the mechs stepped in from the back hallway.

 

Wormholer bullets and Firecrackers impacted all around the mechs, until they were completely obliterated.  The team stepped into the hallway.

 

Intrepid replaced his Wormholer and put on his Bat Lord Gear, while Luke and Mara pulled out Elite Cleavers. "We'll be in close combat here, it's too risky to use a Wormholer." Luke explained.


Jay nodded, and he and Katie pulled out their own swords.  They navigated through a maze of corridors and pathways, all the while looking out for Maelstrom.  Being awesome Figdroids, Jay and Katie could already detect Maelstrom thingies by just looking through walls.  Strangely, the halls were clear.

Shortly after, they were once again back at the basement.  The walls were completely covered in purple goo, and the support beams creaked and seemed to be crumbling.  All the old machinery that covered all the desks now had a weird purple aura around it.  Mech factory?

 

"Bomb time." Mara announced, and began assembling a large device from pieces from her backpack.  As soon as she put in the last piece, a display appeared on it and Mara set to work setting the bomb.

 

In addition to that happening, an alarm rang and sudennly all the machine pieces turned into Stromling Mechs.  But these weren't normal Stromling Mechs.  They still had their blasters, but they were of a more advanced appearance.  And in their left hands they held lances with spikes lining the forward half.  They also were more armored with shoulder, arm, and leg armor pads and heavier plating across their chests and heads.

 

"Uh oh!"

Chaptah 40

 

The mechs charged, and it was everything Jay, Katie, Intrepid, and Luke could do to keep these new Maelstrom toys from ruining their mission.  Despite all the Slashing and poking away with their swords and, uh, staff, nothing seemed to be working.


"Don't let them near deh bomb!" Intrepid screamed, slamming his shield into the sword hand of the closest mech and then stabbing at its face.  His bat staff merely bounced off, and the mech responded by swinging its gun barrel to Intrepid's head.  He ducked at the last second and detonated a flash bang, which stunned every mech in the room but only for a few seconds.

 

Everyone went all-out on the dazed Stromling Mechs, but their weapons bounced off their armored bodies harmlessly.  Luke began rewiring one of his flash bangs. "Now everybody close your eyes!" he yelled, and quickly tightened one wire before throwing it into the center of the mechs.

 

Even though their eyes were closed, Jay's and Katie's optical sensors still detected an immensely white flash of light, but unlike Intrepid and Luke who got blinded, they weren't hurt and neither was Mara, since she was hiding behind the bomb.  The mechs however all broke into a million pieces again just from the sheer power of IMMEEEEEENSELY WHIIIIIIIITE LIIIIIIIGHT!!!!!!!

 

"Blinded by the light!" Intrepid sang, and Luke socked him.

 

Then a Stromling Bomb Squad dropped from the ceiling and ran for the bomb.  Jay and Katie jumped into action and quickly dispatched them, taking the bait.

 

Half the farthest wall smashed open, and as dramatic opera sudennly blasted for ten seconds, black light began flowing into the room.  And enter the biggest Maelstrom arachnid any Nexus Force soldier had ever seen, and would never see again after their next and last few seconds of their lives.

 

The Scorpion Queen.

 

And on its back was a cloaked figure.


"Yayz I'm not blinded anymore!" Intrepid cheered. "Oh wait, no, I'm not happy anymore.... AH GIANT ARACHNIDZ!!!!!! HEEEELPZZZ!!"
