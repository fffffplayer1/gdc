Chapter 46

 

Intrepid's eyebrows jumped so high they almost hovered above his head like in Dora the Explorer. O___o "I hope you are serious." he jeered at Typhonus Jr.

 

In response, Typhonus Jr. erupted in another fit of cackles. "You'd be surprised," he wheezed, and with only a touch split the Staff of Doom down the middle.  Maelstrom fog oozed out of the jagged crack, then being unleashed in full upon the environment as the two halves fell to the ground.

 

Cleverly hidden within the Staff of Doom, was a Katana of Doom.  Carved from a block of the strongest pure Maelstrominite Mineral with a blade so sharp its ends were nearly invisible, and complete with all the powers of the old Staff of Doom multiplied by some random number, the Katana of Doom was quite intimidating not just as a sword, but as a weapon of mass destruction, its overall strength rivalling that of an Atomic bomb.  Combined with a guy claiming to be DEH DARKITECT's son, you have five Atomic bombs.  It most most certainly was a Katana of DOOM.

 

As soon as the words left Typhonus Jr.'s mouth two paragraphs ago, his swung the Katana of Doom and a lightning bolt extended from its tip, headed straight at the group of good guys.

 

Each good guy did different things.

 

1) Jay ran to the right.

2) Katie ran to the left.

3) Luke ran to the right and spawned an Anvil of Armor.

4) Mara ran to the left and spawned a Fountain of Imagination.

5) Shirley launched several rockets and ran to the left.
6) Intrepid spawned a Cauldron of Life and threw a shield around himself.

 

At the exact same time Intrepid's shield activated around him, the lightning bolt happened to just make it into that space that the shield would occupy.  With a massive flash and then the smell of burnt stuff, Intrepid's shield had suddenly become a grotesque thingy, a sphere with ugly blue and purple lines running across it.  And it was rooted to the ground, leaving Intrepid not just protected from the outside, but trapped inside too.


IT'S A TRAP!!!
