Chapter 42

 

A blinding blue flash illuminated the whole of Avant Gardens from the Paradox Lab, and the wind picked up to immense speeds as all the Maelstrom in the air was sucked into the whirlwind of blue.  Then as quickly as it started, the wind returned to calm speeds and all of a sudden Avant Gardens was the bright green happy place it had been before.


Intrepid, Luke, and Mara all cheered and started dancing. "We did it, we did it, we did it, YEAH!!!  Thumbs up to that!!"

 

Mission Sucessful?  Yes, this stage of it.  Compared to what would happen next, things were only starting to heat up....

 

* * * * *

 

Since then, Leek Works has commenced and completed several more operations across the Nimbus System, with the Nexus Force never really realizing that they had some elite help from behind enemy lines.  Over a course of nine missions, Avant Gardens was cleared, Guppy Forest was cleared, Favorable Valley was cleared, and the final mission before Leek Works would take a break and let the Nexus Force finish was in the Rocky Forest of Rocks at Crux Prime, a few miles out of Nexus City.

 

But the most bizarre thing about these missions was not the impracticality and constant threat of perpetual doom, (Ha!  A pun. ) but the weird bosses the team would encounter in the final stages of each engagement.  First it was that overgrown Scorpion Queen, then in the Guppy Forest campaign they had to do battle with a massive steroidal Ape.  Favorable Valley unveiled the largest Dragon ever seen, larger than Butterscorch even.  And all three of them had been ridden by a mysterious hooded figure.

 

So, where did these super bosses come from?  Who's the mysterious hooded figure?  Why am I making puns about Perpetual Doom?  All questions will be answered....

 

On Crux Prime.

Chapter 43

 

The Nexus Force was stretched thin across four major battlegrounds around Nexus City and its famous tower called, erm, Nexus Tower.  And in a daring spy and recon mission, it had been learned that only too soon there would be a fifth battleground too.

 

Spread much too thin already, there would be no way the Nexus Force would be able to repulse this fifth strike.  And not just any normal strike, but an invasion strike.

 

This would be the final hammer blow of the war, and the Maelstrom would prevail.

 

Unless the Invasion Force, consisting of

  • Ten thousand assorted Stromlings.
  • Five thousand Stromling Mech invaders.
  • Four thousand Dark Spiderling invaders.
  • Three thousand Dark Ronin invaders.
  • Fifteen hundred Ape invaders.
  • One thousand Dragon invaders.
  • And one very big boss.

was to be stopped.

 

By an elite team called Leek Works.

 

....

 

The air of Crux Prime was thick with impending doom.  And an eternal doom would come befall from the sky, if the most elite team of Nexus Force renegades EVER was to fail.

 

And in a situation like this, with supplies running low and only the benefit of numbers, overwhelming weapons fire, superior armor, and advanced technology at their disposable, the only way to make a sizable impact on the Maelstrom army was to attack it straight forward and bluntly.

 

Attack it they did.

 

"PREPARE TO BE PERPETUALLY DOOMED!!!" Intrepid screamed, and he, Jay, Katie, Luke, and Mara leapt from their hiding spot behind some random tree and unleashed the power of five Wormholers; two waves of Flash Bangs, Firecrackers, and Big Ones; and two Paradox Robot Defense mechs flanking them upon the overwhelming force of 49001 Maelstrom land and air soldiers.

 

The very basic of Stromlings got to eat the ground within a second of the bombardment.  The next two seconds involved even more basic stromlings falling, and the next three seconds had the stronger and faster Chainsaw and Hammer Stromlings charging the elitest of teams and engaging them in melee combat.

Part 2

 


A chainsaw ripped through the tip of Jay's wormholer, disconnecting the chain and ending its stream of bullets.  Still, it was a heavy thing, and Jay quickly swung it into the evil Chainsaw Stromling that dared approach him.  With an awesome whack, the Chainsaw Stromling was smashed.

 

Mara detontaed a Flash Bang, blinding the swarm of Stromlings in their tracks before they could do more damage.  Luke lobbed a Big One at them, and with a mighty explosion the Stromlings and Jay and Katie who were unfortunately standing right next to them were blasted away, though the latter two survived since they are invincible to conventional weapons.


Still, there was another group of Chainsaw and Hammer Stromlings fastly approaching.  Intrepid provided cover fire over Jay's and Katie's heads as they backtracked to where Mara had the Paradox Mechs set up a small barrier which they could take cover behind.

 

"How many of them have we taken out?!" Jay yelled.

 

"Fifty more or less, then I lost count!!" Intrepid replied. "And who said fighting was easy?!" He reached down into the bag of Firecrackers and grabbed three, which he tossed all at the same time into the mass of chainsaws and hammers.  The ones within ten feet of the impact centers went "poof".

 

An hour of battle progressed, in which everything heated up.  It was almost completely a gunfight, with Stromling Mechs taking cover behind nearby trees and firing into Mara's fort.  Twice, Dark Ronin and Spiderlings had attacked from the rear and Dragons could be heard in the distance.  Mobility was extremely limited, with mech shots whizzing overhead every other second.  The Paradox Mechs had been wiped out, and they were down to two Wormholers, no Big Ones, five Firecrackers, ten Flash Bangs, and little hope for survival.

 

Go down fighting?  Most certainly.
