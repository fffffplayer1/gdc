Chapter 59

 

Surprising himself with staying cool and not faltering, Jay said slowly, "So what you're telling me, I.... all of us," he waved his arm around to include Fig and Katie, who was now watching them with silent intentness, "are 'copied' from the Maelstrom?"

 

"In some ways," Dr. Overbuild answered, "but not all.  No offense to Fig, but there is one thing he lacks that we really needed to 'borrow' if you were to be true to your potential.  It was your Imagination."

 

Jay couldn't help but feel flabbergasted, and couldn't formulate a reply.  Katie came to his rescue with two syllables. "Say what?" she said to the doctor. "Repeat, please."

 

"When the weapon of Darkitect Junior was created, the logic center that Baron Typhonus personally installed went against our knowledge of Maelstrom logic, no pun intended." Overbuild somewhat explained. "It gave him the power of a natural minifigure's mind, and all of the Imagination with it." At Jay's and Katie's still perplexed expressions, Overbuild said, "Here's a hint, Imagination is what powers the power of emotion, again no pun intended."

 

"Oh...." Jay said. "Fig lacks emotion?"

 

Fig nodded. "Indeed, I do not have the particular weakness of emotion, weaklings."

 

"Hey!" Katie exclaimed, in mock offense.

 

"It was an attempt at a joke," Fig defended himself, turning to Katie. "I am not devoid of being able to sense emotion."

 

"Yeah I know, me too, somewhat." Katie said.

 

"Well, I just wanted to clear that up." Overbuild concluded. "Not even the mastermind behind the Maelstrom is without some form of Imaginative thinking.  When all is said and done, we still have a bit of Baron Typhonus to thank for helping to bring you wonderful two into the Universe." He stared at Jay and Katie each for a few long seconds. "And now it is time for us all to go home."
