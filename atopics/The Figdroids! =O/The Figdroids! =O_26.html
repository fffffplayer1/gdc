Chaptah 41

 

The Scorpion Queen reared up on its hind-most legs and emitted a loud shriek that could be heard even by the deaf, before turning control back to gravity and smashing its forelegs into the ground.  A mini-earthquake rumbled through the Paradox Lab, and all the work tables turned to dust.

 

"Now how do we smash that thing?!" Jay yelled amidst shriekes and hisses from the Scorpion Queen as it stomped its way towards them.

 

"Well-" Luke started.

 

Katie interrupted him. "Simple, we don't!"

 

"That's not what I was going to say!" Luke shot back. "Once get the bomb triggered, this whole place will go up in flames and that Scorpion thingy with it!"

 

"AND WHAT ABOUTZ US?!?!" Intrepid screamed. "WERZ ALL GONNA DIE!!!!"

 

Luke slammed the blunt end of his sword into Intrepid's helmet, silencing him. "Set the bomb for two minutes, Mara." he ordered. "Now let's get that Scorpion outtta the way!"

 

Luke lobbed fifteen turbocharged Flash Bangs one after the other directly at the Scorpion Queen's face, and it exploded on impact.  Again, white light filled the room and for a few horrible seconds, the Scorpion Queen let out a screech louder than the previous ones.  Then it charged towards where the group had been, but ended up smashing its head and its stinger head-on into the wall.

 

"Go!" Luke screamed, and everyone piled out through the hole on the opposite wall, leaving the Scorpion Queen and its blinded rider with the bomb.  The hole lead into a large tunnel which after a few feet began sloping upwards.

 

The Scorpion Queen's shriekes still echoed through the air as they emerged from a door in a small building some hundred feet away from the Paradox Lab.  Intrepid, having regained his normalness nodded towards the hills above them and stated, "Climb."

 

Two minutes later, they were standing at the top of the nearest hill with their eyes set on the flaming ruins of the Paradox Research Facility.
